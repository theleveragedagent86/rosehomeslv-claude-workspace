## Is Your Home Priced Too High for the Current Market?

The number one reason homes sit on the market in Las Vegas is overpricing. Even 5% above market value can dramatically reduce the number of showings and offers. Buyers today have access to the same data agents do, and they know when a home is priced above comparable sales in the neighborhood.

A comparative market analysis can reveal exactly where your home should be priced. If your home has been on the market for more than 30 days without a single offer, pricing is almost certainly a factor worth examining.

## Are Your Listing Photos Turning Buyers Away?

In today's market, over 95% of home searches start online. If your listing photos are dark, cluttered, or taken with a phone camera, buyers will scroll right past your home. Professional real estate photography can increase the number of showings significantly and is one of the highest ROI investments a seller can make.

Ryan Rose works with professional photographers on every listing to ensure homes make the strongest possible first impression online. Quality visuals are not optional in the Las Vegas market.

As a Las Vegas real estate specialist, Ryan Rose has helped many homeowners successfully relist and sell homes that sat on the market with their previous agent. The key is identifying the specific factors that held the home back and addressing each one before going back on the market.
