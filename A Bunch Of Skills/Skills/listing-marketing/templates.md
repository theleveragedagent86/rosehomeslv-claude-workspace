## Social Post Voice Guide

- Warm and knowledgeable, like a trusted neighbor who happens to be an expert
- Short sentences. No em-dashes. No corporate speak.
- First-person, always from Ryan's perspective
- Specific over vague: "3-car garage in Henderson" beats "amazing garage"
- End with a low-pressure CTA: "DM me for details" or "Link in bio"
- Emoji use is optional — keep it light if used (1–2 max per post)
