## Official Community Info
- HOA Website: https://www.suncityaliante.org/home/ (Sun City Aliante); Club Aliante and Aliante Master Association managed separately
- HOA Management: Aliante Master Association oversees the overall community; Sun City Aliante has its own HOA; Club Aliante has its own HOA
- HOA Fees: Aliante Master Association fee approximately $51/month; Sun City Aliante HOA approximately $139/month (includes $51 master plan fee plus $70 Sun City fee plus association management, clubhouse, security, grounds maintenance, and recreational amenities); Club Aliante HOA approximately $251/month (includes Aliante Master Association fees)
- Guard Gate: Partial — Club Aliante is a guard-gated community with 328 homes; the rest of Aliante is not guard-gated but has controlled access points and active HOA management
- Age Restrictions: Sun City Aliante is a 55+ age-restricted community; the remainder of Aliante has no age restrictions
- Key Amenities: Aliante Golf Club (18-hole championship course), Aliante Nature Discovery Park (23.4 acres with lake, waterfall, dinosaur playground, splash pad, amphitheater), Deer Springs Park, Aviary Park, 24-mile interconnected trail system, Sun City Aliante clubhouse with indoor pool, fitness center, tennis courts, pickleball courts, bocce ball courts, horseshoe pits, arts & crafts studio, ballroom, library, aerobics & dance studio, billiards room, steam room/sauna, continuing education center, Aliante Casino + Hotel + Spa (AAA Four Diamond resort)

## Real Estate Overview
- Price Range: $347,000 to $849,000 (general Aliante); Sun City Aliante mid-$300,000s to over $400,000; Club Aliante starting at $525,000+
- Median Price: $444,445 to $459,900 (varies by source and time period); average sale price approximately $467,667; average price per sq ft $203.86
- Home Styles: Single-family homes (single-story and two-story), townhomes, condominiums (Villagio), guard-gated luxury homes (Club Aliante), age-restricted 55+ homes (Sun City Aliante with 7 single-story floor plans in Wildflower, Cypress, and Preserve collections)
- Lot Sizes: Nicely sized lots described as "larger" with mature, low-maintenance desert landscaping and 2-3 car garages; homes up to 6 bedrooms and 5 baths in general Aliante; Club Aliante homes 2,196–3,732 sq ft; Sun City Aliante homes 1,155–2,105 sq ft
- Year(s) Built: Community opened May 2003; homes built from 2002 to present; Sun City Aliante built 2003–2008; Club Aliante built 2005–2007
- Builders: Original builders — D.R. Horton, KB Home, Pardee Homes, Pulte Homes (four of the top six builders in Las Vegas Valley at the time); Del Webb (Sun City Aliante); developed by North Valley Enterprises (joint venture of American Nevada Corporation and Del Webb Corporation); Pulte Homes built Club Aliante
- New Construction: Community largely built out with approximately 6,500 homes of a planned 7,500; some newer sections and resale activity remain active
- Gated: Partial — Club Aliante is a guard-gated community (328 homes); Sun City Aliante is age-restricted with controlled access; remainder of Aliante is non-gated but has controlled community access points

## Lifestyle & Location
- Drive to Strip: Approximately 15 miles / 30 minutes
- Drive to Airport: Approximately 15-16 miles / 25-28 minutes to Harry Reid International Airport
- Drive to Downtown: Approximately 20 minutes
- Nearest Hospital: ER at Aliante (freestanding emergency department of MountainView Hospital), 7207 N Aliante Pkwy — 11,000 sq ft facility with 12 exam rooms, board-certified emergency physicians, open 24 hours; UMC Quick Care Aliante at 5860 Losee Road (open 7 days, 8am–8pm)
- Safety: Aliante has a crime score of A+; crime rates run approximately 50-60% below North Las Vegas citywide averages; newer homes, good lighting, and engaged community contribute to safety; one of the safest neighborhoods in North Las Vegas
- Pet Friendly: Aliante Nature Discovery Park has open green spaces for leashed dogs; 24-mile trail system for dog walking; Craig Ranch Regional Park nearby has off-leash dog areas; Aliante Casino + Hotel + Spa offers pet-friendly hotel rooms; Aliante Animal Hospital located at 3310 W Elkhorn Rd
- Retirement Friendly: Sun City Aliante is a dedicated 55+ active adult community with approximately 2,028 single-story homes; full amenity center with indoor pool, fitness center, arts & crafts studio, ballroom, library, continuing education center, tennis, pickleball, bocce, horseshoe pits; Del Webb community; proximity to ER at Aliante and UMC Quick Care; quiet, well-maintained streets
- Notable: First master-planned community in North Las Vegas; 1,905-acre mixed-use community; situated at the base of the scenic Sheep Mountains; was the sixth top-selling community in the US in 2004; approximately 25,000+ residents; many military families due to proximity to Nellis Air Force Base; median household income approximately $105,090; over 400 acres dedicated to recreational and public use; the community name "Aliante" is derived from a type of Italian glider aircraft
