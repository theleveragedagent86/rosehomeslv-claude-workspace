## Lifestyle & Location
- Drive to Strip: Approximately 17-20 minutes (approximately 12 miles)
- Drive to Airport: Approximately 20 minutes (approximately 15.6 miles to Harry Reid International Airport)
- Drive to Downtown: Approximately 15 minutes (via Highway 613/Highway 11)
- Drive to Red Rock Canyon: Approximately 10-15 minutes
- Nearest Hospital: Summerlin Hospital Medical Center — 657 N. Town Center Dr, Las Vegas, NV 89144; 454-bed acute care hospital; 24/7 emergency department; 30+ specialty areas; approximately 5-10 minutes from Eagle Hills
- Safety: Guard-gated community with 24-hour security; the gated entrance and security patrols provide an added layer of safety beyond standard Summerlin community policing
- Pet Friendly: Internal greenbelt walking trail suitable for dog walking; nearby Bruce Trent Park and Willows Park offer additional outdoor space; no specific pet policy restrictions found beyond standard HOA rules
- Retirement Friendly: Not a 55+ community, but the quiet, guard-gated environment, single-story home options, proximity to Summerlin Hospital Medical Center, walking trails, and low-traffic streets make it appealing for retirees
- Notable: Eagle Hills is one of the most prestigious guard-gated communities in Summerlin; its 160 custom homes on large lots create an exclusive, low-density feel; the community sits adjacent to TPC Summerlin (home of a PGA Tour event); lush landscaping and mature trees give the neighborhood an oasis-like character unusual for the Las Vegas desert; the verdant greenbelt and gardens make residents "forget they're living in the desert"
