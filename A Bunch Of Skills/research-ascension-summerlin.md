## Official Community Info
- HOA Website: https://ascensionsummerlin.com/
- HOA Management: Not found (managed under Summerlin master-plan association structure)
- HOA Fees: $255/month (Ascension sub-HOA) + $67/month (Summerlin Master HOA) = $322/month total. One-time amenity fee of $5,000. No SID/LID fees.
- Guard Gate: Yes. Staff-gated community with 24/7 security and controlled access.
- Age Restrictions: None. Ascension is not age-restricted (not a 55+ community). Open to all ages.
- Key Amenities: Exclusive 7,500 sq ft community clubhouse featuring two swimming pools, hot tub, fitness center, four pickleball courts, golf simulator room, basketball court, social lounge, fire pit, walking/biking trails, bicycle repair station, activity lawn area, food truck area, event spaces, 77-space parking area. Clubhouse is for Ascension residents only.

## Real Estate Overview
- Price Range: ~$1,200,000 (Incline entry-level, now sold out) to $4,000,000+ (Crestline with premium lot and views)
- Median Price: ~$2,330,000 (based on recent listing data)
- Home Styles: Single-story and two-story luxury single-family homes. No townhomes or condos. Modern architecture with soaring 14'-22' great room ceilings, storefront windows, 16' slider doors, gourmet kitchens with Wolf appliances, primary suite retreats, flexible living spaces, covered loggias.
- Lot Sizes: Pulte average ~8,000 sq ft; Toll Brothers average ~10,000 sq ft; premium lots up to 15,000-20,000 sq ft. Lot premiums: Pulte $25,000-$300,000; Toll Brothers $15,000-$1,000,000+
- Year(s) Built: Sales began late 2023; first homes delivered 2024; community approximately 85% complete as of February 2026; estimated full completion 3-5 years from 2024 start (approximately 2027-2029)
- Builders: Toll Brothers and Pulte Homes exclusively. Six subdivisions total (three per builder).
- New Construction: Yes, actively building. Two subdivisions sold out (Incline by Pulte, Ridgeline by Toll Brothers). Four subdivisions still have dirt lots and/or quick move-in homes available (Highrock, Crestline, Caprock, The Pointe). Quick move-in homes available $2,150,000-$2,575,000 with move-in dates March-September 2026.
- Gated: Yes, fully guard-gated community with staff-gated entry and 24/7 security.

## Lifestyle & Location
- Drive to Strip: ~20 minutes
- Drive to Airport: ~20 minutes (Harry Reid International Airport)
- Drive to Downtown: ~25 minutes (Downtown Las Vegas / Fremont Street area)
- Nearest Hospital: Summerlin Hospital Medical Center, 657 N. Town Center Drive, Las Vegas, NV 89144. 454-bed acute care hospital on a 40-acre campus. Approximately 10-15 minutes from Ascension.
- Safety: Summerlin South crime rate is 1,224 per 100,000 people, which is 47% lower than the national average. Violent crime rate is 15.9 vs. national average of 22.7. Property crime rate is 30 vs. national average of 35.4. Guard-gated communities in Summerlin South add an additional layer of security. Chance of being a violent crime victim: 1 in 556.
- Pet Friendly: Summerlin has 300+ parks and 200+ miles of interconnected trails. Barkin' Basin Dog Park is nearby with separate areas for large and small dogs, agility equipment, and shaded benches. The Gardens Park (20+ acres) offers dog-friendly walking paths. Ascension's internal trails are also suitable for dog walking. Red Rock Canyon trails are dog-friendly (on leash).
- Retirement Friendly: Ascension is NOT age-restricted and is open to all ages. It is not a 55+ community. However, single-story floor plans are available in multiple collections (Ridgeline, Highrock, Incline, The Pointe), which appeal to retirees. The community is close to Summerlin Hospital Medical Center. Summerlin does have dedicated 55+ communities nearby (Sun City Summerlin, Siena, Regency at Summerlin) for those seeking age-restricted living.
- Notable: Ascension is located within The Peaks village, one of the most prestigious villages in Summerlin, alongside Mesa Ridge, The Ridges, and the exclusive Summit Club. The community spans 261 acres (some sources say 231 acres) and will have 561 total homes across six subdivisions when complete. High demand has led to two sold-out subdivisions and waiting lists for remaining lots. Strong equity gains have been reported in 2025. Out-of-state relocators are a significant buyer demographic. No SID (Special Improvement District) fees, which is notable for new Las Vegas construction. Build times range from 6-18 months depending on builder and plan.
