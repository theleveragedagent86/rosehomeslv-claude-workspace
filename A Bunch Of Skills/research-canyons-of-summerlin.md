## Official Community Info
- HOA Website: https://www.summerlink.com/residents/ (Summerlin master HOA portal)
- HOA Management: Summerlin North homeowners association (master HOA); most subdivisions within The Canyons also have their own sub-HOA with additional fees and CC&Rs
- HOA Fees: Monthly fees range from $30 to $1,000+ depending on the subdivision; an additional yearly SID (Special Improvement District) fee also applies
- Guard Gate: Yes — multiple subdivisions are guard-gated, including Aventura, Bellacere, Canyon Fairways, Eagle Rock, Mira Villa Condos, and Palisades. The Canyons is described as a gated community overall.
- Age Restrictions: None for the general community; MorningStar Senior Living at The Canyons (490 S Hualapai Way) offers independent living, assisted living, and memory care for seniors
- Key Amenities: TPC Las Vegas championship golf course, TPC Summerlin (nearby private course), Cottonwood Canyon Park (69 acres), 200+ miles of interconnected trails in Summerlin, JW Marriott Las Vegas Resort & Spa, community gyms (Lifetime Fitness, David Barton Gym, 24 Hour Fitness), walking paths, mature landscaping, business parks

## Real Estate Overview
- Price Range: ~$600,000 (some parts/condos) to $5,000,000+ (custom estates in Bellacere and Canyon Fairways)
- Median Price: Not found (specific to The Canyons); Summerlin North overall median is $533,000; however most Canyons single-family homes list above $1,000,000
- Home Styles: Custom estates, semi-custom homes, single-family detached, condominiums (Cierra, Dakota, Mira Villa, Peachy Canyon), townhomes, Mediterranean-style luxury homes, modern villas, sprawling estates
- Lot Sizes: Varies by subdivision; Bellacere has lots supporting 3,000-16,000+ sq ft homes; Canyon Fairways homes exceed 4,000-10,000+ sq ft; Eagle Rock homes are two-story with 3-car garages
- Year(s) Built: Construction began 1995; most homes built late 1990s through early 2000s; Eagle Rock built 1997-2002
- Builders: Avante Homes, Beazer Homes, Capital Pacific Homes, Christopher Homes, Warmington Homes, Westmark Homes, plus various custom home builders
- New Construction: The Canyons is largely built out; only resale homes generally available (e.g., Eagle Rock is "fully built out")
- Gated: Yes — the village as a whole is described as gated; multiple individual subdivisions are guard-gated (Aventura, Bellacere, Canyon Fairways, Eagle Rock, Mira Villa, Palisades)

## Lifestyle & Location
- Drive to Strip: 20-25 minutes (can be longer during peak traffic)
- Drive to Airport: 20-30 minutes via South I-215 (Harry Reid International Airport, formerly McCarran)
- Drive to Downtown: ~15 minutes via Highway 613/Highway 11 (a straight shot)
- Nearest Hospital: Summerlin Hospital Medical Center, 657 N Town Center Drive, Las Vegas, NV 89144 — a 454-bed acute care hospital on a 40-acre campus; located within Summerlin directly adjacent to The Canyons' eastern boundary along Town Center Drive (estimated 1-3 minute drive)
- Safety: Not found (no official crime statistics sourced specific to The Canyons; however, The Canyons features guard-gated subdivisions and is within the master-planned community of Summerlin, which is generally regarded as one of the safer areas in the Las Vegas valley)
- Pet Friendly: Dogs allowed on leash at Cottonwood Canyon Park (signs posted requiring leash and handler); 200+ miles of interconnected trails for walking; PetSmart located at nearby Canyon Pointe shopping center
- Retirement Friendly: MorningStar Senior Living at The Canyons offers independent living, assisted living, and memory care (168 total suites); proximity to Summerlin Hospital Medical Center; quiet guard-gated subdivisions; golf course lifestyle; flat terrain suitable for walking; nearby medical offices and facilities
- Notable: The Canyons is described as one of the most affluent villages in Summerlin; the neighborhood of Tournament Hills (in the adjacent Hills South Village, sharing the TPC golf course) is considered by many to be the most prestigious address in Las Vegas; the community is nestled against the backdrop of Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area (10-15 min drive); one-third of Summerlin's total acreage is designated open space; Summerlin is an award-winning master-planned community
