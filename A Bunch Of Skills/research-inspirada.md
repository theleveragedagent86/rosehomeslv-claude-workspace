## Official Community Info
- HOA Website: https://www.inspiradaca.org/
- Community Marketing Website: https://inspirada.com/
- HOA Management: Inspirada Community Association (office at 2000 Via Firenze, Henderson, NV 89044; phone: 702-260-7939; website platform by Clubessential)
- HOA Fees: Base Inspirada Community Assessment is $95/month ($255/quarter, approximately $85/month base assessment cited in some sources). Additional fees may apply depending on location — e.g., Toll Brothers Clubhouse fee is $41/month for select collections.
- Guard Gate: Partial — Inspirada has multiple gated subdivisions with guard-gated entry points, but the overall master plan is not entirely guard-gated.
- Age Restrictions: None for the master plan overall. However, Mera Henderson is a 55+ active adult apartment community located within Inspirada at 2300 Via Inspirada.
- Key Amenities: Seven parks spanning 85 acres, 35+ miles of multi-use trails, multiple heated pools, Solista Community Center (5,000 sq ft), Toll Brothers Clubhouse (pools, fitness center, conference room, caterer's kitchen, kids' playground), soccer/multi-use fields, Little League fields, basketball courts, tennis courts, volleyball courts, pickleball courts, skate area, splash pads, dog parks, amphitheaters, bocce ball courts, picnic ramadas, exercise stations along trails, roving security services, year-round community events.

## Real Estate Overview
- Price Range: $419,000 to $1,100,000+
- Median Price: $549,000 (February 2026); median sale price over last 12 months: $538,250 (up 6% year-over-year)
- Average Sale Price: $571,668
- Days on Market: 54 days average
- Current Listings: 99 homes for sale (as of February 2026)
- Home Styles: Single-family homes (one-story and two-story), townhomes, condos, mid-rise apartments
- Home Sizes: Townhomes from ~1,366 sq ft; single-family homes from ~1,558 sq ft up to 4,343 sq ft. Typical range 1,600 to 4,000 sq ft.
- Lot Sizes: Small by Las Vegas standards; lower-priced homes have minimal yard space. Some Toll Brothers and Pardee resales offer more generous backyards.
- Year(s) Built: Community opened in 2007; construction ongoing through 2026+
- Builders: Toll Brothers, KB Home, Tri Pointe Homes (formerly Pardee Homes), Beazer Homes, Century Communities. (Pardee rebranded to Tri Pointe.) Active Tri Pointe neighborhoods include Arden (2,014-2,735 sq ft, up to 5 BR), Capri (1,640-1,924 sq ft townhomes, up to 3 BR + loft), and Strada.
- New Construction: Yes, actively building. Community ranked #12 among Top-Selling Master-Planned Communities. 39 distinct neighborhoods. At completion, planned for 11,500 homes across 1,940 acres.
- Gated: Partial — multiple gated subdivisions with guard-gated entry; the overall master plan is not entirely guard-gated.
- Total Acreage: 1,940 acres (some sources say 2,000 acres), divided into seven villages of 200-250 acres each, with at least 4 neighborhoods (20-60 acres) per village.

## Lifestyle & Location
- Drive to Strip: ~15 minutes
- Drive to Airport (Harry Reid International): ~15-18 minutes
- Drive to Downtown Las Vegas: ~15-20 minutes (can increase to 30 minutes with I-15 traffic)
- Drive to Henderson Executive Airport: ~6 minutes
- Nearest Hospital: Southern Hills Hospital freestanding ER at 2475 Via Inspirada (opening late 2026; $15 million, 10,860 sq ft, 24/7 full-service emergency care). Currently: Henderson Hospital (303-bed facility), Saint Rose Dominican Hospitals (Rose de Lima and Siena campuses), Anthem Hills Medical Center (2540 W Horizon Ridge Pkwy). Inspirada Pediatrics and Inspirada Primary Care also serve the community.
- Highway Access: I-15, I-215, I-515, SR 582, SR 564 — all easily accessible
- Safety: Henderson ranked #2 safest large city in the U.S. (AdvisorSmith study based on FBI NIBRS data). Inspirada reports crime rates below the Henderson average. Modern design with well-lit streets, organized layouts, trail patrols, and active community programming create natural surveillance. Resident-reported concern: youths on mini-bikes and e-bikes in the Inspirada/Madeira Canyon area.
- Pet Friendly: Two dedicated dog parks (Aventura and Attesa, with large and small dog areas); additional dog parks at Montagna and Capriola. 35+ miles of dog-friendly walking/biking trails. Henderson certified as pet-friendly city by Better Cities For Pets (Mars Petcare) since 2019. Chicken N Pickle also has a dog park.
- Retirement Friendly: Mera Henderson 55+ Active Adult Apartments at 2300 Via Inspirada (by Sparrow Living); 1-BR from $1,664/mo, 2-BR from $2,164/mo. Maintenance-free lifestyle. Community is within 18 minutes of Harry Reid International Airport. Multiple medical facilities nearby (see hospital section). Quiet, well-maintained streets. Sentiero Park designed with age-friendly playgrounds and inclusive accessibility.
- Community Events: Year-round programming including yoga classes, film screenings, creative writing workshops, holiday celebrations, farmers markets, fitness classes, movie nights, sports leagues.
- Population: Estimated 9,600+ current residents; projected 22,000+ at buildout.
- Notable: Inspirada borders Sloan Canyon National Conservation Area with access to 1,700+ ancient petroglyphs. Community designed around centralized open-space corridor so every resident is within a 5-minute walk of park amenities. Italian-themed naming convention throughout (Aventura, Solista, Capriola, Potenza, Montagna, Sentiero, etc.).
