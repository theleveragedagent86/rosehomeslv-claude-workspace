## Official Community Info
- HOA Website: https://www.summerlink.com/residents/summerlin-north-community-association/
- HOA Management: Summerlin North Community Association (phone: 702-838-5500)
- HOA Fees: $74/month master association fee for Summerlin North (effective January 1, 2026, increased from $65). This includes $37/month for the Summerlin Council portion (parks, pools, events, recreational programming). Individual sub-communities (Belaire Estates, Corta Bella, Eagle Hills, Tournament Hills, Country Club Hills, etc.) have additional sub-HOA fees on top of the master fee. Tournament Hills sub-HOA is $480/month + $47/month Summerlin masterplan.
- Guard Gate: Partial. The Hills (original village) is NOT guard gated. The Hills South contains four guard-gated communities: Eagle Hills, Tournament Hills, Country Club Hills, and Corta Bella. All four have 24-hour guard gate access.
- Age Restrictions: None (all ages welcome)
- Key Amenities: The Hills Park (7-acre park with amphitheater, tennis courts, volleyball, basketball, playgrounds, picnic ramadas), Summerlin Library and Performing Arts Center, Nevada Ballet Theatre studios, TPC Summerlin private golf course, 200+ mile Summerlin trail system, Trails Village Center shopping

## Real Estate Overview

### The Hills (Original Village / Summerlin North)
- Price Range: $300,000s to $700,000s (standard neighborhoods); Belaire Estates and Corta Bella command higher prices
- Median Price: Summerlin North overall median is $533,000 (2026 data, up 6.4% year-over-year)
- Home Styles: Single-family homes (one-story and two-story), some multi-family
- Home Sizes: ~1,400 sq ft to 3,000+ sq ft
- Lot Sizes: Standard suburban lots
- Year(s) Built: 1990-1996 (first families moved in March 1991)
- Builders: Not specifically documented for most neighborhoods; Coleman Homes (now Toll Brothers) built Corta Bella
- New Construction: No (established community, all resale)
- Gated: Corta Bella is guard-gated (126 homes, 24-hour guard); Belaire Estates is gated with its own community park. Most neighborhoods are non-gated.
- Neighborhoods (11 total): Amarante, Belaire Estates, Cherry Creek, Copper Ridge, Cypress Grove, Evergreen, Hillcrest Estates, Panorama Pointe, Serenata, Visions, Willow Tree
- Acreage: 394 acres
- Zip Code: 89134

### The Hills South
- Price Range: Upper $800,000s to $10,000,000+ (luxury estate homes)
- Median Price: Not available for this specific village; Summerlin South overall median is ~$645,000
- Home Styles: Townhouses, condos, semi-custom homes, custom estates. Styles include contemporary, classic, Mediterranean.
- Home Sizes: ~1,500 sq ft (townhouses/condos) to 20,000+ sq ft (luxury estates)
- Lot Sizes: Standard lots up to 2/3 acre (Eagle Hills has 1/3, 1/2, and 2/3 acre lots)
- Year(s) Built: 1991-2000
- Builders: Christopher Homes (Country Club Hills), Coleman Homes/Toll Brothers (Corta Bella), various custom builders in Eagle Hills and Tournament Hills
- New Construction: No (established community, all resale)
- Gated: Four guard-gated communities: Eagle Hills (160 homes, 24-hour guard), Tournament Hills (~132 homes), Country Club Hills (~175 homes), Corta Bella (126 homes, 24-hour guard). Non-gated: Montaire, Santa Barbara, The Terraces (condos), Terraces on the Green (condos).
- Neighborhoods (8 total): Eagle Hills, Tournament Hills, Country Club Hills (includes The Enclave), Corta Bella, Montaire, Santa Barbara, The Terraces, Terraces on the Green
- Acreage: 725 acres
- Zip Code: 89134
- Developer: The Howard Hughes Corporation

### Combined (The Hills + The Hills South)
- Total Acreage: ~1,119 acres
- Total Neighborhoods: 19
- Price Spectrum: $300,000s (The Hills resale) to $10,000,000+ (The Hills South luxury estates)

## Lifestyle & Location
- Drive to Strip: 20-25 minutes (longer during rush hour, weekends, or major events)
- Drive to Airport: ~24 minutes to Harry Reid International Airport
- Drive to Downtown Las Vegas: ~15 minutes via Highway 613/Highway 11
- Drive to North Las Vegas Airport (VGT): ~10 miles
- Nearest Hospital: Summerlin Hospital Medical Center, 657 N. Town Center Drive, Las Vegas, NV 89144 (approximately 5 minutes by car)
- Safety: Located within the Summerlin master-planned community, which is consistently rated among the safest areas in the Las Vegas Valley. Guard-gated communities (Eagle Hills, Tournament Hills, Country Club Hills, Corta Bella) have 24-hour security with roving patrols.
- Pet Friendly: Extensive 200+ mile trail system ideal for dog walking. Multiple parks with open spaces. Summerlin is widely regarded as pet-friendly with numerous walking paths.
- Retirement Friendly: No age restrictions in any community. Proximity to Summerlin Hospital Medical Center. Quiet, tree-lined streets. Single-story home options available. Close to shopping, dining, and medical services. Summerlin is frequently cited as a top retirement destination in Las Vegas.
- Notable: The Hills was the very first village built in Summerlin, with the first families moving in March 1991. The community is the historical heart of Summerlin. The Hills South is home to TPC Summerlin, where Tiger Woods won his first PGA Tour victory in 1996. The Summerlin Library and Performing Arts Center and Nevada Ballet Theatre are located within the community. 89% of Summerlin residents cite walking trails as the most important community amenity. Wide, tree-lined streets with bike lanes and walkways throughout. The Hills is conveniently located near The Trails, The Pueblo, The Crossing, and The Canyons villages.
