## Official Community Info
- HOA Website: https://www.summerlink.com/residents/summerlin-north-community-association/
- HOA Management: Summerlin North Community Association (managed via Summerlink / Summerlin Community Management)
- HOA Fees: Master association fee of $74/month per unit as of January 1, 2026 (increased from $65/month). This includes $37/month for the Summerlin Council portion which funds parks, pools, events, and recreational programming. Sub-association fees for specific neighborhoods within The Pueblo vary from $50-$150/month depending on the subdivision, and are paid in addition to the master fee.
- Guard Gate: No — The Pueblo is not a guard-gated community overall. However, certain condo/townhome subdivisions within The Pueblo (La Posada Condos, Red Hills Condos) are individually gated.
- Age Restrictions: None for the single-family home neighborhoods. Destinations Pueblo is a separate 55+ active adult apartment community located at 8600 Scholar Lane within the village boundaries.
- Key Amenities: Pueblo Park (60+ acres), Pueblo Shopping Center (34,000 sq ft retail), miles of walking/biking trails, basketball court, playgrounds, picnic ramadas, BBQ areas, desert interpretive garden, exercise stations, community restrooms. Condo communities (Buena Vista, La Posada, Red Hills) have their own pools, clubhouses, spas, and exercise rooms.

## Lifestyle & Location
- Drive to Strip: 20-25 minutes (~12 miles); can be longer during rush hour or major events
- Drive to Airport: 22-30 minutes (~16 miles to Harry Reid International Airport)
- Drive to Downtown Las Vegas: ~15 minutes (~11 miles via Highway 613)
- Drive to Downtown Summerlin: ~12 minutes (less than 10 miles)
- Nearest Hospital: Summerlin Hospital Medical Center, 657 N Town Center Dr, Las Vegas, NV 89144 — 485-bed Joint Commission accredited hospital with 30+ specialty areas including cardiology, surgery, pediatrics, emergency medicine, orthopedics
- Safety: Summerlin North ranks in the 82nd percentile for safety nationally. Violent crime rates are 70-85% below national averages. Violent crime rate: 2.572 per 1,000 residents. Property crime can be slightly elevated due to affluence of the area. 55% of residents report feeling "pretty safe."
- Pet Friendly: Yes — Pueblo Park has extensive walking/jogging trails; Three Dog Bakery located in Pueblo Place shopping center; generous sidewalks throughout neighborhoods. Note: Destinations Pueblo 55+ apartments do NOT allow cats or dogs.
- Retirement Friendly: Yes — Destinations Pueblo is a dedicated 55+ active adult apartment community (8600 Scholar Ln) with 1-2 bed units (700-970 sq ft), poolside cabanas, fitness classes, gourmet cooking lessons, state-of-the-art fitness center. The broader community offers quiet streets, proximity to Summerlin Hospital, walkable parks, and easy access to medical facilities including Pueblo Medical Imaging.
- Notable: The Pueblo was the first village in all of Summerlin to come to market (1992). It is the easternmost village in Summerlin. Pueblo Park is frequently called "Las Vegas' best kept secret." The community is adjacent to Desert Shores to the north. The Pueblo features two houses of worship within the village. The Las Vegas Farmers Market operates nearby on N. Rampart Blvd. Architecture intentionally mimics ancestral pueblo structures. The village is a 568-acre mixed-use residential community with 18 distinct neighborhoods offering condos, townhomes, single-family, and semi-custom homes.
