## Real Estate Overview
- Price Range: ~$1,099,000 to $8,999,000+ (active listings); top sales have reached $14.5M (7 Falcon View Court, 2020)
- Median Price: ~$3.49M (March 2026 listing data)
- Home Styles: Custom estates, semi-custom homes, single-story luxury homes, two-story estates, townhomes/condos (Fairway Hills), contemporary, Mediterranean-inspired, desert contemporary
- Home Sizes: 1,958 sq ft (Fairway Hills townhomes) to 22,240 sq ft (custom estates); Promontory homes range from 6,000 to 20,000 sq ft
- Lot Sizes: 0.117 acres to 6.45 acres (Rimrock has largest lots); Arrowhead lots approximately 1 acre with golf course and Strip views
- Year(s) Built: Community developed starting in 2000; Club Ridges built in 2007; homes built from ~2003 to present; Fairway Hills developed in two phases over 15 years
- Builders: Blue Heron (top custom builder), Sun West Custom Homes, William Lyon Homes/Taylor Morrison (Topaz Ridge, Sterling Ridge, Silver Ridge), Toll Brothers (Fairway Hills), plus various custom home builders
- New Construction: Yes — custom lots still available; Amara Golf Club adding 15 boutique villas (2,000–2,500 sq ft) and custom residences (4,000–6,000 sq ft) expected 2027
- Gated: Yes — entire community is guard-gated. Two 24-hour guard gates at main entrances plus secondary gates within each subdivision. The Pointe has triple-gate security.

## Lifestyle & Location
- Drive to Strip: ~20 minutes (16 miles)
- Drive to Airport: ~22–30 minutes (16–19 miles to Harry Reid International Airport)
- Drive to Downtown: ~15 minutes (11 miles to Downtown Las Vegas)
- Drive to Downtown Summerlin: ~8 minutes
- Drive to Red Rock Canyon: ~15 minutes
- Nearest Hospital: Summerlin Hospital Medical Center, 657 N. Town Center Dr., Las Vegas, NV 89144 (~10 minutes drive)
- Safety: A+ safety rating; 24/7 guard-gated security with physical human guards and private roving patrols; Summerlin is statistically one of the safest areas in Nevada
- Pet Friendly: Walking trails throughout community and Summerlin's 150-mile trail system; Red Rock Canyon nearby for outdoor activities; specific dog park data not found within The Ridges itself
- Retirement Friendly: Fairway Hills offers low-maintenance townhome/condo living; proximity to Summerlin Hospital Medical Center; quiet, secure guard-gated environment; no age restriction (not 55+); single-story home options in Topaz Ridge and one other neighborhood
- Notable: Named by Forbes as one of America's most exclusive places to live; sits at Summerlin's highest altitude (~3,200–4,000 feet elevation); 800-acre community; cooler temperatures than valley floor due to elevation; panoramic views of Red Rock Canyon to the west and Las Vegas Valley to the east; zip code 89135
- Demographics: 3,009 residents (US Census); median age 45; average individual income $160,810
