## Official Community Info
- HOA Website: https://www.rhodesranchhoa.net/ (also: https://rhodesranchlife.com/rr-hoa-association/)
- HOA Management: Terra West Management Services (previously managed by FirstService Residential)
- HOA Fees: Approximately $155-$225 per month (sources vary; multiple sources cite $155/month as base, with some citing up to $225/month for certain sections). Among the lowest in Las Vegas for a guard-gated, golf course community. No SID/LID expenses reported.
- Guard Gate: Yes — fully guard-gated with 24/7 staffed entrances. All visitors verified before entry. Community patrol included in HOA fees.
- Age Restrictions: None for the main Rhodes Ranch community. However, Mera Rhodes Ranch is a separate 55+ active adult apartment community located adjacent to the community (opened late 2021, by Sparrow Partners).
- Key Amenities:
  - 18-hole Rhodes Ranch Golf Club (par-71, designed by Ted Robinson Sr., 6,909 yards from back tees)
  - 35,000 sq. ft. R-Club Recreation Center (weight/cardio room, aerobics room, sauna, pickleball courts, racquetball courts, full-length indoor basketball court, handball courts, table tennis, billiards, craft rooms, meeting rooms, main lobby with fireplace and piano)
  - Fun Zone Water Park — the only private water park of its kind in Southern Nevada (16 slides, 13,000 sq. ft. lagoon pool with wading areas, 10,000 sq. ft. water park section with 3 speed slides, 5 kid slides, rope bridges, squirt gun stations, 25-meter lap pool, two 8-person spas, one 12-person spa, four covered cabanas)
  - Exterior lighted basketball court
  - Two sand volleyball courts
  - Four lighted tennis courts
  - Children's playground with activity structures
  - Scenic two-mile circular walking path winding through the community
  - Miles of additional walking and biking trails
  - Landscaped ponds with ducks and geese
  - Ranch House Grille restaurant and full bar at the golf club

## Real Estate Overview
- Price Range: ~$150,000 (entry-level condos) to $1,200,000+ (estate properties with golf course views)
- Median Price: ~$466,536 (median sale price, Feb 2026, down 2.3% YOY); median list price ~$625,150 (March 2026)
- Home Styles: Single-family homes (one-story and two-story), condos/townhomes, custom estates. Architectural styles range from Mediterranean-inspired to modern contemporary.
- Square Footage: ~1,200 to 4,800+ sq. ft. (smallest homes ~1,600 sq. ft.; largest approaching 5,000 sq. ft.)
- Lot Sizes: ~5,000 to 8,000 sq. ft. for standard sections; 0.25 to 0.5+ acres for estate properties. Older homes tend to have larger lots.
- Year(s) Built: 1997 (community developed); first homes occupied 1998; most construction through 2010s. Community is largely built out.
- Builders: Rhodes Homes (original developer, Rhodes Design and Development), Harmony Homes, Dunhill Homes (took over after Rhodes Companies Chapter 11 bankruptcy in 2009), Richmond American Homes, Pardee Homes, KB Homes, Pulte Homes, Del Webb, D.R. Horton
- New Construction: Limited. The community has been largely sold out for several years. Approximately 3,795 single-family homes built, with plans for build-out to ~4,000 homes.
- Gated: Yes — entire community is guard-gated with 24/7 staffed entrances and community patrol.
- Neighborhoods/Sections within Rhodes Ranch: The Falls (Apache Springs — 360 condo units, built 2003-2005, 843-1,237 sq. ft.), Auburn Hills, Canyon Trail, The Champions, The Collection, The Fairways, The Greens, The Irons, The Lakes, The Links, The Majors, Pacific Mist, Palms Bay, Spinnaker (by Royal Construction), Summerchase, Travina, Tuscany (east side, built by Rhodes and later Dunhill Homes), Vistana, and others.
- Price Segments: Standard sections $350,000-$550,000; golf course homes $500,000-$900,000; estate properties $700,000-$1,200,000+; condos mid-to-upper $200,000s
- Average Price Per Sq. Ft.: ~$257 (as of March 2026)

## Lifestyle & Location
- Drive to Strip: ~15 minutes (11 miles via I-215)
- Drive to Airport: ~15-19 minutes (11 miles to Harry Reid International Airport, formerly McCarran)
- Drive to Downtown: ~20 minutes (16 miles)
- Nearest Hospital: Southern Hills Hospital & Medical Center (9300 W Sunset Rd, Las Vegas, NV 89148) — approximately 0.6 miles away. HealthSouth Desert Canyon Rehabilitation Hospital — approximately 0.1 miles away.
- Safety: Crime grade of A (CrimeGrade.org). Crime rate 23.15 per 1,000 residents. Violent crime rate 2.291 per 1,000 (86th percentile for safety). Property crime rate 12.95 per 1,000. Theft safety 95th percentile. Overall safer than 81% of U.S. neighborhoods. Southwest portion of community considered safest.
- Pet Friendly: Community is recognized as pet-friendly. Extensive walking paths (2-mile loop plus additional trails) ideal for dog walking. Nearby parks with open space. Pet services (dog walking, pet sitting) serve the area. No dedicated on-site dog park mentioned, but nearby public dog parks include Desert Breeze Dog Park and Barkin' Basin. PetSmart available at Arroyo Market Square.
- Retirement Friendly: No age restriction in main community, welcoming to retirees. Mera Rhodes Ranch 55+ active adult community adjacent (opened 2021). Southern Hills Hospital 0.6 miles away. Quiet, safe, guard-gated environment. Low-maintenance living options available. Golf course on-site. Recreation center with low-impact activities. Walking paths throughout. Single-story home options available (26 currently listed at median $500K).
- Notable: Known as the "Oasis in the Southwest." Community is 6 miles southwest of the Las Vegas Strip. Positioned as more affordable than comparable guard-gated golf communities (30-50% lower than Southern Highlands; significantly lower than Summerlin gated communities). Easy access to I-215 Beltway. Durango Casino & Resort ($780M) opened December 2023 at the community's doorstep. Community speed limit is 25 mph, making it safe for pedestrians and cyclists. Population ~9,341 residents. Median household income ~$90,655. Vacancy rate 2.0% (lower than 85.9% of U.S. neighborhoods). Cost of living ~1.1% above Nevada average, 4.3% above national average. Zip codes: 89148 (primary), 89113. Demographics: Asian 29.1%, Italian ancestry 8.8%, Mexican 7.3%, German ancestry 6.8%, English ancestry 4.8%. 29.1% foreign-born. 7.3% of residents 5+ primarily speak Tagalog. 43.1% employed in executive/management/professional occupations. Only 1.9% of children live below federal poverty line.
