## Real Estate Overview
- Price Range: Below $100,000 (condos) to mid $500,000s (larger single-family homes up to ~4,000 sq ft)
- Median Price: $365,000 to $455,000 depending on source and time period (Redfin Sep 2025: $365K; Homes.com Jul 2025: $455K; Zillow typical home value: $379,954; Redfin 12-month median: $435,000-$439,900)
- Home Styles: Single-story homes, two-story homes, condos, townhomes; Spanish Colonial-style with brick driveways and desert landscaping
- Lot Sizes: Varies; some homes on 1/4+ acre lots; typical suburban lots
- Year(s) Built: Primarily early 1990s (developed by DR Horton); many homes built 1970-1999, with some built 2000-present
- Builders: DR Horton (original/primary developer); nine different floor plan layouts
- Floor Plans: 1,930 SF to 3,180 SF (DR Horton original); broader range from ~1,200 SF to ~4,000 SF across all subdivisions; 3-5 bedrooms, 2-3 car attached garages
- New Construction: Limited — Whitney Ranch is a mature, established community; The Presley at Whitney Ranch is a newer apartment development
- Gated: Not guard-gated; some apartment complexes within the area are gated (e.g., Montego Bay Apartments)

## Lifestyle & Location
- Drive to Strip: ~12 miles, approximately 15-20 minutes
- Drive to Airport: ~6 miles, approximately 15-18 minutes to Harry Reid International Airport
- Drive to Downtown: ~12 miles, approximately 15-20 minutes (Great Basin Highway/I-515 provides direct route; residents report 15 minutes via Great Basin Hwy)
- Nearest Hospital: Henderson Hospital at 1050 W. Galleria Drive (~1 mile, within Whitney Ranch area); St. Rose Dominican Hospital (Siena Campus) at 3001 St. Rose Parkway; Galleria Urgent Care at 600 Whitney Ranch Dr (walking distance)
- Safety: Crime score of 4 (on par with average U.S. neighborhood per Apartments.com); 44th percentile for safety overall (CrimeGrade.org); violent crime rate 6.416 per 1,000 residents; property crime rate 66.08 per 1,000 residents; residents describe neighborhood as safe and quiet
- Pet Friendly: Yes — dogs welcome on leash at Whitney Mesa Nature Preserve; dog-friendly trails; Henderson recognized as pet-friendly city by Better Cities For Pets program (Mars Petcare); multiple nearby dog parks in Henderson (Reunion Trails, Acacia Dog Park, Paseo Vista)
- Retirement Friendly: Not a dedicated 55+ community, but senior-friendly features include free recreation center membership for seniors 60+ and veterans, proximity to Henderson Hospital and medical facilities, quiet suburban streets, walkable layout, nearby 55+ communities (Sun City Anthem, Sun City MacDonald Ranch)
- Notable: One of Henderson's first master-planned communities; 85-acre community with 1,219 homes; population ~12,965; walkable and bikeable with some transit; urban-suburban mix feel; more affordable than newer Henderson communities like Green Valley Ranch while offering similar amenities; most residents rent; Whitney Mesa Nature Preserve offers spectacular sunrise/sunset views from mesa top; median age 38.6 years; 82.12% white-collar workers; zip code 89014
