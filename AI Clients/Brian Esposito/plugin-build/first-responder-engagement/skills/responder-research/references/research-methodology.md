# Responder Research -- Methodology and Sources

## Why This Is Different From Lender Research

The `/lender-research` skill has a structured data source (MMI production export) to seed its target list with verified, ranked candidates. **There is no MMI equivalent for first responders.**

Discovery for this skill is purely from:
1. Instagram bio searches
2. Hashtag mining
3. Following department / institutional accounts to find individuals
4. Cross-referencing follower / following lists of known accounts

This makes the research more time-intensive and the verification step more important. It's easy to find an account that says "Vegas firefighter" in the bio but is actually a fitness influencer using the title for cred. Verify activity and content type before adding.

## Verification Checklist

Before adding any account, verify ALL of these:

1. **Vegas geography:** Confirmed Vegas / Henderson / Clark County area (not just NV)
2. **Public account:** Public, not private (or the skill cannot engage)
3. **Activity:** Posts at least monthly (last 3 posts dated within 60 days)
4. **Content mix:** Mixes work and personal community content (not 100% selfies, not 100% political takes)
5. **Apolitical:** Does NOT primarily post about police reform, military politics, vaccine debates, immigration, etc.
6. **Comments enabled:** Most recent posts have comment sections open
7. **Real person:** Verify it's an actual first responder, not someone using the title as a brand persona
8. **Not already on list:** Check existing target-accounts.md to avoid duplicates

## Data to Collect Per Account

| Field | Required? | Where to Find |
|-------|-----------|--------------|
| Instagram handle | Yes | Instagram |
| Full name | If public | Instagram bio |
| Agency / Branch | Yes | Bio or post content |
| Category | Yes | Determine: Military, Police, Fire/EMS, Healthcare |
| Post frequency | Yes | Check last 5 posts for dates |
| Content themes | Yes | Scan last 10 posts |
| Tone notes | Optional | Casual, formal, family-focused, work-focused, etc. |

---

## Category Balance

Aim for rough balance across the four categories:

- Military / Veterans: 5-8 accounts
- Police / Law Enforcement: 5-8 accounts
- Fire / EMS: 5-8 accounts
- Healthcare: 5-8 accounts

If one category is dominating, prioritize researching the underrepresented categories on the next run.

---

## Red Flags (Do NOT Add)

- Account has "official voice" disclaimer suggesting they post for the department (we want personal accounts)
- Account is mostly memes, fitness content, or lifestyle (we want work + community mix)
- Recent posts have heavy political framing
- Account has been involved in public controversy (Google their name)
- Account is private
- Comments are disabled on most posts
- Account is dormant (no posts in 60+ days)
