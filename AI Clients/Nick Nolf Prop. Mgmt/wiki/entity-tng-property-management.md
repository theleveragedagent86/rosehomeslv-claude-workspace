# TNG (The Next Generation) Property Management

## Overview
Las Vegas-based property management company owned by [[entity-nick-nolf]]. Manages approximately 500+ investor-owners and their rental properties.

## Systems
- **AppFolio** — Primary property management software (top-tier AI package). Handles work orders, tenant/owner data, maintenance communication, leases, texting. See [[entity-appfolio]].
- **Gmail** — Single workspace-branded inbox for ALL PM communication (~100,000+ emails)
- **Google Drive** — Document storage with rigid folder hierarchy and naming conventions

## Staff
- **Nick Nolf** — Owner, primary decision-maker
- **Matt** — Licensed property manager, maintenance follow-up, move-in documentation

## Operations
- 22 active work orders at time of initial meeting
- 24/7 maintenance phone line through AppFolio (add-on service)
- Intentionally uses a terrible Google Voice answering machine to push people to email
- All communication funneled to one email address by design

## Vendor Structure
- 4 maintenance groups in AppFolio:
  1. **Default group** — DMG Property Management
  2. **Do Not Use NWHS** — for owners who don't want that vendor
  3. **Home Warranty** — completely separate
  4. **Owner Assigns All Vendors** — for owners who want full control
- **Primary vendor:** NWHS (Northwest Handyman Service) handles ~75% of all work
- Remaining 25% goes to alternative vendors based on owner preference
- Automated vendor routing: e.g., garage door issue goes to Royal Garage Door first; if no response in 15 min, routes to Cactus Garage
- Most vendor groups have only 2 vendors because the primary accepts ~95% of jobs
