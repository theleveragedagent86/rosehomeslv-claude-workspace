# TNG Property Management — AI Workflow Engagement Overview

## The Client

**Nick Nolf** owns and operates **TNG (The Next Generation) Property Management** in Las Vegas. He manages approximately 500+ investor-owners and their rental properties. He has been in property management for 20 years and has never been sued, which he attributes to keeping everything in writing.

## The Engagement

Ryan Rose is building AI automation workflows for Nick using **Claude Code** and **Claude Cowork**. The goal is to automate the repetitive, high-volume operational tasks that currently consume Nick's and Matt's (his licensed property manager/maintenance guy) time — primarily email management, file organization, and maintenance follow-up.

## Three Core Systems

All of Nick's operations run through three platforms:

1. **Gmail** — Single inbox for ALL property management communication (~100,000+ emails). This is the central nervous system.
2. **AppFolio** — Property management software handling work orders, tenant/owner data, maintenance communication, and leases. Considered the "Ferrari of property management."
3. **Google Drive** — Document storage with rigid folder hierarchy and naming conventions. Tens of thousands of documents.

## Priority Stack

1. **Email management** is the #1 need — Nick called it his "biggest thing" multiple times
2. **Vendor work order email processing** is the designated first build (contained slice of email)
3. **AppFolio maintenance follow-up** would save Matt 1-2 hours/day
4. **File organization and attachment handling** supports everything else
5. **Cross-reference auditing** catches human errors between Drive and AppFolio
6. **Photo/video compression** addresses a storage problem
7. **Master email knowledge base** is the long-term "close the loop" goal
