# Workflow: Master Email Knowledge Base / Pattern Learning

## What It Does
Process all ~100,000+ emails in Nick's inbox. Identify behavioral patterns across 500+ investors. Learn the 10-15 special carve-outs. Categorize every type of PM scenario and build a comprehensive reference so Claude can eventually respond autonomously without drafts.

## Why It Matters
This is the "close the loop" goal. Once the knowledge base is complete, Claude moves from draft-and-approve to autonomous response. It transforms the email system from semi-automated to fully automated.

## Complexity Assessment
**Tier 4: Highly Complex** — The most complex workflow by far. Requires processing 100,000+ emails, identifying patterns, learning carve-outs organically, categorizing every scenario type. This isn't a single workflow — it's an ongoing training and refinement project.
