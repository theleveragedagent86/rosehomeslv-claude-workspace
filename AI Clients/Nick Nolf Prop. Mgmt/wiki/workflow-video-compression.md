# Workflow: Video Compression and Organization

## What It Does
Compress move-in walkthrough videos to a quality sufficient for phone viewing and potential legal evidence, then file into the correct property folders in Google Drive.

## Current Problem
- Approximately half a terabyte of uncompressed videos
- Videos come from Matt's phone at move-in
- No compression currently applied

## Quality Requirements
- Viewable on a phone
- Usable as evidence if needed (legal standard)
- Doesn't need to be high quality

## Complexity Assessment
**Tier 2: Moderate** — Similar to photo workflow but significantly heavier on resources. Video compression takes real processing power and may require dedicated tooling outside Claude's direct capabilities. Nick acknowledged this would be "token-heavy."

## Implementation Notes
- May need a separate process/tool for actual compression (ffmpeg, etc.)
- Could be resource-intensive — consider dedicated hardware or cloud processing
- Lower priority than email-related workflows
