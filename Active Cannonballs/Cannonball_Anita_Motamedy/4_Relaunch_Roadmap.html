25 Common Reasons Listings Expire

Understanding what went wrong is the first step toward getting it right.

1

No Video or Virtual Tour
Buyers scroll past homes they can't experience online.
3
Over-Reliance on the MLS
The listing waited for buyers instead of going out to find them.
4
No Targeted Digital Advertising
The home wasn't shown to qualified local buyers on social platforms.
5
Pricing Missed the Market Window

6
No Compelling Opening Weekend Strategy

7
Generic Listing Description
Features were listed, but no lifestyle or emotional story was told.
8
Lack of Agent-to-Agent Promotion

9
Limited Showing Availability

10
Weak Curb Appeal
The first impression didn't earn a second look.
11
Outdated or Distracting Interiors
Small issues created big hesitation.
12
No Clear Buyer Profile
Marketing wasn't tailored to who would love this home most.
