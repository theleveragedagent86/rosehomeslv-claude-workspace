Aliante is a 1,905 acre master planned community in North Las Vegas, Nevada, featuring over 6,500 homes, an 18 hole championship golf course, and more than 24 miles of trails. Since opening in May 2003, Aliante has grown into one of the most desirable neighborhoods in the Las Vegas valley. ## What Makes Aliante a Master-Planned Community? Developed by North Valley Enterprises, Aliante was the first master planned community in North Las Vegas. The community spans nearly 2,000 acres of mixed use development, with homes built by nationally recognized builders including D.R. Horton, KB Home, Pardee Homes, Pulte Homes, and Del Webb. Today, Aliante is home to approximately 25,000 residents and offers a wide variety of housing options, from townhomes and condos at Villagio to guard gated luxury homes in Club Aliante. ## What Types of Homes Are Available in Aliante? Aliante offers something for every stage of life. Single family homes range from $347,000 to $849,000, with a median price around $444,445 and an average price per square foot of $203.86. Sun City Aliante provides 55+ age restricted living with prices in the mid $300,000s to over $400,000, while Club Aliante features 328 guard gated luxury homes starting at $525,000 and above. Townhomes and condos at Villagio round out the options for buyers seeking lower maintenance living. ## What Amenities Does Aliante Offer? Residents enjoy access to Aliante Golf Club, an 18 hole championship course that draws players from across the valley. The 23.4 acre Aliante Nature Discovery Park, Deer Springs Park, and Aviary Park provide green space and recreation for families and outdoor enthusiasts. A 24 mile trail system connects the community for walking and biking, and Sun City Aliante features its own clubhouse with an indoor pool, fitness center, tennis courts, pickleball courts, and bocce ball courts. The Aliante Casino, Hotel, and Spa anchors the community's entertainment and dining scene. ## Why Ryan Rose Recommends Aliante As a Las Vegas real estate expert, Ryan Rose considers Aliante one of the strongest options for buyers looking for value, safety, and lifestyle in the north valley.
