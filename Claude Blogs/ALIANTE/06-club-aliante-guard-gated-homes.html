Club Aliante is a guard gated community of 328 luxury homes located within the Aliante master planned community in North Las Vegas. With home sizes ranging from 2,196 to 3,732 square feet and prices starting at $525,000, Club Aliante offers some of the most exclusive living in the north valley. ## What Makes Club Aliante a Guard Gated Community? Club Aliante provides 24 hour guard gated security, giving residents an added layer of privacy and peace of mind. Built by Pulte Homes between 2005 and 2007, the neighborhood features 328 single family homes on spacious lots with mature desert landscaping. Homes include two and three car garages, open floor plans, and premium finishes throughout. The community sits within the larger Aliante development, which means residents also enjoy access to the golf course, parks, trails, and shopping that make Aliante one of the most complete neighborhoods in North Las Vegas. ## How Much Do Club Aliante Homes Cost? Homes in Club Aliante start at approximately $525,000 and can reach well above that depending on square footage and upgrades. The largest floor plans offer up to 3,732 square feet with as many as six bedrooms and five bathrooms. Monthly HOA fees run approximately $251, which covers the guard gate, common area maintenance, and master association fees. Compared to other guard gated communities in the Las Vegas valley, Club Aliante delivers strong value per square foot while maintaining a premium feel. ## What Amenities Are Near Club Aliante?
