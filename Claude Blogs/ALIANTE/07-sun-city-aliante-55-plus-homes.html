Sun City Aliante offers seven single story floor plans organized into three collections: Wildflower, Cypress, and Preserve. Home sizes range from 1,155 to 2,105 square feet, providing options for buyers who want a cozy low maintenance layout or a more spacious entertaining home. All homes feature single level living with no stairs, which is a key draw for active adults and retirees. Current resale prices range from the mid $300,000s to over $400,000, depending on square footage, upgrades, and lot placement. ## What Are the HOA Fees and Amenities? Monthly HOA fees in Sun City Aliante run approximately $139, which covers access to the community's impressive clubhouse and recreation facilities. Residents also enjoy an arts and crafts studio, ballroom, library, aerobics and dance studio, billiards room, steam room and sauna, and a continuing education center. The social calendar stays active with clubs, classes, and community events throughout the year. ## Is Sun City Aliante a Good Place to Retire?
