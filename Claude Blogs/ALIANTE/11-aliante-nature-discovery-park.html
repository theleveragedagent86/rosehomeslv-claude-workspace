Aliante Nature Discovery Park is a 23.4 acre park located at 2627 Nature Park Drive in North Las Vegas, featuring a man made lake, dinosaur themed playground, splash pad, and amphitheater. Known locally as "Dino Park," it is one of the most popular outdoor destinations in the Aliante master planned community. ## What Is at Aliante Nature Discovery Park? Nature Discovery Park packs an impressive list of amenities into its 23.4 acres. The centerpiece is a man made lake complete with a waterfall, surrounded by walking paths and mature landscaping. Kids flock to the multi story dinosaur themed playground, which features climbing structures, slides, and oversized dinosaur sculptures that make it one of the most unique play areas in the Las Vegas valley. Tennis courts, volleyball courts, bocce courts, soccer fields, and horseshoe pits give families and fitness enthusiasts plenty of options beyond the playground. ## Does Aliante Nature Discovery Park Have a Splash Pad? Yes, the splash pad at Nature Discovery Park operates from May 1 through September 30, with hours from 9am to 8pm daily. During the hot summer months, the splash pad becomes one of the busiest spots in North Las Vegas, drawing families from across the valley. The park also includes individual and group picnic sites, making it easy to plan a full day outdoors. Public restrooms and off street parking keep visits convenient for families with young children. ## Is There an Amphitheater at the Park? Nature Discovery Park features an outdoor amphitheater that hosts community events and seasonal performances throughout the year. The amphitheater seating area overlooks the lake, creating a scenic backdrop for concerts and gatherings. A 0.6 mile walking loop circles the park, offering an easy route that takes approximately 10 minutes to complete. Trail hours run from 5am to 11pm, giving early risers and evening walkers plenty of access. ## Ryan Rose's Take on Nature Discovery Park Ryan Rose considers Nature Discovery Park one of the top reasons families choose to buy homes in Aliante.
