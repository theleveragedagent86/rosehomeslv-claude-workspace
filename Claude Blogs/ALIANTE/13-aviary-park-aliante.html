Aviary Park is a multi sport community park located on Aviary Way in the Aliante neighborhood of North Las Vegas. With night lighted baseball diamonds, soccer fields, basketball courts, and a large playground, it serves as one of Aliante's most active recreation hubs. ## What Sports Facilities Does Aviary Park Offer? Aviary Park stands out from other Aliante parks with its night lighted baseball diamonds, which allow leagues and casual games to continue well after sunset. Soccer fields provide space for youth and adult leagues, while basketball courts stay busy with pickup games throughout the week. The combination of lighted fields and multiple sport options makes Aviary Park the go to location for organized sports in the Aliante community. Few neighborhood parks in North Las Vegas offer this level of sports infrastructure. ## Does Aviary Park Have a Playground and Picnic Areas? Yes, Aviary Park features a large playground area designed for a range of ages, keeping younger visitors busy while older kids and adults use the sports facilities. Both group and individual picnic shelters are available, making the park a popular choice for team celebrations, birthday parties, and weekend family gatherings. Public restrooms and off street parking keep logistics simple, even during tournament weekends when the park sees heavier traffic. ## How Does Aviary Park Connect to Other Aliante Amenities? Aviary Park ties into the broader Aliante trail system, giving residents the ability to walk or bike to the park from almost anywhere in the community. The 24 mile network of concrete pathways connects Aviary Park to Nature Discovery Park, Deer Springs Park, and the Aliante Golf Club area.
