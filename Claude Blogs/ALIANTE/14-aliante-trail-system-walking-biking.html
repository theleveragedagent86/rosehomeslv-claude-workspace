The Aliante trail system spans 24 miles of multi use pathways designed for walking, biking, and running throughout the Aliante master planned community in North Las Vegas. These concrete trails connect parks, schools, shopping centers, and neighborhoods, making Aliante one of the most walkable communities in the Las Vegas valley. ## How Long Is the Aliante Trail System? The full Aliante trail system covers 24 miles of interconnected concrete pathways that wind through the entire master planned community. Every pathway measures a minimum of 8 feet wide, giving walkers, joggers, and cyclists plenty of room to share the trail safely. Landscaping lines both sides of the trails, providing shade and visual appeal that sets these paths apart from standard sidewalks. The system was designed as a core feature of the Aliante community, not added later, which means the trails flow naturally between neighborhoods and amenities. ## Where Do the Aliante Trails Connect? The trail network links virtually every corner of the community. Residents can walk or bike from their front door to Nature Discovery Park, Deer Springs Park, Aviary Park, the Aliante Golf Club, and shopping at Aliante Marketplace without ever needing to drive. A popular loop within the system is the 0.6 mile Nature Discovery Park trail, which circles the park's man made lake and is accessible from 5am to 11pm daily. The connectivity encourages an active lifestyle and reduces car dependence for daily errands and recreation. ## Are the Aliante Trails Good for Biking? Yes, the 8 foot wide concrete paths accommodate casual cyclists and commuters comfortably. The relatively flat terrain of North Las Vegas makes the trails accessible for all fitness levels, from families with young riders to experienced cyclists looking for a smooth training route. The trails also work well for strollers, wheelchairs, and electric scooters, making them inclusive for all residents. Morning and evening hours tend to be the most popular times on the trails, especially during the warmer months. ## Ryan Rose's Take on the Aliante Trail System Ryan Rose frequently highlights the 24 mile trail system as one of the features that gives Aliante a lifestyle advantage over other North Las Vegas neighborhoods.
