Aliante offers a surprisingly diverse dining scene for a master planned community in North Las Vegas. From upscale steakhouse fare at MRKT to family owned breakfast spots at Aliante Marketplace, residents enjoy quality restaurants without driving to the Strip. ## What Are the Best Restaurants in Aliante North Las Vegas? The dining options in Aliante center around two main hubs: Aliante Casino + Hotel + Spa and Aliante Marketplace. The casino alone features five signature restaurants, including MRKT Sea and Land steakhouse, The Salted Lime Mexican cantina, and FARM, a locally sourced American kitchen open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Quick Eats at Aliante also offers seven fast casual outlets including Dairy Queen, Johnny Rockets, and Capriotti's. Aliante Marketplace, located at the southwest corner of the 215 Beltway and Aliante Parkway, adds even more variety. Rocco's NY Pizzeria serves authentic New York style pizza with house made dough and sauces. Waffles Cafe is a family owned breakfast and lunch spot beloved by locals. Namba Sushi and Rivas Mexican Grill round out the international options. ## Where Do Aliante Residents Go for Date Night? MRKT Sea and Land is the top choice for a special evening out. The chic steakhouse features Black Angus filet mignon, porterhouse, and rib eye alongside fresh salmon, lobster, and scallops. A wine list of over 5,000 bottles and live piano music on Friday and Saturday evenings creates an upscale atmosphere. Entrees typically range from $31 to $50, making it a refined option without Strip level prices. ## Which Casual Spots Are Worth Trying? Their daily happy hour from 5 to 7 p.m. offers half off all drinks and pizzas.
