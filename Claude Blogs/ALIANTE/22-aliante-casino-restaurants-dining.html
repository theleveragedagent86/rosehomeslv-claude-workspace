Aliante Casino + Hotel + Spa is an AAA Four Diamond resort that houses five signature restaurants under one roof in North Las Vegas. Whether you crave a hand cut steak, fresh margaritas, or a quick breakfast, the casino's dining lineup covers every mood and budget. ## What Restaurants Are Inside Aliante Casino? The casino features a curated collection of dining experiences. MRKT Sea and Land is the flagship steakhouse, serving Black Angus filet mignon, porterhouse, rib eye, fresh salmon, lobster, and scallops in an upscale setting. The Salted Lime is a lively Mexican cantina known for its giant margarita bar, authentic south of the border fare, and late night hours. FARM offers locally sourced American comfort food for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, with favorites like skillet scrambles, burgers, meatloaf, and chicken fried steak. Beyond those three, Aliante Casino also offers Medley Buffet and Bistro 57 for additional variety. A Starbucks and the Quick Eats food court round out the options with seven fast casual outlets including Dairy Queen, Johnny Rockets, Rubio's, and Capriotti's. ## Is Aliante Casino Dining Affordable Compared to the Strip? One of the biggest advantages of dining at Aliante Casino is the value. MRKT entrees typically range from $31 to $50, well below comparable steakhouses on the Las Vegas Strip. The Salted Lime offers a daily happy hour from 5 to 7 p.m. with half off all drinks and pizzas, plus $5 select appetizers. FARM provides hearty American meals at casual prices. Residents save both time and money by dining locally rather than making the 25 minute drive south. ## Do You Need Reservations at Aliante Casino Restaurants?
