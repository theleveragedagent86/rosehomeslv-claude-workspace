The Salted Lime at Aliante Casino + Hotel + Spa has earned its reputation as the coolest hotspot in North Las Vegas. This lively Mexican cantina pairs authentic south of the border fare with a giant margarita bar, making it a favorite gathering spot for Aliante residents. ## What Makes The Salted Lime So Popular? The restaurant centers around its massive margarita bar, which anchors the entire dining experience. Classic margaritas sit alongside creative concoctions featuring fresh fruit purees and unique flavor combinations. The spicy margarita is a standout favorite among regulars. Beyond drinks, the food menu delivers generous portions of Mexican favorites like skirt steak marinated in garlic and cilantro, three cheese quesadillas, oversized flautas, and al pastor burritos. Other popular dishes include chicken enchiladas, shrimp tacos, carnitas, elotes, and vegetable fajitas. The menu balances traditional preparations with enough variety to keep repeat visits interesting. ## Does The Salted Lime Have Happy Hour Specials? Yes, and the happy hour is one of the best deals in North Las Vegas. Every day from 5 to 7 p.m., The Salted Lime offers half off all drinks and pizzas, plus $5 select appetizers. A late night happy hour runs from 12 to 2 a.m. with the same discounts. During these windows, margaritas drop to the $4 to $5 range, and $3 house wines are also available. For residents looking for an affordable night out without leaving the neighborhood, these specials are hard to beat. ## What Are the Hours and Do You Need Reservations? The Salted Lime keeps late hours, making it one of the few quality dining options open past midnight in North Las Vegas. Reservations are available through OpenTable but are generally not required. The restaurant accommodates walk ins most nights, though weekends tend to fill up during happy hour. The casual, energetic atmosphere works equally well for date night, group outings, or a solo seat at the margarita bar. ## A Local Take on The Salted Lime Ryan Rose considers The Salted Lime one of the strongest lifestyle selling points in Aliante.
