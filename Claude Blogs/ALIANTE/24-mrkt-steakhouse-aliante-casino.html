MRKT Sea and Land is the signature steakhouse inside Aliante Casino + Hotel + Spa, offering upscale dining without a drive to the Las Vegas Strip. This chic North Las Vegas restaurant pairs premium Black Angus steaks with fresh seafood in an elegant yet approachable setting. ## What Does MRKT Sea and Land Serve? The menu splits cleanly between land and sea. By land, MRKT features hand cut Black Angus selections including filet mignon, porterhouse, and rib eye, all considered among the best steaks in the North Las Vegas area. By sea, the kitchen serves fresh salmon, lobster, scallops, and crab, with a classic Surf and Turf option for guests who want both. Starters, salads, and sides round out the offerings for a complete fine dining experience. The wine program is equally impressive. MRKT maintains a cellar of over 5,000 bottles spanning 300 labels, with 20 wines available by the glass. A pianist performs in the bar area every Friday and Saturday evening, adding an extra layer of sophistication. ## How Much Does MRKT Cost? Entrees typically range from $31 to $50, which represents significant value compared to comparable steakhouses on the Las Vegas Strip. The restaurant also runs periodic specials, and guests have noted deals like a $30 dinner special and a $40 lobster and filet combination. For a steakhouse with this caliber of wine list and live entertainment, the pricing sits well below what you would pay at a Strip resort. ## What Are MRKT's Hours and Reservation Details? MRKT is open Tuesday through Saturday. Weeknight hours run from 4 to 9 p.m., while Friday and Saturday extend to 10 p.m. The restaurant is closed on Sunday and Monday. Reservations are available through OpenTable or by calling (702) 692-7320. Booking ahead is recommended for Friday and Saturday evenings, especially if you prefer a booth or patio seat near the fireplace. ## A Local Perspective on MRKT Ryan Rose regularly highlights MRKT as one of Aliante's hidden gems.
