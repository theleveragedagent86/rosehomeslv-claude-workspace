Aliante Casino + Hotel + Spa is an AAA Four Diamond resort located right inside the Aliante master planned community in North Las Vegas. Aliante Casino earned its Four Diamond rating from AAA thanks to a combination of upscale amenities and consistent service quality. The property features over 200 hotel rooms and suites, a 100,000 plus square foot gaming floor, more than 2,000 slot machines, and 40 table games. A dedicated all paper bingo hall adds a classic touch that sets it apart from many modern casinos. The 650 seat showroom hosts live entertainment ranging from tribute acts to nationally touring comedians and musicians. Whether you are a guest or a local resident, the variety of experiences under one roof makes this property feel much larger than a neighborhood casino. ## Does Aliante Casino Have a Pool and Spa? Yes, the resort features a heated pool area with a relaxed, tropical vibe that residents and hotel guests can enjoy.
