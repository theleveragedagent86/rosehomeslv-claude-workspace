Aliante Golf Club is an 18 hole championship course designed by Gary Panks, located in the heart of the Aliante community in North Las Vegas. The course plays 7,022 yards at par 72 and has been a centerpiece of the neighborhood since it opened in December 2003. ## What Is the Course Layout at Aliante Golf Club? The Gary Panks design features a course rating of 73.2 and a slope rating of 139, making it a challenging but enjoyable round for players at all skill levels. The layout includes 54 bunkers, 14 bridges, 4 ponds, and 2 waterfalls that create a visually striking desert golf experience. A meandering arroyo comes into play on 14 of the 18 holes, adding a strategic element that rewards accurate tee shots and smart course management. Multiple tee boxes allow beginners to play a shorter, more forgiving round while low handicappers can test themselves from the back tees at full yardage. ## How Do You Book Tee Times at Aliante Golf Club? Tee times can be booked online through the Aliante Golf Club website or by calling the pro shop directly. Early morning and late afternoon twilight rates offer the best value, especially during the cooler months when demand is highest. Many Aliante residents take advantage of local player programs and seasonal promotions that reduce green fees throughout the year. ## What Practice Facilities Does Aliante Golf Club Offer? The club features a full driving range, putting green, chipping green, and a dedicated practice bunker. These facilities make it easy for residents to work on every part of their game without traveling across town. Private and group lessons are available through the pro shop for golfers looking to improve. ## Is There a Restaurant at Aliante Golf Club?
