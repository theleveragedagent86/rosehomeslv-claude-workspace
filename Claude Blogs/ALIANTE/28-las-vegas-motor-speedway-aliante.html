Las Vegas Motor Speedway sits just minutes from the Aliante community in North Las Vegas, giving residents easy access to one of the country's premier motorsports venues. Whether you follow NASCAR, NHRA drag racing, or off road events, living in Aliante puts you closer to the action than nearly any other Las Vegas neighborhood. ## How Far Is Las Vegas Motor Speedway from Aliante? The speedway is roughly a 10 to 15 minute drive from most homes in Aliante, depending on traffic. The venue is located along Las Vegas Boulevard North, making the route straightforward with no need to navigate Strip congestion. On race weekends, Aliante residents can leave their homes well after fans from Henderson or Summerlin have already hit the road and still arrive with time to spare. ## What Events Does Las Vegas Motor Speedway Host? The speedway hosts a packed annual calendar that includes two major NASCAR Cup Series race weekends, NHRA Nationals drag racing, the Mint 400 off road race, and Electric Daisy Carnival. Smaller events like drifting competitions, driving experiences, and car shows fill the schedule throughout the year. The venue also offers the Richard Petty Driving Experience and exotic car racing programs for fans who want to get behind the wheel themselves. Beyond motorsports, the facility grounds host large scale concerts, festivals, and community events that draw hundreds of thousands of visitors annually. ## Does Living Near the Speedway Affect Aliante Home Values? Proximity to a major entertainment venue is generally a positive factor for home values in the area. The speedway brings tourism dollars, supports local businesses, and adds to the overall appeal of North Las Vegas as a destination. Noise from events is minimal in Aliante due to the distance and the direction of the track relative to the neighborhood. ## A Local Perspective on Speedway Access Ryan Rose finds that proximity to Las Vegas Motor Speedway is a meaningful bonus for many Aliante buyers, especially those who enjoy motorsports or want to attend major events without the hassle of a long commute. Families also appreciate how easy it is to make a day trip to the speedway for special exhibitions or kids' events.
