Aliante is widely considered one of the best neighborhoods for families in North Las Vegas, combining top rated schools, expansive parks, and a well maintained master planned layout across 1,905 acres. With roughly 25,000 residents and a median household income around $105,090, this community attracts families looking for suburban quality without leaving the Las Vegas valley. ## What Makes Aliante So Good for Families? The foundation starts with planning. Aliante was designed from the ground up as a master planned community, which means the streets, parks, schools, and commercial areas were all built with intention. That kind of coordinated development creates a neighborhood where families can walk to parks, bike on dedicated trails, and access daily essentials without long drives. The community includes roughly 6,500 homes across a variety of price points and sizes. Whether you are a young family buying your first home or upgrading to something larger, Aliante offers floor plans and neighborhoods that fit different stages of life. The HOA maintains common areas, landscaping, and shared amenities, which keeps the neighborhood looking consistent and well cared for. ## How Are the Schools in Aliante? Education is one of the strongest selling points. Goynes Elementary carries an 8 out of 10 rating, making it one of the top elementary options in the area. Somerset Academy Aliante holds a B+ grade and ranks as the number 16 K through 8 school in Nevada, offering a charter option for families who want an alternative to traditional public schools. Shadow Ridge High School rounds out the lineup with a solid B rating and a strong athletics and activities program. Having quality schools within the community means shorter bus rides, easier involvement in school events, and a built in social network for both kids and parents. ## What Parks and Activities Are Available for Kids? Aliante is loaded with outdoor space. Nature Discovery Park covers 23.4 acres and features the popular Dino Park playground, open fields, and walking paths. Deer Springs Park and Aviary Park add more options for sports, picnics, and everyday play. The 24 mile trail system connects many of these spaces, making it easy for families to walk or bike between parks without using busy roads. For dog owning families, the trail system and dedicated green spaces also make Aliante one of the more pet friendly communities in the valley. ## Ryan Rose on Why Families Love Aliante Ryan Rose has helped dozens of families find their home in Aliante and sees the same reaction from nearly every buyer.
