Sun City Aliante is one of the premier 55 plus active adult communities in the Las Vegas valley, offering retirees a lifestyle built around resort style amenities, single story living, and year round sunshine in North Las Vegas. Built by Del Webb, this community within Aliante delivers the combination of comfort and activity that makes retirement in Southern Nevada so appealing. ## What Is Sun City Aliante? Sun City Aliante is a 55 plus age restricted community developed by Del Webb, one of the most recognized builders of active adult neighborhoods in the country. The community includes approximately 2,028 single story homes across seven floor plans ranging from 1,155 to 2,105 square feet. Every home is designed for single level living, which is a priority for retirees who want to age in place comfortably. The HOA runs approximately $139 per month and covers community maintenance, access to all clubhouse amenities, and upkeep of shared spaces. That monthly cost is very competitive compared to similar 55 plus communities in the Las Vegas area, especially given the breadth of what is included. ## What Amenities Does the Sun City Aliante Clubhouse Offer? The clubhouse is the center of daily life in Sun City Aliante, and it is stacked with options. Residents have access to an indoor pool, a full fitness center, tennis and pickleball courts, bocce ball, and horseshoe pits. Inside, you will find an arts and crafts studio, a ballroom for community events, a library, an aerobics and dance studio, billiards, a steam room and sauna, and a continuing education center. The variety is intentional. Whether you prefer group fitness classes, creative hobbies, competitive sports, or quiet reading, the clubhouse has a dedicated space for it. The community also organizes regular social events, clubs, and outings that keep residents connected and active. ## Is Healthcare Accessible for Retirees in Aliante? Healthcare access is a critical factor for retirees, and Aliante delivers on that front. The ER at Aliante, operated by MountainView Hospital at 7207 N Aliante Pkwy, is a 24 hour emergency facility with 11,000 square feet and 12 exam rooms. It is located right within the Aliante community, meaning emergency care is minutes away rather than a long drive across town. UMC Quick Care at 5860 Losee Rd provides additional walk in medical services nearby. ## Ryan Rose on Retiring in Aliante Ryan Rose has guided many retirees into Sun City Aliante and sees it as one of the best values in the Las Vegas 55 plus market. The single story homes are well built, the amenities rival what you would find at a private club, and the $139 monthly HOA keeps costs predictable. Add in the proximity to the Aliante Casino, the golf club, and everyday shopping, and you have a retirement lifestyle that stays active without feeling overwhelming.
