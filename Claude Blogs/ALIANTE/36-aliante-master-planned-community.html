Yes, Aliante is a master planned community and it was the first of its kind in North Las Vegas. Spanning 1,905 acres at the base of the Sheep Mountains, Aliante has grown into one of the most popular residential neighborhoods in the Las Vegas Valley since opening in May 2003. ## What Makes Aliante a Master Planned Community? Aliante was developed by North Valley Enterprises, a joint venture between American Nevada Corporation and Del Webb Corporation. The community was designed from the ground up with a mix of residential, commercial, and recreational spaces all woven into a unified plan. Over 400 acres are dedicated to parks, trails, and public amenities, giving the neighborhood a cohesive feel that sets it apart from piecemeal suburban development. Four of the top six homebuilders in the Las Vegas Valley participated in Aliante's construction, including D.R. Horton, KB Home, Pardee Homes, and Pulte Homes. That lineup helped produce a wide range of home styles and price points. With approximately 6,500 homes completed out of a planned 7,500, the community now supports more than 25,000 residents. ## How Big Is Aliante and What Amenities Does It Include? The 1,905 acre footprint includes the Aliante Golf Club, an 18 hole championship course designed by Gary Panks, along with Aliante Nature Discovery Park, miles of walking and biking trails, and multiple neighborhood parks with sports courts and playgrounds. The Aliante Casino Hotel and Spa, an AAA Four Diamond resort, sits within the community and offers dining, entertainment, and a full service spa without residents ever needing to leave the neighborhood. ## Was Aliante Always This Popular? Aliante gained national attention quickly. By 2004, it ranked as the sixth top selling master planned community in the entire United States. The name itself comes from a type of Italian glider aircraft, reflecting the soaring ambition behind the project. That early momentum attracted retail, dining, and services that turned Aliante into a self contained community rather than just a collection of subdivisions. ## A Local Perspective on Aliante's Master Plan Ryan Rose points out that Aliante's master planned structure is one of the biggest reasons buyers gravitate toward this part of North Las Vegas.
