Aliante is located in North Las Vegas, not the City of Las Vegas. The community sits in the 89084 zip code near the intersection of I-215 and Aliante Parkway, at the base of the scenic Sheep Mountains in the northwest portion of North Las Vegas. ## Is Aliante Part of North Las Vegas or Las Vegas? Aliante falls within the municipal boundaries of North Las Vegas, which is a separate incorporated city from the City of Las Vegas. While the two cities share the same metropolitan area and many of the same services, they have different city councils, tax structures, and local ordinances. This distinction matters for homebuyers because property taxes, utility providers, and city services can differ between the two jurisdictions. Many people assume Aliante belongs to Las Vegas because of its proximity to the I-215 beltway and its modern, master planned design. However, North Las Vegas has invested heavily in infrastructure and community development over the past two decades, and Aliante is the flagship example of that growth. ## Why Does the Distinction Between North Las Vegas and Las Vegas Matter? For real estate purposes, the city designation affects everything from property tax rates to school zone assignments. North Las Vegas homes in Aliante are served by the Clark County School District, just like Las Vegas, but the city services including police, fire, and parks departments are run by North Las Vegas. Buyers should also know that home values in Aliante have historically been competitive with similar communities in Las Vegas and Henderson, often offering more square footage for the price. ## Where Exactly Is Aliante Located? Aliante occupies 1,905 acres at the northern end of the Las Vegas Valley. The community is bordered by the I-215 beltway, providing quick access to Centennial Hills, Summerlin, and the Las Vegas Strip.
