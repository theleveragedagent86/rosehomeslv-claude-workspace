Yes, Aliante has a golf course. The Aliante Golf Club is an 18 hole championship course designed by Gary Panks, located right inside the Aliante master planned community in North Las Vegas. ## What Kind of Golf Course Is at Aliante? Aliante Golf Club opened in December 2003 and plays 7,022 yards from the back tees with a par of 72. The course carries a rating of 73.2 and a slope of 139, making it a solid challenge for experienced players while remaining accessible for recreational golfers from the forward tees. The layout features 54 bunkers, 14 bridges, 4 ponds, and 2 waterfalls woven throughout the desert landscape at the base of the Sheep Mountains. One of the defining features of the course is a meandering arroyo that comes into play on 14 of the 18 holes. This natural wash creates strategic decisions off the tee and rewards accurate iron play. The mountain backdrop adds scenic value that golfers typically associate with more expensive private clubs. ## What Practice Facilities Does Aliante Golf Club Offer? The club includes a driving range, a putting green, a chipping green, and a dedicated practice bunker. These facilities make it easy for residents to work on every part of their game without leaving the neighborhood. Lessons and clinics are available through the pro shop for golfers looking to improve. ## Does Aliante Golf Club Have Dining and a Pro Shop? Yes. The clubhouse features a fully stocked pro shop along with a bar and grill that includes a covered patio overlooking the Sheep Mountains. The patio is a popular gathering spot for golfers after their rounds and for residents who simply want to enjoy the views with a meal. The relaxed atmosphere makes it feel more like a neighborhood hangout than a formal country club. ## A Local Perspective on Aliante Golf Ryan Rose highlights the Aliante Golf Club as a major draw for buyers who want golf course access without the cost of a private membership.
