Aliante in North Las Vegas is one of the best neighborhoods in the valley for first-time homebuyers, offering entry-level prices starting around $347,000, low HOA fees, and a full suite of master-planned amenities. If you want a safe, family-friendly community without stretching your budget, this neighborhood deserves a close look. ## What Can First-Time Buyers Expect to Pay in Aliante? The median home price in Aliante ranges from $444,445 to $459,900, but entry-level options bring the barrier to ownership down significantly. Single-family homes at the lower end of the market start around $347,000, and townhomes and condos in the Villagio section offer even more affordable price points. The average price per square foot sits at $203.86, which is competitive for a master-planned community of this caliber. Monthly costs stay manageable as well. The master HOA fee is only $51 per month, far lower than what you will find in many comparable Las Vegas neighborhoods. That savings adds up over the life of a mortgage and keeps your total housing payment more predictable. ## Are Schools and Safety Good for Families in Aliante? Aliante earns an A+ crime score, making it one of the safest communities in the Las Vegas metro. Well lit streets, a maintained trail system, and an active homeowners association all contribute to that reputation. For first-time buyers starting a family or planning to, safety is often the top priority, and Aliante delivers. The schools serving Aliante are well regarded. Goynes Elementary holds an 8 out of 10 rating, and Somerset Academy earns a B+ grade. Shadow Ridge High School and Saville Middle School round out the options for older students. Having quality schools within the community means shorter commutes and stronger neighborhood connections. ## What Amenities Come With Living in Aliante? First-time buyers in Aliante get access to amenities that rival more expensive communities. The 24 mile trail system is perfect for walking, biking, and jogging. Aliante Nature Discovery Park, known locally as Dino Park, is a favorite for families with young children. The Aliante Golf Club offers a championship course designed by Gary Panks, and the Aliante Casino Hotel and Spa provides dining and entertainment without leaving the neighborhood. These amenities are part of what makes Aliante feel like more than just a place to live. They create a lifestyle that first-time buyers often do not expect to find at this price range. ## A Local Perspective for First-Time Buyers I have helped many first-time buyers find their homes in Aliante, and the reaction is almost always the same: surprise at how much community you get for the money. Between the low HOA, the parks and trails, and the safety record, Aliante checks every box that matters when you are making your first purchase. It is a neighborhood that grows with you.
