Aliante is known for being the first master-planned community in North Las Vegas, combining resort-style amenities, a championship golf course, and a family-friendly atmosphere across 1,905 acres at the base of the Sheep Mountains. Since opening in May 2003, it has grown into one of the most recognized neighborhoods in the Las Vegas Valley. ## Why Was Aliante a Groundbreaking Community? Aliante made history as the first master-planned community in North Las Vegas, setting a new standard for what the city could offer residents. The name itself comes from an Italian glider aircraft, reflecting the community's vision of soaring above expectations. By 2004, Aliante had become the sixth top-selling community in the entire United States, a remarkable achievement for a neighborhood in its early years. The original plan called for 7,500 homes across the development, and roughly 6,500 have been completed. That buildout gives Aliante a mature, established feel while still maintaining the infrastructure and design standards of a thoughtfully planned neighborhood. ## What Attractions Is Aliante Famous For? The Aliante Casino Hotel and Spa is one of the community's most recognizable landmarks. It holds AAA Four Diamond status and offers dining, entertainment, and a resort-style pool that residents and visitors enjoy year-round. The casino gives Aliante a social anchor that most suburban neighborhoods simply do not have. Aliante Golf Club, designed by Gary Panks, is a championship-level course that draws golfers from across the valley. The Aliante Nature Discovery Park, affectionately called Dino Park by locals, is a favorite among families with its dinosaur-themed play areas and splash pad. A 24 mile trail system winds through the community, connecting parks, schools, and neighborhoods for walking and biking. ## What Kind of Community Does Aliante Offer? Aliante is a multigenerational neighborhood. Sun City Aliante, a Del Webb 55 plus community, sits within the broader development and provides active adult living with its own clubhouse, pools, and social programming. Families with children benefit from well-rated schools like Goynes Elementary and Somerset Academy, while young professionals appreciate the proximity to the Las Vegas Strip, about 30 minutes south. The community earns an A+ crime score and keeps HOA master fees at just $51 per month. The median household income of approximately $105,090 reflects a stable, working-professional demographic that takes pride in the neighborhood. ## A Local View on What Makes Aliante Special Having worked with buyers and sellers throughout Aliante, I can say that the community's identity runs deeper than its amenities list. There is a genuine sense of neighborhood here that you feel when you drive through the tree-lined streets and see families on the trails.
