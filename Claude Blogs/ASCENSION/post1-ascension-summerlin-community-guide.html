Ascension Summerlin is a 261 acre guard gated luxury community in The Peaks village of Summerlin, featuring 561 single family homes built exclusively by Toll Brothers and Pulte Homes. If you are searching for new construction luxury living in one of Las Vegas's most prestigious neighborhoods, Ascension deserves a close look. ## Where Is Ascension Summerlin Located? Ascension sits within The Peaks village, one of the most sought after villages in all of Summerlin. The Peaks is home to elite communities including Mesa Ridge, The Ridges, and the exclusive Summit Club. This places Ascension in the company of some of the finest residential addresses in Southern Nevada. The community is approximately 20 minutes from the Las Vegas Strip and 20 minutes from Harry Reid International Airport. ## What Types of Homes Are Available in Ascension? The community offers single story and two story luxury single family homes with no townhomes or condos. Architectural details include soaring 14 to 22 foot great room ceilings, storefront windows, 16 foot slider doors, gourmet kitchens with Wolf appliances, and covered loggias. Lot sizes range from approximately 8,000 square feet for Pulte collections to 10,000 square feet or more for Toll Brothers, with premium lots reaching 15,000 to 20,000 square feet. Home prices start around $1,200,000 and extend beyond $4,000,000 for Crestline homes with premium lots and views. ## What Amenities Does Ascension Offer? Residents enjoy an exclusive 7,500 square foot community clubhouse that serves as the social hub. The amenity package includes two swimming pools, a hot tub, a fitness center, four pickleball courts, a golf simulator room, a basketball court, a social lounge, and fire pit gathering areas. Walking and biking trails wind through the community, and there is even a bicycle repair station, an activity lawn, and a dedicated food truck area for community events. ## Is Ascension Still Being Built? Sales launched in late 2023, and the first homes were delivered in 2024. As of early 2026, the community is approximately 85% complete. Two of the six subdivisions have sold out: Incline by Pulte Homes and Ridgeline by Toll Brothers. Four subdivisions remain active, so new construction opportunities still exist.
