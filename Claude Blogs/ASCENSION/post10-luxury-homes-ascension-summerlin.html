Luxury Homes in Ascension Summerlin

Ascension in Summerlin is home to some of the finest luxury residences in Las Vegas, with prices ranging from approximately $1,200,000 to over $4,000,000. Built exclusively by Toll Brothers and Pulte Homes across six collections, these homes feature modern architecture, premium finishes, and expansive lots within a fully guard gated 261 acre community.

What Luxury Features Come Standard at Ascension?

Every home at Ascension is designed with modern luxury in mind. Standard features include 14 to 22 foot great room ceilings, storefront windows, and 16 foot slider doors that open to covered loggias. Kitchens feature Wolf appliances and premium cabinetry. Floor plans range from 2,297 square feet in the entry level Incline collection up to nearly 5,000 square feet in Toll Brothers' Crestline series, with configurations offering three to five bedrooms and up to five and a half bathrooms.

How Large Are the Lots at Ascension?

Lot sizes at Ascension are generous by Las Vegas standards. Pulte Homes lots average approximately 8,000 square feet, while Toll Brothers lots average around 10,000 square feet. Premium lots reach 15,000 to 20,000 square feet and command significant premiums: $25,000 to $300,000 for Pulte positions and $15,000 to over $1,000,000 for the most coveted Toll Brothers locations. These larger lots provide space for custom pools, outdoor kitchens, and resort style landscaping.

What Amenities Do Luxury Homeowners Enjoy at Ascension?

Beyond the homes themselves, Ascension delivers a private club lifestyle. The 7,500 square foot clubhouse anchors the community with resort style pools, a state of the art fitness center, pickleball courts, and a golf simulator. The entire community is guard gated with 24/7 security. Located within The Peaks village of Summerlin, residents live alongside the most exclusive neighborhoods in the valley, including Mesa Ridge, The Ridges, and Summit Club. Monthly HOA dues of $322 cover all of these amenities with no SID or LID fees.

According to Ryan Rose, who has worked extensively in Ascension and Summerlin's luxury market, the combination of new construction quality, generous lot sizes, and The Peaks village address makes Ascension one of the best values in Las Vegas luxury real estate. He recommends that buyers tour homes from both builders to compare finishes, floor plans, and available lot positions before making a decision.
