What Is Crestline at Ascension by Toll Brothers?

Crestline is the flagship two story collection by Toll Brothers inside Ascension, the guard gated luxury community in Summerlin. With floor plans starting above 4,700 square feet and prices from $2,054,995, Crestline delivers the largest new construction homes available in the neighborhood.

How Big Are the Crestline Floor Plans?

Crestline offers three distinct two story floor plans: the Rosecrest at 4,783+ square feet, the Summerhill at 4,888+ square feet, and the Suncrest at 4,998+ square feet. Every model includes five bedrooms, five and a half bathrooms, and a three car garage. These are among the most spacious new builds anywhere in Summerlin today.

Starting prices range from $2,054,995 for the Rosecrest to $2,164,995 for the Suncrest. Buyers should expect lot premiums to add anywhere from $15,000 to over $1 million depending on location, views, and lot size. Premium lots in Ascension can reach 15,000 to 20,000 square feet, giving homeowners generous outdoor space for pools, courtyards, and covered loggias.

What Features Come Standard in Crestline Homes?

Toll Brothers is known for its luxury specification, and Crestline homes reflect that reputation. Expect modern desert architecture with soaring great room ceilings reaching 14 to 22 feet, expansive storefront style windows, and 16 foot slider doors that blur the line between indoor and outdoor living. Wolf appliances come standard in the kitchens. Covered loggias extend the entertaining space into the backyard, a feature that suits the Las Vegas climate perfectly.

As a two story collection, Crestline appeals to families who want separation between living areas and bedrooms. The five bedroom layouts provide flexibility for home offices, media rooms, or multigenerational living. Ryan Rose often points out that Crestline's two story design gives buyers significantly more square footage per dollar compared to the single story collections in Ascension.

Is Crestline a Good Value in Ascension?

Among the active collections in Ascension, Crestline offers the most interior space at the lowest price per square foot. For buyers who prioritize living area over a single story layout, these homes represent a compelling option in a community that is already 85% built out. With only 561 total homesites across the entire 261 acre neighborhood, remaining inventory is limited. Buyers considering Crestline should act while lot selection is still available.
