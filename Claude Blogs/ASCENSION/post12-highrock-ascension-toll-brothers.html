What Is Highrock at Ascension by Toll Brothers?

Highrock is the premier single story collection by Toll Brothers in Ascension, Summerlin's guard gated luxury community. With three floor plans ranging from 3,623 to 3,940+ square feet and prices starting at $1,998,995, Highrock delivers expansive one level living designed for the Las Vegas lifestyle.

What Floor Plans Are Available in the Highrock Collection?

Toll Brothers offers three models in the Highrock collection. The Cliffside starts at 3,623 square feet with three bedrooms, three and a half bathrooms, and a base price of $1,998,995. The Highcrest offers 3,750+ square feet with three to four bedrooms and three to four and a half baths, starting at $2,042,995. The Sky Edge tops the lineup at 3,940+ square feet with four bedrooms and four and a half baths, priced from $2,249,995.

Every Highrock home includes a three car garage and sits on Toll Brothers' generous lot sizes, which average around 10,000 square feet. Premium lots can extend to 15,000 or even 20,000 square feet, with lot premiums ranging from $15,000 to over $1 million depending on position and mountain views.

What Makes Highrock Different from Other Ascension Collections?

Highrock fills the sweet spot between the sold out Ridgeline collection and the larger two story Crestline homes. Buyers who want single story convenience with true luxury scale will find Highrock compelling. Standard features include modern desert architecture, great room ceilings soaring 14 to 22 feet, storefront windows that flood the interior with natural light, and 16 foot slider doors opening to covered loggias. Wolf appliances come standard in every kitchen.

Ryan Rose frequently recommends Highrock to buyers who want the prestige of Toll Brothers craftsmanship without the staircase. Single story homes in this size range are increasingly rare in Summerlin, making Highrock especially attractive to both primary residents and those planning for long term livability.

How Much Inventory Is Left in Highrock?

Ascension is approximately 85% complete as of early 2026, with 561 total homesites across the entire 261 acre community. The Ridgeline collection, which offered slightly smaller single story homes, has already sold out entirely. That puts additional pressure on Highrock inventory. Quick move in homes in Ascension are currently listed between $2,150,000 and $2,575,000 with availability from March through September 2026. Buyers interested in Highrock should secure a lot before selection narrows further.
