What Is The Pointe at Ascension by Pulte Homes?

The Pointe is Pulte Homes' premier single story collection inside Ascension, the guard gated luxury neighborhood in Summerlin. With floor plans ranging from 3,475 to 4,006 square feet and prices starting at $1,975,990, The Pointe offers expansive one level living with only a few lots remaining.

What Floor Plans Does The Pointe Offer?

The Pointe collection features single story homes between 3,475 and 4,006 square feet. Floor plans include three to four bedrooms with three and a half to four and a half bathrooms, plus a three car garage standard across every model. These homes sit on Pulte's lots, which average approximately 8,000 square feet, with premium positions commanding lot premiums from $25,000 to $300,000.

At nearly 4,000 square feet in the largest plan, The Pointe rivals the square footage of many two story homes while keeping everything on a single level. This layout appeals to buyers who value accessibility, open sightlines, and the convenience of main floor primary suites without sacrificing entertaining space.

What Makes The Pointe Stand Out in Ascension?

Modern desert architecture defines every home in The Pointe. Great room ceilings stretch from 14 to 22 feet, framed by storefront windows that capture natural light and mountain views. Sixteen foot slider doors open seamlessly to covered loggias, creating indoor outdoor flow suited perfectly to the Las Vegas climate. Wolf appliances are standard in the kitchen, matching the luxury finishes found throughout the community.

Ryan Rose considers The Pointe one of the strongest single story offerings in all of Summerlin right now. The combination of Pulte's construction quality, the Ascension guard gate, and the generous floor plan sizes creates a package that competes directly with custom builds at a fraction of the timeline and uncertainty.

How Much Inventory Remains at The Pointe?

The Pointe has only a few lots remaining as of early 2026. Ascension as a whole is approximately 85% complete, with 561 total homesites across 261 acres. Pulte's smaller Incline collection has already sold out completely, and The Pointe is heading in the same direction. Quick move in homes across Ascension currently range from $2,150,000 to $2,575,000, with move in dates available from March through September 2026. Buyers who want a single story Pulte home in this community should not wait; once these lots sell, there are no plans for additional single story inventory from Pulte in Ascension.
