Is Ascension Summerlin Guard Gated?

Yes, Ascension in Summerlin is a staff gated community with 24/7 security and controlled access at every entry point. Located within the prestigious Peaks village of Summerlin South, Ascension offers one of the most secure residential environments in the Las Vegas valley.

What Kind of Security Does Ascension Summerlin Have?

Ascension operates a staffed gate system rather than a simple key card or remote entry. Trained security personnel verify every visitor around the clock, ensuring that only residents and approved guests enter the community. This level of access control places Ascension alongside the most exclusive guard gated neighborhoods in Summerlin, including The Ridges and Mesa Ridge.

How Does Guard Gated Access Affect Daily Life?

Living behind a staffed gate means lower through traffic, quieter streets, and an added layer of safety for families and residents of all ages. Summerlin South already reports crime rates 47% lower than the national average, and the gated entry reinforces that sense of security. Residents can expect a smooth check in process for deliveries, contractors, and guests through the guard station. The community's 561 home plan across 261 acres keeps density low, which further reduces congestion at the gate.
