Ascension Summerlin Clubhouse and Amenities

Ascension Summerlin features an exclusive 7,500 square foot clubhouse packed with resort style amenities reserved solely for its residents. This guard gated community in the Peaks village of Summerlin South delivers a lifestyle experience that rivals many of the valley's top private clubs.

What Amenities Does the Ascension Clubhouse Offer?

The clubhouse anchors the community with two swimming pools, a hot tub, and a fully equipped fitness center. Four dedicated pickleball courts, a basketball court, and a golf simulator room give residents year round options for staying active. Social spaces include a lounge area, fire pit gathering zones, and dedicated event spaces designed for community functions and private gatherings.

Are There Outdoor Amenities Beyond the Clubhouse?

Ascension extends its amenity package well beyond the clubhouse walls. Walking and biking trails connect residents to Summerlin's 200 plus mile trail network, with Red Rock Canyon views as a constant backdrop. An activity lawn area and food truck zone add variety to weekends and community events. A bicycle repair station supports the active lifestyle that draws many buyers to this neighborhood, and a 77 space parking area ensures easy clubhouse access for all residents.

Is the Clubhouse Exclusive to Ascension Residents?

The Ascension clubhouse is reserved exclusively for community residents, not shared with other Summerlin neighborhoods. This exclusivity is a significant differentiator from many master planned communities where amenities serve thousands of households. With only 561 total homes across six subdivisions, residents enjoy low wait times and uncrowded facilities. Access to these amenities requires a one time $5,000 amenity fee along with monthly HOA dues of $322, which covers both the Ascension sub association and Summerlin master association.

Ryan Rose's Local Insight

Ryan Rose points out that the Ascension clubhouse stands apart because of its resident only access and the breadth of its offerings. Many buyers relocating to Las Vegas compare amenity packages across guard gated communities, and Ascension consistently ranks among the strongest.
