What Is The Peaks Village in Summerlin?

The Peaks is one of the most prestigious villages in Summerlin, located in Summerlin South and home to several of Las Vegas's most exclusive guard gated communities. This village includes Ascension, Mesa Ridge, The Ridges, and the ultra private Summit Club.

Which Communities Are Inside The Peaks Village?

The Peaks village brings together four distinct luxury neighborhoods, each with its own character and price range. Ascension is the newest addition, featuring 561 homes by Toll Brothers and Pulte with prices from approximately $1.2 million to over $4 million. Mesa Ridge and The Ridges offer guard gated luxury with custom home options. The Summit Club sits at the pinnacle as an ultra exclusive private golf club community with some of the highest price points in all of Nevada.

Why Is The Peaks Village Considered Prestigious?

The Peaks earns its reputation through a combination of guard gated security, elevated terrain with Red Rock Canyon views, and proximity to premier Las Vegas amenities. Summerlin South reports crime rates 47% lower than the national average, adding a layer of safety that complements the gated entries. The village sits roughly 20 minutes from the Las Vegas Strip and airport, with Downtown Summerlin's shopping and dining just minutes away. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is a 10 to 15 minute drive, ensuring convenient access to healthcare.

How Does Ascension Fit Within The Peaks?

Ascension occupies 261 acres within The Peaks and represents the village's most active new construction opportunity. With six subdivisions, an exclusive 7,500 square foot clubhouse, and homes built by nationally recognized builders, Ascension brings modern luxury to a village historically defined by custom estates. Ryan Rose works closely with buyers evaluating all four Peaks communities and notes that Ascension appeals to those who want new construction combined with the village's established prestige.
