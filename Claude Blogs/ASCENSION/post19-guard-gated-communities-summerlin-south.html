Guard Gated Communities in Summerlin South

Summerlin South is home to the highest concentration of guard gated luxury communities in the Las Vegas valley, anchored by The Peaks village. Buyers seeking staffed gate security, premium amenities, and strong property values will find their best options here.

Which Guard Gated Communities Are in Summerlin South?

Four guard gated communities define the luxury landscape of Summerlin South. Ascension is the newest, offering 561 homes by Toll Brothers and Pulte across 261 acres, with prices from approximately $1.2 million to over $4 million. The Ridges features ultra luxury custom homes behind its own guarded entry. Mesa Ridge provides guard gated living within The Peaks village at a range of price points. The Summit Club operates as the most exclusive option: a private golf club community with membership requirements and the area's highest home values.

How Do These Communities Compare on Security and Access?

Each of these four communities operates staffed or guard gated entry points with around the clock security. Summerlin South as a whole reports crime rates 47% lower than the national average, and guard gated access adds another layer of control. Ascension uses a staff gated model with 24/7 personnel verifying all visitors. The Ridges and Mesa Ridge follow similar protocols. The Summit Club maintains the most restrictive access, limiting entry to members and their approved guests only.

What Amenities Set These Communities Apart?

Ascension's exclusive 7,500 square foot clubhouse includes two pools, pickleball courts, a golf simulator, and a fitness center reserved solely for its residents. The Ridges offers parks, trails, and custom lot sizes that appeal to buyers seeking space and privacy. Mesa Ridge provides a guard gated setting with access to Summerlin's broader trail network.
