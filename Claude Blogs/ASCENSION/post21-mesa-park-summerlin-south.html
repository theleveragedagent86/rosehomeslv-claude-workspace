What Is Mesa Park in Summerlin South?

Mesa Park is a 19 acre community park in the Mesa Village area of Summerlin South, offering tennis courts, basketball courts, pickleball courts, and a playground for residents of all ages. For families living in nearby communities like Ascension, Mesa Park serves as one of the most versatile recreational spaces in the area.

What Amenities Does Mesa Park Offer?

Mesa Park covers 19 acres of well maintained green space designed for active lifestyles. The park features dedicated tennis courts, full size basketball courts, and pickleball courts that attract players throughout the week. A modern playground gives younger residents a safe place to burn off energy while parents relax nearby.

Located in the Mesa Village area of Summerlin South, the park sits within easy reach of Ascension and other Peaks Village neighborhoods. Summerlin is home to more than 300 parks and over 200 miles of interconnected trails, and Mesa Park stands out as one of the most activity rich options in the southern portion of the master planned community.

Why Do Summerlin South Residents Love Mesa Park?

The combination of court sports and open green space makes Mesa Park a daily destination for fitness minded residents. Pickleball, one of the fastest growing sports in the country, draws consistent crowds to the dedicated courts here. Basketball and tennis players benefit from well maintained surfaces and enough courts to avoid long waits, even on weekends.

The park also functions as a social hub for the surrounding neighborhoods. Families gather at the playground area, and the open lawns provide space for everything from yoga sessions to casual games of catch. The proximity to Summerlin's trail system means residents can jog or bike to the park without needing to drive.
