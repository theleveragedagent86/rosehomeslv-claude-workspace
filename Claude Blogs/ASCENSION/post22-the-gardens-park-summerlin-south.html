What Is The Gardens Park in Summerlin South?

The Gardens Park is a 20+ acre green space in Summerlin South featuring lush lawns, sports courts, and shady walking paths perfect for year round outdoor activity. Residents of Ascension and other nearby Summerlin communities use this park as a go to destination for exercise, relaxation, and family time.

What Makes The Gardens Park Stand Out?

Spanning more than 20 acres, The Gardens Park ranks among the largest neighborhood parks in Summerlin South. The park's defining feature is its mature tree canopy, which lines walking paths and provides shade that makes outdoor activity comfortable even during warmer months. Lush lawns stretch across the grounds, offering open space for picnics, group fitness, and casual sports.

Sports courts at The Gardens Park serve tennis, basketball, and pickleball players looking for a quality surface close to home. The shaded walking paths loop through the park and connect to portions of Summerlin's 200+ mile trail network, giving walkers and joggers extended route options without ever crossing a major road.

How Close Is The Gardens Park to Ascension?

The Gardens Park sits in Summerlin South, the same section of the master planned community that includes The Peaks Village where Ascension is located. This proximity means residents can reach the park in minutes by car or on foot via Summerlin's trail system. With more than 300 parks spread across the community, Summerlin South residents benefit from some of the highest park density in the Las Vegas Valley.

The park's location also places it near Red Rock Canyon, which is just 10 to 15 minutes west by car. Residents who enjoy The Gardens Park for daily walks often use it as a starting point before heading to more rugged terrain on weekends.
