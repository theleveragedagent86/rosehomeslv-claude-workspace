Where Is Barkin' Basin Dog Park in Summerlin?

Barkin' Basin Dog Park is Summerlin's dedicated off leash dog park, featuring separate areas for large and small dogs, agility equipment, and shaded benches for owners. Located within the Summerlin master planned community, it is one of the most popular amenities for dog owners living in neighborhoods like Ascension.

What Does Barkin' Basin Dog Park Include?

Barkin' Basin provides a fully fenced, off leash environment designed specifically for dogs and their owners. The park is divided into separate areas for large and small dogs, ensuring that smaller breeds can play safely without being overwhelmed by bigger, more energetic companions. Each section offers plenty of room to run and socialize.

Agility equipment throughout the park gives dogs a chance to practice jumps, tunnels, and obstacles. Shaded benches line the perimeter so owners can sit comfortably while watching their pets. Water stations and waste disposal areas keep the park clean and functional for daily visitors.

Is Summerlin a Dog Friendly Community?

Summerlin consistently ranks as one of the most dog friendly communities in the Las Vegas Valley. With more than 200 miles of interconnected trails and over 300 parks, dog owners have no shortage of walking routes and outdoor spaces. Barkin' Basin anchors the dog friendly lifestyle by providing a purpose built space where pets can exercise off leash in a controlled environment.

For Ascension residents in The Peaks Village of Summerlin South, the combination of trail access and a dedicated dog park makes pet ownership significantly more convenient. The community's low crime rate, 47% below the national average, adds peace of mind for owners who walk their dogs early in the morning or after dark.
