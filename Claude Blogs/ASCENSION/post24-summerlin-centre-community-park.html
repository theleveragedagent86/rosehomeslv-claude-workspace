What Is Summerlin Centre Community Park?

Summerlin Centre Community Park is a 20+ acre palm tree lined park featuring soccer fields, picnic spots, trails, and a shaded play area in the heart of Summerlin. It serves as one of the largest and most popular gathering spaces for families living in communities like Ascension in Summerlin South.

What Can You Do at Summerlin Centre Community Park?

Summerlin Centre Community Park spans more than 20 acres of landscaped grounds anchored by rows of mature palm trees. The park includes full size soccer fields that host youth leagues and adult pickup games throughout the year. Picnic areas with tables and shade structures make it easy to plan a family outing or a casual weekend lunch outdoors.

A network of walking trails winds through the park, connecting to portions of Summerlin's 200+ mile trail system. The shaded play area gives younger children a comfortable place to climb and explore, even during the warmer months when sun exposure is a concern for parents. The combination of active sports fields and passive relaxation spaces makes this park useful for residents of every age.

How Far Is Summerlin Centre from Ascension?

Summerlin Centre Community Park is centrally located within the Summerlin master planned community, easily accessible from The Peaks Village where Ascension is situated. The drive takes just minutes, and cyclists or joggers can reach the park via Summerlin's interconnected trail network without traveling on busy streets.

The park's central position also places it near shopping, dining, and entertainment options along the Summerlin Parkway corridor. Residents often combine a park visit with errands, making it a practical stop during the day rather than a separate trip.
