How Close Is Red Rock Canyon to Ascension in Summerlin?

Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area is approximately 10 to 15 minutes west of Ascension in Summerlin, making it one of the closest luxury communities to this world class outdoor destination. Residents enjoy quick access to hiking, cycling, rock climbing, and one of the most scenic drives in the American Southwest.

What Can You Do at Red Rock Canyon?

Red Rock Canyon offers a 13 mile scenic loop drive that draws visitors from around the world, but for Ascension residents it is practically a backyard amenity. The conservation area features dozens of hiking trails ranging from easy walks to challenging summit routes. Rock climbing, wildlife observation, and photography are equally popular, with the canyon's signature red sandstone formations providing a stunning backdrop for every activity.

Is Red Rock Canyon Good for Cycling?

The 13 mile scenic loop is one of the premier cycling routes in southern Nevada, attracting both recreational riders and competitive cyclists. The road features moderate elevation changes and sweeping desert views that make every ride memorable. Ascension residents can reach the loop entrance in minutes, and the Summerlin trail network offers a potential bike route to the canyon that continues to expand. Serious cyclists living in Ascension consider this proximity a major lifestyle perk.
