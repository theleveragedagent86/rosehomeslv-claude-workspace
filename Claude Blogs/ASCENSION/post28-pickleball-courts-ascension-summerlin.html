Does Ascension Summerlin Have Pickleball Courts?

Yes, Ascension in Summerlin features four dedicated pickleball courts exclusively for residents, located at the community's 7,500 square foot clubhouse. Pickleball players in Ascension also have access to additional courts at nearby Summerlin parks, making this one of the best neighborhoods in Las Vegas for the sport.

What Are the Pickleball Courts Like at Ascension?

The four pickleball courts at Ascension are part of the private clubhouse complex, reserved for community residents and their guests. The courts sit alongside a full suite of amenities including two swimming pools, a fitness center, and a golf simulator room. Because these courts are gated and exclusive to Ascension homeowners, wait times stay short and the playing experience remains top quality.

Are There Other Pickleball Courts Near Ascension?

Beyond the private courts, Ascension residents can find additional pickleball at Mesa Park and Oak Leaf Park in Summerlin South. Mesa Park is a popular destination for organized play and casual matches alike, while Oak Leaf Park provides another convenient option just minutes from the community. Having both private and public court access means Ascension residents can play as often as they like without competing for limited time slots.

Why Is Pickleball So Popular in Summerlin?

Pickleball has become one of the fastest growing sports in the Las Vegas Valley, and Summerlin's investment in dedicated courts reflects that demand. The sport appeals to all ages and fitness levels, making it ideal for a community like Ascension where families, professionals, and retirees live side by side. Ryan Rose points out that communities with built in pickleball facilities are increasingly attractive to buyers, especially those relocating from states where outdoor play is limited by weather. In Las Vegas, the mild winters mean year round court time.

What Else Can You Do at the Ascension Clubhouse?

The pickleball courts are just one piece of Ascension's amenity package. Residents also enjoy a basketball court, activity lawn, social lounge with fire pit, food truck hosting area, and a bicycle repair station. The clubhouse serves as the community's social hub, hosting events and providing a gathering space that extends well beyond athletics.
