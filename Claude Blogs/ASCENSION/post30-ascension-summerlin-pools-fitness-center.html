Does Ascension Summerlin Have a Pool and Fitness Center?

Ascension in Summerlin features two swimming pools, a hot tub, and a fully equipped fitness center inside its 7,500 square foot private clubhouse. These amenities are exclusive to Ascension residents, providing a resort style experience without ever leaving the neighborhood.

What Are the Pools Like at Ascension?

Ascension's two swimming pools offer space for both lap swimming and casual relaxation, complemented by a hot tub for unwinding after workouts or long days. The pool area is part of the clubhouse complex, which means residents enjoy a clean, well maintained environment with limited crowds. The Las Vegas climate allows for pool use across most of the year, making these amenities a true daily resource rather than a seasonal bonus.

What Equipment Does the Fitness Center Have?

The Ascension fitness center is housed within the clubhouse and gives residents a convenient alternative to commercial gym memberships. Alongside the fitness center, the community provides a basketball court and four pickleball courts for residents who prefer sports over traditional workouts. A golf simulator room rounds out the athletic offerings, giving golfers a chance to practice their swing without driving to the course.

What Other Amenities Are at the Ascension Clubhouse?

The 7,500 square foot clubhouse extends well beyond fitness and pools. Residents gather at the social lounge and fire pit area for evening events and casual meetups. The property includes an activity lawn, a food truck hosting area, event spaces for private functions, and a bicycle repair station for cycling enthusiasts. Ryan Rose considers the Ascension clubhouse one of the most complete amenity packages in Summerlin, noting that buyers consistently rank the facilities among their top reasons for choosing the community.

Is the Clubhouse Open to the Public?

No, the Ascension clubhouse and all its amenities are reserved exclusively for community residents and their guests. This private access keeps facilities well maintained and ensures a comfortable experience at every visit. For homebuyers comparing gated communities in Summerlin, the combination of two pools, a fitness center, pickleball courts, and a golf simulator in one resident only setting is difficult to match.
