What Should Families Know About Victoria Fertitta Middle School in Summerlin?

Victoria Fertitta Middle School is the zoned middle school for Ascension in Summerlin South, serving grades 6 through 8 with academic proficiency rates that exceed both district and state averages. With a GreatSchools rating of 6 out of 10 and a Niche grade of B, Fertitta provides Ascension families with a solid public school option during the critical middle school years.

How Does Fertitta Middle School Compare Academically?

Fertitta consistently outperforms Clark County School District averages in both core subjects. The school's 2024 to 2025 mathematics proficiency rate reached 41.2%, compared to 33% across the district. Reading proficiency stands at 54.7%, well above the county's 43.9% average. These results place Fertitta among the stronger middle school performers in CCSD, ranking 58th among Nevada's best public middle schools according to Niche.

What Is the School Environment Like at Fertitta?

The school enrolls approximately 1,216 students and operates with a student to teacher ratio of 26 to 1. Fertitta serves as the natural feeder school from Sandra B. Abston Elementary, creating continuity for families who begin their children's education at the zoned elementary level. U.S. News ranks the school 40th among Nevada middle schools, reflecting its competitive standing within the state.
