What Restaurants Are at Downtown Summerlin Near Ascension?

Downtown Summerlin is the dining hub closest to Ascension, located roughly 11 minutes by car. Wolfgang Puck Bar & Grill brings celebrity chef quality to the table with a contemporary American menu. Lazy Dog Restaurant & Bar offers a casual, family friendly atmosphere with comfort food and craft beers. Public School 702 delivers a creative gastropub experience with shareable plates and an inventive cocktail list.

What About Tivoli Village Restaurants Near Ascension?

Tivoli Village, about 18 minutes from Ascension, adds even more variety. Al Solito Posto is known for handmade pastas and charcuterie boards in an intimate Italian setting. Echo & Rig combines a full service butcher shop with a refined steakhouse, while Kabuki offers fresh Japanese cuisine. El Dorado Cantina rounds out the options with elevated Mexican fare.
