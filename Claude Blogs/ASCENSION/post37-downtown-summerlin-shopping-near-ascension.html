What Shopping Is Available at Downtown Summerlin Near Ascension?

Downtown Summerlin is the premier open air shopping destination near Ascension in Summerlin South, featuring more than 125 shops and restaurants across a walkable outdoor layout. Located approximately 11 minutes by car from Ascension, it serves as the go to retail center for residents who want major brands and specialty stores without a long commute.

What Stores Can You Find at Downtown Summerlin?

The center is anchored by Dillard's and Macy's, giving shoppers full department store access. Nordstrom Rack offers designer merchandise at reduced prices, while H&M and Old Navy provide everyday fashion staples. For beauty and self care, Sephora, Ulta Beauty, and Bath & Body Works are all on site. Specialty retailers like Michael Kors, True Religion, and Victoria's Secret round out the fashion lineup.

Is Downtown Summerlin More Than Just Shopping?

Downtown Summerlin goes well beyond retail. Regal Cinemas provides a full movie theater experience, making it easy to pair dinner with a film. The PGA Tour Superstore draws golfers from across the valley. Dining options include Wolfgang Puck Bar & Grill, Lazy Dog Restaurant & Bar, Grape Street wine bar, and Maggiano's Little Italy, so you can spend an entire afternoon or evening without leaving the complex.

How Does Downtown Summerlin Benefit Ascension Residents?

Proximity to a shopping center of this scale adds real value to daily life in Ascension. Residents can handle errands, browse new arrivals, grab lunch, and catch a movie all in one trip.
