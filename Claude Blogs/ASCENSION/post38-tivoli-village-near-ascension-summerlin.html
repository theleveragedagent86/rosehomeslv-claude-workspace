What Is Tivoli Village and How Close Is It to Ascension Summerlin?

Tivoli Village is a European inspired lifestyle center located about 18 minutes from Ascension in Summerlin South, offering more than 25 boutique stores and restaurants in an upscale, pedestrian friendly setting. For Ascension residents who enjoy curated shopping and quality dining, Tivoli Village delivers an experience that feels distinctly different from a traditional mall.

What Restaurants Are at Tivoli Village Near Ascension?

The dining scene at Tivoli Village caters to a range of tastes. Al Solito Posto is a standout for handmade pastas and charcuterie boards in a warm Italian atmosphere. Echo & Rig pairs a working butcher shop with a refined steakhouse menu, making it one of the most unique dining concepts in Summerlin. Brio Tuscan Grille serves Italian dishes and housemade pasta, while El Dorado Cantina brings elevated Mexican cuisine to the village.

What Kind of Shopping Does Tivoli Village Offer?

Tivoli Village focuses on boutique and specialty retail rather than big box stores. Jeff White Custom Jewelry provides bespoke jewelry design, and Vasari carries curated fashion pieces. The Ritual Salon and Spa and DavidBartonGym offer wellness and fitness services on site. Additional shops include home decor boutiques and lifestyle brands that you will not find at larger shopping centers nearby.

Why Do Ascension Residents Choose Tivoli Village?

The appeal of Tivoli Village lies in its atmosphere. Cobblestone walkways, Mediterranean architecture, and shaded courtyards create a relaxed, resort like feel. Leone Cafe provides a casual stop for coffee and light bites, while Kabuki and Made LV expand the dining options further. It complements Downtown Summerlin nicely, giving Ascension residents two very different retail and dining experiences within a short drive.
