TPC Summerlin Golf Near Ascension

TPC Summerlin, one of the most prestigious private golf clubs in Nevada, is located just minutes from the Ascension community in Summerlin South. Residents who love the game can access a PGA Tour caliber course without a long drive.

What Makes TPC Summerlin a Top Golf Destination?

Designed by Bobby Weed and opened in 1991, TPC Summerlin is a par 72, 7,243 yard championship course that sits at approximately 2,700 feet of elevation. The layout winds through natural arroyos and canyons, with bentgrass greens, water features, and native desert landscaping creating a striking contrast. Five sets of tees on every hole make the course playable for golfers at every skill level.

Does TPC Summerlin Host Professional Tournaments?

TPC Summerlin serves as the home course for the Shriners Children's Open, the second event on the PGA Tour's FedExCup schedule each season. Golf Digest has recognized it as one of America's best golf courses, and Nevada Business Magazine ranked it the number one private golf course in the state. The 12 acre practice facility includes two short game areas and a 10,000 square foot practice green.

How Close Is TPC Summerlin to Ascension?

Ascension residents can reach TPC Summerlin in roughly 10 minutes by car. The proximity makes it easy to enjoy a round of golf, attend a PGA Tour event, or take advantage of the club's dining and social amenities. For those seeking a second option, The Summit Club, an ultra exclusive private club, is also nearby within The Peaks village of Summerlin.
