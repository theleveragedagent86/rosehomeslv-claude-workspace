Durango Casino and Resort Near Ascension

Durango Casino and Resort is a premier entertainment destination located just minutes from the Ascension community in Summerlin South. This $750 million Station Casinos property offers dining, gaming, and resort amenities without the crowds of the Las Vegas Strip.

What Does Durango Casino and Resort Offer?

Spanning 71 acres at the 215 Beltway and West Durango Drive, the resort features a 200 room hotel tower, 83,000 square feet of casino space with approximately 2,000 slot machines and 40 table games, and a state of the art sportsbook. The Bel Aire Backyard provides a poolside retreat with private cabanas, and 20,000 square feet of meeting space accommodates events of all sizes. Over 4,200 complimentary parking spaces and 40 electric vehicle charging stations make access easy for locals.

What Are the Best Restaurants at Durango Casino?

Durango delivers a diverse dining lineup that rivals Strip resorts. Nicco's serves prime cuts and fresh seafood, Mijo Modern Mexican brings upscale Latin flavors, and Summer House offers a relaxed atmosphere with seasonal dishes. The George provides craft cocktails in a refined lounge setting. For casual bites, Eat Your Heart Out is a communal food hall featuring popular outposts from Los Angeles, New York, and Philadelphia alongside local favorites.

How Far Is Durango Casino From Ascension?

Ascension residents can reach Durango Casino and Resort in approximately 10 minutes via the 215 Beltway. The short drive makes it practical for a weeknight dinner, weekend entertainment, or hosting guests who want a resort experience close to home. Red Rock Casino Resort and Spa is another nearby option, roughly 11 minutes from the community.
