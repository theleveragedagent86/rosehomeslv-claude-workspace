How Far Is Ascension Summerlin From the Strip?

Ascension Summerlin is approximately 20 minutes from the Las Vegas Strip by car under normal traffic conditions. This location in Summerlin South gives residents easy access to world class entertainment while maintaining the quiet, suburban feel of a master planned community.

What Is the Best Route From Ascension to the Strip?

The most direct route follows Summerlin Parkway east to Interstate 15 south, which connects to the Strip's major exits. During off peak hours, the drive typically takes 18 to 22 minutes. Rush hour traffic, particularly between 4:00 PM and 6:30 PM, can add 10 to 15 minutes. Residents heading to the northern end of the Strip near the Wynn or Encore can often arrive in under 20 minutes, while destinations near Mandalay Bay at the southern end may take closer to 25 minutes.

How Far Is Ascension From the Airport and Downtown?

Harry Reid International Airport is roughly 20 minutes from Ascension, following a similar route via I 15. Downtown Las Vegas and the Fremont Street Experience sit about 25 minutes away via Summerlin Parkway and US 95. These drive times make Ascension practical for residents who travel frequently or enjoy the dining and nightlife scenes in multiple parts of the valley.

Does the Location Balance Access and Privacy?

Ascension's position in Summerlin South provides the best of both settings: proximity to the Strip's restaurants, shows, and entertainment, combined with the peace of a guard gated community surrounded by desert open space. Red Rock Canyon is just 10 to 15 minutes west, and Downtown Summerlin's 125 plus shops are even closer. Residents rarely feel far from anything, yet the neighborhood itself stays quiet and removed from tourist traffic.
