How Does Ascension Summerlin Compare to Mesa Ridge?

Ascension and Mesa Ridge are both guard gated luxury communities in The Peaks village of Summerlin South, offering similar price points but very different buying experiences. Ascension features brand new construction from Toll Brothers and Pulte Homes, while Mesa Ridge is an established community with resale inventory from various builders.

What Sets Ascension Apart From Mesa Ridge?

Ascension is one of the newest luxury communities in all of Summerlin, with home sales beginning in 2023. The community is planned for 561 homes across two premier builders: Toll Brothers and Pulte Homes. Buyers can select from modern floor plans with contemporary architecture, and pricing ranges from $1.2M to over $4M. Mesa Ridge, while also guard gated and located in The Peaks, offers primarily resale homes from a wider variety of builders across different construction eras.

Which Community Offers Better Amenities?

Ascension includes a dedicated residents only clubhouse featuring resort style pools, a fitness center, pickleball courts, basketball courts, and an expansive activity lawn. This amenity package is exclusive to Ascension homeowners and their guests. Mesa Ridge residents benefit from Summerlin's broader amenity network, including trails, parks, and community centers, but do not have a comparable private clubhouse within their gates.

Is Ascension or Mesa Ridge a Better Investment?

New construction in Ascension allows buyers to customize finishes and lock in builder warranties, which appeals to those who want a move in ready experience with modern energy efficiency and smart home features. Mesa Ridge's resale market can offer more immediate availability and, in some cases, larger lot sizes from earlier development phases. Ryan Rose notes that both communities benefit from The Peaks village location, strong guard gated security, and Red Rock Canyon proximity, making either a solid long term investment in Summerlin South.

Ryan Rose's Local Insight

The decision between Ascension and Mesa Ridge often comes down to new construction versus established resale. Buyers who want to select their lot, customize finishes, and enjoy a brand new clubhouse tend to gravitate toward Ascension. Those who prefer a move in ready home in a mature, landscaped neighborhood with proven resale history find Mesa Ridge compelling. Both communities share the same prestigious Peaks village address and guard gated lifestyle.
