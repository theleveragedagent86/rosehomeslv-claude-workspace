Is Ascension Summerlin a Good Place to Retire?

Ascension in Summerlin South is an excellent retirement option for buyers who want luxury guard gated living without the restrictions of a 55+ community. The community is open to all ages and offers single story floor plans, resort style amenities, and top tier security.

Does Ascension Have Single Story Homes for Retirees?

Ascension offers several single story floor plan options across its two builders, Toll Brothers and Pulte Homes. Collections including Highrock and The Pointe feature single level layouts designed for comfortable everyday living. Previously available single story options in the Ridgeline and Incline collections have sold out, reflecting strong demand from buyers who prefer main floor living. These homes range from approximately $1.2M to over $4M depending on the collection and customization.

What Makes Ascension Appealing for Retirement?

Guard gated security is one of the top reasons retirees choose Ascension over non gated alternatives. The community's private clubhouse provides daily lifestyle conveniences: a fitness center, resort style pools, pickleball courts, and an activity lawn for socializing. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is located roughly 10 to 15 minutes away, offering peace of mind for healthcare access. The broader Summerlin community adds over 300 parks and 200 miles of trails for active retirees.

Are There 55+ Alternatives Near Ascension?

Ascension is not age restricted, which means retirees live alongside families and professionals in a multigenerational setting. For buyers who prefer a dedicated 55+ environment, nearby options include Sun City Summerlin, Siena, and Regency at Summerlin.
