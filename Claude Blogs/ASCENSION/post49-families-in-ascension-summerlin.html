Is Ascension Summerlin a Good Community for Families?

Ascension in Summerlin South is one of the best family friendly luxury communities in Las Vegas, offering guard gated security, top rated zoned schools, and a private clubhouse with activities for all ages. The community has no age restrictions, making it ideal for families with children of any age.

What Schools Are Zoned for Ascension Summerlin?

Families in Ascension benefit from strong public school options within the Clark County School District. The community is zoned for Abston Elementary, Fertitta Middle School, and Durango High School. For families seeking private education, Bishop Gorman High School is located approximately five miles from the community. This combination of public and private options gives parents flexibility in choosing the best academic fit for their children.

What Family Amenities Does Ascension Offer?

The Ascension clubhouse serves as the social hub for families, featuring resort style pools, basketball courts, pickleball courts, and an expansive activity lawn. Children and teens have dedicated spaces for recreation without leaving the guard gated community. Beyond the gates, Summerlin offers over 300 parks and more than 200 miles of trails, providing endless outdoor activities for active families. The community's location in Summerlin South also places families near shopping, dining, and entertainment at Downtown Summerlin.

How Safe Is Ascension for Families With Children?

Guard gated access is one of the primary reasons families choose Ascension over non gated neighborhoods. The controlled entry point limits traffic to residents and their guests, creating a safer environment for children playing outdoors or walking to neighborhood amenities.
