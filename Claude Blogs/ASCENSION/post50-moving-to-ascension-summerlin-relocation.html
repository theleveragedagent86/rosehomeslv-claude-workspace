What Are Nevada's Tax Benefits for New Residents?

Nevada has no state income tax, which is one of the primary motivators for buyers relocating from states like California, Illinois, and New York. Ascension also has no SID or LID fees, which are special improvement district and local improvement district assessments that some new construction communities in Las Vegas charge. This means your ongoing costs are limited to your mortgage, property taxes, HOA dues, and standard homeowner expenses. For high income professionals and retirees, the tax savings alone can be substantial over time.

How Convenient Is Ascension's Location for Newcomers?

Ascension sits in Summerlin South with convenient access to the rest of the Las Vegas valley.
