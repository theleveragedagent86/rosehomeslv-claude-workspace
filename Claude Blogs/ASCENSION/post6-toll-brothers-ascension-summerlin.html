Toll Brothers is one of two exclusive builders at Ascension in Summerlin, offering luxury single family homes ranging from approximately $1,768,000 to well over $2,500,000. What Collections Does Toll Brothers Offer at Ascension?

Toll Brothers builds three collections within Ascension: Ridgeline, Highrock, and Crestline. Ridgeline, now sold out with 74 homes completed, featured single story plans between 3,415 and 3,615+ square feet starting around $1,768,000. Highrock offers single story homes from 3,623 to 3,940+ square feet, with prices starting at $1,998,995. Crestline is the two story collection, spanning 4,783 to 4,998+ square feet with five bedrooms and pricing from $2,054,995.

What Makes Toll Brothers Homes at Ascension Stand Out?

Every Toll Brothers home at Ascension features modern architecture with 14 to 22 foot great room ceilings, storefront windows, and 16 foot slider doors that blur the line between indoor and outdoor living. Covered loggias, Wolf appliances, and premium finishes come standard. Lot sizes average around 10,000 square feet, with premium lots reaching 15,000 to 20,000 square feet. Lot premiums range from $15,000 to over $1,000,000 for the most desirable positions.
