Pulte Homes at Ascension Summerlin

Pulte Homes is one of two exclusive builders at Ascension in Summerlin, delivering luxury single family homes priced from approximately $1,000,000 to over $2,000,000. With three collections offering a range of sizes and layouts, Pulte provides an accessible entry point into one of Las Vegas's most prestigious guard gated communities.

What Collections Does Pulte Homes Build at Ascension?

Pulte Homes offers three collections at Ascension: Incline, The Pointe, and Caprock. Incline, now sold out, featured single story plans from 2,297 to 2,875 square feet starting around $1,000,000. The Pointe delivers single story homes between 3,475 and 4,006 square feet from $1,975,990, though only a few lots remain. Caprock is the two story collection, ranging from 3,981 to 4,530 square feet with pricing starting at $1,699,990.

How Do Pulte Homes at Ascension Compare in Features?

Pulte Homes at Ascension feature modern architecture with open concept floor plans, high ceilings, and premium finishes throughout. Lot sizes average approximately 8,000 square feet, with lot premiums ranging from $25,000 to $300,000 depending on position and views. Every Pulte home sits within the fully guard gated Ascension community, providing access to the 7,500 square foot clubhouse, resort style pools, fitness center, pickleball courts, and golf simulator.

Which Pulte Collection Is the Best Value at Ascension?

Caprock currently offers the most square footage per dollar at Ascension, with two story plans exceeding 4,500 square feet starting at $1,699,990. For buyers who prefer single story living, The Pointe provides expansive layouts but availability is extremely limited. The sold out Incline collection represented the most affordable option, and resale homes from that collection now trade at prices reflecting strong equity gains reported throughout 2025.

According to Ryan Rose, who has worked extensively in Ascension and the broader Summerlin market, Pulte Homes delivers exceptional value at this price point. He notes that the Caprock collection in particular appeals to families relocating from out of state who want maximum living space within a premier gated community.
