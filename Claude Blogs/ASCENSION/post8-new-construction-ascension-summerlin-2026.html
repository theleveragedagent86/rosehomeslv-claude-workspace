New Construction at Ascension Summerlin 2026

New construction homes are still available at Ascension in Summerlin as of 2026, but inventory is limited with the community approximately 85% complete. Four active collections from Toll Brothers and Pulte Homes offer quick move in opportunities priced between $1,699,990 and over $2,575,000.

What New Construction Is Still Available at Ascension in 2026?

Of the six original collections at Ascension, four remain active. Toll Brothers offers Highrock (single story homes from $1,998,995) and Crestline (two story homes from $2,054,995). Pulte Homes offers Caprock (two story homes from $1,699,990) and The Pointe (single story homes from $1,975,990), though The Pointe has only a few lots remaining. Two collections, Incline by Pulte and Ridgeline by Toll Brothers, are completely sold out.

Are There Quick Move In Homes at Ascension Right Now?

Yes, quick move in homes at Ascension are priced between $2,150,000 and $2,575,000 with move in dates ranging from March through September 2026. These homes are already under construction or nearing completion, which means buyers can see exactly what they are purchasing. Quick move in homes eliminate the typical 12 to 18 month wait associated with building from scratch.

What Should Buyers Know About Buying New Construction at Ascension?

Ascension spans 261 acres and will contain 561 total homes when fully built out. The community carries no SID or LID fees, which is a significant financial advantage over some competing developments. HOA dues are $322 per month and cover access to the guard gate, 7,500 square foot clubhouse, pools, fitness center, pickleball courts, and golf simulator. Lot premiums vary widely: Pulte lots range from $25,000 to $300,000, while Toll Brothers lot premiums run from $15,000 to over $1,000,000 for premium positions.
