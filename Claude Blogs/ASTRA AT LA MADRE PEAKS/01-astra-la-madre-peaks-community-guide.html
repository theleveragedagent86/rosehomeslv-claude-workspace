Astra at La Madre Peaks is purpose built for custom estate living. Unlike other luxury neighborhoods in the valley, there are no production homes here. Buyers purchase individual lots and work with their own architects and builders to create one of a kind residences. The community requires a minimum home size of 4,500 square feet for single level builds and 6,000 square feet for multi level designs. The development spans 167 homesites with lot sizes ranging from roughly 21,000 to over 59,000 square feet, which translates to approximately half an acre up to two or more acres per lot.
