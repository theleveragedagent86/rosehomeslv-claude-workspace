Astra at La Madre Peaks in Summerlin and MacDonald Highlands in Henderson are two of the top luxury guard gated communities in the Las Vegas Valley, each offering a different lifestyle and location. ## How Does Astra at La Madre Peaks Compare to MacDonald Highlands? Astra at La Madre Peaks is a brand new development by Howard Hughes Holdings featuring 167 custom homesites across 171 acres at 4,125 feet elevation, the highest point in the Las Vegas Valley. Lot prices range from $2.6 million to $5.79 million, with finished homes expected to reach $7 million to $20 million or more. Homesites range from 0.49 acres to over 2 acres, and the community includes 24 hour guard gated access. MacDonald Highlands is an established luxury community in Henderson anchored by the DragonRidge Country Club and its championship golf course. Home prices typically range from $2 million to $15 million or more. The community offers custom and semi custom homes with mountain and Las Vegas Strip views from its elevated position in the McCullough Range on the southeast side of the valley. ## What Sets These Two Communities Apart? Location is the primary distinction. Astra sits on the western edge of Summerlin with direct proximity to Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area and the Spring Mountains. MacDonald Highlands is located in Henderson on the opposite side of the valley, offering a different perspective on the Strip and surrounding terrain. Ryan Rose notes that buyers often have strong preferences for the west side or east side of the valley based on commute patterns, school districts, and lifestyle priorities. Astra is a ground up opportunity where buyers purchase a lot and build a fully custom home. MacDonald Highlands offers both resale luxury homes and some remaining custom build opportunities. Buyers who want complete control over design from the foundation up will find more flexibility at Astra, while those seeking a finished luxury home in an established setting may gravitate toward MacDonald Highlands. ## Choosing Between Summerlin and Henderson Luxury Both communities deliver guard gated privacy, panoramic views, and access to world class amenities. Astra provides the largest homesites and highest elevation in the valley for buyers building new. MacDonald Highlands offers the advantage of an established neighborhood with a private golf club already in place. The right choice depends on whether you prioritize new construction flexibility or the amenities of a mature community.
