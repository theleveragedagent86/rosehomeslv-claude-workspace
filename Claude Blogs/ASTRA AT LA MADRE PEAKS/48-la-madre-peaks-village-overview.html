La Madre Peaks Village is the newest village within the Summerlin master planned community, bringing together luxury custom estates, single family homes, and age restricted living in one of the most scenic locations in the Las Vegas Valley. ## What Is La Madre Peaks Village in Summerlin? La Madre Peaks Village is a large scale residential development on the western edge of Summerlin, situated at the base of the Spring Mountains near Red Rock Canyon. The village includes multiple communities from different builders, anchored by Astra at La Madre Peaks, the ultra luxury guard gated enclave developed by Howard Hughes Holdings. Astra features 167 custom homesites across 171 acres with lot prices from $2.6 million to $5.79 million. Beyond Astra, the village includes several production home communities at various price points. Esplanade at Red Rock by Taylor Morrison will offer 398 attached and detached single story homes on 88.54 acres, priced from the $800,000s, designed for the 55 plus buyer. Golden Grove by Richmond American Homes is a 91 lot single family subdivision on 12.75 acres, and Agave Ridge by Tri Pointe Homes brings 99 single family lots across 29.45 acres. ## What Builders and Communities Are Part of La Madre Peaks Village? The village represents a mix of nationally recognized builders. Ryan Rose highlights that this diversity gives buyers options across a wide price spectrum, from the $800,000s to $20 million or more, all within the same village footprint. Additional communities from KB Homes and other builders are planned for future phases, expanding the village further. Astra at La Madre Peaks sits at the highest elevation point in the village at 4,125 feet, delivering panoramic views of the Strip, the desert floor, and the surrounding mountain ranges. The lower elevation communities still benefit from the natural terrain and proximity to outdoor recreation, though their view corridors differ from those at the top. ## Why La Madre Peaks Village Stands Out in Summerlin La Madre Peaks Village is one of the last major development areas in Summerlin, which means buyers here are purchasing in a location that cannot be replicated once buildout is complete. The village benefits from Summerlin's established infrastructure including top rated schools, parks, trail systems, and commercial centers like Downtown Summerlin and Tivoli Village. The combination of ultra luxury, move up, and active adult housing within one village creates a unique ecosystem where multiple generations and lifestyles coexist.
