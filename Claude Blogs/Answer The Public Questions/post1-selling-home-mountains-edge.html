This means pricing strategy matters more than ever. Recent data shows homes in this community sell for a median price per square foot of $246, with properties ranging from $329,000 for townhomes to over $1.2 million for premium single family residences. The community's appeal to buyers remains strong due to its 3,500 acres of master planned living, proximity to Red Rock Canyon, and access to Exploration Peak Park. Homes here attract families, professionals commuting to the Strip (15 to 20 minutes), and buyers relocating from higher cost markets like California. ## Steps to Sell in Mountain's Edge Start by obtaining a comparative market analysis to price your home accurately. In today's market, approximately 50% of Mountain's Edge homes sell below asking price, 32% sell at asking, and 18% sell above. Overpricing leads to extended days on market and eventual price reductions that signal desperation to buyers. Next, order your HOA resale package from the Mountain's Edge Master Association. Nevada Revised Statutes 116.4109 requires sellers to provide this documentation to buyers. The package includes CC&Rs, financial statements, reserve studies, and disclosure of any outstanding violations or assessments on your property. Prepare your home by addressing obvious repairs, enhancing curb appeal with desert landscaping touch ups, and considering professional staging.
