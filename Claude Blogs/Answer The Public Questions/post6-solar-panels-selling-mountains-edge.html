How Do Solar Panels Affect Your Mountain's Edge Home Sale?

Solar panels can significantly boost your Mountain's Edge home's value and speed of sale, but the impact depends almost entirely on whether you own the system outright or have it under a lease or power purchase agreement. Owned solar panels increase home values by approximately 4% to 7%, while leased systems can actually complicate the selling process and deter some buyers.

Owned Solar Systems: A Clear Selling Advantage

Las Vegas receives over 310 sunny days annually, making solar energy systems especially attractive to buyers. Homeowners with owned solar systems in Mountain's Edge can expect their panels to add approximately $9,000 to $29,000 in value based on the community's median home prices. Studies also show that homes with owned solar sell approximately 20% faster than comparable non solar properties.

The average Las Vegas homeowner saves roughly $1,909 per year on electricity with solar panels, with cumulative savings exceeding $39,000 over the typical 25 year lifespan of a system. For buyers comparing two similar Mountain's Edge homes, the one with owned solar offers lower long term ownership costs, which translates directly into willingness to pay more upfront. Owned systems transfer to the new owner like any other fixture attached to the property, making the sales process straightforward.

Leased Solar: Potential Complications

Leased solar panels or power purchase agreements (PPAs) require the buyer to qualify for and assume the remaining lease obligation. This adds paperwork, can delay escrow, and reduces your buyer pool because not every purchaser will qualify or want to take on a long term energy contract. Some buyers view leased panels as a liability rather than an asset, particularly if the lease terms are unfavorable or the remaining obligation is substantial.

If you have a leased system, discuss your options with your agent before listing. In some cases, buying out the lease before selling (if financially feasible) simplifies the transaction and removes a potential objection from buyers.

Premium Features That Add Extra Value

Solar installations paired with battery storage systems (such as the Tesla Powerwall), universal EV chargers, or smart energy management panels command higher premiums from eco conscious and technology focused buyers. These features are increasingly sought after in Mountain's Edge, where newer construction and modern homeowners value energy independence and sustainability.
