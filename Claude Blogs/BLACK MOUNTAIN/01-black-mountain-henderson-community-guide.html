Black Mountain is a master planned residential community in Henderson, Nevada, nestled at the base of the McCullough Range and named after the volcanic peak that anchors its southern skyline. With approximately 8,842 residents, a mix of gated and non gated neighborhoods, and home prices ranging from $300,000 to over $1,000,000, Black Mountain offers something for nearly every buyer. ## Where Is Black Mountain Located in Henderson? Black Mountain sits in the southeastern portion of Henderson, roughly 16 miles from the Las Vegas Strip and just two miles from Downtown Henderson. The commute to Harry Reid International Airport averages 16 to 20 minutes, while a trip to the Strip typically takes 20 to 25 minutes. That balance of proximity and separation is one reason the neighborhood continues to attract families, retirees, and professionals who want easy access to the city without living in the middle of it. ## What Types of Homes Will You Find in Black Mountain? The community features single family detached homes, townhomes, condos, and active adult residences. Most homes were built between 1998 and 2005, with architectural styles ranging from ranch and Spanish inspired designs to modern custom builds. Sizes span from roughly 1,232 square feet to over 5,000 square feet, and standard lot sizes start at one third of an acre. Lennar is currently building new construction at Black Mountain Ranch, with collections like Highpointe priced from $390,990 to $433,990 and floor plans between 1,600 and 3,300 square feet. ## What Sub Communities Make Up Black Mountain? Black Mountain is not a single neighborhood but a collection of distinct sub communities, each with its own character. Heritage at Black Mountain Ranch is a gated 55+ active adult community with a clubhouse, pool, and fitness center. Juniper Trails is a gated section featuring a pool, spa, greenbelt BBQ areas, and a dog park. Bighorn at Black Mountain offers condos with an exclusive clubhouse. Black Mountain Ranch includes an onsite park and splash park, and Lennar's new construction is transforming the former Black Mountain Golf and Country Club site into up to 1,100 new homes. ## A Local Expert's Perspective As a Las Vegas real estate specialist, Ryan Rose often recommends Black Mountain to buyers who want the feel of a smaller, established community with the convenience of Henderson's amenities nearby. The neighborhood's mix of price points, lot sizes, and lifestyle options makes it one of the most versatile areas in the Las Vegas valley. Many homeowners here also enjoy sweeping Strip views from their properties, a feature that is hard to find at this price range elsewhere in Henderson.
