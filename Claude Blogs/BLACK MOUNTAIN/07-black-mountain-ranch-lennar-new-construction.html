Lennar's Black Mountain Ranch is the largest new construction community in Henderson's Black Mountain area, built on the site of the former Black Mountain Golf Course that closed in 2018. With seven distinct collections and up to 1,100 homes approved by the Henderson City Council, this master planned development is reshaping the neighborhood. ## What Collections Does Lennar Offer at Black Mountain Ranch? Lennar currently offers seven collections at Black Mountain Ranch: Daybreak, Heritage, Founders Village I, Founders Village II, Highpointe, Landmark, and Juniper Trails. Each collection targets a different buyer profile. The Highpointe collection, for example, features homes from 1,600 to 3,300 square feet priced between $390,990 and $433,990. Heritage is a gated 55 plus active adult community with its own clubhouse, pool, and fitness center. The Founders Village collections are built on the former Founders Nine portion of the golf course, where a 200 home cap was set by the city council. This keeps density lower in that section compared to the rest of the development. ## What Amenities Come with Black Mountain Ranch? Black Mountain Ranch includes a community park and splash park for residents. The Heritage 55 plus section adds a dedicated clubhouse, swimming pool, and fitness center behind its own gated entry. Juniper Trails, another gated section, provides a pool, spa, BBQ area, and dog park. HOA fees remain competitive for the area. Black Mountain Ranch's general HOA runs approximately $35 per month, while the Heritage 55 plus community is about $123 per month. ## How Does Black Mountain Ranch Compare to Other Henderson New Builds? Black Mountain Ranch stands apart because of its location at the base of Black Mountain, giving many homesites elevated views of the Las Vegas Strip. The development sits roughly 16 miles from the Strip and 12 miles from Harry Reid International Airport, making commutes manageable. ## Ryan Rose on Buying New Construction at Black Mountain Ranch Ryan Rose recommends that buyers interested in Black Mountain Ranch visit multiple collections before committing, since the range of home sizes, price points, and community features varies significantly across the seven options.
