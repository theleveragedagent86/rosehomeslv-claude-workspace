Each gated section manages its own access controls independently, so gate styles and entry procedures vary. ## Which Parts of Black Mountain Are Not Gated? The majority of Black Mountain's residential areas are not gated. Many of the established single family neighborhoods built between 1998 and 2005 have open street access. Several of Lennar's newer Black Mountain Ranch collections, including Daybreak, Founders Village I, Founders Village II, Highpointe, and Landmark, are also non gated communities. HOA fees vary across Black Mountain's subdivisions, and gated communities generally carry higher monthly costs. Heritage at Black Mountain Ranch charges approximately $123 per month, covering gate maintenance, the clubhouse, and community amenities. The general Black Mountain Ranch HOA is roughly $35 per month for non gated sections.
