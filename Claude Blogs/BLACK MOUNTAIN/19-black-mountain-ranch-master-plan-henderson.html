Black Mountain Ranch is an active master planned community by Lennar in Henderson, Nevada, built on the site of the former Black Mountain Golf Course. The community features multiple collections with homes ranging from $345,990 to $997,990, spanning 1,696 to 3,948 square feet. With an onsite park, splash park, dog park, and playground, Black Mountain Ranch is one of Henderson's most dynamic new construction neighborhoods. ## What Collections Are Available at Black Mountain Ranch? Black Mountain Ranch includes several distinct collections, each targeting a different buyer profile. Landmark offers mid century modern styled single family homes starting from $542,990. Highpointe provides multi family and townhome options starting from $345,990, making it the most affordable entry point. Founders Village II delivers larger estate style homes starting from $831,990, designed for buyers who want maximum space and premium finishes. Additional collections include Daybreak, Founders Village I, and Heritage (the 55+ active adult section). Across all collections, floor plans range from two to five bedrooms with two to three and a half bathrooms. Lennar's Everything's Included program means upgrades like granite countertops, stainless steel appliances, and smart home technology come standard. ## What Amenities Does Black Mountain Ranch Offer? The community amenities at Black Mountain Ranch cater to families, active adults, and pet owners alike. The onsite park and splash park give families outdoor recreation space without leaving the neighborhood. A dedicated dog park provides off leash space for pet owners, while BBQ and picnic areas throughout the community encourage outdoor socializing. The location adds another layer of value. Residents are close to Heritage Park, Veterans Memorial Park, Cowabunga Bay water park, and the Henderson trail system that connects to Black Mountain's hiking routes. ## How Does Black Mountain Ranch Compare to Other Henderson Communities? Black Mountain Ranch stands apart because it combines new construction pricing with an established Henderson location. Unlike master plans on the far edges of the valley, this community sits in a mature part of Henderson with existing infrastructure, schools, shopping, and medical facilities already in place.
