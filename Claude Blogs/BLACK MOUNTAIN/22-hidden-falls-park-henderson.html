Hidden Falls Park is a 60 acre community park located at 281 W. Horizon Dr. in Henderson, NV. It serves as one of the largest and most versatile parks in the Black Mountain area, combining playgrounds, sports facilities, trails, and a dedicated dog park into a single destination. The park is open daily from 6:00 a.m. to midnight. ## What Features Does Hidden Falls Park Have? Hidden Falls Park offers something for nearly every age group and interest. Families will find two distinct playground areas, one designed for toddlers and another with more challenging climbing structures for older children. A splash pad provides relief during the hot summer months and is built with features for kids of different ages. Sports enthusiasts can use the basketball court and skate park, which includes street style features like handrails, ledges, and stairs. Open grass areas give plenty of room for pickup games, kite flying, or simply relaxing outdoors. ## Does Hidden Falls Park Have Picnic Areas and Trails? The park is well equipped for gatherings and cookouts. Covered picnic shelters, tables, and BBQ grills are spread throughout the grounds. Restrooms are conveniently located near the main activity areas. Walking trails wind through the 60 acre site and connect to the Amargosa Trailhead, giving hikers and joggers direct access to a longer trail network. This connection makes Hidden Falls Park a popular starting point for residents who want a quick neighborhood walk or a more ambitious trek along the Amargosa Trail system. ## Is There a Dog Park at Hidden Falls Park? Yes. Hidden Falls Park includes a dedicated, fenced dog park where dogs can run off leash. This makes the park especially appealing to pet owners in the Black Mountain community who want a convenient option without driving across town. The dog park sits within the larger park grounds, so you can combine a dog outing with playground time or a family picnic. ## A Local Favorite in the Black Mountain Community Ryan Rose regularly points clients toward Hidden Falls Park when discussing the lifestyle benefits of living near Black Mountain.
