Bark Park at Heritage Park is the City of Henderson's largest dog park, located at 350 S. Racetrack Rd. in Henderson, NV 89015. This 5 acre facility was designed with direct input from pet owners and regular dog park users, and it delivers a unique experience that goes well beyond a basic fenced lot. If you live near Black Mountain and have a dog, this is the top destination in the area. ## What Makes Bark Park Different From Other Dog Parks? Bark Park stands out because of its size and amenities. The 5 acre grounds include separate dog runs for large and small breeds, a full agility course, a walking trail, and dog bone themed benches throughout the park. Drinking stations serve both pets and their owners. The highlight is a splash pad built exclusively for dogs. Open daily from 7:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. between May 1 and September 30, the splash pad gives dogs a place to cool off during Henderson's hottest months. No other dog park in Henderson offers this feature. ## What Are the Rules at Bark Park? The City of Henderson maintains clear rules to keep the park safe and enjoyable. Owners may bring a maximum of three dogs at a time. All dogs must be leashed before entering the park grounds and when exiting the designated off leash areas. Children under age 5 are not permitted inside the fenced off leash zones. The park also hosts classes, programs, and small dog shows throughout the year. These events give pet owners a chance to socialize and train their dogs in a structured environment. ## How Far Is Bark Park From Black Mountain?
