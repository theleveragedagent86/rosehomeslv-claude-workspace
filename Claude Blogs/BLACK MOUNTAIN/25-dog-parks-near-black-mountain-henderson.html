Black Mountain residents in Henderson, NV have access to several dog parks within a short drive of their neighborhood. The City of Henderson operates 16 off leash dog parks across the city, and the ones closest to Black Mountain offer everything from basic fenced areas to full agility courses and splash pads. Here is a guide to the best options nearby. ## What Is the Best Dog Park Near Black Mountain? Bark Park at Heritage Park is the top choice. Located at 350 S. Racetrack Rd., it is Henderson's largest dog park at 5 acres. The facility features separate runs for large and small dogs, an agility course, a walking trail, dog bone themed benches, and drinking stations. A dog only splash pad operates daily from 7:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. between May and September. The drive from most Black Mountain homes takes about 5 to 10 minutes. ## Does Hidden Falls Park Have a Dog Park? Yes. Hidden Falls Park at 281 W. Horizon Dr. includes a dedicated fenced dog park within its 60 acre grounds. This is one of the closest options for residents on the south side of Black Mountain. The park also features playgrounds, a splash pad, a skate park, and picnic areas, making it a great choice for families who want to bring the dog along for a full day out. ## Are There Other Off Leash Options in Henderson? Beyond Bark Park and Hidden Falls, Henderson offers additional dog parks spread across the city. The Juniper Trails community includes its own dedicated dog park, and several neighborhood parks throughout Henderson have fenced off leash areas. The city continues to add pet friendly amenities as new communities develop. For dog owners who prefer trail time over park time, the River Mountains Loop Trail and several routes in the Black Mountain trail network allow leashed dogs. Black Mountain Grill also offers a pet friendly patio, giving you a dining option after a morning hike with your dog. ## Why Pet Friendly Amenities Matter to Homebuyers Ryan Rose sees a growing number of buyers who prioritize pet friendly neighborhoods when searching for homes in Henderson.
