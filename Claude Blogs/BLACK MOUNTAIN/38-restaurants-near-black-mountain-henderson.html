Black Mountain in Henderson, NV has a surprisingly strong dining scene for a residential neighborhood. Within a few minutes of most homes, residents can choose from 24/7 American fusion, upscale Italian, authentic Japanese ramen, quick serve Mexican, fish tacos, and teriyaki bowls. Two shopping centers along Horizon Ridge Parkway anchor the local restaurant options, and a full service grill on Eastern Avenue rounds out the choices. ## What Restaurants Are Closest to Black Mountain? Black Mountain Marketplace at the corner of W Horizon Drive and W Horizon Ridge Parkway holds several dining options in one convenient stop. Il Chianti Italian Steak and Seafood serves homemade pasta, seafood, and steaks for dinner Tuesday through Sunday. Roberto's Taco Shop and Wahoo's Fish Taco cover quick serve Mexican cravings, while Teriyaki Madness offers customizable made to order bowls. Horizon Village Square, just east along Horizon Ridge Parkway, is home to Ohjah Noodle House, a local favorite for tonkotsu ramen, chicken katsu, and Japanese appetizers. The restaurant is open daily for lunch and dinner with fast service and generous portions. ## Where Can You Eat Late Night Near Black Mountain? Black Mountain Grill at 11021 S Eastern Ave operates 24 hours a day, seven days a week. The menu covers breakfast, lunch, and dinner with wood fired pizzas, steaks, ribs, and Pacific Rim fusion dishes. Prime Rib night runs on Fridays and Saturdays starting at 4pm. A pet friendly patio, gaming lounge, and free Wi Fi make this a versatile neighborhood hangout at any hour. ## Are There Any Upscale Dining Options? Il Chianti is the standout for fine dining in the immediate area. Chef Rodolfo Janeo Jr. brings over 30 years of experience to a menu of handmade pasta, grilled lamb chops, and fresh seafood. The restaurant offers a three course prix fixe menu for $37 on Tuesdays and Wednesdays, and Cheese Wheel tableside pasta service on Thursdays and Sundays. ## A Neighborhood Built for Everyday Convenience Ryan Rose regularly points out that Black Mountain's dining options reflect the kind of walkable, self contained neighborhood that buyers want in Henderson. Having Italian fine dining, ramen, tacos, and a 24/7 grill within minutes of home means fewer trips across town and more time enjoying where you live.
