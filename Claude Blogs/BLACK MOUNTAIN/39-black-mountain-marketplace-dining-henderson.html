Black Mountain Marketplace is a 44,373 square foot neighborhood shopping center at the northwest corner of W Horizon Drive and W Horizon Ridge Parkway in Henderson, NV. The plaza serves as the primary dining hub for Black Mountain residents, with restaurants ranging from upscale Italian to quick serve Mexican and teriyaki bowls. Everything is within a short drive or walk from most homes in the community. ## What Restaurants Are in Black Mountain Marketplace? Il Chianti Italian Steak and Seafood is the anchor dining tenant. This upscale restaurant serves homemade pasta, grilled lamb chops, fresh seafood, and Italian wines for dinner Tuesday through Sunday. Chef Rodolfo Janeo Jr. has over 30 years of experience, and the restaurant hosts Cheese Wheel pasta nights on Thursdays and Sundays. Teriyaki Madness offers customizable teriyaki bowls made to order with your choice of protein, vegetables, and sauce. Roberto's Taco Shop provides fast, affordable Mexican food that locals rely on for a quick lunch. Wahoo's Fish Taco serves fish tacos and casual seafood in a laid back atmosphere. Tropical Smoothie rounds out the options with smoothies and light fare for a quick stop between errands. ## Is Black Mountain Marketplace Walkable from Nearby Homes? The marketplace sits right at the intersection of two main roads in the Black Mountain neighborhood. Homes along Horizon Ridge Parkway, Horizon Drive, and the surrounding streets are within a short walk or bike ride. The center's location at a major corner means easy access from multiple directions, and parking is plentiful for those driving from other parts of the community. ## How Does the Marketplace Compare to Other Henderson Dining Areas? Black Mountain Marketplace is smaller than the large retail corridors along Eastern Avenue or St. Rose Parkway, but that is part of its appeal. The plaza feels like a neighborhood gathering spot rather than a commercial strip. You can grab a smoothie, pick up tacos, sit down for fine Italian dining, or order a teriyaki bowl all in one compact location without fighting traffic or hunting for parking. ## Your Neighborhood Dining Room Ryan Rose helps buyers understand that access to everyday conveniences like Black Mountain Marketplace is what separates a good neighborhood from a great one.
