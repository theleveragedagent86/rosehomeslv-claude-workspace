Black Mountain Marketplace is a neighborhood shopping center located at the northwest corner of W Horizon Dr and W Horizon Ridge Pkwy in Henderson, NV. The center spans approximately 44,373 square feet and serves as the primary retail hub for Black Mountain residents. Its walkable location within the neighborhood makes it one of the most convenient shopping options in the area. ## What Stores Are at Black Mountain Marketplace? Black Mountain Marketplace features a mix of dining and service oriented businesses that cater to daily needs. Popular restaurants in the center include Il Chianti, Teriyaki Madness, Roberto's Taco Shop, Wahoo's Fish Taco, and Tropical Smoothie Cafe. These options give residents a solid variety of quick service and sit down meals without leaving the neighborhood. Beyond dining, the center includes service providers and smaller retail shops that round out the tenant mix. The layout is designed as a traditional strip center with ample parking and easy access from both Horizon Dr and Horizon Ridge Pkwy. ## How Close Is Black Mountain Marketplace to Black Mountain Homes? Because the center sits at the edge of the neighborhood itself, most Black Mountain residents can reach it in just a few minutes by car or even on foot. This proximity is a significant selling point for homebuyers who value having everyday essentials and dining within walking distance. Compared to centers that require a drive across town, Black Mountain Marketplace delivers true neighborhood level convenience. ## Is Black Mountain Marketplace the Only Shopping Near Black Mountain? Not at all. Horizon Village Square is located nearby along Horizon Ridge Pkwy and adds even more retail options. An Albertson's grocery store sits near the intersection of W Horizon Ridge Pkwy and E Horizon Dr, providing full service grocery access. For a larger mall experience, Galleria at Sunset is roughly 4 to 5 miles north and features over 120 stores including Macy's and H&M. ## Why Black Mountain Marketplace Matters to Buyers Ryan Rose often highlights Black Mountain Marketplace when discussing the walkability and lifestyle perks of the Black Mountain neighborhood. Having a well stocked shopping center right at the edge of the community saves residents time and adds daily convenience that many suburban neighborhoods lack. For buyers who want both a quiet residential setting and easy access to shops and restaurants, this center checks both boxes.
