Horizon Village Square is a neighborhood retail center located along Horizon Ridge Pkwy in Henderson, NV. It sits adjacent to the Black Mountain community and provides residents with everyday shopping, dining, and pharmacy access. The center is anchored by a CVS and features a variety of smaller shops and restaurants including Ohjah Noodle House. ## What Stores and Restaurants Are at Horizon Village Square? The center's tenant mix is built around daily convenience. CVS serves as the primary anchor, offering pharmacy services, health products, and general household items. Ohjah Noodle House is a standout dining option known for its Japanese noodle bowls and hibachi style dishes. Additional shops and service providers fill out the center, giving residents options for errands and quick meals in a single stop. The layout is a standard neighborhood plaza with surface parking and direct access from Horizon Ridge Pkwy. This makes it easy to pull in for a prescription refill or a lunch run without navigating heavy traffic or distant parking structures. ## How Far Is Horizon Village Square from Black Mountain? Horizon Village Square is immediately adjacent to the Black Mountain neighborhood along the Horizon Ridge Pkwy corridor. Most residents can reach the center in under five minutes by car. For those on the western edge of the community, it is close enough to walk or bike. This proximity gives Black Mountain homeowners a second convenient retail option alongside the nearby Black Mountain Marketplace. ## What Other Shopping Is Nearby? The Horizon Ridge Pkwy corridor is home to several retail centers within a short drive. Black Mountain Marketplace sits at the corner of W Horizon Dr and W Horizon Ridge Pkwy and features restaurants like Teriyaki Madness and Roberto's Taco Shop. An Albertson's grocery store is also located near this intersection for full service grocery shopping. For a larger retail experience, Galleria at Sunset is approximately 4 to 5 miles north and offers over 120 stores. ## Convenient Shopping for Black Mountain Residents Ryan Rose frequently mentions Horizon Village Square as one of the everyday conveniences that makes Black Mountain attractive to buyers.
