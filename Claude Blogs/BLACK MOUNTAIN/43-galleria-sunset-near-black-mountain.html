Galleria at Sunset is an indoor shopping mall located approximately 4 to 5 miles north of the Black Mountain neighborhood in Henderson, NV. The mall features 123 stores and serves as the largest enclosed retail destination in the Henderson area. Major anchors include Macy's, Kohl's, H&M, and Forever 21, along with a food court and casual dining options throughout the property. ## What Stores Are at Galleria at Sunset? The mall covers a wide range of shopping categories. Fashion retailers include H&M, Forever 21, and a selection of specialty clothing stores. Macy's and Kohl's anchor opposite ends of the mall and offer department store selections spanning clothing, home goods, and accessories. Beyond fashion, shoppers will find electronics, jewelry, beauty, and gift retailers throughout the corridors. The food court provides quick options for families shopping with kids, while sit down restaurants near the entrances offer a more relaxed dining experience. The variety makes Galleria at Sunset a practical destination for seasonal shopping, gift buying, and browsing in a climate controlled environment. ## How Far Is Galleria at Sunset from Black Mountain? The drive from Black Mountain to Galleria at Sunset takes roughly 10 to 15 minutes depending on traffic. The most direct route follows Horizon Ridge Pkwy north to the Sunset Road corridor. This puts a full scale mall experience within easy reach while keeping Black Mountain residents insulated from the commercial density that surrounds larger retail hubs. ## What Else Is Near Galleria at Sunset? Sunset Station sits adjacent to the mall and adds entertainment options including a 13 screen movie theater, 72 lane bowling center, and a 5,000 seat outdoor amphitheater. This pairing means a single trip can combine shopping, dining, a movie, and live entertainment. The Water Street District is also located about 2 miles from Black Mountain and offers a more boutique, locally driven shopping and dining experience in Henderson's historic downtown. ## A Full Mall Experience Close to Home Ryan Rose points out that proximity to Galleria at Sunset is one of the lifestyle advantages Black Mountain residents enjoy.
