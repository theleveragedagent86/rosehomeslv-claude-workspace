Sunset Station is a full scale casino and entertainment complex located approximately 4 to 5 miles north of the Black Mountain neighborhood in Henderson, NV. The property features a 21 story hotel tower, a casino floor, 13 screen movie theater, 72 lane bowling center, 542 seat bingo hall, a 5,000 seat outdoor amphitheater, and nine restaurants. It is one of the most complete entertainment destinations in the Henderson area. ## What Entertainment Does Sunset Station Offer? The variety at Sunset Station goes well beyond a typical casino. The 13 screen Regal movie theater gives families and moviegoers a convenient option without driving to Las Vegas. Strike Zone Bowling features 72 lanes and hosts leagues, open bowling, and cosmic bowling nights. The 5,000 seat outdoor amphitheater brings touring musical acts and live performances throughout the year. For gaming, the casino floor offers slots, table games, and a sportsbook. The 542 seat bingo hall is one of the largest in Henderson and draws a dedicated crowd. Whether you prefer a night of cards or a family bowling outing, the property has something to match. ## What Restaurants Are at Sunset Station? Sunset Station houses nine restaurants covering a range of cuisines and price points. Options span from casual counter service spots to more polished sit down dining. This makes the property practical for a quick bite before a movie or a full dinner outing. The restaurant variety also means Black Mountain residents can rotate through different dining experiences without traveling far from home. ## How Far Is Sunset Station from Black Mountain? The drive from Black Mountain to Sunset Station takes roughly 10 to 15 minutes heading north along the Sunset Road corridor. The complex sits adjacent to Galleria at Sunset mall, so a single trip can include shopping, dining, a movie, bowling, or a concert. This clustering of entertainment and retail makes the Sunset Road area a go to destination for Black Mountain residents looking for a full evening out. ## Entertainment Without the Strip Ryan Rose regularly tells buyers that Sunset Station is one of the key reasons Black Mountain offers a complete lifestyle package.
