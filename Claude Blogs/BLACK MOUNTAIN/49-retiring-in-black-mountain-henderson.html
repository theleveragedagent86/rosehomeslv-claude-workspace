Heritage at Black Mountain Ranch is a dedicated 55 plus community with approximately 400 single story homes behind a gated entrance. The community includes a private clubhouse, swimming pool, spa, fitness center, and organized social activities. HOA fees run approximately $123 per month, which covers common area maintenance, landscaping, and access to all amenities. Homes here are designed for single level living with open floor plans, attached garages, and low maintenance yards. For retirees who want the benefits of age restricted living without feeling isolated, Heritage at Black Mountain Ranch delivers. It sits within the larger Black Mountain neighborhood, so residents have easy access to shopping, dining, and recreation beyond the community gates. ## What Healthcare Options Are Near Black Mountain? Access to quality healthcare is a top concern for retirees, and Black Mountain delivers on that front. Henderson Hospital, a 303 bed facility, is within Henderson city limits. St. Rose Dominican Hospital Siena Campus is the largest hospital in Henderson with 219 beds and a full range of services including cardiac care and emergency medicine. West Henderson Hospital and Rose de Lima Campus (145 beds) round out the options. All four facilities are a short drive from Black Mountain, ensuring that emergency and routine care are always close. Multiple urgent care clinics, specialists, and primary care physicians also practice along the Horizon Ridge and Eastern Avenue corridors near the neighborhood. ## What Do Retirees Do in Black Mountain? Black Mountain's location at the base of the McCullough Range makes it ideal for active retirees. The River Mountains Loop Trail offers 34 miles of paved path for walking and cycling, connecting Henderson to Lake Mead. The Black Mountain Recreation Center provides fitness classes, a pool, and social programming. Downtown Henderson's Water Street District, just 2 miles away, hosts farmers markets, live music, and dining. Nevada's lack of state income tax is another draw. Social Security benefits, pension income, and retirement account withdrawals are not taxed at the state level, which can meaningfully stretch a fixed income budget. ## Ryan Rose's Perspective on Retiring in Black Mountain Ryan Rose has helped many retirees settle into Black Mountain, and he highlights the neighborhood's walkability, quiet streets, and proximity to hospitals as key selling points.
