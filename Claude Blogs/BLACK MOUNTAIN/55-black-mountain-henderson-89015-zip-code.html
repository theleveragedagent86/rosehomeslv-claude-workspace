Black Mountain Henderson falls within zip code 89015, one of Henderson's most established residential areas. This zip code covers much of central and eastern Henderson, including Black Mountain and several surrounding neighborhoods. For buyers researching the area, understanding what 89015 includes helps frame the community's demographics, services, and real estate landscape. ## What Area Does Zip Code 89015 Cover? Zip code 89015 spans a large section of Henderson east of Boulder Highway and south of Lake Mead Parkway. It includes Black Mountain, portions of the older Henderson townsite, and areas extending toward the McCullough Range foothills. The zip code encompasses both established neighborhoods built in the late 1990s and early 2000s and some of Henderson's original mid century housing stock closer to downtown. This mix of housing ages means 89015 offers a wide range of property types and price points. Black Mountain represents the more modern, suburban section of the zip code, while areas closer to Water Street reflect Henderson's older residential character. ## What Are the Demographics of 89015? The Black Mountain area within 89015 has a population of approximately 8,842 residents. Median household income ranges from $71,300 to $89,137 depending on the data source, which is consistent with Henderson's overall middle to upper middle class profile. The neighborhood attracts a mix of families, retirees, and working professionals. Niche rates the area with an A grade for crime and safety, and 67% of surveyed residents describe the neighborhood as "very safe." These numbers reflect the broader trend of Henderson consistently ranking among Nevada's safest cities. ## What Services and Amenities Are in 89015? Residents of 89015 have access to a strong network of public services. The Henderson Fire Department and Henderson Police Department both serve the area. Shopping and dining options in 89015 include Black Mountain Marketplace, Horizon Village Square, and the revitalized Water Street District.
