What Is The Canyons in Summerlin

The Canyons is one of Summerlin's most established and sought after residential villages, nestled in the western Las Vegas valley between Summerlin Parkway, Rampart Drive, Alta Drive, and Town Center Drive. With guard gated subdivisions, a TPC golf course, and 69 acres of parkland at its doorstep, this community delivers a rare combination of privacy, luxury, and desert beauty.

Where Is The Canyons Located in Summerlin?

The Canyons sits within zip codes 89144 and 89145, placing residents just 20 to 25 minutes from the Las Vegas Strip and roughly 15 minutes from Downtown Summerlin. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is a one to three minute drive away. The village is bounded by four major corridors, making access to the 215 Beltway and US 95 straightforward for daily commuters.

What Types of Homes Are in The Canyons?

Built primarily between 1995 and the early 2000s by builders like Christopher Homes, Warmington Homes, and Beazer Homes, The Canyons features over 14 subdivisions. Home styles range from Mediterranean custom estates and modern villas to condos and townhomes. Prices start near $600,000 for condos and climb above $5,000,000 for custom estates, with most single family homes listing above $1,000,000.

Is The Canyons a Gated Community?

Yes. The Canyons village itself is gated, and six of its subdivisions offer additional guard gated access: Aventura, Bellacere, Canyon Fairways, Eagle Rock, Mira Villa, and Palisades. This layered security model is one of the reasons families and retirees consistently choose The Canyons over other Summerlin neighborhoods.

What Makes The Canyons Stand Out?

Ryan Rose, a local Las Vegas real estate expert, points to the combination of mature landscaping, proximity to Cottonwood Canyon Park, and walkable access to TPC Las Vegas as features that set The Canyons apart. The community is largely built out, which means tree lined streets, established neighbors, and no construction disruptions. It is the kind of village that feels settled in all the right ways.
