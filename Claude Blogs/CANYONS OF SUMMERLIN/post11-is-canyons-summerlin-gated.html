Is The Canyons in Summerlin a Gated Community?

Yes, The Canyons in Summerlin is a gated community spanning over 750 acres in the western Las Vegas valley. Beyond the main perimeter, six individual subdivisions within The Canyons feature their own guard gates, creating multiple layers of controlled access for residents.

How Many Gated Subdivisions Are Inside The Canyons?

The Canyons contains six guard gated subdivisions: Aventura, Bellacere, Canyon Fairways, Eagle Rock, Mira Villa Condominiums, and Palisades. Each neighborhood maintains its own staffed entry point where security personnel verify residents and guests around the clock. This dual layer of gating sets The Canyons apart from most communities in the Las Vegas valley.

The community sits within clearly defined boundaries, bordered by Summerlin Parkway to the north, Rampart Drive to the east, Alta Drive to the south, and Town Center Drive to the west. These natural borders, combined with controlled entry points, give homeowners a strong sense of privacy and exclusivity that few Summerlin neighborhoods can match.

What Are the Benefits of Living in a Guard Gated Community?

Guard gated communities like The Canyons offer reduced through traffic, visitor verification, and enhanced property security. Residents report that the gated environment contributes to a quieter atmosphere and stronger sense of neighborhood connection. Homes within guard gated sections of The Canyons typically command premium pricing, ranging from luxury condos at Mira Villa to custom estates at Bellacere that exceed $5,000,000.

Beyond security, The Canyons provides access to world class amenities including TPC Las Vegas golf course, the 69 acre Cottonwood Canyon Park, and proximity to the JW Marriott Resort and Spa. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center sits just minutes from the community entrance, adding everyday convenience to the resort style living experience.
