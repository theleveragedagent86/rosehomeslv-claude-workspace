Bellacere Custom Estates: One of Summerlin's Most Exclusive Neighborhoods

Bellacere is a guard gated enclave of custom and semi custom estates inside The Canyons in Summerlin, featuring approximately 57 homes ranging from 3,000 to over 16,000 square feet. With price points reaching well above $5,000,000, Bellacere stands as one of the most prestigious residential addresses in the entire Las Vegas valley.

What Types of Homes Are in Bellacere?

Bellacere offers custom and semi custom estates originally developed by Christopher Homes alongside select custom builders. Lot sizes and floor plans vary significantly, giving homeowners the flexibility to create truly one of a kind properties. Many homes feature sprawling single story layouts, private courtyards, and resort style outdoor living spaces designed for the Summerlin climate.

Several Bellacere estates enjoy direct frontage along TPC Las Vegas, the par 71 championship golf course that hosts the PGA Tour's Shriners Children's Open each year. These golf course homesites offer unobstructed fairway views and a level of privacy that few Las Vegas neighborhoods can provide. The combination of custom architecture and premium positioning makes Bellacere a consistent draw for luxury buyers relocating to Southern Nevada.

What Amenities Are Near Bellacere in The Canyons?

Bellacere residents benefit from the full suite of Canyons amenities, including the 69 acre Cottonwood Canyon Park and immediate proximity to the JW Marriott Resort and Spa. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center sits within a one to three minute drive, and Summerlin Parkway provides quick access to the Las Vegas Strip and downtown corridors. Daily conveniences like dining and shopping along Town Center Drive are just moments from the guard gate.

The neighborhood's guard gated entry adds another layer of security beyond The Canyons' main perimeter, ensuring Bellacere maintains the quiet, low traffic atmosphere that custom home buyers expect at this price point.
