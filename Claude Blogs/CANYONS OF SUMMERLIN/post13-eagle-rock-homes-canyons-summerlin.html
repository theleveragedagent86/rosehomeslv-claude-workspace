Eagle Rock Homes in The Canyons Summerlin: What Buyers Need to Know

Eagle Rock is a guard gated subdivision of single family homes inside The Canyons in Summerlin, built between 1997 and 2002 by Beazer Homes and Warmington Homes. Homes in Eagle Rock typically sell in the $1,000,000 to $1,500,000 range, offering established luxury living within one of Summerlin's most sought after gated communities.

What Style of Homes Does Eagle Rock Offer?

Eagle Rock features two story single family homes with spacious floor plans, many including private swimming pools and three car garages. The neighborhood is fully built out, meaning no new construction disrupts the established streetscape. Mature landscaping and consistent architectural design give Eagle Rock a polished, cohesive feel that newer subdivisions often lack.

Beazer Homes and Warmington Homes, the original builders, were known for quality construction during the late 1990s building period. Homeowners in Eagle Rock benefit from solid structural foundations while many have updated interiors to reflect current design trends. The combination of proven construction and modern renovations makes these properties attractive to buyers who prefer move in ready homes with character.

How Convenient Is Eagle Rock's Location?

Eagle Rock sits approximately one mile from Summerlin Parkway, giving residents fast access to both the Las Vegas Strip and the downtown corridor. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is a one to three minute drive from the community. The JW Marriott Resort and Spa, TPC Las Vegas golf course, and the 69 acre Cottonwood Canyon Park are all within The Canyons' boundaries, providing resort caliber recreation without leaving the neighborhood.

Shopping and dining options along Town Center Drive and Rampart Boulevard round out the daily convenience factor. Eagle Rock's position within The Canyons means residents pass through the main community gate and then through Eagle Rock's own guard gate, delivering dual layer security that families and professionals value highly.
