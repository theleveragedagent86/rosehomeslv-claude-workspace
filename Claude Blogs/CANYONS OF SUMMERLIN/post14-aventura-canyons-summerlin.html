Aventura in The Canyons Summerlin: Guard Gated Luxury Living

Aventura is a guard gated subdivision of single family luxury homes nestled inside The Canyons in Summerlin. As one of six individually gated neighborhoods within the 750 acre Canyons community, Aventura offers residents a layered security experience paired with access to some of the finest amenities in the Las Vegas valley.

What Makes Aventura Stand Out in The Canyons?

Aventura delivers luxury single family living behind its own dedicated guard gate, separate from The Canyons' main community entrance. This dual gated structure means visitors pass through two security checkpoints before reaching the neighborhood, creating an atmosphere of privacy that appeals to professionals, families, and retirees alike. The homes feature upscale finishes, generous lot sizes, and architectural designs that complement the surrounding desert landscape.

The subdivision sits within The Canyons' boundaries along with other prestigious neighborhoods like Bellacere, Eagle Rock, and Palisades. While each subdivision caters to different buyer profiles and price points, Aventura holds its own as a top choice for those seeking guard gated luxury without the commitment of a fully custom build. Homes here offer a polished, turnkey lifestyle that balances elegance with everyday livability.

What Amenities Do Aventura Residents Enjoy?

Living in Aventura means full access to The Canyons' community amenities, starting with TPC Las Vegas, the par 71 championship course where Tiger Woods claimed his first PGA Tour victory in 1996. The 69 acre Cottonwood Canyon Park provides walking trails, open space, and outdoor recreation just steps from home. The JW Marriott Resort and Spa offers dining, spa services, and event spaces adjacent to the community.

For practical needs, Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is a one to three minute drive away. Summerlin Parkway access connects residents to the Las Vegas Strip and downtown in under 20 minutes, while shopping and restaurants along Town Center Drive handle daily errands with ease.
