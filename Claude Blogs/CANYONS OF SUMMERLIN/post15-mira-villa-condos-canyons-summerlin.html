Mira Villa Luxury Condos: Guard Gated Living in The Canyons Summerlin

Mira Villa is a guard gated luxury condominium community inside The Canyons in Summerlin, offering residents resort style amenities with views of the TPC Las Vegas golf course, the Las Vegas Strip, and the surrounding mountain ranges. For buyers who want a Canyons address without the maintenance demands of a single family estate, Mira Villa delivers an exceptional alternative.

What Do Mira Villa Condos Offer?

Mira Villa condominiums feature upscale finishes, open floor plans, and private balconies or patios designed to showcase the views that make this location so desirable. The community includes a resort style swimming pool and spa, giving residents a vacation caliber lifestyle just steps from their front door. Units vary in size and configuration, accommodating everyone from solo professionals to downsizing couples who want luxury without the upkeep of a large estate.

As one of only six guard gated subdivisions within The Canyons, Mira Villa benefits from dual layer security. Residents pass through The Canyons' main entrance and then through Mira Villa's own dedicated guard gate, ensuring a controlled, low traffic environment throughout the community. This structure appeals strongly to buyers who value both convenience and privacy.

Why Choose a Condo Over a Single Family Home in The Canyons?

Mira Villa represents the only condominium option inside The Canyons, making it the exclusive entry point for buyers who prefer a lock and leave lifestyle within this premier community. While neighborhoods like Bellacere and Eagle Rock require homeowners to manage landscaping, pools, and exterior maintenance, Mira Villa's HOA structure handles these responsibilities. This frees residents to enjoy the TPC Las Vegas golf course, Cottonwood Canyon Park's 69 acres of open space, and the JW Marriott Resort and Spa without the time investment that estate ownership demands.

Location benefits match those of every Canyons subdivision: Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is minutes away, Summerlin Parkway provides quick valley access, and Town Center Drive shopping and dining are right outside the community.
