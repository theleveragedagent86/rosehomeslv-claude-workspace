Canyon Crest Homes in The Canyons Summerlin

Canyon Crest is a gated enclave of 90 luxury homes perched along the TPC Las Vegas golf course in The Canyons village of Summerlin. With quarter acre lots, sweeping fairway views, and proximity to Red Rock Canyon, this subdivision delivers one of the most refined residential settings in the western Las Vegas valley.

What Homes Are Available in Canyon Crest?

Canyon Crest features single family homes built on generous quarter acre lots, with most properties priced at $1 million and above. Residences offer open floor plans, spacious interiors, and architectural styles that blend desert contemporary with Mediterranean influences. Many homes include private pools, courtyards, and upgraded landscaping that complements the surrounding golf course terrain.

What Is the Location Like for Canyon Crest Residents?

Situated within The Canyons village in zip code 89144, Canyon Crest residents enjoy direct views of the TPC Las Vegas championship course and the rugged backdrop of Red Rock Canyon. Tivoli Village is just minutes away, offering dining destinations like Echo and Rig Butcher Steakhouse and Brio Tuscan Grille. Downtown Summerlin and Summerlin Hospital Medical Center are both a short drive from the neighborhood.

Why Do Buyers Choose Canyon Crest Over Other Canyons Subdivisions?

With only 90 homes in the entire community, Canyon Crest offers a level of exclusivity that larger subdivisions cannot match. The combination of golf course frontage, mountain views, and gated privacy attracts buyers who want a turnkey luxury lifestyle without the price tag of custom estate communities like Bellacere. The Las Vegas Strip is roughly 15 to 20 minutes east, keeping world class entertainment within easy reach.

What Does a Local Expert Say About Canyon Crest?

Ryan Rose highlights Canyon Crest as one of the best value propositions in The Canyons for buyers seeking golf course living. The mature landscaping and established feel of the neighborhood give it a sense of permanence that newer communities simply cannot replicate. For buyers who want views, space, and Summerlin convenience, Canyon Crest consistently delivers.
