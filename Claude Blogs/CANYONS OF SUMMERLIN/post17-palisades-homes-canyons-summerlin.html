Palisades Homes in The Canyons Summerlin

Palisades is one of six guard gated subdivisions in The Canyons village of Summerlin, featuring approximately 131 semi custom luxury homes built by Christopher Homes along the TPC Las Vegas golf course. For buyers seeking large lots, private courtyards, and championship golf views, Palisades is one of the most desirable neighborhoods in the entire master planned community.

What Are Palisades Homes Like?

Built between 1998 and 2005, Palisades homes range from 3,700 to over 6,500 square feet and sit on quarter to half acre lots. Many properties feature private swimming pools, eight foot block walls for added privacy, and beautifully designed courtyards. Prices in this guard gated community generally start above $1 million, reflecting the quality of construction and premium positioning on the golf course.

What Amenities Do Palisades Residents Enjoy?

Residents of Palisades have access to the TPC Las Vegas Clubhouse, which offers fine dining, well lit tennis courts, a full service lounge, and an Olympic sized swimming pool. Membership to the clubhouse is available to all Palisades homeowners. Beyond the golf club, Tivoli Village and Downtown Summerlin provide shopping, dining, and entertainment within a few minutes of the neighborhood.

How Does Palisades Compare to Other Guard Gated Communities?

Palisades stands apart from neighboring guard gated communities like Bellacere and Eagle Rock through its combination of lot size and semi custom architecture. The community entrance is located off North Hualapai Way, with HOA fees of approximately $260 per month covering guard gate staffing and common area maintenance. At roughly 131 homes, Palisades is larger than some exclusive enclaves but still intimate enough to maintain a quiet, residential atmosphere.
