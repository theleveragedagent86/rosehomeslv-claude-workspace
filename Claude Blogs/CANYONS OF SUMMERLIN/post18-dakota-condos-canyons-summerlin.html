Dakota Condominiums in The Canyons Summerlin

Dakota Condominiums is a gated community of 175 condo units tucked inside The Canyons village of Summerlin, offering golf course views, attached garages, and a location just minutes from Downtown Summerlin. For buyers looking for low maintenance living in one of Las Vegas's most established neighborhoods, Dakota delivers real value.

What Do Dakota Condos Offer?

Dakota features 175 condos arranged in southwestern style triplex buildings with two to three bedrooms, two to three bathrooms, and floor plans ranging from 1,282 to 1,707 square feet. Every unit includes an attached garage, which is uncommon among condo communities in the Summerlin area. Units on the southeastern side of the development enjoy direct views of the TPC Las Vegas championship golf course.

What Amenities Are Available at Dakota?

The community provides a pool, spa, barbecue area, and exercise room for residents. The gated entrance is located off Canyon Run Drive, which transitions into Twin Hills Drive. Beyond the community walls, Tivoli Village and Downtown Summerlin offer shopping, dining, and entertainment. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is just minutes away, making Dakota an appealing option for retirees and medical professionals alike.

How Does Dakota Compare to Other Canyons Condos?

Dakota offers larger floor plans than many competing condo communities in The Canyons, including Cierra Condominiums. The attached garages and triplex building layout give each unit a more residential feel compared to traditional stacked condo buildings. Prices for Dakota condos generally start in the $600,000 range, placing them among the more accessible entry points into The Canyons village. The Las Vegas Strip is roughly 15 to 20 minutes away, keeping city access convenient.

What Does a Local Expert Say About Dakota?

Ryan Rose sees Dakota as one of the smartest entry points into The Canyons for buyers who want the Summerlin lifestyle without the upkeep demands of a single family home. The golf course views, gated security, and walkable access to community amenities make Dakota a consistent performer in terms of both livability and resale value.
