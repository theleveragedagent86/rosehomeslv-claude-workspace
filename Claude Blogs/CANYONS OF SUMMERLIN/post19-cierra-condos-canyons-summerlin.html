Cierra Condominiums in The Canyons Summerlin

Cierra Condominiums is a gated condo community within The Canyons village of Summerlin, offering two story residences with oversized garages, a community pool, and proximity to the TPC Las Vegas golf course. For buyers seeking an affordable foothold in one of Summerlin's most established villages, Cierra is a neighborhood worth exploring.

What Are Cierra Condos Like?

Built between 1998 and 2002, Cierra features two story condos ranging from 1,159 to 1,338 square feet with two to three bedrooms and one to two bathrooms. Each unit comes with an oversized detached garage. Interior features include gas fireplaces, ceiling fans, breakfast bars, pantries, and walk in closets in the primary bedroom. The gated entrance is located off Covington Cross Drive, providing controlled access for residents.

What Amenities and Attractions Are Near Cierra?

The community includes a clubhouse, pool, and spa for residents. Cierra borders the TPC Las Vegas golf course, giving homeowners a green, open backdrop without the price tag of a direct golf course lot. Schools, parks, and Cottonwood Canyon Park are all within close proximity. Downtown Summerlin and Tivoli Village are just minutes away for shopping and dining, and the Las Vegas Strip is roughly 15 to 20 minutes east.

How Does Cierra Compare to Dakota Condos in The Canyons?

While both communities serve condo buyers in The Canyons, Cierra offers a smaller footprint and lower price point than Dakota Condominiums. Cierra units are two story layouts with detached garages, whereas Dakota provides single level triplex designs with attached garages and larger floor plans. Buyers who prioritize affordability and a cozy, low maintenance lifestyle often gravitate toward Cierra, while those wanting more square footage look to Dakota.

What Does a Local Expert Say About Cierra?

Ryan Rose recommends Cierra to first time Summerlin buyers and downsizers who want the security and amenities of The Canyons without stretching their budget. The gated access, community pool, and golf course adjacency make Cierra a solid lifestyle choice. With the continued growth of nearby shopping and dining destinations, property values in this pocket of Summerlin have held steady over the years.
