Scarlett Canyon and Star Canyon Homes in Summerlin

Scarlett Canyon and Star Canyon are two single family home subdivisions within The Canyons village of Summerlin, offering gated living, single story floor plans, and easy access to parks, schools, and the TPC Las Vegas golf course. These neighborhoods represent some of the most approachable price points in one of Summerlin's premier residential villages.

What Homes Are in Scarlett Canyon?

Scarlett Canyon is an all single story gated community built by Woodside Homes, located just south of West Alta Drive between South Hualapai Way and Town Center Drive. Homes range from 1,146 to 1,783 square feet with three bedrooms, two full bathrooms, and two car garages. The architectural style blends contemporary design with Spanish influences, featuring tile roofs and desert landscaping. Prices generally range from the mid $500s to the low $600s, making this one of the most accessible subdivisions in The Canyons.

What Is Star Canyon Like?

Star Canyon is another single family subdivision within The Canyons village, contributing to the diverse housing mix that makes this 754 acre community so appealing. Like Scarlett Canyon, Star Canyon offers single family residences in a gated setting with convenient access to Summerlin Parkway and the 215 Beltway. Both neighborhoods benefit from the shared amenities and parks throughout The Canyons village, including Cottonwood Canyon Park and the walking trails that connect to surrounding open spaces.

What Is Nearby for Scarlett Canyon and Star Canyon Residents?

Both communities sit within minutes of Downtown Summerlin, Tivoli Village, and the Canyon Pointe shopping area. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is a short drive away on North Town Center Drive. Red Rock Casino and Resort is also nearby for dining and entertainment. The Las Vegas Strip is approximately 13 miles east, and daily errands are handled easily with groceries, pharmacies, and restaurants all within the surrounding Summerlin retail centers.
