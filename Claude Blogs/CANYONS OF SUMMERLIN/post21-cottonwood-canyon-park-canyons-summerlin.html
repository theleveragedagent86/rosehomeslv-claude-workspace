Cottonwood Canyon Park: 69 Acres of Trails and Green Space Near The Canyons in Summerlin

Cottonwood Canyon Park is the crown jewel of outdoor recreation near The Canyons in Summerlin, offering 69 acres of trails, playgrounds, and picnic areas at 915 Canyon Bluff Circle, Las Vegas, NV 89144. Whether you walk, run, or bike, this park delivers year round enjoyment just minutes from your front door.

What Makes Cottonwood Canyon Park Worth Visiting?

Spanning 69 acres, Cottonwood Canyon Park blends desert landscaping with lush grassy areas connected by concrete pathways. Covered pavilions with grills make it a go to spot for weekend picnics and family gatherings. Well lit paths and benches line the routes, so early morning joggers and evening walkers feel equally welcome.

The park features dedicated children's play areas and playgrounds that keep younger visitors entertained while parents enjoy the scenery. Dogs are allowed on leash throughout the park, with signs posted reminding handlers to keep pets secured. The alternating patches of turf and native desert plants create a visually appealing landscape unique to the Summerlin area.

How Far Is Cottonwood Canyon Park from The Canyons?

Residents of The Canyons in Summerlin can reach Cottonwood Canyon Park in under five minutes by car or enjoy a short walk along Summerlin's interconnected trail system. The park sits along a 3.5 mile trail corridor that stretches from behind Costco west of Pavilion Center Drive to Hualapai Way, passing through two overpass tunnels at Town Center and Pavilion. This connectivity means you can start your morning run from your neighborhood and end up at the park without ever crossing a busy street.

What Activities Can You Do at Cottonwood Canyon Park?

The park accommodates road biking, running, walking, and casual strolls along its concrete pathways. A 1.25 mile loop option is available for those who prefer a shorter circuit, while the full out and back trail covers 3.5 miles.
