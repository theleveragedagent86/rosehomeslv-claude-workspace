Dog Friendly Parks and Trails Near The Canyons in Summerlin

The Canyons in Summerlin is one of the most dog friendly neighborhoods in Las Vegas, with on leash access to Cottonwood Canyon Park and over 200 miles of interconnected trails perfect for daily walks with your pet. Here is where to take your dog near The Canyons.

Can You Bring Dogs to Cottonwood Canyon Park?

Yes. Cottonwood Canyon Park at 915 Canyon Bluff Circle allows dogs on leash throughout the entire 69 acre property. Signs are posted reminding handlers to keep pets leashed and under control at all times. The park's concrete pathways, grassy areas, and shaded pavilions make it a comfortable outing for both dogs and their owners. Well lit paths and benches along the route let you take breaks as needed during longer walks.

What Trails Near The Canyons Are Good for Dog Walking?

The Cottonwood Canyon Park Trail covers 3.5 miles out and back with a 1.25 mile loop option, all on flat concrete surfaces that are gentle on paws during cooler hours. Hualapai Canyon Trail, at less than one mile, provides a short and quiet route that works well for older dogs or quick morning outings. Both trails connect to Summerlin's 200 plus mile trail network, so you can vary your route daily without driving anywhere.

The 8 foot wide village trails run through natural arroyos and open space corridors, giving dogs room to walk comfortably alongside their owners. Ryan Rose notes that the trail connectivity is a major draw for pet owners considering The Canyons, since it eliminates the need to load dogs into a car just to find a decent walking path.

Where Can You Buy Pet Supplies Near The Canyons?

PetSmart is located at the nearby Canyon Pointe shopping center, just a short drive from The Canyons neighborhood. The store carries food, grooming supplies, toys, and offers veterinary services. Having a full service pet retailer within minutes of home adds daily convenience for dog owners who want quick access to essentials without a long trip across town.
