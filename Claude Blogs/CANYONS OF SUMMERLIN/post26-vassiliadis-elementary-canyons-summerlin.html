Vassiliadis Elementary School in The Canyons Summerlin

Billy and Rosemary Vassiliadis Elementary is the number one ranked elementary school in Nevada and the designated feeder school for The Canyons village in Summerlin. Families in this neighborhood walk to one of the highest performing public schools in the state without ever leaving the community.

What Are Vassiliadis Elementary's Test Scores and Ratings?

Vassiliadis Elementary earns an A minus overall grade from Niche and an 8 out of 10 rating from GreatSchools. The school ranks in the top 5% of all Nevada schools for standardized test performance. Math proficiency sits at 82%, and reading proficiency reaches 76%, both well above Clark County and state averages.

The school serves grades PreK through 5 and enrolls approximately 1,083 students. Its student to teacher ratio is 18 to 1, which is competitive for a high enrollment public elementary in Southern Nevada. These numbers reflect a campus that consistently outperforms neighboring schools across multiple academic benchmarks.
