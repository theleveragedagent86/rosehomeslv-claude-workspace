Sig Rogich Middle School Near The Canyons Summerlin

Sig Rogich Middle School is the designated feeder school for students in The Canyons village in Summerlin, serving grades 6 through 8 at 235 Pavilion Center Drive. It holds a Five Star designation from the Nevada Department of Education and ranks as the number one middle school in the Clark County School District.

What Are Sig Rogich Middle School's Ratings and Test Scores?

Rogich earns a B plus overall grade from Niche and an 8 out of 10 from GreatSchools, placing it at number 12 among all middle schools in Nevada. Math proficiency reaches 56%, and reading proficiency hits 67%, both above district averages. The school enrolls approximately 1,668 students and maintains a reputation for academic consistency across grade levels.

The Five Star designation is awarded by the Nevada Department of Education to schools that demonstrate sustained high performance. Rogich is one of a small number of middle schools in Clark County to carry this distinction, which reflects strong results in both academics and school climate indicators.

What Programs Does Rogich Offer Students from The Canyons?

Rogich provides a comprehensive middle school curriculum that prepares students for the honors, AP, and IB tracks available at Palo Verde High School. The school offers elective programs in the arts, technology, and athletics, giving students exposure to a range of interests before high school. Its location adjacent to The Canyons means most families in the village have a short commute to campus.

The transition from Vassiliadis or Bonner Elementary into Rogich is a well established pathway in this part of Summerlin. Students from both feeder elementary schools merge at Rogich, creating a larger peer group that carries through to Palo Verde High School.
