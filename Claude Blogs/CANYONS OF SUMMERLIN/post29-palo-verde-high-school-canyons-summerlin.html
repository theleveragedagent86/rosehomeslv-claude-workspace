Palo Verde High School Near The Canyons Summerlin

Palo Verde High School is the zoned public high school for families living in The Canyons village in Summerlin, located at 333 Pavilion Center Drive. It offers honors, Advanced Placement, and International Baccalaureate programs, making it one of the most academically versatile high schools in the Las Vegas valley.

What Are Palo Verde High School's Ratings and Test Scores?

Palo Verde earns a B plus overall grade from Niche and a 4 out of 5 from SchoolDigger, ranking number 41 among the best public high schools in Nevada. The school's test scores place it in the top 30% statewide, with 35% math proficiency and 61% reading proficiency. These numbers outperform the Clark County School District average and reflect a campus with a strong college preparatory focus.

The school serves grades 9 through 12 and draws students from multiple Summerlin villages, including The Canyons. Its size allows for a broad course catalog that smaller high schools in the valley cannot match, particularly in advanced academic tracks.

What Academic Programs Does Palo Verde Offer?

Palo Verde runs three distinct advanced tracks: honors courses, the Advanced Placement program, and the International Baccalaureate program. The IB program is especially notable because only a handful of public high schools in Clark County offer it. Students who enter through Sig Rogich Middle School are well prepared for these accelerated options. The school also supports strong extracurricular programs in athletics, performing arts, and student leadership.

For families who value academic rigor at the high school level, the IB program alone sets Palo Verde apart from most public high schools in Southern Nevada. Students can earn college credit and an internationally recognized diploma without attending a private school.
