Is Summerlin a Good Place to Live in Las Vegas

Summerlin is widely considered one of the best places to live in Las Vegas, and The Canyons village is a prime example of why. With top rated schools, guard gated neighborhoods, 200 plus miles of trails, and proximity to world class dining and shopping, Summerlin consistently ranks among the most desirable master planned communities in the country.

What Makes Summerlin So Popular With Families?

Families are drawn to Summerlin for its school quality and safety. The Canyons feeds into Billy and Rosemary Vassiliadis Elementary, rated the number one elementary school in Nevada by Niche with 82% math and 76% reading proficiency. Sig Rogich Middle School holds a Five Star designation, and Palo Verde High School offers honors, AP, and IB programs. Private options like Bishop Gorman, Faith Lutheran, and The Meadows School are all nearby.

How Convenient Is the Location?

Living in The Canyons puts you 20 to 25 minutes from the Las Vegas Strip, 20 to 30 minutes from Harry Reid International Airport, and roughly 15 minutes from Downtown Summerlin. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center sits just one to three minutes away. Daily errands, dining, and entertainment are all within a short drive thanks to Downtown Summerlin, Tivoli Village, and Canyon Pointe shopping centers.

What About Outdoor Recreation?

Summerlin offers over 200 miles of interconnected trails, and The Canyons borders Cottonwood Canyon Park, a 69 acre green space with walking paths and dog friendly areas. Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area is minutes to the west. TPC Las Vegas, a championship golf course, sits within the village. Residents here spend more time outdoors than in almost any other Las Vegas neighborhood.

The village strikes a balance between quiet residential living and the energy of a growing city that is hard to find elsewhere in Las Vegas.
