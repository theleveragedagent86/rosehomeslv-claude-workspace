Private Schools Near The Canyons in Summerlin

Families in The Canyons village in Summerlin have access to several nationally recognized private schools within a short drive. What Private High Schools Are Near The Canyons?

Bishop Gorman High School is the most prominent private high school in Southern Nevada, serving grades 9 through 12 with a college preparatory curriculum and nationally ranked athletic programs. It is located in Summerlin and draws students from across the valley. Faith Lutheran Middle and High School offers grades 6 through 12 in a Christian based academic environment and is also located within Summerlin, providing families with a faith centered alternative to public school options.

Both schools maintain competitive admissions processes and offer financial aid programs. Families in The Canyons benefit from proximity to these campuses, with commute times typically under 15 minutes.

Are There Private Schools for Younger Students Near The Canyons?

The Meadows School is a PK through 12 independent school and one of the most selective private institutions in Southern Nevada. It offers a rigorous liberal arts curriculum starting at the preschool level and continuing through high school graduation. Shenker Academy provides a preschool option near The Canyons for families seeking early childhood education in a private setting. Together, these schools cover the full age range for families who prefer private education from the start.

Having both preschool and K through 12 private options nearby means families can keep children in the private school system without long commutes at any stage.
