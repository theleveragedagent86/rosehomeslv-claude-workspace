Best Restaurants Near The Canyons in Summerlin: Where Locals Actually Eat

Residents of The Canyons in Summerlin enjoy some of the best dining in the Las Vegas Valley, with dozens of acclaimed restaurants just minutes from their front door. From global Asian fusion to authentic Italian, the neighborhood's location puts you within a short drive of Downtown Summerlin, Tivoli Village, and Rampart Commons.

What Are the Top Upscale Restaurants Near The Canyons?

JING Las Vegas at Downtown Summerlin has been one of the highest rated restaurants in the valley since opening in 2019, serving global Asian cuisine with prime steaks, sushi, and seafood at 10975 Oval Park Drive. Harlo Steakhouse and Bar, also at Downtown Summerlin, features Chef Partner Gina Marinelli's expertly sourced cuts in a setting of dark wood and polished marble. La Strega, Marinelli's James Beard nominated Italian trattoria at 3555 S Town Center Drive, rounds out the fine dining trio with farm to table pastas and seafood.

Honey Salt on Rampart Boulevard brings a different energy entirely. Owned by renowned restaurateur Elizabeth Blau and Chef Kim Canteenwalla, this independent restaurant pairs farm to table comfort dishes with a relaxed, welcoming atmosphere. Wolfgang Puck Bar and Grill and Vintner Grill add even more upscale variety for residents who prefer staying close to home.

Where Can You Find Great Casual Dining Near The Canyons?

Downtown Summerlin delivers strong casual options including True Food Kitchen for health conscious meals, Lazy Dog Restaurant and Bar for family friendly fare, and Makers and Finders for standout Latin American brunch. Marufuku Ramen brings authentic Japanese bowls to the neighborhood. Over at Tivoli Village, Echo and Rig combines a working butcher shop with a full steakhouse, while El Dorado Cantina serves organic, non GMO Mexican cuisine that draws a loyal following.
