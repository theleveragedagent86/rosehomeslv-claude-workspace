JING Las Vegas: Global Asian Fusion Dining Minutes from The Canyons in Summerlin

JING Las Vegas is one of the highest rated restaurants in the Las Vegas Valley, offering global Asian cuisine with prime steaks, sushi, and seafood at Downtown Summerlin. For residents of The Canyons in Summerlin, this acclaimed dining destination sits just a short drive from home.

What Makes JING Las Vegas Stand Out?

Since opening in 2019, JING has built a reputation for its globally inspired menu that blends Asian flavors with premium American steakhouse quality. Executive Chef Thomas Griese oversees a kitchen that sources ingredients from local and organic farms, producing dishes like sizzling hot rock Wagyu, hand rolled sushi, and towering seafood presentations. The restaurant holds strong ratings across OpenTable and Yelp, with diners consistently praising both the food and the atmosphere.

The cocktail and wine program matches the ambition of the kitchen. JING's full bar serves craft cocktails alongside an extensive wine list recognized by Star Wine List. Happy hour runs seven days a week from 4pm to 6pm and is available throughout the dining room, lounge, bar, and patio, making it one of the best daily deals in Summerlin.

Where Is JING Located and When Is It Open?

JING sits at 10975 Oval Park Drive in Downtown Summerlin, Las Vegas, NV 89135. The restaurant is open Sunday through Tuesday from 4:00pm to 10:00pm, Wednesday and Thursday from 4:00pm to 11:00pm, and Friday and Saturday from 4:00pm to midnight. Reservations are recommended, especially on weekends, and can be made through OpenTable or by calling the restaurant directly.
