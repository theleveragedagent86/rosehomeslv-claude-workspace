Harlo Steakhouse and Bar: Elevated Dining Near The Canyons in Summerlin

Harlo Steakhouse and Bar brings classic steakhouse elegance to Downtown Summerlin, offering residents of The Canyons one of the finest dining experiences off the Las Vegas Strip. Chef Partner Gina Marinelli has built a menu around expertly sourced prime cuts, fresh seafood, and refined sides that draw diners from across the valley.

What Can You Expect at Harlo Steakhouse?

The menu at Harlo centers on the finest cuts of beef from respected purveyors, including Prime Ribeye and Filet Mignon cooked to perfection and paired with house made sauces. Beyond the steaks, the kitchen delivers innovative appetizers, fresh seafood preparations, and desserts that close the meal on a high note. The 4.6 star rating from over 1,600 OpenTable diners speaks to the consistency Marinelli's team delivers night after night.

The interior sets the tone before the first course arrives. Dark wood, polished marble, and ambient lighting create an atmosphere of old school sophistication. Plush seating encourages long, leisurely meals, making Harlo equally suited for a celebratory dinner or an intimate evening out. The bar program complements the food with crafted cocktails and a curated wine list.

Where Is Harlo and When Can You Visit?

Harlo is located at 1720 Festival Plaza Drive in Downtown Summerlin, Las Vegas, NV 89135. Hours run Sunday through Thursday from 5:00pm to 9:00pm and Friday through Saturday from 5:00pm to 10:00pm. Reservations are strongly recommended and can be made through OpenTable or by calling (702) 333 0402.
