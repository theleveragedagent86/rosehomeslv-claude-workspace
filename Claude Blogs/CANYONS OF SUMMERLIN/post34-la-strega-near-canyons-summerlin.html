La Strega: Authentic Italian Dining Near The Canyons in Summerlin

La Strega is Summerlin's beloved Italian trattoria, bringing James Beard nominated cuisine to residents of The Canyons just minutes from home. Chef Partner Gina Marinelli's farm to table approach to Italian cooking has made this restaurant one of the most sought after reservations in the valley.

What Makes La Strega Special?

Translating to "witch" in Italian, La Strega draws its identity from the legend of the witches of Benevento, with a one of a kind mural spanning multiple walls throughout the space. Earth tones, full moon shaped brass chandeliers, and a sophisticated yet playful atmosphere transport guests to Italy the moment they step inside. The design is intentional, creating an experience that extends well beyond the plate.

The menu showcases housemade pastas, fresh seafood, and regional Italian dishes with a modern twist. Standout favorites include the Maine Lobster Spaghetti, Stracci, Cacio e Pepe, and Peruvian Bay Scallops. Every dish reflects Marinelli's commitment to sourcing the freshest ingredients and preparing them with authentic technique. Sunday brunch adds another dimension, featuring dishes like poached egg insalata with frisee and French toast with infused syrup and blueberries.

Where Is La Strega and When Is It Open?

La Strega is located at 3555 S Town Center Drive, Suite 105, Las Vegas, NV 89135. Dinner service runs Monday through Friday from 5:00pm to 9:00pm, with Sunday brunch served from 10:00am to 2:30pm. Reservations are highly recommended, and calling ahead can help you secure a quieter table if preferred.
