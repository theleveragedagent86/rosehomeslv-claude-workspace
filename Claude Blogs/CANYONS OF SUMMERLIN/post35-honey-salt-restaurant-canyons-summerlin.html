Honey Salt: Farm to Table Comfort Dining Near The Canyons in Summerlin

Honey Salt is one of Summerlin's most beloved independent restaurants, serving farm to table comfort cuisine just minutes from The Canyons. Founded by renowned restaurateur Elizabeth Blau and award winning Chef Kim Canteenwalla, this neighborhood gem has earned a 4.7 star rating from over 4,200 OpenTable diners.

What Makes Honey Salt a Local Favorite?

Honey Salt brings relaxed sophistication to every meal. The menu features elevated comfort dishes built around fresh, locally sourced ingredients. Vibrant salads, shareable plates, and signature entrees reflect Chef Canteenwalla's 30 plus years of international culinary experience spanning Singapore, Thailand, Dubai, and Montreal. The atmosphere encourages lingering over a meal with friends and family, with dishes designed for sharing at the center of the experience.

The restaurant also hosts special events throughout the year, including themed Farm Table Dinners with optional wine pairings. Happy hour runs Monday through Friday from 3:00pm to 6:00pm, offering one of the best afternoon deals in the Summerlin area. Weekend brunch on Saturday and Sunday from 10:00am to 2:30pm draws a loyal following for its creative takes on morning classics.

Where Is Honey Salt and When Can You Go?

Honey Salt is located at 1031 S Rampart Boulevard, Las Vegas, NV 89145, in Rampart Commons near Boca Park. Lunch and dinner hours run Monday through Thursday from 11:30am to 9:00pm and Friday from 11:30am to 10:00pm. Saturday dinner is served from 4:00pm to 10:00pm and Sunday dinner from 4:00pm to 9:00pm. Reservations can be made through OpenTable or by calling (702) 445 6100.
