Echo and Rig Tivoli Village Near The Canyons Summerlin

Echo and Rig is a combined butcher shop and steakhouse at Tivoli Village, located just minutes from The Canyons in Summerlin. Residents of this master planned neighborhood enjoy one of the most unique dining concepts in Las Vegas without a long drive.

What Makes Echo and Rig Different From Other Steakhouses?

Echo and Rig operates as both a full service butcher shop and an upscale steakhouse under one roof. The butcher side lets guests hand select premium cuts of beef, pork, and poultry to take home. The restaurant side serves those same quality cuts prepared by an experienced culinary team. This dual concept is rare in Las Vegas and creates an experience that goes well beyond a typical dinner out.

The menu focuses on high quality proteins, house made charcuterie, and seasonal sides. Guests can expect expertly prepared steaks alongside creative appetizers and craft cocktails. The interior blends industrial design with warm lighting, making it equally suited for a casual weeknight meal or a special occasion.

How Close Is Echo and Rig to The Canyons?

Echo and Rig sits inside Tivoli Village at 410 S Rampart Blvd, which is adjacent to The Canyons along Rampart Boulevard. Most residents can reach the restaurant in under five minutes by car. That proximity makes it a go to option for Canyons homeowners who want a high end meal without heading to the Strip or Downtown Las Vegas.

Tivoli Village itself features European inspired architecture and over 25 boutiques, so dinner at Echo and Rig pairs naturally with an evening of shopping or browsing. The walkable layout of the center makes it easy to explore before or after a reservation.
