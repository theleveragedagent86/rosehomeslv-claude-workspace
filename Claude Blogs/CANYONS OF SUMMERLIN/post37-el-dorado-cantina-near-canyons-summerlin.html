El Dorado Cantina brings authentic Mexican cuisine made with organic and non GMO ingredients to Tivoli Village, just steps from The Canyons in Summerlin. Residents of this neighborhood have access to one of the most popular Mexican restaurants in the Las Vegas Valley without leaving the area.

What Kind of Food Does El Dorado Cantina Serve?

El Dorado Cantina specializes in traditional Mexican dishes prepared with organic, non GMO ingredients. The menu features handcrafted tacos, enchiladas, fresh guacamole, and signature margaritas. Every dish prioritizes quality sourcing, which sets El Dorado apart from typical Tex Mex chains.

The restaurant is known for generous portions and a menu that spans breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Seasonal specials rotate regularly, and the bar program includes an extensive tequila and mezcal selection. The atmosphere blends rustic Mexican design with a modern dining room that works for families, date nights, and group gatherings alike.

El Dorado Cantina occupies a prime spot inside Tivoli Village at 410 S Rampart Blvd. Tivoli Village sits adjacent to The Canyons along Rampart Boulevard, putting the restaurant within a five minute drive for most residents. The European inspired shopping center also offers over 25 boutiques and specialty stores, making it easy to combine dinner with an evening out.
