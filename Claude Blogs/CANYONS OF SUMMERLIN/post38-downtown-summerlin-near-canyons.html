Downtown Summerlin Shopping Near The Canyons

Downtown Summerlin is a 106 acre, open air shopping and entertainment destination with over 125 shops and restaurants located just south of The Canyons in Summerlin. Residents of this neighborhood reach one of the largest retail centers in Southern Nevada in minutes.

What Stores and Attractions Are at Downtown Summerlin?

Downtown Summerlin spans 1.6 million square feet and features anchor stores including Macy's, Apple, Nike, Anthropologie, and a CHANEL Fragrance and Beauty Boutique. The open air layout makes it easy to walk between retailers, restaurants, and entertainment venues throughout the year.

Beyond shopping, Downtown Summerlin is home to the Las Vegas Ballpark, where the Las Vegas Aviators minor league baseball team plays. City National Arena, the official practice facility of the Vegas Golden Knights, also sits on the property. These venues bring year round events, concerts, and seasonal programming that keep the area active well beyond standard retail hours.

How Close Is Downtown Summerlin to The Canyons?

Downtown Summerlin sits just south of The Canyons village, making it one of the closest major retail destinations for residents. The drive takes only a few minutes along Summerlin Parkway or local connector roads. That short distance means Canyons homeowners can run errands, grab dinner, or catch a game without any significant commute.

The center also connects to Summerlin's trail system, so residents who prefer to bike or walk on weekends have additional options for reaching the area. This kind of accessibility is a hallmark of Summerlin's master planned design and one of the reasons The Canyons continues to attract buyers.
