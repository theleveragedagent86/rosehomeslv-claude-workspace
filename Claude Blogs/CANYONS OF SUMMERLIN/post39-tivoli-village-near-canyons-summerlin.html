Tivoli Village Shopping Near The Canyons Summerlin

Tivoli Village is a European inspired shopping destination with over 25 boutiques and specialty stores located adjacent to The Canyons on Rampart Boulevard in Summerlin. Residents of this neighborhood walk, bike, or drive to one of the most distinctive retail experiences in Las Vegas in just minutes.

What Stores and Features Does Tivoli Village Offer?

Tivoli Village sits on 28.43 acres at 410 S Rampart Blvd and features a curated mix of upscale retailers, restaurants, and service providers. The anchor tenant is RH Las Vegas, a 60,000 square foot, four level Restoration Hardware gallery that ranks among the largest in the country. Other standout tenants include Jeff White Custom Jewelry, Vasari, and Ritual Salon and Spa.

The center's architecture draws from Italian and Mediterranean design, with stone facades, covered walkways, and landscaped courtyards. This creates a pedestrian friendly atmosphere that feels more like a European village square than a traditional strip mall. Dining options include Echo and Rig, El Dorado Cantina, and several other restaurants that draw patrons from across the valley.

How Close Is Tivoli Village to The Canyons?

Tivoli Village is essentially next door to The Canyons. The shopping center sits along Rampart Boulevard, which forms part of the boundary for the neighborhood. Most Canyons residents can reach Tivoli Village in under five minutes by car, and some subcommunities are close enough for a short walk or bike ride.

This adjacency is a significant lifestyle benefit. Residents can pick up groceries, visit a salon, browse boutiques, or enjoy a full dinner without ever getting on a highway. That level of convenience is something buyers consistently look for when comparing Summerlin neighborhoods.

What Does Tivoli Village Mean for Canyons Home Values?

Ryan Rose points to Tivoli Village as one of the key amenities that elevates The Canyons above other Summerlin villages. Buyers who tour the neighborhood often visit Tivoli Village during their trip and immediately recognize the lifestyle advantage. Having a destination shopping center with this caliber of tenants within walking distance adds tangible value that shows up in both buyer demand and resale performance.
