Canyon Pointe Shopping Center Near The Canyons Summerlin

Canyon Pointe is a large retail center at 10950 W Charleston Blvd that puts everyday essentials and big box stores just minutes from The Canyons in Summerlin. Residents of this neighborhood handle weekly shopping, home improvement runs, and casual dining without driving across town.

What Stores Are at Canyon Pointe Shopping Center?

Canyon Pointe anchors include Best Buy, Costco, Lowe's, PetSmart, Sprouts Farmers Market, Ross, Marshall's, and Office Depot. This mix covers electronics, groceries, home improvement, pet supplies, and discount retail all in one location. For a single trip that checks multiple errands off the list, Canyon Pointe is hard to beat.

The center also features several popular restaurants including BJ's Restaurant and Brewhouse and Olive Garden. These casual dining options give Canyons residents additional choices for weeknight meals and family gatherings that do not require a reservation or a long wait.

How Far Is Canyon Pointe From The Canyons?

Canyon Pointe sits on West Charleston Boulevard, just minutes from The Canyons by car. The drive is quick and straightforward along major surface streets, with no freeway required. This makes Canyon Pointe one of the most practical shopping destinations for daily errands and larger purchases alike.

While Downtown Summerlin and Tivoli Village offer upscale boutiques and specialty dining, Canyon Pointe fills the everyday retail gap. Having all three centers within a short drive gives Canyons residents a complete range of shopping options that few Summerlin neighborhoods can match.
