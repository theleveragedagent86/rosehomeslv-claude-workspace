TPC Las Vegas Golf Course Near The Canyons Summerlin

TPC Las Vegas is a public championship golf course located minutes from The Canyons in Summerlin, offering residents access to PGA Tour caliber play without a membership requirement. This par 71 layout gives Canyons homeowners one of the best golf experiences in the Las Vegas Valley right in their backyard.

What Makes TPC Las Vegas a Top Public Course?

TPC Las Vegas plays at 7,104 yards across a par 71 desert layout designed by renowned architect Bobby Weed with PGA Tour veteran Fuzzy Zoeller serving as Player Consultant. The course features dramatic elevation changes, panoramic views of the Spring Mountains, and bentgrass greens that rival private club conditions. Five sets of tees on every hole make it playable for golfers of all skill levels.

The course earned its place in golf history when Tiger Woods recorded his first PGA Tour victory here in October 1996, defeating Davis Love III in a playoff. For decades, TPC Las Vegas hosted professional tournament play that drew the biggest names in the sport to Summerlin each fall.

How Close Is TPC Las Vegas to The Canyons?

The Canyons sits within the western corridor of Summerlin, placing TPC Las Vegas just a short drive from the neighborhood. Most residents can reach the clubhouse in under ten minutes. That proximity means a morning round of golf fits easily into a weekend routine without eating up half the day in travel time. The course also neighbors other Summerlin amenities, making it simple to pair golf with lunch at Downtown Summerlin or a stop at Tivoli Village.
