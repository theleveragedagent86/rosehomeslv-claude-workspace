TPC Summerlin Private Golf Near The Canyons

TPC Summerlin is a premier private golf club located in the heart of Summerlin, just minutes from The Canyons neighborhood. Named the number one private golf course in Nevada by Nevada Business Magazine, this par 72 layout offers members an elite playing experience surrounded by stunning desert terrain.

What Makes TPC Summerlin One of Nevada's Best Courses?

TPC Summerlin stretches 7,243 yards across rugged desert terrain, designed by Bobby Weed with PGA Tour veteran Fuzzy Zoeller as Player Consultant. The layout meanders through arroyos and canyons, with lush bentgrass greens, natural water features, and an abundance of honey mesquite and pine trees contrasting dramatically with undisturbed desert washes. Golf Digest has recognized TPC Summerlin as one of America's best golf courses.

The club hosted the Shriners Children's Open for over four decades, welcoming the PGA Tour to Las Vegas each October. The 2024 edition marked the final year of that historic run after Shriners Hospitals for Children chose not to renew their title sponsorship. That legacy of professional tournament play elevates the prestige of a TPC Summerlin membership even further.

How Close Is TPC Summerlin to The Canyons?

TPC Summerlin sits within the Summerlin master plan, placing it just a few minutes from The Canyons by car. Members who live in The Canyons can reach the clubhouse quickly for a morning tee time, lunch on the terrace, or an afternoon practice session. That kind of proximity to a world class private club is a significant draw for golf focused buyers looking at Summerlin real estate.

The club's location also puts members close to Downtown Summerlin shopping, Tivoli Village dining, and direct access to the 215 Beltway for trips across the valley.
