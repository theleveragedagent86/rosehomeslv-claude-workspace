JW Marriott Resort and Spa Near The Canyons Summerlin

The JW Marriott Las Vegas Resort and Spa, now rebranded as The Resort at Summerlin, is a 54 acre Mediterranean style luxury resort located minutes from The Canyons neighborhood. Residents of The Canyons enjoy proximity to one of the most significant resort properties in the Las Vegas Valley without heading anywhere near the Strip.

What Does The Resort at Summerlin Offer?

The property features 548 guest rooms and suites spread across 54 landscaped acres. Amenities include Spa Aquae, a full service spa with renovated treatment rooms and a new Hydra Lounge relaxation area. The resort style pool features waterfalls and whirlpools, and a full pool renovation is underway as part of the property's ongoing $75 million multi year upgrade.

Dining has expanded significantly with new restaurants from nationally recognized chefs. Celebrity chef Fabio Viviani has introduced ai Pazzi and Pearls Oyster and Crudo Bar, while Nom Wah brings New York City's oldest dim sum restaurant to its first West Coast location. James Beard Award winner Shawn McClain operates Wineaux on property. A new Caesars Sportsbook opened inside the resort in early 2026, adding another entertainment option for guests and locals alike.

How Close Is the Resort to The Canyons?

The Resort at Summerlin sits on North Rampart Boulevard, which runs directly along the eastern edge of The Canyons. Most residents can reach the resort entrance in under five minutes. That kind of access makes it practical to use the spa for a weekday afternoon, meet friends at one of the restaurants for dinner, or host out of town guests at a resort that feels worlds away from the congestion of the Strip.

What Does This Resort Mean for Canyons Homeowners?

Ryan Rose points to The Resort at Summerlin as one of the neighborhood's strongest lifestyle selling points. The $75 million renovation signals a major long term commitment to the Summerlin corridor, and having a world class resort within walking distance of The Canyons adds tangible value that buyers consistently respond to. It is one of the clearest examples of how Summerlin's west side continues to attract premium investment.
