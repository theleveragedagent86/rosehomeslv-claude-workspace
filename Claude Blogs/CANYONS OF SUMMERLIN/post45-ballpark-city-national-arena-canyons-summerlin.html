Las Vegas Ballpark and City National Arena Near The Canyons Summerlin

Las Vegas Ballpark and City National Arena sit side by side at Downtown Summerlin, placing two professional sports venues within minutes of The Canyons neighborhood. Residents enjoy easy access to Triple A baseball and NHL practice sessions without leaving the Summerlin area.

What Is Las Vegas Ballpark?

Las Vegas Ballpark is an eight acre stadium at Downtown Summerlin that serves as the home field for the Las Vegas Aviators, the Triple A affiliate of the Athletics. The 2026 season features 75 home games, with opening day set for March 27 against the Salt Lake Bees. The ballpark also hosts MLB regular season games in 2026, with the Athletics facing the Milwaukee Brewers from June 8 through 10 and the Colorado Rockies from June 12 through 14.

The stadium features a massive video scoreboard measuring approximately 31 feet high and 126 feet wide, along with modern concessions, group seating areas, and a fan friendly layout. The Aviators compete in the Pacific Coast League and play a full 150 game schedule each season.

What Is City National Arena?

City National Arena is the official practice facility of the NHL's Vegas Golden Knights. Located directly adjacent to Las Vegas Ballpark at Downtown Summerlin, the arena gives fans a chance to watch professional hockey practices and attend community events throughout the season. The building sits just behind the ballpark's left field wall, creating a compact sports district that is unique among master planned communities in the country.

How Close Are These Venues to The Canyons?

Ryan Rose notes that the Ballpark and City National Arena are among the most underrated perks of living in The Canyons. Both venues sit at Downtown Summerlin, which is a short drive south of the neighborhood along Sahara Avenue or the 215 Beltway. Catching an Aviators game on a Tuesday night or watching Golden Knights practice on a Saturday morning takes less time than most Las Vegas residents spend commuting to work. That walkable sports access, combined with the shopping and dining already available at Downtown Summerlin, makes The Canyons one of the most entertainment rich neighborhoods in the valley.
