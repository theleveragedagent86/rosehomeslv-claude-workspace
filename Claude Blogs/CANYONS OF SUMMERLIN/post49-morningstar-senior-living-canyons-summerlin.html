MorningStar Senior Living at The Canyons Summerlin: What You Need to Know

MorningStar Senior Living at The Canyons brings independent living, assisted living, and memory care directly into one of Summerlin's most desirable neighborhoods, located at 490 S Hualapai Way in Las Vegas.

What Does MorningStar at The Canyons Offer?

The community includes 95 independent living suites, 49 assisted living suites, and 24 memory care suites. This range of care levels allows residents to transition between services as their needs change without leaving the community they call home. Each suite is designed for comfort and accessibility, and the staff provides personalized care plans for assisted living and memory care residents.

Amenities at MorningStar go well beyond the basics. Residents enjoy a swimming pool, hot tub, outdoor bar, and a club room for social gatherings and events. The resort style atmosphere reflects the broader Summerlin lifestyle, where outdoor recreation and community connection are central to daily life.

Why Does the Location in The Canyons Matter?

Sitting within The Canyons gives MorningStar residents immediate access to Summerlin Hospital Medical Center, just one to three minutes away by car. The 454 bed acute care hospital at 657 N Town Center Drive provides emergency services, specialty care, and surgical options. That proximity is a critical advantage for seniors and their families who want medical resources close at hand.

The surrounding neighborhood offers quiet guard gated streets, over 200 miles of Summerlin trails, and Cottonwood Canyon Park's 69 acres of open space. Family members visiting MorningStar can take advantage of nearby dining at Downtown Summerlin and Tivoli Village, making visits easy and enjoyable for everyone involved.
