Schools Near The Canyons in Summerlin

The Canyons in Summerlin is zoned for some of the highest rated public schools in Nevada, including the number one ranked elementary school in the state. Families also have access to nationally recognized private schools within a short drive, making this village one of the strongest school zones in the Las Vegas valley.

What Elementary Schools Serve The Canyons?

Billy and Rosemary Vassiliadis Elementary is the primary feeder school for The Canyons, earning an A minus from Niche and an 8 out of 10 from GreatSchools. It ranks as the number one elementary school in Nevada, with 82% math proficiency and 76% reading proficiency. John W. Bonner Elementary also serves portions of The Canyons area and provides a solid public school alternative for families on the eastern side of the village.

Which Middle and High Schools Are Nearby?

Sig Rogich Middle School serves grades 6 through 8 and holds a Five Star designation from the Nevada Department of Education. Niche rates it B plus, and GreatSchools gives it an 8 out of 10, placing it at number 12 among all middle schools in Nevada. For high school, students attend Palo Verde High School, which offers honors courses, Advanced Placement classes, and the International Baccalaureate program. Palo Verde carries a B plus rating from Niche.

What Private School Options Are Available?

Three nationally recognized private schools operate within a short drive of The Canyons. Bishop Gorman High School serves grades 9 through 12 and is known for both its academic rigor and athletic programs. Faith Lutheran Middle and High School offers grades 6 through 12 in a faith based environment. The Meadows School provides a PK through 12 independent school experience and is one of the most selective private schools in Southern Nevada.
