The Canyons vs Tournament Hills Summerlin: Which Luxury Community Fits You?

The Canyons and Tournament Hills are two of Summerlin's most prestigious neighborhoods, sharing the TPC golf courses yet offering distinct experiences for buyers seeking guard gated luxury in Las Vegas.

How Do The Canyons and Tournament Hills Compare on Price and Size?

The Canyons covers 750 plus acres with 14 plus subdivisions, six of them guard gated. Homes range from condos around $600,000 to custom estates over $5 million. This variety gives buyers multiple entry points into one of Summerlin's most established communities. The neighborhood was built primarily in the late 1990s through early 2000s, so mature landscaping, tree lined streets, and proven resale values define the experience.

Tournament Hills, located in the Hills South Village, is widely considered the most prestigious address in all of Las Vegas. This ultra luxury guard gated enclave features custom homes on premium lots, and price points typically start well above what most Canyons homes command. Buyers choosing Tournament Hills are paying for exclusivity, lot size, and the cachet that comes with one of the valley's most recognized addresses.

What About Golf, Location, and Lifestyle?

Both communities share access to TPC Las Vegas, the par 71 public course that hosts PGA Tour sanctioned events. Tournament Hills also borders TPC Summerlin, the private course home to the Shriners Children's Open. That dual golf course proximity is a key differentiator for serious golfers who want both public and private play within walking distance.

The Canyons offers more diverse housing options and sits closer to Downtown Summerlin, Tivoli Village, and Summerlin Hospital Medical Center. Families benefit from Vassiliadis Elementary (A minus), Rogich Middle (B plus), and Palo Verde High (B plus). Tournament Hills shares these same school boundaries but appeals more to buyers focused on prestige and ultra luxury finishes over neighborhood variety.

Ryan Rose's Local Take

Ryan Rose, a Summerlin real estate specialist, finds that buyers often compare these two communities when they want golf course living with serious security. The Canyons delivers more price range flexibility and a wider selection of home styles, while Tournament Hills is the pick for buyers whose priority is owning in the most exclusive enclave Summerlin offers. Both are excellent long term investments, but the right choice depends on budget and lifestyle goals.
