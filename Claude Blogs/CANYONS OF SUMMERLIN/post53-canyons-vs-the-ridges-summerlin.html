The Canyons vs The Ridges Summerlin: Comparing Two Luxury Villages

The Canyons and The Ridges are both guard gated luxury villages in Summerlin, but they serve different buyers with distinct construction eras, price ranges, and lifestyle priorities.

How Do Prices and Home Styles Differ?

The Canyons was established in 1995 and spans over 750 acres with 14 plus subdivisions. Homes range from condos starting near $600,000 to custom estates exceeding $5 million. The variety of housing types, including condos, townhomes, single family homes, and custom builds, gives buyers a wide range of options within a single village. Most construction dates to the late 1990s and early 2000s, so the community features mature trees and established streetscapes.

The Ridges is Summerlin's newest ultra luxury village, with construction spanning the 2000s through 2010s. Custom homes built by premier luxury builders dominate the landscape, and price points generally sit higher than most offerings in The Canyons. Buyers choosing The Ridges want modern architecture, newer construction, and the prestige of living in Summerlin's most recently developed high end enclave.

What About Security, Golf, and Location?

Both communities feature guard gated access, but The Ridges adds 24/7 security patrols as a standard feature. The Canyons centers around TPC Las Vegas, a public championship golf course hosting PGA Tour events. The Ridges sits near Bear's Best Golf Club, a Tom Fazio designed course that recreates 18 of his most celebrated holes from around the country. Golfers in both communities have premier course access, though the experiences differ significantly.

The Canyons holds a location advantage for daily convenience. Downtown Summerlin, Tivoli Village, Canyon Pointe, and Summerlin Hospital Medical Center are all within minutes. The Ridges sits farther west in Summerlin, offering more seclusion but requiring a slightly longer drive to major retail and dining centers. Schools serving both communities include top rated options like Vassiliadis Elementary and Palo Verde High School.

Ryan Rose's Local Take

Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate expert with deep Summerlin knowledge, sees these two villages attract similar buyers with different timelines. The Canyons appeals to those who want established value, TPC golf course living, and more housing diversity at a broader price range. The Ridges draws buyers who prioritize newer construction, architectural prestige, and the exclusivity of Summerlin's most modern luxury address. Both hold their value exceptionally well in the resale market.
