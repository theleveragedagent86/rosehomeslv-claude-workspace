Luxury Homes in The Canyons Summerlin

The Canyons in Summerlin is home to some of the most prestigious luxury residences in all of Las Vegas, with properties ranging from $1 million to well over $5 million. Spanning 750 acres around the TPC Las Vegas championship golf course, this established community delivers guard gated privacy, sweeping mountain views, and proximity to world class amenities.

What Types of Luxury Homes Are Available in The Canyons?

The Canyons features over 14 subdivisions offering a diverse mix of luxury property types. Custom estates in Bellacere range from 3,000 to over 16,000 square feet, while Canyon Fairways homes span 4,000 to 10,000 plus square feet. Single family luxury homes in Eagle Rock, Aventura, and Palisades typically list between $1 million and $2 million. For buyers seeking low maintenance luxury, Mira Villa offers mid rise condominiums from 2,052 to 3,724 square feet with starting prices just under $1 million.

Which Subdivisions in The Canyons Are Guard Gated?

Six subdivisions within The Canyons offer guard gated security: Aventura, Bellacere, Canyon Fairways, Eagle Rock, Mira Villa, and Palisades. Each of these communities provides 24 hour controlled access, adding an extra layer of exclusivity. Canyon Fairways alone includes roughly 181 custom built homes, many of which sit directly along the TPC Las Vegas fairways. Bellacere, built primarily by Christopher Homes between 2000 and 2008, contains approximately 57 residences that represent the pinnacle of custom luxury in Summerlin.

What Makes The Canyons a Premier Luxury Destination?

Location and lifestyle set The Canyons apart. The TPC Las Vegas championship course, a par 71 layout stretching 7,104 yards, hosts the PGA Tour Shriners Children's Open each year. The JW Marriott Las Vegas Resort and Spa sits within the community, offering 548 rooms across 50 acres. Residents enjoy 200 plus miles of Summerlin trails, 69 acre Cottonwood Canyon Park, and quick access to Downtown Summerlin's 125 plus shops and restaurants.
