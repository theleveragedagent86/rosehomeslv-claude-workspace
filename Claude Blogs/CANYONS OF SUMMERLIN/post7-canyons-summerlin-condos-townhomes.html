Canyons Summerlin Condos and Townhomes

The Canyons in Summerlin offers several condominium and townhome communities, with prices starting around $600,000 and reaching over $2 million for premium units in Mira Villa. These attached homes provide a low maintenance alternative to single family living while keeping residents in one of Las Vegas's most desirable neighborhoods.

What Condo Communities Are in The Canyons Summerlin?

Three primary condominium communities serve buyers in The Canyons: Mira Villa, Dakota Condos, and Cierra Condos. Mira Villa is a guard gated mid rise luxury community with approximately 103 residences ranging from 2,052 to 3,724 square feet. Dakota Condos offer gated access with golf course views along TPC Las Vegas. Cierra Condos provide a clubhouse, pool, and spa for residents seeking resort style amenities at a more accessible price point.

What Do Mira Villa Condos Offer?

Mira Villa stands out as the premier condo option in The Canyons. Units feature two to four bedrooms, single level layouts with direct elevator access, and expansive wrap around balconies with golf course, Strip, and mountain views. Interior finishes include chef kitchens with custom cabinetry, wine coolers, granite counters, and spa inspired baths. Every home includes a private two car garage with storage. Community amenities feature a resort style pool and spa, fitness center, clubhouse with kitchen, outdoor entertainment areas, and scenic walking paths.

Are Condos in The Canyons a Good Fit for Retirees and Professionals?

The Canyons' condo communities appeal to both active retirees and busy professionals who want Summerlin's premier location without the upkeep of a large estate. Residents still enjoy full access to 200 plus miles of Summerlin trails, Cottonwood Canyon Park, and Downtown Summerlin's 125 plus shops. The JW Marriott Las Vegas Resort and Spa, Summerlin Hospital Medical Center, and TPC Las Vegas are all within minutes.
