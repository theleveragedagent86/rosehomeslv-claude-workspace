Centennial Hills offers one of the most diverse selections of home styles in northwest Las Vegas, from compact single story residences to sprawling luxury custom builds on acreage. Whether you want a low maintenance townhome or a two story family home with mountain views, this community delivers options at every price point. ## What Types of Homes Are Available in Centennial Hills? The majority of homes in Centennial Hills are single family residences built between 2000 and 2020 by nationally recognized builders like Lennar, KB Home, D.R. Horton, Toll Brothers, and Pulte. You will find both single story and two story layouts throughout the community. Townhomes and condominiums round out the options for buyers who prefer attached living with less upkeep. Beyond the standard subdivisions, Centennial Hills also includes semi custom and luxury custom homes, some situated on larger acreage lots with horse property zoning. These premium properties often feature upgraded finishes, three car garages, and expansive desert or mountain views that set them apart from production builds. ## What Floor Plans and Square Footages Can Buyers Expect? Entry level homes in Centennial Hills typically range from 1,500 to 2,200 square feet and are priced between $375,000 and $475,000. These floor plans usually include three to four bedrooms, two bathrooms, and an open concept living area that suits first time buyers and smaller households. Mid range homes step up to 2,000 to 2,800 square feet in the $475,000 to $600,000 bracket, often adding a loft, den, or downstairs bedroom for multigenerational flexibility. Premium and luxury floor plans exceed 2,800 square feet and can surpass $800,000, particularly in gated subdivisions built by Toll Brothers or Richmond American.
