Centennial Hills is one of the most popular rental markets in northwest Las Vegas, offering apartment communities with modern amenities, suburban quiet, and easy freeway access. Average rents range from around $1,520 for a one bedroom to $2,302 for a three bedroom, making the area competitive with other established Las Vegas neighborhoods. ## How Much Does It Cost to Rent an Apartment in Centennial Hills? Current average rents in Centennial Hills break down by unit size: studios average approximately $1,541 per month, one bedroom apartments sit around $1,520, two bedrooms run about $1,813, and three bedroom units average $2,302. These figures reflect the area's upper middle income demographic and the quality of newer apartment construction found throughout the community. Most rental communities in Centennial Hills were built in the 2000s and 2010s, so renters benefit from relatively modern layouts, updated kitchens and bathrooms, and common area amenities like pools, fitness centers, and gated parking. Complexes near Centennial Center Boulevard and North Durango Drive tend to offer the best walkability to shopping, dining, and everyday errands. ## What Should Renters Know Before Choosing Centennial Hills? Location within the community matters. The three zip codes that cover Centennial Hills are 89130, 89131, and 89149, and rental pricing can vary depending on which section you choose. Apartments closer to the US 95 freeway in the 89130 area may offer slightly lower rents, while units in 89149 near newer developments tend to command a premium for proximity to parks and top rated schools. Commute times from Centennial Hills are manageable for most Las Vegas workers. The Strip is roughly 20 to 30 minutes away, the airport is a 25 to 35 minute drive, and downtown sits about 20 to 25 minutes out.
