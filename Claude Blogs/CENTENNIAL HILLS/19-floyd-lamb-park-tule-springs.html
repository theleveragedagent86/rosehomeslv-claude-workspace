Floyd Lamb Park at Tule Springs is a 2,000 plus acre oasis located at 9200 Tule Springs Road, just minutes from Centennial Hills in northwest Las Vegas. With stocked fishing ponds, historic ranch buildings, roaming peacocks, and miles of trails, it offers a nature escape that feels worlds away from the city. ## What Can You Do at Floyd Lamb Park at Tule Springs? Floyd Lamb Park features four scenic ponds: Tule Springs Lake, Cottonwood Lake, Mulberry Lake, and Desert Willow Lake. The largest pond is stocked by the Nevada Department of Wildlife with species including largemouth bass, bluegill, crappie, carp, and seasonally stocked rainbow trout and channel catfish. Visitors with a valid Nevada fishing license can cast a line, though catches are limited to three fish per person. A $6 per vehicle entrance fee applies. Beyond fishing, the park offers BBQ pits, reservable picnic shelters, volleyball courts, horseshoe pits, and trails for jogging and biking. Equestrian areas provide space for horseback riding, making it one of the few parks in the Las Vegas area that accommodates riders. The sheer size of the property means you can spend an entire day exploring without seeing the same scenery twice. ## What Is the History Behind Floyd Lamb Park? The park sits on land that was once a privately owned ranch dating back to the 1940s. Historic ranch buildings and barns still stand throughout the property, giving visitors a glimpse into the agricultural roots of this part of the valley. The Tule Springs area also holds significant paleontological importance, with fossil discoveries that connect to the nearby Tule Springs Fossil Beds National Monument. Wildlife is one of the biggest draws. Peacocks have inhabited the park for generations, tracing their lineage back to the original ranch era. Ducks, herons, and other waterfowl populate the pond areas, creating a birding destination that surprises many first time visitors who do not expect this level of natural beauty so close to Las Vegas. ## Why Centennial Hills Residents Love Floyd Lamb Park Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas Realtor specializing in Centennial Hills, considers Floyd Lamb Park one of the most underappreciated attractions near the neighborhood.
