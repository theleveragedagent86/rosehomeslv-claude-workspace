Coral Academy of Science Las Vegas operates a Centennial Hills campus at 7951 Deer Springs Way, serving students in grades K through 8 with a curriculum built around math, science, and technology. With a 10 out of 10 rating from Public School Review and a spot in the top 5% of Nevada schools, this tuition free public charter school is one of the highest performing options in the 89131 zip code. ## What Makes Coral Academy Centennial Hills Stand Out? Coral Academy of Science Las Vegas, commonly known as CASLV, is a state sponsored public charter school with seven campuses across Las Vegas and Henderson. The Centennial Hills campus focuses on grades K through 8 and enrolls roughly 681 students. What sets Coral Academy apart is a strong emphasis on STEM education, with Project Lead the Way (PLTW) curriculum integrated across grade levels. Over 60% of students participate in STEM coursework, and the school has set a goal of reaching 90% participation. Test scores at the Centennial Hills campus consistently outperform district and state averages. Math proficiency sits at 63% and reading proficiency reaches 74%, compared to statewide averages of 28% and 44% respectively. These numbers place the campus at number 15 among Nevada elementary schools and number 10 among middle schools according to Public School Review. Niche assigns the school an A minus overall grade, reflecting strong academics and positive parent feedback. ## Enrollment and Admissions As a charter school, Coral Academy does not use traditional zoned enrollment. Families apply through a lottery based admissions process, with applications typically opening in the late summer for the following school year. Deadlines tend to fall in early February, so families interested in securing a spot should plan ahead. The school is tuition free and open to all Nevada residents, making it an accessible option for families throughout the Las Vegas Valley. The campus hours run from 7:30 AM to 3:30 PM, and before and after school programs through Champions are available for ages 5 through 13. This flexibility is a practical advantage for working parents who need extended care options. ## Why It Matters for Centennial Hills Families Access to a top rated charter school within the neighborhood adds real value to the Centennial Hills community.
