Somerset Academy Sky Pointe is a tuition free public charter school located in the Centennial Hills area of Las Vegas, serving over 2,150 students in grades K through 12 on a single campus at 7038 Sky Pointe Drive. Rated in the top 10% of all Nevada schools for overall test scores, Somerset offers families a complete educational path from kindergarten through high school graduation. ## Academics and Test Scores Somerset Academy Sky Pointe consistently outperforms both district and state averages in standardized testing. Math proficiency reaches 57% and reading proficiency hits 63%, well above Nevada statewide averages of 28% and 44%. The school holds a 10 out of 10 rating from Public School Review and a B grade from Niche. For high school students, the average GPA is 3.51, with an SAT average of 1160 and ACT average of 23. The graduation rate stands at 95%, which exceeds the state average by a significant margin. The academic program includes a range of Advanced Placement courses, dual enrollment through the College of Southern Nevada, and multiple Career and Technical Education (CTE) pathways. Students looking for additional challenge can earn college credits while still in high school, giving them a meaningful head start on postsecondary education. The student to teacher ratio of 23 to 1 keeps class sizes manageable within a large campus environment. ## Programs and Extracurriculars Beyond core academics, Somerset Academy Sky Pointe runs a conservatory program for the performing arts that includes theatre, orchestra, and choir. Students can participate in a variety of clubs and organizations, with nearly half of the student body involved in extracurricular activities according to Niche survey data. The combination of arts programming, CTE tracks, and AP coursework means students have the flexibility to pursue their interests while building a strong academic foundation. Before and after school care is available through Champions for elementary age students, running from 6:00 AM through the start of school and from dismissal through 6:00 PM. This is a practical benefit for working families who need extended supervision options. ## Enrollment Process Somerset Academy uses a lottery based admissions system, meaning families from anywhere in the Las Vegas Valley can apply for a spot. Enrollment applications for the upcoming school year typically open in early fall, and families should check the school website for specific deadlines. Because Somerset serves K through 12, accepted students can remain on the same campus for their entire educational journey, which reduces the transitions that come with switching schools between elementary, middle, and high school. ## What This Means for Homebuyers Having a top rated K through 12 charter school within the neighborhood is a strong draw for families evaluating Centennial Hills.
