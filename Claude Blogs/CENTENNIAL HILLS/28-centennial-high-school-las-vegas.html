Centennial High School is a public high school located at 10200 Centennial Parkway in Las Vegas, serving approximately 3,000 students in grades 9 through 12 within the Clark County School District. The school holds a B grade from Niche and a 91% graduation rate, and it is home to several notable programs including one of Nevada's most decorated track and field teams and an award winning Naval Junior ROTC unit. ## Academics and College Preparation Centennial High School offers a broad academic program designed to prepare students for success beyond graduation. The course catalog includes a wide selection of Advanced Placement classes spanning English, math, science, history, and world languages. Students also have access to dual enrollment coursework through the University of Nevada, Reno, allowing them to earn college credits while completing their high school diploma. The average SAT score is 1160 and the average ACT score is 23, with an overall GPA average of 3.39. The 91% graduation rate places Centennial above the Nevada state average, and the school ranks number 50 among all Nevada high schools according to U.S. News and World Report. For families comparing high school options in the Centennial Hills area, this combination of AP access, dual enrollment, and solid graduation outcomes makes Centennial a reliable choice within the traditional public school system. ## NJROTC Program One of Centennial High School's most distinctive offerings is its Naval Junior Reserve Officers Training Corps program. The NJROTC unit has won the Navy Nationals competition twice, including a record setting victory margin of 200 points in 2009 followed by a narrow 3 point win in 2010. The program takes first year cadets on a weeklong orientation trip to Camp Pendleton in San Diego, where they learn military customs, build leadership skills, and form strong bonds with fellow cadets. Beyond character development, the NJROTC program provides pathways to full ride scholarships at universities across the country, valued at approximately $300,000 each. ## Athletics and Extracurriculars Centennial High School is widely recognized for its track and field program, led by head coach Roy Sessions, whose women's team has captured 13 state championships. The campus includes an on site track, a baseball field, and athletic facilities that support a full range of varsity sports. Beyond athletics, the school offers clubs, student organizations, and arts programs that give students opportunities to explore their interests outside the classroom. ## Location and Community Connection Situated directly on Centennial Parkway, the school is centrally located within the Centennial Hills master planned community. Proximity to parks, shopping, and major roadways makes daily commutes straightforward for families in the 89149 and 89131 zip codes.
