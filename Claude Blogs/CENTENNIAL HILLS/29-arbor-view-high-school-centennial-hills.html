Arbor View High School is a public high school located at 7500 Whispering Sands Drive in northwest Las Vegas, serving approximately 3,177 students in grades 9 through 12 within the Clark County School District. With a B plus grade from Niche, a 96% graduation rate, and a ranking of number 31 among Nevada high schools, Arbor View is one of the strongest traditional public high school options available to Centennial Hills families. ## Academics and AP Offerings Arbor View provides one of the most extensive Advanced Placement programs in the northwest Las Vegas area. The AP course catalog includes AP Literature, AP Language, AP Calculus AB and BC, AP Statistics, AP Biology, AP Chemistry, AP Physics, AP Environmental Science, AP World History, AP U.S. History, AP Government, AP Psychology, AP Computer Science, AP Spanish Language and Literature, AP Japanese, AP African Studies, and AP 2D and 3D Art. Roughly 29% of students participate in at least one AP course according to U.S. News, giving a significant portion of the student body access to college level academics before graduation. State test scores show 26% of students proficient in math and 52% proficient in reading. While these numbers reflect the challenges seen across the Clark County School District, the school's 96% graduation rate and overall Niche grade suggest that students are being well prepared for postsecondary pathways. The school ranks in the top 50% of all Nevada schools for overall test performance. ## Athletics, Arts, and Student Life Arbor View has built a strong reputation for school spirit, competitive athletics, and vibrant student life. The school fields varsity teams across a full range of sports and is one of only three high schools in Las Vegas with a synthetic turf football field using the same type of turf found in NFL stadiums. The Arbor View Aggie Marching Band is the second largest marching band in the state of Nevada, and students regularly participate in art festivals, club activities, and community service projects. The combination of academics, athletics, and arts programming means students have a wide range of options for building a well rounded high school experience. Reviews on Niche frequently highlight the school's supportive staff, engaged student body, and the variety of extracurricular opportunities available. ## Location and Neighborhood Fit Arbor View High School opened in 2005 and sits on the western edge of the Centennial Hills community. The campus is easily accessible from neighborhoods throughout the 89131 and 89149 zip codes, and its proximity to parks, retail centers, and the US 95 freeway makes daily logistics manageable for families.
