Shadow Ridge High School is one of the top public high schools serving Centennial Hills, offering a well rounded education with Advanced Placement courses, dual enrollment options, and competitive athletics. Located in the northwest Las Vegas valley, Shadow Ridge has been a cornerstone of the community since it opened its doors in 2003. ## What Makes Shadow Ridge High School Stand Out? Shadow Ridge High School serves grades 9 through 12 within the Clark County School District and enrolls approximately 3,300 students. The school ranks 44th out of 103 public high schools in Nevada and falls within the top 57 percent statewide. With a GreatSchools rating of 6 out of 10 and a Niche grade of B, Shadow Ridge delivers solid academics in a large school setting that offers students a wide range of opportunities. The school holds a 93 percent graduation rate, which speaks to the commitment of both faculty and students. Average SAT scores reach 1,110 and ACT scores average 24, placing graduates in a competitive position for college admissions. The student to teacher ratio of 25 to 1 keeps class sizes manageable while still allowing for a diverse course catalog. ## Academic Programs and Opportunities Shadow Ridge offers a strong selection of Advanced Placement courses that allow students to earn college credit while still in high school. Dual enrollment classes provide another pathway for motivated learners who want a head start on their college education. Honors courses round out the academic offerings, giving students at every level a chance to push themselves and prepare for the next step. Ryan Rose often hears from families that the variety of academic tracks at Shadow Ridge is a major draw. Whether a student is focused on college prep, career readiness, or exploring different interests, the school provides pathways that match those goals. ## Athletics and Extracurriculars The Shadow Ridge Mustangs compete across multiple sports, with teams in football, basketball, soccer, volleyball, and more. The athletics program emphasizes teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship, and the school has produced standout athletes who have gone on to compete at the collegiate level. Beyond athletics, students can get involved in clubs, student government, and performing arts programs. The combination of academics and extracurriculars creates a balanced high school experience that appeals to families looking for more than just test scores. ## Living Near Shadow Ridge High School Homes zoned for Shadow Ridge High School are in high demand among families prioritizing education.
