Nevada State High School Northwest offers a unique educational model where juniors and seniors take real college courses on actual college campuses, earning both high school and college credit at the same time. Located at 7240 West Azure Drive in Las Vegas, this tuition free public charter school is one of the most innovative options available to Centennial Hills families. ## How Does Nevada State High School Work? Nevada State High School is not a traditional high school. Students in grades 11 and 12 attend classes taught by college professors on college and university campuses across Nevada. The school pays for tuition and fees covering up to 19 college courses, meaning families pay nothing out of pocket for this dual credit experience. With a 9 out of 10 GreatSchools rating and placement in the top 20 percent of Nevada schools, the academic results speak for themselves. The Northwest campus serves approximately 122 students, creating an intimate learning environment that feels more like a college program than a high school. This small cohort model allows for personalized advising and support as students navigate the transition from high school to higher education. ## Academic Performance and Outcomes Students at the Northwest campus achieve strong results. Reading proficiency reaches between 70 and 74 percent, well above the state average of 44 percent. The graduation rate stands at 95 percent, and the average ACT score is 23. These numbers reflect a student body that is motivated, supported, and challenged by college level coursework. Many graduates leave with an associate degree or enough credits to enter college as a sophomore, saving families thousands of dollars in future tuition costs. ## Who Is Nevada State High School Right For? This program is ideal for self motivated students who are ready to take ownership of their education. Because students attend real college classes with adult learners, they need to be comfortable working independently and managing their own schedules. The school provides academic advisors and support staff, but the day to day experience requires maturity and discipline. Students who thrive in traditional high school settings with sports teams, clubs, and daily social activities may find the NSHS model different from what they expect. However, students who are eager to accelerate their education and experience college life early often find it to be exactly the right fit. ## The Real Estate Connection Families who choose Nevada State High School Northwest often value education as a top priority.
