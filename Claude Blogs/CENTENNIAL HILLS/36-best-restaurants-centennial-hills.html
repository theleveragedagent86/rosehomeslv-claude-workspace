Centennial Hills offers a surprisingly deep dining scene for a master planned community in northwest Las Vegas. From upscale steakhouses and authentic Japanese cuisine to neighborhood cafes and Cajun seafood, the restaurants near Centennial Hills give residents plenty of reasons to eat close to home. ## Where Do Centennial Hills Residents Go to Eat? The dining options in and around Centennial Hills cover a wide range of cuisines and price points, making it easy to find the right restaurant for any occasion. Whether you are looking for a weeknight dinner with the family, a date night destination, or a place to celebrate a special event, the neighborhood delivers. Umami on North Durango Drive is a standout for Japanese cuisine, offering all you can eat sushi alongside traditional dishes like sashimi, tempura, and gyoza in a vibrant, modern setting. For upscale steakhouse dining, The Charcoal Room inside Santa Fe Station has earned a Wine Spectator Award and serves some of the finest cuts in the valley, from bone in ribeye to filet mignon paired with an exceptional wine list. Seafood lovers will want to visit The Oyster Bar, also inside Santa Fe Station, where Louisiana inspired dishes like pan roasts, gumbo, and cioppino share the menu with fresh oysters and Alaskan king crab legs. The atmosphere is relaxed and inviting, with a selection of New Orleans style cocktails and craft beers that complement the Cajun flavors. ## Local Favorites and Hidden Gems Bob Taylor's Ranch House on Rio Vista Street has been a Las Vegas institution since 1955. The oldest steakhouse in the city, it serves mesquite grilled steaks and slow smoked prime rib in a rustic Western setting that feels like stepping back in time. Leticia's Cocina and Cantina brings authentic Mexican food to the area with a loyal local following that has grown steadily since 2009. Mimi's Cafe on North Durango Drive offers a warm, home style atmosphere with farm fresh omelets, pancakes, and comfort food classics that make it a go to breakfast and brunch spot. For a more refined experience, Americana Restaurant on Regatta Drive blends American cuisine with international influences, featuring dishes like charred Mediterranean octopus and Muscovy duck stroganoff in an elegant lakeside setting. BJ's Restaurant and Brewhouse at Centennial Centre rounds out the options with casual dining and a solid lineup of craft beers brewed in house. ## A Dining Scene That Adds Real Value A strong restaurant scene is one of the quieter factors that makes a neighborhood appealing to homebuyers.
