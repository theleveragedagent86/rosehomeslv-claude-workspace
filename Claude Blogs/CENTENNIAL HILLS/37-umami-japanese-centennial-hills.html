Umami is an upscale Japanese restaurant located at 6640 North Durango Drive in the heart of Centennial Hills. Known for its emphasis on the savory fifth taste that gives the restaurant its name, Umami has become one of the most popular dining destinations in northwest Las Vegas. ## What Makes Umami Stand Out in Centennial Hills? Umami brings a level of Japanese dining to Centennial Hills that residents previously had to drive to the Las Vegas Strip to find. The restaurant offers an all you can eat sushi format alongside a full menu of traditional and contemporary Japanese dishes, including sashimi, tempura, gyoza, and yakitori skewers. The spicy miso ramen, served with scallions, chicken, and a soft boiled egg, is a standout on cooler desert evenings. Crispy garlic shime dumplings drenched in a spicy garlic sauce are another crowd favorite that regulars return for again and again. The interior is modern and vibrant, creating an atmosphere that works equally well for a family dinner, a night out with friends, or a casual date. Service is attentive without being overbearing, and the kitchen maintains a high standard of quality even with the all you can eat option, which is a rare combination in Las Vegas dining. ## Hours and Location Details Umami is open daily from 11:30 AM to midnight, making it one of the more flexible dining options in the area. Whether you are looking for a midday sushi lunch or a late evening meal after a movie at Centennial Centre, the hours accommodate a wide range of schedules. The restaurant sits in a shopping plaza along North Durango Drive, one of the main commercial corridors running through Centennial Hills, with convenient parking right outside the door. No reservations are required, though the restaurant can fill up during peak weekend hours.
