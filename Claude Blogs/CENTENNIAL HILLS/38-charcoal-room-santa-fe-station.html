The Charcoal Room at Santa Fe Station is an award winning steakhouse located inside the Santa Fe Station Casino at 4949 North Rancho Drive, just minutes from Centennial Hills. With a Wine Spectator Award on its resume and a reputation for exceptional cuts of beef, it ranks among the best fine dining options in northwest Las Vegas. ## What to Expect at The Charcoal Room The Charcoal Room delivers a classic steakhouse experience with a refined atmosphere that feels special without being stuffy. The menu centers on premium steaks, with the filet mignon, bone in ribeye, and New York strip earning consistently high praise from guests. Each cut is prepared with care, and the kitchen's attention to detail extends across the entire menu. Beyond the steaks, the Charcoal Room offers fresh fish, Australian lobster tail, lamb chops, and a surf and turf combination that pairs land and sea beautifully. The bacon wrapped shrimp appetizer and French onion soup are both strong starters that set the tone for the meal ahead. The wine list is extensive and well curated, earning the restaurant its Wine Spectator recognition, and the cocktail menu features classic steakhouse drinks like the Old Fashioned, a Steakhouse Dirty Martini, and an Espresso Martini for after dinner. ## Hours and Dining Details The Charcoal Room is open for dinner Monday through Thursday and Sunday from 5:00 PM to 9:00 PM, with extended hours on Friday and Saturday until 10:00 PM. A daily happy hour runs from 4:00 PM to 5:30 PM, offering a chance to enjoy the restaurant's cocktails and select appetizers at a reduced price before the main dinner service begins. Reservations are available through OpenTable and are recommended, especially for Friday and Saturday evenings when the restaurant tends to fill up. The location inside Santa Fe Station means there is ample free parking, and guests can combine dinner with other amenities at the casino, including bowling and movies. ## A Steakhouse That Centennial Hills Calls Its Own For Centennial Hills residents, The Charcoal Room fills a niche that many suburban communities lack: a genuinely high end steakhouse within a short drive of home. The quality of the food and the caliber of the wine program rival many Strip steakhouses, but the prices and the convenience of the location make it a more practical choice for regular special occasion dining.
