Centennial Centre is a 900,000 square foot shopping plaza located at 8060 W Tropical Parkway in Centennial Hills, featuring over 100 stores and serving as the primary retail hub for residents of northwest Las Vegas. ## What Makes Centennial Centre the Go To Shopping Destination in Centennial Hills? Centennial Centre sits at the intersection of US 95 and the I 215 beltway, making it one of the most accessible shopping destinations in the Las Vegas valley. With over 100 stores spread across 900,000 square feet of retail space, the plaza has everything residents need within a short drive from home. Anchor tenants include Walmart Supercenter, Sam's Club, and The Home Depot, covering everyday essentials, bulk shopping, and home improvement needs all in one stop. Beyond the anchors, Centennial Centre is packed with national retailers that make it easy to handle errands without heading to the Strip or driving across town. Ross Dress for Less and HomeGoods offer discount home and fashion shopping, while Michaels provides arts, crafts, and hobby supplies. For footwear, both Skechers and Famous Footwear have storefronts in the plaza. Families with kids will find Carter's for children's clothing, Five Below for affordable accessories and toys, and GameStop for gaming needs. Beauty and personal care are covered by Sephora, Bath and Body Works, Sally Beauty, and Claire's. ## Shopping, Services, and Dining in One Location One of the things that sets Centennial Centre apart from other shopping centers is the blend of retail and professional services under one roof. H&R Block handles tax preparation, while FedEx and UPS locations make shipping packages a quick errand. Pet owners can pick up supplies at Petco, and clothing options expand further with Tillys and Lane Bryant. The plaza also features a strong dining lineup that pairs well with a day of shopping. From sit down restaurants to grab and go options, the food choices at Centennial Centre mean families can make an afternoon out of their errands.
