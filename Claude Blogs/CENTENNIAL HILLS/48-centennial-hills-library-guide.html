The Centennial Hills Library is a branch of the Las Vegas Clark County Library District located at 6711 N Buffalo Drive, offering a wide range of programs, technology services, and community resources for residents of all ages. ## What Can You Do at the Centennial Hills Library? The Centennial Hills Library is far more than a place to check out books. As one of the busiest branches in the Las Vegas Clark County Library District, it serves as a community resource center for Centennial Hills families, students, and seniors alike. The branch is located at 6711 N Buffalo Drive, right in the heart of the neighborhood, and is open Monday through Thursday from 10 a.m. to 8 p.m. and Friday through Sunday from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. Inside, visitors will find a full collection of print and digital materials, public computers, free Wi Fi access, and dedicated study spaces. The library offers technology help sessions on Wednesdays and Saturdays, where staff members walk visitors through everything from navigating the internet to setting up new devices. For those interested in hands on learning, the branch also provides access to a 3D printer, with certification sessions available for anyone who wants to learn how to use it. ## Programs for Kids, Teens, and Adults One of the library's biggest strengths is its regular programming. Children's storytime sessions, reading clubs, and educational workshops give kids a reason to visit throughout the week. Teen programs provide after school activities and creative outlets in a supervised environment. Adult programming includes book clubs, author events, and educational seminars covering topics from financial literacy to health and wellness. The Centennial Hills Library also partners with organizations like the Tule Springs Fossil Beds National Monument for special events. Ranger led programs bring the story of Ice Age Las Vegas to life, allowing visitors to discover the science behind the fossils found right in the neighborhood's backyard. These unique partnerships set the branch apart from other library locations in the district. ## A Community Gathering Space Beyond books and programming, the Centennial Hills Library functions as a true gathering space for the community. Meeting rooms are available for local organizations and clubs, and the library regularly hosts book sales that draw visitors from across northwest Las Vegas. The bright, modern interior and ample parking make it a welcoming stop for residents running errands along Buffalo Drive. Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas Realtor specializing in Centennial Hills, considers the library one of the neighborhood's underappreciated assets. Families moving to the area are often pleasantly surprised by how active and well resourced the branch is. It is not just a quiet place to read. It is a place where neighbors connect and kids develop a love for learning.
