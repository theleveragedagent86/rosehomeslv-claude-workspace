The Centennial Hills Community Center YMCA at 6601 N Buffalo Drive is a full service fitness and recreation facility operated through a partnership between the City of Las Vegas and the YMCA of Southern Nevada, offering programs for every age group. ## What Facilities Does the Centennial Hills YMCA Offer? The Centennial Hills YMCA is one of the most comprehensive recreation centers in northwest Las Vegas, combining fitness, aquatics, youth programming, and community services in a single location at 6601 N Buffalo Drive. The facility operates through a public private partnership between the City of Las Vegas and the YMCA of Southern Nevada, ensuring it remains accessible and well maintained for residents throughout the neighborhood. Fitness enthusiasts will find a state of the art wellness center with cardio and strength equipment, a dedicated strength room, a cycle studio, and a yoga studio. Group exercise classes run daily, covering everything from high intensity interval training to low impact options for seniors. The aquatics program is equally impressive, featuring an indoor lap pool for year round swimming and a seasonal outdoor water park that is a neighborhood favorite for families during the warmer months. Swimming lessons are available for all ages and skill levels. ## Youth and Family Programs The YMCA's youth programs are a major draw for Centennial Hills families. A licensed preschool on site gives working parents a trusted childcare option steps from home. After school programs provide a safe, supervised environment for school age children, with activities that blend physical fitness, homework support, and social time. Summer and holiday camps keep kids active and engaged during breaks, and a dedicated kids gym and fun zone let younger children burn off energy in a space designed just for them. Family engagement is built into the center's culture. Weekend events, open gym sessions, and family swim hours create regular opportunities for parents and children to spend active time together. The YMCA also offers a teen center where older kids can socialize and participate in structured programs. ## Senior Programming and Community Connection The Centennial Hills YMCA does not just serve families with children. Active adult and senior programming provides fitness classes, social activities, and wellness resources specifically designed for older residents. The center serves as a gathering point for the neighborhood's senior community, helping residents stay physically active and socially connected.
