Centennial Hills residents have access to several quality golf courses within a short drive, including Painted Desert Golf Club, TPC Las Vegas, and Aliante Golf Club, making the neighborhood an appealing home base for golfers. ## What Golf Courses Are Closest to Centennial Hills? Centennial Hills is one of the best positioned neighborhoods in the Las Vegas valley for golfers. Several well regarded courses sit within a short drive of the community, ranging from accessible public courses to championship layouts that have hosted PGA TOUR events. Whether you are a weekend player or a serious competitor, living in Centennial Hills puts quality golf within easy reach. Painted Desert Golf Club is the closest course to the neighborhood, located at 5555 Painted Mirage Road, just minutes from the heart of Centennial Hills. Designed by Jay Morrish and opened in 1987, Painted Desert was one of the first true desert courses in Las Vegas. The par 72 layout stretches 6,840 yards and features narrow fairways lined with natural desert terrain, rewarding accuracy over raw distance. The course is well matured with decades of play and is known for its consistent conditioning and affordable tee times through dynamic pricing. ## TPC Las Vegas: Championship Golf in the Desert For golfers looking for a more premium experience, TPC Las Vegas is the standout option. Located at 9851 Canyon Run Drive, this par 71 course was designed by Bobby Weed and Raymond Floyd and stretches over 7,000 yards of desert canyon terrain. As the premier PGA TOUR property in Las Vegas, TPC Las Vegas draws touring professionals, celebrities, and serious golfers from around the world. Green fees reflect the championship experience, typically ranging from $279 to $395 depending on the day and demand. A fully equipped pro shop, practice facility, and upscale clubhouse dining round out the experience. ## Aliante Golf Club: Great Value in North Las Vegas Aliante Golf Club sits at 3100 West Elkhorn Road in North Las Vegas, making it another convenient option for Centennial Hills residents. Designed by Gary Panks, this par 72 championship layout stretches more than 7,000 yards and features a meandering arroyo that comes into play on 14 holes. Pear trees and Purple Locust trees give the course a lush character rarely seen on desert layouts. Aliante has earned recognition as one of the best golf values in Southern Nevada, with a 40 station driving range, a short game practice area, and a welcoming clubhouse grille. ## Why Golfers Love Living in Centennial Hills Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas Realtor specializing in Centennial Hills, frequently works with buyers who count golf access as a priority.
