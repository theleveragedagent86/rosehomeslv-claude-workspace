Centennial Hills is one of the largest and most complete master planned communities in the Las Vegas valley, offering a full package of homes, parks, schools, shopping, and services within a single neighborhood. ## What Makes Centennial Hills a Master Planned Community? Centennial Hills spans roughly 54 to 56 square miles in northwest Las Vegas across zip codes 89130, 89131, and 89149. Named through a public contest in 2001, it has grown into one of the most sought after addresses in the valley with over 100,000 residents. The community was built with coordinated infrastructure, planned green spaces, and a mix of residential, commercial, and recreational zones. That planning gives it a self contained character and long term stability. ## Builders and Housing Variety Over a dozen builders have contributed to the neighborhood, including Lennar, KB Home, D.R. Horton, Toll Brothers, Woodside Homes, Richmond American, Pardee, Pulte, Harmony, Concordia, Astoria, Spinnaker, William Lyon, and Pinnacle. That variety means homes from the mid $300,000s to above $800,000. The 2026 median list price sits near $535,000, with new construction at a $520,000 median. Both gated and non gated subdivisions are available. ## Parks, Recreation, and Community Services The 120 acre Centennial Hills Park is the community's crown jewel, featuring sports fields, walking trails, playgrounds, a dog park, and event space. A full service YMCA provides fitness programs, childcare, and community classes. The Centennial Hills Library offers modern facilities and programming for all ages. For healthcare, a 336 bed hospital serves the community, eliminating the need to travel across the valley for major medical care. ## Shopping, Dining, and Daily Convenience Centennial Centre anchors the retail experience with over 900,000 square feet of shopping and dining. From grocery stores and home improvement centers to restaurants and personal services, the plaza ensures residents can handle nearly every errand without leaving the neighborhood. Additional retail corridors along the US 95 and CC 215 interchange provide even more options. ## Location and Natural Surroundings Centennial Hills is centered around the I 11/US 95 and CC 215 interchange, with direct freeway access to the Strip (20 to 30 minutes), downtown (20 to 25 minutes), and the airport (25 to 35 minutes). The community borders Tule Springs Fossil Beds National Monument and offers mountain views from elevated lots. A crime grade of B+ makes it safer than 74 percent of neighborhoods nationwide. This is not a transient neighborhood. Families, professionals, and retirees settle here and stay long term, which speaks to the quality of the community's planning.
