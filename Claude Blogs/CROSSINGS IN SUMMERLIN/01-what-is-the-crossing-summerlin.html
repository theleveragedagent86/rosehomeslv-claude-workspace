# What Is The Crossing in Summerlin? The Crossing is a mixed use residential village in Summerlin, Las Vegas, featuring 13 subdivisions, two major parks totaling nearly 20 acres, and homes ranging from $287,000 to over $600,000. Built between 1994 and 2002, it holds the distinction of being the first Summerlin village developed north of Summerlin Parkway. ## What Types of Homes Are in The Crossing? The Crossing offers a diverse housing mix that includes single family homes, condominiums, townhomes, and duplex style units. Home sizes range from approximately 700 to 3,200 square feet, with both single story and two story floor plans available. Builders like Pulte Homes and The Howard Hughes Corporation developed the 13 subdivisions: Aspenglen, Crimson Ridge, Discovery Hills, Discovery Pointe, Highland Hills, Pacific Crest, Pacific Vintage, Red Bluffs, San Marino, Sunset Mesa, Sycamore Ridge, West Hills, and Westridge. ## Is The Crossing a Gated Community? The Crossing is partially gated. The village as a whole is not guard gated, but several sub communities offer that added layer of security. Red Bluffs features a guard gate at night and is gated during the day. Pacific Crest, Sunset Mesa, and Pacific Vintage are all gated neighborhoods. Single family subdivisions throughout the village are generally non gated, giving buyers the choice between secured and open access living. ## What Amenities Are Near The Crossing? Residents benefit from Crossing Park, a 12 acre facility with two lighted soccer fields, shaded play areas, picnic ramadas, and walking paths. Oxford Park adds another 7+ acres with a 90 foot lighted baseball stadium, playground, and open grass. The Summerlin Trail System, stretching over 200 miles, runs nearby. What truly sets The Crossing apart is Summerlin Hospital Medical Center located within the village itself, offering a 485 bed facility with 24 hour emergency care. ## A Local Perspective on The Crossing Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate expert, sees The Crossing as one of Summerlin's most practical communities. The combination of affordable condos, solid single family options, on site medical care, and proximity to top rated schools makes it a neighborhood that works for nearly every stage of life. Its location north of Summerlin Parkway also provides quick access to the 215 Beltway and US 95. ## Interested in The Crossing?
