# Is The Crossing Summerlin a Good Place to Live? Yes, The Crossing is widely considered one of Summerlin's most well rounded villages, offering top rated schools, nearly 20 acres of parks, on site hospital access, and a mix of housing options at price points below many newer Summerlin communities. Its location as the first village north of Summerlin Parkway provides quick access to major highways and employment centers. ## What Are the Best Features of Living in The Crossing? The Crossing delivers practical daily living. Crossing Park spans 12 acres with lighted soccer fields, play areas, and walking paths. Oxford Park adds a 90 foot lighted baseball stadium and open green space. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center sits within the village, providing 485 beds and 24 hour emergency care. The Summerlin Trail System runs nearby with over 200 miles of paths for walking, running, and cycling. A homeownership rate of 66.4% and median household income of $91,765 reflect a stable, invested community. ## How Are the Schools Near The Crossing? Families benefit from exceptional school options. John W. Bonner Elementary, located within The Crossing, carries a GreatSchools rating of 9 out of 10 and earned a 5 Star designation. Sig Rogich Middle School sits adjacent to the community and ranks as the number one middle school in the Clark County School District. Palo Verde High School, also adjacent, holds a B+ Niche rating. Private options including The Meadows School and The Adelson School are both within a few miles. ## Is The Crossing Safe? The Crossing is considered one of the safest communities in the Las Vegas Valley. Several sub communities, including Red Bluffs, Pacific Crest, Sunset Mesa, and Pacific Vintage, are gated. Red Bluffs adds guard gate service at night. Fire Station No. 7 is located within The Crossing, providing rapid emergency response. The overall village layout, combined with Summerlin's community standards, contributes to a secure living environment. ## Ryan Rose's Perspective on Living in The Crossing Ryan Rose considers The Crossing an ideal choice for buyers who want Summerlin quality without the highest Summerlin price tag.
