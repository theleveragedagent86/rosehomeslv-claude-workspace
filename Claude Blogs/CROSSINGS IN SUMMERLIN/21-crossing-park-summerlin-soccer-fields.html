# Crossing Park Summerlin Soccer Fields Crossing Park in The Crossing village of Summerlin features two full sized lighted soccer fields on a 12 acre campus, making it one of the best local venues for youth and adult soccer in the Las Vegas Valley. The park is open daily until 10:30 PM and hosts the Summerlin Youth Soccer League. ## What Soccer Facilities Does Crossing Park Offer? Crossing Park provides two regulation sized soccer fields with full lighting for evening play. The Summerlin Youth Soccer League uses these fields for practices and weekend games throughout the season. Families can walk to the park from most homes in The Crossing, eliminating the need for long drives to scattered recreation sites across the valley. The fields are well maintained and large enough to accommodate multiple age groups simultaneously. ## What Else Can Families Do at Crossing Park? Beyond soccer, Crossing Park spans 12 acres of recreational space. A shaded children's play area keeps younger kids entertained while siblings are on the field. Picnic ramadas offer covered seating for families who want to spend a full afternoon at the park. Community restrooms and walking paths round out the amenities. The park sits at 1111 Crestdale Lane, Las Vegas, NV 89144, within easy reach of every subdivision in The Crossing. ## Can You Play Soccer at Night in Crossing Park? Yes. The lighted fields allow play well after sunset, with the park staying open until 10:30 PM. This is a major advantage during Las Vegas summers when temperatures drop to comfortable levels after dark. Evening games and practices are common, and the lighting quality supports competitive play. Families often combine evening soccer with walks along the park's pathways. ## Local Insight from Ryan Rose Ryan Rose points out that Crossing Park's soccer fields are a standout feature for families considering The Crossing. Having two lighted, full sized fields within walking distance is a convenience that most Las Vegas neighborhoods simply do not offer. Parents save time on commutes to practice, kids can ride bikes to the park, and the evening lighting extends the usable hours significantly.
