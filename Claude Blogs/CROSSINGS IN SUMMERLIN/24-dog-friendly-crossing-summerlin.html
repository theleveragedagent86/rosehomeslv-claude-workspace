# Dog-Friendly Living in The Crossing Summerlin The Crossing in Summerlin is a dog friendly community where residents enjoy over 200 miles of walking trails, on leash access to Crossing Park's 12 acres, and proximity to Summerlin's dedicated dog parks. Dogs are welcome on leash throughout the village's parks and pathways. ## Where Can You Walk Your Dog in The Crossing? Crossing Park is the primary destination for dog owners in the village. The 12 acre park features walking paths, open green spaces, and shaded areas where dogs and owners can enjoy time outdoors. Dogs must remain on leash within the park, which helps keep the environment safe for families and children using the play areas. Oxford Park at 700 Anasazi Dr also offers open grass areas suitable for leashed walks. Both parks stay open into the evening, allowing for comfortable walks after the Las Vegas heat subsides. ## How Do the Summerlin Trails Benefit Dog Owners? The Summerlin Trail System gives dog owners in The Crossing over 200 miles of landscaped, lighted pathways. Morning and evening walks become more than a trip around the block. Residents can vary their routes daily, exploring village trails that connect to parks, schools, and neighboring communities. The paths are well maintained and wide enough for passing other walkers comfortably. Dogs on leash are welcome on all Summerlin trails, making this one of the most walkable communities in the Las Vegas Valley for pet owners. ## Are There Dog Parks Near The Crossing? While The Crossing does not have a dedicated off leash dog park within its boundaries, Summerlin maintains dog parks in other villages within a short drive. These fenced, off leash areas allow dogs to run freely and socialize. The broader Summerlin community continues to expand its pet friendly amenities as new villages develop. Residents of The Crossing also benefit from the proximity to Red Rock Canyon, where leashed dogs are allowed on certain trails. ## Local Insight from Ryan Rose Ryan Rose finds that dog owners consistently rank The Crossing among their favorite Summerlin villages.
