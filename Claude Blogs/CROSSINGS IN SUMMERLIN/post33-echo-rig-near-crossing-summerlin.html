# Echo & Rig Steakhouse Near The Crossing Summerlin Echo & Rig is a full service steakhouse and in house butcher shop located at 400 South Rampart Boulevard in Tivoli Village, approximately two miles from The Crossing in Summerlin. The restaurant combines a curated retail butcher counter with a refined dining room, making it one of the most distinctive steakhouse experiences in Las Vegas. ## What Makes Echo & Rig Different from Other Steakhouses? Echo & Rig stands apart because of its dual concept: a working butcher shop and a full service restaurant under one roof. Guests can browse the butcher counter for premium cuts to take home or sit down in the dining room for a chef prepared steak dinner. The menu features dry aged steaks, fresh seafood, and seasonal sides, all sourced with an emphasis on quality. This butcher to table approach gives diners a level of transparency and freshness that traditional steakhouses rarely offer. ## How Far Is Echo & Rig from The Crossing? Tivoli Village sits along South Rampart Boulevard, roughly two miles south of The Crossing. The drive takes about five minutes in normal traffic. This makes Echo & Rig an easy choice for a weeknight dinner or a weekend celebration without the need to drive to the Las Vegas Strip. Tivoli Village itself offers additional dining, shopping, and entertainment, so residents often pair a meal at Echo & Rig with a stroll through the European inspired outdoor plaza. ## Is Echo & Rig Good for Special Occasions? Echo & Rig's atmosphere balances upscale elegance with a comfortable energy that works for both date nights and group celebrations. The dining room features an open layout with warm lighting and views of the butcher shop. The cocktail and wine program complements the steak focused menu with thoughtfully curated selections.
