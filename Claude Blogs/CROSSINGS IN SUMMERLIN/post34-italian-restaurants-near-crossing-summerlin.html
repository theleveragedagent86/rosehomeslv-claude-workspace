North Italia at Rampart Commons, approximately two miles from The Crossing, serves handmade pastas and wood fired dishes using seasonal ingredients. The menu rotates regularly, keeping the experience fresh for repeat visitors. La Strega Trattoria brings authentic Italian cooking to Summerlin under Chef Partner Gina Marinelli, who emphasizes traditional preparation and quality ingredients. For a more casual, family oriented experience, Maggiano's Little Italy at Downtown Summerlin offers generous family style portions about ten minutes away. ## Where Can You Get the Best Pizza Near The Crossing? Two standout pizza options serve the area well. Osteria Fiorella at Red Rock Casino Resort, roughly five miles from The Crossing, bakes pizzas in a wood burning oven and uses a Josper charcoal oven for select dishes. Grimaldi's at Boca Park, about three miles away, is known for its coal brick oven pizza, a style originating from the famous Brooklyn original.
