# Fine Dining and Steakhouses Near The Crossing Summerlin The Crossing in Summerlin sits within minutes of several fine dining restaurants and premium steakhouses, giving residents access to upscale cuisine without driving to the Las Vegas Strip. Echo & Rig, Harlo, and Wolfgang Puck Bar & Grill are among the standout options in the area. ## What Are the Best Steakhouses Near The Crossing? Echo & Rig at Tivoli Village, roughly two miles from The Crossing, leads the local steakhouse scene with its unique butcher shop and restaurant concept. The menu features dry aged steaks, fresh seafood, and seasonal dishes sourced through the in house butcher counter. Harlo, created by restaurateur Jeffrey Fine, brings a classically elegant steakhouse atmosphere to the Summerlin area. Both restaurants deliver a premium steak experience that rivals Strip destinations at a fraction of the hassle. ## Is There Fine Dining Near The Crossing That Isn't a Steakhouse? Wolfgang Puck Bar & Grill at Downtown Summerlin, about ten minutes from The Crossing, serves California inspired American cuisine from one of the most recognized chefs in the world. The menu includes wood fired pizzas, hand cut pastas, and seasonal entrees in an inviting setting. Honey Salt on South Rampart Boulevard offers upscale comfort food with a polished, neighborhood bistro atmosphere. Osteria Fiorella inside Red Rock Casino Resort adds refined Italian fare to the mix, featuring a wood burning oven and Josper charcoal grill. These options ensure residents can enjoy fine dining variety without leaving the Summerlin corridor. ## How Do Summerlin Steakhouses Compare to the Strip? Summerlin's steakhouses offer several advantages over their Strip counterparts. Pricing is generally more accessible, reservations are easier to secure, and the drive is measured in minutes rather than the time it takes to navigate casino parking. Echo & Rig's butcher shop concept is genuinely unique, not replicated on the Strip. Harlo's intimate setting provides a more personal experience than the high volume steakhouses found in resort casinos.
