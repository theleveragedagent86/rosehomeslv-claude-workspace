# Tivoli Village Near The Crossing Summerlin Tivoli Village sits approximately two miles from The Crossing in Summerlin, offering residents a boutique shopping and dining experience unlike anything else in the Las Vegas valley. This Italian inspired retail center features over 25 stores, specialty restaurants, and professional services in an upscale outdoor setting. ## What Makes Tivoli Village Different from Other Shopping Centers? Tivoli Village stands apart with its European architectural design, featuring stone facades, covered walkways, and landscaped courtyards that feel more like an Italian piazza than a typical strip mall. The center focuses on independent boutiques and curated retailers rather than big box national chains. Jeff White Custom Jewelry, Vasari fine menswear, and a collection of locally owned specialty shops give Tivoli Village a character that draws shoppers from across the valley. The atmosphere alone makes it a popular destination for a casual evening stroll or weekend browse. ## What Restaurants Are at Tivoli Village? Dining is one of Tivoli Village's strongest draws.
