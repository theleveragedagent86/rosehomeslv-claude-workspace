# How Far Is The Crossing Summerlin From the Las Vegas Strip? The Crossing in Summerlin is approximately 12 miles from the Las Vegas Strip, with a typical drive time of 20 to 25 minutes via Summerlin Parkway. This makes it one of the most conveniently located master planned communities for residents who want easy access to the Strip without living in the middle of it. ## How Long Does It Take to Drive From The Crossing to the Strip? The most direct route follows Summerlin Parkway east to Interstate 15, then south toward the resort corridor. During normal traffic conditions, the drive takes roughly 20 minutes. During peak hours, particularly Friday and Saturday evenings, that time can stretch closer to 30 minutes. The straightforward highway connection means there are no complicated surface street routes to navigate. ## How Far Is The Crossing From Harry Reid International Airport? For residents who travel frequently or host out of town guests, Harry Reid International Airport sits about 15.5 to 16 miles from The Crossing. The drive typically takes 22 to 30 minutes depending on traffic. This matters for anyone who flies regularly for work or welcomes visiting family throughout the year. ## What About Downtown Las Vegas? Downtown Las Vegas is actually closer than the Strip, sitting roughly 11 miles from The Crossing. The drive takes approximately 15 minutes using Highway 613. This puts the Fremont Street Experience, Arts District restaurants, and downtown entertainment venues within easy reach for weeknight outings. ## Local Insight from Ryan Rose Ryan Rose, Las Vegas Real Estate Expert, points out that The Crossing's position just north of Summerlin Parkway gives residents the best of both worlds.
