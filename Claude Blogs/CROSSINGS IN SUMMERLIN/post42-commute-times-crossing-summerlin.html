# Commute Times From The Crossing Summerlin Commute times from The Crossing in Summerlin are surprisingly manageable, with most major destinations in the Las Vegas valley reachable in 15 to 30 minutes. The community's direct access to Summerlin Parkway makes daily commuting far smoother than many buyers expect. ## How Long Is the Commute From The Crossing to the Las Vegas Strip? The Las Vegas Strip sits about 12 miles southeast of The Crossing, with a typical drive time of 20 to 25 minutes via Summerlin Parkway and Interstate 15. For the thousands of residents who work in the resort corridor, this is one of the most reliable commute routes in the valley. The parkway carries traffic efficiently to the I 15 interchange. ## How Far Is The Crossing From Downtown Las Vegas? Downtown Las Vegas is roughly 11 miles from The Crossing and takes approximately 15 minutes to reach via Highway 613. This shorter commute makes downtown an even more convenient workplace for residents. The route avoids much of the congestion associated with Strip traffic, which is a significant advantage during shift changes at major employers. ## What About the Airport Commute? Harry Reid International Airport is 15.5 to 16 miles from The Crossing, with drive times ranging from 22 to 30 minutes. The straightforward route south on I 15 keeps the airport accessible even during moderate traffic. ## Does Traffic Affect Commute Times? Morning commutes heading east on Summerlin Parkway tend to be heaviest between 7:00 and 8:30 a.m., with evening return traffic peaking between 4:30 and 6:00 p.m. Even during these windows, the commute from The Crossing to the Strip rarely exceeds 30 to 35 minutes. ## Local Insight from Ryan Rose As a Las Vegas real estate specialist, Ryan Rose often highlights The Crossing's commute advantage when working with buyers relocating to the area.
