# Is The Crossing Summerlin Safe? Yes, The Crossing in Summerlin is widely regarded as one of the safest communities in the Las Vegas valley. The combination of gated neighborhoods, an on site fire station, and Summerlin's overall low crime reputation makes this village a strong choice for safety conscious buyers. ## Why Is Summerlin Considered Safe? Summerlin consistently ranks among the safest large communities in the Las Vegas metro area. The master planned community benefits from well maintained infrastructure, active homeowners associations, and a residential layout that naturally discourages through traffic. Many sub communities within Summerlin feature gated or guard gated entry, adding another layer of security for residents who prioritize controlled access. ## Does The Crossing Have Gated Neighborhoods? Several neighborhoods within The Crossing offer gated entry. These communities restrict vehicle access to residents and their guests, which reduces the likelihood of opportunistic crime. The presence of gates also contributes to a quieter, more private living environment. For buyers who value this added security, The Crossing provides multiple gated options across different price points and home styles. ## What Emergency Services Are Nearby? The Crossing benefits from having City of Las Vegas Fire Station No. 7 located within the village itself. This means emergency response times are exceptionally fast for residents. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center also sits within The Crossing village at 657 N Town Center Dr, offering a 24 hour emergency department with 485 beds.
