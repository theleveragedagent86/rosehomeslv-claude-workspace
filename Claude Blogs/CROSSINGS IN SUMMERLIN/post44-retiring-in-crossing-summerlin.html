# Retiring in The Crossing Summerlin The Crossing in Summerlin is an excellent community for retirees, offering on site hospital access, single story home options, quiet residential streets, and no age restrictions. Retirees here enjoy a relaxed pace of life with every essential service within minutes. ## Is The Crossing a Good Place to Retire? The Crossing checks nearly every box retirees look for in a Las Vegas community. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center sits within the village at 657 N Town Center Dr, providing 24 hour emergency care, a Cancer Center, Heart Center, Children's Medical Center, and a Robotic Surgery Center across its 485 bed facility. Having a full service hospital steps from home is a rare advantage that gives retirees and their families genuine peace of mind. The community also has no age restrictions, so retirees can live alongside families and professionals in a diverse, active neighborhood. ## What Housing Options Work for Retirees? Single story homes are available throughout The Crossing, eliminating the concern of navigating stairs as mobility needs change. For retirees seeking lower maintenance living, condo and townhome communities within the village offer a lock and leave lifestyle with HOA managed exteriors and landscaping. Home prices span a range of budgets, making it possible to find the right fit whether downsizing or relocating from out of state. ## What Is the Lifestyle Like for Retirees? The Crossing's quiet streets make daily walks pleasant and safe. Crossing Park spans 12 acres with walking paths for low impact exercise, and the Summerlin trail system extends over 200 miles. With a median age of 43.3, the community attracts established residents who contribute to a calm, well maintained environment. ## Local Insight from Ryan Rose Ryan Rose, Las Vegas Real Estate Expert, frequently works with retirees relocating to the valley and consistently recommends The Crossing.
