The Crossing offers families access to some of the highest rated schools in the Clark County School District. Bonner Elementary sits within the village with a GreatSchools rating of 9 out of 10 and 5 Star School recognition. Rogich Middle School is adjacent, rated 8 out of 10 and ranked number one in CCSD. Having all three school levels this close eliminates long bus rides and simplifies morning routines. ## What Parks and Outdoor Spaces Do Families Enjoy?
