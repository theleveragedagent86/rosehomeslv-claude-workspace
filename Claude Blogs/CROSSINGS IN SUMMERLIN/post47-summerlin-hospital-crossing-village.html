# Summerlin Hospital at The Crossing Village Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is a 485 bed, Joint Commission accredited hospital located directly within The Crossing village at 657 N Town Center Drive, Las Vegas, NV 89144. For residents of The Crossing, having a full service hospital inside their own village is one of the most significant lifestyle advantages in all of Summerlin. ## What Services Does Summerlin Hospital Offer? Summerlin Hospital operates on a 40 acre campus and provides a comprehensive range of medical services. The hospital houses a Cancer Center, Children's Medical Center, Heart Center, BirthPlace maternity unit, and a Robotic Surgery Center equipped with the da Vinci Xi surgical system. Emergency services run 24 hours a day, seven days a week, giving residents of The Crossing immediate access to critical care at any hour. The breadth of specialties available on a single campus means most medical needs can be addressed without traveling across town. ## How Close Is Summerlin Hospital to Homes in The Crossing? Because Summerlin Hospital sits within The Crossing village itself, many residents live just a few minutes away by car.
