# What is Canyon Fairways in Summerlin Canyon Fairways is an exclusive guard-gated community of approximately 150 custom luxury homes within The Canyons Village of Summerlin. Built between 1998 and 2004, primarily by Christopher Homes, this enclave sits adjacent to the TPC Las Vegas golf course and represents one of the most prestigious residential addresses in the Las Vegas valley. ## Location and Setting Canyon Fairways is located off Canyon Run Drive in Summerlin's 89144 zip code. The community occupies a prime position within The Canyons Village, a 754-acre mixed-use residential area established in 1995. Residents enter through a staffed guard gate that operates 24 hours a day, seven days a week. The surrounding landscape features views of Red Rock Canyon, the Spring Mountains, and the Las Vegas valley. ## Homes and Lots Every home in Canyon Fairways is a custom build, ranging from approximately 3,500 to over 10,000 square feet. Lots measure between one third and one half acre, giving homeowners generous space for private yards, pools, and outdoor living areas. The community includes both single story and two story designs, with architectural styles spanning Mediterranean, Tuscan, desert contemporary, and traditional Southwest influences. ## Amenities Inside the guard gate, a six-acre private park serves as the community's recreation hub. The park includes a children's play area, picnic facilities, two tennis courts, and a basketball court. Residents also benefit from the adjacent TPC Las Vegas golf course, a championship 18-hole layout designed by Bobby Weed and Raymond Floyd. ## Nearby Conveniences Canyon Fairways sits approximately 2.4 miles from Tivoli Village and 3.8 miles from Boca Park, providing quick access to upscale shopping and dining. The JW Marriott Las Vegas Resort and Spa is just minutes away along Rampart Boulevard. Downtown Summerlin and Red Rock Casino Resort are also within a short drive. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose notes that Canyon Fairways offers a rare combination in Summerlin: the privacy and exclusivity of a guard-gated custom home community with the convenience of central Summerlin's shopping, dining, and freeway access. Unlike newer luxury communities built further west, Canyon Fairways sits in an established corridor where everything residents need is already within a few minutes' drive.
