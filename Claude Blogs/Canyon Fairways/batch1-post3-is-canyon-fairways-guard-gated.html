# Is Canyon Fairways Guard Gated Yes, Canyon Fairways is a fully guard-gated community with 24-hour staffed security at its Canyon Run Drive entrance. Every vehicle entering the community is screened by on-site security personnel who verify residents and log all visitors, providing one of the highest levels of residential security available in Summerlin. ## How the Guard Gate Works The guard gate at Canyon Fairways operates around the clock, every day of the year. Security staff check identification, verify visitor authorization, and record license plates for all vehicles entering and exiting. Residents can call ahead to authorize guests, or visitors can be verified in real time at the gate. This staffed approach provides significantly more security than automated or unmanned gate systems. ## What Guard-Gated Means for Daily Life Living behind a guard gate eliminates cut-through traffic, door-to-door solicitors, and unauthorized access to neighborhood streets. Delivery drivers, contractors, and service providers must all be cleared through the gate before entering. For families with children, this means a safer environment where kids can ride bikes and play in the community's six-acre park with an added layer of security. ## Guard-Gated vs Gated Communities There is an important distinction between gated and guard-gated communities in Las Vegas. A gated community uses automated entry systems, typically keypad or remote-controlled gates, with no security personnel present. A guard-gated community like Canyon Fairways employs live security guards who actively manage access. Over 12% of Las Vegas homes are in gated or guard-gated communities, compared to 7% nationally, but true guard-gated communities with 24-hour staffing remain a premium tier. ## Guard Gate Security and Property Values Guard-gated homes in Las Vegas consistently maintain higher property values than comparable non-gated properties. The security infrastructure creates controlled demand, as the fixed number of homes within the gates cannot be expanded. Buyers consistently pay a premium for the privacy and security that guard-gated living provides, and that premium tends to hold through market cycles. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose explains that Canyon Fairways' guard gate is one of its defining features.
