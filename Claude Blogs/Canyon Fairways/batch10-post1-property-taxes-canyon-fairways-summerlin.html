Nevada law caps the assessed value of a property at 35% of its taxable value, and annual tax increases are limited by a 3% cap for owner-occupied primary residences and an 8% cap for other properties. These caps protect homeowners from dramatic year-over-year increases even in a rising market. ## What to Expect in Canyon Fairways For a Canyon Fairways home valued at $1.5 million, annual property taxes generally fall in the range of $7,500 to $12,000.
