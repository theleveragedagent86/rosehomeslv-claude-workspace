# Best Golf Course Communities in Summerlin Summerlin is one of the premier golf destinations in the Las Vegas valley, with multiple championship courses woven through its residential villages. Several guard-gated and luxury communities offer homes directly on or adjacent to these courses, providing golfers with the ultimate combination of lifestyle and convenience. ## Canyon Fairways (TPC Las Vegas) Canyon Fairways is a guard-gated community of approximately 150 custom homes adjacent to TPC Las Vegas, a public-access championship course designed by Bobby Weed and Raymond Floyd. Homes on the northeastern perimeter back directly to the fairways, offering golf course views without requiring a private membership. Prices start around $1 million, making Canyon Fairways one of the most accessible TPC-adjacent communities in Summerlin. ## Tournament Hills (TPC Summerlin) Tournament Hills sits along TPC Summerlin, which hosts the annual Shriners Children's Open PGA Tour event. The community features 132 estate-scale custom homes averaging around $5 million. For golfers who want to live on a course that regularly hosts PGA Tour competition, Tournament Hills is the premier option in the Las Vegas valley. ## The Palisades (TPC Las Vegas) The Palisades is a guard-gated enclave of approximately 130 Tuscan-inspired homes within The Arbors Village, also bordering TPC Las Vegas. Homes range from 2,875 to over 7,000 square feet. The Palisades offers a more intimate golf community experience at a generally lower price point than Canyon Fairways' largest homes. ## Red Rock Country Club Red Rock Country Club is a private, member-only community featuring two Arnold Palmer-designed courses (Arroyo and Mountain). The community includes custom homes and semi-custom residences, with membership required for golf access. This is Summerlin's premier private golf club option for buyers who want exclusive course access and a full country club lifestyle. ## Siena Golf Club Siena is a private golf club community in Summerlin featuring an 18-hole course designed by Rees Jones. The community offers attached and single-family homes within a gated setting. Siena provides a more intimate country club experience than Red Rock Country Club, with a tight-knit membership community. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose helps golf-focused buyers identify which community model fits their playing style and budget.
