# Is Canyon Fairways a Good Place to Live Canyon Fairways is one of the most desirable residential addresses in the Las Vegas valley, offering guard-gated security, custom homes on large lots, championship golf next door, and the full range of Summerlin's amenities within minutes. For buyers seeking luxury, privacy, and convenience in a single package, Canyon Fairways consistently ranks among the best options in Southern Nevada. ## Privacy and Security The 24-hour guard gate creates a quiet, controlled environment that eliminates through traffic and uninvited visitors. With only about 150 homes behind the gate, the community has a more intimate feel than larger guard-gated neighborhoods. Residents know their neighbors, and the security team recognizes familiar faces, creating a small-community atmosphere within one of the country's fastest-growing metro areas. ## Location Advantages Canyon Fairways sits in a central Summerlin corridor with quick access to everything residents need. Tivoli Village and Boca Park provide upscale shopping and dining within minutes. The JW Marriott Resort is practically next door along Rampart Boulevard. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is a short drive away, and the Las Vegas Strip is 15 to 20 minutes east. Red Rock Canyon's scenic drive entrance is about 15 minutes west, giving outdoor enthusiasts easy access to world-class hiking. ## Schools Families in Canyon Fairways are zoned for Bonner Elementary (rated 9 out of 10 on GreatSchools), Sig Rogich Middle School (rated 8 out of 10, top 10% in Nevada), and Palo Verde High School. Several prestigious private schools, including The Meadows School and Bishop Gorman High School, are also within a reasonable commute. ## Community Feel The six-acre private park gives families a gathering place with tennis courts, a basketball court, playground, and picnic areas. The adjacent TPC Las Vegas golf course adds open green space and mountain views that extend well beyond individual lot lines.
