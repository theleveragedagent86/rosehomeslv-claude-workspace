# What Year Were Homes Built in Canyon Fairways Homes in Canyon Fairways were built primarily between 1998 and 2004, with the majority of construction completed in the early 2000s. The community was developed by Christopher Homes, one of Las Vegas's most recognized luxury homebuilders, as part of The Canyons Village within the Summerlin master plan. ## Construction Timeline The first homes in Canyon Fairways began construction in the late 1990s as The Canyons Village took shape around the newly completed TPC Las Vegas golf course (opened in 1996). The pace of building picked up significantly around 2000 and 2001, when most of the community's approximately 150 custom homesites were developed. Construction continued through the early 2000s, with the final homes completed around 2004. ## Custom Build Process Unlike production home communities where a builder constructs homes on a set timeline, Canyon Fairways was developed as a custom home community. Christopher Homes offered homesites where buyers could work with the builder to design and customize their residences. This means homes were not all built at the same time. Some lots were developed early in the community's history while others were built out over several years as buyers purchased and designed their individual properties. ## Construction Quality As custom builds from a premier Las Vegas builder, Canyon Fairways homes were constructed with higher-grade materials and craftsmanship than typical production homes of the same era. Features common to the community include solid block wall construction, upgraded insulation systems, premium window packages, and custom cabinetry. The homes were built to a standard that has held up well over two decades. ## What the Age Means for Buyers Homes that are 20 to 25 years old in the Las Vegas desert climate may need updates to certain systems. HVAC units, pool equipment, water heaters, and roofing materials have typical lifespans that may align with the age of these homes.
