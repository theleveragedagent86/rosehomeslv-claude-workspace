# Canyon Fairways Lot Sizes and Custom Homesites Lots in Canyon Fairways range from one third to one half acre, making them among the largest residential parcels in Summerlin's guard-gated communities. These generous homesites were a defining feature of the community's original design, giving each custom home ample space for private yards, pools, sport courts, and extensive outdoor living areas. ## Lot Size Details The typical Canyon Fairways lot falls between approximately 14,000 and 22,000 square feet, though some parcels are slightly larger. By comparison, most production homes in Summerlin sit on lots of 5,000 to 8,000 square feet. This means Canyon Fairways homeowners enjoy two to four times more land than the average Summerlin homeowner, providing meaningful separation between neighbors and room for features that smaller lots cannot accommodate. ## What the Lots Allow The spacious homesites are what make Canyon Fairways' outdoor living possible. Most properties include private swimming pools, many with spas, water features, or infinity edges. Some homeowners have added sport courts, putting greens, fire pit areas, outdoor kitchens, and guest casitas. The lots are wide enough to provide side-yard access and mature landscaping buffers between neighboring properties. ## Lot Positions Not all lots in Canyon Fairways are equal. The most premium parcels sit along the northeast perimeter, backing directly to the TPC Las Vegas golf course. These golf-course lots offer unobstructed views of fairways and greens with no rear neighbors. Interior lots provide more privacy from the golf course while still benefiting from the community's park and mountain views. Corner lots and cul-de-sac positions offer additional side yard space. ## Landscaping Canyon Fairways sits within Summerlin's water-conscious framework, and most properties feature desert-adapted landscaping with mature trees, decorative rock, drought-tolerant plantings, and efficient irrigation systems. Front yard landscaping must comply with HOA guidelines to maintain the community's cohesive appearance, while backyards offer homeowners more creative freedom. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose emphasizes that lot size is one of Canyon Fairways' strongest competitive advantages. In newer Summerlin communities, even luxury guard-gated homes often sit on quarter-acre lots or smaller. Canyon Fairways' half-acre parcels provide a level of privacy and outdoor living potential that simply does not exist in most of today's new construction.
