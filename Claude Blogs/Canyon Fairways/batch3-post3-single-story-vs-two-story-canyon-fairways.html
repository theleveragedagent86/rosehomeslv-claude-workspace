# Single Story vs Two Story Homes in Canyon Fairways Canyon Fairways offers both single story and two story custom homes, giving buyers a choice based on lifestyle, mobility, and how they want to use their space. The community's large lots of one third to one half acre accommodate both configurations comfortably, and each style has distinct advantages depending on the buyer's priorities. ## Single Story Homes Single story homes in Canyon Fairways typically range from 3,500 to over 5,000 square feet. These homes spread across the lot with a wider footprint, creating spacious floorplans where all living spaces, bedrooms, and entertaining areas are on one level. The single story layout is especially popular with retirees, empty nesters, and buyers who prefer to avoid stairs entirely. The benefits include easy accessibility throughout the home, simpler maintenance (no second-story windows or roofing to access), and a seamless connection between indoor living spaces and outdoor patios and pool areas. Single story homes on Canyon Fairways' large lots rarely feel cramped because the half-acre parcels provide enough room for a generous footprint plus extensive outdoor living. ## Two Story Homes Two story homes in Canyon Fairways range from approximately 4,500 to over 10,000 square feet, with the largest estates in the community typically utilizing two levels. The two story layout allows for separation between public entertaining spaces on the main floor and private bedroom suites upstairs. Many two story Canyon Fairways homes feature upstairs balconies and loft areas that capture elevated views of the TPC Las Vegas golf course and surrounding mountains. For families, the two story configuration provides natural separation between children's bedrooms and the primary suite. The vertical layout also preserves more yard space for pools, sport courts, and outdoor entertaining areas, since the home's footprint is more compact relative to its total square footage. ## How to Choose The decision between single story and two story in Canyon Fairways often comes down to lifestyle stage and view priorities. Buyers who want golf course or mountain views from upper-level rooms gravitate toward two story homes. Those prioritizing accessibility, aging in place, or a more open flow between indoor and outdoor spaces tend to prefer single story. Both configurations are well-represented in the community, so inventory typically includes both options. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose finds that many Canyon Fairways buyers specifically seek single story homes because the large lots make them viable at square footages that would be impossible on smaller parcels. A 5,000-square-foot single story home on a half-acre lot is a rare find in Las Vegas, and Canyon Fairways is one of the few communities where that combination exists consistently.
