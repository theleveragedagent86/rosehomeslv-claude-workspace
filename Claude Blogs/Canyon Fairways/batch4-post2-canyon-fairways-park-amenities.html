# Canyon Fairways Park and Amenities Canyon Fairways features a private six-acre park exclusively for residents, located inside the guard gate. This park serves as the community's recreation and gathering hub, offering tennis courts, a basketball court, a children's playground, and picnic facilities in a secure, traffic-free environment. ## Tennis Courts The park includes two tennis courts available to Canyon Fairways residents. The courts are maintained by the HOA and provide a convenient option for recreational and competitive play without leaving the community. The courts are regulation-sized and suitable for singles and doubles matches. ## Basketball Court A full basketball court sits within the park, giving families and active residents a dedicated space for pickup games and practice. The court's location inside the guard gate means children and teenagers can use it independently with an added layer of safety compared to public parks. ## Children's Playground The playground area is designed for younger residents with age-appropriate equipment, safety surfacing, and shaded areas. For families with children, having a playground within walking distance of every home in the community, and inside the security perimeter, is a practical daily convenience. ## Picnic and Open Space The park includes picnic tables, shade structures, and open grass areas for casual recreation. The six-acre footprint provides enough room for community events, family gatherings, and everyday outdoor activities. The open spaces are maintained by the HOA and contribute to the park-like feel of the community's interior. ## Walkability Within the Gates Canyon Fairways' compact layout of approximately 150 homes means the park is within walking distance of every residence. Wide residential streets with minimal traffic make it easy for families to walk or bike to the park without safety concerns. The park acts as a natural gathering point where neighbors encounter each other regularly, fostering the community feel that smaller guard-gated neighborhoods are known for. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose points out that the six-acre park is a standout amenity for a community of Canyon Fairways' size.
