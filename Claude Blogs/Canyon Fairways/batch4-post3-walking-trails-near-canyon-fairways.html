# Walking and Trails Near Canyon Fairways Canyon Fairways residents have access to an extensive network of walking and biking trails through the Summerlin trail system, which stretches over 200 miles across the master-planned community. Combined with nearby parks and the proximity to Red Rock Canyon, the area around Canyon Fairways offers outdoor recreation options for every fitness level. ## Summerlin Trail System The Summerlin master plan includes over 200 miles of interconnected trails that link neighborhoods, parks, schools, and commercial areas. Canyon Fairways residents can access these paved paths from multiple points near the community, using them for morning walks, jogging, cycling, or simply getting to nearby destinations without driving. The trails are maintained by the Summerlin Council, funded through the monthly master association fee. ## Cottonwood Canyon Park Cottonwood Canyon Park is one of the closest major trail destinations to Canyon Fairways, offering a 70-acre natural open space with jogging and walking paths. The park features a linear trail that follows the natural wash corridor, providing a more natural desert walking experience compared to the paved residential trails. Shade trees and rest areas are positioned along the route. ## Golf Course Perimeter Walking The TPC Las Vegas golf course that borders Canyon Fairways provides additional scenic walking routes along its perimeter. Summerlin's path system includes sections that run alongside the course, giving walkers and joggers views of the maintained fairways and mountain backdrop while staying on dedicated pedestrian paths separate from golf cart traffic. ## Red Rock Canyon Access Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area is approximately 15 minutes west of Canyon Fairways. The scenic loop drive provides access to dozens of trailheads ranging from easy walks to challenging scrambles. Popular trails like Calico Tanks, Keystone Thrust, and Ice Box Canyon are all within a short drive.
