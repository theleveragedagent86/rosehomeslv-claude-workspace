# Cottonwood Canyon Park Near Canyon Fairways Cottonwood Canyon Park is a 70-acre natural open space located near Canyon Fairways in Summerlin, offering walking trails, jogging paths, and a desert landscape that provides a welcome contrast to the manicured streets and golf courses of the surrounding neighborhoods. ## Park Overview Cottonwood Canyon Park follows a natural desert wash corridor, creating a linear park experience that feels more like a nature preserve than a traditional community park. The park stretches through Summerlin's Arbors and Canyons villages, providing a green corridor that connects multiple neighborhoods. The 70-acre footprint makes it one of the larger open spaces in the Summerlin master plan. ## Trails and Walking Paths The park features paved and natural surface trails suitable for walking, jogging, and casual biking. The main path follows the wash corridor, offering a mostly flat route with gentle terrain changes. Shade trees planted along the trail provide relief during warmer months, and rest areas with benches are spaced throughout. The trails connect to Summerlin's broader 200-plus-mile trail network, allowing users to extend their route in multiple directions. ## Natural Desert Environment Unlike Summerlin's developed parks with irrigated grass and sports facilities, Cottonwood Canyon preserves the natural desert landscape. Native vegetation, rock formations, and desert wash features create a setting where residents can experience the Mojave Desert environment without leaving their neighborhood. Birds and small desert wildlife are common along the trail, particularly during early morning and evening hours. ## Dog Walking Cottonwood Canyon Park is a popular destination for Canyon Fairways dog owners. The extended trail provides a longer walking route than what is available within the guard-gated community itself, and the natural terrain gives dogs a more stimulating outdoor experience. Dogs must be leashed on the trails, but the length and variety of the path make it a satisfying outing for both pets and owners. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose recommends Cottonwood Canyon Park to Canyon Fairways residents looking for a daily walking routine that goes beyond the community's internal streets. The park offers just enough wildness to feel like an escape while remaining minutes from home. For families and dog owners, it is one of the most convenient outdoor resources near Canyon Fairways.
