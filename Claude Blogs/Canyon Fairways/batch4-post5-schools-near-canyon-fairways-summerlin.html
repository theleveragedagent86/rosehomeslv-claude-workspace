# Schools Near Canyon Fairways Summerlin Canyon Fairways is zoned for some of the highest-rated public schools in the Las Vegas valley, with Bonner Elementary, Sig Rogich Middle School, and Palo Verde High School serving the community. The combination of strong public school options and nearby private schools makes Canyon Fairways an attractive choice for families prioritizing education. ## Bonner Elementary School Bonner Elementary is the zoned elementary school for Canyon Fairways and carries a 9 out of 10 rating on GreatSchools, placing it among the top elementary schools in Nevada. The school is located approximately 1.6 miles from Canyon Fairways, a drive of about 4 minutes. Bonner serves grades K through 5 and is known for strong academic performance in reading and math proficiency. ## Sig Rogich Middle School Sig Rogich Middle School serves Canyon Fairways students in grades 6 through 8. The school holds an 8 out of 10 GreatSchools rating and ranks in the top 10% of all middle schools in Nevada. Rogich has approximately 1,668 students and offers robust athletics, fine arts, and extracurricular programs. The school is located on Pavilion Center Drive, a short drive from the community. ## Palo Verde High School Palo Verde High School is the zoned high school for Canyon Fairways, located approximately 2.2 miles away. Palo Verde is one of the largest high schools in the Las Vegas valley with over 3,100 students, offering a comprehensive range of Advanced Placement courses, athletics, performing arts, and career and technical education programs. ## Private School Options Several prestigious private schools are within a reasonable commute from Canyon Fairways. The Meadows School, a K through 12 college preparatory institution, is located in Summerlin.
