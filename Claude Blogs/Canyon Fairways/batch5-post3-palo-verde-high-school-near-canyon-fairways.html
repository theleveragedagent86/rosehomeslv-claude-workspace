# Palo Verde High School Near Canyon Fairways Palo Verde High School is the zoned high school for Canyon Fairways, located approximately 2.2 miles from the community in the heart of Summerlin. With over 3,100 students, Palo Verde is one of the largest and most comprehensive high schools in the Las Vegas valley, offering extensive academic, athletic, and extracurricular programs. ## Academics Palo Verde offers a broad curriculum that includes numerous Advanced Placement (AP) courses across subjects including English, mathematics, sciences, history, and world languages. The school provides honors-level coursework for students seeking accelerated learning, and its college-preparatory track helps students prepare for admission to competitive universities. Career and technical education programs provide additional pathways for students interested in specialized fields. ## Athletics Palo Verde competes in the Nevada Interscholastic Activities Association and fields teams across a wide range of sports. The Panthers have competitive programs in football, basketball, baseball, soccer, swimming, tennis, golf, volleyball, and track and field, among others. The school's athletic facilities include a stadium, gym, pool, and practice fields that support year-round competition. ## Fine Arts and Extracurriculars The school's performing arts program includes theater, band, orchestra, and choir, with regular performances and competitions. Student clubs cover a wide spectrum of interests, from academic honor societies to community service organizations. The size of the student body supports the diversity of these offerings, giving students the opportunity to explore a range of interests during their high school years. ## Location and Commute Palo Verde High School is located on Gentle Breeze Drive in Summerlin, approximately a 5-minute drive from Canyon Fairways. The route travels through residential Summerlin streets, making the daily commute easy for both parents driving and older students who drive themselves. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose notes that Palo Verde's combination of size and program depth is an advantage for Canyon Fairways families. The large student body supports a level of course offerings, athletic competition, and extracurricular variety that smaller schools simply cannot match. For families transitioning from Bonner Elementary and Sig Rogich Middle School, Palo Verde continues the strong public school pathway that makes Canyon Fairways' school zoning so attractive.
