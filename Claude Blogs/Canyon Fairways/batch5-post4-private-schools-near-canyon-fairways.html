# Private Schools Near Canyon Fairways Canyon Fairways families have access to several of Southern Nevada's most respected private schools within a comfortable commute. The school offers rigorous academics in a smaller setting, with an emphasis on critical thinking, creativity, and character development. The Meadows is consistently ranked among the top private schools in Nevada, and its Summerlin campus provides convenient access for Canyon Fairways families. ## Bishop Gorman High School Bishop Gorman High School is widely regarded as the premier private high school in Nevada. Located approximately 15 minutes south of Canyon Fairways, Bishop Gorman offers a Catholic college preparatory education with exceptional academics, nationally recognized athletic programs, and a strong alumni network. The school's campus on Maryland Parkway features modern facilities, and its graduates consistently earn admission to competitive universities across the country. ## Alexander Dawson School Alexander Dawson School at Rainbow Mountain serves students from pre-K through 8th grade and is located in Summerlin, within about 10 to 15 minutes of Canyon Fairways.
