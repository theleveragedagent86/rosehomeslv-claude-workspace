# Retiring in Canyon Fairways Summerlin Canyon Fairways is an excellent choice for retirees seeking luxury living, security, and convenience without the restrictions of an age-restricted community. The guard-gated community offers single story home options, low-maintenance living within the HOA framework, and immediate access to golf, dining, medical care, and recreation. ## Not Age-Restricted Canyon Fairways is not a 55-plus or age-restricted community. Retirees live alongside families and working professionals, creating a diverse and active neighborhood rather than an exclusively senior environment. For retirees who want to be part of a vibrant community without age-related restrictions on guests, family visits, or home use, this open structure is a significant advantage. ## Single Story Living Many Canyon Fairways homes are single story, ranging from 3,500 to over 5,000 square feet. These floor plans eliminate stairs entirely, making them well-suited for aging in place. The large lots accommodate wheelchair ramps, accessible landscaping modifications, and single-level outdoor living areas if needed in the future. Retirees who prefer to plan ahead for long-term accessibility find single story Canyon Fairways homes particularly appealing. ## Golf and Recreation Living adjacent to TPC Las Vegas provides retirees with a championship golf course at their doorstep. Angel Park Golf Club adds two more courses within minutes. The community's six-acre park with tennis and basketball courts offers additional physical activity options. For retirees who prioritize an active lifestyle, Canyon Fairways provides the infrastructure to stay engaged without long drives. ## Medical Access Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is a full-service hospital within a short drive, offering emergency services, specialty care, and outpatient facilities. Multiple medical office buildings along Rampart Boulevard house primary care physicians, specialists, and urgent care clinics. The proximity of quality medical care is an important practical consideration for retirees. ## Nevada Tax Advantages Nevada has no state income tax, which benefits retirees drawing from pensions, retirement accounts, and investment income. Combined with relatively moderate property taxes, the overall tax burden for retirees in Nevada is lower than in many other states, allowing retirement savings to stretch further. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose works with many retirees relocating to Las Vegas and consistently recommends Canyon Fairways for those who want luxury without compromise. The guard gate provides peace of mind, the single story options support long-term livability, and the central location means everything from groceries to medical appointments is minutes away.
