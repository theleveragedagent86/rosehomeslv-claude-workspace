# Pet Friendly Living in Canyon Fairways Canyon Fairways is a welcoming environment for pet owners, with large private lots, nearby trails and parks, and a secure guard-gated setting that gives dogs and their owners room to roam safely. The community's spacious homesites and proximity to outdoor recreation make it one of the better options in Summerlin for residents with pets. ## Large Private Yards With lots ranging from one third to one half acre, Canyon Fairways homes offer significantly more private outdoor space than most Summerlin properties. Most backyards are enclosed with block walls, giving dogs a secure area to play and exercise without leaving the property. The yard space is large enough for dogs of all sizes, including breeds that need room to run. ## Walking Within the Gates The community's internal streets are wide, quiet, and free of through traffic thanks to the guard gate. Dog owners regularly walk their pets on the neighborhood streets, enjoying the low traffic volume and the landscaped surroundings. The six-acre community park provides an additional destination for on-leash walks within the security perimeter. ## Nearby Trails and Parks Cottonwood Canyon Park, a 70-acre natural open space nearby, is one of the most popular dog walking destinations for Canyon Fairways residents. The extended trail offers a longer route through desert terrain that dogs find stimulating. The Summerlin trail system adds over 200 miles of paved paths accessible from the area, providing variety for daily walks. ## Dog Parks Barkin' Basin Dog Park is the nearest dedicated off-leash facility, located in Summerlin approximately 10 to 15 minutes from Canyon Fairways. The park features separate areas for large and small dogs, water stations, shade structures, and waste disposal stations. For Canyon Fairways dog owners who want off-leash socialization for their pets, Barkin' Basin provides a well-maintained option. ## HOA Pet Policies Canyon Fairways' HOA allows pets, and the community does not impose the breed or size restrictions found in some condo or townhome associations. Homeowners are expected to keep pets under control in common areas and clean up after their animals. The large lot sizes naturally reduce neighbor-to-neighbor pet conflicts that can arise in denser communities. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose notes that Canyon Fairways is one of the most pet-friendly guard-gated communities in Summerlin. The large yards, quiet streets, and nearby trail access create an environment where pets are part of the daily lifestyle rather than an afterthought.
