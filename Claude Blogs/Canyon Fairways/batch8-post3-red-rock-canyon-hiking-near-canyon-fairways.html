# Red Rock Canyon Hiking Near Canyon Fairways Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area is approximately 15 minutes west of Canyon Fairways, providing one of the most spectacular outdoor recreation destinations in the country right in the community's backyard. The 195,819-acre conservation area features a 13-mile scenic loop drive, dozens of hiking trails, and dramatic sandstone formations that draw visitors from around the world. ## Getting There From Canyon Fairways, the drive to Red Rock Canyon's scenic loop entrance follows Charleston Boulevard west through Summerlin. The route is straightforward with no freeway required, and the transition from suburban Summerlin to the conservation area's desert landscape happens within minutes. For Canyon Fairways residents, a morning hike at Red Rock can begin and end before lunch without any significant travel commitment. ## Popular Trails Red Rock Canyon offers trails for every fitness level. Calico Tanks is a moderate 2.5-mile round trip that rewards hikers with a hidden natural pool and panoramic valley views. Keystone Thrust Trail is an easy 2.2-mile out-and-back that showcases one of the area's most dramatic geological features. Ice Box Canyon is a moderate 2.6-mile hike into a narrow canyon with seasonal waterfalls. Turtlehead Peak provides a challenging 5-mile climb to a 6,323-foot summit with 360-degree views. ## Scenic Loop Drive The 13-mile one-way scenic loop drive provides access to all major trailheads, pullover viewpoints, and picnic areas. The loop is also popular with cyclists, particularly in the cooler morning hours. Canyon Fairways residents who prefer to experience Red Rock without a strenuous hike can drive the loop and stop at viewpoints that showcase the area's iconic red and cream sandstone formations. ## Seasonal Considerations The best hiking months at Red Rock Canyon are October through April, when temperatures are comfortable for extended outdoor activity. Summer temperatures regularly exceed 110 degrees, making early morning the only safe window for desert hiking. Canyon Fairways' proximity means residents can start a hike at sunrise and return home before the midday heat, an advantage that communities further from Red Rock cannot match. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose considers Red Rock Canyon access one of Canyon Fairways' most compelling lifestyle features.
