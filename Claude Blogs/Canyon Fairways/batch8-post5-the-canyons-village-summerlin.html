# The Canyons Village in Summerlin The Canyons is a 754-acre mixed-use residential village within the Summerlin master plan that serves as the home village for Canyon Fairways. Established in 1995, The Canyons encompasses multiple neighborhoods, the TPC Las Vegas golf course, two major business parks, and the JW Marriott Las Vegas Resort, creating a self-contained community within the larger Summerlin framework. ## Village Layout The Canyons occupies a central position within Summerlin, bordered roughly by Summerlin Parkway to the north and Rampart Boulevard providing the primary commercial corridor. The village includes approximately a dozen residential neighborhoods ranging from entry-level homes to guard-gated custom estates. This variety gives The Canyons a more diverse character than single-product villages elsewhere in Summerlin. ## Residential Communities Canyon Fairways is the premier guard-gated community within The Canyons, but the village includes several other notable neighborhoods. Canyon Crest is another guard-gated enclave offering luxury living adjacent to the TPC course. Additional neighborhoods provide single-family homes, townhomes, and attached housing at various price points, giving the village a range of options from the mid-$300,000s to well over $2 million. ## TPC Las Vegas The TPC Las Vegas golf course is the centerpiece recreational feature of The Canyons Village. The 18-hole championship course, designed by Bobby Weed and Raymond Floyd, wraps through the village and provides golf course views and open space buffers for multiple neighborhoods. The course's public accessibility means all residents of The Canyons can play without membership requirements. ## Commercial and Hospitality The Canyons' commercial component sets it apart from purely residential Summerlin villages. The JW Marriott Las Vegas Resort and Rampart Casino sit within the village boundaries, providing resort dining, entertainment, and spa services. Business parks along the Rampart corridor house professional offices, medical practices, and corporate tenants, creating employment centers within walking distance of residential areas. ## Village Parks and Trails The Canyons connects to Summerlin's trail system and includes community parks that serve its residential neighborhoods. The trail network provides pedestrian and cycling links between neighborhoods, the golf course, commercial areas, and adjacent villages like The Arbors. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose explains that The Canyons Village offers something most Summerlin villages do not: a true live-work-play environment. The combination of guard-gated luxury, mid-range housing, a championship golf course, a full-service resort, and commercial office space within a single 754-acre village means daily life can happen almost entirely within the village boundaries. For Canyon Fairways residents, this integration is a quality-of-life advantage that translates into shorter commutes and more time at home.
