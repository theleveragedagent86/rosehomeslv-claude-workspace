# Canyon Fairways vs Tournament Hills Summerlin Canyon Fairways and Tournament Hills are both guard-gated custom home communities in Summerlin adjacent to TPC golf courses, but they differ significantly in price point, home sizes, and overall market positioning. Understanding the differences helps luxury buyers determine which community best matches their budget and lifestyle goals. ## Price Range Canyon Fairways homes generally start around $1 million and range up to approximately $2.5 million. Tournament Hills commands significantly higher prices, with an average around $5 million. For buyers seeking guard-gated custom living at a more accessible price point, Canyon Fairways offers substantial value compared to Tournament Hills' ultra-luxury positioning. ## Home Sizes Both communities feature custom homes, but Tournament Hills skews considerably larger. Tournament Hills includes 132 homes ranging from 3,500 to over 21,000 square feet, with many estates exceeding 10,000 square feet. Canyon Fairways' approximately 150 homes range from 3,500 to just over 10,000 square feet. Buyers who need massive square footage will find more options in Tournament Hills, while Canyon Fairways provides a more balanced range. ## Golf Course Both communities sit adjacent to TPC golf courses, but they border different facilities. Canyon Fairways backs to TPC Las Vegas (formerly TPC at The Canyons), a public-access course. Tournament Hills is positioned along TPC Summerlin, which hosts the annual Shriners Children's Open, a PGA Tour event. Both courses carry the TPC brand and professional maintenance standards. ## Lot Sizes Canyon Fairways lots range from one third to one half acre, which is generous by Summerlin standards. Tournament Hills offers comparable or larger lots for its estate-scale homes. Both communities provide meaningful separation between neighbors, but Tournament Hills' larger homes naturally require more land to accommodate their footprints and grounds. ## Community Size and Feel Canyon Fairways has approximately 150 homes while Tournament Hills has 132. The communities are similar in overall scale, creating intimate environments where residents know their neighbors. Both feature private parks with recreational amenities and 24-hour guard gate security. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose recommends Canyon Fairways for buyers who want guard-gated custom living on a TPC golf course without the $5 million average price tag of Tournament Hills. The two communities share many of the same advantages, including security, large lots, and golf course proximity, but Canyon Fairways delivers them at a meaningfully lower entry point. For buyers with the budget for Tournament Hills, the decision comes down to whether the additional size and prestige justify the premium.
