# Canyon Fairways vs Eagle Hills Summerlin Canyon Fairways and Eagle Hills are both guard-gated custom home communities in Summerlin with similar price points and amenity structures. They represent two of the most comparable options in Summerlin's luxury market, making a direct comparison valuable for buyers choosing between the two. ## Location and Village Canyon Fairways is located in The Canyons Village of Summerlin, directly adjacent to the TPC Las Vegas golf course. Eagle Hills sits in The Hills Village, a different section of Summerlin. The two communities are several miles apart, and their village settings create different neighborhood surroundings and commercial access. Canyon Fairways benefits from proximity to the JW Marriott and Tivoli Village, while Eagle Hills connects to its own set of nearby retail and dining options. ## Home Count and Lot Sizes Canyon Fairways has approximately 150 custom homes on one-third to one-half-acre lots. Eagle Hills contains 160 guard-gated custom homes on one-third to two-thirds-acre lots. Eagle Hills' larger maximum lot sizes give it a slight edge for buyers who prioritize the most expansive parcels, though both communities offer generous land by Summerlin standards. ## Home Sizes and Prices Both communities feature custom homes ranging from approximately 3,500 to over 12,000 square feet, with prices starting around $1 million. The pricing is comparable between the two, making them direct competitors in Summerlin's guard-gated luxury segment. Individual home prices depend on size, condition, lot position, and the extent of any renovations. ## Golf Course Access Canyon Fairways backs directly to the TPC Las Vegas golf course, with many homes enjoying golf course frontage and views. Eagle Hills does not have golf course lots, though residents have access to TPC Summerlin's 18-hole championship course nearby. For buyers who specifically want to live on a golf course, Canyon Fairways has a clear advantage. ## Community Amenities Both communities feature private parks with tennis courts, basketball courts, and open space for families. Eagle Hills' park is approximately 3 acres with a walking trail. Canyon Fairways' park spans 6 acres. Both provide 24-hour guard gate security with staffed entry points. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose advises buyers to weigh the golf course factor heavily when comparing these two communities. If living on a TPC course with fairway views is a priority, Canyon Fairways is the clear choice. If golf course frontage is less important than maximum lot size or village location preferences, Eagle Hills offers a comparable experience.
