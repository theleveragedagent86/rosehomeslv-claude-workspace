# Canyon Fairways vs The Ridges Summerlin Canyon Fairways and The Ridges are both guard-gated luxury communities in Summerlin, but they occupy very different positions in the market. The Ridges is Summerlin's most expensive and exclusive community, while Canyon Fairways offers a more accessible entry into guard-gated custom home living. Understanding the differences in price, amenities, and setting helps buyers determine which community aligns with their priorities. ## Price Comparison Canyon Fairways homes generally range from $1 million to $2.5 million. The Ridges is the most expensive community on the west side of Las Vegas, with property values approximately 25% higher per square foot compared to other custom home communities. Homes in The Ridges regularly list above $3 million, with premium estates exceeding $10 million. For buyers seeking luxury without the ultra-premium price tag, Canyon Fairways provides substantial savings. ## Community Scale The Ridges spans 800 acres across 14 distinct neighborhoods, making it significantly larger than Canyon Fairways' approximately 150 homes. The Ridges includes custom home enclaves, semi-custom neighborhoods, and even townhomes at its entry level (Fairway Hills, priced from the $500,000s). Canyon Fairways is exclusively custom homes, creating a more uniform community character. ## Amenities The Ridges features Club Ridges, a 9,000-square-foot private amenity center with fitness facilities, pools, and tennis courts. Canyon Fairways offers a six-acre park with tennis courts, basketball, and a playground. The Ridges' double-gated security (community gate plus individual neighborhood gates) provides an additional layer of exclusivity. Canyon Fairways has a single guard gate. ## Location and Setting The Ridges sits further west in Summerlin, closer to Red Rock Canyon, with dramatic elevated terrain and canyon views. Canyon Fairways occupies a more central Summerlin position adjacent to TPC Las Vegas, providing quicker access to Tivoli Village, the JW Marriott, and Summerlin Parkway. Buyers who prioritize natural setting and Red Rock proximity may prefer The Ridges, while those who value convenience and golf course adjacency may favor Canyon Fairways. ## Age and Condition Canyon Fairways homes were built between 1998 and 2004, making them 20-plus years old. The Ridges includes newer construction, with some neighborhoods developed in the 2010s and ongoing custom building. Buyers who prefer newer homes with modern features may find more options in The Ridges, though renovated Canyon Fairways homes can offer comparable interior quality. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose often tells clients that Canyon Fairways and The Ridges serve different buyer profiles. The Ridges is for buyers who want the absolute pinnacle of Summerlin luxury and are willing to pay the premium. Canyon Fairways is for buyers who want guard-gated custom living, large lots, and a golf course setting at a price point that represents genuine value.
