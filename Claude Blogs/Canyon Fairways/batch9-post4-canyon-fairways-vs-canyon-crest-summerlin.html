# Canyon Fairways vs Canyon Crest Summerlin Canyon Fairways and Canyon Crest are both guard-gated communities within The Canyons Village of Summerlin, making them the two closest luxury options in the same neighborhood. While they share a village and proximity to TPC Las Vegas, the communities differ in size, home styles, and price positioning. ## Same Village, Different Character Both communities sit within The Canyons Village and share the same general location along Rampart Boulevard in Summerlin's 89144 zip code. They benefit from the same nearby amenities: TPC Las Vegas, the JW Marriott, Tivoli Village, and the Summerlin trail system. The primary differences are found within the gates of each community. ## Home Count and Lots Canyon Fairways has approximately 150 custom homes on one-third to one-half-acre lots. Canyon Crest is a smaller community with fewer homes, creating an even more intimate setting. Both communities were developed during a similar timeframe in the late 1990s and early 2000s, and both feature custom-built residences rather than production floorplans. ## Home Sizes and Styles Canyon Fairways homes range from approximately 3,500 to over 10,000 square feet, with Mediterranean, Tuscan, desert contemporary, and Southwest architectural styles represented. Canyon Crest features a similar range of custom architecture, though the specific homes and lot configurations differ. Both communities include single story and two story options. ## Golf Course Proximity Canyon Fairways' northeastern lots back directly to the TPC Las Vegas golf course, with homes enjoying fairway frontage and views. Canyon Crest also benefits from TPC Las Vegas proximity, though the specific golf course views and adjacency depend on lot position within the community. Buyers specifically seeking golf course frontage should compare available inventory in both communities. ## Pricing Both communities price in the $1 million to $2.5 million range, making them direct alternatives for buyers targeting this segment of the Summerlin luxury market. Because both are fully built out resale communities, pricing depends heavily on individual home condition, lot position, and the extent of any renovations. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose recommends that buyers interested in either Canyon Fairways or Canyon Crest tour both communities before making a decision. Because they share the same village, the external lifestyle, including schools, shopping, dining, and recreation, is identical. The choice between them comes down to the individual homes available, lot positions, community size preference, and specific features of each property.
