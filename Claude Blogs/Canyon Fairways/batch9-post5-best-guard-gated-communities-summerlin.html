# Best Guard Gated Communities in Summerlin Summerlin is home to some of the most prestigious guard-gated communities in the Las Vegas valley, each offering a distinct combination of home styles, price points, and amenities. For luxury buyers evaluating their options, understanding the differences between Summerlin's top guard-gated neighborhoods helps narrow the search to the right fit. ## Canyon Fairways Canyon Fairways in The Canyons Village features approximately 150 custom homes on one-third to one-half-acre lots, starting around $1 million. The community backs to TPC Las Vegas and includes a six-acre private park. Canyon Fairways offers strong value for guard-gated custom living with golf course access and central Summerlin convenience. ## Tournament Hills Tournament Hills contains 132 custom estates adjacent to TPC Summerlin, with an average price around $5 million. Homes range from 3,500 to over 21,000 square feet. This is Summerlin's premier estate community for buyers seeking the largest custom homes and the highest prestige level. ## Eagle Hills Eagle Hills offers 160 guard-gated custom homes on one-third to two-thirds-acre lots in The Hills Village. Prices start around $1 million, making it comparable to Canyon Fairways. Eagle Hills does not have golf course lots, but the community's park and lot sizes are competitive. ## The Ridges The Ridges spans 800 acres with 14 neighborhoods, ranging from townhomes in the $500,000s to custom estates exceeding $10 million. The community features Club Ridges (9,000-square-foot amenity center), double-gated security, and a dramatic elevated setting near Red Rock Canyon. The Ridges is Summerlin's most exclusive address. ## The Palisades (The Arbors) The Palisades is a guard-gated enclave of approximately 130 Tuscan-inspired homes within The Arbors Village, adjacent to TPC Las Vegas. Homes range from 2,875 to over 7,000 square feet. The Palisades offers a more intimate golf course community at a lower price point than Canyon Fairways' largest homes. ## Bellacere (The Arbors) Bellacere features 57 custom homesites in The Arbors with homes from 3,040 to over 16,000 square feet. This ultra-exclusive enclave offers the most architecturally diverse custom homes in the Arbors area, with prices that can exceed $1 million for premium properties. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose helps buyers navigate Summerlin's guard-gated options based on their specific priorities. If golf course living and value matter most, Canyon Fairways and The Palisades stand out. If maximum home size and prestige are the priorities, Tournament Hills and The Ridges lead the field.
