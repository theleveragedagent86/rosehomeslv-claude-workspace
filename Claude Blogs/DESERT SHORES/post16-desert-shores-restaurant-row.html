Restaurant Row at Desert Shores is a lakefront dining cluster at 2620 Regatta Drive, sitting right on Lake Jacqueline. It is the only true lakefront restaurant destination inside Las Vegas city limits. ## What is Restaurant Row at Desert Shores? Restaurant Row anchors Lakeside Village, a small commercial pocket on the southwest shore of Lake Jacqueline. The address is 2620 Regatta Drive, and every tenant looks out over the 24.8 acre lake. Patios sit feet from the water, and you can watch ducks paddle past while you eat. This setup does not exist anywhere else in Las Vegas proper. Lake Las Vegas in Henderson is the only comparable lakefront dining concept, and it is a thirty minute drive east. Restaurant Row gives Desert Shores residents the same ambiance without leaving the neighborhood. ## Which restaurants are at Restaurant Row? Six establishments make up the core lineup. Marche Bacchus is the award winning French bistro and wine shop, with over 950 wine labels and a famous lakefront patio. Americana, led by Chef Stephen Blandino, serves modern New American fine dining in Suite 118. Vino Aromi opened in 2024 with handcrafted pastas and a casual Italian wine bar feel. The Daily Bread LV is the bakery and cafe from Hell's Kitchen winner Chef Scott Cummings, with breakfast, brunch, and outdoor heated seating. Buena Vista Kitchen brings family recipe Mexican breakfast and lunch from Chef Manny Garduno. Super Chill rounds it out with sundaes, soft serve, smoothies, and shaved ice. ## Why Restaurant Row matters to Desert Shores buyers Many Desert Shores homes sit within walking distance of Restaurant Row, which is rare for a Las Vegas master planned community.
