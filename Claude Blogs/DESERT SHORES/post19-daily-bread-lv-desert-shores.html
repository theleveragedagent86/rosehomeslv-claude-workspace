The Daily Bread LV is a bakery and cafe at Lakeside Village in Desert Shores, run by Chef Scott Cummings, a Hell's Kitchen winner. It serves fresh baked goods, sandwiches, and salads with outdoor heated lakefront seating. ## Who is Chef Scott Cummings? Chef Scott Cummings won Hell's Kitchen, the high pressure cooking competition hosted by Chef Gordon Ramsay. After the show, he brought his skills back to Las Vegas and opened The Daily Bread LV at Restaurant Row in Desert Shores. The bakery focuses on fresh, made from scratch breads, pastries, and approachable cafe fare. The format leans casual, which is rare for a chef of his level. You can grab a morning pastry, a sandwich, or a full brunch plate, all from a kitchen that competed at the top of national television. ## What does The Daily Bread LV serve? The menu covers breakfast, brunch, and lunch. Fresh baked breads anchor the sandwich program. Salads, pastries, and seasonal specials round things out.
