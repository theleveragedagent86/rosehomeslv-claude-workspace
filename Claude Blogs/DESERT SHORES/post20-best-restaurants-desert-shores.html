Vino Aromi opened in 2024 with handcrafted pastas, lunch service, happy hour from 3:30 to 5:30, and dinner until 9pm. Buena Vista Kitchen serves Mexican breakfast and lunch from Chef Manny Garduno's family recipes. Super Chill handles dessert with sundaes, soft serve, smoothies, and shaved ice. ## What other restaurants are nearby? Beyond Restaurant Row, Desert Shores residents have several short drive options.
