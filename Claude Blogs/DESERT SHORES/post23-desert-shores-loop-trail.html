The Desert Shores Loop is a 4.5 mile paved trail circling all four lakes, rated moderate, with 150 feet of elevation gain and an average completion time of 1 hour 26 minutes. ## What is the Desert Shores Loop Trail? The Desert Shores Loop is the headline trail of the community. It runs 4.5 miles on smooth paved surfaces and circles the four interconnected lakes that define the neighborhood. AllTrails has cataloged the route with more than 204 user reviews, putting it among the most reviewed neighborhood trails on the northwest side of Las Vegas. The trail is rated moderate, mostly because of the 4.5 mile distance. Total elevation gain is only 150 feet, so there are no real hills. The average finish time is right around 1 hour and 26 minutes for walkers. ## What can you do on the loop? Walking, running, and road biking all work great here. The paved surface is friendly to strollers, road bikes, and roller blades. The loop crosses bridges, weaves past restaurants on the water, and offers constant lake views. Sunrise and sunset are when the trail comes alive. Joggers chase cool morning air, and couples take after dinner walks while the sun drops behind the mountains. The lakes reflect the sky and the energy is unmistakable. ## What is special about this trail in Las Vegas? Most neighborhood trails in Las Vegas run along washes or wide sidewalks. The Desert Shores Loop is one of the only paved community trails in the city that wraps a real water feature for its full length.
