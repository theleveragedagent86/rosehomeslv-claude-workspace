The Desert Shores Community Center is a 2,100 square foot clubhouse at 2500 Regatta Drive with banquet space for up to 100 guests, a commercial kitchen, and lakefront terraces, available for rental by members. ## What is the Desert Shores Community Center? The Desert Shores Community Center sits at 2500 Regatta Drive, right on the water in the heart of the community. The clubhouse is 2,100 square feet of indoor space, designed to host private events while staying connected to the lake views outside. It serves as the hub for community meetings, member gatherings, and private parties. The building is owned and managed by the Desert Shores Community Association (DSCA). Members in good standing can rent the space for milestone birthdays, baby showers, anniversary dinners, and corporate offsites. ## What is inside the clubhouse? The interior is built around an open banquet space with a maximum capacity of 100 guests. Tables, chairs, and a clean event-ready layout make setup simple. The room flows naturally to outdoor lake terraces, so guests can move between inside conversation and outside views. The commercial kitchen is the standout feature. It includes a commercial refrigerator, freezer, and ovens, which means caterers can actually plate hot food on site instead of trucking it in. That detail alone elevates events well beyond the typical HOA clubhouse. ## How do you rent the community center? Rentals run through the DSCA office, and the space is reserved for members of the association. Pricing, deposits, and availability vary by event type and season.
