Desert Shores has an internal trail network of roughly 8.5 paved miles that connects neighborhoods, lakes, parks, and common areas. These paths are the daily fabric of the community; you can walk from your driveway to a coffee shop, the lake, or a park without crossing many streets. Two named trails inside the community show up on AllTrails. The Desert Shores Loop covers 4.5 miles, and the shorter Desert Shores Community Center Loop covers 2.2 miles. Both are paved and open to the public. ## What is the difference between the loops? The 4.5 mile Desert Shores Loop is the full lap. It is rated moderate, climbs 150 feet of total elevation, and averages 1 hour and 26 minutes for walkers. AllTrails has 204+ reviews on this route. The 2.2 mile Desert Shores Community Center Loop is the shorter option. It is rated easy, climbs only 98 feet, and shows up with 143+ AllTrails reviews. Pick it for a quick after-dinner walk or a lap with kids on bikes. ## What about trails just outside Desert Shores? Pueblo Park is less than 10 minutes away and has a 1.5 mile mixed-surface trail with grass and desert landscaping. The park itself adds a basketball court, exercise stations, and jogging paths. Aloha Shores Park, right outside Desert Shores at 7550 Sauer Street, has a paved walking path looping its 5 acres.
