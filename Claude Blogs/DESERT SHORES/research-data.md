## Official Community Info
- **Master Association Website:** https://www.mydesertshores.com/ and http://desertshoreslasvegas.com/
- **Master HOA:** Desert Shores Community Association (DSCA)
- **Master HOA Address:** 2500 Regatta Drive, Las Vegas, NV 89128
- **Master HOA Phone:** 702-254-1020
- **HOA Fees:** Layered structure. Master Association fee plus separate village/lake association fee. Reported ranges: $85–$125/month per some sources; $150–$350/month per others depending on subdivision (condo/townhome vs. SFR). Conflicting data — verify with HOA directly.
- **Guard Gate:** Partially gated. Some sub-villages (e.g., Mar-a-Lago) are gated with private entries and 24-hour security; the master community itself is open access for amenities only to registered members.
- **Age Restrictions:** None
- **Total Residential Units:** 3,351
- **Total Acreage:** 682 acres
- **Year Founded:** 1988 by RA Homes
- **Number of Residential Districts/Sub-villages:** 22
- **Transitioned to Homeowner Control:** November 1992
- **Membership Required for Amenities:** Yes; all residents 14+ must have digital membership ID. Physical cards no longer issued.
- **Guest Limits:** 10 guests per household at Lagoon Beach Park; 4 fishing guests per household.
- **Key Amenities:** Four man-made lakes, lagoon-style swimming pool (200,000 gallons), sand beach, palm trees, 2,100 sq ft clubhouse with lake terraces (capacity 100), fully-equipped commercial kitchen, picnic park, volleyball, basketball, playground, paddle boats (rental in spring/summer), kayaks, fishing access, community docks, miles of walking/biking paths.
- **Boundaries:** N. Rampart Blvd (west), W. Cheyenne Ave (north), N. Buffalo Dr (east), W. Lake Mead Blvd (south).
- **Zip Code:** Primarily 89128 (some areas also 89134)

## Lakes & Water Features

| Lake Name | Size | Activities Allowed | Rules | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Lake Jacqueline | Largest at ~24.8 acres / 1.1 miles long | Fishing, electric boats, rowboats, paddleboats, sculls, single-hull sailboats, pontoon (patio) boats, kayaks, canoes | Catch and release only; one pole per person; non-barbed hooks; no gas motors; boats min 8 ft length; must be registered with DSCA | Home to Restaurant Row; Marche Bacchus and Americana sit on this lake |
| Lake Sarah | ~12 acres | Same as above | Same | Named after granddaughter Sarah |
| Lake Maddison | ~12 acres | Same | Same | Named after granddaughter Maddison |
| Lake Lindsey | ~12 acres | Same | Same | Named after granddaughter Lindsey |

**Notes on Lakes:**
- All four lakes named after the four oldest granddaughters of community founder "Hal."
- Total water surface: approximately 100 acres
- Lakes are privately owned by DSCA and stocked annually each spring
- **Fish Species Stocked:** Bass, Flathead Minnows, Black Crappie, Gambusia, Bluegill, Goldfish, Channel Catfish, Red Ear Sunfish, White Amur
- **Fishing Hours:** Dawn until dusk daily
- **Boating Hours:** Dawn to 10:30 PM daily
- **Approved Bait:** Red worms, night crawlers, mealworms, terrestrial insects, salmon eggs, marshmallows, catfish stink bait. Live freshwater fish prohibited.
- **Paddleboards:** PROHIBITED per official DSCA "Our Lakes" page.
- **Remote-controlled craft:** Sailboats only allowed; other RC boats prohibited.
- **State fishing license:** Not required for residents with DSCA membership credentials.
- **Lakes are exclusive to DSCA members and their guests** — non-residents cannot kayak/fish/boat without an invited resident.

## Lifestyle & Location

- **Drive to Strip:** 12-20 minutes (~12-14 miles)
- **Drive to Harry Reid International Airport:** 18-25 minutes
- **Drive to Downtown Las Vegas:** 15-22 minutes
- **Walk Score:** 39/100 (ranked 22nd most walkable LV neighborhood)
- **Bike Score:** Low (minimal bike infrastructure)
- **Nearest Hospital:** Centennial Hills Hospital Medical Center, 6900 N Durango Dr, ~5-7 miles north
- **Safety / Crime:**
  - Overall crime rate: 44.12 per 1,000 residents annually (CrimeGrade C-)
  - Violent crime: 4.188 per 1,000 (CrimeGrade C+, ~average for US)
  - North part of Desert Shores considered safest
  - Gated entries and private security in some sub-villages
- **Pet Friendly:** Lakes and walking trails popular with dog walkers; nearest dog park is Desert Breeze in Spring Valley
- **Population:** 7,564
- **Median Age:** 50
- **Median Household Income:** $70,641
- **Average Household Income:** $94,800
- **Cost of Living Index:** 109 (9% above national average)
- **Notable / Unique Features:**
  - Only major Las Vegas neighborhood with four privately owned, residents-only lakes
  - Restaurant Row on Lake Jacqueline is the only true lakefront dining destination in Las Vegas proper
  - Lakes named after founder Hal's four oldest granddaughters
  - 8.5 miles of internal community trails
  - Spring Mountains and Red Rock Canyon visible from many homes
  - Pre-dates Lake Las Vegas as a lakefront option
  - "Best kept secret" reputation
