Eagle Hills is a guard gated luxury community in the Summerlin master plan of Las Vegas, featuring 160 custom built homes on spacious lots ranging from a quarter acre to over half an acre. Built between 1995 and 2000, this exclusive enclave offers privacy, security, and resort style amenities in one of the most desirable locations in the valley. ## What Kind of Homes Are in Eagle Hills? Eagle Hills features custom single story and two story residences in a variety of architectural styles, including contemporary, classic, and Mediterranean designs. Home sizes range from approximately 2,924 square feet to over 14,000 square feet, giving buyers a wide spectrum of options. Every home in the community was custom built, meaning no two properties are exactly alike. Lot sizes span from a quarter acre to nearly a full acre, providing generous outdoor space that is rare in Las Vegas. ## Is Eagle Hills a Gated Community? Yes, Eagle Hills is a fully guard gated community with 24 hour security. A staffed guard gate monitors all entry and exit points around the clock, ensuring residents enjoy a high level of privacy and safety. This level of security is one of the top reasons families and retirees choose Eagle Hills over other Summerlin neighborhoods. ## What Amenities Does Eagle Hills Offer? Residents enjoy a private 3 acre grassy park complete with a walking trail, two tennis courts, a basketball court, a playground, and shaded pavilions. The community also features tree lined streets, lush landscaping, and greenbelt paths that connect different sections of the neighborhood. These shared spaces make Eagle Hills feel like a quiet retreat while still being minutes from shopping, dining, and entertainment.
