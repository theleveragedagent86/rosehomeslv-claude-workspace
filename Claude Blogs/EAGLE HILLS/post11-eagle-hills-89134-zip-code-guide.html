Eagle Hills 89134 Zip Code Guide

Eagle Hills is located in the 89134 zip code, one of the most desirable residential zip codes in all of Las Vegas, situated within the master planned community of Summerlin. This guard gated enclave of 160 custom homes offers premium living in a neighborhood consistently ranked among the top in the valley.

What Area Does the 89134 Zip Code Cover?

The 89134 zip code spans a large portion of western Summerlin, stretching across some of the community's most established and sought after neighborhoods. It borders Red Rock Canyon to the west, offering residents stunning mountain views and quick access to outdoor recreation. Major corridors like Rampart Boulevard, Town Center Drive, and the 215 Beltway provide convenient connections throughout the valley.

Why Is 89134 Considered a Top Zip Code in Las Vegas?

Homes in 89134 benefit from proximity to top rated schools, upscale shopping at Downtown Summerlin and Boca Park Fashion Village, and world class golf at TPC Summerlin. The area is known for lower crime rates, well maintained streetscapes, and strong property values. Eagle Hills specifically stands out within this zip code, with custom homes ranging from $800,000 to over $5,000,000 and sizes from 2,924 to 14,000 square feet on generous lots of up to one acre.

What Is It Like Living in Eagle Hills Within 89134?

Eagle Hills residents enjoy 24 hour guard gated security, a private 3 acre park, two tennis courts, a basketball court, walking trails, and a playground. Trails Village Center is within walking distance, providing everyday conveniences like Albertsons and CVS. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is just 5 to 10 minutes away, and downtown Las Vegas is roughly a 15 minute drive.
