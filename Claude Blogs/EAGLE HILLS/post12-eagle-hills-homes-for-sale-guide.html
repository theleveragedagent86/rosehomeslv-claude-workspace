Eagle Hills Summerlin Homes for Sale Guide

Homes for sale in Eagle Hills typically range from $800,000 to over $5,000,000, making this guard gated Summerlin community one of the premier luxury neighborhoods in Las Vegas. With only 160 total homes, inventory is limited and demand stays consistently high.

What Types of Homes Are for Sale in Eagle Hills?

Eagle Hills features custom single story and two story residences built between 1995 and 2000. Architectural styles include contemporary, classic, and Mediterranean designs. Home sizes range from 2,924 square feet to an impressive 14,000 square feet. Lot sizes vary from one quarter acre to two thirds of an acre, with select properties sitting on up to a full acre. Buyers will find high end finishes, private pools, and expansive outdoor living spaces throughout the community.

What Makes Eagle Hills Different from Other Summerlin Communities?

Eagle Hills stands apart because of its fully guard gated entry with 24 hour security and its intimate size of just 160 homes. The community sits adjacent to TPC Summerlin, home to an annual PGA Tour event. Residents have exclusive access to a private 3 acre park, two tennis courts, a basketball court, walking trails, and a children's playground. Tree lined streets and lush greenbelt paths create a quiet, resort like setting that larger subdivisions cannot replicate.

How Competitive Is the Eagle Hills Real Estate Market?

Because Eagle Hills has a fixed inventory of 160 homes, turnover is low and listings move quickly. Buyers should be prepared with financing and a clear understanding of the HOA structure, which runs approximately $707 per month including the Summerlin master association fee. Proximity to Downtown Summerlin, Boca Park Fashion Village, and Red Rock Canyon adds lasting value. The neighborhood is roughly 17 to 20 minutes from the Las Vegas Strip and 20 minutes from Harry Reid International Airport.
