Eagle Hills Amenities and Community Features

Eagle Hills offers residents a private 3 acre park, two tennis courts, a basketball court, walking trails, a playground, and shaded pavilions, all exclusively for its 160 homeowners. These amenities create a resort quality lifestyle within a fully guard gated Summerlin neighborhood.

What Amenities Are Inside Eagle Hills?

The centerpiece of Eagle Hills is its expansive 3 acre grassy park, one of the largest private community parks in Summerlin. Two tennis courts and a full basketball court provide year round recreation. Walking trails and greenbelt paths wind through the neighborhood beneath mature tree lined streets. A children's playground and covered pavilions give families dedicated gathering spaces. Lush landscaping throughout the community maintains a green, manicured environment even in the desert climate.

What Community Features Set Eagle Hills Apart?

Eagle Hills is fully guard gated with 24 hour security, ensuring privacy and controlled access at all times. The community contains only 160 custom built homes on generous lots ranging from one quarter acre to one full acre. This low density design means wider streets, less traffic, and a quieter atmosphere than most Summerlin neighborhoods. The community sits directly adjacent to TPC Summerlin, giving golf enthusiasts a world class PGA Tour venue right next door.

What Nearby Amenities Complement Eagle Hills Living?

Beyond the gates, residents are within walking distance of Trails Village Center, which houses over 40 businesses including Albertsons and CVS. Boca Park Fashion Village is 2 to 3 miles away with Target, Kohl's, and REI. Downtown Summerlin, featuring 125 plus shops and restaurants, is just 3.5 miles from the community. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is a 5 to 10 minute drive, and Red Rock Canyon is only 10 to 15 minutes west.

Ryan Rose's Local Perspective

Ryan Rose highlights Eagle Hills' private park as one of the most underappreciated features in Summerlin's luxury market. Many guard gated communities in this price range lack dedicated green space of this size. He notes that families and active residents consistently rank the tennis courts, walking trails, and playground among the top reasons they chose this neighborhood over competing communities.
