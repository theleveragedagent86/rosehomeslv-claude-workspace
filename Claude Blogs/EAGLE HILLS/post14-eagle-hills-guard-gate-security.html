Eagle Hills Guard Gate and Security

Eagle Hills is a fully guard gated community with 24 hour security staffing, providing residents with round the clock controlled access and one of the most secure living environments in Summerlin. Only authorized residents and their approved guests can enter the neighborhood.

How Does the Eagle Hills Guard Gate Work?

The Eagle Hills guard gate is staffed 24 hours a day, seven days a week. Security personnel verify every vehicle entering the community. Residents use designated access credentials while guests must be pre approved or confirmed at the gate before entry. This system ensures that only authorized individuals pass through, eliminating unwanted solicitation and maintaining a private, peaceful environment for all 160 homeowners.

What Security Features Does Eagle Hills Offer?

Beyond the staffed guard gate, Eagle Hills benefits from its small community size and single point of entry design. With only 160 homes, unfamiliar vehicles and foot traffic are immediately noticeable. The community's tree lined streets and well lit pathways also contribute to a safe atmosphere. Eagle Hills consistently ranks among the safest neighborhoods in the 89134 zip code. Perimeter walls and controlled landscaping further reinforce the boundary between the community and surrounding areas.

Why Does Guard Gated Security Matter for Home Values?

Guard gated communities in Las Vegas consistently hold stronger property values than non gated neighborhoods. Buyers pay a premium for the privacy, safety, and exclusivity that 24 hour security provides. In Eagle Hills, homes range from $800,000 to over $5,000,000, and the guard gate is a key factor supporting those prices. The HOA fee of approximately $707 per month covers security staffing along with maintenance of the community's private park, tennis courts, and common areas.
