How Far Is Eagle Hills from the Las Vegas Strip?

Eagle Hills is approximately 12 miles from the Las Vegas Strip, which translates to a 17 to 20 minute drive under normal traffic conditions. This distance gives residents easy access to world class dining, entertainment, and nightlife while living in a quiet, guard gated Summerlin neighborhood.

What Is the Best Route from Eagle Hills to the Strip?

The most common route takes residents east on the 215 Beltway to Interstate 15 south, which leads directly to the heart of the Strip. During off peak hours, the drive can be as quick as 17 minutes. Rush hour traffic, particularly on I 15 between 4 and 6 PM, may extend the commute to 25 minutes or slightly more. Weekend evenings near the Strip also tend to see heavier congestion around major resort exits.

How Far Is Eagle Hills from Other Key Las Vegas Destinations?

Eagle Hills is well positioned for access across the valley. Downtown Las Vegas is roughly 15 minutes away. Harry Reid International Airport is approximately 20 minutes and 15.6 miles from the community. Red Rock Canyon, a popular outdoor destination, is just 10 to 15 minutes west. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center sits within a 5 to 10 minute drive. For everyday needs, Trails Village Center with over 40 businesses is within walking distance, and Downtown Summerlin's 125 plus shops are only 3.5 miles away.

Does Eagle Hills Offer a Good Balance of Access and Privacy?

Eagle Hills strikes an ideal balance between convenience and seclusion. The community's location in western Summerlin places it far enough from the Strip to avoid the noise and traffic of the tourist corridor. Yet the 215 Beltway connection keeps commute times short to virtually every major destination in the valley. Residents enjoy 24 hour guard gated security, custom homes on lots up to one acre, and a private 3 acre park, all within quick reach of everything Las Vegas has to offer.
