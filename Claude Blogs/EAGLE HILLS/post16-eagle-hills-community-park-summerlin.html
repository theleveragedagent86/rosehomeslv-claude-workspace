Eagle Hills Community Park is a private 3 acre green space at the center of the Eagle Hills neighborhood in Summerlin, featuring a walking trail, playground, two tennis courts, a basketball court, and shaded pavilions. Residents of this guard gated community enjoy exclusive access to one of the most well maintained neighborhood parks in the 89134 zip code. ## What Amenities Does Eagle Hills Community Park Have? Eagle Hills Community Park spans 3 acres of lush, irrigated grass and serves as the centerpiece of the community's greenbelt system. The park includes two full tennis courts, a regulation basketball court, a children's playground, and multiple covered pavilions with seating. A paved walking trail winds through the park and connects to the broader greenbelt paths that link different sections of Eagle Hills. The landscaping is professionally maintained year round, giving the park a resort like appearance that matches the luxury character of the neighborhood. ## Is Eagle Hills Community Park Open to the Public? No, Eagle Hills Community Park is a private amenity reserved exclusively for Eagle Hills residents and their guests. Because Eagle Hills is a 24 hour guard gated community, only residents with gate access can use the park. This private access means the park is never overcrowded, and families can enjoy the space in a safe, quiet setting any day of the week. ## What Makes Eagle Hills Park Different From Other Summerlin Parks? Unlike larger public parks in Summerlin, Eagle Hills Community Park offers a more intimate experience with far fewer visitors.
