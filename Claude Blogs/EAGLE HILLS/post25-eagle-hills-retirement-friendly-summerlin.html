Is Eagle Hills a Good Community for Retirees?

Eagle Hills is not a 55 plus community, but its guard gated privacy, single story home options, flat walking trails, and proximity to Summerlin Hospital make it one of the most retirement friendly neighborhoods in Summerlin. Retirees who want upscale living without age restricted limitations find Eagle Hills offers the quiet, secure lifestyle they are looking for.

What Makes Eagle Hills Appealing for Retirement?

The community's 160 custom homes sit on generous lots ranging from one third acre to two thirds acre. Single story floor plans start at approximately 3,500 square feet, providing spacious one level living that eliminates stair concerns. The guard gate staffed entrance adds a layer of daily security, and the low traffic, tree lined streets create a peaceful atmosphere for walking and outdoor relaxation.

The internal greenbelt walking trail runs through a 3 acre park at the center of the community. It offers a flat, shaded, half mile route ideal for daily exercise. Tennis courts, a basketball court, and pavilions provide additional recreation without requiring a drive. For retirees focused on staying active, these amenities are steps from the front door.

How Close Is Medical Care?

Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is approximately 5 to 10 minutes from Eagle Hills. This full service hospital provides emergency care, surgical services, and specialty medicine. The short drive to medical services gives retirees and their families added peace of mind. Several medical office complexes along the Summerlin Parkway corridor also offer primary care, dental, and specialty practices within a few minutes of the community.

What Else Is Nearby for Retirees?

TPC Summerlin golf course is within walking distance for golf enthusiasts. The Summerlin Library is also nearby for those who enjoy reading and community programming. Red Rock Canyon is a 10 to 15 minute drive for light hiking and scenic drives. Shopping, dining, and entertainment at Downtown Summerlin and The Trails Village center are all within a short drive.
