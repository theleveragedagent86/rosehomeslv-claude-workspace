What Is William Lummis Elementary School?

William Lummis Elementary School is the zoned public elementary school for Eagle Hills in Summerlin, serving grades pre K through 5 with a GreatSchools rating of 9 out of 10. Families in this guard gated community benefit from one of the highest rated elementary schools in the Las Vegas Valley without a long commute.

How Does Lummis Elementary Rank Academically?

Lummis Elementary holds a Niche grade of B+ and ranks number 72 among the best public elementary schools in Nevada. Student proficiency scores tell the story: 63.1% of students meet or exceed math proficiency standards and 67.6% meet or exceed reading proficiency standards. Both figures sit well above Nevada state averages, making Lummis a standout campus in the Clark County School District.

The school offers instruction from pre kindergarten through fifth grade. Its location within the Eagle Hills zoning area means families living in the community are assigned here automatically, eliminating the need for magnet or variance applications. Parents looking for a strong academic foundation will find Lummis delivers consistent results year after year.

What Grade Levels and Programs Does Lummis Offer?

Lummis serves students in pre K through grade 5, providing a full elementary experience in one campus. The school operates within the Clark County School District and follows Nevada Academic Content Standards. Class sizes and teacher quality contribute to the strong proficiency numbers, giving younger students a solid start before they transition to Ernest Becker Middle School for grades 6 through 8.
