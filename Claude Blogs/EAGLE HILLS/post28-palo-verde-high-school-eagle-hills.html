What Is Palo Verde High School?

Palo Verde High School is the zoned public high school for Eagle Hills in Summerlin, serving grades 9 through 12 with a GreatSchools rating of 8 out of 10, a 96% graduation rate, and an average SAT score of 1240. It ranks as the number 19 best high school in Nevada according to U.S. News, making it one of the strongest public high schools available to Summerlin families.

How Does Palo Verde Rank Academically?

Palo Verde earns a Niche grade of B+ and consistently places among the top public high schools in the state. Student proficiency data shows 35% meeting math standards and 61% meeting reading standards. The 96% graduation rate is significantly higher than the state average, and the average SAT score of 1240 reflects strong college readiness among the student body.

These numbers position Palo Verde well above most Clark County high schools. For Eagle Hills families, this means students who progress through Lummis Elementary and Becker Middle School arrive at a high school that delivers strong academic outcomes and college preparation.

What Programs and Athletics Does Palo Verde Offer?

Palo Verde provides a full range of Advanced Placement courses, honors programs, and career and technical education pathways. The school's athletics program competes at the 5A level, fielding competitive teams across multiple sports. Extracurricular clubs, performing arts, and student organizations round out a well balanced high school experience that prepares students for university admissions and career readiness.
