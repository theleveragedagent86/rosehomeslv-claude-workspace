What Is Discovery Charter School Hillpointe Campus?

Discovery Charter School at the Hillpointe Campus is a public charter school serving grades K through 8, located at 8941 Hillpointe Road adjacent to Eagle Hills in Summerlin. It holds a Niche grade of B+ and a SchoolDigger rating of 4 out of 5 stars, placing it in the top 30% of all Nevada schools and giving Eagle Hills families a strong alternative to traditional public school zoning.

How Does Discovery Charter Perform Academically?

Discovery Charter Hillpointe reports 41% math proficiency and 55% reading proficiency among its students. These figures reflect solid performance for a charter campus, particularly in reading. The school's SchoolDigger ranking in the top 30% statewide confirms its standing as a competitive option within the Las Vegas Valley. Charter schools operate with more flexibility in curriculum and teaching methods, which appeals to parents seeking a different educational approach.

Because Discovery Charter serves both elementary and middle school grades on one campus, families can keep students in a single school environment from kindergarten through eighth grade. This continuity eliminates the transition between separate elementary and middle school campuses that the traditional public school path requires.

Where Is the Hillpointe Campus Located?

The Hillpointe Campus sits at 8941 Hillpointe Road, directly adjacent to Eagle Hills. The proximity makes daily drop off and pick up convenient for families living in the community. The campus shares the Hillpointe Road corridor with Adelson Educational Campus, giving this stretch of road two distinct school options within walking or short driving distance of Eagle Hills homes.
