What Is Adelson Educational Campus?

Adelson Educational Campus is a private Jewish school serving pre K through 12th grade, located at 9700 Hillpointe Road near Eagle Hills in Summerlin. It holds a Niche grade of A+ and ranks as the number 3 best private high school in Nevada, with a 100% graduation rate and 100% college attendance rate. For Eagle Hills families seeking elite private education, Adelson is one of the top options in the entire Las Vegas Valley.

How Does Adelson Rank Academically?

Adelson's numbers speak for themselves. The school reports a 100% graduation rate, a 100% college attendance rate, and an average SAT score of 1280. The student to teacher ratio of 6 to 1 ensures personalized attention that larger public schools cannot match. Niche ranks Adelson as the third best private high school in all of Nevada, reflecting consistently strong academic outcomes across every grade level.

The campus serves students from pre kindergarten through 12th grade on a single site. This means families can enroll children at age four and keep them on the same campus through high school graduation. The continuity of environment, relationships, and academic standards creates a cohesive educational experience that few schools in Las Vegas can offer.
