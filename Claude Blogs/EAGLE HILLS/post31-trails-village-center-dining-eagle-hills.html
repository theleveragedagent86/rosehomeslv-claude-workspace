What Restaurants Can You Find at Trails Village Center?

The dining lineup at Trails Village Center covers a wide range of cuisines. The Bar at Trails Village is a 24 hour American restaurant and lounge with over 174 Yelp reviews, known for its pub fare and shaded outdoor patio. Sultan's Wagyu Grill serves Mediterranean dishes, while Einstein's Bagels handles the morning crowd with breakfast sandwiches and coffee. Rachel's Kitchen provides a cafe style menu with healthy options and local favorites.

Beyond the center itself, several acclaimed restaurants sit just minutes away. Honey Salt on Rampart Boulevard delivers farm to table dining from chef Kim Canteenwalla and restaurateur Elizabeth Blau. Nittaya's Secret Kitchen on West Lake Mead Boulevard offers award winning Thai cuisine with over 2,000 Yelp reviews. Egg Works and Lola's A Louisiana Kitchen round out the nearby breakfast and Southern food options.
