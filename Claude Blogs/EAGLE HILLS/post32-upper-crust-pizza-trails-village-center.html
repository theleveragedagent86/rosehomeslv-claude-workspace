What Was Upper Crust Pizza at Trails Village Center?

Upper Crust Pizza was a beloved neighborhood pizzeria at 1910 Village Center Circle in Trails Village Center, just steps from Eagle Hills in Summerlin. Established in 2006, the restaurant served authentic East Coast pizza using dough shipped directly from Boston's North End. The restaurant has since permanently closed, but its legacy as a local favorite speaks to the strong dining culture that surrounds Eagle Hills.

What Made Upper Crust Pizza Stand Out?

Upper Crust Pizza built a loyal following with its Boston style crust, which offered a rich, chewy consistency that set it apart from typical New York style slices found across Las Vegas. The kitchen used premium ingredients including Polly O Ricotta, Boar's Head cold cuts, and Dole pineapple. Beyond pizza, the menu featured homemade lasagna, arancini, and ravioli that gave the spot an Italian deli feel rare in the suburbs.

With 62 Yelp reviews and a strong neighborhood reputation, Upper Crust Pizza was exactly the kind of independent restaurant that makes walkable communities like Trails Village Center attractive to homebuyers. Its closure left a gap, but the center continues to evolve with new tenants filling retail and dining spaces.

What Dining Options Remain at Trails Village Center?

Eagle Hills residents still have plenty of choices within walking distance. The Bar at Trails Village operates 24 hours with pub fare and outdoor seating. Sultan's Wagyu Grill serves Mediterranean cuisine, Einstein's Bagels covers breakfast, and Rachel's Kitchen offers a cafe menu with health conscious options. The Trails Village Center dining scene remains one of the strongest neighborhood food hubs in Summerlin.
