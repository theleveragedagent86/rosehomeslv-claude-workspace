What Is Honey Salt Restaurant Near Eagle Hills?

Honey Salt is a farm to table restaurant at 1031 South Rampart Boulevard in Las Vegas, located just minutes from Eagle Hills in Summerlin. Opened in 2012 by restaurateur Elizabeth Blau and award winning chef Kim Canteenwalla, Honey Salt has earned over 3,100 Yelp reviews and become one of the most popular neighborhood restaurants in the valley. It is the closest upscale dining option for Eagle Hills residents who want a quality meal without heading to the Strip.

What Does Honey Salt Serve?

Honey Salt builds its chef driven menus around seasonal ingredients sourced from small farms. The core menu features comfort food staples alongside lighter wellness focused dishes, with creative specials that rotate throughout the year. Weekend brunch draws consistent crowds, and weekday specials include Tuesday Pot Pie Nights with turkey and lobster options, Wine Wednesdays, and a daily happy hour from 3:30 to 6:00 PM.

The restaurant offers indoor dining, bar seating, and a popular outdoor patio. Lunch runs Monday through Friday, brunch is served Saturday and Sunday morning, and dinner service continues nightly. Reservations are available through OpenTable, and the kitchen also supports takeout orders for nearby residents.

Why Do Summerlin Residents Choose Honey Salt?

Honey Salt fills a niche that few off Strip restaurants manage to sustain. The farm to table concept, combined with a warm neighborhood atmosphere, keeps regulars coming back weekly. Monthly Farm Table Dinners create a communal event that connects the Summerlin dining community around seasonal themes. For Eagle Hills families, Honey Salt is roughly a five minute drive down Rampart Boulevard, making it a practical option for date nights, weekend brunch, or a weekday happy hour after work.
