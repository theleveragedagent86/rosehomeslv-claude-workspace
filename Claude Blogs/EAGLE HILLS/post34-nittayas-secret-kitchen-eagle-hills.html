What Is Nittaya's Secret Kitchen Near Eagle Hills?

Nittaya's Secret Kitchen is an award winning Thai restaurant at 8427 West Lake Mead Boulevard in Las Vegas, roughly ten minutes from Eagle Hills in Summerlin. Established in 2010, the restaurant has earned over 2,000 Yelp reviews and has been recognized among the top 100 restaurants in the world. Chef Nittaya personally prepares each dish, bringing decades of experience from her family's restaurant background in Thailand.

What Makes Nittaya's Secret Kitchen Special?

Nittaya's serves Thai cuisine tapas style, using fresh seasonal produce and carefully selected regional ingredients. The menu features signature dishes including the world famous Spinach Salad, Crying Tiger Steak, Kao Soi, Drunken Noodles, and Cashew Shrimp. Each plate arrives with the kind of presentation typically reserved for fine dining, but in a warm and welcoming setting that feels personal rather than pretentious.

The restaurant also maintains an extensive wine list, which pairs well with the bold flavors of the Thai menu. Happy hour runs Monday through Thursday from 3:00 to 6:00 PM, and Thursday nights feature half priced bottles. Reservations are available through OpenTable, and the upscale decor makes it a strong choice for date nights and special occasions.
