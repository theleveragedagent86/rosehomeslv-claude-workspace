What Is The Bar at Trails Village Center?

The Bar at Trails Village is a 24 hour American restaurant and lounge at 1916 Village Center Circle in Summerlin, located within walking distance of Eagle Hills. With over 174 Yelp reviews and a reputation as a genuine neighborhood hangout, The Bar combines pub style food with a relaxed atmosphere that draws Eagle Hills residents for everything from late night bites to weekend breakfast.

What Does The Bar at Trails Village Serve?

The menu covers a wide range of American favorites including burgers, wings, sandwiches, tacos, and breakfast plates. Signature items include the Carne Asada Tacos along with shrimp and fish taco options. The kitchen operates around the clock, which means Eagle Hills residents have a reliable dining option at any hour. Happy hour specials and a full bar with craft cocktails round out the drink side of the menu.

The restaurant features a spacious, well shaded outdoor patio that has become one of the most popular gathering spots in the Trails Village Center complex. Indoor seating offers a darker, lounge style setting with multiple televisions for sports viewing. Delivery and takeout are also available through major platforms for nights when staying home wins out.
