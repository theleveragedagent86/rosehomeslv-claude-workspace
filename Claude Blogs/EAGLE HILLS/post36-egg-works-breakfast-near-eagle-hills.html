What Is Egg Works Near Eagle Hills?

Egg Works is a beloved Las Vegas breakfast and brunch restaurant with a Summerlin location at 2025 Village Center Circle, just minutes from Eagle Hills in the Trails Village Center. Established in 1988 as The Egg & I and expanded under the Egg Works name in 2005, this family owned restaurant has become one of the most popular breakfast spots in the Las Vegas Valley.

What Makes Egg Works a Summerlin Breakfast Favorite?

Egg Works opens daily at 6:00 AM and serves until 2:00 PM, making it a go to morning destination for Eagle Hills residents. The extensive eight page menu covers everything from classic eggs and omelets to signature sandwiches and homemade soups. Their famous Cincy Chili, served five ways, is a standout item that keeps regulars coming back. Brunch specialties pair well with their Habla Diablo Bloody Marys and fresh mimosas.

With over 1,200 reviews on Yelp and a 4.5 star rating, Egg Works earns consistent praise for generous portions, friendly service, and reasonable prices. Customer favorites include the Eggs Benedict, the smoky chipotle hash with green chili and shredded pork, and the banana muffin. The restaurant also features a full children's menu, making it ideal for families. A pet friendly outdoor patio lets residents bring their dogs along for a weekend breakfast outing.

How Close Is Egg Works to Eagle Hills?

The Trails Village Center location sits within walking distance of Eagle Hills, making Egg Works one of the most convenient dining options for the neighborhood. Residents can reach the restaurant in under five minutes by car without touching a major highway. This proximity to walkable dining and shopping is one of the lifestyle perks that makes Eagle Hills a desirable Summerlin address.
