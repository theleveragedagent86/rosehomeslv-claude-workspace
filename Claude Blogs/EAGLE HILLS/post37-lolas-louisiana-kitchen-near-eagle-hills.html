What Is Lola's A Louisiana Kitchen?

Lola's A Louisiana Kitchen is an authentic Cajun and Creole restaurant located at 1220 N Town Center Drive in Summerlin, bringing genuine New Orleans flavors to Eagle Hills residents. Founded by Lola Elizabeth Pokorny, who was born in New Orleans and raised in Louisiana, the restaurant draws from a family legacy of cooks, chefs, and restaurateurs to deliver some of the best Southern cuisine in the Las Vegas Valley.

What Does Lola's Serve?

The menu at Lola's reads like a trip through the French Quarter. Louisiana style crab cakes made with Blue Crab and proprietary seasonings anchor the appetizer selection. Charbroiled oysters on the half shell arrive flame flavored with house made marinades and topped with Pecorino Romano cheese. Entrees include crawfish, seafood gumbo, shrimp and grits, and the roast beef po'boy with gravy that loyal customers swear by.

Lola's offers Happy Hour Wednesday through Friday from 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM and Saturdays from noon to 4:00 PM. The Summerlin location has been serving the community since 2014 on Town Center Drive, just north of Summerlin Parkway. The restaurant also offers takeout for residents who want to enjoy Louisiana cooking at home.

How Far Is Lola's From Eagle Hills?

Lola's Summerlin location on Town Center Drive is a short drive from Eagle Hills, easily accessible via Summerlin Parkway or local surface streets.
