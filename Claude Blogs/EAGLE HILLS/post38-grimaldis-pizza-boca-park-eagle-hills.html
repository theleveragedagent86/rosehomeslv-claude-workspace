What Is Grimaldi's Pizzeria at Boca Park?

Grimaldi's Pizzeria is a coal fired brick oven pizza restaurant located at 750 South Rampart Boulevard in Boca Park Fashion Village, just a short drive from Eagle Hills in Summerlin. Known for hand tossed dough made fresh daily and baked in signature coal fired ovens, Grimaldi's delivers a crisp yet chewy crust with a distinctive smoky char that sets it apart from every other pizza spot in the area.

What Makes Grimaldi's Pizza Stand Out?

The coal fired brick oven is the centerpiece of the Grimaldi's experience. Each pizza starts with dough made fresh that day, hand tossed and topped with premium ingredients before hitting the intense heat of the coal oven. The result is a thin crust pizza with a perfect balance of crunch and chew that wood fired ovens simply cannot replicate. This technique originated at the original Grimaldi's under the Brooklyn Bridge in New York.

Beyond pizza, the menu features fresh salads, classic Italian appetizers, and a selection of desserts. Grimaldi's also offers handcrafted cocktails and a curated wine list that pairs well with the coal fired flavors. The Boca Park location is open Monday through Thursday from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM, Friday and Saturday until 11:00 PM, and Sunday from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM. With over 850 Yelp reviews, the restaurant maintains strong ratings from both locals and visitors.
