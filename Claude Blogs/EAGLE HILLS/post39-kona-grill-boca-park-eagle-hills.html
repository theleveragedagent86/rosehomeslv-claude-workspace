What Is Kona Grill at Boca Park?

Kona Grill is an upscale casual restaurant at 750 S Rampart Boulevard in Boca Park Fashion Village, offering Eagle Hills residents a blend of contemporary American cuisine and award winning sushi just minutes from home. The restaurant combines a scratch kitchen with a modern bar program, creating a versatile dining destination for everything from weeknight dinners to weekend celebrations.

What Does Kona Grill Serve?

The menu at Kona Grill spans a global selection of American grill favorites, fresh sushi, and specialty craft cocktails. Steak and seafood anchor the entree lineup, while the sushi bar turns out signature rolls like the Picasso Roll and Rainbow Roll. Salads, sandwiches, and homemade desserts round out the options. The restaurant also features seasonal menu items that rotate throughout the year to keep the experience fresh for regular diners.

Kona Grill serves brunch, lunch, dinner, and happy hour across an expansive weekly schedule. Hours run Monday through Thursday from 11:00 AM to midnight, Friday and Saturday from 11:00 AM to 1:00 AM, and Sunday from 10:00 AM to 11:00 PM. With nearly 2,900 Yelp reviews at the Boca Park location, the restaurant has built a loyal following among Summerlin residents who appreciate quality without the drive to the Strip.
