What Are the Best Restaurants Near Eagle Hills?

Eagle Hills in Summerlin sits within minutes of dozens of quality restaurants spanning breakfast, lunch, dinner, and late night dining across three nearby shopping centers. From walkable spots at Trails Village Center to upscale options at Boca Park and Downtown Summerlin, Eagle Hills residents enjoy one of the strongest restaurant networks in the 89134 zip code.

Which Restaurants Are Closest to Eagle Hills?

Trails Village Center is the closest dining hub, sitting within walking distance of Eagle Hills. Egg Works opens at 6:00 AM daily for breakfast and brunch, serving a massive eight page menu that has earned over 1,200 Yelp reviews. Honey Salt delivers farm to table American cuisine with an upscale local feel. Nittaya's Secret Kitchen brings authentic Thai food that consistently ranks among the best in the valley. Upper Crust Pizza rounds out the center with a neighborhood pizza option that locals prefer over national chains.

Just north of Summerlin Parkway on Town Center Drive, Lola's A Louisiana Kitchen serves genuine Cajun and Creole dishes including crab cakes, gumbo, po'boys, and charbroiled oysters. The restaurant has been a Summerlin staple since 2014 and fills a niche that no other local restaurant covers.

What About Boca Park and Downtown Summerlin Dining?

Boca Park Fashion Village, two to three miles south on Rampart Boulevard, adds another layer of dining variety. Grimaldi's Pizzeria bakes coal fired brick oven pizza with fresh daily dough. Kona Grill pairs contemporary American entrees with award winning sushi and stays open until midnight or later most nights. The Cheesecake Factory and Hussong's Cantina provide additional options for larger groups and varied tastes.

Downtown Summerlin, roughly 3.5 miles away, offers Maggiano's Little Italy for upscale Italian dining and Lazy Dog Restaurant for casual American comfort food. With 125 plus shops in the same complex, Downtown Summerlin turns dinner into a full evening out.
