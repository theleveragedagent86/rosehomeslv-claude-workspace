What Is Boca Park Fashion Village?

Boca Park Fashion Village is a major retail and dining destination located approximately 2 to 3 miles from Eagle Hills at 750 South Rampart Boulevard. This open air shopping center features a mix of national retailers, home furnishing stores, and popular restaurants that give Eagle Hills residents access to mid range and upscale shopping without leaving the Summerlin area.

What Stores Are at Boca Park Fashion Village?

The center anchors around Target and Kohl's for general merchandise, with REI providing outdoor and sporting goods perfectly suited to the active Summerlin lifestyle. Home design shoppers will find Pottery Barn and Pottery Barn Kids for furniture and decor, while Ross offers discount retail options. Office Max rounds out the business and office supply needs for homeowners and remote workers.

Dining options at Boca Park add significant value to the shopping experience. Grimaldi's serves coal fired pizza, Cheesecake Factory offers an extensive menu for family outings, and Kona Grill provides upscale casual dining with a Pacific Rim menu. Eagle Hills residents can combine shopping and dining into a single convenient trip.

How Far Is Boca Park from Eagle Hills?

Boca Park sits just 2 to 3 miles south of Eagle Hills along Rampart Boulevard, making it a quick 5 to 7 minute drive. This proximity means Eagle Hills homeowners have a full power retail center within easy reach for everything from weekly Target runs to REI gear pickups before a Red Rock Canyon hike. The short distance also means the center is accessible without dealing with freeway traffic.
