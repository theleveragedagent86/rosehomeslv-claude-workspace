What Is Downtown Summerlin?

Downtown Summerlin is the premier open air shopping, dining, and entertainment destination in the Summerlin master plan, featuring over 125 stores and located approximately 3.5 miles from Eagle Hills. This walkable retail district combines major department stores, specialty boutiques, a movie theater, professional sports venues, and seasonal events into a single destination that serves as the social and commercial hub of western Las Vegas.

What Stores and Shops Are at Downtown Summerlin?

The center is anchored by Macy's and Dillard's, two full line department stores that provide extensive apparel, home goods, and beauty selections. Apple's retail store serves as a tech hub for the community, while Lululemon caters to the active lifestyle that defines Summerlin living. Beyond these anchors, over 125 shops cover fashion, accessories, home decor, beauty, and specialty retail across a beautifully landscaped outdoor setting.

Entertainment options elevate Downtown Summerlin beyond typical retail. Regal Cinemas provides first run movies, the Las Vegas Ballpark hosts Triple A baseball, and City National Arena is the practice facility for the Vegas Golden Knights. Seasonal events include holiday markets, outdoor concerts, and community gatherings that draw visitors from across the valley.

How Far Is Downtown Summerlin from Eagle Hills?

Eagle Hills residents reach Downtown Summerlin in roughly 3.5 miles, typically a 10 minute drive along Summerlin Parkway or local routes. That proximity means access to major retail, professional sports, and entertainment without the 30 to 45 minute drives that many Las Vegas residents face when heading to comparable destinations on the Strip or in Henderson.
