What Is the Las Vegas Ballpark?

The Las Vegas Ballpark is a professional baseball stadium located at Downtown Summerlin, serving as the home field for the Las Vegas Aviators, the Triple A affiliate of the Oakland Athletics. Situated approximately 3.5 miles from Eagle Hills, this modern venue gives residents access to professional baseball, community events, and entertainment throughout the season without a long drive across the valley.

What Can You Experience at Las Vegas Ballpark?

The Aviators play a full minor league season from spring through early fall, offering Eagle Hills families affordable professional baseball in a state of the art facility. The ballpark features premium seating options, group areas, and a family friendly atmosphere that draws fans from across Summerlin and the greater Las Vegas area. Promotional nights, fireworks shows, and themed events keep the schedule fresh throughout the season.

Beyond baseball, the venue hosts concerts, community gatherings, and special events that expand its role as an entertainment anchor for Downtown Summerlin. Combined with adjacent attractions like City National Arena and over 125 retail shops, the Ballpark sits at the center of Summerlin's most dynamic entertainment district.

How Far Is Las Vegas Ballpark from Eagle Hills?

Eagle Hills residents reach the Ballpark in roughly 10 minutes via a 3.5 mile drive along local Summerlin roads. There is no freeway required, which means game night traffic stays manageable. That short distance makes it easy to catch weeknight games after work or bring the family for a Saturday afternoon outing without the planning and travel time that Strip venues demand.
