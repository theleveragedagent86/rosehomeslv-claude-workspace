How Close Is City National Arena to Eagle Hills?

City National Arena, the official practice facility of the Vegas Golden Knights, sits just 3.5 miles from Eagle Hills in Downtown Summerlin. Residents can reach this 146,000 square foot hockey complex in under 10 minutes, making it one of the most convenient pro sports venues for any Summerlin neighborhood.

What Can You Do at City National Arena?

City National Arena is far more than a practice rink. The facility features two regulation NHL sized ice sheets open to both professional athletes and the public. Visitors can watch Golden Knights practices for free, shop at the official team store, and grab a meal at the on site restaurant and bar. Public skating sessions, youth hockey leagues, and figure skating programs run throughout the year, giving Eagle Hills families year round entertainment just minutes from home.

The arena opened in September 2017 and quickly became a community hub within Downtown Summerlin. It sits alongside the Las Vegas Ballpark, Regal Cinemas, and dozens of shops and restaurants, creating an entire entertainment district that Eagle Hills residents can access without fighting Strip traffic.
