How Far Is Summerlin Hospital from Eagle Hills?

Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is approximately 5 to 10 minutes from Eagle Hills, located at 657 N. Town Center Drive in Las Vegas, NV 89144. This proximity to a full service hospital is one of the most practical advantages of living in this guard gated Summerlin community.

What Services Does Summerlin Hospital Offer?

Summerlin Hospital is a 485 bed, Joint Commission accredited facility offering more than 30 specialty areas. Key service centers include The Heart Center, The Robotic Surgery Center, The Birthplace maternity center, The Children's Medical Center, The Cancer Center, The Breast Care Center, and The Rehab Center. The hospital also operates a 24/7 emergency department, ensuring Eagle Hills residents always have rapid access to critical care.

For families, the hospital provides a Level III neonatal intensive care unit and comprehensive pediatric services. Outpatient programs cover cardiac rehabilitation, physical and occupational therapy for adults and children, wound healing with hyperbaric oxygen therapy, and speech therapy. The hospital holds accreditation as both a Chest Pain Center and a Primary Stroke Center for rapid diagnosis and treatment.
