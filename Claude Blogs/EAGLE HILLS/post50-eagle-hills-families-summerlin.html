Is Eagle Hills a Good Neighborhood for Families?

Eagle Hills is one of the best family neighborhoods in Summerlin, offering guard gated security, top rated schools, a private community park, and quick access to healthcare and recreation. Families who prioritize safety, education, and outdoor space consistently rank this 160 home community among their top choices in the 89134 zip code.

What Schools Serve Eagle Hills Families?

Eagle Hills is zoned for William Lummis Elementary School, rated 9 out of 10 on GreatSchools, and Palo Verde High School, rated 8 out of 10 with a 96 percent graduation rate. Families seeking private education have access to Adelson Educational Campus, a Niche A+ rated school serving pre K through 12th grade with a 100 percent graduation rate. This combination of high performing public and private options means Eagle Hills parents rarely need to look far for quality education.

What Family Amenities Does Eagle Hills Offer?

Inside the gates, families enjoy a private 3 acre grassy park, a dedicated playground, two tennis courts, a basketball court, and shaded pavilions for gatherings. The internal greenbelt walking trail gives kids and parents a safe place to walk, jog, or ride bikes without leaving the community. Nearby options include Bruce Trent Park and Willows Park for additional playground space, sports fields, and dog friendly areas. Eagle Hills families also benefit from Red Rock Canyon being just 10 to 15 minutes away for weekend hikes and outdoor exploration.

How Does Security Benefit Families in Eagle Hills?

The 24 hour guard gate and security patrols create an environment where children can play outside with greater freedom. Parents consistently cite the controlled access and low traffic streets as major reasons they chose Eagle Hills over larger, non gated Summerlin subdivisions. Ryan Rose works with many young families who specifically seek out Eagle Hills because it delivers the rare combination of spacious custom homes, top schools, and the peace of mind that comes with round the clock security.

What Healthcare Is Available for Eagle Hills Families?

Summerlin Hospital Medical Center sits just 5 to 10 minutes from Eagle Hills, offering a Level III NICU, a full Children's Medical Center, pediatric therapy services, and a 24/7 emergency department. Having a 485 bed hospital with specialized pediatric care this close removes a significant concern for families with young children.
