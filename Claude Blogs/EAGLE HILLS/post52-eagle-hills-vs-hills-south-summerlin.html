How Does Eagle Hills Fit Within The Hills South in Summerlin?

Eagle Hills is one of the most prestigious guard gated communities located inside The Hills South, a master planned section of Summerlin that contains several luxury enclaves including Canyon Fairways and other custom home neighborhoods. Understanding how Eagle Hills relates to The Hills South helps buyers see exactly what sets this 160 home community apart from the broader village.

What Is The Hills South and What Does It Include?

The Hills South is a master section within Summerlin that spans the western edge of the community near Red Rock Canyon. It contains multiple neighborhoods ranging from semi custom production homes to fully guard gated luxury enclaves. Eagle Hills, Canyon Fairways, and several other communities all fall under The Hills South umbrella. Residents of The Hills South share access to the Summerlin trail system, nearby parks, and community amenities while each individual neighborhood maintains its own identity and governance.

What Makes Eagle Hills Stand Out Within The Hills South?

Eagle Hills distinguishes itself with its own dedicated guard gate providing 24 hour security, a private 3 acre community park, two tennis courts, a basketball court, playground, and internal greenbelt trails. While other Hills South neighborhoods may share common areas or rely on Summerlin's broader amenity network, Eagle Hills keeps its recreation fully within its gates. The community's 160 custom homes sit on lots ranging from a quarter acre to two thirds acre, with some reaching a full acre. Home sizes span 2,924 to 14,000 square feet, placing Eagle Hills among the largest and most private residential options in the entire Hills South section.

How Do Prices Compare Across The Hills South?

Eagle Hills homes range from $800,000 to over $5,000,000, reflecting the custom nature and generous lot sizes within the community. Other neighborhoods in The Hills South may start lower but typically offer smaller lots and less exclusivity.
