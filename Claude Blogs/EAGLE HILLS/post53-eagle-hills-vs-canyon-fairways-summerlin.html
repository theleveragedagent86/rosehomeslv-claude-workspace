How Does Eagle Hills Compare to Canyon Fairways in Summerlin?

Eagle Hills and Canyon Fairways are both guard gated communities within The Hills South section of Summerlin, sitting adjacent to TPC Summerlin golf course. Eagle Hills offers larger lot sizes and a higher price ceiling, while Canyon Fairways provides a slightly lower entry point for buyers seeking the same golf course proximity and security.

How Do Home Prices and Sizes Compare?

Eagle Hills homes range from $800,000 to over $5,000,000, with sizes spanning 2,924 to 14,000 square feet on lots from a quarter acre to two thirds acre. Canyon Fairways homes start around $700,000 and reach approximately $3,000,000, with generally smaller lot sizes compared to Eagle Hills. Both communities were built in the late 1990s to early 2000s and feature custom and semi custom construction. Buyers looking for the most expansive lots and largest floor plans will find more options in Eagle Hills, while Canyon Fairways offers a competitive alternative at a lower price range.

What Amenities Does Each Community Provide?

Eagle Hills maintains a private 3 acre park with two tennis courts, a basketball court, a playground, shaded pavilions, and greenbelt walking trails inside its guard gate. Both communities benefit from the Summerlin trail system and share proximity to TPC Summerlin, where the PGA Tour Shriners Children's Open takes place annually. Eagle Hills' 160 home count creates an intimate, low traffic environment where residents know their neighbors. Canyon Fairways operates with a similar guard gated model, providing 24 hour controlled access and a quiet residential atmosphere.

Which Community Is the Better Fit?

The decision between Eagle Hills and Canyon Fairways often comes down to lot size priorities and budget. Buyers who want the largest possible homesites, maximum privacy, and the full range of private amenities will find Eagle Hills delivers at the highest level in The Hills South. Ryan Rose works with buyers comparing these two communities regularly and notes that Eagle Hills attracts those who place a premium on space and exclusivity, while Canyon Fairways appeals to buyers who want guard gated golf course living at a more accessible price point. Both communities benefit from strong resale values driven by limited inventory and the TPC Summerlin address.
