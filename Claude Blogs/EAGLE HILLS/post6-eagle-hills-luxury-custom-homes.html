Eagle Hills is home to 160 luxury custom built residences in the heart of Summerlin, each one individually designed and constructed between 1995 and 2000. With prices ranging from $800,000 to over $5,000,000, the community represents one of the most exclusive collections of custom homes in all of Las Vegas. ## What Makes Eagle Hills Homes Luxury? Every home in Eagle Hills was built by a private custom builder, meaning no two floor plans are the same. Architectural styles range from contemporary and classic to Mediterranean, with interior square footage spanning 2,924 to over 14,000 square feet. High end finishes, oversized lots, and thoughtful design details set these properties apart from production built neighborhoods. Many homes feature private pools, courtyards, and outdoor living spaces that take advantage of the generous lot sizes. ## How Big Are the Lots in Eagle Hills? Lot sizes in Eagle Hills range from a quarter acre to nearly a full acre, which is exceptionally rare in the Las Vegas valley. This extra space gives homeowners privacy, room for custom landscaping, and a true estate feel that most Summerlin communities simply cannot match. The tree lined streets and mature landscaping throughout the neighborhood reinforce the sense that Eagle Hills is an oasis within the desert. ## Are There Any New Construction Homes in Eagle Hills? No new construction is available in Eagle Hills. All 160 homes were completed between 1995 and 2000, and the community is fully built out. Buyers seeking a home here must purchase a resale property, which adds to the exclusivity since inventory is typically very limited. When a home does come to market, it often draws significant interest from luxury buyers across the valley.
