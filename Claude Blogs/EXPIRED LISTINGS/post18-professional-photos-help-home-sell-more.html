Video and Drone Photography Take It Further

Still photos are the foundation, but video and aerial photography add another level of buyer engagement. Listings with video tours generate 403% more inquiries than those without. Aerial and drone photography helps homes sell 68% faster by showcasing the property's lot, neighborhood, and surrounding area in ways ground-level photos simply cannot.

Matterport and similar 3D tour technology have become increasingly important for serious listings. Homes with 3D tours sell 31% faster, command a 9% higher sale price, receive 87% more online views, and attract 49% more qualified leads. These tools let buyers explore your home room by room from anywhere in the world, which is especially valuable for out-of-state buyers relocating to Las Vegas.
