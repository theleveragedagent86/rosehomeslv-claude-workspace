Reset the Clock with a Fresh Start

When a listing sits too long, buyers begin to assume something is wrong with the property. That perception becomes its own obstacle. The most effective way to overcome listing fatigue is to take the home off market, make targeted improvements, and relist as a completely new listing. This resets your days on market counter and gives buyers a reason to take a fresh look. In a valley where average days on market run 55 to 62 days, starting clean matters more than most sellers realize.
