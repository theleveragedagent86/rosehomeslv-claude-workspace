Green Valley is located in the southeastern portion of Henderson, Nevada, approximately 10 miles south of the Las Vegas Strip and just 5.5 to 7 miles from Harry Reid International Airport. The community spans 8,400 acres across four zip codes: 89014 (Green Valley North), 89074 (Green Valley South), 89012 (Green Valley South and Ranch), and 89052 (eastern portions of Green Valley Ranch).

Henderson, Not Las Vegas

Green Valley sits entirely within the city of Henderson, which is a separate incorporated city from Las Vegas. Henderson is the second largest city in Nevada with a population exceeding 350,000 residents and consistently ranks among the safest large cities in the country. While Henderson shares the Las Vegas valley with its larger neighbor, it maintains its own city government, police department, parks system, and municipal services. This distinction matters for property taxes, city services, and the strong sense of community identity that Henderson residents value.

Boundaries and Access Points

Green Valley is bordered by major arterials that make navigation simple. Green Valley Parkway runs north to south through the heart of the community. Interstate 215 (the Bruce Woodbury Beltway) provides freeway access along the southern edge, connecting residents to I-15, the Strip, and the airport in minutes. Key east to west corridors include Sunset Road, Warm Springs Road, Horizon Ridge Parkway, and Paseo Verde Parkway. Eastern Avenue anchors the community's eastern boundary.

Three Distinct Areas

Green Valley is divided into three main sections. Green Valley North (89014) is the oldest area, built primarily from the late 1970s through the 1990s. Green Valley South (89074 and 89012) began construction around 1985 and is known for its lush landscaping and larger lots. Green Valley Ranch (89012 and 89052) is the newest and most upscale section, developed from 1994 to 2002, featuring 31 sub associations with 18 gated neighborhoods and elevated views of the Strip and surrounding mountains.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose notes that Green Valley's central Henderson location delivers exceptional convenience. Residents reach the airport in 9 to 15 minutes, the Strip in 15 to 25 minutes, and downtown Las Vegas in about 20 minutes. The community also sits 15 to 20 minutes from Lake Las Vegas and roughly 30 minutes from Hoover Dam, giving homeowners easy access to both metropolitan amenities and outdoor recreation.
