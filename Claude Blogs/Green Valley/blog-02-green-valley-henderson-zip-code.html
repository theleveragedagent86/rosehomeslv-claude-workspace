Where Is Green Valley Located in Henderson?
Green Valley vs Green Valley Ranch Henderson
Is Green Valley Henderson Safe?

Green Valley in Henderson, Nevada spans four zip codes: 89014, 89074, 89012, and 89052. Each zip code corresponds to a different section of this 8,400 acre master planned community, and understanding which zip code covers which area helps buyers target their home search to the right neighborhood.

89014: Green Valley North

Zip code 89014 covers Green Valley North, the oldest section of the community. Homes here were built primarily from the late 1970s through the 1990s. This area includes roughly 3,560 acres with over 12,000 residences ranging from ranch style and Mediterranean homes to condos and custom estates. Home prices in 89014 range from approximately $205,000 for condos to over $3 million for custom properties. Green Valley High School, the Galleria at Sunset mall, and Wildhorse Golf Club are all located within this zip code.

89074: Green Valley South

Zip code 89074 covers portions of Green Valley South, sometimes called the "Emerald of the Desert" for its lush landscaping and mature tree lined streets. Construction in this area began around 1985. Homes range from approximately $215,000 to $1.6 million. This zip code includes Cornerstone Park, Arroyo Grande Sports Complex, and Legacy Golf Club. The Paseo Verde Library, Henderson's flagship 43,000 square foot library, is also located here at 280 S Green Valley Parkway.

89012: Green Valley South and Ranch Overlap

Zip code 89012 covers parts of both Green Valley South and Green Valley Ranch, making it one of the more diverse zip codes in the area. This zone includes some of the community's most desirable guard gated neighborhoods, including The Estates at Green Valley Ranch and Roma Hills. Nearby MacDonald Highlands, with homes reaching into the millions, also falls within 89012. The Henderson Multigenerational Center at 250 S Green Valley Parkway sits in this zip code.

89052: Green Valley Ranch East

Zip code 89052 covers the eastern portions of Green Valley Ranch along with adjacent communities like Seven Hills. Within Green Valley, 89052 includes The District at Green Valley Ranch shopping center, Green Valley Ranch Resort Spa and Casino, and several gated residential neighborhoods. Coronado High School (rated 7 out of 10 on GreatSchools) serves students in this area. St. Rose Dominican Hospital's Siena Campus, ranked number 2 in Nevada by U.S. News, is also located within 89052.
