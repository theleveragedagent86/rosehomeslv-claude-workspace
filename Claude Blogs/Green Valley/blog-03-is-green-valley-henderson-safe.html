Green Valley is one of the safest communities in the Las Vegas valley. Henderson, the city where Green Valley is located, consistently ranks among the top two or three safest large cities in the United States among those with populations over 300,000. Organizations including MoneyGeek, AdvisorSmith, and SafeWise have all confirmed Henderson's position near the top of national safety rankings.

Henderson Crime Statistics

Henderson's violent crime rate stands at 272.4 per 100,000 residents, which is 24.1 percent lower than the national average. The property crime rate of 1,715 per 100,000 is 2.6 percent below the national average as well. For context, Henderson is approximately 4.5 times safer than Las Vegas when comparing violent crime rates: Henderson records about 2.1 violent crimes per 1,000 residents compared to Las Vegas at 9.5 per 1,000. Year over year trends show continued improvement, with violent crime decreasing 7 percent, property crime dropping 17 percent, and total crime declining 15 percent.

Green Valley Specifically

Within Henderson, Green Valley runs approximately 20 to 30 percent below the citywide average for crime. Green Valley South is ranked the number one neighborhood in Henderson for overall livability by AreaVibes. Green Valley Ranch ranks second, and Green Valley North ranks fourth. The Henderson Police Department employs 556 officers serving the city's 350,000 plus residents and participates in the FBI's National Incident Based Reporting System for transparent crime data. In resident surveys conducted by Niche, 94 percent of Henderson residents report feeling "very safe" or "pretty safe" in their neighborhoods.

Gated Community Options

For buyers seeking additional security, Green Valley Ranch offers 18 gated neighborhoods among its 31 sub associations. Guard gated communities like The Estates at Green Valley Ranch, Roma Hills, Grand Legacy Estates, and The Fountains provide staffed entry points with visitor verification. These neighborhoods combine Henderson's already strong safety record with private security infrastructure.
