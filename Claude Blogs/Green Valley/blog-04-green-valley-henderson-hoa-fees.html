Green Valley vs Green Valley Ranch Henderson
Where Is Green Valley Located in Henderson?

Green Valley Henderson operates under a multi layered HOA structure where most homeowners pay both a master community association fee and a sub association fee. Total monthly HOA costs in Green Valley typically range from $100 to $300 depending on the neighborhood, with some basic communities as low as $65 per month and guard gated luxury areas running higher.

Master Association Fees

The master association fee covers community wide services and typically runs $65 to $75 per month. In Green Valley Ranch, the Green Valley Ranch Community Association (GVRCA) manages 1,310 acres and over 4,000 homes across 31 sub associations. GVRCA fees cover common area maintenance, parks and trails upkeep, pedestrian and bike path maintenance, community events, professional management, insurance, and reserve fund contributions. The GVHRA (Green Valley Homeowners Recreation Association) covers 1,048 homes in Green Valley proper, managing shared pools, clubhouses, and greenbelt areas.

Sub Association Fees

On top of the master fee, most neighborhoods charge an additional sub association fee for neighborhood specific services. These fees cover landscaping within your immediate neighborhood, gated entry and security staffing (in gated communities), swimming pools and spas, fitness facilities, clubhouse operations, and exterior building maintenance for condos and townhomes. Sub association fees vary widely based on what amenities your specific neighborhood offers.

What to Expect by Community Type

Non gated single family neighborhoods in Green Valley typically see total combined fees (master plus sub) between $100 and $175 per month. Gated communities with staffed entry points and private amenities generally fall between $175 and $300 per month. Condo and townhome communities may run slightly higher due to exterior maintenance obligations covered by the HOA. Guard gated luxury communities like The Estates at Green Valley Ranch and Roma Hills carry premium fees reflecting their private security and enhanced landscaping services.

Management Companies

Several professional firms manage Green Valley HOAs. Terra West Management (702 362 6262) handles numerous communities across the area. CAMCO Nevada, FirstService Residential, and Desert Living (a family owned Henderson specialist since 2002) also manage multiple Green Valley sub associations. GVRCA and GVHRA maintain on site staff and offices for resident inquiries.
