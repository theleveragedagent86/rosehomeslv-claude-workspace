Where Is Green Valley Located in Henderson?
Green Valley Henderson Zip Code Guide

Green Valley Henderson is served by some of the highest rated schools in the Clark County School District, with multiple elementary schools earning 9 and 10 out of 10 on GreatSchools and a charter high school ranked number one in Nevada. The combination of strong public, charter, and private options makes Green Valley one of the most attractive communities in the Las Vegas valley for families prioritizing education.

Top Rated Elementary Schools

Several Green Valley elementary schools consistently earn top ratings. Elise L. Wolff Elementary (1001 Seven Hills Drive, 89052) and Hannah Marie Brown Elementary (10 Chapata Drive, 89012) both hold perfect 10 out of 10 GreatSchools ratings. John Vanderburg Elementary (2040 Desert Shadow Trail, 89012) rates 9 to 10 out of 10. Frank S. Lamping Elementary (2551 Summit Grove Drive, 89052), Estes M. McDoniel Elementary (1831 Fox Ridge Drive, 89014), and Selma F. Bartlett Elementary (1961 Wigwam Parkway, 89074) each earn 8 to 9 out of 10.

Strong Middle Schools

Del E Webb Middle School (2200 Reunion Drive, 89052) leads the area with a 9 out of 10 rating, serving parts of Green Valley Ranch and Seven Hills. Mannion Middle School (155 E Paradise Hills Drive, 89002) also earns a 9 out of 10. Thurman White Academy of the Performing Arts (1661 Galleria Drive, 89014) is an 8 out of 10 rated performing arts magnet school that draws students from across Henderson. Bob Miller Middle School (2400 Cozy Hill Circle, 89052) rates 7 out of 10 and serves the Green Valley Ranch zone.

High Schools and Charter Options

Coronado High School (1001 Coronado Center Drive, 89052) earns a 7 out of 10 and is ranked number 13 in Nevada by U.S. News, serving Green Valley Ranch, Seven Hills, and parts of Anthem. Green Valley High School (460 N Arroyo Grande Boulevard, 89014) rates 6 out of 10 and offers one of only four International Baccalaureate (IB) programs in the state of Nevada. For charter options, Coral Academy of Science Sandy Ridge Campus rates 10 out of 10 and ranks as the number one charter high school in Nevada and number four overall statewide. Nevada State High School (233 N Stephanie Street, 89014) rates 9 out of 10 and allows students to earn free college credits and potentially an associate's degree before graduating.

Private Schools

Henderson International School (1165 Sandy Ridge Avenue, 89052) maintains a 7 to 1 student teacher ratio with tuition around $22,800 per year. Green Valley Christian School (711 Valle Verde Court, 89014) serves pre kindergarten through 12th grade with approximately 600 students and tuition around $8,760 per year. Touro University Nevada, located within Green Valley at 11 Sunset Way, adds a higher education presence to the community.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose advises buyers with school age children to verify exact school zone boundaries before making an offer, as zones can shift at street level. The difference between a Coronado zone and a Foothill zone, or a Wolff zone and a different elementary, can significantly affect both daily life and resale value.
