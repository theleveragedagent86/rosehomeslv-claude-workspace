Green Valley vs Green Valley Ranch Henderson
Where Is Green Valley Located in Henderson?
Cornerstone Park Green Valley Henderson

The District at Green Valley Ranch is a 406,260 square foot open air shopping and dining destination located at 2240 Village Walk Drive in Henderson (89052). Opened on April 15, 2004, it was the Las Vegas valley's first mixed use retail project and has become the social and commercial hub of the Green Valley Ranch community. The center is managed by Vestar Development and connects via pedestrian walkway to the adjacent Green Valley Ranch Resort.

Shopping at The District

The District features a curated mix of national and specialty retailers spread across seven buildings. Anchor tenants include REI, Pottery Barn, and Sephora. Active lifestyle brands like Lululemon and Athleta draw fitness minded shoppers. Anthropologie, Brighton Collectibles, Pandora, LUSH Cosmetics, Drybar, Chico's, and Bath and Body Works round out the retail lineup. The center's open air design with central park areas, fountains, and landscaped walkways creates an experience that feels more like a village stroll than a traditional shopping trip.

Dining Options

The District's restaurant collection rivals many Strip dining corridors. North Italia serves upscale Italian cuisine with house made pastas. Echo and Rig combines a full service butcher shop with a modern steakhouse. King's Fish House specializes in fresh seafood. The Cheesecake Factory and P.F. Chang's provide familiar favorites. For more casual options, Settebello Pizzeria Napoletana serves authentic Neapolitan pizza, Flower Child focuses on healthy bowls and salads, Shake Shack offers burgers and frozen custard, Rachel's Kitchen provides breakfast and lunch, and Lucille's Smokehouse BBQ serves Southern style barbecue. Crazy Pita Rotisserie and Grill and Pkwy Tavern add Mediterranean and craft beer options respectively.

Events and Community

The District hosts regular community events including weekly Thursday gatherings, Market in the Alley featuring local artisans, The Makers Hive Market, seasonal wine walks, and FIT4MOM Stroller Strides fitness classes on Tuesdays and Thursdays. A central splash pad area provides warm weather entertainment for young children. The center is pet friendly, and weekend mornings regularly see residents walking dogs through the outdoor corridors.
