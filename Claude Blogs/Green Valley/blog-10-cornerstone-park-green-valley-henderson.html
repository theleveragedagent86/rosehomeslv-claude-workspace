Cornerstone Park is one of Henderson's premier outdoor spaces, located at 1600 Wigwam Parkway in the Green Valley South section of Henderson (89074). The park features a large pond with water features, extensive walking trails, exercise stations, and bird watching areas that draw residents from across the community year round. A Phase II expansion is currently underway to add even more amenities to this already popular destination.

Park Features and Amenities

The centerpiece of Cornerstone Park is its pond, which attracts a variety of bird species and creates a peaceful setting for morning walks and evening strolls. Paved walking trails loop around the water and extend through landscaped areas with mature trees providing shade. Exercise stations are positioned along the trails for residents who want to combine their walk with a workout. Picnic shelters and open lawn areas offer space for family gatherings, and the park includes playgrounds for younger children.

Bird Watching and Nature

Cornerstone Park has earned a reputation as one of the best bird watching locations in the Henderson area. The pond environment attracts ducks, herons, and other waterfowl that are uncommon in the surrounding desert landscape. Early morning visitors regularly spot a variety of species, making the park a quiet retreat that feels far removed from the urban environment just minutes away. The landscaped areas around the pond support additional wildlife and provide a green oasis within the community.

Location and Access

The park sits along Wigwam Parkway in Green Valley South, easily accessible from Green Valley Parkway and Pecos Road. Nearby amenities include Legacy Golf Club (130 Par Excellence Drive), Arroyo Grande Sports Complex, and the residential neighborhoods of Green Valley South. The Paseo Verde Library at 280 S Green Valley Parkway is approximately one mile north. Parking is available on site, and the park connects to Henderson's broader trail network for extended walks or bike rides.
