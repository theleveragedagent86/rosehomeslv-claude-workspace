Parks and Recreation in Green Valley Henderson
Green Valley Henderson: A Family Friendly Community

Wildhorse Golf Club in Henderson offers an outstanding public golf experience that balances quality course design with affordable rates. Located at 2100 W Warm Springs Road, this 18 hole, par 70 municipal course has become a favorite destination for both local golfers and visitors seeking accessible championship level play. Whether you're a seasoned golfer or just beginning your game, Wildhorse provides the perfect setting to enjoy golf in the beautiful Henderson valley.

A Redesigned Municipal Gem

Wildhorse Golf Club is owned and operated by the City of Henderson, making it an excellent public golf resource for the community. The course underwent a significant redesign in 2004 that transformed it into a modern championship layout. The redesign improved drainage, updated amenities, and enhanced the overall playing experience while maintaining accessibility for golfers of all skill levels. The city's commitment to maintaining this course as a public facility means affordable rates without sacrificing quality standards.

Championship Course Layout and Design

The 18 hole course spans a par 70 layout with well maintained fairways and strategically placed hazards that challenge players while rewarding good decision making. The course design takes advantage of the natural Henderson landscape, featuring scenic mountain views and carefully landscaped greens. Each hole presents its own unique character, from water features to bunker complexes. The course is walkable for those who prefer to hoof it, and golf carts are available for those who want a more leisurely pace through the round.

Affordable Golf for Everyone

One of Wildhorse's greatest advantages is its affordable green fee structure. As a publicly owned municipal course, rates remain accessible compared to private clubs and high end resort courses throughout the Las Vegas area. This makes Wildhorse an ideal choice for residents of Green Valley and Henderson who want regular golf without expensive membership dues. The course regularly offers specials and packages for local players, making it even more economical for those who play frequently.

Amenities and Services

The facility includes a full service pro shop, driving range, short game practice area, and comfortable clubhouse with dining options. Professional instruction is available for players looking to improve their game, with certified PGA instructors on staff. The pro shop stocks quality equipment and apparel from major manufacturers. The snack bar and restaurant serve food before and after your round, making it convenient to spend a full day at the course.

The affordability combined with the professional course design makes it accessible for all golfers, whether you visit weekly or occasionally.
