Condos in Green Valley Henderson

Green Valley Henderson home prices in 2026 range from approximately $205,000 for condos in Green Valley North to over $3 million for custom estates in guard gated enclaves like The Estates at Green Valley Ranch. The median sale price across the broader Green Valley area falls between $433,000 and $595,000 depending on which section you are searching, with Henderson overall posting a median of $497,000 to $505,000.

Prices by Section

Green Valley Ranch commands the highest prices with median sales between $499,000 and $595,000 and a price per square foot of $288 to $311. Green Valley North is the most affordable section with median prices in the $451,000 to $475,000 range. Green Valley South falls in between at $433,000 to $499,000. Across all sections, the average price per square foot ranges from $265 to $312, with Green Valley Ranch consistently at the top of that range due to its newer construction, higher elevation, and gated community premiums.

Market Conditions

The Green Valley market is showing buyer friendly conditions heading into 2026. Henderson inventory has risen to approximately 2,002 available homes with 4.9 months of supply, up from 3.3 months the prior year. Days on market have increased to 47 to 72 days depending on the sub area, up from 31 to 50 days previously. The sale to list price ratio stands at 97.8 percent, and 23.6 percent of listings have taken price reductions. Only 13.1 percent of homes are selling above asking price, giving buyers more negotiating leverage than they have had in recent years.

Prices by Property Type

Condos in Green Valley range from $225,000 to $400,000. Townhomes fall between $330,000 and $450,000. Starter and smaller single family homes list from $395,000 to $500,000. Mid range single family homes run $500,000 to $800,000. Luxury and custom homes range from $800,000 to $2 million, and guard gated estate properties start at $1 million and reach $6 million or higher in communities like MacDonald Highlands. Gated communities generally command a premium of $200,000 to $500,000 over comparable non gated homes.
