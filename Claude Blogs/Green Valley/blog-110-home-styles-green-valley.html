Primary Architectural Styles in Green Valley

Green Valley homes showcase several dominant architectural styles reflecting their era of construction. Ranch style homes, typical of earlier development phases, feature horizontal lines, clean designs, and practical floor plans. Mediterranean and Spanish revival styles emerged in later phases, featuring arched doorways, terracotta tile roofing, and stucco exteriors inspired by Old World design. Contemporary styles in the most recent phases feature clean lines, large windows, and modern materials. These diverse styles create visual interest throughout the community while allowing residents to choose homes matching their personal aesthetic preferences. Walking through different Green Valley neighborhoods reveals this architectural evolution over the community's development history.

Lot Sizes and Property Configurations

Green Valley offers varied lot sizes across different neighborhoods. Non gated communities typically feature standard lots ranging from 5,000 to 8,000 square feet, accommodating homes with yards suitable for families and outdoor activities. Guard gated communities offer larger lots, often from one third acre to one acre or larger, providing more privacy and space. These larger lots in guard gated communities support the Mediterranean and Spanish revival style homes that dominate those neighborhoods. The variation in lot sizes means Green Valley has options for buyers prioritizing more space as well as those preferring lower maintenance properties.

Green Valley Home Sizes and Types

Green Valley offers three primary housing types with varying sizes. Single family homes range from approximately 1,400 square feet to over 11,500 square feet, accommodating everything from starter families to luxury buyers. Townhomes, popular in certain neighborhoods, typically range from 1,000 to 1,700 square feet and offer lower maintenance alternatives to single family homes. Condos in Green Valley range from approximately 872 to 1,400 square feet and provide the most affordable ownership options. The median single family home in Green Valley Ranch, one of the largest neighborhoods, is approximately 1,976 square feet, representing a comfortable size for most families.
