Art Festival of Henderson

The Art Festival of Henderson celebrates visual creativity on Mother's Day weekend, featuring more than 100 artists displaying paintings, sculptures, photography, and crafts. This highly anticipated annual event transforms the community into an outdoor gallery, attracting art enthusiasts from throughout the region. Green Valley residents can explore diverse artistic styles, meet talented creators, and purchase original works supporting local artists. The festival's vibrant atmosphere, live entertainment, and community energy make it a highlight for families and cultural enthusiasts alike. The event celebrates human creativity and provides opportunities to discover local talent.

St. Patrick's Day Festival and Parade

Each March, Henderson celebrates Irish heritage with its St. Patrick's Day Festival and Parade, bringing carnival rides, Celtic music, and pop-up Irish pubs to the community. The festive atmosphere welcomes residents of all backgrounds to participate in the celebration. Live performances, food vendors, and family-friendly activities create a joyful environment where neighbors connect and celebrate together. The parade showcases colorful floats, marching groups, and community organizations, turning streets into celebration zones. Whether seeking Irish cultural experiences or simply enjoying festive community spirit, the St. Patrick's Day Festival offers something for everyone.

Henderson Bluegrass Festival

Music lovers in Green Valley look forward to October's Henderson Bluegrass Festival, offering free admission and authentic bluegrass performances at Water Street Plaza. The festival celebrates a beloved American musical tradition, featuring talented musicians performing familiar favorites and introducing audiences to contemporary bluegrass artists. The relaxed outdoor setting, family-friendly atmosphere, and lack of admission charges make the festival accessible to all residents. Bluegrass performances inspire dancing, toe-tapping, and joyful gatherings that create lasting community memories.

Jazz in the Park Series

The Jazz in the Park series, celebrating 35 years of excellence, brings live jazz performances to outdoor venues each May and June. Tickets sell out quickly despite being free, illustrating the community's passion for live music and outdoor entertainment. This long-running series reflects Henderson's cultural maturity and commitment to fostering appreciation for sophisticated musical traditions. Green Valley residents can enjoy world-class jazz performances in intimate outdoor settings, creating magical summer evenings with family and friends. The series' endurance and popularity demonstrate the community's vibrant cultural scene.
