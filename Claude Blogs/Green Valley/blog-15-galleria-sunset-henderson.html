Where Is Green Valley Located in Henderson?

The Galleria at Sunset is Henderson's only indoor shopping mall, located at 1300 West Sunset Road in the Green Valley North section of Henderson (89014). Opened on February 28, 1996, at a cost of $150 million, the mall spans 1,080,208 square feet and houses 123 to 140 stores across two levels. Phone: (702) 434 0202.

Anchor Stores and Major Retailers

The Galleria is anchored by Macy's, Dillard's, JCPenney, Kohl's, and Dick's Sporting Goods. These department stores provide a full range of shopping from fashion and home goods to athletic equipment. The mall's interior features additional national brands including H&M, Hollister, Foot Locker, Victoria's Secret, and dozens of specialty retailers. A food court with multiple dining options serves shoppers throughout the day.

Location and Convenience

The mall sits along Sunset Road, one of Henderson's major east to west corridors, making it easily accessible from all sections of Green Valley. Sunset Station Hotel and Casino is located directly adjacent to the mall, and the surrounding Sunset Road commercial corridor includes additional grocery stores, restaurants, banks, and service businesses. Residents of Green Valley North live within a 5 to 10 minute drive of the Galleria, while Green Valley South and Ranch residents can reach it in 10 to 15 minutes.

Community Role

The Galleria at Sunset was Henderson's first major indoor retail destination when it opened in 1996, and it continues to serve as the community's traditional shopping center. While The District at Green Valley Ranch offers an upscale open air alternative, the Galleria provides the practical, everyday shopping that families rely on for department store purchases, back to school shopping, and holiday gift buying. The climate controlled indoor environment is particularly valued during the summer months when outdoor temperatures exceed 100 degrees.
