Is Green Valley Henderson Safe?

Henderson is certified as a "Better City for Pets" by Mars Petcare and offers 16 plus off leash dog parks across the city, with several located within or near Green Valley. Dog owners in Green Valley have access to dedicated off leash areas ranging from compact neighborhood parks to the city's flagship 5 acre dog facility.

Dog Parks in and Near Green Valley

Bark Park at Heritage Park (350 S Racetrack Road) is Henderson's largest dog park at 5 acres, featuring an agility course, a doggie splash pad that operates May through September, and four separate dog runs for different sizes and temperaments. Cactus Wren Dog Park (2900 Ivanpah Drive, 89074) is located within Green Valley South and offers three separate areas for dogs along with a playground and walking paths for the humans.

Dos Escuelas Park, also known as Nicolai's Para Dog Park, is a local favorite among Green Valley Ranch residents for its convenient location and well maintained facilities. Discovery Park Dog Park at 2011 Paseo Verde Parkway (89012) sits adjacent to the BMX track and baseball fields. Additional nearby options include Paseo Vista Dog Park, Amador Vista Dog Park, and Arroyo Grande Dog Park.

Pet Services in the Area

Green Valley Animal Hospital at 6150 Mountain Vista Street (89014) is open seven days a week and offers wellness exams, dental care, surgical services, a pet hotel, and grooming. Parkway Springs Animal Hospital at 2221 N Green Valley Parkway provides boarding, dental, and surgical services. Valley Ranch Animal Hospital, Town Center Animal Hospital, and American Pet Hospital offer additional veterinary care options throughout the community. PetSmart and Petco both have multiple Henderson locations for supplies and grooming.
