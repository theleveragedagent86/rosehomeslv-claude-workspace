Green Valley Henderson offers access to 11 golf courses within a short drive, ranging from affordable municipal courses to exclusive private country clubs. This concentration of golf options makes Green Valley one of the strongest golf communities in the Las Vegas valley, appealing to both serious golfers and those who enjoy occasional rounds.

Public and Municipal Courses

Wildhorse Golf Club at 2100 W Warm Springs Road (89014) is a city owned 18 hole, par 70 course that was redesigned in 2004, offering the most affordable green fees in the area. Legacy Golf Club at 130 Par Excellence Drive (89074) is an 18 hole, par 72 Arthur Hills design recognized as a "Top 10 in Nevada" by Golf Digest. Chimera Golf Club is an 18 hole public course featuring the first TrackMan driving range in Nevada along with Strip views. Revere Golf Club in the Henderson foothills offers 36 holes across two courses (Lexington and Concord) with panoramic valley views. Reflection Bay Golf Club at Lake Las Vegas provides a scenic lakeside public option.

Resort and Semi Private Courses

Rio Secco Golf Club at 2851 Grand Hills Drive (89012) is a Rees Jones designed 18 hole resort course that is home to the Butch Harmon School of Golf. Desert Willow Golf Course at 2020 W Horizon Ridge Parkway (89012) is a semi private, 18 hole, par 60 executive course located within Sun City MacDonald Ranch.

Private Country Clubs

DragonRidge Country Club in MacDonald Highlands (89012) is a private 18 hole course set in the McCullough Mountains with Las Vegas skyline views and full country club amenities. Anthem Country Club is a private 18 hole course with an active membership community. SouthShore Country Club at Lake Las Vegas features the first private Jack Nicklaus designed course in Nevada.
