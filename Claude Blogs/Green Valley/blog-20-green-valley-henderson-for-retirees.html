Is Green Valley Henderson Safe?

Green Valley Henderson is one of the Las Vegas valley's top retirement destinations, with 20.4 percent of Henderson's population aged 65 and older and nine dedicated 55 plus communities in the Henderson area. The combination of no state income tax, 297 sunny days per year, top ranked safety, world class healthcare, and abundant recreation makes Green Valley exceptionally attractive to retirees from across the country.

55 Plus Communities

Sun City Anthem is the largest active adult community in the area with 7,144 homes built between 1998 and 2008. Located at 2450 Hampton Road (89052), it features three clubhouses totaling over 130,000 square feet, 60 plus chartered clubs, and the 36 hole Revere Golf Club. Home prices range from approximately $350,000 to $2 million. Sun City MacDonald Ranch offers 2,500 plus homes in a 55 plus golf community near Black Mountain. Other options include Solera at Anthem, Heritage at Cadence (Lennar's newest active adult community), Del Webb at Lake Las Vegas, and Vintage at Seven Hills (55 plus rental apartments).

Healthcare Access

Retirees benefit from outstanding healthcare proximity. St. Rose Dominican Hospital's Siena Campus at 3001 St. Rose Parkway is ranked number 2 in Nevada by U.S. News and World Report, with 366 beds and a Level III Trauma Center. Henderson Hospital at 1050 Galleria Drive has 303 beds. The ER at Green Valley Ranch, a freestanding 24/7 emergency department, opened in August 2024 to provide immediate emergency care within the community. Multiple urgent care and specialty medical offices line the Green Valley Parkway and St. Rose Parkway corridors.

Recreation and Lifestyle

The Henderson Multigenerational Center at 250 S Green Valley Parkway offers fitness facilities, pools, walking tracks, and group classes at municipal rates. Eleven golf courses are within a short drive. The Paseo Verde Library provides programs including book clubs, Tai Chi, and genealogy resources. The District at Green Valley Ranch and GVR Resort offer walkable dining and entertainment. Henderson's 74 parks and 300 plus miles of trails support active outdoor lifestyles during the 8 to 9 month outdoor season.
