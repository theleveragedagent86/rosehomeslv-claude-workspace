Where Is Green Valley Located in Henderson?

St. Rose Dominican Hospital Siena Campus is the primary hospital serving Green Valley Henderson, located at 3001 St. Rose Parkway (89052). Ranked number 2 in Nevada by U.S. News and World Report, the hospital provides 366 beds, a Level III Trauma Center, a Level III NICU, a Joint Commission certified Joint Replacement Center, and a Robotic Surgery Center of Excellence. It has also been named the eighth most beautiful hospital in the world. Phone: (702) 616 5000.

Emergency and Urgent Care

In addition to the Siena Campus emergency department, the ER at Green Valley Ranch is a freestanding 24/7 emergency facility that opened in August 2024 within the heart of the community. This standalone ER is staffed by board certified emergency physicians and equipped with X ray, CT scan, ultrasound, and laboratory services. For non emergency situations, CareNow Urgent Care at 2269 N Green Valley Parkway (89014) operates Monday through Friday 8am to 9pm and weekends 8am to 8pm.

Additional Hospital Access

Henderson Hospital at 1050 Galleria Drive (89011) is a 303 bed Joint Commission accredited acute care facility with its own emergency department and cardiac care unit. Phone: (702) 963 7000. St. Rose Dominican Rose de Lima Campus at 102 E Lake Mead Parkway (89015) has 145 beds and holds the distinction of being the first fully accredited hospital in Southern Nevada. Together, these facilities give Green Valley residents access to over 800 hospital beds within a 15 minute drive.

Medical Corridor

The St. Rose Parkway and Green Valley Parkway corridors are home to dozens of specialty medical practices, primary care offices, dental clinics, and outpatient facilities. Ace Primary Care at 1701 N Green Valley Parkway Suite 7B (89074) and Genesis Medical Group at 100 N Green Valley Parkway (89074) provide primary care options within the community. Touro University Nevada at 11 Sunset Way (89014) adds a medical education presence with its osteopathic medicine and health sciences programs.
