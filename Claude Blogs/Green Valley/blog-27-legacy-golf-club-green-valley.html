Green Valley Henderson for Retirees

Legacy Golf Club is located at 130 Par Excellence Drive in the Green Valley South section of Henderson (89074). Designed by renowned architect Arthur Hills, the 18 hole, par 72 public course has been recognized as a "Top 10 Course in Nevada" by Golf Digest and consistently rates among the best public courses in the Las Vegas valley.

Course Details

The Legacy Golf Club stretches across 6,211 yards from the forward tees to 7,233 yards from the championship tees. The course features bentgrass greens, wide fairways, and desert landscaping that frames each hole against the surrounding mountain views. The signature "Devil's Triangle" holes (11, 12, and 13) challenge golfers with a combination of water hazards, strategic bunkering, and elevation changes. The course is walkable, which is uncommon for Las Vegas area layouts.

Facilities and Amenities

The clubhouse features a full service pro shop, Wildfire Grille restaurant, and event spaces for tournaments and private functions. The driving range and practice area include a putting green and chipping area. Lessons are available through the club's PGA professionals. Legacy hosts regular tournaments, league play, and seasonal events throughout the year.

Proximity to Green Valley Homes

Legacy Golf Club sits adjacent to Cornerstone Park and is surrounded by residential neighborhoods in Green Valley South. Homes along the Legacy Golf Club perimeter and nearby streets in Green Valley South (89074) range from $433,000 to $900,000. Several neighborhoods offer direct golf course views, making them particularly appealing to golfers who want their home course visible from their backyard.
