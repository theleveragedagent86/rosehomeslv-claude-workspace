The Henderson Multigenerational Center is located at 250 S Green Valley Parkway (89012) in the heart of Green Valley. This 84,120 square foot city owned recreation facility serves residents of all ages with fitness, aquatics, sports, and community programming. Phone: (702) 267 4000.

Aquatics

The center features both indoor and outdoor swimming pools. The indoor pool is heated year round and hosts lap swimming, water aerobics, and learn to swim programs. The outdoor pool and splash pad operate seasonally from approximately Memorial Day through Labor Day. Swim lessons are available for children and adults at all skill levels.

Fitness and Sports

A full fitness center includes cardio equipment, free weights, and weight machines. The indoor walking and jogging track allows year round exercise regardless of weather. A 32 foot rock climbing wall provides a unique fitness challenge. Basketball courts accommodate open play, leagues, and youth programs. Racquetball courts are available for reservation. Group fitness classes run throughout the week and include yoga, spin, Zumba, Pilates, and strength training.

Youth and Senior Programming

Youth programs include after school activities, summer camps, sports leagues, and educational workshops. Senior programs feature specialized fitness classes, social gatherings, card games, and educational seminars. The center hosts community events throughout the year including holiday celebrations, health fairs, and cultural programming. Meeting rooms and event spaces are available for rental by community groups and private parties.

Membership and Access

Henderson residents receive discounted rates on memberships and program registration. The center is operated by the City of Henderson Parks and Recreation Department. Annual and monthly membership options provide access to the fitness center, pools, track, and climbing wall with group fitness classes included. Drop in rates are also available for occasional visitors.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose considers the Multigenerational Center one of Green Valley's best kept secrets. The facility rivals many private gym memberships in quality but at municipal pricing. Its location on Green Valley Parkway makes it accessible from virtually every section of the community, and the breadth of programming from toddler swim classes to senior fitness makes it genuinely useful for every age group in a household.
