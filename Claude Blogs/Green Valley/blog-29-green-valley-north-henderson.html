Green Valley North is the original section of the Green Valley master planned community, located in the 89014 zip code. As the first area developed after Green Valley's founding in 1978, it offers the most mature landscaping, the most established neighborhoods, and the most affordable price points in the community, with homes ranging from approximately $205,000 for condos to over $3 million for larger single family properties.

Location and Boundaries

Green Valley North is bounded roughly by Sunset Road to the north, Warm Springs Road to the south, Pecos Road to the west, and Valle Verde Drive to the east. The section sits closest to the Galleria at Sunset mall and Sunset Station Hotel and Casino. Green Valley Parkway runs through its center, providing the main commercial and retail corridor. Access to I 215 Bruce Woodbury Beltway and I 515 connects residents to the Strip, the airport, and the rest of the valley.

Schools and Amenities

Green Valley High School at 460 N Arroyo Grande Boulevard serves the area and offers one of only four International Baccalaureate programs in Nevada. Nevada State High School at 233 N Stephanie Street allows students to earn free college credits. Thurman White Academy of the Performing Arts at 1661 Galleria Drive is an 8 out of 10 rated middle school offering a specialized arts curriculum. The Paseo Verde Library at 280 S Green Valley Parkway provides community programs and resources.

Home Styles and Pricing

Green Valley North features a mix of condos ($205,000 to $400,000), townhomes ($330,000 to $450,000), and single family homes ($395,000 to $800,000 and above). Construction dates primarily range from the late 1970s through the mid 1990s, meaning homes have larger lots and more mature landscaping compared to newer Henderson communities. The established trees provide significantly more shade than newer developments, a practical benefit in the desert climate.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose recommends Green Valley North to buyers who prioritize value, mature streetscapes, and proximity to everyday commercial amenities. While newer communities in Henderson offer updated finishes, Green Valley North delivers the most square footage per dollar in the Green Valley area, and many homes have been thoughtfully updated by long term owners who invested in their properties over the decades.
