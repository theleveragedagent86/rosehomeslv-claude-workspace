Cornerstone Park Green Valley Henderson

Green Valley South occupies the central portion of the Green Valley master planned community, spanning zip codes 89074 and parts of 89012. Often referred to as the "Emerald of the Desert" for its lush landscaping and green corridors, this section was developed primarily in the late 1980s through the mid 1990s and offers a balance of mature neighborhoods, community parks, and mid range pricing between $433,000 and $499,000.

Location and Character

Green Valley South is bounded roughly by Warm Springs Road to the north, Wigwam Parkway to the south, and extends between Pecos Road and Valle Verde Drive. The section earned its "Emerald" nickname because of the extensive community landscaping, mature tree canopy, and green common areas that distinguish it from the surrounding desert landscape. Cornerstone Park, one of Henderson's premier parks, anchors the section at 1600 Wigwam Parkway.

Schools

Green Valley South is served by several highly rated schools. Del E. Webb Middle School at 2200 Reunion Drive (89052) rates 9 out of 10 on GreatSchools. Frank Lamping Elementary and Selma Bartlett Elementary serve the area's elementary students with ratings of 8 to 9 out of 10. Coronado High School at 1001 Coronado Center Drive (89052) rates 7 out of 10 and ranks 13th in Nevada. Henderson International School at 1165 Sandy Ridge Avenue offers private K through 12 education with tuition starting at $22,800 per year.

Home Styles and Pricing

Homes in Green Valley South range from approximately $215,000 for condos to $1.6 million for larger custom properties. The median falls between $433,000 and $499,000 for single family homes. Construction dates span the late 1980s through mid 1990s, with home sizes typically ranging from 1,500 to 3,500 square feet. Lots are generally larger than those found in newer Henderson communities, and the mature landscaping provides a settled, established feel.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose considers Green Valley South the sweet spot of the community. It offers the tree lined, established character of Green Valley North with slightly newer construction and better proximity to amenities like Legacy Golf Club and Cornerstone Park. For buyers who want the Green Valley lifestyle without the premium of Green Valley Ranch's gated communities, this section delivers excellent value.
