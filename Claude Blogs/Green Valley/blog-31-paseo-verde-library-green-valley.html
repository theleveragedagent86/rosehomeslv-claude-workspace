Parks in Green Valley Henderson

The Paseo Verde Library is located at 280 S Green Valley Parkway in Henderson (89012), serving as the primary Henderson District Public Library branch for Green Valley residents. The modern facility offers far more than book lending, functioning as a community hub for education, technology access, and cultural programming.

Facilities and Collections

The library features an extensive book and media collection, public computers with internet access, free Wi Fi throughout the building, and quiet study rooms for individual work and group collaboration. A dedicated children's section includes age appropriate materials, interactive learning stations, and a separate story time area. The teen section provides a designated space for young adults with its own reading materials, computers, and programming.

Programs and Events

Paseo Verde Library runs a robust schedule of free programs for all ages. Children's programming includes weekly story times for babies, toddlers, and preschoolers, plus STEAM activities and summer reading programs. Adult programs feature book clubs, Tai Chi classes, computer literacy workshops, genealogy resources, and author events. The library also hosts citizenship preparation classes, English language learning resources, and job search assistance. Henderson libraries participate in the interlibrary loan system, giving cardholders access to materials from across the Henderson District network.

Community Space

Meeting rooms are available for community groups and nonprofit organizations. The library hosts regular exhibits featuring local artists and historical displays about Henderson's development. The location on Green Valley Parkway places it within easy reach of the Henderson Multigenerational Center (one mile south at 250 S Green Valley Parkway) and multiple residential neighborhoods.
