Green Valley Henderson for Retirees
Condos in Green Valley Henderson

Single story homes are among the most sought after property types in Green Valley Henderson, driven by strong demand from retirees, buyers with mobility considerations, and families who prefer the convenience of single level living. Green Valley's earlier development phases in the 1980s and 1990s produced a substantial inventory of single story floor plans, making this community one of the better sources for single level homes in the Henderson market.

Pricing and Availability

Single story homes in Green Valley typically range from $395,000 to $800,000 for standard single family properties, with luxury single story estates in gated communities reaching $1 million and above. These homes generally range from 1,200 to 2,800 square feet on lots that are often larger than those found in newer Henderson communities. Because single story homes represent a smaller percentage of overall inventory and face consistently high demand, they tend to sell faster and at slightly higher price per square foot premiums than comparable two story homes.

Where to Find Single Story Homes

Green Valley North (89014) offers the most affordable single story options due to its older construction dates. Green Valley South (89074) provides mid range single story homes with more mature landscaping and proximity to Cornerstone Park and Legacy Golf Club. Green Valley Ranch (89052 and 89012) includes single story options within both gated and non gated neighborhoods, though two story homes are more common in this newer section. Sun City MacDonald Ranch, located adjacent to Green Valley, is entirely single story as a 55 plus community.

Benefits of Single Story Living

Beyond the obvious accessibility advantages, single story homes in the Las Vegas valley offer practical benefits: lower cooling costs because heat does not rise to a second floor, easier maintenance with no second story exterior to service, and stronger wind resistance during the occasional desert windstorm. Many Green Valley single story homes also feature covered patios, private pools, and spacious yards that take advantage of the single level footprint.
