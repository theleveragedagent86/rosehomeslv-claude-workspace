Green Valley North Henderson

Green Valley High School is located at 460 N Arroyo Grande Boulevard in Henderson (89014), serving students in the Green Valley North section of the community. Opened in 1991 as the first high school built specifically for the Green Valley community, GVHS rates 6 out of 10 on GreatSchools and is one of only four schools in the Clark County School District to offer the International Baccalaureate (IB) diploma program.

International Baccalaureate Program

The IB program at Green Valley High School is a rigorous, internationally recognized pre university curriculum that prepares students for success at competitive colleges and universities worldwide. Students who complete the full IB diploma demonstrate academic discipline, critical thinking, and global awareness that colleges weigh heavily in admissions decisions. The program requires coursework across six subject groups, an extended essay, community service, and a Theory of Knowledge course. Green Valley HS is one of only four schools in the Las Vegas valley offering this program.

Athletics and Activities

Green Valley High School competes in the 5A classification of Nevada high school athletics. The school has a strong athletics tradition across football, basketball, baseball, soccer, swimming, track and field, tennis, and golf. The Gators mascot represents the school's athletic teams. Beyond sports, GVHS offers numerous clubs, student organizations, performing arts programs, and academic competition teams that provide well rounded extracurricular engagement.

Campus and Facilities

The campus features modern classroom facilities, science laboratories, a performing arts center, athletic fields and courts, a swimming pool, and a student commons area. Recent facility upgrades have kept the campus competitive with newer Henderson high schools despite its 1991 opening. The school serves approximately 2,800 to 3,000 students across grades 9 through 12.
