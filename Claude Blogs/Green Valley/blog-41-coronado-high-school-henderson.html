Green Valley Henderson for Families

Coronado High School is located at 1001 Coronado Center Drive in Henderson (89052), serving students primarily from Green Valley Ranch and Seven Hills. Rated 7 out of 10 on GreatSchools and ranked 13th among all Nevada high schools by U.S. News and World Report, Coronado is one of the highest performing public high schools in the Henderson area.

Academics

Coronado offers Advanced Placement (AP) courses across multiple subject areas including English, history, sciences, mathematics, and world languages. The school's AP participation rate and pass rate exceed the Clark County School District averages. Coronado also offers honors level courses for students seeking accelerated academic pathways. The school serves approximately 2,500 to 3,000 students across grades 9 through 12 and has maintained strong graduation rates and college enrollment numbers.

Athletics and Activities

Coronado competes in the 5A classification of Nevada Interscholastic Activities Association (NIAA) sports. The Cougars athletic program spans football, basketball, baseball, softball, soccer, volleyball, tennis, golf, swimming, track and field, and wrestling. Coronado has produced state championships across multiple sports and is consistently competitive in conference play. The school also offers robotics, debate, performing arts, JROTC, and numerous academic and social clubs.

Location and Zoning

The campus sits in the southern portion of the Green Valley area, drawing students from Green Valley Ranch, Seven Hills, and surrounding Henderson neighborhoods in the 89052 zip code. Del E. Webb Middle School (9/10 GreatSchools rating) feeds into Coronado, as do several elementary schools rated 8 to 10 out of 10. This feeder pattern creates a strong K through 12 educational pipeline that is a major selling point for family homebuyers in the area.
