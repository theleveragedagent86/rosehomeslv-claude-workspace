Elise L. Wolff Elementary School is located at 1001 Seven Hills Drive in Henderson (89052), serving students in kindergarten through 5th grade from the Seven Hills and Green Valley Ranch communities. Rated a perfect 10 out of 10 on GreatSchools, Wolff Elementary is one of the highest rated public elementary schools in the entire Las Vegas valley.

Academic Excellence

Wolff Elementary's 10 out of 10 rating reflects exceptional performance across standardized assessments in math, reading, and science. The school consistently outperforms Clark County and Nevada state averages in proficiency rates. The teaching staff emphasizes both academic rigor and student support, with programs designed to challenge advanced learners while providing resources for students who need additional assistance. Class sizes are managed within Clark County School District guidelines.

Programs and Community

The school offers a comprehensive elementary curriculum that includes art, music, physical education, and technology instruction alongside core academics. The PTA is actively engaged in school events, fundraising, and community building. Annual events, science fairs, and cultural celebrations create a sense of community among families. Before and after school care programs are available to support working parents.

Feeder Pattern

Wolff Elementary feeds into Del E. Webb Middle School (9/10 on GreatSchools), which then feeds into Coronado High School (7/10, ranked 13th in Nevada). This K through 12 progression of 10/10, 9/10, and top 15 state ranked schools represents one of the strongest public school pipelines available in Southern Nevada. The consistency of quality across all three levels is a significant factor in the premium that Seven Hills and adjacent Green Valley Ranch homes command.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose identifies Wolff Elementary's 10 out of 10 rating as one of the single most impactful factors driving home demand in Seven Hills and the southern portions of Green Valley Ranch. Families relocating from states with strong public schools are often specifically searching for top rated elementary options, and Wolff consistently delivers at the highest level. Homes within this school zone are among the fastest selling in the Henderson market.
