Discovery Park is located at 2011 Paseo Verde Parkway in Henderson (89012), offering one of the most diverse recreational experiences among Green Valley's parks. The park combines active sports facilities, a BMX track, dog park, playgrounds, and walking paths into a single multi use destination that serves families, athletes, and pet owners alike.

BMX Track

Discovery Park's BMX track is one of the few public BMX facilities in the Las Vegas valley, attracting riders from across Henderson and beyond. The track features berms, jumps, and straightaways suitable for riders of varying skill levels. Local BMX organizations use the track for practice sessions and organized events throughout the year. The facility provides a unique recreational option that sets Discovery Park apart from other Green Valley parks.

Sports and Play Areas

The park includes baseball and softball fields with dugouts and bleacher seating for organized league play and practice. Two playgrounds serve different age groups, with equipment designed for both younger children and older kids. Open grass areas provide space for informal sports, picnics, and general recreation. Picnic shelters with tables are available for family gatherings and group events.

Dog Park

Discovery Park's off leash dog area is one of several dog parks in the Green Valley area, providing a fenced space for dogs to run and socialize. The dog park includes separate areas for large and small dogs, water stations, and waste disposal stations. Its location within the larger park means dog owners can combine a visit to the dog park with playground time for kids or a walk on the paths.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose highlights Discovery Park as a standout example of Henderson's approach to multi use park design. The combination of a BMX track, dog park, sports fields, and playgrounds in one location means different members of a family can all find something to enjoy simultaneously. Homes within walking distance of Discovery Park, particularly in the Green Valley South and Ranch sections along Paseo Verde Parkway, benefit from this versatile amenity.
