Academic Excellence and High Achievement

John Vanderburg Elementary consistently demonstrates outstanding academic performance across all subject areas. Beyond academics, John Vanderburg emphasizes social, emotional, and physical development. The school recognizes that strong fundamentals in elementary school create foundations for lifelong success, so faculty invest in whole child development rather than academics alone.
