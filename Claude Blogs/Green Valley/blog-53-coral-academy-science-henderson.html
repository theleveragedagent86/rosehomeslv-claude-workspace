Nevada State High School Henderson

Coral Academy of Science Henderson represents the pinnacle of educational excellence in Southern Nevada, earning recognition as Nevada's number one charter high school and ranking fourth overall in the state. With multiple campuses throughout the Henderson area, including the Sandy Ridge Campus serving grades 8 through 12 and the Windmill Campus serving grades 5 through 7, both rated a perfect 10 out of 10 on GreatSchools, Coral Academy provides exceptional STEM focused education for ambitious students. Families relocating to Henderson specifically seek neighborhoods with access to this prestigious institution.

World Class STEM Curriculum

Coral Academy of Science Henderson centers its educational philosophy around science, technology, engineering, and mathematics with rigorous coursework and hands on learning experiences. Students engage in advanced laboratory work, coding, robotics projects, and cutting edge scientific research. The school's STEM emphasis prepares students not just for college success but for leadership roles in technology and science fields. Teachers hold advanced degrees in their subject areas and bring professional expertise from STEM industries into the classroom, creating an educational experience that mirrors real world applications.

Competitive Science and Robotics Programs

Beyond traditional academics, Coral Academy maintains highly competitive Science Olympiad teams, robotics programs, and mathematics teams that consistently rank among Nevada's finest. These clubs and competitions attract gifted and talented students who thrive in collaborative problem solving environments. Students develop project management skills, learn to work effectively on teams, and gain invaluable experience competing at regional, state, and national levels. These extracurricular programs supplement classroom learning and provide opportunities for students to apply their knowledge in meaningful ways that build resumes and create pathways to prestigious universities.

College Preparation and Future Success

Coral Academy's rigorous curriculum and strong academic culture prepare students for success at elite universities and competitive college programs. The school's focus on critical thinking, scientific inquiry, and technical skills aligns with the demands of higher education and modern careers. Students graduating from Coral Academy possess the knowledge, skills, and confidence to excel in college level coursework and pursue demanding careers in STEM fields. The school's track record of college acceptance and student achievement demonstrates the effectiveness of its educational approach.
