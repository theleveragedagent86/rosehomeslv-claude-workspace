Green Valley Henderson for Families

Nevada State High School in Henderson represents an innovative approach to secondary education, combining traditional high school academics with college level coursework through partnerships with local universities. Located at 233 N Stephanie St in Henderson's 89014 zip code, this Newsweek Top 100 ranked school empowers students to earn an associate's degree by the time they graduate from high school at no cost. With a GreatSchools rating of 9 out of 10, Nevada State High School offers an exceptional educational opportunity for college bound students seeking a head start on their university careers.

Free College Coursework During High School

One of Nevada State High School's most distinctive features is its partnership with College of Southern Nevada, University of Nevada Las Vegas, and Nevada State University that allows students to enroll in free college courses while still in high school. This dual enrollment program enables motivated students to accumulate college credits without paying tuition, generating significant savings for families and accelerating students toward degree completion. By graduation, students can have earned a full associate's degree, placing them ahead of their peers and opening doors to accelerated bachelor's degree programs.

Career and Academic Readiness

The school's college aligned curriculum ensures that students develop the academic skills, work ethic, and time management abilities necessary to succeed in higher education. Students experience college classes, interact with college instructors, and navigate college expectations while still in high school where they receive additional support. This bridge program significantly increases student persistence in college and improves degree completion rates. Students graduate with the confidence and preparation to pursue bachelor's degrees, professional certifications, and fulfilling careers in competitive fields.
