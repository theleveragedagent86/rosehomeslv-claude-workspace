History of Green Valley Henderson

Touro University Nevada stands as a prestigious institution of higher education located right in the heart of Green Valley Henderson. Situated at 11 Sunset Way, this university has become a cornerstone of medical and health sciences education in Southern Nevada, attracting students from across the nation to pursue careers in healthcare. For families considering Green Valley as their home, having a world-class university in the community adds significant value to the area and creates opportunities for education and professional development.

Academic Programs at Touro University Nevada

Touro University Nevada offers comprehensive graduate and professional programs focused on health sciences and education. The university operates a College of Osteopathic Medicine that prepares students for careers as osteopathic physicians, emphasizing a whole-body approach to patient care. Beyond medicine, Touro offers a College of Nursing with programs ranging from entry-level to advanced practice roles. The Physician Assistant Program trains students to work alongside physicians in clinical settings, while the College of Education develops future educators for K-12 schools throughout Nevada and beyond. These programs consistently rank among the top in the nation for their innovation and student outcomes.

Impact on the Green Valley Community

The presence of Touro University Nevada has transformed Green Valley into an education hub within Henderson. The university employs hundreds of faculty and staff members, contributing to the local economy and bringing a diverse, intellectually engaged population to the area. Students and faculty members patronize local businesses, restaurants, and services, creating additional economic benefits. The institution also collaborates with local healthcare providers and schools, strengthening community partnerships and creating internship and employment opportunities for Green Valley residents.

Campus and Student Life

Touro's Green Valley campus provides state-of-the-art facilities designed for hands-on learning and research. The location offers students proximity to some of Nevada's best healthcare facilities and school districts, providing valuable clinical rotation and practicum opportunities. Students benefit from Green Valley's family-friendly atmosphere, excellent schools, and recreational amenities. The campus is well-integrated into the community, with students and faculty participating in local events and volunteer initiatives throughout Henderson.
