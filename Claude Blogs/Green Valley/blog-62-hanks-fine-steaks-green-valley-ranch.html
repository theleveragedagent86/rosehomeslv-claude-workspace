A Restaurant with History and Heritage

Hank's Fine Steaks carries a name rich in Vegas history, honoring Hank Greenspun, the legendary Las Vegas publisher and businessman. Greenspun's influence shaped much of modern Las Vegas development and innovation, and the restaurant celebrates this legacy through its refined approach to dining. The connection to such an important figure in Vegas history adds a layer of authenticity and prestige to the Hank's experience, making it more than just another steakhouse.

The Fine Dining Experience

What truly sets Hank's apart is its commitment to classic fine dining excellence. The restaurant specializes in premium steaks prepared with expert technique and the finest ingredients. Whether you're selecting a perfectly aged ribeye, a tender filet mignon, or a succulent tomahawk steak, each cut receives the attention and care that steakhouse perfection demands. The kitchen's craftsmanship shines through in every dish.

The Piano Bar Atmosphere

Hank's elevates the dining experience with a sophisticated piano bar that creates an ambiance perfect for special occasions, romantic dinners, or business entertainment. This combination of exceptional food and refined entertainment transforms a meal into a memorable evening.
