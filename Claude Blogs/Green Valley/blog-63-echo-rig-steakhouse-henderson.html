Green Valley Ranch Resort in Henderson

Echo and Rig Steakhouse represents a modern approach to fine dining, combining the expertise of an upscale restaurant with the craft of an artisanal butcher shop. Located at The District at Green Valley Ranch, this innovative establishment brings a fresh concept to Henderson's dining scene. For residents and visitors seeking exceptional meat dishes and a transparent view into quality sourcing, Echo and Rig delivers an experience that goes beyond traditional steakhouse dining.

The Steakhouse and Butcher Concept

Echo and Rig's unique dual purpose model sets it apart from conventional restaurants. By combining steakhouse dining with an on site butcher shop, the venue offers patrons visibility into the sourcing and preparation of the finest cuts. This transparency appeals to discerning meat enthusiasts who appreciate understanding where their food comes from and how it's prepared. Customers can browse premium selections at the butcher counter and then enjoy those same high quality cuts prepared in the restaurant's kitchen.

Premium Quality and Sourcing

The integration of a butcher shop allows Echo and Rig to maintain exceptional standards in meat selection and quality. The butchers work directly with the restaurant to ensure only the finest cuts reach both the dining room and the retail counter. This vertical integration between butchering and cooking creates a level of quality control that elevates every dish served. Diners benefit from this commitment to excellence with steaks and preparations that reflect careful attention to every detail.

The District Location

Echo and Rig's placement within The District at Green Valley Ranch puts it at the heart of Henderson's entertainment and dining hub. The District has established itself as a premier destination for shopping, dining, and entertainment, and Echo and Rig fits perfectly within this community gathering space. The convenient location makes it easily accessible for quick steakhouse lunches or special evening celebrations.

A Modern Dining Philosophy

Rather than relying on old steakhouse traditions, Echo and Rig embraces contemporary approaches to meat preparation and dining experience. This modern philosophy attracts diners seeking innovation alongside quality, those who want exceptional food without the stuffiness sometimes associated with traditional fine dining. The atmosphere balances sophistication with approachability.
