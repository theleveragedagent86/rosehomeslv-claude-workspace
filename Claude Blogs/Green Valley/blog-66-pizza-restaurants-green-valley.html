Pizza Rock at Green Valley Ranch Resort

Pizza Rock stands as a premier destination for pizza lovers throughout Henderson. Located at the Green Valley Ranch Resort, this acclaimed restaurant was founded by Chef Tony Gemignani, a world champion pizza maker who has elevated pizza craftsmanship to an art form. The kitchen features multiple traditional wood-fired ovens imported from Italy, each one contributing to the restaurant's authentic preparation of pizzas that honor centuries of Italian tradition. The menu showcases signature pies alongside creative offerings that highlight seasonal ingredients and Chef Gemignani's innovative approach to flavor combinations. The upscale casual atmosphere provides the perfect setting for family dinners, date nights, or special celebrations. The craft beverage program complements the pizza selections with carefully curated wines, beers, and cocktails. Pizza Rock has become a culinary landmark that puts Green Valley on the map as a destination for serious food enthusiasts.

Settebello Pizzeria Napoletana at The District

Settebello Pizzeria Napoletana brings authentic Neapolitan pizza traditions to The District at Green Valley Ranch. This restaurant specializes in pizzas made with imported Italian flour, San Marzano tomatoes, and fresh mozzarella di bufala, prepared in a traditional wood-fired oven that reaches proper Neapolitan cooking temperatures. Every pizza is crafted by skilled pizzaiolos who have trained in the time honored techniques of Naples, Italy. The menu features classic Neapolitan varieties as well as seasonal specials that reflect Italian culinary principles. The dining room captures the charm of an Italian pizzeria with warm lighting, rustic decor, and an open kitchen design that allows diners to watch the pizza making process. Settebello's commitment to authenticity and quality has made it a favorite among Green Valley residents seeking genuine Italian pizza experiences without traveling to Italy.
