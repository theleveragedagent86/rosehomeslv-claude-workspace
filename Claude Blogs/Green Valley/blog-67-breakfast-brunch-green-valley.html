Lucky Penny Café at Green Valley Ranch

Lucky Penny Café operates as a 24-hour dining destination at the Green Valley Ranch Resort, making it an ideal choice for breakfast at any time of day or night. This casual, welcoming restaurant serves all the classic breakfast favorites including eggs prepared every way, pancakes, waffles, French toast, breakfast burritos, and house-made pastries. The menu also offers healthier options for guests prioritizing nutrition while still enjoying satisfying morning meals. The coffee program features premium blends and specialty espresso drinks prepared by skilled baristas. The bright, comfortable dining room and efficient service make Lucky Penny Café a go-to spot for Green Valley families needing a quick breakfast or wanting to linger over the morning paper. The 24-hour availability appeals to those with non traditional schedules who still crave a proper breakfast experience.
