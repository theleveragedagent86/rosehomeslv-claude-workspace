Lee's Family Forum stands as Henderson's premier sports and entertainment arena, hosting world-class athletic events that draw fans from across Southern Nevada. This state-of-the-art facility has become the cultural hub for sports enthusiasts and families seeking exciting entertainment options. For Green Valley residents, the proximity to such a versatile venue adds tremendous value to community living, offering easy access to professional sports, concerts, and major events without lengthy commutes.

Home of the Henderson Silver Knights

The most prominent tenant at Lee's Family Forum is the Henderson Silver Knights, an ECHL professional hockey team that serves as the Golden Knights' primary feeder team. This affiliation means Green Valley residents can watch high-level hockey performances featuring players developing toward NHL careers. The Silver Knights provide an exciting alternative to downtown venues, bringing professional sports entertainment directly to the Henderson community and establishing the city as a serious sports destination.

Multi-Sport and Entertainment Hub

Beyond hockey, Lee's Family Forum hosts an impressive array of professional sports teams and events. The Vegas Knight Hawks bring arena football to the facility, delivering fast-paced action perfect for family outings. The NBA G-League Ignite, the league's elite development team, plays at the forum, attracting basketball fans interested in watching future NBA stars. Additionally, the Vegas Thrill of the Pro Volleyball Federation competes here, showcasing world-class women's professional volleyball. This diverse sports calendar ensures something entertaining occurs throughout the year.

A Community Entertainment Asset

Lee's Family Forum extends beyond sports, hosting concerts, conventions, graduation ceremonies, and corporate events. The versatile arena accommodates entertainment of all types, making it an essential part of Henderson's event infrastructure. Families living in Green Valley benefit from having such a comprehensive entertainment venue within easy reach, providing entertainment options that rival much larger metropolitan areas.

Impact on Green Valley Living

The presence of major professional sports teams and events significantly enhances the lifestyle appeal of living in Green Valley. Residents can support local teams, enjoy professional-quality entertainment, and participate in a vibrant sports culture without leaving Henderson. This contributes to community pride and offers excellent entertainment destinations for families with diverse interests.
