Sun City Anthem Henderson stands as one of the premier age 55+ communities in Southern Nevada, offering an unmatched lifestyle for active retirees and older adults. Located in the heart of Green Valley, this Del Webb masterpiece combines resort style amenities with a vibrant social environment that keeps residents engaged and connected. Whether you're considering retirement in Henderson or looking to downsize, Sun City Anthem deserves serious consideration.

The Scale and Scope of Sun City Anthem

Sun City Anthem is Del Webb's largest community, featuring over 7,144 homes built between 1998 and 2008. This substantial development spans across multiple neighborhoods, each offering distinct architectural styles and price points. The sheer size creates a thriving community ecosystem where you'll find genuine neighbors, established friendships, and countless opportunities for social engagement. The long construction timeline ensured thoughtful planning and infrastructure development that supports the entire community's needs.

Exceptional Clubhouse Amenities

Three impressive clubhouses totaling over 130,000 square feet serve as the community's social heart. These aren't basic recreation centers. They feature state of the art fitness facilities, multiple swimming pools, restaurants, ballrooms, and entertainment venues. The Anthem is the main clubhouse and houses an acclaimed restaurant, fitness center, and event spaces. The Sonata clubhouse offers additional pools and recreation areas, while the Cachet clubhouse rounds out the amenities portfolio. Beyond these anchor facilities, you'll discover over 60 chartered clubs ranging from golf leagues to art groups, hobby enthusiasts, and adventure travel clubs.

Championship Golf at Your Doorstep

Golf enthusiasts will appreciate the Revere Golf Club, an exclusive 36 hole championship golf course designed exclusively for Sun City Anthem residents. With two distinct courses offering varying difficulty levels and stunning desert vistas, this amenity adds tremendous value to your investment. Whether you're a serious golfer or casual player, the Revere provides endless opportunities for both competition and recreation. The courses consistently rank among the best in the Las Vegas valley.

Price Range and Inventory

Sun City Anthem properties range from approximately $350,000 to over $2 million, offering options for various budgets and preferences. Homes range from single story cottages and townhomes to spacious multi level estates with luxury finishes. This broad price spectrum ensures both first time retirees and affluent empty nesters find their perfect fit. Market inventory fluctuates, but the community's popularity ensures consistent options for serious buyers.

Location and Accessibility

Located at 2450 Hampton Road, Henderson 89052, Sun City Anthem sits conveniently near shopping, dining, and medical facilities. The Green Valley location provides easy access to the Boulder Highway, US 95, and Henderson's main thoroughfares. Major shopping at District at Green Valley and restaurants throughout the area are minutes away. St. Rose Hospital is nearby for healthcare needs, and the community sits within acclaimed school districts serving the broader Henderson area.
