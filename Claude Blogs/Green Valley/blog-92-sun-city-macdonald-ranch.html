Sun City MacDonald Ranch represents another jewel in Del Webb's impressive portfolio of 55+ communities, offering golfers and active retirees a distinctive lifestyle nestled against the scenic Black Mountain landscape. With over 2,500 homes and an 18 hole championship course, MacDonald Ranch combines natural desert beauty with exceptional amenities designed for the discerning adult community. This established neighborhood delivers the perfect balance of recreation, community, and mountain scenery.

A Desert Golf Community Like No Other

Sun City MacDonald Ranch is a purpose built golf community where the Desert Willow Golf Course serves as the centerpiece. This 18 hole championship course features a Par 60 layout and operates as a semi private facility available to residents and select outside members. The course was thoughtfully designed to integrate with the natural desert topography, offering scenic views of Black Mountain and surrounding peaks. Every hole presents interesting strategic challenges while maintaining accessibility for players of all skill levels.

The Desert Willow Experience

Desert Willow Golf Course spans meticulously maintained fairways that wind through native desert landscaping. The Par 60 layout makes it ideal for residents who prefer shorter rounds or want to play multiple times weekly. The course features multiple sets of tees accommodating different abilities, ensuring both newer golfers and accomplished players find the game enjoyable. Practice facilities include a driving range and chipping area, and the clubhouse provides dining and social gathering space overlooking the course. The semi private status gives residents priority tee times and member rates.

Community and Lifestyle Benefits

Beyond golf, Sun City MacDonald Ranch includes amenities common to Del Webb communities. Residents enjoy clubhouse facilities, fitness centers, pools, and organized activities. The neighborhood feel remains strong with neighbors bonded by shared interests in golf and active retirement living. Home styles range from single story cottages to multi bedroom estates, accommodating various household sizes and budgets. The established community means mature landscaping, neighborhood character, and the comfort of living where infrastructure is proven and reliable.

Location Near Black Mountain

The proximity to Black Mountain provides a distinctive advantage. Residents enjoy scenic vistas, protected open space, and natural buffer from development. This location positions MacDonald Ranch slightly east and south from central Henderson, offering a more secluded feel while maintaining reasonable access to shopping, dining, and medical services. The natural setting appeals to those valuing desert beauty and outdoor orientation in their retirement years. The mountain backdrop creates a unique community identity within the broader Las Vegas valley.

Pricing and Inventory

Sun City MacDonald Ranch pricing typically reflects its golf community status and established reputation. Properties range from accessible entry level homes to luxury estates, with pricing influenced by specific location within the community, home size, and condition. Like other established 55+ communities, inventory shifts seasonally as residents transition and new owners arrive. The community's consistent appeal ensures steady market activity and recognized resale value for property owners.
