Hospital Overview and Accreditation

Henderson Hospital operates as a 303 bed acute care facility serving Henderson and surrounding communities since its opening. The hospital maintains Joint Commission accreditation, the nation's leading healthcare organization accreditation program. This rigorous certification requires ongoing compliance with strict quality standards, safety protocols, and patient care requirements. Joint Commission accreditation represents verification that Henderson Hospital meets or exceeds national healthcare excellence benchmarks. The facility continually invests in technology, training, and infrastructure to maintain this recognition and serve its community effectively.

Emergency Department Services

The hospital's emergency department provides around the clock care for acute medical conditions, trauma, and urgent injuries. Fully staffed with emergency medicine physicians, nurses, and support personnel, the ED handles everything from minor injuries to life threatening emergencies. The department features modern diagnostic equipment including CT scanners, X ray facilities, and laboratory services enabling rapid patient assessment and treatment. When emergencies occur, knowing that advanced emergency care sits minutes away provides reassurance for Green Valley residents. The ED maintains capabilities for emergency stabilization and transfer to specialized facilities when required.

Cardiac Care Excellence

Henderson Hospital offers comprehensive cardiac services addressing heart disease, congestive heart failure, and related cardiovascular conditions. The cardiac care program includes interventional cardiology, cardiac catheterization, and coronary angiography services. For patients experiencing chest pain or cardiac emergencies, the hospital's proximity and specialized equipment mean potentially life saving treatment arrives quickly. The cardiac care team works closely with referring physicians to coordinate patient care and develop comprehensive treatment plans. This specialized service significantly benefits retirees and older adults managing cardiac health concerns common to their age group.

Location and Accessibility

Located at 1050 Galleria Drive, Henderson 89011, the hospital sits conveniently throughout the Green Valley area. The Galleria Drive location provides reasonable access from most Henderson neighborhoods, and the hospital maintains ample parking for visitors and patients. Main emergency department signage and wayfinding make it easy to locate the ED when time matters most. The hospital's central Green Valley position means residents rarely face lengthy travel times during medical emergencies. Public transportation and ride services provide additional options for patients unable to drive themselves to the facility.

Contact and Information

The main hospital phone line at (702) 963-7000 connects callers with department lines, scheduling, and information. For emergencies, always dial 911 rather than calling the hospital directly, as emergency services provide rapid transport and immediate care initiation. The hospital's website offers additional information about departments, physician directories, and services. Pre registration online streamlines future hospital visits, allowing staff to have your information available when you arrive for scheduled procedures.
