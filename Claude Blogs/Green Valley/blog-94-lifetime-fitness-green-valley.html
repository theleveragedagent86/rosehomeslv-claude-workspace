Court Sports and Recreation

Life Time operates eight professional quality tennis courts and dedicated pickleball courts, serving growing numbers of players enjoying this dynamic racquet sport. Pickleball courts accommodate the sport's explosive growth as active adults discover this engaging, less impact intensive sport. Beyond court sports, the facility hosts various recreational programs, leagues, and tournaments building community among members while providing competitive opportunities for interested players.
