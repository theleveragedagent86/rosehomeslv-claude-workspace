Parks in Green Valley Henderson NV

Henderson's WinterFest and Electric Light Parade is the city's signature holiday celebration that transforms the Water Street District into a winter wonderland. Attracting over 15,000 visitors annually, this spectacular event has become a beloved tradition for families throughout the Las Vegas valley. The parade features elaborate floats, community performances, and the main attraction: an impressive drone show that lights up the night sky above Downtown Henderson. Whether you're a longtime resident or considering a move to Green Valley, attending WinterFest is a quintessential Henderson experience that showcases the community's vibrant festive spirit.

A December Tradition

Every December, Henderson comes alive with holiday cheer when WinterFest takes over the Water Street District. The event combines the charm of a classic Christmas parade with modern technology and entertainment. Families line the streets to watch the Electric Light Parade, which features beautifully decorated floats illuminated with thousands of twinkling lights. Local businesses, nonprofits, and community organizations participate, making it a true celebration of Henderson's diverse and engaged population. The parade route winds through the heart of Downtown Henderson, creating a festive atmosphere that fills the evening air with holiday music and laughter.

Drone Show Spectacular

One of the most anticipated highlights of WinterFest is the drone show that takes flight above the Water Street District. Hundreds of synchronized drones create a breathtaking aerial display with choreographed movements and dynamic lighting effects. The show tells stories through formation changes and color patterns, creating a magical experience that's perfect for visitors of all ages. This innovative technology has elevated Henderson's holiday celebration to rival major metropolitan events, bringing something truly unique to southern Nevada. The drone show demonstrates the city's commitment to offering world class entertainment and memorable experiences for residents and guests alike.

Water Street District Location

The Water Street District is the perfect venue for WinterFest, offering plenty of space for crowds while maintaining an intimate, walkable community atmosphere. Located in the heart of Downtown Henderson, the district features restaurants, shops, and gathering spaces that come alive during the event. Before or after the parade, visitors can explore local dining options, grab hot cocoa, or enjoy other seasonal attractions. For Green Valley residents, the Water Street District is just a short drive away, making it convenient to participate in this annual celebration without traveling far from home.
