What Is Horizon's Edge Henderson NV

Horizon's Edge is a gated community of over 290 single family homes built by D.R. Horton between 2014 and 2017, nestled at the base of Black Mountain in Henderson, Nevada. This desert contemporary neighborhood offers terraced lots with sweeping valley and mountain views, making it one of the most visually striking communities in the Las Vegas metro area.

Where Is Horizon's Edge Located in Henderson?

Horizon's Edge sits in a prime south Henderson location between I-215 and US-515/I-11, giving residents quick access to the entire valley. The Las Vegas Strip is roughly 20 minutes away, Harry Reid International Airport is about 15 to 18 minutes, and downtown Henderson is just a 10 minute drive. This positioning makes daily commutes and weekend outings equally convenient.

What Types of Homes Are in Horizon's Edge?

The community is divided into two sections. The original Horizon's Edge features over 200 homes ranging from 1,910 to 3,180 square feet, with three to five bedrooms, two to three and a half bathrooms, and three car garages. Horizon's Edge South added 89 homes with slightly larger floor plans between 2,620 and 3,220 square feet, featuring three to four bedrooms and three and a half to four and a half bathrooms. Both sections showcase contemporary desert architecture with rooftop decks, sky decks, and casita guest rooms.

What Makes Horizon's Edge Different From Other Henderson Communities?

The terraced lot design is the standout feature here. Over 200 lots are built into the hillside, which means many homes enjoy unobstructed views of the Las Vegas valley without the luxury price tag. Homes currently range from the low $300,000s to the mid $500,000s. Energy efficient construction and modern architectural lines give the community a feel that is distinctly different from the typical stucco subdivisions found throughout the valley.
