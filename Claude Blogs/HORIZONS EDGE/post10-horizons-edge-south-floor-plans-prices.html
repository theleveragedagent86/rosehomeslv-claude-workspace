Horizon's Edge South Floor Plans and Prices

Horizon's Edge South in Henderson, NV offers four floor plans ranging from 2,620 to 3,220 square feet, with current resale prices falling in the low $300,000s to mid $500,000s. Built by D.R. Horton, these contemporary desert homes feature some of the most generous standard inclusions in the Henderson market.

What Are the Horizon's Edge South Floor Plans?

The 2620 plan provides 2,620 square feet with 3 bedrooms and 3.5 bathrooms. The 2820 plan offers 2,820 square feet with 3 bedrooms and 3.5 bathrooms, originally starting at $473,990. The 3020 plan delivers 3,020 square feet with 4 bedrooms and 4.5 bathrooms, originally priced from $483,990. The largest option, the 3220 plan, features 3,220 square feet with 4 bedrooms and 3.5 bathrooms, originally starting at $493,990.

What Features Come Standard in Horizon's Edge South Homes?

Every home in Horizon's Edge South includes opaque 8 foot glass panel garage doors, casita or guest room options, and multiple outdoor living spaces including up to three decks and courtyard or atrium entries. Interior finishes feature Zodiac Quartz countertops, GE stainless steel appliances, and pre wired surround sound. The Tuxedo home automation system and alarm system come standard in all plans.

How Much Do Horizon's Edge South Homes Cost Today?

Since the community is fully built out with 89 homes, all sales are resales. Current prices generally fall within the low $300,000s to mid $500,000s, depending on the floor plan, lot position, and upgrades. The terraced lots mean homes with superior mountain or valley views typically command higher prices. HOA fees for the entire Horizon's Edge community are just $50 per month, keeping ongoing costs low.
