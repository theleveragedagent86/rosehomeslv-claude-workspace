Parks Near Horizon's Edge Henderson

Horizon's Edge in Henderson, NV is surrounded by several well maintained parks, giving residents easy access to outdoor recreation, playgrounds, dog parks, and scenic trails. Whether you need a quick walk around the block or a full day outdoors, the parks near this community deliver variety and convenience.

What Parks Are Closest to Horizon's Edge?

The nearest parks to Horizon's Edge include Horizon Crest Park, Reunion Trails Park, Acacia Park, and Cornerstone Park. Horizon Crest Park sits just minutes away and features a basketball court, playground, dog park with three separate sections, and viewing platforms with panoramic city and mountain views. Reunion Trails Park covers 17 acres and offers a splash pad, electronic game playground, lighted basketball courts, and a walking track.

Which Parks Have Dog Parks and Playgrounds?

Families and pet owners will find plenty of options nearby. Acacia Park spans 16 acres and includes separate large and small dog areas, playgrounds, a splash pad, and lighted multi purpose fields. Reunion Trails Park also has a dedicated dog park alongside shaded picnic tables and a large grass field. Cornerstone Park, located at 1600 Wigwam Pkwy, adds 31 acres of open space with a lake, walking trails, wildlife viewing, and the Earth's Harmonies Art Installation.

Are There Nature or Walking Trails Near Horizon's Edge?

Yes. Cornerstone Park features walking trails and exercise stairs along its 31 acre grounds, including Railroad Lake and open grass areas. The Paseo Verde Trailhead at 804 Paseo Verde Pkwy provides a bocce ball court, playground, and picnic shelters. Horizon's Edge also sits at the base of Black Mountain, connecting residents to trail systems like the Amargosa Trail and Black Mountain Trail for more rugged outdoor adventures.
