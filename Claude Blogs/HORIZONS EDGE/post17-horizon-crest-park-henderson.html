Horizon Crest Park Henderson NV

Horizon Crest Park is a neighborhood park in Henderson, NV located at 675 Annet St, offering a basketball court, three section dog park, playground, and panoramic viewing platforms. It serves as one of the closest recreational spaces to the Horizon's Edge community.

What Amenities Does Horizon Crest Park Have?

Horizon Crest Park packs a solid range of amenities into a compact footprint. The park includes a basketball court, a playground, picnic ramadas and shelters, restrooms, and an open turf area for pickup games or lounging. Public art installations and educational kiosks add character throughout the grounds. Parking is available on site, making it easy to visit even if you live a few minutes away.

Does Horizon Crest Park Have a Dog Park?

Yes. Horizon Crest Park features a dog park divided into three separate sections, including a dedicated small dog area. This setup allows dogs of all sizes to run off leash safely. The multi section design helps reduce crowding during busy hours and gives pet owners peace of mind when bringing smaller breeds.

What Are the Views Like at Horizon Crest Park?

One feature that sets Horizon Crest Park apart from other Henderson parks is its viewing platforms. Positioned to take advantage of the park's elevation near the base of Black Mountain, these platforms offer panoramic views of the Las Vegas valley and surrounding mountain ranges. Sunset visits are especially popular with residents who enjoy the shifting light across the desert landscape.
