Reunion Trails Park Henderson NV

Reunion Trails Park is a 17 acre park in Henderson, NV located at 44 Chapata Dr, featuring a splash pad, lighted basketball courts, an electronic game playground, a dog park, and access to the Amargosa Trailhead. It is one of the largest and most versatile parks near the Horizon's Edge community.

What Amenities Does Reunion Trails Park Offer?

Reunion Trails Park covers 17 acres and delivers a wide range of recreational options. The park includes lighted basketball courts, shaded picnic tables, a large grass field, and a walking track that loops through the grounds. An electronic game playground offers interactive play equipment that goes beyond standard park structures. A splash pad provides seasonal relief from Henderson's summer heat.

Does Reunion Trails Park Have a Dog Park?

Yes. Reunion Trails Park includes a dedicated dog park where pets can run off leash. Combined with the Amargosa Trailhead access, this makes the park a strong choice for dog owners who want variety in their daily routine. You can let your dog play in the enclosed area, then take a walk along the Amargosa Trail without leaving the park grounds.

Is Reunion Trails Park Good for Families?

Reunion Trails Park is one of the most family friendly parks in the Henderson area. The splash pad keeps younger children entertained during warmer months, while the electronic game playground appeals to older kids looking for something more engaging than traditional equipment. The shaded picnic tables and large grass field make it easy to spend an entire afternoon here. Lighted courts extend usability into the evening hours.
