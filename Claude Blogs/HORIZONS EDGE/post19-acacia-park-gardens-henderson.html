Acacia Park and Demonstration Gardens Henderson

Acacia Park is a 16 acre park in Henderson, NV located at 50 Casa Del Fuego St, featuring water efficient demonstration gardens, separate dog parks for large and small dogs, a splash pad, playgrounds, and lighted sports fields. It is one of the most complete parks near the Horizon's Edge community.

What Makes Acacia Park Unique in Henderson?

Acacia Park stands out from other Henderson parks thanks to its Acacia Demonstration Gardens, which showcase water efficient landscaping techniques suited to the desert climate. These gardens serve as both a visual attraction and an educational resource for homeowners interested in sustainable yard design. The park spans 16 acres and combines this unique feature with a full suite of traditional recreational amenities.

What Amenities Are at Acacia Park?

The park includes lighted ball fields, lighted basketball courts, and lighted multi purpose fields, making it one of the few Henderson parks with extensive evening use options. Covered picnic shelters and barbecues support gatherings of all sizes. Playgrounds, open grass areas, restrooms, and picnic tables round out the core amenities. A splash pad provides seasonal water play for younger visitors.

Does Acacia Park Have a Dog Park?

Acacia Park features separate dog parks for large and small dogs, which is a notable advantage for pet owners with smaller breeds. The divided layout keeps interactions safe and lets dogs of similar sizes play together. Combined with the dog parks at nearby Horizon Crest Park and Reunion Trails Park, Horizon's Edge residents have three distinct off leash options within a short drive.
