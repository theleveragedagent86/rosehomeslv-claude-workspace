Horizon's Edge Home Prices 2026

Homes in Horizon's Edge Henderson currently range from the low $300,000s to the mid $500,000s, with the Henderson median sitting near $495,000 as of January 2026. This gated community at the base of Black Mountain continues to offer strong value for buyers seeking contemporary desert architecture without a luxury price tag.

What Do Homes Cost in Horizon's Edge Right Now?

Pricing in Horizon's Edge depends largely on which section of the community a home sits in and whether it features premium views. The original Horizon's Edge homes range from 1,910 to 3,180 square feet with three car garages, and these tend to land in the mid $300,000s to mid $500,000s. Horizon's Edge South homes, which are slightly larger at 2,620 to 3,220 square feet, generally fall in the upper portion of that range. Terraced lots with unobstructed valley views command the highest prices.

How Do Horizon's Edge Prices Compare to Henderson Overall?

The Henderson citywide median home price hovered around $495,000 in early 2026. Horizon's Edge offers a wide spread below and around that median, which is notable given the community's gated entry, contemporary design, and mountain setting. Buyers in the low $300,000s can find entry level options, while those seeking larger floor plans with rooftop decks and valley views will reach the mid $500,000s. Compared to newer master planned communities in Henderson, Horizon's Edge delivers a distinctive architectural style at a competitive price point.

Are Horizon's Edge Homes a Good Investment?

All homes in Horizon's Edge were built by D.R. Horton between 2014 and 2017, so the community is fully built out with no new construction competing for buyers. The limited inventory of just over 290 homes, combined with the unique terraced lot design, helps maintain demand. Energy efficient construction and features like sky decks and casitas add lasting appeal. HOA fees remain modest at $50 per month, keeping total ownership costs manageable.
