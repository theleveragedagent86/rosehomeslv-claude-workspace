Cornerstone Park Henderson Near Horizon's Edge

Cornerstone Park is a 31 acre destination park in Henderson, NV located at 1600 Wigwam Pkwy, featuring a lake, walking trails, wildlife viewing, public art, and a farmers market. It is one of the largest and most popular parks accessible to residents of Horizon's Edge.

What Is There to Do at Cornerstone Park?

Cornerstone Park offers more variety than most Henderson parks. The centerpiece is Railroad Lake, a 31 acre lake surrounded by walking trails and open grass areas. Lighted basketball courts and lighted volleyball courts serve athletes, while large pavilions with a warming kitchen support events and group gatherings. Exercise stairs provide a workout option along the park's terrain, and restrooms are available throughout the grounds.

Does Cornerstone Park Have Wildlife and Art?

Yes. Cornerstone Park is known for its wildlife viewing opportunities along Railroad Lake, where birds and other desert wildlife are regularly spotted. The Earth's Harmonies Art Installation adds a creative element to the landscape, making the park popular with visitors looking for more than just sports fields. The combination of nature and public art gives Cornerstone Park a character that most community parks simply do not offer.

Is There a Farmers Market at Cornerstone Park?

Cornerstone Park hosts a farmers market that draws residents from across Henderson. The market provides access to local produce, prepared foods, and artisan goods in a scenic outdoor setting alongside Railroad Lake. The corporate plaza area and large pavilions give the market ample space. For Horizon's Edge residents, it adds a regular community event within a short drive of home.

A Local Perspective on Cornerstone Park

Ryan Rose considers Cornerstone Park the crown jewel of Henderson's park system for Horizon's Edge residents. The lake, the art installation, and the farmers market create a park experience that feels more like a small nature preserve than a typical suburban amenity.
