Black Mountain Trail Near Horizon's Edge

Black Mountain Trail is a 6.8 mile out and back hiking trail located just minutes from Horizon's Edge in Henderson, NV, offering some of the most stunning panoramic views in the Las Vegas valley. If you live in Horizon's Edge or are considering the neighborhood, this trail is one of the biggest outdoor perks right at your doorstep.

What Can You Expect on Black Mountain Trail?

Black Mountain Trail sits within the Sloan Canyon National Conservation Area, making it one of the most scenic desert hikes accessible from any Henderson neighborhood. The 6.8 mile route is rated strenuous, with class 2 rock scrambling sections near the summit that reward hikers with sweeping views of the Las Vegas valley, Red Rock Canyon, and the Spring Mountains. The best time to tackle this trail is between September and May, when temperatures are more manageable.

Is Black Mountain Trail Dog Friendly?

Yes, dogs are welcome on Black Mountain Trail as long as they are kept on a leash. This makes it a great option for active pet owners in Horizon's Edge who want to bring their four legged companions along for a challenging desert hike. Just be sure to bring plenty of water for both you and your dog, especially during warmer months.

Local Insight

One thing only locals know: the trailhead can get crowded on weekend mornings during peak season. If you want the summit views to yourself, aim for a weekday morning start. The trail connects to the broader Sloan Canyon NCA network, so experienced hikers can extend the adventure well beyond the standard route.
