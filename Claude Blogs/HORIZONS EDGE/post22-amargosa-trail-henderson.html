Amargosa Trail Henderson NV

The Amargosa Trail is a paved, multi mile walking and biking path that runs through Henderson, NV, connecting multiple parks and trailheads along the south border of Paseo Verde Parkway. For residents of Horizon's Edge and surrounding communities, it serves as a central artery for outdoor recreation.

Where Does the Amargosa Trail Go?

The Amargosa Trail follows the south side of Paseo Verde Parkway, linking several Henderson parks and community trailheads into one continuous route. Its paved surface makes it accessible to walkers, joggers, cyclists, and families with strollers. Lighted sections of the trail are open from 5 AM to 11 PM, giving early risers and evening exercisers plenty of flexibility.

Is the Amargosa Trail Good for Families?

Absolutely. The flat, paved design makes the Amargosa Trail one of the most family friendly outdoor options in Henderson. Parents with young children appreciate the smooth surface and the connections to nearby parks with playgrounds and picnic areas. The lighted portions also add a layer of safety for those walking after sunset.

Horizon's Edge sits in a prime location for trail access, with the Amargosa Trail running nearby along Paseo Verde Parkway.
