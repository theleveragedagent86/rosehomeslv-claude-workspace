Paseo Verde Trail Henderson NV

The Paseo Verde Trail is a paved multi use path that runs along Paseo Verde Parkway in Henderson, NV, stretching from St. Rose Parkway to Valle Verde Parkway. It is one of the most popular walking and biking routes for residents of Horizon's Edge and the surrounding Henderson communities.

What Does the Paseo Verde Trail Pass Through?

The Paseo Verde Trail connects several key Henderson landmarks along its route. As you travel the path, you will pass by multiple parks, the District at Green Valley Ranch shopping center, the Henderson Multigenerational Center, and the Henderson Pavilion. This makes it more than just a fitness trail; it is a practical corridor that links daily destinations on foot or by bike.

Is the Paseo Verde Trail Good for Biking?

Yes, the Paseo Verde Trail is fully paved and designed for both walkers and cyclists. The smooth surface and wide path accommodate multiple users comfortably, making it ideal for casual bike rides, commuting to nearby shops, or weekend family outings. Cyclists appreciate the relatively flat terrain and the scenic desert landscaping along the route.

How Does the Paseo Verde Trail Benefit Horizon's Edge Residents?

Having a trail of this caliber nearby is a real asset for homeowners in Horizon's Edge. Ryan Rose, a Henderson real estate specialist, points out that walkable access to trails and community amenities consistently ranks among the top features buyers look for in this part of the valley. The Paseo Verde Trail delivers exactly that kind of everyday convenience.
