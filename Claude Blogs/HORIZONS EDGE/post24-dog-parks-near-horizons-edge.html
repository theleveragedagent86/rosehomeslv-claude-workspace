Dog Parks Near Horizon's Edge Henderson

Horizon's Edge in Henderson, NV has three dog parks within close reach, each with separate areas for large and small dogs. If you are a pet owner looking for off leash options near this neighborhood, you have excellent choices.

Which Dog Parks Are Closest to Horizon's Edge?

The three dog parks nearest to Horizon's Edge are Horizon Crest Park, Reunion Trails Park, and Acacia Park. Horizon Crest Park at 675 Annet Street features three separate sections, including a dedicated small dog area. Reunion Trails Park at 44 Chapata Drive offers its own dog park, and Acacia Park at 50 Casa Del Fuego Street provides separate large and small dog enclosures.

Does Horizon Crest Park Have a Small Dog Area?

Yes, Horizon Crest Park includes a designated small dog section as part of its three section dog park layout. This separation gives owners of smaller breeds peace of mind while their dogs play off leash. The park is one of the closest amenities to Horizon's Edge, making it a convenient daily stop for dog owners in the neighborhood.

Are There Trails Near Horizon's Edge That Allow Dogs?

Beyond the fenced dog parks, Horizon's Edge residents can also take leashed dogs on Black Mountain Trail, a 6.8 mile hiking route within the Sloan Canyon National Conservation Area. Ryan Rose, who works with homebuyers throughout Henderson, frequently highlights the combination of dog parks and dog friendly trails as one of the features that sets Horizon's Edge apart for pet owners.

Local Insight

For dog owners who want variety, the best routine is rotating between the three parks depending on the day. Horizon Crest tends to be busiest on weekend mornings, while Acacia Park sees more activity in the late afternoon. Reunion Trails Park is often the quietest of the three, making it a solid choice if your dog prefers a calmer environment.
