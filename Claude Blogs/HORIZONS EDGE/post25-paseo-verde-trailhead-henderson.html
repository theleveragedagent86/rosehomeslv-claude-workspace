Paseo Verde Trailhead Henderson NV

The Paseo Verde Trailhead is a fully equipped recreation hub located at 804 Paseo Verde Parkway in Henderson, NV, offering much more than just trail access. For Horizon's Edge residents, it serves as a convenient gathering spot with amenities that appeal to families, fitness enthusiasts, and casual visitors alike.

What Amenities Does the Paseo Verde Trailhead Have?

The Paseo Verde Trailhead goes well beyond a simple trail entrance. The facility includes a bocce ball court, open grass areas, picnic shelters with tables, a playground, and public restrooms. This combination of features makes it a destination in its own right, whether you plan to hit the trail or simply spend an afternoon outdoors with family.

Where Is the Paseo Verde Trailhead Located?

You will find the Paseo Verde Trailhead at 804 Paseo Verde Parkway, Henderson, NV 89012. It sits along the Paseo Verde Trail corridor, connecting to the broader network of paved paths that run through this part of Henderson. The location provides easy access from Horizon's Edge and neighboring communities without a long drive.

Why Is the Paseo Verde Trailhead Popular with Families?

The playground and picnic shelters make this trailhead especially appealing to families with young children. Parents can let kids play while older family members walk or bike the connecting trails. Ryan Rose, a Henderson real estate expert, notes that amenities like the Paseo Verde Trailhead are a major draw for families relocating to the Horizon's Edge area, as they provide free, close to home recreation options year round.

Local Insight

The bocce ball court at Paseo Verde Trailhead is one of the few public courts in Henderson, and it tends to fly under the radar. Weekend mornings are a great time to claim a court and enjoy a game before the afternoon heat sets in. The restrooms on site also make this trailhead a practical starting point for longer walks along the Paseo Verde and Amargosa trail systems.
