Ulis Newton Elementary School in Henderson, NV: What Parents Should Know

Ulis Newton Elementary School is the zoned K through 5 school for families in Horizon's Edge, and it consistently ranks in the top 20% of all Nevada schools. If you are considering a move to this Henderson neighborhood, the quality of the local elementary school is one of the strongest selling points.

How Does Ulis Newton Elementary Score Compared to Other Henderson Schools?

Ulis Newton Elementary holds a B+ rating on Niche and a 6 out of 10 on GreatSchools. What makes these numbers stand out is the academic performance relative to the surrounding district. Math proficiency sits at 49.4% and reading proficiency reaches 58.9%, both more than 15 points above the Clark County School District average. Those margins tell you this school is doing something right in the classroom.

What Grades Does Ulis Newton Elementary Serve?

The school serves students in kindergarten through fifth grade. Families in the Horizon's Edge community are zoned directly for Ulis Newton, which means no lottery or application process is required. The school feeds into Jack and Terry Mannion Middle School, another highly rated campus in the area.

Why Do Families in Horizon's Edge Choose Ulis Newton?

Proximity and performance are the two biggest factors. Being the zoned school for Horizon's Edge means short commutes for families, and the above average test scores give parents confidence in the instruction their children receive.
