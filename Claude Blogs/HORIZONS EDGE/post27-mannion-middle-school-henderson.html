Mannion Middle School Henderson NV: The Highest Scoring Middle School in Henderson

Jack and Terry Mannion Middle School is the highest scoring comprehensive middle school in Henderson, NV, and it is the zoned middle school for families living in Horizon's Edge. What Are Mannion Middle School's Ratings and Rankings?

Mannion Middle School holds a B+ grade on Niche and earned a 9 out of 10 from GreatSchools, placing it among the top middle schools in the Las Vegas metro area. U.S. News ranked it #36 among all middle schools in Nevada. The school also carries a 5 star rating from the state of Nevada, which is the highest designation a school can receive.

What Grades Does Mannion Middle School Serve?

Mannion serves students in grades 6 through 8. Students from Ulis Newton Elementary, the zoned elementary school for Horizon's Edge, feed directly into Mannion. After completing eighth grade, students move on to Foothill High School, keeping the school pipeline entirely within the Henderson area.
