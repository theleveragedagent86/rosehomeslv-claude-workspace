Private and Charter Schools Near Horizon's Edge Henderson NV

Families in Horizon's Edge have access to several private and charter school options beyond the zoned public schools, including Calvary Chapel Green Valley Christian Academy and Pinecrest Academy of Nevada. These alternatives give parents flexibility to match their child's education to their specific values and learning style.

What Private Schools Are Near Horizon's Edge?

Calvary Chapel Green Valley Christian Academy is one of the closest private school options for Horizon's Edge families. Located at 2615 W Horizon Ridge Pkwy, it serves students in kindergarten through 12th grade with a smaller student body of approximately 129 students. That small campus size allows for more individualized attention and a tight knit school community that appeals to many Henderson families.

What Charter Schools Serve the Horizon's Edge Area?

Pinecrest Academy of Nevada, Horizon campus, is a charter school located in the Henderson area that provides a tuition free alternative to traditional public schools. Charter schools operate with more curricular flexibility than standard public schools while still being publicly funded. For families who want something different from the zoned schools but prefer not to pay private tuition, Pinecrest offers a compelling middle ground.

How Do Private and Charter Schools Compare to the Zoned Public Schools?

The zoned public schools for Horizon's Edge, including Ulis Newton Elementary and Mannion Middle School, carry strong ratings and above average test scores. Private and charter options provide different advantages: smaller class sizes, specialized curricula, or faith based instruction. Ryan Rose recommends that families tour multiple campuses before making a decision, as the best school for any child depends on individual needs and priorities.
