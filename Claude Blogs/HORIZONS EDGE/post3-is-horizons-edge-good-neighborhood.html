Is Horizon's Edge a Good Neighborhood

Yes, Horizon's Edge is a well regarded gated community in Henderson, Nevada, known for its safe streets, contemporary architecture, and terraced lots with valley and mountain views. It consistently attracts buyers who want a distinctive living experience at a reasonable price point.

How Safe Is the Horizon's Edge Area?

Henderson ranks as one of the safest cities in the country, earning the number two spot on SafeWise's 2024 Safest Cities list. The city's violent crime rate sits at just 2.1 per 1,000 residents, roughly half the national average of 4.0. Horizon's Edge adds another layer of security as a gated community, giving residents controlled access and added peace of mind.

What Are the Schools Like Near Horizon's Edge?

Families in Horizon's Edge are zoned for some of Henderson's strongest public schools. Mannion Middle School is the highest scoring middle school in Henderson, holding a 9 out of 10 on GreatSchools and a five star state rating. Ulis Newton Elementary earns a B+ on Niche and ranks in the top 20 percent statewide. Foothill High School carries a 94 percent graduation rate. Private and charter options like Pinecrest Academy Horizon are also nearby.

What Do Residents Like About Living in Horizon's Edge?

The terraced lot design is the feature residents mention most often. Over 200 lots are built into the hillside at the base of Black Mountain, providing views that feel premium without the premium cost. Homes range from the low $300,000s to the mid $500,000s, and the community's contemporary desert architecture stands apart from typical Henderson subdivisions. Amenities like rooftop decks, sky decks, and casitas make the homes feel custom. The location between I-215 and US-515/I-11 puts the Strip about 20 minutes away and the airport roughly 15 minutes out.
