What Is Pinecrest Academy of Nevada, Horizon Campus?

Pinecrest Academy is a publicly funded charter school that operates independently from the Clark County School District. The Horizon campus is located in the Henderson area, making it accessible to families in Horizon's Edge and surrounding communities. Is Pinecrest Academy Free to Attend?

Yes, Pinecrest Academy is a charter school, which means it is publicly funded and charges no tuition. Enrollment is open to any student, though admission may be subject to a lottery if demand exceeds available seats. This makes it an attractive option for families who want an alternative to the zoned schools without the financial commitment of a private institution like Calvary Chapel Green Valley Christian Academy.
