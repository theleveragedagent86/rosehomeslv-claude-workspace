Best Restaurants Near Horizon's Edge Henderson

Horizon's Edge residents have a standout lineup of restaurants right along Horizon Ridge Parkway, from upscale Italian to craft American dining. Whether you just moved in or you have lived here for years, these spots deliver quality meals within minutes of your front door.

What Are the Top Restaurants Near Horizon's Edge?

The Horizon Ridge Parkway corridor is the dining hub for this part of Henderson. Il Chianti, located at 72 W Horizon Ridge Pkwy, offers upscale Italian in a refined setting. For a more casual vibe, Giuseppe's Bar & Grille at 2630 W Horizon Ridge Pkwy pairs Italian favorites with a sports bar atmosphere. CRAFTkitchen, also nearby, focuses on locally sourced, craft driven American dishes.

Are There Any New Restaurants Near Horizon's Edge?

Hayworth is the newest addition to the scene, having opened in February 2026 at Horizon Ridge Parkway and S Stephanie Street. Chef Alex Reznik brings a Jewish and Mediterranean inspired menu to an 80 seat dining room with a 30 seat terrace. It has quickly become one of the most talked about openings in Henderson this year.

What About Mexican and Pizza Options?

AyAyAy! Mexican Cuisine at 1570 W Horizon Ridge Pkwy serves both brunch and dinner, while Fausto's Mexican Grill at 2654 W Horizon Ridge Pkwy is a reliable local favorite. Pizza lovers can choose between Carmine's Pizza Kitchen, known for authentic family recipes with over 395 Yelp reviews, and Rosati's Pizza for Chicago style pies.
