Italian Restaurants on Horizon Ridge Parkway

Horizon Ridge Parkway in Henderson is home to some of the best Italian dining in the Las Vegas valley, with at least five options ranging from casual pizza joints to upscale trattorias. Residents of Horizon's Edge and surrounding communities can enjoy authentic Italian food without ever leaving the neighborhood corridor.

Which Italian Restaurants Are on Horizon Ridge Parkway?

Il Chianti, at 72 W Horizon Ridge Pkwy Suite 100, is the go to choice for upscale Italian. The menu features traditional dishes in an elegant setting that works well for special occasions and business dinners. North Italia, located in the broader Horizon Ridge area, offers another polished Italian dining experience with a modern twist on classic recipes.

Where Can You Get Casual Italian on Horizon Ridge?

Giuseppe's Bar & Grille at 2630 W Horizon Ridge Pkwy blends a full Italian menu with a sports bar setting, making it a great spot for game nights and relaxed weeknight dinners. Carmine's Pizza Kitchen at 2520 W Horizon Ridge Pkwy is a family favorite, known for authentic recipes passed down through generations. With over 395 reviews on Yelp, it consistently draws strong praise from locals.

Is There Mediterranean Italian Dining Nearby?

Chef's Roma Kitchen at 1550 W Horizon Ridge Pkwy offers an Italian and Mediterranean fusion menu and is open Monday through Sunday from 4 PM to 2 AM. That late night schedule makes it one of the few quality dining options available after typical restaurant hours. A Local's Perspective

Having this many Italian options within a single parkway is unusual for a suburban community. It gives residents the variety you would expect in a major dining district, all within a few minutes of home. For families relocating to Henderson, the dining scene along Horizon Ridge Parkway is often a pleasant surprise during their first home tour.
