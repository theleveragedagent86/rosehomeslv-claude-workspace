What Kind of Food Does Hayworth Serve?

Hayworth specializes in Jewish and Mediterranean cuisine, a combination that is rare in the Henderson dining scene. Chef Alex Reznik brings an inventive approach to traditional dishes, blending Middle Eastern and Mediterranean flavors with modern technique. The menu is designed to appeal to both adventurous diners and those who prefer familiar comfort food with a refined touch.
