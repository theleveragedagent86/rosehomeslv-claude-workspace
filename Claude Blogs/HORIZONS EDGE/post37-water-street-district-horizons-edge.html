Water Street District Henderson Near Horizon's Edge

Water Street District is Henderson's vibrant downtown hub, located approximately four miles from Horizon's Edge. This walkable entertainment and shopping destination blends local boutiques, dining, and community events into one of the most popular gathering spots in the city.

What Shops and Stores Are at Water Street District?

Water Street District features a curated mix of independent boutiques and specialty retailers. Gold Casters offers unique jewelry finds, while Archer + Jane Kids Shop caters to families with children's clothing and gifts. The district emphasizes local and independent businesses, giving it a character that sets it apart from typical strip malls. Nearby Lake Mead Crossing adds big box options like Target, Ross, Marshall's, and Ulta for those who want both experiences in one trip.

What Events and Entertainment Does Water Street District Offer?

The district is known for its year round programming, including live music, seasonal festivals, and community gatherings. A public ice rink draws families during cooler months, and the outdoor jumbotron screens events and movies for visitors. These features make it more than a shopping destination; it functions as Henderson's social centerpiece.

How Far Is Water Street District from Horizon's Edge?

The drive from Horizon's Edge to Water Street District is roughly four miles, making it an easy 10 to 15 minute trip depending on traffic.
