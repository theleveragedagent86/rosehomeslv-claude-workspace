Horizon's Edge HOA Fees Henderson

HOA fees in Horizon's Edge Henderson are $50 per month, making this one of the most affordable gated communities in the Henderson area. That modest monthly cost covers the maintenance of common areas and the gated entry that residents value for privacy and security.

What Do the Horizon's Edge HOA Fees Cover?

The $50 monthly fee supports the upkeep of the community's shared spaces, landscaping in common areas, and the gated access points. Horizon's Edge is a relatively compact community of just over 290 homes, which helps keep costs low. There are no age restrictions, so the community welcomes families, professionals, and retirees alike. The HOA maintains the clean, contemporary aesthetic that defines the neighborhood's streetscape.

How Do Horizon's Edge HOA Fees Compare to Other Henderson Communities?

At $50 per month, Horizon's Edge sits well below the typical range for gated communities in Henderson. Many comparable neighborhoods charge $100 to $200 or more per month, particularly those with pools, clubhouses, or fitness centers. Horizon's Edge keeps fees low by focusing on essential community maintenance rather than resort style amenities. For buyers who prefer lower ongoing costs and a private, gated setting, this fee structure is a strong selling point.

Are There Any Additional Costs Beyond the HOA Fee?

Beyond the monthly HOA fee, homeowners should account for standard ownership expenses including property taxes, homeowners insurance, and individual home maintenance. Because all homes were built by D.R. Horton between 2014 and 2017 with energy efficient construction, utility costs tend to be reasonable. The homes feature modern insulation, efficient HVAC systems, and contemporary building standards that help reduce monthly energy bills. Special assessments are always possible with any HOA but are not a recurring concern in this community.
