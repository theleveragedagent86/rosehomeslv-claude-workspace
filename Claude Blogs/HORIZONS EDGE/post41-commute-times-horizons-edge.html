Commute Times From Horizon's Edge Henderson

Horizon's Edge in Henderson, NV offers some of the shortest commute times in the Las Vegas metro area, thanks to its location between I-215 and US-515/I-11. Whether you work on the Strip, near the airport, or in downtown Henderson, you can expect a quick and convenient drive from this gated community.

How Far Is Horizon's Edge From the Las Vegas Strip?

The Las Vegas Strip is approximately 14 miles from Horizon's Edge, putting your commute at roughly 20 minutes under normal traffic conditions. That is a manageable daily drive for anyone working in the hospitality or entertainment industry. Freeway access from the neighborhood makes getting onto I-215 or US-515 quick and painless.

How Long Does It Take to Get to the Airport From Horizon's Edge?

Harry Reid International Airport sits about 12 miles from the community, which translates to a 15 to 18 minute drive. For frequent travelers or anyone in the aviation industry, this proximity is a major perk. You can leave your home and be at the terminal well within half an hour, even during moderate traffic.

What About Commuting to Downtown Henderson or Downtown Las Vegas?

Downtown Henderson's Water Street District is only about 4 miles away, making it a 10 minute trip for errands, dining, or work. Downtown Las Vegas is farther at roughly 18 miles, but still reachable in about 25 minutes via the freeway. Ryan Rose, a local Henderson real estate expert, notes that the dual freeway access at Horizon's Edge is one of the features buyers appreciate most.

Local Insight

One thing many buyers overlook is that Horizon's Edge sits at the base of Black Mountain, which means you are driving downhill toward the freeway in most directions. Morning commutes feel effortless, and even during peak hours, the on ramps from this part of Henderson tend to avoid the worst bottlenecks that plague other areas of the valley.
