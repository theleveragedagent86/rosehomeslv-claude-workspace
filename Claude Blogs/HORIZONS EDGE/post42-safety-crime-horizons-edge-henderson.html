What Are the Crime Rates in Henderson NV?

Henderson's violent crime rate is just 2.1 per 1,000 residents, which is nearly half the national average of 4.0. Property crime comes in at 15.2 per 1,000, well below the national average of 20.0. Overall, Henderson's total crime rate is 23.5% lower than the Nevada state average, placing it among the safest cities of its size in the country.
