Horizon's Edge Henderson for Families

Horizon's Edge is one of the best family friendly neighborhoods in Henderson, NV, offering top rated schools, a gated community, low crime, and homes with enough space for growing households. What Schools Are Near Horizon's Edge Henderson?

The schools serving Horizon's Edge are among the highest performing in the area. Ulis Newton Elementary ranks in the top 20% statewide and holds a B+ rating on Niche. Mannion Middle School earned a 9 out of 10 on GreatSchools, making it the highest scoring middle school in Henderson. Foothill High School boasts a 94% graduation rate, giving parents confidence in the full K through 12 pipeline.

Are the Homes in Horizon's Edge Big Enough for Families?

Homes in Horizon's Edge range from 1,910 to 3,220 square feet, with both single story and two story floor plans available. That variety means families can find a layout that works whether they need a home office, a playroom, or extra bedrooms for multiple children. The HOA fee is just $50 per month, keeping monthly costs manageable for family budgets.
