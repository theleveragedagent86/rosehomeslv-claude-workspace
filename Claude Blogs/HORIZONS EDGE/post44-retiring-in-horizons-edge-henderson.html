Retiring in Horizon's Edge Henderson

Horizon's Edge in Henderson, NV is an excellent retirement destination that combines safety, healthcare proximity, and low maintenance living without restricting you to a 55+ community. Retirees here enjoy a quiet, well kept neighborhood with single story home options and easy access to everything they need.

Is Horizon's Edge a 55+ Community?

No, Horizon's Edge is not an age restricted community. It is open to buyers of all ages, which means retirees benefit from a multigenerational neighborhood rather than an isolated enclave. Single story floor plans with ground floor bedrooms are available, making these homes accessible for those who prefer to avoid stairs. Home sizes range from 1,910 to 3,220 square feet, offering plenty of space without the burden of a large estate.

How Close Is the Nearest Hospital to Horizon's Edge?

St. Rose Dominican Hospital, Siena Campus is just minutes from Horizon's Edge, located at 3001 St. Rose Parkway in Henderson. This 366 bed facility is ranked #2 in Nevada and provides comprehensive medical services. There is also an urgent care location at 2705 W Horizon Ridge Parkway, Suite 120, for non emergency visits. Having top tier healthcare this close is a significant factor for retirees choosing where to settle.

What Makes Henderson a Good City for Retirees?

Henderson's safety record speaks for itself: the city ranked #2 on SafeWise's 2024 Safest Cities list, with a violent crime rate of just 2.1 per 1,000 residents. The HOA at Horizon's Edge is only $50 per month, keeping fixed costs predictable on a retirement budget.
