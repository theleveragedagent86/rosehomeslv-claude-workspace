Horizon's Edge Amenities and Community Features

Horizon's Edge in Henderson, NV offers a curated set of amenities focused on luxury desert living, including terraced lots with valley and mountain views, rooftop sky decks, and contemporary architecture throughout. This guard gated community of roughly 290 homes delivers a boutique lifestyle that larger master planned neighborhoods simply cannot replicate.

What Amenities Does Horizon's Edge Offer Residents?

Horizon's Edge was built exclusively by D.R. Horton between 2014 and 2017, and every home reflects a commitment to energy efficient construction and modern design. Floor plans range from 1,910 to 3,220 square feet, with three car garages in the original section and two car garages in the South section. Many homes feature casitas or guest rooms, making them ideal for multigenerational living or hosting visitors.

Are There Outdoor Living Features at Horizon's Edge?

One of the most distinctive features of Horizon's Edge is the terraced lot design, which gives homeowners sweeping views of the Las Vegas Valley and the surrounding mountains. Rooftop decks and sky decks are built into many of the floor plans, providing private outdoor spaces perfect for watching sunsets over the desert. The community sits at the base of Black Mountain, adjacent to the Sloan Canyon National Conservation Area with its 1,700 plus petroglyphs.

How Much Are HOA Fees at Horizon's Edge?

HOA fees at Horizon's Edge are just $50 per month, which is remarkably low for a gated community in Henderson. There are no age restrictions, so the neighborhood welcomes families, professionals, and retirees alike. The guard gate adds a layer of security and privacy that residents consistently appreciate.
