Henderson Pavilion Near Horizon's Edge

The Henderson Pavilion is a premier outdoor amphitheater located just minutes from Horizon's Edge, offering live concerts, community events, and cultural performances throughout the year. For residents of this Henderson neighborhood, it is one of the most accessible entertainment venues in the valley.

What Is the Henderson Pavilion?

The Henderson Pavilion is an outdoor amphitheater operated by the City of Henderson, situated along the Paseo Verde Trail corridor. It hosts a wide range of events, from live music concerts and holiday celebrations to community gatherings and performing arts shows. The venue provides a relaxed, open air setting that takes full advantage of Southern Nevada's climate.

How Far Is the Henderson Pavilion From Horizon's Edge?

Horizon's Edge residents enjoy a short drive to the Henderson Pavilion, making it easy to attend evening events without a long commute home. The venue sits near the Henderson Multigenerational Center, another popular community resource that offers fitness programs, recreation, and social activities for all ages. Both facilities are connected by the Paseo Verde Trail, which also serves as a walking and cycling path.

What Other Attractions Are Near Horizon's Edge?

Beyond the Pavilion, Horizon's Edge is positioned at the base of Black Mountain and adjacent to the Sloan Canyon National Conservation Area, home to over 1,700 petroglyphs. The Las Vegas Strip is roughly 20 minutes away, and Harry Reid International Airport is about 15 to 18 minutes by car. Henderson itself was ranked the number two safest city by SafeWise in 2024, adding peace of mind to the entertainment and outdoor options nearby.
