Schools Near Horizon's Edge Henderson

Families in Horizon's Edge are zoned for some of Henderson's top rated public schools, including Mannion Middle School, the highest scoring middle school in the city. The area also offers strong private and charter school alternatives within a short drive.

What Elementary School Serves Horizon's Edge?

Ulis Newton Elementary School serves students in kindergarten through fifth grade. The school ranks in the top 20 percent statewide and holds a B+ rating on Niche with a 6 out of 10 on GreatSchools. Parents in Horizon's Edge appreciate the school's consistent performance and its proximity to the community. It provides a solid academic foundation for younger students in the neighborhood.

What Middle School Do Horizon's Edge Students Attend?

Jack and Terry Mannion Middle School is the zoned option for grades six through eight, and it is a standout. Mannion holds a five star state rating, a B+ on Niche, and a 9 out of 10 on GreatSchools, making it the highest scoring middle school in Henderson. This is often a major draw for families considering a move to Horizon's Edge. The school's academic reputation adds real value to homes in the community.

What About High School Options?

Foothill High School serves grades nine through twelve and carries a B rating on Niche. The school boasts a 94 percent graduation rate and an average SAT score of 1180. Foothill offers a well rounded experience with strong academics and extracurricular programs. For families planning long term in Horizon's Edge, the K through 12 pipeline of Newton, Mannion, and Foothill is consistently reliable.

Are There Private or Charter Schools Nearby?

Beyond public schools, families have access to Pinecrest Academy Horizon, a nearby charter school serving kindergarten through eighth grade. Calvary Chapel Green Valley Christian Academy is a private K through 12 option with smaller class sizes and an enrollment of about 129 students. Both provide alternatives for families seeking different educational approaches without a long commute from Horizon's Edge.
