Is Horizon's Edge a Gated Community?

Yes, Horizon's Edge in Henderson, NV is a gated community. Residents describe the neighborhood as gated, providing an added layer of security and privacy for homeowners in this contemporary desert community at the base of Black Mountain.

What Kind of Security Does Horizon's Edge Have?

Horizon's Edge features a guard gate that controls access to the community. This gated setup helps manage traffic flow and limits access to residents and their guests. For families and professionals who prioritize safety, the gated entry is one of the standout features that sets Horizon's Edge apart from other Henderson neighborhoods.

Does the Gated Access Affect HOA Fees?

The HOA fees at Horizon's Edge are remarkably low at just $50 per month. This is well below the average for gated communities in the Las Vegas valley, making Horizon's Edge an appealing option for buyers who want the benefits of a gated neighborhood without the hefty monthly dues. The HOA covers community maintenance and the upkeep of shared amenities.

What Else Makes Horizon's Edge Stand Out?

Beyond the gated entry, Horizon's Edge offers terraced lots with sweeping valley and mountain views, contemporary desert architecture, and homes ranging from 1,910 to 3,220 square feet. The community was built by D.R. Horton between 2014 and 2017, with over 200 homes in the original section and 89 homes in Horizon's Edge South. Prices currently range from the low $300,000s to the mid $500,000s.
