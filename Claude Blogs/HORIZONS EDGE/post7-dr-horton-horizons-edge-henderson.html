D.R. Horton Homes in Horizon's Edge Henderson

D.R. Horton is the exclusive builder of every home in Horizon's Edge, Henderson, NV. Both the original Horizon's Edge community and Horizon's Edge South were developed entirely by D.R. Horton between 2014 and 2017, offering contemporary desert architecture across more than 290 homes.

What Did D.R. Horton Build in Horizon's Edge?

In the original Horizon's Edge section, D.R. Horton built over 200 single family homes across six distinct floor plans. These range from 1,910 square feet with 4 bedrooms and 3 bathrooms up to 3,180 square feet with 4 bedrooms and 3.5 bathrooms. Every home features the builder's signature contemporary desert design with clean lines and modern finishes.

What About Horizon's Edge South?

D.R. Horton followed up with Horizon's Edge South, a smaller section of 89 homes. These plans start at 2,620 square feet and go up to 3,220 square feet. The South section included upgraded standard features such as opaque 8 foot glass panel garage doors, Zodiac Quartz countertops, GE stainless steel appliances, and the Tuxedo home automation system. Original starting prices ranged from $473,990 to $493,990.

How Do D.R. Horton Homes Hold Their Value Here?

Homes in Horizon's Edge currently range from the low $300,000s to the mid $500,000s. The community's terraced lots offer valley and mountain views, and the energy efficient construction includes tankless water heaters, 16 S.E.E.R. HVAC systems, and enhanced R15/R38 insulation. Each home also came with a 10 year structural warranty from D.R. Horton, giving buyers added confidence in the build quality.

Local Insight

Ryan Rose notes that D.R. Horton's decision to use terraced lots in Horizon's Edge was a smart move that many buyers overlook at first. The elevation changes give even smaller floor plans impressive sightlines, and the contemporary architecture stands out from the typical stucco subdivisions that dominate the Henderson market.
