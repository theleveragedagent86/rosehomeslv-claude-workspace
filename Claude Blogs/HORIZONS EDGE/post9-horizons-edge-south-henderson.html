Horizon's Edge South Henderson NV

Horizon's Edge South is a gated community of 89 contemporary desert homes in Henderson, NV, built by D.R. Horton between 2015 and 2017. Located at the base of Black Mountain, this section of the larger Horizon's Edge development features some of the most architecturally distinctive homes in the area.

What Makes Horizon's Edge South Different From the Original?

While both sections share the same gated entry and D.R. Horton craftsmanship, Horizon's Edge South was designed with upgraded standard features and larger floor plans. The South section starts at 2,620 square feet and goes up to 3,220 square feet, compared to the original's range of 1,910 to 3,180 square feet. Standard inclusions in the South section feature Tuxedo home automation systems, opaque 8 foot glass panel garage doors, and pre wired surround sound.

Where Is Horizon's Edge South Located?

Horizon's Edge South sits in the southern corridor of Henderson, positioned between I-215 and US-515/I-11. The location puts residents about 20 minutes from the Las Vegas Strip and roughly 15 to 18 minutes from Harry Reid International Airport. Black Mountain trails are accessible nearby, making the area popular with outdoor enthusiasts who want hiking access close to home.

What Are Homes Priced at in Horizon's Edge South?

Current home prices in Horizon's Edge South fall within the broader community range of the low $300,000s to the mid $500,000s. Original starting prices from D.R. Horton ranged from $473,990 for the 2820 plan to $493,990 for the 3220 plan. With the community fully built out, all purchases are now resales. HOA fees remain a modest $50 per month for the entire Horizon's Edge community.

Local Insight

Ryan Rose finds that buyers often discover Horizon's Edge South after initially searching in the original section. The South homes tend to command a slight premium due to the larger floor plans and upgraded finishes, but the price per square foot can actually be more competitive. It is one of Henderson's best kept secrets for buyers who want modern architecture without a custom home price tag.
