Inspirada is a 1,940 acre master planned community in Henderson, Nevada, designed around Italian inspired architecture, 85 acres of parks, and over 35 miles of trails. With 39 distinct neighborhoods and plans for 11,500 homes at full buildout, Inspirada has become one of the most sought after communities in the Las Vegas valley. ## Where Is Inspirada Located in Henderson? Inspirada sits in the southern portion of Henderson, Nevada, bordering the Sloan Canyon National Conservation Area. The community offers convenient access to major destinations: roughly 15 minutes to the Las Vegas Strip, 15 to 18 minutes to Harry Reid International Airport, and 15 to 20 minutes to downtown Las Vegas. Henderson itself has been ranked the #2 safest large city in the United States, adding another layer of appeal for families and professionals. ## How Big Is Inspirada and What Does It Include? The community spans seven villages of 200 to 250 acres each, with homes built by nationally recognized builders including Toll Brothers, KB Home, Tri Pointe Homes, Beazer Homes, and Century Communities. Home prices currently range from $419,000 to over $1,100,000, with a median sale price of $549,000 as of February 2026. Residents enjoy seven parks, multiple heated pools, splash pads, sports courts for tennis, pickleball, basketball, and volleyball, plus dog parks, an amphitheater, and bocce ball courts. ## Is Inspirada a Gated Community? Inspirada features partial gating, with multiple subdivisions offering guard gated entry points. The overall master plan is not entirely gated, but many individual neighborhoods within Inspirada provide that added layer of privacy and security. The community also provides roving security services throughout the development. ## What Makes Inspirada Different From Other Henderson Communities? Every home in Inspirada sits within a five minute walk of park amenities, a design principle that sets it apart from many competing master plans. The Solista Community Center offers 5,000 square feet of gathering space, while the Toll Brothers Clubhouse provides additional recreation. Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate specialist, notes that Inspirada's trail system connecting to the Sloan Canyon National Conservation Area gives residents direct access to over 1,700 Native American petroglyphs, a feature no other Henderson community can match. As a Las Vegas real estate specialist, Ryan Rose often recommends Inspirada to buyers who want a newer community with strong amenities and room to grow.
