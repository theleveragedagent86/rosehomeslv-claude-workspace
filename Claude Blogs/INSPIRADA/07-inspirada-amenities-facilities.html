Inspirada Amenities and Facilities

Inspirada offers one of the most impressive amenity packages in the Las Vegas valley, with seven parks spanning 85 acres, 35+ miles of multi-use trails, multiple heated pools, and a full slate of sports courts and community gathering spaces. Every resident lives within a five-minute walk of a park.

What Parks and Pools Does Inspirada Have?

Inspirada's seven parks each bring something different to the community. Aventura Park covers 20+ acres and features an Olympic-sized heated pool, soccer fields, four tennis courts, three basketball courts, two volleyball courts, a splash pad, a dog park, and ramadas. Solista Park includes two resort-style pools, a 5,000 square foot community center, an amphitheater, bocce ball courts, and hiking trail access. The newest addition, Sentiero Park, opened in November 2025 with basketball courts, ramadas, playgrounds, and a heated pool.

Montagna Park, which opened in May 2024, spans 26 acres with professional baseball fields, pickleball courts, dog parks, and a splash pad. Potenza Park stands out with musical-themed play elements including a ground piano and Neos interactive game system, plus a heated pool and skate area. Capriola Park and Attesa Park round out the lineup with additional pools, courts, amphitheaters, and twin dog parks.

What Fitness and Recreation Options Are Available?

Beyond the parks, the Toll Brothers Clubhouse provides a fitness center, conference room, caterer's kitchen, kids' playground, and pool access. Exercise stations line the 35+ miles of trails, giving runners and walkers built-in workout opportunities. Sports options include soccer fields, Little League baseball diamonds, basketball, tennis, volleyball, pickleball, bocce ball, and skateboarding. Ryan Rose frequently highlights the trail system as one of Inspirada's biggest selling points for active buyers.

What Community Events and Programs Does Inspirada Offer?

Inspirada runs year-round programming that brings neighbors together. The community hosts yoga sessions, outdoor film screenings, creative writing workshops, holiday celebrations, farmers markets, fitness classes, movie nights, and organized sports leagues. The Solista Community Center and multiple amphitheaters serve as hubs for these events. The community also borders Sloan Canyon National Conservation Area, home to 1,700+ petroglyphs, adding world-class hiking right at the doorstep.

A Local Perspective on Inspirada's Amenities

Few master-planned communities in the valley match Inspirada's combination of park acreage, trail mileage, and programming. The fact that seven distinct parks serve different interests means families with young kids, active adults, and pet owners all find amenities designed specifically for them. The base HOA fee of $95 per month covers access to all master plan amenities, which represents strong value given the scope of what's included.
