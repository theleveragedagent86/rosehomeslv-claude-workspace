Montagna Park Henderson NV Montagna Park is a 26 acre recreation destination in Henderson, NV, located at 3495 Via Altamira within the Inspirada master planned community. It opened on May 22, 2024, making it Henderson's 73rd public park.

What Amenities Does Montagna Park Have?

Montagna Park delivers a wide range of amenities for residents of all ages. The park features two professional sized baseball fields, pickleball courts, multi use sports fields, and modern play structures for children. A splash pad provides relief during Henderson's hot summers, while a desert revegetation area showcases native landscaping and supports local ecology. Dog parks on site give pet owners a dedicated space to let their dogs run off leash.

At 26 acres, Montagna Park is one of the largest parks in Inspirada's seven park network spanning 85 total acres.
