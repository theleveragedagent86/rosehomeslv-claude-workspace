Sentiero Park Inspirada Henderson Sentiero Park is the seventh and final park in Inspirada's master planned park system, opening in November 2025 as Henderson's 75th public park. The $4.68 million facility adds approximately 5 acres of recreation space to the community.

What Amenities Does Sentiero Park Offer?

Sentiero Park includes a lit basketball court, picnic ramadas, and age friendly playgrounds designed for multiple age groups. Shade structures and open play grass areas provide flexible space for informal recreation. The park also features restrooms and a resident only heated pool, giving Inspirada homeowners a private aquatic amenity. These features round out the community's park network with a focus on everyday convenience.

When Did Sentiero Park Open?

Sentiero Park opened in November 2025, making it one of Henderson's newest parks. The city designated it as Henderson's 75th park. Construction cost $4.68 million, funded through the community's development plans. As the final park in Inspirada's original seven park master plan, its opening marked the completion of a recreation vision that spans 85 total acres across the community.

How Does Sentiero Park Connect to Inspirada's Trail System?

Like all Inspirada parks, Sentiero Park ties into the community's 35+ mile paved trail network. Residents can walk, bike, or jog to the park from surrounding neighborhoods using connected pathways with exercise stations and distance markers along the route. The centralized open space corridor ensures most residents live within a five minute walk of the park or another outdoor amenity.
