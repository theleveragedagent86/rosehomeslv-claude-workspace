Inspirada Dog Parks Henderson Inspirada has four dedicated dog parks spread across its community in Henderson, NV, making it one of the most pet friendly master planned neighborhoods in the Las Vegas valley. Henderson has been a certified pet friendly city by Better Cities For Pets (Mars Petcare) since 2019.

Which Inspirada Parks Have Dog Parks?

Four of Inspirada's seven parks include dedicated off leash dog areas. Aventura Park features separate large and small dog sections, letting owners choose the right environment for their pet. Attesa Park offers twin dog parks, doubling the capacity for canine recreation. Capriola Park includes its own dog park, and Montagna Park added dog parks when it opened in 2024. This distribution means most Inspirada residents live within a short walk of an off leash area.

Are Inspirada's Trails Dog Friendly?

Beyond the fenced dog parks, Inspirada's 35+ mile paved trail system is fully dog friendly for leashed walks. The trails connect all seven parks and surrounding neighborhoods, providing scenic routes with exercise stations, distance markers, and rest areas. Dog owners can plan long walks through the community's centralized open space corridor without needing to drive anywhere.

What Makes Henderson a Pet Friendly City?

Henderson earned its Better Cities For Pets certification from Mars Petcare in 2019, recognizing the city's commitment to pet friendly infrastructure. Inspirada contributes significantly to that reputation with its network of dog parks, walkable trails, and pet welcoming outdoor spaces. Nearby Chicken N Pickle also operates a dog park, expanding options just outside the community.
