Inspirada Pools and Splash Pads Inspirada offers at least six heated pools and multiple splash pads spread across its seven parks in Henderson, NV. This resort level aquatic access is included for residents of the 1,940 acre master planned community.

Which Inspirada Parks Have Pools?

Five parks and one clubhouse provide pool access throughout Inspirada. Aventura Park features an Olympic sized heated pool, the community's largest. Solista Park offers two resort style pools. Potenza Park, Attesa Park, and the newest Sentiero Park each have heated pools, with Sentiero's designated as resident only. The Toll Brothers Clubhouse provides additional pool access for select neighborhoods. Having heated pools means residents can swim comfortably from early spring through late fall, extending the season well beyond summer.

Where Are the Splash Pads in Inspirada?

Splash pads are located at three parks within the community. Aventura Park pairs its Olympic sized pool with a splash pad, creating a full aquatic center for families. Capriola Park features an interactive splash pad designed for younger children. Montagna Park, which opened in 2024, added another splash pad option on the community's southern end. These zero depth water features let toddlers and young kids enjoy water play safely without pool supervision concerns.

Do You Need a Membership for Inspirada Pools?

Inspirada's pools and splash pads are community amenities included with homeownership in the master planned community. There is no separate gym or club membership required. Sentiero Park's heated pool carries a resident only designation, ensuring a more private experience. The trail system connects all pool and splash pad locations, so residents can walk or bike to their preferred aquatic amenity within minutes.
