Pinecrest Academy Inspirada Henderson: A Top Rated K 8 School

Pinecrest Academy of Nevada Inspirada is the highest ranked public elementary school in Nevada and one of the strongest K through 8 programs in the state. Located at 2840 Via Contessa inside the Inspirada master planned community, this public charter school serves roughly 1,191 students with a STEAM focused curriculum that consistently produces exceptional results.

What Are the Ratings for Pinecrest Academy Inspirada?

Pinecrest Academy Inspirada holds an impressive list of accolades. U.S. News ranks it as the number one elementary school and number three middle school in Nevada. Niche gives it an A grade and names it the number one best public K through 8 school in the state. GreatSchools assigns it a 9 out of 10 rating. The Nevada Department of Education has awarded Pinecrest Inspirada a 5 star rating every single year from 2018 through 2025.

The school also earned the National Blue Ribbon School designation, making it the first charter school in Nevada to receive that honor. These rankings reflect sustained academic excellence rather than a single strong year.

How Do Pinecrest Inspirada Students Perform Academically?

Student proficiency rates at Pinecrest Inspirada are well above state averages. Eighty two percent of students test proficient in math, and 85 percent test proficient in reading. The STEAM focused curriculum integrates science, technology, engineering, arts, and mathematics into daily instruction. This approach prepares students for advanced coursework and competitive academic environments.

Why Do Families in Inspirada Choose Pinecrest Academy?

Families moving to Inspirada often cite Pinecrest Academy as a primary factor in their decision. The school sits within the community itself, meaning many students can walk or bike to campus. Combined with Inspirada's 7 parks spanning 85 acres, 35 plus miles of trails, and a family friendly atmosphere, the neighborhood offers a complete package for households prioritizing education.
