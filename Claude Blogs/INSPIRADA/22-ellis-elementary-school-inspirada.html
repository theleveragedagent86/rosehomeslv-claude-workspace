Robert Sandy Ellis Elementary School in Inspirada Henderson

Robert and Sandy Ellis Elementary is a public Pre K through 5th grade school located within the Inspirada master planned community in Henderson, Nevada. It holds a 4 star rating from the Nevada Department of Education and serves as a strong neighborhood school option for families living in and around Inspirada.

What Grades Does Ellis Elementary Serve?

Ellis Elementary enrolls students from Pre Kindergarten through 5th grade. As a traditional public school within the Clark County School District, it offers open enrollment to families in its attendance zone. Families living in Inspirada are zoned for Ellis Elementary, which means students can attend without a lottery or application process. The school's location inside the community allows many families to walk to campus.

How Is Ellis Elementary Rated by the State of Nevada?

The Nevada Department of Education has awarded Ellis Elementary a 4 star rating. This places the school above average among Nevada public elementary schools. The 4 star designation reflects solid academic performance, student growth metrics, and overall school climate. For families who prefer a traditional public school setting over a charter school, Ellis Elementary provides a well rated option without leaving the Inspirada neighborhood.

What Other Schools Are Near Ellis Elementary in Inspirada?

Inspirada is home to multiple strong school options beyond Ellis Elementary. Pinecrest Academy of Nevada Inspirada, a public charter school serving grades K through 8, holds a 5 star rating and ranks as the number one public elementary school in Nevada. Pinecrest Academy Sloan Canyon, located nearby, also carries a 5 star rating. This concentration of quality schools within a single community is unusual in the Las Vegas valley and gives Inspirada families real choices.
