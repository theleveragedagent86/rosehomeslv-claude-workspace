Pinecrest Academy Sloan Canyon Henderson NV

Pinecrest Academy Sloan Canyon is a public charter school near the Inspirada master planned community in Henderson, Nevada, and it carries a 5 star rating across all segments from the Nevada Department of Education. This campus is part of the Pinecrest Academy of Nevada network, which operates some of the highest performing schools in the state.

What Is the Rating for Pinecrest Academy Sloan Canyon?

The Nevada Department of Education awards Pinecrest Academy Sloan Canyon a 5 star rating across all evaluated segments. A 5 star designation is the highest possible score under the state's accountability framework and reflects strong academic performance, student growth, and school climate. This rating places Pinecrest Sloan Canyon among a small group of top performing public schools in Nevada.

Families near Inspirada have access to an unusually strong cluster of schools.
