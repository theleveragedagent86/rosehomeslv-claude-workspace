Best Schools in Henderson NV 2026

Henderson, Nevada is home to several of the highest rated public schools in the state, with multiple campuses earning 5 star ratings from the Nevada Department of Education in 2026. Families relocating to Henderson will find a strong concentration of top performing schools, particularly in and around the Inspirada master planned community.

Which Henderson Schools Have 5 Star Ratings in 2026?

Pinecrest Academy of Nevada Inspirada stands out as the top performing school in Henderson and the entire state. U.S. News ranks it the number one elementary school in Nevada and the number three middle school. Niche names it the number one best public K through 8 school in the state with an A grade. The school has earned a 5 star rating from the Nevada Department of Education every year from 2018 through 2025 and was the first charter school in Nevada to receive the National Blue Ribbon School designation.

Pinecrest Academy Sloan Canyon, located near Inspirada, also holds a 5 star rating across all segments from the Nevada Department of Education. Together, these two Pinecrest campuses give the southern Henderson area some of the strongest school options available anywhere in Clark County.

What Are the Test Scores at Top Henderson Schools?

At Pinecrest Academy Inspirada, 82 percent of students test proficient in math and 85 percent test proficient in reading. These numbers significantly exceed state averages. The school serves approximately 1,191 students in grades K through 8 with a STEAM focused curriculum that integrates science, technology, engineering, arts, and mathematics. Robert and Sandy Ellis Elementary, also inside Inspirada, carries a solid 4 star rating and serves Pre K through 5th grade.

Why Does School Quality Matter for Henderson Home Buyers?

School ratings directly influence home values. Neighborhoods with top rated schools consistently see stronger demand and higher resale prices. In Inspirada, where three well rated schools operate within or adjacent to the community, home prices range from $419K to over $1.1 million with a median around $549K.
