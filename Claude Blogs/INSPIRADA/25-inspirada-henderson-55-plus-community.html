Inspirada Henderson 55 Plus Community Options

Inspirada in Henderson, Nevada offers active adult living through Mera Henderson, a 55 plus apartment community located at 2300 Via Inspirada. Developed by Sparrow Living, Mera provides a maintenance free lifestyle within one of Henderson's most amenity rich master planned communities.

What Is the 55 Plus Community at Inspirada Henderson?

Mera Henderson is an active adult apartment community designed for residents aged 55 and older. One bedroom floor plans start at $1,664 per month, and two bedroom units start at $2,164 per month. The community offers a maintenance free lifestyle, eliminating the responsibilities of home upkeep, yard work, and exterior repairs. Mera sits within the 1,940 acre Inspirada community, giving residents access to 7 parks spanning 85 acres, 35 plus miles of trails, and community events throughout the year.

What Amenities and Services Are Near Mera Henderson?

Inspirada provides a strong infrastructure for active adults. Sentiero Park features age friendly playgrounds and inclusive accessibility designed for residents of all ages and mobility levels. Community programming includes yoga classes, film screenings, creative writing workshops, and holiday celebrations. For healthcare, multiple medical facilities operate nearby. Henderson Hospital is a 303 bed facility in close proximity. Saint Rose Dominican Hospitals serve the area as well. A new Southern Hills Hospital freestanding ER is scheduled to open in late 2026 at 2475 Via Inspirada, a $15 million, 10,860 square foot facility offering 24/7 emergency care.

Is Inspirada a Safe Place for Retirees?

Henderson ranks as the number two safest large city in the United States, which is a significant factor for retirees choosing where to live. Mera Henderson is located within 18 minutes of Harry Reid International Airport, making travel convenient for residents who want to stay connected with family or explore new destinations. The community borders Sloan Canyon National Conservation Area, offering access to desert hiking and natural landscapes without leaving the neighborhood.
