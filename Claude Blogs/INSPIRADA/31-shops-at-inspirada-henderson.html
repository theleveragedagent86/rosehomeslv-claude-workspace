The Shops at Inspirada Henderson

The Shops at Inspirada is a neighborhood retail center located at the corner of Via Inspirada and Bicentennial Parkway, giving Inspirada residents walkable access to everyday essentials without leaving the community.

What Stores Are at The Shops at Inspirada?

The center features a mix of services, dining, and convenience retail. Current tenants include Great Clips, Jersey Mike's Subs, Marco's Pizza, Stacks and Yolks, 7 Eleven, Liquor World, Church's Martial Arts, a UPS Store, and Inspirada Dental. The lineup covers routine errands like haircuts and package shipping alongside quick dining options.

Stacks and Yolks serves as a popular breakfast and brunch spot for residents, while Jersey Mike's and Marco's Pizza handle the weeknight takeout crowd. The 7 Eleven and Liquor World fill convenience gaps that keep residents from making unnecessary trips to larger shopping centers.

Where Is The Shops at Inspirada Located?

The center sits at one of Inspirada's main intersections, making it accessible by car, bike, or foot from most neighborhoods within the 1,940 acre community. Its position along Via Inspirada places it along the daily commute path for many residents heading toward Henderson Executive Airport or the 215 Beltway. This is a significant convenience factor in a community of over 9,600 residents.

What Other Shopping Options Are Near Inspirada?

For broader retail needs, The District at Green Valley Ranch offers stores like REI, Lululemon, Anthropologie, and Williams Sonoma just a short drive via the I 215. Galleria at Sunset provides department store anchors including Macy's, JCPenney, Dillard's, and Dick's Sporting Goods. The upcoming Centurion at Inspirada Town Center will add 78,756 square feet of retail including a Sprouts grocery store directly within the community, expected to open by the end of 2026.
