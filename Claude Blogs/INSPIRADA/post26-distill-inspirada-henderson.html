What Is Distill Inspirada in Henderson?

Distill Inspirada is a 24 hour sports bar and dining destination located at 2293 Via Inspirada in the heart of the Inspirada community in Henderson, NV. It combines a full service restaurant with a gaming bar, craft cocktails, and live sports viewing in a spacious, welcoming setting.

What Kind of Food Does Distill Inspirada Serve?

The menu goes well beyond typical bar fare. Starters include Maryland jumbo lump crab cakes and ahi poke stacks, while entrees range from blackened cowboy ribeye to sesame crusted Atlantic salmon. Comfort classics like country fried steak and a full lineup of burgers round out the offerings.

Distill also features an impressive selection of craft beers and signature cocktails. Whether you are stopping in for a quick lunch or settling in for game day, the menu delivers variety for every appetite.

What Makes Distill a Great Gathering Spot for Inspirada Residents?

The layout centers around a large horseshoe style bar, with multiple screens broadcasting live sports throughout the space. A separate dining room offers a quieter atmosphere for families and larger groups. The restaurant frequently hosts live music, adding another layer of entertainment beyond the screens.

Being open 24 hours daily, Distill fills a unique role in the Inspirada neighborhood. Late night bites, early morning meetups, and everything in between are covered. With over 150 reviews on Yelp, the restaurant has built a loyal following among locals who value consistency and convenience.
