What Is Romano Mercato Italiano in Henderson?

Romano Mercato Italiano is an authentic Italian restaurant and market located at 2016 Via Firenze in Henderson, NV, just steps from the Inspirada community. It combines a full dining menu with an Italian market where guests can purchase fresh pasta, imported goods, and homemade specialties to take home.

What Does the Menu at Romano Mercato Italiano Include?

The kitchen produces homemade pasta, wood fired pizza, focaccia, and soups from scratch daily. Signature dishes include arancini (Italian rice balls) filled with ground beef, peas, and mozzarella or spinach and ricotta. Sandwiches are served on house baked bread with traditional Italian meats like salami, mortadella, capicola, and provolone.

Pasta selections feature classics such as spaghetti and meatballs and vodka rigatoni with sausage. The restaurant also serves homemade gelato for dessert, making it a complete Italian dining experience from appetizer to finish. Hours run Tuesday through Saturday from 7:30 AM to 8:00 PM and Sunday from 7:30 AM to 7:00 PM.

Why Do Inspirada Residents Love Romano Mercato Italiano?

The attached market sets Romano Mercato apart from other Italian restaurants in Henderson. Residents can grab a quick lunch, sit down for a full dinner, or simply stop in to pick up fresh pasta and imported ingredients for a meal at home.
