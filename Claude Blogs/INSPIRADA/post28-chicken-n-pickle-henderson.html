What Is Chicken N Pickle in Henderson?

Chicken N Pickle is a 38,000 square foot entertainment and dining complex at 3381 St. Rose Parkway in Henderson, NV. It combines a chef driven restaurant, sports bar, 14 pickleball courts, a dog park, beer garden, yard games, and event spaces into one massive venue just minutes from the Inspirada community.

What Food and Drinks Does Chicken N Pickle Henderson Offer?

The restaurant centers on rotisserie chicken, chicken wings, and seasonal salads prepared by an in house culinary team. Two dining areas and a sports bar provide different atmospheres depending on your mood. Craft cocktails and local brews fill the drink menu.

A rooftop patio adds another level of dining with open air views, while the beer garden offers a more casual option. The menu is designed to fuel an active day of pickleball or yard games without requiring a separate stop for food.

What Activities Are Available at Chicken N Pickle Henderson?

The facility features 14 pickleball courts, with six indoor and eight outdoor, making it one of the largest pickleball venues in the Henderson area. Beyond the courts, guests can enjoy a variety of yard games, a dedicated dog park, and both indoor and outdoor event spaces suitable for corporate gatherings and private parties.

The venue is backed by NFL stars Travis Kelce and Patrick Mahomes, which has helped drive national attention. Hours run Monday through Thursday from 8:00 AM to 11:00 PM, Friday and Saturday from 8:00 AM to midnight, and Sunday from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Over 500 Yelp reviews reflect its popularity with locals and visitors alike.
