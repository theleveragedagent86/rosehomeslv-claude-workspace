What Is Block 9 Thai Street Eats in Henderson?

Block 9 Thai Street Eats is a Bangkok inspired Thai restaurant at 3483 Saint Rose Parkway, Suite 100, in Henderson, NV. Created by Chef Nittaya Parawong, the Henderson location brings authentic Thai street food to the St. Rose Parkway corridor just minutes from the Inspirada community.

What Kind of Thai Food Does Block 9 Serve?

Chef Nittaya built the concept around the flavors of Thai street food, drawing on her childhood in Thailand to create dishes that are quick, approachable, and packed with authentic flavor. The menu features curries, noodle dishes, and stir fries that showcase traditional Thai cooking techniques and ingredients.

The focus is on bold, uncompromising flavors rather than watered down versions of Thai classics. Each dish reflects the kind of food you would find at a bustling street stall in Bangkok, prepared with the consistency and quality of a full service restaurant. Block 9 is open Monday through Sunday from 11:00 AM to 9:00 PM.

Why Is Block 9 Popular with Henderson Residents?

With over 220 Yelp reviews at the Henderson location, Block 9 has earned a strong local following. The Summerlin original built such demand that Chef Nittaya expanded to St. Rose Parkway, giving residents on the south side of the valley their own location.

The restaurant fills a gap in the Inspirada area dining scene by offering a cuisine type that few nearby competitors match. For residents who want authentic Thai without driving across the valley, Block 9 delivers a reliable and flavorful option within a short drive from home.
