What Are the Best Restaurants Near Inspirada in Henderson?

Inspirada residents have access to a diverse and growing dining scene along Via Inspirada and St. Rose Parkway in Henderson, NV. From 24 hour sports bars and authentic Italian markets to Thai street food and pickleball entertainment complexes, the options cover nearly every cuisine and occasion.

Which Restaurants Are Within the Inspirada Community?

Distill Inspirada at 2293 Via Inspirada is the community's go to sports bar, open 24 hours daily with a full menu of craft cocktails, entrees, and gaming. Romano Mercato Italiano at 2016 Via Firenze serves homemade pasta, wood fired pizza, and gelato with an attached Italian market for take home goods.

The Shops at Inspirada add even more variety with Marco's Pizza, Jersey Mike's, and Stacks and Yolks for breakfast. Jong Ro Chicken and Noodle Master round out the immediate area with Korean and Asian noodle options. These spots are all walkable or a short drive for Inspirada homeowners.

What Dining Options Are Along St. Rose Parkway Near Inspirada?

Chicken N Pickle at 3381 St. Rose Parkway combines a chef driven restaurant with 14 pickleball courts, a dog park, and beer garden in a 38,000 square foot complex. Block 9 Thai Street Eats at 3483 Saint Rose Parkway serves authentic Bangkok style dishes from Chef Nittaya Parawong.

Eureka! Henderson offers a gastropub experience with craft beer and elevated American fare. Shogun Tonkatsu and Curry brings Japanese comfort food to the area, while Lexie's Bistro delivers a polished American bistro menu. Breakfast Junkies has become a local favorite for morning meals along the corridor. Marinelli's Pasta Bar provides another Italian option within a short drive.
