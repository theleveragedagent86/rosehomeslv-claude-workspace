Who Are the Builders in Inspirada Henderson?

Inspirada features five major homebuilders: Toll Brothers, KB Home, Tri Pointe Homes (formerly Pardee Homes), Beazer Homes, and Century Communities. Each builder brings a distinct style, price point, and floor plan selection to this 1,940 acre master planned community in Henderson, NV.

What Does Toll Brothers Offer in Inspirada?

Toll Brothers is the luxury tier builder in Inspirada. Their homes tend to feature larger floor plans, upgraded finishes, and more generous backyard space compared to other builders in the community. Toll Brothers also operates a private clubhouse within Inspirada that includes pools, a fitness center, a conference room, a caterer's kitchen, and a kids' playground. Access to that clubhouse carries an additional $41 per month fee on top of standard HOA costs.

Resale Toll Brothers homes in Inspirada often command premium pricing. Buyers looking for larger lots and high end interior finishes frequently gravitate toward these neighborhoods.

What About KB Home and Other Builders in Inspirada?

KB Home provides a more budget conscious entry point into Inspirada. Their homes are known for energy efficiency and personalization options during the build process. Tri Pointe Homes is actively building new construction in Inspirada with communities like Arden (2,014 to 2,735 sq ft, up to 5 bedrooms), Capri (1,640 to 1,924 sq ft townhomes), and Strada. Beazer Homes and Century Communities round out the builder lineup with additional single family options across the community's 39 distinct neighborhoods.

Home prices across all builders currently range from $419,000 to over $1,100,000, with a median sale price of $549,000 as of February 2026.
