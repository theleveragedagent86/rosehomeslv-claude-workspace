Are New Construction Homes Available in Inspirada in 2026?

Yes. Inspirada is actively building new homes in 2026, with Tri Pointe Homes leading current construction efforts. The community ranked as the #12 Top Selling Master Planned Community nationally, and new neighborhoods continue to open across the development's 1,940 acres. Inspirada is planned for 11,500 homes total across seven villages, and that buildout is still underway.

Which Builders Are Offering New Construction in Inspirada?

Tri Pointe Homes has three active new construction communities in Inspirada. Arden offers single family homes from 2,014 to 2,735 square feet with up to 5 bedrooms. Capri features townhomes ranging from 1,640 to 1,924 square feet with up to 3 bedrooms plus a loft. Strada is also currently selling. These communities provide a mix of price points and layouts for different buyer needs.

Other builders including Toll Brothers, KB Home, Beazer Homes, and Century Communities have built extensively in Inspirada. Resale inventory from these builders is also available, with 99 homes currently listed for sale across the community.

What Do New Construction Homes Cost in Inspirada?

New and resale homes in Inspirada range from $419,000 to over $1,100,000. The median sale price reached $549,000 in February 2026, reflecting a 6% year over year increase. The average sale price sits at $571,668, with homes spending an average of 54 days on market. Townhomes start around 1,366 square feet, while single family homes range from approximately 1,558 to 4,343 square feet.
