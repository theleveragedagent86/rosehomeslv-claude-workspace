What Are the Luxury Home Options in Inspirada Henderson?

Inspirada offers luxury homes priced from the upper $700,000s to over $1,100,000. These properties feature premium finishes, larger floor plans up to 4,343 square feet, and in some cases, more generous lot sizes than typical Henderson master planned communities. Toll Brothers and Tri Pointe Homes (formerly Pardee Homes) are the primary builders behind the luxury tier in Inspirada.

Which Builders Offer Luxury Homes in Inspirada?

Toll Brothers is the standout luxury builder in the community. Their Inspirada homes include upgraded countertops, cabinetry, and flooring, along with backyard spaces that are larger than average for the area. Toll Brothers resale homes often carry premium pricing due to their build quality and lot selection. Toll Brothers homeowners also have access to a private clubhouse featuring pools, a fitness center, a conference room, a caterer's kitchen, and a playground for an additional $41 per month.

Tri Pointe Homes' Arden community offers larger floor plans ranging from 2,014 to 2,735 square feet with up to 5 bedrooms. These homes deliver modern open layouts, designer kitchens, and covered outdoor living areas that appeal to buyers seeking space without sacrificing a newer build.

What Makes Inspirada Attractive for Luxury Buyers?

Beyond the homes themselves, Inspirada's infrastructure supports a luxury lifestyle. The community spans 1,940 acres with 39 neighborhoods, multiple parks, trail systems, pools, and splash pads. Gated subdivisions with guard gated entry provide an added layer of privacy and security. The upcoming Centurion Town Center will add 78,756 square feet of retail and dining by December 2026, giving luxury homeowners walkable access to shopping and restaurants.

With a median home price of $549,000 and an average of $571,668, the luxury segment represents the upper range of a community that continues to appreciate. Inspirada posted a 6% year over year price increase as of February 2026.
