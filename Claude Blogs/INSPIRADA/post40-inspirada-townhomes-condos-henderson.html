Are There Townhomes and Condos in Inspirada Henderson?

Yes. Inspirada offers townhomes, condos, and mid rise apartments alongside its single family homes. Townhomes start at approximately 1,366 square feet, and new townhome construction is actively available through Tri Pointe Homes' Capri community. Prices across Inspirada begin at $419,000, making the townhome and condo segment the most accessible entry point into the community.

What Townhome Options Are Currently Available?

Tri Pointe Homes' Capri neighborhood is the primary active townhome community in Inspirada. Capri offers floor plans ranging from 1,640 to 1,924 square feet with up to 3 bedrooms plus a loft. These attached homes feature modern open layouts, two car garages, and contemporary finishes that match the design standards of Inspirada's single family homes.

Resale townhomes from earlier phases of development are also available. With 99 total homes currently listed for sale in Inspirada, buyers have options across multiple price points and sizes. The median sale price across the community is $549,000, though townhomes and condos typically fall below that figure.

Who Should Consider a Townhome or Condo in Inspirada?

Townhomes and condos in Inspirada appeal to first time buyers, downsizers, and investors. The lower price point compared to single family homes provides access to the same community amenities, parks, trails, and pools without the cost of a detached home. HOA fees cover exterior maintenance in many townhome communities, reducing the upkeep burden for owners who prefer a lock and leave lifestyle.

Investors also find value here. Inspirada's 6% year over year price appreciation and strong demand keep rental and resale prospects favorable. The upcoming Centurion Town Center, with 600 apartments and 78,756 square feet of retail opening in December 2026, is expected to increase walkability and desirability for the entire community.
