Are Inspirada Homes a Good Investment in Henderson?

Inspirada investment properties in Henderson offer strong fundamentals for both long term appreciation and rental income. With a median home price of $549,000 as of February 2026 and 6% year over year appreciation, this master planned community continues to attract investor interest backed by measurable growth.

Why Do Investors Choose Inspirada in Henderson NV?

Inspirada spans 1,940 acres across 7 villages and 39 neighborhoods, with a planned buildout of 11,500 homes. The community is still actively growing, which means investors can enter at various price points ranging from $419,000 to over $1,100,000. Townhomes starting around 1,366 square feet offer accessible entry, while larger single family homes up to 4,343 square feet appeal to premium rental markets.

Demand drivers extend beyond housing. The 24 acre Centurion Town Center is set to open in December 2026 with 600 apartments and new retail, adding density and commercial amenities that directly support property values. A freestanding Southern Hills Hospital emergency room is also opening late 2026, adding another essential service within reach of the community.

What Are the Numbers for Inspirada Investment Properties?

The average sale price in Inspirada sits at $571,668 with homes spending an average of 54 days on market. That turnover rate signals healthy demand without excessive speculation. Currently 99 homes are listed for sale, giving investors a range of options across builders like Toll Brothers, KB Home, Tri Pointe Homes, Beazer Homes, and Century Communities.

HOA fees remain reasonable at $95 per month for the base assessment, with an additional $41 per month for Toll Brothers clubhouse access. These costs are manageable relative to rental income potential, especially given Henderson's ranking as the number 2 safest large city in the United States and its proximity of 15 minutes to the Las Vegas Strip and 15 to 18 minutes to the airport.
