Does Inspirada Have Gated Neighborhoods with Guard Gates?

Yes, Inspirada features multiple gated subdivisions with guard gated entry points throughout the community. While Inspirada as a whole is not fully gated, several individual neighborhoods within its 1,940 acres provide controlled access for added privacy and security.

Which Inspirada Neighborhoods Are Guard Gated?

Inspirada contains 39 neighborhoods spread across 7 villages, and a number of these subdivisions operate behind guard gates or controlled entry systems. Builders like Toll Brothers and select Pardee resale neighborhoods have historically offered gated options with larger lot sizes and premium floor plans. These gated sections function as private enclaves within the broader master planned community.

The guard gated neighborhoods typically feature homes at the higher end of Inspirada's price range, which spans from $419,000 to over $1,100,000. Buyers seeking gated living will find single family homes ranging from approximately 1,558 square feet to 4,343 square feet behind these controlled access points.
