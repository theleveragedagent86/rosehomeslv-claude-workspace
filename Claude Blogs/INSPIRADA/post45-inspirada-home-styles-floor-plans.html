What Home Styles and Floor Plans Are Available in Inspirada?

Inspirada offers single family homes in one story and two story configurations, townhomes, condos, and mid rise apartments. Floor plans range from approximately 1,366 square feet for townhomes to 4,343 square feet for the largest single family designs, with five active builders delivering distinct architectural styles across the community.

What Builders Offer Floor Plans in Inspirada?

Toll Brothers, KB Home, Tri Pointe Homes, Beazer Homes, and Century Communities each build in Inspirada with their own signature designs. Tri Pointe's active neighborhoods include Arden, offering 2,014 to 2,735 square feet with up to 5 bedrooms, and Capri, a townhome collection featuring 1,640 to 1,924 square feet with up to 3 bedrooms plus a loft. The Strada collection adds another option from Tri Pointe.

Toll Brothers and Pardee resale homes tend to feature more generous backyard space compared to newer construction. KB Home and Century Communities focus on efficient layouts that maximize livable square footage on Inspirada's characteristically compact lots.

How Do Inspirada Home Sizes Compare Across Price Points?

Single family homes start at approximately 1,558 square feet and scale up to 4,343 square feet. The median home price of $549,000 in February 2026 sits within reach of mid range floor plans, while the full price spectrum runs from $419,000 to over $1,100,000. Buyers at each tier find purpose built options rather than one size fits all designs.

Townhomes beginning around 1,366 square feet provide a lower maintenance alternative with modern finishes and open concept layouts. These work well for first time buyers, downsizers, and investors looking for strong rental appeal. The community's 39 neighborhoods across 7 villages ensure that similar styles are grouped together, maintaining neighborhood cohesion.
