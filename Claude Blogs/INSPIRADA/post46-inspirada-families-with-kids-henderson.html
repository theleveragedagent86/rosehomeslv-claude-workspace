Is Inspirada a Good Community for Families with Kids?

Inspirada is one of the top family friendly master planned communities in Henderson, NV. With highly rated schools, seven parks spanning 85 acres, and over 35 miles of connected trails, families with children find everything they need within walking distance of home.

What Schools Are Available Near Inspirada?

Pinecrest Academy Inspirada is the standout. It holds the rank of #1 elementary school in Nevada and has earned a National Blue Ribbon designation. The school follows a STEAM curriculum, and students score 82% proficient in math and 85% proficient in reading. Ellis Elementary carries a 4 star rating from the Nevada Department of Education, while Pinecrest Sloan Canyon holds a 5 star rating.

Having three strong school options within the community means families can avoid long commutes to quality education. That alone sets Inspirada apart from many Henderson neighborhoods.

What Parks and Play Areas Does Inspirada Offer for Kids?

Inspirada includes seven parks across 85 acres. Playgrounds, splash pads, and sports courts are spread throughout the community. Sentiero Park features age friendly playground equipment with inclusive accessibility for children of all abilities. Most residents live within a five minute walk of park amenities.

The 35 mile trail system connects every park, making it safe for kids to bike or walk between play areas without crossing busy streets. Sports courts support basketball, pickleball, and tennis for older children and teens.
