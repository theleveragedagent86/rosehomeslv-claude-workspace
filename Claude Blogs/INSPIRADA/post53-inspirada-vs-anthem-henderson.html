How Does Inspirada Compare to Anthem in Henderson NV?

Inspirada and Anthem are both master planned communities in Henderson, but they represent different eras of development and distinct lifestyle experiences. Here is how they stack up.

What Sets Inspirada and Anthem Apart?

Anthem opened in 1998, giving it nearly a decade head start over Inspirada, which launched in 2007. That age difference shows in the landscaping and tree canopy. Anthem's mature trees and established streetscapes give it a settled feel, while Inspirada's newer construction offers modern floor plans and energy efficient building standards. Inspirada is still actively building with Toll Brothers, KB Home, Tri Pointe, Beazer, and Century Communities. Anthem is largely built out.

Anthem includes the Anthem Country Club, a private guard gated community with its own golf course and clubhouse. Inspirada does not have a private golf club, but it offers 85 acres of parkland, 35+ miles of trails, multiple heated pools, and direct access to Sloan Canyon National Conservation Area. Anthem sits closer to major shopping corridors along St. Rose Parkway and Eastern Avenue, giving residents quicker access to retail and dining.

How Do Prices and Location Compare?

Inspirada's median home price is $549K as of February 2026, ranging from $419K to over $1.1M. Anthem generally trends higher, especially in the Country Club and guard gated sections. Buyers seeking new construction will find more options in Inspirada since Anthem's inventory is primarily resale. Inspirada's base HOA is $95 per month, though Anthem HOA fees vary by sub community.

Both communities are in Henderson, ranked the #2 safest large city in the U.S. Inspirada is 15 minutes from the Strip and 15 to 18 minutes from the airport. Anthem sits slightly closer to established commercial areas but roughly the same distance to the Strip. Inspirada will gain significant nearby retail when Centurion Town Center opens in December 2026 with Sprouts and additional shops.

Which Henderson Community Is the Better Fit?

Ryan Rose guides buyers through this comparison regularly. Families prioritizing top schools often lean toward Inspirada for access to Pinecrest Academy, the #1 ranked elementary in Nevada. Buyers wanting mature landscaping, a country club lifestyle, or proximity to established shopping may prefer Anthem. Both communities deliver the safety and quality of life Henderson is known for.
