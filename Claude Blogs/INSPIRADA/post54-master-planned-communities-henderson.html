What Are the Best Master Planned Communities in Henderson NV?

Henderson is home to several of southern Nevada's top master planned communities, each offering a distinct combination of price, amenities, and lifestyle. Here is what buyers need to know about the leading options.

Which Master Planned Communities Are in Henderson?

The three most prominent master planned communities in Henderson are Inspirada, Cadence, and Anthem. Each serves a different segment of the market. Inspirada covers 1,940 acres with 7 villages and is still actively building. Cadence spans 2,200 acres and opened in 2015, making it the newest of the three. Anthem opened in 1998 and is largely built out, offering a more established neighborhood feel with mature landscaping.

Other notable Henderson communities include Green Valley Ranch, MacDonald Highlands, and Seven Hills. Green Valley Ranch is one of the original master planned communities in the valley. MacDonald Highlands is a luxury hillside community with custom home sites. Seven Hills offers guard gated neighborhoods and elevated views of the Las Vegas valley.

How Do Prices Compare Across Henderson Communities?

Inspirada's median home price sits at $549K as of February 2026, with a range of $419K to over $1.1M. Cadence offers a similar price range, while Anthem generally trends higher, particularly in its Country Club and guard gated sections. MacDonald Highlands and Seven Hills occupy the upper end of the Henderson market. Inspirada's base HOA is $95 per month, though fees vary across all communities depending on the specific sub association.

New construction availability is strongest in Inspirada and Cadence. Inspirada's active builders include Toll Brothers, KB Home, Tri Pointe, Beazer, and Century Communities. Buyers looking for resale homes with larger lots and mature trees will find more options in Anthem, Green Valley Ranch, and Seven Hills.
