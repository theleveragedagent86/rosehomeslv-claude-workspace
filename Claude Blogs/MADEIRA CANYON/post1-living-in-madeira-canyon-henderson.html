What Is It Like Living in Madeira Canyon Henderson?

Living in Madeira Canyon means enjoying one of Henderson's most private and elevated communities, with panoramic views of the Las Vegas Strip and Black Mountains from nearly every vantage point. This master planned community of approximately 1,400 homes sits at 2,700 feet above sea level, making it the highest residential development in Southern Nevada.

A Community That Feels Worlds Away

Madeira Canyon delivers a unique combination of seclusion and convenience that few Henderson neighborhoods can match. Tucked into the foothills south of Anthem, the community feels removed from the bustle of the valley while remaining just 30 to 45 minutes from the Strip. As one longtime resident described it, you simply do not venture out here unless you live here, and that privacy is exactly what draws families and professionals to this enclave.

The community includes approximately eight distinct neighborhoods, ranging from non gated sections with homes starting in the $500,000s to guard gated enclaves where luxury properties exceed $1.5 million. Whether you prefer a single story layout or a spacious two story home with over 4,000 square feet, Madeira Canyon offers floor plans to match nearly every lifestyle.

Amenities and Outdoor Access

Residents enjoy the 20 acre Madeira Canyon Park, which features lighted baseball and soccer fields, tennis courts, a splash pad, playgrounds, and walking paths with stunning valley views. For outdoor enthusiasts, the Sloan Canyon National Conservation Area borders the community to the south. This 48,438 acre preserve offers hiking trails, wildlife viewing, and the famous Petroglyph Canyon with over 1,700 ancient rock art designs.

Schools and Family Life

Families benefit from access to some of Henderson's top rated schools. Wallin Elementary holds a B+ rating on Niche and ranks in the top 10 of Clark County elementary schools. Del E. Webb Middle School and Liberty High School continue the strong academic trajectory for older students.
