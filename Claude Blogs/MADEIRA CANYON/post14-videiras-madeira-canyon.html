Videiras at Madeira Canyon: Guard Gated Luxury Living

Videiras represents the most exclusive neighborhood within Madeira Canyon, offering fewer than 100 luxury homes behind guard gates with some of the most commanding views in Southern Nevada. Buyers seeking maximum privacy and premium positioning find Videiras delivers on both fronts.

Guard Gated Security

Entry to Videiras requires passing through a staffed guard gate, ensuring only residents and their approved guests access the neighborhood. This controlled entry provides peace of mind for homeowners who travel frequently or simply value knowing exactly who enters their community. The intimate size of Videiras means guards quickly recognize residents, creating a personalized security experience.

Home Sizes and Styles

Videiras homes typically range from 3,000 to over 4,500 square feet, featuring four to six bedrooms, three to five bathrooms, and three car garages as standard. Floor plans include both single and two story options, with many homes offering casitas or multigenerational suites. Construction quality reflects the luxury price point, with upgraded finishes throughout.

Architectural styles follow Madeira Canyon's Spanish Mediterranean theme, with stucco exteriors, tile roofs, and courtyards common throughout the neighborhood. Many homes feature outdoor living spaces designed to take advantage of the spectacular views.

Views and Lot Positions

Videiras occupies some of Madeira Canyon's most elevated terrain, translating to exceptional views for most homes. Properties facing north and west enjoy panoramic Strip vistas, particularly stunning after dark when city lights create a glittering backdrop. South facing homes look toward the McCullough Range and Sloan Canyon wilderness.

Lot sizes in Videiras tend toward the larger end of Madeira Canyon's range, providing more separation between homes and enhanced privacy. Many properties back to natural desert or open space rather than neighboring structures.

Current Pricing

Homes in Videiras typically list between $800,000 and $1.4 million, with exceptional properties occasionally exceeding this range. Limited inventory means available listings often attract quick interest from qualified buyers. Resales in Videiras appear infrequently, typically just a handful per year.

Community Access

Videiras residents enjoy full access to Madeira Canyon's community amenities, including the 20 acre park, sports facilities, and trail connections. The neighborhood's location within Madeira Canyon means schools, hiking access, and community events remain convenient despite the added privacy.

Is Videiras Right for You?

Ryan Rose recommends Videiras for buyers prioritizing security, privacy, and views above all else. The neighborhood suits executives, frequent travelers, and families seeking Henderson's most exclusive addresses. Those preferring more neighborhood interaction or tighter budgets may find other Madeira Canyon sections more appropriate.
