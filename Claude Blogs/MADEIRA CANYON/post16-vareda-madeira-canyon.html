Vareda at Madeira Canyon: Gated Single Story Homes

Vareda offers a compelling middle ground within Madeira Canyon, providing gated entry and single story living without the premium pricing of guard gated enclaves like Videiras or The Club. Buyers seeking controlled access and one level floor plans find Vareda delivers both at relatively accessible price points.

Gated Entry

Vareda features automatic gate access rather than staffed guard gates, striking a balance between security and cost. Residents use remotes or codes to enter, preventing casual pass through traffic while keeping HOA fees more manageable than full guard gated communities. This arrangement appeals to buyers who want controlled entry without paying for 24 hour staffing.

Single Story Focus

The neighborhood was designed primarily around single story floor plans, making it attractive to buyers who prefer one level living. Reasons for choosing single story include aging in place considerations, mobility concerns, easier maintenance, and personal preference. Vareda's emphasis on single story options sets it apart from Madeira Canyon neighborhoods dominated by two story homes.

Home Sizes and Floor Plans

Vareda homes typically range from approximately 1,780 to 2,800 square feet, featuring three to four bedrooms and two to three bathrooms. Floor plans emphasize open concepts with split bedroom arrangements common throughout the neighborhood. Most homes include two or three car garages and covered patios suitable for outdoor living.

Construction by Pulte Homes ensures consistent quality throughout Vareda, with standard features including tile roofing, stucco exteriors, and desert landscaping consistent with Madeira Canyon's overall aesthetic.

Current Pricing

Vareda homes typically list between $600,000 and $850,000, positioning the neighborhood between Madeira Canyon's most affordable sections and its luxury enclaves. The combination of gated entry and single story living justifies moderate premiums over non gated neighborhoods while remaining well below Videiras pricing.

Community Access

Vareda residents enjoy full access to Madeira Canyon amenities including the 20 acre community park, walking trails, and proximity to Sloan Canyon National Conservation Area. The neighborhood's central location within Madeira Canyon means short drives to Wallin Elementary and other community facilities.

Ideal Buyers

Ryan Rose recommends Vareda for empty nesters, retirees not requiring 55+ communities, and buyers with mobility considerations who still want an active lifestyle. The neighborhood also attracts professionals who prefer single story convenience and appreciate gated privacy without maximum security requirements.
