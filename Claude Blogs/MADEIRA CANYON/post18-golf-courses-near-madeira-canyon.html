Golf Courses Near Madeira Canyon Henderson

While Madeira Canyon does not include an on site golf course, the community sits within easy reach of Henderson's finest fairways. Golfers living in Madeira Canyon can tee off at championship courses within 10 to 15 minutes, enjoying world class layouts without the premium pricing of golf course communities.

Rio Secco Golf Club

Located approximately 10 minutes from Madeira Canyon in the Seven Hills community, Rio Secco Golf Club offers one of Southern Nevada's most respected layouts. Designed by Rees Jones, the course features dramatic elevation changes and challenging desert terrain. Rio Secco also hosts the Butch Harmon School of Golf, attracting serious players seeking instruction from one of golf's most celebrated teachers.

The course operates as a semi private facility, with public tee times available alongside member play. Madeira Canyon residents frequently establish regular games at Rio Secco due to its proximity and quality.

Revere Golf Club

Revere Golf Club in Anthem, roughly 12 minutes from Madeira Canyon, operates two distinct 18 hole courses: Lexington and Concord. Both layouts wind through dramatic desert terrain with Las Vegas valley views serving as constant backdrops. Revere consistently ranks among Nevada's top public access facilities.

The clubhouse offers dining and event facilities, making Revere popular for golf outings and tournaments. Afternoon rates provide affordable options for Madeira Canyon residents seeking value.

Anthem Country Club

Anthem Country Club sits approximately 8 minutes from Madeira Canyon, offering private membership golf in a prestigious setting. The Hale Irwin designed course provides challenging play with exceptional conditioning. Membership includes access to fitness facilities, dining, and social events beyond golf.

Some Madeira Canyon residents maintain Anthem Country Club memberships, enjoying the private club experience while living in a non golf community. This arrangement allows golfers to choose their ideal home environment independent of course proximity.

Additional Options

Dragon Ridge Country Club in MacDonald Highlands offers another private option approximately 15 minutes away, featuring a Tom Fazio design with stunning mountain views. Chimera Golf Club and Wildhorse Golf Club provide additional public access alternatives within 20 minutes of Madeira Canyon.

Why Golfers Choose Madeira Canyon

Ryan Rose works with golfers who prefer Madeira Canyon's views and privacy over golf course frontage. Madeira Canyon offers separation from course activity while keeping excellent golf minutes away.
