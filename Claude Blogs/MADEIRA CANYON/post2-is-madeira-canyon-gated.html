Is Madeira Canyon Gated? Understanding Your Security Options

Madeira Canyon offers both gated and non gated neighborhoods, giving buyers flexibility based on their privacy preferences and budget. The community is not entirely guard gated, but three of its approximately eight neighborhoods feature controlled access with enhanced security measures.

Guard Gated Neighborhoods

The Club at Madeira Canyon provides the highest level of security in the community. This 55+ active adult neighborhood includes 24 hour guard gates, nighttime security patrols, and roaming security throughout the day. With approximately 500 to 546 homes, The Club offers residents peace of mind along with exclusive clubhouse amenities.

Videiras represents another guard gated option within Madeira Canyon. This smaller enclave of under 100 luxury homes features controlled entry and attracts buyers seeking maximum privacy. Homes in Videiras typically range from $800,000 to over $1.2 million.

Vareda offers gated access (though not guard gated) with a focus on single story floor plans starting around 1,780 square feet. This neighborhood appeals to buyers who want controlled entry without the higher price point of the luxury enclaves.

Non Gated Neighborhoods

The remaining neighborhoods in Madeira Canyon, including Andorra, Castillo, Moreno, Santana, and Valencia, do not have gated entry. However, the community's geographic position provides natural security benefits. Situated at the highest elevation in Southern Nevada with limited entry points, Madeira Canyon's location creates an inherent barrier that discourages pass through traffic.

Residents in non gated sections still benefit from the overall community's secluded character. The winding roads and foothill terrain mean visitors rarely stumble upon Madeira Canyon unless they intend to be there.

Security Beyond the Gates

Henderson consistently ranks among the safest large cities in the United States, and Madeira Canyon's location in the 89044 zip code reflects that reputation. The combination of community design, limited access roads, and Henderson's strong police presence contributes to a secure environment throughout all neighborhoods.
