Wallin Elementary School: Madeira Canyon's Neighborhood School

Wallin Elementary School serves as Madeira Canyon's designated elementary school, earning a B+ rating and ranking among the top 10 elementary schools in Clark County. The school's location adjacent to Madeira Canyon Park creates an ideal setting for students living in the community, with many families able to walk or bike to campus.

Academic Performance

Wallin Elementary consistently outperforms district and state averages on standardized assessments. Recent data shows approximately 59% of students meeting or exceeding math proficiency standards and 68% achieving reading proficiency benchmarks. These scores place Wallin significantly above Clark County School District averages, reflecting strong instructional quality and engaged families.

The school serves approximately 700 students across grades kindergarten through fifth, maintaining manageable class sizes that allow for individual attention. The student to teacher ratio averages approximately 18 to 1, favorable compared to many Nevada public schools.

School Environment

Wallin Elementary occupies a modern campus designed specifically for the Madeira Canyon community. Facilities include technology equipped classrooms, a library media center, cafeteria, gymnasium, and outdoor play areas. The campus connects directly to Madeira Canyon Park, providing expanded outdoor space for physical education and recess activities.

The school emphasizes a positive learning culture with active parent involvement through PTA programs, volunteer opportunities, and family events throughout the academic year. This engaged community contributes to the school's strong performance and welcoming atmosphere.

Special Programs

Wallin Elementary offers programs addressing varied student needs, including gifted and talented education (GATE), English language learner support, and special education services. Enrichment activities extend learning beyond core curriculum, with opportunities in arts, music, and STEM related projects depending on current programming.

Before and After School Care

Working parents find childcare options available through school based programs and nearby providers. The Safekey program operates on campus, providing supervised care before and after school hours. This convenience helps Madeira Canyon families manage work schedules while ensuring children remain in safe, familiar environments.

Location Advantages

Wallin Elementary's position within Madeira Canyon creates unique benefits for community residents. Children can walk to school through neighborhood sidewalks, building independence while parents appreciate the safety of contained community streets. The adjacent park means students access quality recreational facilities during school hours without bus transportation.
