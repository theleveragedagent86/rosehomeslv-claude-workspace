Del E. Webb Middle School: Serving Madeira Canyon Students

Del E. Webb Middle School serves Madeira Canyon students in grades six through eight, providing the bridge between elementary education at Wallin and high school at Liberty. Located in the Anthem area approximately 10 minutes from Madeira Canyon, the school offers comprehensive middle school programming for the community's adolescent students.

Academic Programs

Del E. Webb Middle School delivers Clark County School District's standard middle school curriculum including English language arts, mathematics, science, and social studies. The school offers accelerated coursework for students ready to advance beyond grade level expectations, helping prepare motivated learners for high school honors and Advanced Placement tracks.

Elective offerings expand student experiences beyond core academics. Options typically include visual arts, performing arts, music programs including band and orchestra, world languages, and technology courses. These electives allow students to explore interests and develop skills that continue through high school.

School Facilities

The campus includes modern classroom buildings, science laboratories, a library media center, gymnasium, and outdoor athletic facilities. The school's Anthem location provides a well maintained campus environment consistent with Henderson's high expectations for public facilities.

Technology integration supports contemporary learning approaches, with computer access and digital resources available for student use. The school continues updating facilities and equipment to match evolving educational needs.

Athletics and Activities

Del E. Webb Middle School fields competitive athletic teams in sports including basketball, volleyball, soccer, track and field, and others depending on current offerings. Middle school athletics provide students opportunities to develop teamwork, physical fitness, and competitive experience before high school sports.

Clubs and extracurricular activities extend beyond athletics, with student organizations covering academic interests, community service, and social connections. These programs help students build friendships and explore passions outside the classroom.

Transportation

Clark County School District provides bus transportation for Madeira Canyon students attending Del E. Webb Middle School. Bus stops within the community reduce morning and afternoon logistics for families. Parents can also arrange drop off and pickup using the school's designated traffic patterns.

Transition Support

The school provides transition programs helping sixth graders adjust from elementary school environments and preparing eighth graders for high school. Counseling staff support students navigating the social and academic changes characteristic of middle school years.

Parent Involvement

Del E. Webb Middle School encourages parent engagement through volunteer opportunities, parent teacher organizations, and regular communication about student progress. Staying involved during middle school years helps families support students through this transitional period.
