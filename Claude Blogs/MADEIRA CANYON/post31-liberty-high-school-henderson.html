Liberty High School: Serving Madeira Canyon Students

Liberty High School serves as the zoned high school for Madeira Canyon students, providing comprehensive secondary education for grades nine through twelve. Located in the Henderson area, Liberty offers the academic programs, extracurricular activities, and athletic opportunities families expect from quality public high schools.

Academic Programs

Liberty High School delivers Clark County School District's comprehensive high school curriculum, including college preparatory coursework across core subjects. Students access Advanced Placement (AP) classes in multiple disciplines, allowing motivated learners to earn college credit while still in high school. Honors tracks provide accelerated learning for students ready for advanced challenges.

Career and Technical Education (CTE) programs prepare students for workforce entry or continued technical training. These pathways cover various industries and provide hands on experience alongside traditional academics. The combination of college prep and career focused options serves diverse student goals.

Athletics

Liberty High School competes in Nevada Interscholastic Activities Association (NIAA) athletics, fielding teams across numerous sports including football, basketball, baseball, softball, soccer, volleyball, swimming, track and field, and others. The Patriots athletic program provides student athletes opportunities for competition, teamwork development, and potential college recruitment.

Athletic facilities include a stadium, gymnasium, baseball and softball diamonds, tennis courts, and practice fields. These resources support both competitive programs and physical education classes.

Extracurricular Activities

Beyond athletics, Liberty High School offers clubs and organizations covering academic interests, performing arts, community service, and social connections. Students participate in activities ranging from debate and academic decathlon to drama productions and music ensembles. These programs help students explore interests, build leadership skills, and create lasting friendships.

School Facilities

The Liberty High School campus includes modern classroom buildings, science laboratories, computer facilities, a library media center, performing arts spaces, and cafeteria. The school continues updating technology and facilities to support contemporary educational approaches.

Transportation

Clark County School District provides bus transportation for Madeira Canyon students attending Liberty High School. The commute from Madeira Canyon to campus takes approximately 15 to 20 minutes depending on traffic and specific pickup locations. Many upperclassmen with vehicles drive themselves once they obtain licenses.

College Preparation

Liberty High School's counseling department supports students through college application processes, including transcript preparation, recommendation coordination, and application guidance. SAT and ACT preparation resources help students maximize standardized test performance. Graduates regularly attend universities throughout Nevada and across the country.

School Performance

Ryan Rose encourages families to research current school performance metrics when evaluating Madeira Canyon. High school ratings, graduation rates, and college acceptance statistics change over time. The Nevada Department of Education provides current data for informed decision making.
