Schools Near Madeira Canyon: A Parent's Complete Guide

Madeira Canyon families have access to some of Henderson's highest rated public schools, with Wallin Elementary ranking in the top 10 of all Clark County elementary schools and strong options continuing through middle and high school. The community falls within the Clark County School District, the fifth largest district in the United States.

Wallin Elementary School

Shirley and Bill Wallin Elementary School sits adjacent to Madeira Canyon Park, making drop off and pickup convenient for residents. The school serves grades pre K through 5 and earns a B+ rating on Niche with an A minus designation on some platforms. With approximately 702 students and an 18 to 1 student teacher ratio, Wallin provides personalized attention that larger schools cannot match.

Academic performance at Wallin exceeds district and state averages, with 59 percent of students proficient in math and 68 percent proficient in reading. The school offers a Gifted and Talented program and ranks in the top 7.2 percent of all Nevada elementary schools according to SchoolDigger.

Del E. Webb Middle School

Students from Madeira Canyon transition to Del E. Webb Middle School for grades 6 through 8. The school holds a B+ rating on Niche and scores 8 out of 10 on GreatSchools, placing it among Henderson's stronger middle school options. With approximately 1,640 students, Del E. Webb offers diverse extracurricular activities including archery, chess club, art programs, and competitive sports teams.

Liberty High School

Liberty High School serves Madeira Canyon students for grades 9 through 12. The school maintains a B+ rating on Niche and boasts a 93 percent graduation rate. Liberty offers 16 Advanced Placement courses for college bound students and fields competitive athletic programs.

Private and Charter Alternatives

Families seeking alternatives to public schools have several nearby options. Pinecrest Academy Inspirada offers a tuition free charter option for grades K through 8, while Pinecrest Academy Sloan Canyon serves students through 12th grade. Henderson International School and Foothills Montessori provide private education alternatives within reasonable driving distance.
