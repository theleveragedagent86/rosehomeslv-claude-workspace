The Club at Madeira Canyon: Complete Guide to Henderson's 55+ Community

The Club at Madeira Canyon is a guard gated 55+ active adult community offering approximately 500 to 546 single family homes with exclusive amenities, 24 hour security, and some of the most spectacular views in the Las Vegas valley. Built by Del Webb and completed around 2017, this enclave within the larger Madeira Canyon community attracts retirees and active adults seeking resort style living.

Location and Views

Situated at approximately 3,000 feet above sea level, The Club at Madeira Canyon sits at the highest point of an already elevated community. Residents enjoy unobstructed panoramic views of the Las Vegas Strip, city lights, and the surrounding McCullough Mountain range. The nighttime vistas from the clubhouse and many home sites are frequently described as breathtaking.

Home Styles and Pricing

The Club offers five distinct floor plans ranging from approximately 2,300 to over 4,500 square feet. Homes feature three to five bedrooms, two and a half to four and a half bathrooms, and attached two to three car garages. Both single story and two story options accommodate different lifestyle preferences.

Current resale prices in The Club at Madeira Canyon typically range from $900,000 to $1.8 million, reflecting the premium location, security, and amenities. All construction was completed by Del Webb, known for energy efficient building practices including programmable thermostats, tankless water heaters, and high efficiency windows.

Clubhouse and Amenities

The hilltop clubhouse serves as the social hub for Club residents. Amenities include a resort style swimming pool with cabanas, hot tub and spa, state of the art fitness center, aerobics and dance studio, tennis and pickleball courts, basketball court, and multi purpose meeting rooms. On site massage therapists offer services at the clubhouse for an additional fee.

A full time activities director coordinates monthly events including cocktail parties, wine tastings, potlucks, holiday celebrations, and the popular Spring Fling each March.

Security and Privacy

The Club features 24 hour guard gates with controlled entry, nighttime security patrols, and roaming daytime security. HOA fees of approximately $245 per month cover all security services and amenity access.
