Madeira Canyon Home Prices 2026: Current Market Analysis

Madeira Canyon home prices currently range from approximately $515,000 to over $2 million, with a median sale price around $770,000 as of early 2026. This Henderson community has seen steady appreciation of roughly 7 percent year over year, reflecting strong demand for elevated properties with Strip views and proximity to outdoor recreation.

Current Price Ranges by Neighborhood

Entry level opportunities in Madeira Canyon begin in neighborhoods like Castillo and Moreno, where homes typically list between $515,000 and $700,000. These non gated sections offer the same community amenities and school access as premium neighborhoods at more accessible price points. Most homes in this range feature three to four bedrooms and 1,800 to 2,500 square feet of living space.

Mid range buyers will find options in Andorra, Santana, and Valencia, where prices generally fall between $700,000 and $1 million. These neighborhoods offer larger floor plans, many exceeding 3,000 square feet, with upgraded finishes and better view lots.

Luxury properties in The Club at Madeira Canyon, Videiras, and Vareda command premium prices from $900,000 to over $1.8 million. Guard gated security, exclusive amenities, and the most elevated lots with panoramic Strip views justify the higher investment in these enclaves.

Market Trends and Days on Market

Homes in Madeira Canyon currently average approximately 54 days on market, indicating a balanced environment where well priced properties move efficiently without the frenzy of a seller's market. Buyers have time to make informed decisions while sellers still achieve strong returns.

The community's fully built out status (construction ended around 2017) means no new inventory will dilute existing home values. All transactions involve resales, which tends to support price stability in established neighborhoods.

Price Per Square Foot

Current price per square foot in Madeira Canyon averages between $250 and $350, depending on neighborhood, lot position, and view quality. Homes with direct Strip views or backing to open desert command the highest premiums, while interior lots trade at more moderate levels.

Investment Outlook

The community's elevation and privacy create a distinctive value proposition that competing neighborhoods cannot easily replicate.
