## Utilities

**Electric — Overton Power District No. 5 (OPD5)**
- Public utility since 1935
- Mesquite office: 731 Turtleback Rd; 702-346-5710
- Avg residential rate ~11.5¢/kWh; avg monthly bill ~$165
- 2024 power purchase agreement drove ~6.37% rate increase (+$8.40/mo)
- Source: https://www.opd5.com/

**Water — Virgin Valley Water District (VVWD)**
- 500 Riverside Rd; 702-346-5731
- Serves ~18,000 residents from 9 wells in Basin 222
- Tiered by meter size; bills due by 15th; 10% late fee
- 3-year rate increase approved Nov 2023
- Source: https://vvwdnv.com/

## Outdoor/Adventure

**Gold Butte National Monument**
- Access: 5 mi south of Mesquite via I-15 Exit 112 to SR-170
- 300+ miles of primitive routes
- High-clearance/4WD required for inner 19 mi
- Whitney Pocket key destination ~20 mi in
- Virgin Peak is highest summit
- Source: https://www.blm.gov/visit/gold-butte-national-monument

**Logandale Trails System**
- ~35 mi SW of Mesquite
- 200+ miles of OHV/ATV/horseback/hiking trails
- BLM-managed
- Best season: winter–spring
- Source: https://www.blm.gov/visit/logandale-trails

**Snow Canyon State Park (Utah)**
- ~50 miles from Mesquite (1 hr drive via I-15)
- Red sandstone, lava tubes
- Trails: Jenny's Canyon, Whiptail (paved), Hidden Pinyon, Johnson Canyon, Cinder Cone, Padre Canyon (6.3 mi)
- Source: https://www.visitutah.com/places-to-go/parks-outdoors/snow-canyon-state-park

**Mesquite OHV Streets**
- Street-legal OHV use on designated city streets
- Source: https://www.mesquitenv.gov/resources/off-highway-vehicles

**Fishing**
- **Mesquite Urban Pond** (Hafen Lane Park): stocked seasonally by NDOW; rainbow trout, channel catfish
- **Virgin River**: largemouth bass, rainbow trout, Utah chub
- **Lake Mead** (~1.5 hr south): striped bass at Echo Bay Marina; Boulder Beach Pier
- Clark County limits: 10 trout, 15 warmwater game fish (max 10 black bass)
- NV fishing license required age 12+
- Source: https://www.ndow.org/waters/mesquite-urban-pond/

## Real Estate & Communities

### Sun City Mesquite (Del Webb / Pulte)
- 55+ age-restricted
- 2,014-acre master plan (812 acres developed)
- Construction began 2007
- Plan for 3,750 single-family homes
- 21 ranch floor plans across 6 collections
- 30,000 sq ft Pioneer Center
- On-site Conestoga Golf Club (Gary Panks design, 7,204 yd par-72)
- HOAs: Sun City Mesquite HOA + Anthem Mesquite Master Association
- Source: https://suncitymesquite.org/

### Highland Fairways
- First senior (55+) community in Mesquite
- 127 single-family homes built 2001–2004 by Parker Construction
- 1,378–1,824 sq ft; 4 floor plans
- HOA includes landscaping, sewer, trash, cable, pest control
- Mesquite Vistas Country Club access (pool, pickleball, bocce, shuffleboard)
- Source: https://www.highlandfairways.net/

### Sunset Greens
- Gated; surrounded by CasaBlanca golf course
- ~741 residents
- Mostly townhomes + some SFR
- HOA ~$220/mo (landscape, 2 pools/spas, RV parking, 20% CasaBlanca discount)

### Other Communities
- **New Horizon** — luxury custom homes by Nevada Residential Construction, adj. Falcon Ridge Golf
- **Val Vista** — semi-custom homes by NRC
- **Sorrento** — single-story ranch floor plans, golf-course adjacent
- **Anthem at Mesquite** — within Sun City master plan

### Market Metrics (2025–2026)
- Median home price: ~$383,650
- Median sale: ~$409,900
- Median rent: ~$1,975 (houses $1,200–$2,490)
- Market favors buyers: ~5.4-month supply, ~90 days on market (late 2025)
- **Best time to buy**: August–December (higher inventory, less demand)

### Short-Term Rentals / Airbnb
- Mesquite Vacation Home Rental Ordinance — Title 2, Chapter 13
- Annual license required for rentals <30 days
- Max occupancy: 2 adults/bedroom + 4 additional, capped at 10
- Required local contact, landline, parking/noise/cleaning rules
- Transient Occupancy Tax due monthly
- STRs prohibited in unincorporated Mesquite Township (Clark County 2022 ordinance exempts Mesquite Township)
- Airbnb performance: avg nightly rate $266, RevPAR $113, occupancy 42.5%, avg host earns ~$34,894/yr; March strongest, September softest; 134 active listings (98.5% entire home)
- Source: https://www.cityofmesquite.com/DocumentCenter/View/21691/Short-Term-Rental-Reference-Guide

## Mesquite NV History

- Settled 1880 by Mormon pioneers as "Mesquite Flat"
- Part of Mormon cotton/grape corridor
- Established on third attempt after Virgin River floods (June 1882 flood washed out canals)
- Early adobe construction (nearest lumber 85 mi; nearest railroad 150 mi at Milford UT)
- Name shortened to "Mesquite" in 1898
- Incorporated 1984
- Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mesquite,_Nevada
