Charles A. Hughes Middle School Mesquite NV: Grades 6-8 Guide

Charles A. Hughes Middle School serves grades 6 through 8 in Mesquite, NV, as a public middle school within the Clark County School District. Located at 550 Hafen Lane, Mesquite, NV 89027, this school bridges the transition from elementary to high school education for students in the community.

School Overview and Educational Focus

Charles A. Hughes Middle School is one of the primary secondary education institutions in Mesquite, part of a three-school public pipeline alongside Virgin Valley Elementary and Virgin Valley High School. The school operates within CCSD, which serves over 300,000 students across its vast service area. Parents and students can contact the school at 702-346-3250 for program information, enrollment details, or academic questions.

The middle school maintains a student-to-teacher ratio of approximately 16 to 1, consistent with other Clark County schools in the Mesquite area. This ratio allows educators to provide meaningful classroom instruction while maintaining the supportive atmosphere that smaller community schools offer compared to larger urban district schools.
