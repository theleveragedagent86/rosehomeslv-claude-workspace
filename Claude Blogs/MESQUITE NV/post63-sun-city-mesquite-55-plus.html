Sun City Mesquite 55+ Community: Living the Dream

Sun City Mesquite is a premium 55+ active adult community developed by Del Webb with a $154 per month HOA fee, offering exceptional amenities including the 30,000 square foot Pioneer Center, on-site dining at The 1880 Grille, and access to the championship Conestoga Golf Course. This flagship retirement community redefines what active aging looks like in Mesquite.

The Pioneer Center: Heart of Community Life

The centerpiece of Sun City Mesquite is the Pioneer Center, a 30,000 square foot recreational facility designed specifically for active adults. This state-of-the-art complex features resort-style swimming pools, a comprehensive fitness center with modern equipment, professional-grade tennis courts, and a full-service spa. Residents enjoy year-round activities, fitness classes, social events, and wellness programs designed to promote healthy, active lifestyles.

The Pioneer Center serves as the social hub where residents build friendships, pursue fitness goals, and enjoy recreational activities without leaving their community. The facility's design and amenities rival those found in luxury resort destinations, creating an environment where residents can thrive physically and socially.

Dining Excellence at The 1880 Grille

Sun City Mesquite's on-site restaurant, The 1880 Grille, brings fine dining to your community. Located with scenic views overlooking the Conestoga Golf Course, the restaurant offers a full menu prepared by experienced chefs. Residents enjoy elegant dining without leaving home, making it perfect for date nights, social gatherings, or casual meals with friends. The ambiance combines comfort with sophistication, creating a destination that draws residents and guests alike.

Championship Golf at Conestoga Golf Course

Conestoga Golf Course serves as the home course for Sun City Mesquite residents. This championship layout offers challenging play for skilled golfers while remaining accessible to those refining their game. The course's design incorporates Mesquite's natural landscape, creating memorable rounds with beautiful desert views. For golf enthusiasts, having a premium course as your home course transforms retirement into an active, engaging lifestyle. Whether you play multiple times weekly or occasionally, the course provides excellent maintenance, professional instruction, and social opportunities through tournament play and member events.
