Crime Rate in Mesquite NV: Is It Safe?

Mesquite NV ranks as the #4 safest city out of 15 Nevada communities, with a crime rate of 22.99, which is 37% lower than the national average. If you're considering relocating to Mesquite or planning to invest in property here, safety is likely a top priority. The good news: crime statistics show Mesquite consistently outperforms national benchmarks.

How Safe Is Mesquite NV Compared to the Nation?

The overall crime rate in Mesquite sits at 22.99, significantly below the national average of 33.37. This 37% difference translates into real-world safety that residents notice. What makes these numbers even more impressive is the breakdown of violent versus property crime. Violent crime in Mesquite registers at 156 per 100,000 residents, a staggering 57.7% lower than the national average. Property crime, at 1,298 per 100,000, runs 33.6% below national levels. Most notably, the city recorded zero homicides in the latest reported year, a testament to the peaceful community environment.

Is Crime Trending Up or Down in Mesquite?

The trajectory matters as much as the snapshot. Year-over-year, Mesquite saw a 27.2% decrease in crime, and from 2023 to 2024, crime declined by 7%. This downward trend suggests that local law enforcement initiatives and community efforts are working effectively. For potential homebuyers or families considering a move, these declining statistics offer confidence that safety is improving, not deteriorating.
