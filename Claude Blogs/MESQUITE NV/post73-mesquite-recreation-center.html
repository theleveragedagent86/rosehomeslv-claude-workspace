The Mesquite Recreation Center at 100 W Old Mill Road in Mesquite NV features three indoor pickleball courts, fitness programs, and drop in recreation for all ages, with pickleball sessions Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday from 11:30 am to 2:30 pm. ## What Can You Do at the Mesquite Recreation Center? The Mesquite Recreation Center serves as the hub for indoor recreation in Mesquite NV, anchoring pickleball, fitness classes, youth programs, and seasonal activities for residents and visitors. The facility houses three dedicated indoor pickleball courts that run scheduled drop in play on a consistent weekly rhythm. Drop in rates are $6 for adults 18 through 54, $3 for players 55 and older, and free for active members. Beyond pickleball, the center offers general fitness programming, group exercise classes, and youth activities that rotate through the calendar. For specific program times or league registrations, residents can reach facility coordinator Nicholas Montoya at 702-346-8732. The center sits within walking distance of downtown Mesquite and is a short drive from most residential neighborhoods. ## When Is Pickleball Open at the Mesquite Recreation Center? Indoor pickleball at the Mesquite Recreation Center runs Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday from 11:30 am to 2:30 pm on three dedicated courts. That consistent schedule has turned the facility into the unofficial gathering spot for the local pickleball community, which includes a mix of full time residents and snowbirds who winter in Mesquite. Players of all skill levels rotate in, and the climate controlled indoor space is especially valuable during the hot summer and cold winter mornings when outdoor courts at Hafen Lane Park feel less appealing. ## What Other Recreation Options Does Mesquite Offer?
