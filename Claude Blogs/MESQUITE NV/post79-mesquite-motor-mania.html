Mesquite Motor Mania is the annual classic car show held January 16 through 18, 2026 at CasaBlanca Resort in Mesquite NV, drawing hot rods, muscle cars, and vintage vehicles for a three day automotive celebration. The event runs 8 am to 6 pm Friday and Saturday with free public viewing on the CasaBlanca grounds at 950 W Mesquite Blvd. ## What Is Mesquite Motor Mania? Mesquite Motor Mania is a classic car show staged at CasaBlanca Resort Casino, with hundreds of restored vehicles lining the property at 950 W Mesquite Blvd. The 2026 show takes place January 16 to 18 and features hot rods, muscle cars, street rods, and classic trucks displayed across the resort's expansive outdoor parking and event areas. Public viewing hours run 8 am to 6 pm on Friday and Saturday, giving spectators two full days to walk the show. The event pairs the car show with activities inside CasaBlanca, including entertainment in the Showroom, dining at the resort's restaurants, and hotel packages for participants who travel in from out of state. Mesquite's mild January weather, with highs typically in the 50s and 60s, makes it a favored winter destination for car clubs from across the Southwest. ## Where Does the Mesquite Classic Car Show Take Place? The show is centered at CasaBlanca Resort Casino, 950 W Mesquite Blvd, the anchor property on the west end of town. CasaBlanca provides the parking lots, event space, and entertainment venue that host the weekend. The resort sits directly off Interstate 15 at Exit 120, making it easy for participants to trailer cars in from Las Vegas, St. George, or Southern California. For attendees looking to make a weekend of it, the CasaBlanca RV Park on property offers 45 sites with full 30 and 50 amp hookups, and the resort's hotel rooms fill quickly during motor mania weekend.
