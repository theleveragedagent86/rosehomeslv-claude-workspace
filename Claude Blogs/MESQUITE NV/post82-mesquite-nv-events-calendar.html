The Mesquite NV events calendar runs year round, anchored by Mesquite Motor Mania in January, the Hot Air Balloon Festival later that same month, the Spring Festival on April 25, Mesquite Days from April 29 to May 2, and weekly farmers markets from spring through fall. The City of Mesquite maintains the official calendar at mesquitenv.gov/events. ## What Are the Biggest Events in Mesquite NV? The Mesquite NV events calendar leads with Mesquite Motor Mania January 16 to 18, 2026 at CasaBlanca Resort, featuring hot rods and classic cars. The following weekend, January 23 to 25, brings the Mesquite Hot Air Balloon Festival with 30 plus balloons launching from the Oasis parking lot across from CasaBlanca. These two January weekends draw thousands of snowbirds who time their Mesquite visits around the back to back events. Spring shifts into Mesquite Spring Festival on April 25, 2026, followed immediately by Mesquite Days from April 29 through May 2. Mesquite Days includes the community parade, the Mayor's Pancake Breakfast, the Mesquite Mile Fun Run, a pickleball tournament, and the Ms. Senior Mesquite Pageant. ## Does Mesquite Have Year-Round Events? Beyond the headline festivals, Mesquite sustains year round programming through its farmers markets and recurring community events. The Downtown Mesquite Farmers Market operates Saturdays from 9 am to 1 pm at Front Street Station, April through November, while the Mesquite Plaza Friday Night Farmers Markets run year round. Together they anchor weekend activity in the city center. Golf tournaments, pickleball leagues at the Mesquite Recreation Center, and resort entertainment at CasaBlanca, Eureka, and Virgin River casinos fill in the calendar between marquee festivals. Mild fall and winter weather, with highs in the 50s and 60s even in January, keep outdoor programming active through the cooler months. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose points out that buyers in Mesquite NV should pay attention to the events calendar when they tour homes, because the rhythm of the city's festivals signals how active the community is year round.
