Highland Fairways Mesquite NV is the first 55+ senior community built in Mesquite, featuring 127 single family homes constructed between 2001 and 2004 by Parker Construction. The low maintenance lifestyle and Mesquite Vistas Country Club access make it a favorite for active retirees. ## What Makes Highland Fairways Mesquite NV Unique? Highland Fairways holds the distinction of being the original age restricted community in Mesquite, established before Sun City and Sunset Greens arrived. The neighborhood contains 127 single story homes across 4 floor plans, ranging from 1,378 to 1,824 square feet. Every home was built by Parker Construction, giving the community a consistent architectural character. Lot sizes are modest, which keeps exterior upkeep manageable for residents who want to spend time golfing and traveling rather than maintaining a yard. Buyers typically find values that stretch further here than in newer master planned communities. ## What Does the HOA Cover at Highland Fairways? The Highland Fairways HOA bundles an unusually comprehensive set of services into the monthly fee. Coverage includes front yard landscaping, sewer, trash, basic cable television, and pest control. This all in one approach is one reason snowbirds gravitate to the community; homes can be left for months without exterior concerns. Residents also enjoy deeded access to Mesquite Vistas Country Club amenities, including the pool, pickleball courts, bocce ball, and shuffleboard.
