What Is Mountain Trails in Summerlin?

Mountain Trails is an exclusive, guard gated enclave of 103 custom homes nestled within The Trails Village in Summerlin, Las Vegas. Known locally as "Half Acres," this luxury community features half acre to full acre homesites where no two residences look alike.

What Makes Mountain Trails Different from Other Summerlin Communities?

Mountain Trails stands apart because every home was custom built between 1996 and 2004. Architectural styles range from Mediterranean to contemporary to desert modern, giving the neighborhood a distinctive, curated feel. With only 103 homes on oversized lots, the community offers a level of privacy and space that is rare in the Las Vegas valley.

The community sits behind a 24 hour guard gate with entry off Snow Trail near West Lake Mead Boulevard. Residents and their guests access the neighborhood by appointment only, adding an extra layer of security and exclusivity. Home prices currently range from approximately $1,000,000 to over $5,000,000, with a median around $2,640,000.

What Amenities Does Mountain Trails Offer?

Inside the gates, Mountain Trails features a private community park with two tennis courts, a half basketball court, a playground shaded by mature trees, and a covered picnic pavilion. Residents also enjoy access to The Trails Community Center and swimming pool just outside the gates.

Beyond the immediate neighborhood, Summerlin's 150+ mile trail system connects Mountain Trails to hundreds of parks, open spaces, and Red Rock Canyon. Downtown Summerlin's shopping, dining, and entertainment options are just minutes away.

A Local Perspective on Mountain Trails

Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate specialist, considers Mountain Trails one of Summerlin's best kept secrets for buyers seeking custom luxury without the price tags found in newer communities like The Ridges. The mature landscaping, established neighborhood feel, and generous lot sizes create an atmosphere that newer developments simply cannot replicate.
