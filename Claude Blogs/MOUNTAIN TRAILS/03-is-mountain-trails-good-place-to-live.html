Is Mountain Trails a Good Place to Live in Las Vegas?

Yes, Mountain Trails is one of the best places to live in Las Vegas for buyers who want privacy, space, and luxury within a guard gated Summerlin community. The neighborhood consistently ranks among the most desirable residential enclaves in the valley.

What Do Residents Like About Living in Mountain Trails?

Mountain Trails offers a rare combination of large lots and custom architecture just minutes from everything Las Vegas has to offer. All 103 homes sit on half acre to full acre parcels, giving residents the kind of breathing room that most Summerlin neighborhoods simply cannot match. The 24 hour guard gate provides peace of mind, while the private park, tennis courts, and playground create a true neighborhood atmosphere.

Safety is another major draw. Summerlin's violent crime rate sits at 15.9 per 1,000 residents, well below the national average of 22.7. Inside a guard gated community like Mountain Trails, residents enjoy an added layer of security that makes the neighborhood especially appealing to families and retirees.

How Convenient Is the Location?

Mountain Trails residents can reach the Las Vegas Strip in 15 to 20 minutes, the airport in 25 to 30 minutes, and downtown Las Vegas in 20 to 25 minutes. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is roughly two miles away. Downtown Summerlin, with its restaurants, shops, and entertainment venues, is a short drive from the community.

Families benefit from access to well regarded schools, including Palo Verde High School, which earns a GreatSchools rating of 8 out of 10 and offers both AP and IB programs. Private options like Adelson Educational Campus and The Meadows School are within two to three miles.

A Local Perspective on Mountain Trails Living

Ryan Rose often recommends Mountain Trails to buyers who want the feel of a private estate without leaving the city. The mature trees, quiet streets, and oversized lots create an environment that feels worlds away from the energy of the Strip, yet everything is still remarkably close. For those who value both seclusion and convenience, Mountain Trails delivers on both fronts.
