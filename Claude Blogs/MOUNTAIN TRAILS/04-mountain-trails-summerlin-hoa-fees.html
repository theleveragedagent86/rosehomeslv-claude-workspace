Mountain Trails Summerlin HOA Fees

Mountain Trails HOA fees total an estimated $100 to $200 per month, which includes both the Summerlin Master Association fee and a separate sub association fee for the Mountain Trails community. For a luxury guard gated neighborhood, these fees are remarkably reasonable.

What Do Mountain Trails HOA Fees Cover?

The fee structure has two components. The Summerlin Master Association fee runs approximately $55 to $65 per month and covers access to Summerlin's extensive network of parks, the 150+ mile trail system, and community wide maintenance. The Mountain Trails sub association fee covers the 24 hour guard gate, private community park maintenance, tennis courts, playground, basketball court, and the covered picnic pavilion.

Residents also gain access to The Trails Community Center and swimming pool through their association membership. These shared amenities serve the broader Trails Village while the private park and guard gate remain exclusive to Mountain Trails homeowners.

How Do Mountain Trails HOA Fees Compare to Other Summerlin Communities?

At $100 to $200 per month total, Mountain Trails HOA fees are competitive with other guard gated communities in Summerlin. Many newer luxury communities charge significantly more for comparable amenities. The relatively modest fee structure reflects the community's established nature; the infrastructure has been in place since the late 1990s, and the 103 home community shares costs efficiently across all homeowners.

It is worth noting that HOA fees can adjust over time based on community needs, reserve fund requirements, and maintenance costs. Prospective buyers should always verify current fees during the due diligence process, as the amounts listed here are estimates based on recent data.

A Local Perspective on Mountain Trails HOA Value

Ryan Rose points out that Mountain Trails delivers exceptional value for its HOA fees. Guard gated security alone would justify much of the monthly cost, and residents also receive a private park, sports courts, and full access to Summerlin's community wide amenities. Compared to what homeowners pay in newer luxury developments, the total monthly obligation in Mountain Trails is a pleasant surprise for most buyers.
