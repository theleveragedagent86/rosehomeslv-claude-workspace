Schools Near Mountain Trails Summerlin

Mountain Trails families have access to several highly rated public and private schools, including zoned elementary options within The Trails Village, a strong middle school, and Palo Verde High School with its AP and IB programs. Both public and private education are well represented near this Summerlin community.

Which Public Schools Serve Mountain Trails?

Two elementary schools serve the Mountain Trails area. William R. Lummis Elementary and Richard H. Bryan Elementary both serve grades K through 5 and sit within The Trails Village, making drop off and pickup convenient for families. For middle school, students are zoned for Ernest A. Becker Sr. Middle School, which serves grades 6 through 8 in the 89134 zip code.

Palo Verde High School is the zoned high school for Mountain Trails students. With a GreatSchools rating of 8 out of 10 and a Niche grade of B+, Palo Verde offers both Advanced Placement and International Baccalaureate programs. The school serves approximately 3,243 students and provides a wide range of academic and extracurricular opportunities.

What Private Schools Are Nearby?

Two of Nevada's top private schools sit within minutes of Mountain Trails. Adelson Educational Campus, located roughly two miles away, serves pre K through 12th grade and earns a Niche A+ rating. It ranks as the number two best private K through 12 school in Nevada, with an impressive 6 to 1 student to teacher ratio.

The Meadows School is approximately three miles from Mountain Trails and also earns a Niche A+ rating. The school offers 24 AP courses and boasts a 100 percent college placement rate for its graduates. Both private options provide families with outstanding alternatives to the public school system.
