Mountain Trails Custom Homes and Architecture

Mountain Trails in Summerlin is a collection of 103 individually designed custom homes where no two residences are alike. Located within The Trails Village, this guard gated community showcases architectural styles ranging from Mediterranean to contemporary to desert modern, all set on half acre to full acre lots.

What Architectural Styles Are Found in Mountain Trails?

Mountain Trails features three primary architectural styles: Mediterranean, contemporary, and desert modern. Mediterranean homes include stucco exteriors, clay tile roofs, and arched entryways. Contemporary builds favor clean lines, expansive glass, and open floor plans. Desert modern residences blend natural stone and earth tones with the surrounding landscape. Each home was custom built between 1996 and 2004 by private builders rather than production homebuilders.

Home sizes in Mountain Trails range from approximately 3,758 to 13,106 square feet. Many properties include features like motor courts, private pools, outdoor kitchens, and resort style entertaining areas. The generous lot sizes allow for architectural details that would be impossible on standard Summerlin lots, including extended driveways, guest casitas, and expansive rear yard pavilions.

How Are Mountain Trails Homes Different from Production Builds?

Every home in Mountain Trails was designed and built independently, meaning buyers chose their own architects, floor plans, and finishes. This sets the community apart from nearby neighborhoods where a handful of builders repeated similar models. The result is a streetscape with genuine variety, where a Mediterranean villa might sit next to a sleek contemporary residence.

Because these are true custom builds, interior layouts vary significantly from home to home. Buyers considering Mountain Trails should expect unique configurations, premium materials, and one of a kind design elements throughout. Current home prices range from approximately $1,000,000 to over $5,000,000, with a median near $2,640,000.

A Local Perspective on Mountain Trails Architecture

Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate specialist, notes that the custom nature of Mountain Trails homes means each property requires individual evaluation. Homes with updated interiors and modern systems tend to command top dollar, while original condition properties present opportunities for buyers who want to renovate on premium Summerlin land.
