Mountain Trails Luxury Homes Summerlin

Mountain Trails is one of Summerlin's most prestigious luxury home communities, featuring 103 custom residences behind a 24 hour guard gate in The Trails Village. Home prices range from approximately $1,000,000 to over $5,000,000, with a median price near $2,640,000.

What Makes Mountain Trails a Luxury Community?

Mountain Trails earns its luxury status through a combination of oversized lots, custom architecture, and round the clock security. Every home sits on a minimum half acre, with some properties spanning a full acre. Residences range from 3,758 to 13,106 square feet and include features like private pools, motor courts, outdoor kitchens, and resort style entertaining spaces.

The community is guard gated with entry by appointment only, providing a level of privacy that few Summerlin neighborhoods can match. With only 103 homes on quiet, tree lined streets, Mountain Trails offers a secluded atmosphere within minutes of Downtown Summerlin, Tivoli Village, and Red Rock Canyon.

How Does Mountain Trails Compare to Other Luxury Communities in Summerlin?

Mountain Trails offers a distinct alternative to newer luxury communities like The Ridges. While The Ridges features homes built in the 2000s and 2010s at higher price points, Mountain Trails delivers comparable lot sizes and custom construction at a lower median price. Homes in Mountain Trails were built between 1996 and 2004, and many have been extensively updated by their owners.

The community also benefits from mature landscaping that newer developments have not yet achieved. Established trees, lush yards, and a settled neighborhood feel create an environment that takes decades to develop. Proximity to Summerlin Hospital, Costco, Trader Joe's, and major highways including the 95 and 215 adds everyday convenience to the luxury setting.
