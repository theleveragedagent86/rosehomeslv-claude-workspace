Mountain Trails Investment Potential 2026

Mountain Trails in Summerlin presents strong investment potential in 2026 due to its limited inventory of 103 custom homes, half acre minimum lot sizes, and a guard gated location within one of Las Vegas's most desirable master planned communities. The current median home price sits near $2,640,000, with values supported by scarcity and land size.

Why Is Mountain Trails a Strong Investment in 2026?

The primary investment driver for Mountain Trails is supply constraint. With only 103 homes in a fully built out community, new inventory cannot be created. When a property lists for sale, it represents one of very few opportunities to own a half acre or larger lot inside a guard gated Summerlin neighborhood. This scarcity supports pricing stability even during broader market fluctuations.

Land value plays a significant role in the investment equation. Half acre lots within Summerlin's guard gated communities are not being developed in newer neighborhoods, where lot sizes trend smaller. As Las Vegas continues to grow and available land diminishes, the underlying land value of Mountain Trails properties is positioned to appreciate over the long term.

What Price Trends Define Mountain Trails in 2026?

Home prices in Mountain Trails currently range from approximately $1,000,000 to over $5,000,000. The wide range reflects the diversity of the housing stock: a 3,758 square foot home on a half acre lot will price differently than a 13,106 square foot estate on a full acre. Buyers at the lower end of the range often find properties with original finishes that offer value add renovation potential.

The community's location within The Trails Village provides convenient access to Downtown Summerlin, Tivoli Village, Red Rock Canyon, and major highways including the 95 and 215. Proximity to Summerlin Hospital Medical Center, just two miles away, and Harry Reid International Airport, roughly 25 to 30 minutes by car, adds practical value for residents and future resale appeal.
