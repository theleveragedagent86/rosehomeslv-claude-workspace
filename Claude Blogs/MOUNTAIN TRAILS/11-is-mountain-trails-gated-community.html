Is Mountain Trails a Gated Community?

If you are searching for a guard gated neighborhood in Summerlin with true privacy, Mountain Trails delivers exactly that. This exclusive enclave of 103 custom homes operates behind 24 hour security, making it one of the most private residential communities in the Las Vegas valley.

What Type of Gated Community Is Mountain Trails?

Mountain Trails is not simply gated; it is guard gated. The distinction matters. A gated community uses a keycard, code, or remote to control access. A guard gated community stations a live security officer at the entrance around the clock. Mountain Trails features 24 hour guard gate staffing at its single entry point off Snow Trail near West Lake Mead Boulevard.

Access to Mountain Trails is by appointment only. Visitors must be approved by a resident before the guard will allow entry, which means no solicitors, no random traffic, and no uninvited guests. This appointment only protocol sets Mountain Trails apart from most gated communities in Summerlin.

How Does Guard Gate Security Benefit Residents?

The controlled access creates a neighborhood where children play outside freely and residents walk the streets at any hour. Summerlin already reports a violent crime rate of 15.9 per 1,000 residents compared to the national average of 22.7. Mountain Trails adds a private security layer on top of that, with both LVMPD Summerlin Area Command and private Summerlin Council patrols covering the area.

Beyond security, the guard gate preserves the quiet character of the neighborhood. With only 103 homes on half acre to full acre lots, traffic inside the community stays minimal. There are no cut through drivers, no delivery trucks circling aimlessly, and no noise from adjacent streets.

A Local Perspective on Mountain Trails

Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate specialist, notes that Mountain Trails offers guard gated living at a price point that undercuts newer luxury communities like The Ridges. Buyers who prioritize privacy, mature landscaping, and generous lot sizes consistently rank Mountain Trails among the top guard gated neighborhoods in Summerlin. The appointment only access policy is a feature that many homeowners consider essential once they have experienced it.
