Mountain Trails Guard Gate and Security

Security is the first thing you notice when approaching Mountain Trails in Summerlin. A staffed guard gate operates 24 hours a day, seven days a week, controlling every vehicle that enters this exclusive community of 103 custom homes.

How Does the Mountain Trails Guard Gate Work?

Mountain Trails has a single entry point off Snow Trail near West Lake Mead Boulevard. A uniformed security officer is stationed at the gate at all times. Residents pass through with identification, while visitors must be pre approved by a homeowner before the guard will grant access.

This appointment only system means that every person entering the neighborhood has been verified. Delivery drivers, service providers, and guests all require advance authorization. The result is a community where unknown vehicles simply do not appear on the streets.

What Additional Security Does Mountain Trails Have?

The guard gate is just one layer of the security framework. LVMPD Summerlin Area Command provides law enforcement coverage for the broader area, while the Summerlin Council funds private security patrols that supplement police presence throughout the master planned community. Mountain Trails benefits from both.

Summerlin's overall safety record reinforces the value of this layered approach. The violent crime rate sits at 15.9 per 1,000 residents, well below the national average of 22.7. Property crime registers at 30 per 1,000, compared to 35.4 nationally. Inside a guard gated community with appointment only access, those already low numbers drop further.

A Local Perspective on Mountain Trails Security

Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate specialist, points out that the security infrastructure at Mountain Trails is a primary driver for buyers with families and those relocating from out of state. The peace of mind that comes with 24 hour staffed entry cannot be replicated by a keypad or remote controlled gate. Clients who tour Mountain Trails frequently cite the guard gate experience as the moment they decide this community is the right fit.
