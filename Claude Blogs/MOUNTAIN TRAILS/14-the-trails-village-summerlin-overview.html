The Trails Village Summerlin Overview

The Trails Village is one of Summerlin's original residential villages and the home base for Mountain Trails, one of the most exclusive guard gated communities in Las Vegas. Understanding The Trails Village is essential for anyone considering a home in this part of Summerlin.

What Is The Trails Village in Summerlin?

The Trails Village is a master planned village within Summerlin that encompasses multiple residential neighborhoods, including Mountain Trails. It was one of the earliest developed areas in Summerlin, which means mature landscaping, established infrastructure, and a strong sense of community identity.

The village is anchored by several key landmarks: The Trails Community Center (Summerlin's first, at 6,000 square feet with a swimming pool), The Trails Park spanning 14.6 acres, and the Summerlin Library and Performing Arts Center with its 284 seat theater. The Nevada Ballet Theatre also calls this village home.

What Shopping and Services Are in The Trails Village?

Trails Village Center is a 175,000 square foot retail hub with more than 50 shops and services. Anchor tenants include Albertsons and CVS, and the center also houses a U.S. Post Office. Residents handle groceries, prescriptions, banking, and dining without leaving the village.

This walkable convenience is a significant advantage for Mountain Trails homeowners. The guard gated community sits within minutes of everyday necessities, yet remains insulated from commercial traffic behind its 24 hour security gate.

How Does The Trails Village Connect to the Rest of Summerlin?

Summerlin's 150+ mile trail system threads through The Trails Village, connecting residents to dozens of parks, open spaces, and neighboring villages. Downtown Summerlin's shopping and entertainment district is approximately three miles away. Red Rock Canyon is a 15 minute drive, and the Las Vegas Strip can be reached in 15 to 20 minutes via Summerlin Parkway or the 215 Beltway.

A Local Perspective on The Trails Village

Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate specialist, considers The Trails Village one of the most complete neighborhoods in Summerlin. Between the community center, the performing arts venue, the shopping center, and 14.6 acres of parkland, residents rarely need to travel far for anything. For buyers drawn to Mountain Trails specifically, the surrounding village infrastructure adds significant lifestyle value that newer Summerlin developments are still building out.
