Mountain Trails Community Park: A Private Oasis in Summerlin

Few neighborhoods in Summerlin can claim a park that sits behind a guard gate. Mountain Trails Community Park is exactly that: a private, residents only recreation space tucked inside one of The Trails Village's most exclusive enclaves.

What Amenities Does Mountain Trails Community Park Offer?

Mountain Trails Community Park features two regulation tennis courts, a half basketball court, and a children's playground shaded by mature trees. A covered picnic pavilion provides a gathering spot for families and neighbors year round. The park's compact layout keeps everything within easy walking distance of every home in the 103 residence community.

Who Can Use Mountain Trails Community Park?

Because Mountain Trails is a guard gated neighborhood, the community park is reserved for residents and their guests. This means shorter wait times for courts, a quieter playground environment, and a level of privacy that public parks simply cannot match. For families with young children, knowing exactly who shares the space offers real peace of mind.

How Does the Park Compare to Other Summerlin Parks?

Summerlin boasts more than 250 parks across its villages, yet very few are private. Mountain Trails Community Park may be smaller than regional facilities like The Trails Park, but exclusivity is the advantage. Residents also have full access to the larger parks nearby, including The Trails Park's pool complex and sports fields, giving them the best of both worlds.
