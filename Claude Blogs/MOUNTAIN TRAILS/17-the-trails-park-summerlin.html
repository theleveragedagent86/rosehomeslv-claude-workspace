The Trails Park Summerlin: 14 Acres of Sports, Pools, and Playgrounds

The Trails Park is one of the largest and most fully equipped neighborhood parks in all of Summerlin. Spanning 14.6 acres at 9550 Trails Center Drive, it serves as the recreational anchor for The Trails Village and the Mountain Trails community just steps away.

What Facilities Are Available at The Trails Park?

The Trails Park packs a serious amount of recreation into its acreage. Residents have access to a lighted baseball field, two lighted softball fields, a full basketball court, and two separate children's play areas. A large meadow provides open green space for pickup games, picnics, or simply stretching out under the sky. Picnic tables are scattered throughout the grounds for family gatherings.

Does The Trails Park Have a Swimming Pool?

Yes, and it goes well beyond a basic pool. The Trails Park features a swimming pool with a full bathhouse, a separate wading pool for younger children, and a splash pad for toddlers. During the hot Las Vegas summers, having a pool complex within walking distance is a genuine lifestyle advantage. The facility sits adjacent to The Trails Community Center, Summerlin's first community center at 6,000 square feet.

How Close Is The Trails Park to Mountain Trails Homes?

Mountain Trails sits within The Trails Village, making The Trails Park a neighborhood amenity rather than a cross town drive. Residents can walk or bike to the park using the village's internal pathways. This proximity is one reason families consistently rank The Trails Village among Summerlin's most desirable locations for raising children.
