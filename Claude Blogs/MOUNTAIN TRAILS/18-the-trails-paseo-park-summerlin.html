The Trails Paseo Park Summerlin: Green Space and Walking Paths in The Trails Village

Not every park needs ball fields and splash pads to earn a spot in your daily routine. The Trails Paseo Park is a quieter, greener complement to the larger recreation areas in The Trails Village, designed for walking, relaxing, and connecting neighbors on foot.

What Is a Paseo Park and Why Does It Matter?

A paseo park is a linear green space built around walking paths, common in master planned communities like Summerlin. The Trails Paseo Park threads through The Trails Village, offering tree lined pathways and open green areas that link residential streets to larger parks, schools, and the village center. Think of it as the connective tissue that makes a neighborhood truly walkable.

What Can You Do at The Trails Paseo Park?

The Trails Paseo Park is built for low key, everyday recreation. Morning walkers, joggers, and dog owners use the paths daily. The open green space works well for stretching, yoga, or letting kids run freely in a traffic free setting. Because the paseo connects to The Trails Village's broader pathway network, residents can start a walk here and extend it for miles through Summerlin's 200 plus mile trail system.

How Does the Paseo Park Benefit Mountain Trails Residents?

Mountain Trails homeowners live within The Trails Village, so the paseo park system is right outside the guard gate. These pathways provide a safe, pleasant route for walking to The Trails Park, the community center, or neighborhood retail. For families and retirees alike, having car free connections to daily destinations adds real quality of life and supports property values in the surrounding area.
