William R. Lummis Elementary School: A Top Choice for Mountain Trails Families

Families in Mountain Trails, Summerlin have access to one of the area's most respected elementary schools just minutes from their front door. William R. Lummis Elementary serves as a zoned public school for this guard gated community and consistently earns praise from parents throughout The Trails Village.

What Grades Does Lummis Elementary Serve?

William R. Lummis Elementary serves students in grades K through 5 within the Clark County School District. The school is located at 9000 Hillpointe Road, Las Vegas, NV 89134, placing it squarely within The Trails Village boundaries. School hours run from 9:00 AM to 3:11 PM, with office hours beginning at 7:30 AM for early drop off coordination.

What Programs and Resources Does Lummis Elementary Offer?

Lummis Elementary provides a well rounded academic experience backed by several notable achievements. The school was selected as one of just 40 CCSD schools for the College Football Playoff Foundation's Life Changing Teacher Award program, which came with a $50,000 donation. The campus holds Cognia accreditation and a CS100 School Badge designation, reflecting its commitment to computer science education.

Beyond academics, students benefit from counseling services, after school clubs, SafeKey childcare, MAP and SBAC testing preparation, and an active PTA that organizes community fundraising events. The school's mission centers on creating "a safe learning environment that stimulates excitement for learning, is sensitive to the needs of all learners and promotes high levels of achievement and respect."

Why Do Mountain Trails Families Choose Lummis Elementary?

Proximity is a major advantage. Mountain Trails residents in the 89134 zip code are zoned directly to Lummis Elementary, eliminating long commutes for young students. The school's combination of accredited academics, technology focus through its CS100 designation, and supportive extracurricular programming makes it a natural fit for families in this luxury Summerlin community. Parents also appreciate the smaller neighborhood school atmosphere compared to some of the larger CCSD campuses.
