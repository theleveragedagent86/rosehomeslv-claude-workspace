Palo Verde High School: The Academic Powerhouse Near Mountain Trails

Mountain Trails families in Summerlin send their high schoolers to one of Nevada's top ranked public high schools. Palo Verde High School combines rigorous AP and IB coursework with a large, active campus that prepares students for college and beyond.

What Makes Palo Verde High School Stand Out Academically?

Palo Verde High School serves grades 9 through 12 and is located at 333 S Pavilion Center Drive, Las Vegas, NV 89144. The school holds a GreatSchools rating of 8 out of 10 and a Niche grade of B+, ranking 26th out of 103 high schools in Nevada. With approximately 3,243 students enrolled, Palo Verde is one of the larger campuses in the Clark County School District.

The academic highlight is its dual offering of both Advanced Placement (AP) and International Baccalaureate (IB) programs. Students can pursue college level coursework through either pathway, giving them flexibility to match their learning style and academic goals. Few public high schools in the Las Vegas Valley offer both AP and IB under one roof, making Palo Verde a standout choice for academically motivated students.

How Does Palo Verde Prepare Students for College?

College readiness is woven into the Palo Verde experience. The IB program, in particular, is recognized worldwide and can earn students college credit before they ever set foot on a university campus. AP courses provide similar advantages, with passing exam scores accepted at most U.S. colleges and universities. The combination of these programs, along with a strong counseling department, gives students a clear runway toward higher education.

Beyond academics, Palo Verde offers a full slate of athletics, performing arts, and student organizations. The campus supports competitive sports teams, award winning fine arts programs, and dozens of clubs that help students build leadership skills and well rounded college applications.
