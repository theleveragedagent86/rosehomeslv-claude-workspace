Best Restaurants Near Mountain Trails Summerlin: A Complete Dining Guide

Mountain Trails in Summerlin puts residents within minutes of a diverse restaurant scene that spans quick bites at Trails Village Center to upscale dining at Downtown Summerlin. This guard gated community of 103 custom homes enjoys one of the strongest dining corridors in all of Las Vegas.

What Restaurants Are Within Walking Distance of Mountain Trails?

Trails Village Center sits less than one mile from Mountain Trails and anchors the closest dining options. Residents can walk or make a quick drive to Chipotle Mexican Grill, Mountain Mike's Pizza, Einstein Bros. Bagels, Nekter Juice Bar, Port of Subs, McDonald's, and Starbucks. Foxtail Coffee is also expected to open soon, adding a specialty option to the center. These cover the everyday essentials: morning coffee, quick lunches, and casual dinners.

What Full Service Restaurants Are Near Mountain Trails?

The full service dining scene centers around Downtown Summerlin and the surrounding corridor, three to five miles from Mountain Trails. Top options include Harlo Steakhouse and Bar for prime cuts and craft cocktails, Honey Salt for farm to table American cuisine, and Al Solito Posto for house made Italian pasta. Lola's A Louisiana Kitchen brings Cajun and Creole flavors, while Nittaya's Secret Kitchen offers highly rated Thai dishes. True Food Kitchen serves health conscious diners, and Lazy Dog Restaurant and Bar provides a casual American menu. Vintner Grill delivers American bistro fare with a popular patio, and Grape Street rounds out the mix with a wine bar concept.

What International Cuisine Is Available?

Beyond American and Italian, Mountain Trails residents have access to a strong range of international flavors nearby. Marufuku Ramen specializes in rich, authentic Japanese ramen. JING Las Vegas offers Asian cuisine, and La Neta Cocina y Lounge serves elevated Mexican dishes. Makers and Finders brings Latin inspired coffee and brunch to the neighborhood. This variety means residents rarely need to venture beyond Summerlin for quality dining in any category.
