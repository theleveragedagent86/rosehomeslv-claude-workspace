Trails Village Center Shopping Near Mountain Trails Summerlin

Mountain Trails residents have a full service shopping center less than one mile from their front door. Trails Village Center puts everyday retail, banking, and services within a quick walk or drive, making it one of the most convenient neighborhood perks in all of Summerlin.

What Stores Are at Trails Village Center?

Trails Village Center spans approximately 175,000 square feet and houses roughly 50 shops and service providers. The anchor tenant is Albertsons, which covers grocery needs for the surrounding community. CVS Pharmacy sits adjacent for prescriptions and health essentials. Residents also have access to national retailers like Bath & Body Works, Hallmark, and AT&T for electronics and wireless service.

Banking options are particularly strong at this center. Bank of America, Chase, US Bank, and Wells Fargo all operate branches here, giving Mountain Trails homeowners multiple choices for in person financial services without leaving the neighborhood.

What Services and Conveniences Are Available?

Beyond retail, Trails Village Center functions as a daily errand hub. The UPS Store handles shipping and packing needs. Great Clips provides walk in haircuts. Starbucks anchors the casual meetup and morning coffee crowd. For fitness, 24 Hour Fitness operates a full gym location in the center, allowing residents to work out on their own schedule without a long commute.

The variety of tenants means Mountain Trails homeowners can handle groceries, banking, fitness, personal care, and shipping all in a single stop. That kind of walkable convenience is a major quality of life factor and a genuine selling point for the neighborhood.
