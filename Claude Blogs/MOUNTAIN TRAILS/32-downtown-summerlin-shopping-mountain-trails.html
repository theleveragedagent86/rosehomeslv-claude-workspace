Downtown Summerlin Shopping Near Mountain Trails

Mountain Trails homeowners are roughly three miles from one of the largest open air shopping destinations in the Las Vegas Valley. Downtown Summerlin brings 125 plus shops and restaurants together in a single walkable district, giving residents access to major retail without ever touching the Strip.

What Stores Are at Downtown Summerlin?

Downtown Summerlin features a mix of department stores, specialty retailers, and national brands. Anchor tenants include Macy's, Dillard's, and Nordstrom Rack, covering fashion and home goods across multiple price points. H&M provides affordable trend driven apparel. PGA Tour Superstore caters to golf enthusiasts, a natural fit given the number of courses throughout Summerlin.

The center also attracts unique tenants not commonly found elsewhere in Las Vegas. CHANEL Fragrance & Beauty opened its first off Strip location here, signaling the caliber of the surrounding community. Whole Foods Market recently opened a 46,500 square foot flagship store in 2026, adding a premium grocery option to the retail mix.

What Else Can You Do at Downtown Summerlin?

Downtown Summerlin goes beyond traditional shopping. Dave & Buster's offers dining and entertainment under one roof. The district hosts seasonal events, live music, and community gatherings throughout the year, functioning as a social hub for the west side of the valley. With over 125 shops and restaurants combined, it serves as a true lifestyle center rather than a standard mall.

The three mile drive from Mountain Trails means residents can reach Downtown Summerlin in about five to seven minutes depending on traffic. That proximity puts high end retail and dining within easy reach for everyday outings or weekend plans.
