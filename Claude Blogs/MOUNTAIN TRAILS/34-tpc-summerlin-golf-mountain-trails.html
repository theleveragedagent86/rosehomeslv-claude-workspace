TPC Summerlin Golf Near Mountain Trails

Mountain Trails sits near one of the most prestigious golf courses in Nevada. TPC Summerlin is a private, member exclusive club that hosts the PGA Tour's Shriners Children's Open each fall, bringing world class tournament golf to the doorstep of this Summerlin community.

What Is TPC Summerlin?

TPC Summerlin is a Tournament Players Club designed by renowned golf course architect Bobby Weed. The course was purpose built to host professional tour events, and its layout reflects that pedigree. It serves as the permanent home of the Shriners Children's Open, a PGA Tour event that draws top ranked players from around the world every October. The tournament has been a fixture on the Las Vegas sports calendar for decades.

Membership at TPC Summerlin is private and exclusive, offering members access to the championship course, practice facilities, and clubhouse amenities. The club maintains the high standards expected of the TPC network, which operates premier golf properties across the country.

Are There Public Golf Options Nearby?

Golfers who prefer public access can play TPC Las Vegas, a PGA Tour affiliated course that welcomes non members. This public option gives Mountain Trails residents the ability to play TPC caliber golf without a private membership. Between TPC Summerlin for members and TPC Las Vegas for public play, the area provides golf at the highest level for every type of player.

Summerlin as a whole features multiple courses woven into the master plan, reinforcing its reputation as one of the top golf communities in the Southwest. Mountain Trails homeowners benefit from this concentration of golf amenities whether they play competitively or simply enjoy the lifestyle.
