Summerlin Library and Performing Arts Center Near Mountain Trails

Mountain Trails residents have a cultural gem right in their own village. The Summerlin Library and Performing Arts Center sits within The Trails Village, combining a full public library with a 284 seat theater that hosts live performances, community events, and educational programming throughout the year.

What Is the Summerlin Library and Performing Arts Center?

Located at 1771 Inner Circle Drive in the 89134 zip code, this facility serves dual purposes. The library branch provides books, digital resources, and community programming for all ages. The attached performing arts center features a 284 seat theater designed for intimate performances where every seat offers a clear sightline to the stage.

The theater hosts a rotating calendar of community theater productions, concerts, dance recitals, author talks, and film screenings. Nevada Ballet Theatre also operates from The Trails Village, adding professional caliber dance to the local arts scene. The combination of library services and live performance makes this a cultural anchor for the entire west side of Las Vegas.

How Close Is This Venue to Mountain Trails?

Because both the library and the performing arts center sit within The Trails Village, Mountain Trails homeowners can reach this venue in minutes. That proximity means families can attend a Saturday matinee, browse the library stacks, or catch an evening concert without any significant drive. For a master planned community, having a dedicated performing arts space this close is uncommon and adds measurable lifestyle value.
