Mountain Trails Pet-Friendly Dog Walking Trails

Dog owners searching for a Las Vegas neighborhood with real outdoor access will find Mountain Trails in Summerlin hard to beat. Between the community's private park and Summerlin's massive trail network, this guard gated enclave gives pets and their owners miles of walking options right outside the front door.

What Trails Are Available for Dog Walking Near Mountain Trails?

Summerlin maintains over 150 miles of interconnected walking and biking trails that wind through the master planned community. Many of these pathways are dog friendly, offering paved and natural surface options for owners who prefer variety. The trail system connects neighborhoods, parks, and village centers, so walks can range from a quick loop to a longer route through multiple Summerlin villages.

Mountain Trails sits within The Trails Village, which was designed with outdoor recreation as a core feature. Residents can access the trail network from several entry points near the community, making it easy to start a morning walk without driving anywhere.

Which Parks Near Mountain Trails Welcome Dogs?

Inside the guard gate, Mountain Trails Community Park provides a quiet green space where residents walk dogs on leash in a controlled, low traffic setting. Because the park sits behind the gate, it sees far less congestion than public Summerlin parks.

Just outside the community, The Trails Park spans 14.6 acres and features open meadow areas ideal for dog walking. The park's grassy fields give dogs room to stretch while owners enjoy the views of the Spring Mountains to the west. With over 250 parks spread across Summerlin, pet owners never run out of new spots to explore within a short drive.
