Mountain Trails Summerlin for Families

Families moving to Las Vegas want great schools, safe streets, and outdoor space for kids to play. Mountain Trails in Summerlin delivers all three inside a guard gated community that sits within one of the highest rated master plans in Nevada.

What Schools Serve Mountain Trails Families?

Mountain Trails is zoned for Lummis Elementary and Bryan Elementary, both located within the Summerlin area. Middle school students attend Becker Middle School, and high schoolers go to Palo Verde High School, one of the most recognized public high schools in Clark County. Families who prefer private education have strong options nearby, including Adelson Educational Campus approximately two miles away and The Meadows School roughly three miles from the community.

This combination of public and private school access gives parents flexibility at every grade level without long commutes to campus. Many Mountain Trails families walk or carpool to nearby schools, keeping morning routines manageable.

What Parks and Activities Are Available for Kids?

The Trails Park sits near Mountain Trails and spans 14.6 acres with a splash pad, playgrounds, and sports fields that keep children active year round. The Trails Community Center, a 6,000 square foot facility, hosts classes and youth programs that range from arts to athletics. Inside the guard gate, Mountain Trails Community Park offers a quieter green space where younger children can play on low traffic streets under the watch of nearby parents.

Summerlin's trail system adds over 150 miles of walking and biking paths that connect neighborhoods and parks. Families use these trails for weekend bike rides, evening walks, and outdoor exploration without ever leaving the master plan.

Ryan Rose's Local Insight

Ryan Rose finds that families choose Mountain Trails because the guard gate creates a neighborhood where kids can ride bikes and play outside with a level of freedom that open communities rarely provide. The school zoning is strong across all levels, and having The Trails Park with its splash pad and playgrounds within walking distance means parents do not need to load up the car for everyday activities. It is one of the most complete family packages in Summerlin.
