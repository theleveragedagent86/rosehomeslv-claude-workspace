Mountain Trails vs Tournament Hills in Summerlin

Two of Summerlin's most exclusive guard gated communities sit just minutes apart, yet each delivers a distinct living experience. Here is how Mountain Trails and Tournament Hills compare for luxury buyers in 2026.

How Do Mountain Trails and Tournament Hills Compare on Lot Size and Home Prices?

Mountain Trails contains 103 custom homes on half acre to full acre lots, built between 1996 and 2004. Home prices range from approximately $1,000,000 to over $5,000,000, with a median around $2,640,000. Architectural styles include Mediterranean, contemporary, and desert modern, with home sizes spanning 3,758 to 13,106 square feet.

Tournament Hills also features custom homes on large lots within its own guard gated village near TPC Summerlin golf course. Prices fall in a similar range, though Tournament Hills tends to attract buyers who prioritize proximity to championship golf. Both communities offer the oversized lots and privacy that production home neighborhoods simply cannot match.

What Are the Key Lifestyle Differences Between These Two Communities?

Mountain Trails sits within The Trails Village and is known locally as "Half Acres." Residents enjoy a private community park, access to over 150 miles of Summerlin trails, and proximity to Trails Village Center for daily shopping and dining. The neighborhood's mature landscaping and established streetscape give it a settled, serene atmosphere.

Tournament Hills draws buyers who want to live steps from TPC Summerlin, home of the annual Shriners Children's Open. The community's layout wraps around the golf course, offering fairway views from many homesites. Both communities provide 24 hour guard gate security, but their day to day lifestyles cater to different priorities.

Ryan Rose's Take on Mountain Trails vs Tournament Hills

Ryan Rose advises buyers to consider what matters most in daily living. Mountain Trails offers more consistent lot sizes, a tighter knit community of just 103 homes, and a location that balances seclusion with convenience. Tournament Hills appeals to golf enthusiasts who want course frontage. Both represent exceptional value among Summerlin's guard gated options, and inventory in either community rarely lasts long on the market.
