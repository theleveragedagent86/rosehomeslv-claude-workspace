Guard Gated Communities in Summerlin Compared

Summerlin is home to some of the most sought after guard gated communities in the Las Vegas valley. For buyers exploring their options, here is how Mountain Trails stacks up against the competition.

Which Guard Gated Communities Are in Summerlin?

Summerlin's guard gated communities include Mountain Trails, Tournament Hills, The Ridges, Canyon Fairways, Spanish Hills, Queensridge, and The Pointe. Each offers 24 hour security, custom homes on large lots, and a level of privacy that standard Summerlin neighborhoods do not provide. These communities collectively contain some of the most valuable residential real estate in southern Nevada.

Mountain Trails stands out with its 103 custom homes on half acre to full acre lots, built between 1996 and 2004. Prices range from $1,000,000 to over $5,000,000. The Ridges commands the highest prices, often exceeding $10,000,000 for newer construction. Tournament Hills, Canyon Fairways, and Spanish Hills fall in varying ranges depending on lot size, home age, and location within each community.

How Does Mountain Trails Compare to Other Guard Gated Options?

Mountain Trails offers one of the best combinations of lot size and value among Summerlin's guard gated neighborhoods. The community's half acre minimum lot sizes are comparable to The Ridges, yet the median price of approximately $2,640,000 sits well below what similar square footage would cost in newer communities. Home sizes range from 3,758 to 13,106 square feet across Mediterranean, contemporary, and desert modern styles.

Queensridge and Spanish Hills offer their own distinct appeal with proximity to Badlands Golf Club and elevated valley views, respectively. Canyon Fairways provides a golf oriented lifestyle near TPC Las Vegas. The Pointe features some of the most dramatic ridgeline views in all of Summerlin. Each community serves a different buyer profile, but all share the exclusivity that comes with guard gated access.
