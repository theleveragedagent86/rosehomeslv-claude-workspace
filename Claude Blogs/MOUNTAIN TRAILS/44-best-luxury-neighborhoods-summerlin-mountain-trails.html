Best Luxury Neighborhoods in Summerlin

Summerlin has earned its reputation as the premier master planned community in Las Vegas, and its luxury neighborhoods set the standard for upscale living in southern Nevada. Here are the top communities that buyers should know about in 2026.

Which Summerlin Neighborhoods Offer True Luxury Living?

The top tier of Summerlin luxury includes Mountain Trails, The Ridges, Tournament Hills, Canyon Fairways, Spanish Hills, Queensridge, and The Pointe. All are guard gated, all feature custom homes on oversized lots, and all deliver the privacy and exclusivity that distinguish luxury from simply expensive. These neighborhoods account for some of the highest per square foot values in the entire Las Vegas valley.

Mountain Trails is home to 103 custom residences on half acre to full acre lots, built between 1996 and 2004. With prices ranging from $1,000,000 to over $5,000,000 and a median around $2,640,000, it offers one of the strongest value propositions in the luxury segment. Home sizes span 3,758 to 13,106 square feet across Mediterranean, contemporary, and desert modern architectural styles.

What Sets These Luxury Communities Apart from Each Other?

The Ridges leads on price, with homes regularly exceeding $10,000,000 and the newest construction available in Summerlin's guard gated market. Tournament Hills attracts golf enthusiasts with its location adjacent to TPC Summerlin. Spanish Hills offers sweeping valley views from elevated homesites. Queensridge provides a classic, estate style atmosphere with mature grounds.

Mountain Trails differentiates itself through its combination of generous lot sizes, an intimate community of just 103 homes, and a location within The Trails Village that provides walkable access to shopping, dining, and Summerlin's 150 plus mile trail system. The community's mature landscaping and established streetscape create a sense of permanence that newer developments are still building toward.

Ryan Rose's Take on Summerlin's Luxury Market

Ryan Rose notes that Summerlin's luxury market continues to appreciate steadily because supply is inherently limited. Guard gated communities like Mountain Trails will never add new homes, which creates consistent demand among qualified buyers. For those entering the luxury market, Mountain Trails represents an opportunity to own a custom home on a half acre lot at a price point well below The Ridges, while enjoying the same caliber of security, privacy, and Summerlin lifestyle.
