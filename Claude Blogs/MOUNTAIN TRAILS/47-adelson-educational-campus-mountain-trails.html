Adelson Educational Campus Near Mountain Trails Summerlin

Families in Mountain Trails who want a private school with small class sizes and a faith based curriculum have one of Nevada's top ranked options just two miles from their front door. Adelson Educational Campus serves students from pre kindergarten through 12th grade and consistently earns top marks statewide.

What Grades Does Adelson Educational Campus Serve?

Adelson Educational Campus is a private Jewish day school offering a full PK through 12 program on a single campus in Summerlin. The school enrolls approximately 655 students and maintains a 6:1 student to teacher ratio, one of the lowest in the Las Vegas valley. That ratio allows teachers to personalize instruction and identify academic needs early, which is a major reason families choose Adelson over larger alternatives.

Niche rates Adelson A+ overall and ranks it the number two Best Private K 12 School in Nevada. The school reports a 100% graduation rate, and its college preparatory track prepares students for competitive university admissions. The curriculum blends rigorous academics with Jewish values and cultural education, though families of all backgrounds attend.

How Much Does Adelson Educational Campus Cost?

Tuition ranges from approximately $19,000 for lower school grades to $31,200 for upper school. Financial aid and scholarships are available. While the cost is significant, families in Mountain Trails often view it as part of a broader investment in their children's education, particularly given the small class sizes and individualized attention that larger schools cannot replicate.

How Far Is Adelson from Mountain Trails?

The campus sits roughly two miles from Mountain Trails, making the daily commute about five minutes by car. That proximity means parents spend less time in the carpool line and more time at home, which matters in a valley where school commutes can easily stretch to 20 or 30 minutes.
