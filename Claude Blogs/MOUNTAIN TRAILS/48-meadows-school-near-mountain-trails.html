The Meadows School Near Mountain Trails Summerlin

The Meadows School is one of the most academically rigorous private schools in Nevada, and it sits roughly three miles from Mountain Trails. Families in this guard gated Summerlin community can access a PK through 12 college preparatory program without a long daily commute.

What Makes The Meadows School Stand Out?

The Meadows School earns an A+ rating from Niche and enrolls approximately 950 students across all grade levels. The school offers 24 Advanced Placement courses, giving upper school students the ability to earn college credit before graduation. Average SAT scores reach 1410 and average ACT scores hit 31, both well above national medians. The school reports 100% college placement, meaning every graduate moves on to a four year institution.

An 11:1 student to teacher ratio keeps class sizes manageable while still allowing the school to offer a wide range of electives, athletics, and extracurricular programs. The Meadows campus includes dedicated arts facilities, science labs, and athletic fields that rival many small college campuses.

How Much Does The Meadows School Cost?

Tuition at The Meadows School ranges from approximately $33,180 for lower school to $38,840 for upper school. Financial aid is available for qualifying families. The tuition reflects the school's investment in faculty, facilities, and a curriculum designed to prepare students for selective universities. Families in Mountain Trails often weigh this cost against the value of test scores, college placement rates, and the individualized attention that a mid sized private school provides.

How Close Is The Meadows School to Mountain Trails?

The Meadows School campus is located roughly three miles south of Mountain Trails, translating to a drive of about seven to ten minutes depending on traffic. Morning drop off and afternoon pickup fit easily into a daily routine without the extended commutes that families in other parts of the valley face when choosing private education.
