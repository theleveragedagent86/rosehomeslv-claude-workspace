Mountain Trails 89134 Zip Code Guide

Zip code 89134 covers one of the most established sections of Summerlin, and Mountain Trails sits at the heart of it. Understanding the area surrounding this zip code helps buyers see the full picture before committing to a home purchase.

What Area Does the 89134 Zip Code Cover?

The 89134 zip code spans a large portion of central and western Summerlin, including The Trails Village where Mountain Trails is located. This area contains a mix of master planned neighborhoods, commercial centers, parks, and schools. Major landmarks within 89134 include Downtown Summerlin, TPC Summerlin golf course, the Summerlin Library and Performing Arts Center, and Summerlin Hospital Medical Center, which sits roughly two miles from Mountain Trails.

Mountain Trails itself contains 103 custom homes on half acre to full acre lots, built between 1996 and 2004. Home sizes range from 3,758 to 13,106 square feet in Mediterranean, contemporary, and desert modern styles. The community operates behind a 24 hour guard gate with entry by appointment only, making it one of the most private residential enclaves in the 89134 zip code.

What Are Home Prices Like in 89134?

Within Mountain Trails, prices range from $1 million to over $5 million, with a median around $2.64 million. The broader 89134 zip code offers a wider range of housing options at various price points, but Mountain Trails represents the luxury tier. HOA fees in Mountain Trails run approximately $100 to $200 per month, which covers guard gate staffing and common area maintenance.

What Is the Commute from 89134?

Residents in 89134 benefit from strong connectivity to the rest of the valley. The Las Vegas Strip is 15 to 20 minutes from Mountain Trails. Harry Reid International Airport takes 25 to 30 minutes. Downtown Las Vegas is reachable in 20 to 25 minutes. The 215 Beltway and Summerlin Parkway provide direct freeway access, keeping commute times predictable even during peak hours. Summerlin's 150 plus miles of trails and 250 plus parks also mean recreation is always within walking or biking distance.
