MacDonald Highlands Neighborhoods: Every Gate, Street, and Enclave Explained

MacDonald Highlands contains more than 16 distinct neighborhoods across its 1,320 acres, each with its own character, price range, and level of privacy. Understanding the layout is essential before touring because the community spans from intimate 8 lot custom enclaves to 120 unit attached home sections, with prices ranging from roughly $475,000 to over $25 million.

The Original Custom Home Enclaves

Five original neighborhoods were designed exclusively for custom builds on oversized lots. Lairmont is the most exclusive: a 12 acre enclave with only 16 golf course lots behind a private second gate, with prices starting above $1.2 million. Dragon Gate offers just 12 hillside lots on a single private street at elevated ridge positions. Glenbrook Canyon is the most secluded area in the entire community, with 14 mountainous custom home sites on 0.99 to 1.76 acres starting above $1.1 million. The Palisades and Stone Mountain Ridge occupy the highest elevations, ideal for multi level terraced estates with the most dramatic panoramic views in the community.

Builder Neighborhoods

Christopher Homes operates three neighborhoods. SkyVu features 102 properties on 0.1 to 0.49 acres with multi level and single story luxury estates priced from $2.5 million to over $6 million. Vu offers 120 attached homes (the community's townhome style option) on 0.07 to 0.27 acres. Vu Pointe provides 74 single story properties situated above the DragonRidge Clubhouse with triple fairway views on 0.15 to 0.31 acre lots.

Blue Heron, the builder behind the valley's record $25.25 million sale, operates Dragon Rock (51 properties behind a secondary gate on 0.27 to 1.23 acres), Dragon Peak Drive (31 custom homesites on 0.49 to 2.46 acres behind a secondary gate), and The Peak (ultra luxury new builds listed at $13.5 million to $20 million). LIVV Homes builds NEO, the world's first net zero luxury community, with 89 oversized lots featuring Tesla Powerwall and solar panels at prices from $3.1 million to $4.5 million. Obsidian Rock offers only 8 custom homesites spread across approximately 34 acres.

Guard Gates and Access Points

Two 24 hour manned guard gates control entry: the Valle Verde Gate on the west (off Horizon Ridge Parkway) and the Stephanie Gate on the east (off Stephanie Street), with a third gate planned. Multiple interior secondary gates add another layer of privacy for sub neighborhoods including Lairmont, Dragon Rock, Dragon Peak, Vu Pointe, and the former Toll Brothers section.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose recommends that buyers narrow their neighborhood preferences before scheduling a tour, since driving the entire community takes considerable time given the winding mountain roads and multiple gated sections. Each neighborhood delivers a distinctly different lifestyle, from the intimate seclusion of Glenbrook Canyon to the modern net zero living of NEO to the ultra luxury statement of The Peak.
