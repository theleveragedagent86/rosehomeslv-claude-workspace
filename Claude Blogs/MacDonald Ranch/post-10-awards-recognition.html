Is MacDonald Highlands the Best Luxury Community in Henderson?

MacDonald Highlands Awards and Recognition: Best Master Planned Community to Golf Digest Rankings

MacDonald Highlands and DragonRidge Country Club have accumulated an impressive collection of awards and industry recognition over the past two decades. These accolades come from local and national publications, homebuilding industry organizations, and golf media, collectively validating what residents already know: this is one of the premier luxury communities in the western United States.

Community Awards

The Las Vegas Review Journal's Best of Las Vegas awards have repeatedly recognized MacDonald Highlands. The community earned a Gold award in the Best Master Planned Community category in 2021 and followed with a Bronze award in subsequent years. These reader voted awards reflect the community's standing among Las Vegas Valley residents, not just industry insiders. MacDonald Highlands is also nationally recognized among the top gated communities in the United States, a distinction that draws both domestic and international luxury buyers to the community.

DragonRidge Country Club Recognition

DragonRidge Country Club has earned its own substantial portfolio of recognition. Golf Digest ranked the course number 12 in their Best in State rankings for 2025 and 2026, placing it among Nevada's finest private courses alongside Shadow Creek and Wynn Golf Club. The Las Vegas Review Journal named DragonRidge the Gold Winner for Best Private Golf Course in 2021. The club also earned a Bronze award for Best Place to Get Married, reflecting the popularity of its Grand Highlands Ballroom with its sweeping Las Vegas Strip views and 5,000 square foot cherrywood floor event space that accommodates up to 400 guests.

The club's hosting credentials include Tiger Woods' Tiger Jam III and IV charity tournaments and the Wendy's 3 Tour Challenge, events that brought national television exposure to the DragonRidge course and MacDonald Highlands community. A $10.5 million plus capital improvement investment since 2020, including a comprehensive 2023 course renovation focused on water conservation, demonstrates the club's ongoing commitment to maintaining award level quality.

Builder Awards Within the Community

The builders active in MacDonald Highlands bring their own award credentials. Blue Heron, the firm behind the valley's record $25.25 million sale, won the NAHB (National Association of Home Builders) New American Home designation in both 2013 and 2015. Christopher Homes, builder of SkyVu, Vu, and Vu Pointe, has earned more than 150 industry awards over 30 plus years of building in Las Vegas. These builder accolades further reinforce the quality standard that MacDonald Highlands maintains across its neighborhoods.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose notes that awards and rankings serve as third party validation for buyers evaluating multiple luxury communities. When a community, its golf club, and its builders have all independently earned recognition, it creates a layered credibility that is difficult for competing communities to replicate. MacDonald Highlands' collection of awards from both consumer voted and industry judged programs provides that kind of comprehensive endorsement.
