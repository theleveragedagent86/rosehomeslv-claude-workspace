Hospitals and Healthcare Near MacDonald Highlands: Henderson Hospital, St. Rose Dominican, and More

MacDonald Highlands residents have access to multiple hospitals and medical facilities within a 10 to 15 minute drive, including a Level III Trauma Center and one of the most architecturally recognized hospital campuses in the country. For buyers evaluating the practical considerations of living in a hilltop community, healthcare proximity is an important factor, and Henderson delivers strong options.

Henderson Hospital

Henderson Hospital is a 303 bed acute care facility located approximately 10 to 15 minutes from MacDonald Highlands. The hospital provides emergency services, surgical care, cardiac care, orthopedics, and a range of inpatient and outpatient services. As one of the newer hospitals in the Henderson market, the facility features modern equipment and updated infrastructure. Henderson Hospital serves as a convenient first option for MacDonald Highlands residents seeking emergency care or scheduled procedures.

St. Rose Dominican Hospital, Siena Campus

St. Rose Dominican Siena Campus is a 366 bed hospital and Level III Trauma Center located approximately 10 to 15 minutes from MacDonald Highlands. The Siena Campus has been named one of the eight most beautiful hospitals in the world, and its architectural quality reflects the premium healthcare experience the facility provides. As a Level III Trauma Center, Siena handles a higher acuity of emergency cases than a standard emergency room, giving MacDonald Highlands residents access to advanced trauma capabilities without a drive to the Las Vegas medical district. The hospital offers comprehensive services including cardiac care, cancer treatment, maternity services, and surgical specialties.

Additional St. Rose Dominican Campuses

St. Rose Dominican operates multiple campuses in the Henderson and Las Vegas area. The Rose de Lima Campus provides additional inpatient and outpatient services in the Henderson area. The Dignity Health network that includes the St. Rose Dominican system ensures continuity of care and access to specialists across the broader healthcare system.

West Henderson Hospital

West Henderson Hospital expands the healthcare options available to the southwest Henderson area. The facility adds capacity and reduces wait times across the Henderson hospital network, benefiting MacDonald Highlands residents who now have more options when choosing where to receive care.

Medical Offices and Specialists

The Henderson medical corridor along Stephanie Street, Green Valley Parkway, and Horizon Ridge Parkway includes medical office buildings housing primary care physicians, dentists, ophthalmologists, dermatologists, and specialty practices. MacDonald Highlands residents can access routine medical appointments within a 10 to 15 minute drive. The concentration of medical professionals in the Henderson area has grown significantly as the city's affluent population has expanded, attracting specialists who serve the community's healthcare needs.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose notes that healthcare access is a particularly important consideration for MacDonald Highlands' retiree population and for families with young children. The proximity to a Level III Trauma Center at St. Rose Dominican Siena and the availability of Henderson Hospital's emergency services provide peace of mind that advanced medical care is minutes away despite the community's elevated, secluded location. For buyers with ongoing medical needs, Ryan recommends verifying that preferred specialists practice in the Henderson area before finalizing a move.
