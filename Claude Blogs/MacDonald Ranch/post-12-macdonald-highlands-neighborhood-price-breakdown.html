How Much Do Homes Cost in MacDonald Highlands? Complete Price Breakdown by Neighborhood

MacDonald Highlands contains more than 16 distinct neighborhoods, and pricing varies dramatically depending on which enclave you choose. Homes range from the upper $1 million range in the Vu community to over $20 million at The Peak. Here is what each major neighborhood costs as of early 2026.

Christopher Homes Neighborhoods

SkyVu offers 102 multi level and single story luxury estates on lots ranging from 0.10 to 0.49 acres. Current pricing runs approximately $2.3 million to $6 million or more, with home sizes between 3,500 and 6,200 square feet. These homes sit at the highest elevations within the Christopher Homes portfolio and feature some of the best 360 degree views in the community.

Vu Pointe includes 66 to 74 single story homes positioned above the DragonRidge Clubhouse on lots of 0.15 to 0.31 acres. Resale pricing ranges from the upper $1 million range to $3.5 million or more, with floor plans between 2,800 and 4,800 square feet offering triple fairway views. Vu, the community's attached home option, features 120 residences on smaller lots with resale prices starting in the upper $1 million range and reaching $3 million or more for premium units.

Blue Heron Neighborhoods

Dragon Rock sits behind a secondary gate with 51 properties on lots from 0.27 to 1.23 acres. Base pricing for Blue Heron's semi custom Helix Collection starts around $2.2 million to $2.6 million, with finished homes selling significantly higher. Home sizes range from approximately 4,688 to 7,749 square feet. The Peak represents Blue Heron's ultra luxury tier, where new builds and spec homes list from $8.5 million to over $20 million, occupying the highest elevation and most dramatic ridgeline positions in the entire community.

LIVV Homes NEO

NEO by LIVV Homes is the world's first net zero luxury community, with 84 to 96 lots featuring Tesla Powerwalls, solar panels, and Sub Zero/Wolf appliances standard. Pricing ranges from approximately $3 million to $7 million, with home sizes between 3,036 and 6,800 square feet across several floor plan options including the Velour, Thia, and Paragon models.

Custom and Legacy Neighborhoods

Lairmont, the most exclusive enclave, sits behind a private second gate with only 16 golf course lots on 12 acres. Custom estates here range from $3.5 million to over $21 million. Glenbrook Canyon offers 14 mountainous custom home sites on 0.99 to 1.76 acres starting from $1.1 million for lots, making it the most secluded area in MacDonald Highlands. Foothills Village, originally built by Toll Brothers and now resale only, offers homes from approximately $2.2 million to $3.75 million for 3,481 to 6,315 square foot floor plans.
