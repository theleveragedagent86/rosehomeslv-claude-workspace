How to Schedule a Tour of MacDonald Highlands: Guard Gate Access Explained

You cannot simply drive into MacDonald Highlands to look around. The community is protected by two 24 hour manned guard gates with roving security patrols, and every visitor must be pre registered by a resident, property owner, or licensed real estate agent before entering. Here is exactly how the process works if you want to tour homes.

The Two Guard Gates

MacDonald Highlands has two staffed entry points that operate around the clock: the Valle Verde Gate on the west side (accessed from Horizon Ridge Parkway) and the Stephanie Gate on the east side (accessed from Stephanie Street). Both gates require identification and verification before granting entry. Security personnel check every vehicle, and guests receive windshield passes that must be displayed while inside the community.

How to Get In for a Home Tour

The most reliable way to tour MacDonald Highlands is to work with a real estate agent who has active listings or MLS access within the community. Your agent contacts the listing agent to arrange showing access, and the listing agent or homeowner registers your name and vehicle with the guard gate in advance. When you arrive, you provide identification and the guard confirms your appointment before allowing entry.

Without a pre arranged appointment, the guard gate will turn you away. There are no public roads inside MacDonald Highlands and no way to enter the community without authorization.

What About Open Houses?

Open houses do occur in MacDonald Highlands, though less frequently than in non gated communities. When a listing agent hosts an open house, they register the event with community security and the guard gate allows visitors during the specified time window. These events are typically advertised on the MLS and agent websites, so monitoring listing platforms or working with an agent who tracks MacDonald Highlands inventory is the best way to learn about upcoming open house opportunities.

Secondary Gates Within the Community

Several MacDonald Highlands neighborhoods sit behind additional private gates, creating a double gated experience. Lairmont, Dragon Rock, Dragon Peak, Vu Pointe, Glenbrook Canyon, and the Toll Brothers area each have their own secondary gate. Touring homes in these neighborhoods requires an additional layer of access coordination, which your agent handles as part of the showing process.
