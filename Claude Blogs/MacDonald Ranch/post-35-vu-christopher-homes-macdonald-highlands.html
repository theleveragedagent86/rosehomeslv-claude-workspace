Vu by Christopher Homes: Single Story Luxury in MacDonald Highlands

Vu by Christopher Homes is a luxury neighborhood within MacDonald Highlands that emphasizes single story living with dramatic Las Vegas Strip views. Featuring contemporary desert architecture and Christopher Homes' signature disappearing glass walls, Vu delivers the prestigious MacDonald Highlands address in a more accessible format than the community's ultra luxury custom home neighborhoods, making it an attractive entry point for buyers who want the views and the guard gated security without a $5 million plus price tag.

Floor Plans and Design

Vu homes are primarily single story designs ranging from approximately 2,800 to 4,500 square feet with 3 to 4 bedrooms. The single story format is a significant draw for buyers who prefer single level living, whether for accessibility, aging in place considerations, or simply the open flow that single story layouts provide. Christopher Homes designed Vu floor plans with the signature indoor outdoor living concept: multi slide glass walls that open entire great rooms to covered patios, creating a seamless transition to outdoor entertaining spaces with pool, spa, and Strip views.

Pricing and Value

Vu homes typically trade in the $1.8 million to $3.5 million range, positioning them as one of the more attainable neighborhoods within MacDonald Highlands. For that investment, buyers get a newer Christopher Homes build with premium finishes, a guard gated MacDonald Highlands address, and many of the same panoramic views that homes twice the price enjoy. Lot sizes in Vu are more compact than SkyVu, generally ranging from 0.10 to 0.25 acres, which also means lower maintenance and landscaping costs for homeowners.

Who Buys in Vu

Vu attracts a specific buyer profile: high net worth individuals and couples who want luxury quality without excessive square footage. This often includes empty nesters downsizing from larger homes elsewhere in the valley, California relocators who want a premium address at a lower entry point than they would pay in comparable California communities, and executives who travel frequently and prefer a low maintenance home that requires minimal upkeep during extended absences. The single story format also appeals to retirees who plan to age in place and want to avoid stairs.

Community Integration

Vu residents have full access to all MacDonald Highlands community amenities and are eligible for DragonRidge Country Club membership. The neighborhood's location within the community provides convenient access to the club, community parks, and the trail system. Like all MacDonald Highlands neighborhoods, Vu sits within the 24 hour guard gated perimeter and benefits from the community's security infrastructure, including both the Valle Verde and Stephanie Street gates.
