Four Seasons Private Residences Las Vegas: Everything You Need to Know

The Four Seasons Private Residences Las Vegas is a $1.3 billion development bringing two residential towers (24 and 25 stories) with 171 luxury residences and 6 standalone villas to MacDonald Highlands in Henderson. With over $725 million in sales already secured and approximately 70 percent of units sold, this is the first high rise residential project built in the Las Vegas Valley since the Great Recession and the most significant luxury development in Southern Nevada history. Here is what buyers and the broader MacDonald Highlands community need to know.

The Residences

The Four Seasons Private Residences offer 171 units across two towers plus 6 ground level villas. Residences range from approximately 2,000 to over 10,000 square feet, with pricing starting at approximately $3.5 million for entry level units and reaching $30 million for the signature penthouses. Unit configurations include two bedroom, three bedroom, and four bedroom layouts, with penthouses offering expanded floor plans and premium finishes. The 6 standalone villas provide a single family home experience with Four Seasons services and are among the most exclusive residences in the project.

Amenities and Services

The Four Seasons brand brings a hospitality experience that no other Las Vegas residential community offers. Wolfgang Puck Fine Dining Group serves as the exclusive food and beverage provider, delivering a caliber of on site dining that residents would otherwise need to drive to the Strip to access. The amenity package includes three resort style pools (including an adults only option), a 5,000 square foot fitness center with dedicated yoga and Pilates studios, a 3,500 square foot spa with treatment rooms, a social club lounge, private dining rooms, and extensive outdoor entertaining terraces.

Four Seasons service staff provide 24/7 concierge, valet parking, housekeeping (available on request), package handling, and the signature Four Seasons hospitality that the brand is known for globally. Residents have access to the Four Seasons app for service requests, maintenance coordination, and amenity reservations. This level of service eliminates the daily management tasks that come with traditional homeownership while maintaining the privacy and exclusivity of a residential community.

Location Within MacDonald Highlands

The Four Seasons towers are being built within MacDonald Highlands on elevated terrain that provides Strip views from upper floors that will rival or exceed the views from the community's hillside homes. The project sits within MacDonald Highlands' guard gated perimeter, which means Four Seasons residents benefit from both the property's own security infrastructure and the community's 24 hour manned gates. Access to DragonRidge Country Club, community parks, and walking trails extends to Four Seasons residents, integrating the towers into the broader MacDonald Highlands community rather than creating a separate enclave.

The Development Team

The Four Seasons Private Residences are being developed by Discovery Land Company, one of the most respected luxury community developers in the United States, in partnership with Four Seasons Hotels and Resorts. Discovery Land's portfolio includes some of the most exclusive residential communities in America, and their involvement provides confidence in both the build quality and the long term management of the property. The architecture features a contemporary design that complements the desert landscape while providing the tower height needed to capture MacDonald Highlands' signature panoramic views.

Who Is Buying

The buyer profile for Four Seasons residences skews toward ultra high net worth individuals who want luxury without maintenance. Many buyers are splitting time between multiple residences and want a Las Vegas base that is fully managed and serviced in their absence. California tax migrants represent a significant buyer segment, drawn by the combination of Four Seasons lifestyle, Nevada's zero state income tax, and MacDonald Highlands' established prestige. Corporate executives, entertainment industry figures, professional athletes, and international buyers round out the mix. The average buyer age trends older than the broader MacDonald Highlands community, reflecting the appeal of the lock and leave, fully serviced lifestyle to established wealth.
