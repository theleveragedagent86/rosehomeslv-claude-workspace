DragonRidge Country Club Membership: Types, Costs, and How to Join

DragonRidge Country Club offers several membership tiers designed to fit different lifestyles and usage patterns, from avid golfers who play multiple times per week to families who primarily want access to dining, fitness, and social amenities. Membership is available exclusively to MacDonald Highlands residents, and the total number of memberships is permanently capped to ensure the course and facilities never feel overcrowded. Here is what you need to know about membership types, estimated costs, and the process for joining.

Membership Types

DragonRidge offers five primary membership categories, each providing different levels of access to the club's amenities.

Full Golf Membership provides unlimited access to the 18 hole championship course, the 60,000 square foot practice facility, all four dining venues, the fitness and athletic center, tennis and pickleball courts, the swimming pool, and the full social events calendar. This is the flagship membership for serious golfers and families who want the complete DragonRidge experience.

Social and Family Social Membership includes access to all dining venues, the fitness and athletic center, tennis and pickleball courts, the pool, and social events. Golf is limited to approximately 6 rounds per year, making this ideal for residents who want the club lifestyle without being avid golfers. The Family Social tier extends these benefits to the member's immediate family.

National Membership is designed for MacDonald Highlands residents who maintain a primary home more than 75 miles from the community. National members receive approximately 24 rounds of golf per year plus full access to dining, fitness, and social amenities when in town. This tier serves the growing number of MacDonald Highlands owners who split time between Las Vegas and another residence.

Young Executive Membership targets residents ages 26 to 40, offering approximately 24 rounds of golf per year plus full social, dining, and fitness access at a reduced initiation fee. This tier recognizes that younger professionals may not play golf as frequently but still want full participation in the club's social and lifestyle offerings.

Corporate Membership allows businesses to provide club access to executives and clients. Details on corporate membership terms are available directly from the membership office.

Estimated Costs

DragonRidge does not publicly disclose current membership pricing, which is standard practice for high end private clubs. However, based on available information and comparable private club benchmarks in the Las Vegas Valley, estimated costs are as follows. Full Golf initiation fees are estimated at $25,000 to $150,000 or more depending on availability and the specific membership era, with annual dues estimated at $10,000 to $30,000. Social memberships carry lower initiation fees and reduced annual dues. Young Executive and National memberships fall between Social and Full Golf in pricing. These estimates should be confirmed directly with the DragonRidge membership office, as pricing can change based on demand and available membership slots.

The Membership Process

Joining DragonRidge typically involves contacting the Membership Manager (Tabitha Bondurant, 702 835 8135), scheduling a club tour that includes the course, clubhouse, dining venues, and athletic facilities, reviewing membership options and current pricing, completing an application, and receiving approval from the membership committee. The process is designed to be welcoming rather than exclusionary: MacDonald Highlands residents who want to join are generally able to, subject to availability in their desired membership category.

Membership Is Not Mandatory

An important point for prospective MacDonald Highlands buyers: DragonRidge membership is not mandatory for residents. You can live in MacDonald Highlands without being a club member. However, the community parks, walking trails, dog parks, and community events are available to all residents regardless of club membership. The decision to join DragonRidge is a lifestyle choice based on how much you value golf, the dining program, fitness facilities, and the social community that the club provides.
