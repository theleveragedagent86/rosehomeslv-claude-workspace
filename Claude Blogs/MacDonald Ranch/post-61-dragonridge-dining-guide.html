DragonRidge Dining Guide: Montrose Steakhouse, Onyx Bar, Dragon Grille, and Red Dragon Sushi

DragonRidge Country Club houses four distinct dining venues within its mountaintop clubhouse, giving MacDonald Highlands members access to everything from fine dining steaks to casual family meals to craft cocktails to fresh sushi, all with panoramic Las Vegas Valley views. For members who dine at the club regularly, the dining program often becomes the amenity they use most frequently, eliminating the need to drive to the Strip or downtown Henderson for a quality meal. Here is what each venue offers.

Montrose Steakhouse

Montrose is DragonRidge's fine dining destination, serving premium steaks and seafood in an elegant setting with sweeping Strip views through floor to ceiling windows. The restaurant delivers the kind of experience that competes directly with the best steakhouses on the Las Vegas Strip: prime cuts, fresh seafood, an extensive wine list, and attentive service in a refined atmosphere. What Montrose offers that Strip steakhouses cannot is the intimacy of a private club dining room. There are no tourists, no hour long waits, and no adjacent convention crowds. Reservations are straightforward, the staff knows members by name, and the experience is consistently excellent without the production that Strip dining sometimes requires. Montrose is ideal for date nights, celebrations, hosting business associates, and any evening where members want a premium dining experience minutes from home.

Dragon Grille

Dragon Grille serves as the club's casual, family friendly dining venue. The menu covers a broader range of approachable fare, from burgers and sandwiches to salads, pasta, and comfort food favorites, all executed at a level above what "casual" might imply. Dragon Grille is where families with children dine comfortably, where members grab lunch after a round of golf, and where weeknight dinners happen without the formality of Montrose. The atmosphere is relaxed but still elevated by the clubhouse setting and mountain views. For members with school age children, Dragon Grille provides a dining option where kids are genuinely welcome without compromising the food quality or ambiance that adults expect.

Onyx Bar

Onyx Bar is the social heartbeat of DragonRidge. Offering craft cocktails, an extensive spirits selection, and a small plates menu, Onyx draws members for happy hour, after dinner drinks, and especially for the Thursday night live music that has become one of the club's most popular recurring events. The bar's design creates an atmosphere that feels more like a high end lounge than a traditional country club bar, with a modern aesthetic, quality lighting, and seating arrangements that encourage conversation. For MacDonald Highlands residents who value social connection, Onyx is where it happens. The Thursday live music evenings regularly draw a crowd of members who come for the entertainment, stay for the drinks and conversation, and leave having deepened their connections within the community.

Red Dragon Sushi Bar

Red Dragon Sushi Bar brings fresh Japanese cuisine to the DragonRidge clubhouse, offering sushi, sashimi, rolls, and Japanese specialties that provide a welcome alternative to the American fare at the other venues. Quality sushi in Henderson has historically required a drive to the Strip or specific restaurant clusters, so having a dedicated sushi bar within the club is a convenience that members appreciate. Red Dragon serves both as a standalone dining destination and as a complement to drinks at Onyx Bar, with members often combining the two for an evening of cocktails and sushi with views.

Additional Dining Options

Beyond the four primary venues, DragonRidge offers additional dining through the Center Court Cafe (serving Starbucks coffee and quick bites near the athletic center) and the Poolside Snack Bar (seasonal food and beverages at the pool). These casual options ensure that members can grab a coffee before a workout or enjoy lunch poolside without needing to change into dining room appropriate attire. The breadth of dining options across six total service points means DragonRidge members can eat at the club for every meal if they choose, from morning coffee to post round lunch to a fine dining dinner, all within the same mountaintop facility.
