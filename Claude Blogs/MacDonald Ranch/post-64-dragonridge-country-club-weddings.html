DragonRidge Country Club Weddings: The Grand Highlands Ballroom with Strip Views

The Grand Highlands Ballroom at DragonRidge Country Club is one of the most stunning wedding and event venues in the Las Vegas Valley. Spanning 5,000 square feet with a cherrywood dance floor and sweeping panoramic views of the Las Vegas Strip, the ballroom accommodates up to 400 guests for weddings, galas, corporate events, and celebrations. The Las Vegas Review Journal recognized DragonRidge with a Bronze Winner award for Best Place to Get Married, and the venue's combination of mountaintop setting, Strip views, private club exclusivity, and full service catering makes it a premier choice for couples seeking a luxury Las Vegas wedding that feels nothing like the Strip's wedding chapel experience.

The Venue

The Grand Highlands Ballroom sits atop the DragonRidge clubhouse in the McCullough Mountains at approximately 2,500 to 2,700 feet elevation, providing views that extend across the entire Las Vegas Valley. The Strip skyline is visible through the ballroom's windows, creating a backdrop that is particularly dramatic during evening events when the city lights create a glittering panorama. The cherrywood dance floor is the ballroom's centerpiece, surrounded by flexible seating and staging areas that can be configured for different event styles and sizes. The 5,000 square foot space is large enough for significant events (up to 400 guests) while still feeling intimate for smaller gatherings through creative layout and design choices.

Outdoor Ceremony Options

Beyond the ballroom, DragonRidge offers outdoor ceremony and cocktail reception spaces that take full advantage of the mountaintop setting. Outdoor terraces adjacent to the clubhouse provide ceremony locations with natural mountain and desert landscape backdrops, and the Las Vegas Strip visible in the distance. The desert climate delivers approximately 297 sunny days per year, making outdoor ceremonies a reliable option for most of the calendar, with the cooler months (October through April) and morning or evening hours in summer being the most comfortable for outdoor events. The combination of an outdoor ceremony with mountain views followed by an indoor reception in the Grand Highlands Ballroom with Strip views creates a wedding experience that showcases two of Las Vegas' most compelling visual assets in a single event.

Catering and Service

DragonRidge's culinary team handles all food and beverage for ballroom events, bringing the same quality that drives the club's four dining venues to the wedding and events space. Couples work with the catering team to design custom menus that can range from plated multi course dinners to creative stations and passed appetizer receptions. The club's wine and spirits program provides an extensive selection for bar service, and the team has experience executing everything from intimate 50 person dinners to full 400 person galas. Because DragonRidge is a private club, the event planning process benefits from a dedicated team that manages a limited number of events rather than the high volume approach of commercial event venues.

Why DragonRidge Over Strip Venues

Couples choosing DragonRidge for their wedding are typically seeking several things that Strip venues cannot provide. First, genuine exclusivity: the private club setting means your wedding guests are the only people in the building, not sharing space with hotel guests, casino patrons, and convention attendees. Second, natural beauty: the mountain setting and desert landscape provide a visual foundation that Strip venues, however luxurious, cannot replicate. Third, parking and logistics: guests drive to a single mountaintop location with ample parking rather than navigating the Strip's traffic, valet lines, and hotel corridors. Fourth, the view: having the Strip skyline as a distant backdrop is more visually compelling in photographs and guest experience than being amid the Strip itself, where the perspective is too close to appreciate the full cityscape.

Events Beyond Weddings

The Grand Highlands Ballroom also hosts corporate events, charity galas, milestone celebrations (birthdays, anniversaries), holiday parties, and private dinners. MacDonald Highlands residents who are DragonRidge members can reserve the ballroom for personal events, making it a convenient option for hosting significant gatherings without the complexity of finding and booking an outside venue. The club's event coordination team manages setup, catering, audiovisual, and breakdown, allowing hosts to focus on their guests rather than logistics.

Ryan Rose notes that the Grand Highlands Ballroom is one of MacDonald Highlands' most impressive amenities for prospective buyers to see during their community tour. Even buyers who are not planning a wedding are struck by the space's beauty, the Strip views, and the quality of the club's event capabilities. For MacDonald Highlands residents, having a world class event venue within the community gates means celebrating life's milestones in a setting that reflects the quality of the community itself.
