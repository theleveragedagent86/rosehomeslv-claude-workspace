Christopher Homes in MacDonald Highlands: 30 Years and 150+ Awards

Christopher Homes, founded by Christopher Stuhmer, is one of MacDonald Highlands' most prolific builders with three distinct neighborhoods totaling nearly 300 properties within the community. With over 30 years of experience and more than 150 industry awards, Christopher Homes has shaped a significant portion of MacDonald Highlands' residential landscape, offering luxury homes across a range of price points and configurations that serve everyone from young executives to active retirees.

SkyVu: The 360 Degree View Neighborhood

SkyVu is Christopher Homes' flagship MacDonald Highlands community, consisting of 102 properties on lots ranging from 0.10 to 0.49 acres. Positioned at some of the highest elevations in MacDonald Highlands, SkyVu homes offer 360 degree views that encompass the Las Vegas Strip, the McCullough Mountains, and the broader valley. The neighborhood includes both multi level estates and single story luxury homes, with prices typically ranging from $2.5 million to $6 million or more. SkyVu's elevated position and Christopher Homes' attention to view optimization mean that virtually every home in the neighborhood enjoys significant panoramic views, a distinction that is not guaranteed in all MacDonald Highlands neighborhoods.

Vu: MacDonald Highlands' Attached Home Option

Vu consists of 120 attached homes on lots from 0.07 to 0.27 acres, making it MacDonald Highlands' most accessible entry point for buyers who want the community's guard gated lifestyle, DragonRidge Country Club access, and Henderson location without the price premium of a detached custom estate. The attached format provides a lower maintenance living experience that appeals to buyers who travel frequently, maintain multiple residences, or simply prefer not to manage a large property. Vu homes deliver the MacDonald Highlands address and community benefits in a more efficient package.

Vu Pointe: Single Story Living Above the Clubhouse

Vu Pointe offers 74 single story properties on lots from 0.15 to 0.31 acres, positioned directly above the DragonRidge Country Club clubhouse with triple fairway views. The single story format is increasingly sought after in the luxury market as buyers prioritize convenience and aging in place considerations. Vu Pointe's location above the clubhouse means residents can walk to dinner at Montrose Steakhouse, drinks at Onyx Bar, or a workout at the fitness center, a proximity that few MacDonald Highlands neighborhoods can match. The combination of single story living, golf course views, and clubhouse walkability makes Vu Pointe particularly popular with active retirees and empty nesters.

Resale Value and Market Position

Christopher Homes properties in MacDonald Highlands have demonstrated strong resale performance, benefiting from both the builder's reputation and the community's overall appreciation trends.
