Bob Miller Middle School: Why MacDonald Highlands Families Love This 9/10 Rated School

Bob Miller Middle School is the primary middle school serving MacDonald Highlands families, and it consistently ranks among the best middle schools in Nevada. Rated 9 out of 10 by GreatSchools and ranked 17th in the state, Bob Miller provides the academic bridge between MacDonald Highlands' top rated elementary schools and Henderson's competitive high school options.

Academics and Test Scores

Bob Miller Middle School enrolls approximately 1,449 students and posts proficiency rates of 53% in math and 67% in reading. While those numbers may seem modest in isolation, they significantly outperform Clark County School District averages, where district wide middle school proficiency rates run considerably lower. The school's ranking at 17th in Nevada reflects its consistent performance relative to peers across the state. Named after former Nevada Governor Bob Miller, the school benefits from the engaged parent community that the MacDonald Highlands and surrounding affluent Henderson neighborhoods provide.

Programs and Opportunities

Bob Miller offers a range of academic and extracurricular programs designed to challenge students and prepare them for success at schools like Foothill High. The school provides honors and advanced coursework in core subjects, giving high performing students the academic rigor they need during the critical middle school years. Extracurricular activities span athletics, arts, student government, and academic clubs, creating well rounded opportunities for students with diverse interests. The school's facilities support a full middle school experience with dedicated spaces for science, technology, arts, and physical education.

The Parent Community

One of the factors that distinguishes Bob Miller from many Clark County middle schools is the level of parent involvement. Families from MacDonald Highlands, Anthem, Seven Hills, and other affluent Henderson communities contribute significantly to the school through PTA organizations, volunteer programs, and academic support. This involvement creates a culture of accountability and aspiration that positively influences student outcomes. Teachers at Bob Miller benefit from working in an environment where education is valued at home, which translates to classroom engagement and academic achievement.

Location and Access

Bob Miller Middle School is located at 2200 East Robindale Road in Henderson, a convenient drive from MacDonald Highlands. The school's position within the broader Henderson education corridor means students transition smoothly from zoned elementary schools and feed naturally into Foothill High School, maintaining social connections and academic continuity throughout their K through 12 experience.
