Best Restaurants Near MacDonald Highlands: From Fine Dining to Neighborhood Favorites

MacDonald Highlands residents have DragonRidge Country Club's four dining venues inside the gates, but the surrounding Henderson area offers a deep and diverse restaurant scene for the evenings when you want to venture out. Most of the best options are concentrated along the Horizon Ridge Parkway corridor and at Green Valley Ranch Resort, all within a 10 to 15 minute drive of the guard gates.

Neighborhood Favorites

Chef's Roma Kitchen on Horizon Ridge Parkway has become a go to for MacDonald Highlands residents who crave authentic Italian cooking in an intimate setting. The restaurant specializes in handmade pastas and traditional Italian preparations with a focus on quality ingredients. CHARM Thai Kitchen offers elevated Thai cuisine that goes well beyond typical takeout fare, with a menu that draws Henderson residents from across the city. Kitchen Table provides a comfortable American dining experience with seasonal menus and a warm neighborhood feel that appeals to families and couples alike.

Settebello Pizzeria Napoletana serves authentic Neapolitan pizza cooked in a wood fired oven, using imported Italian ingredients and traditional techniques. The restaurant has earned recognition as one of the best pizza spots in the Las Vegas valley. Metro Pizza, a Las Vegas institution with decades of history, operates a Henderson location convenient to MacDonald Highlands for casual family pizza nights. Naga Thai provides another strong Thai option in the area, and The Broken Yolk Cafe covers weekend brunch with generous portions and a casual atmosphere.

Green Valley Ranch Resort Dining

Green Valley Ranch Resort and Casino, approximately 8 minutes from MacDonald Highlands, houses several dining options that serve as the closest resort caliber restaurants to the community. The resort's restaurant lineup provides options from casual to upscale without requiring a drive to the Strip. The property's position as the neighborhood casino resort means MacDonald Highlands residents can access quality dining, bars, and entertainment within minutes.

The District at Green Valley Ranch

The District at Green Valley Ranch, also about 8 minutes away, features an outdoor mixed use development with a restaurant row that includes multiple dining options. The center hosts a Friday farmer's market that attracts food focused Henderson residents looking for fresh local produce, artisan goods, and prepared foods. The walkable layout makes The District a popular destination for an evening out that combines dinner with shopping and people watching.

What's Coming

The arrival of Wolfgang Puck Fine Dining Group at the Four Seasons Private Residences will add a world class culinary destination within MacDonald Highlands itself. When the towers open in late 2026, residents will have celebrity chef caliber dining inside the guard gates for the first time, further reducing the need to drive to the Strip for exceptional meals.
