Naked City Las Vegas is a historic urban neighborhood located just west of the north end of the Las Vegas Strip, known for its affordable housing, proximity to major attractions, and ongoing revitalization efforts. ## What Is Naked City Las Vegas and Where Is It Located? Naked City sits within the 89102 ZIP code, bordered roughly by Sahara Avenue to the south and the STRAT Tower area to the east. The neighborhood earned its colorful name decades ago when showgirls and entertainers who worked on the Strip would sunbathe in their yards between shifts. Today it remains one of the most centrally located residential areas in all of Las Vegas, just a two to five minute drive from the Strip. The housing stock in Naked City dates primarily from the 1950s through the 1980s, with a mix of single family ranch style homes (about 35% of units) and apartments or multi family properties (about 62%). There is no HOA governing the neighborhood, which means no monthly dues and no gate restrictions. The area functions as an open urban community with public streets and easy access to transit, parks, and services. ## What Is the History of Naked City Las Vegas? Naked City developed alongside the early growth of the Las Vegas Strip. Workers in the entertainment industry settled here because of the short commute, and the neighborhood became a working class community with deep roots. Over the decades, the area experienced periods of disinvestment, but recent efforts have brought new energy. The "Philly at 215" project at 215 Philadelphia Avenue marks the first new residential construction in Naked City in over 65 years, adding 81 studio units and 6 one bedroom units. City Councilwoman Olivia Diaz has championed broader revitalization plans, and the adjacent Gateway District continues to attract new commercial development. ## What Amenities Are Near Naked City? Residents enjoy access to Bob Baskin Park and Stupak Park for outdoor recreation. The SX Sahara Express and Route 119 bus lines provide public transit options, and the Walk Score of 54 means many errands can be handled on foot.
