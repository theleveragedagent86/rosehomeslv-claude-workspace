# Naked City Studios and Downtown Spaces Las Vegas Naked City Studios and Downtown Spaces is a Mid Century Modern art complex at 1800 Industrial Road that houses galleries, artist studios, and shops on the edge of the Las Vegas Arts District. ## What Are Naked City Studios and Downtown Spaces in Las Vegas? Downtown Spaces and Naked City Studios occupy a building originally constructed in 1964. The complex sits at 1800 Industrial Road, placing it at the boundary between the Naked City neighborhood and the Las Vegas Arts District. Inside, visitors find rotating gallery exhibitions, working artist studios, and curated retail shops. Vegas Seven named it "Best Downtown Art Hive," recognizing its role as a creative anchor for the surrounding community. The complex reflects the broader creative energy that has taken hold in this part of Las Vegas. Artists and small business owners have gravitated to the area because of its affordable rents and central location near the Strip. The building's Mid Century Modern architecture gives it a visual identity that fits the neighborhood's postwar history. ## Who Uses Naked City Studios? The studios serve a mix of visual artists, photographers, designers, and makers who rent individual workspaces within the complex. Gallery spaces host exhibitions that rotate regularly, drawing visitors from across the valley. Small shops within the building sell handmade goods, vintage items, and locally produced art. The complex functions as both a production space and a public destination, creating foot traffic that benefits nearby businesses. ## How Does This Connect to the Broader Arts Scene? Naked City Studios sits at the northern edge of the 18b Las Vegas Arts District, a designated 18 block cultural zone approximately 1.5 miles to the south. The Arts District hosts the popular First Friday monthly art walk, which brings thousands of visitors to the area. The proximity of Naked City Studios to this established arts corridor has made the Naked City neighborhood increasingly attractive to creative professionals looking for affordable live and work spaces.
