# Las Vegas Arts District Near Naked City The Las Vegas Arts District, also known as 18b, is an 18 block cultural neighborhood located approximately 1.5 miles south of Naked City, offering galleries, murals, cafes, and the popular First Friday art walk. ## What Is the Las Vegas Arts District Near Naked City? The Las Vegas Arts District spans 18 blocks south of downtown, earning it the shorthand name 18b. The district features art galleries, colorful street murals, antique shops, and independent cafes that attract visitors throughout the week. Its signature event, First Friday, is a monthly art walk that draws thousands of people to browse open galleries, enjoy live music, and shop from local vendors. The district sits roughly 1.5 miles south of the Naked City neighborhood, connected by a short drive or bike ride along Industrial Road and Main Street. ## Why Does the Arts District Matter for Naked City Residents? Proximity to the Arts District gives Naked City residents easy access to one of the most vibrant cultural hubs in the Las Vegas valley. The creative energy has been spreading northward, with spaces like Naked City Studios and Downtown Spaces at 1800 Industrial Road acting as a bridge between the two areas. Artists and entrepreneurs priced out of the Arts District have increasingly looked to Naked City for more affordable studio and living space, bringing new investment and activity to the neighborhood. ## How Does the Arts District Affect Home Values Nearby?
