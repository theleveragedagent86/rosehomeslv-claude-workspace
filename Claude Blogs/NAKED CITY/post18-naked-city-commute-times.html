# Commute Times from Naked City to the Strip, Airport and Downtown Naked City sits within a 2 to 5 minute drive of the Las Vegas Strip, a 10 to 15 minute drive to Harry Reid International Airport, and a 5 to 7 minute drive to Fremont Street in downtown Las Vegas. ## How Far Is Naked City from the Las Vegas Strip? Naked City is immediately adjacent to the north end of the Las Vegas Strip, making it one of the closest residential neighborhoods to the resort corridor. The STRAT Hotel and Casino is roughly 0.3 miles from the neighborhood, and the SAHARA Las Vegas sits about 0.5 miles away. A typical drive to the north end of the Strip takes 2 to 5 minutes depending on traffic. Even during peak hours on Las Vegas Boulevard, residents can reach major Strip properties quickly by using parallel routes along Industrial Road or Paradise Road. ## How Long Does It Take to Drive to the Airport? Harry Reid International Airport is approximately 5 to 6 miles south of Naked City. Under normal traffic conditions, the drive takes between 10 and 15 minutes. The most direct routes run south along Interstate 15 or Las Vegas Boulevard. Morning rush hour and major convention weekends can add 5 to 10 minutes to the trip, but the overall commute remains shorter than what most Las Vegas neighborhoods experience. Residents who work in hospitality, aviation, or tourism related industries will find this proximity especially practical. ## How Close Is Downtown Las Vegas from Naked City? Downtown Las Vegas and the Fremont Street Experience are roughly 1.5 to 2 miles north of Naked City. The drive typically takes 5 to 7 minutes using Las Vegas Boulevard or Main Street. This puts downtown dining, entertainment, and the growing Fremont East district within easy reach. The SAHARA Las Vegas also connects to the Las Vegas Monorail system, giving residents an additional transit option for reaching destinations along the eastern side of the Strip without dealing with traffic or parking.
