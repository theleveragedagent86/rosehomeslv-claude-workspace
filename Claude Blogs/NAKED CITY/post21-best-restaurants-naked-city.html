# Best Restaurants Near Naked City Las Vegas The best restaurants near Naked City Las Vegas range from revolving fine dining at The STRAT to acclaimed New American fare on Spring Mountain Road, all within a few miles of this centrally located neighborhood. ## What Are the Best Restaurants Near Naked City? Naked City sits between two of the most active dining corridors in Las Vegas, giving residents and visitors easy access to a wide range of cuisines and price points. The STRAT, located roughly 0.3 miles from the neighborhood, is home to Top of the World, a revolving fine dining restaurant on the 106th floor with panoramic views of the valley. Just half a mile away, SAHARA Las Vegas offers Mother Wolf and Balla Italian Soul for upscale Italian, along with Chickie's and Pete's for a more casual sports bar experience. ## Where Do Locals Eat Near the Strip? Along the W Sahara Avenue corridor, Bespoke Kitchen serves an all day breakfast and lunch menu about half a mile from Naked City. Honey Salt provides farm to table American cuisine with fresh, seasonal dishes. For something quick and affordable, Special Noodle offers handmade Chinese noodles at a wallet friendly price. These spots cater to the everyday dining needs of locals rather than the tourist crowd. ## Is Chinatown Close to Naked City? Spring Mountain Road, the heart of Las Vegas Chinatown, is roughly 1.5 miles from Naked City. China Mama draws consistent praise for its Crispy Beef and handmade pastry, and reservations are recommended during peak hours. Sparrow + Wolf, a James Beard semifinalist restaurant on Spring Mountain, combines New American and Asian fusion techniques with live fire cooking. Weera Thai on W Sahara Avenue brings authentic Northeastern Thai flavors to the area and is open daily from 11am to 10pm. Ryan Rose recommends starting your Naked City dining exploration with Bespoke Kitchen for a casual morning meal and Sparrow + Wolf for a memorable dinner, as both represent the quality and variety that make this part of Las Vegas worth exploring beyond the Strip.
