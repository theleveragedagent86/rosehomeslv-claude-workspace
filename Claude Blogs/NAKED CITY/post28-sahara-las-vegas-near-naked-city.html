# SAHARA Las Vegas Resort and Casino Near Naked City SAHARA Las Vegas is a historic Strip resort roughly half a mile from Naked City, featuring a full casino, restaurants like Mother Wolf and Balla Italian Soul, the Magic Mike Live show, and a Las Vegas Monorail stop. ## What Restaurants Are at SAHARA Las Vegas? SAHARA Las Vegas offers several dining options that appeal to both visitors and neighborhood residents. Mother Wolf serves Italian American cuisine inspired by the flavors of Rome, while Balla Italian Soul provides a more casual Italian dining atmosphere. Chickie's and Pete's is a sports bar known for its signature Crabfries and American comfort food, making it a popular spot for watching games. The variety of restaurants within a single resort means Naked City residents have multiple dining choices within a short walk or drive from their front door. ## What Entertainment Does SAHARA Las Vegas Offer? Magic Mike Live is one of the headline entertainment offerings at SAHARA Las Vegas, drawing audiences from across the valley and beyond. Yes, SAHARA Las Vegas is home to the northernmost stop on the Las Vegas Monorail. The monorail connects to several major Strip resorts and the Las Vegas Convention Center, making it a convenient transit option for residents who work along the Strip or attend events at the convention center. From Naked City, reaching the SAHARA Monorail station takes just a few minutes, giving residents access to a transit line that runs the length of the resort corridor without needing to drive or park.
