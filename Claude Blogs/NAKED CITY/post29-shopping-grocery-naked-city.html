# Grocery Stores and Shopping Near Naked City Las Vegas The closest grocery stores to Naked City Las Vegas are Walmart Supercenter at 4505 W Charleston Boulevard and the Chinatown markets along Spring Mountain Road, both roughly 1.5 miles from the neighborhood. ## Where Is the Nearest Grocery Store to Naked City? Naked City does not have a major grocery store within its immediate boundaries. The nearest full service option is Walmart Supercenter at 4505 W Charleston Boulevard, approximately 1.5 miles west of the neighborhood. This location carries a complete grocery selection along with electronics, household goods, and pharmacy services. For specialty items, 99 Ranch Market in the Spring Mountain area is about two miles away and offers a wide selection of Asian groceries, produce, and prepared foods. An Indian Grocery Store at 4601 W Sahara Avenue provides South Asian specialty items roughly two miles from the neighborhood. ## What Shopping Options Exist in and Near Naked City? Within Naked City, the Gateway District features indie retail including vintage clothing shops, antique stores, and small boutiques. These independent businesses give the neighborhood a distinctive character and support the local creative community. The STRAT and SAHARA Las Vegas both offer resort retail and gift shops within half a mile of the neighborhood for souvenirs and convenience items. For a more upscale shopping experience, Tivoli Village is approximately five miles away and features boutique stores, restaurants, and a curated retail environment. ## Is There a Walkable Shopping Area Near Naked City? The Gateway District shops within Naked City itself are the most walkable retail options for residents. For grocery shopping, most residents drive or rideshare to Walmart or the Chinatown corridor. The neighborhood's Walk Score of 54 out of 100 reflects the fact that some errands require a car, though day to day convenience items can be found at smaller shops and resort retail nearby. The ongoing redevelopment of the Gateway District may bring additional retail options closer to residents in the future. One tip Ryan Rose shares with buyers considering Naked City is to plan grocery runs around a weekly trip to Walmart or the Chinatown markets, where the combination of mainstream and specialty groceries covers most household needs within a short drive.
