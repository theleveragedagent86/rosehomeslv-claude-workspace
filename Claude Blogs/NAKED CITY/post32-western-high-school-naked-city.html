# Western High School Las Vegas Review Western High School is the zoned public high school for Naked City Las Vegas, serving grades 9 through 12 with approximately 2,745 students and an 83% graduation rate at a campus located roughly two miles from the neighborhood. ## What Are Western High School's Academics Like? Western High School holds a C minus rating on Niche, reflecting both its challenges and its ongoing efforts to serve a diverse student body. The school reports an average SAT score of 870 and an average ACT score of 19. With 2,745 students enrolled, Western is one of the larger high schools in the Clark County School District. The 83% graduation rate falls slightly below the national average but demonstrates that the majority of students complete their diploma requirements. Families in Naked City should note that Western is the default zoned school, meaning enrollment does not require an application or lottery. ## Are There Better School Options Near Naked City? Families seeking alternatives have several options within a short drive. Rancho High School, located approximately three miles from Naked City, operates as a public magnet school with a Niche rating of B and a GreatSchools score of 6 out of 10. Rancho boasts a 94% graduation rate and enrolls 2,994 students. For younger children, Hyde Park Middle School earns a B plus on Niche with a GreatSchools score of 8 out of 10 and a 46% math proficiency rate among its 1,339 students. Crestwood Elementary, just half a mile from Naked City, serves 559 students with a Niche C rating and a GreatSchools score of 5 out of 10. ## Should School Ratings Affect a Naked City Home Purchase?
