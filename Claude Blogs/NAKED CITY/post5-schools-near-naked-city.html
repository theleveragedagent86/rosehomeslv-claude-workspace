Schools near Naked City Las Vegas include several public elementary, middle, and high school options within a short drive, with ratings ranging from below average to above average depending on the campus. ## What Schools Serve the Naked City Las Vegas Area? Families living in Naked City have access to Clark County School District campuses at every grade level. Crestwood Elementary School sits about half a mile from the neighborhood and serves pre K through 5th grade with an enrollment of approximately 559 students. Niche rates it a C, and GreatSchools gives it a 5 out of 10. Hollingsworth Elementary, roughly one mile away, is another nearby option, though its ratings fall below average. For middle school, Hyde Park Middle School stands out as a strong choice. Located about two miles from Naked City, it enrolls around 1,339 students and earns a B+ from Niche and an 8 out of 10 from GreatSchools. Its math proficiency rate of 46% is well above the district average. Fremont Middle School is another nearby option at about 2.5 miles, though its math proficiency of 17.5% and below average ratings make it a less competitive choice. ## What Are the High School Options Near Naked City? Western High School is the zoned high school for much of the Naked City area, located roughly two miles away. It enrolls about 2,745 students, carries a C minus rating from Niche, and reports an 83% graduation rate. Average SAT scores sit at 870 and ACT scores at 19. Rancho High School, a public magnet school about three miles from Naked City, offers a notably stronger academic profile. It earns a B from Niche and a 6 out of 10 from GreatSchools, with a 94% graduation rate and nearly 3,000 students.
