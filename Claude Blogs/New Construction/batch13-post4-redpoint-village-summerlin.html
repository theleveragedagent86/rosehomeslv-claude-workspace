# Redpoint Village Summerlin New Construction Guide Redpoint Village is one of the most connected and walkable destinations for new construction in Summerlin, drawing buyers who want a modern home within easy reach of Downtown Summerlin's retail, dining, and entertainment district. ## Where Redpoint Village Sits in the Summerlin Master Plan Redpoint Village occupies the southern portion of the newer western Summerlin development, roughly bounded by the 215 Beltway to the east and the ongoing westward expansion of the master plan. Within Redpoint, there is also a sub-neighborhood known as Redpoint Square, which features a more urban-influenced layout with tighter lot spacing and walkable streetscapes designed to connect residents to retail and commercial uses without requiring a car for every errand. The proximity to Downtown Summerlin is a genuine daily-life advantage for Redpoint residents. The open-air shopping and dining hub at Downtown Summerlin is minutes away by car or accessible via the trail network on foot or by bike. This combination of new construction product and walkable access to retail is relatively rare in Las Vegas, where most new communities are car-dependent by design. ## Builders Active in Redpoint Village Redpoint Village and Redpoint Square have attracted a diverse lineup of builders offering product across multiple price tiers. Richmond American Homes has been active in this area with collections that emphasize design center personalization and upscale standard finishes. Toll Brothers has offered luxury single-family homes on the larger lots within Redpoint, with prices that can exceed seven figures on premium sites. Taylor Morrison has also brought communities to the Redpoint area, and their buydown incentives in 2026 have included tiered rates starting at 2.99% in the first year. Buyers looking for more accessible price points will find that some Redpoint Square communities offer attached and smaller detached homes starting closer to the upper $500s, making this one of the more affordable entry points into a true Summerlin address. The trade-off for lower pricing is typically smaller square footage and reduced lot size, both of which are consistent with the village's more urban design intent. ## What to Know About HOA Fees and SID in Redpoint Redpoint Village buyers face the same dual-HOA structure found throughout newer Summerlin communities: a sub-community association and the Summerlin master HOA. For some communities in Redpoint Square, there may also be a commercial-adjacent or mixed-use component that affects fee structure, so buyers should carefully review the full HOA disclosure package before signing. SID assessments apply in Redpoint Village as they do in most newer Summerlin development. The infrastructure in this part of Summerlin is newer, which typically means the SID balance is higher than in villages built earlier in the master plan. Buyers should request the specific SID balance for their lot at the time of contract to accurately model total monthly housing costs. ## Local Insight As a Las Vegas real estate specialist, Ryan Rose sees Redpoint Village as one of the smartest long-term bets in Summerlin for buyers who prioritize walkability and lifestyle convenience over pure square footage per dollar.
