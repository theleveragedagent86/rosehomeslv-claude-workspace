# Complete Guide to Henderson New Construction 2026 Henderson is the most sought-after city for new construction in the Las Vegas Valley, and in 2026 it remains one of the strongest markets in the country for buyers seeking quality, safety, and long-term value. ## Why Henderson Leads the Las Vegas Valley Henderson accounts for roughly 26% of all new home sales in the greater Las Vegas area, making it the second-largest new construction market in the valley. The city consistently ranks among the safest in the United States, with a violent crime rate of just 2.1 per 1,000 residents, placing it at or near the top of national safety rankings. Schools in Henderson feed into some of the highest-rated zones in Clark County School District, with Coronado High School, Liberty High School, and Green Valley High School all drawing strong reputations. These fundamentals drive demand year after year from families relocating from California and other high-cost states. ## Prices and Entry Points in Henderson 2026 New construction in Henderson starts in the mid-$400s for smaller production homes and rises to well over $1 million in luxury enclaves like Lake Las Vegas and MacDonald Highlands. The most active price bands fall between $500,000 and $750,000, where buyers find three to five bedroom homes from builders including Lennar, KB Home, Toll Brothers, Beazer Homes, and Tri Pointe. Henderson also carries LID (Local Improvement District) assessments on many newer communities, which run $500 to $3,000 or more annually and transfer to the buyer at closing. Understanding LID before you make an offer is essential, and an experienced agent will make sure those numbers are factored into your true monthly cost. ## Active Communities and Builder Options The two flagship master-planned communities in Henderson are Cadence and Inspirada. Cadence, ranked the number one master-planned community in the United States by RCLCO, has 12,250 total planned homes and approximately two to three years of buildout remaining. Inspirada is even closer to completion, with roughly 75 homes left across the entire community before it closes out entirely. Beyond those two anchors, Lake Las Vegas continues to attract new construction from Tri Pointe and Taylor Morrison, while Del Webb at Lake Las Vegas serves the active adult market. Each community has a distinct character, price point, and set of builders, so knowing which one fits your lifestyle is the starting point for any Henderson search. ## The Henderson Commute Reality Henderson sits 16 to 24 minutes from the Las Vegas Strip via the I-215 beltway, which runs cleanly around the south end of the valley. Most Henderson residents commute to employment centers in Green Valley, the medical corridor near St. Rose Hospital, or downtown Las Vegas. Lake Las Vegas extends that commute to 25 to 35 minutes but delivers a resort environment that many buyers find worth the extra drive time. The beltway also connects Henderson directly to the airport, which is a practical advantage for frequent travelers. ## Local Insight As a Las Vegas real estate specialist, Ryan Rose works regularly with buyers choosing between Henderson and other parts of the valley, and the question he hears most often is whether Cadence or Inspirada is the right fit.
