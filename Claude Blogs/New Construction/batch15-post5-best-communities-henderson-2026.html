# Best New Construction Communities in Henderson 2026 Henderson offers several distinct new construction options in 2026, from the massive Cadence master-planned community to the intimate final phases of Inspirada and the luxury resort atmosphere of Lake Las Vegas. Here is what each community offers and who it serves best. ## Cadence: Scale, Amenities, and Multiple Builders Cadence stands as Henderson's largest and most active new construction community, holding the number one national ranking by RCLCO. With 12,250 total planned homes and roughly two to three years of buildout remaining, Cadence gives buyers the widest selection of floorplans, builders, and price points in Henderson. Builders active in Cadence include Lennar, KB Home, Century Communities, Beazer Homes, and Toll Brothers. Prices range from the mid-$300s to well over $600,000 depending on the neighborhood and builder. Amenities include community pools, a fitness center, trails, and parks, along with the upcoming Cadence Crossing entertainment complex opening in 2026. Cadence works well for buyers who want a vibrant, established community feel without sacrificing the benefits of new construction. ## Inspirada: Boutique Feel, Final Opportunity Inspirada is the other major Henderson master-planned community, and it is nearly complete. Approximately 75 homes remain across KB Home, Toll Brothers, and Tri Pointe before the community closes out permanently. Inspirada has a slightly more intimate scale than Cadence, with pedestrian-friendly design, multiple pools, and pocket parks woven throughout. Prices have historically ranged from the mid-$300s to $1.25 million, and remaining inventory skews toward the mid-range and upper-end homes from Toll Brothers and Tri Pointe. Buyers who want Inspirada need to act now; there will not be a second chance at new construction once the final homes are sold. ## Lake Las Vegas: Luxury and Resort Lifestyle Lake Las Vegas sits about 25 to 35 minutes from the Strip along Lake Mead Parkway and offers one of the most distinctive living environments in the valley. Built around a 320-acre private lake, the community draws buyers seeking a resort atmosphere with an upscale residential feel. Active builders include Tri Pointe, Taylor Morrison, and the Del Webb brand serving the 55-plus market from the mid-$400s. At the ultra-luxury end, La Cova offers waterfront homes priced from $2 million to $4 million, and Four Seasons Private Residences at MacDonald Highlands is targeting buyers up to $27.5 million. Lake Las Vegas is ideal for buyers for whom the daily environment matters as much as the home itself. ## MacDonald Highlands: Guard-Gated Prestige MacDonald Highlands is Henderson's premier guard-gated luxury enclave, positioned above the valley on the eastern ridgeline with panoramic views of the Las Vegas skyline. New construction here includes custom and semi-custom options, and the Four Seasons Private Residences project is adding a condominium component that will further elevate the community's profile. Price points start in the upper $700s and extend to multimillion-dollar custom estates. This community suits buyers who place high value on exclusivity, security, and views. ## Local Insight As a Las Vegas real estate specialist, Ryan Rose recommends that buyers approaching the Henderson market in 2026 start with a clear understanding of their timeline and budget, because the best communities are not interchangeable. Cadence is the right call for buyers who want variety and time to decide; Inspirada is for buyers who know they want that community and are ready to move. Lake Las Vegas and MacDonald Highlands serve buyers with specific lifestyle priorities that go well beyond price.
