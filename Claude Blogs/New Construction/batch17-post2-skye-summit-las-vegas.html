# Skye Summit Las Vegas: Everything About the Newest 3,500 Home Community Skye Summit is the most anticipated new master planned community coming to Las Vegas, and it is generating serious interest from buyers who want to get in early on a development that will reshape the northwest valley over the next decade. Here is everything you need to know before the sales doors open. ## The Scale and Scope of Skye Summit Skye Summit sits on 505 acres in northwest Las Vegas, positioned to become one of the largest master planned communities built in the valley in years. The full buildout calls for 3,500 homes, which puts it in the same conversation as communities like Inspirada that have taken more than a decade to reach completion. The community is expected to span its construction timeline from first sales in early 2027 through an estimated completion window around 2034 to 2035. The builder lineup is an all-star roster by Las Vegas standards. KB Home is confirmed as the first builder to open sales, followed by Pulte, Taylor Morrison, Tri Pointe Homes, Century Communities, and D.R. Horton. That diversity of builders means buyers at multiple price points will find options here, from entry level to move up and potentially luxury product. ## Location and Access Skye Summit occupies northwest Las Vegas territory, an area that already claims 29% of all new home sales in the metro. The northwest corridor has proven itself as a desirable address through the success of communities like Skye Canyon, Providence, and Centennial Hills. Skye Summit extends that corridor further and benefits from the established infrastructure and amenities already built around it. Access to the I-215 beltway connects residents quickly to the rest of the valley. The Strip is reachable within approximately 20 to 25 minutes under normal conditions, and Summerlin's retail, dining, and entertainment options are close at hand. ## What Buyers Should Know Right Now First sales are expected to open in early 2027, which means buyers interested in the earliest phases have time to prepare but should not wait too long to get their names on interest lists. KB Home, the first builder on site, is known for ENERGY STAR certified construction and a build to order model that gives buyers meaningful customization before dirt is broken on their specific home. One key financial consideration: Skye Summit sits in the northwest corridor where SID assessments (Special Improvement District fees) are common. Buyers should ask specifically about any SID obligations attached to their lot, as these annual costs of $500 to $3,000 or more run in addition to standard HOA fees and property taxes. ## Local Insight As a Las Vegas real estate specialist, Ryan Rose has been tracking Skye Summit closely and sees it as one of the most significant new community launches in northwest Las Vegas in recent memory.
