# Southern Highlands New Construction: Guard Gated Luxury in Southwest Las Vegas If you want new construction with a guard gated entrance, resort style community infrastructure, and a world class golf course woven into the neighborhood, Southern Highlands is the address in southwest Las Vegas that delivers all of it. This established luxury community continues to attract buyers who want security and prestige alongside their new home. ## What Makes Southern Highlands Different Southern Highlands is built around the Southern Highlands Golf Club, a Robert Trent Jones Jr. designed course that serves as both an amenity and a visual backdrop for much of the community. The guard gated entry creates a level of controlled access that is rare in Las Vegas suburban development, and it contributes meaningfully to the lifestyle and the resale value dynamics here. Pricing in Southern Highlands reflects the luxury positioning. New construction and resale product ranges from approximately $400,000 at the entry tier up to $1.5 million and beyond for larger custom and semi-custom homes. That span makes Southern Highlands accessible to a broader segment than many buyers expect, though the true luxury inventory with golf course views and premium finishes sits well above the entry price. ## The Southwest Las Vegas Location Advantage Southwest Las Vegas, sometimes referred to as the Enterprise and Spring Valley corridors, accounts for about 25% of new home sales across the entire metro. Southern Highlands anchors the southern portion of this region, positioned near I-15 with convenient access to the airport, the Strip, and the premium retail and dining of Town Square and the Las Vegas South Premium Outlets. Commute times from Southern Highlands to the Strip run roughly 15 to 20 minutes without traffic, making it one of the more practical luxury addresses in the valley for residents who work or entertain near the resort corridor. The area also benefits from proximity to some of the stronger school options in the southwest, with open enrollment in Clark County giving residents additional flexibility. ## Builders and Inventory in 2026 Southern Highlands has a mix of active builder programs and established resale inventory. The community is mature enough that buyers can evaluate both new construction from production builders and well maintained resale homes from the earlier build phases. For buyers specifically seeking new construction, working with a buyer's agent who knows which builders are currently pulling permits and which phases are still selling is essential, as Southern Highlands is not a single builder community and inventory availability shifts frequently. The guard gated nature of the community also affects how you tour it.
