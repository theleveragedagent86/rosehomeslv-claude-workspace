# Mountains Edge New Construction: The Family Friendly Sweet Spot Mountains Edge has earned its reputation as one of the best family oriented communities in southwest Las Vegas, and in 2026 it continues to offer new construction options at a price point that puts it within reach for a wide range of buyers. If you are looking for a community built around outdoor space and neighborhood identity without the luxury premium, Mountains Edge deserves a close look. ## What Makes Mountains Edge a Family Destination The centerpiece of outdoor life in Mountains Edge is Exploration Peak Park, a destination sized regional park that gives families hundreds of acres of trails, open space, and some of the best views of the Las Vegas valley from an elevated desert ridgeline. The park is not a token amenity; it is the kind of feature that shapes how a community actually lives day to day. Children grow up with meaningful outdoor access, and the trail network draws active adults who want more than a neighborhood loop. Beyond the park, Mountains Edge was designed from the ground up as a master planned community with an internal network of smaller neighborhood parks, walking paths, and community gathering points. The scale of the community, which spans a significant portion of the southwest valley, means there is enough density to support retail, dining, and services within a short drive of any home. ## Pricing and New Construction Options in 2026 Mountains Edge enters the new construction conversation at around $350,000, which makes it one of the more accessible addresses in the southwest corridor of Las Vegas. The top of the range for the community runs to approximately $700,000, creating a wide band that accommodates first time buyers, move up buyers, and larger families who need four or more bedrooms. The southwest Las Vegas corridor as a whole accounts for roughly 25% of all new home sales in the metro, and Mountains Edge represents a meaningful share of that activity. Builders active in the community deliver standard production home packages with the typical Las Vegas builder features: energy efficient construction, modern open floor plans, and full builder warranty programs. ## What to Consider Before You Buy Mountains Edge sits in Clark County, which means buyers are subject to the same SID and HOA considerations that apply across the valley. Checking whether a specific community within Mountains Edge carries a Special Improvement District assessment is important before comparing it to communities that do not. Some areas of the southwest corridor have these additional fees; others do not. School zones within Mountains Edge vary, as they do throughout Clark County School District.
