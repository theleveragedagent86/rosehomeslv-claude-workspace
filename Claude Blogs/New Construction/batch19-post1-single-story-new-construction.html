# Single Story New Construction in Las Vegas: Every Option Las Vegas may be one of the best markets in the country for buyers who want a brand new single story home. Builders here offer more one story floor plans than almost anywhere else in the Southwest, and the options span every price point from the high $300,000s to well over a million dollars. ## Why Single Story Is So Popular in Las Vegas The demand for single story homes in Las Vegas is driven by several overlapping buyer groups: retirees who want to age in place, families who prefer to keep everyone on one floor, and buyers with mobility considerations. The flat desert terrain also makes single story construction straightforward and cost effective compared to markets where land is more limited. Because builders can spread square footage horizontally rather than stacking it vertically, floor plans here tend to feel more open and livable than their counterparts in other states. Most major builders in Las Vegas offer dedicated single story collections. Lennar includes one story plans in nearly every community it operates in the valley. KB Home builds single story options across communities in North Las Vegas, Henderson, and the northwest. DR Horton, which ranked second in Las Vegas closings with 1,663 homes sold, offers entry level single story plans starting in the high $300,000s in North Las Vegas communities. Pulte, Toll Brothers, and Richmond American all carry premium one story plans in communities like Summerlin, Henderson, and the southwest corridor. ## Where to Find Single Story New Construction North Las Vegas remains the most affordable area for single story new homes in 2026, with starting prices from the high $300,000s through the mid $400,000s. Communities near Skye Canyon in the northwest offer a mix of single and two story plans with single story options typically starting around $500,000. Henderson communities including Cadence and Inspirada carry one story selections in the mid $400,000s to upper $600,000s depending on the builder and lot premium. Summerlin offers single story plans at several villages including Stonebridge, Kestrel, and Redpoint, with most entry level single story homes starting around $600,000 and rising well above $800,000 for larger footprints. For buyers 55 and older, Heritage at Stonebridge by Lennar is entirely single story and designed around active adult living. Esplanade at Red Rock by Taylor Morrison, opening for sales in Q2 2026, is also a dedicated 55 plus single story community starting from the $800,000s. ## Square Footage and Floor Plan Considerations Single story new construction in Las Vegas typically ranges from around 1,400 square feet on the compact end to over 3,500 square feet for larger luxury plans. Buyers should be aware that larger footprints on a single floor require a wider lot, which can mean a higher lot premium, particularly in master planned communities where corner lots and end positions are priced accordingly. Structural options like great room extensions, expanded primary suites, and covered patio additions are worth selecting at contract since they cannot be added later. ## Local Insight As a Las Vegas real estate specialist, Ryan Rose notes that single story inventory moves faster than two story in most price ranges, particularly in Henderson and Summerlin.
