The Palisades is an exclusive guard gated community in Summerlin, Las Vegas, built by Christopher Homes between 1998 and 2005. With only 131 semi custom estates nestled along the TPC Las Vegas golf course, this neighborhood offers a rare combination of luxury, privacy, and location within The Canyons Village of Summerlin North. ## What Makes the Palisades Summerlin Unique? The Palisades stands apart from other Summerlin communities because of its intimate size and prestigious builder. Christopher Homes designed every residence in the neighborhood, creating a cohesive Tuscan and old world architectural style throughout. Homes range from 2,875 to 7,084 square feet with lot sizes between one quarter acre and one half acre. Eight foot block walls between properties ensure residents enjoy genuine privacy. The community features 47 homes positioned directly along TPC Las Vegas fairways, giving nearly a third of all residents golf course frontage. Floor plan models include the Monet at 3,758 square feet, the Van Gogh at 4,375 square feet, the Renoir at 4,800 square feet, and the Picasso at 5,243 square feet. Both single story and two story layouts are available, many with interior courtyards that reflect the Mediterranean design influence. ## What Amenities Does the Palisades Offer? Residents enjoy 24 hour guard gated access off North Hualapai Way, a community park and sports area at the entrance, a basketball court, and manicured green spaces. The neighborhood sits within one mile of Summerlin Hospital Medical Center, and Downtown Summerlin shopping is just minutes away. Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate expert who knows the Palisades well, often highlights the community's walkable access to trails and parks as a major draw for active residents. The Palisades is located in the 89144 zip code, roughly 12 miles from the Las Vegas Strip with a typical drive time of 17 to 25 minutes. Summerlin North consistently ranks among the safest areas in the valley, with violent crime rates 70 to 85 percent below the Las Vegas average. ## Why the Palisades Remains One of Summerlin's Most Sought After Neighborhoods Because the community is fully built out with no new construction planned, available homes are limited to resale inventory. That scarcity, combined with the TPC golf course setting and Christopher Homes craftsmanship, keeps demand strong year after year. Families also appreciate that the Palisades has no age restrictions, welcoming residents of all stages of life.
