The Palisades Summerlin is zoned for John W. Bonner Elementary, Sig Rogich Middle School, and Palo Verde High School, all of which rank among the top rated public schools in the Las Vegas valley. Families in this guard gated Summerlin North community have access to strong academic options from kindergarten through twelfth grade. ## Which Public Schools Serve the Palisades Summerlin? John W. Bonner Elementary serves Palisades students in pre K through fifth grade. The school holds a Niche rating of A minus and a GreatSchools score of 9 out of 10, placing it among the highest performing elementary schools in the Clark County School District. Ethel W. Staton Elementary is another nearby option with matching ratings, offering families an additional strong choice in the immediate area. Sig Rogich Middle School covers grades six through eight for Palisades residents. Rogich earned National Blue Ribbon School recognition and carries a Niche rating of B plus with a GreatSchools score of 8 out of 10. The school is well regarded for its academic programs and extracurricular offerings. Palo Verde High School serves grades nine through twelve and ranks number 41 among the best public high schools in Nevada according to Niche, also holding a B plus rating. ## Are There Private School Options Near the Palisades? Several respected private schools are within driving distance of the Palisades. The Meadows School, a pre K through 12 institution, is consistently ranked as one of the top private schools in Las Vegas. Alexander Dawson School at Rainbow Mountain sits within Summerlin on a 33 acre campus and serves students from pre K through eighth grade. Faith Lutheran Academy offers a pre K through 12 program with AdvancED accreditation. While not the zoned school for the Palisades, it represents yet another quality option in the Summerlin area. ## Why School Quality Matters for Palisades Homeowners Strong school ratings contribute directly to property values in the Palisades and throughout Summerlin.
