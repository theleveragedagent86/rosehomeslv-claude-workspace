Luxury homes in the Palisades Summerlin start above $1 million, with larger and fully updated estates commanding significantly higher prices. This exclusive guard gated enclave in The Canyons Village of Summerlin North features just 131 semi custom residences, making it one of the most sought after luxury addresses in all of Las Vegas. ## What Makes Palisades Homes Considered Luxury? Every home in the Palisades was designed and built by Christopher Homes, a builder with more than 45 years of award winning craftsmanship in the Las Vegas Valley. Residences range from 2,875 to 7,084 square feet, situated on generous lot sizes of one quarter acre to one half acre. The Tuscan and old world architectural style creates a cohesive, estate like atmosphere throughout the community. Eight foot block walls between properties add a level of privacy rarely found in master planned neighborhoods. Interior features commonly found in Palisades homes include private swimming pools, interior courtyards, wine cellars, casitas, and open concept floor plans with high end finishes. Many residences have been extensively remodeled since their original construction between 1998 and 2005, incorporating modern smart home technology and designer kitchens while preserving the classic architectural character. ## How Do Palisades Luxury Homes Compare to Other Summerlin Communities? The Palisades offers something most Summerlin luxury communities cannot: direct golf course frontage on a PGA Tour championship venue. Forty seven of the 131 homes are positioned along TPC Las Vegas fairways, providing sweeping views of manicured greens and the surrounding desert mountains. Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate specialist who works extensively with Palisades buyers, notes that this combination of exclusivity, golf course setting, and Christopher Homes construction quality is nearly impossible to replicate elsewhere in the valley. Residents also enjoy optional membership to the TPC Summerlin clubhouse, which features a 36,000 square foot facility with fine dining, five lighted tennis courts, a Junior Olympic pool and spa, and a full fitness center with a Pilates studio. The community itself includes a park, green space, and basketball court at the entrance. ## Why Luxury Buyers Continue to Choose the Palisades Because the Palisades is fully built out with no new construction available, every purchase is a resale transaction.
