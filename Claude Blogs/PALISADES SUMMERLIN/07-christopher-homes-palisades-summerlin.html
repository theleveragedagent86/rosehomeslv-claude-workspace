Christopher Homes is the sole builder of the Palisades Summerlin, having constructed all 131 semi custom estates in this guard gated community between 1998 and 2005. As one of Las Vegas's most decorated luxury homebuilders, Christopher Homes brought more than four decades of craftsmanship to every residence in the neighborhood. ## Who Is Christopher Homes? Christopher Homes is a locally owned, family operated luxury homebuilder based in the Las Vegas Valley. The company has earned more than 150 industry awards over its 45 plus year history, including honors for Best Community in the Nation and Builder of the Year from the National Association of Home Builders. In 1999, one of the Palisades model homes was named National Home of the Year at the Best in American Living Awards, bringing national attention to this Summerlin community. The builder is known for its semi custom approach, offering buyers a selection of thoughtfully designed floor plans with the flexibility to personalize finishes and features. This philosophy is evident throughout the Palisades, where every home shares a cohesive Tuscan and old world architectural style while maintaining individual character through custom upgrades and interior courtyard designs. ## What Did Christopher Homes Build in the Palisades? Christopher Homes created four primary floor plan models for the Palisades community. The Monet is a single story plan at 3,758 square feet with four bedrooms, three and a half bathrooms, and a three car garage. The Van Gogh offers two stories and 4,375 square feet with three bedrooms, a home office, and a three car garage. The Renoir expands to 4,800 square feet across two stories with four bedrooms, four and a half bathrooms, and a four car garage. The Picasso is the largest standard plan at 5,243 square feet, featuring four bedrooms, five and a half bathrooms, and a home office.
