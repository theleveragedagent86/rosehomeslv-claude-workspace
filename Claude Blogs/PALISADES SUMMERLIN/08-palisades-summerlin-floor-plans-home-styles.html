Christopher Homes designed four named floor plans for the Palisades, each inspired by a master artist. The Monet is the community's single story option at 3,758 square feet, offering four bedrooms, three and a half bathrooms, and a three car garage. For buyers who prefer a two story layout, the Van Gogh provides 4,375 square feet with three bedrooms, three and a half bathrooms, a dedicated home office, and a three car garage. The Renoir steps up to 4,800 square feet across two stories, featuring four bedrooms, four and a half bathrooms, a library and study, and a four car garage. The Picasso is the largest standard plan at 5,243 square feet, with four bedrooms, five and a half bathrooms, a home office, and a three car garage. Several homeowners have expanded their residences through custom additions, with the largest homes in the community reaching 7,084 square feet. ## What Architectural Style Defines the Palisades? Every home in the Palisades reflects a cohesive Tuscan and Mediterranean design language. Exterior features include stucco facades, clay tile roofs, arched entryways, and interior courtyards that create private outdoor living spaces within the footprint of each lot. Eight foot block walls between properties reinforce the estate like atmosphere and provide genuine privacy on lots ranging from one quarter acre to one half acre.
