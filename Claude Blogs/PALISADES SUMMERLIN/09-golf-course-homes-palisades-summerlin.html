Golf course homes in the Palisades Summerlin sit directly along the fairways of TPC Las Vegas, a par 72 championship course that hosts the annual Shriners Children's Open on the PGA Tour. Forty seven of the community's 131 homes enjoy direct golf course frontage, making the Palisades one of the premier golf course neighborhoods in the Las Vegas Valley. ## Which Palisades Homes Have Golf Course Views? The golf course lots in the Palisades are concentrated along the south and east sides of the community, where properties border the TPC Las Vegas layout. These homes offer unobstructed views of manicured fairways, the Spring Mountains to the west, and the surrounding desert landscape. Both single story and two story floor plans are represented among the 47 golf course positions, with two story homes providing elevated vantage points from upper level primary suites and balconies. Golf course properties in the Palisades range from approximately 2,875 to over 7,000 square feet, situated on lots of one quarter acre to one half acre. Because these positions are among the most desirable in the community, they typically command a premium over interior lot homes. The combination of Christopher Homes construction, generous lot sizes, and direct TPC frontage creates a level of exclusivity that few Las Vegas golf communities can match. ## What Is TPC Las Vegas? TPC Las Vegas, also known as TPC Summerlin, features a Bobby Weed designed championship course stretching 7,243 yards. The 36,000 square foot clubhouse anchors a 12 acre practice facility and offers amenities that extend well beyond golf. Palisades residents are eligible for membership, which provides access to fine dining, five lighted tennis courts, a pickleball court, a Junior Olympic pool and spa, and a fitness center with a Pilates studio. Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate specialist who has helped numerous buyers find golf course homes in the Palisades, notes that TPC membership transforms the Palisades lifestyle into something more like a private resort. Corporate, personal, young professional, and social membership tiers make the club accessible to residents with varying levels of golf involvement. ## Living on the Course in the Palisades Daily life on a Palisades golf course lot means waking up to views of pristine greens and watching PGA Tour caliber play during tournament season each October. Residents enjoy the serenity of open green space without sacrificing the privacy that defines this neighborhood.
