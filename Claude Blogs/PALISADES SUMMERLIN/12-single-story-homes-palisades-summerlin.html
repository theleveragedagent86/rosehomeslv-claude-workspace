This layout features four bedrooms, three and a half bathrooms, and a three car garage. The open floor plan connects the main living areas seamlessly, while the interior courtyard brings natural light deep into the home. Like all homes in the Palisades, the Monet sits on generous lots ranging from one quarter acre to one half acre. That extra land gives single story buyers the advantage of a more expansive footprint spread across a single level, with room for private pools, covered patios, and outdoor entertaining spaces. The Tuscan architectural style and eight foot block walls ensure that single story residents enjoy the same privacy as their two story neighbors. ## Why Buyers Prefer Single Story Homes in the Palisades Single story homes appeal to a wide range of buyers in the Palisades. Many are professionals and retirees who want luxury living without stairs. Others are families who appreciate the ease of a single level layout for everyday life. Because the community has no age restrictions, single story homes attract buyers across all demographics. Accessibility is another key factor. Single story living eliminates concerns about mobility over time, making the Monet floor plan a strong choice for buyers planning to stay in their home long term. The wide hallways, open great rooms, and ground level primary suites that Christopher Homes incorporated into the design reflect thoughtful planning for comfort at every stage of life.
