TPC Las Vegas is a PGA TOUR championship golf course located directly adjacent to the Palisades in Summerlin. With 47 homes in the community fronting TPC fairways, residents enjoy one of the most coveted golf course settings in all of Las Vegas. ## What Is TPC Las Vegas? TPC Las Vegas is a par 72, 7,243 yard course designed by Bobby Weed. It serves as the host venue for the Shriners Children's Open, one of the most watched PGA TOUR events each season. The course combines dramatic desert terrain with lush, tournament quality conditions that attract players and spectators from around the world. The facility includes a 36,000 square foot clubhouse featuring fine dining and a comfortable lounge. A 12 acre practice facility gives golfers of every skill level room to refine their game. Beyond golf, TPC Las Vegas offers five lighted tennis courts, a pickleball court, a Junior Olympic pool and spa, and a full fitness center with a dedicated Pilates studio. ## How Close Is TPC Las Vegas to the Palisades? The Palisades shares a direct boundary with TPC Las Vegas. Residents living along the course enjoy unobstructed views of manicured fairways and the surrounding mountain landscape. Walking or driving to the clubhouse takes just minutes, making this one of the most convenient golf course neighborhoods in the valley. TPC Las Vegas operates as a private club with several membership tiers. Options include Corporate, Personal, Young Professional, and Social memberships, each offering different levels of access to the golf course, clubhouse, dining, and athletic facilities. Social memberships are ideal for residents who want to enjoy the clubhouse and fitness amenities without a golf commitment. ## Local Insight Living next to a PGA TOUR venue adds more than views.
