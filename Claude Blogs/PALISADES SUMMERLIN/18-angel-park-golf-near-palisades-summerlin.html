Angel Park Golf Club is one of the most popular public golf destinations near the Palisades in Summerlin. Located at 100 S Rampart Blvd, this Arnold Palmer designed facility has been voted the Best Golf Course in Las Vegas 11 of the past 12 years, making it a favorite among residents and visitors alike. ## What Does Angel Park Golf Club Offer? Angel Park features two full 18 hole courses designed by Arnold Palmer, one of the most legendary names in golf history. The courses wind through the Summerlin landscape and offer challenging yet accessible layouts for golfers of all abilities. Beyond the main courses, the facility includes an 18 hole lighted natural grass putting course that provides a unique evening activity for families and friends. A lighted driving range allows golfers to practice well after sunset, which is especially appealing during the warm summer months. The "Cloud Nine" short course gives beginners and experienced players alike a chance to work on their short game in a relaxed setting. These varied offerings make Angel Park far more than a standard golf course. ## How Far Is Angel Park from the Palisades? Angel Park Golf Club sits just minutes from the Palisades along Rampart Boulevard, one of Summerlin's primary corridors. The short drive makes it easy for residents to fit in a round of golf, a practice session, or an evening on the putting course without a long commute.
