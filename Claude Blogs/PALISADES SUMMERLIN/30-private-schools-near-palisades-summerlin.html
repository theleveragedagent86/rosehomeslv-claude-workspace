The Palisades Summerlin offers access to several of the most respected private schools in the Las Vegas valley, giving families alternatives to the public school system at every grade level. From pre kindergarten through twelfth grade, private school options near the Palisades feature smaller class sizes, specialized curricula, and strong college placement records. ## What Private Schools Are Closest to the Palisades? Alexander Dawson School at Rainbow Mountain sits within Summerlin itself, making it the most convenient private option for Palisades families. The school serves students from pre kindergarten through eighth grade on a 33 acre campus. With just 489 students and a student to teacher ratio of 8 to 1, Dawson provides a highly personalized learning environment. The school is a Stanford Challenge Success partner, meaning its academic approach is designed to promote student wellbeing alongside rigorous academics. ## Is The Meadows School a Good Option for Palisades Families? The Meadows School is consistently ranked as the top private school in Las Vegas and serves students from pre kindergarten through twelfth grade. The school maintains an 11 to 1 student to teacher ratio and has placed graduates at Princeton, Harvard, and other highly selective universities. For Palisades families seeking a comprehensive private education that covers every grade level, The Meadows offers a single institution solution that eliminates school transitions. The school is within driving distance of the Palisades, and many Summerlin families consider it the premier private academic option in the valley. ## What About Faith Lutheran Academy? Faith Lutheran Academy provides a pre kindergarten through twelfth grade education with AdvancED accreditation and an "Exemplary School" designation. The school enrolls approximately 2,000 students with a student to teacher ratio of 17 to 1. Faith Lutheran combines a faith based curriculum with strong academics and extracurricular programs, including competitive athletics.
