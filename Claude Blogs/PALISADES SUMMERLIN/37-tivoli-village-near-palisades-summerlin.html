Tivoli Village offers Palisades Summerlin residents a shopping and dining experience unlike anything else in Las Vegas. Located at 400 S Rampart Blvd, this European inspired retail center sits roughly five minutes from the Palisades and features more than 25 boutiques, upscale restaurants, and specialty shops in an architectural setting designed to feel like a Mediterranean village. ## What Makes Tivoli Village Different From Other Shopping Centers? The first thing most visitors notice about Tivoli Village is the architecture. Stone facades, arched walkways, and courtyard spaces create an atmosphere that feels more like a European plaza than a Las Vegas shopping center. The design is intentional, with covered outdoor seating areas, fountains, and landscaped courtyards that encourage visitors to linger. This approach to retail sets Tivoli Village apart from the larger, busier shopping centers nearby and creates a more relaxed shopping experience. ## What Stores and Restaurants Are at Tivoli Village? Tivoli Village focuses on specialty retail and fine dining rather than big box stores. Restoration Hardware anchors the center with a massive showroom that draws design enthusiasts from across the valley. Jeff White Custom Jewelry offers locally crafted fine jewelry, while VASARI provides high end men's grooming and retail. Dining options include Echo & Rig, a butcher shop and steakhouse known for its dry aged beef program, and Leone Cafe, a popular spot for Italian inspired coffee and pastries. The mix of independent businesses and curated national brands gives the center a boutique feel. ## How Close Is Tivoli Village to the Palisades?
