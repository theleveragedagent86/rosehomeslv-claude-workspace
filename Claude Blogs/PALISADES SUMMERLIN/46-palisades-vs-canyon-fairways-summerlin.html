The Palisades and Canyon Fairways are two of the most sought after guard gated neighborhoods in Summerlin's Canyons Village. Both communities sit along the TPC Las Vegas golf course, both were built by Christopher Homes, and both attract buyers searching for luxury living in Summerlin North. So what sets them apart? ## How Do the Palisades and Canyon Fairways Compare in Size and Layout? The Palisades is an intimate community of just 131 homes on lots ranging from one quarter to one half acre. Canyon Fairways is similarly exclusive, with homes positioned along TPC Summerlin fairways and comparable lot sizes. Both neighborhoods were developed during the late 1990s and early 2000s, giving them a mature, established feel with full landscaping and tree lined streets. Where they differ is in architectural personality. The Palisades features Tuscan and old world inspired designs with stone accents, arched entryways, and courtyard layouts. Canyon Fairways shares some of that Mediterranean influence but offers its own distinct floorplans and exterior finishes. Buyers choosing between the two often come down to which home style resonates more on a personal level. ## What About Pricing and Home Sizes? Both communities sit firmly in the million dollar plus price range. Palisades homes range from approximately 2,875 to over 7,000 square feet, and Canyon Fairways offers a comparable spread of living space. Because Christopher Homes built both neighborhoods, the construction quality and material standards are nearly identical. Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate specialist familiar with both communities, notes that resale values in each neighborhood have remained strong due to limited inventory and consistent demand from luxury buyers. ## Which Community Offers Better Golf Course Access? This is one of the closer comparisons in all of Summerlin. In the Palisades, 47 of the 131 homes sit directly along TPC Las Vegas fairways, giving more than a third of homeowners unobstructed course views. Canyon Fairways similarly wraps around sections of TPC Summerlin, with many homes enjoying direct frontage. The golf lifestyle in both communities is a defining feature rather than an added bonus. ## Local Insight Both neighborhoods share the same Canyons Village location, meaning residents enjoy identical access to Downtown Summerlin shopping, Summerlin Hospital, Red Rock Canyon, and the 215 Beltway. The daily experience of living in either community is remarkably similar, which is why the decision often comes down to specific lot position, home layout, and availability at the time of purchase.
