The Palisades and The Ridges are two of Summerlin's most prestigious guard gated communities, but they sit at different points on the luxury spectrum. Buyers considering either neighborhood are typically comparing established elegance against newer ultra luxury construction. Here is what separates them and where they overlap. ## How Do the Palisades and The Ridges Compare in Age and Construction? The Palisades was built by Christopher Homes between 1998 and 2005, featuring 131 homes in a Tuscan and old world architectural style. Every home in the community comes from the same builder, which gives the neighborhood a unified design identity. The Ridges is a newer community with homes built by multiple custom builders, resulting in a wider range of architectural styles, from desert contemporary to modern minimalist. Because of its newer construction timeline, The Ridges tends to feature more current building technologies, open concept layouts, and floor to ceiling glass designed to showcase mountain and city views. ## What Is the Price Difference Between These Communities? Both neighborhoods start at the million dollar mark, but The Ridges reaches considerably higher. It consistently ranks among the most expensive residential areas in all of Las Vegas, with some estates selling well above five million dollars. The Palisades offers a more accessible entry point into guard gated Summerlin luxury, with homes ranging from approximately 2,875 to over 7,000 square feet on quarter to half acre lots. Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate specialist who guides buyers through Summerlin's luxury tier, notes that the Palisades delivers a strong value proposition for buyers who want guard gated golf course living without the ultra premium pricing found in The Ridges. ## How Do the Golf Course Settings Compare? Golf plays a prominent role in both communities. In the Palisades, 47 homes front TPC Las Vegas fairways, one of the most recognized courses in the valley and home to a PGA Tour event for over two decades. The Ridges is positioned near Bear's Best Golf Course, a Tom Fazio design that features recreated holes from some of the best courses in the world. Both settings provide sweeping green views and a resort style atmosphere, though the courses themselves attract different types of players. ## Local Insight Location within Summerlin is another consideration. The Palisades sits in Summerlin North's Canyons Village, offering quick access to Downtown Summerlin and the 215 Beltway. The Ridges is located further south within Summerlin, closer to the base of Red Rock Canyon. Both positions provide easy access to hiking, shopping, and the broader Las Vegas metro area, but the drive times and surrounding retail options differ slightly.
