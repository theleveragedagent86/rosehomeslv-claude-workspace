Summerlin North is home to one of the highest concentrations of guard gated communities in the Las Vegas Valley. Buyers searching for privacy, security, and luxury in a master planned setting will find multiple options within The Canyons Village and surrounding areas. Here is a guide to the guard gated neighborhoods that define this part of Summerlin. ## Which Guard Gated Communities Are in The Canyons Village? The Canyons Village anchors the guard gated landscape of Summerlin North. The Palisades is one of the most recognized, with 131 Christopher Homes built between 1998 and 2005 on quarter to half acre lots. Forty seven of those homes sit directly along TPC Las Vegas fairways, and every home features Tuscan or old world architectural styling. Canyon Fairways is another Christopher Homes community within The Canyons, positioned along TPC Summerlin with a similar luxury profile and guard gated entry. Mira Villa, Bellacere, Country Club Hills, Country Rose Estates, and Eagle Rock round out the additional guard gated options in this village. Each neighborhood has its own distinct lot sizes, architectural character, and home count, but they all share the same controlled access model and proximity to the TPC Las Vegas corridor. ## What Makes Guard Gated Living Different from Standard Gated Communities? A guard gated community staffs its entry point with live security personnel 24 hours a day, seven days a week. This is different from a standard gated neighborhood that relies on key codes, remote controls, or call boxes without an on site attendant. In guard gated communities like the Palisades, every visitor is verified before the gate opens. There is no through traffic, no soliciting, and no unscreened access at any hour. Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate specialist who works extensively in Summerlin's guard gated neighborhoods, explains that the presence of a live guard adds both a deterrent and a layer of familiarity. Guards recognize residents, vehicles, and regular service providers, which creates a more personal security experience than automated systems alone can provide. ## What Are the Advantages of Choosing Summerlin North for Guard Gated Living? Location is a major factor. Summerlin North's guard gated communities sit within minutes of Downtown Summerlin's 125 plus shops and restaurants, Summerlin Hospital, the 215 Beltway, and Red Rock Canyon. Residents get the privacy of a gated enclave without sacrificing convenience.
