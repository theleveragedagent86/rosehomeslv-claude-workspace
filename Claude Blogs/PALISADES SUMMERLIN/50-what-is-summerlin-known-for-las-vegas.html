Summerlin is the largest and most established master planned community in the Las Vegas Valley. Developed by the Howard Hughes Corporation, it spans 22,500 acres along the western edge of the city with nearly one third of that land designated as open space. For residents and prospective buyers alike, Summerlin is known for its combination of outdoor recreation, top tier schools, walkable retail, and a wide range of housing options from starter homes to ultra luxury estates. ## What Makes Summerlin Different from Other Las Vegas Communities? Scale and planning set Summerlin apart. The community contains multiple villages, each with its own character, amenities, and price points. The Canyons, The Trails, The Hills, The Paseos, Stonebridge, and Redpoint are just a few of the distinct villages that make up the larger Summerlin footprint. This village structure means residents can find a neighborhood that fits their lifestyle without leaving the master plan's infrastructure of trails, parks, and commercial centers. Over 150 miles of interconnected trails wind through Summerlin, connecting neighborhoods to parks, shopping centers, and schools. That trail network is one of the community's most recognized features and a major reason active buyers choose Summerlin over other parts of Las Vegas. ## What Shopping and Dining Does Summerlin Offer? Downtown Summerlin serves as the community's main commercial hub, with more than 125 shops and restaurants anchored by major retailers and a growing list of local dining concepts. The open air layout makes it a social gathering point for residents throughout the year. Beyond Downtown Summerlin, additional retail centers, grocery stores, and medical facilities are distributed throughout the community's villages. ## How Are the Schools in Summerlin? Summerlin supports 16 public schools and 11 private schools across its footprint. The community consistently draws families because of its school options and the walkability many neighborhoods offer to nearby campuses. Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate specialist who helps families relocate to Summerlin, notes that school zoning is one of the top three factors buyers consider when choosing a specific village or neighborhood within the master plan. ## What About Golf and Outdoor Recreation? Summerlin is home to multiple golf courses, including TPC Las Vegas, Angel Park, and Bear's Best. Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area sits just minutes from Summerlin's western boundary, providing year round access to hiking, rock climbing, and scenic drives. The combination of golf, trails, and proximity to protected desert landscapes gives Summerlin a recreation profile that few master planned communities in the country can match. ## Local Insight Drive times from Summerlin to the Las Vegas Strip range from 17 to 25 minutes depending on the village, and McCarran International Airport is approximately 24 to 25 minutes away. This balance of suburban privacy and metro accessibility is a core reason Summerlin continues to attract buyers from across the country.
