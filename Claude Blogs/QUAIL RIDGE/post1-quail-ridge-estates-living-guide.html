Living in Quail Ridge Estates Henderson means calling home a 101-home guard-gated luxury community originally developed by American Nevada Corporation between 1982 and 1989. It was Henderson's first guard-gated custom home neighborhood. ## What Makes Quail Ridge Estates Henderson Special? Quail Ridge Estates sits inside the 89014 ZIP code in the Green Valley North area of Henderson. The community holds 101 custom estate homes on lots that range from 13,000 to over 30,000 square feet, often described as half-acre lots. Homes range from 3,243 to 12,712 square feet, with four or more bedrooms and three or more bathrooms typical. Many properties include pools, mature landscaping, and gourmet kitchens. All 101 homesites are built out, so no new construction exists. Some original homes have been torn down and rebuilt, while others have been extensively renovated. Around 1,258 residents call this community home, and there is no age restriction, making it open to families of every stage. ## What Is the Lifestyle Like Inside the Gates? A 24-hour staffed guard gate controls access, and the security perimeter wraps the entire neighborhood. Residents enjoy a community clubhouse, two tennis courts, a basketball court, and tree-lined walking paths. The streets carry a mature canopy of trees that softens the desert sun, and wide sidewalks make the interior of the community walkable, even though Green Valley North as a whole has a Walk Score around 37. The location places residents about 10 to 20 minutes from the Las Vegas Strip, roughly 3.5 miles away. Harry Reid International Airport sits about 3 miles north. Downtown Las Vegas is 20 to 25 minutes away. Sunset Park and Wildhorse Golf Club border the community. ## Who Tends to Live in Quail Ridge Estates? The community attracts buyers who want guard-gated security paired with custom architecture and large lots. Families like the proximity to Nate Mack Elementary, Thurman White Academy of the Performing Arts, and Green Valley High School.
