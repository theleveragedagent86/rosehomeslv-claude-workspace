# Sunset Regional Park Near Quail Ridge Estates: Full Amenity Guide Sunset Regional Park sits directly across Sunset Road from Quail Ridge Estates, giving residents instant access to 324 acres of recreation. It is the crown jewel of the Clark County park system. ## What Amenities Does Sunset Regional Park Offer? Sunset Regional Park spans 324 total acres with 214 developed for active use. The park has been a Clark County staple since 1967. The address is 2601 E Sunset Rd, Las Vegas, just across Sunset Road from the Quail Ridge Estates gates. Sports facilities include 8 lighted hard tennis courts, 9 softball fields, 2 Little League fields, 8 basketball courts, and 7 sand volleyball courts. The park also features a disc golf course and a fitness course for adults who want a workout outside a gym. ## What Is There for Kids and Families at Sunset Park? Families have plenty to choose from. Sunset Park includes 5 playgrounds spread across the developed acreage and a splash pad for hot summer afternoons. There are more than 30 picnic areas with tables and shade. The fishing pond allows casual angling, and the RC boat ramp is a draw for hobbyists who run remote-controlled boats. Miles of walking paths thread through the park, connecting all of the major activity zones. ## Are There Dog Areas at Sunset Park? Yes. Sunset Regional Park has 2 enclosed dog runs with separate areas for large dogs and small dogs. The dog runs are free to use and operate during standard Clark County park hours.
