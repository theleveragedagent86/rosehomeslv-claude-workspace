# Wildhorse Golf Club Near Quail Ridge Estates Wildhorse Golf Club sits adjacent to Quail Ridge Estates and gives residents a public 18-hole course with a Howard Hughes pedigree, all within walking distance of the gates. ## What Is Wildhorse Golf Club? Wildhorse Golf Club is an 18-hole, par 70 municipal public course at 2100 W Warm Springs Rd, Henderson 89014. The course was originally built in 1959 and once belonged to Howard Hughes. A full renovation by Curley/Schmidt in 2004 reshaped the layout into the version golfers play today. The course is walkable, which is unusual for Las Vegas valley golf and a real perk for players who prefer to stay out of a cart. Public access means you do not need a country club membership to tee off. ## What Amenities Does Wildhorse Have? Wildhorse Golf Club includes a practice range, a pro shop, and an on-site restaurant and bar. The clubhouse setup makes it easy to play a round, grab lunch, and head home, all within minutes of Quail Ridge Estates. The par 70 layout works for casual golfers and serious players alike. Quail Ridge Estates buyers who prioritize golf often use Wildhorse as their daily course because of the proximity and the public access. ## Is Wildhorse Golf Club Public? Yes. Wildhorse is a municipal public course with public tee times.
