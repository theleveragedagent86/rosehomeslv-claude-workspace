# Schools Serving Quail Ridge Estates: A Family Guide Schools serving Quail Ridge Estates in Henderson include Nate Mack Elementary, Thurman White Academy of the Performing Arts, and Green Valley High School. All three are part of the Clark County School District and provide K-12 continuity for families in the neighborhood. ## What schools are zoned for Quail Ridge Estates? Quail Ridge Estates families have three zoned public schools, all within CCSD. Nate Mack Elementary serves grades PK and K through 5. Thurman White Academy of the Performing Arts serves grades 6 through 8. Green Valley High School serves grades 9 through 12. Together, these schools cover the full K-12 path without requiring families to switch attendance zones as their kids grow up. ## How are these schools rated? Each of the three zoned schools earns solid marks from the major rating sites. Nate Mack Elementary holds a GreatSchools rating of 7 out of 10, a Niche grade of B, a SchoolDigger 4-star rating, and a Nevada Department of Education 5-star designation. Thurman White Academy carries a GreatSchools rating of 8 out of 10, a Niche score of 4.06 out of 5, and a US News rank of 39 in Nevada. Green Valley High School holds a Niche grade of B+ with a 4.09 out of 5 rating and ranks 33 in Nevada. ## What programs are available? Thurman White is a performing arts magnet program for middle school students, with enrollment around 1,585 kids. Green Valley High School offers both Advanced Placement and International Baccalaureate course tracks. Green Valley's performing arts students have performed at Carnegie Hall and at presidential inaugurations, giving the school a nationally recognized arts reputation. Enrollment at Green Valley is roughly 2,640 students. ## Are there private school options nearby? Yes. Henderson families also choose from private and charter alternatives such as the Adelson Educational Campus, Faith Lutheran Henderson Campus, and Pinecrest Academy charter schools. These are not zoned schools, so admission and tuition policies vary by campus.
