It is a public magnet program serving grades 6 through 8, with a GreatSchools rating of 8 out of 10 and around 1,585 students enrolled. ## What is Thurman White Academy? Thurman White Academy of the Performing Arts is a Clark County School District public magnet school in Henderson. It blends a standard middle school curriculum with a performing arts magnet track, giving students access to dedicated arts coursework alongside core academics. The campus serves grades 6, 7, and 8, and acts as the bridge between Nate Mack Elementary and Green Valley High School for Quail Ridge Estates families. ## How is Thurman White Academy rated? The school holds an 8 out of 10 rating on GreatSchools and a 4.06 out of 5 score on Niche, where it ranks number 41 among Nevada middle schools. SchoolDigger gives Thurman White a 4-star rating, and US News ranks the school number 39 in Nevada. Reading proficiency sits at 64 percent and math proficiency at 31 percent on recent state assessments. ## How big is Thurman White Academy? Total enrollment is approximately 1,585 students. That is a sizable middle school by Henderson standards, which is part of why the magnet program can support a full performing arts curriculum.
