Green Valley High School earns a Niche rating of 4.09 out of 5 with an overall grade of B+. It ranks number 33 among Nevada high schools on Niche. The school sits within the Clark County School District and consistently grades above the Las Vegas area average for public high schools, making it one of the higher rated zoned high schools in Henderson. ## What programs does Green Valley High School offer? Green Valley offers both Advanced Placement (AP) coursework and an International Baccalaureate (IB) program.
