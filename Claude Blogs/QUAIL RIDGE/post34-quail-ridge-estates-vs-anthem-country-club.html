Quail Ridge Estates is a guard-gated community of 101 custom homes built between 1982 and 1989 in Green Valley North, ZIP 89014. Median list price reached $1,774,950 in March 2026. Anthem Country Club is a guard-gated golf-course community built primarily between 1999 and 2008 in the Anthem master plan in southern Henderson.
