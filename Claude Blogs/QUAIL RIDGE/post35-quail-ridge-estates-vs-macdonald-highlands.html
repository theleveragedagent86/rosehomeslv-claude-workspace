Quail Ridge Estates and MacDonald Highlands are two guard-gated luxury communities in Henderson with very different personalities. Quail Ridge Estates is an established 101-home enclave with mature flat lots; MacDonald Highlands is a hillside community wrapping DragonRidge Country Club with dramatic Strip views. ## How do Quail Ridge Estates and MacDonald Highlands compare? Quail Ridge Estates sits in Green Valley North, ZIP 89014, with 101 custom homes built between 1982 and 1989 by American Nevada Corporation. Median list price reached $1,774,950 in March 2026. MacDonald Highlands sits on the Henderson hillside off MacDonald Ranch Parkway and surrounds DragonRidge Country Club with its private golf course and clubhouse. MacDonald Highlands has been building since 1996 and continues with new luxury construction today, with prices ranging from $1 million into the $20 million plus range for the highest-end estates. ## Which community has better views? MacDonald Highlands is known for its hillside elevation and Strip views from many homes, which is the main reason buyers seek it out. Quail Ridge Estates is set on flatter ground with mature tree-lined streets rather than panoramic vistas. Buyers who prioritize dramatic views and modern hillside architecture lean toward MacDonald Highlands; buyers who want established neighborhood feel and shorter Strip commutes (3.5 miles, about 15 minutes) lean toward Quail Ridge Estates. Ryan Rose frequently tours both with luxury buyers weighing the tradeoff. ## What about price and home style? Quail Ridge Estates offers a lower price point of entry overall, with custom homes from 3,243 to 12,712 square feet on lots from 13,000 to over 30,000 square feet. The architecture reflects 1980s custom luxury, often updated through renovations. MacDonald Highlands skews newer and pricier, with ongoing new construction allowing buyers to build modern estates on view lots. The country club access at DragonRidge adds an amenity layer that Quail Ridge Estates does not match, while Quail Ridge counters with a clubhouse, tennis, basketball, and walking paths inside the gates. ## Local insight Ryan Rose frequently fields questions about Henderson hillside luxury versus established gated communities and explains that the decision usually comes down to views and golf access versus mature streetscape and value.
