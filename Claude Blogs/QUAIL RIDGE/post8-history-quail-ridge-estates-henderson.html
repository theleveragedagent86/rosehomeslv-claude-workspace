Quail Ridge Estates was built between 1982 and 1989 by American Nevada Corporation, the developer behind much of Green Valley. According to Henderson Luxury Residences, Quail Ridge Estates holds the distinction of being Henderson's first guard-gated custom home community. That distinction matters because it influenced how later Henderson neighborhoods were designed. The combination of half-acre custom lots, an active guard gate, and a private community clubhouse became a model that newer guard-gated developments would echo over the following decades. ## How has Quail Ridge Estates changed over time?
