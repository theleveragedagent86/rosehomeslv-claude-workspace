# Research Data: Quail Ridge (Quail Ridge Estates), Henderson NV

**Important context:** The most documented "Quail Ridge" community in the Las Vegas valley is **Quail Ridge Estates** in Henderson NV (Green Valley North area, ZIP 89014). It is a 101-home guard-gated luxury custom home community, NOT a typical mid-priced subdivision. There is also a Quail Ridge apartment community at 5292 S Maryland Pkwy, Las Vegas 89119 (UNLV district), which is unrelated. No documented stand-alone "Quail Ridge" master-planned community was found in Spring Valley or Summerlin. Research below focuses on Quail Ridge Estates, Henderson.

## Official Community Info
- HOA Website: https://quailridge.qualifiedproperty.com/ (Qualified Property Management portal listed for "Quail Ridge Estates HOA"); also referenced at https://www.nevada-homeowners-associations.com/ for board listings
- HOA Management: Qualified Property Management appears to host the resident portal (per qualifiedproperty.com subdomain). Direct management company name not definitively confirmed; verify with HOA directly. (source: https://quailridge.qualifiedproperty.com/)
- HOA Fees: Approximately $250 per month (sources: https://luxuryhomesofhenderson.com/neighborhoods/quail-ridge, https://www.lakelasvegasresidences.com/quail-ridge-homes-for-sale-henderson-12-acre-lots/). One reference to a $750 fee found, possibly quarterly. Verify with HOA directly.
- Guard Gate: Yes. 24-hour guard gated; described as "Henderson's first guard gated custom home community" (source: https://hendersonluxuryresidences.com/henderson-nv-guard-gated-custom-homes-quail-ridge/)
- Age Restrictions: None (all-ages community)
- Key Amenities: Community clubhouse, two tennis courts, basketball court, tree-lined streets, walking paths

## Real Estate Overview
- Price Range: Entry historically starts in upper $700s; current listings significantly higher. Median list price $1,774,950 as of March 2026 (4 active listings, average 41 days on market)
- Median Price: ~$1.77M (per active listings, March 2026; small sample of 4 homes)
- Home Styles: Custom-built luxury single-family estates; varied architectural styles; 4+ bedrooms, 3+ bathrooms typical; many with pools, mature landscaping, gourmet kitchens, expansive outdoor living
- Lot Sizes: 13,000 sq ft to ~30,000+ sq ft (roughly 1/3 acre to 3/4+ acre); commonly described as "half-acre lots"
- Year(s) Built: 1982 to 1989 (with many homes since renovated/updated)
- Builders: Originally developed by American Nevada Corporation; each home individually custom-built (no single builder)
- New Construction: No. Established mature community; 101 homesites all built out. Some homes have been torn down/rebuilt or extensively renovated
- Gated: Yes, fully guard-gated with 24-hour security

## Lifestyle & Location
- Drive to Strip: ~10-20 minutes (3.5 miles)
- Drive to Airport (Harry Reid International): ~10-15 minutes (~3 miles)
- Drive to Downtown Las Vegas: ~20-25 minutes
- Walk Score: Green Valley North area Walk Score ~37 (car-dependent); Quail Ridge Estates itself is described by Nextdoor residents as "walkable" within the neighborhood thanks to wide sidewalks and walking paths, but outside the gates it is car-dependent
- Nearest Hospital: Dignity Health St. Rose Dominican Hospital, Rose de Lima Campus (102 E Lake Mead Pkwy, ~145 beds); Siena Campus also serves area (3001 St Rose Pkwy, ~219 beds)
- Safety: Henderson named 2nd safest large city in US (Advisor Smith); Henderson overall crime rate 22.83 vs national 33.37; violent crime 3.05 vs national 13.32; property crime 19.78 vs national 20.05. Quail Ridge is guard-gated, adding security layer.
- Pet Friendly: Yes; mature trees, wide sidewalks, and walking paths inside the gates; Sunset Park dog runs nearby (separate large/small dog areas)
- Retirement Friendly: Suitable for retirees seeking luxury; not 55+ restricted; close to two St. Rose Dominican hospitals; quiet streets, low traffic, guard-gated security
- Notable: Henderson's first guard-gated custom home community; one of the earliest Green Valley developments; mature tree canopy; ~1,258 residents in the neighborhood per Nextdoor census reference
