Queensridge is an 800 acre, guard gated luxury community in the western Las Vegas valley, known for its European inspired architecture and resort style amenities. Often called "Little Europe," this prestigious neighborhood has attracted notable residents including Andre Agassi and Steffi Graf. ## Where Is Queensridge Located? Queensridge sits along the western edge of Las Vegas, bordered by Alta Drive to the north and West Charleston Boulevard to the south. The community is just minutes from Red Rock Canyon, Tivoli Village, and Downtown Summerlin. Residents enjoy a roughly 20 minute drive to the Las Vegas Strip and 20 to 25 minutes to Harry Reid International Airport. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is only 2.6 miles away. ## What Makes Queensridge Unique? Queensridge is divided into two guard gated sections: Queensridge North and Queensridge South, each with its own staffed entrance. The community features 24 hour roaming security, a clubhouse, fitness center, pools, spas, lighted tennis courts, greenbelts, and walking trails. Homes were built between 1996 and 2007 by builders like Christopher Homes, Executive Home Builders, and Pulte Homes. Styles range from custom single family estates to townhomes, tri level residences, and luxury high rise condos at One Queensridge Place. ## Who Lives in Queensridge? The community has a population of approximately 2,710 residents with a median age of 55 and an average household income of $107,388. There are no age restrictions, so Queensridge welcomes families, professionals, and retirees alike. Sub communities within Queensridge include Queensridge North, Queensridge South, One Queensridge Place, Tudor Park, Windsor, Fairway Pointe, and Fairway Homes. ## A Local Perspective As a Las Vegas real estate specialist, Ryan Rose considers Queensridge one of the most well rounded luxury communities in the valley.
