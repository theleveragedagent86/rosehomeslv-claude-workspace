Is Queensridge a Gated Community?

Yes, Queensridge is a fully guard gated community in Las Vegas with 24 hour security and controlled access at every entrance. This 800 acre neighborhood, located in the 89145 zip code, is one of the most well secured residential communities in the Las Vegas valley.

How Does the Queensridge Guard Gate Work?

Queensridge features separate guard gated entrances for its two main sections. Queensridge North is accessed from Alta Drive, while Queensridge South has its entrance along West Charleston Boulevard. Each gate is staffed around the clock by security personnel who verify every visitor before granting entry. Residents use electronic access credentials to pass through quickly.

Visitors must present identification and be confirmed by the resident they are visiting before security allows them through. Delivery drivers, contractors, and service providers follow the same verification process. This level of screening ensures that only authorized individuals enter the community at any time.

What Sub Communities Are Behind the Gates?

Several distinct neighborhoods operate within Queensridge's guard gated perimeter, including Queensridge North, Queensridge South, Tudor Park, Windsor at Queensridge, Fairway Pointe, and Fairway Homes. One Queensridge Place, the luxury condominium tower, adds its own additional layer of security with concierge service and private access controls. Built between 1996 and 2007, these sub communities share the overarching security infrastructure while maintaining their individual character.

How Does Queensridge Compare to Other Gated Communities?

Queensridge stands apart from many gated communities in Las Vegas because it combines guard staffed gates with 24 hour roaming security patrols throughout the neighborhood. Some gated communities rely solely on electronic gates without staffed checkpoints. Queensridge's approach provides a higher level of oversight, contributing to the A minus crime grade that the 89145 zip code maintains according to neighborhood safety data.
