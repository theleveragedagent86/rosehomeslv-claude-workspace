Queensridge Guard Gate and 24 Hour Security

Queensridge provides one of the most comprehensive security systems of any residential community in Las Vegas, with staffed guard gates at every entrance and 24 hour roaming patrols throughout the 800 acre property. This multilayered approach to safety is a defining feature that distinguishes Queensridge from communities that rely on electronic gates alone.

How Are the Guard Gates Staffed?

Queensridge operates two primary guard gated entrances. The North entrance is located on Alta Drive, and the South entrance sits along West Charleston Boulevard. Each gate is staffed with trained security personnel around the clock, every day of the year. Guards verify identification for all visitors, delivery drivers, and service providers before granting access.

Residents receive electronic credentials that allow efficient passage through the gates without stopping for manual verification each time. Guest lists can be submitted in advance so expected visitors experience a smooth entry process. Unannounced visitors are held at the gate until the resident they wish to see confirms authorization.

What Does 24 Hour Roaming Security Include?

Beyond the guard gates, Queensridge employs roaming security patrols that circulate through the community's streets continuously. These patrols monitor for unusual activity, enforce community rules, and serve as a visible deterrent. The combination of fixed gate positions and mobile patrols creates overlapping coverage that addresses both perimeter and interior security.

One Queensridge Place, the luxury condominium tower within the community, adds a further layer with its own concierge service and private access controls. Residents of the tower benefit from both the community wide security infrastructure and building specific protections including valet service and lobby monitoring.

What Is the Safety Record for the Queensridge Area?

The 89145 zip code, where Queensridge is located, carries an A minus crime grade according to neighborhood safety data. The community's proactive security measures contribute significantly to this rating. Located approximately 2.6 miles from Summerlin Hospital Medical Center, residents also have quick access to emergency medical services when needed.

Ryan Rose advises buyers that Queensridge's security infrastructure is funded through HOA assessments, currently $340 per month for the general community. This investment covers not only guard gate staffing and roaming patrols but also maintenance of the community's greenbelts, walking trails, and shared amenities.
