Queensridge Clubhouse and Amenities

Queensridge offers residents a full suite of resort style amenities, including a community clubhouse, fitness center, multiple pools, spas, lighted tennis courts, greenbelts, and walking trails. These shared facilities are a core reason families and professionals choose this 800 acre guard gated community in Las Vegas.

What Does the Queensridge Clubhouse Offer?

The Queensridge clubhouse serves as the social hub for the community, providing space for events, gatherings, and resident activities. Adjacent to the clubhouse, the fitness center is equipped for a full range of workouts, from cardio to strength training. Residents access these facilities as part of their HOA membership, which is currently $340 per month for the general community.

Multiple swimming pools and spas are positioned throughout the community, giving residents options close to their specific neighborhood. Lighted tennis courts accommodate both daytime and evening play, which is particularly valuable during Las Vegas summers when cooler evening hours are preferred for outdoor activity.

What Are the Outdoor Amenities at Queensridge?

Queensridge's greenbelts and walking trails wind through the community, connecting neighborhoods and providing scenic routes for exercise and leisure. The landscaped pathways offer views of the European inspired architecture that has earned Queensridge the nickname "Little Europe." These trails are well maintained and accessible year round.

The community's 800 acres provide generous spacing between homes, creating a sense of privacy even in shared outdoor areas. Mature landscaping throughout the greenbelts adds shade and visual appeal that newer communities have not yet achieved.

What Additional Amenities Does One Queensridge Place Provide?

Residents of One Queensridge Place, the luxury condominium tower within the community, enjoy an elevated tier of amenities. The building features a 25 seat private movie theater, a 13,000 plus square foot spa and gym facility, both indoor lap and outdoor pools, a wine cellar, poker room, billiards room, game room, barista cafe, catering kitchen, formal dining room, board room, library, concierge service, valet parking, and guest casitas. These exclusive amenities are funded through One Queensridge Place HOA fees, which range from $1,200 to $3,800 per month depending on unit size.

Ryan Rose recommends that buyers tour the amenity spaces in person, as photos rarely capture the full scope of what Queensridge provides. The combination of community wide facilities and the elevated offerings at One Queensridge Place creates a living experience that rivals the finest resort properties in Las Vegas.
