Queensridge Zip Code and Location Guide

Queensridge is located in the 89145 zip code in Las Vegas, Nevada, situated approximately 10 miles northwest of the Las Vegas Strip. This 800 acre guard gated community sits in one of the most desirable corridors of the valley, offering quick access to shopping, dining, healthcare, and outdoor recreation.

Where Exactly Is Queensridge in Las Vegas?

Queensridge is bordered by Alta Drive to the north and West Charleston Boulevard to the south, with Rampart Boulevard running along its eastern edge. The community occupies a prime position between the western edge of Las Vegas proper and the beginning of Summerlin. Queensridge North is accessed from Alta Drive, while Queensridge South has its guard gated entrance on West Charleston Boulevard.

The 89145 zip code encompasses Queensridge and the surrounding area, with a population of approximately 2,710 residents within the community itself. The median age is 55, and the average household income is $107,388, reflecting the established, affluent character of the neighborhood.

What Is Nearby Queensridge?

Shopping and dining are remarkably close. Tivoli Village, a European inspired outdoor shopping center with boutiques and restaurants, is roughly half a mile away. Boca Park shopping center sits adjacent to the community, providing everyday retail and grocery options. Downtown Summerlin, with its larger selection of stores, restaurants, and entertainment venues, is approximately three to five miles west.

Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is just 2.6 miles from Queensridge, providing emergency and specialty medical services. Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area is a 10 to 15 minute drive, offering world class hiking and scenic drives for outdoor enthusiasts.

What Are the Commute Times from Queensridge?

The Las Vegas Strip is roughly a 20 minute drive from Queensridge under typical traffic conditions. Harry Reid International Airport is approximately 20 to 25 minutes away, making travel convenient for frequent flyers. Downtown Las Vegas is about 20 minutes east. These commute times make Queensridge practical for professionals working in hospitality, entertainment, or any Strip corridor industry.
