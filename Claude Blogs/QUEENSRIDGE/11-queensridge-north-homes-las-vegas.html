Queensridge North is the northern section of the guard gated Queensridge community in west Las Vegas, accessible through a staffed entrance on Alta Drive. This enclave features custom and semi custom single family homes built between 1996 and 2007, primarily by Christopher Homes, and is recognized for its European inspired architecture and mature, tree lined streetscapes. ## What Defines Queensridge North? Queensridge North occupies a significant portion of the 800 acre Queensridge master plan. The neighborhood is entirely guard gated with 24 hour roaming security patrols. Homes here showcase a variety of European architectural styles, including French, English, and Italian influences, which earned the broader Queensridge community the nickname "Little Europe." Lot sizes tend to be generous, with many properties backing up to lush greenbelts and walking trails. ## Home Sizes and Price Ranges Single family homes in Queensridge North typically range from approximately 3,000 to over 10,000 square feet. Prices vary based on lot size, finishes, and location within the section, generally starting around $800,000 and reaching well above $5,000,000 for larger custom estates. The overall Queensridge community averages roughly $473 per square foot in resale transactions, with a median sale price near $1,080,000 to $1,250,000. ## Who Built Queensridge North Homes? Christopher Homes served as the primary builder for much of Queensridge North, constructing custom and semi custom residences during the community's development years of 1996 through 2007. These homes feature high end finishes, vaulted ceilings, and open floor plans designed for both daily living and entertaining. Many properties include private pools, courtyards, and three car garages. ## Community Amenities and Location Queensridge North residents have full access to shared community amenities, including the Queensridge clubhouse, fitness center, swimming pools, spas, lighted tennis courts, and walking trails. The neighborhood is approximately 20 minutes from the Las Vegas Strip and 20 to 25 minutes from Harry Reid International Airport. Shopping and dining at Tivoli Village and Downtown Summerlin are just a short drive away. ## Is Queensridge North Right for You?
