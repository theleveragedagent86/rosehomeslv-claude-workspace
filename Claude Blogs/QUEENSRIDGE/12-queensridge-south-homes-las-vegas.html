Queensridge South is the southern section of the prestigious Queensridge community in west Las Vegas, with its guard gated entrance located on West Charleston Boulevard. Like its northern counterpart, Queensridge South features custom and semi custom single family homes built primarily by Christopher Homes, set among European inspired streetscapes and lush landscaping. ## How Does Queensridge South Differ from Queensridge North? While both sections share the same overarching community amenities and architectural standards, Queensridge South has its own dedicated guard gated entrance on West Charleston Boulevard. This gives residents direct access to the Charleston corridor, which connects to Summerlin, Downtown Las Vegas, and the 215 Beltway. Queensridge North, by contrast, is accessed from Alta Drive. Each section operates with its own staffed security gate and 24 hour roaming patrols. ## Home Styles and Pricing Homes in Queensridge South range from semi custom residences around 3,000 square feet to expansive custom estates exceeding 8,000 square feet. Architectural styles draw from French, English, and Italian traditions, consistent with the "Little Europe" character that defines the broader community. Pricing in Queensridge South generally starts near $750,000 for smaller resale homes and can exceed $5,000,000 for premium properties. Community wide, the average sale price sits around $1,882,280 at roughly $473 per square foot. ## Builder History Christopher Homes was the primary developer for Queensridge South, constructing residences during the community's build out from 1996 through 2007. These homes are known for quality craftsmanship, open layouts, and upgrades such as marble flooring, chef's kitchens, and resort style backyards with pools and outdoor living areas. ## Proximity and Convenience Queensridge South residents benefit from quick access to West Charleston Boulevard, placing Tivoli Village, Downtown Summerlin, and Red Rock Canyon within a short drive. The Las Vegas Strip is approximately 20 minutes east, and Harry Reid International Airport is reachable in 20 to 25 minutes. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is roughly 2.6 miles from the community. ## Community Amenities All Queensridge South homeowners enjoy access to the shared community clubhouse, fitness center, pools, spas, lighted tennis courts, greenbelts, and walking trails. The Queensridge Owners Association manages the common areas and enforces the community's architectural guidelines. ## Considering Queensridge South? Queensridge South appeals to buyers who want guard gated luxury with convenient Charleston Boulevard access. The combination of established landscaping, quality construction, and proximity to west Las Vegas dining and retail makes this section a strong option for both families and professionals.
