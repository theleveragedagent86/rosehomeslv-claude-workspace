One Queensridge Place is a twin tower luxury high rise condominium within the Queensridge community in west Las Vegas. Built between 2006 and 2007 by Executive Home Builders, the towers stand approximately 233.5 feet tall and contain 219 residences across 43 distinct floor plans. Units range from 2,100 to 16,000 square feet, making this one of the most expansive condo offerings in the Las Vegas valley. ## Tower Design and Unit Sizes The two towers at One Queensridge Place were designed to provide a single family home experience in a vertical format. Floor plans start at 2,100 square feet for smaller residences and extend to 16,000 square feet for full floor penthouses. Many units feature floor to ceiling windows, private terraces, gourmet kitchens, and custom finishes selected by original buyers during the construction phase. The 43 available floor plans ensure significant variety within the towers. ## HOA Fees and What They Cover Monthly HOA fees at One Queensridge Place range from approximately $1,200 to $3,800, depending on unit size and location within the towers. These fees cover an extensive list of services and amenities, including concierge, valet parking, 24 hour security, building maintenance, water, sewer, trash removal, and access to all shared facilities. The HOA structure reflects the full service nature of the property. ## Resort Level Amenities One Queensridge Place offers amenities that rival many Las Vegas resorts. The property includes a 25 seat private movie theater, a 13,000 plus square foot spa and fitness center, indoor and outdoor swimming pools, a wine cellar, poker room, billiards room, barista cafe, and guest casitas for visiting friends and family. Concierge and valet services operate around the clock. ## Pricing and Market Position Condos at One Queensridge Place typically list from the high $500,000s for smaller units to well over $5,000,000 for penthouses and premium floor positions. The community wide average of $473 per square foot provides a general benchmark, though high rise units with Strip or mountain views often command a premium. One Queensridge Place remains one of the most recognizable luxury condo addresses in Las Vegas. ## Location Within Queensridge The towers sit within the broader 800 acre Queensridge community, giving residents access to the guard gated security, greenbelts, and walking trails shared by all Queensridge homeowners.
