Queensridge luxury homes in Las Vegas represent some of the finest residential properties in the valley, with custom estates ranging from roughly $1 million to over $16 million. Built between 1996 and 2007 within an 800 acre guard gated community, these homes feature European inspired architecture, generous lot sizes up to half an acre, and direct access to resort caliber amenities. ## What Defines a Luxury Home in Queensridge? Luxury properties in Queensridge are distinguished by their custom and semi custom construction, oversized lots, and architectural detail that draws from European design traditions. The community earned the nickname "Little Europe" for this reason. Homes in Queensridge North and Queensridge South were designed as one of a kind residences, with many featuring private courtyards, pool areas, and expansive views of the western Las Vegas valley and Spring Mountains. ## What Price Ranges and Sizes Are Available? Queensridge luxury homes range from approximately 3,000 square feet for smaller custom residences to over 21,000 square feet for the largest estates. The median sale price across the community falls between $1,080,000 and $1,250,000, while premier estates on premium lots command upwards of $10 million to $16 million. The average price per square foot is roughly $473, which reflects the quality of materials and craftsmanship throughout the community. ## Who Built Queensridge's Luxury Homes? Christopher Homes served as the original master developer for Queensridge, establishing the community's standard for luxury custom construction. Executive Home Builders constructed the twin towers at One Queensridge Place, and Pulte Homes built the Tudor Park residences. Many original homes have undergone significant renovations and upgrades over the years, keeping interiors current while preserving the classic exterior character. ## What Amenities Do Luxury Homeowners Enjoy? Queensridge residents have access to a clubhouse, fitness center, multiple pools and spas, lighted tennis courts, greenbelts, and walking trails. The community is fully guard gated with 24 hour roaming security and staffed entrances at both the north and south sections. Notable past and present residents include Andre Agassi and Steffi Graf, underscoring the community's reputation among discerning buyers. ## Ryan Rose's Perspective Ryan Rose considers Queensridge one of the premier luxury destinations in Las Vegas for buyers who want established, quality construction paired with genuine neighborhood privacy. The mature landscaping, proximity to Tivoli Village and Red Rock Canyon, and the variety of home styles make it a compelling alternative to newer luxury communities.
