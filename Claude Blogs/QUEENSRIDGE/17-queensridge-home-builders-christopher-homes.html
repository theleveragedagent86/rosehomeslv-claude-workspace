Christopher Homes was the original master developer of Queensridge, establishing the community's reputation for luxury custom construction when building began in 1996. Christopher Homes is a Las Vegas based luxury home builder known for developing some of the valley's most prestigious communities. In Queensridge, Christopher Homes set the architectural tone by designing and building custom single family estates with European inspired exteriors, premium materials, and generous lot sizes up to half an acre. Their work in Queensridge North and Queensridge South established the community's "Little Europe" identity that continues to define the neighborhood today. ## What Other Builders Worked in Queensridge? Beyond Christopher Homes, two other major builders shaped the community. Executive Home Builders constructed One Queensridge Place, the twin luxury high rise towers completed in 2006 and 2007. Pulte Homes built Tudor Park, an English Village style sub community with three floor plans ranging from 2,535 to 2,816 square feet, featuring distinctive tri level layouts with master lofts. ## What About New Construction? Lennar Homes is developing new construction on the former Badlands golf course site adjacent to Queensridge. This project represents the first significant new residential development near the community in nearly two decades.
