Windsor at Queensridge is a gated community of free standing single family homes located at the northeast corner of the Queensridge master plan in west Las Vegas. As one of several distinct sub communities within the 800 acre Queensridge development, Windsor offers its own private entrance while providing full access to the broader Queensridge amenities and guard gated security. ## Location Within Queensridge Windsor sits at the northeast portion of Queensridge, near the Alta Drive entrance to the community. This positioning places Windsor residents close to Tivoli Village, Downtown Summerlin, and the restaurants and shops along Rampart Boulevard. The Las Vegas Strip is approximately 20 minutes away, and Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is just 2.6 miles from the community. ## Home Characteristics Windsor at Queensridge features detached single family residences built during the community's primary development period of 1996 through 2007. Homes in Windsor are characterized by the same European inspired architectural style found throughout Queensridge, with well maintained landscaping and private yards. Floor plans in Windsor generally fall in the mid range for the overall community, offering comfortable square footage for families and professionals who want a single family home without the scale of the larger custom estates found elsewhere in Queensridge. ## Community Access and Security Like all Queensridge sub communities, Windsor benefits from the master community's 24 hour guard gated security and roaming patrols. Windsor also has its own gated entry, adding an additional layer of privacy. Residents have full access to the Queensridge clubhouse, fitness center, swimming pools, spas, lighted tennis courts, greenbelts, and walking trails. ## How Windsor Compares to Other Queensridge Sub Communities Queensridge includes multiple sub community options at different price points and property types. Windsor's single family homes occupy a middle tier between the more compact Fairway Pointe townhomes (starting around $500,000) and the expansive custom estates that can exceed $5,000,000. For buyers who want a traditional single family layout within a guard gated setting, Windsor provides a focused option without the maintenance demands of a larger estate property. Other Queensridge sub communities include Queensridge North, Queensridge South, Tudor Park, Fairway Pointe, and One Queensridge Place condominiums. ## Is Windsor at Queensridge Right for You? Windsor appeals to buyers seeking the security, amenities, and prestige of the Queensridge address in a manageable single family home format. The gated within gated setup provides extra privacy, while the northeast location offers convenient access to surrounding retail and dining.
