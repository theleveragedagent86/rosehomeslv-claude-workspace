Queensridge accommodates a wider range of buyers than many people expect. While the community is known for its multi million dollar custom estates, entry level options exist through townhomes and condominiums that start around $500,000. This guide breaks down what first time Queensridge buyers and luxury buyers can expect at different price tiers within the 800 acre guard gated community. ## Entry Level Options: $500,000 to $1,000,000 Buyers entering Queensridge at this level typically look at two sub communities. Fairway Pointe offers attached townhomes ranging from 1,400 to 1,732 square feet, with prices starting around $500,000. These homes provide a maintenance friendly lifestyle with access to all Queensridge amenities, including the clubhouse, fitness center, pools, and 24 hour guard gated security. One Queensridge Place, the luxury high rise within the community, also has smaller units that can fall in this range, offering a condominium lifestyle with panoramic Las Vegas views. ## Mid Range Options: $1,000,000 to $3,000,000 The mid range captures a large portion of available Queensridge inventory. This tier includes single family homes in Queensridge North, Queensridge South, Windsor, and Tudor Park. Tudor Park homes are tri level residences ranging from 2,535 to 2,816 square feet, built by Pulte Homes. Windsor features detached single family homes at the northeast corner of the community. Buyers in this range can expect European inspired architecture, private yards, and generous lot sizes with mature landscaping. ## Luxury and Estate Level: $3,000,000 and Above At the upper end, Queensridge is home to custom estates built by Christopher Homes and Executive Home Builders. These properties range from 5,000 to over 21,000 square feet and can reach $16,000,000 or more. Features commonly include private pools, motor courts, home theaters, wine cellars, and expansive outdoor living areas. One Queensridge Place penthouse units also compete at this price tier, with residences up to 16,000 square feet. ## Key Differences for Each Buyer Type First time Queensridge buyers benefit from starting with a townhome or smaller condo, gaining access to the guard gated lifestyle and community amenities without the carrying costs of a large estate. HOA fees cover security, grounds maintenance, and shared facilities. Luxury buyers, by contrast, prioritize customization, lot size, and architectural uniqueness, and should expect higher HOA contributions proportional to property size and services. ## What Both Buyer Types Share Regardless of price point, every Queensridge resident benefits from the same 24 hour security, guard gated access, and community amenities. The location advantages are identical: roughly 20 minutes to the Strip, proximity to Tivoli Village and Downtown Summerlin, and 2.6 miles to Summerlin Hospital. The community ranks among the top 15% highest income neighborhoods in America.
