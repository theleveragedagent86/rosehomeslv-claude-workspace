Queensridge residents have access to one of the strongest restaurant corridors in western Las Vegas. Within a mile of the community gates, Tivoli Village and Boca Park Fashion Village anchor the dining scene with dozens of options spanning steakhouses, Italian, Mexican, Asian fusion, and American comfort food. This guide covers the standout restaurants closest to Queensridge, organized by location. ## Tivoli Village Restaurants Tivoli Village sits roughly half a mile east of Queensridge along S. Rampart Blvd. and houses several of the area's top restaurants. Echo and Rig is a modern steakhouse with an in house butcher shop, serving wood fired steaks and a popular weekend brunch. Al Solito Posto, led by Chef James Trees, offers refined Italian classics and a Wine Spectator award winning cellar in an upscale casual setting. Brio Italian Grille serves Tuscan inspired cuisine with wood fired flatbreads and house made pasta. El Dorado Cantina specializes in organic, non GMO Mexican cuisine and was named Best Mexican Restaurant in Las Vegas by the Review Journal. Kabuki rounds out the village with Japanese dining and sushi. ## Restaurants on Rampart Blvd. Directly across from Tivoli Village, Honey Salt at 1031 S. Rampart Blvd. is a farm to table restaurant from Elizabeth Blau and Chef Kim Canteenwalla, known for seasonal menus and a strong happy hour. North Italia, at 1069 S. Rampart Blvd., serves handmade pastas, pizzas, and craft cocktails in a polished setting. Both are under a two minute drive from Queensridge. ## Boca Park Fashion Village Boca Park sits adjacent to Queensridge and adds several popular chains and independents. Grimaldi's Pizzeria serves coal fired brick oven pizza in the style of the original Brooklyn location. Kona Grill offers Asian American fusion with a full sushi bar. The Cheesecake Factory provides a broad American menu in a high volume, family friendly format. ## Quick Reference Tivoli Village (0.5 miles): Echo and Rig, Al Solito Posto, Brio Italian Grille, El Dorado Cantina, Kabuki, PKWY Tavern. Rampart Blvd. (0.5 miles): Honey Salt, North Italia. Boca Park (0.5 miles): Grimaldi's, Kona Grill, The Cheesecake Factory. ## Why the Location Matters For homebuyers evaluating Queensridge, the walkable and drivable proximity to this dining cluster is a meaningful lifestyle factor. Few guard gated communities in Las Vegas offer this density of quality restaurants within a one mile radius.
