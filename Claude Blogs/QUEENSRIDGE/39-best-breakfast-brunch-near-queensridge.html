Queensridge residents looking for breakfast and brunch have several strong options within minutes of the community. From longstanding family owned spots to upscale weekend brunch destinations, the surrounding area offers enough variety that locals rarely need to venture far. Here are the top breakfast and brunch restaurants near Queensridge. ## Jamms Restaurant Jamms is a family owned breakfast and lunch spot at 1029 S. Rainbow Blvd., roughly one mile from Queensridge. Established in 1997, the restaurant has built a loyal following with its signature Pot of Bread, served hot at the table in rotating flavors like cinnamon raisin, chocolate chip banana, and parmesan artichoke. The menu features omelets, skillets, stuffed French toast filled with warm cream cheese and apple pie filling, chicken and waffles, and hearty breakfast burritos. Portions are generous and prices are reasonable, which explains its consistent ranking among the top breakfast spots in Las Vegas. Hours are Monday through Friday from 7:30 a.m. to 2:00 p.m. and weekends from 7:00 a.m. to 2:30 p.m. ## The Bagel Cafe The Bagel Cafe at 301 N. Buffalo Dr. is a New York style deli and bakery about one mile north of Queensridge. The kitchen bakes over 20 flavors of bagels and bialys daily, hand whips a dozen cream cheese varieties, and cures its own corned beef, brisket, and pastrami in house. Smoked fish is sliced to order. Beyond the deli counter, the full menu covers breakfast classics, sandwiches, soups, and dinner entrees. Hours run Monday from 7:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m., Tuesday through Friday from 7:00 a.m. to 8:00 p.m., and weekends from 7:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. ## Honey Salt Weekend Brunch Honey Salt at 1031 S. Rampart Blvd. serves brunch every Saturday and Sunday from 10:00 a.m. to 2:30 p.m. Chef Kim Canteenwalla and restaurateur Elizabeth Blau designed a brunch menu that reflects the same farm to table philosophy as their dinner offerings. The setting is polished, the cocktails are well crafted, and the proximity to Queensridge (under half a mile) makes it a convenient option for a weekend morning. ## Echo and Rig Weekend Brunch Echo and Rig at Tivoli Village offers Saturday and Sunday brunch from 10:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m. The brunch menu features fresh pressed juices, small plates, and heartier entrees that complement the restaurant's signature steakhouse style. The Tivoli Village setting adds to the experience, making it a popular spot for Queensridge residents hosting visiting family or friends. ## El Dorado Cantina Brunch El Dorado Cantina at 430 S. Rampart Blvd. in Tivoli Village runs a weekend brunch on Saturdays and Sundays from 10:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m. with bottomless mimosas and bloody marys. The organic, non GMO focused menu extends to the brunch offerings, giving guests a Mexican inspired alternative to the traditional American brunch format.
