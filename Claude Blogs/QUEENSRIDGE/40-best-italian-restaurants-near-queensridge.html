Queensridge residents have several Italian restaurants within a short drive, ranging from refined dining rooms with award winning wine programs to casual pizzerias serving coal fired pies. Here are the top Italian restaurants closest to Queensridge. ## Al Solito Posto Al Solito Posto at 420 S. Rampart Blvd. in Tivoli Village is the standout Italian restaurant nearest to Queensridge. Chef Partner James Trees runs an open kitchen turning out refined classics like rigatoni alla vodka, chicken parmesan, and Sunday gravy. The restaurant earned recognition from Robb Report, Thrillist, USA Today, and TIME magazine, which named it among the World's Greatest Places in 2021. The wine cellar holds a Wine Spectator award, and the Negroni Bar offers a range of Italian aperitifs and barrel aged cocktails. Hours are Sunday through Thursday from 4:00 p.m. to 10:00 p.m. and Friday through Saturday from 4:00 p.m. to 11:00 p.m. Reservations are recommended. ## North Italia North Italia at 1069 S. Rampart Blvd. sits less than half a mile from Queensridge and serves handmade pastas, hand tossed pizzas, and seasonal entrees in a modern, upscale casual setting. The kitchen makes pasta fresh daily, and the chef's daily pizza specials rotate based on seasonal ingredients. Happy hour runs Monday through Thursday from 3:00 p.m. to 6:00 p.m. The restaurant is open Monday through Friday from 11:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. with brunch available on weekends. ## Brio Italian Grille Brio Italian Grille occupies a prominent space in Tivoli Village at 420 S. Rampart Blvd. The menu draws from Tuscan traditions, featuring wood fired flatbreads, house made pastas, premium steaks, and seafood. An authentic Italian wood burning oven anchors the kitchen. The restaurant is open daily from 11:00 a.m. to 11:00 p.m., with brunch on Saturdays and Sundays. Happy hour runs Monday through Friday from 3:00 p.m. to 6:30 p.m. The setting includes outdoor dining, white tablecloths, and a full bar. ## Grimaldi's Pizzeria Grimaldi's Pizzeria in Boca Park Fashion Village at 750 S. Rampart Blvd. brings coal fired brick oven pizza to the Queensridge area. Dough is made fresh daily and hand tossed before being baked in a signature coal fired oven, producing the crisp yet chewy crust with smoky char that made the original Brooklyn location famous. Hours are Sunday through Thursday from 11:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. and Friday through Saturday from 11:00 a.m. to 11:00 p.m. ## Giordano's Giordano's, also in Boca Park, serves Chicago style deep dish pizza for residents who prefer a thicker, stuffed pie.
