During peak morning and evening rush hours, the drive may extend to 25 or 30 minutes depending on which section of the Strip you are targeting. For early morning flights, travel time can drop below 20 minutes. Evening and weekend departures during convention season may push drive time closer to 30 minutes. ## Downtown Las Vegas Access Downtown Las Vegas, including the Fremont Street district and the Arts District, is roughly 20 minutes from Queensridge.
