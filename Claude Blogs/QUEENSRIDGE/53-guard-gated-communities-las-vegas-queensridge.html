Las Vegas has a significant number of guard gated communities, particularly in the western and southern portions of the valley. For buyers seeking controlled access, 24 hour security, and a private residential setting, Queensridge is one of the most established options. Here is how it compares to other guard gated neighborhoods in the area. ## What Guard Gated Means in Las Vegas A guard gated community staffs its entry points with live security personnel who verify every visitor before granting access. This is distinct from "gated" communities that rely only on automated gates or key fob entry. Guard gated communities typically also employ roaming security patrols within the neighborhood, monitor common areas, and maintain strict visitor registration protocols. In Las Vegas, guard gated status is a significant differentiator in the luxury market. ## Queensridge Security Infrastructure Queensridge operates two staffed guard gates. The north entrance is located on Alta Drive and the south entrance on West Charleston Boulevard. Both gates are staffed around the clock. In addition to gate personnel, 24 hour roaming security patrols cover the community's interior streets and common areas. Residents register visitors in advance, and unannounced guests are verified before entry. This layered approach to security is one of the primary reasons buyers choose Queensridge. ## Other Guard Gated Communities in Las Vegas Several other communities in the valley offer guard gated living at a comparable level. **The Ridges** is a guard gated village within the Summerlin master plan, situated along the western edge near Red Rock Canyon. Homes in The Ridges tend to start above $1 million and feature modern desert architecture on larger lots. **MacDonald Highlands** in Henderson offers elevated lots with views of the Strip and surrounding mountains. The DragonRidge Country Club sits within this community, and home prices range from the high six figures into the multi millions. **Southern Highlands** is a large guard gated community in the southern valley with a private golf course, multiple parks, and a range of price points from the mid $400,000s to over $2 million. **Tournament Hills** and **Eagle Hills** are smaller guard gated pockets within Summerlin that offer more modest pricing while still providing staffed entry and controlled access. **Canyon Gate** is one of the older guard gated communities in Las Vegas, located near the intersection of Sahara and Fort Apache, with a private golf course and homes generally in the $500,000 to $900,000 range. ## How Queensridge Stands Out Queensridge distinguishes itself through a combination of location, maturity, and price point. Its position in western Las Vegas provides quick access to Red Rock Canyon, Tivoli Village, and the Summerlin corridor. The community was developed by the Peccole family and includes approximately 800 acres of single family homes, townhomes, and the One Queensridge Place luxury towers. Median home prices range from $1.08 million to $1.25 million, and HOA dues of approximately $340 per month cover the full security operation along with landscaping and amenities.
