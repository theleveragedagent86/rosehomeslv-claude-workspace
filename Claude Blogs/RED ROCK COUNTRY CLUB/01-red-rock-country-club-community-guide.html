Red Rock Country Club is a 735 acre guard gated luxury community in the heart of Summerlin, Las Vegas, known for its two Arnold Palmer designed championship golf courses, resort style amenities, and custom estate homes. With approximately 1,000 residences built between 1999 and 2006, this master planned enclave remains one of the most prestigious addresses in Southern Nevada. ## What Makes Red Rock Country Club Unique? Red Rock Country Club stands apart as the only golf course community in Las Vegas featuring two Arnold Palmer designed courses: the Arroyo Course (open to the public) and the Mountain Course (private, members only). The community spans several distinctive subdivisions, including The Estates, The Vistas, Eagle Hills, Canyon Fairways, and Falcon Ridge. Home sizes range from 1,938 to over 10,780 square feet, with lot sizes between 0.15 and 0.87 acres. Builders like Toll Brothers, Sunrise Colony Company, and Merlin Custom Home Builders crafted the original residences. ## What Amenities Does Red Rock Country Club Offer? Residents enjoy access to a 44,000 square foot Main Clubhouse and a 9,500 square foot Sports Clubhouse. The amenity package includes nine lighted tennis courts, four pickleball courts, three swimming pools, a therapeutic spa, a mini water park, and a full fitness center. Three on site dining venues, Tot Stop childcare services, walking trails, and biking paths round out the lifestyle offerings. Two 24 hour guard gated entrances with video surveillance and transponder based access ensure privacy and security throughout the community. ## Where Is Red Rock Country Club Located? Red Rock Country Club sits in the western Summerlin corridor, just minutes from Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area. The Las Vegas Strip is roughly 20 minutes by car, and Harry Reid International Airport is about 18 minutes away. Downtown Summerlin and Tivoli Village offer premier shopping and dining within a short drive. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is approximately 10 minutes from the community gates. ## Ryan Rose's Local Perspective As a Las Vegas real estate expert who has helped buyers navigate Red Rock Country Club for years, Ryan Rose considers this community one of the best values in luxury living across the valley.
