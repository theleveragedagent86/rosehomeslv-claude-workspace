The Arroyo Golf Club at Red Rock Country Club is an Arnold Palmer Signature public golf course that gives every Las Vegas golfer access to world class links in the heart of Summerlin. ## What Is the Arroyo Golf Club at Red Rock Country Club? The Arroyo Golf Club is a 6,883 yard, par 72 public course designed by Arnold Palmer and Ed Seay. Built in 2003, it features Bermuda grass fairways and Bentgrass greens with a links style layout that ribbons through the rugged desert terrain surrounding Red Rock Canyon. Unlike the private Mountain Course next door, the Arroyo is open to daily fee players year round. The course sits within the Red Rock Country Club community in Summerlin, but it operates independently for public access. Golfers enjoy panoramic views of the Las Vegas cityscape and the towering sandstone bluffs of Red Rock Canyon from nearly every hole. Hole number seven is especially popular for its sweeping vista of the entire valley. ## What Facilities Does the Arroyo Golf Club Offer? The Arroyo has its own dedicated clubhouse built specifically for the daily fee player. Inside you will find a full service golf shop, men's and women's locker rooms, and a bar and grill with terrace dining overlooking the course. This separation from the private club side means public guests receive their own premium experience without sharing space with the country club membership. Green fees vary by season, with peak rates during the cooler months from October through May when Las Vegas golf demand is highest. Tee times can be booked online or by phone, and the course accommodates players of all skill levels thanks to multiple tee placements on every hole. ## Why Local Golfers Choose the Arroyo As a real estate expert who works with buyers in the Red Rock Country Club community, Ryan Rose often hears that the Arroyo Golf Club is one of the top reasons people choose to live in this part of Summerlin.
