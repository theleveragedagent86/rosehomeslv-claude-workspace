The Mountain Course at Red Rock Country Club is a private, members only Arnold Palmer designed championship layout stretching over 7,001 yards through some of the most dramatic desert scenery in Las Vegas. ## What Makes the Mountain Course at Red Rock Country Club Special? Designed by Arnold Palmer and Ed Seay, the Mountain Course opened in 1999 as the flagship private course for the Red Rock Country Club community. At 7,001 yards with a par of 72, the course carries a rating of 72.3 and a slope of 130, placing it among the more challenging private layouts in the Las Vegas valley. Five tee placements on every hole allow members of all skill levels to find a comfortable yet competitive round. The design emphasizes elevation changes, strategic bunkering, and narrow fairways that demand accuracy off the tee. Multi tiered greens add complexity to approach shots, while dramatic water features and native desert landscaping frame each hole. The backdrop of Red Rock Canyon's sandstone formations creates a setting that few private clubs anywhere in the country can rival. ## Who Can Play the Mountain Course? Access to the Mountain Course is restricted to Red Rock Country Club members and their accompanied guests. Golf members receive waived green fees and can book tee times up to 14 days in advance. Executive Golf members also have course access, though green fees apply and advance booking is limited to five days. Sports, Tennis, and Social Dining members do not have golf privileges on this course. The club also hosts member tournaments, league play, and social golf events throughout the year. An onsite driving range with six practice tees gives members a dedicated space to work on their game before heading to the first tee. ## A Real Estate Advantage for Golf Lovers Ryan Rose sees firsthand how the Mountain Course influences home values within Red Rock Country Club.
