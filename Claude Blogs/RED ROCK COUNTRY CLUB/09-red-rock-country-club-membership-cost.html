Golf Membership sits at the top tier with an initiation fee of $40,000 to $45,000 and monthly dues of $1,050 to $1,100. Golf members receive waived green fees on both Arnold Palmer designed courses, 14 day advance tee time reservations, and full access to all club amenities including the 44,000 square foot Main Clubhouse, three pools, fitness center, and all dining venues. Executive Golf Membership offers course access at a lower entry point: $25,000 to $30,000 initiation with $730 to $770 in monthly dues. Green fees still apply, and advance tee time booking is limited to five days. Executive members enjoy full access to the Sports Clubhouse, restaurants, and all social events. ## What Are the Non Golf Membership Options? Sports Membership requires a $10,000 initiation fee and $430 to $440 per month. Members gain access to nine tennis courts, four pickleball courts, the Sports Clubhouse, dining venues, and club social events. This tier suits residents who want the full athletic club experience without golf. Tennis and Pickleball Membership has the lowest athletic tier entry at $4,000 initiation and $295 to $300 monthly. Members can use all court facilities and attend social events, with dining access at most venues. Social Dining Membership is the most accessible option at $1,500 initiation and $225 to $235 per month, providing private restaurant access and event participation.
