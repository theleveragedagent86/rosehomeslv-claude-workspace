Red Rock Country Club is a private, guard gated residential community in Summerlin with a separate private country club, though not all residents are required to hold club membership. ## Is Red Rock Country Club a Private Community? Yes, Red Rock Country Club is a private, guard gated community with two 24 hour staffed entrances that control all vehicle and pedestrian access. The neighborhood spans approximately 735 acres and includes roughly 1,000 homes built between 1999 and 2006. Every resident pays HOA fees of around $250 per month, which cover security, landscaping, and common area maintenance through the Red Rock Country Club Homeowners Association. The residential community and the country club operate as two distinct entities. Owning a home inside the gates does not automatically include club membership, and club membership is not required to purchase property. This structure gives buyers the flexibility to enjoy the security and prestige of the guard gated neighborhood while choosing whether or not to invest in country club amenities. ## What Parts of Red Rock Country Club Are Open to the Public? The Arroyo Golf Club is the one major amenity accessible to non residents and non members. This Arnold Palmer Signature public course operates its own clubhouse with a golf shop, locker rooms, and bar and grill. Daily fee players can book tee times and enjoy a championship course experience without any membership commitment. Everything else within the club requires membership: the private Mountain Course, the 44,000 square foot Main Clubhouse, nine tennis courts, four pickleball courts, three swimming pools, fitness center, spa, and all dining venues. Five membership tiers range from Social Dining at $1,500 initiation up to full Golf Membership at $40,000 to $45,000, giving residents options that match their lifestyle priorities. ## How Privacy Affects Home Values at Red Rock Ryan Rose consistently sees that the private, guard gated nature of Red Rock Country Club supports strong resale values. Buyers in this price range prioritize security, exclusivity, and controlled community standards, all of which the dual gate system delivers. The optional membership model also broadens the buyer pool, since the community attracts both avid golfers and families who simply want a prestigious Summerlin address behind guarded gates.
