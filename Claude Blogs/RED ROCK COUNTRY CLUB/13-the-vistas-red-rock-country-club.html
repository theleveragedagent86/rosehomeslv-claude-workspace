The Vistas occupies some of the highest terrain within Red Rock Country Club, giving homeowners sweeping views of the Spring Mountains, Red Rock Canyon, and the Las Vegas Strip. With only 74 properties in the subdivision, this is one of the more intimate enclaves in the community. Lot sizes range from 0.23 to 0.39 acres, providing generous space for custom outdoor living while maintaining a cohesive neighborhood feel. Like The Estates, The Vistas features its own secondary gated entrance beyond the community's two main 24 hour guard gates.
