Canyon Fairways earns its name from its positioning directly along the golf course corridors at Red Rock Country Club. Homeowners in this subdivision wake up to manicured fairway views, with many properties backing directly onto the course. The combination of golf course frontage and mountain backdrop creates some of the most desirable homesites in the entire 735 acre community. Like all Red Rock Country Club subdivisions, Canyon Fairways is protected by the community's two 24 hour guard gated entrances. Residents benefit from transponder based access, video surveillance, and regular security patrols. The subdivision's location along the fairways also means minimal neighboring development behind the homes, preserving open sightlines and a sense of space that few properties can offer. ## What Are Canyon Fairways Homes Like?
