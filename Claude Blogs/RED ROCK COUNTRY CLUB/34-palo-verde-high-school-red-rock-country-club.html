Palo Verde High School enrolls approximately 3,141 students and operates on a 4x4 block schedule within the Clark County School District. SchoolDigger awards the school 4 out of 5 stars, and it ranks in the top 30 percent of all Nevada schools for overall test scores. The school earned its International Baccalaureate World School designation, giving students access to a globally recognized college preparatory curriculum. Proficiency rates at Palo Verde exceed state averages across both core subjects. Approximately 35 percent of students reach proficiency in math, compared to the Nevada average of about 28 percent. In reading and language arts, 61 percent score proficient or above, outpacing the statewide benchmark of 44 percent. Students enrolled in IB courses often achieve higher individual scores and earn college credit before graduation. ## What Programs and Activities Does Palo Verde Offer? Palo Verde's course catalog spans Advanced Placement, International Baccalaureate, Honors, and career focused electives. The school supports extracurricular programs ranging from competitive athletics to performing arts and academic clubs. Students attend on an alternating block schedule, allowing eight credits per school year. The campus is located at 333 South Pavilion Center Drive, Las Vegas, NV 89144, just minutes from the Red Rock Country Club gates.
