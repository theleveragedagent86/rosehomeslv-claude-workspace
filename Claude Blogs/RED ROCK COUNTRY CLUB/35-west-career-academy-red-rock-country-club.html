This public magnet school in Summerlin offers nine specialized career programs for students in grades 9 through 12. ## What Programs Does West Career and Technical Academy Offer? West Career and Technical Academy (WCTA) enrolls approximately 1,511 students and operates as a magnet school within the Clark County School District. The school offers nine distinct programs of study: Biomedical Sciences, Biotechnology, Business Management, Cybersecurity, Digital Art and Design, Engineering, Environmental Sciences, Nursing Assistant, and Sports Medicine. Each program provides hands on training alongside rigorous academics, preparing students for both college and direct career entry. Unlike zoned public schools, WCTA requires an application for admission. Students from across the Las Vegas Valley compete for spots, and acceptance is based on academic performance and program interest. The campus is located at 11945 West Charleston Boulevard in Summerlin, placing it within a short drive of Red Rock Country Club. ## How Does West Career and Technical Academy Rank? U.S. News ranks WCTA 284th nationally and 3rd among all Nevada high schools. The school also earned a National Blue Ribbon School designation from the U.S. Department of Education, recognizing its exceptional academic achievement. On Niche, the school receives high marks for both academics and college preparation. The career focused curriculum sets WCTA apart from traditional high schools. Students complete multi year pathways that include industry certifications, internship opportunities, and college credit courses.
