Red Rock Country Club features a 44,000 square foot main clubhouse and a 9,500 square foot sports clubhouse, offering resort level amenities to members and residents in Summerlin, Las Vegas. ## What Amenities Does Red Rock Country Club Offer? The main clubhouse anchors the community's social and dining scene. Three dining venues operate on site: Oasis Grill serves lunch and dinner, Palmer Lounge offers happy hour and small bites, and Cabana Grill provides poolside dining during the summer months. The clubhouse also houses event space and serves as the hub for member programming throughout the year. The sports clubhouse focuses on fitness and racquet sports. Nine lighted tennis courts and four pickleball courts support more than 25 USTA and NTA teams per season, making this one of the most active racquet sport communities in the Las Vegas Valley. A full fitness center with group fitness classes rounds out the athletic offerings. ## What Are the Pool and Spa Facilities Like? Red Rock Country Club operates three swimming pools: a resort style family pool, an adults only pool, and a heated lap pool. The family pool area includes a mini water park designed for younger children, which makes it a popular gathering spot during the warmer months. The adults only pool offers a quieter atmosphere for residents looking to relax without the energy of the family area. The Red Rock Spa and therapeutic spa services provide members with on site wellness options. Tot Stop childcare services are also available, allowing parents to work out, play tennis, or enjoy a meal while their children are supervised by staff. ## Beyond the Clubhouse The community extends its amenities well past the clubhouse doors. Walking trails and biking paths wind through the neighborhood, connecting residents to the natural desert landscape that surrounds the property. Red Rock Country Club also serves as a wedding venue, with a ceremony lawn that accommodates up to 300 guests against a backdrop of mountain views and manicured golf course fairways. Two championship golf courses, three pools, nine tennis courts, multiple dining options, spa services, and children's programs all exist within the community gates.
