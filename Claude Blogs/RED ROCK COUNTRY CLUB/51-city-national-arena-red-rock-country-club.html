City National Arena is the official practice facility of the Vegas Golden Knights, located at 1550 S Pavilion Center Dr in Downtown Summerlin, roughly five minutes from Red Rock Country Club. This 146,000 square foot complex brings professional hockey, public skating, and year round entertainment to the doorstep of one of Las Vegas's premier residential communities. ## What Is City National Arena and What Can You Do There? City National Arena opened in September 2017 as the headquarters and practice facility for the Vegas Golden Knights NHL team. The building houses two regulation ice rinks, a team store, a restaurant and bar, skate rental, and a snack bar. Fans can watch Golden Knights practices for free on most practice days, giving residents a behind the scenes look at professional hockey. Public skating sessions run throughout the week, making the arena a popular destination for families. The facility also hosts youth hockey programs through the Junior Golden Knights organization, along with adult hockey leagues and learn to skate classes. It serves as the home venue for the UNLV Rebels hockey program and the Las Vegas Thunderbirds as well. ## How Close Is City National Arena to Red Rock Country Club? The drive from Red Rock Country Club to City National Arena takes about five minutes along West Charleston Boulevard and Pavilion Center Drive. The arena sits within Downtown Summerlin, which means residents can combine a visit with shopping, dining, or a stop at the adjacent Las Vegas Ballpark. No freeway driving is required for the trip. This proximity turns Golden Knights fandom into a neighborhood activity. Dropping in to watch a morning practice, catching a public skate session after school, or picking up team gear at the official store are all casual errands rather than major outings.
