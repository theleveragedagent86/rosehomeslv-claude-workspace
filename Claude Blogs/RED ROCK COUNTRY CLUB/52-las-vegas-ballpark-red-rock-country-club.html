Las Vegas Ballpark is a 10,000 seat minor league baseball stadium located at 1650 S Pavilion Center Dr in Downtown Summerlin, about five minutes from Red Rock Country Club. Home to the Las Vegas Aviators, the Triple A affiliate of the Oakland Athletics, it delivers professional baseball in a modern, fan friendly setting just minutes from the community gates. ## What Makes Las Vegas Ballpark a Top Entertainment Venue? Las Vegas Ballpark opened in 2019 and quickly established itself as one of the premier minor league stadiums in the country. The facility features 22 luxury suites, club seating, berm seating, party zones, picnic tables, a kids' zone, multiple bars, and a pool beyond the outfield wall. Its video board measures just under 4,000 square feet, making it the largest screen in minor league baseball. The Aviators play a full Pacific Coast League schedule from April through September, offering dozens of home games each season. Beyond baseball, the ballpark hosts concerts, community events, and special programming throughout the year. The venue's design creates a festival like atmosphere that draws crowds from across the valley. ## How Far Is Las Vegas Ballpark from Red Rock Country Club?
