Red Rock Country Club and Southern Highlands are two of the most sought after luxury communities in Las Vegas, but they differ significantly in size, price point, location, and lifestyle focus. ## How Do Red Rock Country Club and Southern Highlands Compare on Price and Size? Red Rock Country Club is located in Summerlin West and consists of approximately 1,000 homes built between 1999 and 2006. The community is fully built out with only resale inventory available. The median home price sits around $2.6 million, reflecting its position as one of the most exclusive residential enclaves in the Las Vegas Valley. Southern Highlands is located in south Las Vegas and contains over 6,000 homes with construction dating from 1999 to the present. The median home price ranges from approximately $600,000 to $800,000 depending on the specific section, though custom estates in the guard gated portions can reach well into the millions. This broader price range gives Southern Highlands a wider buyer audience compared to Red Rock Country Club's luxury focused market. ## How Do the Golf Courses and Amenities Compare? Red Rock Country Club features two Arnold Palmer designed championship golf courses: the Arroyo Course (public) and the Mountain Course (private). HOA fees average around $250 per month. The community's amenities include a 44,000 square foot clubhouse, nine tennis courts, four pickleball courts, three pools, and a full service spa. Southern Highlands offers one golf course through the Southern Highlands Golf Club. The community spans a much larger footprint with amenities that vary by sub community, and HOA fees differ across the various neighborhoods within the master plan. Both communities feature guard gated sections, though Red Rock Country Club is entirely guard gated while only portions of Southern Highlands have controlled access. ## How Do the Locations Differ? Red Rock Country Club's Summerlin West location places residents just 5 to 10 minutes from Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area, with Downtown Summerlin and Tivoli Village shopping nearby. Southern Highlands sits closer to Interstate 15 and Henderson, offering more convenient access to the southern Las Vegas corridor and the M Resort area. The Strip is roughly equidistant from both communities at approximately 20 minutes by car. ## Ryan Rose's Comparison Ryan Rose advises buyers that the choice between these two communities often comes down to lifestyle priorities and budget.
