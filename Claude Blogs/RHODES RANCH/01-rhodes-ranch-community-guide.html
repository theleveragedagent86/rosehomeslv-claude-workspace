Rhodes Ranch is a 1,330 acre, guard gated, master planned golf community in southwest Las Vegas, offering residents resort style amenities, top rated schools, and easy access to the Strip and Red Rock Canyon. Whether you're buying your first home or upgrading to a golf course estate, Rhodes Ranch consistently ranks among the most desirable neighborhoods in the 89148 zip code. ## What Makes Rhodes Ranch a Master Planned Community? Developed by Rhodes Homes starting in 1997, Rhodes Ranch spans more than 18 subdivisions with roughly 3,795 single family homes, condos, and custom estates. The community is built around the Ted Robinson Sr. designed Rhodes Ranch Golf Club, an 18 hole, par 71 course stretching 6,909 yards through landscaped fairways and ponds. Sections range from The Falls (360 condos) to luxury enclaves like The Champions, The Collection, and Tuscany, each with distinct home styles and price points. Homes range from 1,200 square feet to over 4,800 square feet, with lot sizes from 5,000 square feet up to half an acre for estate properties. Builders include nationally recognized names like Pardee, KB Home, Pulte, DR Horton, and Richmond American. Most homes were built between 1997 and the early 2010s, giving the community an established, mature landscape. ## What Amenities Does Rhodes Ranch Offer Residents? The 35,000 square foot R Club Recreation Center anchors community life with fitness facilities, event spaces, and activity programming. The Fun Zone Water Park features 16 slides, a lagoon pool, lap pool, and spas, making it one of the best neighborhood water parks in all of Las Vegas. Residents also enjoy basketball and volleyball courts, tennis courts, a 2 mile walking path, and landscaped ponds throughout the community. Security is a major draw: Rhodes Ranch provides 24/7 guard gated access with staffed entrances managed by Terra West Management Services. HOA fees run between $155 and $225 per month depending on the section. Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate expert who works extensively in southwest Las Vegas, considers Rhodes Ranch one of the best values for guard gated living in the valley. ## Why Buyers Keep Choosing Rhodes Ranch Location ties everything together. Rhodes Ranch sits about 15 minutes from the Las Vegas Strip and 15 to 19 minutes from Harry Reid International Airport. Southern Hills Hospital is just 0.6 miles away, and families benefit from well rated schools including Don and Dee Snyder Elementary and Desert Oasis High School. The nearby Durango Casino and Resort, which opened in December 2023, and the UnCommons mixed use district have added dining, shopping, and entertainment options that didn't exist a few years ago. For residents who value security, community amenities, and a central southwest location, Rhodes Ranch delivers.
