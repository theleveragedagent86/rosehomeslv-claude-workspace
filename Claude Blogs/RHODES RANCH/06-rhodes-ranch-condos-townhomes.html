Rhodes Ranch offers some of the most accessible homeownership options in southwest Las Vegas, with condos starting in the mid $200,000s and townhomes listed around a median of $442,000. Whether you are a first time buyer, a downsizer, or an investor, these attached homes provide guard gated living at a fraction of single family home prices. ## What Condos Are Available in Rhodes Ranch? The primary condo community in Rhodes Ranch is The Falls at Apache Springs, located at 9050 West Warm Springs Road. Built between 2003 and 2005 by Rhodes Homes, the complex includes 360 units ranging from 843 to 1,237 square feet. Floor plans offer one to three bedroom layouts, and select units feature gas fireplaces, granite countertops, and stainless steel appliances. As of early 2026, condos in The Falls typically list between $200,000 and $300,000, with a median price per square foot near $298. The Falls is a gated two story community with landscaped grounds and its own set of amenities. Residents also gain full access to the Rhodes Ranch R Club recreation center, the Fun Zone Water Park, and the community walking paths. HOA fees for condos generally cover exterior maintenance, landscaping, and shared amenities, making budgeting straightforward for owners. ## What About Townhomes in Rhodes Ranch? Rhodes Ranch townhomes offer more space than condos while keeping monthly costs below single family home levels. Townhome listings in early 2026 carry a median price of roughly $442,000, with sizes typically ranging from 1,200 to 1,800 square feet. These properties often include two story layouts, attached garages, and private patios or courtyards. Ryan Rose notes that townhomes in Rhodes Ranch tend to move faster than condos, averaging about 50 days on market compared to 73 days for condos. Townhome sections sit within the broader guard gated community, which means residents enjoy 24/7 staffed security entrances and access to every Rhodes Ranch amenity. The 35,000 square foot R Club, six lane lap pool, basketball courts, and tennis facilities are all included with standard HOA dues of $155 to $225 per month. ## Why Condos and Townhomes Here Stand Out Rhodes Ranch stands apart from other southwest Las Vegas communities because of the sheer depth of amenities bundled into HOA membership. Buyers at the condo and townhome price point rarely find a guard gated community with an 18 hole golf course, a 16 slide water park, and a full service recreation center. The 89148 zip code also sits just 15 minutes from the Las Vegas Strip and 15 to 19 minutes from Harry Reid International Airport, making commutes and travel convenient. For buyers exploring attached homes in a secure, amenity rich setting, Rhodes Ranch delivers exceptional value.
