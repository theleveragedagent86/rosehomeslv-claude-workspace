Golf course homes in Rhodes Ranch offer direct fairway views, mountain backdrops, and the rare ability to live steps from a Ted Robinson designed 18 hole course inside a guard gated community. These properties typically range from $500,000 to $900,000 and represent some of the most scenic real estate in southwest Las Vegas. ## What Is the Rhodes Ranch Golf Course? Rhodes Ranch Golf Club opened on November 6, 1997, and occupies 162 acres at the heart of the community. Designed by legendary architect Ted Robinson Sr., the par 71 championship course stretches 6,909 yards from the back tees and features four tee sets ranging from 4,977 to 6,582 yards to accommodate all skill levels. The course earned a 4 star rating from Golf Digest and is widely praised for what Robinson himself called "the finest par 3 collection" he ever designed. The layout weaves through over 3,000 mature palm trees, with lakes and ponds creating risk reward opportunities on seven different holes. Signature hole No. 3 is a par 3 exceeding 200 yards from the back tees, guarded by water on the right and a deep pot bunker at the front. Hole No. 16, another par 3, demands a carry over water with bunkers and slopes punishing anything offline. The course operates as semi private, with green fees ranging from $25 to $85. ## Which Homes Have Golf Course Views? Several Rhodes Ranch sections sit directly along the fairways. The Fairways, The Greens, The Irons, The Links, and The Majors all offer lots that back to the course, providing unobstructed views of rolling greens, water features, and the Spring Mountains beyond. Homes in these sections range from approximately 2,000 to 4,800 square feet, with many featuring rear patios, covered loggias, and second story balconies designed to maximize the panoramic setting. Golf course lots command a premium over interior lots, typically listing $500,000 to $900,000 depending on size and orientation. Ryan Rose frequently works with buyers who prioritize course frontage and notes that south and west facing lots along holes 3, 7, and 16 tend to generate the strongest demand due to their combination of water features and mountain vistas. ## Why Golf Course Living Here Stands Apart Rhodes Ranch is one of a handful of Las Vegas communities where the golf course, guard gate, and full amenity package exist within a single HOA. Homeowners pay $155 to $225 per month and receive access to the 35,000 square foot R Club recreation center, the Fun Zone Water Park, tennis and basketball courts, and a two mile walking path that circles the entire community. That means golf course homeowners enjoy both the serenity of fairway views and the energy of a resort style neighborhood. The location is equally compelling. Rhodes Ranch sits just 15 minutes from the Strip, under 20 minutes from Harry Reid International Airport, and minutes from Red Rock Canyon. For golfers and view seekers alike, few communities match this combination.
