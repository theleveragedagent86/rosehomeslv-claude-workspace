Rhodes Ranch offers one of the most complete amenity packages of any master planned community in Las Vegas, including an 18 hole golf course, a 35,000 square foot recreation center, a water park with 16 slides, sports courts, walking trails, and landscaped ponds throughout its 1,330 acres in southwest Las Vegas. ## What Amenities Are Included at Rhodes Ranch? The centerpiece of community life is the R Club Recreation Center, a 35,000 square foot facility located at 333 Rhodes Ranch Parkway. Inside, residents have access to a weight and cardio room with equipment overlooking the pool area, an aerobics and stretch room, a full length indoor basketball court, racquetball and handball courts, and pickleball courts. The main lobby features a fireplace, piano, and a community library. Additional spaces include a billiards room, table tennis area, craft rooms, a card room, kitchen, and meeting rooms used by neighborhood groups throughout the year. The Fun Zone Water Park is the crown jewel for families. The 10,000 square foot water park section features three speed slides, five kid slides, rope bridges, squirt gun stations, and an oversized water bucket that tips every five minutes. Adjacent to the water park, a 13,000 square foot lagoon pool provides open swimming, while a 25 meter lap pool serves fitness swimmers. Three spas seat up to 28 people combined, and four covered cabanas offer shaded relaxation. ## Does Rhodes Ranch Have a Golf Course? Rhodes Ranch Golf Club is an 18 hole, par 71 championship course designed by Ted Robinson Sr., stretching 6,909 yards. The course has earned a 4 star rating from Golf Digest and is open to both residents and the public. The Ranch House Grille restaurant at the golf club serves as a casual dining option for the community. Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate expert, frequently highlights that Rhodes Ranch is one of the few guard gated golf communities in Las Vegas where HOA fees remain between $155 and $225 per month while covering access to this full range of amenities. ## What Outdoor Amenities Does Rhodes Ranch Offer? Beyond the recreation center and golf course, Rhodes Ranch includes a 2 mile circular walking path, additional walking and biking trails, an exterior lighted basketball court, two sand volleyball courts, four lighted tennis courts, and a children's playground. Landscaped ponds with ducks and geese are scattered throughout the community, adding a park like feel to daily walks and jogs. The R Club also offers group fitness classes including yoga, Zumba, boot camp, Tai Chi, and total body conditioning, along with personal training by appointment. Whether you are drawn to the golf course, the water park, or the fitness center, Rhodes Ranch packs more amenities into one community than most neighborhoods offer across an entire zip code.
