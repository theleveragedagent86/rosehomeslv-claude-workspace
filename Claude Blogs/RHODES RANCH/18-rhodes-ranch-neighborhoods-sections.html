Rhodes Ranch contains more than 18 distinct neighborhoods and subdivisions within its guard gates, offering everything from affordable condos to luxury single family estates across 1,330 acres in southwest Las Vegas (89148). With roughly 3,795 homes built by multiple builders since 1997, buyers can find a section that fits nearly any budget and lifestyle. ## What Are the Neighborhoods Inside Rhodes Ranch? The major subdivisions within Rhodes Ranch include Auburn Hills, Canyon Trail, The Champions, The Collection, The Fairways, The Greens, The Irons, The Lakes, The Links, The Majors, Pacific Mist, Palms Bay, Spinnaker, Summerchase, Travina, Tuscany, and Vistana. Each neighborhood has its own character, lot sizes, and price range, though all share access to the full suite of community amenities including the R Club Recreation Center, Fun Zone Water Park, and Rhodes Ranch Golf Club. The community also includes The Falls (Apache Springs), a 360 unit condominium development that provides an entry level option within the guard gates. These condos range from 843 to 1,237 square feet and were built between 2003 and 2005, making them a popular choice for first time buyers and investors. ## Who Built the Homes in Rhodes Ranch? Multiple builders have contributed to Rhodes Ranch over its nearly three decades of development. Rhodes Homes and Dunhill Homes were among the original builders, constructing many of the homes in neighborhoods like Tuscany on the east side of the community. Harmony Homes and Century Communities also built sections within the master plan. Royal Construction developed the Spinnaker neighborhood. This variety of builders means home styles range from traditional single story floor plans to two story Mediterranean designs, with square footage spanning from under 1,000 square feet in the condos to over 4,000 square feet in the luxury sections. Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate expert, explains that the diversity of neighborhoods inside Rhodes Ranch is one of its greatest strengths. A buyer can start with a condo in The Falls, move up to a family home in Auburn Hills or Summerchase, and eventually settle into a golf course lot in The Fairways or The Links, all without ever leaving the community. ## Which Rhodes Ranch Neighborhood Is Right for You? Choosing the right section depends on your priorities. Golf course lots in The Fairways and The Links offer direct course views and larger lots. The Lakes and Pacific Mist sit near the community ponds and walking paths for a quieter setting. Families with children often gravitate toward Summerchase and Canyon Trail, which are close to the recreation center and playground areas. Retirees and downsizers may prefer The Collection or Vistana for their manageable floor plans and low maintenance lots.
