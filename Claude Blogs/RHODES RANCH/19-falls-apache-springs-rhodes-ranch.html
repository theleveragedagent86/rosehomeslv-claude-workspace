The Falls at Rhodes Ranch, officially recorded as Apache Springs Condos, is a 360 unit gated condominium community located at 9050 W Warm Springs Road inside the Rhodes Ranch master plan in southwest Las Vegas (89148). These condos offer one of the most affordable entry points into a guard gated golf course community in the Las Vegas valley. ## What Are The Falls at Rhodes Ranch? Built between 2003 and 2005 by Rhodes Ranch developers, The Falls consists of two story condominium buildings set within landscaped grounds with gated security. Units range from 843 to 1,237 square feet and are available in one, two, and three bedroom floor plans. The development sits on the western edge of the Rhodes Ranch master plan near Fort Apache Road, placing it within walking distance of shopping, restaurants, professional offices, and St. Rose San Martin Campus Hospital, which is less than one mile away. The Falls has its own dedicated amenities beyond the main Rhodes Ranch facilities. The complex includes a community clubhouse, three swimming pools, a spa, a basketball court, and a playground. These amenities are managed separately by FirstService Residential, which handles the day to day operations and maintenance of the condominium association. ## How Much Do Condos at The Falls Cost? Condos at The Falls typically sell in the mid to upper $200,000s, making them among the most affordable options inside Rhodes Ranch. Buyers pay HOA fees that cover the condo specific amenities, building exterior maintenance, landscaping, and insurance for common areas. As Rhodes Ranch residents, Falls homeowners also have full access to the R Club Recreation Center, Fun Zone Water Park, walking trails, sports courts, and all other master community amenities included in the main Rhodes Ranch HOA. Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate expert, points out that The Falls is a particularly strong option for first time homebuyers and real estate investors. The combination of guard gated security, resort style amenities, and a price point well below the median for single family homes inside Rhodes Ranch makes these condos a practical path into one of the most amenity rich communities in southwest Las Vegas. ## Who Should Consider The Falls? The Falls works well for several buyer profiles. First time buyers gain entry to a guard gated community without stretching into a higher price bracket. Investors benefit from steady rental demand driven by the community's security and amenities. Downsizers and retirees who want low maintenance living with access to the golf course, recreation center, and walking paths will find The Falls a convenient fit. The proximity to Warm Springs Road shopping and dining adds everyday convenience without needing to drive across town.
