The Rhodes Ranch R Club Recreation Center is a 35,000 square foot facility located at 333 Rhodes Ranch Parkway that serves as the social and fitness hub for the entire Rhodes Ranch community in southwest Las Vegas (89148). Access to the R Club is included in Rhodes Ranch HOA fees, making it an exclusive benefit for residents of this guard gated community. ## What Is Inside the R Club Recreation Center? The R Club packs a full range of fitness and recreation options into one building. The weight and cardio room features state of the art equipment with views overlooking the outdoor pool area. A dedicated aerobics and stretch room hosts group fitness classes throughout the week. The facility also includes a full length indoor basketball court, racquetball courts, handball courts, and pickleball courts for competitive and casual play. Beyond athletics, the R Club offers spaces designed for socializing and hobbies. The main lobby features a fireplace, piano, and overstuffed seating where residents gather or read from the community library. A billiards room, table tennis area, and craft rooms provide additional recreation. Meeting rooms, a card room, and a kitchen are available for neighborhood groups, private events, and community gatherings. ## What Fitness Classes Does the R Club Offer? The R Club runs a rotating schedule of group fitness classes led by certified instructors. Current offerings include Zumba, yoga, Tai Chi, boot camp, total body conditioning, stretch and flexibility training, and circuit training. Kids programs include yoga for kids and youth fitness training. Personal trainers are also available by appointment for residents who prefer one on one coaching. The center is open Monday through Saturday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate expert, considers the R Club one of the most underrated amenities in southwest Las Vegas. Many guard gated communities charge separate membership fees for fitness facilities, but at Rhodes Ranch, the full recreation center is bundled into the $155 to $225 monthly HOA, which also covers 24/7 security, community maintenance, and water park access. ## What Outdoor Amenities Surround the R Club? The R Club sits at the center of a roughly 10 acre recreation campus. Immediately outside the building, the Fun Zone Water Park features 16 slides, a 13,000 square foot lagoon pool, a 25 meter lap pool, three spas seating up to 28 people, and four covered cabanas. The outdoor complex also includes a lighted basketball court, two sand volleyball courts, four lighted tennis courts, and a children's playground. A 2 mile circular walking path connects the recreation area to the rest of the community, passing landscaped ponds with resident ducks and geese along the way.
