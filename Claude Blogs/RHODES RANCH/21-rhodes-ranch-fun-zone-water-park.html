The Rhodes Ranch Fun Zone Water Park is the only private residential water park of its kind in Southern Nevada, giving residents exclusive access to 16 water slides, a 13,000 square foot lagoon pool, and multiple spas right inside the community gates. Located at the heart of the 1,330 acre Rhodes Ranch community in southwest Las Vegas, the Fun Zone is the standout amenity that sets this neighborhood apart from every other master planned community in the valley. ## What Are the Water Slides and Pools at the Rhodes Ranch Fun Zone? The Fun Zone Water Park divides into two main sections. The 10,000 square foot water park area features three high speed slides built for thrill seekers and five kid friendly slides designed for younger swimmers, along with rope bridges, squirt gun stations, and a massive water bucket that cascades every few minutes. The 13,000 square foot lagoon pool includes shallow wading areas for toddlers and deeper zones for laps and recreation. Beyond the slides and lagoon, the Fun Zone includes a 25 meter lap pool for serious swimmers, two eight person spas, and one 12 person spa for adults looking to unwind. Four large covered cabanas provide shaded seating for families who want to spend the full day poolside. The entire water park complex is staffed with lifeguards during operating hours. ## Who Can Use the Rhodes Ranch Fun Zone Water Park? Access is limited to Rhodes Ranch residents and their registered guests, which keeps the park from getting overcrowded even on the hottest summer weekends. Residents gain entry through their HOA membership, with fees ranging from $155 to $225 per month depending on the section. Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate expert who works extensively in the Rhodes Ranch area, notes that the Fun Zone alone makes the HOA investment worthwhile for families with children. The Fun Zone sits adjacent to the 35,000 square foot R Club Recreation Center, which adds a full fitness facility, basketball court, two sand volleyball courts, four lighted tennis courts, and a children's playground. Together, the R Club and Fun Zone create a resort caliber amenity package that many buyers compare to what you would find at a Las Vegas hotel pool. ## Why Families Love the Fun Zone For families evaluating southwest Las Vegas communities, the Fun Zone often tips the decision in favor of Rhodes Ranch. Having a private water park steps from your front door eliminates the need for expensive day passes at commercial water parks, and children can spend entire summers without leaving the neighborhood. The combination of water slides, pools, spas, and covered cabanas makes the Fun Zone one of the best kept residential amenities in Las Vegas.
