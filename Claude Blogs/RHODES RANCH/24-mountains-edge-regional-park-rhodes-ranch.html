Mountain's Edge Regional Park is a 163 acre fitness focused park located at 8101 West Mountains Edge Parkway, just minutes from Rhodes Ranch in southwest Las Vegas. With paved walking trails, outdoor fitness stations, multi sport fields, and expansive open space, it gives Rhodes Ranch residents a large scale park experience that complements the community's own recreation amenities. ## What Trails and Fitness Amenities Does Mountain's Edge Regional Park Have? The park features multiple trail options for different fitness levels. The inner loop is a smooth half mile circuit that wraps around the main plaza, fields, and playgrounds, making it ideal for light jogs, stroller walks, or quick laps with a dog. The Mountain's Edge Trail extends beyond the park into the surrounding desert terrain, forming a 2.3 mile paved loop that is 10 feet wide and accommodates walkers, runners, cyclists, and dog owners comfortably side by side. The outdoor fitness area includes multiple exercise stations spread along the trail, allowing residents to combine cardio and strength training in a single workout. A multi sport wall with target markings supports practice for lacrosse, tennis, soccer, and other sports. The park's fitness focus distinguishes it from traditional Las Vegas parks and attracts residents who want more than just a walking path. ## What Else Can Families Do at Mountain's Edge Regional Park? Large multi purpose turf fields serve soccer, flag football, and other organized sports, making the park a hub for youth athletics in the southwest valley. Shaded picnic areas with ramadas, benches, and tables provide spots for post game meals or weekend gatherings. The playground areas offer equipment for children of various ages, and the park's open layout gives families plenty of room to spread out. The park is still expanding. The first 18 acre phase is fully built out with trails, fields, and fitness features, and additional phases will develop the remaining acreage over time.
