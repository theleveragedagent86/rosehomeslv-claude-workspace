While there is no dedicated dog park within the community itself, Rhodes Ranch residents have several excellent options for exercising and socializing their dogs in southwest Las Vegas. ## What Dog Walking Options Exist Inside Rhodes Ranch? The Rhodes Ranch community walking path is a two mile circular paved loop that winds past landscaped ponds, golf course fairways, and mature desert landscaping. The flat, wide path accommodates dog walkers comfortably alongside joggers, cyclists, and families with strollers. Residents regularly walk their dogs along this path morning and evening, and the guard gated security means less worry about stray animals or through traffic. The community's sidewalk network and landscaped common areas also provide additional routes for shorter daily walks. Dogs must be kept on leash within the community, but the variety of routes means you can vary your daily walk without leaving the gates. The ponds along the walking path are a favorite stop for dogs who enjoy watching the resident ducks and geese. ## What Off Leash Dog Parks Are Near Rhodes Ranch? Desert Breeze Dog Park, located at 8275 Spring Mountain Road, is one of the closest off leash options for Rhodes Ranch residents. The park features three separate fenced runs divided by dog size, with water taps, benches, and waste stations throughout. Park hours run from 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM daily. Barkin' Basin Dog Park at 7351 West Alexander Road offers nearly eight acres of off leash space with three fenced areas for small, medium, and large dogs. The park provides shaded seating, water fountains for dogs and owners, and lush grass for running and play.
