Rhodes Ranch Golf Club is an 18 hole, par 71 championship course designed by Ted Robinson Sr. that sits at the heart of one of southwest Las Vegas's most popular guard gated communities. The semi private club at 20 E Rhodes Ranch Pkwy offers play for both residents and the public, with a driving range, pro shop, putting green, and on site dining at the Ranch House Grille. ## What Makes the Rhodes Ranch Golf Course Special? The course opened on November 6, 1997, and stretches 6,909 yards from the back tees across 162 acres of rolling fairways lined with more than 3,000 mature palm trees. Four tee sets ranging from 4,977 to 6,909 yards make the layout accessible to beginners while still challenging low handicappers. Golf Digest awarded Rhodes Ranch a 4 star rating, and the course is widely considered one of the best public access options in the Las Vegas valley. Ted Robinson Sr. described the par 3 collection at Rhodes Ranch as the finest he ever designed. Water features come into play on seven holes, creating risk reward decisions that reward smart course management over raw power. The generous greens and wide landing areas keep pace of play moving without sacrificing strategic depth. ## What Are the Signature Holes at Rhodes Ranch? Hole No. 3 is the course's signature par 3, stretching over 200 yards from the back tees with water guarding the right side and a deep pot bunker protecting the front of the green. It demands a precise long iron or hybrid and is the most photographed hole on the property. Hole No. 16 is another standout par 3 that requires a full carry over water, with bunkers, palm trees, and sloping terrain punishing anything offline. The back nine features several holes with mountain views toward the Spring Mountains and Red Rock Canyon, adding a dramatic backdrop that sets Rhodes Ranch apart from flat desert layouts. The combination of water, elevation changes, and mature landscaping gives the course a lush, resort quality feel that surprises first time visitors. ## What Facilities Does the Golf Club Include? The clubhouse features a fully stocked pro shop carrying equipment, apparel, and accessories from major brands. A grass driving range and large putting green allow players to warm up before their round or work on their game independently. Private and group lessons are available through the club's teaching professionals for golfers looking to improve.
