Rhodes Ranch Golf Club green fees range from approximately $25 for twilight play to $85 for prime morning tee times, making it one of the most affordable championship courses in the Las Vegas valley. The semi private club at 20 E Rhodes Ranch Pkwy welcomes public players daily and offers Rhodes Ranch residents preferred pricing throughout the year. ## How Much Does It Cost to Play Rhodes Ranch Golf Club? Green fees vary by time of day and season. Prime morning rates during peak season run up to approximately $85, while afternoon and twilight rates can drop to $25 or less. Most midday rounds fall in the $35 to $55 range, and online booking platforms frequently offer additional discounts below rack rate. Individual golf carts are available for an additional $40 fee for players who prefer to ride solo rather than share a cart. Rhodes Ranch residents receive preferred rates that reduce the cost of regular play. The community's semi private structure means homeowners can walk to the first tee and play at a fraction of what comparable Las Vegas courses charge. Seasonal pricing adjusts for summer heat and winter demand, with the lowest rates typically available June through September. ## How Do You Book Tee Times at Rhodes Ranch? Tee times can be booked directly through the Rhodes Ranch Golf Club website at rhodesranchgolf.com, by calling the pro shop, or through third party platforms like GolfNow and TeeOff. Online booking is available up to several days in advance, and early reservation is recommended during peak season from October through May when Las Vegas golf tourism surges. The club enforces a 24 hour cancellation policy. Cancellations made within 24 hours of the tee time may result in a charge, and no shows are billed the full green fee. Ryan Rose, who helps homebuyers find properties at Rhodes Ranch, notes that residents who play frequently find the combination of preferred rates and walk on availability makes the course one of the best daily play values in southwest Las Vegas. ## What Is Included with a Round at Rhodes Ranch? Every green fee includes access to the grass driving range and putting green for pre round warmups. The fully stocked pro shop carries equipment, apparel, and accessories, and the club's teaching professionals offer private and group lessons for players looking to sharpen their skills. After your round, the Ranch House Grille serves breakfast, lunch, and dinner with fairway views, a full bar, and happy hour specials from 5 PM to 8 PM. ## Is Rhodes Ranch Worth the Green Fee?
