The Ranch House Grille is an American bar and grill located inside the Rhodes Ranch Golf Club clubhouse at 20 E Rhodes Ranch Pkwy in southwest Las Vegas. Open daily from 7 AM to 9 PM, the restaurant serves breakfast, lunch, and dinner with fairway views, a full bar, and a relaxed atmosphere that draws golfers, residents, and neighborhood regulars alike. ## What Kind of Food Does the Ranch House Grille Serve? The menu features classic American bar and grill fare with a range of appetizers, entrees, and daily specials. Starters include tortilla chips and dip, hummus and naan, warm pretzel bites, pepperoni pizza, and pork pot stickers. The garlic parmesan wings are a popular choice among regulars. Entree highlights include the Mahi Wulfe, a mahi mahi dish prepared in a white wine sauce with artichoke hearts and sun dried tomatoes, alongside a full lineup of burgers, sandwiches, and grilled options. Taco Tuesday runs every Tuesday from 5 PM to 8 PM and has become one of the restaurant's most popular weekly events. The kitchen accommodates a variety of dietary preferences, and the menu rotates seasonal items to keep things fresh for frequent diners. ## What Are the Hours and Happy Hour Specials? The Ranch House Grille opens at 7 AM for breakfast and stays open through dinner service until 9 PM, seven days a week. Happy hour runs daily from 5 PM to 8 PM with discounted drinks and bar bites. The full bar stocks a wide selection of beer, wine, and cocktails, and multiple flat screen televisions throughout the dining area and bar make it a go to spot for watching sports. The restaurant's location inside the golf club means the patio offers views of the course, with fairways and the Spring Mountains visible during afternoon and evening dining. Whether you are grabbing a post round beer or meeting friends for a weeknight dinner, the setting adds something most neighborhood restaurants cannot match. ## Who Eats at the Ranch House Grille?
