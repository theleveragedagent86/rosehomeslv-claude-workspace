Sierra Vista High School is a public high school that serves some sections of Rhodes Ranch in southwest Las Vegas. Located at 8100 West Robindale Road, this grades 9 through 12 campus enrolls approximately 3,149 students and has earned a spot on the 2025 Advanced Placement School Honor Roll. ## What Are the Ratings for Sierra Vista High School? Sierra Vista High School holds a B minus overall grade on Niche and a 4 out of 10 on GreatSchools. Reading proficiency sits at 45%, which edges slightly above the 44% Nevada state average. Math proficiency comes in at 16%, which falls below state benchmarks. The student to teacher ratio is 24 to 1, which is typical for larger Clark County School District campuses. Despite the mixed test score picture, Sierra Vista distinguishes itself through program depth. The school's placement on the AP School Honor Roll recognizes campuses where a significant number of students participate in and succeed on Advanced Placement exams. That distinction matters for college bound students who want rigorous coursework on their transcripts. ## What Programs Does Sierra Vista High School Offer? Sierra Vista features approximately 20 Advanced Placement courses spanning subjects like AP Biology, AP Calculus AB and BC, AP Chemistry, AP Computer Science, AP Environmental Science, AP English Language and Composition, AP Psychology, AP United States Government and Politics, and AP World History. That breadth of AP options rivals many private schools in the valley. The school also invests heavily in Career and Technical Education. A major renovation project converted over 26,000 square feet of space into specialized HVAC learning labs, classrooms, offices, and storage areas to support hands on career training.
