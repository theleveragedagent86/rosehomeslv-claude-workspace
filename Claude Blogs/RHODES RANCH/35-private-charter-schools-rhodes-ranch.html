Rhodes Ranch families have access to several highly rated private and charter school options beyond the public school system. From the A minus rated Pinecrest Academy of Nevada to prestigious campuses like Bishop Gorman High School, parents in this southwest Las Vegas community can find quality alternatives at every grade level. ## What Charter Schools Are Near Rhodes Ranch? Pinecrest Academy of Nevada at St. Rose is the standout charter option for Rhodes Ranch families. This tuition free, K through 8 campus earns an A minus on Niche and an impressive 8 out of 10 on GreatSchools. Academic performance is well above state averages, with 65% of students reaching math proficiency and 72% reaching reading proficiency. U.S. News ranks Pinecrest St. Rose 19th among Nevada elementary schools and 6th among Nevada middle schools, making it one of the top performing public charter campuses in the Las Vegas valley. Because Pinecrest is a charter school, enrollment is open to students across Clark County regardless of neighborhood zoning. However, demand is high and admission typically involves a lottery process. Families in Rhodes Ranch benefit from the relatively short drive to the St. Rose campus, which keeps commute times manageable. ## What Private Schools Are Near Rhodes Ranch? Several respected private schools serve the southwest Las Vegas corridor. Bishop Gorman High School is a Catholic college preparatory school rated among the best private high schools in Nevada. The campus boasts a 98% college bound rate, and the Class of 2025 produced multiple National Merit Scholarship finalists. Tuition runs approximately $14,000 per year for high school students. Faith Lutheran Middle School and High School offers a Christian education for grades 6 through 12 with an A minus rating on Niche. The school enrolls over 2,000 students, provides approximately 20 AP courses, and sends 88% of graduates to four year colleges. Tuition for the 2026 to 2027 year is $17,100 annually.
