Rhodes Ranch residents have access to a diverse and growing restaurant scene that spans everything from a neighborhood grill inside the gates to upscale steakhouses and international kitchens just minutes away. The southwest Las Vegas dining landscape around this guard gated community has expanded dramatically in recent years, giving homeowners quality options without a long drive. ## What Are the Best Restaurants Near Rhodes Ranch? The Ranch House Grille sits inside the community at 20 E Rhodes Ranch Pkwy, making it the closest option for a casual meal or a drink after golf. Open daily from 7am to 9pm with happy hour from 5 to 8pm and a popular Taco Tuesday special, the American bar and grill is a genuine neighborhood gathering spot. Just outside the gates, Shake Shack at 8745 W Warm Springs Rd serves its signature burgers, crinkle fries, and frozen custard in a fast casual setting. Sgrizzi Ristorante and Bar is a family owned gem offering handcrafted pastas, dry aged steaks, and private dining rooms for special occasions. The Black Sheep brings upscale Vietnamese cuisine to the area and has built a loyal local following, while Amazing Thai 2 and Saigon Corner Restaurant round out the Asian dining options with noodle soups, curries, and traditional Vietnamese dishes. ## What Upscale Dining Options Are Near Rhodes Ranch? UnCommons, a 40 acre mixed use development roughly three quarters of a mile from Rhodes Ranch, has become the area's premier dining destination. Rare Society is a modern retro steakhouse with wood fired grills, in house dry aging, and sustainably sourced seafood. Mijo brings coastal Mexican flavors to the neighborhood with wood burning grills, prime meats, and fresh whole fish preparations. AMARI Italian Kitchen and Wine Shop, All'Antico Vinaio (a world famous Florentine sandwich shop), and Blue Bottle Coffee add even more variety to the UnCommons lineup. For a complete fine dining steakhouse experience, Nicco's features USDA Prime, dry aged, and Wagyu beef.
