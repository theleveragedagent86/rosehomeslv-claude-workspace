Rhodes Ranch Town Center is a 130,000 square foot shopping center located at the corner of Durango Drive and Warm Springs Road, directly adjacent to the Rhodes Ranch community in southwest Las Vegas. The Vons anchored plaza gives residents a convenient lineup of grocery, dining, banking, and everyday services within walking distance of the guard gates. ## What Stores Are at Rhodes Ranch Town Center? The center is anchored by a full service Vons grocery store, making daily shopping runs quick and easy for Rhodes Ranch residents. Additional tenants include Bank of America, AT&T, AAA Insurance, Buffalo Wild Wings, Tropical Smoothie Cafe, Little Caesars, Coldstone Creamery, Subway, and Chop Stop. The mix of national brands and local services covers most routine errands in a single stop, from banking and wireless service to a fast weeknight dinner. Built in 2004 to serve the growing residential population around Rhodes Ranch, the plaza sits at 7415 to 7445 South Durango Drive in the 89113 zip code. Its position at a signalized intersection with dedicated turn lanes makes access straightforward whether you are coming from inside the community or from the I-215 beltway, which is less than a mile to the north. ## What Other Shopping Is Near Rhodes Ranch Town Center? The Town Center is just the starting point for shopping in this part of Las Vegas. IKEA Las Vegas is roughly three quarters of a mile away, and the 40 acre UnCommons mixed use development sits just across the I-215 interchange with restaurants, boutiques, and weekly community events. For larger retail trips, Arroyo Market Square offers big box stores including Walmart, Sam's Club, Best Buy, and Home Depot within a short drive south on Durango or Rainbow Boulevard.
