UnCommons is a 40 acre mixed use development located roughly three quarters of a mile from Rhodes Ranch, near the intersection of Durango Drive and the I-215 beltway in southwest Las Vegas. The walkable district combines chef driven restaurants, specialty retail, weekly community events, and Class A office space in a modern setting that has become one of the most popular gathering spots for Rhodes Ranch residents. ## What Restaurants and Shops Are at UnCommons? The dining lineup features nationally recognized names alongside local favorites. Rare Society serves dry aged steaks and craft cocktails, AMARI Italian Kitchen offers house made pastas, and All'Antico Vinaio brings its famous Florentine sandwiches to Las Vegas. Blue Bottle Coffee, Salt and Straw ice cream, and Urth Caffe round out the cafe scene, while General Admission provides a lively sports bar atmosphere. Mijo Modern Mexican, Todo Bien, and Wineaux wine bar add variety to the restaurant mix. Specialty retail at UnCommons includes Speakeasy Candle Co. and Drybar, with new tenants continuing to open. STIX ASIA, an 18,000 square foot Asian food hall featuring 12 dining stalls, is expected to open in 2026 and will add another major draw to the development. ## What Events Does UnCommons Host? UnCommons runs a full calendar of free and low cost community events throughout the year. The Las Vegas Farmers Market brings local vendors to the property on a regular schedule. Trivia Night at General Admission draws weekly crowds, and Ladies Night at Siempre J.B. offers midweek drink specials. Paddock Auto Oasis, a monthly car showcase held on the last Friday of each month, combines automotive culture with music and food.
