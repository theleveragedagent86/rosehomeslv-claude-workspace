Arroyo Market Square is a 940,000 square foot retail power center located at 7200 Arroyo Crossing Parkway, roughly two to three miles south of Rhodes Ranch in southwest Las Vegas. The shopping center features more than 70 stores anchored by Walmart, Sam's Club, and Home Depot, giving Rhodes Ranch residents access to major retail without a long drive. ## What Stores Are at Arroyo Market Square? The anchor tenants handle the big shopping categories. Walmart Supercenter covers groceries and general merchandise, Sam's Club provides bulk buying, and Home Depot serves home improvement needs. Best Buy, Macy's, Marshalls, Ross, Ulta, PetSmart, Tilly's, Michael's, Famous Footwear, and OfficeDepot fill out the retail mix with everything from electronics and clothing to pet supplies and arts and crafts. Dining options within the center include Sammy's Woodfired Pizza, Jason's Deli, Chipotle, Nevada Chicken Cafe, and several fast casual spots.
