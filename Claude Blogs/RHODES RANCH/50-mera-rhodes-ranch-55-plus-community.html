Mera Rhodes Ranch is a 55 plus active adult apartment community located at 9170 West Oquendo Road in southwest Las Vegas, adjacent to the main Rhodes Ranch neighborhood. Developed by Sparrow Partners and opened in late 2021, Mera offers maintenance free rental living with resort style amenities, daily social programming, and easy access to the shopping, dining, and healthcare options along the Durango Drive corridor. ## What Does Mera Rhodes Ranch Offer Residents? Mera Rhodes Ranch consists of 173 apartment homes in a four story building with units ranging from 615 to 1,341 square feet. One bedroom apartments start around $1,645 per month and two bedroom units start from approximately $2,229. Apartments feature open concept floor plans, stainless steel appliances, granite countertops, walk in closets, in unit laundry, and private balconies or terraces. Detached garage and carport parking options are available. The community's 12,000 square foot clubhouse anchors daily life at Mera with a community kitchen, fitness studio, game room, craft room, theatre room, and a fully stocked library. Outside, residents enjoy a swimming pool with sundeck, pickleball courts, a pet park, fire pit gathering areas, and landscaped courtyards. The maintenance free lifestyle means no yard work, no exterior upkeep, and no surprise repair bills. ## What Activities and Events Does Mera Offer? Social programming at Mera runs daily and includes weekly happy hours, art sessions, line dancing classes, movie nights, fitness classes, and organized excursions to local attractions. Residents have attended group outings to shows at The Sphere and other Las Vegas entertainment venues. Bocce ball, gardening activities, and themed parties round out a calendar designed to keep residents connected and active. The pet friendly community welcomes dogs and cats with breed and weight restrictions, and the on site pet park gives residents a convenient spot for daily walks. Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate expert, points out that Mera appeals to active adults who want the social energy of a dedicated 55 plus community without the commitment of homeownership. ## How Does Mera Connect to the Rhodes Ranch Area? Mera sits adjacent to the main Rhodes Ranch guard gated community, giving residents easy access to the Rhodes Ranch Golf Club, nearby walking trails, and the full range of southwest Las Vegas amenities. Southern Hills Hospital is less than a mile away, and the Durango Casino Resort, grocery stores, restaurants, and pharmacies are all within a few minutes drive. The I-215 Beltway provides quick connections to the Las Vegas Strip, Harry Reid International Airport, and Summerlin.
