Rhodes Ranch and Southern Highlands are both guard gated golf communities in southwest Las Vegas, but Rhodes Ranch offers comparable amenities at 30 to 50 percent lower home prices. Buyers deciding between the two will find that lifestyle preferences and budget are the biggest factors separating these popular neighborhoods. ## How Do Rhodes Ranch and Southern Highlands Compare on Price? Rhodes Ranch has a median home price near $466,000 with monthly HOA fees between $155 and $225. The community spans 1,330 acres and includes roughly 3,795 homes built from 1997 onward, with a mix of single story and two story floor plans ranging from starter homes to custom estates. The lower price point makes Rhodes Ranch one of the most accessible guard gated golf communities in the Las Vegas Valley. Southern Highlands carries a median home price above $700,000 with significantly higher HOA dues. The community is larger in total acreage, with construction beginning in 1999 and continuing with luxury builds today. Southern Highlands is positioned as a premium community, which is reflected in both its home prices and its private, Steve Wynn designed golf course that requires a membership for play. ## What Amenities Does Each Community Offer? Both communities feature 18 hole golf courses, recreation centers, swimming pools, walking trails, and guard gated security with staffed entrances around the clock. Rhodes Ranch's golf course is a public access, par 71 layout designed by Ted Robinson Sr., meaning residents and visitors can book tee times without a membership. The R Club Recreation Center at Rhodes Ranch offers 35,000 square feet of fitness, social, and pool amenities, and the community also includes the private Fun Zone Water Park for residents. Southern Highlands has a private country club golf course, a separate community center, and extensive trail systems along the foothills. Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate expert, notes that many buyers who tour both communities are surprised by how closely Rhodes Ranch matches Southern Highlands in day to day amenities while keeping costs substantially lower. Both communities also sit along the Durango Drive and Las Vegas Boulevard corridors, giving residents similar commute times to the Strip, the airport, and downtown. ## Which Community Is the Better Fit? Choosing between Rhodes Ranch and Southern Highlands often comes down to budget and exclusivity preferences. Rhodes Ranch delivers guard gated golf course living, resort style amenities, and a strong sense of community at a price point that works for first time buyers, growing families, and retirees alike. Southern Highlands appeals to buyers seeking a higher end address with a private club atmosphere and larger estate lots. If you want the security and lifestyle of a guard gated golf community without stretching your budget, Rhodes Ranch is worth a serious look.
