Southwest Las Vegas is home to several guard gated communities, including Rhodes Ranch, Southern Highlands, Canyon Gate Country Club, Spanish Trail, and The Ridges in Summerlin. Each offers staffed security entrances and private amenities, but they differ significantly in price, age, and lifestyle. ## What Guard Gated Communities Are in Southwest Las Vegas? Rhodes Ranch is a 1,330 acre guard gated community built starting in 1997 with approximately 3,795 homes. It features an 18 hole, par 71 golf course designed by Ted Robinson Sr., the Fun Zone Water Park, and the 35,000 square foot R Club Recreation Center. HOA fees range from $155 to $225 per month and the median home price sits near $466,000, making it one of the most affordable guard gated golf communities in the valley. Southern Highlands is a larger guard gated community that began construction in 1999 and centers around a private golf course designed for Steve Wynn. Median home prices exceed $700,000 and HOA fees are considerably higher than Rhodes Ranch. The community appeals to buyers seeking a premium address with a private country club atmosphere. Canyon Gate Country Club, built in 1989, is one of the oldest guard gated communities in the area and shares a connection with Rhodes Ranch through architect Ted Robinson Sr., who designed both golf courses. ## How Do Prices and Amenities Compare Across Communities? Spanish Trail is another established guard gated option in the southwest, featuring a 27 hole golf course and homes that range from older villas to newer custom builds. Prices vary widely depending on the section and home age. The Ridges, located in the western edge of Summerlin, is an ultra luxury guard gated enclave where homes typically start above $1 million and reach well into the multimillion dollar range. Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate expert, often works with buyers who are comparing these communities side by side. Rhodes Ranch consistently stands out for offering the full guard gated golf community experience at a fraction of the cost of Southern Highlands, Spanish Trail, or The Ridges. For buyers who want staffed security, a golf course, recreation facilities, and a water park without a seven figure price tag, Rhodes Ranch provides that combination in the southwest valley. ## Which Guard Gated Community Fits Your Budget? The range across these communities is wide. Entry level guard gated living starts in Rhodes Ranch near the mid $300,000s, while The Ridges and parts of Southern Highlands can exceed $3 million. Canyon Gate and Spanish Trail fall in between, with older inventory that sometimes requires updating. All five communities benefit from southwest Las Vegas infrastructure, including the 215 Beltway, Durango Drive, and proximity to Red Rock Canyon, the Strip, and Harry Reid International Airport.
