Ridgebrook is a 100 acre residential village in Summerlin South, Las Vegas, featuring 486 single family homes across four distinct neighborhoods surrounding a central 6.3 acre park. This all ages community in zip code 89135 offers views of the Spring Mountains and convenient access to shopping, dining, and top rated schools. ## What Makes Ridgebrook Different from Other Summerlin Communities? Ridgebrook stands out as a "mini village" within the larger Summerlin master plan. Built between 2001 and 2003, the community includes four neighborhoods: Echo Ridge by Pulte Homes, Granite Peaks by KB Homes, Southstar by RS Development, and Sunset Ridge by both Pulte and KB Homes. Each subdivision offers its own floor plans and architectural character, but all share the same central park and community amenities. The community is home to approximately 2,522 residents. Homes range from 1,423 to 2,633 square feet, with both single story and two story options available. Stucco exteriors and lush green landscaped yards give Ridgebrook a cohesive, well maintained appearance throughout all four subdivisions. ## What Amenities Does Ridgebrook Offer Residents? The centerpiece of Ridgebrook is its 6.3 acre park, which includes a lighted tennis court, full sized basketball court, picnic pavilion, children's play area, interactive water play area, open green space, walking trail, and community restrooms. Ridgebrook is not a gated community, so the park is accessible and welcoming to all residents. The Summerlin South master association, managed by The Howard Hughes Corporation, maintains shared spaces and organizes recreational programming funded through the Summerlin Council. Judy and John L. Goolsby Elementary School sits within the community itself, making school drop off a short walk for many families. The Alexander Dawson School at Rainbow Mountain, a private college preparatory school, is adjacent to Ridgebrook on a 33 acre campus. ## Why Ryan Rose Recommends Ridgebrook for Las Vegas Homebuyers As a Las Vegas real estate specialist, Ryan Rose has helped dozens of families find homes in Ridgebrook.
