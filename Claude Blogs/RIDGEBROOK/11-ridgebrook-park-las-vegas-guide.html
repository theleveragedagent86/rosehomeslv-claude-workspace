Ridgebrook Park is a 6.3 acre community park located at 3600 Ridge Hollow Dr in the Ridgebrook village of Summerlin, Las Vegas. This central green space serves as the heart of the neighborhood, offering lighted courts, a playground, water play area, and open fields for residents and visitors. ## What Is Ridgebrook Park and Where Is It Located? Ridgebrook Park sits at the center of the Ridgebrook community, a 100 acre village with 486 homes spread across four subdivisions. The park is surrounded by these neighborhoods, making it an easy walk for nearly every household in the area. Its address is 3600 Ridge Hollow Dr, Las Vegas, NV 89135, placing it in the western part of the Las Vegas Valley near Summerlin. The park is open daily until 10:30 PM, giving families and fitness enthusiasts plenty of time to enjoy the facilities after work or school. Shade trees and a large grassy area make it comfortable even during warmer months. Community restrooms are available on site for convenience. ## What Amenities Does Ridgebrook Park Offer? Ridgebrook Park packs a wide variety of features into its 6.3 acres. A lighted tennis court and full sized basketball court provide space for competitive play well into the evening hours. A children's playground and interactive water play area keep younger visitors entertained, while a picnic pavilion offers a shaded spot for gatherings. A paved walking trail circles the park perimeter, ideal for morning jogs or evening strolls. Open green space and a large grassy area give families room to spread out for games, picnics, or relaxation.
