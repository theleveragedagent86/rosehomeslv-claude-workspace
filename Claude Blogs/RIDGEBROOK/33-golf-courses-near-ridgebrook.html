Ridgebrook in Summerlin sits in one of the best locations in Las Vegas for golf, with four courses within minutes of the neighborhood. Whether you prefer a public round with mountain views or membership at a private club, residents of this 89135 community have outstanding options right outside their door. ## What Golf Courses Are Closest to Ridgebrook? The nearest course to Ridgebrook is the Arroyo Golf Club at Red Rock Country Club, located just north of the community across Desert Inn Road. This Arnold Palmer Signature Course is open to public play and stretches 6,883 yards with a par 72 links style layout. The Arroyo features Bermuda grass fairways, Bentgrass greens, and panoramic views of Red Rock Canyon and the Las Vegas skyline. Siena Golf Club sits just south of Ridgebrook along West Flamingo Road. Designed by Brian Curley and Lee Schmidt, this par 72 course spans 6,843 yards and is known for its quarter mile long Lake Siena with cascading waterfalls on the first hole. The 13,500 square foot clubhouse includes The Siena Bistro, a popular dining spot after a round. ## Are There Private Golf Clubs Near Ridgebrook? Red Rock Country Club borders Ridgebrook directly to the north and offers a private Mountain Course designed by Arnold Palmer. This par 72 layout features over 7,000 yards of rolling fairways, multiple water features, and tiered greens. The 738 acre guard gated community also includes three clubhouses, nine tennis courts, and three swimming pools. Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas Realtor specializing in Summerlin, points out that Ridgebrook's proximity to both public and private golf makes it especially appealing to golf enthusiasts who want quality courses without a long drive. Bear's Best Las Vegas on West Flamingo Road is also nearby and is currently being transformed into a premium private club with a projected reopening in late 2026. ## A Golfer's Neighborhood in Summerlin Few Summerlin communities can match Ridgebrook's access to top tier golf. With the Arroyo and Siena for public play and Red Rock Country Club for private membership, residents can play year round within minutes of home. The upcoming private club at the former Bear's Best site will add another elite option to the area.
