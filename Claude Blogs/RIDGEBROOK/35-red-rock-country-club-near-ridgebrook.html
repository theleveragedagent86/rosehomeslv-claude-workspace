Red Rock Country Club is a 738 acre guard gated golf community located directly north of Ridgebrook across Desert Inn Road in Summerlin. This private community features two Arnold Palmer designed golf courses, three clubhouses, and luxury homes ranging from $800,000 to over $10 million, making it one of the most prestigious residential communities in the Las Vegas Valley. ## What Golf Courses Does Red Rock Country Club Offer? Red Rock Country Club includes two distinct 18 hole courses, both designed by Arnold Palmer. The private Mountain Course is reserved for club members and features over 7,000 yards of rolling fairways, multiple water features, and tiered greens set against the backdrop of the Spring Mountains. This par 72 layout is considered one of the top private courses in Nevada. The Arroyo Course is the public access option, operating as the Arroyo Golf Club. This 6,883 yard par 72 links style layout is open to daily fee players and includes its own dedicated clubhouse with a full service golf shop, locker rooms, and a bar and grill. Ridgebrook residents can reach the Arroyo Course in about three minutes by car, making it one of the most convenient public golf options in all of Summerlin. ## What Amenities Does Red Rock Country Club Have Beyond Golf? The community's Main Clubhouse spans 44,000 square feet and serves as the social hub for members, hosting events, dining, and gatherings throughout the year. A separate 10,000 square foot Sports Clubhouse offers fitness classes, a gym, and access to nine tennis courts, four pickleball courts, and three swimming pools.
