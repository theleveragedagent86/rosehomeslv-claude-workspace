Ridgebrook in Summerlin South is one of the more family friendly neighborhoods in the Las Vegas valley, with strong schools, a dedicated community park, and homes designed for growing households. ## What Schools Do Ridgebrook Families Use? Families in Ridgebrook are zoned for Goolsby Elementary, which holds an A minus rating and sits within the community itself. That walkable proximity to an elementary school is a major draw for parents with young children. Older students attend Fertitta Middle School, located 2 to 3 miles away, and Durango High School, about 4 to 5 miles from the neighborhood. For families seeking private education, Alexander Dawson School is adjacent to Ridgebrook and serves students from pre kindergarten through eighth grade. Having both strong public and private school options within minutes of home gives parents flexibility in choosing the right fit for each child. ## Is Ridgebrook Safe for Families? Ridgebrook sits in a generally safe, low crime area of Summerlin South. The neighborhood has a population of approximately 2,522 residents spread across 486 homes, creating a close knit atmosphere where neighbors recognize each other. Ryan Rose hears consistently from families that the quiet streets and cul de sac layouts make Ridgebrook feel secure for kids riding bikes or walking to the park. The community is non gated, which keeps the feel open and accessible while still benefiting from Summerlin's overall reputation for safety and well maintained public spaces. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is roughly 10 minutes away, adding practical reassurance for families. ## What Family Activities Are Available in Ridgebrook? Ridgebrook Park is the centerpiece of community life for families. The 6.3 acre park includes a playground, a water play area, tennis courts, basketball courts, and a walking trail. It provides a gathering spot for after school play, weekend sports, and neighborhood socializing without needing to drive anywhere. Red Rock Canyon is a 10 to 15 minute drive and offers hiking trails suitable for families with children of varying ages. Downtown Summerlin provides shopping, dining, and seasonal events that families enjoy year round. The combination of on site recreation and nearby attractions keeps family life active and convenient.
