# What Is Reverence in Summerlin? Reverence is a 300 acre guard gated village in Summerlin West, developed exclusively by Pulte Homes starting in 2017. Located at the northernmost edge of the Summerlin master plan, the community sits at roughly 3,200 feet above sea level along the base of the Spring Mountains, with Red Rock Canyon as its western backdrop. ## Community Overview Reverence includes 655 residences spread across 23 floor plans in six distinct collections. Homes range from 1,579 to 4,815 square feet, with architectural styles including Craftsman, Prairie, Hacienda, Farmhouse, and Desert Contemporary. The community falls within the 89138 zip code. The neighborhood is divided into two main sections. The Heights at Reverence is a luxury guard gated enclave with approximately 480 homes across seven neighborhoods, offering homes from roughly 2,156 to 4,815 square feet. Monument at Reverence is the newest addition, featuring townhome style living with three floor plans ranging from 1,654 to 1,869 square feet. ## Amenities and Access Residents of The Heights get exclusive access to the 16,000 square foot Reverence Clubhouse, which features a resort style pool with beach entry, a 1,600 square foot lap pool, spa, lighted pickleball courts, and the 9,663 square foot Skye Fitness center. The eight acre Reverence Park adds tennis courts, basketball, sand volleyball, a walking track, playground, and an amphitheater. The community connects to Summerlin's 200 plus mile trail system, and future trail extensions will link directly to Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area. Downtown Summerlin, with over 125 shops and restaurants, is just minutes south. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose has worked with buyers exploring Reverence since its early phases and sees it as one of Summerlin's most complete communities. The elevation and setback from the valley floor give it noticeably cooler evening temperatures in summer, and the western mountain views are hard to match anywhere else in the master plan.
