# Reverence Summerlin Home Prices in 2026 Homes in Reverence at Summerlin currently range from the mid $400,000s for townhomes in the Monument collection to over $1.1 million for larger single family homes in The Heights. As of early 2026, the average price per square foot in Reverence sits around $530, well above the broader Summerlin median. ## Price Ranges by Collection The Monument at Reverence neighborhood offers the most accessible entry point. These townhome style homes from Pulte feature three floor plans between 1,654 and 1,869 square feet, with prices starting in the high $400,000s. The Heights at Reverence is where most of the community's single family homes are located. Floor plans here range from approximately 2,156 to 4,815 square feet across four collections. Resale homes in The Heights typically list between $650,000 and $1.1 million depending on size, lot position, and whether the home has mountain or Strip views. ## What Drives Pricing in Reverence Several factors keep Reverence pricing above the Summerlin average.
