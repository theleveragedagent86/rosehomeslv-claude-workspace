# The Clubhouse at Reverence Summerlin The Clubhouse at Reverence is a 16,000 square foot private amenity center exclusively for residents of The Heights at Reverence. It won two Silver Nugget awards after opening and remains one of the most complete clubhouse facilities in any Summerlin community. ## Pool and Outdoor Features The centerpiece is a resort style pool with beach entry and a wet deck, designed more like a boutique hotel than a neighborhood amenity. A separate 1,600 square foot lap pool sits alongside for fitness swimmers, and there is a heated spa adjacent to both pools. The pool deck includes lounge seating, shaded cabanas, and mountain views to the west. Outside the pool area, the clubhouse grounds feature lighted pickleball courts, which have become one of the most popular amenities in the community. The outdoor spaces also include patio areas with fire features for evening gatherings. ## Skye Fitness Center Skye Fitness occupies 9,663 square feet within the clubhouse complex. The facility includes a full range of cardio and strength equipment, private rooms for group fitness classes, and glass doors that open directly to the outdoors. The design takes advantage of Reverence's elevation, with views of both the mountains and the Las Vegas valley from the workout floor. ## Indoor Spaces Inside the clubhouse, residents have access to a recreation room with ping pong and pool tables, a lounge area, and flexible event space for community gatherings. Reverence regularly hosts social events and activities for residents through the clubhouse programming. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose points out that the clubhouse is one of the main reasons buyers choose Reverence over other guard gated communities in Summerlin. Many competing communities offer pools and fitness rooms, but few match the scale and design quality of the Reverence Clubhouse. It is exclusive to Heights residents, which keeps it from feeling overcrowded even on busy weekends. To see the clubhouse and community firsthand, contact Ryan Rose to arrange a tour of Reverence.
