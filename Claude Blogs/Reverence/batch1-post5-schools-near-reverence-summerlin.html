# Schools Near Reverence in Summerlin Families considering Reverence in Summerlin have access to a strong mix of public, private, and charter schools within the Clark County School District. Summerlin is home to more than two dozen schools, including 16 public campuses and 10 nationally recognized private institutions. ## Public Elementary Schools Reverence falls within the Summerlin West area, and nearby elementary options include Judy and John L. Goolsby Elementary, which holds a 9 out of 10 rating on GreatSchools. Several other well rated elementary campuses serve the surrounding Summerlin villages, giving families solid choices depending on exact zoning. ## Middle and High Schools Rogich Middle School in The Paseos Village and Faiss Middle School in The Vistas Village are two of the closest public middle schools to Reverence. Both serve the broader Summerlin West community. For high school, Palo Verde High School is the primary public option. Palo Verde enrolls over 3,100 students in grades 9 through 12 and ranks among the top public high schools in Nevada. The school offers Advanced Placement courses, competitive athletics, and a range of extracurricular programs. ## Private School Options Summerlin's private school landscape includes Faith Lutheran Academy, which earns an A plus rating on Niche, and The Meadows School, a college preparatory school serving pre kindergarten through 12th grade. Both are within a reasonable drive from Reverence and attract families from across the valley.
