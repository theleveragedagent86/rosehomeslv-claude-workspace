# Private Schools Near Reverence Summerlin Reverence residents have access to several of the top private schools in Nevada, all within a 15 to 25 minute drive. Options range from faith-based campuses to college preparatory academies, covering preschool through 12th grade. ## Faith Lutheran Faith Lutheran is one of the largest private Christian schools in the country, serving grades 6 through 12 from its campus in the Summerlin area. The school offers a Christ-centered education alongside strong academics, with tuition around $16,275 per year. Faith Lutheran Academy serves younger students in grades K through 5 and holds an A+ rating on Niche, giving families a continuous private education path from kindergarten through high school graduation. ## The Meadows School The Meadows School sits on 40 acres in Summerlin and serves approximately 950 students from preschool through 12th grade. With a 9 to 1 student-teacher ratio and upper school tuition around $33,000 per year, Meadows is positioned as the premier college preparatory option in the valley. The school has a strong reputation for placing graduates at competitive universities, and the small class sizes allow for individualized attention that larger public schools cannot match. ## Adelson Educational Campus The Adelson Educational Campus is the only preschool through 12th grade Jewish day school in Nevada. The school enrolls approximately 553 students with a 9 to 1 student-teacher ratio and holds an A Niche grade, ranking among the top 5 private high schools in the state. ## Bishop Gorman High School Bishop Gorman is the valley's premier Catholic high school, located on a 52-acre campus near Summerlin. The school has a national reputation for its athletic programs, particularly football and basketball, while also maintaining strong college preparatory academics. Bishop Gorman is a practical option for Reverence families who want a combination of competitive athletics and faith-based education. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose notes that access to multiple private school options is a factor for many families considering Reverence.
