# The Crossing Park Near Summerlin The Crossing Park is a 12-acre community park located at 1111 Crestdale Lane in Summerlin North. The park serves as a recreational hub for the surrounding Crossing Village and is one of the larger neighborhood parks in the Summerlin master plan. ## Park Amenities The Crossing Park features two full-sized lighted soccer fields, making it a popular destination for youth and adult soccer leagues. The park also includes a shaded children's play area, picnic ramadas for group gatherings, and community restrooms. The lighted fields extend the park's usability into the evening hours, which is particularly valuable during the hotter months when outdoor activity is more comfortable after sunset. The park stays open daily until 10:30 PM. ## Location and Access The Crossing Park is located in Summerlin North, approximately 15 to 20 minutes from Reverence via the 215 Beltway. While not the closest park to Reverence, it serves as a destination for residents involved in organized soccer or looking for a different park experience than the ones in Summerlin West. The park sits within The Crossing Village, one of Summerlin's established neighborhoods with mature landscaping and well-maintained infrastructure surrounding the park. ## Park Rules Standard Summerlin park rules apply at The Crossing Park. No alcoholic beverages or glass containers are permitted. Dogs must be on a leash at all times and are not allowed in activity or sports areas. No camping or lodging is allowed in the park. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose points out that the Summerlin park system gives residents access to different types of parks throughout the community, each with its own character and amenities.
