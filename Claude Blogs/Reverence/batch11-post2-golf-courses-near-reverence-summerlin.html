# Golf Courses Near Reverence Summerlin Summerlin is home to ten golf courses, making it one of the most golf-rich communities in the Las Vegas valley. Reverence residents can reach multiple public, semi-public, and private courses within a 10 to 20 minute drive, with options ranging from executive layouts to championship-caliber designs. ## Public and Semi-Public Courses Highland Falls Golf Club sits at over 3,000 feet of elevation and offers panoramic views of the Las Vegas Strip. The course features rolling hills on the front nine that become steeper on the back side, with rich fairways and strategically placed bunkering. The elevated setting makes it one of the most scenic rounds in the valley. Palm Valley Golf Club is Golf Summerlin's longest and most challenging course, designed for players who want a full test of their game. The course rewards accuracy and course management, and the length makes it a favorite among experienced golfers. Eagle Crest Golf Club is built into the side of a mountain and operates as an executive course that tests players on every club in the bag. The shorter format makes it a practical option for golfers who want to fit in a round without a five-hour commitment. The Arroyo Golf Club, an Arnold Palmer designed course at Red Rock Country Club, is open to public play and features desert canyon views throughout the round. The course is one of the more visually striking layouts in the Summerlin area. ## Private Courses TPC at Summerlin is the premier private golf club in the community. The championship course was designed by Bobby Weed and is a member of the Tournament Players Club network operated by the PGA Tour. The club has hosted professional events and is one of the most exclusive golf memberships in the Las Vegas market. Siena Golf Club is an 18-hole Brian Curley and Lee Schmidt designed course surrounded by large single-story homes with golf course frontage and views.
