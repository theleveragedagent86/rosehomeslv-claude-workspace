# Medical Facilities Near Reverence Summerlin Access to quality healthcare is a practical consideration for homebuyers, and Reverence residents are well positioned with multiple hospitals, urgent care centers, and specialty medical offices within the Summerlin area. The primary hospital serving the community is less than 15 minutes away. ## Summerlin Hospital Medical Center Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is the anchor healthcare facility for the area, located at 657 N. Town Center Drive. The 496-bed, Joint Commission accredited hospital has been serving the Summerlin community since 1997 and provides a comprehensive range of medical services. Specialty centers at Summerlin Hospital include The Heart Center, The Robotic Surgery Center, The Birthplace (maternity), The Children's Medical Center, and The Cancer Center. The hospital is accredited as both a Primary Heart Attack Center and a Primary Stroke Center, meaning it meets the standards for rapid diagnosis and treatment of cardiac and stroke emergencies. ## Emergency Services Beyond the main hospital, Valley Health System operates freestanding emergency departments in the Summerlin area, including ER at South Summerlin. These standalone emergency rooms provide the same level of emergency care as a hospital-based ER but are positioned closer to residential neighborhoods for faster access. For Reverence residents, the combination of Summerlin Hospital and the freestanding ERs means emergency care is typically within a 10 to 15 minute drive regardless of the time of day. ## Nearby Hospitals MountainView Hospital, Southern Hills Hospital, and Dignity Health St. Rose Dominican West Flamingo Campus are all within a reasonable driving distance from Summerlin West. These additional hospitals provide options for specialized care, second opinions, or situations where the closest facility may be at capacity. ## Primary Care and Specialists The Town Center Drive corridor and surrounding medical office complexes house a concentration of primary care physicians, dentists, pediatricians, and specialists. Las Vegas Medical Group operates a Summerlin office within the Valley Health System network, and numerous independent practices serve the area. Most residents can find their primary care physician, dentist, and common specialists within a 10-minute drive. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose notes that healthcare access is especially important for retirees and families with young children considering Reverence. The proximity to Summerlin Hospital and the growing number of medical offices along Town Center Drive mean that routine appointments, urgent care visits, and even emergencies are handled close to home.
