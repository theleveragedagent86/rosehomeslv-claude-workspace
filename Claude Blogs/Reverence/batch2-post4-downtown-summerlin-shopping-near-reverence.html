# Downtown Summerlin Shopping Near Reverence Downtown Summerlin is the primary retail and entertainment destination for Reverence residents, located just a few minutes south of the community along the 215 Beltway. With over 125 shops, restaurants, and entertainment venues in an open air setting, it serves as the commercial center of the Summerlin master plan. ## Retail and Shopping The center features a mix of national retailers and local boutiques. Major anchors include Macy's, Dillard's, and a range of specialty stores spanning fashion, home goods, electronics, and sporting goods. The open air layout makes it more of a walkable district than a traditional enclosed mall, with wide sidewalks, landscaping, and seasonal decorations. New additions continue to expand the retail lineup, with recent openings adding more dining and lifestyle brands to the mix. The center regularly updates its tenant roster, keeping the experience fresh for regular visitors. ## Dining Options Restaurants at Downtown Summerlin cover a wide range of cuisines and price points. Options include Yard House for elevated bar food and one of the largest draft beer selections in the area, along with casual spots, fast casual chains, and sit down restaurants. The dining scene has grown steadily since the center opened, with new restaurant concepts arriving regularly. ## Entertainment A multiplex movie theater anchors the entertainment side, and the center hosts seasonal events including a holiday ice rink, farmers market, and live music throughout the year. Dave and Buster's provides arcade and dining entertainment for families. ## Distance from Reverence The drive from Reverence to Downtown Summerlin takes roughly 5 to 10 minutes depending on traffic, making it practical for daily errands, weeknight dinners, or weekend shopping. The 215 Beltway provides a direct route south. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose points out that proximity to Downtown Summerlin is one of the lifestyle advantages Reverence buyers value most. Having over 125 shops and restaurants within a short drive means residents rarely need to venture to the Strip or across the valley for retail, dining, or entertainment. It is one of the reasons Summerlin consistently ranks as one of the most self contained communities in Las Vegas.
