# Retiring in Reverence Summerlin Reverence in Summerlin is not an age restricted community, but its guard gated security, resort style amenities, and low maintenance living options make it a strong choice for retirees who want an active lifestyle without the limitations of a 55 plus development. ## Why Retirees Choose Reverence The combination of 24 hour guard gate security and a walkable community layout appeals to retirees prioritizing safety and convenience. The 16,000 square foot clubhouse provides daily social and fitness opportunities without leaving the neighborhood, and the eight acre Reverence Park offers flat walking paths for low impact exercise. Unlike dedicated retirement communities such as Sun City Summerlin, Reverence has no age restrictions. This means retirees live alongside families and professionals, creating a more diverse community atmosphere. For retirees who want to live near children or grandchildren in the same neighborhood, this flexibility is a significant advantage. ## Amenities That Fit Retirement Living The Skye Fitness center offers 9,663 square feet of fitness space with group classes, making it easy to maintain a workout routine. The lighted pickleball courts have become especially popular with active adults. The resort pool, spa, and clubhouse lounge provide relaxed gathering spaces for socializing. The community also connects to over 200 miles of Summerlin trails and sits minutes from Red Rock Canyon, giving retirees who enjoy hiking, walking, or cycling direct access to world class outdoor recreation. ## Low Maintenance Options Monument at Reverence offers townhome style homes starting in the high $400,000s, designed around a lock and leave lifestyle. The townhome format reduces exterior maintenance compared to a detached single family home, which appeals to retirees who travel frequently or simply prefer less yard work. ## Nevada Tax Advantages Nevada has no state income tax, which is a significant draw for retirees relocating from higher tax states like California. Social Security benefits, pensions, and retirement account withdrawals are all free from state income tax. Property taxes in Clark County also remain well below the national average. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose has helped several retirees relocate to Reverence from both California and the Midwest.
