# Commute Times from Reverence Summerlin to the Las Vegas Strip Reverence sits in Summerlin West, roughly 20 miles northwest of the Las Vegas Strip. The typical drive time ranges from 25 to 40 minutes depending on traffic, time of day, and which part of the Strip you are heading to. ## Route Options The most direct route from Reverence to the Strip follows the 215 Beltway south to the I-15 freeway, then south on I-15 to the Strip exits. During off peak hours, this route takes approximately 25 to 30 minutes. During rush hour (roughly 7:00 to 9:00 AM and 4:00 to 6:30 PM), the drive can stretch to 35 to 40 minutes, primarily due to congestion on the I-15 interchange. An alternative route uses Summerlin Parkway east to US-95 south, then connects to I-15. This route avoids the 215/I-15 merge and can be faster during certain traffic patterns, though the total distance is slightly longer. ## Commute to Other Key Destinations The Strip is not the only employment center in the valley.
