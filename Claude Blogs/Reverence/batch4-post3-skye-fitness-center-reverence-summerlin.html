# Skye Fitness Center at Reverence Summerlin Skye Fitness is the 9,663 square foot fitness facility inside the Reverence Clubhouse, exclusive to Heights at Reverence residents. It is one of the largest private community fitness centers in Summerlin and was designed to function as a full gym replacement, not just a token amenity room. ## Equipment and Layout The facility includes a full range of cardio equipment (treadmills, ellipticals, bikes, rowers) and a dedicated strength training area with free weights, machines, and functional training space. The layout is open and spacious, avoiding the cramped feel of smaller community fitness rooms. The defining design feature is the floor to ceiling glass doors along one wall that open directly to the outdoors. This brings in natural light and mountain views during workouts, and during cooler months the doors can open fully to create an indoor outdoor training environment. At 3,200 feet elevation, the air quality and temperatures are noticeably better than the valley floor. ## Group Fitness Skye Fitness includes private rooms designated for group fitness classes. The community organizes regular programming including yoga, strength classes, and other group formats. Having dedicated class space means group sessions do not interfere with the main gym floor. ## Compared to a Gym Membership A comparable gym membership in the Las Vegas area runs $40 to $100 or more per month depending on the facility. Skye Fitness is included in the Reverence HOA, so there is no additional cost. For residents who would otherwise join a gym, this effectively offsets a portion of the monthly HOA fee. The convenience factor is significant too. Skye Fitness is walkable from most Heights homes, meaning there is no commute to the gym and no excuses to skip a workout. Early morning and late evening access makes it practical for any schedule. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose considers Skye Fitness one of Reverence's best kept advantages. Many guard gated communities include a basic fitness room, but few invest in a nearly 10,000 square foot facility with mountain views, glass walls, and dedicated class space. He often walks buyers through the gym during tours because photos do not fully capture the scale and quality of the space.
