# Guard Gated Communities in Summerlin Summerlin is home to several guard gated communities, each offering different price points, amenities, and lifestyles behind the security of a 24 hour staffed gate. For buyers who prioritize controlled access and community exclusivity, Summerlin provides more guard gated options than any other area in the Las Vegas valley. ## Reverence Reverence is a 300 acre Pulte Homes community in Summerlin West with 655 homes, developed starting in 2017. The Heights section offers single family homes from roughly 2,156 to 4,815 square feet, priced from $650,000 to over $1.1 million. Monument adds townhomes from the high $400,000s. Amenities include a 16,000 square foot clubhouse, Skye Fitness center, resort pool, pickleball courts, and an eight acre park. HOA is approximately $250 per month total. ## The Ridges The Ridges is Summerlin's ultra luxury guard gated community, featuring custom and semi custom homes from multiple builders. Prices generally start above $1.5 million and can exceed $5 million. The community does not have a shared clubhouse but offers large lots, custom architecture, and proximity to Bear's Best golf course. It is the most exclusive address in Summerlin. ## Red Rock Country Club Red Rock Country Club is a golf centric guard gated community with two championship courses designed by Arnold Palmer and Jack Nicklaus. Homes range from the $600,000s to well over $2 million. The community includes a full clubhouse with dining, fitness, and social amenities. Golf membership is separate from the HOA and carries additional monthly dues. ## Tournament Hills Tournament Hills is one of Summerlin's original guard gated neighborhoods, located near the TPC Las Vegas golf course. The community features older, established homes from the late 1990s and early 2000s, with prices typically ranging from the $700,000s to $1.5 million. The mature landscaping and larger lots give it a more traditional feel. ## Key Differences The main factors distinguishing these communities are price point, age of construction, and amenity focus. Reverence offers the newest construction with the most complete amenity package at a mid range price. The Ridges is for buyers seeking ultra luxury and custom homes. Red Rock Country Club is the choice for serious golfers. Tournament Hills appeals to buyers who want established character and mature landscaping. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose helps buyers compare guard gated options across Summerlin on a regular basis. He recommends starting with lifestyle priorities rather than price. A buyer who values a clubhouse and fitness center will gravitate toward Reverence. A golfer will lean toward Red Rock Country Club. A buyer seeking maximum privacy and custom architecture will prefer The Ridges. Starting with what matters most narrows the search quickly.
