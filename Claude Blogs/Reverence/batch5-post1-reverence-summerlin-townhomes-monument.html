# Reverence Summerlin Townhomes: Monument by Pulte Homes Monument at Reverence is the only townhome neighborhood within the Reverence village in Summerlin. Developed by Pulte Homes, it offers a lock and leave lifestyle inside a guard gated community, starting in the high $400,000s. For buyers who want the Reverence address without the price tag of a single family home in The Heights, Monument is the entry point. ## The Three Floor Plans All three Monument plans are two story with contemporary architecture and open concept layouts. The Branton is the most compact at 1,654 square feet. It works well for singles, couples, or investors looking for the smallest footprint with the lowest price point. The Cornwall adds flex space at 1,736 square feet, giving buyers a bit more room to work with for a guest area or home office. The Danbury is the largest at 1,869 square feet and includes a dedicated office space, making it the most popular choice for remote workers. Each plan includes a two car garage, modern finishes, and energy efficient construction that meets current building codes. ## What Monument Buyers Get Monument residents have full access to the Reverence guard gate, community trails, and the eight acre Reverence Park with its tennis, basketball, volleyball courts, walking track, and playground. The one limitation is that the private Heights Clubhouse, Skye Fitness center, resort pool, and pickleball courts are exclusive to Heights residents. The townhome format means reduced exterior maintenance. The HOA covers common area landscaping and shared structural elements, so owners spend less time on yard work and upkeep compared to a detached home. ## Who Buys at Monument Monument attracts a wide range of buyers: first time buyers entering the Summerlin market, downsizers who want less space but a premium location, remote workers who prioritize lifestyle over square footage, and investors drawn to the guard gated address at a sub $500,000 price point. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose sees Monument as one of the best value plays in Summerlin's guard gated market. No other gated community in the area offers new construction at this price point. He recommends the Danbury for buyers who work from home and the Branton for investors or downsizers focused on keeping costs low while staying inside the gate.
