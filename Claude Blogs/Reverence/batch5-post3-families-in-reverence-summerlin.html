# Families in Reverence Summerlin: What to Know Reverence is one of the most family friendly guard gated communities in Summerlin, combining safe streets, quality schools, and a deep set of outdoor amenities that kids and parents actually use daily. The community is not age restricted, and families with children make up a significant portion of the resident base. ## Safety for Kids The 24 hour guard gate means only authorized vehicles enter the community. For families, this translates to kids riding bikes, playing in front yards, and walking to the park without the traffic concerns of open neighborhoods. The streets inside Reverence are low speed residential roads with no through traffic, which makes the entire community feel like a contained, safe environment. ## Parks and Recreation The eight acre Reverence Park is the primary gathering spot for families. It includes a playground, two lighted tennis courts, a full basketball court, sand volleyball, a walking track, and covered picnic areas. The park is open daily until 10:30 PM, giving families plenty of time for after dinner activities during the warmer months. For Heights residents, the resort pool with beach entry is family friendly, and the lap pool and spa provide options for parents who want their own space. The clubhouse hosts community events throughout the year, many of which are family oriented. ## Schools Reverence is zoned within the Clark County School District, with access to well rated schools including Judy and John L. Goolsby Elementary (9 out of 10 on GreatSchools) and Palo Verde High School, one of the top public high schools in Nevada. Private options including Faith Lutheran Academy and The Meadows School are within driving distance. ## Home Sizes for Families The Heights collections offer homes up to 4,815 square feet with four to five bedrooms, which provides room for growing families. Many floor plans feature the primary suite on the ground floor with additional bedrooms upstairs, a layout that works well when kids need their own space. Monument townhomes can also work for smaller families, particularly the 1,869 square foot Danbury plan. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose regularly works with families relocating to Las Vegas who want both safety and community. He notes that Reverence checks both boxes better than most alternatives because the guard gate provides real security while the park, trails, and clubhouse create spaces where neighbors actually interact.
