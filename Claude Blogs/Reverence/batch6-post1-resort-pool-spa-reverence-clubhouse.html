# Resort Pool and Spa at the Reverence Clubhouse The pool complex at the Reverence Clubhouse is designed to feel like a boutique resort rather than a standard community pool. Exclusive to Heights at Reverence residents, the aquatic facilities include a resort style main pool, a dedicated lap pool, and a heated spa, all set against the backdrop of the Spring Mountains. ## Resort Pool The main pool features a beach entry (also called a zero entry), which slopes gradually into the water rather than starting with steps or a ledge. This design is family friendly for young children and creates a relaxed, resort like atmosphere for adults. A wet deck area adjacent to the pool provides shallow water lounging space. The pool deck includes lounge chairs, shaded cabanas, and seating areas. The western mountain views from the pool area are a signature feature, making sunset swims one of the community's most popular experiences. ## Lap Pool A separate 1,600 square foot lap pool sits alongside the resort pool, providing dedicated lanes for fitness swimming. The separation means lap swimmers are not competing with recreational users, which is a practical advantage that most community pools do not offer. The lap pool is sized and designed for actual workout swimming, not just casual use. ## Heated Spa The heated spa is adjacent to both pools and available year round. During the cooler winter months in Las Vegas (when evening temperatures can drop into the 40s and 50s), the spa gets heavy use from residents who want to enjoy the outdoor water amenities without the cold of an unheated pool. ## Pool Area Atmosphere The clubhouse pool area is where much of Reverence's social life happens, particularly on weekends and during the warmer months. The resort layout encourages lingering rather than quick dips, and the cabana seating makes it practical to spend an afternoon with a book or laptop. Fire features on the patio extend the gathering space into the evening. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose highlights the pool complex as one of the amenities that separates Reverence from other guard gated communities. Many gated neighborhoods have a pool, but few have a resort pool, a separate lap pool, and a spa in one complex. The beach entry, cabanas, and mountain views make it feel like a destination rather than a neighborhood add on. Contact Ryan Rose to tour the Reverence Clubhouse pool complex.
