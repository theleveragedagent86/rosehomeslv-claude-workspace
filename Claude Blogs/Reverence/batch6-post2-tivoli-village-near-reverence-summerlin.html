# Tivoli Village Near Reverence Summerlin Tivoli Village is an upscale outdoor shopping and dining destination located a short drive southeast of Reverence in Summerlin. Its European inspired architecture, curated tenant mix, and patio dining atmosphere make it a popular complement to the larger Downtown Summerlin center for Reverence residents. ## What Makes Tivoli Village Different While Downtown Summerlin is a large scale retail center with national chains and high foot traffic, Tivoli Village has a more intimate, boutique feel. The architecture draws from Italian and Mediterranean influences, with stone facades, covered walkways, fountains, and landscaped courtyards. The scale is smaller and the atmosphere is quieter, which appeals to residents looking for a more relaxed outing. The tenant mix leans toward specialty retailers, locally owned boutiques, design showrooms, and professional services rather than big box stores. This makes Tivoli Village a destination for specific shopping trips and dining rather than general retail browsing. ## Dining at Tivoli Village The dining options at Tivoli Village skew upscale casual to fine dining, with several restaurants offering outdoor patio seating. The patio atmosphere is especially popular during the cooler months (October through April) when Las Vegas weather is ideal for outdoor dining. Wine bars, craft cocktail lounges, and dessert spots round out the food and drink scene. For Reverence residents, Tivoli Village serves as the go to spot for date nights, business dinners, and occasions that call for a step above casual. The drive from Reverence takes approximately 10 to 15 minutes. ## Shopping The retail lineup includes home furnishing stores, clothing boutiques, and specialty shops that are not found at Downtown Summerlin. Several wellness and beauty businesses also operate within the village, adding a self care and lifestyle component to the shopping options. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose recommends Tivoli Village to buyers who value a walkable, aesthetically appealing dining and shopping experience.
