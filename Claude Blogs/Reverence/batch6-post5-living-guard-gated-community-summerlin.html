# Living in a Guard Gated Community in Summerlin Guard gated communities are one of the defining features of Summerlin's luxury real estate landscape. Multiple communities across the master plan offer 24 hour staffed gate access, each with a different combination of amenities, home styles, and price points. For buyers considering this lifestyle, understanding what guard gated living actually means on a day to day basis helps set realistic expectations. ## What the Guard Gate Provides A 24 hour staffed guard gate means every vehicle entering the community passes through a checkpoint. Visitors must be pre approved by residents or verified at the gate before entering. Delivery drivers, service providers, and guests all go through the same screening. This controlled access eliminates drive through traffic and significantly reduces opportunistic crime, package theft, and solicitation. The practical effect is a noticeably quieter neighborhood. Streets inside guard gated communities carry only residential traffic, which means less noise, lower vehicle speeds, and a safer environment for walking, cycling, and outdoor play. ## The HOA Component Guard gated communities require higher HOA fees to fund gate staffing, maintenance, and the amenities that typically come with gated living. In Summerlin, monthly HOA fees for guard gated communities generally range from $200 to $400 or more depending on the level of amenities. Buyers should factor this into their monthly housing budget alongside the mortgage, insurance, and property taxes. The trade off is that well funded HOAs maintain common areas, landscaping, and amenities at a consistently high standard. This protects property values and ensures the community looks the same in year 10 as it did in year 1. ## Privacy and Community Guard gated communities strike a balance between privacy and community. The gate provides a boundary from the outside, while shared amenities like clubhouses, pools, parks, and trails create social spaces within. Residents tend to use these shared spaces more actively than in open neighborhoods because the amenities feel exclusive and well maintained. The community size matters here. Smaller gated communities (under 700 homes) like Reverence tend to feel more connected, while larger ones can feel more spread out. ## Property Value Premium Guard gated homes in Summerlin typically sell at a 10 to 20 percent premium over comparable non gated homes in the same area. This premium reflects the security, exclusivity, and amenity access that comes with gated living. The premium has held steady over time, making guard gated homes a strong store of value. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose helps buyers weigh the guard gated decision in almost every Summerlin transaction. He notes that once buyers experience the quiet streets, the controlled access, and the well maintained amenities, most will not go back to an open neighborhood.
