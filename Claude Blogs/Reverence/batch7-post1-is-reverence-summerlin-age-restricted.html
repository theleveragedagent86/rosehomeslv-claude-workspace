# Is Reverence Summerlin Age Restricted? No. Reverence in Summerlin is not an age restricted community. There are no minimum age requirements for buyers, and residents of all ages live in the community including families with young children, working professionals, and retirees. ## How Reverence Differs from 55 Plus Communities Las Vegas has several age restricted communities where at least one resident in each household must be 55 or older. Sun City Summerlin, Sun City Anthem, and Solera are examples. These communities limit who can purchase and sometimes restrict how long younger family members can stay. Reverence has none of these restrictions. Any buyer who qualifies for a home purchase can buy in the community regardless of age. This makes it an option for multigenerational families who want grandparents and adult children living in the same neighborhood, which is not possible in a 55 plus community. ## Why Retirees Still Choose Reverence Despite not being age restricted, Reverence attracts a significant number of retirees and active adults. The guard gated security, resort style clubhouse, Skye Fitness center, pickleball courts, and walking trails all appeal to retirees who want an active lifestyle without the constraints of an age restricted HOA. The Monument townhomes also offer a low maintenance, lock and leave format that retirees who travel frequently find practical. Starting in the high $400,000s, Monument provides an entry point that is competitive with many 55 plus communities in the valley. ## Benefits of a Non Age Restricted Community Living in a mixed age community keeps property demand broad.
