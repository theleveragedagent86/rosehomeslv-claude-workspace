# What Builder Developed Reverence in Summerlin? Pulte Homes is the sole builder and developer of Reverence in Summerlin. Unlike many master planned communities that feature multiple builders with competing designs, Reverence was developed exclusively by Pulte from its opening in 2017 through the current Monument townhome phase. ## About Pulte Homes Pulte Homes is one of the largest homebuilders in the United States, operating in markets across the country. The company has built homes in the Las Vegas valley for decades and has a track record of delivering communities with consistent design standards and build quality. Pulte is known for its Life Tested design philosophy, which prioritizes how homeowners actually use their spaces rather than maximizing raw square footage. In Reverence, this approach shows up in practical details throughout the floor plans: oversized pantries, drop zones near the garage entry, flexible bonus rooms, and primary suites positioned on the ground floor in many plans. ## What Single Builder Means for Reverence Having one builder for the entire community creates several advantages. Every home in Reverence shares a consistent construction standard, which means the community ages uniformly rather than having pockets of higher and lower quality construction. The architectural styles (Craftsman, Prairie, Hacienda, Farmhouse, and Desert Contemporary) were all designed by the same team, so while the exteriors vary, the overall aesthetic is cohesive. Single builder communities also tend to have fewer disputes between builders and the HOA, since Pulte designed the community infrastructure, amenities, and homes as a unified plan rather than negotiating shared spaces among competing builders. ## Current and Completed Neighborhoods Pulte developed The Heights at Reverence first, delivering approximately 480 single family homes across seven neighborhoods and four collections. These homes range from roughly 2,156 to 4,815 square feet. Monument at Reverence is the newest neighborhood, offering townhome style living with three floor plans between 1,654 and 1,869 square feet. Monument represents the final development phase within the 300 acre community. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose notes that buyers who have purchased Pulte homes in other markets often seek out Reverence specifically because of the builder's reputation. The consistent build quality across the community also simplifies the buying process on resale, since buyers can research Pulte's construction methods once and apply that knowledge to any home in Reverence.
