# Luxury Homes in Reverence Summerlin The Heights at Reverence is where Summerlin's luxury guard gated living meets modern Pulte Homes construction. Single family homes in The Heights range from approximately 2,156 to 4,815 square feet with prices from roughly $650,000 to over $1.1 million, placing them in the upper tier of Summerlin real estate without crossing into the ultra luxury pricing of communities like The Ridges. ## What Defines Luxury in Reverence Luxury in Reverence is a combination of home quality, community amenities, and location. The homes themselves feature open concept floor plans, gourmet kitchens with center islands and walk in pantries, oversized primary suites with spa style bathrooms, and multi car garages. Finishes include granite or quartz countertops, tile flooring, and upgraded cabinetry. Many homes include covered patios designed for outdoor living, which is particularly valuable given the mountain views available from western lots. The guard gate, clubhouse access, and 3,200 foot elevation add layers of exclusivity that go beyond the home itself. These community level features are what move Reverence from "nice Summerlin neighborhood" to "luxury community." ## View Lots and Premiums The most desirable luxury homes in Reverence sit on the western edge of The Heights, facing the Spring Mountains and Red Rock Canyon. These view lots command a premium of $50,000 to $100,000 over comparable interior lots. East facing lots on higher ground offer Las Vegas Strip views, which are especially dramatic at night. View lots are limited by the community's topography, and when they come on the market, they tend to attract strong interest quickly. ## Larger Floor Plans The largest Pulte floor plans in Reverence reach 4,815 square feet and include five bedrooms, bonus rooms, and three car garages. These plans are typically two story with the primary suite on the ground floor, which gives owners single level living for daily use with guest rooms and additional living space upstairs. For buyers who need space for a home office, home gym, or multigenerational living, the larger Heights collections deliver without requiring a custom build. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose positions Reverence as one of the best values in Summerlin's luxury tier. Buyers get newer construction (2017 and later), a full amenity package, and guard gated security at a price point significantly below The Ridges. For buyers who want luxury living without a $2 million price tag, Reverence consistently delivers the best combination of home quality and community features.
