# Palo Verde High School in Summerlin Palo Verde High School is the primary public high school serving Summerlin West communities including Reverence. Located in the heart of Summerlin, the school enrolls over 3,100 students in grades 9 through 12 and consistently ranks among the top public high schools in Nevada. ## Academics Palo Verde offers a comprehensive academic program that includes Advanced Placement (AP) courses across multiple subjects, honors level classes, and career and technical education pathways. The school's AP participation rate is above average for Clark County, and students regularly earn qualifying scores for college credit. The school operates on a standard Clark County School District calendar and follows the district's graduation requirements. College preparation resources include counseling services, SAT/ACT prep support, and partnerships with local colleges for dual enrollment opportunities. ## Athletics and Extracurriculars Palo Verde fields competitive teams across a full range of varsity sports including football, basketball, baseball, soccer, swimming, tennis, and track and field. The school has a history of strong performances in regional and state competitions, and its athletic facilities include a stadium, gymnasium, pool, and practice fields. Beyond athletics, the school offers clubs and organizations spanning academic interests, performing arts, student government, and community service. The extracurricular landscape is broad enough that most students can find activities aligned with their interests. ## Location and Access from Reverence Palo Verde High School is located at 333 South Pavilion Center Drive in Las Vegas, approximately 10 to 15 minutes south of Reverence via the 215 Beltway. The commute is straightforward and avoids the heavier traffic corridors on the east side of the valley. ## School Zoning School zoning in Clark County is determined by residential address.
