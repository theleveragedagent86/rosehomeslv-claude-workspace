# Rogich Middle School Near Reverence Summerlin Rogich Middle School is one of the closest public middle schools to Reverence in Summerlin, located in The Paseos Village. The school serves students in grades 6 through 8 and is part of the Clark County School District. ## Academics and Programs Rogich Middle School offers a standard Clark County middle school curriculum with core subjects in English, math, science, and social studies. The school also provides elective options including performing arts, visual arts, physical education, and technology courses. Honors level classes are available for students who qualify, providing an accelerated academic track heading into high school. The school feeds into Palo Verde High School, which is one of the top rated public high schools in Nevada. This academic pipeline gives families a clear path from middle school through graduation within the Summerlin school system. ## Location and Access Rogich Middle School is located in The Paseos Village of Summerlin, approximately 10 to 15 minutes south of Reverence. The drive follows the 215 Beltway corridor, which is the same route used for most Summerlin commutes. The school is well positioned within the Summerlin master plan and draws students from multiple surrounding villages. ## School Environment Summerlin middle schools benefit from the overall community demographics: well maintained neighborhoods, active parent involvement, and strong HOA standards in the surrounding areas. These factors contribute to a positive school environment and consistent expectations for student behavior and academic effort. ## Zoning Considerations Middle school zoning in Clark County is determined by residential address, and boundaries can change.
