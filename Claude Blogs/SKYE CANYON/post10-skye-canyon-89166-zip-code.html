The 89166 ZIP code covers Skye Canyon, Providence, and parts of northwest Las Vegas, with a population of 27,060, a median household income of $106,060, and a CrimeGrade rating of B+ that ranks it among the safer ZIPs in the metro.

Where is 89166?

The 89166 ZIP code sits in northwest Las Vegas, near the US 95 and 215 freeway corridor. It includes Skye Canyon, the neighboring Providence master plan, and additional parts of the northwest growth area. The total population is 27,060, with about 5,108 of those residents living inside Skye Canyon proper.

Elevation in the ZIP averages about 2,188 feet, which is high enough to bring slightly cooler temperatures and broader mountain views than the valley floor. Centennial Hills Hospital is the nearest major medical center.

Who lives in 89166?

Demographics for 89166 lean family oriented. About 75 percent of households are families, the median age is 36, and the average household size is 2.96. The population is 48.9 percent White, 20.8 percent Hispanic, and 10.3 percent Black, with a 48 percent male and 52 percent female split. The median household income of $106,060, reported for 2023, runs above the Las Vegas metro average.

School options in or near 89166 include Arbor View High School with its STEM magnet program, Centennial High School, Shadow Ridge High School, and the K through 12 Somerset Academy. Safety scores are a draw too. CrimeGrade rates the area B+, which ranks it among the safer ZIP codes in Las Vegas.
