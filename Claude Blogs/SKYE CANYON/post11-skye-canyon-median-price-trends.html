Skye Canyon's median home price reached $586,500 in February 2026, up 2.6% year over year, according to Redfin. The northwest Las Vegas master-planned community continues steady growth alongside ongoing new construction.

What is the current median price in Skye Canyon?

The February 2026 median sale price in Skye Canyon sits at $586,500, with other market sources placing values between $569,820 and $595,000 depending on how they slice the data. Year over year, prices climbed 2.6%, a modest but consistent gain that reflects healthy demand in the 89166 zip code. Active inventory typically runs between 100 and 150 homes at any given time, which keeps the market balanced rather than overheated.

Price ranges within Skye Canyon stretch from the $380s for LGI Topaz townhomes to over $900,000 for Toll Brothers luxury builds in Montrose. This wide spread means the community attracts first-time buyers, move-up families, and luxury purchasers alike. The mix of new construction and resale homes also keeps the median fluid as builders release new phases.

What is driving Skye Canyon price trends?

Several factors support continued price stability. Skye Canyon ranks among the top 50 master-planned communities in the United States, sometimes climbing to number 22 by new home sales, which signals strong national buyer interest. Resident satisfaction hits 92%, and the 89166 zip code reports a median household income of $106,060, both of which support sustained demand.

Ryan Rose points out that buyers shopping Skye Canyon often weigh new construction against resale, since the community is still actively building toward its planned 6,500 to 9,000 homes on 1,700 acres. New phases can shift the median upward as builders price in newer designs and amenities, while resale homes from the 2014 to 2020 era often offer better value per square foot.
