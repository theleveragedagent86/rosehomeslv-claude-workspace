What Skye Canyon homes are available under $500K?

The most affordable Skye Canyon homes in 2026 are at LGI Topaz, a 49-site townhome enclave that opened in April 2026. Floor plans run from the Charleston at 1,256 square feet to the Wheeler at 1,665 square feet, with the Frenchman and Matterhorn in between. Pricing starts in the $380s and includes the CompleteHome Plus package with appliances, blinds, and upgraded finishes.

Beazer Ravenna is a gated single-family community with plans from 1,916 to 2,532 square feet that historically launched in the low $300s, though current pricing has risen with the market. Century Eaglepointe offers two-story homes from 2,126 to 2,328 square feet in the mid-$400s, including up to five bedrooms with loft and courtyard options on some plans.

Why are these the affordable Skye Canyon options?

The Skye Canyon median sale price reached $586,500 in February 2026, so any home under $500K sits well below that benchmark. Ryan Rose points out that price-conscious buyers in Skye Canyon should focus on townhomes and smaller single-family plans where the price per square foot stays close to the community average of $267 to $271. Inventory under $500K moves quickly because demand from first-time buyers and downsizers is steady year-round.

Most homes in this segment are newer construction with energy-efficient features, so monthly operating costs stay lower than older Las Vegas resale stock. Buyers should also factor in the Skye Canyon master HOA plus any sub-association fee for gated villages like Ravenna.
