What is the current median home price in Skye Canyon?

Skye Canyon's February 2026 median sits at $586,500 according to Redfin, with other tracking sources reporting figures between $569,820 and $595,000. The year over year change is positive at 2.6 percent, reflecting steady appreciation in northwest Las Vegas. Price per square foot averages between $267 and $271 across the community.

At any given time, roughly 100 to 150 homes are listed for sale inside Skye Canyon. That inventory mix includes resale homes, new construction, and quick move in inventory from active builders. Buyers can usually find both single story and two story floor plans on the market.

What is the price range across Skye Canyon?

The low end starts in the $380s with LGI Topaz townhomes, while the high end pushes past $900K with Toll Brothers luxury product. Ryan Rose, who frequently fields questions about Skye Canyon, explains that the wide price band lets buyers stay inside one master plan whether their budget is closer to $400K or closer to a million.

Active new construction builders in Skye Canyon include Toll Brothers, Lennar, Pulte, Century Communities, Beazer, LGI, Woodside, Richmond American, Pardee, and Taylor Morrison. Quick move in homes typically close in 30 to 90 days, while dirt starts run 6 to 10 months from contract. That timeline flexibility means motivated buyers and patient buyers can both find a fit.
