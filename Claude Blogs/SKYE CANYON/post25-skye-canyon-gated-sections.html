Skye Canyon is not a fully gated master plan, but it includes multiple gated sub-villages such as Aviano, Eaglepointe, Ravenna, Madera, Skye Mesa, Belterra, Pinewood, and Sage Reserve. All residents share access to the master amenities regardless of whether their specific neighborhood is gated.

Which Skye Canyon neighborhoods are gated?

Gated sub-villages in Skye Canyon include Aviano, Eaglepointe, Ravenna, Madera, Skye Mesa, Belterra, Pinewood, and Sage Reserve. These sections add private entry gates and, in many cases, a private interior streetscape, while still tying into the broader Skye Canyon master plan.

Non-gated neighborhoods sit alongside the gated sections and use the same public streets that connect to Skye Canyon Park Drive and Grand Canyon Drive. Both gated and non-gated residents share Skye Center, Skye Fitness, the parks, and the trail system.

How do HOA fees compare?

The Skye Canyon master HOA is $83 per month for every resident. On top of that, gated sub-villages typically carry an additional sub-association fee that ranges from roughly $50 to $120 per month, depending on the level of gated services and amenities.

Based on Ryan Rose's experience with gated vs non-gated buyers, the decision usually comes down to monthly budget and resale priorities. Gated homes often hold a small premium at resale, while non-gated homes deliver more amenity value per HOA dollar for buyers who do not need the gate.
