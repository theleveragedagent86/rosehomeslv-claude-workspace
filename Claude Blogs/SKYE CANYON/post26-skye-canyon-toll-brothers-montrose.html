What does the Montrose Collection at Skye Canyon offer?

Montrose is Toll Brothers' single-story luxury collection inside the Skye Canyon master plan in zip code 89166. Floor plans run from 2,031 to 2,370 square feet, with 10 foot ceilings creating an open, airy feel that buyers seeking a luxury ranch layout often request. Pricing starts in the $600s, placing Montrose at the upper end of the Skye Canyon new construction market.

The Montrose Collection sits in a mature section of Skye Canyon with full access to community amenities. Buyers get high-end standard finishes typical of Toll Brothers, including upgraded cabinetry, quartz or stone countertops, and energy-efficient construction. The single-story format appeals to move-down luxury buyers and active adults who want main-floor living without sacrificing square footage.

Who is the Montrose Collection best suited for?

Ryan Rose points out that Toll Brothers buyers at Montrose typically include empty nesters trading down from larger two-story homes, retirees relocating from California or the Pacific Northwest, and professional couples who want a luxury single-story without leaving the master plan amenities behind. The 2,031 to 2,370 square foot range is generous for a ranch plan, making it competitive with similar single-story luxury offerings in Summerlin and Inspirada.

Skye Canyon's master HOA runs $83 per month, with sub-village HOAs adding $50 to $120 depending on the section. Buyers should confirm the exact Montrose sub-HOA amount and gate status at contract, as some Toll Brothers sections are gated while the master plan as a whole is not.
