What is the Vista Rossa Collection at Skye Canyon?

Vista Rossa is the premier luxury anchor of Toll Brothers' Skye Canyon offerings. The collection features two-story homes ranging from 2,880 to 3,249 square feet, with the Montoro and Loma floor plans giving buyers options for layout and room count. Configurations include up to 6 bedrooms plus a loft, making this collection well suited for large families, multi-generational households, or buyers who want dedicated office and guest spaces.

Pricing for Vista Rossa starts in the $700K range and climbs based on lot premium, structural options, and design center upgrades. The Toll Brothers design center experience is a meaningful part of the cost, since buyers typically add $50,000 to $150,000 or more in upgrades depending on finish level.

How does Vista Rossa compare to other Skye Canyon options?

When clients ask Ryan Rose about Lennar and Pulte in Skye Canyon and how they stack up against Toll Brothers, the answer usually centers on scale and finish. Vista Rossa plans are larger than most Lennar and Pulte offerings in the master plan and come standard with higher-end finishes. The two-story format with up to 6 bedrooms is a defining feature; few Skye Canyon collections push past 3,000 square feet.

Vista Rossa buyers share the master amenities including Skye Center, Skye Fitness, parks, and trails, with master HOA of $83 per month plus the sub-village fee. The exact sub-HOA and gate status for Vista Rossa should be confirmed with the builder at contract.
