What was Belterra at Skye Canyon?

Belterra was Lennar's flagship two-story collection inside the Skye Canyon master plan, sold in two phases known as Belterra I and Belterra II. Floor plans ran from 1,560 to 2,270 square feet, designed for families needing flexible room counts, attached two-car garages, and modern open-concept layouts. The collection sold through during the active build phase and is now closed to new construction.

Every Belterra home was built under Lennar's Everything's Included program. That meant smart-home integration, appliance packages, and standard upgrade levels came with the base price rather than as add-ons. Buyers who purchased new at Belterra avoided the design center markup that other builders charge for similar features.

How do buyers get into Belterra today?

When clients ask Ryan Rose about Lennar in Skye Canyon, Belterra often comes up first because of its size range and family-friendly layouts. Since both phases are sold out, the only path in is the resale market. Inventory turns over regularly, and pricing tracks the broader Skye Canyon median, which sat at $586,500 in February 2026 per Redfin.

Belterra homes share full access to the master plan amenities including Skye Center, Skye Fitness, parks, and trails. The master HOA runs $83 per month, with the Belterra sub-HOA on top. Buyers should confirm the exact sub-HOA amount and whether the specific section is gated, since gate status varies across Skye Canyon villages.
