What is Hawthorne at Skye Canyon?

Hawthorne is Lennar's current active village inside the Skye Canyon master plan, marketed as an active-lifestyle community within the larger family-oriented master. The collection is actively building and actively selling, which gives buyers the chance to pick from available quick move-in homes or to build to order depending on inventory. Hawthorne is one of Lennar's two main entries inside Skye Canyon alongside the now sold-out Belterra collection.

Every Hawthorne home is built under Lennar's Everything's Included program. That means smart-home technology, appliance packages, and standard upgrade levels are bundled into the base price. Buyers do not face the same design center pressure that other builders create, which makes Hawthorne pricing simpler to evaluate against resale options.

Who is Hawthorne best suited for?

When clients ask Ryan Rose about Lennar in Skye Canyon, Hawthorne is usually the first option to walk because it is the active selling community. The active-lifestyle marketing angle appeals to buyers who want a slightly more curated village feel inside the broader master plan, with full access to the Skye Canyon amenity package. Pricing should be confirmed at the sales office, since active-build communities update base prices frequently based on phase release and incentives.

Hawthorne buyers share the master amenities including Skye Center, Skye Fitness, parks, and trails. The Skye Canyon master HOA runs $83 per month, with the Hawthorne sub-HOA adding $50 to $120 depending on the specific section. Gate status for the Hawthorne village should be confirmed directly, since the Skye Canyon master plan is not fully gated and gate status varies by sub-village.
