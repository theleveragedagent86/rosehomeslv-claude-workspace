What is Pinewood at Skye Canyon?

Pinewood is Pulte Homes' gated village inside the Skye Canyon master plan. The community launched in 2021 and offers 6 floor plans across single-story and two-story configurations, giving buyers room to choose by lifestyle and household size. The Parklane plan is among the offerings, alongside other Pulte favorites adapted to the Skye Canyon lot grid.

Pulte's standard build quality applies across Pinewood, with modern design features and energy-efficient construction. Pricing sits in the mid-range for Skye Canyon, which places it below the Toll Brothers Vista Rossa luxury tier and roughly in line with other mid-market builders in the master plan. Current pricing should be confirmed with the sales office, since Pulte adjusts base prices and incentives by phase release.

Why does Pinewood being gated matter?

Ryan Rose, who has walked Pulte Pinewood model homes, notes that the gated status is a meaningful differentiator inside Skye Canyon. The master plan as a whole is not gated, so the gated sub-villages like Pinewood appeal to buyers who specifically want the controlled access. That feature tends to support resale value, especially for buyers relocating from California where gated communities are more common.

Pinewood buyers share the master amenities including Skye Center, Skye Fitness, parks, and trails. The master HOA runs $83 per month with the Pinewood sub-HOA on top, falling within the $50 to $120 range typical for Skye Canyon sub-villages. Single-story plans inside Pinewood are particularly competitive, since single-story new construction is in shorter supply across the master plan.

Local Insight from Ryan Rose

Pinewood is a strong middle-ground option in Skye Canyon. Buyers who want gated access without the luxury price tag of Toll Brothers find it a good fit, and the mix of single-story and two-story plans accommodates both downsizers and growing families. Ryan Rose can walk Pinewood models and compare them against Hawthorne, Montrose, and current resale inventory inside the master plan.
