Century Aspen at Skye Canyon offers two-story homes ranging from 1,700 to 2,300 square feet. This Century Communities village sits inside the Skye Canyon master plan in northwest Las Vegas with full access to Skye Center amenities.

What does Century Aspen at Skye Canyon offer?

Aspen is one of several Century Communities villages inside Skye Canyon. The community features two-story floor plans in the 1,700 to 2,300 square foot range, which places it in the mid-size segment of Century's Skye Canyon lineup. Buyers shopping Aspen typically want a multi-level layout without stretching into the larger 2,400+ square foot plans found in nearby Northglenn.

Every Aspen homeowner shares the master Skye Canyon amenity package. That includes the Skye Center clubhouse, Skye Fitness facility, five parks, and miles of trails connecting throughout the community. The master HOA runs $83 per month with a sub-HOA on top that varies by neighborhood between $50 and $120 per month.

Who is Century Aspen built for?

Ryan Rose points out that Century Aspen buyers are usually families or move-up buyers who want a two-story home in Skye Canyon without paying the premium of Toll Brothers Montrose or the larger Northglenn plans. The 1,700 to 2,300 square foot range works well for households needing three or four bedrooms with reasonable utility costs.

Aspen also benefits from Skye Canyon's location near US 95, which gives commuters a direct route into the broader Las Vegas valley. Northwest Las Vegas continues to grow, and Skye Canyon has become one of the most active master plans in the 89166 zip code.

Local Insight from Ryan Rose

Aspen lands in a useful price band for buyers who want new construction in Skye Canyon but do not need a 2,500+ square foot plan. The two-story design also keeps the lot footprint smaller, which can help with overall pricing.
