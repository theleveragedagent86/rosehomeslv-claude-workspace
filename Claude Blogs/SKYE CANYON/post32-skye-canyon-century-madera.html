Century Madera at Skye Canyon is a gated village of all single-story homes ranging from 1,636 to 1,816 square feet. Located in the Skye Canyon master plan in northwest Las Vegas, Madera features 10 foot ceilings, quartz baths, and a modern aesthetic.

What makes Century Madera at Skye Canyon different?

Madera stands out from other Century Communities villages at Skye Canyon for three reasons. It sits behind a gate, which is uncommon in the master plan. Every home is single-story, with no two-story floor plans offered. And the size range stays compact at 1,636 to 1,816 square feet, which keeps the layout efficient and the utility footprint manageable.

Inside the homes, Madera leans modern. Ten foot ceilings open up the main living spaces, quartz countertops come standard in the baths, and optional courtyards are available on select plans. The aesthetic skews contemporary rather than traditional, which appeals to buyers looking for cleaner lines in a new build.

Who buys at Century Madera?

When clients ask Ryan Rose about Madera single-story homes, the answer usually points to two buyer types. The first is downsizers who want one-level living without stairs but still want new construction with modern finishes. The second is small households who value a gated address and prefer the privacy of a smaller community within the larger master plan.

Madera homeowners pay the Skye Canyon master HOA of $83 per month plus a sub-HOA that covers the gate and Madera specific costs. They still get full access to Skye Center, Skye Fitness, the five parks, and trail system that serves the entire master plan.
