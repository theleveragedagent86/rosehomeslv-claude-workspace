Century Northglenn at Skye Canyon offers larger two-story homes from 2,396 to 2,605 square feet with 4 to 5 bedrooms and an optional casita. This Century Communities village sits inside the Skye Canyon master plan in northwest Las Vegas.

What does Century Northglenn at Skye Canyon include?

Northglenn is the larger family-focused village among Century's Skye Canyon lineup. Floor plans run from 2,396 to 2,605 square feet, which puts Northglenn above Aspen and well above Madera in total living space. The homes are two-story with 4 to 5 bedroom configurations depending on the plan.

One of the most useful Northglenn features is the optional casita. A casita gives multigenerational households, long term guests, or work-from-home buyers a separate ground floor space with its own access. That kind of layout is rare at this price point in Skye Canyon, which makes Northglenn a strong fit for buyers who need flexibility.

Who is Northglenn built for?

Ryan Rose, who has toured Northglenn floor plans, places this community in front of two buyer types most often. The first is growing families who need 4 or 5 bedrooms plus dedicated living areas on the main level. The second is multigenerational households where parents, in-laws, or adult children need their own space, which is where the casita option really pays off.

Like every Century village in Skye Canyon, Northglenn buyers get full access to the master amenity package: Skye Center clubhouse, Skye Fitness, five parks, and the trail system. Master HOA is $83 per month with a sub-HOA on top that varies by sub-community.

Local Insight from Ryan Rose

Northglenn is the Century plan I send to buyers who say the word casita on the first call. Multigen housing is one of the strongest demand drivers in the Las Vegas market right now, and Northglenn is one of the few new construction options inside Skye Canyon that addresses it directly.
