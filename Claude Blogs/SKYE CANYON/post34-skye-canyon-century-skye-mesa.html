Century Skye Mesa at Skye Canyon launched in 2021 as Century Communities' premium village inside the Skye Canyon master plan. The community features modern energy efficient construction with strong access to Skye Canyon's master amenity package.

What is Century Skye Mesa at Skye Canyon?

Skye Mesa is one of the newer Century Communities releases inside Skye Canyon, having launched in 2021 after Century took over the remaining master plan acreage. It positions itself as a premium product within Century's Skye Canyon lineup, with a focus on modern design language and energy efficient construction practices.

The community sits inside the Skye Canyon master plan, which means homeowners get full access to Skye Center, Skye Fitness, the five parks, and the trail system. The master HOA stays at $83 per month with a Skye Mesa sub-HOA layered on top to cover the village specific costs.

Who is Skye Mesa built for?

Based on Ryan Rose's experience with Skye Mesa, this village tends to attract buyers who want a newer release date and the latest Century floor plans rather than legacy designs. Buyers prioritizing energy efficiency, modern finishes, and the most current Century construction standards usually start their search here when comparing Century villages.

Skye Mesa also benefits from its position inside the broader Skye Canyon master plan. Buyers get the master amenity access along with Skye Canyon's location in northwest Las Vegas off US 95. The 89166 zip code continues to be one of the most active new construction submarkets in the Las Vegas valley.
