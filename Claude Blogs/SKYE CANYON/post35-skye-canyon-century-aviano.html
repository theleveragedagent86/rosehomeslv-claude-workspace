Century Aviano at Skye Canyon is a gated village offering all single-story homes across 3 floor plans from 1,805 to 2,029 square feet. Aviano residents also enjoy a private community pool on top of the Skye Canyon master amenities.

What does Century Aviano at Skye Canyon offer?

Aviano is one of the most desirable Century Communities villages at Skye Canyon for buyers who want one-level living behind a gate. The community offers 3 floor plans ranging from 1,805 to 2,029 square feet, every home is single-story, and the entire village is gated. The size range slots Aviano just above Madera in living space.

What separates Aviano from the rest of Skye Canyon is the private community pool. Aviano residents get their own pool in addition to full access to Skye Center, Skye Fitness, the five master parks, and the trail system. That kind of layered amenity stack is rare inside the master plan.

Who is Aviano built for?

Ryan Rose recommends Aviano to downsizers because the single-story footprint, gated entry, and private pool address the three things this buyer pool asks about most. The 1,805 to 2,029 square foot range gives downsizers enough room for guests or a home office without the maintenance burden of a 2,500+ square foot two-story plan.

Aviano also works for families who specifically want one-level living. With 3 floor plans to choose from, buyers can find a layout that fits their household without compromising on the gate or pool access. The Aviano sub-HOA covers the gate and pool maintenance on top of the $83 per month Skye Canyon master HOA.

Local Insight from Ryan Rose

Aviano is the Century village I put at the top of the list for downsizers who want gated single-story living plus a community pool. That combination is hard to find in northwest Las Vegas at this price point. Madera offers gated single-story too, but only Aviano has the private pool.
