Century Cantaro I at Skye Canyon

Century Cantaro I at Skye Canyon offers two-story new construction homes from 1,792 to 2,119 square feet, with up to 5 bedrooms plus a loft. It sits inside the master-planned Skye Canyon community in northwest Las Vegas, ZIP 89166.

What floor plans does Century Cantaro I offer?

Cantaro I features two-story plans ranging from 1,792 to 2,119 square feet. Buyers can choose layouts with up to 5 bedrooms, and a flexible upstairs loft is part of the floor plan mix. The footprint targets families who want more bedrooms without jumping into a larger square footage tier.

This sub-village is part of Century Communities at Skye Canyon, and it shares the master amenities every Skye Canyon homeowner accesses. Master HOA dues run $83 per month, and the sub-community has its own HOA fee on top. Sub-village gated status varies inside Skye Canyon, so confirm Cantaro I specifics before writing an offer.

Who fits the Cantaro I floor plan?

Ryan Rose points out that Cantaro buyers tend to be growing families and move-up buyers who want a fifth bedroom or a dedicated loft without paying for a full 3,000-square-foot home. The mid-range Century plans land between entry-level new construction and the larger Cantaro II and Eaglepointe footprints. That makes Cantaro I a good fit for buyers stretching from a starter home but still watching the monthly payment.

Skye Canyon's median sale price in February 2026 was $586,500, and Cantaro I tends to price near or just below that line depending on lot premiums and options. Move-in ready inventory, lot orientation, and design center upgrades all swing the final number.
