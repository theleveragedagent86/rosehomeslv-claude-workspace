Century Cantaro II at Skye Canyon

Century Cantaro II at Skye Canyon offers two-story new construction homes from 2,581 to 2,947 square feet, with up to 5 bedrooms. This sub-village sits inside Skye Canyon in northwest Las Vegas, ZIP 89166, and shares the master community amenities.

What does Century Cantaro II include?

Cantaro II is the larger sibling to Cantaro I and runs from 2,581 to 2,947 square feet across its two-story plans. Buyers can configure up to 5 bedrooms, and the larger footprint usually adds a separate game room, a bigger primary suite, or a more spacious kitchen and great room. These plans land in the upper-mid range of Century's Skye Canyon lineup.

Every Cantaro II home is part of the master-planned Skye Canyon community. Master HOA dues are $83 per month, with a separate sub-community fee on top. Gated status varies across Skye Canyon sub-villages, so check Cantaro II specifically before assuming.

Who fits the Cantaro II layout?

Ryan Rose points out that Cantaro II buyers usually need a real fifth bedroom, a dedicated office, or a second living space for older kids. Families moving up from a 1,800 to 2,200 square foot home often land here because the jump to 2,500 plus square feet changes how the household actually uses the home day to day. The trade-off is a higher monthly payment and a larger maintenance footprint.

With Skye Canyon's February 2026 median at $586,500, Cantaro II homes typically price above the community median given the extra square footage and bedroom count. Lot premiums, design center options, and incentives at contract drive most of the variation in final price.

Local Insight from Ryan Rose

Cantaro II makes sense when the square footage actually gets used, multi-generational households, a real work-from-home office, or growing kids who need separation. Buyers who do not need the extra bedroom often get better value in Cantaro I or a comparable Eaglepointe plan.
