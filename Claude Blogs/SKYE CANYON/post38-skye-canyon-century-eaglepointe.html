Century Eaglepointe at Skye Canyon

Century Eaglepointe at Skye Canyon offers two-story new construction homes from 2,126 to 2,328 square feet, with up to 5 bedrooms and large lofts or courtyards. Pricing starts from the mid-$400s, making it one of the stronger value points for new construction at Skye Canyon.

What floor plans does Eaglepointe offer?

Eaglepointe is a tightly defined plan range, 2,126 to 2,328 square feet, all two-story. The plans accommodate up to 5 bedrooms, and Century built in large lofts or interior courtyards as flex space depending on the elevation. That gives buyers room to add a game room, a dedicated home office, or an indoor-outdoor sitting area without redesigning the layout.

Eaglepointe sits inside the master-planned Skye Canyon community in ZIP 89166. Master HOA dues are $83 per month, with a sub-community fee on top. Gated status varies between Skye Canyon sub-villages, so confirm Eaglepointe's specifics with the builder before assuming.

Why is Eaglepointe a Skye Canyon value play?

When clients ask Ryan Rose about Eaglepointe value, the answer usually comes back to price per square foot relative to the rest of Skye Canyon's new construction. Starting in the mid-$400s for plans above 2,100 square feet keeps Eaglepointe under the February 2026 Skye Canyon median of $586,500. That gap leaves room for design center upgrades, lot premiums, or just a lower monthly payment.

The loft or courtyard options matter for resale, too. Buyers who use the flex space as a real fourth or fifth bedroom protect their bedroom count when it is time to sell, which is usually the most important number on a listing.
