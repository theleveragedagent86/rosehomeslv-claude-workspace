Beazer Sage Reserve at Skye Canyon (Skye Hills) is now sold out, but resale opportunities still exist. The community featured 4 single-story plans plus 1 two-story plan from 1,750 to 3,179 square feet, with DOE Zero Energy Ready certification and an optional Tesla Solar Roof.

What made Sage Reserve different?

Sage Reserve was a flagship Beazer community for energy-efficient construction at Skye Canyon. Every home earned DOE Zero Energy Ready certification, which means the homes are built to a level where a renewable energy system can offset most or all of the annual energy use. Beazer also offered a Tesla Solar Roof option, an unusual feature in Las Vegas new construction.

The plan mix included 4 single-story floor plans plus 1 two-story plan, ranging from 1,750 to 3,179 square feet. Featured plan names include Summit, Zion, and Everett. That mix is heavy on single-story product, which is a meaningful match for the Skye Canyon buyer pool that consistently asks for single-level living.
