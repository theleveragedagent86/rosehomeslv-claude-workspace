Townhomes in Skye Canyon: Complete Guide

Townhomes are a small but important slice of Skye Canyon. The master plan is mostly single-family detached, so the few attached product offerings stand out for buyers who want a lower price point or less maintenance.

Who builds townhomes in Skye Canyon?

LGI Homes is the primary townhome builder in Skye Canyon today. Topaz at Skye Canyon opened in April 2026 with 49 townhome sites. Pricing starts in the low $380s, and the four floor plans range from 1,256 square feet (Charleston) to 1,665 square feet (Wheeler). Every Topaz home includes appliances, blinds, and smart home features through LGI's CompleteHome Plus package.

No other active builder inside Skye Canyon is selling new townhomes right now. Toll Brothers, Lennar, Pulte, Century, and Beazer are all detached single-family in this master plan, which is why Topaz fills a real gap for first-time buyers, investors, and downsizers.

Why consider a Skye Canyon townhome?

When clients ask Ryan Rose about townhome options in Skye Canyon, the conversation usually starts with three buyer types: first-time buyers who want to break in below $400K, investors looking for a low maintenance rental, and downsizers who want the master plan amenities without a big yard.

Townhome owners get the same access to Skye Canyon Park, the fitness center, pools, dog park, and trail network as single-family residents. The HOA structure on a townhome covers more exterior maintenance, which is the trade-off for the lower price and smaller footprint.
