Skye Fitness is the heart of the active healthy living brand at Skye Canyon. The roughly 10,000 square foot resident gym sits inside the Skye Canyon Park amenity hub and serves every household in the master plan.

What is Skye Fitness and what does it include?

Skye Fitness is a state-of-the-art resident-only fitness center at 10115 W. Skye Canyon Park Dr., Las Vegas NV 89166. The roughly 10,000 square foot facility includes a full equipment floor, a dedicated yoga room, a sports field, a basketball court, and an on-site juice bar.

The Junior Olympic pool, spa, and splash pad sit on the same property. That means residents can lift weights, take a yoga class, shoot hoops, and cool off in the pool without leaving the amenity campus. Hours are posted at the facility and shift slightly by season.

Who can use Skye Fitness?

Skye Fitness is resident-only. Each household may bring up to 5 guests, and the resident must remain present the entire time. Access is included in the master HOA fee, which currently runs about $83 per month.

Ryan Rose points out that Skye Fitness is the amenity buyers ask about most often after they tour the community. Pairing a true 10,000 square foot gym with a Junior Olympic pool inside one master plan is rare in northwest Las Vegas, and many residents tell him they cancelled outside gym memberships after moving in.
