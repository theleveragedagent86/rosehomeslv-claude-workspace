The Skye Center Clubhouse is the social heart of Skye Canyon. The 8,000 square foot building sits next to Skye Fitness and houses Aspire Coffee House, multi-purpose event rooms, and gathering space for the whole community.

What is the Skye Center Clubhouse?

Skye Center is an 8,000 square foot clubhouse at 10111 W. Skye Canyon Park Dr., adjacent to the Skye Fitness facility. The building works as a community hub. Residents use it for yoga classes, fitness classes, neighborhood events, holiday gatherings, and casual meetups.

The multi-purpose event spaces inside Skye Center support classes during the day and larger community events on weekends. Because the building sits next to Skye Fitness and the Junior Olympic pool, residents can drop in for coffee, a class, or an event without driving anywhere else.

What is Aspire Coffee House inside the clubhouse?

Aspire Coffee House is a women-owned cafe that opened inside Skye Center in July 2021. The shop serves Starbucks beans, espresso drinks, pastries, and casual breakfast items. It functions as the unofficial living room of Skye Canyon, where neighbors run into each other and remote workers post up with a laptop.

When clients ask Ryan Rose about the Skye Center, he describes it as the piece that turns Skye Canyon from a subdivision into a community. A real coffee shop owned by a neighbor, plus event space built for residents, gives the community a daily gathering point most master plans never get.
