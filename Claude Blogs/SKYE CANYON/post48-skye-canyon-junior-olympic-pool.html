The Junior Olympic pool at Skye Canyon is one of the most-used amenities in the master plan. Lap swimmers, families with kids, and residents looking to cool off all share the same campus next to Skye Fitness.

Where is the Junior Olympic pool at Skye Canyon?

The Junior Olympic pool sits on the Skye Canyon Park amenity campus, on the same property as Skye Fitness at the corner of Skye Canyon Park Drive. The pool runs lap lanes for fitness swimmers and offers a recreational area for families. A spa and a separate splash pad sit adjacent to the pool.

Hours run seasonally, with extended hours during the summer. The facility is gated and resident-only.

Who can use the pool and how does access work?

Access is included in the master HOA, which runs about $83 per month. The pool follows the same rules as Skye Fitness. Each household may bring up to 5 guests, and the resident must remain on-site for the duration of the guest visit.

Ryan Rose, who has toured the Junior Olympic pool with multiple buyer families, tells clients that the splash pad next to the main pool is what closes the deal for parents with younger kids. Older kids and adults get real lap lanes, the toddlers get their own water feature, and everything sits inside one secure resident-only campus.

Local Insight from Ryan Rose

A Junior Olympic pool inside the HOA is unusual for northwest Las Vegas. Most master plans give you a recreational pool or a lap pool, not both, and almost none pair it with a splash pad and a 10,000 square foot gym next door.
