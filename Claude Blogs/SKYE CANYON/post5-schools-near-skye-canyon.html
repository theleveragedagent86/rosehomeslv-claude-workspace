Schools near Skye Canyon include Somerset Academy Skye Canyon (charter K-8, ranked top 18 of 379 Nevada elementaries), Scherkenbach, Bilbray, and Divich CCSD elementaries, Arbor View High School with a STEM magnet, and a brand new CCSD elementary opening in 2027-28.

Which schools serve Skye Canyon students?

Somerset Academy Skye Canyon anchors the community as a K-8 charter school. It ranks in the top 18 of 379 Nevada elementaries, sits in the 95.3rd percentile, beats 96.7 percent of Nevada middle schools, and posted 68.3 percent math proficiency in 2024-25. Enrollment runs around 996 students.

For CCSD families, three elementary options serve different parts of Skye Canyon. Scherkenbach Elementary on Iron Mountain Road sits about 5 minutes away and earns strong parent reviews. Bilbray Elementary on Brent Lane and Divich Elementary, which grew enrollment 9 percent in 2023-24, round out the public elementary mix.

What about middle school, high school, and future capacity?

Arbor View High School handles most Skye Canyon students at the high school level and offers a STEM magnet program, located roughly 10 minutes away. Some addresses are zoned to Centennial High School or Shadow Ridge High School instead, and Sig Rogich Middle School serves some Skye Canyon households. Coral Academy Centennial Hills, a STEM focused charter K-8, is another popular option nearby.

Based on Ryan Rose's experience working with Skye Canyon buyers and sellers, the most important update on the horizon is the new CCSD Skye Canyon Elementary breaking ground summer 2026 at Skye Canyon Park Drive and Shaumber Road. The 850 capacity campus is scheduled to open for the 2027-28 school year, which will relieve current zoning pressure and give the master plan a dedicated public elementary inside the community.

Local Insight from Ryan Rose

Ryan Rose tells buyers with school age children to verify the assigned CCSD elementary address by address, because Skye Canyon spans multiple attendance zones. If a Somerset Academy charter seat matters to the family, get on the lottery list early because waitlists fill quickly. The 2027-28 CCSD opening is a real differentiator and worth factoring into long term plans.
