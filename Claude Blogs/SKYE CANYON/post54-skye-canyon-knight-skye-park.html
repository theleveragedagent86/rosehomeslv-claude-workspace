Knight Skye Park is the Skye Canyon neighborhood park with the largest pickleball footprint and the biggest open lawns. It includes 3 pickleball courts, sprawling lawns, a covered tot lot, a splash pad, a gazebo, restrooms, and on-site parking, and it is open dawn to dusk.

What Is Knight Skye Park?

Knight Skye Park is one of four neighborhood parks in Skye Canyon, alongside Eagle Canyon, Skye View, and Big Skye. Its standout feature is the 3 pickleball courts, the most of any single park in the community. The wide lawns make it a popular spot for picnics, casual sports, and dog walking, and the covered tot lot keeps younger kids in shade.

The park also offers a splash pad for warm-weather use, a gazebo for shaded gatherings, and on-site restrooms. Parking is included, which is not true of every neighborhood park in the community, and access is open dawn to dusk for residents covered by the master HOA at $83 per month.

What Activities Does It Support?

Ryan Rose recommends Knight Skye Park to families with kids of mixed ages because the layout handles toddlers, pickleball-playing adults, and casual ball games on the lawn at the same time. The covered tot lot is the easiest sell for parents of younger children, and the 3 pickleball courts mean residents rarely have to wait for a court during off-peak hours.

The combination of parking, restrooms, and a gazebo also makes Knight Skye a realistic spot for small family gatherings. Trails and bike lanes connect it back to Skye Canyon Park and the other neighborhood parks for residents who prefer to move between amenities without driving.
