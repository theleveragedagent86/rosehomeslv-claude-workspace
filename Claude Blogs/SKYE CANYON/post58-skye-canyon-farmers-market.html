Las Vegas Farmers Market at Skye Canyon

The Las Vegas Farmers Market sets up at Skye Canyon every Thursday from 2pm to 6pm. It runs year-round at 10111 W. Skye Canyon Park Dr. The market is family-friendly, dog-friendly, and one of the most consistent weekly gatherings in the community.

What vendors show up each week?

The market draws a steady mix of local food vendors. Rod's Produce brings fresh local produce. Graze Food Truck serves charcuterie. Luchkoff Patisserie sells French desserts. A sourdough bread vendor and a fresh donut vendor round out the bakery options. There's also a Jamaican jerk chicken tent for hot prepared food on site.

The mix changes a little week to week, but the core vendors tend to return. Most regulars know which stand to hit first and where the line forms early. If you go for the donuts, plan to arrive closer to 2pm than to 6pm.

Why is this market different from other Las Vegas markets?

Ryan Rose, who attends the Thursday farmers market, notes that the Skye Canyon location works for residents because it's walkable from many homes inside the community. Most Las Vegas farmers markets require a drive into the valley. Here, families can walk over with kids or dogs, pick up dinner ingredients, eat from the food vendors, and head home.

The year-round schedule also matters. Many outdoor markets in the valley shut down in peak summer or scale back in winter. Skye Canyon's market keeps running, which makes it a reliable weekly fixture instead of a seasonal one.
