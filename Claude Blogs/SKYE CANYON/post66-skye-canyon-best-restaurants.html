Skye Canyon residents have a growing set of dining options right inside the community, anchored by Skye Bar and Grill, Sicilian Guys Trattoria, Sakura Ya, Cooper's Kitchen and Tap, Omelette Cafe, and Aspire Coffee House. Most sit within walking distance of Skye Canyon Marketplace.

What are the best restaurants in Skye Canyon?

Skye Bar and Grill at 9830 W. Skye Canyon Park Dr. leads the local pack with 294 plus Yelp reviews and a full bar, American menu, and family friendly sports bar feel. Sicilian Guys Trattoria at 9800 W. Skye Canyon Park Dr. Suite 190 serves Chicago and Sicilian style pizza alongside Italian pastas. Sakura Ya at 9750 W. Skye Canyon Park Dr. handles the authentic Japanese category with sushi, sashimi, ramen, and traditional plates.

Cooper's Kitchen and Tap rounds out the sit down list with a highly reviewed American gastropub menu. For breakfast, Omelette Cafe is a family owned spot residents grab on weekends. Aspire Coffee House inside Skye Center handles the coffee and light bite category with a vegan friendly menu.

What about fast casual and chains in Skye Canyon?

Around Skye Canyon Marketplace, residents also have El Pollo Loco, Cafe Rio, Tropical Smoothie, Menchie's Frozen Yogurt, Island Flavor, Subway, Burger King, Domino's, and a Starbucks inside Smith's. Ryan Rose points out that Skye Canyon dining still skews limited for a master plan of this size, but the independent options inside the community already cover American, Italian, Japanese, breakfast, and coffee.

For destination dining, residents typically drive south on US 95 to Centennial Hills or Downtown Summerlin, both within about a 15 minute drive depending on time of day.

Local Insight from Ryan Rose

Ryan Rose tells buyers that the in community dining inside Skye Canyon is one of the underrated selling points. Most northwest Las Vegas master plans require a drive for any sit down meal. At Skye Canyon, a resident can walk from a home in Skye Center to coffee, dinner, and pizza without ever leaving the community.
