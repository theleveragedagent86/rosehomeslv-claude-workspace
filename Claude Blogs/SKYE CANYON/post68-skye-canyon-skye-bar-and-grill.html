Skye Bar and Grill at 9830 W. Skye Canyon Park Dr. is the community's flagship sit down restaurant, with 294 plus Yelp reviews, an American menu, a full bar, and a sports bar atmosphere with multiple TVs. It is consistently ranked as a Skye Canyon resident favorite.

What is Skye Bar and Grill at Skye Canyon?

Skye Bar and Grill is an independent, full service American restaurant located near Skye Canyon Marketplace. The menu covers burgers, sandwiches, salads, wings, and entrees you would expect from a neighborhood grill, plus a full bar with beer, wine, and cocktails. The room is built for sports viewing, with TVs at the bar and across the dining area.

Despite the sports bar feel, the restaurant is family friendly during dinner hours. Tables, booths, and a patio give residents flexible seating, and the kitchen is set up to handle casual lunches, dinner with kids, and adult only nights out.

Why is Skye Bar and Grill a Skye Canyon community favorite?

Ryan Rose, who has eaten at Skye Bar and Grill multiple times with clients, notes that the appeal is simple. The food is solid, the staff knows regulars by name, and it is the only full bar inside the master plan. With 294 plus Yelp reviews, the restaurant has built a loyal following well beyond just Skye Canyon residents.

The location matters too. Residents from Eaglepointe, Skye Mesa, and the surrounding villages can walk or take a short drive to grab a meal without crossing US 95. For game nights, the bar fills up with neighbors rather than strangers, which is part of why locals rate the room so highly.
