Sicilian Guys Trattoria sits at 9800 W. Skye Canyon Park Dr. Suite 190, inside Skye Canyon Marketplace. The family owned independent serves Chicago style and Sicilian style pizza alongside Italian pastas and entrees, with a strong local following.

What is Sicilian Guys Trattoria at Skye Canyon?

Sicilian Guys Trattoria is a casual Italian restaurant tucked into the Skye Canyon Marketplace retail strip near Smith's. The menu is anchored by two pizza styles, Chicago deep dish and traditional Sicilian, which is a rare combination in northwest Las Vegas. The kitchen also turns out classic pastas, baked dishes, and Italian entrees.

The room is casual and family friendly, designed for sit down dinners, quick lunches, or takeout. Because the restaurant is independent and family owned, the menu reflects a single kitchen's recipes rather than a chain template, which is part of why locals come back.

Why do Skye Canyon residents recommend Sicilian Guys?

Ryan Rose recommends Sicilian Guys to clients who want pizza inside the community without ordering from a national chain. Deep dish and Sicilian crusts are not easy to find in northwest Las Vegas, so the trattoria fills a real gap. The pasta menu also gives families a non pizza option for weeknight dinners.

Location helps. Sicilian Guys is steps from the Smith's grocery anchor in Skye Canyon Marketplace, so a resident can grab dinner and run errands in the same trip. The Suite 190 storefront keeps the parking situation simple and the waits manageable on weeknights.
