Cooper's Kitchen and Tap sits within easy reach of Skye Canyon along Skye Canyon Park Drive. It is an American gastropub with elevated comfort food, a solid beer list on tap, and a $$ price point.

What is Cooper's Kitchen and Tap?

Cooper's leans into the gastropub format. That means classic American plates done with a little more care than a sports bar, paired with a rotating selection of beers on tap. Burgers, shareables, and elevated dinner entrees anchor the menu, and the bar gives it the energy of a neighborhood watering hole.

The interior fits date nights, casual dinners with friends, and family meals. It is highly reviewed by locals and has become one of the go-to sit-down options for Skye Canyon residents who do not want to drive south to Centennial Hills or Downtown Summerlin.

How does it fit into Skye Canyon dining?

When clients ask Ryan Rose about pub-style dining inside their new neighborhood, Cooper's is usually the first answer. It fills a gap that newer master-planned communities often have, which is a grown-up gastropub that works for both a Tuesday burger and a Friday night out.

The location near Skye Canyon Park Drive puts it within a short drive of nearly every village inside the community. Residents off Skye Pointe Drive or Skye Mesa Drive can be at the door in under ten minutes, which is rare in northwest Las Vegas where many dining trips still mean a longer haul.
