What is on the menu at Nittaya's?

The kitchen focuses on classic Thai dishes done right. Expect pad thai, a full range of curries from red to green to massaman, rice plates, noodle bowls, and the traditional starters Thai diners look for. The menu is approachable for first-time guests and detailed enough for regulars who know exactly how spicy they want it.

The Centennial Hills location is the closest of the chain to Skye Canyon, but Nittaya's has built a reputation across multiple Las Vegas spots, so the consistency is part of the appeal. You know what you are getting whether you stop in for lunch on a weekday or pick up takeout on a Friday night.

Is it worth the drive from Skye Canyon?
