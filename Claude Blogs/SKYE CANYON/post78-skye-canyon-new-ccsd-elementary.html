New CCSD Skye Canyon Elementary Opening 2027-28

A brand new CCSD elementary school is coming to Skye Canyon. Ground breaks summer 2026, doors open for the 2027-28 school year, and the K-5 campus will hold about 850 students.

Where will the new Skye Canyon elementary be built?

The new school site sits near Skye Canyon Park Drive and Shaumber Road, inside the community itself. That location was selected to put a zoned CCSD elementary within walking or short driving distance of most Skye Canyon neighborhoods, which today rely on schools several minutes outside the community.

CCSD plans to break ground in summer 2026 with an opening target of the 2027-28 school year. The campus is designed for around 850 K-5 students.

Why is CCSD building a new school here?

Skye Canyon's family base keeps growing. Roughly 75% of households in 89166 are families, and existing nearby elementaries have absorbed steady enrollment increases. The new school is meant to relieve pressure on those neighboring campuses and give many Skye Canyon addresses a closer zoned option.

Ryan Rose, who has tracked the new CCSD school project since the site was announced, tells buyers that the opening will likely shift zoning boundaries. Families touring homes in 2026 should weigh both the current zoned school and the projected re-zone for 2027-28.

Local Insight from Ryan Rose

This new school is a real value driver. When a CCSD elementary opens inside a master planned community, it usually supports home values in surrounding sections.
