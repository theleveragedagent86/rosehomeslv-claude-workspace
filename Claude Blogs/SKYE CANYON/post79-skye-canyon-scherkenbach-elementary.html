Scherkenbach Elementary Near Skye Canyon

Scherkenbach Elementary is a CCSD public K-5 school on Iron Mountain Road, about 5 minutes from Skye Canyon. It serves as the zoned elementary for many homes in the community.

What do parents need to know about Scherkenbach?

Scherkenbach is a Clark County School District elementary serving kindergarten through fifth grade. The Iron Mountain Road location keeps the drive short for most Skye Canyon families, typically around 5 minutes door to door depending on the neighborhood.

The school is well rated by parents and currently handles a significant share of Skye Canyon's zoned elementary assignments. Even after the new CCSD Skye Canyon elementary opens in 2027-28, Scherkenbach will remain a zoned option for some addresses inside the community.

How does Scherkenbach fit into the school decision?

For families who want a guaranteed zoned seat rather than relying on a charter lottery, Scherkenbach is the workhorse choice. Buyers should verify their specific address at ccsd.net to confirm assignment, since boundary lines can run mid-block in newer developments.

Ryan Rose recommends Scherkenbach to parents who want a stable, parent endorsed CCSD option close to home. It pairs well with Somerset Academy as a backup if the charter lottery doesn't land in their favor.
