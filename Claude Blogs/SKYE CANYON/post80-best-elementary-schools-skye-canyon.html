Best Elementary Schools Near Skye Canyon

Skye Canyon families have strong elementary options inside and around the community. Charter, zoned CCSD, and a brand new school opening in 2027 give parents real choice in northwest Las Vegas.

Which elementary schools serve Skye Canyon?

The top tier choice is Somerset Academy Skye Canyon, a K-8 public charter inside the community ranked in the top 18 of 379 Nevada elementaries. Admission is lottery based. Scherkenbach Elementary on Iron Mountain Road is the workhorse zoned CCSD K-5, well rated by parents and about 5 minutes away.

Bilbray Elementary on Brent Lane serves some zoned addresses, and Divich Elementary, a CCSD K-5 that grew enrollment 9% in 2023-24, is another option. Coral Academy Centennial Hills is a STEM focused K-8 charter about 10 minutes from the community.

What about the new school coming in 2027?

CCSD is building a new K-5 elementary near Skye Canyon Park Drive and Shaumber Road. It breaks ground summer 2026 and opens for the 2027-28 school year with about 850 student capacity. The new campus will become the primary zoned elementary for many Skye Canyon addresses and is designed to relieve neighboring schools.
