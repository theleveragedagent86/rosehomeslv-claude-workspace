What does Smith's Marketplace at Skye Canyon offer?

Smith's Marketplace is the Kroger owned flagship format, larger and more service heavy than a standard Smith's. The Skye Canyon store covers 125,000 square feet and includes full grocery, fresh bakery, deli, floral department, pharmacy, and an attached fuel center. An in store Starbucks runs inside the building.

The store also operates a beer and wine bar with 12 beer taps, 24 seats, and two televisions. That feature is unusual for a Las Vegas grocery store and turns Smith's into a casual after work stop, not just a shopping run. The overall floor plan is built for one stop trips.

Why is Smith's important to Skye Canyon homeowners?

When clients ask Ryan Rose about Smith's, the answer is simple. The store is the single largest reason Skye Canyon residents can handle daily errands without driving down the 95. Pharmacy, fuel, groceries, coffee, and floral all sit under one roof inside the master plan.

The fuel center matters more than buyers expect. Filling up while picking up groceries cuts a separate stop out of the weekly routine. Combined with the in store Starbucks and the beer bar, Smith's becomes both an errand destination and a quick social stop for neighbors.
