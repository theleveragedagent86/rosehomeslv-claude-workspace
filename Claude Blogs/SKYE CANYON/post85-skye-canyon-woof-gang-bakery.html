Woof Gang Bakery and Grooming sits inside Skye Canyon Marketplace, steps from Smith's. The shop combines fresh baked pet treats, professional grooming, and pet supplies, making it one of the few pet focused businesses inside the community.

What does Woof Gang Bakery at Skye Canyon offer?

Woof Gang Bakery and Grooming is a national chain with an independently operated franchise inside Skye Canyon Marketplace. The store covers three lanes of pet care under one roof. The bakery side offers fresh baked treats made for dogs and cats. The grooming side handles full service appointments with professional groomers.

The retail floor stocks pet supplies including food, toys, leashes, collars, and accessories. Because the location is built into Skye Canyon Marketplace next to Smith's and the rest of the retail strip, residents can drop a dog at grooming and run errands at the grocery store during the appointment.

Why does Woof Gang matter to Skye Canyon dog owners?

Based on Ryan Rose's experience with dog owner buyers, Skye Canyon attracts a high percentage of pet households. The community runs Skye View Park and Big Skye Park with dedicated dog park areas, and the trail system through the master plan is heavily used by dog walkers. A grooming and pet bakery inside the same walkable Marketplace is a natural fit.

The convenience factor is real. Many Las Vegas dog owners drive 15 to 20 minutes for grooming. Skye Canyon residents can walk or bike to Woof Gang from large portions of the community. That changes the cost and friction of regular grooming appointments.

Local Insight from Ryan Rose

Ryan Rose tells dog owner clients that Woof Gang is one of the small signals that Skye Canyon was planned around pet households, not just families. Combined with the dog parks at Skye View and Big Skye and the wide trail network, having grooming and a pet bakery inside the Marketplace makes Skye Canyon one of the friendlier northwest Las Vegas master plans for dogs.
