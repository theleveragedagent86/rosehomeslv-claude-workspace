TPC Las Vegas Golf From Skye Canyon

TPC Las Vegas, also known as The Canyons, sits about 15 minutes from Skye Canyon. The public par 71 course measures 7,016 yards and was designed by Bobby Weed with consultation from Raymond Floyd.

What kind of course is TPC Las Vegas?

TPC Las Vegas is a public course with PGA-quality conditions. It plays as a par 71 across 7,016 yards from the back tees, which gives serious players plenty of length while still working for mid-handicappers from forward tees. Bobby Weed designed the course with consultation from PGA Tour player Raymond Floyd, and the layout uses the desert terrain to create natural elevation changes and protected fairways.

The clubhouse offers strong member-style amenities including dining, locker rooms, a pro shop, and practice facilities. Pricing sits at the premium end for Las Vegas public golf, which reflects the conditioning and the TPC brand.

Why is it convenient for Skye Canyon golfers?

Based on Ryan Rose's experience with TPC golfers, the 15 minute drive from Skye Canyon is one of the shortest commutes to a tour-quality public course in the Las Vegas valley. Early tee times work especially well because residents avoid most of the morning traffic heading south on US-95. The proximity makes TPC a realistic regular round rather than a special occasion course.

Skye Canyon golfers also have Painted Desert Golf Club nearby for a more casual round, which gives the neighborhood two solid public options at different price points. Between the two, weekly golfers can stay close to home and still get variety in their rotation.
