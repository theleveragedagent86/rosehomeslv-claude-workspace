## Official Community Info
- HOA Website: https://skyecanyon.com/skye-life/community-association/
- Developer: Olympia Companies (original); Century Communities acquired ~400 acres in 2020
- HOA Fees: $83/month master ($996/year); sub-association $50-$120/month
- Guard Gate: Master plan NOT gated; many sub-villages (Aviano, Eaglepointe, Ravenna, Madera, Skye Mesa, Belterra, Pinewood, Sage Reserve) ARE gated
- Age Restrictions: None
- Total Homes Planned: ~6,500 (some sources cite up to 9,000)
- Total Acreage: 1,700 acres
- Key Amenities: Skye Center (8,000 sf clubhouse with Aspire Coffee), Skye Fitness (10,000 sf gym, Junior Olympic pool, spa, yoga room), Skye Canyon Park (15 acres), Eagle Canyon Park, Skye View Park, Knight Skye Park, Big Skye Park, miles of trails, community garden, weekly farmers market

## Real Estate Overview

- Price Range: ~$380s (LGI Topaz townhomes) to $900K+ Toll Brothers luxury
- Median Price: $586,500 (Feb 2026, Redfin); other sources $569,820-$595,000
- YoY Growth: +2.6%
- Price/sf: ~$267-$271
- Year Built: Groundbreaking 2014, opened 2016, building through 2026+
- Inventory: ~100-150 homes at any time
- New Construction: Yes, quick move-ins 30-90 days; dirt starts 6-10 months
- Gated: Master plan NOT gated; many individual neighborhoods are
- Total Homes Planned: ~6,500 to 9,000 at full buildout

## Lifestyle & Location

- Drive to Strip: 23-40 min (US-95)
- Drive to Airport: 28-40 min
- Drive to Downtown LV: ~19 min
- Drive to Downtown Summerlin: ~16 min
- Drive to Red Rock Canyon: 25-35 min
- Drive to Mt. Charleston (Kyle Canyon): 20-30 min
- Drive to Lee Canyon Ski: ~50 min
- Nearest Hospital: Centennial Hills Hospital, 262 beds, 6900 N. Durango Dr. (Valley Health)
- Safety: CrimeGrade B+; ~2.9 crimes/1,000; below citywide. 89166 among safest ZIPs. New LVMPD substation planned 2027
- Population (89166): 27,060
- Skye Canyon Pop: ~5,108 residents, median age 36
- 89166 Median HH Income: $106,060 (2023)
- Demographics: 48.9% White, 20.8% Hispanic, 10.3% Black; 75% families
- Elevation: ~2,188 ft
- Notable: Top 10/22 master plan nationally; 92% resident satisfaction
- Resident-cited downsides: Limited sit-down restaurants (improving), some sub-villages tight lot spacing, dual HOA structure

## Skye Canyon Programs & Events

- **Chalktober Fest**: chalk art competition + fall festival, 30+ vendors, pumpkin patch, cash prizes ($300/200/100), Best 3D, Best Color, People's Choice; ~3,000 attendees; benefits Project 150
- **Skye Canyon Fit Fest**: 5K + 8K races, yoga, Bootcamp, DanceWorkz, marketplace, kids rock climbing
- **Las Vegas Farmers Market at Skye Canyon**: Every Thursday 2-6pm at 10111 W. Skye Canyon Park Dr.; Rod's Produce, Graze Food Truck, Luchkoff Patisserie, sourdough, Jamaican jerk, donut vendor
- Group Yoga Classes (outdoor)
- Stargazing Events
- Dive-In Movies (pool)
- Outdoor Movie Nights
- Family Game Night
- Color the Canyon (color run)
- Pick a Pumpkin giveaway
- Concerts at Skye Canyon Park
- Holiday Celebrations
- Skye Serves (Project 150 partnership)

**Resident-only:** Skye Fitness gym, Junior Olympic pool + spa, splash pad, multi-use field, basketball court. Up to 5 guests, resident must be present.
