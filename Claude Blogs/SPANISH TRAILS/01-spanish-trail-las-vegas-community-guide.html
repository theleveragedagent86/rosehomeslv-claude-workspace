Spanish Trail is a prestigious, guard gated community in the 89113 zip code of Las Vegas, spanning 640 acres with over 1,200 homes across 11 distinct neighborhoods. Built between 1984 and 2005 by the Blasco family, this master planned enclave offers everything from townhomes to custom luxury estates. ## What Makes Spanish Trail Different From Other Las Vegas Communities? Spanish Trail stands apart with its 27 hole championship golf course designed by legendary architect Robert Trent Jones Jr. The community features a 50,000 square foot clubhouse, 12 illuminated tennis courts, two aquatic centers with pools and spas, and a renovated fitness center. Every resident enjoys 24 hour guard gated security with controlled entry at every access point. The 11 neighborhoods within Spanish Trail cater to a range of lifestyles. The Villas offer more compact living on 5,900 square foot lots, while The Estates provide sprawling custom homes on half acre parcels. Other subdivisions include Carmel, Links, The Gardens, The Courtyards, The Springs, and The Islands, each with its own character and price point. ## Who Lives in Spanish Trail Las Vegas?
