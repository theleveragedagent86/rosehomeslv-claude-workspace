Each offers a distinct combination of course design, residential lifestyle, and amenity access. ## Which Private Golf Communities Stand Out in Las Vegas? Spanish Trail is one of the original private guard gated golf communities in Las Vegas, featuring a 27 hole Robert Trent Jones Jr. championship course that hosted the PGA Las Vegas Invitational five times. The community spans 640 acres with over 1,200 homes and prices ranging from around $360,000 to roughly $8 million. Southern Highlands Golf Club, developed by Steve Wynn, offers an exclusive private club experience with a highly regarded course south of the Strip. DragonRidge Country Club sits within the MacDonald Highlands community in Henderson, providing dramatic elevation and mountain views throughout its layout. Canyon Gate Country Club is another established guard gated option on the west side of Las Vegas with a Ted Robinson designed course. Las Vegas Country Club holds the distinction of being the oldest country club in the city, located near the Strip with a storied history in professional golf. ## What About Summerlin and Henderson Golf Communities? Red Rock Country Club in Summerlin features two Arnold Palmer designed courses within a guard gated community, offering both private and semi private playing options. TPC Las Vegas, also in Summerlin, is a PGA Tour owned facility known for hosting professional events and providing a tournament caliber experience. Bear's Best Las Vegas is a Jack Nicklaus concept course that recreates 18 of his favorite holes from courses around the world. Ryan Rose often helps buyers compare Summerlin golf communities with established options like Spanish Trail, noting that each area offers different advantages in terms of home age, lot size, and proximity to the Strip. Siena Golf Club in Summerlin provides a semi private option for buyers who want golf access without a full private club commitment. Anthem Country Club in Henderson rounds out the southeast valley with a Keith Foster designed course surrounded by mountain scenery. ## How to Choose the Right Golf Community Selecting the right golf community depends on several factors beyond the course itself. Membership structure matters: some communities require membership with home purchase, while others like Spanish Trail keep club membership separate from homeownership. Location preferences vary as well, with Summerlin communities offering proximity to Red Rock Canyon and western amenities, while Spanish Trail and Canyon Gate provide easier access to the Strip and airport. Home prices across Las Vegas golf communities range from the mid $300,000s at Spanish Trail's entry level to well over $10 million in Southern Highlands and MacDonald Highlands. Understanding total cost of ownership, including HOA fees, club dues, and property taxes, is essential before committing.
