Spring Valley High School is a public magnet school located at 3750 South Buffalo Drive in Las Vegas, known for its strong Advanced Placement and International Baccalaureate programs. The school serves students in grades 9 through 12 and holds a GreatSchools rating of 7 out of 10, placing it among the higher rated high schools near Spanish Trail. ## What Makes Spring Valley High School Stand Out? Spring Valley offers 20 Advanced Placement courses with a 45 percent AP participation rate, one of the highest among CCSD high schools in the area. The school is also an authorized International Baccalaureate campus, offering the Middle Years Programme for 9th and 10th graders and then the Diploma Programme or Career related Programme for 11th and 12th graders. Students can also pursue dual enrollment courses to earn college credit before graduating. Beyond the AP and IB tracks, the school provides nine career and technical education pathways, an AVID program, and a Navy JROTC program. This variety means students with different academic interests and career goals can find a path that fits their ambitions, all within a single campus. ## How Does Spring Valley Perform Academically? Spring Valley High School has an enrollment of approximately 2,677 students with a student to teacher ratio of 25 to 1. The combination of IB, AP, dual enrollment, and CTE programs creates a campus culture that emphasizes academic achievement across multiple tracks. Ryan Rose considers Spring Valley's breadth of advanced programs a standout feature for families in the Spanish Trail area who want rigorous academic options for their teens. The school also offers 32 sports and a wide range of clubs and organizations. Students who participate in extracurriculars alongside their advanced coursework benefit from a balanced high school experience that colleges and employers both value. ## Is Spring Valley High School Accessible from Spanish Trail? Spring Valley High School is located along Buffalo Drive, west of the I-15 and within the broader Spring Valley area where Spanish Trail sits. The drive from Spanish Trail takes roughly 10 minutes depending on traffic, making it a realistic option even for students involved in early morning or after school activities. Because Spring Valley is a magnet school, students from across CCSD can apply for admission to its IB and specialized programs. Families in Spanish Trail benefit from the proximity but should be aware that magnet enrollment follows a separate application process.
