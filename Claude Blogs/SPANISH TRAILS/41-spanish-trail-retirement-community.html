Spanish Trail is one of the most retiree-friendly neighborhoods in Nevada, ranking higher than 96.3% of communities statewide for retirement livability. Spanish Trail attracts retirees because of its combination of low-traffic streets, 24-hour guard-gated entry, and resort-style amenities that support an active but relaxed daily routine. The community includes a 27-hole golf course, 12 tennis courts, two aquatic centers, and a 50,000 square foot clubhouse, all within the neighborhood itself. Residents do not need to drive across town for fitness, dining, or social activities.
