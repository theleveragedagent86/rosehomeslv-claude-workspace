During peak hours, especially on Friday and Saturday evenings, the drive may take slightly longer near the Strip itself. However, the westside location of Spanish Trail means residents are moving against the flow of incoming traffic much of the time, which helps keep commute times predictable. ## How Far Is Spanish Trail from Harry Reid International Airport?
