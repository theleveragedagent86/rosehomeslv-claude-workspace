This proximity to world class hiking, scenic drives, and rock climbing makes Spanish Trail one of the best positioned communities for outdoor recreation in the Las Vegas Valley. ## How Far Is Red Rock Canyon from Spanish Trail? Red Rock Canyon is located approximately 12 miles west of Spanish Trail, accessible via West Charleston Boulevard. The drive typically takes 15 to 20 minutes depending on traffic, making it easy for residents to enjoy a morning hike or afternoon scenic drive without a major time commitment. The conservation area spans over 195,000 acres of Mojave Desert landscape, featuring towering red sandstone formations, seasonal waterfalls, and Joshua tree forests. The 13 mile scenic drive loop is the most popular way to experience Red Rock Canyon, offering pullouts with views of Calico Hills, Keystone Thrust, and other geological formations. Spanish Trail residents frequently use this loop for weekend outings, photography sessions, and family drives throughout the cooler months. ## What Outdoor Activities Are Available at Red Rock Canyon?
