Most Spring Valley HOAs cover common area landscaping, community lighting, neighborhood signage, and general maintenance of shared spaces. Communities with pools, parks, or clubhouses include the upkeep of those amenities in the monthly fee. Beyond monthly dues, buyers should budget for potential one time costs.
