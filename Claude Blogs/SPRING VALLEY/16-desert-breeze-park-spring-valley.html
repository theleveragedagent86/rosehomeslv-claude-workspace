Desert Breeze Park is one of the largest and most popular parks in the Spring Valley area of Las Vegas. Located at 8275 Spring Mountain Road, this regional park offers athletic fields, playgrounds, a skate park, dog park, walking paths, and much more for residents of all ages. ## What Does Desert Breeze Park Offer in Spring Valley? Desert Breeze Park spans a generous footprint at the corner of Spring Mountain and Durango, making it easily accessible for Spring Valley residents. The park features soccer, baseball, football, and softball fields, along with indoor and outdoor basketball courts. Two regulation roller hockey rinks sit on the west side of the park, and a 20,000 square foot skate park draws riders from across the valley. Beyond the sports facilities, the park includes shaded picnic areas, playgrounds with rock climbing walls, and a paved walking and jogging loop that stretches over a mile. Clean restrooms are conveniently located near the play areas, and mature trees throughout the park provide welcome shade during warmer months. ## What Events Are Held at Desert Breeze Park? Desert Breeze Park serves as a venue for some of the city's best known outdoor events. The Extreme Thing Sports and Music Festival and the Bite of Las Vegas have both called this park home, drawing large crowds each year. These events take advantage of the park's open green spaces and ample parking. For everyday visitors, the park is open from 6 AM to 11 PM daily.
