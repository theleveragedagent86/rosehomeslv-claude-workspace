Exploration Peak Park is an 80 acre regional park located near Spring Valley at 9275 South Buffalo Drive in the Mountain's Edge community. The park's centerpiece is a hiking trail that leads to the 2,846 foot summit of Exploration Peak, where visitors enjoy panoramic views of the entire Las Vegas Valley. ## What Can You Do at Exploration Peak Park? The park offers 24 developed acres of recreation space alongside open desert terrain and trails. Families can enjoy an Old West themed playground, picnic areas, horseshoe pits, and volleyball courts at the base of the park. An outdoor amphitheater hosts community events throughout the year, and replicas of a western town and an archaeological dig site give kids a unique hands on experience. The main attraction is the Exploration Peak Trail, a short but rewarding hike that gains roughly 265 feet of elevation over about half a mile. At the summit, a large seating area surrounded by informational signs explains the natural, urban, and industrial history of the landscapes visible from the top. On clear days, you can see Red Rock Canyon, the Las Vegas Strip, and the surrounding mountain ranges. ## Is Exploration Peak Park a Good Hike Near Spring Valley? The summit trail is rated moderate and is accessible for most fitness levels, making it a great option for Spring Valley residents who want a quick outdoor adventure without driving far. The Exploration Peak Loop extends the experience to about 1.3 miles for those looking for a longer walk. Dogs are welcome on leash, which adds to the park's appeal for active pet owners. Ryan Rose points out that the trail has limited shade and the gravel surface can be slippery in spots, so sturdy footwear and sun protection are recommended. The best months for hiking are October through April, when temperatures are comfortable and the views are especially crisp. ## A Local Favorite for Spring Valley Outdoor Enthusiasts Exploration Peak Park gives Spring Valley residents easy access to hiking and open space without the need to travel to Red Rock Canyon.
