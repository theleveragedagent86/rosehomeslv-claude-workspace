Desert Breeze Skate Park is a 20,000 square foot concrete skate facility located inside Desert Breeze Park in Spring Valley. It is one of the largest and most popular skateparks in the Las Vegas Valley. ## What Does Desert Breeze Skate Park Offer? The skate park features a well designed layout with ramps, rails, ledges, and bowls that cater to both street and transition skating styles. The entire facility is built from poured concrete, which provides a smooth and durable riding surface. Shade structures, benches, and drinking fountains are available on site for breaks between sessions. The park is designed primarily for intermediate to advanced level skaters, though beginners will find certain sections accessible for building skills. Skateboards, inline skates, and BMX bikes are all welcome at the facility. Helmets and protective gear are strongly recommended for all riders. ## What Are the Hours and Rules for the Skate Park? Desert Breeze Skate Park is open daily from 6 AM to 11 PM, with lighting available for evening sessions. There is no admission fee to use the skate park. The facility is part of the larger Desert Breeze Park complex at 8275 Spring Mountain Road, Las Vegas, NV 89147.
