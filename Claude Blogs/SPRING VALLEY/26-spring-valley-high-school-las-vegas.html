Spring Valley High School is the primary public high school serving the Spring Valley community in Las Vegas, located at 3750 South Buffalo Drive. With a B+ Niche rating, a 7 out of 10 GreatSchools score, and a 94% graduation rate, it ranks among the stronger public high schools in Nevada. ## What Programs Does Spring Valley High School Offer? Spring Valley High School serves approximately 2,677 students in grades 9 through 12 and is part of the Clark County School District. The school offers Advanced Placement courses, International Baccalaureate coursework, Honors classes, and dual enrollment options that allow students to earn college credits while still in high school. SchoolDigger ranks the school 40th out of 103 Nevada high schools. The IB program is a standout feature that draws students from across the valley. Dual enrollment partnerships give upperclassmen the chance to take courses through the College of Southern Nevada, reducing future tuition costs. These academic pathways make Spring Valley High competitive with magnet and charter school alternatives in the Las Vegas area. ## What Sports and Activities Are Available? Spring Valley High School fields 32 varsity sports teams, making it one of the most athletically comprehensive campuses in CCSD. Students can participate in football, basketball, baseball, soccer, swimming, wrestling, tennis, golf, and track and field, among other sports. The school competes in the NIAA Class 4A division. Beyond athletics, the school offers clubs and extracurricular organizations in areas including debate, robotics, student government, drama, and community service.
