Wilbur and Theresa Faiss Middle School is a public school serving students in grades 6 through 8 in the central portion of Spring Valley, Las Vegas. Part of the Clark County School District, Faiss is one of three middle school options available to families in the community. ## What Does Faiss Middle School Offer Students? Faiss Middle School provides the standard CCSD curriculum covering math, science, English language arts, and social studies, along with elective courses in technology, visual arts, performing arts, and physical education. The school serves students from several Spring Valley elementary schools and prepares them for high school coursework. The campus includes standard middle school facilities such as a gymnasium, library, computer labs, and outdoor athletic fields. Students can participate in school sports, academic clubs, and student leadership organizations. Faiss also offers support services for English language learners and students who qualify for special education programs. ## Where Is Faiss Middle School Located in Spring Valley? Faiss Middle School sits in the central area of Spring Valley, serving families in neighborhoods generally between West Flamingo Road and West Tropicana Avenue. The school's attendance zone is determined by CCSD and can change from year to year, so Ryan Rose always recommends verifying the current boundary before finalizing a home purchase. Central Spring Valley offers a range of housing options, from established single family homes built in the 1980s and 1990s to newer townhome developments. Families zoned for Faiss benefit from relatively short commute times and proximity to neighborhood amenities including parks and shopping along South Rainbow Boulevard. ## How Does Faiss Middle School Connect to the High School Pipeline? Students at Faiss Middle School typically feed into Spring Valley High School or one of the nearby high schools depending on their specific attendance zone. Spring Valley High School holds a B+ Niche rating and offers IB, AP, and dual enrollment programs, giving Faiss graduates access to strong academic pathways.
