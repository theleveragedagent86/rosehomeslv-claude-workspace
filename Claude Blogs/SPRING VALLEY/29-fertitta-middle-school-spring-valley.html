Victoria Fertitta Middle School is a public school serving students in grades 6 through 8 in the Spring Valley area of Las Vegas. Fertitta Middle School provides the core CCSD curriculum in math, science, English language arts, and social studies. The school supports academic enrichment through honors level coursework and intervention programs for students who need additional support. As one of the newer CCSD middle school facilities in the area, Fertitta features updated classrooms, a modern gymnasium, computer labs, and athletic fields.
