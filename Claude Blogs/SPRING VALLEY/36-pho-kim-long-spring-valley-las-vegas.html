Pho Kim Long is one of the most popular Vietnamese restaurants in Spring Valley, located at 4029 West Spring Mountain Road in the heart of the Las Vegas Chinatown corridor. With over 4,500 Yelp reviews and late night hours that stretch past 2 AM on weekdays, it has earned a loyal following among locals and visitors alike. ## What Makes Pho Kim Long a Chinatown Favorite? Pho Kim Long specializes in traditional Vietnamese pho noodle soup, served with your choice of protein in a slow simmered broth alongside fresh herbs, bean sprouts, and lime. The menu also features popular dishes like fresh spring rolls, honey garlic pork chops, wok style noodles, and authentic rice plates. Vietnamese and Chinese cooking styles are both represented, giving guests a wide selection to choose from. The restaurant is open Monday through Thursday from 8 AM to 2:30 AM, and Friday through Sunday from 8 AM to 4:30 AM. Those extended hours make it a go to destination for late night diners coming off the Strip or wrapping up a night out in Spring Valley. A second location at Town Square keeps standard hours from 11 AM to 11 PM for those further south on Las Vegas Boulevard. ## Where Is Pho Kim Long on Spring Mountain Road? The restaurant is about a five minute drive west of the Las Vegas Strip, making it easily accessible from nearly anywhere in the valley.
