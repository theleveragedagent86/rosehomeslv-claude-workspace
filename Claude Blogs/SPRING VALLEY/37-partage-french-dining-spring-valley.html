Partage is a modern French fine dining restaurant at 3839 Spring Mountain Road in the Spring Valley Chinatown corridor. Led by Chef Yuri Szarzewski, a Chopped champion and veteran of multiple Michelin starred kitchens in France, Partage delivers one of the most refined dining experiences in Las Vegas outside the Strip. ## What Is the Dining Experience at Partage? Partage offers surprise tasting menus in 5, 7, and 9 course formats, crafted by Chef Yuri and Pastry Chef Ludovic using seasonal ingredients sourced for each monthly rotation. Diners do not choose individual dishes. Instead, the kitchen presents a curated progression of courses that changes every month, keeping the experience fresh for repeat visitors. Chef Yuri trained at some of France's most celebrated kitchens, including L'Ambassade, Le Bristol, Palace George V, and the Shangri La Palace in Paris. He moved to Las Vegas in December 2015 and opened Partage in 2018. The restaurant earned Restaurant of the Year honors in its opening year and has maintained a strong reputation for technical precision and creative presentation. ## Where Is Partage Located in Spring Valley? Partage sits at 3839 Spring Mountain Road, in the same Chinatown corridor known for its dense concentration of Asian restaurants. The location might surprise first time visitors expecting a French fine dining setting on the Strip, but that is part of what makes Partage distinctive. The intimate dining room, open Tuesday through Saturday from 5:30 PM to 9:30 PM, keeps the focus on the food rather than spectacle. Next door, the team operates Le Club by Partage, a champagne lounge featuring more than 80 curated selections along with savory bites and desserts. Ryan Rose considers Partage one of the hidden gems that elevate Spring Valley's dining scene well beyond its casual reputation. ## Why Partage Stands Out in the Local Scene The combination of Michelin caliber technique and a neighborhood setting creates something rare in the Las Vegas market.
