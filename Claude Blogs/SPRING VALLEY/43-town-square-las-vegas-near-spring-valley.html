Town Square Las Vegas is a premier open air shopping, dining, and entertainment destination located just east of Spring Valley. Sitting at the junction of Las Vegas Boulevard, I 15, and the 215 Beltway, it is one of the closest major retail centers for Spring Valley residents. ## What Is Town Square Las Vegas? Town Square spans 100 acres and features 22 architecturally distinctive buildings blending Old World and contemporary design styles. The center includes over 115 stores and restaurants, an 18 screen AMC movie theater, and 252,000 square feet of Class A office space. A central park area with interactive water features and a children's playground makes it a family friendly destination beyond standard retail. Major retailers at Town Square include H&M, Whole Foods Market, The Container Store, Old Navy, and Guitar Center. The open air layout creates a walkable streetscape that feels more like a town center than a traditional shopping mall, with wide sidewalks, landscaped courtyards, and seasonal programming throughout the year. ## What Restaurants and Entertainment Are at Town Square? Dining options at Town Square range from upscale to casual. Brio Italian Grille, Lazy Dog Restaurant and Bar, and Yard House are among the sit down options, while quick service spots provide everyday lunch and dinner choices. Double Helix Wine and Whiskey Lounge draws an evening crowd for craft cocktails and curated wine lists. Entertainment extends beyond the AMC theater. The children's park features treehouses, mazes, and interactive play equipment, making it a popular weekend destination for families. Town Square also hosts seasonal events, live music, and outdoor movie nights in the central park area. The center is open from 10 AM to 9 PM Monday through Thursday, with extended hours on Friday and Saturday. ## How Close Is Town Square to Spring Valley?
