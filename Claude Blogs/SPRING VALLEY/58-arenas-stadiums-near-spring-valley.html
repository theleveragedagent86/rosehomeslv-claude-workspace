Spring Valley residents enjoy some of the shortest drives to Las Vegas's two major sports and entertainment venues: T-Mobile Arena and Allegiant Stadium. Both facilities sit within minutes of the neighborhood, giving Spring Valley homeowners easy access to NHL hockey, NFL football, concerts, and world class events. ## How Far Is T-Mobile Arena From Spring Valley? T-Mobile Arena, located on the west end of the Las Vegas Strip near Park MGM, sits roughly 10 to 15 minutes from most areas of Spring Valley. The 20,000 seat venue serves as the home of the Vegas Golden Knights (NHL) and hosts major concerts, UFC events, and award shows throughout the year. Routes via Flamingo Road or Tropicana Avenue provide direct access with minimal freeway travel. Game nights and concert events can add traffic around the venue, but Spring Valley residents benefit from approaching from the west side rather than fighting Strip congestion from the east or north. Many fans find they can leave home 30 minutes before puck drop and arrive comfortably. ## How Close Is Allegiant Stadium to Spring Valley? Allegiant Stadium, home of the Las Vegas Raiders (NFL), sits just south of the Strip near Russell Road and Interstate 15. The drive from Spring Valley typically takes 10 to 18 minutes depending on traffic and game day conditions. The 65,000 seat domed stadium also hosts UNLV Rebel football, international soccer matches, and large scale concerts. For Raiders season ticket holders, living in Spring Valley means avoiding the long commutes that fans in Summerlin, Henderson, or North Las Vegas face on game days. The neighborhood's proximity to I-15 and the 215 Beltway provides multiple route options to reach the stadium without relying on a single corridor. ## Why Does Venue Access Matter for Spring Valley Homebuyers?
