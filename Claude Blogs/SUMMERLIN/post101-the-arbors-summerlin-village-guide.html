Living in The Arbors Summerlin: Village Guide

The Arbors stands as one of the most established and recognizable villages in all of Summerlin. Situated in Summerlin North within the 89144 zip code, this family oriented community has attracted homeowners for decades thanks to its central location, mature landscaping, and deep roster of sub-communities. With roughly 29 distinct neighborhoods tucked inside its borders, The Arbors offers variety that few single villages can match.

Ryan Rose, a local Las Vegas Realtor, considers The Arbors one of the strongest options for buyers who want an established Summerlin address without the premium pricing found in some of the newer villages to the south and west.

Sub-Communities Inside The Arbors

One of the defining features of The Arbors is its sheer number of neighborhoods, each with its own character. Amber Hills, Arbor Grove, and Arbor View offer classic single family homes that appeal to growing families. Brookfield and Chardonnay bring slightly different lot sizes and architectural styles. Napa Hills, Oak Hills, and Vineyards lean into a more refined streetscape, while Rosewood and Royal Woods provide quiet cul de sac living that many buyers specifically seek out.

Scarlett Canyon and Woodlands round out the lineup with additional options spanning various price points and floor plans. This breadth of choice means buyers at nearly every budget level can find something that fits within The Arbors.

Home Prices and Market Snapshot

The Arbors falls within the 89144 zip code, where the median home price currently sits around $575,000. That figure reflects an 11.1% year over year increase, with homes averaging 49 days on market. The combination of steady appreciation and reasonable days on market signals a healthy, competitive environment without the frenzied pace that some newer areas experience.

Compared to Summerlin South and West villages, The Arbors generally offers more square footage per dollar, largely because the housing stock is more established. Buyers who prioritize value relative to location often land here first.

Source: teamcarver.com, thesummerlinagent.com

Location and Lifestyle Advantages

The Arbors benefits from a central position within Summerlin North, providing quick access to Summerlin Parkway and the 215 Beltway. Daily errands, dining, and entertainment at Downtown Summerlin are all within a short drive. Residents also enjoy proximity to some of Summerlin's community parks and the extensive 200 mile trail network that connects villages throughout the master plan.

Families with school age children will find solid options in the area. The village feeds into several well regarded schools, and the Summerlin school landscape continues to be a major draw for relocating families.

Who Should Consider The Arbors

The Arbors is an excellent fit for buyers who want a proven, family friendly neighborhood with mature trees, established HOA standards, and a wide selection of home styles. It suits first time buyers, growing families, and anyone who values a central Summerlin address at a price point that remains accessible compared to newer construction in the area.
