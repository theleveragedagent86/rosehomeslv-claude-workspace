Living in The Trails Summerlin: Village Guide

The Trails is one of Summerlin North's most well known villages, situated in the 89134 zip code with easy access to Summerlin Parkway. Known for its established neighborhoods and convenient positioning, The Trails has been a go to destination for families and professionals seeking a settled, community oriented lifestyle within one of Las Vegas's premier master planned developments.

As Realtor Ryan Rose often explains to buyers exploring Summerlin for the first time, The Trails offers the kind of neighborhood stability that only comes with time and thoughtful planning.

Neighborhoods Within The Trails

The Trails contains a diverse collection of sub-communities, each catering to different preferences and price points. Cantabria brings a Mediterranean influenced aesthetic that stands out among the village's offerings. Canyon Oaks and Mountain Trails appeal to buyers who want proximity to the natural terrain along the western edge of the valley. Sierra Ridge and Sunset Crest provide elevated homesites with views that stretch across the city.

Country Rose Estates offers larger lots for those who want more space, while Deerfield and Desert Trails deliver classic single family layouts at approachable price points. Summer Trail, Willow Bend, and Highline round out the options, giving buyers at almost every budget a viable path into this village.

Market Overview and Home Values

Homes in The Trails fall within the 89134 zip code, which encompasses a broad range of Summerlin North properties. Pricing varies by sub-community, with more modest options starting in the mid $400,000 range and premium homes in neighborhoods like Cantabria and Sierra Ridge pushing well above the area median. Demand remains consistent thanks to the village's location near Summerlin Parkway, which provides one of the fastest commute routes in the northwest valley.

Buyers comparing Summerlin home prices across villages will find The Trails occupies a competitive middle ground, offering established construction and mature landscaping at values that still make sense relative to newer developments.

Location and Daily Convenience

The Trails sits in a prime corridor within Summerlin North, putting residents minutes from Summerlin Parkway and the 215 Beltway. Grocery shopping, dining, medical offices, and fitness options are all clustered nearby. The village's proximity to Summerlin's network of community parks and the 200 mile trail system gives residents walkable and bikeable access to outdoor recreation.

Schools serving The Trails are among the reasons families choose this village. Several well rated public and private institutions sit within a short drive, and the broader Summerlin school landscape remains one of the top draws in the Las Vegas Valley.

Is The Trails Right for You

The Trails works well for buyers who want an established Summerlin village with a wide selection of neighborhoods and a location that keeps commuting manageable. It appeals especially to families, working professionals, and anyone who values a neighborhood that feels lived in and well maintained rather than brand new.
