Living in The Canyons Summerlin: Village Guide

The Canyons is one of the most distinctive villages in Summerlin North, known for housing some of the community's most exclusive and prestigious neighborhoods. Located in the 89144 zip code, The Canyons sets itself apart with three guard gated communities and a collection of sub-communities that consistently rank among the most sought after addresses in the master plan.

For buyers searching for elevated living within Summerlin North, Realtor Ryan Rose points to The Canyons as a village that delivers both privacy and prestige without requiring a move to the newer (and often pricier) developments further south.

Guard Gated Communities: Canyon Fairways, Mira Villa, and The Palisades

What truly distinguishes The Canyons from other Summerlin villages is the presence of three guard gated neighborhoods. Canyon Fairways offers golf course living with homes positioned along manicured fairways, attracting buyers who want the combination of security, views, and a resort caliber setting. Mira Villa provides a more intimate gated experience with custom and semi-custom homes that feature upgraded finishes throughout. The Palisades rounds out the trio with spacious lots and architectural variety that appeals to established families and executives.

Guard gated communities in Summerlin command a premium, and for good reason. Controlled access, lower traffic, and a heightened sense of exclusivity create a living experience that non-gated neighborhoods simply cannot replicate.

Additional Sub-Communities

Beyond the gated enclaves, The Canyons includes several other noteworthy neighborhoods. Aventura and Bellacere offer well appointed single family homes with contemporary floor plans. Canyon Ridge provides solid mid range options that attract both move up buyers and long term investors. Star Canyon and Talon Pointe deliver additional variety in terms of square footage, lot size, and price point.

This mix of gated and non-gated options within a single village gives buyers flexibility. You can enter The Canyons at a more accessible price in one of the open neighborhoods and potentially move up to a guard gated community down the road.

Source: thesummerlinagent.com

Pricing and Market Conditions

The Canyons shares the 89144 zip code with The Arbors, where the median home price currently sits around $575,000 with an 11.1% year over year increase. However, homes within The Canyons' guard gated communities typically trade well above that median, with many properties in Canyon Fairways, Mira Villa, and The Palisades listing north of $800,000 and frequently exceeding $1 million.

Lifestyle and Location

The Canyons sits in a quiet pocket of Summerlin North with convenient access to the 215 Beltway and Summerlin Parkway. Residents benefit from proximity to Summerlin's park system and the 200 mile trail network that winds through the community. Top rated Summerlin schools serve the area, making The Canyons a strong choice for families who want both security and academic quality.

Who Should Consider The Canyons

The Canyons appeals to buyers who value privacy, exclusivity, and a refined neighborhood atmosphere. If guard gated living in Summerlin North interests you, this village should be at the top of your list. It also works for buyers who want to start in a non-gated sub-community with a clear path to upgrading within the same village.
