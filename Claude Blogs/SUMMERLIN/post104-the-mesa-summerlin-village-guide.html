Living in The Mesa Summerlin: Village Guide

The Mesa represents the premium tier of Summerlin South living. Located in the 89135 zip code, this village has earned a reputation for higher end construction, thoughtful community design, and home values that reflect the desirability of its location. With a median home price of approximately $790,000 in the surrounding zip code, The Mesa attracts buyers who are ready to invest in one of Summerlin's most polished residential settings.

According to Ryan Rose, a Realtor specializing in Summerlin properties, The Mesa is where buyers tend to land when they want newer construction and modern amenities in a location that still feels connected to everything Summerlin offers.

Sub-Communities in The Mesa

The Mesa's sub-communities each bring something distinct to the table. Mesa Ridge is perhaps the most recognized name in the village, offering a range of single family homes with contemporary desert architecture and open floor plans built for how people actually live today. Cielo features homes with elevated views and clean, modern lines that appeal to design conscious buyers.

Vista Dulce translates to "sweet view," and the neighborhood lives up to the name with western facing homesites that capture stunning sunsets over Red Rock Canyon. Adelina and Amado provide additional options with slightly different price points and architectural styles. Ladera and Monte Bello complete the lineup, giving buyers multiple entry points into a village that consistently performs well in terms of both livability and resale value.

Market Pricing and Trends

The 89135 zip code carries a median home price near $790,000, placing it well above the Summerlin North averages found in villages like The Arbors and The Trails. This premium reflects the newer construction, larger lot sizes, and upgraded standard features that define most homes in The Mesa. Buyers relocating from higher cost markets in California and the Pacific Northwest often find The Mesa delivers a similar quality of living at a fraction of what they left behind.

What Makes The Mesa Stand Out

Summerlin South villages like The Mesa benefit from their proximity to Red Rock Canyon, the community's trail system, and some of the best parks in the master plan. The 200 miles of trails running through Summerlin are especially accessible from The Mesa's western position, and morning hikes with Red Rock views become part of the daily routine for many residents.

The school options in Summerlin South continue to attract families, and the village's location offers a straightforward commute to the 215 Beltway, Downtown Summerlin, and the broader Las Vegas Valley.

Is The Mesa the Right Fit

The Mesa is ideal for move up buyers, relocating professionals, and families who want a modern Summerlin South address at a price point below the ultra luxury communities like The Ridges. It delivers strong home values, newer construction standards, and a lifestyle that balances suburban comfort with easy access to outdoor recreation.
