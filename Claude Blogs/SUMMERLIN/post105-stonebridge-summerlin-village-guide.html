Living in Stonebridge Summerlin: Village Guide

Stonebridge is one of the newest chapters in the Summerlin story. Established in 2017 and spanning 502 acres in Summerlin West, this village sits within the 89138 zip code and represents the kind of modern, thoughtfully designed community that continues to push Summerlin's reputation forward. With a median home price near $840,000 in the surrounding zip code, Stonebridge attracts buyers who want the latest in design, energy efficiency, and community planning.

Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas Realtor who works extensively in Summerlin, describes Stonebridge as the village that appeals most to buyers who prioritize new construction and are willing to invest in a community still building momentum.

Sub-Communities in Stonebridge

Stonebridge includes nine distinct sub-communities, each developed by leading national and regional builders. Bixby Creek and Bristle Vale offer single family homes with open concept layouts and the kind of smart home technology that newer construction delivers as standard. Caledonia brings a slightly elevated product line with premium finishes and larger floor plans.

Graycliff and Heritage appeal to buyers looking for mid range options that still benefit from the village's newer infrastructure and community amenities. Scots Pine, Shadow Point, Skye Knoll, and Westcott round out the offerings, providing options across various price points and home sizes. Whether you need a three bedroom starter or a five bedroom executive home, Stonebridge has builders delivering both.

Pricing and the Summerlin West Market

The 89138 zip code carries the highest median home price among Summerlin's primary zip codes at approximately $840,000. This figure reflects the concentration of newer construction in the area, where modern floor plans, upgraded appliances, and current building standards all contribute to higher per square foot values. Stonebridge embodies this trend.

Buyers coming from markets like Southern California or the Bay Area often find Stonebridge hits a sweet spot. The homes feel brand new because many of them are, and the pricing, while premium for Summerlin, remains significantly below comparable new construction in coastal markets. Village Amenities and Outdoor Access

Stonebridge's western position puts it closer to Red Rock Canyon than most other Summerlin villages. Morning trail runs with canyon views, weekend hikes, and evening walks along Summerlin's 200 mile trail system are part of daily life here. The village also benefits from newer parks and open spaces that were purpose built as part of the development plan.

Access to quality schools remains strong, and the 215 Beltway connects Stonebridge to the rest of the valley efficiently. The trade off for living on the western edge of Summerlin is a slightly longer commute to central Las Vegas, but most residents find the setting and home quality more than compensate.

Who Stonebridge Is Built For

Stonebridge is the right choice for buyers who want new construction in a Summerlin address, are comfortable with a premium price point, and value modern home design paired with outdoor access. It works especially well for relocating families, tech professionals working remotely, and anyone who wants to move in without the renovation projects that older Summerlin villages sometimes require.
