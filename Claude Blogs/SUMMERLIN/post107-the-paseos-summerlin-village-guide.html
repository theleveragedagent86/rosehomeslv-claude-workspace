Living in The Paseos Summerlin: Village Guide

The Paseos stands as one of the largest and most diverse villages in all of Summerlin West. With approximately 29 sub-communities spread across the 89138 zip code, this village offers a range of home styles, price points, and neighborhood personalities that few other Summerlin villages can match. The Spanish influenced naming convention that runs through The Paseos reflects the design language found in much of the architecture here, where stucco exteriors, tile roofs, and courtyard entries create a cohesive Mediterranean feel.

According to Ryan Rose, a Realtor specializing in Summerlin properties, The Paseos is where buyers often land when they want the variety of a large village combined with the polish of Summerlin West's newer infrastructure.

Sub-Communities Worth Knowing

The sheer number of neighborhoods within The Paseos means there is genuine diversity in what buyers can find. Montecito and Los Altos tend to attract buyers looking for larger single family homes with upgraded finishes. Granada and Cordova offer mid range options that balance square footage with value. Allegra, Altura, and Esperanza deliver entry level opportunities for first time Summerlin buyers or those looking to downsize within the community.

Other notable sub-communities include Andalusia, Barcelona, Belmonte, Catalina, Coronado, Delano, Escala, Mariposa, Palomar, Santa Rosa, Santaluz, Segovia, Serrano, and Tevare. Each neighborhood carries its own builder, price range, and floor plan selection. This is the kind of village where two homes a block apart can feel entirely different from one another.

Pricing in The Paseos

As part of the 89138 zip code, The Paseos falls within the area carrying a median home price near $840,000. However, the wide range of sub-communities means actual prices vary significantly. Smaller homes in neighborhoods like Allegra may come in well below that median, while premium properties in Montecito or Santaluz can exceed it. This price diversity is one of the village's greatest strengths, allowing buyers at multiple budget levels to find a home here.

Village Life and Amenities

The Paseos benefits from Summerlin West's modern amenity infrastructure. Residents have access to well maintained community parks, walking paths that connect to Summerlin's broader 200 mile trail system, and proximity to Downtown Summerlin's shopping, dining, and entertainment options. The village's western location also means shorter drives to Red Rock Canyon for weekend recreation.

Schools in the area serve families well, and the 215 Beltway keeps commutes to the Strip, Henderson, and the airport manageable even from this far western position.

Who Should Consider The Paseos

The Paseos fits a wide range of buyers precisely because of its size and variety. First time buyers, growing families, empty nesters, and relocating professionals can all find something that works within this single village. It rewards the buyer who takes time to tour multiple sub-communities rather than settling on the first option.
