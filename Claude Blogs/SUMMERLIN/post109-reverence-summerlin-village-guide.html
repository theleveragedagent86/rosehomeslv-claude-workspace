Living in Reverence Summerlin: Guard-Gated Luxury

Reverence occupies a unique position in the Summerlin landscape. This 300 acre guard-gated community in Summerlin West delivers the kind of security, exclusivity, and premium home quality that buyers expect from Summerlin's guard-gated enclaves. Located within the 89138 zip code, Reverence was developed primarily by Pulte Homes and features 24 hour guard gate access, ensuring that the community remains private and controlled.

Ryan Rose, a Realtor who works with luxury buyers throughout Summerlin, points to Reverence as one of the strongest guard-gated options in Summerlin West for buyers who want newer construction behind a gate.

What Reverence Offers

Pulte Homes designed Reverence as a cohesive community rather than a collection of unrelated neighborhoods. The result is a village where the architecture, landscaping, and common areas share a consistent design language. Homes range from spacious single family residences to the Monument at Reverence collection, which includes townhome style living starting in the $500,000 range. This lower entry point is notable because it allows buyers to access a guard-gated Summerlin address at a price point that would not be possible in communities like The Ridges or Tournament Hills.

The single family homes in Reverence feature Pulte's signature floor plans with open living areas, gourmet kitchens, and the kind of energy efficient construction that comes standard with modern builds. Many homes offer views of the surrounding desert and mountains, a benefit of the community's western positioning within Summerlin.

Guard-Gated Living in Summerlin West

The 24 hour guard gate at Reverence is not just a checkpoint. It creates a different pace of life within the community. Streets are quieter, through traffic is nonexistent, and there is a sense of privacy that open villages simply cannot replicate. For buyers coming from gated communities in other cities, this is a familiar and valued feature. For those experiencing it for the first time, the difference is noticeable from the moment they drive through the entrance.

Reverence is one of the more accessible guard-gated communities in Summerlin in terms of pricing. While The Ridges and similar enclaves command prices well into the millions, Reverence provides the guard-gated experience at a broader range of price points. This makes it particularly attractive to move up buyers and professionals who want the security without the ultra luxury price tag.

Location and Surroundings

Reverence benefits from its Summerlin West address. Red Rock Canyon is close, the trail system connects to the broader Summerlin network, and Downtown Summerlin's shopping and dining is a short drive east. The 215 Beltway keeps commute times reasonable for those working in other parts of the valley. Community parks and recreational spaces are woven into the surrounding area.

Who Reverence Serves Best

Reverence is the right community for buyers who want guard-gated security, appreciate consistent Pulte Homes quality, and are looking for newer construction in Summerlin West. It serves everyone from the townhome buyer at Monument starting around $500,000 to the single family buyer investing at the upper end of the village's range.
