Honey Salt Summerlin: Farm to Table Restaurant Review

Some restaurants succeed because of spectacle. Others succeed because they become part of the fabric of a neighborhood. Honey Salt falls firmly in the second category. Since opening in 2012, this farm to table restaurant on Rampart Boulevard has become one of those places Summerlin residents consider their own. It is the kind of spot where regulars have a favorite table and the chef might stop by to say hello.

Ryan Rose frequently recommends Honey Salt to clients who are relocating to the area and want to get a feel for the community's personality. It represents Summerlin's dining culture at its most genuine.

The Team Behind Honey Salt

Honey Salt is the creation of Elizabeth Blau and Chef Kim Canteenwalla, two of the most respected figures in the Las Vegas restaurant world. Blau is a hospitality industry leader whose consulting firm has shaped dining concepts across the country. Canteenwalla brings the culinary vision, drawing on a career that spans fine dining kitchens around the globe. Together, they built Honey Salt as a neighborhood restaurant with real ambition in the kitchen.

Chef Canteenwalla is known for personally greeting guests, which sets a tone that filters through the entire experience. This is not a restaurant that relies on a celebrity chef's name while someone else runs the kitchen. The hands on approach is part of what keeps the quality so consistent year after year.

Source: honeysalt.com

Menu Highlights

The menu changes with the seasons, which keeps things fresh for the regulars who visit frequently. The bacon wrapped meatloaf has earned a devoted following as a signature comfort dish, and the monkey bread with bourbon caramel is the kind of dessert that people plan their meal around. Seasonal ingredients mean the menu always has something new to try, but the kitchen's core identity stays consistent.

The brunch service deserves special mention. Weekend brunch at Honey Salt is one of the most popular reservations in Summerlin, drawing crowds for a menu that balances indulgent dishes with lighter, produce forward options. It is a Saturday tradition for many families in the area.

Source: honeysalt.com, Yelp

Atmosphere and Location

Located at 1031 S Rampart Blvd in the 89145 zip code, Honey Salt occupies a bright, welcoming space with banquettes, warm lighting, and an energy that feels equally appropriate for a Tuesday dinner or a special occasion. The design avoids the dark, heavy aesthetic of many upscale restaurants in favor of something lighter and more inviting. Pricing sits in the moderate to upper moderate range, accessible enough for regular visits without sacrificing quality.

With over 3,245 Yelp reviews, the restaurant has maintained strong ratings across more than a decade of service, which is a remarkable feat in a city where restaurants come and go constantly.

Honey Salt in the Summerlin Dining Scene

Honey Salt belongs on any list of the best restaurants near Summerlin and is a natural fit for date night or family celebrations alike. Its Rampart Boulevard location also places it near other excellent dining options, making this stretch of road one of the most rewarding food corridors in the area.
