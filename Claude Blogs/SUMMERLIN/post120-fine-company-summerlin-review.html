Fine Company Summerlin: Cocktail Bar and Eatery Review

Downtown Summerlin has steadily added restaurants that give residents real reasons to eat locally rather than heading to the Strip. Fine Company, the contemporary American eatery from Chef Roy Ellamar, may be the strongest argument yet. Since opening, it has quickly become one of the most talked about additions to the Summerlin dining scene, earning recognition from Eater Las Vegas as one of the Best New Off Strip Restaurants of 2023.

According to Ryan Rose, Fine Company represents exactly the kind of elevated but approachable dining that Summerlin residents have been asking for in the Downtown Summerlin corridor.

Chef Roy Ellamar's Background

Chef Roy Ellamar built his reputation at the Bellagio, where he ran the kitchen at Harvest before branching out on his own. That pedigree shows up in the attention to detail and the quality of execution at Fine Company, but the restaurant itself feels nothing like a casino restaurant. The atmosphere is relaxed, the portions are generous, and the menu reads like something designed for regular neighborhood visits rather than special occasions only.

Ellamar's decision to open in Downtown Summerlin rather than on the Strip says something about where serious chefs see opportunity in Las Vegas right now. The neighborhood dining market is growing, and Fine Company is proof that off Strip locations can attract top tier culinary talent.

What to Order at Fine Company

The Banana Bread has become the restaurant's unexpected signature, a pastry so good it has developed its own following on social media and among locals who stop in specifically for it. The Whipped Feta is another must order starter that pairs perfectly with the cocktail menu. For heartier options, the Salt and Pepper Shrimp delivers crisp, well seasoned bites, and the Pastrami sandwich is one of the best lunch items in the entire Summerlin area.

The cocktail program is thoughtful and well executed, which makes Fine Company work equally well as a place to grab drinks and a small plate or to settle in for a full meal. The menu covers enough ground that it serves multiple purposes depending on the time of day and the mood.

Source: finecompanylv.com, Yelp

Location and Hours

Fine Company is located at 10970 Rosemary Park Drive within Downtown Summerlin, the area's premier open air shopping and dining center. Current hours run daily from 8 AM to 5 PM, making it primarily a breakfast through lunch and afternoon destination. That schedule works well for weekend outings, work from home lunch breaks, and morning meetings over coffee and pastry.

With 319 Yelp reviews and growing, Fine Company is still building its audience, but the quality of the food and the strength of Ellamar's reputation suggest it will continue to gain traction.

Fine Company in the Summerlin Dining Landscape

Fine Company adds to an already strong roster at Downtown Summerlin and earns consideration among the best restaurants near Summerlin. It also pairs well with a day of shopping and exploring Downtown Summerlin, making it a natural anchor for a weekend afternoon out.
