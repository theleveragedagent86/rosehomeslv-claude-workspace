Osteria Fiorella Summerlin: Italian Dining Review

When a James Beard Award winning chef opens a pasta bar inside a casino resort, it could go one of two ways. Osteria Fiorella at Red Rock Casino goes the right way. Chef Marc Vetri brought his beloved Philadelphia pasta bar concept to the west side of Las Vegas, and the result is one of the most compelling Italian dining experiences in Summerlin.

The Rose Homes LV team frequently fields questions from relocating buyers about Summerlin's dining options. Osteria Fiorella is a restaurant that Ryan Rose points to as evidence that this side of the valley has developed a food scene that can compete with anywhere in the city.

Chef Marc Vetri and the Fiorella Concept

Marc Vetri earned his James Beard Award as Best Chef Mid Atlantic, building his reputation through a string of acclaimed restaurants in Philadelphia. Osteria Fiorella is an expansion of that work, built around the idea of a lively Italian pasta bar where the food is serious but the atmosphere stays relaxed. The name Fiorella is an homage to Marc's grandmother, and that personal connection carries through the menu and the spirit of the place.

The restaurant sits inside Red Rock Casino Resort at 11011 W Charleston Blvd. The setting combines a warm, casual interior with an outdoor patio that catches the energy of the resort without feeling overly formal. It is the kind of place you can visit in jeans on a Tuesday or dress up for on a Saturday.

What to Order at Osteria Fiorella

The pasta is the main event. Ricotta gnocchi with brown butter is one of the standout dishes, delivering the kind of pillowy texture that only comes from a kitchen making everything by hand. The spaghetti with Bianco di Napoli tomatoes keeps things simple and lets the quality of the ingredients speak for itself. Sweet corn agnolotti is another favorite that showcases Vetri's ability to coax big flavor out of seasonal produce.

Sal's Meatballs deserve their own mention. Named in tribute to Marc's father, they anchor the appetizer section and set the tone for the meal. The kitchen also turns out wood fired pizzas from a Josper charcoal oven, giving the crusts a smoky depth that most pizza ovens cannot replicate.

The Dining Experience

Osteria Fiorella sits at the $$$ price point, which feels appropriate for the caliber of cooking. With 437 Yelp reviews and a strong reputation among locals, the restaurant has established itself as a regular destination rather than a one time occasion spot. The atmosphere is warm and lively without being loud, and the patio seating adds a nice option during cooler months in the desert.

For anyone already familiar with the dining options at Red Rock Casino, Fiorella stands out as the property's strongest Italian offering.

How Osteria Fiorella Fits Summerlin Dining

Osteria Fiorella ranks among the best Italian restaurants in Summerlin and adds serious culinary weight to a neighborhood already rich with dining options. Combined with spots like La Strega, Italian food lovers in Summerlin have access to a level of quality that used to require a drive to the Strip.
