## Meraki Greek Grill Summerlin Review Greek and Mediterranean food has a growing presence in the Summerlin area, and Meraki Greek Grill stands out as one of the most popular options on the west side. Located near the intersection of Charleston Boulevard and Fort Apache Road, Meraki has earned a 4.7 star rating across over 940 reviews, making it one of the highest rated casual restaurants in the 89135 and 89138 zip codes. ## What Does the Menu Look Like? Meraki Greek Grill keeps things focused on the classics done well. Gyros are the anchor of the menu, available with your choice of protein and built with fresh pita, house made tzatziki, and generous portions of meat. The chicken souvlaki is another popular order, featuring marinated and grilled chicken served with rice, salad, and warm pita on the side. Greek salad, hummus, falafel, and spanakopita fill out the appetizer and sides section. Nothing on the menu tries to reinvent the wheel, which is exactly what has earned Meraki its loyal customer base. The quality of the ingredients and the consistency of the kitchen are what keep people returning multiple times a month. Pricing sits firmly in the moderate range. Most entrees and combo plates fall between $12 and $20, making Meraki a comfortable choice for a quick lunch, a family dinner, or a casual meal after running errands along the Charleston corridor. ## How Is the Dining Experience? The space is clean, bright, and designed for efficiency. Meraki operates in a fast casual format where you order at the counter and your food is brought to your table. This makes it ideal for lunch breaks and families with younger kids who may not want to sit through a full service dining experience. Despite the casual setup, the food quality punches above its weight class. The staff is consistently praised in reviews for being welcoming and attentive. For takeout, Meraki handles online and phone orders efficiently, making it a reliable option for busy Summerlin residents who want quality Greek food without the wait. ## Where Is Meraki Greek Grill?
