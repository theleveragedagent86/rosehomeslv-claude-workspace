## The Willows Village Summerlin: Neighborhood Guide The Willows is one of the established villages in Summerlin South, known for mature landscaping, a resort style community pool, and a wide range of housing options spread across more than 20 sub communities. For buyers considering Summerlin South, The Willows offers the kind of settled, tree lined neighborhood feel that newer construction simply cannot replicate. ## What Sub Communities Make Up The Willows? The Willows contains 21 sub communities, giving buyers significant variety in terms of home styles, lot sizes, and price points. Notable sub communities include Autumn Ridge, Brentwood, Cambridge Court, Crown Ridge, Desert Willow, Glenbrook, Heather Glen, Sierra Woods, Willow Creek, Willow Falls, and Willow Glen. Each neighborhood has its own character, ranging from single story homes on quieter cul de sacs to larger two story properties with mountain views. This variety is part of what makes The Willows appealing to a broad range of buyers. Whether you are looking for a starter home in a well maintained community or a more spacious property with upgraded features, there is likely a sub community within The Willows that fits. ## What Are the Village Amenities? The Willows Pool is the centerpiece amenity for the village. It is a resort style facility with a beach entry design, meaning the pool gradually slopes into the water rather than requiring a traditional ladder or step entry. This makes it popular with families who have young children, as well as residents who simply prefer a more relaxed poolside experience. Beyond the pool, The Willows benefits from the broader Summerlin parks and trail system. Community parks dot the village, and residents have easy access to the Summerlin Trail network for walking, jogging, and cycling. The mature landscaping throughout The Willows gives the neighborhood a lush, established feel that stands in contrast to the desert aesthetic of newer Summerlin West developments. ## Where Does The Willows Sit Within Summerlin? The Willows is located in the Summerlin South section of the master plan, primarily within the 89135 zip code. It sits in a premium area of Summerlin South, with convenient access to Charleston Boulevard, Town Center Drive, and the 215 Beltway. Shopping, dining, and schools are all within a short drive, and Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area is just minutes to the west. ## What Should Buyers Know About The Willows? Because The Willows is an established village, most homes were built in the late 1990s and early 2000s. Buyers should factor in the age of major systems like roofing, HVAC, and water heaters when evaluating properties. The tradeoff is that you get mature trees, larger lots in many cases, and a neighborhood that has already proven itself over two decades of homeownership. HOA fees in The Willows cover access to the village pool, common area maintenance, and landscaping in shared spaces. The Summerlin master association fee applies on top of the sub community HOA. ## Work With Rose Homes LV in The Willows Ryan Rose at Rose Homes LV has helped buyers and sellers throughout The Willows and the surrounding Summerlin South villages.
