The Cliffs Village Summerlin: Neighborhood Guide

The Cliffs represents a newer chapter in Summerlin's development, a village within Summerlin South that brings together modern construction, diverse housing options, and the premium positioning that the South section is known for. Spanning portions of the 89135 and 89148 zip codes, The Cliffs appeals to a wide range of buyers, from active adults seeking a 55 plus community to families and professionals drawn to newer Summerlin neighborhoods. Ryan Rose, who guides buyers through Summerlin's evolving landscape, highlights The Cliffs as a village where newer inventory and thoughtful planning come together.

Sub Communities in The Cliffs

The Cliffs contains 10 sub communities: Granite Heights, Ironwood, Jade Ridge, Nova Ridge, Oluna, Onyx Point, Regency at Summerlin, Skystone, Terra Luna, and Vistara. The names reflect the desert and geological influences that shape much of Summerlin South's identity. Each sub community offers a distinct product type, from single family homes in Skystone and Granite Heights to the specialized living at Regency.

Nova Ridge and Oluna represent some of the more recent additions to The Cliffs, bringing contemporary floor plans, energy efficient construction, and the open concept layouts that today's buyers expect. Jade Ridge and Onyx Point similarly offer newer homes with modern finishes. For buyers who want to purchase in Summerlin without inheriting someone else's design choices, The Cliffs provides that fresh start.

Regency at Summerlin: 55 Plus Living by Toll Brothers

The standout community within The Cliffs is Regency at Summerlin, a gated 55 plus neighborhood developed by Toll Brothers. Regency caters to active adults who want the quality and craftsmanship that Toll Brothers is known for, combined with the amenities and social structure of an age restricted community. The gated entrance provides security, while the interior features its own recreational facilities designed specifically for the 55 plus demographic.

For buyers comparing 55 plus options in Summerlin, Regency at Summerlin offers a markedly different experience than Sun City Summerlin. Sun City is the original, larger, and more established 55 plus community in Summerlin North. Regency is newer, smaller, and built to the standards of a premium national builder. Both serve the active adult market, but they attract different buyer profiles based on budget, preferred home style, and community size.

Summerlin South Premium

The Cliffs benefits from its Summerlin South address. This section of the master plan tends to carry higher price points, newer construction, and closer proximity to Red Rock Canyon and the western mountain views that make Summerlin's South and West sections so desirable. Access to the 215 Beltway, quality schools, and the growing retail and dining options along the southern corridor keep daily life convenient.
