Active Toll Brothers Communities

Cordillera, located within Kestrel at Redpoint Square, features three story townhomes with rooftop decks. This product type appeals to buyers who want a modern urban feel without leaving Summerlin's suburban infrastructure. Raven Crest sits in Kestrel Commons and offers a similar three story layout with rooftop living, targeting a slightly different price bracket and floor plan configuration.

The Loughton is a coming soon community in Summerlin South with pricing starting in the low $500s. For buyers watching for Toll Brothers' most accessible entry point, The Loughton represents the floor of the builder's Summerlin pricing. It fills a gap that previously forced value conscious buyers to look at other builders entirely.

Luxury and 55 Plus Options

Regency at Summerlin is Toll Brothers' age restricted community, designed for the 55 and older demographic. With 434 homes planned and floor plans ranging from 1,665 to 2,659 square feet, Regency offers single story living with resort style amenities. The community has its own clubhouse, pool complex, and social calendar, creating a self contained lifestyle that draws retirees from across the country.

Ascension takes the builder's presence into guard gated territory, with 561 homes planned behind controlled access. This community targets the upper end of Summerlin's new construction market, where buyers expect privacy, larger lot sizes, and architectural distinction that separates their home from production models.
