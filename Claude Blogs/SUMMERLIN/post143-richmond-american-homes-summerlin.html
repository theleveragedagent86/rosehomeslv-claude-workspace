The Design Center Advantage

Richmond American's Home Gallery is where the builder truly differentiates itself. Rather than choosing from a short list of included option packages, buyers visit a dedicated design center and work with a consultant to select countertops, cabinetry, flooring, tile, fixtures, and architectural details. The experience is closer to working with an interior designer than checking boxes on a builder option sheet.

This personalization carries financial implications that buyers should understand. Upgrades at the design center add to the base price, and it is easy for selections to push the final cost well above the starting figure. Having a Realtor present during the design process helps keep spending aligned with the home's expected appraised value and the buyer's overall budget.
