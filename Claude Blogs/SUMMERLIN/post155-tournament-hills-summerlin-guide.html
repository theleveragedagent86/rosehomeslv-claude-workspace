Tournament Hills Summerlin: Guard-Gated Community Guide

Tournament Hills carries one of the most recognizable names in Summerlin real estate. Located in The Hills South village within Summerlin North, this guard-gated community sits near TPC Summerlin, the private golf club that hosts the PGA Tour's Shriners Children's Open each fall. The proximity to TPC, combined with custom and semi-custom homes on generous lots, has made Tournament Hills home to some of the most prestigious addresses in the Las Vegas valley.

According to local Realtor Ryan Rose, "Tournament Hills is the kind of community where the name alone tells you what to expect. These are serious homes in a serious setting, and the resale values have reflected that consistency for decades."

Custom Homes Near TPC Summerlin

The housing stock in Tournament Hills is built around custom and semi-custom construction, which gives the community an architectural diversity that production neighborhoods cannot achieve. Homes range from elegantly updated estates to properties that have been meticulously maintained in their original condition, each reflecting the tastes and priorities of individual owners rather than a single builder's template.

Floor plans tend toward the generous end of the spectrum, with square footage, ceiling heights, and room counts that cater to buyers expecting space for both everyday living and large scale entertaining. Many properties feature dedicated home offices, theater rooms, wine storage, and multi-car garages that accommodate collections or workshop space. The lots themselves are among the largest available in Summerlin's guard-gated inventory, providing ample room for pools, outdoor kitchens, and landscaped grounds.

The TPC Summerlin Connection

Living near TPC Summerlin is not just a talking point. The proximity to one of the most well known private golf courses in the region shapes the community's identity and its market appeal. Select homes within Tournament Hills offer views of the TPC course, and the overall atmosphere of the neighborhood benefits from bordering a meticulously maintained championship layout.

During the annual Shriners tournament, the area takes on an energy that residents either embrace as a highlight of the calendar year or manage with practiced ease. The event brings national visibility to the neighborhood and reinforces the prestige that attracted many homeowners in the first place. For golf members, the walk or short drive to the clubhouse is a daily convenience that becomes part of the lifestyle rather than a special occasion.

The Hills South Village and 89134

Tournament Hills sits within The Hills South, a village that includes several of North Summerlin's most established neighborhoods. The 89134 zip code covers this section of Summerlin and includes a mix of housing types, but Tournament Hills represents the upper tier of what the zip code has to offer.

The village's location provides efficient access to the 215 Beltway, Summerlin Parkway, and the commercial centers that serve North Summerlin residents. Schools in the area are well regarded, parks and trails are within easy reach, and the overall infrastructure reflects the maturity of a section of Summerlin that has been fully developed for years. For families, the combination of a guard-gated environment with strong neighborhood schools creates a particularly compelling package.

Touring Tournament Hills

Tournament Hills is a community that rewards in person visits. The scale of the lots, the quality of the construction, and the feel of the neighborhood are best appreciated by driving through the streets and seeing how the homes relate to the landscape and to one another. Photos and virtual tours convey the basics, but the sense of arrival that comes with passing through the guard gate and entering the community is something that needs to be experienced firsthand.
