Eagle Hills Summerlin: Guard Gated Community Guide

Eagle Hills is one of Summerlin's most established guard gated communities, tucked inside The Hills South village in Summerlin North. With approximately 160 custom built homes spread across generous lots, Eagle Hills delivers the kind of privacy and architectural variety that production neighborhoods simply cannot replicate. The community sits within the 89134 zip code and has been a draw for luxury buyers since homes first went up in the mid 1990s.

Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas Realtor focused on Summerlin's luxury market, considers Eagle Hills one of the most underrated guard gated options in the entire master plan.

Homes and Architecture

Eagle Hills homes were built between 1995 and 2000 by various custom builders, which is why the streetscape carries more visual diversity than typical Summerlin neighborhoods. Floor plans range from around 2,924 square feet to well over 14,000 square feet for the largest estates. Architectural styles span contemporary, classic, and Mediterranean influences, and no two homes look alike. Lot sizes start at roughly a quarter acre and extend beyond a half acre for the premium positions, giving homeowners the kind of yard space that is increasingly rare in Las Vegas.

The custom nature of Eagle Hills means that renovated homes sit alongside original condition properties. Buyers will find a wide price spread depending on updates, lot position, and total square footage. Security and Community Amenities

Eagle Hills operates a 24 hour staffed guard gate, meaning a live attendant screens every visitor entering the community. This is a step above electronic gate access and provides an added layer of security that residents consistently cite as a top reason for choosing Eagle Hills over non gated alternatives.

Inside the gates, a private three acre park serves as the community gathering space. The park includes a walking trail, two tennis courts, a basketball court, a playground, and covered pavilions for events and gatherings. Tree lined streets and lush landscaping throughout the community create a manicured feel that sets Eagle Hills apart from surrounding neighborhoods. The adjacent golf courses add to the visual openness and give many homes elevated views or fairway frontage.

Location and Lifestyle

Eagle Hills benefits from its position in Summerlin North, with convenient access to the 215 Beltway, Downtown Summerlin shopping, and multiple golf courses. The neighborhood's proximity to Tournament Hills places it in one of the most concentrated luxury corridors in the master planned community. Residents are minutes from dining, retail, and medical facilities while living behind gates on streets that feel removed from the pace of the Las Vegas Valley.

For buyers exploring the broader Summerlin living experience, Eagle Hills represents a category of home that blends custom quality with master plan infrastructure. The community will appeal most to buyers who value lot size, architectural individuality, and the peace of mind that comes with staffed gate security.
