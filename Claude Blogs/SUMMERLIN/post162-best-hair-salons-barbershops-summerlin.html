Best Hair Salons and Barbershops in Summerlin

Whether you just moved to Summerlin or have lived here for years, finding a great hair salon or barbershop is one of those small quality of life details that makes a neighborhood feel like home. Summerlin offers a solid mix of upscale salons, neighborhood barbershops, and independent stylists spread across its commercial corridors.

Realtor Ryan Rose has helped countless people settle into Summerlin, and one question that comes up more often than you might expect is where to find a good haircut nearby. Here is what the local scene looks like.

Upscale Salons and Specialty Experiences

Scissors and Scotch at Tivoli Village has become one of the standout grooming destinations in Summerlin. The concept combines a full service salon and barbershop with a lounge atmosphere, complete with cocktails during your appointment. It appeals to both men and women looking for a cut that feels more like an experience than an errand. Tivoli Village itself is a walkable retail district with restaurants and shopping, so it is easy to build a full afternoon around a salon visit.

Several other high end salons operate along the Charleston and Rampart corridors, offering everything from precision color work to keratin treatments and extensions. Many of these salons employ stylists with advanced certifications and training from brands like Redken, Aveda, or Oribe. If you are particular about color matching or balayage technique, Summerlin has specialists who focus on those services exclusively.

Source: Tivoli Village directory, Yelp, local salon listings

Barbershops for Men and Kids

The barbershop scene in Summerlin ranges from classic neighborhood shops to modern grooming studios. Traditional barbershops offering straight razor shaves, fades, and beard trims can be found in several shopping centers throughout the community. These shops tend to build loyal followings, and many operate on a walk in basis with reasonable wait times during weekday hours.

For families with kids, several Summerlin barbershops cater specifically to younger clients with kid friendly chairs, screens, and patient barbers who know how to work with children who might not sit still easily. If you have recently relocated with a young family, asking neighbors in your village for a barbershop recommendation is one of the fastest ways to find a good fit.

Independent Stylists and Suite Rentals

A growing number of stylists in Summerlin operate independently out of salon suite rentals. These private studio spaces allow individual stylists to manage their own business and pricing, giving clients a more personalized experience.

You will find salon suite complexes in several Summerlin shopping centers. The advantage for clients is that independent stylists often have more scheduling flexibility, including evening and weekend availability. Many build their reputations through Instagram and word of mouth, so social media is a useful tool for discovering talented stylists in the area.

Source: Local business directories, Google Maps, salon suite operators

National Chains and Budget Options

Summerlin also has its share of national salon chains for quick, budget friendly cuts. These are convenient for routine maintenance trims or when you need an appointment on short notice. While they may not offer the same level of customization as a boutique salon, they fill an important gap for busy residents who value speed and consistency.
