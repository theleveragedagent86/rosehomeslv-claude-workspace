Summerlin is one of the most pet friendly communities in the Las Vegas valley. With over 300 parks, dedicated dog parks, and miles of walking trails, it is no surprise that dogs are a fixture of daily life here. Keeping those dogs groomed and healthy requires reliable pet service providers, and the community has plenty of options to choose from.

Realtor Ryan Rose works with many buyers who consider pet friendliness a priority when choosing a neighborhood. He points out that Summerlin's walkability and abundance of green space make it especially attractive to dog owners relocating to Las Vegas.

Grooming Services Available in Summerlin

Most grooming salons in the area offer a full menu of services. A standard grooming visit typically includes a bath, haircut or breed specific trim, nail trimming, ear cleaning, and teeth brushing. Many salons also provide add on services like de shedding treatments, flea baths, and coat conditioning, which are especially useful in the dry desert climate that can leave a dog's skin and coat feeling rough.

Pricing varies by breed, size, and coat condition. A basic bath and brush for a small to medium dog generally runs $30 to $50, while a full groom for a larger breed with a thick coat can range from $60 to $100 or more. Regular grooming every four to eight weeks is recommended for most breeds, though desert dust and outdoor activity in Summerlin may call for more frequent visits.

Mobile Grooming: A Growing Option

Mobile grooming services have become increasingly popular in Summerlin, particularly among busy professionals and families who value convenience. A mobile groomer brings a fully equipped van to your driveway, handles the entire appointment on site, and returns your dog clean and trimmed without you ever leaving home.

This option is also ideal for dogs that experience anxiety in salon settings. The one on one attention in a quieter environment can make the grooming experience less stressful. Mobile grooming typically costs a bit more than salon visits, but many pet owners find the convenience and comfort well worth the premium.

Beyond Grooming: Other Pet Services

Summerlin residents have access to a wide range of pet services beyond grooming. Doggy daycare facilities provide socialization and exercise for dogs whose owners work long hours. Pet sitting and dog walking services are readily available through both local companies and app based platforms. For veterinary needs, the community is home to multiple well regarded animal hospitals and clinics, which is covered in more detail in our veterinarian guide.

Pet supply stores throughout the area stock everything from premium food to desert specific products like paw wax, which protects dogs' pads from hot pavement during summer months when ground temperatures can exceed 150 degrees.
