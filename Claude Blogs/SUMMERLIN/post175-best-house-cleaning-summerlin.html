What Cleaning Services Cost in Summerlin

For a standard cleaning of a typical Summerlin home, expect to pay between $120 and $250 per visit. Pricing depends on the square footage of the home, the number of bedrooms and bathrooms, and the scope of work included. Many homes in Summerlin range from 2,000 to 4,000 square feet or more, which means cleaning takes longer and costs more than what residents may have experienced in smaller properties elsewhere.

Deep cleaning runs higher, generally between $200 and $400. This level of service covers areas that standard cleaning does not always address: inside appliances, baseboards, window tracks, ceiling fans, light fixtures, and detailed bathroom scrubbing. Most providers recommend a deep clean as a starting point for new clients, followed by recurring standard cleanings to maintain the results.

Recurring service plans, whether weekly, biweekly, or monthly, typically come with discounted per visit pricing. Biweekly cleaning is the most popular frequency among Summerlin homeowners and strikes a good balance between keeping the home consistently clean and managing cost.

Move In, Move Out, and Post Construction Cleaning

Summerlin's active real estate market and ongoing new construction create steady demand for specialty cleaning services. Move in and move out cleaning ensures a home is spotless for the next occupant, which is especially important for sellers who want to make the best possible impression during showings and final walkthroughs.

Post construction cleaning is another service that sees consistent demand in Summerlin. New builds and major renovations leave behind construction dust, adhesive residue, paint overspray, and fine debris that a standard cleaning cannot handle. Professional post construction cleaning typically requires multiple passes and specialized equipment. If you are purchasing a new build in one of Summerlin's developing areas, budget for this service before moving your furniture in.

Choosing a Cleaning Service

Look for companies that are licensed and insured. Ask whether they bring their own supplies and equipment or expect you to provide them. Confirm whether the same team will service your home each visit, as consistency leads to better results over time. References and online reviews are helpful, but a trial visit is the best way to evaluate whether a cleaning company meets your standards.

For homeowners who also need help with exterior maintenance, pairing a cleaning service with a landscaping company and pest control provider covers the major recurring maintenance needs for a Summerlin property.
