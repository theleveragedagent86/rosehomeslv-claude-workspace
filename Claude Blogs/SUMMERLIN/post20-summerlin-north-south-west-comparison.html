Summerlin is divided into three main sections, North, South, and West, each offering distinct home styles, price points, and lifestyles that cater to different buyer priorities. ## What Defines Summerlin North? Summerlin North, centered around zip code 89134, is the most established section of the community. Villages here include The Hills, The Trails, Sun City Summerlin (a large 55 plus community), The Canyons, The Arbors, The Gardens, and The Willows. Mature landscaping, tree lined streets, and a settled neighborhood feel characterize this area. Home prices tend to be more accessible than South or West, making it attractive to buyers seeking value within the Summerlin brand. Sun City Summerlin alone offers over 7,700 homes with its own golf courses, recreation centers, and social programming for active adults. ## What Sets Summerlin South Apart? Summerlin South, primarily in zip code 89135, is home to some of the community's most prestigious addresses. The Ridges, Summit Club, and Red Rock Country Club all sit within this section, along with villages like The Cliffs, Siena (a guard gated 55 plus community), and The Mesa. The average home value in Summerlin South runs approximately $712,766, reflecting a 2.2% year over year increase. Buyers drawn to luxury estates, golf course living, and guard gated security tend to focus their search here. The elevated terrain provides the best views of both Red Rock Canyon and the Las Vegas Strip. ## How Is Summerlin West Different? Summerlin West is the newest section, still actively developing with villages such as Reverence, Stonebridge, Redpoint, Kestrel, and Grand Park. The median home price sits around $800,000, with a modest 0.3% annual increase. Homes here feature the most modern construction, open floor plans, and energy efficient systems. Days on market average 98, giving buyers more time and negotiating room than in the other two sections. Families and out of state relocators gravitate to Summerlin West for the combination of new schools, updated amenities, and proximity to Red Rock Canyon trailheads. ## Which Section Is Right for You?
