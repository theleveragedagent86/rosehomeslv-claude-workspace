CCSD School Calendar and Key Dates for Summerlin Parents

Families relocating to Summerlin need to understand the Clark County School District calendar well before the moving truck arrives. CCSD is the fifth largest school district in the country, and its schedule drives everything from childcare planning to vacation bookings to the best time to close on a home. Knowing the key dates in advance takes a lot of the stress out of a family move.

The Traditional CCSD School Year

Most Summerlin schools follow the traditional CCSD calendar, which runs from mid August through late May or early June. The first day of school typically lands in the second or third week of August, and the final day falls in the last week of May or the first days of June. This schedule applies to the majority of elementary, middle, and high schools that serve Summerlin neighborhoods.

Within the school year, three major breaks shape the family calendar. Fall break generally falls in late October and provides a long weekend or a full week depending on the year. Winter break spans from approximately the week before Christmas through the first week of January. Spring break lands in late March or early April, aligning roughly with Easter. These breaks are consistent enough to plan around but the exact dates shift slightly each year, so checking the official CCSD calendar at ccsd.net is always recommended.

Year Round Tracks and Alternative Schedules

While most Summerlin schools follow the traditional calendar, CCSD does operate some year round schools across the valley. Year round tracks rotate students through shorter, more frequent breaks throughout the calendar year rather than concentrating time off during summer. These schools are less common in the Summerlin area, but parents who are open to the format should ask about availability during enrollment. The schedule can actually be convenient for families who prefer to travel during off peak months.

Planning a Move Around the School Calendar

Summer is by far the easiest window for a family relocation. Closing on a Summerlin home in June or July gives families time to unpack, explore the neighborhood, and handle enrollment paperwork before school starts in August. The best school zones in Summerlin tend to see strong demand during this window, so starting the home search in spring puts families in the best position.

Mid year transfers are absolutely possible within CCSD. The district accommodates students who move during the school year, and zoned schools will enroll new students at any point. That said, the transition is smoother when it coincides with the start of a new semester or follows one of the major breaks. Moving over winter break, for example, lets a child start fresh in January without missing instructional days.

Important Enrollment and Registration Dates

CCSD opens online registration for the upcoming school year in the spring, typically around March or April. Parents can register through the Infinite Campus portal on the CCSD website. Required documents include proof of residency (a utility bill or lease agreement works), immunization records, and a birth certificate. Families who are still in the process of purchasing a home can often use a contract or closing documents as temporary proof of address.

For families weighing neighborhoods based on school quality, it pays to verify school zoning before making an offer. CCSD zone boundaries occasionally shift, and confirming your home address feeds into the right school removes any surprises after closing.
