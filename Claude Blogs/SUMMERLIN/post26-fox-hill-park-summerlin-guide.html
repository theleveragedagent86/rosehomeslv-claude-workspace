## Fox Hill Park Summerlin: Las Vegas' Best Playground Fox Hill Park is an 18 acre adventure playground in Summerlin featuring 20+ play structures, a zip line, and a 32 foot climbing tower, making it the top family destination in Las Vegas. ## What Makes Fox Hill Park So Popular with Families? Fox Hill Park stands out as the crown jewel of Summerlin's 300+ park system for one simple reason: it was designed to thrill kids of every age. Spread across 18 acres, the park packs in more than 20 individual play structures, each built to challenge and delight. The centerpiece is "The Orange Beast," a massive, unmistakable climbing and sliding feature that has become a landmark for Summerlin families. Children line up to conquer it on weekends, and parents gather nearby to watch and socialize. Beyond The Orange Beast, the park delivers experiences you simply won't find at a typical neighborhood playground. A full zip line lets older kids soar across an open stretch of the park, while a towering 32 foot climbing structure tests balance and bravery. Younger children have dedicated areas with age appropriate equipment, so toddlers and preschoolers can explore safely while their siblings tackle the bigger features. ## Is Fox Hill Park Free to Visit? Yes. Fox Hill Park is completely free and open to the public. There's no entry fee, no reservation required, and no membership needed. The park includes shaded seating areas, restrooms, and open green space for picnics or casual sports. Many families make a full morning or afternoon of their visit, packing coolers and blankets for an extended outing. ## When Is the Best Time to Visit Fox Hill Park? Timing matters in the Las Vegas climate. With summer highs reaching 100 to 115 degrees, the ideal months for outdoor play are October through April. Summerlin sits at a higher elevation than central Las Vegas, keeping temperatures about 5 to 10 degrees cooler, which helps extend comfortable outdoor hours. Early mornings and late afternoons are the sweet spots during warmer months. Weekend mornings tend to be the busiest, so weekday visits offer a more relaxed experience. ## How Does Fox Hill Park Connect to the Summerlin Trail System? One of the best features of Fox Hill Park is its connectivity. The park links into Summerlin's 200+ mile trail network, meaning families can walk, jog, or bike to the park from many surrounding villages.
