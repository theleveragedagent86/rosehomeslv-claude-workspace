## Summerlin Community Parks: 300+ Parks Overview Summerlin is home to more than 300 parks, including 32 major community parks ranging from 5 to 20 acres, making it one of the most park rich master planned communities in the entire country. ## How Many Parks Does Summerlin Have? The number is staggering: over 300 parks spread across Summerlin's villages and neighborhoods. These range from small pocket parks tucked between residential streets to expansive 20 acre community parks with full amenity packages. Every village in Summerlin is designed around a central community park, which means no resident lives far from quality green space and recreation facilities. The 32 major community parks, each spanning 5 to 20 acres, form the backbone of the system. These larger parks serve as social hubs where neighbors gather for sports leagues, birthday parties, holiday events, and everyday outdoor activity. Smaller neighborhood parks fill in the gaps, ensuring that even a short walk from any front door leads to open space. ## What Amenities Do Summerlin Parks Include? The variety is exceptional. Across the park system, residents have access to 20 tennis courts, 18 pickleball courts, 17 baseball and softball fields, 14 basketball courts, 11 soccer fields, 4 community centers, 3 swimming pools, 3 sand volleyball courts, 1 football field, and 1 outdoor roller skating rink. Several parks also feature interactive water play structures, which are especially popular with young children during warmer months. Fox Hill Park, the system's flagship, draws families from across the valley with its 20+ play structures, zip line, and iconic 32 foot climbing tower. But every village park brings something worthwhile: shaded pavilions, walking loops, open fields, and dedicated play areas that serve residents of all ages. ## How Are the Parks Connected? Summerlin's 200+ mile trail system ties the parks together into a continuous network. Residents can walk, jog, or bike from one park to the next without ever crossing a major road in many areas. This connectivity transforms the parks from isolated destinations into an integrated outdoor lifestyle system. Morning joggers, afternoon cyclists, and evening strollers all benefit from the seamless connections between green spaces. ## Do Summerlin Parks Have Community Programming? Yes. The four community centers host organized activities, fitness classes, seasonal events, and youth programming. Sports leagues for adults and children operate across the park system's fields and courts throughout the year. The community calendar stays active even during summer months, with early morning and evening programming designed around the climate. ## What Ryan Rose Tells Buyers About Summerlin's Parks Ryan Rose, a Realtor who helps clients buy and sell in Summerlin, emphasizes that the park system is not just an amenity; it's infrastructure.
