## TPC Summerlin: PGA Tour Home Course TPC Summerlin is the only PGA TOUR venue in Las Vegas, hosting the Shriners Children's Open each fall on a Bobby Weed designed course with Fuzzy Zoeller as design consultant. ## What Makes TPC Summerlin Unique? TPC Summerlin holds a distinction no other course in the Las Vegas Valley can claim: it is a permanent stop on the PGA TOUR. Each October, the Shriners Children's Open brings the world's top golfers to Summerlin for a tournament that draws tens of thousands of spectators and national television coverage. That annual event alone puts TPC Summerlin in a class occupied by only a handful of clubs nationwide. The course itself was designed by Bobby Weed with input from Fuzzy Zoeller, blending strategic desert golf with the natural terrain of the western Las Vegas Valley. Rolling elevation changes, sweeping views of Red Rock Canyon, and meticulously maintained conditions define the playing experience. The layout rewards thoughtful shot placement over raw power, making it a favorite among members who appreciate course management. ## Is TPC Summerlin Open to the Public? TPC Summerlin operates as a private membership club. Access is limited to members and their guests, along with select corporate and charity events throughout the year. The Shriners Children's Open provides the one major opportunity for the general public to walk the grounds and experience the course up close. Membership details are handled directly through the TPC network, and availability fluctuates based on demand. ## How Does the Shriners Children's Open Impact Summerlin? The tournament is more than a sporting event. It generates significant charitable contributions, with proceeds benefiting the Shriners Children's healthcare system. For the Summerlin community, the event brings national visibility, increased foot traffic to local businesses, and a sense of pride in hosting a world class competition. Properties near TPC Summerlin often see heightened buyer interest around tournament season, as the event showcases the lifestyle and scenery of the area. ## What Is It Like Living Near TPC Summerlin?
