## Bear's Best Golf Club Summerlin: Nicklaus Design Bear's Best Las Vegas is a Jack Nicklaus designed public course in Summerlin that recreates 18 of his favorite holes from courses around the world, all in one stunning desert setting. ## What Is the Concept Behind Bear's Best? Bear's Best Las Vegas takes a completely original approach to course design. Jack Nicklaus selected 18 of his most iconic holes from courses he designed across the globe and rebuilt them in the foothills of Summerlin. Each hole is a faithful reproduction of the original, giving golfers the chance to play legendary layouts from courses they may never visit otherwise. The result is a "greatest hits" collection from one of golf's most celebrated architects, all within a single round. ## Is Bear's Best Open to the Public? Yes. Bear's Best operates as a premium public course, meaning anyone can book a tee time without a membership. This accessibility sets it apart from Summerlin's private clubs. Green fees reflect the caliber of the experience, positioning Bear's Best as a step above standard municipal courses while remaining available to all golfers. The course is popular with both residents and visitors, making advance reservations a smart move during peak season from October through April. ## What Are the Playing Conditions Like? The course sits near The Ridges, one of Summerlin's most exclusive residential areas, and the elevation provides sweeping views of Red Rock Canyon and the Las Vegas skyline. Desert landscaping frames each fairway, and the Nicklaus design philosophy emphasizes strategic options off the tee. Conditions are maintained to a high standard year round, benefiting from the dry desert climate that keeps fairways firm and greens fast. The layout plays at a challenging length from the back tees but offers multiple tee boxes to accommodate all skill levels. ## How Does Bear's Best Compare to Other Summerlin Courses? Among public options, Bear's Best occupies the premium tier. Angel Park Golf Club offers a more accessible price point with two Arnold Palmer courses, while Bear's Best delivers a more curated, boutique experience. For golfers seeking the prestige of a Nicklaus design without the commitment of a private club, Bear's Best fills that gap perfectly. ## Local Market Insight According to Ryan Rose of Rose Homes LV, homes near Bear's Best and The Ridges area attract buyers who appreciate both golf access and high end surroundings.
