## Angel Park Golf Club: Public Golf in Summerlin Angel Park Golf Club offers two Arnold Palmer designed public courses in Summerlin with panoramic Red Rock views, making it the most accessible championship golf experience in western Las Vegas. ## What Courses Does Angel Park Offer? Angel Park Golf Club features two full 18 hole courses, both designed by Arnold Palmer. The Mountain Course plays through elevated terrain with dramatic views of the Red Rock Canyon foothills, offering a more challenging layout with significant elevation changes. The Palm Course provides a slightly more forgiving experience with wider fairways and a more traditional desert feel. Together, the two courses give golfers variety without leaving the property, and both deliver the quality you expect from a Palmer design. ## How Affordable Is Public Golf at Angel Park? Angel Park positions itself as the everyday golfer's home course in Summerlin. Green fees are significantly lower than the premium rates at Bear's Best, making it realistic to play multiple times per month without straining the budget. Twilight rates, loyalty programs, and seasonal promotions bring costs down further. For Summerlin residents who want regular access to quality golf, Angel Park offers the best value in the community. ## What Sets Angel Park Apart from Other Summerlin Courses? Location and views define the Angel Park experience. Situated at the base of the Red Rock foothills, the courses offer unobstructed panoramic scenery that most private clubs in the valley cannot match. The open desert landscape frames every hole, and on clear days, the contrast between the emerald fairways and the rust colored mountains creates a visual experience unique to this part of Las Vegas. The club also operates a short game practice facility and putting course, giving players additional ways to sharpen their skills. ## Is Angel Park Good for Beginners? Absolutely. The Palm Course in particular welcomes newer golfers with its wider landing areas and more straightforward hole designs. Angel Park also offers instruction programs and clinics for players looking to improve. Multiple tee box options on both courses allow beginners to play from forward positions, keeping the round enjoyable while they develop their game. ## A Realtor's Take on Angel Park Living Ryan Rose with Rose Homes LV frequently shows homes near Angel Park to buyers who want golf course proximity at an attainable price point.
