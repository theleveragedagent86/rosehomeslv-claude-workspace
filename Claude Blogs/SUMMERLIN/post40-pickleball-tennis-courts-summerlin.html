## Pickleball and Tennis Courts in Summerlin Summerlin provides 18 dedicated pickleball courts and 20 tennis courts spread across four community centers, giving residents year round access to racquet sports in the Las Vegas sun. ## Where Are the Pickleball Courts in Summerlin? Summerlin's 18 pickleball courts are distributed across the community's four recreation centers, ensuring that most residents live within a short drive or bike ride of a court. The sport has surged in popularity across Las Vegas, and Summerlin has responded by expanding dedicated court space in recent years. Courts are maintained by the Summerlin Council and available to residents as part of community membership. Many courts feature lighting for evening play, extending usable hours well past sunset during the warmer months. ## How Many Tennis Courts Does Summerlin Have? Summerlin maintains 20 tennis courts throughout the community. Like the pickleball facilities, these courts are connected to the four community centers and available to association members. Courts are surfaced for desert conditions, built to handle the intense heat and UV exposure common in southern Nevada. Both recreational and competitive players will find well maintained facilities, and organized leagues and clinics run throughout the year for various skill levels. ## Can You Play Both Sports at the Same Facility? Yes. Several of Summerlin's community centers offer both pickleball and tennis courts on the same campus, along with additional amenities like basketball courts, pools, and fitness areas. This makes it easy for families or groups with mixed interests to visit one location and each find something to enjoy. The four community centers also serve as social hubs, hosting events, classes, and seasonal programming beyond racquet sports. ## What Other Sports Facilities Does Summerlin Offer? Beyond pickleball and tennis, Summerlin provides 14 basketball courts, 3 community pools, and hundreds of miles of trails for walking, running, and cycling. The 200 plus mile trail system connects many of these facilities, so residents can jog or bike to a court rather than drive. This network of active living amenities is one of the defining features of the Summerlin lifestyle and a major draw for health conscious buyers. ## On the Ground in Summerlin Ryan Rose, Summerlin real estate specialist with Rose Homes LV, sees growing demand from buyers who specifically ask about pickleball access.
