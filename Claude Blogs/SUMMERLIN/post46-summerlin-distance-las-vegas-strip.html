## Summerlin Distance to the Las Vegas Strip Summerlin sits roughly 20 minutes from the Las Vegas Strip by car, giving residents quick access to world class entertainment while living in a quiet, master planned community on the western edge of the valley. ## How Far Is Summerlin from the Las Vegas Strip? The drive from Summerlin to the heart of the Las Vegas Strip covers about 17 miles and typically takes around 20 minutes. Summerlin Parkway feeds directly onto US 95, which connects to the I 15 corridor running along the Strip. During off peak hours, the commute can be as short as 15 minutes. Rush hour traffic or major event nights may push the drive closer to 30 minutes, but the route remains straightforward with minimal turns or confusing interchanges. For residents who prefer not to drive, rideshare services like Uber and Lyft make the trip convenient and affordable. RTC bus routes also serve major corridors between Summerlin and central Las Vegas, though service is more limited compared to the inner valley. ## How Close Is Summerlin to the Airport? Harry Reid International Airport sits about 25 to 30 minutes from Summerlin, depending on traffic. The route follows I 215 south and connects to I 15, offering a direct path without navigating through residential streets. This proximity makes Summerlin an attractive option for frequent travelers and professionals who rely on easy airport access. ## What Other Key Destinations Are Nearby? Downtown Las Vegas is roughly 20 to 25 minutes away, providing access to the Arts District, Fremont Street, and a growing food and nightlife scene. In the opposite direction, Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area is just 10 to 15 minutes from most Summerlin neighborhoods. This balance between urban convenience and natural beauty is what makes the location so appealing. The I 215 Beltway rings the western and southern edges of Summerlin, connecting residents to Henderson, the south valley, and beyond. Whether commuting to work, catching a flight, or heading out for a weekend hike, Summerlin keeps every major destination within a comfortable reach. ## A Local Perspective Las Vegas Realtor Ryan Rose frequently highlights Summerlin's location as one of its strongest selling points.
