## Summerlin vs Henderson: Which Is Better for Las Vegas Homebuyers? Summerlin and Henderson are both top tier master planned communities in the Las Vegas Valley, but they serve different lifestyles and budgets. Here is how they compare across the categories that matter most. ## How Do Home Prices Compare Between Summerlin and Henderson? Summerlin carries a higher median home price of roughly $686,000 in 2026, reflecting a 9.8% year over year increase. Henderson, depending on the neighborhood, ranges from $500,000 to $600,000 at the median. Communities like Green Valley and Anthem sit on the more affordable end, while MacDonald Ranch pushes closer to Summerlin territory. Buyers stretching for more square footage or a newer build will generally find better value in Henderson, though Summerlin commands a premium for its established reputation and westside location near Red Rock Canyon. ## Which Community Has Better Schools and Amenities? Both communities boast highly rated public and private schools, making either a strong pick for families. Summerlin offers 26 schools within its borders, more than 300 parks, and over 200 miles of interconnected trails. Henderson counters with its own impressive park system, the Galleria at Sunset, and The District at Green Valley Ranch for shopping and dining. Summerlin edges ahead with Downtown Summerlin, a walkable retail and entertainment hub that includes the Las Vegas Ballpark. Henderson offers proximity to Lake Mead and the Henderson Bird Viewing Preserve, giving nature lovers a different kind of outdoor access. ## What About Commute Times and Convenience? Henderson wins for anyone who needs easy airport access, sitting just 15 to 20 minutes from Harry Reid International. Summerlin is roughly 20 minutes to the Las Vegas Strip via Summerlin Parkway, but airport trips can stretch to 30 minutes or more. Both communities have strong retail infrastructure, grocery options, and medical facilities. Henderson's southeast location also puts residents closer to the Boulder City corridor and outdoor recreation along the Colorado River. ## Which Community Is Growing Faster? Summerlin continues expanding westward through Summerlin West, with new construction from builders at Esplanade at Red Rock, The Loughton, and Iris Glen. Henderson is also growing, with Inspirada and Cadence adding thousands of new homes. Henderson's population of roughly 330,000 already dwarfs Summerlin's 130,000 residents, though Summerlin functions as a master planned community within Las Vegas city limits rather than its own municipality. ## A Local Perspective Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas Realtor with Rose Homes LV, frequently helps buyers weigh these two communities side by side.
