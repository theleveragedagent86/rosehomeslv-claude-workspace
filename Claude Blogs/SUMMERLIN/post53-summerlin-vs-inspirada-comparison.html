## Summerlin vs Inspirada: Community Comparison for Las Vegas Homebuyers Summerlin and Inspirada are both master planned communities in the Las Vegas Valley, but they sit on opposite sides of town and cater to different buyer profiles. This comparison breaks down where each one shines. ## How Do Home Prices and Value Compare? Summerlin's 2026 median home price sits near $686,000, backed by decades of appreciation and strong demand. Inspirada, located in Henderson, offers a significantly lower entry point that appeals to first time buyers and young families looking for modern construction without the premium. Inspirada's newer builds often include energy efficient features and contemporary floor plans that compete well on a cost per square foot basis. Summerlin's price reflects not just the home itself but the depth of its surrounding infrastructure, retail options, and community prestige. ## Which Community Offers More Amenities? Summerlin has a massive head start here. With over 300 parks, 200 miles of trails, 10 golf courses, and Downtown Summerlin as its retail anchor, the community provides a lifestyle that few developments in the country can match. Inspirada counters with modern amenity centers, resort style pools, parks, and well maintained common areas designed for an active outdoor lifestyle. For buyers who want everything within walking or biking distance, Inspirada's compact layout delivers convenience. But for sheer variety, Summerlin's scale is hard to beat. ## What About Location and Access? Inspirada sits in the southeast valley, putting residents closer to Harry Reid International Airport and the Henderson dining and shopping scene along St. Rose Parkway. Summerlin occupies the western edge of the valley, with Red Rock Canyon as its backyard and Summerlin Parkway providing a direct route to the Strip in about 20 minutes. The right location depends on where you work, how often you travel, and whether mountain views or southeast convenience matters more to your daily routine. ## How Do the Communities Compare for Families? Both communities are built with families in mind. Summerlin offers 26 schools and a deep network of youth sports leagues, community events, and recreation programs. Inspirada feeds into well regarded Henderson schools and provides a tight knit neighborhood atmosphere where families get to know each other quickly. Summerlin's larger footprint means more variety in school choice, while Inspirada's smaller scale fosters a sense of close community that some families prefer. ## Local Market Perspective Las Vegas Realtor Ryan Rose with Rose Homes LV notes that Inspirada attracts buyers who want new construction and modern amenities at a price point below Summerlin's floor. Summerlin appeals to those willing to invest more for a nationally recognized community with unmatched trail access and westside mountain scenery. ## Exploring Both Communities?
