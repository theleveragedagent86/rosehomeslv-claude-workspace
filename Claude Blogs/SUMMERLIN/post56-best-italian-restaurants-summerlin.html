## Best Italian Restaurants in Summerlin: Where to Find Incredible Pasta, Pizza, and More Summerlin has quietly become one of the best neighborhoods in Las Vegas for Italian dining, with a lineup that ranges from cozy trattorias to upscale chef driven destinations worth planning your evening around. ## What Are the Top Italian Restaurants in Summerlin? The Italian dining scene in Summerlin spans several distinct culinary styles. Trattoria Reggiano at Downtown Summerlin is a longtime community staple, known for its eggplant parmesan and brick oven pizza at a moderate price point. Live music from Wednesday through Saturday adds an energy that pairs well with a glass of Chianti and shared plates. Al Solito Posto at Tivoli Village takes a more refined approach, earning 4.5 stars for dishes like pappardelle Bolognese and a wood fired Margherita that rivals anything on the Strip. The atmosphere here feels imported straight from a Milan side street. For a true fine dining experience, La Strega on South Town Center Drive stands out. Chef Gina Marinelli brings creative technique to classic flavors, with signature dishes like squid ink bucatini and hand pulled burrata that demonstrate both precision and personality. Osteria Fiorella inside Red Rock Casino offers another elevated option, helmed by James Beard recognized chef Marc Vetri, whose handmade pastas have earned national attention. ## Where Should You Go for a Special Occasion? Limoncello on the west side of Summerlin draws inspiration from the Amalfi Coast, and its linguini lobster is the kind of dish that turns a Tuesday into a celebration. The ambiance and attention to detail make it ideal for anniversaries or milestones. Spiedini Fiamma near the JW Marriott brings Sardinian influences to the table at a moderate to upscale price point, offering flavors you will not find anywhere else in the valley. ## Is Summerlin Better Than the Strip for Italian Food? Many residents and visitors would say yes. The restaurants here focus on quality ingredients and welcoming service rather than spectacle. You will find shorter waits, easier parking, and menus driven by craft rather than celebrity. For anyone living in or visiting the west side, Summerlin delivers an Italian dining experience that holds its own against any neighborhood in Las Vegas. ## A Local Perspective Ryan Rose of Rose Homes LV notes that the concentration of outstanding Italian restaurants is one of the things residents consistently mention when talking about lifestyle in Summerlin.
