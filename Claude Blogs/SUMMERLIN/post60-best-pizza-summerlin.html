## Best Pizza in Summerlin: Local Favorites and Hidden Gems You Need to Try Finding great pizza in Summerlin is easier than you might expect, with options spanning Brooklyn style slices, Chicago deep dish, and artisan pies that have earned loyal followings across the west side of Las Vegas. ## Who Makes the Best Pizza in Summerlin? Dom DeMarco's has been the answer to that question for years. This Brooklyn style pizzeria has earned Best of Las Vegas honors four years running, and one bite of their signature pie explains why. The crust has that perfect balance of crisp and chew, and the sauce carries the kind of flavor that only comes from a recipe refined over decades. If you have not been, it belongs at the top of your list. Rocco's NY Pizza on Village Center Circle brings another slice of East Coast authenticity to Summerlin. The portions are generous, the prices are fair, and the no nonsense approach to classic New York pizza has made it a go to for families and late night cravings alike. For anyone who grew up folding their slices in half, Rocco's will feel like home. ## What If You Prefer Deep Dish or Specialty Pizza? Giordano's on West Charleston satisfies the deep dish crowd with its famous Chicago style stuffed pizza. Each pie takes time to prepare, so plan accordingly, but the layers of cheese, sauce, and fillings are worth every minute of the wait. It is a destination for anyone who believes pizza should be an event, not just a meal. ## Are There Budget Friendly and Casual Pizza Spots? Santina's keeps things simple with handmade dough and straightforward toppings at prices that make it easy to order for the whole family. Side Piece Pizza takes a more artisan approach, crafting creative pies with quality ingredients while keeping the vibe relaxed and approachable. Both spots prove that outstanding pizza does not require a premium price tag. ## How Does Summerlin Pizza Compare to the Rest of Las Vegas? The west side holds its own against any pizza corridor in the valley. Between Dom DeMarco's award winning pies, Giordano's deep dish heritage, and the variety of neighborhood shops, Summerlin residents rarely need to drive far for a slice that satisfies. The density of quality options within a few miles is one of those everyday conveniences that makes the community feel complete. ## Local Insight from Ryan Rose Rose Homes LV founder Ryan Rose considers the pizza scene in Summerlin a reflection of the community's diversity.
