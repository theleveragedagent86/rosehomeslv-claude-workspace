## Best Brunch Spots in Summerlin: Where Locals Actually Go Summerlin has quietly become one of the best brunch destinations in the Las Vegas valley. From farm fresh plates to steakhouse spreads, these are the spots that locals keep coming back to on weekends. ## Honey Salt Located on Rampart Boulevard, Honey Salt is a farm to table favorite that has earned a loyal following among Summerlin residents. The menu rotates with seasonal ingredients, but the brioche burger and weekend specials are consistently worth the trip. Expect a warm, inviting atmosphere with indoor and patio seating. Prices fall in the moderate to upscale range, making it a solid pick when you want something polished without the Strip price tag. ## Fine Company Tucked inside Downtown Summerlin, Fine Company brings a desert inspired twist to modern American brunch. The open kitchen concept lets you watch your meal come together, and the plating leans artistic without being pretentious. The menu balances creative dishes with familiar comfort, and the atmosphere feels bright and contemporary. This is a great option for anyone exploring the [Downtown Summerlin dining and shopping scene](/blogs/downtown-summerlin-shopping-dining). ## Echo and Rig If your ideal brunch involves a butcher shop and a cocktail, Echo and Rig at Tivoli Village delivers. Their weekend brunch blends steakhouse quality with morning classics, and the cuts are sourced from their in house butchery. The Tivoli Village setting adds a walkable, European village feel to the experience. You can learn more about dining options in this area in our guide to [Tivoli Village restaurants](/blogs/tivoli-village-restaurants-summerlin). ## Via Brasil Steakhouse Via Brasil takes the all you can eat format and elevates it with over 50 items, including carved ribeye and lamb. Their brunch service runs on the traditional rodizio model, where servers bring skewers of grilled meats directly to your table. It is one of the more indulgent brunch experiences in the area and well suited for groups or special occasions. ## Lotus of Siam Party Brunch For something completely different, the Lotus of Siam Party Brunch at Red Rock Casino combines Thai cuisine with a social atmosphere. This 21 and over event features a DJ, craft cocktails, and a curated menu from the James Beard Award recognized kitchen. It is more of a weekend event than a traditional brunch, but the food quality sets it apart. Learn more about dining at the resort in our [Red Rock Casino restaurants guide](/blogs/red-rock-casino-restaurants-summerlin). ## Waterside Cafe Waterside Cafe at Desert Shores offers a lakeside setting that feels miles away from the desert. The menu covers brunch staples like avocado toast and eggs Benedict, and the patio seating along the water makes it one of the most scenic casual dining spots in Summerlin. Pricing is approachable, making it a reliable weekday or weekend option. ## A Realtor Who Knows the Neighborhood Ryan Rose with Rose Homes LV has lived and worked in the Summerlin area long enough to know which neighborhoods put you walking distance from the best food.
