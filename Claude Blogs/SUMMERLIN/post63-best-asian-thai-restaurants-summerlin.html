## Best Asian and Thai Restaurants in Summerlin: From Award Winners to Hidden Gems Summerlin's dining scene includes a strong selection of Asian and Thai restaurants, ranging from a James Beard Award winner to neighborhood spots with devoted followings. Here is where to find the best bowls, curries, and fusion plates in the area. ## Lotus of Siam at Red Rock Casino Lotus of Siam needs little introduction. The original location earned a James Beard Award, and the Red Rock Casino outpost brings the same caliber of Northern Thai cooking to Summerlin. The menu features over 90 dishes, with standout curries, papaya salads, and seafood preparations that justify the restaurant's national reputation. Pricing is moderate to upscale, and reservations are strongly recommended. For more on dining at the resort, see our [Red Rock Casino restaurants guide](/blogs/red-rock-casino-restaurants-summerlin). ## Nittaya's Secret Kitchen Nittaya's, located in Rampart Commons, is the kind of place that builds its reputation entirely through word of mouth. The crispy rice salad is a must order, and the corn fritters have earned a following of their own. With 4.5 stars across review platforms, this is consistently one of the highest rated Thai restaurants in the valley. The space is modest, the portions are generous, and the flavors are bold without relying on excessive heat. ## BaanBaan Thai House BaanBaan Thai House serves traditional Thai dishes in a setting that works well for groups and family dinners. The menu covers the expected classics, from pad thai to green curry, with enough depth to satisfy regulars who want to explore beyond the staples. Service tends to be friendly and unhurried, which fits the neighborhood restaurant feel. ## Buldogis Buldogis brings Korean fusion to Summerlin with a fast casual format that has racked up over 1,755 reviews and a 4.5 star average. Their Korean inspired hot dogs and rice bowls are the signature items, and the price point makes it easy to visit regularly. It is one of the more creative and affordable Asian dining options in the area. ## 8 Noodle Bar Located inside Red Rock Casino, 8 Noodle Bar offers a Pan Asian menu with a focus on noodle soups, dim sum, and wok fired dishes. The setting is upscale casual, and the convenience of the casino location makes it a popular choice for Summerlin residents who want quality without a long drive. For a broader look at options in this complex, check out the [Red Rock Casino dining guide](/blogs/red-rock-casino-restaurants-summerlin). ## P.F. Chang's at The Gramercy P.F. The menu spans Chinese American favorites, and the happy hour specials make it a practical choice for casual dinners or after work meals.
