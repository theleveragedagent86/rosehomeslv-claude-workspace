## Best Bars and Happy Hour in Summerlin: Top Spots for Drinks and Deals You do not need to drive to the Strip for a great cocktail or a solid happy hour deal. Summerlin has a growing bar scene with everything from speakeasy style lounges to casual neighborhood spots with generous drink specials. ## Hawthorn Grill at JW Marriott Hawthorn Grill runs one of the most popular happy hours in the area, offered daily from 4 to 6 PM. During that window, cocktails are 50% off and beers start at $5. The setting inside the JW Marriott is upscale but comfortable, and the food menu holds its own alongside the drinks. It is a strong choice for unwinding after work without the noise of a casino floor. ## Rouge Room at Red Rock Casino Rouge Room leans into Parisian inspired decor with plush seating, moody lighting, and live music on select nights. The cocktail menu is curated rather than sprawling, and the bartenders know their way around a proper drink. It feels like a destination rather than just a hotel bar. For more dining and nightlife at the resort, see our [Red Rock Casino guide](/blogs/red-rock-casino-restaurants-summerlin). ## BelAire Lounge at Durango Casino BelAire Lounge inside Durango Casino brings a sleek, modern vibe with weekend DJ sets and a cocktail program that leans contemporary. The space feels fresh and well designed, and the energy picks up later in the evening. It is a solid option for residents on the Summerlin South and Southwest side of the valley. ## Wax Rabbit at Durango Casino Also at Durango, Wax Rabbit takes a different approach with a speakeasy concept that includes burlesque entertainment on select nights. The cocktail menu nods to prohibition era classics with a modern twist, and the intimate setting makes it feel like a true discovery rather than a casino afterthought. ## Wineaux at UnCommons Wineaux is a wine focused bar at the UnCommons development, offering glasses in the $12 to $20 range along with regular tasting events. The space is relaxed and well suited for date nights or catching up with friends over a curated flight. It is a nice counterpoint to the cocktail and beer bars that dominate the area. ## Mijo Modern Mexican Mijo Modern Mexican runs "Mijo Hour" Monday through Thursday from 4 to 6 PM, with discounted margaritas and Mexican inspired bites. The atmosphere is vibrant and colorful, and the food quality goes well beyond typical happy hour fare. It is a favorite among locals who want flavor and value in the same stop. ## JING at Downtown Summerlin JING offers happy hour seven days a week from 4 to 6 PM, which is uncommon for this caliber of venue. The space transitions from a stylish restaurant to a livelier lounge as the evening progresses. Its location within [Downtown Summerlin](/blogs/downtown-summerlin-shopping-dining) makes it easy to pair with shopping or dinner nearby. ## More Happy Hour Options Dom DeMarco's runs happy hour from 2 to 6 PM daily, and Aces N Ales offers $3 wells Sunday through Thursday from 4 to 7 PM. Both are no frills options with honest pricing. ## The Rose Homes LV Perspective Ryan Rose, founder of Rose Homes LV, frequently highlights Summerlin's walkable entertainment districts as a lifestyle advantage for buyers.
