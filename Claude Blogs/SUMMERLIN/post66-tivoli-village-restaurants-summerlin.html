## Tivoli Village Restaurants: Complete Dining Guide for Summerlin Tivoli Village is one of the most distinctive dining destinations in Summerlin, combining European inspired architecture with a restaurant lineup that covers everything from two story steakhouses to artisan chocolate shops. ## What Are the Best Restaurants at Tivoli Village? Echo & Rig anchors the fine dining scene at Tivoli Village with a concept unlike anything else in Las Vegas. The two story restaurant houses an on site butcher shop on the ground floor, where guests can hand select cuts before heading upstairs to the dining room. Expect beautifully marbled steaks, creative charcuterie boards, and a cocktail program that takes the craft seriously. Pricing falls in the $$$ to $$$$ range, and reservations are recommended on weekends. Al Solito Posto brings refined Italian to Tivoli Village with 4.5 star consistency. The pappardelle Bolognese is a standout, and the wood fired Margherita pizza rivals anything on the Strip. The atmosphere channels a relaxed Milan side street, making it a natural fit for date nights or long family dinners. El Dorado Cantina rounds out the upscale options with organic Mexican cuisine at a moderate to upscale price point, sourcing premium ingredients that set it apart from typical cantina fare. ## Where Should You Go for a Casual Bite? PKWY Tavern serves as the neighborhood gathering spot, with an approachable menu and lively bar that works equally well for lunch or a late night out. Kabuki offers dependable sushi and Japanese dishes at a moderate price point, ideal for a quick weeknight dinner. Leone Cafe provides a lighter option for coffee and pastries, while Pressed Juicery caters to the health conscious crowd with cold pressed blends and smoothies. ## What About Dessert and Specialty Shops? Tivoli Village stands out for its specialty food destinations. Jean Marie Auboine Chocolatier crafts artisan chocolates and confections that make for perfect gifts or a post dinner indulgence. Milkcow Cafe draws a younger crowd with its Instagram worthy desserts and creative soft serve combinations. Ada's Food + Wine offers a curated wine bar experience paired with a seasonal small plates menu that rewards repeat visits. ## Why Is Tivoli Village a Dining Destination? The layout of Tivoli Village makes restaurant hopping feel effortless. Wide cobblestone walkways connect the shops and restaurants, and outdoor seating is available at most locations throughout the year. The European village design creates an ambiance that simply does not exist at other Summerlin shopping centers.
