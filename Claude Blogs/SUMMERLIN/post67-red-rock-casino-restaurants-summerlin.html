## Red Rock Casino Restaurants: Complete Dining Guide for Summerlin Red Rock Casino Resort and Spa houses one of the most impressive collections of restaurants on the west side of Las Vegas, giving Summerlin residents a resort caliber dining scene just minutes from home. ## What Are the Top Fine Dining Options at Red Rock Casino? T-Bones Chophouse leads the fine dining lineup with prime steaks, an extensive wine list, and a polished atmosphere that makes it a natural choice for celebrations and business dinners. Pricing sits at the $$$$ level, and the quality justifies it. Osteria Fiorella brings James Beard recognized chef Marc Vetri's handmade pastas and Italian cuisine to the west side, delivering a dining experience that rivals the best the Strip has to offer. Blue Ribbon Sushi provides a premium sushi and Japanese option with a menu built around pristine fish and creative rolls. Rouge Room adds a unique element to the property with its Parisian inspired lounge concept. The cocktail program and small plates menu create a sophisticated evening out that feels more like a boutique hotel bar in Paris than a casino restaurant. ## Where Can You Find Great Casual Dining? Lotus of Siam at Red Rock deserves special attention. The original location earned a James Beard Award, and this outpost offers the same 90 plus Thai dishes that earned that distinction. The Party Brunch on weekends is a popular 21 and over event that blends Thai cuisine with a lively social atmosphere. Naxos Taverna serves Greek seafood in a warm, Mediterranean setting, while Hearthstone Kitchen and Cellar focuses on wood fired dishes and an approachable farm to table menu. The Oyster Bar has earned Best of Las Vegas recognition for its fresh shellfish and chowders at a moderate price point. 8 Noodle Bar covers Asian comfort food with noodle soups and stir fry dishes, and Lucille's BBQ brings Southern style barbecue to the resort with generous portions and a family friendly vibe. ## What About Quick Bites and Drinks? Lucky Penny operates as the 24 hour casual option, perfect for late night cravings or a quick breakfast. Yard House draws beer enthusiasts with over 130 taps and a menu designed for sharing.
