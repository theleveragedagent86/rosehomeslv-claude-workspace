## Tivoli Village Complete Shopping and Dining Guide for Summerlin Tivoli Village at 400 South Rampart Boulevard is a roughly 30 acre European style shopping and dining destination that stands apart from every other retail center in the Las Vegas Valley. With cobblestone walkways, thoughtful architecture, and a curated mix of boutiques and restaurants, it has become one of the defining lifestyle features of the Summerlin community. ## What Stores and Boutiques Are at Tivoli Village? The retail lineup at Tivoli Village leans toward specialty and luxury rather than big box. Restoration Hardware operates a massive showroom that draws design enthusiasts from across the valley. Saatva offers a mattress gallery experience, while STITCHED and D'Annata provide upscale men's and women's fashion. Jeff White Custom Jewelry and David Tupaz Couture bring bespoke options for those seeking something one of a kind. The Shade Store handles custom window treatments, and Hub Modern rounds out the home design category with contemporary furniture and decor. ## What Wellness and Service Options Are Available? Tivoli Village has developed a strong wellness and personal care cluster. The NOW Massage provides walk in and appointment based massage services in a calming, modern space. Perspire Sauna Studio offers infrared sauna sessions for recovery and relaxation, and Stretch Lab focuses on assisted stretching. Scissors & Scotch combines a premium barbershop experience with a cocktail lounge, while Sev Laser handles medical aesthetics. KidStrong provides structured fitness programming for children, giving parents a wellness stop and kids activity in one trip. ## Where Should You Eat at Tivoli Village? Dining at Tivoli Village ranges from refined to casual. Echo & Rig is a two story steakhouse with an on site butcher shop on the ground level. Al Solito Posto delivers 4.5 star Italian with standout pappardelle and wood fired pizza. El Dorado Cantina specializes in organic Mexican cuisine at a moderate to upscale price point. For lighter options, Leone Cafe and Pressed Juicery cover coffee and fresh juices, Jean Marie Auboine Chocolatier crafts artisan confections, and Milkcow Cafe draws dessert lovers with creative soft serve. Ada's Food + Wine provides a wine bar atmosphere with seasonal small plates. ## What Makes Tivoli Village Different from Other Summerlin Shopping Centers? The design itself sets Tivoli Village apart. The European village layout, lush landscaping, and wide pedestrian corridors create an experience that feels more like strolling through a Mediterranean town center than visiting a strip mall. Professional services including De Castroverde Law, Merrill Lynch, and Lexicon Bank also call Tivoli Village home, adding a layer of convenience for residents who want to handle business and leisure in the same location.
