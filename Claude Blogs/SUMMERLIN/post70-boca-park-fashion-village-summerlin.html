## Boca Park Fashion Village: Shopping and Dining Guide for Summerlin Boca Park Fashion Village at 750 South Rampart Boulevard is one of the most complete shopping destinations near Summerlin, combining major retailers, specialty stores, and a strong dining lineup in a single open air center that residents rely on for everyday needs and weekend outings alike. ## What Stores Can You Find at Boca Park? The retail mix at Boca Park covers a wide range. Target and Kohl's serve as the primary anchors, making the center a practical stop for household essentials. Ross provides off price fashion, while REI draws outdoor enthusiasts preparing for hikes at Red Rock Canyon and beyond. Pottery Barn brings home furnishing and decor options, and Office Max handles office and school supplies. Total Wine and More rounds out the specialty retail with one of the largest selections of wine, beer, and spirits in the valley. The variety here makes Boca Park the kind of place where a single trip can cross multiple items off the list. ## Where Should You Eat at Boca Park? Dining options at Boca Park lean toward popular, well established brands with broad appeal. The Cheesecake Factory provides its famously extensive menu and generous portions, making it a reliable choice for groups who cannot agree on a single cuisine. Grimaldi's serves coal fired brick oven pizza in the tradition of its Brooklyn roots, and Kona Grill blends sushi and American cuisine in a contemporary setting. Hussong's Cantina brings Baja style Mexican food with a lively atmosphere, while Panera covers the fast casual lunch crowd. Sambalatte offers specialty coffee and a focused cafe menu for anyone looking for a quieter stop between errands. ## What Amenities Set Boca Park Apart? Boca Park offers several conveniences that elevate the shopping experience beyond the basics. Valet parking is available for those who prefer to skip the lot, and personal shopper services cater to visitors looking for a more curated experience. The open air layout keeps the center feeling spacious and accessible, and the central Summerlin location means most residents in the area can reach Boca Park within a ten minute drive. ## How Does Boca Park Compare to Other Summerlin Shopping Centers? While Tivoli Village offers a boutique, European inspired atmosphere and Downtown Summerlin provides an entertainment driven experience, Boca Park fills the practical, everyday role. It is the center where residents run errands, grab a quick lunch, and still find quality dining options for a night out.
