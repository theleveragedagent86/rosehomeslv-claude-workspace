## 89144 Real Estate Guide: Central Summerlin and The Arbors The 89144 zip code sits at the geographic center of Summerlin and has posted some of the strongest appreciation numbers in the community. With a median home price of $575,000 and a remarkable 11.1% year over year gain, Central Summerlin continues to attract buyers who want established neighborhoods with modern convenience. ## Central Location Advantages What makes 89144 stand out is its positioning within the Summerlin master plan. Residents here have quick access to Downtown Summerlin, the 215 beltway, Summerlin Parkway, and the full network of Summerlin trails and parks. Schools, shopping, dining, and medical facilities are all within a short drive, and in many cases within walking or biking distance. This central footprint means less commute time and more daily convenience compared to neighborhoods on the outer edges of the community. ## The Arbors, The Canyons, and The Crossing The Arbors is one of Summerlin's most recognized and desirable villages. Developed through the late 1990s and early 2000s, The Arbors features a mix of single story and two story homes with generous floor plans, mature trees, and well maintained common areas. The neighborhood has a settled, family friendly character that newer construction simply cannot match on day one. The Canyons sits adjacent to The Arbors and offers a similar blend of established homes with slightly different architectural styles. The Crossing rounds out the primary villages in this zip code, providing additional housing options at varied price points. Together, these three villages create a cohesive, well connected corridor of residential living in the heart of Summerlin. ## Market Performance in 2026 The median home price in 89144 is $575,000, reflecting an 11.1% increase over the prior year. Price per square foot runs $304, and homes are selling in an average of 49 days, up from 31 days the previous year. Approximately 82 homes sold during the most recent monthly reporting period, indicating healthy transaction volume and sustained demand. The double digit appreciation here outpaces most other Summerlin zip codes, driven by limited inventory in these established villages and strong buyer preference for central locations. Compared to the [luxury pricing in 89138](/blogs/summerlin-luxury-guard-gated-homes) or the [entry level options in 89128](/blogs/89128-real-estate-guide-summerlin), 89144 occupies a sweet spot that appeals to a broad base of purchasers. ## Ideal Buyer Profile Families with school age children gravitate toward 89144 for the combination of neighborhood character, school access, and proximity to parks and recreation. Move up buyers transitioning from their first home often land here when they want more space without jumping to the $800K plus price points found in Summerlin West. The area also appeals to professionals who commute to different parts of the valley and value the central positioning. ## Local Knowledge That Makes a Difference The Rose Homes LV team, led by Ryan Rose, brings firsthand knowledge of how The Arbors, The Canyons, and The Crossing compare in terms of floor plans, HOA structures, and resale value.
