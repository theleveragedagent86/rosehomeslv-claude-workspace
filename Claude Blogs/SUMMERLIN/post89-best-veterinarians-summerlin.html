## Best Veterinarians in Summerlin Summerlin is one of the most pet friendly communities in Las Vegas, and the veterinary care options reflect that. From general practice clinics to specialty facilities and a dedicated cat hospital, pet owners in Summerlin have access to experienced vets without having to drive across the valley. Here is a look at the standout options. ## Town Center Animal Hospital Town Center Animal Hospital is AAHA accredited, which puts it in a select group of veterinary practices that meet higher standards for diagnostics, surgery, and patient care. The clinic earned Best of Summerlin recognition in 2020 and continues to be a top choice for routine checkups, vaccinations, dental work, and more complex procedures. AAHA accreditation is voluntary, so clinics that pursue it are generally committed to a higher standard of care. ## Vegas Valley Pet Hospital Vegas Valley Pet Hospital is a locally owned practice that has built a reputation for personalized service. The clinic offers general wellness exams, preventive care, and sick visit treatment. Being locally owned means you are more likely to see the same vet on repeat visits, which matters for building a relationship with your pet's care provider. ## Summerlin Animal Hospital Established in 1993, Summerlin Animal Hospital is one of the longest running veterinary practices in the community. Dr. Frank Reynolds leads the team, and the clinic's longevity speaks to the trust it has earned among Summerlin pet owners over three decades of practice. The clinic covers standard wellness, surgery, and urgent care needs. ## South Shores Animal Hospital South Shores Animal Hospital is another AAHA accredited facility in the area. Like Town Center, the accreditation signals a commitment to quality that goes beyond the baseline requirements for veterinary practices in Nevada. South Shores handles everything from routine wellness visits to diagnostics and surgical care. ## Sun City Animal Hospital Sun City Animal Hospital has been serving the Summerlin area since 1993. The clinic provides general veterinary services and has a long track record with pet owners in Sun City Summerlin and surrounding neighborhoods. Its location makes it a convenient choice for residents on the north side of the community. ## Trailwood Cat Hospital Trailwood Cat Hospital is a cat only veterinary practice with Fear Free certification. That means the staff is trained to minimize stress and anxiety for feline patients during visits. For cat owners who have dealt with the challenge of getting their cat into a carrier and through a waiting room full of dogs, a cat only clinic can make the experience significantly better for everyone involved. ## How to Choose a Vet in Summerlin AAHA accreditation, years of operation, specialty focus, and location all factor into the decision. For pet owners who are new to the area, visiting a clinic for an initial wellness check before committing long term is a practical way to evaluate the fit.
