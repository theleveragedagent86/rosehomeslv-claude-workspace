Best Gyms and Fitness Centers in Summerlin

Summerlin residents take fitness seriously. Between the year round sunshine and an active outdoor culture, this master planned community attracts people who prioritize health and wellness. Whether you want a premium athletic club with every amenity imaginable or a straightforward gym that keeps costs low, Summerlin delivers across the board.

As a local Realtor, Ryan Rose frequently fields questions from buyers about the fitness scene in Summerlin. Here is a breakdown of the top options available in 2026.

Life Time Summerlin: The Premium Standard

Life Time at 10721 W Charleston Boulevard is one of the most impressive fitness facilities in the entire Las Vegas Valley. This Diamond level location spans 152,000 square feet and operates from 4am to midnight, giving members flexibility that few other clubs can match.

The amenities here go far beyond standard gym equipment. Members enjoy both indoor and outdoor pools, a full service spa, a cafe serving healthy meals, and the Kids Academy program for families with young children. Monthly membership starts at $150 and up, but many residents consider it a worthwhile investment given the sheer volume of offerings under one roof. If you are exploring Summerlin as a family, Life Time often becomes a central hub for daily routines.

Source: lifetime.life

EoS Fitness: Best Value in the Valley

For those who want a quality gym without the premium price tag, EoS Fitness operates multiple locations throughout northwest Las Vegas and the Summerlin corridor. EoS has built a loyal following thanks to its budget friendly membership structure paired with facilities that punch well above their weight class.

Members get access to lap pools, the MOVEoS Cinemas (cardio theaters where you can watch movies while working out), and turf training areas for functional fitness. The no frills pricing combined with clean, well maintained facilities makes EoS a top pick for residents who want to stay active without overcommitting on monthly costs.

Source: wellhub.com

24 Hour Fitness Summerlin

The 24 Hour Fitness Summerlin location operates at the Super Sport level, which includes group fitness classes, personal training options, and a solid range of cardio and strength equipment. It sits in a convenient spot for residents across several Summerlin villages, and the brand recognition means new arrivals often gravitate here first.

While it does not match Life Time for sheer scale, the Super Sport tier provides more than enough for dedicated gym goers who prefer a familiar national chain with reliable hours and consistent equipment standards.

Source: 24hourfitness.com

Outdoor Fitness in Summerlin

Gyms are only part of the story. Summerlin features over 200 miles of interconnected trails perfect for running, cycling, and outdoor boot camps. Many residents combine a gym membership with regular use of these trails, and the mild weather for most of the year makes outdoor training viable well beyond what residents in other cities might expect.

Downtown Summerlin also hosts free Fitness on The Lawn sessions on Tuesdays from 7:30 to 8:30pm, featuring yoga and HIIT classes open to everyone. These community events add another dimension to the fitness culture here.

Choosing the Right Gym for Your Lifestyle

The right choice depends on what you value most. Families with children often gravitate toward Life Time for the Kids Academy and pool access. Budget conscious members find EoS delivers excellent value. Those seeking reliable access to a proven national brand appreciate 24 Hour Fitness.

Ryan Rose works with buyers relocating to Summerlin regularly and understands how neighborhood amenities, including gym proximity, factor into home buying decisions. If fitness and an active lifestyle matter to you, Summerlin is one of the strongest communities in Las Vegas for both indoor and outdoor options.
