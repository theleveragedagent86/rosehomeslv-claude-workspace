## 2. REMAINING VILLAGE DATA

### The Willows (South Summerlin)
- **Developed:** 1997
- **Size:** 731 acres, 20 subdivisions
- **Zip Code:** 89135
- **Status:** Fully built out (resale only)
- **Character:** Premier master-planned village with diverse housing from condos to luxury estates
- **Price Range:** ~$400,000 to $2.85M+
- **Home Styles:**
  - Entry level: Castlewood (1,158-1,667 sq ft), Autumn Ridge (1,432-1,628 sq ft) -- low-to-mid $400Ks
  - Mid-range: Bella Veranda (2,578-3,001 sq ft), Wood Glen (2,670-3,792 sq ft) -- $600K-$900K
  - Luxury: Heritage Glen, Eden Ridge, Willow Creek (guard-gated) -- up to $2.85M+
- **Gating:** Willow Creek is guard-gated; Bella Veranda and Heather Glen are gated
- **Amenities:** 34+ acres of parks, community pool, tennis courts, baseball fields, soccer fields, basketball courts, playgrounds, walking trails; 10.5- and 12.7-acre parks
- **Proximity:** 125+ shops and restaurants at Downtown Summerlin
- **Sources:** [Rose Homes](https://www.rosehomeslv.com/blog/buying-home-willows-summerlin), [Las Vegas Real Estate](https://www.lasvegasrealestate.org/the-willows-summerlin/), [Thomas Group](https://www.thethomasgrouplv.com/the-willows-summerlin-neighborhood-guide/)

### The Gardens (South Summerlin)
- **Developed:** 2000
- **Size:** 410 acres
- **Zip Code:** 89135
- **Status:** Fully developed (resale only)
- **Character:** Charming and tranquil village known for beautiful green spaces, mature trees, and convenient 215 Beltway access
- **Subdivisions:** Ivy Glen, Wisteria Hills, Garden Glen, Giverny, Chelsea Gardens, Marbella Ridge, Stratford Court, Country Gardens, Glenleigh Gardens
- **Home Styles:** Traditional southwest design with stucco exterior and tile roofs; attached garages, open floor plans, spacious backyards, gourmet kitchens
- **Gating:** Glenleigh Gardens is gated
- **Amenities:** Gardens Park (weekly farmers market), two tennis courts, two basketball courts, roller hockey, volleyball, horseshoes, shuffleboard, bocce ball
- **Notable:** Gardens Plaza shopping; Element boutique hotel (opened 2008); Alexander Dawson School at Rainbow Mountain (2000)
- **Adjacent:** The Willows, Siena, Red Rock Country Club, Trilogy, The Ridges
- **Sources:** [Neighborhoods in LV](https://neighborhoodsinlasvegas.com/the-gardens-village/), [Las Vegas Real Estate](https://www.lasvegasrealestate.org/the-gardens-summerlin/), [Redfin](https://www.redfin.com/neighborhood/497684/NV/Summerlin-South/The-Gardens)

### Summerlin Centre

### Grand Park (Summerlin West -- Newest)
- **Status:** Actively developing (2024-2026+)
- **Location:** West of 215 Beltway, north of Far Hills Avenue
- **Character:** One of the most dynamic new districts in Summerlin West
- **Grand Park (the park):** 90+ acres -- Summerlin's single biggest park
  - Phase 1 completed December 2025: 3 lighted baseball/softball fields with concession building, 6 lighted pickleball courts, full-sized lighted basketball court (4 hoops), covered playground, climbing play structure, seasonal splash pad, outdoor adult fitness stations
- **Active Neighborhoods:** 5+ neighborhoods with 34 unique floorplans
- **Builders:** KB Homes, SHAWOOD, Taylor Morrison, Tri Pointe, Toll Brothers, Richmond American, Pulte, Lennar
- **Specific Communities:**
  - Aberdeen by Tri Pointe (131 two-story lots)
  - Caldwell Park by KB Homes (opened May 2025, 228 attached/detached homes)
  - Arcadia by SHAWOOD (opened Feb 2025, 40 two-story homes)
  - Primrose Park by Richmond American (76 luxury homes, 3,410-3,690 sq ft, from ~$1.1M)

## 5. ADDITIONAL REAL ESTATE

### SID & LID Fees Explained
- **SID** = Special Improvement District; **LID** = Local Improvement District
- **Purpose:** Fund major infrastructure (roads, drainage, sewer lines, parks, street lighting, landscaping) in master-planned communities
- **How it works:** Developers front infrastructure costs, then pass the debt to homeowners through annual assessments on property tax bills
- **Typical Cost:** $500 to $3,000+ yearly (billed semi-annually alongside property tax)
- **Duration:** 10-20 years from district creation date (NOT purchase date); new owners inherit remaining timeline
- **Outstanding Balances:** Typically $5,000-$30,000+ on newer homes
- **Prepayment:** Early payoff possible but 3% prepayment penalty (e.g., $600 on $20,000 balance)
- **Key Distinction:** NOT HOA fees; these are government-level debt obligations that "run with the land" and transfer automatically to new buyers
- **Communities with SIDs:** Primarily Summerlin and Skye Canyon; also Inspirada, Cadence
- **Warning:** Unpaid SIDs create potential foreclosure risk
- **Sources:** [Rose Homes SID/LID](https://www.rosehomeslv.com/blog/sid-lid-taxes-explained-las-vegas-new-construction), [Rose Homes SID/LID Explained](https://www.rosehomeslv.com/blog/sid-lid-fees-las-vegas-explained), [Neighborhoods in LV](https://neighborhoodsinlasvegas.com/what-are-sids-and-lids-in-the-las-vegas-valley/), [Summerlin West SID PDF](https://summerlin.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/Summerlin-West-SID_Jan2020.pdf)

### Clark County Property Tax Rate (2025-2026)
- **Effective Rate:** ~0.48% (significantly lower than national average)
- **Rate Structure:** 117 different tax districts in Clark County with varying rates
- **Unincorporated Areas:** Lowest rates, ~$2.50 per $100 assessed value
- **Combined Tax Rate Limit:** $3.64 per $100 of assessed valuation (FY 2025-2026)
- **Growth Factor:** 13.0% for FY 2025-2026 calculations
- **Nevada Cap:** Property tax increases limited to 3% per year for primary residences (Tax Abatement)
- **How Assessed Value Works:** Taxable value = 35% of replacement cost minus depreciation (NOT market value)
- **Sources:** [Clark County Treasurer](https://treasurer.co.clark.nv.us/TaxRate-District/), [SmartAsset](https://smartasset.com/taxes/nevada-property-tax-calculator), [Tax-Rates.org](https://www.tax-rates.org/nevada/clark_county_property_tax), [Virtuance](https://www.virtuance.com/blog/property-tax-rates-las-vegas/)

### Airbnb / Short-Term Rental Rules
- **Summerlin:** Properties NOT eligible for STR licenses in Las Vegas -- effectively prohibited in the master-planned community
- **Clark County (unincorporated):** Governed by Code Chapter 7.100
  - Properties must be 1,000+ feet apart
  - 10 PM curfew for outdoor amenities
  - Guests may only use radios/stereos/speakers indoors
  - Properties cannot be within 2,500 feet of a hotel/resort
- **Legal Status (as of Dec 2025):** Federal Judge Miranda Du paused fines, penalties, and liens against homeowners for STR activities; ruled Clark County laws "likely violate constitutional rights"
- **Platform Provisions Blocked:** Aug 2025 injunction prevents Clark County from requiring Airbnb/Vrbo to verify licenses or deactivate unlicensed rentals
- **Licensed Listings:** Only 208 STR listings licensed by the county as of Oct 2025
- **Bottom Line:** Legal landscape in flux; Summerlin HOA/CC&Rs generally prohibit STRs regardless of county rules
- **Sources:** [Airbnb Help Center Clark County](https://www.airbnb.com/help/article/4009), [Steadily STR Laws 2026](https://www.steadily.com/blog/airbnb-short-term-rental-laws-regulations-las-vegas), [LV Review-Journal Injunction](https://www.reviewjournal.com/news/civil-courts/court-orders-injunction-blocking-enforcement-of-clark-county-short-term-rental-ordinance-3597909/), [Clark County STR](https://www.clarkcountynv.gov/government/departments/administrative_services/intergovernmental_relations/short-term-rentals)
