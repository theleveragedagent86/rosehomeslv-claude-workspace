What Is Seven Hills Henderson?

Is Seven Hills Henderson a Good Place to Live?

The Butch Harmon School of Golf is located at the Serket Golf Club (formerly Rio Secco Golf Club) within Seven Hills Henderson at 2651 Grand Hills Drive. Founded by Butch Harmon, who has been ranked the number one golf instructor in America by Golf Digest since 2003, the school offers private lessons, multi day clinics, and corporate instruction on a four acre private practice facility that is among the most advanced in the country.

World Class Instruction

Butch Harmon's roster of former students includes Tiger Woods, Phil Mickelson, Greg Norman, Ernie Els, Fred Couples, Rickie Fowler, and numerous other PGA Tour professionals. The school at Serket Golf Club brings that same caliber of instruction to recreational golfers through one day clinics, two day clinics, and three day golf school programs. Instruction is provided by Butch Harmon and his handpicked staff of teaching professionals, with programs that include computerized video analysis, club fitting, and on course playing lessons.

The school's facilities include a four acre private practice area with a driving range, two putting greens totaling over 16,000 square feet, two chipping greens exceeding 8,000 square feet, and a 70 yard pitching area with 12 target bunkers. Seven state of the art video rooms capture swings from every angle, and the facility includes a biomechanics studio, a fitness studio, and Odyssey putting studio with SAM PuttLab technology. The school is open Monday through Sunday from 8 AM to 5 PM.

A Seven Hills Lifestyle Amenity

The Butch Harmon School of Golf continued operations after the 2025 rebranding of Rio Secco Golf Club to Serket Golf Club. For Seven Hills residents, having a world class golf instruction facility within their community adds a lifestyle amenity that is virtually unmatched in residential Southern Nevada. The school attracts students from around the world, and Seven Hills residents enjoy the convenience of having this resource minutes from their front door.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose notes that the Butch Harmon School of Golf is one of the features that sets Seven Hills apart from every other master planned community in the Las Vegas valley. No other residential community in Southern Nevada has a golf instruction facility of this caliber embedded within its borders.
