# Seven Hills Henderson: Complete real estate blog content library

**Seven Hills is a 1,300-acre, Italian-inspired master-planned community in southwest Henderson, Nevada (ZIP 89052), featuring approximately 3,000 homes across 25 distinct neighborhoods, with median sold prices of $650,000–$700,000, five guard-gated enclaves, the Rio Secco Golf Club (now rebranded Serket Golf Club), and panoramic Las Vegas Strip and mountain views.** Built by American Nevada Corporation between 1995 and 2003, Seven Hills sits roughly 7 miles from the Strip and consistently ranks among Southern Nevada's most desirable residential communities. Henderson is the **#2 safest large city in America** (SafeWise 2024), and Seven Hills' 89052 zip code has violent crime rates **54% below the national average**.

| **Terrain** | Rolling foothills of McCullough Mountain Range |
| **Views** | Panoramic Las Vegas Strip, mountain, and valley views |

| **Distance to Strip** | ~7 miles (~15–20 minute drive) |
| **Distance to airport** | ~8 miles to Harry Reid International; ~4 miles to Henderson Executive |

### Entry points and boundaries
Seven Hills is bounded by Eastern Avenue (west), St. Rose Parkway (north), and borders Henderson Executive Airport to the northwest. Entry points are via **Eastern Avenue** (west side) and **Executive Airport Drive** (east side), connecting to Seven Hills Drive and Grand Hills Drive.

### Gated vs. non-gated structure
**The overall community is NOT fully guard-gated** — it has open entry points. However, it contains **five 24-hour guard-gated communities** and several auto-gated neighborhoods within it:

**Guard-gated (24/7 staffed):**
1. Terracina (~460 custom homes)
2. Venezia (~70 custom homes)
3. Rapallo (estimated 40–60 custom homes)
4. Renaissance (217 semi-custom homes by Pardee)
5. Presidio (smaller custom enclave)

**Auto-gated (no guard):** Avalon, The Terraces, Palazzo Monte, Villa Villagio, Majestic Hills, Rose Ridge, and others.

**Non-gated/open access:** Naples, Crescendo, Palermo West, Meridiana, and others.

### Notable residents (reported)
Pierre Omidyar (eBay founder), Mike Tyson, Bryce Harper (MLB), and various Las Vegas entertainers and sports figures.

|-------------------|-------------------|-------|

| **Mid-range neighborhoods** | $200–$350/month total | Master + enhanced sub-HOA |
| **Guard-gated luxury** | Up to $558/month total | Master + guard gate staffing + enhanced services |

### What HOA fees include
- Maintenance of common areas, medians, and landscaping
- Park maintenance (Sonata, Puccini, Vivaldi, Allegro parks)
- Community entrance maintenance and beautification
- Guard gate staffing and 24/7 security patrols (for gated/guard-gated sections)
- Front-yard landscaping (in some higher-fee communities)
- Insurance on common areas
- Management fees and reserve fund contributions

### Key HOA rules
- **Architectural review required** before exterior modifications (paint, patio covers, doors, landscaping)
- **Drought-resistant landscaping** often required
- Front yards must meet community maintenance standards
- Vehicle parking restrictions (street parking limitations)
- Italian/Mediterranean aesthetic maintained through strict design guidelines
- CC&Rs, bylaws, and articles of incorporation govern all homeowners
- **Resale packages** required when selling ($150–$400 per association; rush fees $50–$200 additional)
