What Is Seven Hills Henderson?

Coral Academy of Science Las Vegas is a state sponsored, tuition free public charter school system with multiple Henderson campuses near Seven Hills. The Sandy Ridge Campus at 1051 Sandy Ridge Avenue serves grades six through twelve and holds an A rating from Niche and a 10 out of 10 from GreatSchools, ranking number six among charter high schools in Nevada. Coral Academy offers a STEM focused college preparatory curriculum as a zone free alternative to the traditional public school pipeline.

Academic Performance

Coral Academy's Sandy Ridge Campus serves approximately 842 students with math proficiency at 55 percent and reading proficiency at 74 percent, both above the rates at most public schools in the area. The average GPA among students is 3.72, the graduation rate is 95 percent, and average standardized test scores include a 1280 SAT and 27 ACT. The student to teacher ratio is 29 to 1. U.S. News has ranked the Sandy Ridge campus as the number one high school in Henderson.

The school uses the Project Lead The Way (PLTW) curriculum to emphasize science, technology, engineering, and mathematics across all grade levels. Students participate in Science Olympiads, science fairs, Math Counts competitions, and robotics programs. The Sandy Ridge campus team placed third in the 2025 Nevada Science Bowl.

Enrollment and Access

As a zone free charter school, Coral Academy accepts students from any address through open enrollment, making it available to Seven Hills families regardless of their assigned school zone. Coral Academy also operates additional Henderson campuses at Eastgate Road (K through 7), on Windmill (grades 3 through 6), and at Cadence (K through 12). The tuition free model means families can access high performing STEM education without private school costs.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose notes that Coral Academy gives Seven Hills families an additional high performing school option beyond the already strong public school pipeline of Wolff Elementary, Del Webb Middle, and Coronado High. For families with children who are particularly interested in STEM subjects, Coral Academy's focused curriculum, high test scores, and competitive academic programs provide an excellent complement to the traditional public school options. Having both a 10 out of 10 rated elementary school and a top ranked charter school within the immediate area strengthens Seven Hills' overall appeal to education focused families.
