Coronado High School is the primary high school serving Seven Hills Henderson, located at 1001 Coronado Center Drive in Henderson. Coronado holds an A minus rating from Niche and ranks number 17 among public high schools in Nevada. The school serves approximately 3,156 students in grades nine through twelve with a 91 percent graduation rate, which exceeds the Nevada state average of 82 percent.

Academics and Advanced Placement

Coronado High School achieves math proficiency rates of 41 to 43 percent and reading proficiency rates of 65 to 69 percent, both above Nevada state averages. The student to teacher ratio is 27 to 1. Coronado offers the Academy of Advanced Placement program, and 41 percent of students participate in at least one AP course. All students are encouraged to take AP courses regardless of whether they are formally enrolled in the Academy program.

U.S. News ranks Coronado number 13 in Nevada and 1,715 nationally. Niche also ranks the school number 10 among public high schools in the Las Vegas area and number 23 in Nevada for STEM education. The school opened in 2000, giving it over two decades of established programming and community connection.

Athletics and Extracurriculars

Coronado offers more than 13 athletic programs including baseball, basketball, cross country, flag football, football, golf, lacrosse, soccer, softball, tennis, track and field, volleyball, and wrestling. The school competes through the Nevada Interscholastic Activities Association and has developed strong programs across multiple sports. Beyond athletics, students have access to art education programs, career presentations, and college preparation seminars.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose highlights Coronado High School as a significant factor in Seven Hills' appeal to families with teenagers. A 91 percent graduation rate, strong AP participation, and a top 20 state ranking make Coronado one of the strongest public high schools in the Las Vegas valley. The complete K through 12 pipeline from Wolff Elementary to Del Webb Middle to Coronado High gives Seven Hills families a consistent, above average public school experience from kindergarten through high school graduation.
