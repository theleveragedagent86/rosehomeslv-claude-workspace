What Is Seven Hills Henderson?

Crescendo is one of the largest non gated neighborhoods in Seven Hills Henderson, comprising approximately 330 single family homes with sizes ranging from 1,700 to 2,800 square feet. As a production home community, Crescendo offers one of the more accessible entry points into Seven Hills single family living while still providing full access to the community's parks, trails, golf course, and schools.

Home Features and Pools

Crescendo's homes include three to five bedroom floor plans in single story and two story configurations built by production builders during Seven Hills' peak construction phase. A distinguishing feature of Crescendo is that more than 50 percent of homes in the neighborhood have private swimming pools, one of the highest pool ratios among Seven Hills' production neighborhoods. This pool prevalence reflects both the Las Vegas climate and the lifestyle expectations of Seven Hills residents.

The Italian and Mediterranean architectural style matches the broader Seven Hills design language, with stucco exteriors, tile roofs, and desert adapted landscaping. Over two decades of ownership have resulted in many homes receiving kitchen and bathroom upgrades, energy efficient improvements, and enhanced outdoor living spaces.

Pricing and Value

Homes in Crescendo generally trade in the $490,000 to $800,000 range, making it one of Seven Hills' most affordable single family options. For buyers who want a Seven Hills address with a private pool, proximity to Wolff Elementary School, and access to the community's trail system and parks, Crescendo provides strong value relative to gated and guard gated alternatives. The neighborhood's non gated status keeps HOA fees at the lower end of the Seven Hills range.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose points to Crescendo as the sweet spot for families who want to live in Seven Hills without stretching into luxury pricing. The high percentage of pool homes, the walkable access to Wolff Elementary and Allegro Park, and the full Seven Hills amenity package make Crescendo a practical choice for buyers who prioritize lifestyle over gated exclusivity. Ryan often shows Crescendo to families relocating to Henderson who want the best schools and community amenities at a price point that leaves room in their budget for other priorities.
