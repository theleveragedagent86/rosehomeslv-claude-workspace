Is Seven Hills Henderson Safe?

What Is Seven Hills Henderson?

Guard Gated Communities in Seven Hills Henderson

The crime rate in Seven Hills Henderson is well below both national and Las Vegas metro averages. The 89052 ZIP code reports a violent crime index of 10.5 compared to the national average of 22.7 according to BestPlaces, making it approximately 54 percent safer than the typical American community for violent crime. Property crime in 89052 registers at 26.4, also below the national average of 35.4.

Henderson's City Wide Crime Statistics

Henderson's violent crime rate sits at approximately 272 to 303 incidents per 100,000 residents according to NeighborhoodScout, significantly below the national average. The city recorded 8 homicides in 2024, and the overall crime rate dropped 12 percent compared to 2023, continuing a positive downward trend. According to AreaVibes, 67 percent of Henderson residents surveyed reported feeling safe walking alone at night.

Henderson's safety record has earned it the number two safest large city ranking in America from SafeWise, a distinction based on FBI crime data analysis for cities with populations over 300,000. The city's violent crime rate of approximately 2.1 per 1,000 residents is substantially lower than the Las Vegas metro area, making Henderson one of the preferred locations for families and retirees who prioritize safety.

How Seven Hills' Design Contributes to Safety

Seven Hills' master planned design adds layers of security beyond the city's already low crime rates. The community's defined entry points, cul de sac street layouts, and residential only zoning limit through traffic and create natural surveillance. Five neighborhoods (Terracina, Venezia, Rapallo, Renaissance, and Presidio) operate 24/7 staffed guard gates with private security patrols. Several additional neighborhoods use automatic gates for controlled access.

Henderson Police Department's South Area Command covers the Seven Hills area, and a fire station at 891 Coronado Center Drive sits in the 89052 ZIP code. The active HOA presence throughout Seven Hills ensures properties are maintained and community standards are enforced, which research consistently links to lower crime rates in residential areas.
