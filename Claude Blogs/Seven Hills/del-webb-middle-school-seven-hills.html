Del E. Webb Middle School is the primary middle school serving Seven Hills Henderson, located at 2200 Reunion Drive in Henderson. Webb Middle holds a B plus rating from Niche and an 8 out of 10 from GreatSchools, ranking number 23 among Nevada middle schools. The school serves approximately 1,640 students in grades six through eight and provides the academic bridge between Wolff Elementary and Coronado High School for most Seven Hills families.

Academics and Performance

Del Webb Middle School achieves 44 percent math proficiency and 63 percent reading proficiency among its students, placing it above the Nevada state average for middle school performance. The student to teacher ratio is 26 to 1. The school opened in August 2005 and is one of the newer public middle schools in southern Nevada, benefiting from modern facilities and updated technology infrastructure.

Students have access to laptop computers and educational software that supports both classroom instruction and independent learning. The school's academic program prepares students for Coronado High School's Advanced Placement courses and college preparatory track, creating a consistent educational pipeline from elementary through high school for Seven Hills families.

Extracurricular Activities

Webb Middle School offers a range of clubs and activities including archery, chess, art, and anime clubs. Athletic programs include flag football teams for both boys and girls, and the school has a dance team that performs at festivals and local events. These extracurricular options give students opportunities to explore interests beyond core academics and build social connections during the middle school years.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose notes that the quality of the middle school feeder is an important factor for families planning to stay in Seven Hills long term. Del Webb Middle School's B plus rating and above average test scores provide a solid transition from Wolff Elementary's top tier performance. The school's proximity to Seven Hills and its pipeline to Coronado High School mean that families can keep their children in consistently strong public schools from kindergarten through graduation without leaving the Henderson area.
