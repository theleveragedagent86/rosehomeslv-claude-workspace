Seven Hills Henderson Community Events

Seven Hills Henderson offers a range of family friendly activities centered around the community's four major parks, connected trail system, sports courts, and community event calendar. Families with children of all ages find that the combination of outdoor recreation, top rated schools, and organized community events creates a daily lifestyle that supports active, connected family living.

Parks and Outdoor Recreation

Sonata Park is the community's largest park at 12 acres with basketball courts, tennis courts, soccer fields, a playground, a splash pad, and barbecue areas. Allegro Park sits adjacent to Wolff Elementary and features soccer fields and playground equipment, making it a natural after school gathering spot. Puccini Park offers tennis, basketball, and volleyball courts for families with older children interested in court sports. Vivaldi Park provides a baseball diamond, playground, and some of the community's best panoramic views. All four parks are connected by the Seven Hills Trail, allowing families to walk or bike between parks without crossing vehicle traffic in most sections.

Community Events and Activities

Movies in the Park at Sonata Park provides free outdoor film screenings during cooler months, where families bring chairs and blankets to enjoy films on the park's natural hillside. Youth soccer games at Sonata and Allegro Parks create weekend activity for families with children in organized sports. The Seven Hills HOA organizes community barbecues and holiday events throughout the year. The nearby Sloan Canyon National Conservation Area offers family hiking with the opportunity to see Native American petroglyphs, and the Henderson Bird Viewing Preserve provides an educational nature experience 15 minutes from the community.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose considers Seven Hills one of Henderson's best communities for families because the activities are built into the daily residential experience rather than requiring long drives to separate facilities. Kids can walk to school at Wolff Elementary, play at Allegro Park after school, ride bikes on the trail system, and attend Movies in the Park on summer evenings, all within the community boundaries. This self contained family lifestyle is one of the primary reasons families with young children consistently choose Seven Hills over other Henderson neighborhoods.
