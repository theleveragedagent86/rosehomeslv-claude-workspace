Luxury Homes in Seven Hills Henderson

Golf course homes in Seven Hills Henderson are properties with direct views of or adjacency to the Serket Golf Club (formerly Rio Secco Golf Club), the 18 hole par 72 championship course designed by Rees Jones that winds through the heart of the community. These homes command premium pricing due to their views of manicured fairways, the privacy buffer that the course provides, and the aesthetic appeal of living alongside a championship golf landscape.

Neighborhoods with Golf Course Frontage

Renaissance is the neighborhood with the most extensive golf course exposure, with three sides of the community adjacent to the Serket Golf Club. Many Renaissance homes look directly onto fairways and greens, providing daily views of the course without the need for a club membership. The Marquis by Blue Heron development sits between two Serket fairways, giving its 33 contemporary home sites direct golf course frontage. Select homes in Terracina, Venezia, Rapallo, and Presidio also enjoy golf course views from their elevated positions, though these views are often combined with Strip and mountain panoramas.

Golf Course View Premiums

Homes with confirmed golf course views in Seven Hills typically sell for a premium over comparable homes without view exposure. The premium varies by neighborhood and the quality of the view, but buyers should expect golf course facing properties to command a meaningful price advantage. The most valuable golf course positions combine fairway views with additional Strip or mountain views, creating a multi directional panorama that is rare even in luxury Las Vegas communities.
