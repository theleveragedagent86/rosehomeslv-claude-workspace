Shopping Near Seven Hills Henderson

Restaurants Near Seven Hills Henderson

Seven Hills Henderson residents have access to a strong selection of grocery stores within one to three miles of the community. From budget friendly options like Grocery Outlet to specialty stores like Trader Joe's and Seafood City, the Eastern Avenue and St. Rose Parkway corridors provide convenient choices for every shopping style and budget.

Closest Grocery Options

Grocery Outlet at 3499 St. Rose Parkway opened in January 2025 and sits approximately one mile from Seven Hills, making it the closest full service grocery option. Vons at 2511 Anthem Village Drive is roughly 1.3 miles away and serves as a reliable conventional grocery store. Smith's Food and Drug at Horizon Marketplace on South Eastern Avenue is about 1.5 miles from the community and offers full grocery, pharmacy, and fuel services.

For buyers who prefer specialty grocery, Trader Joe's at 10345 South Eastern Avenue (in Eastern Commons) is approximately two miles from Seven Hills. Seafood City Supermarket, also in Eastern Commons at 10405 South Eastern Avenue, offers one of the valley's largest selections of Asian and Pacific Islander groceries and fresh seafood. Costco at St. Rose Square (3543 St. Rose Parkway) provides warehouse bulk shopping about two miles from the community.

Additional Options Within a Short Drive

Sprouts Farmers Market at 635 South Green Valley Parkway is approximately 2.6 miles from Seven Hills and caters to shoppers who prioritize organic and natural foods. For a broader selection of health focused groceries, Whole Foods Market is available at a slightly longer drive in the Green Valley area. The concentration of grocery options along the Eastern Avenue corridor means most Seven Hills residents can choose between three or four stores without driving more than five minutes.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose points out that grocery accessibility is one of the practical advantages buyers notice quickly when visiting Seven Hills. Despite the community's residential, almost retreat like feel, having Trader Joe's, Costco, Smith's, and Vons all within about two miles means daily life is genuinely convenient. The 2025 opening of Grocery Outlet on St. Rose Parkway added another affordable option that further reduced the need for long grocery runs.
