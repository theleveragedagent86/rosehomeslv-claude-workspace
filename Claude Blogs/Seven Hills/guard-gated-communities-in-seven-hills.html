Is Seven Hills a Gated Community?

Seven Hills Henderson has five guard gated neighborhoods with 24/7 staffed guard gates and controlled access. These five communities represent the highest tier of residential security and exclusivity within the broader Seven Hills master planned community: Terracina, Venezia, Rapallo, Renaissance, and Presidio.

The Five Guard Gated Neighborhoods

Terracina is the most prestigious of the five, featuring custom estates on lots ranging from a quarter acre to nearly one acre with homes up to 15,000 square feet. Venezia is an enclave of approximately 70 custom homes on one third to one half acre lots, sharing a connected guard gated entry with Terracina while maintaining its own distinct neighborhood identity. Rapallo was among the earliest luxury subdivisions in Seven Hills, with approximately 44 custom homes built between 1999 and 2002 on lots up to nearly nine tenths of an acre.

Renaissance stands apart as the only guard gated community built by a production builder. Pardee Homes constructed 217 semi custom homes between 2001 and 2003 with sizes ranging from 2,800 to over 4,600 square feet, making it the most accessible entry point into guard gated Seven Hills living. Presidio is a smaller custom home enclave built in the late 1990s and early 2000s with elevated terrain that provides panoramic views of the Serket Golf Club, the Las Vegas Strip, and the surrounding mountains.

Guard Gated vs Auto Gated

It is important to understand the distinction between guard gated and auto gated neighborhoods in Seven Hills. The five guard gated communities have live security guards staffing the entrance 24 hours a day, seven days a week. Several other Seven Hills neighborhoods use auto gated systems with remote controlled access but no live guard on site. Both types provide controlled access, but the guard gated communities offer a higher level of security screening and visitor management.

Pricing Across Guard Gated Communities

Pricing varies significantly among the five guard gated neighborhoods. Renaissance offers the most accessible pricing in the $800,000 to $1,400,000 range. Presidio generally trades between $1 million and $3 million. Rapallo ranges from approximately $1 million to $5 million. Venezia and Terracina occupy the top tier, with listings commonly appearing between $1.5 million and $6 million or higher depending on lot size, custom finishes, and view premiums.
