Is Seven Hills Henderson a Good Place to Live?

Seven Hills Henderson residents have access to multiple hospitals and medical facilities within a short drive. St. Rose Dominican Hospital Siena Campus and Henderson Hospital are the two closest full service hospitals, both located within the Henderson city limits. Several urgent care centers are also nearby, providing quick access to medical attention for non emergency situations.

Nearest Hospitals

St. Rose Dominican Hospital Siena Campus is located at 3001 St. Rose Parkway in Henderson, making it one of the closest hospital options for Seven Hills residents. The Siena Campus is a full service acute care hospital operated by Dignity Health, specializing in cardiac care, emergency services, and comprehensive medical and surgical treatment. The hospital has received national recognition for cardiac care quality.

Henderson Hospital at 1050 West Galleria Drive is a 303 bed facility accredited by The Joint Commission. Henderson Hospital offers emergency care, childbirth services, cardiac care, inpatient and surgical services, and radiology. The hospital has received a Patient Safety Excellence Award, reflecting its commitment to quality medical care for the Henderson community.

Urgent Care and Additional Options

For non emergency medical needs, Southwest Medical operates an urgent care clinic at 2845 Siena Heights Drive in Henderson with hours from 7 AM to 7 PM seven days a week, offering X ray and lab services. CareNow Urgent Care near Eastern Avenue and Horizon Ridge provides walk in care seven days a week for minor illnesses and injuries. St. Rose Dominican Rose de Lima Campus in downtown Henderson adds another full service hospital option within the broader Henderson area.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose notes that healthcare access is one of the practical considerations that buyers sometimes overlook when evaluating a community. Seven Hills' proximity to St. Rose Dominican Siena Campus and Henderson Hospital means that emergency medical care is never more than a short drive away. The availability of urgent care centers along the St. Rose Parkway and Eastern Avenue corridors adds everyday convenience for routine medical visits. For retirees and families with young children, this combination of hospital proximity and urgent care access provides genuine peace of mind.
