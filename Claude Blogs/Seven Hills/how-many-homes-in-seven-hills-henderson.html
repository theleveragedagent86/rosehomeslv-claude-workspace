What Is Seven Hills Henderson?

Neighborhoods in Seven Hills Henderson

Types of Homes in Seven Hills Henderson

Seven Hills Henderson contains approximately 3,000 homes across 25 distinct neighborhoods on 1,300 acres. The community is essentially built out, meaning new construction is limited to occasional custom homes on remaining vacant lots in neighborhoods like Palazzo Monte, Terracina, and the Marquis by Blue Heron development. This fixed housing supply is one of the factors that supports property values in the community over time.

Housing Breakdown

The approximately 3,000 homes in Seven Hills include a mix of condominiums, townhomes, production single family homes, semi custom homes, and custom estates. Avalon provides condominium units, The Terraces offers townhomes, and the production neighborhoods of Crescendo, Naples, Palermo West, and Meridiana contain the largest number of single family homes. The guard gated communities of Terracina (approximately 460 homes), Renaissance (217 homes), Venezia (approximately 70 homes), Rapallo (approximately 44 homes), and Presidio contribute the luxury tier. Marquis adds 33 contemporary home sites built by Blue Heron.

Limited New Construction

Unlike newer Henderson communities such as Inspirada that are still actively building thousands of homes, Seven Hills has reached near full build out. The only new construction opportunities are on scattered vacant lots that occasionally come to market in Palazzo Monte, Terracina, and the Marquis development. This means buyers entering Seven Hills are primarily purchasing resale homes, and the limited ability to add new supply creates a structural constraint on inventory that benefits existing homeowners.
