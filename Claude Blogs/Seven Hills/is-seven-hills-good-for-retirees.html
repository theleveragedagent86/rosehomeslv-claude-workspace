Hospitals Near Seven Hills Henderson

Seven Hills Henderson is an excellent choice for retirees who want an active, amenity rich lifestyle in a community that is not restricted to a specific age group. Unlike dedicated 55 plus communities, Seven Hills welcomes residents of all ages while offering the golf, parks, healthcare access, and quality of life features that retirees prioritize. Nevada's zero state income tax on all retirement income adds a significant financial advantage for retirees relocating from higher tax states.

Financial Advantages for Retirees

Nevada does not tax Social Security benefits, pension distributions, IRA withdrawals, or 401(k) distributions at the state level. Retirees relocating from states like California, New York, or New Jersey can save thousands of dollars annually on the same retirement income. Clark County's property tax rate of approximately 0.77 percent is below the national median of 1.02 percent, and Nevada caps annual increases on primary residences at three percent. Healthcare costs in the Henderson area run approximately 13 percent below the national average, adding further savings for retirees on fixed incomes.

Healthcare and Lifestyle

St. Rose Dominican Hospital Siena Campus and Henderson Hospital are both within a short drive of Seven Hills, providing full service emergency, cardiac, and surgical care. Urgent care clinics along St. Rose Parkway and Eastern Avenue handle routine medical needs. The Serket Golf Club and Butch Harmon School of Golf within Seven Hills give golf enthusiasts a world class course and instruction facility steps from home. The community's four major parks, connected trail system, and proximity to the 48,438 acre Sloan Canyon National Conservation Area support active outdoor lifestyles year round in a climate that averages over 300 days of sunshine.

Housing Options and Nearby 55 Plus Communities

Seven Hills includes single story homes in several neighborhoods, townhomes in The Terraces, and condominiums in Avalon, all of which can suit retirees who prefer single level living or lower maintenance options. For retirees who prefer a dedicated 55 plus community, Sun City Anthem is located nearby with over 7,100 households, three clubhouses totaling more than 130,000 square feet of amenities, two 18 hole golf courses, and over 50 clubs and interest groups.
