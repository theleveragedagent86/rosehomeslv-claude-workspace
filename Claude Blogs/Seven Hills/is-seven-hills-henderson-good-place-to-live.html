Is Seven Hills Henderson Safe?

Schools Serving Seven Hills Henderson

Seven Hills Henderson consistently ranks as one of the best places to live in Nevada. Niche.com ranks Seven Hills among the top 7 best places to live in the state and among the top 7 best places to raise a family in Nevada. The community combines top rated schools, a strong safety record, extensive parks and trails, and a wide range of housing options from condos under $250,000 to custom estates above $8 million.

What Residents Value Most

Seven Hills sits in Henderson, the number two safest large city in America according to SafeWise. The 89052 ZIP code reports violent crime rates approximately 54 percent below the national average, and the master planned community's design with defined entry points and cul de sac streets adds another layer of security. Five guard gated neighborhoods offer even more privacy for residents who prioritize it.

The school quality is a major draw for families. Elise L. Wolff Elementary holds a 9 to 10 out of 10 GreatSchools rating and is located within the community. Coronado High School has earned an A rating from Niche and ranks among the top 20 public high schools in Nevada, with a 91 percent graduation rate and over 60 clubs and extracurricular programs.

Lifestyle and Convenience

Seven Hills is a car dependent community with a Walk Score around 23, which is typical for Henderson's master planned neighborhoods. However, daily conveniences are close by: Trader Joe's, Costco, Smith's, and Albertsons are all within two miles, and St. Rose Dominican Hospital (Siena Campus) is roughly a five minute drive. The Las Vegas Strip is approximately 15 to 22 minutes away depending on your destination.

The community's Italian inspired design, rolling terrain with mountain and Strip views, and four major parks connected by paved trails create an environment that residents describe as upscale, family friendly, and nature oriented. The Serket Golf Club (formerly Rio Secco) and its Butch Harmon School of Golf add a world class recreational amenity at the community's center. The median household income in the 89052 ZIP code is approximately $97,000, reflecting the community's established, professional resident base.
