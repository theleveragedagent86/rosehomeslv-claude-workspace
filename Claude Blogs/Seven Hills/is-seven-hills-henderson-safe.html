What Is Seven Hills Henderson?

Crime Rate in Seven Hills Henderson

Guard Gated Communities in Seven Hills Henderson

Seven Hills Henderson is one of the safest residential communities in the Las Vegas valley. Henderson ranks as the number two safest large city in America according to SafeWise, and the 89052 ZIP code where Seven Hills is located reports a violent crime index of 10.5, which is approximately 54 percent below the national average of 22.7 according to BestPlaces.

Henderson's Safety Record by the Numbers

Henderson's violent crime rate sits at approximately 2.1 incidents per 1,000 residents, significantly lower than both the national average and the Las Vegas metropolitan area. The city's overall crime rate dropped 12 percent in 2024 compared to the prior year, continuing a downward trend. Property crime in the 89052 ZIP code also falls below the national average, with an index of 26.4 compared to the national baseline of 35.4 per BestPlaces.

South Henderson, where Seven Hills is located, consistently reports the fewest crimes of any section within the city. The master planned design of Seven Hills itself contributes to safety through defined entry points, cul de sac street layouts, and active HOA oversight that creates natural surveillance throughout the community.

Guard Gated Security Within Seven Hills

Five neighborhoods within Seven Hills operate 24/7 staffed guard gates: Terracina, Venezia, Rapallo, Renaissance, and Presidio. These communities employ private security patrols in addition to the gate staffing. Several other neighborhoods use automatic gates for controlled access without guard personnel. Henderson Police Department's South Area Command covers Seven Hills, and a fire station sits in the 89052 ZIP code at 891 Coronado Center Drive, very near the community.
