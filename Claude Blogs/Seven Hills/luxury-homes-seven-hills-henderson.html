Seven Hills Henderson Homes for Sale

Terracina Seven Hills Henderson

Luxury homes in Seven Hills Henderson are concentrated in the community's five guard gated neighborhoods and the Marquis by Blue Heron development. These properties represent the top tier of residential real estate in Henderson, with custom estates ranging from approximately $1 million to well over $6 million featuring expansive floor plans, premium finishes, and views of the Las Vegas Strip, Serket Golf Club, and surrounding mountain ranges.

Guard Gated Luxury

Terracina is Seven Hills' premier luxury neighborhood with approximately 460 custom homes on lots ranging from a quarter acre to nearly one acre. Homes in Terracina can exceed 15,000 square feet with features including resort style pools, home theaters, wine cellars, and multi car garages. Venezia offers approximately 70 custom homes on generous lots with a quieter, more intimate atmosphere. Rapallo's 44 custom homes on lots up to nearly nine tenths of an acre represent some of the earliest luxury construction in Seven Hills. Presidio is a smaller custom enclave with elevated views, and Renaissance provides guard gated semi custom living at a more accessible price point.

Contemporary Luxury

The Marquis by Blue Heron development brings a contemporary architectural style to Seven Hills' luxury market. With 33 home sites featuring floor to ceiling windows, rooftop terraces, clean modern lines, and sustainable design elements, Marquis appeals to buyers who prefer a desert modern aesthetic over traditional Mediterranean and Italian styles. Blue Heron's award winning construction and innovative design have earned national recognition, including a model home featured at the National Association of Home Builders convention.
