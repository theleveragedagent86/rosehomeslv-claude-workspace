Shopping Near Seven Hills Henderson

M Resort Spa Casino is located at 12300 Las Vegas Boulevard South in Henderson, making it one of the closest full service resort casinos to Seven Hills. Opened in March 2009, M Resort is the southernmost casino resort in the Las Vegas valley and provides Seven Hills residents with upscale dining, spa services, gaming, and entertainment without driving to the Strip.

Resort Amenities

M Resort features a 92,000 square foot casino floor with over 1,300 slot and video poker machines and 40 table games. The 16 story tower contains 390 guest rooms equipped with modern technology including Bose sound systems and floor to ceiling windows. The resort opened with nine restaurants and five bars, with dining managed directly by ownership rather than third party operators, resulting in a consistent quality of food and service across the property.

Spa Mio occupies 23,000 square feet and offers 16 treatment rooms, sauna and steam facilities, Jacuzzi areas, a full service salon, and a barbershop. Two outdoor pools with cabanas and daybeds provide a resort style atmosphere for relaxation. The property also includes a state of the art fitness center and over 60,000 square feet of conference and event space, including a 17,400 square foot ballroom.

Convenience for Seven Hills Residents

M Resort's location on Las Vegas Boulevard South makes it a quick drive from Seven Hills for dinner, spa visits, or entertainment. The resort fills a niche for Seven Hills residents who want a luxury resort experience close to home without the traffic and crowds of the Las Vegas Strip. Many residents use M Resort for special occasion dining, spa days, and hosting visiting friends and family in a resort setting that feels elevated without being overwhelming.
