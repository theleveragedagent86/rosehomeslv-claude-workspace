Guard Gated Communities in Seven Hills Henderson

Marquis at Seven Hills is a gated community of 33 custom home sites developed by Blue Heron, one of Southern Nevada's premier luxury home builders. Marquis stands out within Seven Hills for its desert contemporary architectural style, a departure from the Italian and Mediterranean designs that characterize most of the community. Homes in Marquis range from approximately 4,000 to over 10,000 square feet with prices starting around $1.5 million and reaching $3 million or higher.

Blue Heron Contemporary Design

Blue Heron is a full service architecture, interior design, and construction firm with over two decades of experience building luxury homes in the Las Vegas valley. Their Marquis homes feature floor to ceiling windows, rooftop terraces, clean modern lines, and open floor plans designed to frame the surrounding views. Some homes include rare features for the Las Vegas market such as elevators and basements, reflecting the custom nature of each build.

The neighborhood sits between two fairways of the Serket Golf Club (formerly Rio Secco Golf Club), giving many homes direct views of the championship course. The elevated position within Seven Hills also provides panoramic views of the Las Vegas Strip and surrounding mountain ranges. Blue Heron's emphasis on green building and energy efficient construction adds a sustainability dimension that appeals to environmentally conscious luxury buyers.

Market Position

Marquis occupies a unique space in Seven Hills' luxury market. While the guard gated custom communities like Terracina and Venezia feature traditional Mediterranean architecture, Marquis offers a modern aesthetic that attracts buyers who prefer contemporary design. With only 33 home sites, the neighborhood is one of the most exclusive in Seven Hills, and listings are rare. A Marquis model home was featured as "The Great American Home" at the National Association of Home Builders convention, reflecting the caliber of design and construction.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose recommends Marquis to buyers who want luxury living in Seven Hills but prefer a modern, contemporary home over traditional Mediterranean design. Blue Heron's reputation for quality construction, innovative design, and attention to detail makes Marquis one of the most compelling luxury options in Henderson. The combination of only 33 homes, golf course frontage, and an award winning builder creates a neighborhood that stands entirely on its own within Seven Hills.
