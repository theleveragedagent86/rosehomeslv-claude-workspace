What Is Seven Hills Henderson?

Guard Gated Communities in Seven Hills Henderson

Types of Homes in Seven Hills Henderson

Seven Hills Henderson contains approximately 25 distinct neighborhoods organized into three tiers of access: guard gated, auto gated, and non gated. This variety gives buyers options at every price point from condominiums under $300,000 to custom estates exceeding $6 million, all within the same 1,300 acre master planned community with shared parks, trails, schools, and the Serket Golf Club.

Guard Gated Neighborhoods

Five neighborhoods have 24/7 staffed guard gates with the highest level of security and exclusivity. Terracina features approximately 460 custom homes on lots up to nearly one acre with prices from $1.5 million to $6 million or higher. Venezia has approximately 70 custom homes on one third to one half acre lots. Rapallo contains approximately 44 custom homes built between 1999 and 2002. Renaissance is the only guard gated community built by a production builder (Pardee Homes), with 217 semi custom homes from $800,000 to $1.4 million. Presidio is a smaller custom enclave with elevated views and prices from $1 million to $3 million.

Auto Gated Neighborhoods

Several neighborhoods use automatic gate systems with remote controlled access but no live guard on site. Palazzo Monte offers homes from approximately 2,500 to 4,400 square feet on hillside lots with vacant land still available for custom builds. Villa Villagio features similar sized homes built between 1997 and 2003 with a high percentage of pool homes. Majestic Hills is another popular gated option in the upper price range. These neighborhoods generally price between $600,000 and $1,200,000 and provide a meaningful security upgrade over non gated areas at lower HOA costs than the guard gated tier.

Non Gated Neighborhoods

The non gated neighborhoods make up the largest share of Seven Hills' housing stock and include the most accessible price points. Crescendo has 330 homes with more than 50 percent featuring pools. Naples, Palermo West, Meridiana, and Aventine are production home neighborhoods with sizes ranging from 1,180 to 2,800 square feet and prices generally in the $490,000 to $800,000 range. Avalon offers condominiums from $250,000 to $320,000, and The Terraces provides townhomes from $300,000 to $500,000 with a private community pool.
