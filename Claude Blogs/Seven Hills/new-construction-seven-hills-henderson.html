How Many Homes Are in Seven Hills Henderson?

Marquis at Seven Hills Henderson

Palazzo Monte Seven Hills Henderson

New construction opportunities in Seven Hills Henderson are extremely limited because the community is nearly built out after more than two decades of development. With approximately 3,000 homes across 25 neighborhoods on 1,300 acres, Seven Hills has very few remaining buildable lots. Buyers seeking new construction in Seven Hills are limited to scattered vacant lots in a handful of neighborhoods and the occasional spec home built by a custom builder on one of those lots.

Where New Construction Is Possible

The Marquis by Blue Heron development still has available home sites among its 33 total lots. Blue Heron builds desert contemporary homes ranging from 4,000 to over 10,000 square feet with prices starting around $1.5 million. Palazzo Monte occasionally has vacant hillside lots available for custom builds, and Terracina has scattered remaining lots where buyers can work with a custom builder of their choice. These opportunities are rare and tend to attract serious buyers quickly when they come to market.

Custom Build Process

Building new construction in Seven Hills requires navigating the community's architectural review process, which ensures that new homes comply with Seven Hills' design standards and the neighborhood specific guidelines of each subdivision. The Seven Hills HOA and individual neighborhood HOAs must approve architectural plans before construction begins. Buyers should plan for a timeline of 12 to 24 months from lot purchase through construction completion, depending on the complexity of the home and the permit approval process with the City of Henderson.
