What Is Seven Hills Henderson?

Palazzo Monte is a gated community within Seven Hills Henderson featuring homes on hillside lots with sizes ranging from approximately 2,500 to 4,400 square feet. Built between 2001 and 2003, Palazzo Monte uses an auto gated entry system rather than a 24/7 staffed guard gate, providing controlled access at a lower HOA cost than the guard gated enclaves. Vacant lots are still occasionally available for custom builds, making Palazzo Monte one of the few Seven Hills neighborhoods where buyers can build new construction on an existing lot.

Homes and Lot Features

Palazzo Monte's floor plans include three to six bedroom configurations in single and two story designs. The neighborhood's hillside positioning gives many homes elevated views of the Las Vegas valley and surrounding mountains. The Italian and Mediterranean architectural style is consistent with the broader Seven Hills aesthetic, and the mature landscaping that has developed over two decades gives the community an established character that newer developments cannot replicate.

The availability of vacant lots for custom builds sets Palazzo Monte apart from most other Seven Hills neighborhoods. Buyers who want to design a home from the ground up within an established gated community can acquire a lot and work with a custom builder to create a residence tailored to their specifications. This option is increasingly rare in Seven Hills as the community reaches full build out.

Pricing and Market Position

Homes in Palazzo Monte generally trade in the $600,000 to $1,200,000 range, positioning the neighborhood between the production home neighborhoods and the guard gated custom estates in Seven Hills' pricing hierarchy. The auto gated entry provides a meaningful security upgrade over non gated neighborhoods while keeping HOA costs below the guard gated tier. For buyers who want gated living in Seven Hills without the premium pricing of Terracina, Venezia, or Rapallo, Palazzo Monte offers a compelling middle ground.
