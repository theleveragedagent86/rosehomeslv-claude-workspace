Types of Homes in Seven Hills Henderson

Seven Hills Henderson Weather and Climate

Pool homes are one of the most popular property types in Seven Hills Henderson, driven by the Las Vegas valley's 300 plus days of sunshine and a climate that supports outdoor swimming for eight to ten months of the year. Across Seven Hills' 25 neighborhoods, a substantial percentage of single family homes include private swimming pools, with some neighborhoods like Crescendo featuring pools in more than half of all homes.

Pool Home Availability by Price Range

Pool homes in Seven Hills span the community's full price spectrum. In production neighborhoods like Crescendo, Naples, and Palermo West, pool homes generally list in the $500,000 to $800,000 range. Gated communities including Palazzo Monte and Villa Villagio offer pool homes from approximately $600,000 to $1,200,000 with larger lots and more elaborate pool designs. Guard gated neighborhoods such as Terracina, Venezia, and Rapallo feature resort style pool and spa complexes with waterfalls, outdoor kitchens, and fire features on properties priced from $1 million to well over $6 million.

Pool Design and Maintenance

Pool designs in Seven Hills range from standard rectangular pools with automatic covers to custom freeform designs with grottos, infinity edges, and integrated spas. The desert climate requires consistent pool maintenance including chemical balancing, filter cleaning, and occasional resurfacing, with monthly pool service costs typically ranging from $100 to $200 for basic maintenance. Many Seven Hills homeowners have added solar pool heating systems that extend the swimming season into the cooler months at minimal operating cost.
