Puccini Park is one of the four major parks in Seven Hills Henderson, covering approximately 4.5 acres at 1899 Seven Hills Drive. Open daily from 6 AM to 10 PM, Puccini Park is the community's primary sports court destination with tennis courts, a basketball court, and volleyball courts alongside a playground, picnic areas, and trail connections.

Sports Courts and Recreation

Puccini Park offers the most concentrated collection of sports courts in Seven Hills. The park includes tennis courts, a basketball court, and volleyball courts, making it the go to location for residents who enjoy court sports. A playground with swings and slides provides recreation for younger children, while open grass areas and picnic shelters with barbecue grills accommodate family gatherings and weekend cookouts. Restrooms are available on site.

Paved walking trails within Puccini Park connect to the broader Seven Hills Trail system, linking the park to Sonata Park, Vivaldi Park, and Allegro Park. This connection allows residents to combine a sports session at Puccini with a longer walk or jog through the community's trail network without driving between parks.

Water Conservation Upgrades

The City of Henderson completed a water conservation project at Puccini Park that removed approximately 25,000 square feet of irrigated turf, saving an estimated 1,375,000 gallons of water annually. The conversion replaced traditional grass areas with drought adapted landscaping that reduces maintenance costs while fitting the natural desert environment of the surrounding foothills. The sports courts, playground, and key recreational areas were preserved while non essential turf was replaced.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose highlights Puccini Park as a key amenity for active residents and families with children who enjoy organized and casual sports. The combination of tennis, basketball, and volleyball courts in one location is uncommon for a community park in Henderson, and residents appreciate not having to drive to a separate recreation center for court time. For buyers who value an active outdoor lifestyle, proximity to Puccini Park and the connected trail system is a practical daily advantage that adds to Seven Hills' quality of life.
