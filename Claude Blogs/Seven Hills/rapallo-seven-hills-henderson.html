Guard Gated Communities in Seven Hills Henderson

Rapallo is one of the original luxury enclaves in Seven Hills Henderson, a 24/7 guard gated community of approximately 44 custom homes built between 1999 and 2002. With lot sizes ranging from roughly one third to nearly nine tenths of an acre, Rapallo was among the earliest guard gated neighborhoods established in Seven Hills and remains one of the most sought after addresses in the community.

Custom Homes on Generous Lots

Rapallo's homes were individually designed and built by custom builders, with finished sizes typically ranging from approximately 3,000 to over 10,000 square feet. Floor plans include single story, two story, and tri level configurations with three to seven bedrooms and three to five or more car garages. Many homes feature resort style amenities including swimming pools, spas, home theaters, and double master suites.

The neighborhood's elevated position within Seven Hills provides many homes with panoramic views of the Las Vegas Strip and the surrounding mountain ranges. Rapallo's proximity to the Serket Golf Club (formerly Rio Secco Golf Club) adds golf course views to the package for homes positioned along the course edge. The Italian and Mediterranean architectural style is consistent throughout the neighborhood, reflecting Seven Hills' Tuscan design inspiration.

Pricing and Market Position

Rapallo homes generally list in the $1 million to $5 million plus range, with pricing driven by lot size, square footage, view orientation, and the quality of custom finishes and renovations. Recent listings have appeared at the higher end of this range, reflecting the premium that buyers place on Rapallo's combination of lot sizes, custom architecture, and established landscaping. With only about 44 homes in the community, turnover is limited and listings tend to attract serious luxury buyers.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose highlights Rapallo as one of Seven Hills' most underrated guard gated communities. Built before Terracina and Venezia, Rapallo offers some of the most established landscaping and mature streetscapes in Seven Hills. The lot sizes rival those in Venezia, and the Strip views from elevated positions are among the best in the community. For buyers who prioritize privacy, space, and an intimate neighborhood scale, Rapallo's 44 home enclave delivers a residential experience that is difficult to replicate anywhere in Henderson.
