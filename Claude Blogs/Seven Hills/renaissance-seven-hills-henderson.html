Is Seven Hills a Gated Community?

What Is Seven Hills Henderson?

Renaissance is one of five guard gated neighborhoods in Seven Hills Henderson, a 217 home community built by Pardee Homes between 2001 and 2003. What makes Renaissance unique among the guard gated enclaves is that it is the only one developed by a production builder rather than individual custom builders, offering a more accessible entry point into guard gated Seven Hills living with homes ranging from approximately 2,800 to over 4,600 square feet.

Semi Custom Homes by Pardee

Pardee Homes (now Tri Pointe Homes) built Renaissance with six base floor plans including two single story designs and four two story designs, offering over 19 variations by square footage. The community includes 80 single story homes and 137 two story homes, with three to five bedrooms and two to three car garages. While each home follows one of the base plans, Pardee's semi custom approach allowed buyers to select from a wide range of finishes, upgrades, and layout modifications.

Three sides of the Renaissance community are adjacent to the Serket Golf Club (formerly Rio Secco Golf Club), giving many homes direct views of the championship fairways designed by Rees Jones. The Italian and Mediterranean architectural style blends seamlessly with the broader Seven Hills aesthetic, and the mature landscaping that has developed over two decades gives the neighborhood an established, refined character.

Pricing and Market Position

Renaissance sits in the middle of Seven Hills' luxury pricing spectrum. Homes typically trade in the $800,000 to $1,400,000 range depending on square footage, lot position, renovation level, and golf course proximity. This makes Renaissance significantly more affordable than the custom estate neighborhoods of Terracina, Venezia, and Rapallo while still offering the 24/7 guard gated security, golf course views, and elevated location that define Seven Hills' premium communities.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose considers Renaissance the best value in guard gated Seven Hills. Buyers who want the security, prestige, and lifestyle of a guard gated community but do not need a 6,000 plus square foot custom estate will find Renaissance hits a rare sweet spot. The Pardee build quality has held up well, many homes have been thoughtfully updated over the years, and the golf course adjacency on three sides is a feature that even some of the more expensive neighborhoods cannot match.
