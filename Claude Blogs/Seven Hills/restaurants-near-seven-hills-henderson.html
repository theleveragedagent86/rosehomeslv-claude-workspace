Shopping Near Seven Hills Henderson

Grocery Stores Near Seven Hills Henderson

Seven Hills Henderson residents have access to a strong selection of dining options within a short drive of the community. On site, the Serket Golf Club (formerly Rio Secco) houses Janelas Restaurant and Bar with golf course views. Along nearby Eastern Avenue and the St. Rose Parkway corridor, a range of cuisines from Cuban to American grill to craft gastropub dining are all within one to two miles.

Dining on Eastern Avenue

Cubanidad 1885 at 10575 South Eastern Avenue brings Cuban and Cuban Chinese cuisine to the Seven Hills area, with a 4.7 star Google rating from over 1,000 reviews. The restaurant is open daily from 10 AM to 8 PM and sits approximately one mile from Seven Hills' western entrance. Black Mountain Grill at 11021 South Eastern Avenue is a women owned New American restaurant with a lodge atmosphere, open 24 hours a day, seven days a week, making it one of the few around the clock dining options near the community.

Egg Works on South Eastern Avenue is a local favorite for breakfast and brunch, open weekday mornings through early afternoon. For a quick, quality meal, the Eastern Avenue corridor also includes several chain and independent options that handle everyday dining without a long drive.

St. Rose Parkway Corridor

Eureka! Henderson at 3354 St. Rose Parkway opened in 2025 and brings scratch made gastropub fare and craft cocktails featuring over 40 whiskeys. Their signature Bone Marrow Burger has become a local talking point. The Great Greek Mediterranean Grill on St. Rose Parkway offers generous portions of Greek and Mediterranean cuisine in a family friendly setting. Both restaurants sit within two miles of Seven Hills' northern boundary.

For more upscale options, M Resort (approximately 12 minutes from Seven Hills) offers Anthony's Prime Steak and Seafood (a Wine Spectator Award winner) and Jayde at 16, a rooftop Asian restaurant with panoramic Strip views. The District at Green Valley Ranch, roughly 10 minutes away, features The Cheesecake Factory, P.F. Chang's, and King's Fish House among its 20 plus dining options.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose notes that dining accessibility near Seven Hills has improved steadily, particularly with recent additions along the St. Rose Parkway corridor. While Seven Hills itself is primarily residential by design, the concentration of quality restaurants on Eastern Avenue and St. Rose Parkway means most residents are within a five to ten minute drive of a wide variety of cuisines. The proximity to The District at Green Valley Ranch and M Resort adds another tier of dining options for special occasions.
