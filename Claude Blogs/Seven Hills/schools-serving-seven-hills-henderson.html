What Is Seven Hills Henderson?

Elise L Wolff Elementary School Seven Hills Henderson

Seven Hills Henderson is served by some of the highest rated public schools in the Clark County School District. The community's zoned schools include Elise L. Wolff Elementary (located within Seven Hills), Del E. Webb Middle School, and Coronado High School, all of which consistently earn strong ratings from GreatSchools and Niche. Several additional school options are available nearby for families seeking alternatives.

Elementary Schools

Elise L. Wolff Elementary is the primary elementary school for Seven Hills families, located at 1001 Seven Hills Drive adjacent to Allegro Park. The school serves grades PK through 5 with approximately 755 students and an 18 to 1 student teacher ratio. Wolff Elementary holds a 9 to 10 out of 10 rating on GreatSchools and offers a Gifted and Talented program. The school opened in 2001 and feeds into Del E. Webb Middle School and then Coronado High School.

Glen C. Taylor Elementary at 2655 Siena Heights Drive also serves portions of the Seven Hills area. Taylor holds an 8 out of 10 GreatSchools rating with about 628 students and feeds into Bob Miller Middle School before connecting to Coronado High School. Frank S. Lamping Elementary, located in the nearby MacDonald Ranch area, is a Governor's STEAM School with STEM designation and a William McCool Science Center that includes a three quarter scale space shuttle simulator.

Middle and High Schools

Del E. Webb Middle School at 2200 Reunion Drive is the primary middle school for Seven Hills, serving grades 6 through 8 with an 8 out of 10 GreatSchools rating and approximately 1,640 students. Bob Miller Middle School at 2400 Cozy Hill Circle serves additional portions of the area and offers a Gifted and Talented program.

Coronado High School at 1001 Coronado Center Drive is the zoned high school for Seven Hills students. Coronado serves grades 9 through 12 with a 91 percent graduation rate and an A rating from Niche, ranking among the top 20 public high schools in Nevada. The school has earned the highest "Five Star" designation from CCSD and offers over 60 clubs, with 41 percent of students participating in Advanced Placement courses. Coral Academy of Science on Sandy Ridge Avenue provides a charter alternative, earning recognition as one of the top ranked high schools in Henderson.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose considers the school quality in Seven Hills one of its most significant advantages for families. Having Wolff Elementary physically located within the community means younger children can attend a top rated school without a lengthy commute, and the direct feeder pattern through Webb Middle School to Coronado High School gives families a clear, high quality educational path from kindergarten through graduation.
