What Is Seven Hills Henderson?

Butch Harmon School of Golf at Seven Hills

Golf Course View Homes in Seven Hills Henderson

Serket Golf Club, formerly known as Rio Secco Golf Club, is an 18 hole championship course designed by renowned architect Rees Jones at the heart of Seven Hills Henderson. The par 72 layout stretches up to 7,332 yards across three distinct terrain sections and is home to the world famous Butch Harmon School of Golf. The course opened in 1997 and underwent a $2.1 million renovation led by Rees Jones in 2017, with the rebrand to Serket taking place in 2025.

A Course Built Across Three Landscapes

What sets Serket apart from most Las Vegas area courses is its three part terrain design. Six holes play through steep desert canyons, six cross an elevated plateau with panoramic Las Vegas Strip views, and six wind through a natural desert wash. This variety creates a round that feels like three different courses in one, with each section presenting unique challenges and scenery. The greens feature X treme bentgrass, known for fast speeds and subtle breaks that reward precise putting.

Serket is open to the public as a semi private facility, meaning Seven Hills residents and visitors alike can book tee times. Clark County residents receive discounted local rates, and Caesars Rewards members qualify for tier based pricing. The club also offers an Executive Passholder Program, though it has been sold out with a waitlist in recent years.

The Butch Harmon School of Golf

Located on the Serket grounds, the Butch Harmon School of Golf is one of the most recognized instruction facilities in the world. Butch Harmon has coached Tiger Woods, Phil Mickelson, Ernie Els, Greg Norman, Adam Scott, and Dustin Johnson among many other professional golfers. Residents of Seven Hills have the unique advantage of accessing world class instruction minutes from their front door.

The Serket clubhouse features Janelas Restaurant and Bar, offering breakfast, lunch, and brunch with views overlooking the golf course. The facility has hosted notable events including the Wendy's 3 Tour Challenge (which raised over $50 million for the Dave Thomas Foundation) and various charity golf tournaments.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose considers Serket Golf Club one of Seven Hills' most significant lifestyle assets. For buyers who value golf course living, guard gated communities like Renaissance, Terracina, and Venezia offer homes directly bordering the fairways. Even non golfers benefit from the course's preserved green space and the dining and social opportunities the clubhouse provides. Golf Digest has ranked the course among the top 15 to 16 in Nevada, and Golfweek placed it at number six for best public access courses in the state.
