Commute Times from Seven Hills Henderson

What Is Seven Hills Henderson?

Seven Hills Henderson is approximately eight miles from Harry Reid International Airport (formerly McCarran International Airport), with a typical drive time of 15 to 20 minutes depending on traffic and which part of the community you are departing from. The most direct routes use Interstate 215 and Interstate 11 (formerly I 515) to reach the airport terminals.

Routes and Drive Times

The primary route from Seven Hills to Harry Reid International Airport follows State Route 146 from the community to the Interstate 11 corridor heading north toward the airport. During typical traffic conditions, this drive takes 15 to 20 minutes. Rush hour traffic along the I 215 and I 11 interchange can add five to ten minutes, but the freeway infrastructure in southern Henderson provides multiple route options to avoid congestion.

Seven Hills' position in the southern foothills of Henderson places it closer to the airport than many Henderson communities farther east, such as Anthem and Lake Las Vegas. The approximately eight mile distance makes Seven Hills practical for frequent travelers who need reliable airport access without the traffic density of living closer to the Strip.

Henderson Executive Airport

Seven Hills is also located near Henderson Executive Airport, a general aviation facility less than a mile from the community. Henderson Executive Airport serves private aircraft, charter flights, and corporate aviation, making it a convenient option for residents who use private air travel. The proximity of both a major commercial airport and a general aviation airport is an uncommon advantage that adds practical value for business travelers and frequent flyers.
