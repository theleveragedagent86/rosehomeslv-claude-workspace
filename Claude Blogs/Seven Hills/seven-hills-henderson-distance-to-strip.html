What Is Seven Hills Henderson?

Commute Times from Seven Hills Henderson

How Far Is Seven Hills Henderson from the Airport?

Seven Hills Henderson sits approximately 7 to 13 miles from the Las Vegas Strip depending on your specific destination. The south end of the Strip near Mandalay Bay is closest at roughly 7 miles, while the center Strip around Caesars Palace is closer to 13 miles. Drive times typically range from 15 to 22 minutes in normal traffic conditions.

Getting to the Strip from Seven Hills

The most common route from Seven Hills to the Strip runs west on St. Rose Parkway to Interstate 15 northbound. This route provides direct highway access and avoids most local surface street congestion. Alternatively, residents can take Eastern Avenue north to connect with I 215 (the Bruce Woodbury Beltway), which loops around to I 15 and offers a reliable alternative when St. Rose Parkway is busy.

For residents who work on the Strip or visit regularly, the commute is one of the shorter options among Henderson communities. Southeast Henderson traffic tends to be lighter and more predictable than what residents in Summerlin or northwest Las Vegas experience, particularly during morning and evening rush hours.

Distances to Other Key Destinations

Harry Reid International Airport is approximately 8 miles from Seven Hills, a drive of roughly 15 to 16 minutes via I 215. Henderson Executive Airport sits even closer at about 4 miles, making it convenient for residents who use private aviation. Downtown Henderson and the Water Street District are approximately 5 to 10 miles east depending on your starting point within Seven Hills. Downtown Las Vegas (Fremont Street) is roughly 20 to 22 miles, a 25 to 30 minute drive via I 11 or I 15.

Ryan Rose points out that Seven Hills' location gives residents a rare balance in the Las Vegas valley.
