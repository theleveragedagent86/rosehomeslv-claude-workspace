What Is Seven Hills Henderson?

Seven Hills Henderson Home Prices in 2026

Types of Homes Available in Seven Hills Henderson

Seven Hills Henderson offers approximately 3,000 homes across 25 distinct neighborhoods, with a current median sold price near $699,000 according to Redfin. The community includes everything from entry level condos in Avalon to multimillion dollar custom estates in guard gated Terracina and Venezia, making it one of the most diverse luxury communities in the Las Vegas valley.

What Buyers Can Expect on the Market

Homes in Seven Hills typically spend around 50 to 59 days on the market before going under contract. The Redfin Compete Score for Seven Hills sits at 37 out of 100, meaning the market is somewhat competitive but not as heated as peak pandemic years. Buyers have room to negotiate on many listings, with recent data showing roughly 64 percent of homes selling below asking price.

The average price per square foot in Seven Hills currently runs around $291 per Redfin, though this varies significantly by neighborhood. Production single family homes in non gated sections like Crescendo and Naples typically fall between $490,000 and $800,000. Semi custom homes in guard gated Renaissance range from $800,000 to $1,400,000. Custom estates in Terracina, Venezia, and Rapallo can reach well above $5 million.

Neighborhoods and Price Tiers

Condos in Avalon provide the most affordable entry point, with units ranging from the low $200,000s to around $440,000 for larger floor plans. Townhomes in The Terraces typically list between $300,000 and $500,000. For buyers seeking guard gated security without custom home pricing, Renaissance offers 217 semi custom homes built by Pardee with three sides bordering the Serket Golf Club (formerly Rio Secco).

At the top of the market, Terracina features approximately 460 custom homes on quarter acre to nearly one acre lots, with prices ranging from $1 million to over $8 million. The newest additions to Seven Hills are the Marquis estates by Blue Heron, a collection of 33 contemporary luxury homes situated on two Serket Golf Club fairways.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose notes that Seven Hills represents one of the best value propositions in Henderson's luxury market. The community's wide price range means first time buyers, move up buyers, and luxury seekers can all find a fit within the same master planned community. Because Seven Hills is largely built out with limited new construction, inventory tends to move steadily without the oversupply issues that newer communities sometimes face.
