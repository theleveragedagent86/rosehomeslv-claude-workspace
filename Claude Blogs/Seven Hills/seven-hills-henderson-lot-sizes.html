What Is Seven Hills Henderson?

Lot sizes in Seven Hills Henderson vary dramatically by neighborhood, ranging from attached condominium and townhome lots to custom estate parcels approaching one acre. Understanding the lot size differences across Seven Hills' 25 neighborhoods is essential for buyers who prioritize outdoor living space, privacy, and the ability to add features like pools, outdoor kitchens, and guest houses.

Lot Sizes by Neighborhood Tier

Production home neighborhoods like Crescendo, Naples, Palermo West, and Meridiana typically feature lots in the 5,000 to 8,000 square foot range, which is standard for Las Vegas valley suburban development. These lots accommodate the home footprint, a two car garage, front yard landscaping, and a backyard with room for a pool in many cases.

The auto gated neighborhoods of Palazzo Monte and Villa Villagio offer larger lots, with many parcels exceeding 8,000 square feet and hillside positions that provide more usable outdoor space. Guard gated custom communities feature the largest lots in Seven Hills. Terracina lots range from approximately a quarter acre to nearly one acre. Venezia lots typically fall between one third and one half acre. Rapallo offers lots up to nearly nine tenths of an acre. These generous parcel sizes allow for custom estates with resort style outdoor amenities, motor courts, and significant separation from neighboring properties.
