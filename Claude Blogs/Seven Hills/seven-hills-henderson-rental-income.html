HOA Rental Restrictions

Before purchasing a rental investment in Seven Hills, it is essential to verify the specific HOA rental policies for the target neighborhood. Guard gated communities generally have stricter rules regarding tenant screening, minimum lease terms, and the number of rental properties allowed at any given time. Some neighborhoods may require HOA approval of tenants, prohibit short term rentals entirely, or cap the percentage of homes that can be rented simultaneously. These restrictions protect property values for owner occupants but can limit flexibility for investors.
