Seven Hills Henderson falls within the Clark County School District and is primarily zoned for Elise L. Wolff Elementary School, Del E. Webb Middle School, and Coronado High School. This K through 12 pipeline places Seven Hills among the strongest public school zones in the Las Vegas valley, with all three schools rated above state averages in academic performance.

The Public School Pipeline

Wolff Elementary (pre K through grade 5) is located directly within Seven Hills at 1001 Seven Hills Drive and holds a 10 out of 10 GreatSchools rating. Del Webb Middle School (grades 6 through 8) at 2200 Reunion Drive holds a B plus from Niche and an 8 out of 10 from GreatSchools. Coronado High School (grades 9 through 12) at 1001 Coronado Center Drive holds an A minus from Niche with a 91 percent graduation rate and ranks number 17 among public high schools in Nevada.

The consistent quality across all three levels gives families confidence that their children will receive above average public education from kindergarten through high school graduation. The Wolff to Webb to Coronado pipeline keeps students within the same peer groups and community throughout their school years, building lasting friendships and a strong sense of neighborhood belonging.

Charter and Private School Options

Seven Hills families also have access to Coral Academy of Science Las Vegas, a tuition free charter school with a Sandy Ridge campus near the community that holds an A from Niche and a 10 out of 10 from GreatSchools. Henderson International School at 1165 Sandy Ridge Avenue is a private school serving pre K through grade 8 on a 14 acre campus with small class sizes. Calvary Chapel Green Valley Christian Academy at 2615 West Horizon Ridge Parkway offers a faith based K through 12 option nearby.
