Is Seven Hills Henderson Safe?

Seven Hills is a 1,300 acre master planned community in southwest Henderson, Nevada (ZIP 89052) featuring approximately 3,000 homes across 25 distinct neighborhoods, with a median sold price near $700,000 and five guard gated enclaves surrounding the Serket Golf Club (formerly Rio Secco). Built by American Nevada Corporation between 1995 and 2003, Seven Hills sits roughly seven miles from the Las Vegas Strip in one of the safest large cities in the United States.

Italian Inspired Design Across Rolling Foothills

Named after the Seven Hills of Rome, the community draws its design inspiration from Tuscan hill towns. The rolling terrain of the McCullough Mountain Range foothills creates natural elevation changes throughout the neighborhood, giving many homes panoramic views of the Las Vegas Strip, surrounding mountains, and the valley below. One third of the community's acreage (over 430 acres) is permanently preserved as open space, a ratio that sets Seven Hills apart from most Las Vegas area developments.

The 25 neighborhoods within Seven Hills offer something for nearly every buyer. Entry level condos in Avalon start in the low $200,000s, while custom estates in guard gated Terracina and Venezia reach $8 million and above. Production single family homes from builders like Pardee, Lennar, and Kimball Hill typically range from $490,000 to $800,000. The five guard gated communities (Terracina, Venezia, Rapallo, Renaissance, and Presidio) provide 24/7 staffed security for residents seeking additional privacy and exclusivity.

Schools, Safety, and Everyday Living

Families in Seven Hills benefit from some of Henderson's top rated schools. Elise L. Wolff Elementary, located within the community adjacent to Allegro Park, holds a 10 out of 10 rating on GreatSchools. Students feed into Del E. Webb Middle School (8 out of 10) and Coronado High School, which earned an A rating from Niche and ranks among the top 20 public high schools in Nevada. Henderson itself ranks as the number two safest large city in America according to SafeWise, and the 89052 ZIP code reports violent crime rates 54 percent below the national average.

Daily conveniences sit within minutes of the community. Trader Joe's, Costco, Smith's, and Albertsons are all within two miles. St. Rose Dominican Hospital (Siena Campus) is roughly a five minute drive. The community connects to 8.5 miles of internal walking and biking trails linking four major parks: Sonata, Vivaldi, Puccini, and Allegro. At the heart of Seven Hills, Serket Golf Club offers a championship par 72 course designed by Rees Jones, home to the world renowned Butch Harmon School of Golf.

Local Expert Insight

As a Las Vegas real estate specialist who works extensively in Henderson, Ryan Rose considers Seven Hills one of the valley's most complete communities for buyers at almost any price point. The combination of Henderson's safety record, top tier schools, a championship golf course, and that rare one third open space preservation makes it a community that holds its value exceptionally well. Henderson's 10 year appreciation rate of nearly 130 percent reflects the sustained demand for neighborhoods like Seven Hills.
