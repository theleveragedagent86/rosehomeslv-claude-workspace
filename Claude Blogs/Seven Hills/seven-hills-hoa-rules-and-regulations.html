Is Seven Hills a Gated Community?

What Is Seven Hills Henderson?

Seven Hills Henderson is governed by the Seven Hills Master Community Association along with individual sub HOAs for each of the 25 neighborhoods. All homeowners are bound by CC&Rs (covenants, conditions, and restrictions), bylaws, and articles of incorporation that establish the rules for maintaining the community's appearance, property values, and quality of life.

Architectural Review Process

One of the most significant HOA requirements in Seven Hills is the architectural review process. Homeowners must submit plans and receive approval before making exterior modifications to their property. This includes changes to paint colors, patio covers, doors, windows, landscaping, fencing, and any structural additions. The review process ensures that modifications align with the community's Italian and Mediterranean design guidelines, preserving the cohesive architectural character that defines Seven Hills' streetscape.

The architectural standards maintain a Tuscan and Mediterranean aesthetic throughout the community, with guidelines covering exterior materials, color palettes, roof styles, and overall design compatibility. Homeowners who want to modernize or update their homes work within these guidelines, though some flexibility exists particularly in the guard gated custom home communities where individual expression is more common.

Landscaping and Property Maintenance

Seven Hills enforces drought resistant landscaping requirements in line with Southern Nevada's water conservation mandates. Front yards must meet community maintenance standards, and homeowners are expected to keep visible portions of their property well groomed and free of clutter. In some higher fee communities, the HOA handles front yard landscaping maintenance directly as part of the monthly dues, removing the burden from individual homeowners.

Vehicle parking restrictions are enforced throughout Seven Hills, with limitations on street parking, commercial vehicle storage, and RV or boat storage in driveways. These rules help maintain clean sightlines and prevent neighborhood congestion, which is particularly valued in the guard gated sections where privacy and aesthetics are a priority.
