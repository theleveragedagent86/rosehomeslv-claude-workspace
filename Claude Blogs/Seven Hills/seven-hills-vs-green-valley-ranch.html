What Is Seven Hills Henderson?

Seven Hills vs Anthem Henderson

Seven Hills vs Southern Highlands Las Vegas

Seven Hills and Green Valley Ranch are both established master planned communities in Henderson, built around the same time on similar sized parcels of land. While they share a Henderson address and access to many of the same schools, their character, price points, and lifestyle offerings are distinctly different.

Community Scale and Layout

Seven Hills covers approximately 1,300 acres with around 3,000 homes across 25 neighborhoods, with more than one third of the community dedicated to open space. Green Valley Ranch spans approximately 1,310 acres but accommodates around 4,000 homes and condos across 31 neighborhoods, making it a denser community overall. Green Valley Ranch also dedicates significant acreage to commercial development including The District at Green Valley Ranch, a major open air shopping and dining destination that sits within the community itself.

Price Points and Home Types

Seven Hills commands higher prices overall, with a recent median in the mid $600,000s to $700,000s compared to Green Valley Ranch's median in the high $500,000s to low $600,000s. Seven Hills' five guard gated neighborhoods offer custom estate living from $800,000 to well over $6 million. Green Valley Ranch's luxury tier is anchored by The Estates at Green Valley Ranch, a guard gated enclave of 88 custom homes in the $1 million to $3 million range. Both communities include condos, townhomes, and production single family homes, but Seven Hills concentrates more inventory at higher price points.

Amenities and Convenience

Seven Hills is built around the Serket Golf Club (formerly Rio Secco), an 18 hole championship course with the Butch Harmon School of Golf, plus nine parks and an internal trail network. Green Valley Ranch's signature amenity is The District, which provides walkable access to national retailers, restaurants, and entertainment without leaving the community. Green Valley Ranch also features multiple parks with splash pads, dog parks, and sports courts, plus the Green Valley Ranch Resort and Spa. Seven Hills is approximately seven miles from the Strip, while Green Valley Ranch is about 11 miles away.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose finds that buyers choosing between Seven Hills and Green Valley Ranch are often weighing two different lifestyle priorities. Seven Hills appeals to those who value a more exclusive, golf oriented community with elevated terrain and Strip views. Green Valley Ranch appeals to buyers who want integrated shopping and dining within walking distance, broader price point options, and a more connected suburban experience. Both communities offer excellent Henderson schools and quality of life, and Ryan helps buyers match their priorities to the right community.
