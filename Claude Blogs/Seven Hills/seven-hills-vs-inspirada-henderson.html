Seven Hills vs Anthem Henderson

Seven Hills vs Green Valley Ranch Henderson

Seven Hills and Inspirada are both master planned communities in Henderson, but they represent different generations of development and serve different buyer profiles. Seven Hills is an established 1,300 acre community with approximately 3,000 homes built primarily in the late 1990s and 2000s, while Inspirada is a newer, larger development spanning approximately 1,940 acres with a planned build out of 11,500 homes that has been under active construction since 2004.

Price Points and Home Types

Seven Hills' median home price sits in the mid $600,000s to $700,000s, with options ranging from condominiums around $250,000 to custom guard gated estates exceeding $6 million. Inspirada offers more accessible new construction pricing, with Toll Brothers townhomes starting in the $460,000 to $530,000 range and single family homes available from various builders at competitive price points. Inspirada's newer construction appeals to buyers who want modern floor plans and builder warranties, while Seven Hills' established neighborhoods offer mature landscaping, proven resale values, and a wider range of luxury options.

Community Character and Amenities

Seven Hills was designed with an Italian and Tuscan aesthetic and centers around the Serket Golf Club and Butch Harmon School of Golf, with five guard gated neighborhoods and more than one third of the community dedicated to open space. Inspirada follows a New Urbanism design philosophy emphasizing walkability, community connectivity, and active park spaces, with over 200 acres of parks, walking trails, and open space. Seven Hills feels like an established luxury enclave, while Inspirada has the energy and modern finishes of a community still in its growth phase.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose advises buyers that the choice between Seven Hills and Inspirada often comes down to whether they prioritize established luxury and character or new construction and affordability. Seven Hills delivers a mature community with proven property values, guard gated options, and a championship golf course. Inspirada offers brand new homes with modern floor plans in a growing community that is still developing its full amenity package. Both communities benefit from Henderson's excellent schools, safety ratings, and quality of life, and Ryan helps buyers determine which experience best matches their priorities.
