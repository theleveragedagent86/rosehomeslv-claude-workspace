What Is Seven Hills Henderson?

Seven Hills vs Anthem Henderson

Guard Gated Communities in Seven Hills Henderson

Seven Hills and MacDonald Highlands are two of Henderson's most prominent luxury communities, both located in the southern foothills with mountain and Strip views. However, they serve different segments of the luxury market, with Seven Hills offering a broader range of price points and MacDonald Highlands positioning itself firmly in the ultra luxury tier.

Size, Scale, and Price

Seven Hills covers approximately 1,300 acres with around 3,000 homes across 25 neighborhoods, ranging from condominiums starting around $250,000 to custom estates exceeding $6 million. MacDonald Highlands spans approximately 1,200 acres but with significantly fewer homes, estimated at 250 to 350 residences. Median home prices in MacDonald Highlands recently reached the $2.9 million to $3.9 million range, compared to Seven Hills' median in the mid $600,000s to $700,000s. MacDonald Highlands is designed as an exclusive mountaintop enclave, while Seven Hills functions as a full service master planned community with options at every price level.

Golf and Lifestyle

Seven Hills is centered around the Serket Golf Club (formerly Rio Secco), an 18 hole par 72 championship course designed by Rees Jones that is open to the public and home to the Butch Harmon School of Golf. MacDonald Highlands features DragonRidge Country Club, an 18 hole championship course designed by Jay Morrish and David Druzisky that operates as a private members only club with a full service fitness center, tennis courts, and multiple dining venues. Seven Hills is approximately seven miles from the Las Vegas Strip, while MacDonald Highlands sits about 15 miles away at a higher elevation along the Black Mountain and McCullough Mountain foothills.

Architecture and Character

Seven Hills' architectural style is predominantly Italian and Mediterranean with Tuscan design influences throughout the community. MacDonald Highlands is known for its desert elegance aesthetic, incorporating Frank Lloyd Wright inspired designs, contemporary desert modern styles, and natural materials that blend with the surrounding landscape. Seven Hills' 25 neighborhoods create a village like atmosphere with parks, trails, and community events. MacDonald Highlands emphasizes privacy, exclusivity, and panoramic views from its elevated mountain setting.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose advises buyers that the choice between Seven Hills and MacDonald Highlands typically comes down to budget and lifestyle priorities. Buyers seeking guard gated custom estate living with Strip views can find comparable quality in Seven Hills' Terracina, Venezia, and Rapallo neighborhoods at a fraction of MacDonald Highlands pricing. MacDonald Highlands appeals to ultra luxury buyers who prioritize maximum exclusivity, a private golf club membership, and the prestige of one of Henderson's most elite addresses. Both communities deliver exceptional quality of life, and Ryan helps buyers determine which experience best aligns with their goals.
