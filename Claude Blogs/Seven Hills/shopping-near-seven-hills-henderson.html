What Is Seven Hills Henderson?

Restaurants Near Seven Hills Henderson

Grocery Stores Near Seven Hills Henderson

Seven Hills Henderson is a primarily residential community by design, but residents have convenient access to several shopping centers within one to two miles. From the neighborhood strip center at the community entrance to a Costco anchored plaza and a Trader Joe's anchored center on Eastern Avenue, daily errands require only a short drive. Larger retail destinations including an open air lifestyle center and Henderson's largest indoor mall are within 10 to 15 minutes.

Shopping Within and Adjacent to Seven Hills

The Commons at Seven Hills sits at 835 Seven Hills Drive right at the community entrance, offering a small neighborhood strip center with a 7 Eleven, Anytime Fitness, and Seven Hills Pet Hospital. For more substantial shopping, Eastern Commons at 10345 to 10575 South Eastern Avenue is anchored by Trader Joe's and Seafood City Supermarket, with additional retailers including At Home. St. Rose Square at 3543 St. Rose Parkway features a Costco along with an In N Out Burger and other convenience retailers.

Horizon Marketplace, anchored by Smith's Food and Drug, is located on Eastern Avenue and provides full grocery shopping plus pharmacy and everyday services. All of these shopping centers sit within approximately one to two miles of Seven Hills' entry points, making routine errands quick and accessible despite the community's residential focus.

Larger Shopping Destinations Nearby

The District at Green Valley Ranch at 2240 Village Walk Drive is approximately 10 minutes from Seven Hills and offers the closest upscale shopping experience. This open air Mediterranean style lifestyle center features over 40 retailers including Anthropologie, Pottery Barn, Williams Sonoma, Sephora, Lululemon, and REI, along with more than 20 restaurants. The District also hosts a Thursday farmers market and a vintage carousel.

Galleria at Sunset at 1300 West Sunset Road is Henderson's largest indoor shopping mall, approximately 12 to 15 minutes from Seven Hills. The mall is anchored by Macy's, JCPenney, Dillard's, Kohl's, and Dick's Sporting Goods, with over 100 additional specialty stores and a food court. For buyers relocating from other cities, Galleria at Sunset provides the familiar indoor mall experience with climate controlled shopping during Las Vegas summer months.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose notes that Seven Hills' shopping accessibility is better than many buyers expect for a community that feels this secluded. Having Trader Joe's, Costco, and Smith's all within two miles handles most weekly shopping, and The District at Green Valley Ranch covers the lifestyle and specialty retail side. The intentional absence of commercial development within Seven Hills keeps the community quiet and residential, while the Eastern Avenue and St. Rose Parkway corridors put retail within easy reach.
