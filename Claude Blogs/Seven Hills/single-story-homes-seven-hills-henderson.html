Is Seven Hills Henderson Good for Retirees?

Seven Hills Henderson Homes for Sale

Single story homes are available throughout Seven Hills Henderson across multiple neighborhoods and price ranges. From production homes in non gated neighborhoods to custom estates in the guard gated enclaves, buyers who prefer single level living can find options that match their budget and lifestyle without compromising on the Seven Hills amenity package.

Single Story Options by Neighborhood

Renaissance, the guard gated community built by Pardee Homes, includes 80 single story homes among its 217 total residences, with sizes ranging from approximately 2,800 to over 3,500 square feet. This makes Renaissance one of the best sources of single story guard gated living in Seven Hills. Production neighborhoods including Crescendo, Naples, and Palermo West offer single story floor plans in the 1,400 to 2,200 square foot range at prices generally between $490,000 and $750,000.

The custom estate neighborhoods of Terracina, Venezia, Rapallo, and Presidio include single story and split level designs among their custom builds, though these properties command premium prices starting above $1 million. For buyers who want single level living without the stairs of a two story home but with the square footage and finishes of a luxury property, the guard gated custom enclaves offer custom single story designs that are hard to find in production home communities.

Why Single Story Homes Are in Demand

Single story homes in Seven Hills appeal to retirees, buyers with mobility considerations, families with young children, and anyone who prefers the convenience of living on one level. The Las Vegas market consistently shows strong demand for single story floor plans, and these homes tend to hold their value well due to their broader appeal across multiple buyer demographics. In Seven Hills specifically, single story homes represent a smaller percentage of total inventory than two story designs, which can create competitive situations when well priced listings come to market.
