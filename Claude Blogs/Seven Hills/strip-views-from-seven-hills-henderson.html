What Is Seven Hills Henderson?

Guard Gated Communities in Seven Hills Henderson

Many homes in Seven Hills Henderson offer direct views of the Las Vegas Strip skyline, one of the community's most distinctive selling points. The elevated terrain in the southern Henderson foothills positions homes at various heights above the valley floor, and properties on higher elevations with north facing orientations can see the iconic Strip corridor illuminated against the desert sky, particularly dramatic at dusk and after dark.

Where the Best Views Are

Strip views in Seven Hills are most prominent in the guard gated neighborhoods that occupy the community's highest elevations. Rapallo, Presidio, and portions of Terracina and Venezia offer some of the most expansive view corridors due to their elevated hillside positions. The Marquis by Blue Heron development, situated between two Serket Golf Club fairways, provides Strip views framed by the contemporary floor to ceiling windows that define Blue Heron's architectural style. Select homes in the auto gated neighborhoods of Palazzo Monte and Villa Villagio also capture Strip views from upper levels or elevated lot positions.

View Premiums and Considerations

Homes with unobstructed Strip views in Seven Hills command meaningful price premiums over comparable homes without view exposure. The exact premium varies by neighborhood and the quality of the view, but buyers should expect to pay 10 to 20 percent or more above comparable non view properties for confirmed Strip sightlines. Mountain views to the south and west and golf course views of the Serket Golf Club also carry premiums, and some properties combine multiple view corridors for a panoramic effect.

Buyers should verify views in person at different times of day, as lighting conditions and atmospheric clarity affect the quality of the view. Evening tours provide the best sense of what the Strip view looks like when the lights are on, which is when the view has its greatest visual impact.
