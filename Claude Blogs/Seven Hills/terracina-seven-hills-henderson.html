Guard Gated Communities in Seven Hills Henderson

Terracina is the most prestigious neighborhood in Seven Hills Henderson, a 24/7 guard gated enclave of custom luxury estates on lots ranging from a quarter acre to nearly one acre. Homes in Terracina feature individually designed floor plans with up to 15,000 square feet of living space, and the neighborhood offers some of the most dramatic Las Vegas Strip, golf course, and mountain views available in residential Henderson.

Custom Estates on Expansive Lots

Terracina's custom homes began construction around 2000 and continued through the late 2000s, with individual homeowners selecting their own architects and builders. Floor plans typically include three to seven or more bedrooms, multi car garages, and resort style amenities such as guest houses, home theaters, wine cellars, and infinity edge pools. The Italian and Mediterranean architectural style dominates the streetscape, though some homeowners have introduced contemporary and desert modern designs through renovations or new builds on remaining lots.

The neighborhood shares a connected guard gated entry with Venezia, though each community maintains its own distinct identity. Terracina's elevated terrain provides premium lot positions with panoramic views of the Las Vegas Strip to the north and the surrounding mountain ranges. Many homes sit directly along the Serket Golf Club (formerly Rio Secco), adding golf course frontage to the view package.

Pricing and Market Position

Homes in Terracina generally start above $1 million, with many properties listing between $1.5 million and $5.5 million. Select estates at the top of the market can exceed these ranges depending on lot size, view orientation, and custom finishes. Terracina competes in the same luxury tier as MacDonald Highlands and Anthem Country Club, but within the more intimate scale of Seven Hills' 1,300 acre community.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose considers Terracina one of Henderson's best kept luxury secrets. While MacDonald Highlands draws more attention in the ultra luxury conversation, Terracina offers comparable privacy, lot sizes, and Strip views at a lower price point. The connection to Serket Golf Club and the Butch Harmon School of Golf adds a lifestyle amenity that is difficult to match. For buyers seeking custom estate living with guard gated security in a top rated Henderson school zone, Terracina deserves serious consideration.
