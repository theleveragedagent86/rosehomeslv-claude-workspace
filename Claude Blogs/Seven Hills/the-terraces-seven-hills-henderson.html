What Is Seven Hills Henderson?

The Terraces is the townhome neighborhood within Seven Hills Henderson, built in 1997 as one of the earliest residential communities in the development. Offering two to four bedroom townhomes ranging from approximately 990 to over 2,000 square feet, The Terraces provides a lower maintenance alternative to single family homes while giving residents full access to Seven Hills' parks, trails, and community amenities.

Townhome Layout and Features

The Terraces features a mix of one story and two story townhome floor plans with two car garages. Depending on the plan, homes may include features such as vaulted ceilings, lofts, split floor plans, fireplaces, and downstairs master bedrooms. The neighborhood's streets are wider than typical townhome communities, providing a more spacious and open feel than many attached home developments in the Henderson area.

The Terraces is the only neighborhood in Seven Hills with its own private community swimming pool, an amenity that is separate from the broader Seven Hills park system. This gives Terraces residents a dedicated recreation space steps from their front door. The neighborhood is gated with controlled access, adding a layer of security and privacy to the townhome living experience.

Pricing and Value

Townhomes in The Terraces generally fall in the $300,000 to $500,000 range, positioning them between Avalon's condominiums and Seven Hills' production single family homes in terms of affordability. For buyers who want a Seven Hills address with lower maintenance responsibilities and a more accessible price point than detached homes, The Terraces offers a practical balance of value and lifestyle.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose points out that The Terraces is often overlooked by buyers focused on Seven Hills' single family and luxury inventory. However, the neighborhood's combination of townhome convenience, a private pool, gated access, and the Seven Hills community amenity package makes it one of the best values in the community. As one of the earliest neighborhoods built in Seven Hills, The Terraces also benefits from mature landscaping and an established neighborhood feel that newer developments cannot replicate.
