Seven Hills Henderson Home Prices in 2026

Terracina Seven Hills Henderson

Seven Hills Henderson offers one of the widest ranges of home types in the Las Vegas valley, from affordable condos under $250,000 to custom luxury estates exceeding $8 million. Across 25 distinct neighborhoods, buyers can choose from condos, townhomes, production single family homes, semi custom homes, and fully custom estates, all within a single 1,300 acre master planned community.

Condos and Townhomes

Avalon at Seven Hills is the community's primary condo neighborhood, built in 1999 with one, two, and three bedroom floor plans ranging from approximately 810 to 1,550 square feet. Avalon includes a fitness center, two pools, a spa, and a clubhouse. Horizons at Seven Hills offers another condo option with units starting around 660 square feet. Condo prices in Seven Hills generally range from the low $200,000s to around $440,000. The Terraces provides townhome living with two to four bedroom floor plans built in 1997, typically listing between $300,000 and $500,000.

Single Family Production and Semi Custom Homes

The majority of Seven Hills homes fall into the production single family category, built by nationally recognized builders including Pardee Homes, Lennar, Kimball Hill Homes, Presley Homes, and Astoria Homes. These neighborhoods include Crescendo (330 homes, with over half featuring pools), Naples, Palermo West, and Meridiana, typically offering one and two story floor plans in the $490,000 to $800,000 range.

Renaissance stands as Seven Hills' semi custom option: 217 homes built by Pardee Homes within a 24/7 guard gated enclave. Floor plans range from approximately 2,800 to 4,600 square feet across 80 single story and 137 two story homes. Three sides of Renaissance border the Serket Golf Club, and prices generally fall between $800,000 and $1,400,000.

Custom Luxury Estates

The guard gated enclaves of Terracina, Venezia, Rapallo, and Presidio contain Seven Hills' custom estate homes, with floor plans reaching up to 15,000 square feet on lots up to nearly one acre. These homes feature individually designed floor plans, three to seven bedrooms, multi car garages, and amenities such as guest houses, theater rooms, wine cellars, and resort style pools. The newest addition is Marquis Seven Hills by Blue Heron, a collection of 33 contemporary luxury estates on two Serket Golf Club fairways featuring open floor plans, glass walls, and rooftop terraces.

Architectural Styles

The dominant architectural style throughout Seven Hills is Tuscan and Italian Renaissance, reflecting the community's design inspiration from Italy's hill towns. Mediterranean and Spanish styles are also common, with stucco walls, terracotta tile roofs, and arched doorways defining much of the streetscape. Contemporary and desert modern architecture is growing, particularly in Marquis by Blue Heron and in Terracina where homeowners are renovating or rebuilding with updated designs.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose points out that Seven Hills' range of home types means buyers at very different life stages and price points can all find a fit. A first time buyer in an Avalon condo, a growing family in Crescendo, and a luxury buyer in Terracina all share the same parks, trails, schools, and community infrastructure. That kind of diversity within one master planned community is genuinely uncommon in Henderson.
