What Is Seven Hills Henderson?

Guard Gated Communities in Seven Hills Henderson

Venezia is one of the most exclusive neighborhoods in Seven Hills Henderson, a 24/7 guard gated enclave of approximately 70 custom homes on one third to one half acre lots. Homes in Venezia range from 3,500 to 8,500 square feet with three to five bedrooms and three to five car garages, built between 2001 and 2007. The community shares a connected entry with neighboring Terracina but maintains its own separate guarded gate.

Custom Estate Living

Venezia's homes were individually designed and built by custom builders, giving each residence a unique floor plan and character. The architectural style is predominantly Italian and Mediterranean, consistent with Seven Hills' Tuscan design inspiration. Lots in Venezia are among the largest in Seven Hills, with the half acre parcels providing substantial privacy and outdoor space for resort style pools, landscaping, and entertaining areas.

The neighborhood's elevated position within Seven Hills provides many homes with panoramic views of the Las Vegas Strip, the Serket Golf Club fairways, and the surrounding mountain ranges. The connection to Terracina's guard gate creates a shared luxury enclave with combined security coverage, while each community retains its own distinct neighborhood identity and HOA.

Pricing and Availability

Venezia is one of Seven Hills' highest priced neighborhoods. Recent listings have appeared in the $4 million to $6 million range, reflecting the custom build quality, lot sizes, and view premiums. With only about 70 homes total, inventory turnover is limited, and properties in Venezia tend to attract serious luxury buyers who value privacy, space, and proximity to the Serket Golf Club and Butch Harmon School of Golf.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose recommends Venezia to buyers who want guard gated custom estate living on generous lots without the ultra premium pricing of MacDonald Highlands. The combination of half acre lots, custom architecture, and shared guard gated access with Terracina creates a residential experience that rivals the valley's most prestigious enclaves. Because only about 70 homes exist in Venezia, each listing is genuinely rare, and Ryan advises interested buyers to be prepared to move quickly when properties come to market.
