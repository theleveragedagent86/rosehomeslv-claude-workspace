What Is Seven Hills Henderson?

Villa Villagio is a gated community within Seven Hills Henderson featuring homes ranging from approximately 2,250 to 4,400 square feet. Built between 1997 and 2003, Villa Villagio is one of the earlier neighborhoods established in Seven Hills and uses an auto gated entry system for controlled residential access. Homes typically include three to five bedrooms with features such as island kitchens, walk in pantries, and private swimming pools.

Home Features and Design

Villa Villagio's homes reflect the Italian and Mediterranean architectural style that defines Seven Hills, with stucco exteriors, tile roofs, and design elements inspired by Tuscany. Many homes have been updated over the years with modern kitchens, renovated bathrooms, and upgraded outdoor living spaces. The neighborhood's 1997 construction start makes it one of the oldest in Seven Hills, which means buyers benefit from mature streetscapes and established landscaping that gives Villa Villagio a settled, residential feel.

A notable percentage of homes in Villa Villagio feature private swimming pools, reflecting the lifestyle expectations of buyers in this price range and the Las Vegas climate that supports year round outdoor living. The community's floor plans include single story and two story options, giving buyers flexibility based on their preference for single level living or additional square footage on multiple floors.

Pricing and Market Position

Villa Villagio homes generally trade in the $600,000 to $1,200,000 range, similar to neighboring Palazzo Monte. This positions Villa Villagio as an upper tier gated community that offers more space and privacy than the production home neighborhoods while remaining more accessible than the guard gated custom enclaves. The auto gated entry provides controlled access without the higher HOA costs associated with 24/7 guard staffing.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose considers Villa Villagio one of Seven Hills' best kept secrets. The neighborhood's combination of generous square footage, gated access, mature landscaping, and a central location within the community makes it an excellent value for buyers who want more than a production home but are not shopping in the guard gated price tier. For buyers relocating from outside Nevada, Villa Villagio's established character and pool friendly lifestyle often stand out as exactly the kind of residential experience they are looking for.
