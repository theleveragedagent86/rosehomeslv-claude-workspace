Walking Trails in Seven Hills Henderson

Vivaldi Park is one of the four major parks in Seven Hills Henderson, covering approximately seven acres at 1249 Seven Hills Drive. Open daily from 6 AM to midnight, Vivaldi Park is known for its scenic views, baseball diamond, and connection to the broader Seven Hills trail system that links all four community parks.

Amenities and Recreation

Vivaldi Park features a baseball diamond, a playground with swings and equipment suitable for younger children, picnic tables, picnic shelters available for reservation, restrooms, and open grassy fields. Paved walking paths wind through the park and connect to the Seven Hills Trail, which links Vivaldi to Sonata Park, Allegro Park, and Puccini Park via a network of wide concrete paths. The trail passes under Seven Hills Drive through flood control structures, allowing residents to walk between parks without crossing vehicle traffic.

The park's elevated position within Seven Hills provides some of the community's best views. From Vivaldi Park, residents can see Mount Charleston, Potosi Mountain, the Las Vegas Strip, and Henderson Executive Airport. These panoramic sightlines make Vivaldi a popular spot for evening walks, especially during cooler months when the views are at their clearest.

Trailhead Access

Vivaldi Park serves as a trailhead for the Seven Hills Trail, a paved loop that connects the community's four major parks. The trail is four to six feet wide with benches and picnic tables placed along the route, making it accessible for walkers, joggers, and families with strollers. The connection between Vivaldi and Sonata Park is one of the most popular segments, giving residents a scenic walking route between the two largest recreational areas in Seven Hills.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose considers Vivaldi Park one of Seven Hills' hidden gems. While Sonata Park draws the most attention as the largest park in the community, Vivaldi's combination of views, trail access, and a more relaxed atmosphere makes it a favorite among longtime residents. Families with young children appreciate the playground and open fields, while walkers and joggers use the trailhead as a starting point for loops through the entire Seven Hills park system. For buyers who prioritize outdoor recreation and scenic views, proximity to Vivaldi Park adds real quality of life value.
