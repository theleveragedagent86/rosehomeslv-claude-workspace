Is Seven Hills Henderson a Good Place to Live?

Elise L. Wolff Elementary School is the primary elementary school serving Seven Hills Henderson, located directly within the community at 1001 Seven Hills Drive. Wolff Elementary holds a 10 out of 10 rating from GreatSchools and an A minus from Niche, with 67 percent math proficiency and 73 percent reading proficiency, placing it in the top five percent of Nevada elementary schools for overall test scores.

Academics and Programs

Wolff Elementary serves approximately 750 students in pre kindergarten through grade five with a student to teacher ratio of 18 to 1. The school ranks number 10 among all Nevada elementary schools on Niche, reflecting consistently strong academic performance across grade levels. Students have access to laptop computers for both in class and at home use, coding programs, and online resources for math and reading support.

The school offers a Gifted and Talented program for qualifying students and operates the Safekey before and after school program, which provides supervised recreation based activities including arts, crafts, and group activities. These programs give working parents reliable childcare options while maintaining an educational environment outside regular school hours.

Location Within Seven Hills

Wolff Elementary sits adjacent to Allegro Park at the heart of Seven Hills, giving students direct access to the park's soccer fields, playground, and trail connections during school activities and after school recreation. The school's location within the community means many Seven Hills families can walk or bike to school, reducing morning commute stress and strengthening the neighborhood's family oriented character.

Local Expert Insight

Ryan Rose identifies Wolff Elementary as one of the primary reasons families choose Seven Hills over other Henderson communities. A 10 out of 10 GreatSchools rating within walking distance of home is a powerful draw for buyers with young children. The school's academic performance, combined with its location inside the community adjacent to Allegro Park and the trail system, creates a family lifestyle that is difficult to match elsewhere in the Las Vegas valley.
