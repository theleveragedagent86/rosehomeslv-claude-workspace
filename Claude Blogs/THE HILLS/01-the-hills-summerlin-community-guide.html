The Hills is one of Summerlin's original village communities, a 1,119 acre residential area in the 89134 zip code that combines established neighborhoods with guard gated luxury enclaves. Built between 1990 and 2000, The Hills includes 19 distinct neighborhoods and remains one of the most sought after addresses in the Las Vegas Valley. ## What Makes The Hills Summerlin Unique? The Hills holds a special place in Summerlin's history as the master planned community's first residential village. The first families moved in during March 1991, and Lummis Elementary opened in 1993 as Summerlin's very first school. The original village sits on 394 acres and features 11 neighborhoods including Belaire Estates, Cherry Creek, Copper Ridge, and Panorama Pointe. Homes in the original village range from the $300,000s to the $700,000s, with sizes spanning roughly 1,400 to 3,000 square feet. The Hills South adds another 725 acres of luxury living, featuring four guard gated communities: Eagle Hills, Tournament Hills, Country Club Hills, and Corta Bella. These enclaves offer semi custom and custom estates priced from the upper $800,000s to over $10,000,000. Home sizes in The Hills South reach up to 20,000 square feet on lots as large as two thirds of an acre. ## What Amenities Are Near The Hills Summerlin? Residents enjoy direct access to The Hills Park, a 7 acre green space perfect for families and outdoor recreation. The Summerlin Library and Performing Arts Center sits within the community, along with the Nevada Ballet Theatre. TPC Summerlin, home to the PGA Tour's Shriners Open, borders The Hills South and offers world class golf. The Trails Village Center provides everyday shopping and dining within minutes. Summerlin's 200 plus mile trail system connects The Hills to Red Rock Canyon and dozens of parks throughout the master plan. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is roughly a 5 minute drive, and the Las Vegas Strip is just 20 to 25 minutes away. ## Ryan Rose's Take on The Hills As a Las Vegas real estate expert, Ryan Rose considers The Hills one of the best values in Summerlin for buyers who want an established community with mature landscaping, proven neighborhood stability, and proximity to top amenities.
