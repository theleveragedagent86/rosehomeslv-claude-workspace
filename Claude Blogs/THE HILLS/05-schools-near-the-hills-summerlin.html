Families in The Hills have access to a strong mix of public, charter, and private schools within a short drive. ## What Public Schools Serve The Hills Summerlin? Lummis Elementary is the zoned public school for much of The Hills and holds special significance as the first school ever built in Summerlin, opening in 1993. It serves Pre K through 5th grade, enrolls approximately 510 students, and maintains a 19 to 1 student to teacher ratio. Its 9 out of 10 GreatSchools rating makes it one of the top elementary schools in the Las Vegas Valley. Becker Middle School serves grades 6 through 8 and opened the same year as Lummis. It offers a Gifted and Talented program and carries a 6 out of 10 GreatSchools rating. Palo Verde High School rounds out the public school pathway with an 8 out of 10 rating, a 96% graduation rate, and an average SAT score of 1240. Palo Verde is consistently recognized as one of the stronger public high schools in Clark County. ## What Private and Charter Schools Are Nearby? Discovery Charter School offers a public charter option for grades K through 8, with approximately 400 students and a Niche grade of B plus. It provides an alternative to the traditional public school track without tuition costs. Families seeking a private education have two premier options nearby. The Meadows School is the top ranked private school in the area, serving Pre K through 12th grade with 24 Advanced Placement courses and tuition of approximately $33,180 per year. Adelson Educational Campus occupies a $65 million facility and offers Pre K through 12th grade education at approximately $19,000 per year.
