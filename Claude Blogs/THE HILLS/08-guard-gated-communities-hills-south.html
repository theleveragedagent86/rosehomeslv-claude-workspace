The Hills South in Summerlin contains four guard gated communities, each offering 24 hour staffed security, custom homes, and proximity to TPC Summerlin golf course. These enclaves represent some of the most exclusive residential addresses in Las Vegas, with homes ranging from the upper $800,000s to well over $10 million. ## What Are the Four Guard Gated Communities? Eagle Hills is the largest of the four with 160 custom homes built between 1995 and 2000. Lots range from one third to two thirds of an acre, and homes span 2,924 to over 14,000 square feet. The community includes a private three acre park and maintains a 24 hour staffed guard gate. Tournament Hills features approximately 132 custom homes with monthly sub HOA fees of around $480 plus the $47 Summerlin masterplan fee. The community sits adjacent to TPC Summerlin, giving many residents direct golf course views. Security includes 24 hour guard staffing and roving patrols. Country Club Hills contains roughly 175 homes and is home to "The Enclave," also known as Billionaire's Row. This exclusive section holds seven of the largest and most expensive residences in Las Vegas. The community wraps around TPC Summerlin and offers some of the most dramatic course frontage in the valley. Corta Bella rounds out the four with 126 homes originally constructed by Coleman Homes (now Toll Brothers). The community provides 24 hour guard gate access and a more uniform architectural style compared to the fully custom enclaves, making it an attractive entry point into guard gated Hills South living. ## How Do These Communities Compare? Eagle Hills and Country Club Hills command the highest prices, with estates regularly exceeding $3 million and trophy properties surpassing $10 million. Tournament Hills offers similar custom home quality at a slightly lower price tier. Corta Bella provides the most accessible guard gated option, with production style luxury homes that appeal to buyers seeking security without the custom home price tag. All four communities share key advantages: 24 hour staffed guard gates, controlled visitor access, security patrols, and locations within minutes of Summerlin Hospital, Downtown Summerlin, and Red Rock Canyon. The Las Vegas Strip is 20 to 25 minutes away, and Harry Reid International Airport is approximately 24 minutes. ## Choosing the Right Guard Gated Community Ryan Rose recommends that buyers start by defining their priorities. Families who want the largest lots and a community park gravitate toward Eagle Hills. Golf enthusiasts favor Tournament Hills or Country Club Hills for course proximity. Buyers seeking guard gated security at a lower entry price often find Corta Bella to be the ideal match. Each community has a distinct character, and touring all four reveals differences that listings alone cannot convey.
