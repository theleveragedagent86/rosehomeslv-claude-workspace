Eagle Hills is a prestigious guard-gated community of 160 custom homes nestled within The Hills South village of Summerlin. With spacious lots ranging from one third to two thirds of an acre, this enclave delivers privacy, luxury, and resort style living just minutes from Downtown Summerlin. ## What Makes Eagle Hills One of Summerlin's Most Desirable Guard-Gated Communities? Eagle Hills stands apart for its generous lot sizes and architectural diversity. Homes here range from approximately 2,924 square feet to over 14,000 square feet, offering everything from elegant single story residences to sprawling custom estates. Architectural styles span contemporary, classic, and Mediterranean designs, giving the streetscape a distinctive character you won't find in typical production home neighborhoods. Built between 1995 and 2000, these residences were crafted by respected custom builders who prioritized quality materials and thoughtful floor plans. Many homes feature soaring ceilings, expansive great rooms, resort style pools, and outdoor living spaces designed for the desert climate. The tree lined streets and lush landscaping create a surprisingly green, serene environment. ## What Amenities Does Eagle Hills Offer Residents? The community's crown jewel is a three acre grassy park that serves as a private gathering space for residents. This park includes a walking trail that winds through mature landscaping, two tennis courts, a full basketball court, a children's playground, and shaded pavilions perfect for weekend gatherings. Greenbelt paths connect different sections of the neighborhood, encouraging walking and cycling in a safe, quiet setting. The 24 hour staffed guard gate ensures controlled access around the clock, giving families and retirees alike peace of mind. Eagle Hills residents also enjoy full access to Summerlin's extensive trail network, community centers, and nearby shopping at Downtown Summerlin and Tivoli Village. ## How Do Eagle Hills Homes Compare in Size and Price? The range of home sizes in Eagle Hills is remarkable. Entry level homes around 3,000 square feet appeal to buyers seeking guard gated security without the maintenance burden of an oversized estate. At the top end, properties exceeding 10,000 square feet rival the most exclusive estates anywhere in Las Vegas.
