Country Club Hills is a guard-gated community of approximately 175 luxury homes within The Hills South village of Summerlin, built by the renowned Christopher Homes. Adjacent to TPC Summerlin, this enclave is home to some of the most impressive estates in the entire Las Vegas Valley. ## What Makes Country Club Hills a Premier Summerlin Community? Christopher Homes established a reputation for building Las Vegas's finest luxury residences, and Country Club Hills stands as one of their signature achievements. The community's roughly 175 homes feature exceptional craftsmanship, premium materials, and floor plans designed for both grand entertaining and comfortable daily living. Many properties offer direct views of TPC Summerlin's fairways and the surrounding Spring Mountains. The 24 hour staffed guard gate provides controlled access to the community, ensuring a level of privacy that attracts high profile residents. The streets are quiet, meticulously maintained, and lined with mature desert landscaping that has had decades to fill in. Country Club Hills feels established in a way that newer luxury communities simply cannot replicate. ## Who Lives in Country Club Hills? Country Club Hills has long attracted business leaders, executives, and notable figures seeking both luxury and discretion. The community's most famous section, known as "The Enclave" or "Billionaire's Row," contains seven of the largest and most expensive homes in Las Vegas. Multiple billionaires have called this section home, cementing Country Club Hills' status at the very top of the Las Vegas luxury market. Beyond The Enclave, the broader community offers a range of custom estate homes that appeal to affluent buyers who value quality construction, generous lot sizes, and proximity to world class golf. The neighborhood fosters a close knit atmosphere despite its exclusivity, with residents who take pride in their community. ## How Does the TPC Summerlin Location Add Value? Living adjacent to TPC Summerlin means more than convenient tee times. The course serves as a permanent green buffer, protecting views and ensuring the surrounding land will never be developed. Properties backing onto the course benefit from expanded sightlines, natural beauty, and the prestige associated with one of Nevada's most celebrated golf venues. ## A Local Perspective on Country Club Hills Ryan Rose considers Country Club Hills one of the most significant luxury communities in all of Southern Nevada.
