Corta Bella is a guard-gated community of 126 luxury homes that uniquely spans both The Hills and The Hills South villages in Summerlin. Originally built by Coleman Homes (now Toll Brothers), this intimate enclave offers well maintained residences behind 24 hour security in one of Las Vegas's most established luxury corridors. ## What Is Corta Bella and Where Is It Located? Corta Bella occupies a distinctive position within Summerlin's geography, straddling the boundary between The Hills and The Hills South. This dual village location gives residents convenient access to the amenities, parks, and trail systems associated with both communities. The neighborhood sits within the western edge of Summerlin, close to Red Rock Canyon and the Spring Mountains, which provide a dramatic natural backdrop. The community's 126 homes were built by Coleman Homes, a respected luxury builder later acquired by Toll Brothers, one of the nation's largest premium homebuilders. This builder pedigree translates to solid construction quality, thoughtful architectural details, and floor plans that have aged well over the decades. ## What Can Buyers Expect from Corta Bella Homes? Corta Bella homes reflect the refined tastes of their era, with features like vaulted ceilings, open living areas, and indoor outdoor flow designed for the desert climate. Many homeowners have invested in significant upgrades over the years, including modern kitchens, updated flooring, and enhanced outdoor entertaining spaces with pools, spas, and covered patios. The community's smaller size creates a notably quiet, close knit atmosphere. With only 126 homes, residents tend to know their neighbors, and the streets see minimal through traffic. The 24 hour staffed guard gate reinforces this sense of seclusion, controlling access and providing consistent security coverage. ## How Does Corta Bella Compare to Nearby Guard-Gated Communities? Corta Bella offers an appealing entry point into The Hills' guard gated market. While neighboring communities like Eagle Hills and Tournament Hills feature larger custom estates on bigger lots, Corta Bella provides guard gated luxury at a comparatively accessible price point. This makes it attractive to buyers who want the security, prestige, and Summerlin location without the maintenance demands of a 10,000 plus square foot estate. ## A Local Perspective on Corta Bella Ryan Rose views Corta Bella as one of the smartest values in The Hills for buyers seeking guard gated living.
