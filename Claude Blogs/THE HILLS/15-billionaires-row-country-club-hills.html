Billionaire's Row is the exclusive seven home enclave within Country Club Hills in Summerlin's The Hills South, featuring some of the largest and most expensive residential properties in all of Las Vegas. Billionaire's Row, also known as The Enclave, sits within the guard gated Country Club Hills community adjacent to TPC Summerlin golf course. The section consists of just seven estate properties, each built on oversized lots with square footage and amenities that dwarf even the most impressive luxury homes in the valley. These are not merely large houses; they are compound scale estates with features typically reserved for properties in Beverly Hills or Palm Beach. The homes here were custom built to the exact specifications of their original owners, incorporating private theaters, wine cellars, indoor sports courts, multi car garages designed for collectors, and resort caliber pool complexes. Every detail reflects the level of wealth and expectation associated with the billionaire buyers who commissioned them. ## Who Has Lived on Billionaire's Row?
