The Hills Summerlin offers one of the widest ranges of home styles and sizes in the Las Vegas Valley, spanning everything from 1,400 square foot single family homes in the original village to 20,000 square foot custom estates in The Hills South. ## What Home Styles Are Available in The Hills Summerlin? The original Hills village features traditional single family homes built between 1990 and 1996. Most are one story or two story layouts designed by production builders, with floor plans that emphasize open living areas and desert landscaping. Neighborhoods like Cherry Creek, Copper Ridge, and Cypress Grove represent classic 1990s Summerlin architecture with stucco exteriors, tile roofs, and attached garages. The Hills South introduces a much broader architectural palette. Semi custom and custom estates in Eagle Hills, Tournament Hills, and Country Club Hills showcase styles ranging from contemporary to Mediterranean to classic Southwest. Builders like Christopher Homes and Coleman Homes (now Toll Brothers) crafted many of these residences on lots as large as two thirds of an acre. Corta Bella, a guard gated enclave of 126 homes, offers an exclusive setting within The Hills South. ## What Are the Home Sizes in The Hills? In the original village, homes typically range from approximately 1,400 square feet to just over 3,000 square feet. These are well suited for first time buyers, downsizers, and families looking for established neighborhoods with mature trees and proven resale value. Prices in the original village run from the $300,000s to the $700,000s. The Hills South dramatically expands the size range. Townhouses and condos in neighborhoods like The Terraces and Terraces on the Green start around 1,500 square feet, while custom estates in Country Club Hills and Tournament Hills can exceed 10,000 square feet. The largest properties along Billionaires Row in Country Club Hills reach up to 20,000 square feet, with asking prices from the upper $800,000s to over $10,000,000. ## How Do Gated Communities Differ in The Hills? Belaire Estates in the original village is gated and includes a private community park, offering a sense of exclusivity at a more accessible price point. In The Hills South, four guard gated communities provide 24 hour security: Eagle Hills, Tournament Hills, Country Club Hills, and Corta Bella. Guard gated neighborhoods tend to command premium pricing due to the added privacy and controlled access. ## Ryan Rose's Take on Home Selection Ryan Rose helps buyers match the right neighborhood to their lifestyle and budget.
