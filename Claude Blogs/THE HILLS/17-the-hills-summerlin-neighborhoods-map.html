The Hills Summerlin encompasses 19 distinct neighborhoods spread across two villages totaling 1,119 acres in the 89134 zip code. Understanding the layout helps buyers pinpoint the right location for their budget and lifestyle. ## How Are The Hills Neighborhoods Organized? The Hills is divided into two main sections: the original Hills village (394 acres) and The Hills South (725 acres). The original village sits in the northern portion and contains 11 neighborhoods. The Hills South extends to the south and east, bordering TPC Summerlin golf course, and includes 8 neighborhoods with a stronger emphasis on luxury and guard gated living. The original Hills village neighborhoods include Amarante, Belaire Estates, Cherry Creek, Copper Ridge, Cypress Grove, Evergreen, Hillcrest Estates, Panorama Pointe, Serenata, Visions, and Willow Tree. These were built between 1990 and 1996 as part of Summerlin's very first residential village. Homes here are primarily single family residences priced from the $300,000s to the $700,000s. ## What Neighborhoods Are in The Hills South? The Hills South features eight neighborhoods: Eagle Hills, Tournament Hills, Country Club Hills, Corta Bella, Montaire, Santa Barbara, The Terraces, and Terraces on the Green. This section opened in 1991 and was developed through 2000 by The Howard Hughes Corporation. Prices range from the upper $800,000s to over $10,000,000, with home sizes reaching up to 20,000 square feet. Four of these neighborhoods are guard gated: Eagle Hills, Tournament Hills, Country Club Hills, and Corta Bella. Country Club Hills is home to the famous Billionaires Row, where the most expensive properties in Summerlin are located. The Terraces and Terraces on the Green offer townhouse and condo options for buyers who want The Hills South address at a more accessible price point. ## Which Neighborhoods Offer Gated Access? Buyers seeking security and privacy have multiple options. In the original village, Belaire Estates is gated and features a community park exclusive to its residents. In The Hills South, the four guard gated communities offer 24 hour staffed entry points. Corta Bella contains 126 homes in an intimate guard gated setting, while Eagle Hills provides larger estate lots with elevated desert views. ## Ryan Rose's Neighborhood Guidance Ryan Rose regularly guides buyers through the distinct character of each Hills neighborhood.
