Custom homes in The Hills South span a remarkable range of architectural styles. Buyers will find contemporary designs with clean lines and walls of glass, classic Mediterranean villas with courtyards and clay tile roofs, and traditional Southwest estates with natural stone and desert integration. Christopher Homes, one of Las Vegas's premier luxury builders, developed several neighborhoods in The Hills South. Coleman Homes (now Toll Brothers) also contributed to the area, along with various independent custom builders. The largest custom properties are concentrated in Country Club Hills and Tournament Hills, where lots reach up to two thirds of an acre. Home sizes in these neighborhoods range from approximately 5,000 square feet to over 20,000 square feet. Country Club Hills includes the famous Billionaires Row, where the most exclusive estates in all of Summerlin are located. ## How Much Do Custom Homes Cost in The Hills South? Custom estates in The Hills South are priced from the upper $800,000s to over $10,000,000. Semi custom homes in neighborhoods like Montaire and Santa Barbara typically fall in the $800,000 to $2,000,000 range, while fully custom estates in the guard gated communities command significantly higher prices. Eagle Hills, Tournament Hills, Country Club Hills, and Corta Bella all require passing through staffed guard gates, which adds to both the privacy and the property values. Since all homes in The Hills South are resale, buyers benefit from seeing exactly what they are purchasing: finished landscaping, established HOA track records, and completed construction with no builder delays.
