The Hills Park is a seven acre community park located at 9100 Hillpointe Dr in Summerlin, serving as the recreational centerpiece of The Hills neighborhood. With lighted tennis courts, a sand volleyball court, children's play areas, and a large pavilion, this park delivers far more than a typical subdivision green space. ## What Amenities Does The Hills Park Offer? The Hills Park packs an impressive range of amenities into its seven acres. A large central picnic pavilion anchors the space, surrounded by seven individual picnic ramadas that are perfect for family gatherings and weekend cookouts. The open lawn amphitheater and stage host community events throughout the year, creating a neighborhood gathering point that few Las Vegas communities can match. For active residents, the park features two lighted tennis courts, a half basketball court, and a sand volleyball court. The lighted courts are especially valuable during summer months when cooler evening play is preferred. A dedicated children's play area gives families a safe, close to home option for outdoor time. ## Where Is The Hills Park Located? The Hills Park sits between Becker Middle School and Lummis Elementary School on Hillpointe Drive, making it a natural after school destination for families. Its central position within The Hills means most residents can walk or bike to the park in minutes. The park also connects to Summerlin's 200+ mile trail system, so joggers and cyclists can use it as a starting point for longer routes through the community. ## Is The Hills Park Good for Events and Gatherings?
