Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area sits approximately 17 miles west of The Hills in Summerlin, placing one of Nevada's most spectacular natural landscapes just a 10 to 15 minute drive from home. This proximity to world class hiking, rock climbing, and scenic drives is one of the defining lifestyle advantages of living in The Hills. ## How Far Is Red Rock Canyon from The Hills Summerlin? The drive from The Hills to Red Rock Canyon takes roughly 10 to 15 minutes heading west on Charleston Boulevard. That short commute puts residents within easy reach of the 13 mile scenic drive, dozens of hiking trails, and rock climbing areas that attract visitors from around the world. For weekend adventurers or after work hikers, this kind of access turns a major outdoor destination into a neighborhood amenity. ## What Can You Do at Red Rock Canyon? The 13 mile scenic loop drive offers stunning views of the canyon's signature red sandstone formations and is accessible to all vehicles. For hikers, trails range from easy nature walks to challenging scrambles. The Calico Tanks trail is a popular 2.5 mile round trip route that rewards hikers with panoramic views of the Las Vegas Valley from its summit. Rock climbers will find over 2,000 documented routes across the canyon's sandstone walls. Wildlife viewing, photography, and picnicking round out the recreational options. The conservation area spans nearly 196,000 acres, so repeat visits reveal new terrain every time. ## Do You Need Reservations for Red Rock Canyon? From October 1 through May 31, timed entry reservations are required between 8 AM and 5 PM. This system was introduced to manage peak season crowds and protect the landscape. Reservations can be made online, and locals who visit frequently often secure their spots early in the week. During summer months (June through September), no reservation is needed, though extreme heat limits comfortable hiking to early morning hours. ## A Local Perspective on Red Rock Canyon Access Ryan Rose considers proximity to Red Rock Canyon one of the strongest selling points for The Hills and surrounding Summerlin neighborhoods.
