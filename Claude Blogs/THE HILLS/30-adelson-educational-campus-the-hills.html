The Adelson Educational Campus is a private Pre K through 12 school located at 9700 Hillpointe Road in The Hills village, offering a college preparatory curriculum on a $65 million campus that welcomes students of all faiths. ## What Schools Make Up the Adelson Educational Campus? The campus houses three distinct schools under one umbrella: Milton I. Schwartz Hebrew Academy serving Pre K through 4th grade, Adelson Middle School for grades 5 through 8, and Adelson Upper School covering grades 9 through 12. This structure allows students to progress from early childhood through high school graduation without ever changing campuses. Approximately 510 students attend across all three divisions, keeping class sizes small and fostering close relationships between faculty and families. Founded through the generosity of the Adelson family, the campus opened in 2008 and was built to provide a world class educational environment in Southern Nevada. While rooted in Jewish values and tradition, enrollment is open to families of all backgrounds and beliefs. ## What Facilities Does the Adelson Campus Offer? The $65 million investment is evident across the campus. Highlights include a 350 seat performing arts theater, dedicated science laboratories, athletic fields, and modern classroom spaces designed for collaborative learning. These facilities rival many small college campuses and provide students with resources that extend well beyond typical K through 12 offerings. The performing arts program, in particular, benefits from professional grade production capabilities that give students real world experience in theater and music. ## How Much Does Tuition Cost at the Adelson School? Tuition at the Adelson Educational Campus runs approximately $19,000 per year. Financial aid and scholarship options are available for qualifying families. For parents weighing the investment, the campus delivers small class sizes, a college preparatory track, and access to facilities that most public and charter schools cannot match. ## Local Insight from Ryan Rose Ryan Rose notes that the Adelson campus is a significant draw for families relocating to Las Vegas who prioritize private education.
