The Meadows School is one of the top rated private schools in Nevada, and it sits just minutes from The Hills in Summerlin. Families living in The Hills enjoy a short commute to this prestigious Pre K through 12 campus at 8601 Scholar Lane, making it one of the most convenient private education options in the Las Vegas Valley. ## What Makes The Meadows School Stand Out? The Meadows School serves approximately 950 students across four divisions: Beginning School, Lower School, Middle School, and Upper School. The student to faculty ratio is 11:1, giving students meaningful access to their teachers throughout each academic day. The Upper School offers 24 Advanced Placement courses along with additional Advanced Topics classes. The school's academic results speak for themselves: a 100% graduation rate, an average SAT score of 1410, and an average ACT score of 31. The Washington Post ranked The Meadows the second most challenging high school in Nevada, a recognition that reflects the rigor of its curriculum. ## How Much Is Tuition at The Meadows School? Tuition at The Meadows School is approximately $33,180 to $34,840 per year depending on grade level. The school offers multiple payment plan options, including a single payment plan, a two payment plan, and a ten month installment plan. Financial aid is also available for families who demonstrate need, supporting the school's commitment to attracting students from a broad range of economic backgrounds. ## How Close Is The Meadows School to The Hills Summerlin?
