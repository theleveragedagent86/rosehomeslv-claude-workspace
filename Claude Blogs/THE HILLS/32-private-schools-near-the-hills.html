The Hills in Summerlin is surrounded by some of the highest rated private schools in the Las Vegas Valley, with options spanning Pre K through 12th grade across multiple educational philosophies. Families here can choose from college preparatory, faith based, and independent programs, all within a short drive of home. ## What Are the Best Private Schools Near The Hills? The Meadows School on Scholar Lane is consistently ranked among the top private schools in Nevada. This Pre K through 12 campus serves about 950 students with an 11:1 student to faculty ratio, 24 AP courses, and a 100% graduation rate. Average SAT scores reach 1410, and average ACT scores hit 31. Tuition runs approximately $33,180 to $34,840 per year. The Adelson Educational Campus sits directly within The Hills Village at 9700 Hillpointe Road, making it the closest private school option for residents. This $65 million campus houses three schools covering Pre K through 12th grade. While rooted in Jewish community values, Adelson welcomes students of all faiths. The student to teacher ratio is 7:1, tuition is approximately $19,000 per year, and the campus features a 350 seat performing arts theater. ## Are There Faith Based Private Schools Near The Hills? Faith Lutheran Middle School and High School ranks as the top Christian high school in Nevada according to Niche. Located in Summerlin, it offers a strong academic program grounded in Lutheran values. Bishop Gorman High School, also nearby, holds an A rating from Niche and is ranked among the top five private high schools in the state. Both schools draw heavily from The Hills and surrounding Summerlin communities. ## What About Elementary and Middle School Options? Beyond The Meadows and Adelson, families in The Hills can consider Alexander Dawson School for an independent option. Embrace Academy in Summerlin currently serves kindergarten through eighth grade, with a high school program expected to open in Fall 2026. Combined with strong public options like Lummis Elementary (GreatSchools 9 out of 10), The Hills offers school choices at every grade level. ## A Local Perspective on Private Education Ryan Rose helps many families evaluate neighborhoods based on school proximity and quality.
