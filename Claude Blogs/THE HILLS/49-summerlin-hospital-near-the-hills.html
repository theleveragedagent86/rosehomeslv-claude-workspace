Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is a 485 bed, Joint Commission accredited facility offering more than 30 specialty areas. Core departments include The Heart Center for cardiology and cardiovascular surgery, The Cancer Center for oncology treatment, The Children's Medical Center for pediatric care, and the BirthPlace for Women and Children for maternity and labor services. The hospital also operates a Robotic Surgery Center featuring da Vinci Xi Surgical Systems, which support minimally invasive procedures across multiple specialties. A 24/7 emergency room provides immediate care for urgent medical situations, and a freestanding Emergency Department opened in early 2025 to expand capacity for the growing Summerlin population. ## How Close Is Summerlin Hospital to The Hills?
