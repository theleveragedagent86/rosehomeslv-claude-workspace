What Is The Paseos in Summerlin?

The Paseos is a 768 acre village in Summerlin West, Las Vegas, NV 89138, developed by The Howard Hughes Corporation and home to over 28 sub neighborhoods with more than 50 unique home designs. Since its development began in 2003, The Paseos has grown into one of Summerlin's most desirable communities for families, retirees, and professionals alike.

Where Is The Paseos Located in Summerlin?

The Paseos sits within the Summerlin West section of the master planned Summerlin community, tucked against the foothills near Red Rock Canyon. The village falls within the 89138 zip code and offers stunning western views of the canyon and surrounding mountains. At roughly 3,000 feet in elevation, The Paseos enjoys temperatures about 10 degrees cooler than the Las Vegas valley floor during summer months. Residents are approximately 20 to 25 minutes from the Las Vegas Strip and 15 minutes from downtown Las Vegas.

What Types of Homes Are in The Paseos?

The Paseos features a wide variety of housing options, from townhomes and condos starting around $400,000 to luxury estate homes exceeding $2.6 million. Prominent builders include KB Home, Lennar, Pulte Homes, Toll Brothers, and Woodside Homes. The architectural style leans Southwestern and Spanish, with hacienda and mission style designs featuring clay barrel roof tiles and earth toned stucco exteriors. Home sizes range from 1,305 square feet for Coronado condos up to nearly 4,000 square feet for larger single family homes.

What Amenities Does The Paseos Offer?

Two standout parks anchor the community. The Paseos Park spans 12.5 acres and includes soccer fields, basketball courts, a splash pad, and picnic pavilions. Fox Hill Park covers 18 acres and features more than 20 play structures, a 32 foot climbing tower, a zip line, and an 18 hole disc golf course. The Paseos Trail System connects residents to over 150 miles of Summerlin trails for walking, running, and biking. Select neighborhoods like Traccia and Santaluz offer guard gated security, while the broader village is approximately 72% owner occupied.
