Givens Elementary School Near The Paseos in Summerlin

If you're raising a family in The Paseos, one of the first things you'll want to know is where your kids will go to school. Linda Rankin Givens Elementary is right inside the community, making morning drop off as easy as it gets.

What Are the Test Scores at Givens Elementary?

Givens Elementary earns an 8 out of 10 from GreatSchools and a B+ from Niche. U.S. News ranks it #23 among all Nevada elementary schools. The school sits in the top 5% statewide for overall test scores, which tells you a lot about the quality of instruction happening inside those walls.

Math proficiency lands at 69.6%, and reading proficiency comes in at 70.8%. Both figures outpace most Clark County schools by a wide margin. Parents in The Paseos consistently praise the teaching staff and the emphasis on student growth.

Is Givens Elementary Within Walking Distance of The Paseos?

Yes. Givens Elementary sits within The Paseos village, so many families live just minutes from campus. That walkability factor is a major draw for buyers with young children. No long bus rides, no stressful commutes through traffic. Just a short walk or drive through the neighborhood.

The Paseos spans 768 acres with nearly 30 sub neighborhoods, and Givens serves as one of the primary K through 5 options for residents. The school feeds into Sig Rogich Middle School and Palo Verde High School, giving families a clear academic pathway from kindergarten through graduation.
