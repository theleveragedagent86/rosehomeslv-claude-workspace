Sig Rogich Middle School Near The Paseos in Summerlin

Middle school is where students start building the academic habits that carry them through high school and beyond. For families in The Paseos, Sig Rogich Middle School is the next step after elementary, and it happens to be the top ranked middle school in all of Clark County.

What Are the Rankings and Test Scores at Sig Rogich Middle School?

Sig Rogich earns a B+ from Niche and an 8 out of 10 from GreatSchools. U.S. News ranks it #12 among all Nevada middle schools. The school also holds a Five Star designation from the Nevada School Performance Framework, which is the highest rating a school can receive.

Perhaps the most impressive stat is this: Sig Rogich ranks #1 out of 59 middle schools in Clark County School District. Math proficiency sits at 56%, and reading proficiency reaches 67%. Both numbers significantly exceed district averages for the same grade levels.

Where Is Sig Rogich Middle School Located?

The school sits at 235 Pavilion Center Drive, just minutes from The Paseos. Students who attend Givens, Vassiliadis, or Staton Elementary typically feed into Sig Rogich for grades 6 through 8. The campus is easily accessible from anywhere within The Paseos village.

After Sig Rogich, students move on to Palo Verde High School, which is located right next door on the same stretch of Pavilion Center Drive. That proximity makes the transition from middle school to high school seamless for Paseos families.

Why Does Sig Rogich's Five Star Rating Matter for Homebuyers?

Nevada's Five Star designation reflects consistent academic achievement, strong leadership, and positive school culture. Schools do not earn this rating by accident. For homebuyers, a Five Star middle school in your attendance zone adds tangible value to your property. Families relocating to Las Vegas actively search for homes within top rated school zones, and Sig Rogich consistently delivers.

The Paseos covers 768 acres with nearly 30 sub neighborhoods, and the vast majority of them feed into Sig Rogich. That broad coverage makes almost any home in the village a smart choice for families with middle school age children.
