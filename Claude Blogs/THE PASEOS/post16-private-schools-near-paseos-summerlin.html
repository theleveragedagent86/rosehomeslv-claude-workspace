Private Schools Near The Paseos Summerlin

Families in The Paseos have access to five well regarded private schools, all within a short drive through the Summerlin community. From faith based campuses to college preparatory programs, these schools offer smaller class sizes and specialized curricula that attract families across the Las Vegas valley.

What Private Schools Are Near The Paseos?

The Paseos sits within reach of some of the most respected private institutions in Southern Nevada. Faith Lutheran Middle and High School serves grades 6 through 12 and is one of the closest private options. Bishop Gorman High School, a premier Catholic college preparatory school, covers grades 9 through 12 and is located within the broader Summerlin area. Both schools consistently rank among the top private schools in Nevada.

Which Private Schools Offer Elementary and K Through 12 Programs?

For younger students, The Alexander Dawson School provides a private education from pre kindergarten through 8th grade right in Summerlin. Adelson Educational Campus offers a full K through 12 experience on a single campus, also in Summerlin. The Meadows School rounds out the options with a pre kindergarten through 12th grade program, giving families a single school path from early childhood through graduation. Each of these campuses emphasizes small class sizes and individualized instruction.

How Far Are These Private Schools From The Paseos?

All five private schools sit within the greater Summerlin and west Las Vegas area, making commutes manageable for Paseos families. Faith Lutheran and Bishop Gorman are each a short drive along Summerlin Parkway or the 215 Beltway. Adelson, Dawson, and The Meadows are similarly accessible. Families living in The Paseos benefit from the central location within Summerlin, keeping school commutes well under 20 minutes for most campuses.
