Bishop Gorman High School Near The Paseos

Bishop Gorman High School is one of the premier private high schools in all of Nevada, and it sits within easy driving distance of The Paseos in Summerlin. Serving grades 9 through 12, Bishop Gorman attracts students from across the Las Vegas valley with its nationally recognized athletics and rigorous college preparatory academics.

What Makes Bishop Gorman a Top High School?

Bishop Gorman has built a reputation as one of the strongest private high schools in the western United States. The school consistently produces Division I college athletes and maintains high college acceptance rates. Academics follow a Catholic college preparatory model with Advanced Placement courses, honors tracks, and a focus on critical thinking. The campus features modern facilities that support both classroom learning and competitive athletics at the highest level.

How Close Is Bishop Gorman to The Paseos?

Bishop Gorman sits within the broader Summerlin area, making the drive from The Paseos straightforward. Families can reach the campus quickly using Summerlin Parkway or the 215 Beltway. The commute is comparable to reaching most public high schools zoned for the area. For families living in The Paseos, Bishop Gorman offers a private school option without the long commute that families in other parts of Las Vegas face.

How Does Bishop Gorman Compare to Other High Schools Near The Paseos?

The Paseos is zoned for Palo Verde High School, which is the designated public high school. Bishop Gorman offers an alternative for families seeking a private, faith based education with smaller class sizes and a different academic culture. Faith Lutheran Middle and High School is another private option, serving grades 6 through 12 with a Christian curriculum. Bishop Gorman tends to draw families focused on competitive athletics alongside strong academics. Each school serves a different set of priorities.
