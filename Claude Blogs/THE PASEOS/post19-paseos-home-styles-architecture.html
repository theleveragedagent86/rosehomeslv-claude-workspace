The Paseos Home Styles and Architecture

The Paseos features Southwestern and Spanish inspired architecture with over 50 unique home designs spread across more than 30 sub communities. From compact condo flats to sprawling luxury estates, the village delivers one of the widest ranges of home styles found in any single Summerlin neighborhood.

What Architectural Style Defines The Paseos?

Hacienda and mission style designs set the tone throughout The Paseos. Clay barrel roof tiles, earth toned stucco exteriors, and arched entryways create a cohesive desert aesthetic across the village. Courtyards, patios, loggias, and balconies appear throughout the community, reflecting an emphasis on indoor outdoor desert living. These design elements work naturally with the Summerlin landscape and the views toward Red Rock Canyon to the west.

What Types of Homes Are Available in The Paseos?

The Paseos offers single family detached homes, two and three story townhomes, condominium flats, semi custom homes, custom builds, and luxury estates. Home sizes range from 1,305 square feet in the Coronado condo community to 3,943 square feet in larger single family neighborhoods. Lot sizes vary from standard residential plots up to 23,000 square feet in the Los Altos enclave. Prices span from roughly $400,000 for condos and townhomes to over $2.6 million for estate properties.

Which Sub Communities Showcase Different Home Styles?

With more than 30 sub communities, The Paseos covers nearly every housing category. Coronado Condos delivers compact, low maintenance living. Townhome communities like Traccia and Allegra offer multi story layouts. Single family neighborhoods such as Andalusia, Barcelona, and Montecito feature traditional detached homes on standard lots. Los Altos stands out with oversized lots up to 23,000 square feet for buyers seeking privacy and space. Guard gated communities like Traccia and Santaluz add an extra layer of exclusivity.

Local Insight

Ryan Rose, Las Vegas Real Estate Expert, considers the architectural variety in The Paseos one of its greatest strengths. Most Summerlin villages specialize in one or two home types, but The Paseos covers the full spectrum under one cohesive Southwestern design language. Whether a buyer wants a turnkey condo or a custom estate on a quarter acre lot, they can find it here without leaving the village.
