Builders in The Paseos Summerlin

Seven major homebuilders have shaped The Paseos since development began in 2003, creating more than 30 sub communities with over 50 unique home designs. KB Home, Lennar, Pulte Homes, Ryland Homes, Toll Brothers, William Lyon Homes, and Woodside Homes each brought distinct floor plans and price points to this Summerlin village.

Which Builders Developed The Paseos?

KB Home was among the earliest builders in The Paseos, delivering entry level and mid range single family homes. Lennar has been one of the most active builders in the village, contributing multiple sub communities with a range of sizes and price points. Toll Brothers brought its signature semi custom and luxury approach, offering larger homes with premium finishes. Pulte Homes, Ryland Homes, William Lyon Homes, and Woodside Homes each added their own neighborhoods, rounding out the diverse housing mix.

Which Builders Are Most Active in The Paseos Today?

Lennar, Toll Brothers, and Woodside Homes have been the most active with newer construction in The Paseos. Lennar continues to deliver homes across multiple price ranges, making it one of the most visible builders in the village. Toll Brothers focuses on the higher end of the market with semi custom options and luxury finishes. Woodside Homes has contributed several newer neighborhoods that appeal to move up buyers. Ryland Homes has since merged with Standard Pacific to form CalAtlantic, which later merged with Lennar.

What Price Ranges Do These Builders Cover?

The range of builders gives The Paseos an unusually wide price spectrum for a single Summerlin village. Homes start around $400,000 for condos and townhomes and climb past $2.6 million for luxury estate properties. KB Home and Lennar anchor the more accessible price points. Toll Brothers and select Woodside Homes neighborhoods target buyers in the upper market. Home sizes range from 1,305 square feet in the Coronado condo community to 3,943 square feet in larger single family neighborhoods.

Local Insight

Ryan Rose, Las Vegas Real Estate Expert, points out that having seven established builders in one village gives buyers real leverage. Each builder used different floor plans, lot configurations, and finish levels, so you can comparison shop without ever leaving The Paseos. Lennar and Toll Brothers homes tend to hold strong resale value here, and the newer Woodside Homes construction appeals to buyers who want modern features without waiting for a new build.
