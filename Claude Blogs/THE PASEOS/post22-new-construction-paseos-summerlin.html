New Construction Homes in The Paseos

Yes, new construction homes are available in The Paseos. Development in this Summerlin village began in 2003, and some neighborhoods are still being built out, making it one of the few established communities where buyers can find brand new homes alongside resale inventory.

Which Builders Are Active in The Paseos?

Lennar Homes, Toll Brothers, and Woodside Homes have been the most active builders delivering new construction in The Paseos. Lennar is known for its "Everything's Included" approach, bundling upgrades into the base price. Toll Brothers focuses on the luxury segment with semi custom options. Woodside Homes offers competitive pricing with modern floor plans. The Howard Hughes Corporation serves as the master developer for all of Summerlin, maintaining architectural standards and community planning across every new phase.

What Should Buyers Know About Buying New Construction Here?

New builds in The Paseos come with current building codes, modern energy efficiency, and full builder warranties. Buyers can often select finishes, flooring, and layout options during the construction phase. However, new construction pricing typically runs higher per square foot than comparable resale homes. The tradeoff is lower maintenance costs in the first several years and the ability to personalize the home before move in. With more than 50 home designs spread across 30 plus sub communities, buyers have a wide range of styles and price points.

How Does New Construction Affect Home Values in The Paseos?

New construction tends to support and lift surrounding property values. When builders price new homes at current market rates, nearby resale homes benefit from updated comparable sales. The Paseos' 72% owner occupancy rate reflects a neighborhood where homeowners are invested in long term value. Summerlin consistently ranks among the top master planned communities in the United States, and continued development signals ongoing builder confidence in the area's growth trajectory.

Local Insight

One advantage of buying new construction in The Paseos is timing. Builders often release inventory in phases, and getting on a builder's interest list early can mean first pick of lots and floor plans. The best homesites, especially those with western views toward Red Rock Canyon, go quickly.
