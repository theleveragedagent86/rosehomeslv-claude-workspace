The Paseos Townhomes and Condos

The Paseos offers townhomes and condos starting around $400,000, making it one of the most accessible entry points into Summerlin's west side. These attached homes deliver the community's amenities and location at a fraction of the cost of single family properties.

What Townhomes Are Available in The Paseos?

Santa Rosa is the primary townhome community within The Paseos. Units range from 1,488 to 2,220 square feet with two and three story layouts. These townhomes feature attached garages, open floor plans, and modern finishes that appeal to first time buyers, downsizers, and investors alike. The multi level designs maximize living space on smaller footprints, providing a sense of spaciousness without the maintenance demands of a traditional single family lot.

What About Condos in The Paseos?

Coronado is the go to condo community in The Paseos, with units starting at approximately 1,305 square feet. These condos offer a lower price point while still delivering the benefits of living within a Summerlin master planned village. Residents enjoy access to the same parks, trails, schools, and shopping that single family homeowners use. For buyers priced out of the single family market or those who prefer a lock and leave lifestyle, Coronado provides a practical solution in a desirable location.

Are Townhomes and Condos a Good Value in The Paseos?

At roughly $400,000 and up, townhomes and condos in The Paseos sit well below the village's median single family home price of $725,000 to $840,000. The 72% owner occupancy rate across The Paseos indicates a stable community where property values hold. Summerlin's ranking as a top master planned community nationally, combined with proximity to Red Rock Canyon and top rated schools, gives attached homes here a value proposition that many competing Las Vegas neighborhoods cannot match.

Local Insight

Townhomes and condos in The Paseos attract a specific buyer profile: professionals who want Summerlin's west side lifestyle without the upkeep of a large lot. HOA fees cover exterior maintenance and common areas, which simplifies ownership. These units also tend to rent well, making them solid options for investors looking at the 89138 zip code.
