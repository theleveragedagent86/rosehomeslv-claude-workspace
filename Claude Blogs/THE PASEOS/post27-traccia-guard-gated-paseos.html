Traccia Guard Gated Neighborhood in The Paseos

Traccia is one of two guard gated neighborhoods in The Paseos village of Summerlin. With a staffed entry gate, premium home designs, and a location inside one of Las Vegas's most desirable villages, Traccia delivers a level of privacy and security that few communities in the 89138 zip code can match.

What Makes Traccia Guard Gated?

Traccia features a staffed guard gate at its entrance, meaning every vehicle and visitor is verified before entering the community. This is different from a standard gated neighborhood that uses a remote or keypad. A guard gated community provides real time security oversight, which deters solicitors, reduces through traffic, and adds a layer of accountability that automated systems cannot offer. Traccia residents benefit from this around the clock staffed presence, making it one of the most secure places to live in The Paseos.

What Are Homes Like in Traccia?

Traccia sits at the luxury tier of The Paseos, with homes priced above the village median range of $725,000 to $840,000. The neighborhood features larger lots, upgraded architectural finishes, and designs that reflect the premium positioning within the village. Homes in Traccia appeal to buyers who want high end construction, spacious interiors, and outdoor living space, all protected behind a guard gate. The community is part of the broader Paseos landscape, so residents still enjoy village amenities like parks, trails, and proximity to Downtown Summerlin.

How Does Traccia Compare to Other Paseos Neighborhoods?

The Paseos has over 30 sub neighborhoods, but only two are guard gated: Traccia and Santaluz. Most other neighborhoods in the village are either gated with automated entry or completely open. Traccia stands apart because of its staffed security, which commands higher home values and slightly higher HOA fees. Buyers who want the Summerlin lifestyle with maximum privacy consistently put Traccia at the top of their list.

Local Insight

Traccia is one of the neighborhoods I recommend first when buyers tell me they want guard gated living in Summerlin without moving to a standalone luxury enclave. You get the security, the home quality, and the convenience of being inside The Paseos with full access to parks, trails, schools, and Red Rock Canyon just minutes away. It checks every box for families and professionals who value privacy.
