Santaluz Guard Gated Neighborhood in The Paseos

Santaluz is one of the two guard gated neighborhoods in The Paseos, offering staffed entry security, luxury home designs, and a premier location in Summerlin's 89138 zip code. For buyers who want privacy and high end living inside a master planned village, Santaluz is one of the best options in Las Vegas.

What Kind of Security Does Santaluz Offer?

Santaluz uses a staffed guard gate, meaning a security professional verifies every visitor before granting access. This is the highest tier of residential security available in The Paseos. Unlike standard gated communities that rely on keypads or remotes, a guard gated entry adds human oversight that prevents unauthorized access, reduces solicitation, and creates a controlled environment. Residents report that the staffed gate is one of the top reasons they chose Santaluz over other neighborhoods in the village.

What Are Homes Like in Santaluz?

Santaluz homes fall into the luxury segment of The Paseos, with prices above the village median of $725,000 to $840,000. The neighborhood features thoughtful floor plans, upgraded materials, and generous lot sizes that give homeowners space for pools, outdoor kitchens, and landscaping. Architectural styles align with the Mediterranean and desert contemporary designs found throughout The Paseos, but with the premium finishes that distinguish guard gated living. Santaluz is part of the broader Paseos village, so residents have full access to parks, walking trails, and nearby schools.

How Does Santaluz Compare to Traccia?

Traccia and Santaluz are the only two guard gated communities in The Paseos, and both deliver top tier security and luxury positioning. The main differences come down to home designs, lot configurations, and street layout. Buyers often tour both neighborhoods before deciding. Both carry slightly higher HOA dues than non guard gated communities due to the cost of staffing the gate. Price points in both communities run well above the village average, reflecting the added value of guard gated access and the quality of construction.

Local Insight

Santaluz is a neighborhood I show frequently to buyers relocating from guard gated communities in other states. They immediately recognize the value of the staffed gate, and they appreciate that Santaluz sits inside The Paseos rather than in an isolated enclave. You get guard gated security with walkable access to parks, trails, and the broader Summerlin amenity network. That combination is hard to find in Las Vegas at this price point.
