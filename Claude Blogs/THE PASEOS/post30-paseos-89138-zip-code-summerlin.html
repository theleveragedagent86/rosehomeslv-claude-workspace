The Paseos 89138 Zip Code Guide

The Paseos sits within the 89138 zip code, which covers Summerlin West in Las Vegas. This zip code is one of the most desirable in the valley, combining higher elevation, proximity to Red Rock Canyon, strong schools, and a median home price range of $725,000 to $840,000.

What Area Does 89138 Cover?

The 89138 zip code encompasses the western portion of Summerlin, including The Paseos and surrounding villages. The area sits at approximately 3,000 feet of elevation, which translates to temperatures roughly 10 degrees cooler than the Las Vegas valley floor during summer months. The western boundary backs up to Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area, providing protected desert views that will never be developed. Residents of 89138 enjoy a 20 to 25 minute drive to the Las Vegas Strip, a 25 minute drive to Harry Reid International Airport, and approximately 15 minutes to downtown Las Vegas.

What Are the Demographics of 89138?

The 89138 zip code has a 72% owner occupancy rate, which is significantly higher than the Las Vegas metro average. This signals neighborhood stability and long term commitment from homeowners. The average household size is 2.7 people, reflecting a mix of families, couples, and professionals. The high owner occupancy rate also means fewer rental turnovers, which keeps streets quieter and community engagement stronger. These fundamentals support steady property values and make 89138 one of the more resilient zip codes in Southern Nevada.

What Does the Housing Market Look Like in 89138?

Within The Paseos alone, prices range from approximately $400,000 for townhomes and condos to over $2.6 million for guard gated luxury estates. The median price across the zip code falls between $725,000 and $840,000. The village covers 768 acres with more than 30 sub neighborhoods and 50 plus home designs, giving buyers a wide selection at multiple price points. Guard gated communities like Traccia and Santaluz sit at the top of the market, while entry level attached homes in Coronado and Santa Rosa offer the most accessible path into the zip code.
