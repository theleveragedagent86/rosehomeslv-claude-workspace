Downtown Summerlin Dining Near The Paseos

Downtown Summerlin offers over 125 restaurants within one mile of The Paseos, making it one of the most convenient dining destinations in all of Las Vegas. From casual spots to upscale kitchens, residents can walk, bike, or drive to a full range of options in minutes.

What Restaurants Are at Downtown Summerlin?

Downtown Summerlin sits at 1980 Festival Plaza Drive, roughly one mile east of The Paseos. The open air shopping and dining district features a mix of national chains and local favorites spread across a walkable outdoor layout. You will find everything from quick service lunch spots to sit down restaurants with patio seating. The district continues to expand, with new restaurant openings adding to the variety each year.

What Types of Food Can You Find?

The dining scene at Downtown Summerlin covers Italian, Japanese, American, Mexican, seafood, and Mediterranean cuisines. Popular options include upscale steakhouses, wood fired pizza, farm to table concepts, and fast casual favorites. Weekend brunch is a big draw, with several restaurants offering special menus and outdoor seating overlooking the plaza. Happy hour specials run throughout the week, making it a go to spot for after work dining.

Is Downtown Summerlin Walkable from The Paseos?

The Paseos is located in western Summerlin, and Downtown Summerlin sits approximately one mile to the east along the Summerlin trail system. Some residents can walk or bike to the district in under 15 minutes. By car, the drive takes roughly three to five minutes with convenient parking throughout the center. The short distance makes Downtown Summerlin a natural extension of daily life for Paseos residents.
