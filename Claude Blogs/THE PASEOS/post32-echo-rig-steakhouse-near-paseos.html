Echo and Rig Steakhouse Near The Paseos

Echo and Rig is a combined butcher shop and steakhouse at Tivoli Village, roughly three to four miles from The Paseos in Summerlin. It is one of the top upscale dining destinations in the area, known for hand cut steaks, a curated cocktail menu, and a unique retail butcher experience inside the restaurant.

What Makes Echo and Rig Different from Other Steakhouses?

Echo and Rig stands out because it operates a full service butcher shop alongside the dining room. Guests can browse dry aged cuts, specialty meats, and house made sausages before or after their meal. The butcher shop is open during regular business hours, allowing residents to pick up premium cuts for home cooking. This dual concept sets it apart from every other steakhouse in the Summerlin corridor. The restaurant itself features a modern industrial design with an open kitchen and comfortable bar area.

Where Is Echo and Rig Located?

The restaurant is located at Tivoli Village, 400 S Rampart Boulevard, approximately three to four miles southeast of The Paseos. The drive takes about eight to ten minutes depending on traffic. Tivoli Village is an upscale open air shopping center with European inspired architecture, making the entire visit feel like an event. Parking is plentiful, and valet service is available during peak hours.

What Should You Order at Echo and Rig?

The menu features USDA Prime steaks, bone in ribeyes, filet mignon, and seasonal specials. Seafood options include oysters on the half shell and pan seared fish. The cocktail program rotates seasonally with handcrafted drinks that pair well with the steak forward menu. Weekend brunch is also popular, drawing crowds from across Summerlin and the surrounding neighborhoods.
