Best Restaurants Near The Paseos Summerlin

The Paseos sits at the center of one of the strongest dining corridors in Las Vegas, with dozens of restaurants spread across Downtown Summerlin, Tivoli Village, Boca Park, and the W Charleston Boulevard corridor. Residents have access to nearly every cuisine within a ten minute drive.

What Are the Top Restaurants Near The Paseos?

The standout options start with Echo and Rig at Tivoli Village, a combined butcher shop and steakhouse serving USDA Prime cuts and seasonal cocktails. El Dorado Cantina, also at Tivoli Village, delivers organic Mexican cuisine with fresh margaritas and tableside guacamole. Brio Tuscan Grille rounds out the Tivoli Village lineup with Italian classics in a polished setting. These three restaurants alone give Paseos residents access to steakhouse, Mexican, and Italian dining within a single shopping center.

What About Casual and Family Friendly Options?

The W Charleston Boulevard corridor offers a strong mix of casual restaurants just minutes from The Paseos. Islands Restaurant serves American comfort food in a relaxed atmosphere. Sushi Tower handles Japanese and sushi cravings with fresh rolls and sashimi. Toros Spanish Tapas Kitchen brings a shareable plate concept that works well for groups. China One covers Chinese takeout and dine in. For families, Hash House a Go Go at Canyon Pointe is famous for oversized portions and twisted farm food that keeps kids and adults equally satisfied.

Where Can You Find More Options?

Downtown Summerlin, roughly one mile east, holds over 125 dining options ranging from fast casual to upscale. Boca Park adds Grimaldi's coal fired pizza, The Cheesecake Factory, and Kona Grill for Asian American fusion. BJ's Restaurant and Brewhouse at Canyon Pointe is another reliable choice for craft beer and a broad menu. Between these four dining hubs, Paseos residents rarely need to venture more than ten minutes for a quality meal.
