What Stores Are at Downtown Summerlin?

Downtown Summerlin spans a massive open air layout at 1980 Festival Plaza Drive, offering a mix of national retailers, local boutiques, and specialty shops. Major anchors include Macy's, Dillard's, and a wide selection of fashion, home, and lifestyle brands. The shopping center continuously adds new tenants, keeping the retail mix fresh and relevant for Summerlin residents. Whether you need everyday essentials or high end purchases, Downtown Summerlin covers it all without a long drive.

What Dining and Entertainment Options Are Available?

Beyond retail, Downtown Summerlin is a full lifestyle destination. Dozens of restaurants range from casual eateries to upscale dining experiences. The Las Vegas Ballpark hosts the Aviators Triple A baseball team, making it a go to spot for family outings during the season. City National Arena serves as the practice facility for the Vegas Golden Knights, drawing hockey fans and event attendees year round. Seasonal events, concerts, and a weekly farmers market add even more reasons to visit.
