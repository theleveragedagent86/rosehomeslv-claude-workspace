Tivoli Village Shopping Near The Paseos

Tivoli Village is a boutique shopping destination located at 400 South Rampart Boulevard, roughly three to four miles from The Paseos. This European inspired center offers 25 plus specialty shops, luxury services, and outdoor dining in a walkable, dog friendly setting.

What Kind of Stores Are at Tivoli Village?

Tivoli Village caters to shoppers looking for something beyond the typical retail chain. Jeff White Custom Jewelry offers handcrafted pieces designed in house. Vasari carries curated men's and women's clothing with a focus on quality fabrics and tailored fits. Additional boutiques throughout the center specialize in home decor, gifts, and unique lifestyle products. This is the spot in Summerlin for one of a kind finds that you will not see at a standard mall.

What Else Does Tivoli Village Offer?

Beyond shopping, Tivoli Village is known for its spa and fitness options, making it a destination for self care as well as retail. Multiple outdoor dining patios line the walkways, creating a relaxed atmosphere that works for both casual lunches and evening outings. The center is dog friendly, so residents from The Paseos often walk their pets through the grounds while browsing shops. The architecture draws from Mediterranean and European design, giving the entire complex a distinct feel that sets it apart from other Summerlin shopping centers.

How Far Is Tivoli Village from The Paseos?

Tivoli Village sits about three to four miles south of The Paseos along Rampart Boulevard. The drive takes roughly eight to ten minutes depending on traffic. Residents heading to Boca Park Fashion Village can combine both trips easily, as the two centers are located on the same stretch of Rampart. This corridor gives Paseos homeowners access to both boutique and mainstream retail without leaving the Summerlin area.
