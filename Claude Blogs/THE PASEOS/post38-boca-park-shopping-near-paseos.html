Boca Park Fashion Village Near The Paseos

Boca Park Fashion Village at 750 South Rampart Boulevard brings a curated mix of national retailers and upscale casual dining to the Summerlin area, approximately three to four miles from The Paseos. This open air center focuses on fashion, home, and lifestyle brands that fill the gap between boutique shopping and big box retail.

What Stores Are at Boca Park Fashion Village?

Boca Park delivers a strong lineup of recognizable brands. Target anchors the center for everyday needs. REI serves the outdoor enthusiasts drawn to nearby Red Rock Canyon. Pottery Barn and Pottery Barn Kids handle home furnishing and decor. Anthropologie, GAP, and Sephora cover fashion and beauty. Drybar and LensCrafters round out the personal care and optical options. Total Wine provides one of the largest beverage selections in the valley. The mix makes Boca Park a reliable one stop destination for Paseos residents handling multiple errands in a single trip.

What Dining Options Are at Boca Park?

The dining scene at Boca Park leans toward high end casual. Grimaldi's serves coal fired pizza in a Brooklyn inspired setting. The Cheesecake Factory offers its signature expansive menu. Kona Grill brings sushi and American fare with a polished atmosphere. These restaurants make Boca Park a frequent weeknight dinner destination for families and couples living in The Paseos. The outdoor seating at several locations adds a comfortable feel during the cooler months.

How Close Is Boca Park to The Paseos?

Boca Park sits along Rampart Boulevard, roughly three to four miles south of The Paseos. The drive takes about eight to ten minutes. Its location on the same corridor as Tivoli Village means Paseos residents often visit both centers in a single outing. The Rampart corridor effectively serves as the primary shopping artery for this part of Summerlin.

Local Insight

Boca Park fills a specific niche for Paseos residents. It is not as large as Downtown Summerlin and not as boutique as Tivoli Village, but the brand selection hits a sweet spot. I find that families especially appreciate having Target, REI, and Pottery Barn all in one center with quality dining attached. It simplifies the weekly routine.
