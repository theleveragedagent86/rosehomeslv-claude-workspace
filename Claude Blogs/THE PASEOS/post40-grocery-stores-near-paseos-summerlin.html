Grocery and Big Box Stores Near The Paseos

The Paseos in Summerlin offers quick access to multiple grocery and big box options, including Albertsons, Whole Foods Market, and Costco. Residents can handle a full weekly grocery run without driving more than ten minutes from the neighborhood.

Where Is the Nearest Grocery Store to The Paseos?

Albertsons is one of the closest full service grocery stores to The Paseos, located a short drive from the neighborhood. It carries a standard selection of produce, meat, dairy, bakery, and pharmacy services. For residents who prefer organic and specialty products, Whole Foods Market is also a short drive away, offering its signature selection of natural and organic groceries along with a prepared foods counter. Both stores serve as reliable options for everyday grocery needs without requiring a long trip.

Is There a Costco Near The Paseos?

Costco sits at Canyon Pointe, a 600,000 square foot retail center at West Charleston Boulevard and the 215 Beltway. This location handles bulk grocery shopping, household supplies, electronics, and pharmacy services under one roof. Paseos residents typically reach it in under ten minutes. The warehouse format is especially popular with larger families in The Paseos who stock up on groceries and essentials in a single weekly trip. Costco also carries fuel at competitive prices, adding another practical benefit for nearby homeowners.

What Other Big Box Stores Are Nearby?

Target at Boca Park Fashion Village on Rampart Boulevard provides a convenient combination of groceries, clothing, home goods, and electronics. Lowe's at Canyon Pointe covers home improvement and building supply needs. Best Buy at Canyon Pointe handles electronics and appliances. Between these three centers and the grocery options, Paseos residents have a complete retail network within a compact driving radius. This concentration of stores is a direct result of The Paseos' central position within the western Summerlin corridor.
