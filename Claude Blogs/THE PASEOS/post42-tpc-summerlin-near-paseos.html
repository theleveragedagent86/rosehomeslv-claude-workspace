TPC Summerlin Golf Course Near The Paseos

TPC Summerlin is a private 18 hole PGA TOUR course located at 1700 Village Center Circle, just minutes from The Paseos. This championship course hosts the annual Shriners Children's Open, bringing professional golf to the neighborhood every fall.

What Is TPC Summerlin?

TPC Summerlin is a Tournament Players Club facility designed by Bobby Weed, one of the most respected golf course architects in the country. The course plays 7,243 yards at par 72 and features desert landscaping with views of the Spring Mountains and Red Rock Canyon. As a private club, membership includes access to the course, practice facilities, clubhouse dining, and social events. The club sits within the Summerlin master plan, surrounded by some of the area's most desirable residential neighborhoods.

Does TPC Summerlin Host PGA TOUR Events?

Yes. TPC Summerlin is the permanent home of the Shriners Children's Open, a PGA TOUR event held each October. The tournament draws top professional golfers from around the world and attracts thousands of spectators to the Summerlin community. Residents of The Paseos can attend the tournament with a short drive. The event brings national television coverage to the neighborhood and highlights the quality of life in western Las Vegas. Volunteering at the Shriners Open is also a popular activity among local residents.

How Close Is TPC Summerlin to The Paseos?

TPC Summerlin is located in the Village Center area of Summerlin, a short drive east of The Paseos along Village Center Circle. The drive takes approximately five to ten minutes depending on traffic. The proximity makes it easy for golf enthusiasts in The Paseos to maintain an active membership and play regularly. Several other golf options also sit nearby, giving residents multiple choices within a small radius.
