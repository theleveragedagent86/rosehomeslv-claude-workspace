Las Vegas Ballpark Near The Paseos

Las Vegas Ballpark is a modern Triple A baseball stadium located at Downtown Summerlin, roughly one mile from The Paseos. It is home to the Las Vegas Aviators, the Triple A affiliate of the Oakland Athletics, and offers one of the best live sports experiences in the valley.

Where Is Las Vegas Ballpark?

Las Vegas Ballpark sits within the Downtown Summerlin complex at 1650 South Pavilion Center Drive. The stadium opened in 2019 and holds approximately 10,000 fans. Its location at the western edge of the Las Vegas valley provides stunning views of the Spring Mountains beyond the outfield walls. From The Paseos, the drive takes roughly three to five minutes, making it one of the closest professional sports venues to any residential community in Las Vegas.

What Are the Las Vegas Aviators?

The Las Vegas Aviators compete in the Pacific Coast League as the Triple A affiliate of the Oakland Athletics. The team plays a full minor league season from April through September, giving residents access to affordable professional baseball all summer long. The Aviators roster regularly features top prospects on their way to the major leagues. Game nights include promotions, fireworks shows, and family friendly entertainment throughout the season.

What Is the Ballpark Experience Like?

Las Vegas Ballpark was designed with fan comfort in mind. The stadium features wide concourses, multiple food and beverage options, a pool area beyond the outfield, and group event spaces. Ticket prices are significantly lower than major league games, making it an accessible option for families. The sunset views over Red Rock Canyon from the upper deck seating areas are a signature feature. Many Paseos residents hold season tickets and walk or bike to games using the Summerlin trail system.
