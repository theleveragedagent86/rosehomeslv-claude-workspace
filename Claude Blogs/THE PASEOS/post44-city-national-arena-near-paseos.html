City National Arena Golden Knights Near The Paseos

City National Arena is the official practice facility of the Vegas Golden Knights NHL team, located at Downtown Summerlin roughly one mile from The Paseos. Residents can watch the Golden Knights practice and enjoy public ice skating sessions without fighting traffic on the Strip.

What Is City National Arena?

City National Arena opened in 2017 as the practice headquarters for the Vegas Golden Knights. The facility includes two NHL regulation ice sheets, team offices, locker rooms, and a retail store stocked with official Golden Knights merchandise. It sits within the Downtown Summerlin complex, surrounded by shopping and dining options. The arena also serves as a community gathering spot, hosting youth hockey leagues, figure skating programs, and special events throughout the year.

Can You Watch Golden Knights Practices?

Yes. Golden Knights practices at City National Arena are open to the public on most days during the NHL season. Fans can sit in the bleachers and watch players run drills, scrimmage, and prepare for upcoming games. There is no admission charge for practice viewing, making it a free entertainment option for hockey fans in The Paseos. Practice schedules are posted on the team's website and can change based on the game calendar, so checking ahead is recommended.

Is There Public Ice Skating at City National Arena?

City National Arena offers public skating sessions on its community rink throughout the week. Skate rentals are available on site, and sessions are open to all ages and skill levels. The facility also runs youth and adult hockey leagues, learn to skate programs, and birthday party packages. For families in The Paseos, the one mile distance makes it easy to drop in for a skating session on a weekend afternoon.
