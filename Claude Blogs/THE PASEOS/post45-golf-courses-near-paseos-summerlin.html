Golf Courses Near The Paseos Summerlin

The Paseos is surrounded by some of the best golf courses in Las Vegas, with four top rated options all within a short drive. Whether you prefer a private PGA TOUR venue or a public course with mountain views, this corner of Summerlin delivers for golfers at every level.

What Golf Courses Are Near The Paseos?

Four standout courses sit within easy reach of The Paseos. TPC Summerlin is a private 18 hole PGA TOUR course that hosts the annual Shriners Children's Open. TPC Las Vegas is an 18 hole course in the Canyons village of Summerlin, featuring a golf academy, pro shop, and the TPC Grille restaurant. Angel Park Golf Club is a popular public course known for its two 18 hole layouts and a par 3 cloud nine course lit for night play. Bear's Best Las Vegas is a Jack Nicklaus designed course that recreates 18 of the greatest holes Nicklaus has ever designed.

Are There Public Golf Courses Near The Paseos?

Yes. Angel Park Golf Club and Bear's Best Las Vegas both welcome public play and offer tee times without a membership requirement. TPC Las Vegas also offers accessible play options with its Summerlin location. These courses provide a range of price points, from affordable weekday rates to premium weekend rounds. Public golfers in The Paseos can rotate through multiple courses without ever driving more than 15 minutes from home.

Is TPC Summerlin Open to the Public?

TPC Summerlin operates as a private club and requires membership for regular play. However, the course opens to spectators each October for the Shriners Children's Open PGA TOUR event. The club was designed by Bobby Weed and plays 7,243 yards at par 72. Membership includes access to the course, practice facilities, and clubhouse amenities. For golfers seeking a private club experience near The Paseos, TPC Summerlin is the premier option in the area.
