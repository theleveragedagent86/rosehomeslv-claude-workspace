How Far Is The Paseos From the Las Vegas Strip?

The Paseos in Summerlin sits approximately 17 miles west of the Las Vegas Strip, putting residents just 20 to 25 minutes from the world famous resort corridor by car. That short drive gives homeowners the best of both worlds: quiet suburban living with easy access to entertainment, dining, and nightlife.

What Is the Fastest Route From The Paseos to the Strip?

The most direct route runs east along the 215 Beltway to Interstate 15 South, which drops you right onto Las Vegas Boulevard. During non peak hours, this drive typically takes 20 minutes. Rush hour traffic between 4 and 6 PM on weekdays can push that closer to 30 minutes, especially near the I 15 interchange. An alternate route through Flamingo Road or Tropicana Avenue can save a few minutes depending on your destination along the Strip.

How Far Is The Paseos From the Airport?

Harry Reid International Airport is roughly 25 minutes from The Paseos. The route follows the same 215 to I 15 corridor, continuing south past the Strip. Frequent travelers appreciate this proximity, especially compared to communities farther north or west in the valley. Early morning and late night flights are particularly convenient since traffic is minimal during those hours.

Can You Reach Downtown Las Vegas Quickly?

Downtown Las Vegas sits about 15 minutes from The Paseos via Highway 613 and Highway 11. This route bypasses the Strip entirely, making it a faster option for residents heading to the Fremont Street Experience, Arts District, or downtown business offices. The 89138 zip code location in Summerlin West gives The Paseos a geographic advantage for reaching multiple parts of the valley without excessive commute times.
