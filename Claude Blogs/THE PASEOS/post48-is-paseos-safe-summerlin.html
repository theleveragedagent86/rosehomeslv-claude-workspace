Is The Paseos Safe? Safety and Security in Summerlin

The Paseos is one of the safer neighborhoods in the Las Vegas Valley. Situated within the Summerlin master planned community, it benefits from low crime rates, well maintained streets, and multiple gated and guard gated neighborhoods that add layers of security for residents.

What Makes The Paseos a Safe Neighborhood?

Several factors contribute to the safety of The Paseos. The neighborhood has a 72% owner occupied rate, which research consistently links to lower crime and stronger community engagement. Homeowners who live in their properties tend to watch out for their neighbors, maintain their homes, and report suspicious activity. The Summerlin Community Association also maintains common areas, lighting, and landscaping, reducing the environmental conditions that can attract crime. Well lit streets and active pedestrian traffic from the trail system create natural surveillance throughout the community.

Are There Gated Communities in The Paseos?

Yes. The Paseos includes multiple gated and guard gated neighborhoods that provide additional security beyond the community baseline. Traccia and Santaluz are both guard gated, meaning a staffed gatehouse controls vehicle access around the clock. Several other sub neighborhoods within The Paseos feature key card or remote controlled gate entry. These gated options appeal to families, retirees, and anyone seeking an extra measure of privacy and controlled access.

How Does Summerlin Compare to the Rest of Las Vegas?

Summerlin consistently ranks as one of the safest areas in the Las Vegas metropolitan area. The master planned community design, active HOA oversight, and suburban density all contribute to crime rates well below the Las Vegas average. The Paseos sits in Summerlin West within the 89138 zip code, an area that benefits from newer construction, established community standards, and distance from higher traffic corridors. Residents also have access to Las Vegas Metropolitan Police Department resources, with response times typical of suburban Summerlin.

Local Insight

Safety is one of the top three factors my buyers mention when choosing The Paseos. The combination of guard gated options, high owner occupancy, and the overall Summerlin infrastructure creates a neighborhood where families feel comfortable letting kids ride bikes and retirees enjoy evening walks. It is the kind of community where neighbors know each other, and that social connection adds a layer of security no gate can match.
