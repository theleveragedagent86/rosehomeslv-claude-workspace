Schools Near The Paseos Summerlin

The Paseos in Summerlin is zoned for some of the highest rated public schools in Nevada, including Vassiliadis Elementary (ranked #5 in the state), Sig Rogich Middle School (ranked #1 in Clark County School District), and Palo Verde High School (96% graduation rate). Families moving to The Paseos gain access to an exceptional educational pipeline from elementary through high school.

Which Elementary Schools Serve The Paseos?

Three public elementary schools serve The Paseos community. Vassiliadis Elementary holds an A minus rating and an 8 out of 10 score on Niche, placing it at number 5 in Nevada and in the top 1% for both math and reading proficiency. A remarkable 82% of Vassiliadis students test proficient in math. Givens Elementary earns a B plus rating with an 8 out of 10 score, ranking number 23 in the state and in the top 5% overall. Staton Elementary also serves portions of the village and maintains strong academic performance within the Clark County School District.

What Middle and High Schools Are Zoned for The Paseos?

Sig Rogich Middle School is the primary middle school for Paseos families. It carries a B plus rating, an 8 out of 10 score, and ranks number 12 in Nevada. Rogich has earned the Five Star School designation and holds the number 1 ranking among all middle schools in the Clark County School District. Palo Verde High School serves The Paseos at the high school level, with a B plus rating and an 8 out of 10 score. Palo Verde ranks number 19 in Nevada, posts a 96% graduation rate, and averages a 1,240 SAT score. These rankings make the Paseos school zone one of the strongest in the entire Las Vegas valley.

Are There Private School Options Near The Paseos?

Several well regarded private schools sit within a short drive of The Paseos. Faith Lutheran Middle and High School offers a college preparatory curriculum with strong athletics. Bishop Gorman High School is one of the most recognized private schools in Nevada, known for both academics and championship level sports programs. The Alexander Dawson School and The Meadows School provide additional private options. Adelson Educational Campus rounds out the list with its unique dual language and cultural curriculum.
