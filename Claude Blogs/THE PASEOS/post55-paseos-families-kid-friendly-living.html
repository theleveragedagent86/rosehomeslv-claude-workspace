The Paseos Families and Kid Friendly Living

The Paseos is one of the most family friendly neighborhoods in all of Las Vegas. Top rated schools, adventure parks, 150 plus miles of trails, and a safe community with gated options make it an ideal place to raise children.

What Schools Serve The Paseos?

Families in The Paseos have access to some of the highest rated public schools in Clark County. Vassiliadis Elementary holds an A minus rating and ranks number 5 in Nevada. Givens Elementary earns a B plus and ranks in the top 5% of CCSD schools. Sig Rogich Middle School is rated number 1 in the Clark County School District. Palo Verde High School boasts a 96% graduation rate. Several private school options are also nearby, including Faith Lutheran and Bishop Gorman. This combination of public and private school quality is rare in Las Vegas and a major reason families choose The Paseos.

What Parks and Outdoor Activities Are Available for Kids?

Fox Hill Park is the crown jewel for families in The Paseos. This 18 acre park features over 20 play structures, a 32 foot climbing tower, a zip line, and themed play areas designed for multiple age groups. The Paseos Park adds another 12.5 acres with a splash pad, playground, soccer fields, and basketball courts. Both parks connect to the Summerlin trail system, giving families safe walking and biking routes throughout the community. Children can ride bikes to the park, play for hours, and return home without ever crossing a major street.

Is The Paseos Safe for Families?

The Paseos has a 72% owner occupancy rate, which contributes to neighborhood stability and a strong sense of community. Guard gated options in Traccia and Santaluz provide additional security for families who prioritize controlled access. The broader village benefits from Summerlin's master planned design with well lit streets, wide sidewalks, and traffic calming features. The community attracts families who invest in their homes and stay long term, creating the kind of neighborhood where kids grow up knowing their neighbors.

Local Insight

I work with families moving to The Paseos every month, and the number one comment I hear is about Fox Hill Park. Parents are genuinely amazed when they see the scale of the playground and how well maintained it is. The school zoning is the other major draw. Having Vassiliadis Elementary and Sig Rogich Middle in your attendance zone is a combination most Las Vegas neighborhoods simply cannot match. My families with young children also love the trail connections between neighborhoods, which give kids safe routes to walk or bike with friends.
