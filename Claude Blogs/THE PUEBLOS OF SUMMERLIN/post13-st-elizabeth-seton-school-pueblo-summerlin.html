St. Elizabeth Ann Seton Catholic School in The Pueblos of Summerlin

St. Elizabeth Ann Seton Catholic School is a private K through 8 campus located within The Pueblos village of Summerlin, offering college preparatory academics rooted in Catholic tradition. Since opening in 2000, the school has become a trusted option for families seeking faith based education in the 89128 zip code.

What Grades Does St. Elizabeth Ann Seton Serve?

St. Elizabeth Ann Seton Catholic School enrolls students in kindergarten through eighth grade, providing a continuous educational path through the critical elementary and middle school years. The college preparatory curriculum prepares students academically while incorporating Catholic values and community service. The campus opened in 2000, making it the newest of the three schools located within The Pueblos village boundaries. Families appreciate the smaller class sizes and structured environment that a private Catholic school typically provides.

Where Is St. Elizabeth Ann Seton Located in Summerlin?

The school sits inside The Pueblos village of Summerlin North, giving neighborhood families a walkable campus option. The Pueblos is one of the few Summerlin villages with three schools within its own borders: St. Elizabeth Ann Seton, Richard H. Bryan Elementary (CCSD, K through 5), and The Meadows School (private, PK through 12). This concentration of educational choices is a significant draw for families evaluating where to buy. Ryan Rose points out that having both public and private options within the same village is a rare advantage in the Las Vegas market.

What Happens After Eighth Grade for Seton Students?

After completing eighth grade at St. Elizabeth Ann Seton, students have several pathways. Many transition to Cimarron Memorial High School, the CCSD campus serving The Pueblos area for grades 9 through 12. Others continue in private education through The Meadows School or other independent high schools across the valley. The broader Summerlin area offers access to 16 public and 11 private schools, so families have flexibility when planning the next step.
