Ernest A. Becker Sr. Middle School: Serving The Pueblos of Summerlin

Ernest A. Becker Sr. Middle School is the public middle school serving families in The Pueblos of Summerlin, providing CCSD education for grades 6 through 8. Located near the village, Becker Middle School bridges the gap between elementary and high school for students in the 89128 zip code.

What Grades Does Becker Middle School Cover?

Becker Middle School is a Clark County School District campus serving students in sixth through eighth grade. The school draws from several Summerlin North neighborhoods, including The Pueblos village. Students who attend Bryan Elementary School within The Pueblos typically feed into Becker for their middle school years. This transition is smooth for most families because the campus is nearby and well connected to the surrounding residential areas.

How Does Becker Middle School Fit Into The Pueblos School Pipeline?

The Pueblos of Summerlin offers a clear public school pathway: Bryan Elementary for K through 5, Becker Middle School for 6 through 8, and Cimarron Memorial High School for 9 through 12. This continuity means families can settle into The Pueblos knowing their children's school trajectory is mapped out from kindergarten through graduation. Ryan Rose considers this predictable school pipeline one of the strongest selling points when working with families evaluating The Pueblos against other Summerlin villages.

Are There Alternative Middle School Options Near The Pueblos?

Families who prefer private education have options as well. The Meadows School, located within The Pueblos village, serves students PK through 12 and covers the middle school grades internally. St. Elizabeth Ann Seton Catholic School also spans K through 8, offering another private alternative. With 16 public and 11 private schools accessible in the broader area, The Pueblos gives families the flexibility to choose the path that fits their priorities and budget.
