Aromi Italian Restaurant sits right inside Pueblo Place at Rampart and Lake Mead Boulevard, giving residents of The Pueblos of Summerlin a genuine family owned Italian dining experience within walking distance of home.

What Makes Aromi Italian Restaurant Special in Summerlin?

Aromi is an intimate, family owned restaurant that blends classic Italian cooking with creative modern touches. The menu features handmade pastas like short rib ravioli and truffle prosciutto pasta alongside standout dishes such as spaghetti neri in lobster bisque and rabbit ragout pappardelle. Starters include duck prosciutto salad and burrata topped golden beet carpaccio. The restaurant also maintains a carefully curated wine list that pairs thoughtfully with every course.

Does Aromi Offer Weekly Specials or Tasting Menus?

Aromi runs several recurring specials that keep regulars coming back throughout the week. Tuesdays feature a steak dinner with extras, Wednesdays bring the "Tour of Aromi" prix fixe tasting menu, and Thursdays highlight wine promotions. The kitchen also accommodates gluten free, vegan, and vegetarian diners daily, making it accessible for groups with mixed dietary needs.

Where Is Aromi Located Near The Pueblos?

Aromi is located at 2110 N Rampart Blvd, Suite 110, inside the 34,000 square foot Pueblo Place shopping center. For Pueblos of Summerlin residents, this means quality Italian dining is just minutes away rather than a drive to the Strip. Reservations can be made through OpenTable or by calling (725) 777 2476.
