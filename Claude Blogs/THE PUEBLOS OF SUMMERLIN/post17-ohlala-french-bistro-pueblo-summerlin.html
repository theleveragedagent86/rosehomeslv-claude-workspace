Ohlala French Bistro brings authentic Parisian flavors to The Pueblos of Summerlin, tucked inside Pueblo Place at 2120 N Rampart Blvd. Since opening in 2016, this charming bistro has earned a reputation as one of Summerlin's true hidden gems.

What Kind of Food Does Ohlala French Bistro Serve?

Ohlala delivers classic French cuisine with dishes that would feel right at home in a Parisian neighborhood bistro. Starters include traditional escargots, baked brie, and French onion soup. Entrees range from filet mignon with sauce poivre vert and sole meunière to duck magret and moules marinières. Desserts such as chocolate mousse and crème brûlée round out the menu with the kind of finesse you would expect from a proper French kitchen.

Does Ohlala French Bistro Offer Weekend Brunch?

Ohlala serves brunch on Saturdays and Sundays from 11:00 AM to 2:00 PM, and the weekend brunch has developed a loyal following among Summerlin locals. The restaurant is open Tuesday through Friday from 11:00 AM to 9:00 PM and Saturday through Sunday with brunch and dinner service. The bistro is closed on Mondays. Reservations are recommended, especially on weekends.

Why Is Ohlala Considered a Hidden Gem in Las Vegas?

Eater included Ohlala on its list of "12 French Restaurants That Should Be on Any Las Vegas Dining Agenda," placing this neighborhood bistro alongside Strip establishments with much higher price tags. The restaurant was created by Richard Terzaghi, owner of the acclaimed L'Osteria Del Forno in San Francisco, and the Parisian inspired ambiance includes a welcoming bar with wines and cocktails. For Pueblos residents, having a restaurant of this caliber just steps from home is a genuine neighborhood perk.

A Local Perspective on Ohlala

Ryan Rose points to Ohlala French Bistro as one of those neighborhood details that surprises homebuyers who assume quality dining requires a trip to the Strip. The restaurant's presence inside Pueblo Place shopping center, right at the edge of The Pueblos community, speaks to the kind of walkable, elevated lifestyle that defines Summerlin living.
