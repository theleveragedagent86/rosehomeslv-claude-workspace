Omelet House Summerlin is the go to breakfast spot for residents of The Pueblos of Summerlin, located right at the corner of Lake Mead Boulevard and Rampart, just steps from the neighborhood.

What Does Omelet House Summerlin Serve?

Omelet House is a family owned and operated restaurant that specializes in hearty breakfast and lunch fare. The menu centers on generous, oversized omelets with a wide variety of fillings, but the offerings extend well beyond eggs. Chicago style sandwiches are a standout lunch option, and the kitchen serves both meat lovers and vegetarians with equal attention. Portions are large, prices are reasonable, and the atmosphere is the kind of comfortable, no fuss neighborhood spot that earns repeat visits.

What Are the Hours for Omelet House Summerlin?

Omelet House is open seven days a week from 7:00 AM to 2:00 PM, making it ideal for early risers and weekend brunch crowds alike. The address is 2227 N Rampart Blvd, Las Vegas, NV 89128, and you can reach them at (702) 315 2828. No reservations are taken, so arriving early on weekends is recommended to avoid a wait, especially during peak morning hours.

Why Is Omelet House So Popular in Summerlin?

With over 500 reviews on Yelp and consistently high ratings across platforms, Omelet House has built a loyal following in the Summerlin community. The combination of generous portions, family friendly service, and proximity to neighborhoods like The Pueblos keeps locals coming back week after week. It fills a niche that chain breakfast spots simply cannot: a locally owned kitchen where the staff knows your order and the food tastes homemade.
