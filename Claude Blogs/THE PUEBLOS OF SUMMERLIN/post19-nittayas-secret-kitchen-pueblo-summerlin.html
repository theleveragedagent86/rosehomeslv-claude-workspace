Nittaya's Secret Kitchen at 8427 W Lake Mead Blvd has earned its reputation as the best Thai restaurant in Summerlin, and it sits just minutes from The Pueblos of Summerlin neighborhood.

What Makes Nittaya's Secret Kitchen Stand Out?

Chef Nittaya Parawong opened this intimate restaurant in 2010 and personally prepares each dish using family recipes from Thailand. The approach is unlike any other Thai restaurant in the valley: dishes arrive tapas style in small, shareable plates made with fresh, high quality ingredients. Nittaya's has earned recognition that places it among the top 100 restaurants in the world, a remarkable achievement for a small neighborhood spot on Lake Mead Boulevard.

What Are the Hours and How Do You Get a Table?

Nittaya's Secret Kitchen is open Monday through Saturday from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM and Sunday from 11:00 AM to 9:30 PM. Happy hour runs Monday through Thursday from 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM at the bar, offering a great way to sample dishes at a lower price point. Because the restaurant is small and consistently packed, reservations are strongly recommended. You can call (702) 360 8885 to book a table.

Is Nittaya's Secret Kitchen Worth the Drive from The Pueblos?

The restaurant is located on Lake Mead Boulevard, making it a quick and easy drive from The Pueblos of Summerlin. With over 2,000 Yelp reviews and an award winning wine list that pairs well with Thai flavors, Nittaya's offers a dining experience that rivals Strip restaurants at neighborhood prices. The intimate setting means every visit feels personal, and the chef's attention to detail is evident in every plate.
