Pueblo Place Shopping Center Summerlin: Your Neighborhood Shopping Hub

Pueblo Place Shopping Center sits right inside The Pueblos of Summerlin, giving residents a walkable destination for dining, coffee, and everyday services. This 34,000 square foot center at the corner of Rampart Boulevard and Lake Mead Boulevard anchors the community with a curated mix of local favorites.

What Stores and Restaurants Are at Pueblo Place Shopping Center?

Pueblo Place packs variety into its compact footprint. Aromi Italian Restaurant serves handmade pasta and seasonal dishes that draw diners from across the valley. Ohlala French Bistro offers authentic French cuisine with a casual, neighborhood feel. Starbucks provides the daily caffeine stop that keeps the center buzzing from early morning through the afternoon.

What Services Are Available at Pueblo Place?

Beyond dining, Pueblo Place covers practical needs that residents rely on weekly. Shepard Eye Center offers comprehensive eye care and optometry services within walking distance. Best One Cleaners handles dry cleaning and laundry with convenient drop off and pick up. Three Dog Bakery caters to the community's four legged family members with fresh baked treats and specialty pet products.

Why Is Pueblo Place Convenient for Pueblos of Summerlin Residents?

Location makes all the difference. Unlike shopping centers that require a drive across town, Pueblo Place sits within the community itself. Residents can walk, bike, or drive less than two minutes to grab a coffee, pick up dry cleaning, or enjoy a sit down dinner. That kind of proximity to quality retail and dining adds daily convenience and long term property value to The Pueblos neighborhood.
