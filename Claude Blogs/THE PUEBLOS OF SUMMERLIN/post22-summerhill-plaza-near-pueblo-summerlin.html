Summerhill Plaza Near The Pueblos of Summerlin: Shopping, Dining, and Fitness

Summerhill Plaza sits adjacent to The Pueblos of Summerlin at Lake Mead Boulevard and Buffalo Drive, offering a major retail hub with over 163,000 visitors each month. Residents of The Pueblos can reach this plaza in under two minutes by car.

What Stores and Restaurants Are at Summerhill Plaza?

Summerhill Plaza, located at 7501 to 7591 West Lake Mead Boulevard, features a strong mix of dining and retail. Cafe Zupas serves fresh soups, salads, and sandwiches for a quick lunch. Slater's 50/50 brings craft burgers and an extensive beer list for evening outings. Pinkbox Doughnuts draws a loyal following with creative flavors and early morning hours.

Is There a Gym Near The Pueblos of Summerlin?

EOS Fitness anchors Summerhill Plaza as its largest tenant, providing a full service gym within walking distance of The Pueblos. Members have access to weight training, cardio equipment, group classes, and recovery amenities. Having a quality fitness center this close eliminates the excuse of a long commute to the gym, something that matters for residents who value an active Summerlin lifestyle.

What Everyday Services Does Summerhill Plaza Offer?

Beyond food and fitness, the plaza handles daily necessities. Walgreens covers pharmacy and household essentials. Sola Salon Suites provides private salon spaces for hair, nails, and skincare services. AT&T has a retail location for wireless and device needs. Einstein Bros. Bagels and Starbucks round out the breakfast and coffee options for early risers.

A Local Take on Summerhill Plaza

Ryan Rose points to Summerhill Plaza as one of the practical advantages of living in The Pueblos of Summerlin. With 163,000 monthly visitors, the plaza stays vibrant and well maintained. That level of foot traffic supports a healthy retail mix, which means tenants are less likely to turn over and residents can count on their favorite spots staying put for years.
