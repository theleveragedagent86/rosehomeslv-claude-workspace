Tivoli Village Shopping Near The Pueblos of Summerlin: Upscale Retail and Dining

Tivoli Village brings European inspired, upscale shopping and dining to Summerlin, and it sits just a short drive south of The Pueblos of Summerlin. This open air destination offers a boutique experience that complements the everyday retail options closer to home.

What Makes Tivoli Village Different from Other Summerlin Shopping?

Tivoli Village was designed to feel like a European village, with cobblestone walkways, detailed architecture, and shaded courtyards. The tenant mix leans upscale, featuring independent boutiques, art galleries, and specialty retailers that you will not find at standard strip malls. The atmosphere alone sets it apart; visitors come as much for the ambiance as for the shopping itself.

What Restaurants and Shops Are at Tivoli Village?

The dining scene at Tivoli Village ranges from polished sit down restaurants to relaxed patios perfect for weekend brunch. Specialty food shops, wine bars, and dessert spots fill out the culinary options. On the retail side, clothing boutiques, home decor stores, and wellness studios cater to shoppers looking for curated selections rather than mass market inventory. The mix rewards browsing and discovery over quick in and out errands.

How Far Is Tivoli Village from The Pueblos of Summerlin?

The drive south along Rampart Boulevard takes roughly 10 minutes, making Tivoli Village an easy addition to any weekend plans. Its position between The Pueblos and Downtown Summerlin means residents can combine both destinations in a single outing. That efficiency matters for busy families and professionals who want variety without spending hours in the car.
