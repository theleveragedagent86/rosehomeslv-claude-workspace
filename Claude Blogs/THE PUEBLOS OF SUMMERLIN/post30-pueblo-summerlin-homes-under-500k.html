Homes Under $500K in The Pueblos of Summerlin

Yes, you can still buy a home in The Pueblos of Summerlin for under $500,000, and the options include condos, townhomes, and select single family homes across several subdivisions. With a neighborhood median around $430,000, a significant portion of the community's inventory falls within this budget.

What Types of Homes Are Available Under $500K?

Condos in La Posada and Red Hills represent the most affordable entry point, with gated units starting in the upper $300Ks. These range from 900 to 1,300 square feet with two to three bedrooms and resort style amenities including pools, clubhouses, exercise rooms, and guest parking. Buena Vista townhomes, ranging from 1,200 to 1,800 square feet with attached garages, also frequently fall under the $500K mark. Smaller single family detached homes on the lower end of the 1,003 to 3,538 square foot range can occasionally hit this price point as well, particularly in subdivisions with more compact floor plans.

What Do You Get for Under $500K in The Pueblos?

At this price tier, buyers gain access to the full Summerlin village experience: proximity to Pueblo Park's 60 plus acres of trails and recreation, quick access to the 215 Beltway and US 95, and walking distance to Pueblo Place Shopping Center. Every home in The Pueblos shares the community's Southwestern architectural identity with adobe colored stucco, flat rooflines, and desert landscaping. HOA membership through the Summerlin North Community Association is included, covering common area maintenance and community upkeep.

How Does Under $500K in The Pueblos Compare to Other Summerlin Options?

Finding a home under $500K in Summerlin is increasingly competitive, and The Pueblos stands out because of its established character. Ryan Rose notes that buyers in this range often overlook The Pueblos in favor of newer communities, but doing so means missing out on mature landscaping, proven infrastructure, and a neighborhood with more than three decades of stable homeownership. The all resale inventory also means no builder markups or extended construction timelines.

Is Under $500K the Right Budget for The Pueblos?

For condos and townhomes, absolutely. For single family homes, the under $500K range narrows the options to smaller floor plans or properties that may need cosmetic updates. That said, a smaller home in The Pueblos still delivers the Summerlin location advantage, established community amenities, and the distinctive architecture that defines this village. Buyers willing to update interiors can build equity while living in one of Summerlin's most convenient locations.
