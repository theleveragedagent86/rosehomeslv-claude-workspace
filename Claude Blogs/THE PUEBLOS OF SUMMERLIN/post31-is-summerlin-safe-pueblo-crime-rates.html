Is Summerlin Safe? Crime Rates Near The Pueblos

Summerlin is one of the safest communities in the Las Vegas metro area, and residents of The Pueblos of Summerlin benefit from some of the lowest crime rates in the region. If safety is a priority in your home search, this neighborhood delivers.

What Are the Crime Rates in Summerlin North?

Summerlin North, which includes The Pueblos, ranks in the 82nd percentile nationally for safety. Violent crime rates here fall 70% to 85% below national averages, with a violent crime rate of just 2.572 per 1,000 residents. To put that in perspective, you are statistically safer in this part of Summerlin than in the vast majority of U.S. communities. Property crime also trends well below city and national benchmarks.

Do Residents Feel Safe in The Pueblos of Summerlin?

Beyond the statistics, the lived experience confirms the data. Roughly 55% of Summerlin North residents report feeling "pretty safe" in their daily routines. The Pueblos features quiet residential streets, low traffic volume, and a well maintained environment thanks to active HOA oversight. Families, retirees, and professionals all benefit from the peaceful atmosphere that defines this 568 acre neighborhood.

What Makes Summerlin Safer Than Other Las Vegas Areas?

Several factors contribute to the strong safety profile.
