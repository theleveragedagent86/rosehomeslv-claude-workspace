Commute Times from The Pueblos of Summerlin

The Pueblos of Summerlin sits in west Las Vegas with convenient access to the Strip, the airport, downtown, and key employment centers across the valley. Here is what commuters can expect from this Summerlin neighborhood.

How Long Is the Drive from The Pueblos to the Las Vegas Strip?

The Las Vegas Strip is approximately 12 miles from The Pueblos, and the drive typically takes 20 to 25 minutes depending on traffic and time of day. Most residents use Summerlin Parkway to connect to Interstate 15, which runs directly along the Strip corridor. During peak morning and evening hours, expect slightly longer travel times, though Summerlin Parkway generally flows better than surface streets.

How Far Is The Pueblos from Harry Reid International Airport?

Harry Reid International Airport is roughly 16 miles from The Pueblos, with drive times ranging from 22 to 30 minutes. The most direct route follows Summerlin Parkway to I 15 South. This is a straightforward commute that makes travel convenient for frequent flyers or professionals who need regular airport access. Early morning departures typically see the shortest drive times.

What About Commuting to Downtown Las Vegas or Downtown Summerlin?

Downtown Las Vegas is about 11 miles away via Highway 613, putting drive times at roughly 15 minutes outside of rush hour. Downtown Summerlin, which serves as the main retail and entertainment hub for the area, is less than 10 miles from The Pueblos and reachable in approximately 12 minutes. Ryan Rose often emphasizes that this proximity to Downtown Summerlin gives Pueblos residents quick access to over 125 shops, restaurants, and entertainment venues without fighting Strip traffic.

Is The Pueblos a Good Location for Las Vegas Commuters?

The Pueblos strikes a balance between suburban quiet and urban accessibility that appeals to a wide range of buyers. Whether you work on the Strip, near the airport, or in the Summerlin business corridor, commute times remain reasonable.
