How Far Is The Pueblos of Summerlin from the Strip?

The Pueblos of Summerlin is approximately 12 miles from the Las Vegas Strip, putting one of the world's most famous entertainment corridors just 20 to 25 minutes away by car. This distance gives residents easy access to Strip amenities while keeping them in a quiet, residential setting.

What Is the Best Route from The Pueblos to the Strip?

The fastest route from The Pueblos follows Summerlin Parkway east to Interstate 15, then heads south toward the Strip. This drive covers about 12 miles and typically takes 20 to 25 minutes in normal traffic. During major events or Friday and Saturday evenings, travel times can increase by 10 to 15 minutes. Many locals avoid the heaviest congestion by using alternate routes through Spring Mountain Road or Flamingo Road to reach specific Strip properties.

How Far Is The Pueblos from Other Las Vegas Landmarks?

Beyond the Strip, The Pueblos offers manageable distances to several key destinations. Downtown Las Vegas sits about 11 miles away, reachable in roughly 15 minutes via Highway 613. Harry Reid International Airport is approximately 16 miles south, with drive times between 22 and 30 minutes. Downtown Summerlin, the closest major retail center, is less than 10 miles from the community and takes about 12 minutes to reach.

Does Living Near the Strip Affect Home Values in The Pueblos?

Proximity to the Strip without the noise, traffic, and density that come with it is one of the key selling points of The Pueblos. Ryan Rose points out that buyers consistently value the combination of Summerlin's suburban atmosphere with a straightforward connection to Las Vegas entertainment and employment. Homes in The Pueblos benefit from this positioning, attracting both primary residents and those who want to be close to the action on their own terms.

For professionals working on or near the Strip, The Pueblos is a practical home base.
