Destinations Pueblo: 55+ Living in Summerlin

Destinations Pueblo is a dedicated 55+ active adult apartment community located at 8600 Scholar Ln, right within The Pueblos of Summerlin. It offers a resort style lifestyle designed specifically for adults aged 55 and older who want comfort, community, and convenience.

What Units Are Available at Destinations Pueblo?

Destinations Pueblo offers one and two bedroom apartment homes ranging from 700 to 970 square feet. These units are designed for efficient, comfortable living with modern finishes and open layouts. The community is a rental property, making it an excellent option for retirees who prefer the flexibility of leasing over homeownership. This setup eliminates the responsibilities of home maintenance, landscaping, and major repairs.

What Amenities Does Destinations Pueblo Offer?

The amenity package at Destinations Pueblo goes well beyond what most apartment communities provide. Residents enjoy poolside cabanas, a state of the art fitness center, organized fitness classes, and gourmet cooking lessons. The community fosters social connection through regular programming and shared spaces designed for gathering. One important note for prospective residents: Destinations Pueblo does not allow cats or dogs, so pet owners will need to consider other options within The Pueblos.

Why Choose 55+ Living in The Pueblos of Summerlin?

Living within The Pueblos puts Destinations Pueblo residents minutes from Summerlin Hospital Medical Center, a 485 bed facility with over 30 specialty areas. Pueblo Medical Imaging also operates within the community, providing outpatient diagnostic services with more than 20 radiologists on staff. Ryan Rose notes that this concentration of healthcare access, combined with quiet streets and walkable parks, makes The Pueblos one of the strongest 55+ locations in the valley.

How Does Destinations Pueblo Compare to Other 55+ Communities?

Unlike large scale 55+ developments on the outskirts of Las Vegas, Destinations Pueblo benefits from its location inside an established Summerlin neighborhood. Residents have immediate access to shopping at Downtown Summerlin, dining along Rampart Boulevard, and outdoor recreation at Red Rock Canyon.
