Angel Park Golf Club Near The Pueblos of Summerlin: Public Golf Minutes Away

Angel Park Golf Club, one of Las Vegas's most popular public golf courses, sits just minutes from The Pueblos of Summerlin. With two full 18 hole courses and a lighted par 3, Angel Park makes golf accessible to players of every skill level without requiring a private membership.

What Does Angel Park Golf Club Offer?

Angel Park features the Mountain Course and the Palm Course, each offering 18 holes designed by legendary architect Arnold Palmer. The Cloud Nine short course provides a lighted 12 hole par 3 experience, perfect for evening rounds or beginners looking to develop their skills. A full driving range, practice facilities, and professional instruction round out the club's offerings, making it a complete golf destination for casual players and serious competitors alike.

How Far Is Angel Park from The Pueblos of Summerlin?

The drive from The Pueblos to Angel Park Golf Club takes just minutes along Summerlin Parkway or local surface streets. That short commute means residents can squeeze in a quick nine holes after work, practice at the driving range on a lunch break, or enjoy a full round on weekends without dedicating half the day to travel. The convenience transforms golf from an occasional outing into a regular part of daily life.

Why Choose Angel Park Over Private Clubs?

Angel Park provides quality golf without the financial commitment of a private club membership. Residents of The Pueblos who enjoy recreational golf can play as often or as little as they like, paying per round rather than committing to annual dues. For families with junior golfers, Angel Park's instruction programs offer an affordable entry point into the sport. The public format also means visiting friends and family can join for a round without guest restrictions.
