Las Vegas Ballpark Near The Pueblos of Summerlin: Aviators Baseball Minutes Away

Las Vegas Ballpark, home of the Las Vegas Aviators minor league baseball team, sits at Downtown Summerlin just minutes from The Pueblos. Residents enjoy professional baseball, concerts, and community events at one of the newest and most modern ballparks in the Pacific Coast League.

Who Are the Las Vegas Aviators?

The Las Vegas Aviators compete in the Pacific Coast League as a minor league affiliate, drawing fans from across the valley for regular season games from spring through early fall. The ballpark features modern amenities including premium seating, craft food and beverage options, and a family friendly atmosphere that makes game nights accessible to all ages. Fireworks nights, themed promotions, and special events keep the schedule fresh throughout the season.
