Schools Near The Pueblos of Summerlin

The Pueblos of Summerlin offers families access to six quality schools spanning public, private, and private Catholic options, with three campuses located directly within the village. This concentration of educational choices is one of the strongest selling points for families considering this established Summerlin neighborhood.

What Public Schools Serve The Pueblos of Summerlin?

Bryan Elementary School is the zoned public school located within The Pueblos, serving students in kindergarten through fifth grade. For middle school, students feed into Becker Middle School, which is a short drive from the community. High school students in The Pueblos are zoned for either Cimarron Memorial High School or Palo Verde High School, depending on their specific address within the village. All three of these Clark County School District campuses are well established and easily accessible from the neighborhood.

What Private Schools Are in The Pueblos of Summerlin?

Two private schools operate right within the village boundaries. The Meadows School is a highly regarded independent institution offering education from pre kindergarten through 12th grade. Known for its rigorous academics and small class sizes, The Meadows consistently ranks among the top private schools in Nevada. St. Elizabeth Ann Seton Catholic School provides a faith based education for students in kindergarten through eighth grade, combining strong academics with a values centered curriculum. Having both of these campuses within The Pueblos gives families the rare convenience of walkable or very short commute private schooling.

How Close Are Schools to Homes in The Pueblos?

The in village location of Bryan Elementary, The Meadows School, and St. Elizabeth Ann Seton means many families can walk or bike to campus. Becker Middle School, Cimarron Memorial, and Palo Verde are all within a 10 minute drive. Ryan Rose regularly works with families who prioritize school proximity when choosing a Summerlin neighborhood, and The Pueblos stands out for offering more on site options than nearly any other village in the master plan.

Local Insight

Parents in The Pueblos often mention the peace of mind that comes with having school age children so close to campus.
