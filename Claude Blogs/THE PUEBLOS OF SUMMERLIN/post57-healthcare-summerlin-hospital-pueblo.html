How Close Is Summerlin Hospital to The Pueblos?

Summerlin Hospital Medical Center sits approximately 3 miles south of The Pueblos on West Charleston Boulevard, typically a 7 to 10 minute drive. This 485-bed hospital provides emergency services, surgical care, maternity and neonatal care, cardiac services, and a comprehensive cancer center. For Pueblos residents, having a full-service hospital this close provides peace of mind, especially for families with young children and retirees in the Destinations Pueblo 55-plus community.
