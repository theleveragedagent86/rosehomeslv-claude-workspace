The Pueblos of Summerlin 89135 Zip Code Guide

The Pueblos of Summerlin falls primarily within the 89135 and 89134 zip codes in west Las Vegas. Understanding what these zip codes offer helps buyers evaluate not just the neighborhood but the broader area surrounding this established Summerlin village.

What Does the 89135 Zip Code Include?

The 89135 zip code covers a large portion of western Summerlin, including The Pueblos, parts of The Paseos, Red Rock Country Club, The Ridges, and areas approaching Red Rock Canyon. This is one of the most desirable zip codes in Las Vegas, known for higher home values, quality schools, and proximity to outdoor recreation. The zip code extends westward to include some of the most exclusive gated communities in the valley, which contributes to its strong property value reputation.

What Are Home Prices Like in 89135?

The 89135 zip code has a wide price range due to the variety of communities it covers. Entry-level homes in The Pueblos start in the upper $300Ks, while luxury properties in The Ridges and Red Rock Country Club can exceed $5 million. The median home price in 89135 typically runs higher than the Las Vegas valley average, reflecting the premium that Summerlin's western corridor commands. Ryan Rose points out that The Pueblos represents one of the best value opportunities within this zip code, offering established Summerlin living at price points well below the zip code median.

What Schools Serve the 89135 Area?

The 89135 zip code is served by several well-regarded Clark County School District campuses. For The Pueblos specifically, zoned schools include Bryan Elementary, Becker Middle School, and Cimarron-Memorial High School. The zip code also includes Palo Verde High School, which serves portions of the area. Private school options within 89135 include The Meadows School and several faith-based institutions. The concentration of quality school options is a primary reason families choose this zip code.

What Amenities Are in the 89135 Area?

Living in 89135 means access to Downtown Summerlin's 125-plus shops and restaurants, Tivoli Village's upscale retail and dining, Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area, TPC Summerlin golf course, Angel Park Golf Club, and the JW Marriott Resort and Spa. The Summerlin Trail System winds through the zip code, connecting neighborhoods with parks and open spaces. Grocery stores, medical facilities, and fitness centers are abundant throughout the area, making 89135 one of the most self-contained zip codes in Las Vegas.

Is 89135 a Good Investment?

The 89135 zip code has historically shown strong appreciation and resilient values during market corrections, driven by limited land availability on the western edge of the valley and consistent demand for Summerlin properties. Homes in The Pueblos within this zip code benefit from the overall desirability of the area while offering more accessible price points than neighboring luxury communities. For buyers looking at long-term value, purchasing in 89135 through an established village like The Pueblos is a strategic entry point into one of Las Vegas's strongest real estate markets.
