The Ridges is an 800 acre guard gated luxury community in the western hills of Summerlin, Las Vegas, widely recognized by Forbes as one of America's most exclusive places to live. With 15 distinct neighborhoods, world class amenities at Club Ridges, and panoramic views from elevations reaching 4,000 feet, this enclave attracts homeowners who value privacy, prestige, and proximity to Red Rock Canyon. ## What Makes The Ridges Different From Other Summerlin Communities? The Ridges stands apart because of its layered security and elevated setting. Two 24 hour guard gates protect the main community, and each of the 15 subdivisions has its own secondary gate. The Pointe, the most exclusive neighborhood, features triple gate security. Situated at 3,200 to 4,000 feet above sea level, residents enjoy temperatures several degrees cooler than the Las Vegas valley floor. The community opened in 2000 and today is home to roughly 3,009 residents with a median age of 45. The zip code is 89135, and the average household income exceeds $160,000. Homes range from approximately 1,958 square feet to over 22,000 square feet, sitting on lots as large as 6.45 acres. ## How Many Neighborhoods Are Inside The Ridges? Fifteen neighborhoods make up The Ridges, split between nine custom home enclaves and six builder communities. Custom neighborhoods include Arrowhead, Azure, Boulder Ridge, Falcon Pointe, Falcon Ridge, Indigo, Promontory, Redhawk, and Rimrock. Builder neighborhoods include Fairway Hills by Toll Brothers, Silver Ridge and Sterling Ridge by William Lyon, Talon Ridge, The Pointe, and Topaz Ridge. Ryan Rose often helps buyers identify which neighborhood matches their lifestyle, since each one carries its own architectural character and HOA structure. Sterling Ridge is the largest neighborhood with 200 homes, while Falcon Pointe is the most exclusive with just one estate lot. Topaz Ridge is notable for offering all single story floor plans, which appeals to buyers seeking accessible luxury living. ## What Amenities Does Club Ridges Offer? Club Ridges is the community's private 9,000 square foot clubhouse and serves as the social hub for residents. The facility includes a full fitness center, resort style family pool, heated lap pool, five tennis courts, pickleball courts, a basketball court, and a playground. A spa, salon, concierge service, catering kitchen, and event spaces round out the amenities, making it one of the most complete private clubs in the Las Vegas valley. ## A Local Perspective on The Ridges As a Las Vegas real estate expert, Ryan Rose considers The Ridges the benchmark for luxury guard gated living in the valley.
