The Ridges in Summerlin is home to some of the most spectacular luxury custom homes in all of Las Vegas, with properties ranging from $1 million to well over $14 million. Built across nine custom neighborhoods and six builder communities, these estates offer an unmatched combination of architectural artistry, privacy, and desert panoramas. ## What Makes Custom Homes in The Ridges So Unique? Custom homes in The Ridges are designed from the ground up to reflect each owner's vision, with no two estates alike. Builders like Blue Heron and Sun West Custom Homes craft residences featuring floor to ceiling glass walls, disappearing pocket doors, resort style pools, and smart home technology throughout. Homes range from approximately 6,000 square feet in neighborhoods like Promontory to over 22,000 square feet in the largest custom estates. Lot sizes vary dramatically, from a quarter acre in Azure to more than six acres in Rimrock, giving buyers extraordinary flexibility. The architectural styles span desert contemporary, Mediterranean inspired, and modern minimalist, all designed to complement the natural landscape at elevations between 3,200 and 4,000 feet. Many properties include private courtyards, wine cellars, home theaters, and multi car garages with climate controlled storage. The community's guard gated security, with 24/7 human staffed gates, ensures complete privacy for residents. ## Which Neighborhoods Offer the Finest Custom Estates? Promontory stands out as one of the premier custom neighborhoods, with 53 homesites on lots ranging from 0.39 to 2.36 acres and homes between 6,000 and 20,000 square feet. Falcon Ridge offers 61 homesites with lots up to 2.27 acres, while Rimrock provides the most expansive land with lots reaching 6.45 acres across just 20 homesites. Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate specialist, frequently guides buyers through these exclusive enclaves to find the right combination of lot size, elevation, and orientation. For buyers who prefer a semi custom experience, builder communities like Topaz Ridge by William Lyon offer all single story luxury homes, while Fairway Hills by Toll Brothers provides upscale townhomes and condos starting around 1,958 square feet.
