The Ridges in Summerlin offers an impressive variety of home styles and floor plans, from intimate 1,958 square foot townhomes to expansive 22,000+ square foot custom estates. With 15 distinct neighborhoods across 800 acres, buyers can find everything from single story luxury living to multi level architectural masterpieces. ## What Home Styles Are Found in The Ridges? The Ridges features three primary architectural styles that define its visual identity. Desert contemporary homes showcase clean lines, natural stone, flat rooflines, and walls of glass designed to frame mountain and city views. Mediterranean inspired estates incorporate stucco exteriors, tile roofing, arched entryways, and courtyard layouts suited to the desert climate. Modern minimalist designs push boundaries with cantilevered structures, indoor outdoor living spaces, and innovative materials that blur the line between home and landscape. Blue Heron, the community's most recognized custom builder, is known for desert contemporary masterworks that feature disappearing glass walls, infinity edge pools, and seamless integration with the surrounding terrain. Sun West Custom Homes delivers similarly striking designs with attention to sustainability and energy efficiency. Builder communities from William Lyon and Taylor Morrison offer polished semi custom options in styles that complement the broader aesthetic. ## What Floor Plans and Sizes Are Available? Floor plans in The Ridges span an extraordinary range. At the entry level, Fairway Hills by Toll Brothers offers townhomes and condos starting at approximately 1,958 square feet, ideal for buyers who want the Ridges address and amenities without maintaining a large estate. Topaz Ridge provides 88 all single story homes, a popular choice for buyers seeking one level luxury living. Mid range communities like Sterling Ridge (200 homes) and Silver Ridge (83 homes) by William Lyon feature two story plans with open layouts, typically between 3,000 and 5,000 square feet. Ryan Rose often recommends these neighborhoods to families and professionals who want generous space without the scale of a full custom estate. The custom neighborhoods are where floor plans become truly extraordinary. Promontory homes range from 6,000 to 20,000 square feet on lots from 0.39 to 2.36 acres. Rimrock, the most exclusive enclave with just 20 homesites, offers lots up to 6.45 acres for buyers envisioning compound style estates. Falcon Pointe contains a single 3.86 acre estate parcel, representing the ultimate in private luxury. ## Finding the Right Fit in The Ridges Ryan Rose helps buyers match their lifestyle priorities with the right neighborhood, home style, and floor plan within The Ridges.
