Azure is the largest custom home neighborhood in The Ridges, a prestigious guard gated community in Summerlin with 115 properties and a well established reputation for architectural diversity. Situated at elevations between 3,200 and 4,000 feet, Azure offers homeowners stunning views of Red Rock Canyon and the Las Vegas Valley across a wide range of lot sizes. ## What Makes Azure the Largest Neighborhood in The Ridges? Azure contains 115 custom home properties, making it the most expansive neighborhood by property count within The Ridges. Lot sizes range from 0.26 acres to 1.2 acres, providing options for buyers who want a more intimate setting as well as those seeking larger estate parcels. The neighborhood showcases a broad mix of architectural styles, from desert contemporary and modern minimalist to Mediterranean inspired designs, reflecting the creative freedom that custom homeowners enjoy in The Ridges. As one of the most established neighborhoods in the community, Azure features mature landscaping and tree lined streetscapes that give it a settled, refined character. Many homes include features like floor to ceiling glass, open concept floor plans, resort style outdoor living areas, and smart home integration. The variety of lot sizes and home configurations means Azure attracts a diverse range of luxury buyers, from growing families to empty nesters looking to downsize within the community. ## How Much Are HOA Fees and Amenities in Azure? Azure homeowners pay a monthly HOA fee of $672, which is among the more moderate assessments within The Ridges. This fee covers access to Club Ridges, the community's 9,000 square foot amenity center with a full fitness facility, two swimming pools, five tennis courts, pickleball and basketball courts, a playground, spa, and salon. Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate specialist, often recommends Azure to buyers who want the prestige of living in The Ridges with a slightly lower monthly HOA commitment compared to neighborhoods like Promontory or Rimrock. All Azure residents also benefit from 24/7 guard gated security with human staffed entry points, one of the defining features that make The Ridges stand apart from other Summerlin communities. The upcoming Amara Golf Club, featuring a Jack Nicklaus designed course, will add another premium amenity to the area when it opens in late 2026. ## Living in Azure: A Local Perspective Ryan Rose appreciates Azure for its combination of value and variety within The Ridges. With 115 homes to choose from, inventory tends to be more available here than in smaller enclaves, giving buyers more flexibility in timing and negotiation. The neighborhood's central location within the 800 acre community means residents are just minutes from Summerlin's top dining, shopping, and recreation while enjoying the seclusion of a guard gated setting.
