Falcon Ridge is a custom home neighborhood within The Ridges in Summerlin, offering 61 homesites with lot sizes that range from intimate quarter acre parcels to sprawling 2.27 acre estates. Nestled at elevations between 3,200 and 4,000 feet, Falcon Ridge combines architectural flexibility with panoramic views of Red Rock Canyon and the Las Vegas Valley. ## What Types of Homes Are in Falcon Ridge? Falcon Ridge features 61 custom home properties with lot sizes spanning 0.28 acres to 2.27 acres, one of the widest ranges in The Ridges. This variety means buyers can find everything from a thoughtfully scaled luxury residence on a more manageable lot to a grand estate with substantial acreage and complete privacy. Architectural styles throughout Falcon Ridge include desert contemporary, modern minimalist, and transitional designs that complement the surrounding Mojave landscape. Many homes in Falcon Ridge feature open concept floor plans with floor to ceiling glass walls oriented to capture mountain and valley views. Custom builders like Blue Heron have left their mark here, integrating indoor outdoor living spaces, resort style pools, and high end finishes throughout. The mix of lot sizes also means the neighborhood appeals to a range of luxury buyers, whether they prioritize expansive outdoor entertaining space or a more refined, low maintenance footprint. ## What Are the HOA Fees and Amenities in Falcon Ridge? Falcon Ridge homeowners pay a monthly HOA fee of $692, which provides access to Club Ridges and the community's full suite of amenities. Club Ridges is a 9,000 square foot facility offering a fitness center, two swimming pools, five tennis courts, pickleball and basketball courts, a playground, spa, and salon. Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate specialist, notes that Falcon Ridge's HOA sits in the mid range for The Ridges, making it an appealing option for buyers who want generous lot sizes without the highest monthly assessments. The HOA also covers 24/7 guard gated security with human staffed entry points, ensuring residents enjoy complete privacy and peace of mind. With the Amara Golf Club set to open in late 2026 featuring a Jack Nicklaus designed course, Falcon Ridge homeowners will gain yet another world class amenity within walking distance. ## A Local Take on Falcon Ridge Ryan Rose considers Falcon Ridge one of the more versatile neighborhoods in The Ridges because of its wide spectrum of lot sizes. Buyers who want acreage comparable to Promontory or Rimrock can find it here, while those seeking something slightly more contained still have excellent options. The neighborhood's position within the 800 acre community provides easy access to Summerlin's dining, retail, and outdoor recreation without sacrificing the seclusion that defines The Ridges.
