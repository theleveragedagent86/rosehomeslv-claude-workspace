Redhawk is the second largest custom home neighborhood in The Ridges, a guard gated community in Summerlin featuring 82 properties on generous lots with views of Red Rock Canyon. With mature landscaping and a well established streetscape, Redhawk offers luxury buyers a refined neighborhood that feels both settled and private. ## What Homes Can You Find in Redhawk at The Ridges? Redhawk features 82 custom home properties with lot sizes ranging from 0.35 acres to 1.3 acres, providing ample space between residences. The neighborhood is one of the more established areas within The Ridges, and many homes here benefit from mature trees and fully developed desert landscaping that adds character and shade to the streetscape. Architectural styles include desert contemporary, modern, and transitional designs, all built to complement the natural terrain at elevations between 3,200 and 4,000 feet. Homes in Redhawk typically feature open floor plans, high ceilings, and expansive glass walls designed to showcase panoramic views of the Las Vegas Valley and the surrounding mountains. Custom builders have incorporated resort style outdoor living areas, gourmet kitchens, wine storage, home theaters, and multi car garages into many of the estates. The combination of 82 homesites and varied lot sizes means Redhawk generally has more inventory available than smaller neighborhoods like Rimrock or Promontory, giving buyers greater selection. ## What Are the Costs of Living in Redhawk? Redhawk homeowners pay a monthly HOA fee of $657, the lowest among the custom neighborhoods in The Ridges. This fee covers access to Club Ridges, the community's 9,000 square foot amenity center that includes a fitness facility, two swimming pools, five tennis courts, pickleball and basketball courts, a playground, spa, and salon. Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate specialist, points out that Redhawk's lower HOA makes it one of the most accessible entry points into the custom home side of The Ridges. All Redhawk residents enjoy 24/7 guard gated security with human staffed entry points, along with the full range of community amenities that define life in The Ridges. The upcoming Amara Golf Club, a $300 million project featuring a Jack Nicklaus designed course, will add a significant lifestyle upgrade for the entire community when it opens in late 2026. ## Why Redhawk Appeals to Luxury Buyers Ryan Rose frequently recommends Redhawk to buyers who want the custom home experience in The Ridges with a slightly more approachable cost structure. The neighborhood's 82 properties provide enough inventory to offer choices in lot size, orientation, and architectural style without sacrificing the exclusivity of a guard gated setting. Mature landscaping throughout Redhawk gives the neighborhood a sense of permanence that newer communities in Las Vegas simply cannot replicate.
