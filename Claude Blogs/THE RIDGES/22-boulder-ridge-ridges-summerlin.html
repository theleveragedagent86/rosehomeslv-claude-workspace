With lots ranging from just over a quarter acre to nearly three quarters of an acre, Boulder Ridge offers one of the widest ranges of home site options within The Ridges. ## What Is Boulder Ridge at The Ridges? Boulder Ridge is a custom home community located within The Ridges guard gated enclave in western Summerlin, Las Vegas. The neighborhood includes 46 properties spread across lot sizes ranging from 0.27 to 0.73 acres, making it one of the larger custom neighborhoods in The Ridges by home count. Buyers in Boulder Ridge have the flexibility to work with their preferred architect and builder to create a fully personalized estate. Situated at elevations between 3,200 and 4,000 feet, Boulder Ridge benefits from the cooler mountain temperatures and expansive desert views that define The Ridges experience. The neighborhood falls within the 89135 zip code and is part of the 800 acre master planned community that began development in 2000. The natural desert landscaping and rolling terrain give Boulder Ridge a distinct sense of seclusion, even with its relatively larger number of home sites. ## How Much Are HOA Fees in Boulder Ridge? Monthly HOA fees in Boulder Ridge are approximately $722, which is one of the more moderate rates within The Ridges. These fees cover the layered security system, including the primary community guard gate and a secondary gate serving the Boulder Ridge neighborhood. Ryan Rose points out that Boulder Ridge offers strong value for buyers who want the full Ridges lifestyle at a slightly lower monthly commitment compared to some of the smaller, ultra exclusive enclaves. Residents also enjoy full membership access to Club Ridges, the community's 9,000 square foot private clubhouse. Amenities include a fitness center, resort style and heated lap pools, five tennis courts, pickleball courts, a basketball court, spa, salon, and concierge services. These shared facilities add significant lifestyle value beyond the private home site itself. ## A Balanced Option for Custom Home Buyers Ryan Rose frequently recommends Boulder Ridge to buyers who want custom home flexibility without the premium pricing that comes with the smallest, most exclusive neighborhoods in The Ridges. The variety of lot sizes means buyers can find a more modestly scaled estate lot or stretch into a spacious three quarter acre parcel depending on their vision. This range makes Boulder Ridge one of the most versatile custom neighborhoods in all of Summerlin.
