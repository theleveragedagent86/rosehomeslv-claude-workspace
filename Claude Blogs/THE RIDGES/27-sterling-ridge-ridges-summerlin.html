Sterling Ridge is a 200 home neighborhood within The Ridges guard gated community in western Summerlin, Las Vegas. Originally built by William Lyon Homes, now operating under Taylor Morrison, Sterling Ridge holds the distinction of being the largest builder neighborhood in The Ridges by property count. Lot sizes vary dramatically from 0.15 acres to 1.26 acres, giving the neighborhood a diverse character that ranges from more intimate home sites to expansive estate parcels. This variety makes Sterling Ridge one of the most versatile neighborhoods in the 89135 zip code. Buyers can find a range of home sizes and configurations to match their lifestyle, whether they prefer a more manageable lot or a property with significant outdoor living space. The neighborhood sits within the 800 acre Ridges development at elevations between 3,200 and 4,000 feet, delivering views of Red Rock Canyon and the Las Vegas valley that define the western Summerlin hillside experience. ## How Much Are HOA Fees at Sterling Ridge? The monthly HOA fee at Sterling Ridge is approximately $642, which is among the most competitive in The Ridges community. Ryan Rose points out that this lower fee, combined with the neighborhood's scale and lot variety, makes Sterling Ridge one of the most approachable options for buyers who want detached single family living behind The Ridges guard gate. The HOA covers guard gate security staffing, common area maintenance, and community infrastructure. Every Sterling Ridge homeowner has full access to Club Ridges, the 9,000 square foot private clubhouse at the heart of the community. The facility includes a fitness center, resort style and heated lap pools, five tennis courts, pickleball and basketball courts, a playground, spa and salon, and concierge services. With 200 homes contributing to the association, the cost per household remains manageable while still supporting the premium level of service that Ridges residents expect. ## Why Sterling Ridge Stands Out Ryan Rose considers Sterling Ridge an excellent starting point for buyers exploring The Ridges because of its scale and diversity.
