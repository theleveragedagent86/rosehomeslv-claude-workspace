Built in 2007, Club Ridges serves as the social and recreational hub for one of the most exclusive communities in Las Vegas. ## What Amenities Does Club Ridges Include? Club Ridges was designed to give residents a complete lifestyle facility without ever leaving the community gates. The amenity list is extensive. A resort style family pool provides the centerpiece for warm weather gatherings, while a year round 82 degree heated lap pool allows serious swimmers to train in any season. The fitness center features modern equipment in a spacious setting that rivals the best private gyms in the valley. Tennis players have access to five lighted courts, including one championship sized court that can host competitive play. Pickleball courts were added to meet growing demand, and a full sized basketball court rounds out the outdoor sports offerings. A playground gives younger residents their own dedicated space, making Club Ridges a true family destination. ## Spa, Salon, and Concierge Services Beyond the athletic facilities, Club Ridges offers spa services and a salon where residents can book treatments without leaving the community.
