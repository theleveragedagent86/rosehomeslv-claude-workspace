Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area is one of the most spectacular outdoor destinations in the American Southwest, and it sits roughly 15 minutes west of The Ridges in Summerlin. With 200,000 acres of dramatic red sandstone formations, more than 30 miles of hiking trails, and a 13 mile scenic drive, Red Rock Canyon delivers a world class nature experience right in the backyard of Las Vegas luxury living. ## What Is Red Rock Canyon? Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area is a federally protected landscape managed by the Bureau of Land Management. The park is defined by its towering red and cream colored sandstone formations, ancient geological features, and diverse desert ecosystems. Activities include hiking, rock climbing, mountain biking, horseback riding, picnicking, and nature observation. The park's 13 mile scenic drive is one of the most popular attractions, offering stunning views from the comfort of your vehicle. A visitor center near the entrance provides educational exhibits, trail maps, and ranger programs throughout the year. ## How Close Is Red Rock Canyon to The Ridges? The Ridges sits on the western edge of Summerlin, making Red Rock Canyon remarkably accessible. The drive from The Ridges to the park entrance takes approximately 15 minutes, depending on traffic. The park offers something for nearly every outdoor interest. Hikers can choose from more than 30 miles of trails ranging from easy nature walks to challenging summit routes. Rock climbers travel from around the world to tackle the sandstone walls. Mountain bikers have access to designated trails, and horseback riding is available through permitted outfitters. For those who prefer a more relaxed experience, the scenic drive provides pulloffs with interpretive signs, and picnic areas throughout the park offer shaded spots to enjoy a meal surrounded by desert beauty. ## Why Red Rock Canyon Adds Value to The Ridges Proximity to Red Rock Canyon is not just a lifestyle benefit.
