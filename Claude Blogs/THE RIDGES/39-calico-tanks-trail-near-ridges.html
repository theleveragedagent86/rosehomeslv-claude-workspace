Calico Tanks Trail is the number one rated hike in Las Vegas and sits within Red Rock Canyon, roughly 15 minutes from The Ridges in Summerlin. This 2.5 mile round trip trail offers more than 400 feet of elevation gain, stunning red rock formations, and a reward at the top that few hikes in Nevada can match: a clear view of the Las Vegas Strip framed by desert canyon walls. ## What Is the Calico Tanks Trail? Calico Tanks Trail is a moderately challenging hiking route located within Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area. The trail covers approximately 2.5 miles round trip and gains over 400 feet in elevation as it winds through sandstone formations and narrow rock passages. The destination is a natural rock basin, or "tank," that collects rainwater and provides a dramatic overlook of the Las Vegas valley. The trail holds a 4.8 star rating among hikers and is consistently ranked as the best hike in Las Vegas. Its combination of geological beauty, manageable distance, and spectacular views makes it a must do for outdoor enthusiasts living near The Ridges. ## How Close Is Calico Tanks Trail to The Ridges? The trailhead is located along the scenic drive within Red Rock Canyon, approximately 15 to 20 minutes from The Ridges by car. This proximity allows Ridges residents to complete the hike and return home within a couple of hours, making it easy to fit into a weekend morning or even an after work outing during the cooler months. ## What to Expect on the Trail The first portion of the trail follows a sandy wash before transitioning to rock scrambling sections that require some hand and foot navigation. The terrain is varied and engaging, with natural staircases formed by layered sandstone. Hikers pass through shaded slots between rock walls and climb gradually toward the tanks at the summit. At the top, a natural basin collects seasonal rainwater, and the overlook provides panoramic views stretching east across the valley to the Strip and beyond. This viewpoint is one of the most photographed spots in all of southern Nevada. ## Is Calico Tanks Trail Suitable for All Hikers? The trail is rated moderate. It is appropriate for hikers with a reasonable level of fitness, including older children and active adults. The rock scrambling sections require some agility, but no technical climbing equipment is needed. Proper footwear with good grip is strongly recommended. Hikers should bring water, sun protection, and start early during warmer months to avoid midday heat. ## Ryan Rose's Recommendation Calico Tanks Trail is the hike I recommend to every client considering The Ridges.
