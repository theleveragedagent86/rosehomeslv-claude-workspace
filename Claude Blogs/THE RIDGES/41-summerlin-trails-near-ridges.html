The Ridges in Summerlin sits at the western edge of the Las Vegas valley, giving residents direct access to one of the most extensive trail networks in the region. With over 150 miles of interconnected walking, biking, and jogging paths, the Summerlin Trail System is a major lifestyle advantage for anyone living in this guard gated luxury community. ## How Close Are the Summerlin Trails to The Ridges? The Ridges is positioned along the western rim of the Summerlin master planned community, which means trail access is essentially at your doorstep. The Summerlin Trail System connects parks, neighborhoods, schools, and commercial areas across the entire community. Residents of The Ridges can step outside their guard gate and join a paved trail network that stretches from the far southern edge of Summerlin all the way north, with connections branching east toward Downtown Summerlin and the surrounding villages. Ryan Rose notes that this trail connectivity is one of the features that distinguishes The Ridges from standalone luxury communities in the Las Vegas area. Rather than relying solely on private amenities, residents benefit from a public trail system maintained to a high standard by the Summerlin community association. ## What Trails Are Near The Ridges? Several trail segments and routes are especially convenient for Ridges residents. The network includes paved multiuse paths ideal for morning jogs, evening walks, and cycling. Cottonwood Canyon Park Trail is a 1.25 mile linear path that runs through a natural desert wash, offering a quieter and more scenic experience compared to the wider paved routes. For those who want a longer outing, the trail system connects to Fox Hill Park, which sits just east of The Ridges and provides open green space, sports courts, and playground facilities. Beyond the community trails, The Ridges is approximately 15 minutes from Red Rock Canyon, where over 30 miles of hiking trails wind through dramatic sandstone formations and desert terrain. Turtlehead Peak and Calico Tanks are among the most popular routes for Ridges residents who enjoy more challenging terrain. ## Why Trails Matter for Luxury Living Ryan Rose often explains that outdoor access is one of the most underappreciated factors in Las Vegas luxury real estate. The ability to walk, run, or ride from your neighborhood into a 150 mile trail system adds daily value that goes well beyond what a home gym or private fitness center can offer. For buyers who prioritize an active lifestyle, The Ridges delivers both the privacy of a guard gated estate community and the freedom of a world class trail network right outside the gate. The combination of elevation, mountain views, and desert scenery along the western Summerlin trails makes the experience feel far removed from the urban core, even though Downtown Summerlin shopping and dining is just minutes away.
