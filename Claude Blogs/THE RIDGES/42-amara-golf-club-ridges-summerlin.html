Amara Golf Club is a private golf and residential development taking shape inside The Ridges in Summerlin. Formerly known as Bear's Best Las Vegas, the Jack Nicklaus designed course is undergoing a $300 million redevelopment that will transform it into one of the most exclusive private clubs in the western United States when it reopens in October 2026. ## What Is Amara Golf Club? Amara Golf Club occupies the site of the former Bear's Best Las Vegas, an 18 hole, 7,194 yard, par 72 course originally designed by Jack Nicklaus. The course featured replica holes from some of Nicklaus's most celebrated designs around the world. Under new ownership, the property is being reimagined as a fully private club with limited membership and luxury residential components. The $300 million redevelopment includes a complete course renovation, a new clubhouse, and plans for both club villas and custom home sites. Ryan Rose considers this one of the most significant developments in the history of The Ridges community, as it will add an entirely new layer of exclusivity and amenity value to a neighborhood that already ranks among the most prestigious in Las Vegas. ## Membership and Pricing Amara Golf Club will limit total membership to just 250 members. The initiation fee is $250,000, with monthly dues of $4,000. This positions the club alongside the most elite private golf communities in the country. The limited membership count ensures low course traffic and a highly personalized experience, which is consistent with the privacy expectations of Ridges homeowners. Ryan Rose points out that the membership structure will likely attract a national and international buyer pool, bringing additional attention and demand to The Ridges real estate market. For current homeowners, the proximity to a club of this caliber adds meaningful long term value to their properties. ## Residential Plans at Amara Beyond the golf course, Amara Golf Club has announced plans for 15 club villas expected to begin construction in 2027. These villas will range from 2,000 to 2,500 square feet and are designed for members who want a turnkey residence within the club grounds. Custom home sites are also planned, with residences ranging from 4,000 to 6,000 square feet for buyers who want to build a primary or secondary home adjacent to the course. These residential offerings will be separate from the existing Ridges neighborhoods but will share the same guard gated security infrastructure and western Summerlin location that defines the community. ## What This Means for The Ridges The arrival of Amara Golf Club elevates The Ridges from one of the best luxury neighborhoods in Las Vegas to a community with a world class private golf amenity at its core.
