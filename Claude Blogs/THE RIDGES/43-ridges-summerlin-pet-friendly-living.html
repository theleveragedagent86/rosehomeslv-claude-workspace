The Ridges in Summerlin is one of the most desirable luxury communities in Las Vegas, and for pet owners, the neighborhood offers a combination of space, trails, and natural surroundings that make daily life with dogs and other pets genuinely enjoyable. From generous lot sizes to direct access to Summerlin's trail network, The Ridges provides the kind of environment where pets and their owners can thrive. ## Outdoor Space and Lot Sizes One of the biggest advantages of living in The Ridges with pets is the sheer amount of outdoor space available on most properties. Lots in The Ridges range from roughly a quarter acre in builder neighborhoods like Sterling Ridge to nearly five acres in ultra premium enclaves like The Pointe. Many custom homes feature fully walled backyards with room for pets to run, play, and enjoy the outdoors in complete privacy and safety. Ryan Rose frequently works with buyers who list pet friendliness as a top priority, and he notes that The Ridges stands out because the lot sizes allow homeowners to create dedicated outdoor areas for their animals without sacrificing landscape design or entertaining space. ## Walking Trails and Outdoor Access The Ridges connects directly to the Summerlin Trail System, which spans over 150 miles of paved walking, jogging, and biking paths throughout the master planned community. These trails are ideal for daily dog walks, offering scenic routes through desert landscaping with mountain views to the west. The paths are well maintained and wide enough to accommodate walkers, runners, and leashed pets comfortably. Beyond the community trails, Red Rock Canyon is approximately 15 minutes from The Ridges and offers additional outdoor options for adventurous pet owners. While pets must remain on designated trails and roadways within Red Rock Canyon, the proximity gives Ridges residents easy access to longer hikes and nature outings with their dogs. ## Dog Parks in Summerlin While The Ridges does not have a dedicated dog park within its guard gated boundaries, Summerlin offers several off leash dog parks in nearby villages. These community dog parks are just a short drive from The Ridges and provide fenced areas where dogs can socialize and exercise off leash. Fox Hill Park, located near The Ridges, also offers open green space for leashed walks and outdoor time. Ryan Rose points out that the combination of private yard space within The Ridges and public dog parks nearby gives pet owners flexibility that many other luxury communities in Las Vegas cannot match. The guard gated environment also adds a layer of safety, as traffic through the community is limited and speeds are low. ## Veterinary and Pet Services Living in western Summerlin means convenient access to veterinary clinics, pet supply stores, grooming services, and specialty pet care along the Sahara Avenue and Charleston Boulevard corridors. Downtown Summerlin also hosts pet friendly events throughout the year, adding to the community feel for residents who consider their pets part of the family.
