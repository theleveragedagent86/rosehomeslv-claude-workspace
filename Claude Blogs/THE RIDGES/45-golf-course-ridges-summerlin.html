The original course was designed by Jack Nicklaus and featured 18 holes spanning 7,194 yards at par 72. Each hole was a replica of one of Nicklaus's most celebrated designs from courses around the world, making it a unique destination for golf enthusiasts. Under new ownership, the course is being completely redeveloped as a private club. The renovation includes course improvements, a new clubhouse facility, and residential components that will add club villas and custom home sites to the property. The club is scheduled to reopen in October 2026. ## Amara Golf Club Membership Details Amara Golf Club will operate as a strictly private facility with a cap of 250 total memberships. The initiation fee is $250,000 with monthly dues of $4,000. This pricing structure places Amara among the most exclusive golf clubs in the western United States and ensures low course traffic and a highly personalized member experience.
