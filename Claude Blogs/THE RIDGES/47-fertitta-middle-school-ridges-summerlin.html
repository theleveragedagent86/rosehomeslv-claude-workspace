Victoria Fertitta Middle School is the zoned public middle school for families living in The Ridges at Summerlin, serving students in grades six through eight. For families transitioning from Goolsby Elementary, Fertitta provides a natural next step within the same community pipeline. ## Where Is Fertitta Middle School Located? Victoria Fertitta Middle School is located at 9905 West Mesa Vista Avenue in Las Vegas, positioned within easy reach of The Ridges and surrounding Summerlin neighborhoods. The school falls under the Clark County School District and serves as the designated middle school for students living inside The Ridges guard gate. Most families enjoy a commute of less than fifteen minutes from their front door. ## How Does Fertitta Middle School Perform? Fertitta Middle School ranks in the top 30 percent of all schools in Nevada for overall test scores. Math proficiency reaches 41.2 percent compared to the Clark County average of 33 percent and the Nevada average of 35.4 percent. Reading proficiency stands at 54.7 percent, well above the county average of 43.9 percent and the state average of 45.6 percent. The school holds a GreatSchools rating of 6 out of 10 and a Niche grade of B. While these numbers sit below Goolsby Elementary's exceptional marks, Fertitta still outperforms the typical Clark County middle school by a meaningful margin. The campus enrolls approximately 1,216 students and maintains a student to teacher ratio of 26 to 1. ## What Programs Does Fertitta Offer? Fertitta Middle School provides a range of academic and extracurricular programs that prepare students for high school. The school emphasizes core academics along with elective opportunities in technology, arts, and physical education.
