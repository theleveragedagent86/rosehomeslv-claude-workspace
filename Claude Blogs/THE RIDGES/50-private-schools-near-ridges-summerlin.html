Families in The Ridges at Summerlin have access to some of the strongest private schools in Nevada, all within a short drive of the community's guard gate. From nationally recognized college preparatory programs to faith based campuses and boutique K through 8 options, the western Las Vegas valley offers a private school landscape that matches the luxury lifestyle The Ridges is known for. ## The Meadows School The Meadows School is the closest private school to The Ridges, situated in the Ridges Village area of Summerlin. The school serves students from pre kindergarten through twelfth grade and follows a rigorous college preparatory curriculum rooted in the arts and sciences. With approximately 850 students and a student to teacher ratio of 9 to 1, The Meadows provides an intimate academic environment. Tuition reaches approximately $25,000 per year for upper grades, and the school is ranked number one among private high schools in Nevada by Niche. Many families in The Ridges walk or drive just minutes to reach campus. ## Bishop Gorman High School Bishop Gorman is the premier Catholic high school in Las Vegas, serving grades nine through twelve with a 98 percent college bound rate and over 105 state athletic championships. The school offers three academic tracks including College Prep, Honors, and Scholars. Tuition runs approximately $12,700 per year. Bishop Gorman draws heavily from The Ridges and surrounding Summerlin neighborhoods, and its proximity makes it one of the most popular private high school choices for families in the community. ## Faith Lutheran Middle and High School Faith Lutheran serves students in grades six through twelve on a 39 acre campus in the Summerlin Centre area at 2015 South Hualapai Way. The school enrolls approximately 1,935 students with a student to teacher ratio of 17 to 1 and tuition of roughly $12,300 per year. Faith Lutheran is ranked number one among Christian high schools in Nevada and provides a faith based academic environment that appeals to many families in The Ridges. ## Alexander Dawson School at Rainbow Mountains Alexander Dawson School covers pre kindergarten through eighth grade on a 33 acre campus at 10845 West Desert Inn Road, placing it within minutes of The Ridges. The school enrolls about 489 students with a student to teacher ratio of 8 to 1, creating an exceptionally small and personalized learning environment. Families who want a boutique private experience for younger children often choose Dawson before transitioning to Bishop Gorman or Faith Lutheran for high school. ## Adelson Educational Campus The Adelson Educational Campus is the only pre kindergarten through twelfth grade Jewish day school in Nevada. The school provides a dual curriculum combining secular academics with Jewish studies and is located in the Summerlin area. Families in The Ridges who seek a culturally enriched academic experience find Adelson to be a unique option within the valley. ## St. Elizabeth Ann Seton Catholic School St. Elizabeth Ann Seton serves kindergarten through eighth grade and provides a Catholic education foundation that feeds naturally into Bishop Gorman for high school.
