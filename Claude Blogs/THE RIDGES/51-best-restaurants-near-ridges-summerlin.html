Residents of The Ridges at Summerlin enjoy one of the strongest dining scenes in the Las Vegas valley, with dozens of acclaimed restaurants within a ten to fifteen minute drive. From elevated steakhouses and authentic Italian to modern Asian cuisine and laid back brunch spots, the western side of the valley delivers a restaurant landscape that rivals the Strip without the traffic or the crowds. ## Downtown Summerlin Dining Downtown Summerlin is the closest major dining hub to The Ridges, sitting roughly eight minutes from the guard gate. Harlo Steakhouse and Bar by Chef Gina Marinelli has become a destination for prime cuts and refined seafood in a setting that balances sophistication with warmth. JING Las Vegas brings globally inspired Asian cuisine to the neighborhood with prime steaks, premium sushi, and a high energy atmosphere led by Executive Chef Joseph Elevado, a former protege of Chef Nobu Matsuhisa. Marufuku Ramen, which opened its Downtown Summerlin location in early 2026, serves authentic Hakata style tonkotsu ramen alongside Japanese kushiyaki skewers in a stylish space. ## Summerlin Area Favorites Just beyond Downtown Summerlin, La Strega by Chef Gina Marinelli offers a romantic Italian experience built around housemade pastas, traditional meat and seafood dishes, and a curated wine list. Honey Salt, the acclaimed farm to table restaurant from restaurateur Elizabeth Blau and Chef Kim Canteenwalla, has been a Summerlin staple for over a decade with elegant comfort food and a standout brunch. ## Tivoli Village and Boca Park Tivoli Village, roughly ten minutes from The Ridges, is home to Echo and Rig, a combination butcher shop and steakhouse that has earned a loyal following. Brio Tuscan Grille adds another upscale Italian option in the same center. At Boca Park, about ten to fifteen minutes away, Kona Grill provides a sushi and American fusion menu, while Grimaldi's serves coal fired pizza in a casual family friendly setting. ## Breakfast and Brunch Morning dining is equally strong near The Ridges. Fine Company by Chef Roy Ellamar is an all day cafe in Downtown Summerlin offering breakfast sandwiches, waffles, house made baked goods, and specialty coffee in a warm, desert inspired space. Makers and Finders, also in Downtown Summerlin, pairs specialty coffee with a Latin inspired brunch menu that includes bottomless mimosas and gluten free options. ## Why the Dining Scene Matters Proximity to exceptional restaurants is one of the lifestyle advantages that draws buyers to The Ridges.
