The Ridges at Summerlin sits within easy reach of several standout Italian restaurants, giving residents a range of options from romantic trattoria dining to upscale Tuscan fare. Whether you are looking for housemade pasta on a date night or coal fired pizza with the family, the western Las Vegas valley delivers an Italian dining scene that rivals many major cities. ## La Strega La Strega by Chef Gina Marinelli is widely considered the top Italian restaurant in the Summerlin area. Located at 3555 South Town Center Drive, the restaurant is roughly ten minutes from The Ridges and offers a menu built around housemade pastas, traditional Italian meat and seafood, and a thoughtfully curated wine list. Signature dishes include the Maine Lobster Spaghetti, Stracci, and Peruvian Bay Scallops. The name translates to "witch" in Italian, and the dining room features a one of a kind mural telling the story of the witches of Benevento. Earth tones, brass chandeliers, and full moon shaped lighting create a warm and romantic atmosphere that makes La Strega a go to date night destination for Ridges residents. ## Trattoria Reggiano Trattoria Reggiano at Downtown Summerlin provides a more casual Italian experience about eight minutes from The Ridges. The restaurant has built a loyal following in the Summerlin community with its approachable menu of classic Italian dishes, fresh pasta, and wood fired preparations. It is the kind of neighborhood Italian spot where families and couples return regularly because the food is consistently well executed and the atmosphere is comfortable. ## Brio Tuscan Grille Brio Tuscan Grille at Tivoli Village, roughly ten minutes from The Ridges, offers an upscale Tuscan dining experience with a menu inspired by the flavors of northern Italy. The restaurant features grilled steaks, seafood, pasta, and flatbreads in a refined setting with an inviting outdoor patio. Brio appeals to Ridges residents who want a polished Italian meal in one of Summerlin's most attractive shopping and dining destinations. ## Grimaldi's Pizzeria For residents craving pizza specifically, Grimaldi's at Boca Park delivers coal fired brick oven pies about ten to fifteen minutes from The Ridges. The restaurant traces its roots to the original Grimaldi's under the Brooklyn Bridge and has built a reputation for thin crust pizza with a distinctive char and quality ingredients. It is a family friendly option that works well for casual weeknight dinners. ## The Italian Advantage Having multiple Italian restaurants at different price points and atmospheres is one of the lifestyle perks of living near The Ridges. From a special occasion dinner at La Strega to a quick pizza night at Grimaldi's, residents can satisfy any Italian craving without a long drive.
