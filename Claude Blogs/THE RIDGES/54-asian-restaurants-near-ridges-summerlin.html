The western Las Vegas valley has developed an impressive Asian dining scene in recent years, and residents of The Ridges at Summerlin are well positioned to enjoy it. From globally inspired fine dining to authentic ramen and sushi, several outstanding Asian restaurants sit within a ten to fifteen minute drive of the community. ## JING Las Vegas JING at Downtown Summerlin is the standout Asian dining destination near The Ridges, roughly eight minutes from the guard gate. Executive Chef Joseph Elevado, who trained under Chef Nobu Matsuhisa for nine years at NOBU in New York City, leads a kitchen that delivers globally inspired Asian cuisine with a focus on prime steaks, premium sushi, and fresh seafood. Signature dishes include sizzling hot rock Wagyu, hand rolled sushi, and seafood towers. As the evening progresses, the restaurant transforms into a lively social scene with DJ driven music and artistic lighting. JING offers happy hour seven days a week from 4:00 pm to 6:00 pm, with dining available in the main room, lounge, bar, or patio. ## Marufuku Ramen Marufuku Ramen opened its Downtown Summerlin location in early 2026, bringing authentic Hakata style tonkotsu ramen to the neighborhood. The signature Hakata Tonkotsu DX features pork based broth simmered for over fourteen hours to create a rich, creamy, and deeply flavorful soup. Beyond ramen, the menu includes kushiyaki skewers grilled over an open flame, delicate gyoza dumplings, and crispy karaage. The restaurant is located at 2010 Festival Plaza Drive and is open daily, with a stylish interior featuring dark woods, lanterns, and a spacious terrace. ## Kabuki Japanese Restaurant Kabuki at Tivoli Village, roughly ten minutes from The Ridges, offers a broad Japanese menu that includes sushi, sashimi, noodle dishes, and traditional preparations. The restaurant provides a more casual and family friendly Japanese dining experience compared to JING, making it a solid option for weeknight dinners or a relaxed weekend lunch. ## Kona Grill Kona Grill at Boca Park, about ten to fifteen minutes from The Ridges, blends Asian and American influences with a menu that spans sushi rolls, seafood entrees, and grilled dishes. The restaurant features a dedicated sushi bar and a diverse cocktail program. Kona Grill works well for groups with varied tastes because the menu covers a wide range of flavors and styles. ## A Growing Scene The Asian dining landscape near The Ridges continues to expand as Summerlin attracts more acclaimed chefs and restaurant concepts.
