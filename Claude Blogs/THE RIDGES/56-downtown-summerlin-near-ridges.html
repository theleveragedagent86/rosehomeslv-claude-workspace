Las Vegas Ballpark hosts the Las Vegas Aviators, the Triple A baseball affiliate of the Oakland Athletics, in a 10,000 seat stadium that draws families and sports fans from across the valley. City National Arena serves as the practice facility for the Vegas Golden Knights and is open to the public for viewing sessions.
