Las Vegas Ballpark brings professional baseball to the doorstep of The Ridges at Summerlin. Located at Downtown Summerlin roughly eight minutes from the community, this 10,000 seat stadium is home to the Las Vegas Aviators, the Triple A affiliate of the Oakland Athletics. For families and sports fans in The Ridges, the ballpark provides a local entertainment option that rivals anything on the Strip without the drive or the crowds. ## The Las Vegas Aviators The Las Vegas Aviators compete in the Pacific Coast League and play a full home schedule from April through September. As a Triple A affiliate, the team features players on their way to the major leagues, which means residents of The Ridges get to watch high level baseball at accessible ticket prices. The atmosphere is relaxed and family oriented, with promotions, giveaways, and themed nights throughout the season. Many families in Summerlin hold season tickets or attend games regularly as part of their weekly routine. ## Stadium Experience Las Vegas Ballpark opened in 2019 and was designed with the fan experience at the center of every decision. The stadium features wide concourses, premium seating options, suites, and a pool area beyond the outfield wall. Food and beverage options go well beyond standard ballpark fare, with local restaurant concepts and craft beer selections available throughout the venue. The open design of the stadium allows views of the surrounding Summerlin landscape and the Spring Mountains to the west, creating a setting that feels uniquely Las Vegas. ## Family Friendly Entertainment The ballpark is one of the most family friendly venues in the valley. Kids' zones, interactive areas, and between inning entertainment keep younger fans engaged throughout the game. Fireworks shows follow select Friday night games, drawing large crowds from The Ridges and surrounding Summerlin neighborhoods. The combination of affordable pricing, a welcoming environment, and a convenient location makes the ballpark a top choice for families looking for weekend and weeknight activities close to home. ## Location at Downtown Summerlin Sitting within the Downtown Summerlin complex, the ballpark benefits from the surrounding retail and dining scene. Residents of The Ridges often pair a game with dinner at one of the nearby restaurants or a walk through the shops before first pitch. The shared parking infrastructure makes it easy to arrive early and enjoy the full Downtown Summerlin experience.
