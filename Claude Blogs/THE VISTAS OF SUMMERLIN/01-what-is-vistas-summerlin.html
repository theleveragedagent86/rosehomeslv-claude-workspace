What Is The Vistas Summerlin?

The Vistas is an 815 acre master planned village in Summerlin West, Las Vegas, known for its European inspired architecture, iconic 48 foot clock towers, and resort style amenities. Built between 2000 and 2008, it was the first village developed west of the 215 Beltway and remains one of the most sought after communities in the Las Vegas valley.

Located in zip code 89138, The Vistas is home to 26 to 28 distinct subcommunities built by nationally recognized builders including Toll Brothers, Pulte Homes, KB Homes, Richmond American Homes, and Woodside Homes. Home prices range from around $400,000 for townhomes to over $2,500,000 for luxury estates, with most single family homes falling between $550,000 and $1,000,000.

What Makes The Vistas Different From Other Summerlin Villages?

The Vistas stands out for its combination of parks, recreation, and architectural character. Residents enjoy three major parks: the 23 acre Vistas Community Park, North Tower Park, and South Tower Park. The community center spans 7,900 square feet and includes a resident only swimming pool with a large slide and children's lagoon, two lighted tennis courts, a basketball court with six hoops, two softball fields, and multiple children's play areas.

The village offers a mix of gated and non gated subcommunities. Monterossa, Paradiso, Palmilla, Talaverde, and Sage Hills are guard gated, while neighborhoods like Bella Vista, Hillstone, Portofino, and Somerset provide open access. This variety means buyers at nearly every budget can find a home here.

Who Lives in The Vistas?

The Vistas has no age restrictions, making it a true all ages community. You will find young families drawn to top rated schools like Givens Elementary (rated 8 out of 10 on GreatSchools) and Sig Rogich Middle School, retirees who love the walkable parks and trail access, and professionals who appreciate the 20 to 25 minute commute to the Las Vegas Strip via the 215 Beltway. The community consistently ranks in the 97th percentile for safety in Las Vegas.
