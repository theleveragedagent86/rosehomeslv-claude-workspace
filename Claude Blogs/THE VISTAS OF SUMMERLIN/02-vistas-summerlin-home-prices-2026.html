The Vistas Summerlin Home Prices in 2026

Home prices in The Vistas Summerlin currently range from approximately $400,000 for townhomes to over $2,500,000 for luxury estates, with a median price sitting between $600,000 and $650,000 as of early 2026. Most single family homes trade in the $550,000 to $1,000,000 range, making The Vistas accessible to a wide spectrum of buyers looking for established Summerlin living.

What Drives Home Values in The Vistas?

The Vistas is a fully built out village with no new construction, which means inventory is limited to resale homes only. This supply constraint, combined with the community's top rated schools, resort style amenities, and prime Summerlin West location in zip code 89138, keeps demand consistently strong.

Nine nationally recognized builders developed the village between 2000 and 2008, including Toll Brothers, Pulte Homes, Richmond American Homes, and Woodside Homes. That builder diversity created a range of home styles from single story floor plans and two story family homes to luxury estates and Sage Hills townhomes, each with distinct price points.

How Do Prices Compare Across Subcommunities?

Gated subcommunities like Monterossa (Toll Brothers) and Paradiso (Pulte) command premium prices, often exceeding $800,000 for larger floor plans. Non gated neighborhoods such as Bella Vista, Hillstone, and Somerset offer strong value in the $500,000 to $700,000 range. Townhomes in Sage Hills provide the most affordable entry point, starting around $400,000.

Lot sizes typically run 0.14 to 0.15 acres for standard single family homes, though luxury properties sit on larger parcels with mountain or Strip views that push prices well above $1,000,000. HOA costs are also a factor: the master association runs $69 per month, with sub HOA fees adding $35 to $115 monthly depending on the neighborhood.
