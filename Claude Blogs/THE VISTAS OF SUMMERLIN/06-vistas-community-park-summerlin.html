Vistas Community Park Summerlin: What You Need to Know

The Vistas Community Park is a 23+ acre recreational hub sitting right in the heart of The Vistas village in Summerlin, Las Vegas. Located at 11322 Parkside Way, this park is one of the most impressive neighborhood parks in all of Summerlin, offering something for every age group and interest.

What Amenities Does Vistas Community Park Have?

This is not your average community park. Vistas Community Park features two lighted softball and baseball fields with a concession building, two lighted tennis courts, and a lighted basketball court with six hoops. For families with younger kids, there are two separate children's play areas plus group picnic pavilions with BBQ pits.

The park also includes a resident only swimming pool with a large slide, a children's lagoon with frog fountains, lounge areas, and a bathhouse. A large gazebo with seating, tables, and a built in sink makes it a popular spot for birthday parties and community gatherings. Walking paths wind throughout the grounds, and a wide open grassy field provides space for football, soccer, or a simple afternoon picnic.

What Are the Park Hours and Location?

Vistas Community Park is open seven days a week from 8 AM to 10:30 PM. The address is 11322 Parkside Way, Las Vegas, NV 89135, and you can reach the park office at (702) 360-1370. Adjacent to the park sits the 7,900 square foot Vistas Community Center at 11312 Parkside Way, which hosts events and activities year round.

Is the Park Worth It for Homebuyers?

Having a park of this caliber within walking distance is a genuine lifestyle upgrade. Homes near the park benefit from the green space, the recreational options, and the sense of community that comes with a well maintained gathering place. Families, athletes, and retirees alike find reasons to visit regularly.
