North Tower Park Vistas Summerlin: A Neighborhood Gem

North Tower Park is a 7 acre green space located at the north entrance of The Vistas village in Summerlin, Las Vegas. Marked by its iconic 48 foot clock tower, this park serves as both a welcoming landmark and a daily retreat for Vistas residents.

What Does North Tower Park Offer?

North Tower Park features a well designed playground surrounded by mature landscaping that provides natural shade throughout the day. The park includes lush grass areas perfect for laying out a blanket, tossing a ball, or simply relaxing outdoors. Picnic tables are scattered throughout, making it easy to enjoy a meal al fresco.

The 48 foot clock tower at the park's center is one of two matching towers that define The Vistas community. These European inspired structures have become synonymous with the neighborhood and give it a distinctive character you won't find elsewhere in Las Vegas.

Who Is North Tower Park Best For?

This park is ideal for families with young children who want a safe, quiet play space close to home. The mature trees and landscaping create a peaceful atmosphere that also appeals to residents looking for a calm spot to read, walk, or unwind after work. Because it sits right at the neighborhood entrance, it's convenient for daily use without driving anywhere.

How Does North Tower Park Compare to South Tower Park?

Both parks are 7 acres and share the same clock tower design. North Tower Park leans toward a single, well maintained playground with mature landscaping, while South Tower Park offers two separate playgrounds geared toward toddlers and elementary age children. Together, they bookend The Vistas and give residents green space options on both sides of the village.
