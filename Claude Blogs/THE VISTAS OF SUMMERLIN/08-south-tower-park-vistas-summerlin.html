South Tower Park Vistas Summerlin: Playgrounds and Green Space

South Tower Park is a 7 acre park located at the south entrance of The Vistas village in Summerlin, Las Vegas. Anchored by a 48 foot clock tower that mirrors its northern counterpart, this park is especially popular with families who have toddlers and elementary age children.

What Are the Playground Options at South Tower Park?

South Tower Park stands out for its two separate playground areas. One is designed specifically for toddlers with age appropriate equipment and softer surfaces, while the second caters to elementary age children with more challenging play structures. This split design means younger kids can play safely without competing for space with older children.

Beyond the playgrounds, the park features lush green grass, picnic tables, and open space for running and playing. The 48 foot clock tower at the entrance gives the park a distinctive European inspired feel that matches the architectural style found throughout The Vistas community.

Is South Tower Park a Good Park for Young Families?

Absolutely. The dual playground setup makes South Tower Park one of the most family friendly parks in Summerlin for households with children under 10. Parents appreciate having a dedicated toddler area where little ones can explore at their own pace. The picnic tables also make it easy to pack snacks or lunch and turn a playground visit into a longer outing.

How Does South Tower Park Fit Into The Vistas Community?

South Tower Park is one of three parks within The Vistas, alongside North Tower Park and the larger Vistas Community Park. Together, these parks provide over 37 acres of green space and recreation across the 815 acre village. Residents living in the southern subcommunities of The Vistas can walk to South Tower Park in minutes, making it an everyday convenience rather than a weekend destination.
