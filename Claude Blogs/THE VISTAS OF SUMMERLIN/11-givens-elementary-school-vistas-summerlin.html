Why Do Families Choose Linda Rankin Givens Elementary School?

Linda Rankin Givens Elementary School is the neighborhood school for families living in The Vistas of Summerlin, located right within the community at 655 Park Vista Drive, Las Vegas, NV 89135. Serving grades PK through 5, Givens Elementary holds an 8 out of 10 rating on GreatSchools and a B+ on Niche, placing it in the top 5% of all Nevada public schools.

With approximately 725 students and a 20:1 student to teacher ratio, the school strikes a balance between community feel and academic rigor. Math proficiency sits at 66% and reading proficiency at 70%, both well above Clark County averages. For parents in The Vistas, the biggest advantage is proximity: most students can walk or bike to school without ever leaving the neighborhood.

What Are the Academics Like at Givens Elementary?

Givens Elementary opened in 2004 and has built a strong academic reputation in the two decades since. The school consistently outperforms district averages in both standardized testing and parent satisfaction. Teachers emphasize foundational skills in literacy and math while integrating technology and project based learning into everyday instruction.

The school also offers enrichment programs that keep students engaged beyond core subjects. Parents frequently cite the responsive staff and welcoming environment as reasons they chose to stay in The Vistas long term. Having a top rated elementary school within walking distance is a factor that directly supports home values in the community.

How Does Givens Elementary Affect Home Values in The Vistas?

School quality and real estate values are closely linked, and Givens Elementary is a prime example. Homes zoned to highly rated schools typically sell faster and command higher prices.
