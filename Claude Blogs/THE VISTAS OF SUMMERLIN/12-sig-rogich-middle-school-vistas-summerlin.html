What Makes Sig Rogich Middle School Stand Out?

Sig Rogich Middle School is the designated middle school for students in The Vistas of Summerlin, located adjacent to the community at 235 Pavilion Center Drive. Serving grades 6 through 8, Rogich holds an 8 out of 10 rating on GreatSchools, a B+ on Niche, and has earned both a Nevada Five Star designation and the prestigious 2015 National Blue Ribbon School award.

Rogich is ranked number one among 59 Clark County School District middle schools and places in the top 10% of all middle schools statewide. For families in The Vistas, this means their children transition from the highly rated Givens Elementary into one of the strongest middle schools in Southern Nevada without changing neighborhoods.

What Programs Does Sig Rogich Middle School Offer?

Beyond strong core academics, Rogich provides a range of programs that prepare students for high school and beyond. The school offers honors level coursework, competitive extracurriculars, and a supportive environment that encourages academic growth. Students consistently score above district and state averages on standardized assessments.

The Blue Ribbon designation recognizes schools that demonstrate high performance or significant improvement in student achievement. Earning that distinction placed Rogich among the best middle schools in the country, not just in Nevada. Parents in The Vistas often cite this recognition as a key factor in their decision to buy or stay in the neighborhood.

How Does Rogich Middle School Fit Into The Vistas School Pipeline?

One of the biggest advantages of living in The Vistas is the complete K through 12 public school pathway: Givens Elementary, Sig Rogich Middle, and Palo Verde High School. All three schools are rated 8 out of 10 on GreatSchools, creating consistency that is rare in Clark County. Students build friendships and academic habits that carry through from kindergarten to graduation.

This pipeline also influences the real estate market. Homes in school zones with consistently strong ratings tend to hold value better and attract a steady stream of buyer interest, especially from families relocating to Las Vegas from other states.
