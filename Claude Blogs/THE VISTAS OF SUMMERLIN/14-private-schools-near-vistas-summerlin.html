What Private Schools Are Near The Vistas Summerlin?

Families in The Vistas of Summerlin have access to several highly regarded private schools within a short drive. Options range from Catholic and Lutheran institutions to independent college preparatory academies, covering grades PK through 12. While The Vistas is already zoned to excellent public schools, the private school landscape adds another layer of choice for parents who want faith based education, smaller class sizes, or specialized curricula.

The closest private options include Elizabeth Ann Seton Catholic School (approximately 3 miles), Adelson Educational Campus (approximately 4 miles), Faith Lutheran Academy and Faith Lutheran Middle/High School (approximately 4 miles), The Meadows School (approximately 5 miles), Bishop Gorman High School (approximately 5 miles), and Alexander Dawson School (approximately 8 miles).

Which Private Schools Serve Elementary and Middle School Students?

Elizabeth Ann Seton Catholic School serves PK through 8 and is the nearest private option at roughly 3 miles from The Vistas. Faith Lutheran Academy covers K through 8, while its connected campus, Faith Lutheran Middle/High School, picks up grades 6 through 12. The Meadows School offers a full PK through 12 independent program, and Alexander Dawson School serves PK through 8 with a focus on experiential learning.

Adelson Educational Campus provides a PK through 12 Jewish day school experience, making it an option for families seeking both rigorous academics and cultural education. Each of these schools maintains small class sizes and individualized attention that appeal to parents looking for alternatives to the public system.

What About Private High Schools Near The Vistas?

Bishop Gorman High School is the most well known private high school in the Las Vegas valley, located about 5 miles from The Vistas. As a Catholic college preparatory school, Gorman is recognized nationally for both academics and athletics. Faith Lutheran Middle/High School also serves high school students and offers a faith based alternative with strong college placement results. The Meadows School rounds out the options with its independent PK through 12 program.

Between these three schools and the public option of Palo Verde High School, families in The Vistas have four strong high school pathways to choose from, all within a reasonable commute.
