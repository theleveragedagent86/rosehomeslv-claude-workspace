Gated Communities in The Vistas Summerlin

If privacy, controlled access, and a sense of exclusivity matter to you, The Vistas in Summerlin delivers with five gated subcommunities spread across the village. Not every neighborhood here sits behind a gate, but several of the most sought after ones do.

Which Subcommunities in The Vistas Are Gated?

Five subcommunities in The Vistas offer gated entry: Monterossa, Paradiso, Palmilla, Talaverde, and Sage Hills. Each one brings a slightly different feel to the table. Monterossa, built by Toll Brothers between 2003 and 2004, features 123 homes with a Tuscan aesthetic ranging from 2,150 to 3,400 square feet. Paradiso, developed by Pulte Homes, leans toward the higher end with spacious floor plans between 3,000 and 3,800 square feet.

Palmilla and Talaverde were both built by Woodside Homes. Palmilla offers 2,500 to 3,400 square feet of living space with four to five bedrooms, while Talaverde is known for its large single story homes ranging from 2,058 to 2,537 square feet. Sage Hills rounds out the gated options as the only townhome community in The Vistas, built by Pulte Homes with homes up to 1,635 square feet.

What Are the Benefits of Living in a Gated Community Here?

Gated living in The Vistas means controlled vehicle access, reduced through traffic, and a quieter streetscape overall. These communities also tend to carry slightly higher home values due to the added layer of security and perceived exclusivity. Homes in the gated subcommunities generally range from the mid $500,000s up to $2,500,000 and beyond, depending on size and upgrades.

It is worth noting that The Vistas was built between 2000 and 2008, so there is no new construction available. All purchases are resale, which means buyers can often find well maintained homes with mature landscaping and established neighborhood character.

Are There Non-Gated Options in The Vistas?

Yes. Subcommunities like Bella Vista, Hillstone, Portofino, and Somerset are not gated but still benefit from the village's European inspired architecture, nearby parks, and access to top rated Summerlin amenities. Buyers who want the Vistas lifestyle without the gate still have strong options.
