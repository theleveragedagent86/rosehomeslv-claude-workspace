Luxury Homes in The Vistas Summerlin

The Vistas in Summerlin is home to some of the most impressive luxury properties in the west valley. With European inspired architecture, ornate facades, and floor plans exceeding 3,000 square feet, the high end offerings here rival custom home communities across Las Vegas.

What Makes a Home "Luxury" in The Vistas?

Luxury homes in The Vistas typically start above $900,000 and can reach $2,500,000 or more. These properties are concentrated in gated subcommunities like Paradiso, Monterossa, Estancia, and Palmilla, where you will find intricate stonework, arched doorways, expansive great rooms, and resort style backyards.

Paradiso, built by Pulte Homes, features some of the largest floor plans in the village at 3,000 to 3,800 square feet with three to five bedrooms and custom finishes throughout. Estancia by Woodside Homes pushes even larger, offering homes from 2,700 to 3,900 square feet. Monterossa by Toll Brothers delivers that signature Tuscan feel with homes up to 3,400 square feet on lots averaging 0.14 to 0.15 acres.

What Luxury Features Can Buyers Expect?

Upgraded homes in The Vistas often include gourmet kitchens with granite or quartz countertops, travertine flooring, custom cabinetry, multi car garages, and thoughtfully designed outdoor living spaces. Many sellers have invested significantly in renovations since these homes were originally built between 2002 and 2006, so updated properties frequently feature modern finishes layered onto the solid construction of nationally recognized builders.

The European inspired architectural theme throughout the village adds an elevated aesthetic that you simply do not find in most Las Vegas master planned communities. Arched windows, decorative ironwork, and Mediterranean rooflines create undeniable curb appeal.

How Do Luxury Homes Here Compare to Other Summerlin Villages?

The Vistas offers a compelling value proposition for luxury buyers. Comparable homes in newer Summerlin villages often carry higher price tags, while properties in The Vistas provide similar square footage and finish quality at a more accessible price point. The trade off is that all homes are resale, but that also means mature landscaping and established neighborhoods.

"You get Toll Brothers and Pulte quality with European architecture in a prime Summerlin location. For buyers coming from out of state, the value here compared to coastal markets is remarkable."
