Why Lemongrass and Lime Is the Thai Restaurant Vistas Residents Love

If you live in The Vistas of Summerlin, you already know about Lemongrass and Lime Thai Bistro. Tucked into the Vista Commons shopping center, this cozy neighborhood gem has earned six consecutive "Best of Las Vegas" awards for a reason. It is the kind of place where regulars walk in and the staff already knows their order.

What Makes Lemongrass and Lime Stand Out in Summerlin?

Lemongrass and Lime brings authentic Thai flavors to a neighborhood that could easily be dominated by chain restaurants. The menu features traditional dishes prepared with fresh ingredients, and the bistro atmosphere feels intimate without being cramped. Whether you are craving a rich panang curry or a bright papaya salad, the kitchen delivers consistently.

What sets this spot apart from other Thai restaurants in Las Vegas is its commitment to quality over quantity. The menu is focused, the portions are generous, and the prices are fair for the level of cooking you get. Six consecutive "Best of Las Vegas" awards speak louder than any marketing campaign ever could.
