The Best Restaurants Near The Vistas of Summerlin, Las Vegas

Living in The Vistas of Summerlin means you never have to drive far for a great meal. From award winning Thai to hearty American breakfast platters, the dining scene surrounding this community covers every craving. Here is a look at the restaurants Vistas residents rely on most.

What Restaurants Are Within Walking Distance of The Vistas?

The closest dining options sit inside Vista Commons, the 13 acre shopping center that anchors The Vistas. Lemongrass and Lime Thai Bistro is the headliner here, holding six consecutive "Best of Las Vegas" awards for its authentic Thai cuisine. Mark Rich's NY Pizza serves up classic New York style slices, and Dunkin Donuts and Subway handle quick bites. For a sit down meal without leaving your neighborhood, Lemongrass and Lime is tough to beat.

Which Restaurants Are a Short Drive From The Vistas?

Just outside the community along W Charleston Blvd, you will find John Cutter for American comfort food and Pei Wei for fast casual Asian dishes. Distill is a solid choice for a casual bar and restaurant experience with a local feel. Hash House a Go Go, famous for its oversized portions and creative breakfast dishes, is another favorite that Vistas residents frequent regularly.

What About Downtown Summerlin Dining Options?

Downtown Summerlin sits just two to three miles from The Vistas and opens up an entirely different level of dining. True Food Kitchen offers health conscious meals with seasonal menus. Harlo Steak and Bar brings a modern steakhouse experience with seafood options. Public School 702 is a trendy gastropub with craft cocktails and shareable plates. BJ's Restaurant and Brewhouse rounds things out with a family friendly brewpub atmosphere. All of these are within a quick drive from your front door.
