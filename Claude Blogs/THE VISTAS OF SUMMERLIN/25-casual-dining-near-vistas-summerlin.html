Where Can You Get Pizza Near The Vistas?

Mark Rich's NY Pizza at Vista Commons is the top pick for Vistas residents who crave authentic New York style pizza. Located at 11700 W Charleston Blvd inside the community's own shopping center, it serves up hand tossed pies with that classic thin crust and foldable slice that transplants from the East Coast swear by. It is the kind of neighborhood pizza spot every community wishes it had.

What Other Casual Dining Is Near The Vistas?

Vista Commons also houses Subway and Dunkin Donuts for quick bites and morning coffee runs. Just outside the community, Pei Wei offers fast casual Asian dishes that hit the spot when you want something flavorful without a long wait. Distill, located nearby on W Charleston Blvd, is a local bar and restaurant with a laid back vibe and a menu built around American comfort food. John Cutter rounds out the casual options with classic American fare in a relaxed setting.

Is There Casual Dining at Downtown Summerlin Too?

Yes. BJ's Restaurant and Brewhouse at Downtown Summerlin brings a brewpub experience with house brewed beers, pizza, and a broad menu that works for families and groups. It is a short drive from The Vistas and pairs well with an evening of shopping or a movie at Regal Cinemas. Downtown Summerlin's open air layout makes even a casual dinner feel like a night out.
