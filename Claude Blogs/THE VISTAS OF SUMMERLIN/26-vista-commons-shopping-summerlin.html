Vista Commons Shopping Center: The Vistas of Summerlin's Neighborhood Hub

Vista Commons is a 13 acre, 100,000 square foot shopping center located right inside The Vistas of Summerlin at the corner of W Charleston Blvd and Desert Foothills Dr. It is the kind of neighborhood retail hub that makes everyday errands feel effortless, with grocery shopping, banking, dining, and fitness all in one walkable spot.

What Stores Are at Vista Commons in Summerlin?

Albertsons anchors the center, giving Vistas residents a full service grocery store just minutes from home. Banking is covered with both Wells Fargo and Bank of America on site, plus a State Farm insurance office. For quick meals, you will find Subway, Dunkin Donuts, and Mark Rich's NY Pizza, a neighborhood favorite known for authentic hand tossed pies. Muse rounds out the dining options with a more refined experience.

Is Vista Commons Fully Occupied?

Yes. Vista Commons is 100% leased and managed by Vestar, one of the largest privately held retail operators in the western United States. That full occupancy speaks to the strength of the location; the center sees over 40,000 vehicles pass by daily. Tenants also include Anytime Fitness for around the clock gym access and Fantastic Sam's for haircuts without an appointment. It is a complete neighborhood services lineup.

Why Does Vista Commons Matter for Homebuyers?

Having a fully stocked shopping center inside your community changes the daily routine. No hopping on the freeway to grab groceries or run to the bank. Residents of The Vistas can walk or take a two minute drive to handle most of their regular errands. That kind of built in convenience is a real quality of life advantage, and it is one reason The Vistas consistently appeals to families, professionals, and retirees alike.
