Downtown Summerlin Shopping: 125+ Stores Just Minutes From The Vistas

Downtown Summerlin is the premier open air shopping destination near The Vistas of Summerlin, located just two to three miles away. With 106 acres, 1.6 million square feet of retail space, and over 125 shops and restaurants, it is one of the largest and most popular retail centers in the Las Vegas valley.

What Stores Are at Downtown Summerlin?

Dillard's and Macy's serve as the anchor department stores, giving shoppers access to major brands without a long drive. Beyond the anchors, you will find a mix of national retailers, boutique shops, and specialty stores spanning fashion, home goods, beauty, and electronics. The open air layout makes browsing feel more like a stroll through a town center than a trip to the mall.

Is There Entertainment at Downtown Summerlin Too?

Shopping is only part of the draw. Regal Cinemas offers the latest movie releases in a modern theater setting. The Las Vegas Ballpark, home of the Las Vegas Aviators Triple A baseball team, sits right on the property. City National Arena, the official practice facility of the Vegas Golden Knights, is also located at Downtown Summerlin. On any given weekend, you can catch a game, see a movie, shop, and dine all in one trip.

How Far Is Downtown Summerlin From The Vistas?

The drive is roughly two to three miles, making it a quick five to seven minute trip from most homes in The Vistas. That proximity gives residents easy access to major retail, dining, and entertainment without dealing with the congestion of the Las Vegas Strip. It is one of the key lifestyle perks of living in this part of Summerlin.
