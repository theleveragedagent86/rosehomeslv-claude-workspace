TPC Summerlin: PGA Tour Golf Just Minutes From The Vistas

TPC Summerlin is a private golf club located at 1700 Village Center Circle, roughly three miles from The Vistas of Summerlin. It is best known as the home course of the Shriners Children's Open, a PGA Tour event that brings world class professional golf to the Summerlin community every fall.

What Makes TPC Summerlin Special?

The course was designed by Bobby Weed and is consistently ranked among the top private clubs in Nevada. As part of the TPC Network, it carries the same standards of conditioning and service found at PGA Tour venues across the country. The desert layout winds through Summerlin's landscape with mountain views, strategic bunkering, and championship caliber greens. Membership provides access to the course, practice facilities, dining, and club events throughout the year.

What Is the Shriners Children's Open?

The Shriners Children's Open is an annual PGA Tour event held at TPC Summerlin each October. It draws top professional golfers from around the world and generates significant attention for the Summerlin area. For residents of The Vistas, tournament week means having a premier sporting event practically in the backyard. Past champions include some of the biggest names in golf, and the event also raises millions for Shriners Children's hospitals.

How Close Is TPC Summerlin to The Vistas?

TPC Summerlin is approximately three miles from The Vistas, making it a quick five minute drive. That proximity is a real asset for golf enthusiasts who want to live near a top tier course without being directly on the fairway. Residents can easily attend PGA Tour events, enjoy the club's dining options, or simply appreciate living in a golf oriented community with championship pedigree.
