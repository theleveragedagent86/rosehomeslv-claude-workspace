Las Vegas Ballpark: Triple A Baseball Near The Vistas of Summerlin

The Las Vegas Ballpark is home to the Las Vegas Aviators, the city's Triple A minor league baseball team. Located at Downtown Summerlin just two to three miles from The Vistas, it gives residents easy access to professional baseball games, concerts, and community events throughout the year.

What Is the Las Vegas Ballpark Like?

The ballpark opened in 2019 and was purpose built for the Aviators, who serve as the Triple A affiliate for the Oakland Athletics organization. The stadium holds approximately 10,000 fans and features modern amenities including premium seating, a pool deck, group party areas, and a wide selection of food and drink options. The open air design provides views of the surrounding mountains, making even a casual Tuesday night game feel like a special outing.

How Far Is the Ballpark From The Vistas?

The Las Vegas Ballpark sits within the Downtown Summerlin complex, roughly two to three miles from The Vistas. That means a five to seven minute drive to catch a game. For families, it is an affordable and convenient way to enjoy live sports without the price tag or traffic of a Strip event. Season ticket holders from The Vistas can attend games on a whim, which is a luxury that location provides.

What Else Is Near the Ballpark?

Because the ballpark is part of Downtown Summerlin, game days naturally pair with shopping and dining. Over 125 stores and restaurants surround the venue, so you can grab dinner before first pitch or browse shops after the final out. City National Arena, the Vegas Golden Knights' practice facility, is also on site. The entire Downtown Summerlin district functions as a sports, shopping, and entertainment hub for western Las Vegas.
