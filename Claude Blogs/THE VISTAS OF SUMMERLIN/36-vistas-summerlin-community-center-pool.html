The Vistas Summerlin Community Center and Pool: Your Neighborhood Hub

The Vistas of Summerlin offers residents more than just beautiful homes. At the heart of the village sits a 7,900 square foot community center and a resort style swimming pool that make everyday life here feel like a permanent vacation.

What Does The Vistas Community Center Offer?

Located at 11312 Parkside Way, The Vistas Community Center spans 7,900 square feet and hosts a variety of classes and programs for residents. The facility serves as a gathering place for community events, fitness activities, and social programs throughout the year. It is the kind of space that turns neighbors into friends.

What Is the Pool Like at The Vistas?

The resident only swimming pool is one of the standout amenities in The Vistas. It features a large slide, a children's lagoon complete with playful frog fountains, spacious lounge areas, and a bathhouse. Whether you are cooling off on a summer afternoon or watching your kids splash around, the pool area delivers a true resort experience without leaving your neighborhood.

When Can Residents Use These Amenities?

The Vistas Park, which encompasses the community center and pool area, is open seven days a week from 8 AM to 10:30 PM. Residents can reach the facility at (702) 360-1370 for details on hours, classes, and upcoming events. Access to the pool is limited to Vistas residents, keeping the experience uncrowded and enjoyable.
