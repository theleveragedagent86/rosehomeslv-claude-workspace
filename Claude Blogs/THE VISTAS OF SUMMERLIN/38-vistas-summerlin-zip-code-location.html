The Vistas Summerlin Zip Code and Location: Everything You Need to Know

If you are searching for homes in The Vistas of Summerlin, knowing the exact zip code and location helps you zero in on listings fast. Here is a quick breakdown of where this popular village sits and what surrounds it.

What Zip Code Is The Vistas Summerlin In?

The Vistas of Summerlin falls within the 89138 zip code. This zip code covers a portion of Summerlin West and is one of the most sought after residential zip codes in the Las Vegas valley. When searching real estate listings or setting up school zone lookups, 89138 is the number you need.

Where Exactly Is The Vistas Located?

The Vistas is an 815 acre village and the first village west of the 215 Beltway in Summerlin West. Charleston Boulevard borders the village to the south, while Far Hills Avenue runs along the north. Alta Drive cuts through the community from west to east, providing convenient interior access. The location places residents right at the edge of Summerlin's westward expansion, with open desert and Red Rock Canyon views to the west and Las Vegas Strip views to the east.

How Far Is The Vistas From the Las Vegas Strip?

The drive from The Vistas to the Las Vegas Strip takes approximately 20 to 25 minutes via the I-215 Beltway. That distance gives residents a comfortable buffer from the noise and congestion of the Strip while still keeping entertainment, dining, and employment centers within easy reach.
