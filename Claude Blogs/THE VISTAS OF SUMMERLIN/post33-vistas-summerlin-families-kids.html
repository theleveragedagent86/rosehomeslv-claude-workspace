The Vistas of Summerlin for Families With Kids

The Vistas of Summerlin is one of the most family friendly communities in the Las Vegas valley, combining top rated schools, three community parks, a resident pool with a kids' lagoon, and a 97th percentile safety ranking. For parents weighing where to raise their children, The Vistas checks nearly every box.

What Schools Serve The Vistas of Summerlin?

Families in The Vistas have access to highly rated public schools at every level. Linda Rankin Givens Elementary sits within the community itself, earning an 8 out of 10 on GreatSchools. Sig Rogich Middle School is adjacent to The Vistas and holds the same 8/10 rating along with a Blue Ribbon School designation. Palo Verde High School is roughly one to two miles away, also rated 8/10, with a 96% graduation rate. Having all three schools this close means shorter commutes for kids and easier logistics for parents.

What Amenities Does The Vistas Offer for Kids?

The Vistas was designed with families in mind. The community features three parks totaling over 37 acres: Vistas Community Park at 23 acres, plus North Tower Park and South Tower Park at 7 acres each. Kids have access to two dedicated children's play areas, two lighted softball fields, a lighted basketball court with six hoops, and two lighted tennis courts. The resident only swimming pool includes a large slide and a children's lagoon, making summer months a highlight for young families.

Is The Vistas Safe for Families?

Safety is one of The Vistas' strongest selling points for parents. The community ranks in the 97th percentile for safety in Las Vegas, with a violent crime rate of just 1.597 per 1,000 residents. Many of the 26 to 28 subcommunities are gated, and the neighborhood layout encourages foot traffic with walking paths that connect to over 150 miles of Summerlin trails. Kids can walk to school, ride bikes to the park, and play outside with the kind of freedom that parents value.

Local Insight From Ryan Rose

Ryan Rose has helped dozens of families find homes in The Vistas and consistently hears the same feedback: the combination of schools, parks, and safety is hard to match anywhere else in Summerlin. Parents especially appreciate that Givens Elementary is walkable from most parts of the community, and the 7,900 square foot Vistas Community Center hosts activities that keep families engaged year round. It is the kind of neighborhood where kids grow up knowing their neighbors.
