Pet Friendly Living in The Vistas of Summerlin

The Vistas of Summerlin is one of the most pet friendly communities in the Las Vegas valley, with walking paths throughout the neighborhood, access to over 150 miles of Summerlin trails, and Red Rock Canyon just minutes away. If you have a dog, cat, or any other companion, The Vistas gives you the space and outdoor access to keep them happy and active.

What Walking Paths and Trails Are Available for Dogs?

The Vistas features walking paths that wind through the community, connecting neighborhoods to three parks totaling more than 37 acres. These paths are ideal for daily dog walks and provide a safe, well maintained route that stays within the community. Beyond the neighborhood itself, The Vistas connects to Summerlin's trail system, which spans over 150 miles across the master planned community. That means you can vary your walking or jogging route with your dog without ever running out of new ground to cover.

Is Red Rock Canyon Accessible From The Vistas?

Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area sits just west of Summerlin and is a short drive from The Vistas. It offers miles of trails through desert terrain with mountain views that make for memorable outings with your dog. Keep in mind that dogs must be leashed on all trails within the conservation area, and certain areas restrict pet access, so it is worth checking current regulations before heading out. For residents who love the outdoors, having Red Rock Canyon this close is a major lifestyle advantage.

Are The Vistas Parks Good for Pets?

The Vistas Community Park at 23 acres is the largest of the three parks and offers wide open green spaces where residents walk their dogs throughout the day. North Tower Park and South Tower Park each cover 7 acres and provide additional options for shorter walks closer to home. The parks feature lighted pathways that make early morning and evening walks comfortable year round. While there is no dedicated fenced dog park within The Vistas, the trail network and open park spaces give pet owners plenty of room.

The trail connections are the standout feature; you can leash up your dog and walk for miles without needing to drive anywhere.
