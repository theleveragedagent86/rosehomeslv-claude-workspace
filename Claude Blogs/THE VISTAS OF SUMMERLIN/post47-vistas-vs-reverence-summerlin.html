The Vistas vs Reverence Summerlin

The Vistas and Reverence are two of the most desirable villages in western Summerlin, separated by just a short drive but offering distinctly different experiences. If you are weighing these two communities, here is a side by side look at what sets each one apart.

How Do Home Prices and Construction Compare?

The Vistas offers homes from around $400,000 to over $2,500,000, with a median in the $600,000 to $650,000 range. Reverence skews higher, starting around $700,000 and reaching over $2,000,000, reflecting its newer builds and premium positioning. Most homes in The Vistas were built between 2002 and 2006, while Reverence broke ground in 2016 and continues to see new construction. Builders in Reverence include Pulte, Toll Brothers, and Shea Homes, bringing contemporary designs and energy efficient features that appeal to buyers who want the latest in home construction.

What Is the Community Feel Like in Each Village?

The Vistas is an 815 acre village with 26 to 28 subcommunities, giving it a well established, neighborhood feel with mature landscaping and a variety of architectural styles. Reverence is a smaller, more exclusive guard gated community set into the hillside, offering panoramic views of the Las Vegas valley and Red Rock Canyon. If you value privacy and a newer, boutique atmosphere, Reverence delivers that. If you prefer a larger community with more variety in home styles and price points, The Vistas gives you that range.

Which Village Offers Better Amenities and Access?

The Vistas provides 37 plus acres of parks, a community pool, tennis and basketball courts, softball fields, and access to over 150 miles of Summerlin trails. Reverence has its own resort style amenities, including a community center and pool, though on a smaller scale. Both communities sit in western Summerlin near Red Rock Canyon, so outdoor recreation is equally accessible. Schools serving The Vistas, such as Givens Elementary (8/10), Rogich Middle (8/10), and Palo Verde High School (8/10), are strong across the board.

Local Insight From Ryan Rose

Ryan Rose finds that buyers choosing between The Vistas and Reverence often come down to budget and timeline. Reverence attracts those who want brand new construction with no compromise on finishes, and who are comfortable with a higher price point. The Vistas appeals to buyers who want proven value in a fully built out community with established neighbors and mature streetscapes. Both villages share the same western Summerlin advantage: cooler temperatures, mountain proximity, and a quieter pace than the rest of the valley.
