# What Is The Willows in Summerlin? The Willows is a 731 acre residential village in Summerlin, Las Vegas, featuring 20 distinct subdivisions, 34+ acres of parks, and homes ranging from $400,000 to over $2.8 million. This established community, built between 1998 and 2007 by builders like Toll Brothers and Pulte Homes, offers one of the most complete neighborhood experiences in all of Southern Nevada. ## What Makes The Willows Different From Other Summerlin Villages? The Willows stands out for its variety. With 20 subdivisions, residents can choose from condos and townhomes, single family homes, semi custom properties, and luxury custom estates. Some neighborhoods like Willow Creek are guard gated, while others like Bella Veranda and Heather Glen are gated communities. Many subdivisions remain open and accessible, giving buyers flexibility based on their lifestyle and budget. ## What Amenities Does The Willows Offer? Residents enjoy the Willows Park and Pool, which features a beach entry pool, two water slides, a fountain, and a bathhouse. The community also includes the Willows Community Center, tennis courts, basketball courts, baseball and softball fields, soccer fields, and playgrounds connected by walking trails. Three parks totaling over 34 acres include the beloved Aesop's Fables sculptures at Willows Paseo Park. Life Time Fitness, a 200,000 square foot facility, sits right next to the community. ## Where Is The Willows Located? The Willows sits within walking distance of Downtown Summerlin, home to 125+ shops and restaurants. The Las Vegas Strip is roughly 25 minutes by car, while Harry Reid International Airport is about 20 minutes away. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is just 2 to 3 miles from the neighborhood, adding convenience for families and retirees alike. ## A Local Perspective on The Willows Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate expert, considers The Willows one of Summerlin's most well rounded communities. The combination of parks, pool amenities, school access, and proximity to Downtown Summerlin creates a neighborhood that works for first time buyers, growing families, and downsizers alike. Few Summerlin villages offer this much variety in a single location. ## Ready to Explore The Willows?
