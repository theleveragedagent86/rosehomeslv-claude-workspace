# Is The Willows a Good Place to Live in Las Vegas? Yes, The Willows in Summerlin is one of the best places to live in Las Vegas, offering low crime rates, top rated schools, 34+ acres of parks, and a location within walking distance of Downtown Summerlin's 125+ shops and restaurants. This established community consistently ranks among the most desirable neighborhoods in the Las Vegas Valley. ## Is The Willows Safe? The Willows reports a crime rate of 27.44 per 1,000 residents, which falls below both Nevada state and national averages. Several subdivisions add extra layers of security: Willow Creek is fully guard gated, while Bella Veranda and Heather Glen feature gated entrances. The community's well maintained parks, walking trails, and active homeowner associations contribute to a neighborhood that feels secure and well managed. ## What Amenities Make The Willows Stand Out? Residents have access to the Willows Park and Pool, which includes a beach entry pool, two water slides, a fountain, and a bathhouse. The Willows Community Center serves as a gathering hub, and three parks spanning over 34 acres provide space for tennis, basketball, baseball, softball, soccer, and playground activities. Walking trails wind through the community, connecting neighborhoods to parks featuring the Aesop's Fables sculpture collection. Life Time Fitness, a 200,000 square foot facility, sits adjacent to the village. ## How Convenient Is the Location? The Willows puts daily errands and entertainment within easy reach. Downtown Summerlin is walkable and offers more than 125 retail and dining options. The Las Vegas Strip is roughly 25 minutes away by car, and Harry Reid International Airport takes about 20 minutes. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is only 2 to 3 miles from the community, providing peace of mind for families and retirees. ## What Ryan Rose Tells Clients About The Willows Ryan Rose frequently recommends The Willows to clients who want a complete neighborhood experience without compromise.
