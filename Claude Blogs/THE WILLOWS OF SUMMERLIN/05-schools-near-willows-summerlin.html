# Schools Near The Willows Summerlin Families in The Willows Summerlin have access to excellent public and private schools, including D'Vorre and Hal Ober Elementary, Victoria and Frank Fertitta Middle School, Palo Verde High School, and nationally recognized private options like Faith Lutheran Academy and The Meadows School. This variety of educational choices is one of the top reasons families choose this Summerlin community. ## Which Public Schools Serve The Willows? The Clark County School District serves The Willows with a strong lineup of zoned schools. Elementary students attend either D'Vorre and Hal Ober Elementary or John and Judy Goolsby Elementary, both offering K through 5 education. Middle school students feed into Victoria and Frank Fertitta Middle School for grades 6 through 8. High schoolers attend Palo Verde High School, which serves grades 9 through 12 and has a well established reputation in the Summerlin area. ## What Private Schools Are Near The Willows? Several of Southern Nevada's most respected private institutions sit within a short drive of The Willows. Faith Lutheran Academy serves PreK through 12 and holds national recognition for academic excellence. Bishop Gorman High School is widely considered the leading private high school in Southern Nevada. The Meadows School, another nationally recognized campus, offers PreK through 12 education. St. Elizabeth Ann Seton Catholic School provides a K through 8 Catholic education option for families seeking a faith based curriculum. ## Why Do Families Choose The Willows for Schools? The combination of zoned public schools and nearby private campuses gives Willows families flexibility at every grade level. Parents can start children in a public elementary school and transition to a private middle or high school without a long commute. The neighborhood's proximity to Downtown Summerlin and major roads makes school drop offs convenient regardless of which campus a family selects. ## Ryan Rose's Advice for Families Researching Schools Ryan Rose regularly helps families match their school priorities with the right Willows subdivision.
