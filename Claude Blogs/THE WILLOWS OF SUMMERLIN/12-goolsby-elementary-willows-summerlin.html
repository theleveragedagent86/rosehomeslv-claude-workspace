# Goolsby Elementary School Near The Willows in Summerlin Judy and John L. Goolsby Elementary School is one of the highest rated public elementary schools near The Willows in Summerlin. With an A minus rating on Niche and a 9 out of 10 GreatSchools score, Goolsby consistently ranks among the top elementary schools in all of Nevada. ## Where Is Goolsby Elementary Located? Goolsby Elementary is located at 11175 W Desert Inn Road, Las Vegas, NV 89135, within Summerlin South. The campus sits just minutes from The Willows village, making it an accessible option for families in the community. The school is named for John Goolsby, past president and CEO of The Howard Hughes Corporation, and his wife Judy, reflecting deep ties to the Summerlin master plan itself. Office hours run from 7:30 a.m. to 4:00 p.m., with school hours for grades 1 through 5 from 9:10 a.m. to 3:21 p.m. ## What Makes Goolsby Elementary Stand Out? Goolsby serves approximately 729 students in grades Pre K through 5 with a student to teacher ratio of 20:1. Academic performance sets this school apart from most in Clark County. Math proficiency reaches 73%, and reading proficiency hits 75%, both well above district averages. The school also offers a Gifted and Talented program, giving advanced learners access to enrichment beyond the standard curriculum. These numbers place Goolsby among the top performing elementary schools in Nevada, ranked 13th statewide. ## How Does Goolsby Compare to Other Summerlin Elementary Schools?
