# Palo Verde High School Near The Willows in Summerlin Palo Verde High School is the primary public high school serving families in The Willows, Summerlin. Built in 1996, Palo Verde has grown into one of the top performing high schools in Nevada, offering AP courses, International Baccalaureate programs, and a graduation rate that far exceeds the state average. ## Where Is Palo Verde High School Located? Palo Verde High School is located at 333 S Pavilion Center Drive, Las Vegas, NV 89144. The campus sits within Summerlin, just a short drive from The Willows village. Named for the palo verde trees that surround the property, the school occupies a well maintained campus that includes a College of Southern Nevada (CSN) High Tech Center and an adjacent Parks and Recreation facility. These shared resources expand what students can access beyond the traditional classroom. ## What Programs Does Palo Verde High School Offer? Palo Verde serves approximately 3,141 students in grades 9 through 12 with a student to teacher ratio of 29:1. The school offers Advanced Placement courses and an International Baccalaureate program, giving academically motivated students pathways to earn college credit before graduation. The average GPA among students is 3.47, and the average SAT score reaches 1240 with an ACT average of 25. Athletics and extracurricular activities complement the academic offerings, creating a well rounded high school experience. ## How Does Palo Verde Perform Academically? Palo Verde High School maintains a graduation rate of 96%, significantly above Nevada's state average. The school has earned a consistent 4 star rating from SchoolDigger and ranks among the top public high schools in the state. Student demographics reflect Summerlin's diversity, with the student body composed of 43% White, 27% Hispanic, 10% Two or More Races, 10% Asian, and 8% African American students.
