# Private Schools Near The Willows in Summerlin Families in The Willows have access to some of the most respected private schools in Southern Nevada, all within a short drive from the community. From PreK through 12th grade, Summerlin's private school options cover every stage of education with programs that include college preparatory, faith based, and independent curricula. ## Faith Lutheran Academy and Middle School & High School Faith Lutheran operates two campuses near The Willows. Faith Lutheran Academy serves students in grades PreK through 5 and is located within The Willows village itself, offering families a private elementary option right in their own neighborhood. Faith Lutheran Middle School and High School, located at 2015 S Hualapai Way, is the largest private school in Nevada and the largest Lutheran school in the United States. The school serves grades 6 through 12 with tuition around $16,275 per year. Faith Lutheran provides a Christ centered education alongside strong academics and competitive athletics. ## Bishop Gorman High School Bishop Gorman High School sits at 5959 S Hualapai Way on a 52 acre campus in The Mesa Village, just minutes from The Willows. This Catholic college preparatory school enrolls approximately 1,470 students in grades 9 through 12 with a student to teacher ratio of 20:1. Tuition runs approximately $12,900 per year. Bishop Gorman is widely recognized as the leading private high school in Southern Nevada, with nationally ranked athletic programs and a rigorous academic curriculum that sends graduates to top universities across the country. ## The Meadows School The Meadows School is a top rated independent school serving students in grades PreK through 12. With approximately 850 students and a student to teacher ratio of 9:1, The Meadows offers one of the most personalized learning environments in Las Vegas. Tuition reaches approximately $25,000 per year for the highest grade levels. The school emphasizes critical thinking, creativity, and character development within a small school setting that allows teachers to know every student by name. ## Other Private Options in Summerlin Additional private schools near The Willows include St. Elizabeth Ann Seton Catholic School (K through 8), The Alexander Dawson School at Rainbow Mountain, Adelson Educational Campus, and Merryhill School. With more than 10 private schools operating within the Summerlin master plan, families have options across religious affiliations, teaching philosophies, and grade levels. ## A Local Perspective on Private Schools Near The Willows Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate expert, notes that the concentration of quality private schools in Summerlin is a major draw for relocating families.
