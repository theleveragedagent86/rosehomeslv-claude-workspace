# Custom Homes in The Willows Summerlin The Willows in Summerlin is home to some of the most impressive custom properties in Las Vegas, with select subdivisions offering individually designed residences on spacious lots ranging from 14,000 to over 30,000 square feet. These are not cookie cutter tract homes. They represent a rare opportunity to own a one of a kind property inside an established Summerlin village. ## Where Are the Custom Homes in The Willows? Willow Falls is the crown jewel of custom home living in The Willows. Tucked behind a second gate within the guard gated Willow Creek enclave, Willow Falls features approximately 80 custom luxury homes on lots between 14,000 and 18,000 square feet. Toll Brothers developed the broader Willow Creek community, but Willow Falls homes were built by individual owners and custom builders, giving each property a distinct architectural identity. ## What Do Custom Homes in The Willows Look Like? Custom homes in Willow Falls and the larger Willow Creek community range from approximately 2,670 to over 9,400 square feet. Floor plans vary widely because each home was designed to the original owner's specifications. Expect features like gourmet kitchens with professional grade appliances, home theaters, wine cellars, resort style pools with water features, and multi car garages. Many homes incorporate Mediterranean, Tuscan, or contemporary desert architectural elements with stone and stucco exteriors. ## What Do Custom Homes Cost in The Willows? Pricing for custom homes in The Willows typically starts around $900,000 and can exceed $2.8 million for the largest estates in Willow Falls. Lot sizes in this area range from 0.17 to 0.69 acres, with the most expansive properties commanding premium prices. Because The Willows was built between 1998 and 2007, buyers benefit from mature landscaping and established streetscapes that newer communities simply cannot replicate. ## A Local Perspective on Custom Homes in The Willows Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate expert, notes that custom homes in The Willows offer something difficult to find elsewhere in Summerlin. The combination of guard gated security in Willow Creek, generous lot sizes, and proximity to Downtown Summerlin makes these properties highly sought after. Inventory moves quickly when a Willow Falls home hits the market, so serious buyers should be prepared to act. ## Interested in a Custom Home in The Willows?
