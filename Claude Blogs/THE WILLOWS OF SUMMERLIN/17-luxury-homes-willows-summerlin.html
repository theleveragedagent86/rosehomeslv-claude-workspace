# Luxury Homes in The Willows Summerlin The Willows in Summerlin offers luxury living across several subdivisions, with high end homes ranging from approximately $900,000 to over $2.85 million. From guard gated estates in Willow Creek to spacious single story properties in Eden Ridge and Heritage Glen, this community delivers premium Las Vegas real estate without the isolation of a standalone gated enclave. ## Which Subdivisions Have Luxury Homes? Willow Creek is the most prominent luxury subdivision in The Willows. Guard gated and developed by Toll Brothers, it contains homes from 2,670 to 9,476 square feet on lots up to 0.69 acres. Within Willow Creek, the exclusive Willow Falls neighborhood sits behind a second gate and features approximately 80 custom luxury homes on generous lots. Heritage Glen, also by Toll Brothers, offers 83 homes ranging from 3,364 to 5,085 square feet on lots up to 0.47 acres. ## What Features Define Luxury in The Willows? Luxury homes in The Willows share several hallmarks. Grand entryways, vaulted ceilings, and open floor plans are standard in Willow Creek and Heritage Glen properties. Many homes feature gourmet kitchens, expansive primary suites, and resort style backyards with pools, spas, and outdoor kitchens. Lots in the upper tier subdivisions provide privacy that is uncommon in newer Summerlin developments, where lot sizes have shrunk considerably over the past decade. ## How Does The Willows Compare to Other Luxury Communities? The Willows benefits from its mature landscaping, established infrastructure, and location near Downtown Summerlin's 125+ shops and restaurants. Compared to newer luxury communities like The Summit or Regency by Toll Brothers, The Willows offers larger lots at lower price points per square foot. The trade off is that homes were built between 1998 and 2007, so some properties may require updates to match current design trends. ## A Local Perspective on Luxury Living in The Willows Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate expert, sees The Willows as one of the best values in Summerlin luxury real estate. Buyers who prioritize lot size, privacy, and established trees over brand new finishes will find exceptional opportunities here. The guard gated security in Willow Creek adds a layer of exclusivity that appeals to executives, retirees, and families seeking a quieter lifestyle. ## Ready to Explore Luxury Homes in The Willows?
