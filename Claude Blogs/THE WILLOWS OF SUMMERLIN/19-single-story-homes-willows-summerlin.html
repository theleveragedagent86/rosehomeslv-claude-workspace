# Single-Story Homes in The Willows Summerlin The Willows in Summerlin offers one of the strongest selections of single story homes in the Las Vegas valley, with multiple subdivisions dedicated to one level living. Prices for single story properties here range from approximately $400,000 to well over $1 million, spanning compact floor plans under 1,200 square feet to expansive ranch style homes exceeding 5,700 square feet. ## Which Subdivisions Offer Single-Story Homes? Several subdivisions in The Willows focus primarily or exclusively on single story designs. Eden Ridge, built by Pulte Homes, features 57 single story homes from 3,091 to 5,757 square feet on lots up to 0.69 acres. Bella Veranda, a gated community by William Lyon Homes, includes 80 homes between 2,578 and 3,001 square feet, with most built as single story. Castlewood offers smaller single story options from 1,158 to 1,667 square feet. Autumn Ridge by Pulte Homes provides 153 mostly single story homes in the 1,432 to 1,628 square foot range. Willow Springs, also by Pulte Homes, rounds out the single story options. ## Why Is Single-Story Living Popular in The Willows? Single story homes appeal to a wide range of buyers in The Willows. Retirees and active adults value the accessibility and reduced maintenance that one level living provides. Families appreciate the open floor plans and backyard access from primary living areas. The Willows' proximity to parks, walking trails, and the community pool makes single story homes here especially attractive for residents who want an active outdoor lifestyle without navigating stairs at home. ## What Price Range Should Buyers Expect? Entry level single story homes in Castlewood and Autumn Ridge typically start in the low to mid $400,000 range. Mid range single story properties in Bella Veranda generally fall between $600,000 and $800,000. The largest single story homes in Eden Ridge, with their generous lots and premium square footage, can reach well into seven figures. Lot size plays a significant role in pricing, as Eden Ridge offers some of the largest residential lots in the entire Willows community. ## A Local Perspective on Single-Story Living Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate expert, considers The Willows one of the best Summerlin villages for single story buyers. The sheer number of subdivisions offering one level floor plans gives buyers options at nearly every price point. Many newer Summerlin communities have shifted toward two story designs to maximize smaller lots, making The Willows' selection of single story homes increasingly rare and valuable. ## Looking for a Single-Story Home in The Willows?
