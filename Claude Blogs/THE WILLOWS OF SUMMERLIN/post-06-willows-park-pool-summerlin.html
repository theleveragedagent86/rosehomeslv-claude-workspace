The Willows Park and Pool: Summerlin's Best Kept Community Amenity

The Willows Park is a 12.7 acre recreation hub located at 2775 Desert Marigold Lane in Las Vegas, featuring a resident only swimming pool with a beach style entrance, two water slides, and a fountain. For families and active residents in The Willows village of Summerlin, this park delivers an impressive range of amenities that rival many private clubs.

What Amenities Does The Willows Park Offer?

The Willows Park packs serious variety into its 12.7 acres. Athletic facilities include two lighted softball fields, a lighted basketball court with three hoops, and a children's play structure. Community restrooms are available on site, making it easy for families to spend extended time enjoying the grounds. The park also includes a community center and bathhouse that serve as gathering points for neighborhood events.

What Is the Pool Like at The Willows Park?

The swimming pool is restricted to residents of The Willows and features a beach style entrance, which allows young children to wade in gradually. Two water slides add excitement for kids and teens, while a fountain provides a resort like atmosphere. The bathhouse offers convenient changing facilities, so residents can walk or bike over without needing to prepare ahead of time.

Is The Willows Park Good for Kids and Families?

Absolutely. Between the play structure, the pool complex, and the open fields, children of all ages will find something to enjoy. The lighted sports courts mean older kids and adults can play basketball or softball well into the evening. With 34 total acres of parkland spread across three parks in The Willows, this village is one of the most recreation friendly communities in all of Summerlin.
