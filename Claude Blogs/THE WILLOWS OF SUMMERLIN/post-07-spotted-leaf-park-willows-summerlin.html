Spotted Leaf Park in The Willows Summerlin: 10.5 Acres of Sports and Open Space

Spotted Leaf Park is a 10.5 acre recreational park located at 2955 Spotted Leaf Lane in The Willows village of Summerlin, featuring lighted soccer fields, tennis courts, basketball, and a children's play area. It is one of three parks totaling 34 acres that make The Willows one of the most park rich neighborhoods in Las Vegas.

What Facilities Are at Spotted Leaf Park?

Spotted Leaf Park offers two lighted soccer fields, two lighted tennis courts, and one lighted basketball court. A dedicated children's play area gives younger residents a safe place to burn energy. Picnic pavilions are scattered throughout the park, making it a popular spot for birthday parties and neighborhood gatherings. The generous open space areas provide room for informal activities like frisbee, yoga, or simply relaxing on the grass.

Is Spotted Leaf Park Lighted for Evening Use?

Yes, the soccer fields, tennis courts, and basketball court at Spotted Leaf Park are all lighted. This is a significant advantage for residents who work during the day and want to stay active after sunset. Evening soccer games and tennis matches are a regular occurrence here, especially during the cooler months when outdoor activity picks up across the Las Vegas valley.

How Does Spotted Leaf Park Compare to Other Willows Parks?

The Willows village contains three parks covering more than 34 acres across a 731 acre community. While The Willows Park focuses on its pool and softball fields, and Willows Paseo Park offers a unique linear trail experience with storybook sculptures, Spotted Leaf Park is the go to destination for soccer and tennis. Together, these three parks ensure residents are never more than a short walk from quality recreation.
