Willows Paseo Park and Aesop's Fables Sculptures in Summerlin

Willows Paseo Park is an 11 acre linear park that connects the east and west regions of The Willows village in Summerlin, featuring four oversized storybook sculptures inspired by Aesop's Fables. This unique park combines walking trails, sports facilities, and public art into one of the most distinctive green spaces in all of Las Vegas.

What Are the Aesop's Fables Sculptures at Willows Paseo Park?

The park features four oversized storybook sculptures depicting scenes from classic Aesop's Fables, including "The Lion and The Mouse" and "The Tortoise and The Hare." These installations are scaled large enough for children to interact with, making them a favorite stop for families walking through the paseo. The sculptures add a whimsical, storybook character to the neighborhood that sets The Willows apart from other Summerlin villages.

What Other Amenities Does Willows Paseo Park Have?

Beyond the sculptures, Willows Paseo Park delivers a full lineup of recreation options. Baseball fields and basketball courts serve athletes of all ages. Two lighted tennis courts allow for evening play. A children's play area gives younger kids a dedicated space, while walking trails meander through the entire 11 acre stretch. The linear design of the park creates a natural corridor that residents use for daily walks, jogs, and bike rides.

How Does the Paseo Connect The Willows Village?

The Willows Paseo Park runs the length of the village, linking the east and west sides through a continuous green corridor. This linear layout means residents across all 20 subdivisions in The Willows have convenient access to the trail and its amenities. The paseo concept encourages walkability and gives the neighborhood a connected, pedestrian friendly feel that cars simply cannot replicate.

Local Insight from Ryan Rose

Ryan Rose highlights Willows Paseo Park as one of the hidden gems of Summerlin. The Aesop's Fables sculptures are a conversation starter at every open house in The Willows, and families with young children consistently mention them as a reason they love the neighborhood. The linear park design also adds real value by creating a walkable spine through the entire community.
