Dog Friendly Living in The Willows Summerlin

The Willows in Summerlin is an excellent neighborhood for dog owners, with walking trails throughout the village, 34 plus acres of parkland, and open green spaces designed for an active, pet friendly lifestyle. If you are searching for a Las Vegas community where your dog can thrive, The Willows checks every box.

What Makes The Willows Dog Friendly?

Three parks totaling more than 34 acres give dog owners plenty of room to walk, exercise, and socialize their pets. The Willows Park Trail, running 0.5 to 1.4 miles from Willows Park to Town Center Drive, is specifically popular with dog walkers. Open space areas within Spotted Leaf Park and along the Willows Paseo Linear Trail provide additional room for dogs to stretch their legs on leash.

Which Trails Are Best for Dog Walking in The Willows?

The Willows Park Trail is the top choice for daily dog walks, offering a manageable distance with flat terrain and a well maintained surface. The Willows Paseo Linear Trail is another strong option, running the entire length of the village through an 11 acre green corridor. Both trails connect to the broader Summerlin Trail System, which covers more than 200 miles and lets you vary your route to keep walks interesting for you and your dog.

Are There Parks with Open Space for Dogs?

Yes. Spotted Leaf Park at 2955 Spotted Leaf Lane offers 10.5 acres that include dedicated open space, picnic pavilions, and play areas. The Willows Paseo Park adds 11 acres of linear parkland with additional open areas. Across the village's 731 acres and 20 subdivisions, dog owners will find that green space is never far from their front door.

Ryan Rose frequently works with buyers who consider their dog's quality of life a deciding factor in choosing a neighborhood. The Willows consistently ranks as one of his top recommendations for pet owners in Summerlin. The combination of connected trails, generous open space, and a walkable village layout means daily dog walks become a pleasure rather than a chore.
