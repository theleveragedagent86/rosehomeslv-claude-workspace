Willow Creek: Summerlin's Premier Guard Gated Neighborhood in The Willows

Willow Creek stands as the most prestigious address within The Willows village of Summerlin. This guard gated community, master planned by Toll Brothers, delivers luxury living with a level of privacy and security that places it among the top neighborhoods in the Las Vegas valley.

What Makes Willow Creek Special?

Willow Creek is one of the few guard gated communities within The Willows. A staffed guard gate controls all vehicle access, creating a secure perimeter around the entire neighborhood. Homes inside Willow Creek range from 2,670 to an impressive 9,476 square feet, sitting on lots between 0.17 and 0.69 acres. The sub association HOA fee runs between $449 and $505 per month, which covers the guard gate staffing, landscape maintenance, and community upkeep beyond what the master association provides.

Neighborhoods Inside Willow Creek

Willow Creek is not a single subdivision. It contains four distinct neighborhoods, each with its own character. Wood Glen features 76 homes built by Toll Brothers, ranging from 2,670 to 3,792 square feet on lots of 0.17 to 0.3 acres. Heritage Glen, also by Toll Brothers, offers 83 homes between 3,364 and 5,085 square feet on larger lots of 0.22 to 0.47 acres.

Eden Ridge was built by Pulte and includes 57 single story homes spanning 3,091 to 5,757 square feet on lots from 0.26 to 0.69 acres. For buyers seeking the ultimate in privacy, Willow Falls sits behind a second gate within Willow Creek. This double gated pocket holds 80 custom luxury homes on lots measuring 14,000 to 18,000 square feet.

Who Lives in Willow Creek?

Willow Creek attracts established professionals, retirees, and families who value both security and space. The single story options in Eden Ridge appeal to buyers who want one level living without sacrificing square footage. Heritage Glen and Wood Glen draw families who need room to grow. Willow Falls tends to attract buyers at the top of the market who want a custom home in a controlled setting.
