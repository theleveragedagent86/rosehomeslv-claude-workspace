Bella Veranda and Heather Glen: Gated Neighborhoods in The Willows Summerlin

Not every buyer in The Willows needs the full guard gated experience of Willow Creek. Bella Veranda and Heather Glen offer automated gate access at a more accessible price point, giving residents controlled entry without the higher sub association fees that come with staffed security.

Bella Veranda: Single Story Living Behind a Gate

Bella Veranda was built by William Lyon Homes and contains 80 homes constructed between 2000 and 2001. Floor plans range from 2,578 to 3,001 square feet, and the majority of homes are single story. This makes Bella Veranda a strong fit for buyers who want one level living with generous square footage inside a gated perimeter.

The neighborhood's layout is compact enough to feel intimate, yet the home sizes rival many of the larger floor plans found elsewhere in The Willows. With only 80 homes, inventory in Bella Veranda turns over slowly, which means listings here tend to attract serious interest quickly.

Heather Glen: Spacious Homes With Gate Access

Heather Glen is also a gated subdivision within The Willows, offering homes from 2,000 to over 4,000 square feet. Built in the early 2000s, this neighborhood features a mix of single and two story floor plans that appeal to families and buyers who need extra bedrooms or flexible living space.

The size range in Heather Glen gives buyers options that span from comfortable mid range homes to expansive residences that compete with some of Willow Creek's offerings. The key difference is the gate style: Heather Glen uses an automated entry system rather than a staffed guard booth, which keeps HOA costs lower while still providing a barrier to through traffic.

How Do These Compare to Willow Creek?

The main tradeoffs come down to security level and monthly cost. Willow Creek's guard gated sub association runs $449 to $505 per month. Bella Veranda and Heather Glen carry lower sub association fees because automated gates cost less to maintain than a 24 hour staffed booth. All three neighborhoods share the same master association amenities funded by the $76 per month village HOA, including the resident only pool, 34 plus acres of parks, and the Willows Community Center.

Local Insight from Ryan Rose

Ryan Rose often recommends Bella Veranda and Heather Glen to buyers who want gated living without the premium of a guard gated community. Both neighborhoods deliver strong home sizes, quality construction from the early 2000s, and the full Willows amenity package. Ryan points out that these two neighborhoods represent some of the best value in the village for buyers who prioritize controlled access and square footage.
