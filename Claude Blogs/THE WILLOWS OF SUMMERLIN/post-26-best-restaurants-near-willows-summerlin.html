Best Restaurants Near The Willows in Summerlin

Residents of The Willows in Summerlin have some of the best dining in the Las Vegas Valley right at their doorstep. Downtown Summerlin sits adjacent to the village, putting more than 125 shops and restaurants within a short drive or even a quick walk from many Willows homes.

Downtown Summerlin: The Dining Hub

Downtown Summerlin serves as the primary dining destination for Willows residents. This open air retail and entertainment district lines Festival Plaza Drive with dozens of restaurants spanning every cuisine and price point. From upscale steakhouses to casual neighborhood spots, the variety ensures families, couples, and solo diners all find something that fits.

Standout options include Harlo Steakhouse and Bar, where Chef Gina Marinelli delivers elevated cuts in a refined setting. JING Las Vegas brings modern Asian flavors with a high energy atmosphere, while Grape Street Cafe offers California bistro classics that Summerlin locals have loved for over 26 years. La Neta Cocina serves up refined Mexican fare with standout enchiladas and tacos.

Upscale Steakhouse Options

Steak lovers near The Willows have exceptional choices. Echo and Rig Butcher and Steakhouse at Tivoli Village combines an in house butcher shop with a full service restaurant, serving prime cuts like wagyu filet, ribeye cap, and their signature Spencer steak. Harlo Steakhouse and Bar on Festival Plaza Drive offers prime ribeye, filet mignon, and fresh seafood in an atmosphere of dark wood and polished marble.

Casual and Family Friendly Spots

Not every night calls for white tablecloths. BJ's Restaurant and Brewhouse delivers deep dish pizzas, burgers, and craft beer in a relaxed setting that families appreciate. Yard House features one of the largest draft beer selections in the valley alongside a broad American menu. Frankie's Uptown draws crowds with creative comfort food, including short rib poutine and truffle burgers. Sushi Loca rounds out the options with premium sushi, sashimi, and specialty rolls for Japanese food fans.
