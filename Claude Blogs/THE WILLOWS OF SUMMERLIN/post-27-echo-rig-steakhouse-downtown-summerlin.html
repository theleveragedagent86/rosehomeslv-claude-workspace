Echo and Rig Butcher and Steakhouse Near Summerlin

Echo and Rig Butcher and Steakhouse brings a concept you will not find at most Las Vegas restaurants: an in house butcher shop connected directly to a full service dining room. Located at Tivoli Village just minutes from The Willows in Summerlin, this two story steakhouse has become one of the most popular dining destinations on the west side of the valley.

The Butcher Shop and Restaurant Concept

What sets Echo and Rig apart starts before you even sit down. The ground floor houses a working butcher shop where guests can purchase premium cuts to take home. Upstairs, the restaurant transforms those same quality meats into a menu of over 90 items. The kitchen sources USDA prime beef and prepares everything from dry aged steaks to house made sausages daily.

Signature cuts include the Spencer steak, an old school cut taken from the center of the ribeye, along with wagyu filet tenderloin, ribeye cap, hanger steak, and New York strip. The butcher blend burger uses USDA prime beef served with bibb lettuce, Brandywine tomato, aioli, and red onion. Small plates like grilled octopus with gigante beans round out the menu for those who want to explore beyond steak.

Atmosphere and Dining Experience

The two story layout at Tivoli Village gives Echo and Rig a sense of scale that most neighborhood steakhouses lack. Picturesque patios provide outdoor seating that takes advantage of the Summerlin weather, particularly during spring and fall. The interior balances industrial elements with warm wood tones, creating an atmosphere that works for date nights, business dinners, and celebratory gatherings alike.

The cocktail program features mixologist crafted drinks built around fresh ingredients. Weekend brunch service runs Saturday and Sunday from 10 AM to 3 PM, offering yet another reason to visit beyond the dinner menu.

Location and Hours

Echo and Rig sits at 440 S Rampart Blvd in Tivoli Village, a short drive from The Willows. Lunch is served Monday through Friday from 11 AM to 3:45 PM. Dinner runs Monday through Thursday and Sunday from 4 PM to 10 PM, with Friday and Saturday extending to 11 PM. Reservations are recommended, especially on weekends.
