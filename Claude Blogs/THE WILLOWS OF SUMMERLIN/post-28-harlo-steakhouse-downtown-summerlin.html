Harlo Steakhouse and Bar at Downtown Summerlin

Harlo Steakhouse and Bar delivers a refined steakhouse experience right at Downtown Summerlin, making it one of the closest upscale dining options for residents of The Willows. Created by restaurateur Jeffrey Fine and led by Chef Gina Marinelli, Harlo brings old school elegance to the heart of Summerlin's premier retail district.

The Menu at Harlo

Chef Gina Marinelli curates a menu that showcases premium cuts of beef prepared with precision. The Prime Ribeye and Filet Mignon anchor the steak selection, with each cut cooked to order. Beyond beef, the kitchen offers fresh seafood options, inventive appetizers, and a dessert menu that rounds out the experience for guests who want more than steak.

The bar program stands on its own. Harlo Hour runs from 4 PM to 6 PM nightly, with an early start at 3 PM on Fridays. This gives Willows residents a solid after work option that pairs well crafted cocktails with a sophisticated setting without the drive to the Strip.

Atmosphere and Design

Walk into Harlo and the design immediately signals a different level of dining. Dark wood, polished marble, and ambient lighting create an opulent atmosphere that recalls classic American steakhouses. The dining room balances intimacy with energy, making it suitable for anniversaries, business dinners, and nights when you simply want an elevated meal.

One notable touch: Harlo provides its own valet service. For an off Strip neighborhood restaurant, dedicated valet is uncommon and speaks to the level of experience the team aims to deliver. The location at 1720 Festival Plaza Drive puts it within the walkable core of Downtown Summerlin.

Hours and Reservations

Harlo serves dinner Sunday through Thursday from 5 PM to 9 PM, with extended hours on Friday and Saturday until 10 PM. Reservations are strongly recommended, particularly on weekends when the restaurant fills quickly. The proximity to Downtown Summerlin's shops and entertainment makes it easy to pair dinner with an evening out.

Local Insight from Ryan Rose

Ryan Rose highlights Harlo as a prime example of how Summerlin's dining scene has matured. Having a chef driven steakhouse of this caliber steps from Downtown Summerlin's shops gives Willows residents an experience that previously required a trip to the Strip. Ryan considers the walkability factor a real selling point when showing homes in The Willows to buyers who value lifestyle convenience.
