Boca Park Fashion Village Near The Willows in Summerlin

The Willows residents looking for upscale retail and popular national brands will find Boca Park Fashion Village just two to three miles from their front door. This open air shopping center on South Rampart Boulevard delivers a curated mix of fashion, home decor, and dining that complements the nearby Downtown Summerlin experience.

What Stores Are at Boca Park Fashion Village?

Boca Park brings together a lineup of retailers that appeals to shoppers who want variety without the sprawl of a regional mall. Anchor tenants include Target and REI, covering everyday essentials and outdoor gear. Fashion forward brands like Anthropologie, GAP, and Sephora occupy storefronts alongside home furnishing destinations like Pottery Barn and Pottery Barn Kids.

The center also hosts a collection of specialty shops that rotate seasonally. From athletic wear to home accessories, Boca Park fills a niche between big box retail and boutique shopping. It is the kind of center where you can pick up a birthday gift, refresh your wardrobe, and grab camping supplies in a single trip.

Dining at Boca Park

Two of Las Vegas' most popular restaurant chains anchor the dining scene at Boca Park. Kona Grill serves sushi and American fare in a polished setting, while The Cheesecake Factory offers its famously extensive menu. Several smaller restaurants and cafes round out the options, making Boca Park a reliable choice for a meal before or after shopping.

The outdoor layout means many restaurants feature patio seating. During the cooler months in Las Vegas, dining al fresco at Boca Park is one of the quieter pleasures of living on this side of town.

How Far Is Boca Park from The Willows?

Boca Park Fashion Village sits roughly two to three miles south of The Willows along Rampart Boulevard. Most residents reach the center in under ten minutes by car. The short distance makes it practical for quick errands, weeknight dinners, or weekend browsing. Combined with Downtown Summerlin's adjacent location, The Willows puts two major shopping destinations within a tight radius that few other Summerlin villages can match.
