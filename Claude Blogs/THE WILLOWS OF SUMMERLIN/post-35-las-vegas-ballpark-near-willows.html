Las Vegas Ballpark Near The Willows in Summerlin

Professional baseball lives just minutes from The Willows at the Las Vegas Ballpark on Pavilion Center Drive. This $150 million stadium serves as the home of the Las Vegas Aviators, the Triple A affiliate of the Oakland Athletics, and delivers a game day experience that rivals many major league venues.

What Is the Las Vegas Ballpark?

The Las Vegas Ballpark opened at 1650 South Pavilion Center Drive as a purpose built stadium designed to elevate minor league baseball in the valley. The facility seats approximately 10,000 fans and features amenities that go well beyond standard ballpark fare. A pool pavilion in the outfield, luxury suites, party zones, and open concourse areas create an atmosphere that blends sport with social entertainment.

The stadium's modern design includes a mesh exterior that allows desert breezes to flow through the structure. Video boards, premium sound systems, and LED lighting throughout the venue ensure that every seat delivers a quality experience from first pitch to final out.

The Las Vegas Aviators Experience

The Aviators play a full minor league season from April through September, giving Willows residents access to dozens of home games each year. Triple A baseball features players on the verge of major league call ups, meaning the talent level is consistently high. Many current MLB stars passed through Las Vegas on their way to the big leagues.

Beyond the baseball itself, the Aviators front office programs themed nights, fireworks shows, and family events throughout the season. These promotions make the ballpark a versatile entertainment option for date nights, family outings, and group gatherings alike.

How Close Is the Ballpark to The Willows?

The Las Vegas Ballpark sits along Pavilion Center Drive in the Downtown Summerlin corridor, placing it within a very short drive of The Willows. Residents can leave their homes and be in their seats before the national anthem. That proximity also means avoiding the traffic congestion that plagues events on the Strip. After the final out, Willows homeowners are back on their couch in minutes.

City National Arena Next Door

Adjacent to the ballpark at 1550 South Pavilion Center Drive stands City National Arena, the 146,000 square foot practice facility of the Vegas Golden Knights. The arena hosts public skating sessions, youth hockey programs, and team practices open to fans. Together, the ballpark and arena create a sports district that gives The Willows residents year round athletic entertainment within their immediate area.
