Life Time Summerlin Fitness Near The Willows

Residents of The Willows in Summerlin have one of the most impressive fitness facilities in the entire Las Vegas Valley just minutes from their front door. Life Time Summerlin on West Charleston Boulevard delivers a fitness experience that goes far beyond a typical gym membership.

What Makes Life Time Summerlin Stand Out?

Life Time Summerlin spans 200,000 square feet, earning its Diamond level designation as one of the brand's top tier locations nationwide. The facility sits at 10721 W Charleston Blvd, placing it roughly five minutes by car from The Willows village. That short commute makes it realistic for residents to incorporate daily workouts without carving out a large chunk of their schedule for travel time.

Operating hours run from 4 AM to midnight, which means early risers and late night exercisers alike can find time that fits their routine. Few fitness centers in Southern Nevada offer that kind of accessibility.

Facilities and Programming

The sheer variety inside Life Time Summerlin sets it apart from standard gyms. Members get access to indoor and outdoor swimming pools, full size basketball courts, steam rooms, and a comprehensive weight and cardio floor. Unlimited studio classes cover everything from cycling and yoga to high intensity interval training. The kids' area allows parents to work out while their children stay supervised and entertained on site.

Beyond traditional fitness, the facility includes a full service spa, a cafe with health focused menu options, and dedicated spaces for personal training. The outdoor pool deck feels more like a resort than a gym, complete with cabanas and lounge seating.

Why Willows Residents Choose Life Time

The proximity factor cannot be overstated. Living in The Willows means residents can walk their kids to Willows Park for a swim in the community pool, then drive five minutes to Life Time for a studio class or lap swim in an Olympic caliber pool. That combination of neighborhood amenities and a world class fitness facility creates a lifestyle that attracts health conscious buyers to this part of Summerlin.

Local Insight from Ryan Rose

Ryan Rose notes that Life Time membership is a selling point he hears buyers mention regularly when evaluating The Willows. The proximity to a Diamond level facility adds genuine lifestyle value, especially for families who want pool access, group fitness, and childcare all under one roof. Ryan considers it one of the strongest nearby amenities for active households in Summerlin.
