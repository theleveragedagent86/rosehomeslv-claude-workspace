Golf Courses Near The Willows Summerlin

Golfers who live in The Willows enjoy some of the best course access in all of Southern Nevada. Two premier golf destinations sit within minutes of the neighborhood, offering everything from public tee times to PGA Tour caliber conditions.

Red Rock Country Club

Red Rock Country Club operates two Arnold Palmer designed courses just a short drive from The Willows. The Mountain Course is private and reserved for club members, while the Arroyo Course welcomes public play. Both layouts wind through the natural desert landscape with views of Red Rock Canyon as a backdrop, creating a playing experience that rivals resort destinations.

The club's 44,000 square foot clubhouse anchors a broader athletic campus. Members have access to nine tennis courts, four pickleball courts, and three swimming pools. For golfers who want a full lifestyle membership rather than just tee times, Red Rock Country Club delivers across multiple sports.

TPC Summerlin

TPC Summerlin at 1700 Village Center Circle is one of the most recognized golf addresses in Las Vegas. The course hosts the PGA Tour's Shriners Children's Open each fall, drawing top professionals from around the world. TPC Summerlin operates as a private club, so access requires membership. The course conditions reflect that exclusivity, with tournament quality greens and fairways maintained year round.

Living in The Willows places residents within a few minutes of TPC Summerlin, making membership practical for daily play rather than a weekend commitment. That proximity is a meaningful factor for serious golfers evaluating where to buy in the valley.

What This Means for Willows Residents

Having both a private PGA Tour venue and a country club with public access nearby gives Willows residents flexibility. Casual golfers can book a round at the Arroyo Course without a membership commitment. Dedicated players can pursue membership at TPC Summerlin or Red Rock Country Club's Mountain Course for a more exclusive experience. Few neighborhoods in Las Vegas offer that range within such a tight radius.
