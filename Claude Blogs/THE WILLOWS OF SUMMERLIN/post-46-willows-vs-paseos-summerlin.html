The Willows vs The Paseos: Summerlin Village Comparison

Choosing between The Willows and The Paseos comes down to what you value most in a Summerlin village. Both offer parks, trails, and proximity to Downtown Summerlin, but they differ in age, layout, price, and overall feel.

Community Size and Age

The Willows spans 731 acres with 20 subdivisions built between 1998 and 2007. It sits in Summerlin South with a well established streetscape and mature landscaping. The Paseos covers 768 acres in Summerlin West, developed between 2003 and 2012. That newer construction timeline means more modern floor plans and energy efficient features in many Paseos homes.

Both villages are fully built out with only resale homes available. However, The Paseos tends to attract buyers who want newer construction without paying new build prices, while The Willows appeals to those who prefer an established neighborhood with proven property values.

Home Prices and Styles

The Willows offers a wider price range, starting around $400,000 and reaching $2,850,000 or more in the guard gated Willow Creek section. Builders like Toll Brothers, Pulte Homes, and William Lyon Homes created diverse architectural styles across its subdivisions. The Paseos generally prices between $700,000 and $1,000,000 for most listings. Spanish inspired designs in Segovia, modern finishes in Sarasota, and elevated luxury in Altura give The Paseos a cohesive yet varied look.

Parks, Amenities, and Security

The Willows delivers three parks totaling 34 plus acres, a resident only pool with water slides, and a community center. Its partial guard gate setup through Willow Creek provides luxury security options. Master HOA fees run $76 per month with sub association fees varying by neighborhood.

The Paseos features a standout 18 acre adventure themed park with 20 play structures, a 32 foot climbing tower, a zip line, and an 18 hole disc golf course. Gated sections within The Paseos offer controlled access. Both villages connect to the 200 plus mile Summerlin trail system and sit minutes from Downtown Summerlin shopping and dining.

Which Village Fits You?

The Willows works best for buyers who want price diversity, guard gated options through Willow Creek, and a more established community feel. The Paseos is the better pick for families drawn to newer construction and one of Summerlin's most impressive community parks. Both villages deliver strong resale value and access to top rated schools.
