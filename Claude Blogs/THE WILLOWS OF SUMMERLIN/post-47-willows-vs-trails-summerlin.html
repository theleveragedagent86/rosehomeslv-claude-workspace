The Willows vs The Trails: Which Summerlin Village Wins?

The Willows and The Trails are two of Summerlin's most established villages, both offering mature neighborhoods, strong amenities, and easy freeway access. Here is how they compare on the details that matter most to buyers.

Location and Development History

The Trails is the third village ever developed in Summerlin, covering 573 acres in Summerlin North with a 89134 zip code. Construction began in 1993, making it one of the community's oldest and most established areas. The Willows sits in Summerlin South, spanning 731 acres across 20 subdivisions built between 1998 and 2007. Both villages are fully developed with only resale properties available.

The Trails benefits from direct proximity to Summerlin Parkway between Village Center Drive and Anasazi Drive. The Willows sits within walking distance of Downtown Summerlin, giving residents immediate access to premier retail and dining.

Home Prices and Property Types

The Trails offers one of the widest price ranges in all of Summerlin. Condos start near $292,000, while luxury estates can exceed $5,000,000. The typical price range falls between $475,000 and $900,000. Homes range from 2,000 square foot family residences to ultra luxurious custom estates over 13,000 square feet.

The Willows prices from approximately $400,000 to $2,850,000 or more. Builders like Toll Brothers, Pulte Homes, and William Lyon Homes produced diverse styles across its subdivisions. The guard gated Willow Creek section accounts for the highest price points, with luxury homes regularly exceeding $1,200,000.

Parks, Amenities, and Recreation

The Trails features a 14 plus acre park with a lighted baseball field, two lighted softball fields, a community center, swimming pool, wading pool, basketball court, and playground. The Trails Village Center adds 175,000 square feet of neighborhood shopping with nearly 50 shops, plus the Nevada Ballet Theatre and Summerlin Library.

The Willows counters with three parks totaling 34 plus acres, a resident only pool with water slides, and a dedicated community center. Its connection to the 200 plus mile Summerlin trail system keeps active residents engaged year round. TPC Summerlin and its championship golf courses sit near The Trails, adding premium recreational value for golf enthusiasts.

Security and Gating

The Willows offers a partial guard gate through Willow Creek, plus gated neighborhoods in Bella Veranda and Heather Glen. Master HOA runs $76 per month. The Trails does not feature guard gated sections, though individual neighborhoods may have gated entries. This distinction matters for buyers who prioritize staffed security as part of their lifestyle.
