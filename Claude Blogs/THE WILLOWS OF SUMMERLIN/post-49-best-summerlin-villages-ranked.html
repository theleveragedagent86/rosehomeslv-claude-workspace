Best Summerlin Villages Ranked for 2026

Summerlin contains more than 30 villages spread across thousands of acres in western Las Vegas. Not all villages are created equal. Here is how the top Summerlin villages stack up based on home values, amenities, location, and overall livability.

Top Tier: Luxury and Exclusivity

The Ridges consistently ranks as Summerlin's most prestigious village. This 800 acre guard gated community features custom homes from $1,000,000 to over $10,000,000, with a median listing price around $5,600,000. Double gated security, dramatic Red Rock Canyon views, and contemporary desert architecture set it apart. The Hills South, home to Tournament Hills, earns its top tier spot through TPC Summerlin golf course frontage and custom estates ranging from $1,500,000 to $5,000,000 or more.

The Summit Club represents the pinnacle of Summerlin luxury. Custom homes and villas range from 1,463 to over 30,000 square feet, with a Tom Fazio designed private golf course and a $90 million clubhouse. This is Summerlin's most exclusive residential address.

Upper Mid Tier: Strong Value and Amenities

The Willows delivers one of Summerlin's best combinations of price diversity and premium amenities. With homes from $400,000 to $2,850,000 across 20 subdivisions, it serves first time buyers and luxury seekers alike. The guard gated Willow Creek section, three parks spanning 34 plus acres, a resort style pool, and walking distance to Downtown Summerlin make it a standout. Red Rock Country Club earns its ranking through 738 acres of guard gated golf community living, two Arnold Palmer designed courses, and homes from $800,000 to $10,000,000.

Family Favorites

The Paseos scores high among families with its 18 acre adventure park featuring a 32 foot climbing tower, zip line, and disc golf course. Homes price between $700,000 and $1,000,000 in Summerlin West. The Trails, one of Summerlin's original villages, offers 573 acres with homes from $292,000 to $5,000,000 and a 175,000 square foot village shopping center. The Arbors, at 1,206 acres, provides maximum space, four parks, the Summerlin Tennis Club, and in village schools including Palo Verde High School.

New Construction and Active Adult

Stonebridge, Redpoint, and Kestrel lead Summerlin's new construction category with modern floor plans, energy efficient builds, and Red Rock Canyon views. Sun City Summerlin remains the top choice for the 55 plus market, offering diverse home styles, golf, and extensive community programming. Regency at Summerlin provides a guard gated active adult alternative with single story homes and organized activities.
