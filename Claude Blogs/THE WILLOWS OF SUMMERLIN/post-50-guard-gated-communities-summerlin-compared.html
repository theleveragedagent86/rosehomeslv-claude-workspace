Guard Gated Communities in Summerlin Compared

Summerlin offers roughly 18 guard gated communities, each with distinct pricing, amenities, and lifestyles. Whether you want a golf course estate or a secure family neighborhood, this comparison breaks down the top options.

Ultra Luxury: The Ridges and The Summit Club

The Ridges spans 800 acres with custom homes priced from $1,000,000 to over $10,000,000. Its double gated security system, Red Rock Canyon views, and contemporary architecture make it Summerlin's most recognized luxury address. HOA fees range from $642 to $1,272 per month depending on the sub community. The Ridges attracts buyers seeking architectural freedom on large lots, some exceeding four acres.

The Summit Club sits at the absolute top of Summerlin's market. Homes and villas range from 1,463 to over 30,000 square feet, with a private Tom Fazio designed golf course and a $90 million clubhouse. This community redefines private residential living for buyers with no ceiling on their budget.

Golf Community Living: Red Rock Country Club

Red Rock Country Club covers 738 acres with approximately 1,000 luxury homes priced from $800,000 to $10,000,000. The median listing price sits around $1,440,000. Two Arnold Palmer designed championship golf courses anchor the community, one private and one public. Homes range from 2,100 to 9,000 square feet. The guard gated entrance, 24/7 security, mountain views, and proximity to Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area create a resort caliber lifestyle.

Established Luxury: Tournament Hills and Canyon Fairways

Tournament Hills in The Hills South village contains 132 custom homes built between 1993 and 2001. Estates range from 5,000 to over 10,000 square feet, priced between $1,500,000 and $5,000,000 or more. Most homes back onto the TPC Summerlin golf course. HOA dues run $480 per month plus $47 for the Summerlin master plan. Canyon Fairways offers a similar guard gated luxury experience with golf course proximity and custom home designs.

Accessible Guard Gated: Willow Creek in The Willows

Willow Creek provides guard gated living at a more accessible price point than The Ridges or Tournament Hills. Located within The Willows village, this Toll Brothers developed community contains 300 luxury homes across four neighborhoods: Willow Falls, Heritage Glen, Wood Glen, and Eden Ridge. Homes range from 2,670 to over 9,000 square feet, with prices from approximately $1,200,000 to $7,000,000 or more. HOA fees run $449 to $505 per month. Willow Falls adds a second gate inside Willow Creek for maximum privacy.

Willow Creek residents also benefit from The Willows' broader amenities: 34 plus acres of parks, a resort style pool with water slides, and walking distance to Downtown Summerlin. This combination of guard gated security and village level amenities is rare among Summerlin's gated communities.

Active Adult: Regency at Summerlin

Regency at Summerlin serves the 55 plus market with guard gated, single story homes, organized community activities, and shared amenities designed for active adult living. It provides security focused living for buyers who want a maintenance friendly lifestyle without sacrificing community engagement.
