JING Las Vegas Modern Asian Dining at Downtown Summerlin

Few restaurants in the Las Vegas Valley combine globally inspired Asian cuisine, craft cocktails, and a high energy atmosphere the way JING does. Located in Downtown Summerlin at 10975 Oval Park Dr, this upscale dining destination sits within walking distance of The Willows, making it one of the most exciting restaurant options for residents of the neighborhood.

Global Asian Cuisine with Bold Flavors

JING's menu draws from culinary traditions across Asia, spotlighting prime steaks, sushi, and seafood prepared with locally sourced and organic ingredients. The Famous Peking Duck, served with traditional flour wraps, leek, and cucumber, stands out as a signature dish that regulars return for again and again. Miso Black Cod is another standout that consistently earns praise from diners.

Sushi lovers will find an impressive selection of rolls and raw preparations, including the Jing Tuna Pizza featuring a crispy crust topped with fresh tuna. The Kobe Beef On The Rock, sourced directly from Hyogo Prefecture, offers a tableside cooking experience that elevates any dinner into an event. Garlic shrimp noodles and tofu tempura round out a menu that balances indulgence with variety.

Cocktails and Happy Hour

The bar program at JING is as creative as the kitchen. Signature cocktails like the Lychee Martini and Summerlin Cooler showcase the same attention to detail found on the food menu. A full selection of premium sake, wines by the glass, and craft beers complements the cocktail list for guests who prefer something different.

Happy hour runs daily from 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM, offering popular dishes and drinks at reduced prices. This makes JING an accessible option for a casual after work stop or a starting point before a full dinner experience.

Atmosphere and Hours

JING blends a chic, modern aesthetic with warm, welcoming energy. Weekend evenings bring live DJ performances that transform the dining room into one of the most vibrant scenes in Summerlin. The restaurant is open Sunday through Tuesday from 4:00 PM to 10:00 PM, Wednesday and Thursday until 11:00 PM, and Friday and Saturday until midnight.

Local Insight from Ryan Rose

Ryan Rose considers JING a prime example of how Downtown Summerlin elevates the lifestyle for Willows residents. With 125 plus shops and restaurants just steps from the neighborhood, having a dining destination of this caliber within walking distance is a genuine advantage. Ryan notes that JING is the kind of restaurant that makes entertaining out of town guests effortless because it delivers a Strip quality experience without the drive.
