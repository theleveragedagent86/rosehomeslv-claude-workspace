Grape Street Cafe at Downtown Summerlin

Some restaurants earn their reputation over decades, not months. Grape Street Cafe has been a Summerlin neighborhood favorite for over 26 years, and its recent relocation to Downtown Summerlin puts it closer than ever to residents of The Willows. Now operating from 2120 Festival Plaza Dr with a fresh, modernized look, this beloved cafe continues to deliver the kind of consistent, quality dining that locals depend on.

A Summerlin Institution, Reimagined

Originally opened in 1997, Grape Street Cafe built its reputation as a go to gathering spot for Summerlin families and professionals alike. The move to Downtown Summerlin brought a complete design upgrade, including a bold center bar and an interior styled to replicate a wine cellar. The intimate setting feels upscale without losing the approachable, neighborhood character that made the original location so popular.

The restaurant has been recognized as a Top 10 Las Vegas Wine Bar Restaurant by Gayot, a distinction that speaks to the quality of both its wine program and overall dining experience.

Menu and Wine Selection

Grape Street serves California bistro style cuisine with Italian and Mediterranean influences. The menu covers a satisfying range, from hearty meatballs and house made pastas to seafood dishes featuring clams, mussels, and salmon. Lamb and beef preparations round out the entree options for guests who want something more substantial.

Wine is central to the Grape Street experience. The bar pours between 65 and 75 wines by the glass, giving guests an unusually broad selection to pair with their meals. A full lineup of beer and spirits ensures everyone at the table finds something to enjoy.

Happy Hour and Hours

Happy hour runs Tuesday through Friday from 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM with half off appetizers and buy one get one free on beer and wines by the glass. Monday brings a half off deal on all bottles of wine, making it one of the best wine night values in Summerlin. The restaurant is open Monday through Thursday and Sunday from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM, with Friday and Saturday hours extending to 11:00 PM.

Local Insight from Ryan Rose

Ryan Rose highlights Grape Street Cafe as the kind of neighborhood anchor that defines what it means to live in Summerlin. A restaurant that has thrived for 26 years and then reinvested in a brand new Downtown Summerlin location signals real confidence in the area's growth. For Willows residents, having a proven local favorite within walking distance adds everyday value that goes beyond just convenience.
