Retiring in The Willows Summerlin

The Willows offers retirees a rare combination: single story living, walkable trails, world class fitness, and a dedicated 55+ community all within one Summerlin village. Whether you want a low maintenance apartment or a spacious custom home, this neighborhood delivers options at every price point.

Carefree Senior Living at The Willows

Carefree Senior Living sits at 3250 S Town Center Dr and provides 300 apartment homes across nine floorplans exclusively for residents 55 and older. This community offers a maintenance free lifestyle where landscaping, exterior upkeep, and common areas are all handled for you. For retirees who want to downsize without sacrificing location, Carefree places you within walking distance of Downtown Summerlin's restaurants, shops, and entertainment.

Single Story Homes for Every Budget

The Willows includes several subdivisions with single story floorplans that eliminate stairs entirely. Autumn Ridge by Pulte offers homes ranging from 1,432 to 1,628 square feet, ideal for retirees who want manageable space without feeling cramped. Castlewood provides options between 1,158 and 1,667 square feet. For those seeking more room, Eden Ridge by Pulte sits within the guard gated Willow Creek section and ranges from 3,091 to 5,757 square feet. Willow Springs by Pulte adds another layer of choice.

Prices across The Willows range from $400,000 to over $2,850,000, with the master HOA running just $76 per month. That fee covers access to community parks, trails, and maintained common areas throughout the village.

Healthcare, Fitness, and Recreation

Summerlin Hospital Medical Center sits approximately two to three miles away at 657 N Town Center Dr, providing emergency services and specialty care close to home. For daily wellness, Life Time fitness operates a 200,000 square foot facility adjacent to The Willows with pools, group classes, and senior programming.

Red Rock Country Club offers nearby golf, tennis, and pickleball for retirees who want a club lifestyle. The Summerlin trail system stretches over 200 miles and connects directly to The Willows, giving residents paved walking paths right outside their front doors. Many retirees in the community use these trails daily for morning walks and light exercise.

Local Insight from Ryan Rose

Ryan Rose works with retirees relocating to Las Vegas every week and consistently highlights The Willows as a top pick. He notes that the variety of housing, from Carefree's 55+ apartments to guard gated estate homes in Willow Creek, means couples can find the right fit regardless of whether they want to simplify or upgrade. The proximity to Summerlin Hospital and Life Time fitness seals the deal for most of his retired clients.
