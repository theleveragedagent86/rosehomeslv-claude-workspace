City National Arena Golden Knights Near The Willows

Living in The Willows puts residents just minutes from the home base of the Vegas Golden Knights. City National Arena at 1550 S Pavilion Center Dr serves as the team's official practice facility, and it is open to the public for skating, dining, and shopping year round.

Inside City National Arena

City National Arena spans 146,000 square feet and houses two full regulation ice rinks. The main rink is where the Golden Knights hold daily practices during the season, and fans can watch from stadium seating at no charge on most practice days. The second rink hosts youth hockey leagues, figure skating programs, and public skating sessions throughout the week.

The facility goes well beyond ice. A full service restaurant and bar overlooks the main practice rink, giving visitors a chance to eat while watching professional athletes run drills. The official Vegas Golden Knights team store carries jerseys, hats, and exclusive merchandise that is only available at this location.

Public Skating and Youth Programs

Public skating sessions run on a regular schedule and draw families from across the valley. Skate rentals are available on site, so no equipment is needed for a casual visit. The arena also operates youth hockey development programs that have grown steadily since the Golden Knights arrived in 2017.

For families in The Willows, having a world class ice facility this close means kids can participate in hockey and skating programs without a long commute across the valley. That convenience makes the difference between a passing interest and a sustained commitment to the sport.

Location and Access from The Willows

City National Arena sits along Pavilion Center Drive, just off the 215 Beltway near Downtown Summerlin. The drive from The Willows takes only a few minutes, making it practical for weeknight practices, spontaneous skating outings, or a casual lunch at the on site restaurant. The arena's position near Downtown Summerlin also means residents can combine a visit with shopping or dining at more than 125 nearby retailers.

Ryan Rose notes that City National Arena adds a unique entertainment layer to life in The Willows.
