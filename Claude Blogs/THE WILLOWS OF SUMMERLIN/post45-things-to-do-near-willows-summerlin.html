Things to Do Near The Willows Summerlin

The Willows sits in the heart of western Summerlin, surrounded by more outdoor recreation, entertainment, and lifestyle amenities than most Las Vegas neighborhoods can claim. From world class hiking to professional sports venues, residents have a deep roster of activities within a short drive or even walking distance.

Outdoor Recreation

Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area is roughly 15 minutes from The Willows, offering hiking trails, cycling routes, and the iconic 13 mile scenic drive. Residents use it for early morning hikes, weekend family outings, and road cycling sessions along some of the most dramatic terrain in Southern Nevada.

Closer to home, The Willows connects to over 200 miles of the Summerlin trail system. Three community parks totaling more than 34 acres provide open green space, playgrounds, and sports courts. The resident only pool at Willows Park features water slides and shaded gathering areas, giving families a private aquatic retreat without leaving the neighborhood.

Shopping, Dining, and Entertainment

Downtown Summerlin is within walking distance of The Willows and delivers more than 125 shops, restaurants, and entertainment venues. Seasonal events, live concerts, and a weekly farmers market keep the calendar full year round. Red Rock Casino Resort and Spa adds a cinema, bowling alley, full service spa, and multiple dining options to the local entertainment mix.

Cowabunga Canyon Water Park brings 25 slides, a lazy river, and a wave pool to the area, making it a go to destination for families during the summer months. The combination of these venues means residents rarely need to drive to the Strip for a night out or a full day of entertainment.

Sports and Fitness

Las Vegas Ballpark, the $150 million home of the Las Vegas Aviators AAA baseball team, hosts games and concerts throughout the season just minutes away. City National Arena gives hockey fans access to Vegas Golden Knights practices, public skating, and a team store. Life Time Summerlin, a 200,000 square foot fitness complex, offers everything from indoor and outdoor pools to group classes, coworking space, and a full service spa.

Golfers have two elite options nearby. Red Rock Country Club features Arnold Palmer designed courses with public and private access. TPC Summerlin hosts the PGA Tour's Shriners Children's Open each fall and operates as a private club with tournament quality conditions year round.
