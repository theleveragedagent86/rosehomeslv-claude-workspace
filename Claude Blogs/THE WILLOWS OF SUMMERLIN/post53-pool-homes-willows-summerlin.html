Pool Homes in The Willows Summerlin

In the Las Vegas desert, a private pool is more than a luxury. It is a lifestyle essential. The Willows in Summerlin delivers exactly that, with many homes across its larger subdivisions featuring backyard pools that make triple digit summers feel like a vacation.

Pool Home Availability in The Willows

Pool homes are common throughout The Willows, particularly in subdivisions with larger lot sizes. Lots in this community range from 0.17 to 0.69 acres, and homes on the bigger parcels frequently include private swimming pools. Builders like Toll Brothers, Pulte Homes, and William Lyon Homes designed many of these properties with outdoor living in mind, creating backyards that accommodate pools, spas, and covered patios.

The community spans 731 acres across 20 subdivisions, so buyers have plenty of options when searching for the right pool home. Prices throughout The Willows range from approximately $400,000 to over $2,850,000, with pool homes typically commanding a premium over comparable non pool properties.

Why Pool Homes Hold Their Value

In a desert climate where temperatures regularly exceed 100 degrees from June through September, pools are a top priority for many buyers. That consistent demand means pool homes in The Willows tend to sell faster and hold their value better than those without. When it comes time to resell, your pool becomes a significant selling point rather than just a personal amenity.

The Willows is fully built out with no new construction, which means the existing pool home inventory is all that will ever be available. This fixed supply in a high demand neighborhood creates favorable conditions for long term appreciation. Buyers who secure a pool home here are positioning themselves well for the future.

Living the Pool Home Lifestyle

Beyond the obvious benefit of cooling off during hot months, a pool home in The Willows puts you within walking distance of Downtown Summerlin. Imagine spending your morning at the pool, then strolling to restaurants and shops in the afternoon. Red Rock Canyon is also minutes away for hiking and outdoor adventure.

The master HOA fee of $76 per month keeps the community looking sharp. Nevada's absence of state income tax and Clark County's low property tax rate of approximately 0.53% mean more money stays in your pocket for pool maintenance and upgrades.
