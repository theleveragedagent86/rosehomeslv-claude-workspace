Carefree Senior Living at The Willows Summerlin (55+)

Retirement in Las Vegas does not have to mean giving up convenience, community, or comfort. Carefree Senior Living at The Willows offers 55 plus residents a maintenance free lifestyle just steps from one of Summerlin's most desirable villages.

About Carefree Senior Living at The Willows

Carefree Senior Living is located at 3250 S Town Center Dr, Las Vegas, NV 89135, adjacent to The Willows village in Summerlin. This 55 plus community features 300 apartment homes spread across 9 floorplans. It operates as a separate community from the residential neighborhoods of The Willows, but its location gives residents immediate access to everything the village and greater Summerlin have to offer.

The community is designed for active adults who want the freedom of apartment style living without the responsibilities of homeownership. Residents enjoy curated amenities, social programming, and a welcoming environment built around their needs.

Location and Proximity to Services

One of the strongest advantages of Carefree Senior Living is its proximity to essential services. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center sits just 2 to 3 miles away, giving residents peace of mind knowing quality healthcare is minutes from their door. Pharmacies, urgent care clinics, and medical offices are also nearby throughout the Summerlin corridor.

Downtown Summerlin, with its 125 plus shops, restaurants, and entertainment venues, is within walking distance or a short drive. Residents can enjoy dining at spots like La Neta Cocina or Sushi Loca, catch a movie, or browse retail without traveling far from home. Grocery stores, banks, and everyday essentials are all easily accessible.

Why Summerlin Appeals to Active Adults

Summerlin's master planned design creates an ideal setting for seniors. The community connects to a 200 plus mile trail system, perfect for morning walks or leisurely bike rides. Over 34 acres of parks within The Willows alone provide green space for relaxation and light exercise. Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area sits just 15 minutes away for scenic drives and nature outings.

Nevada's tax advantages also benefit retirees directly. The state collects no income tax, which means Social Security benefits, retirement account withdrawals, and pension income remain untaxed. Clark County's property tax rate of approximately 0.53% keeps housing costs predictable for those who eventually transition to homeownership in the area.

A Gateway to The Willows Village

Living adjacent to The Willows means Carefree residents can explore a village with 731 acres, 20 subdivisions, and price points ranging from $400,000 to $2,850,000 or more. For seniors who start at Carefree and later decide to purchase a home, The Willows offers single story options, guard gated neighborhoods like Willow Creek, and a strong sense of community with well maintained parks and pools.
