Sushi Loca at Downtown Summerlin

Quality sushi within walking distance of home changes how you think about weeknight dining. Sushi Loca at Downtown Summerlin brings premium Japanese cuisine to one of Las Vegas's most popular open air shopping destinations, right next to The Willows.

What Sushi Loca Brings to the Table

Sushi Loca focuses on premium sushi, sashimi, and specialty rolls crafted with fresh, high quality ingredients. The menu caters to both traditional sushi enthusiasts and diners who enjoy creative fusion rolls. Each piece reflects careful attention to preparation and presentation, setting the restaurant apart from casual sushi chains.

The sashimi selections highlight the kitchen's commitment to sourcing quality fish, while the specialty rolls offer inventive combinations that keep regulars coming back. Whether you prefer a clean, simple nigiri plate or an elaborate signature roll, Sushi Loca provides an elevated experience without the formality of a Strip restaurant.

Downtown Summerlin Location

Sushi Loca operates within Downtown Summerlin, the 125 plus store open air retail and dining center that anchors the western side of the Summerlin master planned community. The location puts the restaurant within easy reach of residents in The Willows, who can walk or drive just minutes to reach the front door.

Downtown Summerlin's layout encourages a full evening out. Start with sushi, then stroll through the promenade, catch a show, or browse the shops. Free parking eliminates the hassle that comes with dining on the Strip, and the relaxed suburban atmosphere makes it feel like your own neighborhood spot.
