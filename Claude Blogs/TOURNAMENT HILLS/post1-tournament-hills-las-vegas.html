Tournament Hills is an exclusive guard-gated community of just 132 custom luxury homes nestled within the Hills South village of Summerlin, Las Vegas. Built between 1993 and 2001, this intimate enclave sits adjacent to the renowned TPC Summerlin golf course and remains one of the most prestigious addresses in the valley. ## What Makes Tournament Hills Different from Other Summerlin Communities? Tournament Hills stands apart because of its size and exclusivity. With only 132 individually commissioned custom homes, the community offers a level of privacy that larger neighborhoods simply cannot match. Every home was designed by its original owner, meaning no two properties look alike. Architectural styles range from classic and Mediterranean to sleek contemporary designs, with lot sizes spanning from just over a third of an acre to more than 2.5 acres. ## What Amenities Does Tournament Hills Offer Residents? Residents enjoy 24 hour guard-gated entry with roaming security patrols, ensuring peace of mind around the clock. The community features tennis courts, a basketball court, a playground, a shaded picnic pavilion, and a dedicated dog park. Homeowners who join TPC Summerlin also gain access to world class golf facilities just steps from their front door. Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate expert who works closely with buyers and sellers in Tournament Hills, notes that this combination of security, amenities, and location is rare even among Summerlin's luxury communities. ## Where Is Tournament Hills Located? The community's location within Summerlin puts everyday conveniences within easy reach. Downtown Summerlin, with its dining, shopping, and entertainment, is roughly a five minute drive away. Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is within ten minutes. For commuters, the Las Vegas Strip is about 20 to 25 minutes by car, and Harry Reid International Airport is approximately 25 to 30 minutes. Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area is also nearby, giving residents quick access to hiking and outdoor recreation. ## A Local Perspective What many people do not realize about Tournament Hills is that prospective buyers must show proof of funds before they can even tour homes inside the gate. That requirement speaks to the caliber of the community and the privacy its residents value. It is the kind of neighborhood where your neighbors appreciate discretion just as much as you do.
