TPC Summerlin Golf Course at Tournament Hills

TPC Summerlin is a premier private golf course located in the heart of Tournament Hills, offering a par 72 layout stretching 7,243 yards across some of the most stunning terrain in the Las Vegas valley. Since opening in 1991, this course has earned a reputation as one of the finest private clubs in the entire state.

What Makes TPC Summerlin One of Nevada's Best Golf Courses?

Designed by Bobby Weed with PGA legend Fuzzy Zoeller serving as consultant, TPC Summerlin delivers a championship caliber experience for its members. Nevada Business Magazine named it the "#1 Private Golf Course in Nevada" in 2006, and Golf Digest has recognized it as "One of America's Best Golf Courses." The course sits at the western edge of the Las Vegas valley, surrounded by desert mountain views and the custom luxury homes of Tournament Hills.

What Amenities Do TPC Summerlin Members Enjoy?

Beyond the course itself, club members have access to an Olympic sized swimming pool, tennis courts, and a full service clubhouse. These world class amenities make TPC Summerlin more than just a golf destination; it is a lifestyle centerpiece for the Tournament Hills community. The club hosts the annual Shriners Children's Open, a PGA TOUR event that draws top professional golfers from around the world each fall.

How Does TPC Summerlin Connect to Tournament Hills?

Tournament Hills was specifically developed to surround TPC Summerlin, with only 132 custom homes built between 1993 and 2001. Many of these residences feature direct golf course frontage or sweeping views of the fairways. The neighborhood and the course are deeply intertwined, creating one of the most exclusive residential golf communities in Las Vegas.
