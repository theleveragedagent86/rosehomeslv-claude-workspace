Golf Course Homes in Tournament Hills

Golf course homes in Tournament Hills offer direct frontage along TPC Summerlin, one of Nevada's most prestigious private courses, with prices ranging from $1,500,000 to over $13,900,000. These custom luxury estates represent some of the most coveted residential real estate in the entire Las Vegas valley.

What Types of Golf Course Homes Are Available in Tournament Hills?

Tournament Hills features only 132 custom homes, many of which sit directly on TPC Summerlin's fairways and greens. Home styles include classic, Mediterranean, and contemporary designs in both single and two story configurations. Lot sizes range from 0.36 to 2.52 acres, giving homeowners generous space and privacy. Every home was built between 1993 and 2001 with custom architecture tailored to maximize golf course views.

How Much Do Golf Course Homes in Tournament Hills Cost?

The median home price in Tournament Hills is approximately $2,375,000, while the average sits closer to $5,000,000. Properties with premium golf course frontage and larger lots tend to command the highest prices, with select estates listed above $13,900,000. These values reflect the exclusivity of a neighborhood with just 132 homes surrounding a championship caliber PGA TOUR venue.

Why Do Buyers Choose Golf Course Living in Tournament Hills?

Beyond the views, homeowners in Tournament Hills enjoy proximity to TPC Summerlin's clubhouse, Olympic sized swimming pool, and tennis courts. The community is part of Summerlin's "The Hills South" village, placing residents just five minutes from Downtown Summerlin shopping and dining. The combination of world class golf, luxury living, and convenient location makes this neighborhood a standout choice for discerning buyers.
