Is Tournament Hills in Summerlin?

Yes, Tournament Hills is located within Summerlin, specifically in "The Hills South" village of this 22,500 acre master planned community in the western Las Vegas valley. The neighborhood sits in the 89134 zip code and is one of Summerlin's most exclusive residential enclaves.

Where Exactly Is Tournament Hills Within Summerlin?

Tournament Hills is part of The Hills South, a 725 acre mixed use residential village developed within Summerlin by The Howard Hughes Corporation. The neighborhood wraps around TPC Summerlin, a premier private golf course that hosts the annual Shriners Children's Open PGA TOUR event. With only 132 custom homes built between 1993 and 2001, Tournament Hills occupies a small but highly prestigious footprint within the larger Summerlin community.

What Are the Benefits of Being Part of Summerlin?

As a Summerlin neighborhood, Tournament Hills residents enjoy access to the community's extensive network of parks, trails, and amenities. Downtown Summerlin, a major shopping and dining destination, is approximately five minutes away by car. The Las Vegas Strip is a 20 to 25 minute drive, and Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area sits just to the west. Summerlin consistently ranks among the top selling master planned communities in the United States.

How Does Tournament Hills Compare to Other Summerlin Neighborhoods?

Tournament Hills stands apart from most Summerlin neighborhoods due to its direct connection to TPC Summerlin and its ultra limited inventory of 132 custom estates. Home prices range from $1,500,000 to over $13,900,000, with a median around $2,375,000. Lot sizes span 0.36 to 2.52 acres, significantly larger than typical Summerlin subdivisions. This combination of exclusivity, golf course living, and Summerlin's infrastructure makes Tournament Hills one of the most desirable addresses in Las Vegas.
