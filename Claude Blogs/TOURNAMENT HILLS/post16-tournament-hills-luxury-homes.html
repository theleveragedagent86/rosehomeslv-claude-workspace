Tournament Hills Luxury Homes Las Vegas

Tournament Hills is one of the most exclusive luxury home communities in Las Vegas, featuring 132 custom estates priced from $1.5 million to nearly $14 million. Nestled within the Hills South village of Summerlin and adjacent to TPC Summerlin, this guard gated enclave delivers privacy, prestige, and world class living.

What Makes Tournament Hills Homes So Luxurious?

Every home in Tournament Hills was individually commissioned by its original owner, meaning no two properties are alike. These custom estates sit on generous lots ranging from just over a third of an acre to more than 2.5 acres. Interior features often include wine cellars, home theaters, private gyms, and multi car garages accommodating up to eight vehicles. The median home price sits around $2.375 million, while the community average approaches $5 million.

Who Lives in Tournament Hills?

Tournament Hills is home to roughly 230 residents, including notable celebrities who value the community's discretion and security. The neighborhood is fully guard gated with 24 hour security, and prospective buyers must provide proof of funds or a pre qualification letter before scheduling property tours. This level of exclusivity attracts high net worth individuals, executives, and retirees seeking a refined desert lifestyle.

What Amenities Do Tournament Hills Luxury Homes Offer?

Beyond the estates themselves, residents enjoy direct proximity to TPC Summerlin, home to the PGA TOUR's Shriners Children's Open. Custom pools, outdoor living spaces, and panoramic mountain views are standard across the community. The location within Summerlin also provides access to premier shopping, dining, and top rated schools. HOA fees of approximately $527 per month cover the guard gate, common area maintenance, and community upkeep.
