Tournament Hills Custom Home Styles and Architecture

Tournament Hills features a diverse collection of custom architectural styles, including classic, Mediterranean, and contemporary designs spread across 132 individually built estates. Every home in this guard gated Summerlin community was uniquely commissioned, resulting in a neighborhood where no two residences look alike.

What Architectural Styles Are Found in Tournament Hills?

The community showcases three primary design themes: classic, Mediterranean, and contemporary. Classic homes feature traditional layouts with formal living and dining areas, grand entryways, and timeless stone or stucco exteriors. Mediterranean estates draw on Old World influences with clay tile roofs, arched doorways, and courtyard designs. Contemporary builds offer clean lines, expansive glass, and open floor plans that blend indoor and outdoor living.

Are Tournament Hills Homes Single Story or Two Story?

Both single story and two story floorplans are represented throughout the community. Single story homes appeal to buyers who prefer main level living with easy indoor outdoor flow to pools and patios. Two story estates maximize square footage on their oversized lots and often include upstairs balconies with mountain or golf course views. Because these are custom builds from 1993 to 2001, layouts vary dramatically from home to home.

What Custom Features Do These Homes Include?

Interior finishes in Tournament Hills go well beyond standard luxury. Many homes include private wine cellars, dedicated home theaters, personal gyms, and garages built to hold up to eight vehicles. Outdoor spaces are equally impressive, with resort style pools, outdoor kitchens, and landscaped grounds on lots ranging from 0.36 to 2.52 acres. Several properties have been extensively renovated or fully rebuilt to incorporate modern finishes and smart home technology.
