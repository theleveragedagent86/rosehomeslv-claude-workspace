Tournament Hills Lot Sizes and Homesites

Tournament Hills homesites range from 0.36 acres to 2.52 acres, with a community minimum of half acre lots throughout the neighborhood. These oversized parcels are a defining feature of this exclusive 132 home enclave within the Hills South village of Summerlin.

How Big Are the Lots in Tournament Hills?

Lot sizes in Tournament Hills start at just over a third of an acre and extend to more than two and a half acres. The community was designed with a minimum half acre standard, giving every homeowner substantial space for custom landscaping, pools, and outdoor living. Compared to most Las Vegas luxury neighborhoods, these parcels are exceptionally generous and provide true privacy between residences.

Why Do Lot Sizes Matter in Tournament Hills?

Larger lots directly impact property values and livability. In Tournament Hills, the spacious homesites allow for resort style amenities including oversized pools, sport courts, detached guest houses, and multi car garages accommodating up to eight vehicles. The extra acreage also creates natural buffers between neighbors, which is a major draw for high net worth buyers seeking seclusion. Properties on the largest lots command premium prices, with homes listed up to $13.9 million.

Are There Any Vacant Lots Available in Tournament Hills?

Tournament Hills is fully built out with all 132 custom homesites developed between 1993 and 2001. No vacant land remains for new construction within the community gates. However, some owners have demolished original structures and rebuilt entirely, effectively creating new construction on existing lots. This teardown and rebuild approach allows buyers to secure a prime Tournament Hills address with a fully modern home.
