Tournament Hills Investment Potential

Tournament Hills offers strong long term investment potential driven by extreme scarcity, with only 132 custom homes in existence, a guard gated setting, and adjacency to TPC Summerlin's PGA TOUR venue. Limited supply and consistent demand from high net worth buyers create a pricing floor that few Las Vegas communities can match.

Why Is Tournament Hills a Strong Real Estate Investment?

The fundamental investment case for Tournament Hills rests on supply constraints. With the community fully built out and no vacant land available, the total housing stock is permanently fixed at 132 homes. New inventory can only enter the market when existing owners decide to sell, which keeps competition among buyers intense. Homes are priced from $1.5 million to $13.9 million, with a community average near $5 million, reflecting sustained appreciation over decades.

What Factors Drive Property Values in Tournament Hills?

Several factors support continued value growth in this community. The location within Summerlin, one of America's top selling master planned communities, provides access to premier schools, shopping, dining, and healthcare. Direct proximity to TPC Summerlin adds prestige and national visibility through annual PGA TOUR events. The 24 hour guard gate, celebrity residents, and half acre minimum lot sizes reinforce the community's ultra premium positioning. Nevada's lack of state income tax also draws wealthy relocators from higher tax states.

Is Tournament Hills a Good Market for Renovated or Rebuilt Homes?

Because all homes were originally constructed between 1993 and 2001, there is a growing trend of buyers purchasing older estates, demolishing them, and building entirely new custom homes on the existing lots. This teardown and rebuild strategy can significantly increase property values, as a modern build on a prime Tournament Hills lot commands top dollar. Investors and owner occupants alike benefit from the ability to create new construction within an established, irreplaceable community.
