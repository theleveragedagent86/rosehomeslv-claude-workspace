Bruce Trent Park Near Tournament Hills

Bruce Trent Park sits just one minute from Tournament Hills, giving residents of this guard gated Summerlin community instant access to 28 acres of green space, sports courts, and weekly farmers market shopping.

What Does Bruce Trent Park Offer?

Bruce Trent Park spans 28 acres and features a playground, tennis courts, picnic areas, and expansive open fields. The park is one of the largest in the Summerlin area, making it a go to destination for families, fitness enthusiasts, and anyone looking for outdoor recreation near Tournament Hills. Whether you want to rally on the tennis courts or spread out a blanket on the grass, this park delivers variety without a long drive.

Is There a Farmers Market at Bruce Trent Park?

Yes. Bruce Trent Park hosts the Las Vegas Farmers Market every Wednesday. Vendors offer fresh produce, baked goods, and local specialty items. For Tournament Hills residents, the market is a quick walk or drive, making it easy to pick up groceries and support local businesses on a weekday afternoon. The weekly market adds a community gathering element that goes beyond typical park amenities.

How Close Is Bruce Trent Park to Tournament Hills?

Bruce Trent Park is approximately one minute from Tournament Hills by car, located within the 89134 zip code area. The proximity means residents can treat the park as an extension of their own backyard. Families in Tournament Hills frequently use the playground and open fields for after school activities, weekend picnics, and casual sports.
