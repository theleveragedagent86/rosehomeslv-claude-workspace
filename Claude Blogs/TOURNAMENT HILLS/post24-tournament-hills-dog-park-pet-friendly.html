Tournament Hills Dog Park and Pet Friendly Living

Tournament Hills includes a private dog park within the community, and the surrounding Summerlin Trail System offers 150 plus miles of pet friendly paths, making this guard gated neighborhood one of the best options for dog owners in Las Vegas.

Does Tournament Hills Have Its Own Dog Park?

Yes. The Tournament Hills HOA maintains a dog park inside the community, giving residents a dedicated off leash space without leaving the neighborhood. This is a significant perk for a gated community of just 132 custom homes, where shared amenities are less crowded and better maintained than public alternatives. Dog owners can let their pets exercise and socialize steps from their front door.

What Pet Friendly Trails Are Near Tournament Hills?

The Summerlin Trail System connects over 150 miles of walking, biking, and jogging paths throughout the master planned community. Many of these trails are pet friendly, providing Tournament Hills residents with long distance walking routes that go well beyond the neighborhood dog park. The connected trail network means you can vary your route daily, exploring different sections of Summerlin while keeping your dog active and engaged.

Are Nearby Parks Good for Dog Walking?

Bruce Trent Park, The Hills Park, and Pueblo Park are all within five minutes of Tournament Hills and offer open spaces suitable for leashed dog walking. Bruce Trent Park's 28 acres of open fields are particularly popular with dog owners looking for room to roam. Combined with the private dog park and the trail system, Tournament Hills residents have a layered network of pet friendly outdoor options that few Las Vegas neighborhoods can match.
