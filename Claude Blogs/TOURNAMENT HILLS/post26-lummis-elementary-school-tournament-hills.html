William Lummis Elementary School in Tournament Hills

William Lummis Elementary School is the zoned public elementary school for Tournament Hills, serving students in grades Pre K through 5 as part of the Clark County School District. Located on Hillpointe Road in Summerlin, it consistently ranks among the top elementary schools in Nevada.

How Does Lummis Elementary Rank Academically?

Lummis Elementary earns a GreatSchools rating of 9 out of 10 and a Niche grade of B+, placing it in the top 20% of all schools in Nevada for overall test scores. In math, 63.1% of students reached proficiency compared to just 33% across Clark County. In reading, 67.6% of students scored proficient or above, well ahead of the county average of 43.9%. These numbers reflect a school that is outperforming most of its peers across the district and the state.

What Is the Student Environment Like at Lummis Elementary?

Lummis Elementary enrolls approximately 484 students, keeping it on the smaller side for a CCSD campus. The school serves the broader Summerlin community, drawing families from Tournament Hills and surrounding neighborhoods in the Hills South village. Parents frequently praise the supportive teaching staff and the emphasis on academic achievement. The school's size contributes to a more personal learning environment where teachers can focus on individual student progress.
