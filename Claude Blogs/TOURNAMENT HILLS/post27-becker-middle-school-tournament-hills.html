Ernest Becker Middle School and Tournament Hills

Ernest Becker Middle School is the zoned public middle school for Tournament Hills, serving students in grades 6 through 8 within the Clark County School District. The school provides Tournament Hills families a local option for the transition years between elementary and high school.

How Does Becker Middle School Perform Academically?

Becker Middle School holds a GreatSchools rating of 6 out of 10 and a Niche grade of B, placing it in the average range among Nevada middle schools. In reading, 47% of students score proficient or above, which exceeds the state average of 44%. Math proficiency sits at 25%, slightly below the state average of 30%. The school ranks 26th among 59 middle schools in the Clark County School District, putting it in the upper half of district middle schools.

What Is the School Environment Like at Becker Middle School?

Becker Middle School enrolls approximately 1,044 students and serves families across the Summerlin area, including Tournament Hills and nearby neighborhoods. The campus provides standard CCSD middle school programming with core academics, electives, and extracurricular activities. Students who later attend Palo Verde High School benefit from the academic foundation established at Becker, particularly in preparation for honors and Advanced Placement coursework at the high school level.

Do Tournament Hills Families Use Becker or Choose Alternatives?

Some families in Tournament Hills enroll their children at Becker Middle School while others opt for private alternatives like The Meadows School or Faith Lutheran, both located within a short drive.
