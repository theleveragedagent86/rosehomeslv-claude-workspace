What Makes The Meadows School Stand Out Academically?

What Is Tuition at The Meadows School?

Annual tuition at The Meadows School is approximately $33,180 to $34,840, depending on grade level. The school offers three payment plans: a single payment due in July, a two payment plan split between July and February, or a ten payment plan spread from July through April. Financial aid is available for families who demonstrate need, and the school states its commitment to making a Meadows education accessible regardless of a family's ability to pay.
