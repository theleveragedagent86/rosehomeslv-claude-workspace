Private Schools Near Tournament Hills Las Vegas

Tournament Hills families have access to several highly rated private schools within minutes of the community, including The Meadows School, Bishop Gorman High School, Faith Lutheran Middle and High School, and the Adelson Educational Campus. Each offers a distinct approach to college preparatory education.

What Are the Top Private Schools Near Tournament Hills?

The Meadows School is the closest option, located roughly five minutes from Tournament Hills. It serves Pre K through 12th grade with a rigorous college prep curriculum and annual tuition around $33,180 to $34,840. Bishop Gorman High School, a Catholic institution serving grades 9 through 12, holds a Niche grade of A and boasts a 98% college bound rate with over 105 state championships in athletics. Faith Lutheran Middle and High School is the largest private school in Nevada with 1,935 students, a 100% graduation rate, and tuition near $16,275. The Adelson Educational Campus serves K through 12 as the only preschool through 12th grade Jewish day school in Nevada, earning a Niche grade of A.

How Do Private Schools Near Tournament Hills Compare?

Each school serves a different niche. The Meadows School offers the smallest class sizes and the broadest grade range from Pre K through 12. Bishop Gorman draws families seeking Catholic education combined with elite athletics and a near perfect college placement rate. Faith Lutheran provides a Christian college prep environment with strong standardized test scores, including an average SAT of 1,193 and average ACT of 21.7. Adelson Educational Campus appeals to families seeking Jewish day school education with a student teacher ratio of 9 to 1. All four schools are within a short drive from Tournament Hills.
