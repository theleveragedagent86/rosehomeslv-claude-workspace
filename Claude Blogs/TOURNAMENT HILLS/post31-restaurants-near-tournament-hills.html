Best Restaurants Near Tournament Hills Las Vegas

Tournament Hills residents have some of the best dining in the Las Vegas Valley within a five to ten minute drive, ranging from lakeside French bistros to wood fired steakhouses and inventive Thai kitchens.

What Are the Top Restaurants Near Tournament Hills?

Nittaya's Secret Kitchen on West Lake Mead Boulevard delivers upscale Thai cuisine served tapas style, with dishes like Crying Tiger Steak, Kao Soi, and Drunken Noodles prepared personally by chef Nittaya. The restaurant holds a 4.9 star rating on OpenTable and offers half priced bottles on Thursday nights. Marche Bacchus, a French bistro on Lake Jacqueline in Desert Shores, pairs over 950 wine labels with classics like escargot, steak frites, and beef bourguignon while guests enjoy lakeside patio seating. Echo and Rig Butcher and Steakhouse at Tivoli Village serves wood fired steaks and shareable plates like pork belly burnt ends alongside craft cocktails in a lively atmosphere with an on site butcher shop.

Are There French Dining Options Near Tournament Hills?

Two standout French restaurants sit within minutes of Tournament Hills. Marche Bacchus offers daily lakeside happy hour from 3pm to 6pm, live acoustic guitar, and seasonal menus that change with Chef's specials rotating daily. Ohlala French Bistro in Boca Park brings classic Parisian fare to the table, including sole meuniere, warm crusted pistachio goat cheese, and traditional French onion soup, all prepared with organic and local produce whenever possible.

Which Restaurants Offer a Unique Dining Experience?

Honey Salt on Rampart Boulevard takes a farm to table approach to upscale comfort food, featuring caramelized sea scallops and roasted beet salads in a warm neighborhood setting. TPC Summerlin Clubhouse Dining, available to members, rounds out the area with American and fine dining options steps from Tournament Hills. Ryan Rose points out that the variety of dining within minutes of this 132 home guard gated community is one of the lifestyle advantages that consistently surprises new homeowners.
