Marche Bacchus French Bistro Near Tournament Hills

Marche Bacchus is a lakeside French bistro in Desert Shores that offers Tournament Hills residents one of the most distinctive dining experiences in Summerlin, combining over 950 wine labels with seasonal French cuisine on the shores of Lake Jacqueline.

What Makes Marche Bacchus Special?

Marche Bacchus sits directly on Lake Jacqueline, with tables lining the water's edge and a dining room featuring floor to ceiling windows overlooking the lake. The restaurant has earned a loyal following for its romantic lakeside patio seating, live acoustic guitar, and cozy fireplaces. Guests choose between the serene outdoor patio and the beautiful indoor dining room, both offering uninterrupted views of the water. This setting makes it a favorite for date nights, celebrations, and leisurely weekend brunches among Tournament Hills homeowners.

What Is on the Marche Bacchus Menu?

The menu showcases both traditional and contemporary French dishes, with menus changing seasonally and Chef's specials rotating daily for lunch and dinner. Popular dishes include escargot, steak frites, beef bourguignon, mussels, and the Bacchus charcuterie board. Desserts like creme brulee and Basque cheesecake round out the experience. The wine list features over 950 labels, making it one of the most extensive collections in the Las Vegas Valley. Vegetarian, vegan, and children's menus are also available. Daily lakeside happy hour runs from 3pm to 6pm, offering an approachable way to enjoy the ambiance.

How Far Is Marche Bacchus from Tournament Hills?

The restaurant is located at 2620 Regatta Drive in the Desert Shores community, placing it roughly ten minutes from Tournament Hills by car. Ryan Rose considers Marche Bacchus one of the premier dining perks for residents of this 132 home guard gated community adjacent to TPC Summerlin. The combination of lakeside views, world class wine, and French cuisine this close to home is something that genuinely elevates everyday living in the 89134 zip code.
