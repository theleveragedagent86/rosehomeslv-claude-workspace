Echo and Rig Steakhouse Tivoli Village Near Tournament Hills

Echo and Rig Butcher and Steakhouse at Tivoli Village is a modern steakhouse and working butcher shop that gives Tournament Hills residents a top tier dining destination just minutes from home on Rampart Boulevard.

What Is Echo and Rig Known For?

Echo and Rig is chef Sam Marvin's concept that reimagines the traditional steakhouse by combining a restaurant with an on site butcher shop. The menu features over 90 items, including wood fired steaks, shareable small plates like portobello fries and pork belly burnt ends, and craft cocktails. The butcher shop sits to the right of the entrance, where guests can purchase hand cut meats to prepare at home. The atmosphere is upbeat and polished, striking a balance between fine dining and approachable neighborhood gathering spot.

What Are Echo and Rig's Hours and Location?

The restaurant is located at 440 S Rampart Boulevard in Tivoli Village, Las Vegas, NV 89145. Lunch is served Monday through Friday from 11am to 3:45pm, dinner runs Monday through Thursday and Sunday from 4pm to 10pm, and Friday through Saturday from 4pm to 11pm. Weekend brunch is available Saturday and Sunday from 10am to 3pm. Complimentary valet parking is provided by Tivoli Village at the roundabout in front of the restaurant, making arrival effortless for Tournament Hills residents heading out for the evening.
