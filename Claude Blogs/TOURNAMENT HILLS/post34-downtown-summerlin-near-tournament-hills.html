Downtown Summerlin Shopping Near Tournament Hills

Downtown Summerlin is the premier open air shopping destination for Tournament Hills residents, offering over 125 shops and restaurants across 106 acres just five minutes from the guard gate.

What Stores Are at Downtown Summerlin?

Downtown Summerlin spans 1.6 million square feet and anchors its retail lineup with Macy's, Dillard's, and Nordstrom Rack. The center also features H and M, PGA Tour Superstore, Oil and Vinegar, and a recently opened 46,500 square foot Whole Foods Market. A CHANEL Fragrance and Beauty Boutique opened as the brand's first off Strip location, adding a luxury retail option that appeals to Tournament Hills homeowners who prefer shopping close to home rather than driving to the Strip.

What Restaurants Are at Downtown Summerlin?

Dining options cover everything from Italian to modern American. Maggiano's Little Italy, Lazy Dog Restaurant and Bar, True Food Kitchen, and Makers and Finders anchor the food scene. Newer additions like Marufuku Ramen, which opened its second Las Vegas Valley location here in early 2026, and Harlo Steakhouse and Bar keep the restaurant lineup fresh. Dave and Buster's provides family friendly entertainment alongside dining. Hours run Monday through Thursday from 10am to 8pm, Friday and Saturday from 10am to 9pm, and Sunday from 11am to 7pm, though individual tenant hours may vary.

What Else Does Downtown Summerlin Offer Beyond Shopping?

The development includes Las Vegas Ballpark, home of the Las Vegas Aviators minor league baseball team, and City National Arena, the practice facility of the Vegas Golden Knights. A movie theater and seasonal events round out the entertainment calendar. Ryan Rose notes that Downtown Summerlin's combination of retail, dining, sports, and entertainment within a five minute drive is one of the strongest selling points for buyers considering this 132 home community adjacent to TPC Summerlin.
