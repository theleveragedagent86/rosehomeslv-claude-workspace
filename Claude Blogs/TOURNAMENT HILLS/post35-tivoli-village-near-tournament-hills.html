Tivoli Village Shopping Near Tournament Hills

Tivoli Village is an upscale outdoor shopping and dining destination on Rampart Boulevard that offers Tournament Hills residents boutique retail, fine dining, and spa services within a five to ten minute drive.

What Shops Are at Tivoli Village?

Tivoli Village features over 25 stores and boutiques that range from luxury fashion to home decor and wellness products. Jeff White Custom Jewelry sells customized rings, watches, and unique pendants. Vasari carries women's brands like DVF, JBrand, and Alice and Olivia alongside men's options including John Varvatos clothing. The mix of one of a kind boutiques and curated retail gives Tivoli Village a feel that is distinct from larger centers like Downtown Summerlin, attracting shoppers looking for exclusive finds without leaving the Summerlin area.

What Restaurants and Services Are at Tivoli Village?

Dining at Tivoli Village includes Echo and Rig Butcher and Steakhouse, Brio Tuscan Grille, Kabuki, Cantina Laredo, View Wine Bar and Kitchen, and Leone Cafe. The Ritual Salon and Spa offers facials, massages, body treatments, and a serenity lounge with couple's treatment rooms. The center is located at 400 S Rampart Boulevard, Las Vegas, NV 89145, with hours running Monday through Thursday from 10am to 8pm, Friday and Saturday from 10am to 9pm, and Sunday from 11am to 6pm. Complimentary valet service is available at the main entrance roundabout and on the P1 level of the underground parking structure.

Why Do Tournament Hills Residents Prefer Tivoli Village?

Ryan Rose points to Tivoli Village as a lifestyle anchor for Tournament Hills homeowners who value walkable outdoor shopping, quality dining, and personal services in a single destination. The European inspired architecture and lively streetscape create an experience that feels more like a neighborhood gathering place than a shopping center.
