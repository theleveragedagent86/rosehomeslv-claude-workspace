City National Arena Golden Knights Near Tournament Hills

City National Arena, the official practice facility of the Vegas Golden Knights, is roughly five minutes from Tournament Hills in the Downtown Summerlin area.

What Is City National Arena?

City National Arena is a 146,000 square foot, two story facility located at 1550 South Pavilion Center Drive in Summerlin. It opened in September 2017 as the team headquarters and practice home of the Vegas Golden Knights. The building features two regulation ice rinks, team locker rooms for both the Golden Knights and UNLV hockey, sports medicine facilities, coaches offices, a video room, player lounge, and equipment rooms. Public amenities include a team store, restaurant and bar, skate rental, and a snack bar.

Can Tournament Hills Residents Watch Golden Knights Practice?

Yes. Golden Knights practices are open to the public and free to attend on most practice days during the NHL season. Fans can watch from elevated viewing areas overlooking the main rink. The arena also hosts UNLV Rebels hockey games, Junior Golden Knights youth programs, and recreational skating sessions. For families in Tournament Hills, this means access to professional and youth hockey programming just minutes from home without the need to drive to the Strip or downtown Las Vegas.

What Else Is Near City National Arena?

City National Arena sits adjacent to Las Vegas Ballpark and within the Downtown Summerlin retail and entertainment district. Over 125 shops and restaurants surround the arena, so residents can combine a morning practice viewing with lunch, errands, or shopping in a single trip. The five minute drive from Tournament Hills makes this a convenient weekday or weekend destination rather than a planned outing.
