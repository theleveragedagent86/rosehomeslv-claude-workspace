Boca Park Fashion Village Near Tournament Hills

Boca Park Fashion Village is roughly five to ten minutes from Tournament Hills, offering a curated mix of retail stores, dining, and lifestyle services along South Rampart Boulevard.

What Is Boca Park Fashion Village?

Boca Park Fashion Village is an open air shopping center located at 750 South Rampart Boulevard in Las Vegas, Nevada 89145. The center features a blend of national retailers and local boutiques, with anchor tenants including Target, REI, and Total Wine. Dining options range from the Cheesecake Factory to fast casual spots and coffee shops. The center also includes beauty, health, and wellness businesses, making it a one stop destination for errands and leisure. Ample parking and a clean, well maintained layout keep the shopping experience comfortable year round.
