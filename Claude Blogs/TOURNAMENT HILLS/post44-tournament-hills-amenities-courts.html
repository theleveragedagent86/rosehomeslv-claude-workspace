Tournament Hills Amenities: Tennis, Basketball, Playground

Tournament Hills provides residents with private tennis courts, a basketball court, a playground, a shaded picnic pavilion, and a dog park, all maintained by the HOA and reserved exclusively for the 132 households within this guard gated Summerlin community.

What Sports Courts Does Tournament Hills Have?

The community includes dedicated tennis courts and a basketball court, both located within the neighborhood gates. Because Tournament Hills has only 132 homes and a population of roughly 230 people, these facilities see far less crowding than public alternatives. Residents can typically use the courts on their own schedule without reservations or long waits. The tennis courts are well maintained by the HOA, and the basketball court provides a convenient option for pickup games and family activity.

Is the Playground Suitable for Families?

The Tournament Hills playground sits within the community alongside a shaded picnic pavilion, creating a combined recreation area where families can spend time outdoors without leaving the security of the guard gate. The pavilion offers covered seating for gatherings, birthday celebrations, or casual weekend use. For a neighborhood with a median age of 57, the playground serves visiting grandchildren and the handful of families with younger children, while the pavilion appeals to residents of all ages.

What Additional Amenities Come with TPC Summerlin Membership?

Tournament Hills borders TPC Summerlin golf course, and residents who hold club memberships gain access to an Olympic sized swimming pool, additional tennis courts, and a full clubhouse with dining and event space. These amenities supplement the neighborhood's own facilities, giving members a layered recreational experience. The golf course itself hosts the PGA Tour's Shriners Children's Open, adding prestige and tournament quality course conditions that members enjoy year round.

How Do Amenities Compare to Other Summerlin Communities?

Ryan Rose notes that Tournament Hills offers a rare combination for a community its size. Most guard gated neighborhoods with 132 homes do not include tennis courts, a basketball court, a playground, a pavilion, and a dog park all within the gates. Larger master planned communities may have more extensive facilities, but those are shared among thousands of residents. Tournament Hills keeps the ratio of amenities to households exceptionally favorable, which translates to better upkeep and greater accessibility for every homeowner.
