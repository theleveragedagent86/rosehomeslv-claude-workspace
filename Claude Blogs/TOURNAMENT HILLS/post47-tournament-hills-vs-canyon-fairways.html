Tournament Hills vs Canyon Fairways Summerlin

Tournament Hills and Canyon Fairways are both guard gated, custom home communities in Summerlin that share a border with TPC Summerlin, but they differ in size, lot dimensions, and price range. Understanding these distinctions helps luxury buyers identify the right fit.

How Do the Two Communities Compare in Size?

Tournament Hills contains 132 custom homes built between 1993 and 2001, while Canyon Fairways is a smaller enclave of approximately 89 homes built primarily in the 1990s. Both communities are fully built out with no remaining vacant lots, meaning all future inventory depends entirely on existing homeowners choosing to sell. This fixed supply dynamic keeps resale competition high in each neighborhood.

What About Lot Sizes and Home Prices?

Lot sizes represent one of the biggest differences between these two communities. Tournament Hills offers homesites ranging from 0.36 to 2.52 acres, with many properties exceeding half an acre. Canyon Fairways lots tend to be smaller, giving homeowners less outdoor space and privacy. On pricing, Tournament Hills ranges from $1.5 million to $13.9 million, with a community average near $5 million. Canyon Fairways homes fall in a similar overall range but generally sit at a lower average price point due to the more compact lot configurations.

Do Both Communities Have TPC Summerlin Access?

Yes. Both Tournament Hills and Canyon Fairways are adjacent to TPC Summerlin, the PGA TOUR venue that hosts the annual Shriners Children's Open. Residents of either community enjoy proximity to a world class championship golf course, with some homes in both neighborhoods offering direct fairway or course views. The shared golf course connection makes these two communities natural competitors in the luxury market.

Ryan Rose's Local Insight

Ryan Rose notes that buyers choosing between Tournament Hills and Canyon Fairways typically come down to one factor: space. Tournament Hills delivers substantially larger lots, which translates to greater privacy, more room for pools and outdoor entertaining, and stronger long term appreciation. Canyon Fairways offers a similar guard gated lifestyle at a slightly lower entry point, but the lot size premium in Tournament Hills is difficult to replicate anywhere else in Summerlin.
