Best Luxury Neighborhoods in Summerlin Las Vegas

Summerlin is home to several guard gated luxury communities, each offering custom homes, privacy, and proximity to world class amenities. Tournament Hills, The Ridges, Canyon Fairways, Queensridge, and Spanish Hills represent the top tier of this market.

What Makes Tournament Hills Stand Out?

Tournament Hills features 132 custom homes on lots ranging from 0.36 to 2.52 acres, with 24 hour guard gate security and direct adjacency to TPC Summerlin's PGA TOUR venue. Homes are priced from $1.5 million to $13.9 million, with a community average near $5 million. Built between 1993 and 2001, the neighborhood is fully built out, meaning its supply is permanently fixed. The combination of oversized lots, a prestigious golf course neighbor, and finite inventory makes Tournament Hills one of the most sought after addresses in Las Vegas.

How Do The Ridges and Canyon Fairways Compare?

The Ridges is Summerlin's premier modern luxury enclave, with homes built in the 2000s and 2010s featuring contemporary architecture, clean lines, and walls of glass. Prices range from $2 million to over $15 million. The community sits adjacent to Bear's Best Golf Club. Canyon Fairways is a smaller guard gated neighborhood of roughly 89 custom homes, also adjacent to TPC Summerlin, with homes built in the 1990s. Canyon Fairways offers a similar golf course lifestyle to Tournament Hills but on more compact lots.

What About Queensridge and Spanish Hills?

Queensridge stands apart as a guard gated community that includes both custom estate homes and the Queensridge Towers luxury high rise condominiums. The neighborhood is known for lush, manicured grounds and a more traditional estate feel. Spanish Hills, located in the southern portion of the Summerlin area, is a guard gated community prized for its elevated positioning and direct views of the Las Vegas Strip. Both communities cater to buyers seeking distinctive luxury experiences outside the golf course model.

Ryan Rose's Local Insight

Ryan Rose considers Tournament Hills the strongest overall value in Summerlin's luxury tier because of its unmatched lot sizes and the permanence of its 132 home cap. While The Ridges attracts buyers who want newer, modern construction and Queensridge appeals to those who prefer a high rise option, Tournament Hills delivers a combination of space, privacy, and PGA TOUR adjacency that no other Summerlin community can match.
