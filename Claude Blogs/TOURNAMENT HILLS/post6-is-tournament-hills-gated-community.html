Is Tournament Hills a Gated Community?

Yes, Tournament Hills is a guard gated community in Summerlin, Las Vegas. This exclusive enclave of just 132 custom homes features a staffed guard gate with 24 hour security, making it one of the most private residential communities in the Las Vegas valley.

What Type of Security Does Tournament Hills Have?

Tournament Hills operates a 24 hour guard gate, meaning every visitor is checked before entering the community. In addition to the staffed gate, roaming security guards patrol the neighborhood around the clock. This level of protection is a major reason why high profile residents, including reported celebrity homeowners, choose to live here.

What Makes Tournament Hills Different From Other Gated Communities?

Unlike many gated communities that rely solely on a key card or remote entry system, Tournament Hills maintains a live, staffed guard gate at all times. The community is also part of The Hills South village within the Summerlin master plan, which adds an additional layer of community management. With only 132 homes on lots ranging from 0.36 to 2.52 acres, the small size of the neighborhood means security personnel are familiar with every resident.

Do You Need Special Access to Visit Tournament Hills?

Prospective buyers should be aware that proof of funds or a pre qualification letter is required before touring homes inside the gate. This policy helps maintain the privacy and exclusivity that residents expect.
