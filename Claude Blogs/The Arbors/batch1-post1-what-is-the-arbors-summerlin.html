# What is The Arbors in Summerlin The Arbors is one of the largest and most established residential villages in the Summerlin master plan, spanning over 1,200 acres in Summerlin North. Built primarily from the late 1990s through the early 2000s, the community is fully developed and known for its mature landscaping, tree-lined streets, and wide range of housing options from entry-level condos to guard-gated luxury estates. ## Community Overview The Arbors sits east of the 215 Beltway and south of Summerlin Parkway, placing it in one of the most convenient locations within the Summerlin master plan. Downtown Summerlin is just 2.5 miles away, and the Las Vegas Strip is approximately 15 minutes by car. The community falls within the 89144 zip code. Unlike newer Summerlin villages that are still under construction, The Arbors is completely built out. Every home available is a resale, which means buyers benefit from established neighborhoods, mature trees, and a community that has had over two decades to settle into its character. ## Housing Variety One of The Arbors' defining features is the range of housing types within a single village. The community includes gated condo complexes like Amber Ridge and Silver Pine, townhome neighborhoods like Sunset Mesa and Cottonwood Terrace, traditional single-family homes at various price points, and two guard-gated luxury enclaves: The Palisades and Bellacere. This variety means The Arbors attracts everyone from first-time buyers and young professionals to established families and retirees, all within the same village. ## Parks and Amenities The Arbors is home to four parks: Tree Top Park, Arbors Tennis and Play Park, Arbors Paseo Park, and Cottonwood Canyon. The village also includes the 16-acre Arbors Sports Park with an Olympic-sized swimming pool, softball fields, a soccer field, and picnic areas. The Arbors Tennis and Play Park houses the Summerlin Tennis Club with five lighted courts, a pro shop, and a full-time tennis professional. The 45,000-square-foot Veterans Memorial Leisure Services Center sits within the community, offering a fitness center, indoor pool, classes, and recreational programming for all ages. ## Schools The Arbors is served by two top-rated elementary schools, Staton Elementary and Bonner Elementary, both holding 9 out of 10 GreatSchools ratings. Rogich Middle School and Palo Verde High School complete the public school pipeline, giving families a strong academic path from kindergarten through graduation. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose considers The Arbors one of the best values in Summerlin because of its combination of location, variety, and mature community character. The established landscaping and tree canopy give The Arbors a feel that newer communities cannot replicate, and the proximity to Downtown Summerlin makes daily life remarkably convenient.
