# Is The Arbors Summerlin a Good Place to Live The Arbors is one of the most well-rounded villages in Summerlin, offering mature landscaping, top-rated schools, diverse housing options, and a central location that puts shopping, dining, and outdoor recreation within minutes. For buyers who value an established neighborhood with character over new construction, The Arbors consistently ranks among the strongest options in the Summerlin master plan. ## Location and Convenience The Arbors sits in Summerlin North, just 2.5 miles from Downtown Summerlin and its 125-plus stores and restaurants. Red Rock Casino Resort is nearby for dining and entertainment, and Tivoli Village offers upscale shopping and patio restaurants within a short drive. The 215 Beltway and Summerlin Parkway provide quick access to the rest of the valley, with the Las Vegas Strip approximately 15 minutes away. For daily errands, grocery stores, medical offices, banks, and gas stations are all within a few minutes of the community. The central Summerlin North location means residents rarely need to travel far for anything. ## Schools Families in The Arbors are served by some of the highest-rated public schools in Clark County. Staton Elementary and Bonner Elementary both hold 9 out of 10 GreatSchools ratings and rank in the top 5 to 10 percent of all schools in Nevada. Rogich Middle School and Palo Verde High School provide a strong academic path through graduation, with Palo Verde offering AP courses, competitive athletics, and college preparation programs. ## Community Character What sets The Arbors apart from newer Summerlin villages is its maturity. Homes were built in the late 1990s and early 2000s, which means the trees have grown in, the landscaping is lush, and the neighborhood has a settled, established feel. The streets are quieter than newer developments that are still surrounded by active construction. The community includes four parks, the Summerlin Tennis Club, a 16-acre sports park with an Olympic-sized pool, and the 45,000-square-foot Veterans Memorial Leisure Center. These amenities create a self-contained village where residents can exercise, play sports, and gather without leaving The Arbors. ## Safety Summerlin North, where The Arbors is located, ranks in the 82nd percentile for safety nationally, meaning it is statistically safer than approximately 82 percent of neighborhoods across the United States. The guard-gated sections of Palisades and Bellacere add an additional layer of security for residents in those enclaves. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose recommends The Arbors for buyers who want a proven community rather than a promise.
