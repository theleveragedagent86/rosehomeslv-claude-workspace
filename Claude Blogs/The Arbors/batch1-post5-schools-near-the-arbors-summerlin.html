# Schools Near The Arbors Summerlin The Arbors is one of the strongest school zones in the Las Vegas valley, with two 9-out-of-10 rated elementary schools, a well-regarded middle school, and one of Nevada's top public high schools all serving the community. Families in The Arbors have access to a complete K through 12 pipeline within the Clark County School District. ## Elementary Schools Ethel W. Staton Elementary School holds a 9 out of 10 GreatSchools rating and ranks in the top 5 percent of all public schools in Nevada. The school serves 677 students in grades pre-K through 5 with a 20 to 1 student-teacher ratio. Math proficiency reaches 69 percent and reading proficiency hits 73 percent, both well above state averages of 28 and 44 percent respectively. Staton Elementary is located at 1700 Sageberry Drive, directly within The Arbors village. John W. Bonner Elementary School also holds a 9 out of 10 GreatSchools rating and ranks in the top 10 percent statewide. Bonner serves approximately 817 students with math proficiency at 64 percent and reading proficiency at 69 percent. The school is located nearby on Crestdale Lane and serves portions of The Arbors as well as neighboring areas. ## Middle School Sig Rogich Middle School serves grades 6 through 8 and is located within The Arbors village. The school opened in 2000 alongside much of the residential development in the area. Rogich offers honors-level classes, performing arts, visual arts, and technology electives. The school feeds into Palo Verde High School, creating a clear academic path for families. ## High School Palo Verde High School is located within The Arbors at 333 South Pavilion Center Drive, enrolling over 3,100 students in grades 9 through 12. The school consistently ranks among the top public high schools in Nevada and offers Advanced Placement courses, dual enrollment with local colleges, competitive varsity athletics, and a broad range of extracurricular programs. Having the high school physically located within the village is a convenience factor that many families underestimate. Students can walk, bike, or get a short ride to school without navigating major intersections or highways. ## Zoning Verification School zoning in Clark County is tied to specific residential addresses, and different homes within The Arbors may be zoned for different elementary schools.
