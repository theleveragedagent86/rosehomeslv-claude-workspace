# Bellacere Custom Homes in The Arbors Summerlin Bellacere is the most exclusive enclave within The Arbors village, featuring 57 custom homesites behind a guard gate in Summerlin. With homes ranging from 3,040 to over 16,000 square feet, Bellacere represents the highest tier of residential living in the village and one of the more unique custom home opportunities in the broader Summerlin market. ## Custom Homesites Unlike production-built neighborhoods where a handful of floor plans are repeated throughout the community, Bellacere was designed as a custom home enclave. Each of the 57 lots was developed individually, resulting in distinct architectural designs, floor plans, and finishes from home to home. This gives Bellacere a character that is more comparable to a custom estate neighborhood than a typical subdivision. Home sizes in Bellacere vary dramatically, from just over 3,000 square feet for the smaller residences to over 16,500 square feet for the largest estates. This range reflects the variety of buyers who have built or purchased in the community over the years, from downsizing luxury buyers to families who wanted maximum space and customization. ## Guard-Gated Security Bellacere operates its own guard gate with controlled access for residents and guests. The gated entry eliminates through traffic and provides a layer of privacy that is particularly valued by buyers at this price point. The small community size of 57 homes means residents know their neighbors and the community feels intimate and private. ## Community Character Because Bellacere homes were custom built and the community has been established for over two decades, the landscaping is mature and the neighborhood is fully settled. The lots are generous by Summerlin standards, providing space between homes and room for pools, outdoor kitchens, and private yard areas that production homes in the area cannot offer. The custom nature of the homes also means that interiors vary significantly. Some homes have been extensively updated with modern finishes, while others retain their original design, presenting renovation opportunities for buyers who want to personalize their space. ## Pricing and Availability Bellacere homes occasionally list well above $1 million, with the largest and most updated estates commanding premium pricing. Because there are only 57 homes in the community, inventory is extremely limited. It is common for months to pass without a single listing, making Bellacere a community where patience and preparation are essential for buyers. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose describes Bellacere as a hidden asset within The Arbors.
