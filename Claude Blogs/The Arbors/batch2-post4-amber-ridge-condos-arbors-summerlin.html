# Amber Ridge Condos in The Arbors Summerlin Amber Ridge is a gated condominium community within The Arbors village in Summerlin, offering an affordable entry point into one of the most established and amenity-rich areas of the master plan. With resort-style amenities, mountain views, and access to the full Summerlin lifestyle, Amber Ridge is a popular option for first-time buyers, young professionals, and investors. ## Units and Floor Plans Amber Ridge offers one- and two-bedroom condominiums with a variety of floor plans and layouts. Units feature gourmet kitchens, private balconies or patios, and finishes that vary based on when individual units were last updated. The complex was built during the same late 1990s to early 2000s development period as the rest of The Arbors, so the construction is solid and the community is well established. Many units have been renovated by previous owners with updated flooring, countertops, appliances, and fixtures. Buyers looking for a turnkey unit can find updated options, while those looking for value may find original-condition units at lower price points with renovation potential. ## Community Amenities Amber Ridge operates as a gated community with its own amenity package separate from the broader Summerlin amenities. The complex features a resort-style swimming pool, a fitness center, and a putting green. These on-site amenities give residents convenient access to recreation without leaving the complex. In addition to the Amber Ridge amenities, condo owners also have access to the full Summerlin amenity network through their master association dues, including the Arbors Tennis and Play Park, the Veterans Memorial Leisure Center, community pools, and the 200-plus-mile trail system. ## Pricing Condos in Amber Ridge generally fall in the low $200,000s to mid $300,000s, making them among the most affordable options in the Summerlin master plan. At this price point, buyers get a Summerlin address, gated community security, and access to top-rated Summerlin schools and amenities that comparable condos in other parts of the valley cannot offer. The relatively low entry price also makes Amber Ridge attractive to investors looking for rental properties in a desirable school zone with strong tenant demand. ## Location Within The Arbors Amber Ridge sits within the heart of The Arbors village, with walking access to parks, the Summerlin Tennis Club, and the Veterans Memorial Leisure Center. Downtown Summerlin is approximately 2.5 miles away, and the 215 Beltway and Summerlin Parkway provide quick access to the rest of the valley. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose recommends Amber Ridge for buyers who want the Summerlin lifestyle at an entry-level price. The combination of gated security, on-site amenities, and access to the Summerlin school system and parks makes it one of the strongest value propositions for condo buyers in the west side of the valley. He suggests comparing updated units against original-condition units to weigh the renovation investment against the upfront savings.
