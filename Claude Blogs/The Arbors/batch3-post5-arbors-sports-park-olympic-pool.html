# Arbors Sports Park and Olympic Pool Summerlin The Arbors Sports Park is a 16-plus-acre recreation complex in The Arbors village of Summerlin, anchored by an Olympic-sized swimming pool and complemented by ball fields, play areas, and the 45,000-square-foot Veterans Memorial Leisure Services Center. It is one of the largest and most comprehensive park facilities within the Summerlin master plan. ## Olympic-Sized Swimming Pool The centerpiece of the sports park is the Olympic-sized swimming pool, which serves as both a recreational facility and a programming venue. The pool accommodates lap swimming, swim lessons, water aerobics, and open swim sessions. During the summer months, the pool is one of the most popular gathering spots in The Arbors, particularly for families with children. The pool operates on a seasonal schedule with extended hours during the warmer months. Access is available to Summerlin association members. ## Ball Fields and Sports Facilities The sports park includes two lighted softball fields and a soccer field, providing space for organized leagues, practices, and pickup games. The lighted fields allow evening play, which is critical during the summer when daytime temperatures make outdoor sports impractical. The combination of softball and soccer facilities makes the sports park a hub for youth and adult leagues throughout the spring and fall seasons. Weekend mornings during league play bring a community atmosphere with families gathered along the sidelines. ## Children's Play Area and Picnic Pavilions Beyond the pool and fields, the sports park features a children's play area and picnic pavilions suitable for birthday parties, family gatherings, and community events. The play area gives younger children a place to stay entertained while older siblings participate in sports activities, making the park practical for families with kids of different ages. ## Veterans Memorial Leisure Center Adjacent to the sports park, the Veterans Memorial Leisure Services Center adds 45,000 square feet of indoor recreation space. The center includes a fitness center, indoor pool, gymnasium, dance studios, and classrooms offering programs from yoga and Pilates to youth gymnastics and martial arts. The center operates year-round and provides affordable membership and class options. The combination of the outdoor sports park and the indoor leisure center creates a recreation complex that functions in all seasons and weather conditions. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose points out that the Arbors Sports Park is the type of amenity that residents use weekly, not just occasionally. The Olympic pool during summer, the softball fields during league season, and the Veterans Memorial Leisure Center year-round give families and active adults a reason to stay within the community for their recreation needs.
