# Staton Elementary School Near The Arbors Summerlin Ethel W. Staton Elementary School is one of the highest-rated public elementary schools in Nevada, located at 1700 Sageberry Drive directly within The Arbors village in Summerlin. The school holds a 9 out of 10 GreatSchools rating and ranks in the top 5 percent of all public schools in the state. ## Academic Performance Staton Elementary consistently outperforms state and district averages across all core subjects. Math proficiency reaches 69 percent, compared to the Nevada state average of 28 percent. Reading and language arts proficiency hits 73 percent, well above the state average of 44 percent. These numbers place Staton among the top-performing elementary schools in Clark County. The school earned an A-minus rating on Niche and ranks 17th out of 692 public schools statewide, confirming its position as one of the elite elementary campuses in the Las Vegas valley. ## School Details Staton Elementary serves 677 students in grades pre-kindergarten through 5th grade with a student-teacher ratio of 20 to 1. The school was built in 2000 as part of the development of The Arbors village and follows the standard Clark County School District curriculum. Programs include core academics, physical education, art, music, and library instruction. The campus is well maintained and benefits from strong parent involvement through PTA programs and volunteer activities. Active parent engagement is a consistent factor in the school's high performance. ## Location Within The Arbors Staton Elementary sits on Sageberry Drive, the same street that hosts the Arbors Tennis and Play Park and Tree Top Park. This central location within the village means many families are within walking distance of the school, reducing the morning and afternoon commute to a short stroll. The school's position within a residential village rather than along a major road contributes to a quieter, safer campus environment with less traffic congestion during drop-off and pick-up times. ## Zoning School zoning in Clark County is determined by residential address. Not all homes in The Arbors are necessarily zoned for Staton Elementary, as some addresses may fall within the Bonner Elementary boundary.
