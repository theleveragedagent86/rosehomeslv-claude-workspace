# Bonner Elementary School Near The Arbors Summerlin John W. Bonner Elementary School is one of the top-rated public elementary schools serving The Arbors village in Summerlin. The school holds a 9 out of 10 GreatSchools rating and ranks in the top 10 percent of all public schools in Nevada, making it one of two highly-rated elementary options available to Arbors families. ## Academic Performance Bonner Elementary achieves strong academic results across core subjects. Math proficiency reaches 64 percent, more than double the Nevada state average of 30 percent. Reading and language arts proficiency hits 69 percent, well above the state average of 44 percent. Both math and reading proficiency rank in the top 5 percent statewide. The school earned an A-minus rating on Niche, confirming its status as one of the premier elementary campuses in the Clark County School District. ## School Details Bonner Elementary serves approximately 817 students in grades pre-kindergarten through 5th grade with a student-teacher ratio of 19 to 1. The school is located on Crestdale Lane and serves portions of The Arbors as well as adjacent Summerlin neighborhoods. The larger student body compared to some Summerlin elementary schools allows Bonner to offer a wider range of programs and extracurricular activities. The school follows the standard Clark County School District curriculum with additional support for gifted students and English language learners. ## Two Top Elementary Schools The Arbors is one of the few Summerlin villages where families have two 9-rated elementary schools within or immediately adjacent to the community. While individual homes are zoned for either Staton Elementary or Bonner Elementary based on their specific address, both schools deliver exceptional academic outcomes. This dual-school advantage means families purchasing in The Arbors are likely to be zoned for a top-performing campus regardless of which side of the village they choose. ## School Pipeline Bonner Elementary feeds into Sig Rogich Middle School and ultimately Palo Verde High School, providing a consistent academic pipeline through 12th grade. All three schools are located within or near The Arbors village, keeping the daily school routine manageable for families with children at multiple grade levels. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose notes that having two 9-rated elementary schools serving a single village is unusual in the Clark County system and is a significant factor in The Arbors' appeal to families. He recommends that buyers identify which school zones for their preferred address early in the search, though he reassures clients that both Staton and Bonner deliver strong results.
