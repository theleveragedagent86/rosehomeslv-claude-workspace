# Best Restaurants Near The Arbors Summerlin The Arbors sits in one of the strongest dining corridors in the Las Vegas valley, with restaurants ranging from casual family spots to chef-driven fine dining within a 5 to 15 minute drive. The concentration of options at Downtown Summerlin, Red Rock Casino, and Tivoli Village means Arbors residents rarely need to drive to the Strip for a quality meal. ## Downtown Summerlin Downtown Summerlin hosts the largest cluster of restaurants near The Arbors, approximately 2.5 miles away. North Italia serves handmade pastas and wood-fired pizzas in an upscale casual setting. True Food Kitchen offers a health-conscious menu with seasonal ingredients. Seasons 52 provides a weekly rotating menu with lighter portions and an extensive wine list. Yard House covers the casual end with a massive draft beer selection and a broad American menu. For quicker options, Shake Shack and California Pizza Kitchen offer reliable meals for families and groups who want quality without a long wait. ## Red Rock Casino Resort Red Rock Casino is within minutes of The Arbors and houses several standout restaurants. Osteria Fiorella, from James Beard award-winning chef Marc Vetri, serves wood-fired pizzas, handmade pastas, and Italian entrees. Harlo is a classically elegant steakhouse developed by local restaurateur Jeffrey Fine. Lotus of Siam brings Northern Thai cuisine and a Wine Spectator-recognized wine list to the resort. Lucky Penny Cafe operates 24 hours for casual dining at any time. ## Tivoli Village Tivoli Village offers a European-inspired outdoor dining atmosphere approximately 10 minutes from The Arbors. The restaurants here tend toward patio seating, wine-focused menus, and a more intimate setting than the larger Downtown Summerlin options. Tivoli Village is particularly popular for date nights and special occasion dinners. ## Neighborhood Options Beyond the three main dining hubs, the Charleston Boulevard and Summerlin Parkway corridors near The Arbors host a variety of independent restaurants, chain options, and takeout spots that cover everyday dining needs. Sushi, Mexican, Mediterranean, and American grill restaurants are all within a short drive. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose considers the dining access from The Arbors one of its most practical lifestyle benefits.
