# Retiring in The Arbors Summerlin The Arbors is not an age-restricted community, but its combination of walkable parks, affordable condo options, low-maintenance townhomes, a full-service leisure center, and proximity to medical facilities makes it a practical choice for retirees who want an active lifestyle without the limitations of a 55-plus community. ## No Age Restrictions Unlike communities such as Sun City Summerlin, The Arbors has no age requirements or restrictions. Retirees live alongside families, young professionals, and everyone in between. This mixed-age environment appeals to retirees who prefer a vibrant, multigenerational community over one where all residents are in the same life stage. ## Low-Maintenance Housing Options Retirees in The Arbors can choose from condos in Amber Ridge and Silver Pine or townhomes in Sunset Mesa and Cottonwood Terrace, all of which include HOA-maintained exteriors. These options eliminate yard work, exterior painting, and landscaping maintenance, freeing up time for travel, hobbies, and recreation. Condo pricing in the low $200,000s makes The Arbors one of the most affordable entry points into Summerlin for retirees on a fixed income, while still providing access to the full Summerlin amenity package. ## Recreation and Wellness The Veterans Memorial Leisure Center is a standout resource for retirees. The 45,000-square-foot facility offers senior-specific programming including gentle yoga, tai chi, and social activities. The fitness center, indoor pool, and group classes provide options for staying active year-round. The center is known for affordable membership rates and offers veteran discounts. Beyond the leisure center, the Summerlin Tennis Club, the connected park system, and the Cottonwood Canyon trails provide outdoor recreation options that are accessible and well-maintained. ## Medical Access Summerlin Hospital Medical Center is minutes from The Arbors, providing emergency services, specialty care, and primary care access. The medical office complexes along Town Center Drive and Pavilion Center Drive house a concentration of physicians, specialists, and dentists within a short drive. ## Shopping and Dining Convenience With Downtown Summerlin 2.5 miles away and Red Rock Casino nearby, daily errands, dining, and entertainment are all handled within a small radius. This convenience reduces driving and makes The Arbors practical for retirees who want to minimize time on the road. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose works with many retirees who choose The Arbors specifically because it is not age-restricted.
