# Pet Friendly Living in The Arbors Summerlin The Arbors is a practical community for dog owners and pet lovers, with miles of walking trails, multiple parks allowing leashed dogs, and proximity to dedicated off-leash dog parks in the broader Summerlin area. The established landscaping and connected path system make daily dog walking convenient and comfortable. ## Walking Trails Within The Arbors The Cottonwood Canyon linear park provides miles of paved, shaded trails along the southern edge of The Arbors. These trails are flat, well-maintained, and connect to the broader Summerlin trail system. Dogs on leash are welcome on the trails, making them ideal for morning and evening walks directly from the neighborhood. The Arbors Paseo Park connector between Tree Top Park and the Arbors Tennis and Play Park adds another walking route through the heart of the village. The mature tree canopy along these paths provides shade that is essential during the warmer months, both for owner comfort and for protecting dogs' paws from hot pavement. ## Parks Dogs on leash are permitted in The Arbors' parks, including Tree Top Park, Cottonwood Canyon, and the Paseo Park corridor. Dogs are not allowed in sports activity areas or on playing fields, but the walking paths, grass areas, and picnic zones are available for leashed pets. The variety of walking routes within the community means dog owners can vary their daily routine without driving to a different location, which is a practical convenience for owners who walk their dogs multiple times per day. ## Nearby Dog Parks For off-leash play, Barkin' Basin Dog Park in Summerlin offers separate areas for large and small dogs, agility equipment, and grassy fields.
