# The Arbors vs Reverence Summerlin The Arbors and Reverence represent two different eras and philosophies within the Summerlin master plan. The Arbors is a 1,200-acre established village built in the late 1990s and early 2000s, while Reverence is a 300-acre guard-gated community by Pulte Homes that represents Summerlin's newer development in Summerlin West. The choice between them often comes down to whether a buyer prioritizes established character and value or new construction and exclusive amenities. ## New vs Established Reverence offers new and newer construction with modern floor plans, current design trends, and builder warranties. Homes range from townhomes in the Monument collection to single-family homes up to 4,815 square feet across 23 floor plans and 6 collections. Everything in Reverence is either recently built or still being built. The Arbors offers established homes with mature landscaping, settled neighborhoods, and a community that has been functioning for over two decades. No new construction is available, but buyers gain character, shade, and a community that is fully built and proven. ## Guard-Gated vs Mixed Access Reverence is entirely guard-gated, meaning every home in the community sits behind a staffed entry gate. This provides a uniform level of security and traffic control across the entire 300-acre community. The Arbors is primarily non-gated, with guard-gated enclaves in Palisades and Bellacere for buyers who want that security level. The majority of The Arbors' neighborhoods are open-access within the Summerlin master plan. ## Amenities Reverence features a 16,000-square-foot clubhouse, the 9,663-square-foot Skye Fitness Center, an 8-acre park with pickleball, tennis, and basketball courts, and a resort-style pool and spa. These amenities are exclusive to Reverence residents. The Arbors offers the Summerlin Tennis Club, a 16-acre Sports Park with an Olympic-sized pool, the 45,000-square-foot Veterans Memorial Leisure Center, and four connected parks. These amenities are larger in total scale but are shared with the broader Summerlin community. ## Pricing Reverence homes generally start in the mid $500,000s for townhomes and range to over $1 million for larger single-family homes. The Arbors covers a wider price range, from condos in the low $200,000s to luxury estates exceeding $1 million in Bellacere. ## Location The Arbors sits in Summerlin North, 2.5 miles from Downtown Summerlin. Reverence is in Summerlin West at approximately 3,200 feet of elevation, farther from the commercial centers but closer to Red Rock Canyon. The Arbors has a shorter commute to most valley destinations. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose frames the decision simply: Reverence is for buyers who want new construction, a guard-gated perimeter, and a private amenity package. The Arbors is for buyers who want an established community, more housing options at a wider range of price points, and a central location near Summerlin's commercial core.
