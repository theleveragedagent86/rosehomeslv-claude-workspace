# Does The Arbors Summerlin Have a Community Pool Yes. The Arbors has an Olympic-sized swimming pool at the Arbors Sports Park, an indoor pool at the adjacent Veterans Memorial Leisure Center, and additional Summerlin community pools accessible through the master association membership. For a community built in the late 1990s, the pool infrastructure in and around The Arbors is extensive. ## Olympic-Sized Pool at the Sports Park The primary outdoor pool in The Arbors is the Olympic-sized swimming pool located at the 16-acre Arbors Sports Park. This pool accommodates lap swimming, swim lessons, water aerobics, and open recreational swimming. During the summer months, the pool is one of the most popular gathering spots in the village, particularly for families with children. The pool operates on a seasonal schedule with extended hours during the warmer months. Access is available to Summerlin association members as part of their community dues. ## Indoor Pool at Veterans Memorial Leisure Center The Veterans Memorial Leisure Center, located adjacent to the Sports Park at 101 North Pavilion Center Drive, features an indoor pool that operates year-round. This pool provides a climate-controlled option for lap swimming, water aerobics, and swim instruction regardless of weather or season. The indoor pool is particularly valuable during the cooler months from November through March when outdoor pools are typically closed. It allows swimmers to maintain their routine without interruption. ## Additional Summerlin Pools Through the Summerlin master association, Arbors residents also have access to community pools at other Summerlin community centers, including The Trails, The Willows, The Gardens, and The Vistas. These pools offer variety and alternative locations for residents who want a change of scenery or live closer to a different facility. ## Condo Complex Pools Some condo communities within The Arbors, such as Amber Ridge, have their own private pools exclusively for complex residents. These smaller pools offer a more intimate and less crowded alternative to the larger community pools. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose notes that pool access is one of the questions he hears most from buyers considering The Arbors.
