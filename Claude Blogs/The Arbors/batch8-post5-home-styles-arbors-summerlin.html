# Home Styles in The Arbors Summerlin The Arbors offers a wider range of home styles than most Summerlin villages, from affordable condos in the low $200,000s to custom estates exceeding $1 million. This variety exists because the village was developed over several years by multiple builders, each contributing distinct architectural and floorplan approaches across different subdivisions. ## Condos Amber Ridge is the primary condo community within The Arbors, featuring gated access, a resort-style pool, fitness center, and putting green. Units range from approximately 900 to 1,400 square feet with one to three bedrooms. Silver Pine offers another condo option with similar sizing in a quieter setting. Condos in The Arbors generally start in the low $200,000s, making them the most affordable entry point into the village and into Summerlin as a whole. Condo exteriors are maintained by the HOA, and both communities include covered parking. These units appeal to first-time buyers, retirees looking to downsize, and investors seeking rental income in a high-demand area. ## Townhomes Sunset Mesa and Cottonwood Terrace provide attached townhome living with more space than the condos. Townhomes typically range from 1,200 to 1,800 square feet with two to three bedrooms and attached garages. Pricing generally falls between $300,000 and $450,000. The townhome communities offer a middle ground between condo convenience and single-family space. HOA-maintained exteriors reduce owner responsibility while the attached garages and private entries provide a more residential feel than the condo communities. ## Single-Family Homes The largest segment of The Arbors consists of traditional single-family homes built by production builders including Pardee, Richmond American, and others active during the late 1990s and early 2000s. These homes range from approximately 1,400 to 3,500 square feet with three to five bedrooms, two-car or three-car garages, and private yards. Architectural styles lean toward Mediterranean and desert contemporary with stucco exteriors, tile roofs, and desert landscaping. Pricing for single-family homes generally ranges from $450,000 to $700,000 depending on size, lot, condition, and proximity to parks or schools. ## Guard-Gated Luxury The Palisades is a guard-gated enclave of approximately 130 Tuscan-inspired homes adjacent to the TPC Las Vegas golf course. Homes range from 2,875 to over 7,000 square feet with premium finishes, private pools, and golf course or mountain views. The Palisades represents the upper tier of production-built luxury in The Arbors. Bellacere takes luxury further with 57 custom homesites featuring homes from 3,040 to over 16,000 square feet. These are individually designed estates with custom architecture, resort-style backyards, and finishes that reflect each owner's vision. Bellacere homes can exceed $1 million and represent some of the most distinctive properties in all of Summerlin. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose points out that The Arbors' range of home styles allows buyers to enter the community at nearly any budget and potentially move up within the same village as their needs and finances change. A buyer who starts in an Amber Ridge condo can later move to a single-family home without changing schools, parks, or daily routines.
