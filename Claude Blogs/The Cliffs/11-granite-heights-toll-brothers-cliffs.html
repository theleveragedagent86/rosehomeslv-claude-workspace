Granite Heights by Toll Brothers is a gated collection of 85 single story luxury homes perched along the ridgeline in The Cliffs village at Summerlin, Las Vegas. With ceiling heights reaching 10 to 16 feet and panoramic views of both the Las Vegas Valley and the Spring Mountains, this neighborhood stands out as one of the most elevated living experiences in all of Summerlin. ## What Homes Are Available in Granite Heights at The Cliffs? Granite Heights offers contemporary single story floor plans ranging from 3,156 to 3,291 square feet, priced from the low $800,000s. Each home features open layouts designed to maximize natural light and seamlessly blend indoor and outdoor living spaces. Premium lots along the ridgeline provide sweeping views of the Strip to the east and mountain scenery to the west. Toll Brothers is known for its luxury finishes, and Granite Heights is no exception. Buyers can expect gourmet kitchens, spa inspired primary suites, and covered patios that take full advantage of the desert landscape. The community is limited to just 85 homesites, making it one of the more exclusive enclaves within The Cliffs. ## Why Choose a Single Story Home in The Cliffs? Single story living is increasingly popular among buyers seeking accessibility, open sight lines, and ease of maintenance. Granite Heights delivers all three while adding the prestige of a Toll Brothers build. Ryan Rose notes that single story communities at this price point tend to hold strong resale value, especially in guard gated settings like The Cliffs. ## What Makes Granite Heights a Smart Buy in Summerlin? The Cliffs is a guard gated village spanning roughly 450 acres near Hualapai Way and Patrick Lane. Granite Heights residents enjoy access to Oak Leaf Park with its pickleball courts, playgrounds, and climbing tower. The broader Summerlin master plan adds over 200 miles of trails, 10 golf courses, and the dining and shopping at Downtown Summerlin. Ryan Rose recommends Granite Heights to buyers who want a turnkey, view oriented home without the upkeep of a two story layout.
