Ironwood by Toll Brothers is a gated neighborhood of single story homes nestled at the base of the Desert Hills Mountain Range within The Cliffs village at Summerlin, Las Vegas. With three thoughtfully designed floor plans ranging from 2,516 to 2,823 square feet, Ironwood offers a balance of luxury finishes and approachable square footage that appeals to both downsizers and first time luxury buyers. ## What Floor Plans Does Ironwood Offer? Ironwood features three single story designs, each with a three car garage and open concept layouts built for desert living. The Cobalt plan starts at 2,516 square feet with 3 bedrooms and up to 4 bathrooms. The Indigo plan expands to 2,705 square feet, also with 3 bedrooms and flexible bath configurations. The largest option, the Jade plan, offers 2,823 square feet with up to 4 bedrooms and 4 bathrooms. All three plans emphasize indoor outdoor connectivity, with covered patios and living spaces designed to frame the surrounding mountain and valley views. Toll Brothers' design center allows buyers to personalize finishes, from flooring and cabinetry to smart home features and outdoor kitchen packages. ## How Does Ironwood Compare to Other Toll Brothers Neighborhoods in The Cliffs? Toll Brothers operates three neighborhoods within The Cliffs: Regency (a 55 plus community), Granite Heights (larger single story homes along the ridgeline), and Ironwood. Ryan Rose often positions Ironwood as the entry point into Toll Brothers quality at The Cliffs, offering slightly smaller square footage at a more accessible price than Granite Heights while still delivering the same build standards and guard gated setting. ## What Amenities Come with an Ironwood Address? Ironwood sits minutes from the 17 acre Mesa Park and connects to the broader Summerlin trail system spanning over 200 miles. Oak Leaf Park within The Cliffs offers pickleball courts, two playgrounds, and picnic pavilions. Residents also enjoy proximity to Downtown Summerlin for shopping and dining, TPC Summerlin for golf, and Red Rock Canyon for weekend hikes. Ryan Rose highlights the trail access as a major draw for active buyers who want outdoor recreation steps from their front door.
