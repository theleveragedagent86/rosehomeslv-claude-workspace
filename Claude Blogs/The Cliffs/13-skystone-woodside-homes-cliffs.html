Skystone by Woodside Homes is a gated community of 72 two story homes located in The Cliffs village at Summerlin, Las Vegas. With four floor plans ranging from 2,637 to 3,220 square feet and prices starting in the $620,000s, Skystone offers one of the most family friendly options inside this guard gated village. ## What Floor Plans Are Available at Skystone? Skystone features four two story designs, each with a three car garage and flexible bedroom configurations. The Castellato plan starts at 2,637 square feet with 3 to 4 bedrooms and up to 3.5 bathrooms. The Biella plan offers 2,934 square feet with up to 6 bedrooms, making it one of the most versatile layouts in The Cliffs. The Portofino plan provides 2,996 square feet with up to 5 bedrooms and 5.5 bathrooms. The largest option, the Vernazzo, reaches 3,220 square feet with 4 to 5 bedrooms and up to 5.5 bathrooms. All four plans feature open concept living areas on the main level, with secondary bedrooms and bonus rooms upstairs. Woodside Homes is known for including generous closet space, dedicated laundry rooms, and covered outdoor living areas in their standard designs. ## How Does Skystone Compare to Other Neighborhoods in The Cliffs? With only 72 homesites, Skystone maintains an intimate feel within the larger Cliffs community. Its two story designs set it apart from the single story neighborhoods offered by Toll Brothers and Richmond American. Ryan Rose frequently recommends Skystone to families who need bedroom count and square footage without stretching into the $1 million plus price range. The $620,000 to $950,000 range positions Skystone as a strong value play inside a guard gated Summerlin village. ## What Is Daily Life Like at Skystone? Skystone residents have full access to The Cliffs amenities, including Oak Leaf Park with pickleball courts, playgrounds, and a climbing tower. The community connects to the broader Summerlin trail network, and Downtown Summerlin is just minutes away for shopping and dining. Top rated schools in the Summerlin area serve The Cliffs, and Red Rock Canyon is a short drive west. Ryan Rose points out that Skystone's location within The Cliffs gives homeowners guard gated security combined with the walkability and convenience that Summerlin is known for.
