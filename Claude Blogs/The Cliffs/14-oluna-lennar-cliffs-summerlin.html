Oluna by Lennar is a gated neighborhood of contemporary two story homes in The Cliffs village at Summerlin, Las Vegas. With four floor plans ranging from 3,290 to 3,800 square feet, Oluna brings Lennar's signature "Everything's Included" approach to one of the most desirable guard gated communities in the valley. ## What Floor Plans Does Oluna by Lennar Offer? Oluna features four spacious two story designs, each with 4 bedrooms, 3.5 plus bathrooms, and a three car garage. The Venus plan starts at 3,312 square feet, followed by the Nova at 3,390 square feet and the Sol at 3,400 square feet. The largest option, the Terra plan, reaches 3,800 square feet with generous living and entertaining areas on both levels. Lennar's "Everything's Included" model means upgrades that are typically add ons with other builders come standard at Oluna. Buyers receive granite countertops, stainless steel appliances, home automation technology, and premium flooring without negotiating each item separately. Select plans also offer Lennar's Next Gen suite, a home within a home featuring its own kitchenette, living room, bedroom, bathroom, and one car garage, ideal for multigenerational families or guest quarters. ## How Is Oluna Priced Compared to Other Cliffs Neighborhoods? Oluna homes have been priced from the mid $600,000s to the low $700,000s, placing them in the middle of The Cliffs pricing spectrum. For the square footage and included upgrades, Ryan Rose considers Oluna one of the strongest value propositions in the village. Buyers get 3,300 to 3,800 square feet of finished living space inside a guard gated Summerlin community at a price point well below the $1 million threshold that several neighboring subdivisions reach. ## What Sets Oluna Apart for Families and Multigenerational Buyers? The Next Gen suite is a genuine differentiator. Few communities in The Cliffs offer a self contained living space within the main home, making Oluna a top pick for families with aging parents or adult children who want privacy without a separate address. Ryan Rose frequently points multigenerational buyers toward Oluna for this reason. Oluna residents have access to Oak Leaf Park, the Summerlin trail system, Downtown Summerlin shopping and dining, and top rated local schools.
