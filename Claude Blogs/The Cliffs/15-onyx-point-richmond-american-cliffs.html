Onyx Point by Richmond American Homes is a gated community of luxury single story ranch homes in The Cliffs village at Summerlin, Las Vegas. With four floor plans ranging from 3,379 to 4,010 square feet and prices starting around $1,300,000, Onyx Point represents the top tier of single story living in this guard gated village. ## What Floor Plans Are Available at Onyx Point? Onyx Point offers four expansive single story designs, each with a three car garage and open concept living. The Raven plan provides 3,379 square feet with 3 to 4 bedrooms and up to 3.5 bathrooms. The Rocco plan expands to 3,470 square feet with 4 bedrooms and a spa inspired primary suite. The Ryder plan offers 3,790 square feet with 4 bedrooms, 4.5 bathrooms, and a flowing open layout. The largest option, the Robert plan, reaches 4,010 square feet with 5 bedrooms and 4.5 bathrooms. Richmond American is known for its "HomeGallery" design experience, which allows buyers to personalize every detail from structural options to interior finishes. This level of customization, paired with single story convenience, sets Onyx Point apart from other neighborhoods in The Cliffs. ## How Does Onyx Point Compare to Other Luxury Options in The Cliffs? At approximately $1,300,000 and above, Onyx Point sits at the premium end of The Cliffs pricing spectrum. The square footage and bedroom counts rival what many two story homes offer in surrounding neighborhoods, but delivered entirely on one level. Ryan Rose notes that Onyx Point attracts buyers who want the space of a 4,000 square foot home without navigating stairs, a combination that is genuinely rare in Summerlin's guard gated villages. ## Who Is Onyx Point Best Suited For? Onyx Point appeals to move up buyers, empty nesters seeking single level luxury, and professionals who prioritize entertaining space. The Robert plan at over 4,000 square feet can accommodate a home office, guest suite, and dedicated entertaining areas all on one floor. Ryan Rose often recommends Onyx Point to clients relocating from larger markets who expect high end finishes and generous lot sizes in a secure community. Residents enjoy access to Oak Leaf Park, pickleball courts, the Summerlin trail system, and proximity to Downtown Summerlin and Red Rock Canyon.
