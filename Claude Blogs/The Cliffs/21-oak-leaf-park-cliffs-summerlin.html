This neighborhood green space gives residents a dedicated spot for recreation, relaxation, and community gatherings without ever leaving the gates. ## What Amenities Does Oak Leaf Park Offer? Oak Leaf Park features dedicated pickleball courts, making it one of the few village parks in Summerlin with courts for the fastest growing sport in the country. A shaded playground with a climbing tower keeps younger residents entertained, while the central lawn provides open space for pickup games, yoga sessions, or simply stretching out with a book. The park also includes a picnic pavilion with covered seating for family cookouts and neighborhood events. Beyond the courts and playground, Oak Leaf Park includes a demonstration garden that showcases desert adapted landscaping techniques. This living display helps homeowners envision water wise designs for their own yards, a practical touch that reflects Summerlin's commitment to sustainability. The combination of active recreation and passive green space makes the park a daily destination for residents of all ages. ## Why Does Oak Leaf Park Matter for Home Values in The Cliffs?
