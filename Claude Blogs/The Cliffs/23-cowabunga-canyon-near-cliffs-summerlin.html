Cowabunga Canyon Waterpark is a 41 acre seasonal waterpark located at 7055 S Fort Apache Rd, just minutes from The Cliffs guard gated village in Summerlin. Formerly known as Wet 'n' Wild Las Vegas, this attraction gives Cliffs residents a major summer destination practically in their backyard. ## What Rides and Attractions Does Cowabunga Canyon Offer? Cowabunga Canyon features a full lineup of water slides ranging from family friendly tube rides to high speed drop slides built for thrill seekers. The wave pool generates rolling swells that mimic ocean conditions, making it a favorite gathering spot on hot afternoons. A lazy river winds through the park for those who prefer a slower pace, offering shaded stretches and gentle currents perfect for floating with younger children. Beyond the headline attractions, the park includes splash pads, cabana rentals, and food and beverage stations spread across the 41 acre grounds. Season passes and group packages make repeat visits affordable, which is why many families in The Cliffs treat Cowabunga Canyon as an extension of their summer routine rather than a special occasion outing. ## When Is Cowabunga Canyon Open? The waterpark operates seasonally, typically opening in late May and running through early September, with extended weekend hours during peak summer months. Select dates in April and October sometimes appear on the calendar depending on weather patterns. Ryan Rose recommends checking the official Cowabunga Canyon schedule each spring for updated hours and any new attractions, since the park has continued to invest in new slides and amenities in recent years. ## How Close Is Cowabunga Canyon to The Cliffs?
