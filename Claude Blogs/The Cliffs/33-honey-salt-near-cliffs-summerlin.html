Honey Salt is a neighborhood restaurant on South Rampart Boulevard in Summerlin, located just minutes from The Cliffs guard gated village. Opened in 2012 by restaurateur Elizabeth Blau and Chef Kim Canteenwalla, this American comfort bistro has become one of the most beloved dining spots for locals throughout the western Las Vegas valley. ## What Is Honey Salt Known For? Honey Salt combines classic American comfort food with globally influenced technique. Chef Kim Canteenwalla brings more than 30 years of international culinary leadership spanning kitchens in Singapore, Thailand, Dubai, Bali, and Montreal, and served as Executive Chef for MGM Grand in Las Vegas before opening this independent venture. His menu highlights seasonal produce, responsibly sourced proteins, and dishes designed for sharing. Elizabeth Blau, founder and CEO of Blau and Associates, is recognized for building the restaurant portfolios at both Bellagio and Wynn. Together, the couple created Honey Salt as an extension of their home, drawing on the foods and hospitality they have shared with friends and family for years. The restaurant maintains direct relationships with farmers, fishermen, and growers to deliver the freshest ingredients possible. ## How Far Is Honey Salt from The Cliffs? Honey Salt is located at 1031 S Rampart Blvd, roughly a 5 to 10 minute drive from The Cliffs via Rampart Boulevard or Hualapai Way.
