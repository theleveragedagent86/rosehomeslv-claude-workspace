Kona Grill is an upscale American grill and sushi restaurant located at 750 S Rampart Blvd in the Summerlin area, roughly 5 minutes from The Cliffs guard gated village. With a scratch kitchen menu spanning steak, seafood, and award winning sushi, Kona Grill offers one of the most versatile dining experiences near The Cliffs. ## What Does Kona Grill Serve? Kona Grill builds its menu around three pillars: steak, seafood, and sushi. The sushi program features signature Kona Rolls including the Picasso with spicy yellowtail, avocado, and yuzu ponzu, the Rainbow Roll layered with tuna, salmon, shrimp, and yellowtail, and the Las Vegas Roll made with salmon, crab salad, and cream cheese tempura fried with eel sauce. Poke bowls offer a lighter option with a choice of tuna, salmon, or shrimp over sushi rice or field greens. Beyond sushi, the kitchen turns out American grill staples prepared with care, from hand cut steaks to fresh seafood entrees. The restaurant is also known for one of the best happy hours in Summerlin, with discounted appetizers, cocktails, and sushi rolls that draw a consistent after work crowd from the surrounding neighborhoods. ## How Close Is Kona Grill to The Cliffs?
