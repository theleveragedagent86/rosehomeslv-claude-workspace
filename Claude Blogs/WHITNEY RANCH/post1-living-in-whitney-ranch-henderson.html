What Is It Like Living in Whitney Ranch Henderson?

Whitney Ranch is one of Henderson's original master planned communities, spanning 85 acres with 1,219 homes and a population of roughly 12,965 residents. Built primarily in the early 1990s by DR Horton, this established neighborhood offers an affordable entry point into Henderson living with amenities that rival much newer (and pricier) communities nearby.

Where Is Whitney Ranch Located?

Whitney Ranch sits in a convenient pocket of Henderson, Nevada, just 12 miles from the Las Vegas Strip and about 6 miles from Harry Reid International Airport. Daily commutes are manageable, with most drives to the Strip or downtown Las Vegas taking 15 to 20 minutes. Henderson Hospital is roughly one mile away, and Galleria Urgent Care is within walking distance.

What Homes Will You Find Here?

The community features nine distinct floor plans ranging from 1,200 to 4,000 square feet. You will find single story homes, two story homes, condos, and townhomes, many showcasing Spanish Colonial style architecture with brick driveways and desert landscaping. Prices range from below $100,000 for condos up to the mid $500,000s, with median prices falling between $365,000 and $455,000 depending on the source.

What Amenities Does Whitney Ranch Offer?

Residents enjoy the Whitney Ranch Recreation Center, which includes a fitness center, gymnasium, game room, dance and aerobics room, and racquetball courts. The Whitney Ranch Aquatic Complex provides both indoor and outdoor pools. Outdoor enthusiasts will appreciate 14 tennis courts, pickleball courts, four community parks, playgrounds, walking paths, an amphitheater, and even camping spots. The community is not guard gated, and there are no age restrictions.

Local Insight from Ryan Rose

As a Las Vegas real estate specialist, Ryan Rose often recommends Whitney Ranch to buyers who want Henderson quality without the premium price tag of newer communities like Inspirada or Cadence.
