What Does the Whitney Ranch Recreation Center Offer?

If you live in or near Whitney Ranch, the Whitney Ranch Recreation Center at 1575 Galleria Drive is one of the best perks of calling Henderson home. This City of Henderson facility packs a surprising amount into one building, giving residents access to fitness, sports, and social activities year round.

What Facilities Are Inside the Recreation Center?

The recreation center includes a fully equipped fitness center, a full size gymnasium, a game room, and a dedicated dance and aerobics room. Racquetball and wallyball courts give athletes a place to compete indoors, while Kids Corner provides supervised activities so parents can focus on their workouts. A conference room is also available for community meetings and private events.

One of the biggest draws is the pricing. Annual memberships run just $25 for adults, $5 for youth and teens, and are completely free for seniors aged 60 and older as well as veterans. That kind of value is hard to find anywhere in the Las Vegas valley.

Who Uses the Whitney Ranch Recreation Center?

The center serves a wide range of residents, from families with young children who take advantage of Kids Corner to retirees who use the fitness equipment and attend group classes. The gymnasium hosts everything from pickup basketball games to organized league play, making it a true community gathering spot. Its location adjacent to the Whitney Ranch Aquatic Complex means you can combine a gym session with a swim on the same visit.

Local Insight from Ryan Rose

Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas real estate expert, points out that the Whitney Ranch Recreation Center is one of the features that consistently surprises buyers touring the neighborhood. For the cost of a single month at most commercial gyms, residents get a full year of access to facilities that rival private fitness clubs.
