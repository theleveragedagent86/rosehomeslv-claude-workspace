What Is the Whitney Ranch Aquatic Complex?

The Whitney Ranch Aquatic Complex, located at 1575 Galleria Drive in Henderson, gives residents access to year round swimming without the hassle of maintaining a private pool. Situated adjacent to the Whitney Ranch Recreation Center, this City of Henderson facility serves swimmers of all ages and skill levels with both indoor and outdoor pool options.

What Pool Facilities Are Available?

The complex features an indoor pool that operates year round, making it one of the few places in Henderson where you can swim comfortably even during the cooler winter months. An outdoor activity pool opens seasonally, providing a great option for families and recreational swimmers when temperatures climb. The facility supports both competitive and recreational swimming, so whether you are training for a meet or simply cooling off on a Saturday afternoon, there is space for you.

How Much Does It Cost to Use the Aquatic Complex?

Membership follows the same affordable pricing as the adjacent recreation center: $25 per year for adults, $5 for youth and teens, and free for seniors aged 60 and older and for veterans. Considering that many Henderson neighborhoods charge HOA fees for pool access alone, this is an exceptional value. The combined access to the pool and recreation center means one low annual fee covers fitness, sports, and swimming all in one location.
