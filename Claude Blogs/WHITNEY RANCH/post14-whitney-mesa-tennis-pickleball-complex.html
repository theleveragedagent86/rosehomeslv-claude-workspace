What Is the Whitney Mesa Tennis and Pickleball Complex?

The Whitney Mesa Tennis and Pickleball Complex at 1661 Galleria Drive in Henderson is one of the largest public racquet sports facilities in the Las Vegas valley. With dedicated courts for tennis and pickleball, plus a full range of amenities, this complex gives Whitney Ranch residents a serious advantage when it comes to staying active and connected to their community.

How Many Courts Does the Complex Have?

The facility features 4 dedicated pickleball courts, 12 mixed use courts, and 8 tennis courts, totaling 24 playing surfaces. All courts are lit for evening play, which is especially valuable during the hot summer months when cooler nighttime temperatures make outdoor activity far more enjoyable. A pro shop on site offers equipment and accessories, so you can gear up without making a separate trip.

What Other Amenities Are Available?

Beyond the courts, the complex includes restrooms, a covered picnic area, and BBQ pits. These extras make it easy to turn a match into a social outing; you can grill after a game or host a small group gathering without leaving the facility. The covered picnic area provides shade, which anyone who has spent a summer in Henderson knows is not a luxury but a necessity.
