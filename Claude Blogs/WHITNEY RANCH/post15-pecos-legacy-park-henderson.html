What Is Pecos Legacy Park in Henderson?

Pecos Legacy Park, located at 150 Pecos Road in Henderson, is one of the most versatile community parks near Whitney Ranch. With sports courts, playgrounds, open green space, and trail access, this park serves as a daily destination for families, athletes, and anyone looking to spend time outdoors without driving across town.

What Facilities Does Pecos Legacy Park Have?

The park offers a wide range of amenities packed into one well maintained space. You will find a ball field, basketball court, tennis courts, volleyball courts, and horseshoe pits for organized and casual play. A playground keeps younger children entertained while parents relax under the picnic shelters. BBQ grills are available throughout the park, making it a natural spot for weekend cookouts and birthday celebrations.

Is Pecos Legacy Park Connected to Local Trails?

Yes. One of the park's strongest features is its trail connectivity, which links it to the broader network of walking paths and trails running through the Whitney Ranch area. This means you can start a morning walk at the park and extend it through the neighborhood without needing to cross busy roads or navigate disconnected sidewalks. Open grass areas provide additional space for dogs to run, kids to play, or fitness groups to meet. Restrooms on site make longer visits comfortable and convenient.
