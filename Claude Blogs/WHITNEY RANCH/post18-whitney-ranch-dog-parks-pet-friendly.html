Dog Parks and Dog Friendly Areas Near Whitney Ranch

Whitney Ranch is one of the most dog friendly neighborhoods in Henderson, and the options for getting your pup outside go well beyond the backyard. Whether you prefer off leash play or a quiet nature walk together, you will find dedicated spaces within minutes of home.

Off Leash Dog Parks Nearby

Several fully fenced dog parks are a short drive from Whitney Ranch. Reunion Trails Dog Park offers separate areas for large and small dogs, along with shade structures and water stations. Acacia Dog Park is another popular spot with a spacious layout that gives energetic dogs room to run. Paseo Vista Dog Park rounds out the top three, providing a well maintained space with seating for owners and open turf for play.

Each of these parks draws a friendly crowd of regulars, which makes them great places for socializing your dog and meeting fellow pet owners in the Henderson area.

On Leash Trails and Nature Walks

For dogs who prefer structured walks, the Whitney Mesa Nature Preserve is a standout. Located at 1990 Patrick Lane, the preserve welcomes leashed dogs on its 2.5 mile loop trail and connecting paths. You and your dog can explore unique geologic formations, native vegetation, and an active stream corridor without ever leaving the neighborhood. Check out the Whitney Mesa Nature Preserve Trail Guide for route details and tips.

A Pet Friendly City

Henderson has been recognized as a pet friendly city through the Better Cities For Pets program by Mars Petcare. That designation reflects the city's investment in pet infrastructure, from parks and waste stations to policies that welcome four legged residents. Living in Whitney Ranch means your pet benefits from a community that takes animal welfare seriously.
