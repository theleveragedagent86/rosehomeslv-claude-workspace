What Parks Are Near Whitney Ranch?

One of the biggest draws of living in Whitney Ranch is the variety of parks and recreation spaces within walking distance or a short drive. From fitness facilities to nature preserves, Henderson has invested in green spaces and amenities that make this neighborhood especially appealing for active families and outdoor enthusiasts.

Whitney Ranch Recreation Center

Located at 1575 Galleria Drive, the Whitney Ranch Recreation Center is the community's hub for indoor recreation. It features a fitness center, gymnasium, and game room. Programs and classes run year round, serving residents of all ages. This is where many Whitney Ranch families spend weekday evenings and weekend mornings.

Whitney Mesa Nature Preserve

The preserve sits at 1990 Patrick Lane and offers more than four miles of trails through striking geologic formations. An active stream corridor and diverse native flora and fauna set it apart from typical neighborhood parks. Picnic tables, restrooms, and a dedicated ADA accessible trail make it welcoming for all visitors. Dogs on leash are welcome throughout the preserve.

Whitney Mesa Tennis and Pickleball Complex

If racquet sports are your thing, the Whitney Mesa Tennis and Pickleball Complex at 1661 Galleria Drive delivers. The facility includes eight tennis courts, twelve mixed use courts, four pickleball courts, and a pro shop. It is one of the most complete racquet sport venues in the Las Vegas valley.

Pecos Legacy Park

Pecos Legacy Park at 150 Pecos Road rounds out the nearby options with a ball field, basketball court, playground, and volleyball courts. Trail connectivity from this park lets walkers and joggers link up with other paths in the area, creating longer routes without needing to drive anywhere.
