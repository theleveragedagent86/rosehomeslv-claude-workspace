What Are Whitney Ranch Home Prices in 2026?

Whitney Ranch home prices in 2026 range from below $100,000 for condos to the mid $500,000s for larger single family homes. The median sale price falls between $365,000 and $455,000 depending on the data source, making Whitney Ranch one of the more affordable master planned communities in Henderson, Nevada.

How Do Prices Break Down by Home Type?

The community offers nine floor plan layouts originally built by DR Horton in the early 1990s. Homes range from 1,200 square feet to 4,000 square feet, with three to five bedrooms available. Condos and townhomes represent the lowest price point (often below $200,000), while larger single story and two story homes with quarter acre lots push into the $400,000s and $500,000s. Spanish Colonial style architecture with brick driveways and desert landscaping is the dominant aesthetic throughout.

How Does Whitney Ranch Compare to Other Henderson Communities?

Whitney Ranch consistently prices below newer Henderson master planned communities like Inspirada, Cadence, and Seven Hills. The tradeoff is that Whitney Ranch is a mature community with limited new construction, so buyers are purchasing resale homes rather than new builds. However, many homeowners have made significant updates over the years, and the established landscaping gives the neighborhood a settled, tree lined feel that newer communities simply cannot match.

What Affects Home Values in Whitney Ranch?

Several factors support home values here: proximity to Henderson Hospital (roughly one mile away), strong school options like Harriet Treem Elementary and Green Valley High School, and the impressive Whitney Ranch Recreation Center and Aquatic Complex. The community is also just 12 miles from the Las Vegas Strip and 6 miles from the airport, making it attractive for commuters and professionals who want suburban comfort with urban access.
