Green Valley High School: The Go To High School for Whitney Ranch Families

When your child reaches high school age, the quality of their campus matters more than ever. Green Valley High School, serving grades 9 through 12, is the primary high school for families living in Whitney Ranch. With a B+ rating on Niche and a 6 out of 10 GreatSchools score, this Henderson campus offers a well rounded educational experience backed by strong programs and a vibrant student culture.

Academics, IB, and AP Programs

Green Valley High School provides both International Baccalaureate (IB) and Advanced Placement (AP) course options for students aiming to challenge themselves academically. These programs allow motivated students to earn college credit before graduation, giving them a serious head start. With approximately 2,640 students enrolled, the school is large enough to sustain a wide range of course offerings while still maintaining a structured learning environment.

Nationally Recognized Performing Arts

What truly sets Green Valley High apart is its performing arts program. The school's band has performed at Carnegie Hall, marched in the Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade, and played at two presidential inaugurations. These are not local accolades; they reflect national recognition that puts Green Valley High in rare company among public high schools anywhere in the country. For students passionate about music, theater, or performance, this campus delivers opportunities that most schools simply cannot match.
