Best Schools Near Whitney Ranch in 2026: A Complete Guide

One of the biggest reasons families choose Whitney Ranch in Henderson is the quality of nearby schools. Whether you have a toddler, an elementary student, or a teenager, this community offers access to well rated campuses at every level. Here is a look at the best schools near Whitney Ranch heading into 2026.

Harriet Treem Elementary School (K through 5)

Harriet Treem Elementary sits within Whitney Ranch itself, making it the most convenient option for families with young children. The school holds a 7 out of 10 GreatSchools rating and ranks number 94 among Nevada elementary schools. With 43% math proficiency and 47% reading proficiency, Treem consistently performs above the statewide average. Walkability from many Whitney Ranch homes is a major bonus for parents.

Thurman White Academy of the Arts (Grades 6 through 8)

For middle school, Thurman White Academy of the Arts offers something unique. This public magnet school integrates an arts focused curriculum with core academics, drawing students who want to develop creative skills alongside traditional subjects. The school performs above average compared to other Nevada public schools, and its magnet structure creates a focused, motivated student body that benefits every enrolled child.

Green Valley High School (Grades 9 through 12)

Green Valley High School rounds out the Whitney Ranch school pipeline with a B+ Niche rating and both IB and AP academic programs. The school enrolls roughly 2,640 students and is nationally recognized for its performing arts. The band has performed at Carnegie Hall, the Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade, and two presidential inaugurations. Families looking for a high school that balances rigorous academics with extraordinary extracurriculars will find Green Valley High hard to beat.

Children's Choice Learning Center (Pre K)

Families with children who are not yet kindergarten age can look to Children's Choice Learning Center, a top ranked private preschool option in the Henderson area. This center provides structured early education with small class sizes, helping young learners build a strong foundation before entering the public school system at Harriet Treem.
