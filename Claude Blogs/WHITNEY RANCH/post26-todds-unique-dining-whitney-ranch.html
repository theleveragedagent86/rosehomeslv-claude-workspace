Todd's Unique Dining Near Whitney Ranch: A Hidden Gem in Henderson

If you live in Whitney Ranch and have not yet tried Todd's Unique Dining, you are missing out on one of Henderson's best kept culinary secrets. Tucked near the Galleria at Sunset, this locally owned restaurant brings Strip quality cooking to a relaxed neighborhood setting. For Whitney Ranch residents, it is just a short drive from home.

A Chef with Serious Credentials

Todd Clore, the chef and owner, spent years cooking on the Las Vegas Strip before opening his own place in Henderson. That experience shows in every dish. The menu leans creative American, with seasonal ingredients and thoughtful presentations that feel elevated without being pretentious. You get the skill of a fine dining chef paired with the warmth of a neighborhood restaurant.

What to Expect on the Menu

The menu changes regularly, but you can count on inventive takes on familiar dishes. Think perfectly seared proteins, fresh seasonal sides, and sauces that actually complement rather than overpower. The portions are generous, the flavors are layered, and every plate looks like it belongs at a restaurant charging twice the price. Whether you are celebrating a special occasion or just want a great weeknight meal, Todd's delivers.

The Atmosphere

Todd's Unique Dining keeps things intimate and comfortable. The dining room is small, which gives the whole experience a personal feel. You might even see Chef Todd working the kitchen or chatting with guests. Regulars say that once you visit, you will keep coming back. It is that kind of place: warm, genuine, and consistently excellent.
