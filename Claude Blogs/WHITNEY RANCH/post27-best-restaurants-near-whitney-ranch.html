Best Restaurants Near Whitney Ranch Henderson: Where to Eat in 2026

Whitney Ranch residents have access to one of the most diverse dining scenes in Henderson. From chef driven American cuisine to authentic international flavors, the restaurants near this community make it easy to eat well without driving to the Strip. Here are some of the best spots to try in 2026.

Todd's Unique Dining

Chef Todd Clore brings years of Las Vegas Strip experience to this intimate, creative American restaurant near the Galleria at Sunset. The menu rotates with seasonal ingredients, and the quality rivals anything you would find on the Boulevard. It is a true neighborhood gem that Whitney Ranch residents love.

Chile Caliente Restaurant and Bar

Located right on Whitney Ranch Drive, Chile Caliente is the neighborhood's go to spot for Mexican food. The menu is packed with traditional favorites, generous portions, and bold flavors. Its location makes it one of the most walkable dining options for residents in the heart of the community.

Trattoria Rustica

Tucked inside the Whitney Ranch Shopping Center, Trattoria Rustica serves hearty Italian dishes in a warm, welcoming atmosphere. Locals rave about the pasta, the sauces, and the consistent quality that keeps them coming back week after week. It is comfort food done right.

Rodizio Grill at Galleria at Sunset

For something more adventurous, Rodizio Grill offers an authentic Brazilian churrascaria experience. Gauchos carve unlimited grilled meats tableside, and the gourmet salad bar adds variety to every visit. It is perfect for date nights, celebrations, or any meal where you want to go all out.

More Favorites Worth Trying

The Great Greek Mediterranean Grill brings a mom and pop feel with fresh Mediterranean plates. L&L Hawaiian Barbecue delivers island comfort food at affordable prices. Krazy Bao and Kungfu Noodle offer top rated Asian options, while Soru Ramen Sushi and Seafood rounds out the Japanese category. For drinks and bar food, PKWY Tavern and Distill are both popular Henderson hangouts with multiple locations nearby.
