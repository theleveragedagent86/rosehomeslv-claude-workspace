Galleria at Sunset Mall Near Whitney Ranch: Henderson's Largest Shopping Destination

If you live in Whitney Ranch, one of the biggest perks of the location is having Galleria at Sunset less than a mile away. This indoor mall is the largest in Henderson, offering over a million square feet of retail space, more than 120 stores, and a dining scene that makes it easy to spend an entire afternoon without running out of things to do.

What You Will Find Inside

Galleria at Sunset anchors its lineup with major retailers like Macy's, JCPenney, Dillard's, Kohl's, and Dick's Sporting Goods. Between those anchors, you will find popular brands including H&M, Hollister, Foot Locker, Francesca's, and dozens more. Whether you need to update your wardrobe, pick up sporting equipment, or shop for gifts, the variety here covers just about every category.

The mall also features a 600 seat food court with 12 eateries, so grabbing lunch or a quick snack between stores is never a problem. From fast casual favorites to sit down options just outside the mall entrances, you can fuel up without leaving the property.

Convenience for Whitney Ranch Residents

The drive from Whitney Ranch to Galleria at Sunset takes roughly three minutes. That kind of proximity to a full scale mall is something most Henderson neighborhoods simply cannot match. Need a last minute outfit for an event? Want to browse new arrivals on a Saturday morning? It is all right there. The mall is open Monday through Thursday from 10 AM to 8 PM, Friday and Saturday from 10 AM to 9 PM, and Sunday from 11 AM to 6 PM.

More Than Just Shopping

Beyond retail, Galleria at Sunset regularly hosts community events, seasonal pop ups, and family friendly activities. The mall serves as a social hub for Henderson residents, especially during the cooler months when the indoor setting is a welcome change of pace from outdoor heat.
