Sunset Station Hotel and Casino Near Whitney Ranch: Henderson's Premier Entertainment Destination

Whitney Ranch residents do not have to drive to the Las Vegas Strip for a night out. Sunset Station Hotel and Casino sits right in the heart of Henderson, offering gaming, dining, bowling, a movie theater, live entertainment, and family friendly activities all under one roof. It has been a Henderson landmark since 1997 and continues to be one of the most popular locals casinos in the valley.

Dining for Every Taste

Sunset Station's restaurant lineup rivals many Strip resorts. Sonoma Cellar delivers fine dining with an impressive wine list. Cabo serves authentic Mexican favorites in a lively atmosphere. Pasta Cucina covers Italian cravings, while Grand Cafe offers 24 hour casual dining for any schedule. The Oyster Bar is a local favorite for seafood lovers. Quick options include Fatburger, Panda Express, Starbucks, Sbarro, and Ben and Jerry's. The Feast Buffet rounds things out with an all you can eat spread that rotates themes and cuisines.

Entertainment and Activities

The entertainment options at Sunset Station go well beyond the gaming floor's 114,000 square feet of slots and table games. A 72 lane bowling alley makes it one of the largest bowling centers in Henderson. The on site movie theater screens the latest releases without the hassle of driving across town. A 5,000 seat amphitheater hosts concerts and live performances from nationally recognized acts throughout the year.

Families will appreciate Kids Quest, a supervised play area for children ages 30 months to 12 years, featuring climbing walls, creative zones, and games. The outdoor pool and lounge area adds a resort feel for hotel guests and visitors alike.

Proximity to Whitney Ranch

Sunset Station sits just minutes from Whitney Ranch along Sunset Road, making it an easy choice for a spontaneous dinner, a weekend bowling session, or a date night movie. The 457 room AAA Three Diamond rated hotel also makes it a convenient option for hosting out of town guests who want to stay close to your neighborhood.
