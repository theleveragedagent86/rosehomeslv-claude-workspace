Ethel M Chocolates and Botanical Gardens Near Whitney Ranch: A Sweet Henderson Tradition

Not every neighborhood attraction involves shopping or dining. Near Whitney Ranch, you will find one of Henderson's most unique destinations: the Ethel M Chocolates Factory and Botanical Cactus Garden. Located at 2 Cactus Garden Drive, this free attraction combines chocolate making tours with Nevada's largest botanical cactus garden, making it a must visit for residents and visitors alike.

The Chocolate Factory Tour

Ethel M offers a free self guided tour of its working chocolate factory. You will walk through viewing windows that showcase every step of the candy making process, from mixing and molding to packaging. At the end of the tour, guests receive a complimentary chocolate sample. For a deeper experience, you can book a chocolate tasting session priced at $25 for adults, $20 for students ages 13 to 19, and $15 for children 12 and younger. Alcohol pairing tastings are available for $35.

The Botanical Cactus Garden

Step outside the factory and you will find yourself in one of the world's most impressive desert plant collections. The garden features over 300 species of cacti and desert plants spread across beautifully landscaped pathways. Admission is free and the garden is open daily, making it an easy add on to any factory visit or a standalone walk when you want a peaceful outdoor experience close to home.

Holiday Lights and Seasonal Events

Each year from early November through Christmas, the cactus garden transforms into a holiday wonderland with over a million sparkling lights. The Holiday Cactus Garden event draws families from across the valley for evening strolls, visits with Santa, and hot cocoa. It has become one of Henderson's most beloved seasonal traditions and is just a short drive from Whitney Ranch.
