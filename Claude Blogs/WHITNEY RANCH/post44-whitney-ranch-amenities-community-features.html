Whitney Ranch Amenities and Community Features

Whitney Ranch in Henderson, NV packs an impressive amount of amenities into its 85 acres. As one of Henderson's first master planned communities, it was built with lifestyle in mind. From pools and tennis courts to parks and walking paths, here's what residents enjoy every day.

The Recreation Center

The Whitney Ranch Recreation Center is the heart of the community. Membership costs just $25 per year for adults, and it's completely free for seniors 60 and older as well as veterans. Inside, you'll find a fitness center with modern equipment, a gymnasium for basketball and volleyball, and a game room for more relaxed activities.

The center also features Kids Corner, a dedicated space for younger residents, and an amphitheater that hosts community events throughout the year. It's the kind of facility that brings neighbors together and keeps everyone active.

The Aquatic Complex

The Whitney Ranch Aquatic Complex offers both indoor and outdoor pools. Whether you're doing laps for exercise or cooling off with the kids on a summer afternoon, the pools are a standout feature. Having indoor options means you can swim year round, which is a big draw for fitness enthusiasts and families alike.

Courts, Parks, and Paths

Tennis players will appreciate the 14 tennis courts available to residents. Pickleball courts have also been added to meet growing demand for one of the fastest growing sports in the country. Whether you're a seasoned player or just picking up a paddle, there's court time available.

Whitney Ranch includes four parks with playgrounds, open green space, and shaded areas. Walking paths connect these parks throughout the neighborhood, making it easy to stroll from one end of the community to the other. The mature trees that line the streets and paths provide welcome shade, especially during the warmer months.

Local Insight: Ryan Rose, a Las Vegas Realtor who frequently works with buyers in Henderson, says the amenities are a major selling point. "When clients visit the rec center and see everything included for $25 a year, they're usually surprised. You'd pay ten times that at a private gym. Add in the pools, the courts, and the parks, and it's hard to find better value in Henderson."

What About HOA Fees?

Whitney Ranch does have HOA fees that help maintain these amenities and common areas. The fees keep the neighborhood looking sharp and ensure that facilities stay in good condition. For what you get in return, most residents consider it a worthwhile investment.
