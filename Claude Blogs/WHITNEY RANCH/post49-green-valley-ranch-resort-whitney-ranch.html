Green Valley Ranch Resort Near Whitney Ranch

One of the biggest perks of living in Whitney Ranch is having a world class resort practically in your backyard. Green Valley Ranch Resort, Spa and Casino sits just 3 to 4 miles south of Whitney Ranch, giving residents easy access to upscale dining, entertainment, and relaxation without the drive to the Las Vegas Strip.

What Green Valley Ranch Resort Offers

Green Valley Ranch is more than a casino. The property features a full lineup of restaurants, from casual eateries to fine dining options that rival anything on the Strip. Lounges and bars provide spots for evening outings, while the salon and spa offer a true retreat when you need to unwind.

Entertainment at the resort ranges from live music and special events to a movie theater and seasonal pool parties. It is the kind of venue that works for date nights, family celebrations, and weekend getaways without ever leaving Henderson.

A Neighborhood Advantage

Having a resort of this caliber nearby adds real value to the Whitney Ranch lifestyle. Residents can enjoy fine dining and entertainment within minutes, skip the Strip traffic entirely, and still feel like they are experiencing something special. It is one of those everyday luxuries that makes Henderson living so appealing.

The resort also anchors a larger commercial area that includes shopping and additional dining, so a trip to Green Valley Ranch can easily turn into a full afternoon or evening out.
