What Schools Are Near Whitney Ranch Henderson?

Whitney Ranch Henderson is zoned for several well rated public schools and has private options nearby. Families living here have access to Harriet Treem Elementary, Thurman White Academy of the Arts, and Green Valley High School, all within a short drive or walk from the community.

Harriet Treem Elementary School (K through 5)

Located within the Whitney Ranch community, Harriet Treem Elementary holds a 7 out of 10 rating on GreatSchools. The school serves kindergarten through fifth grade and benefits from its walkable location for many Whitney Ranch families. Its solid academic reputation makes it one of the primary reasons families choose this neighborhood.

Thurman White Academy of the Arts (6 through 8)

Also situated within the community, Thurman White Academy of the Arts is a public magnet school with an arts focused curriculum. Middle school students here receive specialized instruction in visual arts, performing arts, and creative disciplines alongside their core academics. The magnet designation means the school draws students from across the district who are passionate about the arts.

Green Valley High School (9 through 12)

Green Valley High School serves Whitney Ranch students at the high school level and carries a B+ rating on Niche. The school offers both International Baccalaureate and Advanced Placement programs for academically driven students. Green Valley High is also nationally recognized for its performing arts programs, making it a natural continuation for students coming from Thurman White Academy of the Arts.

Private School Options

For families seeking private education, Children's Choice Learning Center offers a top ranked Pre K program in the Whitney Ranch area. This provides an early childhood option for families who prefer smaller class sizes and specialized early learning curricula before their children enter the public school system.
