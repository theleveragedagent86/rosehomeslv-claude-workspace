Whitney Ranch Condos and Townhomes for Sale

Looking for an affordable entry point into one of Henderson's most established neighborhoods? Whitney Ranch condos and townhomes offer a lower price tag without sacrificing location or community amenities.

What Condos and Townhomes Are Available in Whitney Ranch?

Whitney Ranch includes a variety of attached housing options alongside its single family homes. Condos in the community can be found starting below $100,000, making them some of the most affordable options in all of Henderson. Townhomes typically fall in a mid range that bridges the gap between condos and detached homes. Both options give buyers access to the same parks, recreation facilities, and neighborhood amenities that the entire community enjoys.

Who Should Consider a Whitney Ranch Condo or Townhome?

First time buyers, downsizers, and investors all gravitate toward Whitney Ranch's attached housing. The lower purchase price means reduced mortgage payments, and the HOA typically covers exterior maintenance, landscaping, and shared amenities. For anyone who wants to live in a master planned Henderson community without the expense of a single family home, these properties check every box.

The location adds even more appeal. Henderson Hospital sits roughly one mile away. The Las Vegas Strip is about 12 miles out, and Harry Reid International Airport is only six miles from the neighborhood. Daily errands, dining, and entertainment are all within a short drive.

What Should Buyers Know Before Making an Offer?

Because Whitney Ranch was primarily built in the early 1990s, some condos and townhomes may need cosmetic updates. That's actually an advantage for savvy buyers: you can purchase at a lower price, renovate to your taste, and build equity quickly. Be sure to review the HOA fees and rules before committing, as they vary by sub association within the community.
