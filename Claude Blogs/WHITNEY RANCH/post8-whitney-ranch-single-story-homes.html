Whitney Ranch Single-Story Homes Henderson

Single story living is in high demand across Henderson, and Whitney Ranch delivers options that many newer communities simply can't match. If avoiding stairs is a priority, here's what you need to know about one level homes in this established neighborhood.

Are There Single-Story Homes in Whitney Ranch?

Yes. While two story layouts are more common in Whitney Ranch, single story homes are available throughout the community. DR Horton, the original builder, designed nine different floor plans when the neighborhood was developed in the early 1990s, and several of those plans were single level. These homes typically range from around 1,200 square feet on the smaller end to well over 2,000 square feet for more spacious layouts.

Why Are Single-Story Homes So Popular in Henderson?

Retirees and active adults consistently seek single story properties for ease of mobility and lower maintenance. But it's not just older buyers: families with young children appreciate the safety of having no stairways, and remote workers love converting a spare bedroom into a ground floor office. In a community like Whitney Ranch, where lot sizes can reach a quarter acre or more, single story homes also tend to have larger backyards relative to their footprint.

What Do Single-Story Homes Cost in Whitney Ranch?

Pricing for single story homes in Whitney Ranch generally falls within the community's broader range of $365,000 to $455,000 at the median level. Smaller or unrenovated one level homes may come in below that range, while updated properties with larger lots can push higher. Compared to single story homes in newer Henderson communities, Whitney Ranch consistently offers more value per square foot.
