# Complete Guide to the Worker Advantage Program for Las Vegas Homebuyers This is the definitive resource for essential workers in Southern Nevada who want to understand, qualify for, and use the Worker Advantage Program's $20,000 homeownership grant. Whether you are a nurse, teacher, firefighter, or construction tradesperson, everything you need to know starts here. ## What the Worker Advantage Program Is The Worker Advantage Program is a [Nevada Housing Division initiative](/nevada-worker-advantage-program-explained) that provides a $20,000 grant to qualifying essential workers purchasing a home in Nevada. Unlike a loan, this money does not need to be repaid. It can be applied to your down payment, [closing costs](/worker-advantage-program-closing-costs), or a [mortgage rate buydown](/buy-down-interest-rate-worker-advantage). The program launched with 900 available slots. As of early 2026, 81 have been claimed, leaving [819 remaining](/worker-advantage-program-available-2026). Funding is first come, first served, with no guarantee of renewal. ## Who Qualifies Eligibility is built on three pillars: occupation, income, and credit. **Occupation:** You must work in one of [47 qualifying essential worker jobs](/jobs-qualifying-worker-advantage-grant) spanning healthcare, education, public safety, and skilled trades. This includes registered nurses, medical assistants, K through 12 teachers, police officers, firefighters, EMTs, electricians, plumbers, carpenters, and dozens more. **Income:** Household income must fall within county based limits set by the Nevada Housing Division. These limits are updated periodically and vary by family size and location. **Credit:** A minimum credit score of 640 is required. This is higher than the 580 minimum for [FHA loans](/worker-advantage-vs-fha-loans-essential), so verify your score early. Full eligibility details are covered in our [qualification guide](/who-qualifies-worker-advantage-nevada). ## How to Apply The application process flows through the [Home Is Possible lender network](/worker-advantage-home-is-possible-lenders). Here is the sequence: 1. Confirm your occupation appears on the qualifying jobs list. 2. Connect with an approved lender who handles Worker Advantage applications. 3. Complete your mortgage pre approval (FHA is the most common loan type paired with this grant). 4. Submit Worker Advantage documentation through your lender. 5. Receive grant reservation and proceed with your home search. 6. Close on your home with the $20,000 applied at settlement. Our step by step walkthrough covers the [full application process](/apply-nevada-20000-down-payment-assistance). ## How Far $20,000 Goes The grant's impact depends on your purchase price. On a $350,000 North Las Vegas townhome, it covers the entire FHA down payment and leaves $7,750 for closing costs. On a $482,000 home at the metro median, it still covers the full down payment with $3,130 remaining. On a $550,000 property, it absorbs the entire $19,250 FHA down payment with $750 to spare. See the [full price breakdown](/how-far-20000-goes-las-vegas-home-2026). ## Where to Buy Essential workers are purchasing homes across all of Southern Nevada's major communities: - [Las Vegas proper](/essential-workers-homes-las-vegas-guide) including the southwest corridor - [Henderson](/essential-workers-homes-henderson-20000) and [Green Valley](/essential-workers-homes-green-valley) - [North Las Vegas](/essential-workers-north-las-vegas-homes), [Aliante](/essential-workers-homes-aliante-20000), and [Centennial Hills](/essential-workers-homes-centennial-hills) - [Mountains Edge](/essential-workers-homes-mountains-edge) and [Southern Highlands](/essential-workers-southern-highlands-20000) - [Summerlin](/essential-workers-homes-summerlin) and [Inspirada](/essential-workers-homes-inspirada-20000) - [Skye Canyon](/essential-workers-homes-skye-canyon) and surrounding master plans Each area offers different price points, commute profiles, and school options.
