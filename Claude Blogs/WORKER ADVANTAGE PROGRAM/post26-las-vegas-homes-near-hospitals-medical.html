# Las Vegas Homes Near Hospitals and Medical Centers If you work at a Las Vegas hospital, your commute matters. Long drives after a 12 hour shift can feel exhausting and even dangerous. The good news is that several Las Vegas neighborhoods put you within minutes of major medical centers, and the [Nevada Worker Advantage Program](/nevada-worker-advantage-program-explained) can help you afford a home nearby. ## Major Hospitals Across Las Vegas Las Vegas has a strong network of hospitals and medical centers spread throughout the valley. Understanding where they sit can help you focus your home search. **Central Las Vegas** is home to UMC at 1800 W Charleston Blvd, Valley Hospital at 620 Shadow Ln, Sunrise Hospital at 3186 S Maryland Pkwy, and Desert Springs Hospital at 2075 E Flamingo Rd. The central corridor offers median home prices near $482,000, with older neighborhoods providing entry level options for healthcare workers. **Southwest Las Vegas** hosts Spring Valley Hospital at 5400 S Rainbow Blvd, Southern Hills Hospital at 9300 W Sunset Rd, and Dignity Health at 4855 Blue Diamond Rd. Median prices in the southwest sit around $455,000, making it a solid option for medical professionals who want newer construction. **Centennial Hills** features Centennial Hills Hospital at 6900 N Durango Dr. Homes here have a median price near $450,000 and the area continues to grow with new master planned communities. ## $20,000 in Down Payment Assistance for Healthcare Workers The [Worker Advantage Program](/nevada-worker-advantage-program-explained) offers $20,000 in down payment assistance to healthcare workers buying their first home in Nevada. The grant comes as a 0% interest, no payment second mortgage over 30 years. Thirteen healthcare positions qualify, including [nurses](/nurses-buying-homes-las-vegas-worker-advantage), medical assistants, home health aides, and pharmacists. That $20,000 can cover most or all of your down payment on an FHA loan for a median priced Las Vegas home. Combined with competitive interest rates, this program makes homeownership realistic for hospital employees who thought it was out of reach. ## Neighborhoods Worth Exploring Near Las Vegas Hospitals Communities near UMC and Valley Hospital include neighborhoods west of the Strip along Charleston and Sahara. You will find condos and single family homes at various price points. Near Sunrise Hospital, the Maryland Parkway corridor offers townhomes and smaller single family options. For those working at Spring Valley or Southern Hills, look at neighborhoods along Rainbow and Sunset. The southwest corridor has seen significant new construction in recent years. Near Centennial Hills Hospital, newer subdivisions along Durango and the 215 Beltway provide modern floor plans and family friendly amenities. ## Ryan Rose: One Local Insight I have helped many nurses and medical professionals buy homes close to their hospitals right here in Las Vegas. The key is matching your shift schedule, budget, and lifestyle preferences with the right neighborhood. Some healthcare workers prefer a five minute drive so they can sleep longer between shifts. Others want a bit more space and accept a 15 minute commute. Either way, the Worker Advantage Program's $20,000 grant gives you more flexibility than you might expect.
