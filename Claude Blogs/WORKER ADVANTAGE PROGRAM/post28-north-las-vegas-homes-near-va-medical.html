# North Las Vegas Homes Near VA Medical Center North Las Vegas offers the most affordable home prices in the valley, and it is home to the VA Southern Nevada Healthcare System. For healthcare workers, veterans, and medical professionals employed at the VA or nearby Dignity Health, buying a home in North Las Vegas stretches your dollar further than almost anywhere else in the metro area. The [Nevada Worker Advantage Program](/nevada-worker-advantage-program-explained) makes it even more attainable with $20,000 in down payment assistance. ## Hospitals in North Las Vegas **VA Southern Nevada Healthcare System** at 6900 N Pecos Rd is one of the largest employers in North Las Vegas. The campus provides comprehensive care to veterans across Southern Nevada and employs hundreds of nurses, physicians, medical assistants, and support staff. **Dignity Health Neighborhood Hospital** at 1550 W Craig Rd offers emergency and inpatient services to the growing North Las Vegas community. As the city expands northward, additional medical facilities are planned to keep pace with population growth. ## Why North Las Vegas Is a Smart Buy for Healthcare Workers North Las Vegas has a median home price of approximately $410,000, the lowest among major communities in the Las Vegas valley. That price difference is meaningful. Compared to the overall Las Vegas median of $482,000, you could save more than $70,000 on your purchase price alone. Newer master planned communities like Aliante, North Creek, and Tule Springs offer modern construction, community amenities, and easy access to the 215 Beltway. Many of these neighborhoods sit within 10 to 15 minutes of the VA Medical Center. ## $20,000 Down Payment Assistance Details The [Worker Advantage Program](/nevada-worker-advantage-program-explained) provides $20,000 as a 0% interest, no payment second mortgage over 30 years. Healthcare workers in [13 qualifying positions](/jobs-qualifying-worker-advantage-grant) can use the funds toward down payment and closing costs. On a $410,000 North Las Vegas home with an FHA loan, the 3.5% down payment comes to $14,350. The Worker Advantage grant covers that completely and leaves over $5,600 for closing costs. For [nurses near the VA](/nurses-homes-north-las-vegas-va-medical) and other medical staff, this program can eliminate the biggest barrier to homeownership. ## Neighborhoods to Consider The Aliante community near the 215 and Aliante Pkwy puts you about 10 minutes from the VA Medical Center. It features parks, trails, a golf course, and a casino with dining options. Homes in Aliante range from the high $300,000s to the low $500,000s. Near Dignity Health on Craig Rd, established neighborhoods along Craig and Losee offer single family homes in the $350,000 to $425,000 range. Newer construction along the 215 northern beltway provides additional options for buyers who want modern features and energy efficient designs. ## Ryan Rose: One Local Insight I regularly work with VA hospital employees and military families looking to buy in North Las Vegas.
