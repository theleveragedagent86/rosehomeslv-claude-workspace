# Summerlin Homes Near Summerlin Hospital Summerlin is one of the most desirable master planned communities in the Las Vegas valley. For healthcare workers at Summerlin Hospital or MountainView Hospital, living in Summerlin means a short commute through tree lined streets, access to over 250 parks and trails, and homes that hold their value well. The [Nevada Worker Advantage Program](/nevada-worker-advantage-program-explained) offers $20,000 in down payment assistance to help healthcare workers buy into this sought after community. ## Summerlin's Two Major Hospitals **Summerlin Hospital Medical Center** at 657 N Town Center Dr sits in the heart of Downtown Summerlin, surrounded by shopping, dining, and entertainment at the Summerlin mall. The hospital is a major employer in the area and provides a full range of medical services. **MountainView Hospital** at 3100 N Tenaya Way anchors the eastern side of Summerlin near the border with the Spring Valley area. Both hospitals employ hundreds of nurses, technicians, therapists, and support staff who benefit from living close to work. ## Understanding Summerlin Home Prices Summerlin's median home price is approximately $686,000, the highest among major Las Vegas communities. However, that number reflects the full range of Summerlin properties, including luxury homes in The Ridges and Red Rock Country Club. More affordable options exist, particularly in older Summerlin villages built in the 1990s and early 2000s. Condos and townhomes in Summerlin can start in the $300,000 to $400,000 range. Smaller single family homes in villages like The Trails, The Willows, and Summerlin North may fall in the $450,000 to $550,000 range. These price points become much more manageable with the Worker Advantage Program. ## How $20,000 Helps in Summerlin The [Worker Advantage Program](/nevada-worker-advantage-program-explained) provides $20,000 as a 0% interest second mortgage with no monthly payments for 30 years. For a $500,000 Summerlin townhome or smaller single family home, the FHA down payment of 3.5% equals $17,500. The grant covers that entirely. [Nurses](/nurses-homes-summerlin-hospital-area), medical assistants, physical therapists, and other [qualifying healthcare positions](/jobs-qualifying-worker-advantage-grant) are all eligible. Combined with a competitive first mortgage rate, the program brings Summerlin within reach for hospital employees who want to live where they work. ## Neighborhoods Near Summerlin Hospitals Near Summerlin Hospital, the Downtown Summerlin area and surrounding villages like The Paseos and Summerlin Centre offer walkable access to restaurants, Red Rock Casino, and the Las Vegas Ballpark. These neighborhoods provide a mix of housing types at various price points. Near MountainView Hospital, communities along Tenaya Way and the western edge of Spring Valley offer slightly lower prices while keeping your commute under 10 minutes. The 215 Beltway connects both hospital areas quickly, so living near one gives you reasonable access to the other. ## Ryan Rose: One Local Insight Summerlin has different villages with distinct personalities, and I help buyers find the right fit rather than just the right price.
