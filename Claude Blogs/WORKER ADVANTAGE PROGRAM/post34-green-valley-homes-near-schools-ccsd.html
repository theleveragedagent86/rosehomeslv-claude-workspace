# Green Valley Homes Near Schools and CCSD Campuses Green Valley in Henderson offers one of the strongest concentrations of well rated CCSD schools in the Las Vegas valley. The established neighborhoods, tree lined streets, and proximity to multiple campuses make Green Valley a top choice for education workers looking to live near the schools where they work. Nevada's Worker Advantage Program provides $20,000 in down payment assistance to help make that possible. ## CCSD Schools Serving Green Valley Green Valley sits within CCSD's Southeast geographic region and is served by several respected campuses. Coronado High School, ranked thirteenth in Nevada, anchors the area and draws students from surrounding neighborhoods. Green Valley High School is another well known campus with strong athletics and academics. The area also includes multiple elementary and middle schools within its master planned grid, giving families school options at every level. CSN High School campuses nearby offer dual enrollment pathways for students pursuing early college credit. ## Green Valley Home Prices Home prices in Green Valley generally range from $400,000 to $650,000, depending on the neighborhood and property size. Older sections of Green Valley near Green Valley Parkway and Sunset Road tend to offer homes in the low to mid $400,000s, while newer and larger properties in Green Valley Ranch and nearby Anthem push into the $500,000s and $600,000s. Condos and townhomes in the area can start below $300,000. All of these price points sit well within the Worker Advantage Program's $832,750 maximum. ## Worker Advantage Program Details Education workers across ten qualifying positions can receive $20,000 structured as a 0% interest second mortgage. There are no monthly payments, and repayment occurs only when you sell, refinance, or complete the 30 year term. Teachers, teacher aides, school counselors, bus drivers, and custodians all qualify. You need a 640 minimum credit score, six months of Nevada residency, and household income at or below $142,350 in Clark County. ## Why Green Valley Appeals to CCSD Staff Green Valley was one of the first master planned communities in the valley, and its maturity brings benefits that newer areas cannot match. Established landscaping, walkable school routes, and close proximity to shopping and dining make daily life convenient. CCSD employees who work at Green Valley area schools can often walk or bike to campus, eliminating commute stress entirely. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose has guided several CCSD employees into Green Valley homes that sit within walking distance of their campus.
