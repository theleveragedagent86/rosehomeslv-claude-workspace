# Henderson Homes for First Responders Near Public Safety Facilities Henderson offers first responders a combination of safe neighborhoods, strong schools, and convenient access to public safety facilities. If you work for Henderson Fire Department or Henderson Police Department, the [Nevada Worker Advantage Program](/nevada-worker-advantage-program-explained) provides $20,000 in down payment assistance to help you buy a home close to where you serve. ## Henderson Fire Department Facilities Henderson Fire Department operates multiple stations across the city, covering everything from residential neighborhoods to the Henderson Executive Airport and the Water Street district. Stations are distributed throughout Green Valley, Anthem, Inspirada, and the Cadence master planned community. This spread means firefighters and EMTs assigned to Henderson can find homes within minutes of nearly any station in the city. Henderson's median home price sits around $495,000, which is slightly higher than the Las Vegas average. However, pockets of value exist in established neighborhoods near older stations, particularly along Boulder Highway and in the original Henderson townsite near Water Street. ## Henderson Police Department Henderson Police Department serves the entire city from its main headquarters and substations positioned to cover the expanding southern and western sections. Officers patrol neighborhoods ranging from the urban core near Downtown Henderson to master planned communities like MacDonald Highlands, Inspirada, and Cadence. Henderson consistently ranks among the safest cities in America, which contributes to strong home values and demand. For officers who want to live where they work, that safety record is both a professional point of pride and a personal benefit. ## $20,000 in Down Payment Assistance The [Worker Advantage Program](/nevada-worker-advantage-program-explained) grants $20,000 as a 0% interest, no payment second mortgage. Twelve public safety positions qualify, including [police officers](/police-officers-homes-henderson-worker-advantage), [firefighters and EMTs](/firefighters-emts-20000-down-payment), correctional officers, and dispatchers. The grant applies to FHA, VA, and conventional loans. At Henderson's $495,000 median, the $20,000 covers roughly 4% of the purchase price. That means an FHA buyer needs almost no additional cash for a down payment, making Henderson homeownership more accessible for first responders than many realize. ## Henderson Neighborhoods Worth Exploring **Green Valley** offers mature landscaping, established schools, and homes in the $450,000 to $550,000 range. Multiple fire stations serve this area and Henderson PD maintains strong coverage. **Inspirada and Cadence** feature newer construction with modern floor plans. Prices start in the mid $400,000s for townhomes and reach into the $500,000s for single family homes. Both communities have fire stations nearby. **Downtown Henderson and Water Street** present the most affordable options in the city. The ongoing revitalization of Water Street has added restaurants, entertainment, and new residential projects while keeping prices below the city median. ## Ryan Rose: One Local Insight Henderson first responders tell me they love living where they work because it feels like protecting their own neighborhood.
