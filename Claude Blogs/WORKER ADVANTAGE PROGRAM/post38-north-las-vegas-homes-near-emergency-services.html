# North Las Vegas Homes Near Emergency Services Facilities North Las Vegas offers first responders some of the most affordable homes in the Las Vegas valley, with convenient access to fire stations, police facilities, and emergency services. The [Nevada Worker Advantage Program](/nevada-worker-advantage-program-explained) provides $20,000 in down payment assistance that makes North Las Vegas especially attractive for public safety workers looking to buy. ## North Las Vegas Fire Department North Las Vegas Fire Department operates stations throughout the city, covering both established neighborhoods and the rapidly expanding northern corridor. The department responds to calls across a growing service area as new master planned communities continue to push north toward the mountains. Fire stations serve neighborhoods along Craig Road, Cheyenne Avenue, and the newer developments near Aliante and Elkhorn. Firefighters and EMTs assigned to North Las Vegas stations can find homes within a short drive of virtually any station in the city. ## North Las Vegas Police Department North Las Vegas Police Department maintains headquarters and patrol divisions that cover the entire city. Officers patrol areas ranging from the older neighborhoods along Las Vegas Boulevard North to newer communities in the Aliante and North Valley areas. The department has expanded its presence as the city's population has grown, adding resources to serve the northern growth corridor. ## $20,000 Down Payment Assistance for Public Safety Workers The [Worker Advantage Program](/nevada-worker-advantage-program-explained) offers $20,000 as a 0% interest, no payment second mortgage over 30 years. [Police officers](/police-officers-homes-las-vegas-worker-advantage), [firefighters and EMTs](/firefighters-emts-20000-down-payment), correctional officers, and dispatchers all qualify among the twelve eligible public safety positions. North Las Vegas has the lowest median home price among the major cities in the valley at approximately $410,000. That $20,000 grant covers nearly 5% of the purchase price here, meaning an FHA buyer could cover the entire down payment with the grant alone and still have money left for closing costs or a rate buydown. ## Neighborhoods to Explore **Aliante** is a master planned community with parks, trails, a golf course, and a casino. Homes range from the high $300,000s to mid $400,000s. Multiple fire stations serve the area and NLV PD maintains active patrol coverage. **Elkhorn and the North Valley** feature newer construction with larger lots. Prices start in the low $400,000s for single family homes. This area is ideal for first responders who want a newer home with space at a price well below Henderson or southwest Las Vegas. **Central North Las Vegas** along Craig Road and Cheyenne offers the most affordable options, with homes in the $300,000s to low $400,000s.
