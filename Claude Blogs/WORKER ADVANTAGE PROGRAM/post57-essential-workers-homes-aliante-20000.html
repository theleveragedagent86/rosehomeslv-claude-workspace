# Essential Workers Buying Homes in Aliante with $20,000 Assistance Aliante is one of the most desirable master planned communities in North Las Vegas, and essential workers can buy here using the $20,000 Worker Advantage grant. With a median home price of $460,000, homes that sell in just 36 days on average, and resort style amenities throughout the community, Aliante offers a quality of life that rivals neighborhoods costing significantly more elsewhere in the valley. ## What Makes Aliante Stand Out Aliante was designed as a complete community from the ground up. The master plan includes the Aliante Nature Discovery Park, walking trails, community pools, and the Aliante Casino and Hotel as its commercial anchor. Residents have convenient access to both I-215 and US-95, which connects them to employers across the valley in 20 to 30 minutes. For essential workers, the location is strategic. The VA Medical Center on N Pecos Road is a short drive east. Centennial Hills Hospital sits just to the southwest. CCSD schools serve the community, and NLV Fire stations provide coverage throughout the area.

## How the $20,000 Grant Works in Aliante The [Worker Advantage Program](/nevada-worker-advantage-program-explained) provides $20,000 at 0% interest with no monthly payments. On a $460,000 home with FHA financing at 3.5% down, the required down payment is $16,100. The grant covers that entirely and leaves $3,900 toward closing costs. That is a meaningful difference for a nurse, teacher, or firefighter who has been saving for years. Instead of needing $20,000 or more in cash reserves, you need the grant plus minimal out of pocket funds. Check the full [income and credit requirements](/worker-advantage-income-credit-requirements) to confirm eligibility. ## Homes Move Fast Here At 36 days on market, Aliante is one of the fastest selling neighborhoods in North Las Vegas. That pace tells you demand is strong, which also means home values have held well. For buyers using Worker Advantage, this is encouraging because you are buying into a community where resale values are supported by consistent demand. However, the speed also means you need to be prepared before you start shopping. Get your pre-approval from a [Home Is Possible approved lender](/worker-advantage-home-is-possible-lenders), verify your profession qualifies among the [47 eligible occupations](/jobs-qualifying-worker-advantage-grant), and have your documentation ready so you can move quickly when the right home appears. ## What You Can Expect at $460,000 Homes in Aliante at the median price point typically offer 1,600 to 2,200 square feet, three to four bedrooms, two car garages, and modern open floor plans. Many homes were built between 2005 and 2015, so they feature granite counters, tile flooring, and covered patios. Some include community pool access through the HOA. For essential workers comparing Aliante to similarly priced areas, the difference is the completeness of the community. You get the amenities, the walkability, and the neighborhood feel without paying Summerlin or Henderson prices. ## Ryan's Local Insight Aliante surprises buyers who assume North Las Vegas is all entry level housing.
