# Essential Workers Buying Homes in Mountains Edge with Worker Advantage Mountains Edge is one of the most popular master planned communities in southwest Las Vegas, and essential workers are discovering it offers an ideal combination of newer homes, strong schools, and neighborhood amenities. Paired with Nevada's Worker Advantage Program, buying here just became significantly more attainable. ## Why Mountains Edge Attracts Essential Workers This active master planned community stretches across the southwest valley with homes ranging from $375,000 to well over $2 million. The median home price currently sits around **$485,000**, placing many properties within comfortable reach for essential worker households earning up to $142,350. Mountains Edge appeals to shift workers for practical reasons. The community provides quick access to Dignity Health Hospitals at Blue Diamond (4855 Blue Diamond Road), Southern Hills Hospital (9300 West Sunset Road), and the LVMPD Enterprise Area Command (6975 West Windmill Lane). Whether you respond to emergencies, care for patients, or build the homes that fill these streets, your commute stays manageable. ## How Worker Advantage Makes Mountains Edge Affordable The [Worker Advantage Program](/post1-nevada-worker-advantage-program-explained.html) provides a **$20,000 grant** structured as a 0% interest second mortgage with no monthly payments. That grant applies directly to down payment and closing costs. On the Mountains Edge median of $485,000 with an FHA loan: - **FHA down payment (3.5%):** $16,975 - **Worker Advantage grant:** $20,000 - **Remaining for closing costs:** approximately $3,025 That $20,000 covers the entire down payment and a meaningful portion of closing costs. Many essential workers can purchase in Mountains Edge with **minimal out of pocket expense** beyond what the grant provides. ## Who Qualifies? Nevada's program covers [47 essential professions](/post10-jobs-qualifying-worker-advantage-grant.html) across healthcare, education, public safety, and construction. Key requirements include: - **Credit score:** 640 or higher - **Household income limit:** $142,350 - **Purchase price cap:** $832,750 - **Nevada residency:** minimum 6 months You do not need to be a first time buyer. If you already own property or purchased a home in the past, you can still [apply for Worker Advantage](/post2-apply-nevada-20000-down-payment-assistance.html). ## Mountains Edge Schools and Family Life Clark County School District serves Mountains Edge with schools rated among the better options in the southwest valley. Families with children find parks, trails, and recreational areas woven throughout the community. The walkability and neighborhood feel draw essential workers who want a suburban lifestyle without sacrificing access to the hospitals, stations, and schools where they work. **One local insight from Ryan Rose:** Mountains Edge consistently shows up in my searches when helping essential workers find homes under $500,000 in established southwest neighborhoods. The community delivers newer construction, solid HOA maintained common areas, and a commute under 20 minutes to most major hospitals and emergency facilities on the south end of the valley. It is one of the best values I see for workers in that price range. ## Take the Next Step Start by confirming your profession qualifies on the [eligible jobs list](/post10-jobs-qualifying-worker-advantage-grant.html), then connect with a [participating lender](/post9-worker-advantage-home-is-possible-lenders.html).
