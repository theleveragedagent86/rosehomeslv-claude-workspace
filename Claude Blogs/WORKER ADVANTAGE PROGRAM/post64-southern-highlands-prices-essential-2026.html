# Southern Highlands Home Prices for Essential Workers in 2026 Southern Highlands carries a reputation as a luxury community, and 2026 prices reflect that positioning. But essential workers who qualify for Nevada's Worker Advantage Program can still find pathways into this prestigious southwest Las Vegas neighborhood. Understanding the pricing landscape helps you plan realistically. ## Current Southern Highlands Pricing As of early 2026, Southern Highlands pricing looks like this: - **Median list price:** $705,500 - **Active listings:** approximately 160 - **Typical range:** $450,000 to $1.5 million - **Entry level options:** limited inventory below $500,000 The median sits significantly higher than the Las Vegas valley average of roughly $482,000. Southern Highlands residents carry an average net worth of $779,866 and a median household income of $126,134, which signals the kind of community essential worker families would be joining. ## How $20,000 Applies at Southern Highlands Prices The [Worker Advantage Program](/post1-nevada-worker-advantage-program-explained.html) provides a **$20,000 grant** at 0% interest with no monthly payments. At Southern Highlands price points, here is what that looks like: **At $500,000 (FHA, 3.5% down):** - Down payment: $17,500 - Grant covers: full down payment plus $2,500 toward closing - Estimated out of pocket: approximately **$3,000 to $5,000** **At $700,000 (conventional, 5% down):** - Down payment: $35,000 - Grant covers: $20,000 of down payment - Your portion: approximately **$15,000 plus closing costs** **At $832,750 (program maximum, conventional 5% down):** - Down payment: $41,637 - Grant covers: $20,000 of down payment - Your portion: approximately **$21,637 plus closing costs** At the higher end, $20,000 still represents meaningful savings, but essential workers should plan for a larger personal contribution compared to purchasing in more [affordable communities nearby](/post61-essential-workers-homes-mountains-edge.html). ## Finding Attainable Listings Inventory under $500,000 in Southern Highlands is very limited, but it does exist. Condos, townhomes, and smaller single family properties occasionally surface in the **mid $400,000s**. Two strategies help essential workers land these listings: 1. **Get preapproved first.** Having Worker Advantage approval through a [participating lender](/post9-worker-advantage-home-is-possible-lenders.html) means you can write offers immediately when attainable listings appear. 2. **Watch new listings daily.** Properties priced under $550,000 in Southern Highlands tend to attract multiple offers within days. ## Eligibility Reminder Worker Advantage covers [47 essential professions](/post10-jobs-qualifying-worker-advantage-grant.html) with these requirements: - **Household income:** $142,350 or less - **Credit score:** 640 minimum - **Purchase price cap:** $832,750 - **Nevada residency:** at least 6 months - **No first time buyer requirement** Healthcare professionals working at nearby St. Rose Dominican Hospital on San Martin (8280 West Warm Springs Road) or Southern Hills Hospital find Southern Highlands offers a particularly short commute. The community also works well for [first responders](/post88-first-responders-homes-mountains-edge.html) and [teachers](/post83-teachers-homes-mountains-edge-worker-advantage.html) assigned to southwest valley schools and stations. **One local insight from Ryan Rose:** I am upfront with essential worker clients about Southern Highlands pricing. It is a stretch for many budgets, but dual income households in qualifying professions can often make the numbers work on properties in the $500,000 to $650,000 range. The $20,000 grant closes a real gap at those price points. If Southern Highlands feels too tight, I usually recommend looking at Mountains Edge first, then revisiting Southern Highlands if your budget allows. ## Plan Your Approach Review the [full program requirements](/post4-worker-advantage-income-credit-requirements.html) and talk to a lender about your realistic price range before you start shopping. Southern Highlands rewards buyers who come prepared and move decisively when the right property appears. --- *Sources: Nevada Housing Division Worker Advantage Program guidelines; Southern Highlands MLS data; U.S. Census ACS demographic estimates; Clark County Assessor records, March 2026.*
