# Essential Workers Buying Homes in Inspirada with $20,000 Assistance Inspirada is one of Henderson's newest master planned communities, and essential workers are taking notice. With active new construction from multiple builders, modern amenities, and Nevada's Worker Advantage Program covering nearly the entire down payment, Inspirada offers a compelling path to homeownership. ## Why Inspirada Works for Essential Workers This growing community in southeast Henderson currently has around **99 active listings** with a median price near **$549,000**. Homes range from $419,000 to over $1.1 million, and the average days on market sits at 54, up slightly from last year. That pace gives buyers breathing room to make thoughtful decisions rather than scrambling through bidding wars. Inspirada provides easy access to I-11 and I-215, putting Henderson Hospital and St. Rose Dominican Siena Campus within a short drive. Public safety professionals working southeast Henderson stations find the location equally convenient. For educators, CCSD serves the community with schools that continue to grow alongside the neighborhood. ## How $20,000 Changes the Math at Inspirada The [Worker Advantage Program](/post1-nevada-worker-advantage-program-explained.html) provides a **$20,000 grant** structured as a 0% interest second mortgage with no monthly payments. At Inspirada's median price, the numbers tell a powerful story. On the Inspirada median of $549,000 with an FHA loan: - **FHA down payment (3.5%):** $19,215 - **Worker Advantage grant:** $20,000 - **Remaining for closing costs:** approximately $785 The grant covers **virtually the entire FHA down payment**. That leaves the remaining balance plus any additional closing cost assistance from your lender. Many essential workers can purchase a newer Inspirada home with almost nothing out of pocket for the down payment itself. ## Who Qualifies? Nevada's program covers [47 essential professions](/post10-jobs-qualifying-worker-advantage-grant.html) in healthcare, education, public safety, and construction. Requirements include: - **Credit score:** 640 or higher - **Household income limit:** $142,350 - **Purchase price cap:** $832,750 - **Nevada residency:** minimum 6 months Previous homeowners qualify. You do not need to be a first time buyer to [apply for Worker Advantage](/post2-apply-nevada-20000-down-payment-assistance.html). ## New Construction Advantage Unlike established communities where resale is the only option, Inspirada features **active new construction from multiple builders**. That means essential workers can purchase a brand new home and apply the $20,000 grant to the transaction. Builder incentives sometimes stack with the grant, creating even more favorable terms. Ask your [participating lender](/post9-worker-advantage-home-is-possible-lenders.html) how builder credits interact with the Worker Advantage funds. **One local insight from Ryan Rose:** Inspirada is the community I point to when essential workers tell me they want something newer in Henderson but assume they cannot afford it. The math at $549,000 with FHA and the $20,000 grant is remarkably tight. The grant covers 104% of the minimum down payment. That surprises almost every buyer I walk through the numbers with. ## Take the Next Step Start by confirming your profession qualifies on the [eligible jobs list](/post10-jobs-qualifying-worker-advantage-grant.html).
