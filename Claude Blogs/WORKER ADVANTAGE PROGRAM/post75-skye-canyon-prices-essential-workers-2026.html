The Skye Canyon market ranges from the mid $400,000s to the low $800,000s depending on home size, lot, and whether the property is new construction or resale. This makes Skye Canyon attainable without a large savings reserve. ## What Makes Skye Canyon Attractive for Essential Workers? Skye Canyon sits in the northwest valley with easy access to US 95 and proximity to Red Rock Canyon. The area continues to grow rapidly, with new retail and dining options following the residential expansion. ## How Does the Income Limit Apply Here?
