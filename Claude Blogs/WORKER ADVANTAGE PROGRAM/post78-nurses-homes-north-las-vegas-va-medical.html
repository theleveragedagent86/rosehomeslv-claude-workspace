# Nurses Buying Homes in North Las Vegas Near the VA Medical Center North Las Vegas offers the most affordable home prices in the valley, and for nurses working at the VA Southern Nevada Healthcare System, living nearby makes both financial and practical sense. The [Nevada Worker Advantage Program](/nevada-worker-advantage-program-explained) adds [$20,000 in down payment assistance](/apply-nevada-20000-down-payment-assistance) that can make homeownership a reality for [nurses](/nurses-buying-homes-las-vegas-worker-advantage) at every stage of their career. ## VA Medical Center and North Las Vegas Hospitals The **VA Southern Nevada Healthcare System** at 6900 N Pecos Rd is one of the largest employers in North Las Vegas. It serves veterans across southern Nevada and employs hundreds of nurses in inpatient, outpatient, and specialty care roles. **Dignity Health North Las Vegas Hospital** at 1550 W Craig Rd provides additional nursing employment in the area. Both facilities are easily accessible from most North Las Vegas neighborhoods. ## North Las Vegas Home Prices for Nurses North Las Vegas has a median home price of approximately $410,000 in 2026, making it the most affordable city in the Las Vegas metro. For nurses earning a typical RN salary in the range of $75,000 to $95,000, this price point creates realistic paths to homeownership that simply do not exist in [Summerlin](/nurses-homes-summerlin-hospital-area) or parts of [Henderson](/nurses-homes-henderson-st-rose). Many neighborhoods near the VA Medical Center along Pecos Road and Losee Road offer single family homes in the $350,000 to $425,000 range. Newer communities in Aliante and the northern reaches of the city provide modern construction with community pools, parks, and walking trails. ## How the $20,000 Grant Helps VA Nurses The [Worker Advantage Program](/nevada-worker-advantage-program-explained) provides $20,000 as a 0% interest second mortgage with no monthly payments over 30 years. [Thirteen healthcare positions qualify](/jobs-qualifying-worker-advantage-grant), and VA nurses holding RN, LPN, or NP credentials are all eligible. On a $380,000 home near the VA campus with an FHA loan, the 3.5% down payment is $13,300. The grant covers that completely and provides $6,700 toward closing costs. For many VA nurses, this means purchasing with little to no money out of pocket. Buyers need a 640 credit score and income at or below $142,350. ## Commute and Lifestyle Near the VA Living within 10 minutes of the VA Medical Center is entirely achievable in North Las Vegas. The neighborhoods east of I-15 along Pecos and Craig roads place nurses close to both the VA and Dignity Health facilities. The 215 Beltway's northern segment connects North Las Vegas to Henderson and Summerlin for nurses who pick up shifts at other hospitals. Aliante, one of North Las Vegas's premier master planned communities, offers a casino resort, golf course, and nature park within a 15 minute drive of the VA campus. ## Ryan Rose: One Local Insight VA nurses often qualify for both the Worker Advantage Program and VA home loan benefits, but they cannot always be combined on the same transaction.
