# CCSD Teachers and Southern Nevada Home Prices in 2026 Southern Nevada home prices in 2026 vary widely by community, and that range creates real opportunities for CCSD teachers using the $20,000 Worker Advantage grant. Understanding where prices sit across the valley helps educators choose a neighborhood that fits both their budget and their school assignment. ## Price Breakdown by Area Henderson's median home price currently sits at $495,000, with strong school ratings and over 230 new construction options under $400,000. Mountains Edge comes in at $485,000, offering newer homes in the southwest with prices starting around $375,000. Las Vegas proper averages $482,000, with the most diverse inventory in the valley. North Las Vegas remains the most affordable major area at approximately $410,000, giving teachers the greatest purchasing power. Summerlin leads at $686,000, reflecting its premium schools and master planned amenities. ## How the $20,000 Grant Changes the Math On a $410,000 home in North Las Vegas, the Worker Advantage grant covers the entire 3.5% FHA down payment with over $5,000 remaining for closing costs. On a $495,000 Henderson home, the grant covers roughly $2,700 more than the FHA minimum down payment. Even in Summerlin, the $20,000 significantly reduces the cash a teacher needs at closing. The grant carries 0% interest and requires no monthly payments for the life of the loan. ## Eligibility for CCSD Teachers You need a minimum 640 credit score, at least six months of Nevada residency, and household income at or below $142,350 in Clark County. The maximum purchase price of $832,750 covers homes in every Southern Nevada community. You do not need to be a first time buyer. ## CCSD's Scale Creates Options CCSD operates 373 schools across five regions. Teachers can request transfers and pursue openings at schools near their preferred neighborhoods. This flexibility means you do not need to limit your home search to the area where you currently teach. A teacher at a North Las Vegas elementary school today may transfer to a Henderson campus next year and already own a home there. ## Worker Advantage vs. Home Is Possible for Teachers Home Is Possible for Teachers provides $7,500 to K through 12 classroom teachers. Worker Advantage delivers $20,000 and covers all 10 education roles including aides, counselors, bus drivers, custodians, librarians, and secretaries. For any CCSD employee, Worker Advantage is the clear choice. ## Local Insight Ryan Rose tracks pricing trends across every major Southern Nevada community and consistently sees CCSD teachers underestimate what the $20,000 grant allows them to afford.
