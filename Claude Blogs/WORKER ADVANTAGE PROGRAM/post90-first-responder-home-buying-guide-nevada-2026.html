# First Responder Home Buying Guide for Southern Nevada 2026 If you protect the public in Southern Nevada, the state wants to help you buy a home. This guide covers the Worker Advantage Program, eligible professions, area pricing, and the steps to go from application to closing in 2026. ## Eligible First Responder Professions The Worker Advantage Program includes 12 public safety professions on its list of 47 eligible careers: 1. Police Officer 2. Firefighter 3. EMT 4. Paramedic 5. Sheriff / Deputy Sheriff 6. Correctional Officer 7. Dispatcher (911 and public safety) 8. State Trooper 9. Border Patrol Agent 10. Search and Rescue Personnel 11. Hazmat Technician / Public Safety Diver 12. Wildland Firefighter At least one borrower on the mortgage must hold a full time position in one of these roles. ## Program Requirements - **Grant amount:** $20,000 as a 0% interest second mortgage - **Monthly payments:** None required - **Repayment triggers:** Sale, refinance, or end of 30 year term - **Credit score:** 640 minimum - **Income limit:** $142,350 household (Clark County) - **Purchase price cap:** $832,750 - **Residency:** 6 months in Nevada - **First time buyer:** Not required The grant applies to down payment, closing costs, or a mortgage rate buydown. You choose how to allocate it. ## 2026 Home Prices by Area First responders work across the valley, and each area offers different price points: | Area | Median Price | FHA Down (3.5%) | Grant Surplus | |---|---|---|---| | North Las Vegas | $410,000 | $14,350 | $5,650 | | Las Vegas | $482,000 | $16,870 | $3,130 | | Mountains Edge | $485,000 | $16,975 | $3,025 | | Henderson | $495,000 | $17,325 | $2,675 | In every major area, the $20,000 grant covers the full FHA down payment and leaves funds for closing costs. ## Steps to Purchase **Step 1: Confirm eligibility.** Verify your profession appears on the approved list and that your household income stays at or below $142,350. **Step 2: Connect with a participating lender.** The Worker Advantage Program requires you to work with a Home Is Possible approved lender who processes the grant alongside your first mortgage. **Step 3: Get pre approved.** Your lender will run credit, verify employment, and confirm you meet the 640 score threshold. **Step 4: Find your home.** Work with a local real estate agent who understands the program and can guide you toward homes that fit your budget and commute. **Step 5: Close and move in.** The $20,000 grant funds at closing alongside your first mortgage. No separate application timeline is needed once your lender has everything in the pipeline. ## Local Insight **Ryan Rose:** I have walked dozens of first responders through this program, from LVMPD officers to CCFD firefighters to AMR paramedics.
