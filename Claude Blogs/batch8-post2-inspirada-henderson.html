Inspirada Henderson: A Community Built Around Parks

Inspirada redefines master planned living in Henderson with 85 acres of parks, 35 miles of interconnected trails, and seven distinctive recreational spaces that place every resident within a five minute walk of outdoor amenities. This thoughtfully designed community in Henderson's southern foothills delivers the connected, active lifestyle that modern families seek.

Community Overview

Spanning nearly 2,000 acres, Inspirada comprises seven villages with approximately 8,500 homes upon completion. The community sits conveniently positioned about 15 minutes from Harry Reid International Airport and 16 miles from the Las Vegas Strip, with easy access to Interstate 15 and the 215 Beltway. Home prices typically range from the mid $400,000s to over $700,000, with median prices around $540,000. Properties average approximately 47 days on market, indicating healthy buyer interest in this Henderson location. Award winning builders including KB Home, Toll Brothers, Tri Pointe Homes, and Century Communities offer diverse floor plans from townhomes to spacious single family residences.

Seven Signature Parks

Inspirada's parks represent the community's defining feature. Aventura Park spans over 20 acres with sports fields, courts, and a heated resident pool. Solista Park serves as the community gathering hub with an amphitheater, bocce ball courts, and the Romano Mercato Italiano café. Potenza Park features unique musical themes with interactive elements including a ground embedded piano keyboard. Capriola Park focuses on movement and sensory play for children. Attesa Park offers basketball courts, playgrounds, and dog parks. Montagna Park adds additional recreational space, while Sentiero Park opened in late 2025 as the seventh and final community park with its own heated pool and modern amenities.

Recreation and Nature Access

Beyond the parks, Inspirada residents enjoy access to four heated pools, volleyball and tennis courts, basketball facilities, soccer and baseball fields, splash pads, and multiple dog parks throughout the community. The 35 mile trail system connects all neighborhoods and parks, encouraging walking, jogging, and cycling as daily transportation. The community also serves as the gateway to Sloan Canyon National Conservation Area, where families can explore more than 1,700 ancient petroglyphs along moderate hiking trails.
