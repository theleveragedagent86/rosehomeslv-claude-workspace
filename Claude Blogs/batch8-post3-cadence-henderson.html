Cadence Henderson: The 50 Acre Central Park Community

Cadence anchors its identity around a stunning 50 acre Central Park that provides Henderson residents with a genuine outdoor gathering space complete with a scenic lake, mountain views, and connection to over 450 acres of parks and trails. This rapidly growing master planned community offers exceptional value and recreation in one of Nevada's safest cities.

Community Scale and Location

Spanning 2,200 acres in Henderson's eastern corridor, Cadence will ultimately feature approximately 13,250 homes across multiple neighborhoods. The community occupies a strategic position near the intersection of Interstate 515 and Interstate 215, placing residents approximately 15 miles from Harry Reid International Airport and within easy reach of Lake Mead National Recreation Area. The historic Water Street District in downtown Henderson sits just minutes away, offering dining, entertainment, and the city's arts scene. Home prices vary significantly based on neighborhood and buyer type, with options ranging from the mid $300,000s in family neighborhoods to the high $400,000s and beyond in specialty sections.

Central Park and Amenities

The 50 acre Central Park serves as Cadence's recreational centerpiece. A small lake surrounded by grassy lawns offers picturesque views of the surrounding mountains and the Las Vegas Strip to the west. The park includes walking and biking trails, an amphitheater for community events, playgrounds, and open spaces for informal recreation. Throughout the broader community, residents access additional amenities including a community pool, splash pads, a fitness court, sports courts, and a dedicated dog park. Thirty acres of interconnected bike and walking paths encourage outdoor activity as part of daily life.

Heritage at Cadence: Active Adult Living

Within Cadence, Heritage at Cadence provides Henderson's premier 55 plus community with approximately 1,000 homes upon completion. This guard gated neighborhood features exclusive access to a 22,000 square foot Heritage Recreation Center with fitness facilities, social spaces, and organized activities designed for active adults. Lennar Homes offers four floor plan designs tailored to the active adult lifestyle, emphasizing single story living, low maintenance features, and community connection.
