Rhodes Ranch Las Vegas: Guard Gated Golf Course Living

Rhodes Ranch delivers resort style living in a guard gated setting, built around a championship golf course designed by Ted Robinson Sr. Established in 1997 at the base of the Spring Mountains in southwest Las Vegas, this 1,330 acre community combines security, recreation, and convenience just six miles from the Las Vegas Strip.

Golf Course and Setting

The Ted Robinson designed Rhodes Ranch Golf Club anchors the community with 6,909 yards of championship golf featuring tropical landscaping, water hazards, and multiple elevation changes that earned a 4 star rating from Golf Digest Magazine. Rolling fairways wind through the community, providing scenic views for residents in golf course homes. While the course operates as a public facility, Rhodes Ranch residents enjoy discounted rates and convenient access. A beautifully landscaped two mile circular path surrounds the course, perfect for walking, jogging, or biking through the community's palm lined streets.

The RClub Recreation Center

Rhodes Ranch residents access the impressive 35,000 square foot RClub recreation center included with HOA membership. Facilities include a fully equipped fitness center, indoor basketball courts, racquetball and handball courts, and six billiards tables. Outdoor amenities feature resort style swimming pools for adults and families, plus the Fun Zone water park area for children. Tennis courts, pickleball courts, and organized recreational programs round out the offerings. The clubhouse hosts regular community events, fitness classes, and social gatherings throughout the year.

Housing Options and Value

Rhodes Ranch offers diverse housing options ranging from entry level condos in the $150,000s to executive single family homes with golf course views approaching $700,000. Home sizes span from efficient single story residences around 1,600 square feet to spacious properties approaching 5,000 square feet. The community features well planned neighborhoods with varying architectural styles, including Mediterranean and Spanish influences with stucco exteriors and tile roofs. Monthly HOA fees of approximately $155 cover all community amenities, making Rhodes Ranch one of the more affordable guard gated golf communities in the Las Vegas valley.

Expert Recommendation

Ryan Rose recommends Rhodes Ranch for buyers seeking the security of guard gated living combined with resort amenities at a reasonable cost. The southwest location provides easy access to the 215 Beltway and convenient proximity to shopping at Arroyo Plaza, Desert Marketplace, and Vons Plaza. Families appreciate the community's emphasis on security with 24/7 guarded entrances and strict speed limits. Southern Hills Hospital and San Martin Hospital both serve the community, ensuring quality healthcare access.
