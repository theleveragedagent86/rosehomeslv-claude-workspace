Aliante North Las Vegas: Master Planned Living with Mountain Views

Aliante established North Las Vegas as a legitimate destination for master planned community living when it broke ground as the city's first large scale residential development. Today, this 1,905 acre community at the base of the Sheep Mountains offers approximately 6,500 homes, over 400 acres of recreational space, and a complete lifestyle package that rivals communities anywhere in the Las Vegas valley.

Community Structure

Developed by American Nevada Corporation and Del Webb Communities, Aliante encompasses multiple distinct neighborhoods serving different buyer segments. The main community features single family homes, townhomes, and condominiums built by respected builders including KB Homes, Pulte, D.R. Horton, and Pardee. Club Aliante provides guard gated luxury living centered around the Aliante Golf Club. Sun City Aliante offers 55 plus active adult residents approximately 2,000 single story homes surrounding the golf course with dedicated amenities. Home prices across the community range from the mid $300,000s to over $800,000 in premium neighborhoods, with median prices around $445,000.

Parks and Recreation

Aliante dedicates over 400 acres to recreational and public use, making it one of the most park rich communities in the valley. The centerpiece is the 20 acre Nature Discovery Park featuring a scenic lake with waterfalls, dinosaur themed playground, amphitheater, picnic areas, and courts for tennis, volleyball, bocce, and horseshoes. A 24 mile interconnecting trail system winds throughout the community, encouraging walking, jogging, and cycling. Aviary Park provides lighted ball fields, soccer fields, basketball courts, and playground equipment. Deer Springs Park offers additional recreation with scenic mountain views from picnic areas.

Lifestyle Amenities

The Aliante Golf Club anchors recreational offerings with an 18 hole course providing stunning views of the Sheep Mountains and Las Vegas skyline. While residents in Sun City Aliante access dedicated community center amenities including fitness facilities, pools, tennis courts, and organized activities, all Aliante residents benefit from the Aliante Casino Hotel and Spa directly across the street. The casino offers dining options, entertainment, an IMAX theater, and services catering to local residents. Shopping conveniences include Costco, Walmart, Target, Best Buy, Winco Foods, and Sam's Club within a five minute drive.
