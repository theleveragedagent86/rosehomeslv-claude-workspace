Centennial Hills Las Vegas: Northwest Living at Its Best

Centennial Hills ranks among the fastest growing and most desirable areas in the Las Vegas valley, offering spacious homes with mountain views at prices that often undercut comparable Summerlin properties. This 56 square mile community in northwest Las Vegas combines excellent schools, abundant parks, and easy outdoor recreation access into a lifestyle package that attracts families, professionals, and retirees alike.

Location and Layout

Positioned in the northwest corner of Las Vegas across zip codes 89131, 89143, and 89149, Centennial Hills provides direct access to both US 95 and the 215 Beltway. The Las Vegas Strip sits approximately 30 minutes south, while Mount Charleston, Red Rock Canyon, and Floyd Lamb State Park offer outdoor recreation within 20 minutes. The community encompasses several notable neighborhoods including Providence, Skye Canyon, Iron Mountain Ranch, and Tule Springs, each with distinct character and price points. Housing options span from condos and townhomes to estate properties and horse properties on acreage, with median prices around $510,000.

The 110 Acre Town Center

The heart of Centennial Hills beats at the 110 acre town center featuring the 900,000 square foot Centennial Center shopping plaza with retail stores, restaurants, and movie theaters. Adjacent facilities include the Centennial Hills Hospital (the largest hospital in the Las Vegas valley), Centennial Hills YMCA Community Center, Centennial Hills Library, and Centennial Hills Park. The park alone offers picnic areas, sand volleyball courts, soccer fields, water play areas, jogging and walking paths, a 3,000 seat amphitheater, and substantial open space for community events including the popular Children's Arts Festival.

Schools and Family Appeal

Centennial Hills operates within the Clark County School District with access to well regarded schools including Arbor View High School, Centennial High School, and multiple elementary and middle school options. The family friendly atmosphere extends through neighborhood parks, community events, and organized youth activities. Gilcrease Orchard provides a unique local experience where families can pick seasonal produce, enjoy hayrides, and connect with agricultural heritage just minutes from suburban development.
