## Summerlin North (7 Villages + Sun City)

### 1. The Hills
Amarante, Belaire Estates, Cherry Creek, Copper Ridge, Cypress Grove, Evergreen, Hillpointe, Las Colinas, Panorama Pointe, Serenata, Valle del Fiori, Visions, Willow Tree

### 2. The Hills South
Corta Bella, Country Club Hills, Eagle Hills, Montaire, Santa Barbara, The Terraces, Terraces on the Green, **Tournament Hills** (guard-gated)

### 3. The Pueblo
Alta Mira, Bonita Canyon, Buena Vista, La Mancha, La Posada, Mirada, Monterrey, Monterrey Este, The Plateau, Red Hills, Santa Fe, Sedona, Sonoma, Tamarisk, Taos Estates, Valencia, Vista Del Oro

### 4. The Trails
Cantabria, Canyon Oaks, Country Rose Estates, Deerfield, Desert Trails, Highline, Highpointe, Indian Hills, La Paz, Mountain Crest, Mountain Trails, Pacific Homes, Sequoia, Sierra Ridge, Summer Trail, Sunset Crest, Willow Bend

### 5. The Canyons
Aventura, Bellacere, Canyon Crest, **Canyon Fairways** (guard-gated), Canyon Ridge, Canyon Terrace, Cierra, Dakota, Eagle Rock, **Mira Villa** (guard-gated), **The Palisades** (guard-gated), Peachy Canyon, Star Canyon, Talon Pointe

### 6. The Arbors
Amber Hills, Amber Ridge, Arbor Grove, Arbor View, Aspen Meadows, Belvedere, Breamoor Heights, Brookfield, Chardonnay, Cottonwood Terrace, Cypress Pointe, Desert Bloom, Fallbrook, Glenmere, Marbella, Napa Hills, Oak Knoll, Oak Hills, Quail Ridge, Rosedale, Rosewood, Royal Woods, Scarlett Canyon, Silver Pines, Sonoma Hills, Sycamore Heights, Vineyards, Westbrook, Woodlands

### 7. The Crossing
Aspen Glen, Crimson Ridge, Discovery Hills, Discovery Pointe, Highland Hills, Pacific Crest, Red Bluffs, San Marino, Sunset Mesa, Sycamore Ridge, Vintage, West Hills, Westridge

### 8. Sun City Summerlin (55+, Separate Association)
~8,000 single-family homes on 2,400 acres. Developer: Del Webb. 3 golf courses (Palm Valley, Highland Falls, Eagle Crest). 4 recreation centers, pools, spas, tennis, bocce, racquetball, 312-seat theater.

### 3. The Ridges (Guard-Gated)
Arrowhead, Azure, Boulder Ridge, Fairway Hills, Fairway Pointe, Falcon Ridge, Promontory, Redhawk, Rimrock, The Pointe, Silver Ridge, Sterling Ridge, Topaz Ridge

### 4. The Mesa
Adelina, Amado, Cielo, Ladera, Mesa Ridge, Monte Bello, Vista Dulce

### 5. The Cliffs
Granite Heights, Ironwood, Jade Ridge, Nova Ridge, Oluna, Onyx Point, **Regency at Summerlin** (55+ gated, Toll Brothers), Skystone, Terra Luna, Vistara

### 6. Ridgebrook
Echo Ridge, Granite Peaks, Southstar, Sunset Ridge

### 7. The Peaks / Ascension (Guard-Gated)
**Ascension** (Toll Brothers and Pulte Homes, 561 homes planned on 261 acres)

### 8. South Square
The Trilogy

### 9. Summerlin Centre (1,004 Acres, Mixed-Use)
Affinity, Allerton Park, Asbury Park, C2 Lofts, Enclave, Fairfield, Gresham Park, Madison Place, Magnolia, Parkside, Pinecrest, Pinedale, Springfield, Thrive, Traccia, Villa Trieste, Westwood, West Park, West Park Villas

### 10. Siena (Guard-Gated 55+, Separate Association)
2,001 single-family and attached homes. Developer: Sunrise Colony Company (built 2000-2006). Only guard-gated 55+ community in Summerlin. Siena Golf Club (Lee Schmidt/Brian Curley design). 18 floor plans. Italian-themed buildings, lake with waterfall.

### 11. Red Rock Country Club (Guard-Gated, Separate Association)
Two Arnold Palmer Signature golf courses (Mountain and Arroyo). Resort-style amenities.

### 12. The Summit Club (Ultra-Luxury, Separate Association)
555 acres. Tom Fazio designed members-only golf course. $90 million, 110,000 sq ft clubhouse. Homes $10M-$20M+.
