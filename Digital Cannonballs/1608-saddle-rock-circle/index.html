05 / The plan

A 12-step relaunch plan written for 1608 Saddle Rock Cir.

Act 01

AUDIT AND REPOSITIONING

Before we relaunch, we find the friction point and fix it. Everything else is theater.
01
Read the original listing the way a buyer did.
I walk through the prior MLS entry, photos, and remarks the same way a buyer in 89117 would on a Saturday morning. The goal is to find the moment a buyer scrolled past, then list those moments out so we can address each one.
02
Set the relaunch price against same-community comps.
We anchor the new price to the Ascot Park median of $545,000 and the closest match at $585,000 on Trail Rider. Your home has 2,235 square feet and five bedrooms, which earns a defensible position inside that band rather than above it.
03
Walk the home in person and build a punch list.
I visit, take notes, and put together a short list of low-cost prep items that move the needle for buyers in this price range. Nothing on the list is meant to be a renovation; it is the difference between a yes and a maybe.
Act 02

PRESENTATION AND STORYTELLING

Rebuild the visual and narrative identity of the home.
04
New photography with a clear shot list.
We schedule a fresh photo shoot built around the rooms that drive buyer decisions in a five-bedroom home. The shot list is written before the photographer arrives so nothing important gets missed.
05
Drone and community context shots.
We capture aerial views of the gated entry, the streetscape, and the proximity to Peccole Ranch amenities. Buyers in this price tier are buying the location as much as the home.
06
Rewrite the listing remarks for the right buyer.
The new remarks speak directly to a Las Vegas family looking for space inside a gated community. Plain language, real details, no filler.
Act 03

TARGETED BUYER OUTREACH

Activate direct channels before and after the MLS goes live.
07
Coming soon window with a buyer list.
For seven to 10 days before MLS, we run coming-soon ads and build a list of interested buyers and agents. The point is to launch with showing requests already on the calendar.
08
Direct outreach to top 89117 buyer agents.
I contact the buyer agents who closed homes in your price range and zip code in the last quarter. They often have one client still searching who fits your home.
09
Paid digital ads with geo and intent targeting.
We run Facebook, Instagram, and Google ads targeted to buyers searching west Las Vegas gated communities and homes between $500,000 and $650,000. Spend is paced over the first three weeks where buyer attention is highest.
Act 04

PERFORMANCE TRACKING AND ITERATION

Measure, interpret, and adjust in real time.
10
Weekly performance report.
Every Monday you receive a one-page report covering showings, online saves, ad performance, and agent feedback. No guessing, no spin, just the numbers.
11
Two-week pricing checkpoint.
At day 14 we look at the data together and decide whether to hold, adjust presentation, or revisit price. The decision is based on what the market told us, not on a calendar.
12
Offer review with a clear comparison.
When offers come in, I lay them out side by side with net proceeds, terms, and risk factors so the choice is obvious. You make the call with the full picture in front of you.
