### 8. Housing-stats accuracy challenge

You asked where I got the market stats. I admitted that the median sold price ($480k) and inventory (7,050) were from the April 2026 LVR market report, but the days-on-market (56) and list-to-sale ratio (97%) were carried over from your March slide without re-verifying. You asked me to source those properly.

I pulled verified April 2026 numbers from multiple sources and updated the doc:
- Median sold price: $480k, down 1% YoY (LVR April 2026 report)
- Avg days on market: 72 to 86 days, up from ~61 days in 2025 (NLS Homes 2026 stats, multiple sources)
- List-to-sale ratio: ~96.97%, rounds to 97% (verified, NLS Homes 2026 stats)
- Inventory: 7,050 active homes, 3.35 months supply (LVR April 2026 report)
- Price cuts: 23.3% of listings had price reductions (NLS Homes 2026 stats)
- 71.5% of single-family homes sold within 60 days in March (LVR April 2026 report)
