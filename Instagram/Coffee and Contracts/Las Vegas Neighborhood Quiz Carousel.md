## Notes for posting

- Replace cover photo if you want something more Vegas-specific than palms (Red Rock, Lake Las Vegas marina, or Fremont neon all work). Palm trees are accurate to Vegas, so original photo is fine to keep.
- Slide 10 needs Ryan's headshot swapped in. The C&C template ships with a placeholder photo to replace.
- Delete any yellow sticky notes / instructional comments inside the Canva file before exporting.
- Slide 4 option C: changed "downtown and the interstate" to "the Strip and the freeway" since that reads more natural for Vegas locals. Revert if you'd rather match the original.
- Result A list mixes Henderson (Lake Las Vegas, Anthem CC, Seven Hills, Sun City Anthem) with Boulder City. All five fit the slow / golf cart / water / charm vibe. Lake Las Vegas and Boulder City are the only true "waterfront" options in the valley, so they anchor the list.
- Result B intentionally leans urban core. None of these are master-planned, which is the point. If a buyer picks mostly B's, they want walkability and character over HOA polish.
- Result D pulls from both sides of the valley (Skye Canyon NW, Cadence/Inspirada SE, Summerlin West). Good geographic spread for the "new construction" personality.
- Caption is ~740 characters, well under the 2,200 cap.
- Engagement mechanic: comments A/B/C/D trigger DM follow-ups. Set up a saved reply in Instagram for each letter so responses go out fast.
