### Story 6: Las Vegas Home Prices and Sales Decline in April 2026
- **Category:** Government and Development
- **County/Area:** Las Vegas Valley
- **Summary:** Las Vegas Realtors reported that the median price of existing single-family homes sold in April 2026 was $473,875, down 1.3% from a year earlier and below the all-time high of $488,995 set in November 2025.
