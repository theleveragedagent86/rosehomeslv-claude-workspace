### Story 1: Tony Roma's Closes at Fremont Casino After 36 Years
- **Category:** Restaurant and Business
- **County/Area:** Downtown Las Vegas (Fremont Street)
- **Summary:** Tony Roma's at the Fremont Casino closed on May 9, 2026, ending a 36-year run at the downtown property. The location opened September 27, 1990, and was reportedly the most profitable Tony Roma's in the world. After the closure, only six Tony Roma's locations remain in the entire United States. Boyd Gaming is replacing it with a new in-house dining concept expected to open in September 2026.
- **Source:** Vital Vegas / Casino.org
- **URL:** https://www.casino.org/vitalvegas/tony-romas-to-close-at-fremont-casino-last-nevada-location/
- **Date:** March 17, 2026 (original report); closure occurred May 9, 2026
- **Why It Matters:** A beloved 36-year downtown fixture is gone, and it was the last Tony Roma's in all of Nevada.
- **Flag:** NOSTALGIA
