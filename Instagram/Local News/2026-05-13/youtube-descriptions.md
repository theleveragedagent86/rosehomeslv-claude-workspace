## Story 1: Mortgage Applications Rise as Homebuyers Adapt to Higher, Volatile Rates

Buyers Are Shrugging Off Higher Rates and Getting Back in the Market
Mortgage applications rose 1.7% last week with purchase apps up 4%, even as the 30-year fixed rate sits nearly 40 basis points above late February levels. Las Vegas and Clark County buyers now have solid inventory and more negotiating power, but the spring market is moving slower than usual.
