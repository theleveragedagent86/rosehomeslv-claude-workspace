# Ad Copy Templates

Templates and examples for writing Meta/Instagram ad copy. Used by the Content Creator agent.

---

## Copy Structure (Every Ad Variation)

| Field | Max Length | Notes |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| **Primary Text** | 125 chars visible (up to 1,000 total) | Front-load the hook. Only 125 chars show before "See More" |
| **Headline** | 40 characters | Punchy, specific, no fluff |
| **Description** | 30 characters | Supporting context only |
| **CTA** | Matches button type | Learn More, Send Message, Sign Up, etc. |

---

## Writing Rules (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

1. **NO em-dashes.** Use commas, periods, or "and" instead.
2. **Warm, local, knowledgeable.** Sound like a trusted neighbor, not a corporation.
3. **Short sentences.** Conversational. Like you're texting a friend who asked about real estate.
4. **Front-load the hook.** The first 125 characters must grab attention AND make sense standalone.
5. **Each variation must feel distinct.** Different angle, different hook, different emotion. Not just word-swapped.
6. **Specific over generic.** "3-car garage with EV charging" beats "spacious garage."
7. **No jargon.** No MLS numbers, no agent-speak, no "boasting about stats."
8. **Ryan Rose positioning.** Naturally woven in as local expert when appropriate. Never forced.

## Templates by Campaign Type

### Listing Ad — Just Listed

**CRITICAL: NEVER include the property address in any ad copy.** Use neighborhood name, features, and price only. The goal is to drive clicks to the landing page.

**Variation A — Feature Hook**
```
Primary: Just listed in [NEIGHBORHOOD]. [STANDOUT FEATURE] you won't find anywhere else at this price. [BEDS]/[BATHS], [SQFT] sq ft.
Headline: New in [NEIGHBORHOOD] | $[PRICE]
Description: See photos and details
CTA: Learn More
```

**Variation B — Lifestyle Hook**
```
Primary: Imagine coming home to [LIFESTYLE BENEFIT] every day. This [NEIGHBORHOOD] home was built for [TARGET BUYER TYPE].
Headline: New in [NEIGHBORHOOD]
Description: Tour this home today
CTA: Learn More
```

**Variation C — Urgency Hook**


**Variation D — Open House Hook**

### Listing Ad — Listing Lead Form

**CRITICAL: NEVER include the property address in any ad copy.** Use neighborhood name, features, and price range. The listing is the HOOK, but the CTA drives to a lead form to capture buyer contact info for similar homes.

**NOTE:** These ads use the Leads objective with Meta Instant Form. The CTA drives to a lead form, NOT a website. Primary text uses the listing to grab attention, then pivots to a broader value proposition for buyers looking in the area.

**Variation A — "Similar Homes" Hook**
```
Primary: Love this [NEIGHBORHOOD] home? There are more like it. [BEDS] beds, [KEY FEATURE], starting in the $[PRICE RANGE]. I'll send you matches as they come up.
Headline: Get [NEIGHBORHOOD] Listings
Description: Homes sent to your inbox
CTA: Sign Up
```

**Variation B — "Alerts" Hook**
```
Primary: This [BEDS]-bed in [NEIGHBORHOOD] is getting a lot of attention. Want to be first to know when similar homes hit the market? I'll set you up with instant alerts.
Headline: Get Home Alerts
Description: New listings, sent first
```

```
Primary: [NEIGHBORHOOD] keeps drawing buyers for a reason. [LIFESTYLE BENEFIT], great schools, and homes like this one. Curious what's available? I'll send you options.
Headline: Homes in [NEIGHBORHOOD]
```

**Variation D — "Price Point" Hook**

## Tone Examples (Do vs. Don't)

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| "Just listed in Champion Village. 3-car garage, chef's kitchen, and a backyard made for hosting." | "Stunning, breathtaking, one-of-a-kind masterpiece awaits you in this prestigious community!" |
| "Curious what your home is worth? I'll send you a real number, not an algorithm." | "As your trusted real estate advisor, I leverage cutting-edge market analytics to deliver exceptional results." |
| "Homes here are selling fast this spring." | "Act now before it's too late! Don't miss this once-in-a-lifetime opportunity!" |
| "I help families find the right home in Vegas." | "I am a top-producing, award-winning real estate professional dedicated to exceeding your expectations." |
