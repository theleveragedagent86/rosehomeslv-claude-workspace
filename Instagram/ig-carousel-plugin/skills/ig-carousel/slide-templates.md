# Slide Output Templates

These are the exact output formats for each slide type. The Content Assembler uses these as templates and fills in the researched data.

## Template: Housing Market Slide

```markdown
## SLIDE [N] — The Scoop on the Housing Market

**Header (keep as-is):**

` ` `
The scoop on the
housing market
` ` `

**Three stat boxes (replace numbers):**

` ` `
$[PRICE]k              [DOM]               [RATIO]%
MEDIAN                 AVG DAYS            LIST-TO-SALE
SOLD PRICE             ON MARKET           PRICE RATIO

[TREND] X% YOY        [TREND] FROM ~XX    [CONTEXT]
                       DAYS LAST YEAR
` ` `

**Bottom "what's that mean for you?" block (replace existing):**

` ` `
...so what's that mean for you?

If you're thinking about buying... [2-3 sentences using actual inventory, list-to-sale ratio, and price cut stats. Factual, not salesy.]

If you're thinking about selling... [2-3 sentences using actual DOM and pricing data. Factual, actionable.]
` ` `

**All numbers verified from these sources:**
- [Stat]: [source name and URL]
- [Stat]: [source name and URL]
[continue for all stats]

> Pull your own MLS snapshot the day you post if you want zip-level or property-type-specific accuracy.
```
