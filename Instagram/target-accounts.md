## Session Coverage Rules

- **Visit every account on this list every session.** Do not pre-select a subset. Open each profile, scan the recent grid, and engage on whatever fresh content qualifies. If a post has already been engaged with (rosehomeslv comment in thread), skip it and move to the next post on the same profile, or scroll deeper to find a fresh one.
- **Order:** Work top to bottom through Active Accounts, then News / Media Accounts.
- **Per-account target:** 1 to 3 fresh engagements per profile when fresh, qualifying content exists. Some sessions will yield zero on a given account if everything recent is already engaged or fails guardrails, that is fine.
- **Skip any post** that touches political, LGBTQ+, or controversial topics per the ig-engage guardrails.
- **Stop immediately** if Instagram throws a "Try Again Later" or any rate limit warning. Report and end the session.
