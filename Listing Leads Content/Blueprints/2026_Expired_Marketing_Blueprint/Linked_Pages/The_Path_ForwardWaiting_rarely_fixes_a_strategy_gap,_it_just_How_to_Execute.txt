Step 2
Submit to your print shop
Finalize Your Design: Complete any edits or adjustments in Canva.
Download as PDF:
Click "Share" in the top right, then select "Download".
Choose PDF Print from the file type options.
Ensure the "Flatten PDF" option is selected for compatibility.
Save Your File: Download the file to your computer.
Send to Print Shop: Submit the flattened PDF file to your print shop for printing.
