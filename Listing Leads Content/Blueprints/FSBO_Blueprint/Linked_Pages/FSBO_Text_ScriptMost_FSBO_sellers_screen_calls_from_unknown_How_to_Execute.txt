Step 2
Sending Your Message via Mass SMS or DM Tool
Log In to Your Mass Messaging Tool: Open your mass SMS or DM platform and log in to your account. (or simply use your phone)
Select or Upload Recipients: Choose your audience (contacts/followers) or selecting recipients within the tool.
Compose the Message: Paste the copied message content into the message field.
Review and Send: Double-check the message for accuracy, then hit "Send" to distribute to all selected recipients.
