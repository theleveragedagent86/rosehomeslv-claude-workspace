Silver Tsunami Prospecting Script

Created for US & Canada
Introduction

For Silver Tsunami sellers, cost of living isn’t just a number — it’s a trigger. A reason to reconsider. A lens through which every next move gets evaluated.

This script uses real-life social proof to start that conversation in a way that feels natural and non-threatening. You’re not asking them to sell. You’re sharing what’s possible — and planting the idea that a smarter, more affordable next chapter might already be within reach. Sometimes, all it takes is the right example to shift someone’s perspective.

Recommended Audience

For best results with this campaign, target this audience.

Silver Sellers

How to Execute
Step 1
Copy Scripts & Messages
Text Message 1
Copy

Hi Tom,I know you’re not expecting this but I thought you’d find it interesting.I just helped one of my clients buy and sell their house, and their housing costs dropped by $2,300/month (42%).Out of curiosity, if you were to make a move, what would you want your all-in monthly costs to stay under?

Step 2
Sending Your Message via Mass SMS or DM Tool
Log In to Your Mass Messaging Tool: Open your mass SMS or DM platform and log in to your account. (or simply use your phone)
Select or Upload Recipients: Choose your audience (contacts/followers) or selecting recipients within the tool.
Compose the Message: Paste the copied message content into the message field.
Review and Send: Double-check the message for accuracy, then hit "Send" to distribute to all selected recipients.
