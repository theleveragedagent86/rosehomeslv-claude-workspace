Introduction

Farming is a long game. But some pieces do double duty.

This postcard builds your presence in the neighborhood and plants a seed before sellers ever think about calling you. The front delivers a data point they'll remember. The back delivers a truth their current agent probably never said out loud — that overpricing doesn't just slow a sale, it poisons one.

That's not a controversial opinion. It's math. And when it arrives in their mailbox with your name on it, you become the agent who tells the truth before you're even hired.

Send this as a standalone drop or layer it into your regular farm rotation. Either way, you're not just staying top of mind — you're shifting how they think about pricing before the conversation starts. That's the kind of credibility that wins listings.

Use this Claude prompt to recreate this with your market's numbers OR simply use the Canva template below to customize and deploy.

How to Execute
Step 1
Edit Canva Template

Click the button below to open the template in Canva.

Open Template in Canva
Step 2
Submit To Your Print Shop

1. Finalize Your Design: Complete any edits or adjustments in Canva.


2. Download as PDF:

Click "Share" in the top right, then select "Download".

Choose PDF Print from the file type options.

Ensure the "Flatten PDF" option is selected for compatibility.


3. Save Your File: Download the file to your computer.


4. Send to Print Shop: Submit the flattened PDF file to your print shop for printing.
