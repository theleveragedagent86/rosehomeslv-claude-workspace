Introduction
Seven days sounds aggressive, and it should make you pause.


This isn't a promise you can make to every seller in every situation. But when three specific conditions align - pricing within 3% of market value, flawless listing photos, and timing before the late April inventory surge - the speed becomes predictable, not lucky. The key is knowing when those conditions actually exist in your market.


This postcard works because it's conditional. You're not claiming every home can sell in a week. You're saying that when the strategy, preparation, and timing are right, fast sales aren't just possible - they're likely.
This positions you as the agent who understands leverage, not the one who overpromises.


The "7-day sale strategy report" gives you an out and an in. If their home qualifies, you've opened a high-urgency conversation. If it doesn't, you're still the expert who assessed their situation honestly. Either way, you're not the agent making empty speed claims - you're the one who knows exactly what it takes.
