How to Execute
Step 1
Customize This Template

Click the button below to open the template in Canva.

Open Template in Canva
Step 2
Submit To Your Print Shop
1. Finalize Your Design: Complete any edits or adjustments in Canva.


2. Download as PDF:
Click "Share" in the top right, then select "Download".
Choose PDF Print from the file type options.
Ensure the "Flatten PDF" option is selected for compatibility.


3. Save Your File: Download the file to your computer.


4. Send to Print Shop: Submit the flattened PDF file to your print shop for printing.
