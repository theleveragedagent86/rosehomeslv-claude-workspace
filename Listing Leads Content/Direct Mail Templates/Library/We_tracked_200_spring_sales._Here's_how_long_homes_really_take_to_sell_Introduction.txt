Introduction
Speed isn't luck. It's strategy.


Everyone thinks homes sell fast in spring because of seasonality. But when you track 200 actual sales and measure what really moves the needle, the pattern becomes clear: pricing strategy, photo quality, and timing windows matter more than the calendar date.


This postcard cuts through the noise with concrete data. Homes priced right from the start sold faster. Overpriced listings took longer and sold for less. Professional photography accelerated sales. And listings that hit the market between March 15 and April 10 got more showings than homes listed later in the season.


This positions you as the agent who studies the market, not the one who repeats tired advice.


It's perfect for homeowners who are considering a spring sale but haven't committed yet. You're not pushing them to list - you're offering insight backed by real market behavior. That shifts the conversation from "should I sell?" to "when and how should I sell?" And that's exactly where you want them.
