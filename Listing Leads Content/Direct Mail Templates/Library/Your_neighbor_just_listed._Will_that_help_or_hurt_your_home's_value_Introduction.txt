Introduction
A new listing in the neighborhood changes everything.
When a neighbor lists, homeowners start doing math. They wonder if their home is worth more - or less - than what just hit the market. They compare square footage, finishes, and lot size. They scroll Zillow. They second-guess their timing.


This postcard intercepts that moment of curiosity before it turns into anxiety or misinformation. It reframes a new listing not as competition, but as a catalyst for smarter decision-making. If the comp is priced right and shows well, it raises values. If it's overpriced or sits too long, it drags perception down for the whole block.


This positions you as the agent who understands market psychology, not just comps.


You're not chasing listings. You're offering clarity and control. The call-to-action is low-pressure, a personalized market position report, but it opens the door to deeper strategy conversations with homeowners who are now paying attention in ways they weren't last week.
