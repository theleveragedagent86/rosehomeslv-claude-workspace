Introduction

Searches for "Can't sell my home" just hit an all-time high. (S/O to BAM for sharing this on Instagram.)

Here's why that matters to you: if people are Googling it, your homeowners are thinking it. And the best way to overcome an objection is to address it before it ever comes up.

This email does exactly that. Instead of waiting for a seller to say "I'm worried my home won't sell," you're getting ahead of it - sharing what's actually causing homes to sit, and positioning yourself as the agent who sees problems before they start.

The tone is calm, not salesy. You're just "sharing what you're seeing right now." That's the kind of insight that builds trust long before someone's ready to list.
