Last fall, 45,000 sellers threw up their hands.

“If we can’t get the price we want, we’re taking our home off the market.”

Now they’re back.

I just read on CNBC that sellers are re-listing properties at the fastest pace in a decade.

What kind of spring season should they expect?

Here’s what we know so far:

Rates are significantly lower than they were a year ago, hovering around ~6%
The right pricing strategy is critical, as buyers cautious about overspending
Last week we saw a 6.1% spike in people trying to buy a home (Trading Economics)

We’ll have a better idea in the coming weeks of what the spring will look like.

I’ll be tracking the data closely.

P.S. Even if you’re in the early stages of thinking about a move, we should talk. It might seem premature but my clients are always less stressed & better prepared the sooner we get the conversation started. 🙂
