Introduction

Homeowners don't know what they don't know.

Most people check Zillow once, decide they have a rough sense of their equity, and stop there. And for a long time, that felt good enough. But the gap between an algorithm's estimate and what a buyer will actually pay has been real - and in some cases, it's been significant.

This email doesn't explain why equity matters. It doesn't pitch a listing conversation. It just surfaces a question the homeowner probably hasn't thought to ask, and offers something specific before asking for anything in return.

That's the posture that opens doors.
