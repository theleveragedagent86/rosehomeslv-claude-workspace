# Listing Leads Weekly Playbook

Reverse-engineered from 5 weeks of the Listing Attraction Plan (Feb 16 - Mar 16, 2026).
This is the repeatable Mon-Fri structure you can follow every week.

---

## Weekly Overview

| Day | Category | Series | What You Do |
|-----|----------|--------|-------------|
| **Monday** | Email + Social | Deal of the Week (DOTW) | Send email blast + post IG story featuring a hot listing |
| **Tuesday** | Email | Email of the Week (EOTW) | Send value-driven email to entire database |
| **Wednesday** | Phone/Text | Text of the Week (TOTW) | Send text message to SOI (sphere of influence) |
| **Thursday** | Social + Direct Mail | IG Post/Poll/Carousel + Mailer | Post to Instagram feed + send postcard/letter |
| **Friday** | Social (Video) | Video of the Week (VOTW) | Post YouTube/Reel with local area content |

---

## MONDAY — Deal of the Week

**100% consistent across all 5 weeks. Always 2 campaigns.**

### Campaign 1: DOTW Email Blast
**Audience:** Entire Database
**Channel:** Email blast app (Mailchimp, Kit, etc.)

**Template structure:**
- **Subject line:** Teaser about the listing. Examples:
  - `Deal of the week: {{sqft}}+ sq ft under {{price_bracket}} in {{neighborhood}}`
  - `POV: You're house hunting in {{neighborhood}} and...`
  - `How is this still on the market?`
  - `Already turning heads...`
  - `2,780 people have looked at this home (here's why)`
- **Preview text:** Hook/curiosity line (e.g., "{{save_count}} Zillow saves before you saw this.")
- **Body:** 3-5 sentences highlighting the listing's appeal, key features as bullet points, CTA: "Want the link sent over?"

**How to pick the listing each week:**
1. Filter MLS for new listings (within last 7 days)
2. Focus on your target price point
3. Stay in your ideal neighborhood or farm area
4. Pick the one with highest saves/views on Zillow — that's your DOTW

### Campaign 2: DOTW Social Shareable
**Channel:** Instagram Story
**Format:** Canva template → IG Story with Poll sticker

**Template structure:**
- Customize Canva template with listing details
- Post as Instagram Story
- Add Poll sticker with options like "Yesss!!!" / "Yes!"
- Follow up with poll responders via DM

**Variations seen:**
- AirDrop style (airdrop notification mockup)
- Google Maps style (map pin design)
- Sketch style (hand-drawn look)
- AI Book style (AI-generated property book)

## TUESDAY — Email of the Week (EOTW)

**100% consistent — always an email campaign. Sometimes 2 variants of the same email.**

**Audience:** Entire Database
**Channel:** Email blast

**Template structure:**
- **Subject:** Short, curiosity-driven (e.g., "Most homeowners forget to ask this", "Haven't seen this in 10 years")
- **Preview text:** Parenthetical follow-up (e.g., "(I didn't want you to be one of them)")
- **Body:** Storytelling format, 3-5 paragraphs, focuses on a homeowner pain point or market insight, ends with a soft CTA asking if they'd like something prepared for them

**Recurring themes:**
- Equity updates / home value curiosity
- Market timing and seller psychology
- Spring selling prep
- "Your timeline" (when to list)
- Hidden costs homeowners don't think about

**Key pattern:** The email is NOT salesy. It's positioned as genuine insight that makes you look knowledgeable. The CTA is always permission-based: "Would it be helpful if I...?"

## WEDNESDAY — Text of the Week (TOTW)

**100% consistent across all 5 weeks.**

**Audience:** SOI (Sphere of Influence) — your warm contacts, not cold database
**Channel:** Text message (personal, not mass blast)

**Template structure:**
- 3-4 lines max
- First name personalization
- Reference something specific and local
- End with a question (open or curiosity-driven)

**Example templates:**
```
Hi [First Name], it's [Your Name].
Just ran the equity on a home about 2 minutes from yours.
They gained about $14k in the last 12 months.
[Area]'s been quietly moving. Some homes more than others.
Have you checked your numbers recently?
```

```
Hi [First Name], it's [Your Name].
Real quick — a home in [neighborhood] just closed $22k
over asking. That's the third one this month.
If you're curious what that means for your place,
I can pull it up. Takes me about 5 min.
```

**Recurring themes:**
- Equity updates with specific dollar amounts
- Neighborhood activity / comps
- "Open loop" curiosity hooks
- ZMA (Zestimate Market Analysis) offers
- Net profit / timeline questions

## THURSDAY — Instagram Content + Direct Mail

**Instagram is consistent (100%). Direct Mail appears ~40% of weeks.**

### Campaign 1: Instagram Post (always present)
**Channel:** Instagram Feed
**Format:** Varies weekly between:
- **IG Poll** (60% of weeks) — Story with poll sticker on a market topic
- **IG Post** (20%) — Feed post with educational carousel or graphic
- **IG Carousel** (20%) — Multi-slide educational content

**Template structure for IG Poll:**
- Canva template → customize → download as PNG
- Post as IG Story with Poll sticker
- Topic ties into the week's theme
- Follow up with poll responders

**Template structure for IG Post/Carousel:**
- Canva template with market data or educational content
- Caption is a mini-essay (150-300 words) with insights
- Uses the "Prompt" copy block — a ready-to-post caption

**Recurring themes:**
- Buyer behavior (hot zones, buyer types)
- Pricing psychology (overpricing consequences)
- Market data in visual format
- Interactive engagement (polls, questions)

### Campaign 2: Direct Mail (appears ~2 out of 5 weeks)
**Channel:** Print → Mail
**Format:** Postcard or letter via Canva template

**Template structure:**
1. Edit Canva template with your info
2. Download as flattened PDF
3. Submit to print shop
4. Mail to farm area

**Types seen:**
- Postcards with market data (e.g., "Overpriced Homes Sell For Less")
- Letters to specific audiences (e.g., "Magic Buyer Letter" to potential sellers)

## FRIDAY — Video of the Week (VOTW)

**100% consistent across all 5 weeks. Always includes a PDF script/showflow.**

**Channel:** YouTube (primary), can also post to Instagram Reels/TikTok
**Format:** 5-15 minute local area video

**Template structure:**
- **Showflow PDF:** Full video script with flow, hooks, and talking points
- **Hook audio:** Pre-recorded hook to model your intro after
- **Canva thumbnail template:** Customize with your area name
- **Video title:** Template with [AREA] placeholder
- **Video description:** Full SEO-optimized description with timestamps, community comparisons, keywords
- **Hashtags:** Ready-to-paste set

**Title patterns:**
- "If I Were Moving To [AREA] in 2026, I'd Move Here"
- "The Best [AREA] Suburbs for Families in 2026 (Top 10 Ranked)"
- "AVOID Moving to The Wrong Area in [AREA] in 2026"
- "Don't Live in This [AREA] City Unless You Can Handle These 5 Facts"
- "Where [Area]'s Ultra Wealthy Choose to Live"

**Description template structure:**
```
Everyone knows [WELL-KNOWN CITY 1], [WELL-KNOWN CITY 2], and [WELL-KNOWN CITY 3].
But if you're relocating to [STATE/REGION], the communities that belong on your
short list might surprise you.

📍 COMMUNITIES COVERED
[TIMESTAMP] — [COMMUNITY 1]
[TIMESTAMP] — [COMMUNITY 2]
...

🏡 QUICK COMPARISON
[COMMUNITY A] — Best for [LIFESTYLE DRIVER] | $[X] – $[Y]
[COMMUNITY B] — Best for [LIFESTYLE DRIVER] | $[X] – $[Y]
...

🔑 KEY TAKEAWAYS
✅ [TAKEAWAY 1]
✅ [TAKEAWAY 2]
...
```

**Key insight:** These videos are designed to rank for relocation searches. They position you as THE local expert for anyone Googling "moving to [your area]."

## Weekly Theme Integration

A strong pattern across weeks: **all 5 days often share a common theme.** For example:

- **Timeline Week (Feb 23):** DOTW email → EOTW about "your timeline" → TOTW text about timeline → IG Poll about timeline → VOTW about avoiding wrong areas
- **Equity Week (Mar 16):** DOTW listing → EOTW about home value → TOTW equity text → IG post about pricing → VOTW about neighborhoods
- **Buyer Focus Week (Mar 9):** DOTW listing → EOTW about market activity → TOTW about ZMA → IG Carousel about "Magic Buyer" → VOTW about family suburbs

**Planning tip:** Pick a theme each week and thread it through all 5 days for cohesive messaging.

## Quick-Reference Weekly Checklist

```
□ MONDAY
  □ Pick Deal of the Week listing (highest saves/views, last 7 days)
  □ Write & send DOTW email blast (Subject + Preview + Body)
  □ Create DOTW Instagram Story (Canva template + Poll sticker)
  □ DM anyone who responds to the poll

□ TUESDAY
  □ Write & send EOTW email (storytelling, value-driven, soft CTA)
  □ Theme: homeowner pain point or market insight

□ WEDNESDAY
  □ Send TOTW text to SOI contacts
  □ Keep it personal, 3-4 lines, end with a question
  □ Follow up on any responses

□ THURSDAY
  □ Create & post IG content (Poll, Post, or Carousel)
  □ If direct mail week: design postcard/letter in Canva, send to print
  □ Follow up on IG engagement

□ FRIDAY
  □ Record VOTW video using showflow/script
  □ Create thumbnail in Canva
  □ Upload to YouTube with optimized title, description, hashtags
  □ Cross-post to IG Reels / TikTok
```
