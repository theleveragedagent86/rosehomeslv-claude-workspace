Introduction

It shows a homeowner what they'd actually walk away with after their mortgage payoff, closing costs, and fees - the number that actually matters to them. It's specific, it's personal, and almost nobody is offering it. This text goes out to anyone who opened yesterday's email. Remember: that subject line was designed to qualify through copy. If someone opened an email about selling this spring, they've already raised their hand, even if just a little. That open is a signal. This text follows that signal with something worth responding to.
