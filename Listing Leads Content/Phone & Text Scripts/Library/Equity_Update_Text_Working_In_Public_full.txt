Introduction
Every CMA you complete is a conversation waiting to happen - most agents just never make the connection. The A.I.R. Method turns that missed opportunity into a system. The Alert is a real result from a real neighbor. The Insight is what that result means for everyone on the street. The Response is one low-stakes question that almost nobody says no to. Here's what makes it powerful: you're not talking about the market in the abstract. You're holding up a mirror. For example, $38,000 in equity in two years lands completely differently when it belongs to someone on their street. They don't have to imagine it. They just have to wonder - what's my number? This is how you generate high-quality seller leads without paying a referral fee. Run a great CMA. Take the most marketable result. Let this script do the rest.
