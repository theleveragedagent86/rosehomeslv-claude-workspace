Introduction
Most prospecting texts don't fail because of the market. And the problem isn't your list. It's that your message doesn't answer the only question that actually matters to a homeowner: why should I care right now? Every text that skips that answer is a dead lead. This campaign is built differently. It leads with proximity. It uses proof. It plants a question the homeowner can't ignore - which category am I in? - and then hands them a reason to find out. You're one text away from your next appointment.
