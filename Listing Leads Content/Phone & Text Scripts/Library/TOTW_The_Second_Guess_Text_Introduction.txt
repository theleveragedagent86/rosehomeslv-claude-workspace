Introduction
This text works because it doesn't sound like it was written by someone who does this for a living. The hesitation built into the opening - I almost talked myself out of sending this - is doing more persuasive work than the words that follow it. That's Chase Hughes' uncertainty principle in action: certainty signals salesperson; uncertainty signals honesty. The moment you admit you weren't sure whether to reach out, the reader's defense attorney flips sides. You're no longer a threat. You're a person. That closing question has generated millions in listing opportunities for agents who trusted it enough to hit send. Send this to everyone who opened yesterday's email.
