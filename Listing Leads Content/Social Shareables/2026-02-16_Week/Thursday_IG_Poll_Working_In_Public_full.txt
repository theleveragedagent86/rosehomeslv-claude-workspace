Introduction

This one takes a little legwork upfront, and that's what makes it work.

Pull CMAs for a few of your recent clients or homeowners in your farm. Find the one where the gap between their online estimate and actual market value is the biggest. That gap is your hook.

Turn it into a two-slide Instagram story. First slide builds curiosity - you just ran a report for a client and the number surprised them. Second slide reveals the gap and drops a poll: "Want one for your place?" Both answer options lead to the same place: a conversation in your DMs.

The reason this converts is because it's real. It's not a hypothetical. You actually ran the numbers, and the difference was big enough to stop someone mid-scroll.

One CMA. Two slides. Inbound leads.
