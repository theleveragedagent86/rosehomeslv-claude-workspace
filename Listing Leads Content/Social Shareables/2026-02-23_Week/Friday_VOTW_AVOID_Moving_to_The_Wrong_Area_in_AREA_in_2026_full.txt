Introduction

This one comes straight from Erik Throm's playbook - and his version has 5,500 views and counting.

The "neighborhood breakdown" video is one of the highest-converting formats on YouTube for real estate agents, and the timing right now is perfect. January through March is when relocation searches spike. People are making 2026 decisions, and they're going to Google and YouTube first, before they ever contact an agent. This video puts you in front of that search.

What makes this template work is the angle. It's not "here are the best neighborhoods." It's "here's who should avoid each one." That framing creates immediate trust - it signals that you're not just selling the area, you're helping someone make the right call for their lifestyle. That's a different kind of agent.

The showflow is ready. The Canva template to create your thumbnail is below. All you need to fill in are your local areas, your pricing, and your face on camera. Customize it once and it works for you indefinitely.
