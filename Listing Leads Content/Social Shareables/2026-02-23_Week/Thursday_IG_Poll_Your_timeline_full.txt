Introduction

Your next listing is probably already following you on Instagram. They're just waiting for a reason to reach out.

The problem isn't that homeowners don't want to sell. It's that they think calling an agent means they're committed to something. So they wait. And while they're waiting, they're watching - your posts, your stories, your content. This poll meets them exactly there.

The visual does the heavy lifting. It shows the gap between when people think they should reach out and when they actually should, and it makes the homeowner feel seen without saying a word to you. Then the poll answers give them three low-stakes ways to self-identify, right there in your stories.

This is how you fill your pipeline before anyone else even knows those clients exist.




How to Execute
Step 1
Customize Canva Template

Click the button below to open the template in Canva.

Open Template in Canva
Step 2

1. Open the Canva template

Swap colors, fonts, logo/photo as needed. Customize the data based on your market.

2. Download your slide

Share → Download → PNG/JPG → Save to your phone.

3. Start a new Story on Instagram

Open Instagram → “+” → Story → Upload your slide.

4 .Add the Poll sticker

Tap the sticker icon (square smiley at the top).

Select Poll, type in your 2–4 answer choices, and place it where the design allows.

5. Post your Story

Tap Your Story to publish.

7. Monitor responses + follow up

Go to your Story → Activity → See Voters/Responses.

DM each person within 24 hours.
