Introduction

This Instagram poll takes real buyer activity from February and turns it into something your audience can't scroll past. You're showing them exactly where buyers are competing right now -specific neighborhoods, specific price points - and then you cut it off. The blur isn't a gimmick. It's a trigger. It turns passive scrollers into active engagers who raise their hand and say, "I want to know more."

And here's what makes this smart: the poll answers are both a yes. "Yes!" or "Curious" - either way, they're opting in. That gives you a reason to follow up in DMs with the full list, start a real conversation, and position yourself as the agent who actually tracks what's happening at the street level.
