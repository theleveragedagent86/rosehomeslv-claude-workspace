Created for US & Canada
Introduction

This Instagram poll takes real buyer activity from February and turns it into something your audience can't scroll past. You're showing them exactly where buyers are competing right now -specific neighborhoods, specific price points - and then you cut it off. The blur isn't a gimmick. It's a trigger. It turns passive scrollers into active engagers who raise their hand and say, "I want to know more."

And here's what makes this smart: the poll answers are both a yes. "Yes!" or "Curious" - either way, they're opting in. That gives you a reason to follow up in DMs with the full list, start a real conversation, and position yourself as the agent who actually tracks what's happening at the street level.

How to Execute
Step 1
Customize Canva Template

Click the button below to open the template in Canva.

Open Template in Canva
Step 2

1. Open the Canva template

Swap colors, fonts, logo/photo as needed. Customize the data based on your market.

2. Download your slide

Share → Download → PNG/JPG → Save to your phone.

3. Start a new Story on Instagram

Open Instagram → “+” → Story → Upload your slide.

4 .Add the Poll sticker

Tap the sticker icon (square smiley at the top).

Select Poll, type in your 2–4 answer choices, and place it where the design allows.

5. Post your Story

Tap Your Story to publish.

7. Monitor responses + follow up

Go to your Story → Activity → See Voters/Responses.

DM each person within 24 hours.
