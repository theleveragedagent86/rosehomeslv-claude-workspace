## RESEARCH REPORT: The Bluffs II at Lake Las Vegas

### Community Overview

- **Official community name:** The Bluffs II at Lake Las Vegas (sometimes "Bluffs 2"; canonical builder spelling is "The Bluffs II")
- **Builder community context:** "The Bluffs II" is the second phase/collection within Century Communities' "The Bluffs" neighborhood inside the Lake Las Vegas master plan. The Bluffs I (sister phase) sits next to it and shares the same sales office/address.
- **Sales office address:** 128 Barkset Way, Henderson, NV 89011
- **Cross streets / access:** Strada Cassano & Strada Veneto. Drive: I-215 East to Lake Mead Pkwy East, left on Lake Las Vegas Pkwy, first right on Strada Cassano, follow to top of hill, paved road down to community
- **City / county:** Henderson, Clark County, Nevada (zip 89011)
- **Master developer:** Lake Las Vegas (Raintree Investment Corp. is the master developer; Lake Las Vegas is a 3,592-acre destination resort masterplan built around a 320-acre man-made lake completed in 1991)
- **Total planned homes (Bluffs II):** NOT FOUND specifically. The Bluffs I sister phase has 90 planned homes per The Thomas Group LV; Bluffs II appears to be a similarly sized adjacent phase but exact count NOT FOUND on builder website.
- **Current phase:** Phase II of "The Bluffs" (Bluffs I is the earlier phase, both currently selling)
- **Year opened / first sales:** Bluffs II actively selling 2024–2026 (floor plan revisions dated 01/05/2024 and 03/25/2024 in image asset URLs)
- **Estimated buildout date:** NOT FOUND (active sales with quick move-in homes still available)
- **Community type:** Gated subdivision within a master-planned resort community (Lake Las Vegas). Single-family, single-story, contemporary collection
- **Context note:** This is a small collection (3 floor plans) inside the much larger Lake Las Vegas masterplan. Buyers get the gated Bluffs neighborhood plus full Lake Las Vegas master amenities (lake, golf, trails, MonteLago Village).

### SID/LID & Special Assessments

- **Is there a SID or LID?** Henderson uses LIDs (Local Improvement Districts). Lake Las Vegas properties may have LID assessments. Del Webb at Lake Las Vegas (nearby comparison) carries an LID of approximately $85/month, varies by parcel.
- **Bluffs II specific LID amount:** NOT FOUND on builder marketing. Recommend buyer pulls APN-specific LID lookup at amgnv.com (Assessment Management Group) or City of Henderson Finance Department.
- **General Lake Las Vegas LID:** Semi-annual bond payments billed with property tax; varies by parcel. Some newer Lake Las Vegas properties have had LID fees eliminated or paid off by builders, verify per home.
- **Annual amount:** Estimate $500–$1,500/year typical for Henderson LID per Clark County market norms; specific to Bluffs II parcel: NOT FOUND.
- **Remaining balance / years left:** NOT FOUND, pull from amgnv.com per APN
- **Prepayment:** 3% prepayment penalty for early payoff per Clark County standard
- **Important note for buyers:** Always pull LID disclosure during contract review; AB 540 allocated $50M statewide to help reduce SID/LID assessments

### Best For Summary

- **The Bluffs II at Lake Las Vegas:** Best for buyers who want a brand new single-story home with canyon or lake views inside a gated, resort-style masterplan, without stretching into the luxury tier. The 1,863 to 2,301 sq ft footprints and 2-3 bedroom layouts work well for downsizers, second-home buyers, and small families who value full Lake Las Vegas amenities (lake, golf, trails, MonteLago Village) and prefer no stairs.

- **The Bluffs I:** Best for buyers who want the same Bluffs neighborhood and Lake Las Vegas lifestyle but at a lower entry price. Smaller 1,663 to 1,831 sq ft floor plans starting in the mid $460s give first-time Lake Las Vegas buyers a way in, while keeping the same single-story format and shared amenities as Bluffs II.

- **Riviera Vista at Lake Las Vegas:** Best for families who need four bedrooms and don't mind stairs. Lennar's "Everything's Included" model bundles smart-home tech, quartz, and stainless appliances into the price, which appeals to buyers who want move-in-ready specs without negotiating design center upgrades.

- **Incanta Lago:** Best for luxury buyers stretching to $900K and up who want larger 2,488 to 3,293+ sq ft homes, lake-access lots on select homesites, and Toll Brothers' high-end finishes. Right fit for executives, second-home buyers from California, and anyone treating Lake Las Vegas as a primary lifestyle purchase rather than a starter or step-up home.

- **Del Webb at Lake Las Vegas:** Best for active adults 55 and over who want a private clubhouse, pickleball, fitness center, and on-site amenity programming on top of the Lake Las Vegas masterplan. Smaller footprints starting at 1,285 sq ft and the 55+ social calendar set it apart from the other Lake Las Vegas options.

### Key Takeaways

- Bluffs II is the only single-story, mid-$500s, all-ages option inside Lake Las Vegas with brand new product, which makes it the natural pick for downsizers and small families who don't want stairs and don't qualify (or don't want) the 55+ Del Webb format.
- Pricing sits in a clear ladder inside the masterplan: Del Webb and Bluffs I anchor the entry tier ($400Ks), Bluffs II and Riviera Vista cluster in the mid-$500s, and Incanta Lago jumps to the $900Ks+. Bluffs II's $236 to $275 base price/sqft is the best value among the all-ages options at this size.
- Combined HOA of about $397/month at Bluffs II is the lowest in the comparison set among full-amenity options. Riviera Vista runs about $420 and Del Webb runs about $438 because of its private 55+ amenity center.
- Bluffs II's main weakness is the small floor plan count (3 plans, all 2-3 bed) and the absence of a four-bedroom option. Buyers needing 4+ bedrooms have to step up to Incanta Lago or step over to a two-story plan at Riviera Vista.
- Every Lake Las Vegas option here shares the same CCSD school zoning (Josh Stevens, B. Mahlon Brown, Basic Academy), so school choice does not differentiate Bluffs II from its neighbors. The differentiators are price tier, single-story vs two-story, and age restriction.
