# Delamar by Pulte Homes: New Construction in Southern Highlands Starting in the Mid $500s Delamar is a new construction community by Pulte Homes located within the Highlands Ranch section of Southern Highlands in southwest Las Vegas. Homes here start in the mid $500,000s and range up to the mid $700,000s. There are four floor plans available, all single-family homes with 3,097 to 4,220 square feet of living space. Pulte Homes is the sole builder. Quick move-in homes are currently available for buyers who want to skip the full build timeline. ## Floor Plans and Pricing Pulte offers four distinct floor plans at Delamar. The Calico plan starts at $574,000 and gives you 3,097 square feet with a practical layout for families. The Egan plan offers the same square footage at $578,000 with a slightly different configuration. The Delano plan bumps up to 3,158 square feet starting at $586,000. For buyers who need more room, the White Pine plan delivers 4,220 square feet starting at $775,000. All homes range from 4 to 5 bedrooms and 3 to 4.5 bathrooms. These are well-sized homes for the price point, especially considering the Southern Highlands location and the included Pulte Homes build quality. ## Current Builder Incentives Pulte is running strong incentives at Delamar right now. Buyers can take advantage of up to $20,000 in closing cost credits, which can significantly reduce your out-of-pocket expenses at the closing table. There is also a 4.99% interest rate available through the builder's preferred lender. On top of that, Pulte is offering design center credits so you can personalize your home with upgraded finishes without paying full price for every selection. These incentives change regularly. It is worth checking the latest offers before you visit, because the builder adjusts them based on inventory and market conditions. ## Community and HOA Details The monthly HOA fee at Delamar is $150. That covers the standard community maintenance and shared amenities within Highlands Ranch. SID and LID (Special Improvement District and Local Improvement District) taxes have not been confirmed for this community, so buyers should ask about those directly during the purchase process. These can add to your monthly costs if they apply. ## Southern Highlands Lifestyle Living at Delamar puts you inside the larger Southern Highlands master plan, which is one of the most established communities in southwest Las Vegas. Residents get access to a 13,000 square foot clubhouse, multiple swimming pools, a spa, and a network of walking and biking trails. Golfers will appreciate the proximity to the Southern Highlands Golf Club, a private course designed by Robert Trent Jones Jr. that consistently ranks among the top courses in Nevada. The community sits about 6 miles from the Las Vegas Strip and has quick access to Interstate 15. Daily errands, dining, and shopping are all close by along St. Rose Parkway and Las Vegas Boulevard South. ## Schools Near Delamar Families moving to Delamar will be zoned for Ries Elementary School (rated 5 out of 10), Tarkanian Middle School (rated 7 out of 10), and Desert Oasis High School (rated 5 out of 10). Tarkanian Middle School is a strong option in the area, and families often consider private school alternatives for elementary and high school depending on their preferences. The Clark County School District allows for school choice applications as well. ## Who Is Delamar Best For? Delamar fits move-up buyers and families who want new construction in an established, amenity-rich master plan without paying the premium you see in guard-gated Southern Highlands neighborhoods. The price range from the mid $500s to the mid $700s hits a sweet spot for buyers who want space, quality, and location. The quick move-in option also makes this community a strong pick for anyone on a tighter timeline. ## Local Insight I have walked these models and driven this area more times than I can count.
