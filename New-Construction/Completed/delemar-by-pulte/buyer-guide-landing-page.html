About

A NEW STANDARD IN SOUTHERN HIGHLANDS

Delamar is one of the most exciting new-construction communities in southwest Las Vegas right now. Built by Pulte Homes, it sits inside the Highlands Ranch area of Southern Highlands. That means you get access to some of the best amenities in the entire valley.

If you want a brand-new home with serious square footage, resort-style living, and a short drive to the Strip, Delamar deserves a close look. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know before you visit the model home or sign a contract.

Whether you are a first-time buyer, moving up, or relocating to Vegas, this guide is designed to help you make a confident and informed decision.

Fees Explained

HOA, SID/LID & FEES

The sub-association HOA is approximately $150 per month. This covers common area maintenance, landscaping, and community upkeep. There may also be a secondary master HOA through Southern Highlands, which could add $50 to $100 per month.

SID and LID charges have not been officially confirmed at Delamar, but they are common in newer Las Vegas communities. If one exists, it will appear on your property tax bill as a separate line item and could add $100 to $200+ per month.

Ask Pulte directly about any SID or LID assessments before you write an offer. Your real estate agent can also pull this information during the due diligence period.

FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Can I use my own real estate agent at Delamar?
Yes, absolutely. Your agent must register with you on your first visit to the model home. The builder pays the commission, so it costs you nothing.
How long does it take to build a new home here?
Most new-construction homes in Las Vegas take 6 to 10 months from contract to close. QMI homes already under construction can be ready much sooner.
Are the base prices all-inclusive?
No. Base prices are before lot premiums, structural options, and design upgrades. QMI homes are typically priced with upgrades already included.
Is there a SID or LID at Delamar?
This has not been officially confirmed. SIDs and LIDs are common in newer Las Vegas communities. Ask the Pulte sales team directly before signing.
What is the HOA, and is there more than one?
The sub-association HOA is approximately $150/month. There may be a secondary master HOA through Southern Highlands. Confirm both amounts with the sales office.
Can I negotiate the price?
Builders rarely lower the base price because it affects comps for the entire community. However, you can often negotiate for additional upgrades, design credits, or closing cost assistance.
What are resale values like in this area?
Resale comps in 89141 currently range from $199 to $210 per sqft. New construction at Delamar falls in the $192 to $208 per sqft range. Southern Highlands has historically held its value well.
Do I have to use Pulte Mortgage?
No. However, the $20K closing cost credit and 4.99% rate are tied to using Pulte Mortgage. Compare offers before deciding.
What warranty comes with a Pulte home?
Pulte typically offers a limited warranty covering workmanship and materials for 1 to 2 years, and structural components for up to 10 years.
Is Delamar a gated community?
Confirm gating and access details directly with the Pulte sales team during your visit. Community access features can vary by section.
