Fees
HOA, SID, AND LID EXPLAINED

HOA (Homeowners Association): Delamar has an HOA of approximately $150 per month. This covers common area maintenance, landscaping, and community upkeep. There may also be a secondary master HOA through Southern Highlands, which could add $50 to $100 per month. Ask the sales team for the exact amounts.

SID (Special Improvement District): A SID is a tax assessment that pays for public infrastructure like roads, sewers, and parks. SID charges are not confirmed at Delamar, but they are common in newer Las Vegas communities. If one exists, it will appear on your property tax bill as a separate line item.

LID (Local Improvement District): A LID works similarly to a SID but covers a smaller geographic area. This has not been confirmed at Delamar but is plausible. Ask Pulte directly about any SID or LID assessments before you write an offer. Your real estate agent can also pull this information during the due diligence period. These charges can add $100 to $200+ per month, so it is important to know upfront.

FAQ
FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
Can I use my own real estate agent at Delamar?
Yes, absolutely. In fact, it is highly recommended. Your agent must register with you on your first visit to the model home. The builder pays the agent's commission, so it costs you nothing.
How long does it take to build a new home here?
Build times vary, but most new-construction homes in Las Vegas take 6 to 10 months from contract to close. QMI homes that are already under construction can be ready much sooner.
Are the base prices all-inclusive?
No. Base prices reflect the starting cost before lot premiums, structural options, and design upgrades. Your final price will depend on the options you choose. QMI homes are typically priced with upgrades already included.
Is there a SID or LID at Delamar?
This has not been officially confirmed. SIDs and LIDs are common in newer Las Vegas communities. Ask the Pulte sales team directly and request a copy of any SID/LID disclosure before signing.
What is the HOA, and is there more than one?
The sub-association HOA is approximately $150/month. There may be a secondary master HOA through Southern Highlands. Confirm both amounts with the sales office.
Can I negotiate the price on a new-construction home?
Builders rarely lower the base price because it affects comps for the entire community. However, you can often negotiate for additional upgrades, design credits, or closing cost assistance. Current incentives at Delamar are already quite strong.
What are the resale values like in this area?
Resale comps in the 89141 zip code currently range from $199 to $210 per sqft. New construction at Delamar falls in the $192 to $208 per sqft range. Southern Highlands has historically held its value well due to the desirable location and amenities.
Do I have to use Pulte Mortgage?
No, you are free to use any lender. However, Pulte's current incentives (including the $20K closing cost credit and 4.99% rate) are tied to using Pulte Mortgage. Compare offers before deciding.
What warranty comes with a Pulte home?
Pulte typically offers a limited warranty that covers workmanship and materials for 1 to 2 years and structural components for up to 10 years. Ask for the full warranty details in writing.
Is Delamar a gated community?
Confirm gating and access details directly with the Pulte sales team during your visit. Community access features can vary by section.
