## Campaign Notes and Optimization Tips

### Launch Checklist
- [ ] Verify Special Ad Category is set to Housing before publishing
- [ ] Confirm Instant Form is connected and test-submit a lead
- [ ] Verify Lofty CRM integration is receiving leads with correct tags
- [ ] Confirm Smart Plan triggers on tag assignment
- [ ] Upload or generate all 4 creative images (1080x1350 recommended for 4:5)
- [ ] Set daily budget and schedule (run continuously, no end date)

### Optimization Cadence

**Week 1-2 (Learning Phase):**
- Do not make changes. Let Meta's algorithm gather data.
- Monitor lead quality in Lofty CRM. Are phone numbers valid? Are people responding?
- Minimum 50 leads needed before making creative decisions.

**Week 3-4:**
- Review cost per lead by ad variation. Pause the worst performer.
- Check qualifying question data. If most leads say "just browsing," tighten the form or test Higher Intent form type.
- Duplicate the top-performing ad and create a slight variation to test.

**Monthly:**
- Refresh creative every 4-6 weeks to avoid ad fatigue.
- Update incentive copy if builder changes offers.
- Review lead-to-appointment rate in CRM. If leads are cheap but not converting, switch form type from More Volume to Higher Intent.

### Creative Best Practices
- Use 4:5 aspect ratio images (1080x1350) for maximum feed real estate.
- Exterior shots and lifestyle images tend to outperform interior shots for top-of-funnel.
- Avoid stock photo aesthetics. AI-generated images should look like real listing photos.
- No text on images. Let the ad copy and headline do the work.
- Test one Reels-format vertical video (9:16) per month if possible.

### Compliance Reminders
- Housing Special Ad Category must be enabled. This limits targeting to location, age (18+), and gender (all). No interest, behavior, or demographic targeting.
- Do not reference specific demographic groups in ad copy.
- Do not use language that could be interpreted as discriminatory.
- All claims (pricing, incentives, rates) must be current and accurate. Update immediately if the builder changes terms.

### Lead Quality Tips
- Speed to lead matters most. First contact within 5 minutes dramatically increases conversion.
- Smart Plan first touch should include something of value: floor plan PDF, virtual tour link, or incentive summary.
- Leads who answer "within 3 months" on the timeline question should be flagged as hot and called immediately.
- Leads who already have an agent may still convert. Do not disqualify automatically.
