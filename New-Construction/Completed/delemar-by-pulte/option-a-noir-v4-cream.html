About

A NEW STANDARD

HIGHLANDS

Delamar is a new-construction community by Pulte Homes in southwest Las Vegas. Located within the Highlands Ranch area of Southern Highlands, it puts you close to resort-style amenities, top-rated golf, and everyday shopping. The community sits just 6 miles from the Las Vegas Strip with easy access to I-15.

Homes range from 2,990 to 4,220 square feet with 4 to 6 bedrooms. Four floor plans include two-story and three-story layouts. Every home is built by Pulte, a national builder known for Life Tested designs that focus on how families actually use their space.

The $150/mo HOA covers common area upkeep and landscaping. Residents enjoy a 13,000 sq ft clubhouse, multiple pools, spa, and the Olympia Sports Park. Model home at 10123 Goswell Park Street, Las Vegas, NV 89141.
