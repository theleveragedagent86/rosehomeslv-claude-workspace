About the Community

A NEW STANDARD IN
SOUTHERN HIGHLANDS

01

Delamar is a new-construction community by Pulte Homes in southwest Las Vegas. Located within the Highlands Ranch area of Southern Highlands, it puts you close to resort-style amenities, top-rated golf, and everyday shopping. Just 6 miles from the Strip.

02

Homes range from 2,990 to 4,220 square feet with 4 to 6 bedrooms and four floor plans, including two-story and three-story layouts. Built by Pulte — known for Life Tested designs that focus on how families actually use their space.

03

The $150/mo HOA covers common area upkeep and landscaping. Residents enjoy a 13,000 sq ft clubhouse, multiple pools, spa, and the Olympia Sports Park. Model home at 10123 Goswell Park Street, Las Vegas, NV 89141.
