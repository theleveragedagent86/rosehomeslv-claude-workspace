Community

A new standard in Southern Highlands

Delamar is a new-construction community by Pulte Homes in southwest Las Vegas. Located within the Highlands Ranch area of Southern Highlands, it puts you close to resort-style amenities, top-rated golf, and everyday shopping. The community sits just 6 miles from the Las Vegas Strip with easy access to I-15.

Homes at Delamar range from 2,990 to 4,220 square feet with 4 to 6 bedrooms. There are four floor plans to choose from, including two-story and three-story layouts. Every home is built by Pulte, a national builder known for Life Tested designs that focus on how families actually use their space.

The HOA is $150 per month and covers common area upkeep and landscaping. Residents enjoy access to Southern Highlands amenities, including a 13,000 square foot clubhouse, multiple pools, a spa, and the Olympia Sports Park. The model home is open for tours at 10123 Goswell Park Street, Las Vegas, NV 89141.
