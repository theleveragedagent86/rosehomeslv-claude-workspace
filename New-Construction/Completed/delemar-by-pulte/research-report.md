## Best For Summary

- **Delamar by Pulte:** Best for move-up families who want the largest floor plans (up to 4,220 sq ft) with rooftop decks and access to Southern Highlands' premium resort amenities, all within 20 minutes of the Strip.
- **Vistamar at Inspirada (Toll Brothers):** Best for buyers who want Toll Brothers quality at a lower price point ($470K-$495K) with 35 miles of trails and 6 parks, though inventory is limited as community nears sellout.
- **Reserves at Aven (KB Home):** Best for budget-conscious families in SW Las Vegas who want a brand-new grand opening community with KB Home's built-to-order customization.
- **Ambridge at Cadence (Woodside):** Best for value buyers who want no SID/LID fees, a 50-acre Central Park, and strong community infrastructure, in exchange for smaller homes and longer Strip commute.
- **Pinewood at Skye Canyon (Pulte):** Best for NW valley buyers drawn to Red Rock proximity and outdoor recreation who also want Pulte's build quality.

## Key Takeaways
- Delamar offers the largest homes in the comparison at ~$192-$208/sq ft
- Southern Highlands amenities (golf, 13K sqft clubhouse, pools) are the strongest lifestyle package
- Closest to the Strip of all compared communities (~6 miles)
- Pulte's $20K closing credit and 4.99% rate are competitive incentives
- Main trade-off is higher entry price vs competition ($574K+ vs $470K-$533K)
