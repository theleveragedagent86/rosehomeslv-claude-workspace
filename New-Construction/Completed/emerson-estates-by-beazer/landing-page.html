About

A GATED 47 HOME
COMMUNITY IN
EAST LAS VEGAS

Emerson Estates is a small gated Beazer Homes community tucked into 89121 on Pacific Street, just off Eastern Avenue and Flamingo Road. Only 47 homes inside the gates, and the builder is now down to the final lots with a 2026 buildout target.

Every home comes with owned rooftop solar at no upcharge. Beazer claims annual energy savings of up to $4,859. All homes are DOE Zero Energy Ready, ENERGY STAR certified, and Indoor airPLUS qualified. Three flexible Beazer Choice Plans give you room to pick the layout that fits your life, including a true single-story option.

The model home is open at 2583 Regency Cove Court. Las Vegas National Golf Course sits right next door. The Las Vegas Strip and Harry Reid International Airport are both 4 to 5 miles west. Call Ryan at 702-747-5921 to walk a model in person.
