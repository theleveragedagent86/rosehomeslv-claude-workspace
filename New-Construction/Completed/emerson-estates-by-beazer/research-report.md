## 10. Builder Reputation, Warranty, and Notes

- **Beazer Homes profile:** Top 10 Las Vegas builder. Known for "Choice Plans" customization (floorplan layout choices included in base price), Mortgage Choice program (no captive mortgage, requires loan-shopping with at least 2 Choice Lenders for full incentive eligibility), and aggressive energy-efficiency program (Energy Series READY with Solar, all-electric homes, ENERGY STAR + Indoor airPLUS + DOE Zero Energy Ready Home).
- **Annual closings (Las Vegas, 2026 reference):** Beazer ranks among the top 10 Las Vegas builders.
- **Customer reviews:** Consumer Affairs reviews are mixed; Yelp Las Vegas page (beazer-homes-las-vegas-5) shows 31 reviews with mixed sentiment. Standard new-construction warranty experience.
- **Warranty:** Standard Beazer 1-year workmanship, 2-year mechanical (plumbing/electrical/HVAC), 10-year structural. Aligns with Nevada NRS 624.602 minimum and the standard 1-2-10 warranty structure.
- **Energy savings claim:** Up to $4,859 annual savings from included owned rooftop solar (per Beazer marketing for Emerson Estates)
- **All-electric design:** Yes. Beazer Las Vegas communities are all-electric to comply with Beazer's national zero-energy-ready strategy.

### Best For Summary

- **Emerson Estates by Beazer Homes:** Best for move-up buyers in the $550K to $620K range who want a brand new gated home with included owned rooftop solar, three flexible Choice Plans including a true single story option, and immediate proximity to Las Vegas National Golf Course, the Strip, and the airport. The standout combination is owned solar at no upcharge, the energy efficiency stack of ENERGY STAR plus Indoor airPLUS plus DOE Zero Energy Ready, and a small 47 home gated footprint. Buyers giving up amenity space inside the gates in exchange for energy savings, larger square footage, and a quieter, lower density neighborhood.
- **Paradise Trails by Signature Homes:** Best for first-time and move-up buyers who want the same gated infill feel and included solar at a meaningfully lower entry point. All two-story product, smaller footprints, and a tighter 29 home community. A strong choice if budget caps out under $500K and the buyer is comfortable in two stories.
- **Talamore by Lennar:** Best for multigenerational households who want a Next Gen suite under one roof and full resort amenities. The 8,000 sf clubhouse and pool deck are the most amenity-rich offering inside the 1 to 3 mile radius. Highest pricing of the comparison set and the most likely to be sold out or in final close-out.
- **Rochelle Estates by Lennar:** Best for buyers who want Lennar Everything's Included pricing and the ability to spec a Next Gen suite or RV garage on select lots. Direct head-to-head with Emerson Estates on square footage and price per sq ft, with a slight edge if the buyer specifically wants a multigen layout or RV space.
- **Tropicana Gardens by LGI Homes:** Best for first-time buyers who need the lowest payment in the east valley and want a true move-in ready package with all appliances and zero money down financing options. Different zip and school zone from Emerson Estates.
- **KB Home 89121 communities:** Best for attainable buyers who want ENERGY STAR new construction in the same general school zone for under $415K. Smaller footprints and lower spec than Emerson Estates, but the only sub $400K options inside the 89121 ring with new construction.

### Key Takeaways

- Emerson Estates is the largest square footage product among direct 89121 competitors at the price point, with a top size of 2,735 sq ft and a true single story Zion plan that Talamore and Rochelle Estates do not match across their full lineup.
- Owned rooftop solar included at no upcharge with claimed annual savings up to $4,859 is a real differentiator. Paradise Trails also markets owned solar, but Talamore, Rochelle Estates, Tropicana Gardens, and KB Home 89121 offerings do not advertise included owned solar in the data captured.
- Emerson Estates loses on amenities. Talamore is the clear winner inside the gates with a clubhouse, pool, and fitness center. Emerson Estates compensates with adjacency to Las Vegas National Golf Course rather than community-built amenities.
- Emerson Estates is mid-pack on price. It sits well above Paradise Trails, Tropicana Gardens, and KB Home 89121, and well below Talamore. Rochelle Estates is the closest direct price-per-sq-ft competitor.
- School zone is a shared weakness. All 89121 comparison communities sit in the same Harris, Orr, Chaparral CCSD pathway, with Chaparral historically scoring 1 to 3 out of 10 on GreatSchools. Buyers prioritizing school ratings should be steered to Henderson or Summerlin product, not any of the 89121 options.
- Most defensible buyer pitch: Emerson Estates is the right pick for the buyer who wants a brand new gated single-family home in the 2,200 to 2,735 sq ft range, with owned solar included, in a 47 home community walking distance to Las Vegas National Golf Course and 10 to 15 minutes from the Strip and the airport. It is a lifestyle and energy efficiency play, not an amenity or school rating play, and it is priced fairly against Rochelle Estates while offering a single story option, a true pop top loft option, and Beazer's Choice Plans flexibility that Lennar's Everything's Included model does not match.
