About

A QUIET CORNER
OF SOUTHWEST
LAS VEGAS

Ironwood is a new infill subdivision of more than 100 two-story homes by Century Communities, set into an established pocket of Southwest Las Vegas. The community grand opened in June 2025, and four floor plans are now selling from the high $400Ks to the high $500Ks.

The 89113 location sits right off the I-215 Durango exit, so you can drop onto the beltway and reach the Strip in about 10 minutes. The UnCommons mixed-use district, with its dining, retail, and fitness, is walking distance away. Durango Casino and Resort, Station Casinos newest property, is just three minutes by car.

Model homes are open daily at 8586 W. Agate Avenue. Every Ironwood home includes Century Home Connect smart home features, quartz countertops, stainless appliances, and a two-car garage. Call Ryan at 702-747-5921 to walk a model in person.
