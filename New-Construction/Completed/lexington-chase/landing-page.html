Lexington Chase is a 76 home neighborhood by Richmond American Homes in the Seasons Collection, opened in January 2026. The address is 8212 Kinleigh Poulson St., right off Durango Drive and Mistral Avenue in the 89148 ZIP code, in the Enterprise corridor of southwest Las Vegas.

Three two-story plans run from 2,320 to 2,780 sqft, all with 2-car garages and pricing from $576,950 to $636,950. That works out to roughly $229 to $249 per square foot, which is competitive for new construction in this part of town.

Location is the headline. The 215 Beltway is less than a mile away, the Strip is about 7 miles or 10 minutes east, and UnCommons, Durango Casino, and Mountain's Edge Marketplace are all within a short drive. Sierra Vista High School feeds the neighborhood, with Vassiliadis Elementary likely zoned. Ask Ryan for the current Richmond American incentive and SID/LID details for the parcel you are considering.
