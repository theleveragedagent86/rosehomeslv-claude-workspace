### Best For Summary

**Lexington Chase:** Best for buyers who want a brand-new (2026) two-story Richmond American home in a small, intimate 76-lot subdivision in 89148 with quick freeway access, Sierra Vista HS feeder, and no master-plan HOA layers. Trade-off: no on-site amenities and a higher entry point than nearby townhomes.

**Gardenia by Beazer:** Best for first-time buyers or down-sizers prioritizing affordability and a lower price point in the same 89148 ZIP. Townhome product with solar included. Trade-off: smaller square footage, attached product, higher price per square foot.

**Pulte at Mountain's Edge:** Best for buyers who want top-3 national builder reputation, full MPC amenities, and flexible pricing from entry to mid-tier. Trade-off: layered HOA structure and slightly farther from the 215 than Lexington Chase.

**Lennar at Mountain's Edge:** Best for buyers chasing the strongest current incentive package (2.75% Y1 buydown plus ~$9K credits) from the #1 national builder, with comparable two-story square footage to Lexington Chase. Trade-off: Mountain's Edge MPC fees and resale density.

**Mountain's Edge (overall):** Best for buyers who want maximum builder choice, established schools, and full master-planned lifestyle. Trade-off: mostly built out, fewer brand-new options, higher ceiling pricing.

### Key Takeaways

- Lexington Chase is the only brand-new (2026 grand opening) Richmond American community in this 1-3 mile radius, offering scarcity value as Mountain's Edge nears full buildout.
- Pricing per square foot at Lexington Chase ($229-$249) is competitive with Pulte and Lennar at Mountain's Edge and significantly cheaper per foot than Gardenia townhomes.
- Lexington Chase trades on-site amenities for a lower HOA load and a smaller, more intimate 76-lot footprint.
- Sierra Vista HS feeder is the consistent thread across most of these communities.
- Lennar currently has the strongest published incentive (2.75% Y1 buydown plus ~$9K credits); buyers should pressure-test Richmond American's HomeAmerican Mortgage offer against it.
