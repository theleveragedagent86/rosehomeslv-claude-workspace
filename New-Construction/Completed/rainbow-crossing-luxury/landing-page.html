About

SOUTHWEST
LAS VEGAS
NEW HOMES

Rainbow Crossing Luxury is a new Pulte Homes community in southwest Las Vegas, ZIP code 89139. The community sits near the corner of Rainbow Boulevard and Blue Diamond Road in the Enterprise area, with the 215 Beltway just 5 to 10 minutes away and the Strip about 15 to 20 minutes from your front door.

Pulte is one of the largest national homebuilders. Their floor plans focus on how families really use their space, with flexible rooms, large kitchens, and clean modern finishes. At Rainbow Crossing Luxury you can choose from four plans, ranging from 1,642 sq ft up to 3,072+ sq ft, with three to six bedrooms.

The lineup includes the single-story Beckham for buyers who want everything on one level, and three two-story plans, the Harper, Duncan, and Ella, with flexible bedroom counts up to six. School zones include Sierra Vista High School, and you are minutes from Mountain's Edge Marketplace, Smith's, Costco at Rainbow and Blue Diamond, and Red Rock Canyon for weekend hikes.
