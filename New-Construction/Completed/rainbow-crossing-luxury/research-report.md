**Rainbow Crossing Luxury** is best for buyers who want a luxury Pulte home in southwest Las Vegas with broad layout flexibility, including the Ella's up-to-6-bedroom multi-generational configuration. It hits a sweet spot in the mid-$600K range and offers solid bedroom flexibility (3-6 BR) for a brand new build. **Rainbow Crossing Estates** is best for buyers who want the same neighborhood and builder as Luxury but prefer a more traditional 1- and 2-story floor plan layout with a slightly larger top-end square footage (up to 4,910 sqft). Confirmed $150/mo HOA. A natural cross-shop for any Luxury prospect.

**Paldona** is best for first-time move-up buyers and budget-conscious families who want into the 89139 ZIP at a lower entry price. Smaller homes and 2- to 3-story plans. Trades square footage and luxury finishes for a payment that runs roughly $1,500/mo less than the target.

**Hayford Collection** is best for buyers willing to shift one ZIP south to 89141 in exchange for similar pricing and a larger top-end home (up to 4,820 sqft). Strong option for buyers who like the southwest valley but want to be closer to Southern Highlands and St. Rose.

**Daylight** is best for buyers stepping up into true estate-style luxury at $1M+. Larger lots, bigger square footage floor (3,488 sqft minimum), and customization options like RV garages. Use this as the comp that shows what the next price tier looks like for a buyer deciding whether to stretch.
