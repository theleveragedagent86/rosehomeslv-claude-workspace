About

GATED TOWNHOMES
IN THE HEART OF
NORTHWEST VEGAS

Rosa Point is a gated new construction townhome community by Beazer Homes inside 89106 in northwest Las Vegas. Two two-story plans, Albany and Cambridge, range from 1,485 to 1,851 sq ft with 3 bedrooms, 2.5 baths, and a rear-entry 2-car garage. Base prices start at $358,990, an entry point that is rare for brand new gated product this close to the urban core.

Every Rosa Point home ships with solar panels included, one EV pre-wire, ENERGY STAR certification, DOE Zero Energy Ready Home, and Indoor airPLUS. That builder spec sheet means lower power bills from day one and a healthier indoor air baseline. Beazer pegs the average monthly energy bill in the high $50s to high $60s.

Location is the other story. The community sits next to the new Hylo Park development at Rancho and Lake Mead, which is bringing a Cardenas grocery, an ice rink at Hylo Park Arena, and additional retail in 2026. US-95 is half a mile east, the Strip is 5 to 6 miles southeast, and downtown Las Vegas is 3 to 4 miles east. The model home is at 1991 Hydrangea Place, open Monday 1 to 6 and Tuesday through Sunday 10 to 6.
