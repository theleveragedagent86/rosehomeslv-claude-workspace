### Best For Summary

**Rosa Point by Beazer Homes** is best for buyers who want a brand-new gated townhome close to the Strip and downtown without the upkeep of a yard. It hits a sweet spot at $359K–$394K with included solar, EV pre-wire, and DOE Zero Energy Ready certification, making it a strong pick for first-time buyers, young professionals, and lock-and-leave second-home owners who want energy-efficient new construction in a redeveloping urban corridor.

**Rosa by Beazer Homes** is best for buyers who want the same Beazer quality and 89106 location but need a yard and detached single-family living. With only the final home remaining and pricing in the mid-$400s to $476K, it suits move-up buyers who want Tesla Solar Roof and Powerwall standard.

**Arcadia by Lennar** was best for budget-conscious townhome buyers who wanted gated living near I-15 at the lowest price point in the area. Now sold out, it sets the pricing floor for what 89106 townhomes traded at recently.

**Kalea Trails by D.R. Horton** is best for buyers who want a smaller, more affordable smart-home townhome and don't mind being further north in 89081. Adjacency to Tropical Breeze Park makes it a strong pick for young families who want park access without HOA-funded amenities.

**Watercolor by Touchstone Living** is best for buyers who prioritize amenities and aggressive financing incentives over location. With a pool, dog park, playgrounds, and HOA-included cable and internet, plus 4.75% fixed-for-life financing, it appeals to buyers who want maximum lifestyle value and are willing to drive further from the Strip.

### Key Takeaways

- **Rosa Point sits in the middle of the price band** — more affordable than its single-family sister Rosa and detached new construction averages ($530K), but commands a premium over Arcadia, Kalea Trails, and Watercolor due to Beazer's energy-efficiency package and newer 2025 product.
- **Strongest location of the comparison set** — Rosa Point is closest to the Strip (5–6 miles), downtown, and US-95, beating both North Las Vegas comps by 5–7 miles.
- **Energy efficiency is the key differentiator** — solar panels, EV pre-wire, ENERGY STAR, DOE Zero Energy Ready, and Indoor airPLUS are all included, which Arcadia, Kalea Trails, and Watercolor do not match at this scale.
- **Weakness: no on-site amenities** — Rosa Point has no pool, clubhouse, or park, while Watercolor offers a full amenity package and Kalea Trails has Tropical Breeze Park next door.
- **Limited floor plan choice** — only 2 plans (Albany and Cambridge) versus Watercolor's 12 plans and Lennar/Horton's broader lineups, narrowing the buyer pool to those whose needs fit the 3 bed / 2.5 bath / 1,485–1,851 sq ft window.
