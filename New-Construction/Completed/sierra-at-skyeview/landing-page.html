About

A QUIETER
CORNER OF
SKYE CANYON

Sierra at Skyeview is a 31 home cluster collection by Century Communities tucked inside the Skyeview neighborhood of Skye Canyon. Three two-story plans range from 1,602 to 1,965 sq ft with 3 to 5 bedrooms, 2.5 to 3 baths, and 2-car garages. Base prices start at $433,990, the most affordable single-family Century entry inside Skye Canyon.

The shared private-drive layout means lower exterior maintenance and a tighter, quieter neighborhood feel. Owners still get full access to Skye Canyon's master-plan amenities, including the Skye Center clubhouse, Skye Fitness with a junior Olympic pool, four community parks, miles of trails, pickleball courts, and a calendar of community events.

Smith's Marketplace, restaurants, and shops sit inside Skye Canyon Marketplace right at Skye Canyon Park Drive. US-95 is a minute away for the commute south to Summerlin or the Strip. The model home is at 8925 Vanhoy Creek St., Las Vegas, NV 89166. Open Monday and Tuesday 10 to 6, Friday 12 to 6, and Saturday and Sunday 10 to 6. Wednesday and Thursday by appointment.

Skye Canyon Amenities
SKYE CENTER
8,142 sq ft clubhouse with Aspire Coffee House, event space, and year-round community programming.
SKYE FITNESS & POOL
9,663 sq ft fitness facility with classes, personal training, and a junior Olympic swimming pool.
SKYE CANYON PARK
15-acre park with pool, splash pads, basketball courts, sports fields, fire pits, and a restaurant.
FOUR COMMUNITY PARKS
Skye View Park, Big Skye Park, Starlight Park, and Skye Canyon Park spread across the master plan.
TRAILS & BIKE LANES
Miles of walking paths, bike lanes, dog parks, and pickleball courts under 4,000 planted trees.
SMITH'S MARKETPLACE
240,000 sq ft retail center anchored by Smith's grocery, plus Cafe Rio, Tropical Smoothie, banks, and salons.
TOP-RATED SCHOOLS
Arbor View High School and Somerset Skye Canyon K-8 charter (8 of 10 GreatSchools) zoned for the community.
FREEWAY ACCESS
US-95 right at Skye Canyon Park Dr, CC-215 Beltway 5 to 10 minutes south. About 30 minutes to the Strip.
