# SkyeView Terra by Century Communities: New Homes in Skye Canyon Starting at $489,990 Terra at SkyeView is a new construction community by Century Communities located inside Skye Canyon in northwest Las Vegas. Homes start at $489,990 and go up to $519,990 for base pricing. Quick move-in homes are available from $530,000 to $550,000. All three floor plans are two-story with 2-car garages and range from 1,792 to 2,119 square feet. If you are looking for a well-priced new build in one of the most popular master-planned communities in the Las Vegas valley, Terra deserves a close look. ## What Homes Are Available in SkyeView Terra? Century Communities offers three floor plans at Terra. The Residence 1792 gives you 1,792 square feet of living space. The Residence 1943 steps up to 1,943 square feet. The Residence 2119 is the largest at 2,119 square feet. Bedroom counts range from 3 to 5, and bathrooms range from 2.5 to 3. All homes are two stories with a 2-car garage. Elevation styles include Santa Barbara, Southern Italian, and Tuscan, so the streetscape has some visual variety. There are currently 5 quick move-in homes available for buyers who want to move faster than a full build timeline allows. ## How Much Does It Cost to Live in SkyeView Terra? At roughly $254 per square foot, Terra offers the best value among the SkyeView collection of communities. For comparison, Mesa in the same collection runs about $290 per square foot and features single-story homes with fewer bedrooms. Sierra has a lower entry point at $443,990 but does not match Terra's balance of size and bedroom count. Arete at Kyle Canyon by KB Home offers more floor plan options and a lower HOA, but fewer community amenities. Beyond the base price, plan for a monthly HOA of about $83 for the Skye Canyon master association. Total HOA fees, including any sub-association, are estimated between $120 and $175 per month. There is also a Special Improvement District (SID) tax estimated at $1,000 to $2,500 per year. SID taxes help pay for the roads, parks, and infrastructure in the area. ## What Builder Incentives Are Available Right Now? Century Communities is running two strong incentive programs at Terra. The Flash Sale offers a 3.375% interest rate (4.225% APR) through their preferred lender. They also have the YES! LIVES HERE program, which gives FHA and VA buyers a 5/1 adjustable-rate mortgage at 3.75% plus up to $3,000 in closing cost credits. These rates can make a big difference in your monthly payment. Incentives change regularly, so confirm the latest offers before you visit the model home. ## What Is Skye Canyon Like? Skye Canyon is a large master-planned community in northwest Las Vegas with a strong lineup of shared spaces. The Skye Center serves as the community hub. Skye Fitness includes a junior Olympic swimming pool. There is a 15-acre park, Eagle Canyon Park with a splash pad, Big Skye Park with dog parks, and miles of trails for walking and biking. Daily errands are simple. Smith's Marketplace is located right inside the community. US-95 is less than 1 mile away, which gives you a straight shot to most parts of the valley. The Las Vegas Strip is about 19 miles south. ## What Schools Are Near SkyeView Terra? Families at Terra will be zoned for Divich Elementary School (rated 3 out of 10), Escobedo Middle School (rated 7 out of 10), and Arbor View High School. Bryan Elementary School, rated 9 out of 10, is also nearby and may be an option through the Clark County School District's school choice program. Escobedo Middle and Arbor View High are both solid schools that families in northwest Las Vegas tend to favor. ## Local Insight I have toured the SkyeView communities and spent a lot of time in Skye Canyon over the years.
