About

WELCOME TO
SKYEVIEW TERRA

SkyeView Terra is a new home community by Century Communities in the heart of Skye Canyon. Located in northwest Las Vegas, this neighborhood puts you close to trails, parks, top-rated shopping, and easy freeway access on US-95. Homes start in the high $400s and offer modern open layouts with 2-car garages.

All three floor plans are two-story designs ranging from 1,792 to 2,119 square feet. You can choose from 3 to 5 bedrooms and pick from Santa Barbara, Southern Italian, or Tuscan elevation styles. Each home comes with a center island kitchen, walk-in closets, and flexible loft space that works as an office, playroom, or extra bedroom.

Skye Canyon is one of the most popular master-planned areas in the Las Vegas valley. The community includes an 8,000 sq ft Skye Center, a junior Olympic pool, splash pads, dog parks, and miles of walking and biking trails. Smith's Marketplace is right on-site for daily errands. Centennial Hills Hospital is about 6 miles away, and the Las Vegas Strip is roughly 19 miles south.

Fees Explained

HOA, SID/LID
& FEES

The Skye Canyon master HOA is $83 per month. This covers community parks, trails, pools, and the Skye Center. There may also be a sub-association fee, bringing your estimated total HOA to $120 to $175 per month. The HOA keeps the neighborhood clean and gives you access to all the Skye Canyon common areas.

SkyeView Terra does have a Special Improvement District (SID) tax. This is an extra property tax that pays for roads, sewers, and other public improvements in the area. The estimated cost is $1,000 to $2,500 per year, billed twice a year. If you want to pay it off early, there is a 3% prepay penalty. Your agent can help you understand exactly how the SID applies to each lot.

There is no LID (Local Improvement District) listed for this community. Between HOA and SID, plan for roughly $270 to $383 per month on top of your mortgage payment. These costs are normal for new construction in the northwest Las Vegas area.

Schools

SCHOOLS &
FAMILY LIFE

Families in SkyeView Terra are zoned for Divich Elementary (rated 3/10), Escobedo Middle School (rated 7/10), and Arbor View High School. Bryan Elementary (rated 9/10) is also nearby and worth asking about for zone options. School ratings come from third-party sites and can change year to year, so always check the latest data.

Skye Canyon is built for families. The 15-acre park has open fields and play areas. Eagle Canyon Park has a splash pad that is perfect for Las Vegas summers. Big Skye Park has separate dog parks for small and large dogs. There are also miles of walking and biking trails that connect through the entire master plan.

Centennial Hills Hospital is about 6.2 miles away for medical needs. Smith's Marketplace is right inside the community for groceries and daily shopping. You will not need to drive far for the things your family uses every day.

Amenities

AMENITIES &

The Skye Center is the heart of the community. This 8,000 square foot clubhouse is where neighbors gather for events and activities. Skye Fitness offers a full gym and a junior Olympic-sized pool. Whether you like to swim laps or just cool off on a hot day, you have options right in your neighborhood.

Outdoor lovers will enjoy the extensive trail system that runs through Skye Canyon. You can walk, jog, or bike without ever leaving the community. Big Skye Park has dog parks, and Eagle Canyon Park has a splash pad and playground. The 15-acre park gives kids and families plenty of open space.

Commuters will appreciate that US-95 is less than 1 mile from the community. The Las Vegas Strip is about 19 miles south. Downtown Summerlin shopping and dining is a short drive away. You get the feel of a quiet, family-friendly neighborhood with quick access to everything Las Vegas has to offer.

FAQ

FREQUENTLY
ASKED QUESTIONS

Do I need my own real estate agent to buy at SkyeView Terra?
Yes, and it does not cost you anything extra. The builder pays the buyer's agent commission. Having your own agent means someone is looking out for your interests, not the builder's. Register with your agent on your very first visit to the model home.
What is the 3.375% Flash Sale rate?
Century Communities is offering a special 3.375% fixed interest rate (4.225% APR) on select homes. This rate is significantly lower than the current market average and can save you hundreds of dollars per month. Ask your agent which homes qualify.
What is a SID and how does it affect my payment?
A Special Improvement District (SID) is a tax that pays for public infrastructure like roads and utilities in the area. At SkyeView Terra, it is estimated at $1,000 to $2,500 per year, billed twice a year. You can prepay it with a 3% penalty. This amount is added on top of your regular property taxes.
Are there quick move-in homes available?
Yes. Century Communities currently has 5 QMI (quick move-in) homes priced between $530,000 and $550,000. These are homes that are already built or nearly finished, so you can move in much faster than building from scratch.
What elevation styles are available?
You can choose from Santa Barbara, Southern Italian, or Tuscan elevation styles. Each one gives your home a unique look from the outside. The floor plan inside stays the same regardless of which elevation you pick.
What is the HOA like in Skye Canyon?
The Skye Canyon master HOA is $83 per month. Combined with any sub-association fees, total HOA is estimated at $120 to $175 per month. This gives you access to the Skye Center, Skye Fitness, the junior Olympic pool, parks, trails, and community events.
How long does it take to build a new home here?
Build times vary, but most new homes in the Las Vegas area take about 6 to 10 months from contract to close. Century Communities can give you a more exact timeline based on the lot and plan you choose. QMI homes are available much sooner.
What schools are zoned for this area?
SkyeView Terra is zoned for Divich Elementary (rated 3/10), Escobedo Middle School (rated 7/10), and Arbor View High School. Bryan Elementary (rated 9/10) is also nearby. School zones can shift, so always confirm with the Clark County School District.
Can I use a VA or FHA loan?
Yes. Century Communities offers a YES! LIVES HERE program with a 5/1 ARM at 3.75% for FHA and VA buyers. This is a great rate for military families or first-time buyers using government-backed loans. Talk to your lender about which option works best for you.
When can I visit the model homes?
The model home at 8592 Vanhoy Creek Street is open Monday through Thursday from 10am to 6pm, Friday from 12pm to 6pm, and Saturday through Sunday from 10am to 6pm. Bring your agent with you on your first visit so they can be registered as your representative.
