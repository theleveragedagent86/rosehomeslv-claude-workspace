About

YOUR NEW HOME
IN SKYE CANYON

SkyeView Terra is a gated community of 196 new two-story homes by Century Communities in Skye Canyon, one of the most popular master plans in northwest Las Vegas. Homes range from 1,792 to 2,119 square feet with 3 to 5 bedrooms and up to 3 bathrooms. Three floor plans offer open layouts, center island kitchens, and spacious primary suites.

Skye Canyon is a 1,700-acre master-planned community with resort-style living built in. Residents enjoy the Skye Center clubhouse, Skye Fitness with a junior Olympic pool, 15-acre Skye Canyon Park, miles of trails and bike lanes, splash pads, dog parks, and year-round events like outdoor movie nights and farmers' markets.

Daily errands are easy with Smith's Marketplace, restaurants, and shops right inside the community. US-95 is less than a mile away for a quick commute. The model home is at 8592 Vanhoy Creek Street, Las Vegas, NV 89166. Open daily, with self-guided NterNow tours also available.
