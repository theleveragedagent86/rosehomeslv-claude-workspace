## Lead Flow: Meta to Lofty CRM

1. **Lead fills out Instant Form** on Meta/Instagram
2. **Lead syncs to Lofty CRM** via Lead Sync workflow
3. **Tag applied:** `skyeview-terra`
4. **Track assignment based on qualifying answers:**
   - ASAP or 1-3 months + Pre-approved = **Hot** track
   - 1-3 months or 3-6 months + Any answer = **Warm** track
   - Just browsing + Any answer = **Nurture** track
5. **Smart plan auto-starts** (texts and emails begin immediately based on assigned track)

## Campaign Notes

### A/B Testing Strategy
- Launch all 4 ad variations at the same time. Let Meta's algorithm distribute spend for the first 7 days.
- After 7 days, review which variations have the lowest cost per lead and highest form completion rate.
- Pause any variation with a CPL more than 2x the best performer.
- Let the winning 2-3 ads run for the remaining 23 days.

### Optimization Tips
- **Day 3-5:** Check that the Instant Form is working and leads are syncing to Lofty. Fix any sync issues immediately.
- **Day 7:** First performance review. Look at CPL, form completion rate, and ad relevance score.
- **Day 14:** Second review. Pause underperformers. If all 4 are close in CPL, keep them all running.
- **Day 21:** If budget allows and CPL is strong, consider increasing daily spend by $5-10.
- **Day 30:** Full campaign review. Document CPL, total leads, and lead quality for future campaigns.

### Lead Quality Notes
- "More Volume" forms generate more leads but may include lower-intent contacts. The smart plan's 3-track system (Hot, Warm, Nurture) handles this automatically.
- Expect roughly 20% Hot, 40% Warm, 40% Nurture based on typical new construction lead distribution.
- The real ROI comes from the smart plan follow-up. Most leads will not convert on the first touch. The 30-day automated plan does the heavy lifting.

### Housing Special Ad Category Compliance
- All ads must run under Housing Special Ad Category. This is not optional.
- You cannot target by age, gender, zip code, or detailed interests.
- Location targeting is limited to a 15-mile minimum radius (we use 20 miles centered on 89101).
- Advantage+ handles the rest. Meta will optimize delivery within these constraints.

### Creative Refresh
- If running beyond 30 days, swap in new creative every 4-6 weeks to prevent ad fatigue.
- Reuse the same hooks with fresh images or updated incentive numbers.
- If Century Communities changes their incentives, update Variation 3 immediately.
