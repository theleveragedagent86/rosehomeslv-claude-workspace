If you are looking at new construction in Skye Canyon, you have probably seen a few different communities. Here is how SkyeView Terra stacks up:

**vs. SkyeView Mesa (single-story homes)**
Mesa offers single-story floor plans, which is great if you want to avoid stairs. But at ~$290 per square foot, Mesa costs more per foot than Terra (~$254/sq ft). Mesa also tops out at 3 bedrooms. Terra gives you up to 5 bedrooms and more overall space.

**vs. SkyeView Sierra (lower entry price)**
Sierra starts at $443,990, which is about $46,000 less than Terra's base. But Terra's Residence 2119 plan gives you 2,119 square feet with up to 5 bedrooms and 3 bathrooms. If you need the extra space and bedrooms, Terra is the better value.

**vs. Arete at Kyle Canyon by KB Home**
KB Home offers more floor plan options (5 plans) and homes up to 2,469 square feet. The monthly HOA is also lower at about $75. **Bottom line:** Terra stands out for its balance of size, price, and access to Skye Canyon's resort-level amenities. It is one of the best values per bedroom in the area.
