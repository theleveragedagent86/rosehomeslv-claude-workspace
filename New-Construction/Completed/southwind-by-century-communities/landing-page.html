About

SIMPLE TWO-STORY
LIVING NEAR
MOUNTAIN'S EDGE

Southwind is a new-construction community by Century Communities in southwest Las Vegas. The community sits at 9809 Lost Horse Avenue, just a short drive from Mountain's Edge Regional Park, Exploration Peak Park, and the Mountain's Edge trail system. The Strip is about 12 miles away with quick access via I-15.

The community keeps things simple with three two-story floor plans ranging from 2,054 to 2,605 square feet. Buyers can pick 3 to 5 bedrooms depending on the plan. Century Communities is a publicly traded national builder with strong financial backing and a focus on attainable price points.

The HOA is just $69 per month, one of the lowest in the southwest valley. Children attend Tyrone Thompson Elementary, a top-rated school with a Niche A-minus grade. Sales office is on site at 9809 Lost Horse Avenue, 702-789-6329.
