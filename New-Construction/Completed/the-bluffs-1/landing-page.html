About

SINGLE-STORY
LIVING INSIDE
THE GATES

The Bluffs I is a 90 home single-story enclave by Century Communities tucked inside the guard-gated Lake Las Vegas resort in Henderson. Three floor plans range from 1,663 to 1,831 sq ft with 2 to 3 bedrooms, 2 baths, and 2-car attached garages. Base prices start at $465,990, the lowest-priced new single-story product currently available inside Lake Las Vegas.

Every home is single-story, so no stairs, no second-floor maintenance, and a layout that works for empty nesters, first-time buyers, and anyone who wants a lock-and-leave second home inside the gates. The model home is at 128 Barkset Way, Henderson, NV 89011.

Buyers get full resort access. That includes the 320-acre private lake, two Jack Nicklaus signature golf courses, the Sports Club, the white-sand beach, paddle boarding and kayaking, the Aqua Park, and MonteLago Village retail and dining. The Strip is about 22 miles away, roughly 25 to 30 minutes via I-215 and I-515.
