### Best For Summary

**The Bluffs I (Century Communities)** is best for buyers who want a brand-new single-story home inside the Lake Las Vegas resort gates without stepping into the $600K luxury tier. It is the lowest-priced new single-story product currently selling at Lake Las Vegas, which makes it the natural fit for downsizers, first-time luxury-resort buyers, and remote workers who want lock-and-leave ease with full access to two Nicklaus golf courses, the Sports Club, and the lake.

**Verona by Taylor Morrison** is best for buyers who need more square footage and don't mind stairs. Multi-story plans with downstairs primary suites, lofts, and private courtyards work well for families or buyers who entertain. The current no-down, no-closing-cost financing program makes it the easiest cash-to-close option in Lake Las Vegas.

**Verona by Beazer Homes** is best for value-focused buyers who want a Lake Las Vegas address at the lowest possible entry price. Beazer's ENERGY SERIES emphasizes utility savings, which appeals to long-term owners watching operating costs over the life of the loan.

**Lago Del Sol by Lennar** is best for move-up buyers and multi-generational households. Plans run 2,310 to 2,668 sq ft with Next Gen suites and RV-garage options, and pricing starts in the low $600Ks. This is the right call when buyers need more space, lake views, and Lennar's Everything's Included finish package.

**Tuscany Village** is best for buyers who want a guard-gated golf-course lifestyle without the Lake Las Vegas premium. With no SID/LID, a $175 master HOA, and a 35,000 sq ft included rec center, the monthly carrying cost runs roughly $1,000 less than The Bluffs I. The trade-off is no lake, no resort beach, and mostly resale inventory, but family-sized floor plans up to 3,649 sq ft are available at prices that start in the high $200Ks.

### Key Takeaways

- **Lowest new single-story entry inside Lake Las Vegas.** The Bluffs I starts at $465,990 for a brand-new single-story home behind the resort gates. Verona by Beazer is close on price but is two-story product, and Lago Del Sol's single-story plans start $175K higher.
- **Best-in-class incentive right now.** Century's 3.875% FHA/VA rate through Inspire Home Loans beats the field on monthly payment for qualified buyers. Taylor Morrison's no-down, no-closing-cost offer is stronger on cash-to-close, so the right pick depends on whether the buyer is payment-sensitive or cash-sensitive.
- **Highest sub-HOA inside the resort.** At ~$397/month total, The Bluffs I carries roughly $240/month more than the other LLV communities, which only pay the ~$153 master fee. The premium covers the inner gate and sub-community landscaping.
- **Limited floor plan selection.** Only 3 plans and a tight 1,663 to 1,831 sq ft range. Buyers who need more than 2-3 bedrooms or want a true 3-car garage will look at Lago Del Sol or Tuscany Village instead.
- **Tuscany Village is the price-conscious alternative.** Roughly $1,000/month cheaper to carry, no SID/LID, larger floor plans available. The Bluffs I wins on newness, single-story-only product, lake/resort lifestyle, and Lake Las Vegas address.
