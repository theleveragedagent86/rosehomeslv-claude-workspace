# The Everett at Emerson Estates: 2,735 sq ft Single-Story Plus Loft from $615K in Las Vegas 89121 The Everett is the largest plan at Emerson Estates by Beazer Homes. It offers 2,735 square feet, 4 bedrooms, 3 baths, a 2-car garage, and a base price of $615,990. The signature feature is a single-story main level with a small upstairs loft, which Beazer markets as a quasi pop-top design. ## What Makes the Everett Stand Out? The Everett gives you a single-story home for daily living, plus a bonus upper loft for entertaining or quiet time. The main level holds an open kitchen, the great room, the primary bedroom suite, the secondary bedrooms, a dedicated study, and the laundry room. The primary suite is wide and includes a spacious walk-in closet right off the bedroom. The laundry sits near the primary, which makes weekly chores far easier than running across the house. The secondary bedrooms are tucked away from the primary for privacy. The dedicated study is a standalone room, not a flex nook off the kitchen, so it can serve as a real home office, a homework room, or a fifth bedroom equivalent without adding stairs to your routine. The kitchen features an open layout with quartz countertops, in line with the rest of the community. Upstairs, the loft sits over part of the home as a small pop-top level. Use it as a game room, a hobby room, a teen hangout, or a guest suite. Because most living happens on the main level, the loft becomes optional living space rather than a daily destination. Like every Emerson Estates home, the Everett is delivered as a Beazer Energy Series READY home with owned rooftop solar, ENERGY STAR, Indoor airPLUS, and DOE Zero Energy Ready Home certification. Beazer markets up to $4,859 in annual energy savings on this stack. The Everett is the right pick for buyers who want one-level main living, a real study, the most square footage in the community, and an extra upstairs bonus room that can stay quiet most of the time. ## The Emerson Estates Community at a Glance Emerson Estates is a gated 47 home Beazer community at 2583 Regency Cove Court, Las Vegas, NV 89121. It sits near Pacific Street and Eastern Avenue, just north of Flamingo Road. For a full overview of pricing, plans, and amenities, see [my Emerson Estates community guide](www.rosehomeslv.com/blog/emerson-estates-by-beazer). The community is walking distance to Las Vegas National Golf Course, about 4 to 5 miles to the Las Vegas Strip, 4 miles to Harry Reid International Airport, and 2 to 3 miles to UNLV. Schools include George E. Harris Elementary, William E. Orr Middle, and most likely Chaparral High School in CCSD. There is no pool or clubhouse inside the gates. The location, the energy efficiency package, and the gated 47 home footprint are the draws. ## Pricing, HOA, and SID Costs Base prices across the three Choice Plans run $549,990 to $615,990. The Everett sits at the top of the price ladder at $615,990 and is the largest plan at 2,735 square feet. Price per square foot lands near $225, which makes the Everett the best dollar-per-foot value of the three plans. Owned rooftop solar is included at no upcharge. The exact monthly HOA fee has not been published. Small gated Las Vegas infill HOAs typically run between $80 and $150 per month. A SID or LID assessment is unlikely on this infill location, but verify the parcel through the Clark County Treasurer or AMG before signing. Add Nevada's effective property tax rate near 0.5%, insurance, and the estimated HOA, and a total monthly PITI on an Everett at $615,990 with 20% down at 6% lands roughly in the low to mid $4,300s. ## How Emerson Estates Compares to Nearby New Construction In the 1 to 3 mile radius, the Everett is the largest single-story-driven plan available. Paradise Trails by Signature Homes is two-story only and tops out around 2,430 square feet from the high $300s. If you want maximum square footage on a mostly single-story plan, with a real study and a bonus upper loft, the Everett is the standout in this part of the valley.
