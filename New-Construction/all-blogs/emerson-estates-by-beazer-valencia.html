# The Valencia at Emerson Estates: 2,532 sq ft Two-Story from $549K in Las Vegas 89121 The Valencia is the two-story plan at Emerson Estates by Beazer Homes. It offers 2,532 square feet, 4 bedrooms, 3 baths, a 2-car garage, and a Quick Move-In has been listed from $549,990. The standout features are a main-level guest suite with its own full bath and a flexible upstairs loft. ## Who Is the Valencia Best For? The Valencia works well for buyers who host family or out-of-town visitors often. The main-level guest suite gives parents, in-laws, college kids, or visiting friends their own space and bathroom on the first floor, with no stairs to climb. That same suite can also work as a home office or a downstairs primary if your needs change later. Upstairs, the second-floor loft is a true flex space. Use it as a game room, a kids' play room, a study area for the family, or a media room. The remaining bedrooms and full baths sit on the second floor, which keeps the daytime living and the guest suite separated from the family sleeping zone. The 2-car garage handles two cars or one car plus storage and a workshop bench. This plan is the lowest publicly listed price in the community as of last refresh, with a Quick Move-In Valencia at Homesite 0014 marketed from $549,990.
