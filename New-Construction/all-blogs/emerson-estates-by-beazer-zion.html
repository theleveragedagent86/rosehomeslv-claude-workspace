# The Zion at Emerson Estates: 2,191 sq ft Single-Story from $551K in Las Vegas 89121 The Zion is the only single-story floor plan at Emerson Estates by Beazer Homes. It offers 2,191 square feet, 3 to 4 bedrooms, 2.5 to 3 baths, a 2-car garage, and an owned rooftop solar system from about $551,990. Everything happens on one level, with a smart layout that separates the primary suite from the secondary bedrooms. ## What Makes the Zion Stand Out? The Zion is built around an open kitchen and great room. The kitchen features quartz countertops and a center island with a sink, and it flows directly into the living and dining space. The primary bedroom sits next to the kitchen and great room, while the secondary bedrooms are tucked on the opposite side of the home for privacy. An expansive foyer greets guests at the front of the home. This is a true single-story plan with no stairs anywhere. Buyers can use Beazer's Choice Plans program to flex the layout to 3 bedrooms plus a study, or a full 4 bedrooms.
