# Emerson Estates by Beazer Homes: 47 Gated New Homes from $549K in Las Vegas 89121 Emerson Estates is a small gated Beazer Homes community of 47 new homes in East Las Vegas, 89121. Base prices run from $549,990 to $615,990, every home includes owned rooftop solar at no upcharge, and the community sits a short walk from Las Vegas National Golf Course. ## What Is Emerson Estates and Where Is It? Emerson Estates is a stand-alone Beazer Homes infill community at 2583 Regency Cove Court, Las Vegas, NV 89121. It is gated, single-family only, and capped at 47 homes on one quiet street. The community sits near the corner of Pacific Street and Eastern Avenue, just north of Flamingo Road in the established east side of the valley. There is no master developer here. Beazer is the only builder, which keeps the design language consistent across the community. As of May 2026, Beazer markets Emerson Estates as a final phase community with the last lots remaining and full buildout expected this year. ## Builders and Floor Plans at Emerson Estates Beazer Homes is the sole builder. Beazer is a top 10 Las Vegas builder known for its Choice Plans program, which lets buyers tweak the floorplan layout at no extra cost, and its Mortgage Choice program, which does not push you toward a captive lender.
