# Sierra at Skyeview by Century Communities: 31 Cluster Homes in Skye Canyon From $433,990 Sierra at Skyeview is a small, 31-home cluster collection by Century Communities inside the Skye Canyon master plan in Northwest Las Vegas. Two-story plans run from 1,602 to 1,965 square feet with base prices from $433,990 to $478,990, making it the lowest-priced single-family Century home in Skye Canyon. ## What Is Sierra at Skyeview and Where Is It? Sierra sits at 8925 Vanhoy Creek St., Las Vegas, NV 89166. It is a Century Communities collection inside the Skyeview district of Skye Canyon, a 1,700-acre master-planned community in the northwest valley. You reach it off US-95 at Skye Canyon Park Drive, with the CC-215 Beltway about 5 to 10 minutes south. The Las Vegas Strip is roughly 22 to 25 miles southeast, a 30 to 40 minute drive. What sets Sierra apart is the boutique scale. With only 31 homes total, the neighborhood feels tighter and quieter than its larger Skye Canyon neighbors. The cluster-home layout uses shared private drives instead of long street fronts, which trims exterior maintenance and shrinks lot upkeep. Buyers still get full access to every Skye Canyon master-plan amenity, including Skye Center, Skye Fitness, parks, and trails. ## Builders and Floor Plans at Sierra at Skyeview Century Communities is the sole builder. They are publicly traded on the NYSE under ticker CCS and known for an online "Buy Now" platform that lets you browse, price, and reserve a home from your couch. Every Sierra home includes the Century Home Connect smart-home package as standard. There are 3 floor plans, all two-story with a 2-car garage. The [Residence 1602 floor plan](www.rosehomeslv.com/blog/sierra-at-skyeview-residence-1602) starts at $433,990 with 1,602 square feet, 3 bedrooms, and 2.5 baths. It is the entry plan and a fit for first-time buyers, single professionals, and small families who want new construction under $440K. The [Residence 1708 floor plan](www.rosehomeslv.com/blog/sierra-at-skyeview-residence-1708) starts at $448,990 with 1,708 square feet, 3 to 4 bedrooms, and 2.5 baths. The upstairs loft can flex into a 4th bedroom, and the primary suite has dual walk-in closets. The [Residence 1965 floor plan](www.rosehomeslv.com/blog/sierra-at-skyeview-residence-1965) tops the lineup at $478,990 with 1,965 square feet, 3 to 5 bedrooms, and 2.5 to 3 baths. It adds a private den on the main floor and an optional downstairs bed and bath, which makes it the most flexible plan for guests, work-from-home, or multi-generational living. ## Pricing, HOA, and SID Costs at Sierra at Skyeview Base prices at Sierra run from $433,990 to $478,990. Current inventory homes price up to about $520,930 once options and lot premiums are added. Recent Century incentives have included a 3.875% 30-year fixed FHA/VA rate, a 3.99% conventional 7/6 ARM with up to $6,000 in closing cost credit, and a 3.75% 5/1 ARM with $3,000 in closing credit. All Century rate programs require their affiliated lender, Inspire Home Loans. Verify the current month's offer at the sales office. The Skye Canyon master HOA is $83 per month, which is one of the lowest in any large Las Vegas master plan. Sierra also has a separate Skyeview district sub-HOA on top of that. The sub-HOA dollar amount is not posted online, so plan to verify the exact figure at the sales office. Realistic combined HOA lands in the $165 to $235 per month range. Skye Canyon also carries SID (Special Improvement District) assessments, typically $2,400 to $4,800 per year valley-wide for similar communities. SID is billed on the property tax statement and transfers with the home, so you want the exact dollar amount in writing on the lot you choose.
