# The Residence 1742 at The Bluffs I: 1,742 Sq Ft Single-Story with a Den from $475,990 The Residence 1742 is the middle plan at The Bluffs I by Century Communities at Lake Las Vegas. It is 1,742 square feet, 2 bedrooms, 2 baths, plus a den that can flex into a 3rd bedroom, single-story, with a 2-car garage, from $475,990. The den is the reason buyers pick this plan over the smaller 1663. ## What Makes the Residence 1742 Stand Out? The 1742 takes the same single-story footprint logic as the 1663 and adds 79 square feet plus a den. That den is the whole pitch. It can serve as a home office, a second living room, a hobby room, or convert into a 3rd bedroom for guests or grandkids who visit a few times a year. The flexibility is what bumps you up from the 1663 without committing to the larger 1831. Standard features include the Century Home Connect smart-home package, a 2-car garage, and the open kitchen, dining, and great room layout shared across all three Bluffs plans. The primary suite is at the back for quiet, with a guest bedroom and bath up front and the flex den in between. With 1,742 square feet on a single level, you get usable everyday space and a real flex room without paying for a full third bedroom you do not need yet. The 1742 is the best fit for buyers who want optionality. If you are sure you only need 2 bedrooms, the [Residence 1663](www.rosehomeslv.com/blog/the-bluffs-1-residence-1663) saves you about $10,000. If you need a true 3-bedroom on day one with a closet and full window egress, the [Residence 1831](www.rosehomeslv.com/blog/the-bluffs-1-residence-1831) is the better call. ## The Bluffs I Community at a Glance The Bluffs I sits at 128 Barkset Way, Henderson, NV 89011, inside the guard-gated Lake Las Vegas resort. Phase I opened in March 2023 and is planned for 90 single-story homes total. Century Communities is the builder, a top-10 national builder traded on the NYSE under ticker CCS. The Strip is about 22 miles, a 25 to 30 minute drive via I-215 and I-515. Lake Las Vegas itself is a 320-acre lake at the center of a Mediterranean-themed resort. Amenities include 2 Jack Nicklaus golf courses (Reflection Bay public, SouthShore private), a Sports Club with tennis and pickleball, a white-sand beach, paddle boarding, kayaking, and the MonteLago Village dining and shopping district. Schools are Josh Stevens Elementary (rated 8 of 10), B. Mahlon Brown IB Middle, and Basic Academy IB High. For a full overview, see [The Bluffs I community guide](www.rosehomeslv.com/blog/the-bluffs-1). ## Pricing, HOA, and SID Costs Base prices at The Bluffs I run from $465,990 to $485,990 across the 3 plans. Quick move-in homes have recently listed in the $525,000 to $570,000 range with options and lot premiums. Lot premiums alone run from $3,000 to $75,000 depending on view and position. Current Century incentives include a 3.875% 30-year fixed FHA and VA rate through Inspire Home Loans, the Century-affiliated lender. The HOA structure is two layers. The Bluffs sub-association is about $244 per month and the Lake Las Vegas master HOA is about $153 per month, for a combined $397 per month. The Lake Las Vegas LID is about $1,020 per year, billed on the property tax statement. On the 1742 base price of $475,990 with 5 percent down and the 3.875% FHA rate, realistic all-in monthly cost lands near $3,093 per month with taxes, HOA, and LID baked in. ## How The Bluffs I Compares to Nearby New Construction The Bluffs I is the lowest-priced new single-story product inside the Lake Las Vegas guard gates. Verona by Taylor Morrison starts around $490,000 and is two-story. Verona by Beazer is in the high $400Ks, also two-story. Lago Del Sol by Lennar starts in the low $600Ks for larger plans. Tuscany Village sits about 3 miles outside the gates with no SID or LID and a much lower $175 per month HOA, but you trade the lake, beach, and Nicklaus golf access. If you want a single-story 3-bedroom-equivalent with a den inside the gates, the 1742 is the only option in this price band.
