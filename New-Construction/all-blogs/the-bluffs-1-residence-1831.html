# The Residence 1831 at The Bluffs I: 1,831 Sq Ft Single-Story 3-Bedroom from $485,990 The Residence 1831 is the largest plan at The Bluffs I by Century Communities at Lake Las Vegas. It is 1,831 square feet, 3 bedrooms, 2 baths, single-story, with a 2-car garage, from $485,990. It is the only true 3-bedroom plan in The Bluffs I lineup and the top of the floor plan range. ## What Makes the Residence 1831 Stand Out? The 1831 is the family-fit plan. It is the only Bluffs I option with three real bedrooms from day one, each with a closet and full window egress, which means it actually counts as a 3-bedroom home for resale. The extra 168 square feet over the 1742 goes into the third bedroom and the wider great room, not wasted hallway. Standard features include the Century Home Connect smart-home package, a 2-car garage, and the open kitchen, dining, and great room layout shared across all three Bluffs plans. The primary suite anchors the back of the home for quiet, with two secondary bedrooms and a shared bath up front. With 1,831 square feet on a single level, the 1831 is a fit for small families, hosts who keep a true guest room, and downsizers who refuse to give up the third bedroom. The 1831 is the right call when you need a real 3-bedroom now or you plan to resell to a family buyer down the road. If you only need 2 bedrooms, the [Residence 1663](www.rosehomeslv.com/blog/the-bluffs-1-residence-1663) saves you about $20,000. If you want flex space without a full third bedroom, the [Residence 1742](www.rosehomeslv.com/blog/the-bluffs-1-residence-1742) sits in the middle. ## The Bluffs I Community at a Glance The Bluffs I sits at 128 Barkset Way, Henderson, NV 89011, inside the guard-gated Lake Las Vegas resort. Phase I opened in March 2023 and is planned for 90 single-story homes total. Century Communities is the builder, a top-10 national builder traded on the NYSE under ticker CCS. The Strip is about 22 miles, a 25 to 30 minute drive via I-215 and I-515. Lake Las Vegas itself is a 320-acre lake at the center of a Mediterranean-themed resort. Amenities include 2 Jack Nicklaus golf courses (Reflection Bay public, SouthShore private), a Sports Club with tennis and pickleball, a white-sand beach, paddle boarding, kayaking, and the MonteLago Village dining and shopping district. Schools are Josh Stevens Elementary (rated 8 of 10), B. Mahlon Brown IB Middle, and Basic Academy IB High. For a full overview, see [The Bluffs I community guide](www.rosehomeslv.com/blog/the-bluffs-1). ## Pricing, HOA, and SID Costs Base prices at The Bluffs I run from $465,990 to $485,990 across the 3 plans. Quick move-in homes have recently listed in the $525,000 to $570,000 range with options and lot premiums. Lot premiums alone run from $3,000 to $75,000 depending on view and position. Current Century incentives include a 3.875% 30-year fixed FHA and VA rate through Inspire Home Loans, the Century-affiliated lender. The HOA structure is two layers. The Bluffs sub-association is about $244 per month and the Lake Las Vegas master HOA is about $153 per month, for a combined $397 per month. The Lake Las Vegas LID is about $1,020 per year, billed on the property tax statement. On the 1831 base price of $485,990 with 5 percent down and the 3.875% FHA rate, realistic all-in monthly cost lands near $3,160 per month with taxes, HOA, and LID baked in. ## How The Bluffs I Compares to Nearby New Construction The Bluffs I is the lowest-priced new single-story product inside the Lake Las Vegas guard gates. Verona by Taylor Morrison starts around $490,000 and is two-story. Verona by Beazer is in the high $400Ks, also two-story. Lago Del Sol by Lennar starts in the low $600Ks for larger plans. Tuscany Village sits about 3 miles outside the gates with no SID or LID and a much lower $175 per month HOA, but you trade the lake, beach, and Nicklaus golf access. The 1831 is the only single-story 3-bedroom new home inside the Lake Las Vegas gates under $500K. Comparable single-story 3-bedrooms next door at Lago Del Sol start about $115,000 higher.
