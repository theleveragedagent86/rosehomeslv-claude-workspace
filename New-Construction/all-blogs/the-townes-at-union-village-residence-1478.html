The Residence 1478 gives you 1,478 square feet across 2 stories, with 3 bedrooms, 2.5 baths, and a 2-car garage. The price per square foot is roughly $239, which is actually the best value per square foot of all 3 base plans at The Townes. Small families who want bonus second-floor space without paying for a full 3rd bedroom upgrade, remote workers who need a quiet office area, and value buyers who care about price per square foot.
