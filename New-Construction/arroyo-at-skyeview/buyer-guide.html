About
THE AFFORDABLE WAY INTO SKYE CANYON

Arroyo at Skyeview is the townhome neighborhood inside Skye Canyon, a 1,700-acre master plan in NW Las Vegas. Skye Canyon is known for its 8,000 sq ft fitness clubhouse, resort pool, trail system, and top-rated elementary school. Most homes in the master plan start north of $430K. Arroyo is the way in under $400K.

Century Communities is the sole builder. You get the same amenities, the same schools, and the same easy drive to Red Rock and US-95 as your single-family neighbors. The trade-off is a townhome footprint with shared walls and a max of 1,729 sq ft.

If you are a first-time buyer, an empty nester, or you simply want a low-maintenance new home in a master plan with serious amenities, Arroyo deserves a careful look. This guide walks you through everything you need to know before you visit or sign.

Fees
HOA, SID, AND FEES EXPLAINED

Arroyo Sub-HOA: about $485/quarter (around $162/month). This covers your townhome exterior, landscaping in common areas, and the day-to-day upkeep that single-family owners would handle themselves. This is what makes Arroyo a lock-and-leave product.

Skye Canyon Master HOA: about $250/quarter (around $83/month). This is the big one. It pays for Skye Fitness, the resort pool, splash pad, dog park, multiple community parks, and the trail system. Every Skye Canyon homeowner pays this, whether they own a $400K townhome or a $900K Toll Brothers home.

Total HOA: about $245/month. Higher than a single-family Skye Canyon home, but that extra money is buying you exterior maintenance.

SID (Special Improvement District): about $137.66/year, billed semi-annually ($68.83 in April and October). A SID is a tax that pays for public infrastructure like roads, water, and sewer. The remaining balance on Arroyo's SID is about $4,130, ending around 2055. There is a 3% penalty if you pay it off early.

Big advantage: Single-family homes in Skye Canyon carry SIDs of $2,400 to $4,800 per year. Arroyo townhomes have a tiny lot allocation, so the SID is one of the lowest in the master plan. That is a real, line-item savings of $200 to $400 per month compared to your single-family neighbors.

FAQ
FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
Can I use my own real estate agent at Arroyo at Skyeview?
Yes, absolutely. In fact, it is strongly recommended. Your agent must register with you on your first visit to the model home. Century pays the agent's commission, so it costs you nothing and you get someone in your corner who can read the contract and protect your interests.
How long does it take to build at Arroyo?
Most new townhomes here take about 6 to 8 months from contract to close. Quick move-in homes already under construction can deliver in weeks. Century is currently showing QMIs available July through September 2026.
Why is the HOA higher than other Skye Canyon homes?
Because Arroyo is a townhome. Your sub-HOA pays for exterior maintenance, common-area landscaping, and shared building upkeep that single-family owners handle themselves. The trade-off is a lock-and-leave lifestyle and lower maintenance time.
Is the SID really that much lower than single-family homes?
Yes. Arroyo's SID runs about $138 per year, compared to $2,400 to $4,800 per year on a single-family Skye Canyon home. That is one of the biggest hidden cost differences in the entire master plan and a real reason to consider Arroyo even if you can afford a larger home.
Can I negotiate the price on a new-construction townhome?
Builders rarely cut the base price because it affects comps for the rest of the community. What you can negotiate is incentives: rate buydowns, closing cost credits, or design center dollars. The current Arroyo incentive package is already strong, so the smart move is to make sure you get every dollar of it.
Do I have to use Century's preferred lender?
No, you can finance with any lender. But the 3.875% rate and the closing cost credit are tied to using Inspire Home Loans. Compare a second lender quote to confirm the package is real and competitive, then make your decision.
What warranty comes with a Century home?
Century typically provides a limited warranty covering workmanship and materials for one to two years, mechanical systems for two years, and major structural components for up to ten years. Ask for the full warranty document in writing before you sign.
Is Arroyo at Skyeview gated?
Confirm gating details directly with the Century sales team on your visit. Skye Canyon includes a mix of gated and non-gated neighborhoods, and access details can vary by section.
What if I want a single-family home in Skye Canyon instead?
Sierra at Skyeview and Terra at Skyeview are the single-family Century communities right next door. Both share the same schools and amenities, but starting prices are $433,990 and $489,990 and the SIDs run $200 to $400 more per month. Ask me to send the comparison side by side.
How are the resale values for townhomes in Skye Canyon?
Skye Canyon has held value well thanks to the master amenities, the Bryan Elementary rating, and the steady inventory shortage in NW Las Vegas under $400K. Townhome resales tend to move quickly because they hit a price point single-family homes cannot match. Ask for a current comp report before you write your offer.
