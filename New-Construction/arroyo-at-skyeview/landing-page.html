About

THE MOST
AFFORDABLE WAY
INTO SKYE CANYON

Arroyo at Skyeview is Century Communities' townhome enclave inside the Skye Canyon master plan in NW Las Vegas. Three two-story plans run from 1,531 to 1,729 sq ft, all with 3 bedrooms, 2.5 baths, and an attached 2-car garage, starting at $372,990.

It is the most affordable new construction in Skye Canyon, and the only attached townhome product in the master plan. You get the same Skye Fitness clubhouse, resort pool, parks, trails, and Aspire Coffee that the higher-priced Toll Brothers single-family buyers next door enjoy.

The SID is also unusually small, about $138 a year, compared to $2,400 to $4,800 a year for many single-family neighbors. That gap alone can change what you qualify for. Model address: 8912 Vanhoy Creek St, Las Vegas, NV 89166.
