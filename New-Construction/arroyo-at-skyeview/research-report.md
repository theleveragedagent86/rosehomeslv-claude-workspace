### Best For Summary

**Arroyo at Skyeview** is best for first-time buyers, young professionals, and downsizers who want a brand-new home in a top-rated master plan without the single-family price tag. The low SID, builder incentives, and lock-and-leave townhome lifestyle make it the most accessible entry point into Skye Canyon.

**Sierra at Skyeview** is best for buyers who want a small, detached single-family home in the same Skyeview pocket. You get a private yard and no shared walls, but you pay roughly $60K-$90K more and take on a higher Skye Canyon SID.

**Terra at Skyeview** is best for move-up buyers who need more square footage and a true single-family home in the $490K-$520K range. It hits the sweet spot between Sierra and the Toll Brothers price jump.

**Toll Brothers Paloma** is best for luxury buyers who want a gated enclave, larger floor plans, and Toll's higher-end finishes while staying in Skye Canyon. Private amenity center plus master plan access is the differentiator.

**Toll Brothers Vista Rossa** is best for high-end buyers and larger families who need 3,000+ sq ft, multi-generational layouts, or 4-5 bedroom homes in Skye Canyon. Best price-per-sq-ft among the luxury tier.

### Key Takeaways

- **Most affordable new construction in Skye Canyon.** Arroyo's $372K-$424K range is roughly $60K-$270K below every other active Skye Canyon offering, making it the only sub-$430K new build in the master plan.
- **Dramatically lower carrying costs.** Arroyo's ~$138/year SID is a fraction of the $2,400-$4,800/year that Skye Canyon single-family homes carry. That gap offsets most of the higher sub-HOA dues.
- **Same elite amenities and schools as $900K neighbors.** Buyers get Skye Fitness, the resort pool, Aspire Coffee, Bryan Elementary (9/10), and Shadow Ridge High at the lowest entry price in the master plan.
- **Quick move-ins with active incentives.** Arroyo is the only community on the list with documented rate buydowns, ~$5K closing cost help, and 3+ QMI homes ready in mid-to-late 2026.
- **Trade-off: townhome product and smaller footprint.** Arroyo tops out at 1,729 sq ft, 3 bed/2.5 bath, and shared walls. Buyers needing 4+ bedrooms, a private yard, or 2,000+ sq ft must step up to Sierra, Terra, or Toll Brothers.
