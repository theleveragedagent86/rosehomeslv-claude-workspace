About

A WALKABLE
HEALTH CAMPUS
IN HENDERSON

The Townes at Union Village is a new construction townhome community by Century Communities, the sole builder, tucked inside Henderson's 170-acre Union Village master plan. Union Village is a one-of-a-kind health-themed development anchored by Henderson Hospital, with more than 50 medical offices and surgery centers across the campus. The Townes grand opening was September 2024, with deliveries continuing through 2027.

The community will hold 80+ two-story townhomes across three Century Communities floor plans, 1,405 to 1,479 sq ft, every home a 3 bedroom, 2.5 bath layout with a 2-car garage. Pricing starts at $349,990, one of the few sub-$400K new construction options inside Henderson with strong incentives still on the table.

What makes Union Village different is the walkability. Residents can walk to Henderson Hospital, the Cowabunga Bay Water Park sits a short stroll away, and the Galleria at Sunset is about two miles up the road. Inside the community itself you get a dog park, a walking trail, and picnic areas. US-95 puts the Strip about 15 miles, 20 to 25 minutes, from the front door.
