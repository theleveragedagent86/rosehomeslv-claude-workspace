## 3. Per-Plan Blog Guidance (passed to Blog Writer)

### Title pattern

`Residence [number] at Townes at Union Village | [sqft] Sqft Henderson Townhome` (target 50 to 60 chars)

Examples:
- `Residence 1405 at Townes at Union Village Henderson NV` (54 chars)
- `Residence 1478 Floor Plan | Townes at Union Village` (51 chars)
- `Residence 1479 Townhome by Century | Union Village NV` (53 chars)

### Description pattern

`[Plan name] is a [sqft] sqft, 3BR / 2.5BA townhome by Century Communities at The Townes at Union Village in Henderson, NV, priced from $[price]. [Differentiator]. Ryan Rose, Real Broker LLC.`

Target 140 to 160 chars. Differentiator should rotate: smart home tech, architectural elevation options, proximity to Henderson Hospital, walkability to Cowabunga Bay.

### Per-plan keyword formulas

- Primary: `Residence [number] Century Communities`, `Townes at Union Village [number] sqft`, `[sqft] sqft townhome Henderson`
- Secondary: `Century Communities floor plans Henderson`, `3 bedroom townhome Union Village`, `Henderson NV townhome under $[ceiling]`
- Long-tail: `Residence [number] floor plan Henderson NV`, `Century Home Connect smart townhome Henderson`, `townhome near Henderson Hospital`

### Blog Writer instruction

For each of the three plans (1405, 1478, 1479), write a 1,000 to 1,400 word blog post following the patterns above. H2 structure must include: floor plan walkthrough, who this plan is for (buyer persona), architectural elevation choices (Desert Contemporary, Spanish Mission, Tuscan), included features (Century Home Connect, granite, tankless water heater), Union Village location benefits, schools (Harriet Treem ES, Francis Cortney JH, Basic Academy of International Studies HS), and how it compares to Symmetry Trails at Cadence and Alderidge at Cadence. Use Article schema with author Ryan Rose. Internal link to the community LP, the master directory, and two of the reference blogs listed in section 6. No em-dashes.
