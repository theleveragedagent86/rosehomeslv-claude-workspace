# Content Rules & Verification Standards

## Formatting Rules

### Punctuation — CRITICAL
- **NEVER use emdashes (—) or regular dashes (-) in post content**
- Use colons, semicolons, commas, or restructure sentences instead
- Parentheses are acceptable for asides
- Hyphens in slugs are the ONE exception (slug formatting only, not prose)

### Headers
- H2 for main sections within the post
- H3 for subsections if needed
- Keep headers descriptive and keyword-rich

### Style
- Conversational but professional tone
- Active voice preferred
- Short paragraphs: 3-4 sentences max
- No bullet points in main content unless listing specific amenities
- 400-600 words per post (target the middle of this range)
- No filler — every sentence must serve the reader

---

## Topic Specificity Rules

Each blog post must cover ONE very specific subject. Never combine topics.

| Correct (specific) | Incorrect (too broad) |
|---|---|
| "Dog Parks in Southern Highlands" | "Parks and Recreation in Southern Highlands" |
| "Italian Restaurants Near Summerlin" | "Dining in Summerlin" |
| "Exploration Peak Park" | "Parks in Henderson" |
| "Del E Webb Middle School Review" | "Schools in Southern Highlands" |
| "Southern Highlands Home Prices 2026" | "Real Estate in Southern Highlands" |

## Content Verification Requirements

### All facts must be verifiable before inclusion.

**Before writing, verify using these sources:**
1. Official HOA and community websites
2. City of Las Vegas / Henderson / North Las Vegas official sites
3. Clark County records
4. Nevada state resources
5. Established business websites (restaurants, attractions)
6. Google Maps for location verification
7. Reputable news sources (Las Vegas Review-Journal, etc.)

**Research process for each topic:**
- Search Google for "[Neighborhood] Las Vegas" → capture all PAA questions
- Search Google autocomplete suggestions for the neighborhood name
- Note "Related Searches" at the bottom of results
- Search for the official community/HOA website

### What NOT to Include
- Unverified statistics or claims
- Guessed or estimated information
- Outdated data presented as current
- Information that cannot be sourced
- Prices or rates that change frequently (unless clearly dated with current year)

## Ryan Rose Positioning

Ryan Rose should appear naturally in every post. Guidelines:

- Include exactly one "Local Insight" paragraph featuring Ryan Rose
- Position as trusted local expert, not as a salesperson
- Phrases that work:
  - "As a Las Vegas real estate specialist, Ryan Rose often recommends..."
  - "Ryan Rose has helped dozens of families find homes in [neighborhood]..."
  - "According to Ryan Rose, who has worked extensively in [neighborhood]..."
- Call to action should be soft and helpful, not pushy
