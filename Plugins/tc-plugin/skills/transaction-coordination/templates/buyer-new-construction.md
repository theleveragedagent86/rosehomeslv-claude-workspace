# Buyer — New Construction Email Templates

All templates use `[BRACKET]` variables. Fill in with real transaction data before sending.
No signature block included — Ryan's Gmail signature is applied automatically.

---

## Template 1: Accepted Offer Email to Buyer

**Subject:** `[PROPERTY ADDRESS] - Accepted Offer`
**To:** `[BUYER NAME]` at `[BUYER EMAIL]`
**Attachment:** Purchase Agreement PDF
**Trigger:** Day 0 — immediately on deal intake

Hello [BUYER NAME],

Thank you for trusting me with your real estate transaction. I am committed to making this process as smooth as possible, and your satisfaction is my number one priority.

Below is a brief summary of your transaction timeline:

- Offer is negotiated/accepted
- Earnest money must be deposited
- Title and lender will gather required documentation to get started
- Sign disclosures
- HOA documents provided and reviewed (if applicable; may be done upfront for new homes)
- Loan documents should be ready within the week of closing. I will coordinate and attend the signing. Please let me know if you are contacted by lender or title directly about a signing appointment so I can be sure to attend.
- Final walkthrough
- Closing

If at any time during this process you have questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to reach out to me directly.

Thank you again for your business, and I look forward to a successful closing!
