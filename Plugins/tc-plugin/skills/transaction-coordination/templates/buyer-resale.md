# Buyer — Resale Email Templates

Most buyer-resale email templates follow the same structure as `buyer-new-construction.md`, with these key differences:
- Seller disclosures (SRPD, Construction Defect Claims) are delivered by the seller
- HOA resale package comes from the seller
- Repair negotiations happen after home inspection
- No builder rep — all communication is direct with the listing agent / escrow / lender

No signature block included — Ryan's Gmail signature is applied automatically.

---

## Template R1: Accepted Offer Email to Buyer

**Subject:** `[PROPERTY ADDRESS] - Accepted Offer`
**To:** `[BUYER NAME]` at `[BUYER EMAIL]`
**CC:** `[REFERRING AGENT NAME]` at `[REFERRING AGENT EMAIL]` (if applicable)
**Trigger:** Day 0 — immediately on deal intake

Hello [BUYER FIRST NAME],

Thank you for trusting me with your real estate transaction. I am committed to making this process as smooth as possible, and your satisfaction is my number one priority.

Below is a brief summary of your transaction timeline:

- Offer accepted [ACCEPTANCE DATE]
- Earnest Money Deposit ($[EMD AMOUNT]) due [EMD DEADLINE] via wire to [ESCROW COMPANY]
- Seller disclosures (SRPD + Construction Defect Claims) delivered by [DISCLOSURE DEADLINE]
- Home inspection + termite inspection scheduled during Due Diligence (deadline [DUE DILIGENCE DEADLINE])
- HOA resale package delivered (you have a 5 calendar day cancellation right after receipt)
- Appraisal contingency deadline [APPRAISAL DEADLINE]
- Loan contingency deadline [LOAN CONTINGENCY DEADLINE]
- Final walkthrough window [WALKTHROUGH WINDOW START] – [WALKTHROUGH WINDOW END]
- Close of Escrow: [COE DATE]
- Loan documents should be ready within the week of closing. I will coordinate and attend the signing. Please let me know if you are contacted by lender or title directly about a signing appointment so I can be sure to attend.

If at any time during this process you have questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to reach out to me directly.

Thank you again for your business, and I look forward to a successful closing!

## Template R4: SRPD Delivery to Buyer

**Subject:** `[PROPERTY ADDRESS] - Seller Real Property Disclosure (SRPD)`
**To:** `[BUYER NAME]` at `[BUYER EMAIL]`
**CC:** `[REFERRING AGENT NAME]` at `[REFERRING AGENT EMAIL]` (if applicable)
**Attachment:** `SRPD.pdf` (from listing `Legal Docs/` folder)
**Trigger:** Event — SRPD received from seller / signed on seller side

Hello [BUYER FIRST NAME],

Attached please find the Seller Real Property Disclosure (SRPD) for [PROPERTY ADDRESS], as required under NRS 113.130.

Please review the disclosure carefully. Under Nevada law, you have the right to rescind the purchase agreement if the SRPD contains information that is material to your decision to purchase the property. Please let me know if you have any questions or concerns after reviewing.

The Construction Defect Claims Disclosure (NRS 40.688) will be sent separately once it is signed by the seller.

Our Due Diligence deadline is [DUE DILIGENCE DEADLINE].

Please let me know if you have any questions.
