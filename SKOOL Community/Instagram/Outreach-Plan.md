# Instagram Outreach Plan — @theleveragedagent

## Objective
Build a following of real estate agents on @theleveragedagent by executing a targeted Instagram outreach strategy. The goal is to attract realtors who want to leverage AI, automation, and systems to scale their business — and funnel them into The Leveraged Agent Skool community.

---

## Target Audience
- Licensed real estate agents (solo agents, team leads, small teams)
- Agents posting actively on Instagram (1–5 posts/week)
- Agents in growth mode: 1–5 years in the business, hungry to scale
- Agents already showing interest in tech, automation, AI, or productivity
- Geographic focus: start with Las Vegas market, then expand nationally

## Phase 3: Outreach Engine (Daily Routine)

### Daily Actions (30–45 min/day)

#### 1. Engage First (15 min)
- Comment on 20–30 posts from target realtors
- Comments must add value — no generic "love this!" comments
- Use the SOAP framework:
  - **S**pecific — reference something in their post
  - **O**pinion — share a perspective or insight
  - **A**sk — end with a question to start a conversation
  - **P**ersonal — make it feel human, not templated

#### 2. DM Warm Outreach (10 min)
- DM 5–10 agents who engaged with your content or whose content you engaged with
- Never pitch on first DM — start a conversation
- DM flow:
  1. **Opener**: Reference their content or something specific about their business
  2. **Bridge**: Find common ground (market, approach, challenge)
  3. **Value drop**: Share a resource, prompt, or tip relevant to them
  4. **Soft invite**: "I run a free community for agents using AI — want the link?"

#### 3. Follow Target Accounts (5 min)
- Follow 10–15 new target agents per day
- Sources: hashtag searches, competitor followers, local agent accounts
- Unfollow non-engagers after 7 days

#### 4. Story Engagement (5 min)
- Reply to 5–10 agent stories with genuine reactions or questions
- Use polls, quizzes, and question stickers in your own stories
