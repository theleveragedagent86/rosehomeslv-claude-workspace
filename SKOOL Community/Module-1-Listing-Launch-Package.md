# Module 1: Listing Launch Package — The Leveraged Agent

## Module Overview

This is the flagship module. Everything from photos-received to listing-fully-live, handled in one coordinated workflow. Members who complete Module 0 have the building blocks (Voice Profile, listing description, social multiplier, email templates). Module 1 stacks all of that into a single, repeatable **Listing Launch** that takes **15-20 minutes instead of 3-4 hours**.

**The core promise:** "I get new listing photos. Twenty minutes later, the listing is fully launched — MLS-ready description, coming-soon campaign, sphere email, neighbor letters, open house promo, social blast, agent outreach. All deployed. All in my voice."

**Who this is for:** Listing agents. Team leads with new inventory. Anyone whose biggest time sink is launching new listings.

**Prerequisite:** **Module 0 must be completed first.** Every lesson in this module builds directly on the Voice Profile, listing description system, social multiplier, and email templates built in Module 0. Trying to do this without that foundation will not work.

**Design philosophy (same as Module 0):**
- Every lesson produces a real, deployable asset
- Screen-share walkthroughs using Ryan's actual listings
- Every lesson ends with a "Post your result in Wins & Results" CTA
- In Cowork: each workflow is a reusable skill/command. In browser: copy-paste templates provided
- **Module 1 unlocks when Module 0 is marked complete.** Once unlocked, all 10 lessons inside Module 1 are available at once — no gates between lessons.

## Module 1 Lessons (10 Total — All Unlocked Once Module 0 Is Complete)

### Lesson 1: The 20-Minute Listing Launch (Overview)
**Format:** Talking head + simple diagram | 8-10 min
**Outcome:** Member understands the full launch sequence and why each piece matters

**Covers:**
- The full launch workflow — what "launched" means (description live, coming soon campaign sent, sphere emailed, neighbors mailed, open house promoted, agents reached, social blasted)
- The old way: 3-4 hours of desk work per listing
- The new way: 20 minutes, everything deployed
- How Module 0 feeds into this: Voice Profile, listing description, social multiplier, email templates all reused
- Preview of the 10 lessons and what each one adds to the stack
- The "launch kit" concept — files you maintain per listing (photos, data sheet, unique selling points)

---

### Lesson 2: The Pre-Launch 24-Hour Checklist
**Format:** Screen recording | 10-12 min
**Downloadable:** Pre-Launch Checklist PDF + Data Sheet Template
**Outcome:** Member has a deployable 24-hour prep ritual they run on every listing

**Covers:**
- What to gather BEFORE you touch any workflow:
  - Photos (named and organized)
  - Property data sheet (beds/baths/sqft/features/updates)
  - Seller's "why" — what makes THIS home special to them (AI can't know this)
  - Neighborhood context (schools, walkability, upcoming developments)
  - Pricing strategy + comparables
- **In Cowork:** save all of this to a listing project folder. Cowork pulls automatically
- **Browser users:** use a Google Doc template provided
- Why the data sheet is the single most important document — garbage in, garbage out
- Demo: Ryan builds a real data sheet for a real listing on screen

---

### Lesson 3: The Coming Soon Campaign
**Format:** Screen recording | 10-12 min
**Downloadable:** Coming Soon Prompt + 3-Post Social Sequence Template
**Outcome:** Member has a 5-7 day pre-launch buzz campaign ready to deploy

**Covers:**
- Why coming-soon matters: builds anticipation, pre-qualifies interest, gets agent calls before MLS
- The 3-post social sequence:
  1. "Something's coming" teaser (5 days out)
  2. Detail reveal — neighborhood, style, 1 standout feature (3 days out)
  3. "Goes live tomorrow" urgency (1 day out)
- Sphere email: "Before this hits the market..."
- Agent DM template: early access to preview showings
- Prompt generates all 3 social posts + 1 email + 1 agent DM in under 5 minutes
- Demo on real listing

---

### Lesson 4: The Full Listing Write-Up (MLS + Public + Agent Remarks)
**Format:** Screen recording | 12-15 min
**Downloadable:** Full Listing Write-Up Prompt
**Outcome:** Three polished remarks ready to paste into MLS

**Covers:**
- Module 0 taught the basic description — this goes deeper
- Three separate outputs, each with different rules:
  - **Public Remarks** (250 words max, consumer-facing, lifestyle-forward, no jargon)
  - **Agent Remarks** (professional, factual, disclosures, showing instructions, co-op structure)
  - **MLS Headline / Title Tag** (60 chars max, keyword-optimized for IDX search)
- Compliance: what NEVER to include (Fair Housing language, unverified claims, subjective superlatives)
- Using Voice Profile for public remarks, neutral-professional for agent remarks
- Demo: all three generated in one prompt, reviewed on screen

---

### Lesson 5: The Launch Day Social Blast
**Format:** Screen recording | 10-12 min
**Downloadable:** Launch Day Social Prompt (expanded from Module 0)
**Outcome:** 7-10 platform-specific posts ready to schedule for launch day

**Covers:**
- This is Module 0's social multiplier on steroids — expanded from 5 posts to 7-10:
  - Instagram feed post
  - Instagram Reel script
  - Instagram Story sequence (3 slides)
  - Facebook business page post
  - Facebook personal profile post (different tone)
  - LinkedIn post (agent-to-agent professional)
  - Agent Facebook group post
  - TikTok script (if applicable)
- Scheduling all 7-10 in Meta Business Suite in one sitting
- Timing strategy: which post goes out when on launch day
- Demo on real listing, actual scheduling queue shown

---

### Lesson 6: The Sphere Email Sequence
**Format:** Screen recording | 10-12 min
**Downloadable:** 3-Email Sphere Sequence Prompt
**Outcome:** 3 emails ready to send, targeted at different segments

**Covers:**
- Not one email — a 3-email sequence to your database:
  1. **Sphere blast** (personal, "here's what I just listed")
  2. **Past buyer follow-up** (targeted to past clients in the area who might know someone)
  3. **Investor / repeat-client email** (if applicable)
- Segmentation: how to split your database and send the right email to each group
- Subject line optimization (3 options per email, A/B test if possible)
- Demo: all 3 generated, segmented, and scheduled in Gmail/BombBomb/whatever they use

---

### Lesson 7: Neighbor Letters & Direct Mail
**Format:** Screen recording | 10-12 min
**Downloadable:** Neighbor Letter Prompt + Vendor Recommendations
**Outcome:** Personalized neighbor letter ready to mail + vendor to print/mail

**Covers:**
- Why neighbor letters still work in 2026 — and why AI makes them scalable
- Two letter types:
  1. **"Your neighbor just listed" letter** (to 100-250 surrounding homes)
  2. **"Just sold in your neighborhood" follow-up** (sent after closing with sale price)
- Personalization at scale — using AI to tailor the letter to the specific neighborhood (mention the park down the street, the new restaurant, the school district)
- Vendor walkthrough: ProspectsPLUS, Wise Pelican, or local printer — pick one, show how to upload
- Compliance: what must be on the envelope/letter (broker info, license, disclaimers)
- Demo: Ryan generates a real neighbor letter for a real listing

---

### Lesson 8: The Open House Marketing System
**Format:** Screen recording | 10-12 min
**Downloadable:** Open House Promo Kit Prompt
**Outcome:** Full open house promo kit ready to deploy

**Covers:**
- What's in the kit:
  - Social posts (IG, FB, Reels) — 3 promo posts leading up to the weekend
  - Email to sphere + past clients in the area
  - Text message template for hot leads
  - Yard sign / directional sign copy
  - Open house sign-in sheet welcome script
  - Post-open house thank-you / follow-up email for everyone who signed in
- Timing: when to post each piece
- The follow-up system: most agents nail the promo but drop the ball on the 48 hours AFTER
- Demo: full kit generated for a real open house Ryan is running

---

### Lesson 9: Agent-to-Agent Outreach
**Format:** Screen recording | 8-10 min
**Downloadable:** Buyer Agent Outreach Prompt + Agent Group Post Template
**Outcome:** System for getting buyer agents to actually show the listing

**Covers:**
- Why this is the most underrated launch move — your listing lives or dies by buyer agents knowing about it
- Three outreach assets:
  1. **Agent Facebook group post** (professional, mentions co-op, highlights buyer fit)
  2. **Direct DM / email to your top 20 buyer agents** (personal, short, specific about the buyer profile)
  3. **Brokerage office announcement** (internal listing to your own office first)
- The "ideal buyer" profile — describe who this house is perfect for (Claude helps write it)
- Follow-up rhythm: when and how to re-engage agents who showed interest
- Demo: Ryan runs the outreach on a real listing

**Format:** Screen recording | 12-15 min

**Outcome:** Single command that runs the entire launch sequence

**Covers:**
- The payoff — everything connected in Cowork as a single skill

  - Coming soon 3-post sequence
  - Launch day 7-10 social posts
  - 3-email sphere sequence
  - Neighbor letter
  - Open house promo kit
  - Agent outreach assets
- Walkthrough of installing the skill

- Demo: Ryan runs a real listing launch start-to-finish on screen in 20 minutes
