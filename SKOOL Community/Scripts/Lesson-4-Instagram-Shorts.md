### SHORT 2 — THE QUICK WIN

**Script:**
Here's a 30-second way to find out if your AI content is garbage. Pull up the last thing AI wrote for you — listing description, email, whatever. Now read it out loud. Not in your head. Out loud. If you hit a single word you would never actually say in a conversation, your AI doesn't know your voice. That's the problem. You're posting content that sounds like it could've come from literally any agent in America. The fix is dead simple — give AI three to five examples of things you've actually written. Old emails. Social posts. MLS descriptions you did yourself. Let it study how you talk. Then test it again. Night and day difference.
