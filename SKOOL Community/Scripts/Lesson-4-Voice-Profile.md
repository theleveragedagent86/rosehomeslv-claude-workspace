**WHAT IS A VOICE PROFILE (1:30–2:15)**

Real quick: A Voice Profile is just a short paragraph — we're talking maybe a hundred, hundred fifty words — that describes how you write. Your tone. Your word choices. Your personality on the page. Think of it as a cheat sheet you hand to Claude that says, "Hey, this is how I talk. Match this."

And then every time you write a prompt, you paste that paragraph in. That's it. That's the whole trick. Claude reads it, adjusts its output, and suddenly the content it gives you actually sounds like something you'd post. Not something a robot would post. Not something your competitor would post. Something *you* would post.

Simple concept. Massive impact. Let me show you how to build one.

Number three — social media. Go to your Facebook or Instagram, find posts where you were actually writing captions. Not resharing articles — posts where you were talking to your audience.

And number four — even text messages work. If you've got a long text where you were explaining something to a client, screenshot it, type it out. That counts.
