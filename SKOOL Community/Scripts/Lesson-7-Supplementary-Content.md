## 3. INSTAGRAM CAPTION

Stop trying to come up with a post every morning. That is the fastest way to be inconsistent on social media.

The agents who actually show up every day are not more creative than you. They are not spending more time than you. They batch. They sit down once a week, plan all seven days, and then they just publish. The creating is already done.

Here is the weekly framework I use. Monday is a market stat. Tuesday is a listing. Wednesday is behind the scenes. Thursday is a tip. Friday is a community spotlight. Saturday is open house or showings. Sunday is personal. That mix is intentional — seven listing posts in a row kills your engagement. Personal and educational posts get you followed. Listing posts sell. You need both.

I run this whole framework through Claude with my Voice Profile and it writes all seven posts in about sixty seconds. Then I schedule everything in Meta Business Suite for the week. Total time start to finish is around fifteen minutes.

If seven feels like a lot, start with three. Monday, Wednesday, Friday. Consistency beats volume every single time. Three posts a week, every week, puts you ahead of most agents in your market.
