# SKOOL Community: Step 1 — Full Group Setup Plan

## Context

Building a premium ($86/month) SKOOL community teaching real estate professionals how to use Claude and Make.com to eliminate repetitive work. This plan covers the complete Step 1: setting up the SKOOL group from the ground up — name, branding, about page, community feed, guidelines, and weekly call setup.

**Key decisions already made:**
- Pricing: $86/month premium
- Tools: Claude (primary), Make.com (secondary)
- Tone: Approachable & practical ("I'm a working agent like you")
- Branding: Starting fresh — no existing assets
- Content format: Mix (screen recordings, talking head, written guides)
- Name: Must be unique — avoid "AI Agent" clichés that realtors hear constantly

### Membership Intake Questions (Asked at Signup)

SKOOL lets you ask questions when someone applies to join. At $86/month, these serve two purposes: qualifying members and personalizing their experience.

**Question 1:** "What's your role in real estate?"
- Options: Residential Agent / Team Lead / Solo Agent / Lender / Commercial Agent / Escrow-Title / Other
- Purpose: Segments your audience so you can tailor communication

**Question 2:** "What's your #1 time-wasting task you'd love to automate?"
- Free text
- Purpose: Gives you content ideas AND lets you send a personalized welcome DM

**Question 3:** "How would you rate your comfort with AI tools?"
- Options: Never used them / Tried a few times / Use them occasionally / Use them daily
- Purpose: Helps you understand your audience mix and calibrate content difficulty

## 4. About Page (Long-Form Sales Copy)

The SKOOL About page is your sales page. It supports text, images, and video. No character limit on the long description.

### Recommended About Page Structure

**Section 1: Hero / Opening Hook**
Lead with the pain point your audience already feels, then present the solution.

Draft:
> You got into real estate to help people and build something — not to spend 60% of your week on listing descriptions, MLS data entry, social media posts, and follow-up emails.
>
> What if you could get all of that done in a fraction of the time — using AI tools that are already available, you just haven't been shown how to use them for real estate?
>
> That's what The Leveraged Agent is.

**Section 2: What This Is (and What It Isn't)**
Set expectations. Differentiate from the generic AI hype.

Draft:
> This isn't a prompt library. It's not "50 ChatGPT tricks for realtors."
>
> This is a step-by-step system for building real workflows that handle the repetitive parts of your business — listing launch packages, MLS input, social media content, transaction management — so you can focus on the parts that actually make you money.
>
> Every workflow taught here is one I use in my own real estate business, every week.

**Section 3: What's Inside (Bullet List)**
Concrete deliverables that justify $86/month.

Draft:
> Inside you get:
> - Step-by-step video tutorials for building AI-powered real estate workflows
> - Ready-to-use templates for listing launches, email campaigns, social content, and more
> - A complete "Start Here" path that gets your first workflow running in 10 minutes
> - Weekly live Q&A calls where you can get help with your specific setup
> - A private community of agents building and sharing automations
> - New workflows and templates added regularly based on member requests

**Section 4: Who This Is For**
Qualify your audience (and disqualify tire-kickers).

Draft:
> This is for you if:
> - You're a real estate agent or team lead who's tired of spending hours on admin work
> - You've heard about AI but haven't figured out how to make it useful for your actual business
> - You want real workflows, not theory — things you can set up this week and use immediately
> - You're willing to invest time learning tools that will save you 10x that time going forward
>
> This is probably not for you if you're looking for get-rich-quick tactics or aren't willing to put in the initial setup time to make these systems work.

**Section 5: About the Founder (Short Bio)**
Establishes credibility through practice, not credentials.

Draft:
> I'm [your name], a licensed real estate agent in [market]. I started using AI to automate my own business after getting tired of spending entire days on listing prep, MLS entry, and social media.
>
> Now my listing launch packages take minutes instead of hours. My MLS input is streamlined. My social media runs on systems I built once. I teach exactly what I use — nothing theoretical, nothing I haven't tested in my own practice.

**Section 6: Call to Action**
The join button is always visible on SKOOL, but reinforce the action.

Draft:
> Join The Leveraged Agent for $86/month and start building the systems that give you your time back. Your first workflow will be running within 10 minutes of joining.

## 6. Community Guidelines

Draft (keep short — nobody reads long rules):

> **The Leveraged Agent Guidelines**
>
> **1. Share what works.** Post your wins, your workflows, your results. This is how we all get better.
>
> **2. Ask questions freely.** No question is too basic. Use the Q&A category and include enough detail that others can help you.
>
> **3. Help when you can.** If you see a question you can answer, jump in. Points are awarded for helping others.
>
> **4. No self-promotion.** Don't pitch your services, courses, or tools to other members. This isn't a lead gen group.
>
> **5. Keep it practical.** We're here to build and deploy workflows, not debate AI philosophy. If you can't use it this week, save it.
>
> **6. Respect the community.** Be professional. Disagreements are fine — personal attacks aren't.
>
> Breaking rules 4 or 6 = removal. No warnings for spam.

## 8. Gamification / Leaderboard Levels

SKOOL lets you customize level names and unlock content at specific levels. This drives engagement and creates natural upsell touchpoints.

| Level | Points | Name | Unlock |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 0 | New Member | Access to Start Here module + community feed |
| 2 | 100 | Builder | Access to all classroom modules |
| 3 | 500 | Operator | Access to bonus templates/resources |
| 4 | 1,000 | Automator | Access to call replays library |
| 5 | 2,500 | Systems Pro | Badge + invitation to provide feedback on new modules before release |

**Points are awarded for:** Posting (wins, Q&A), commenting, completing course modules, attending live calls, participating in challenges.

Note: At $86/month, don't gate too much content behind levels. Members paying premium expect access. Use levels for *bonus* content and recognition, not core content restriction. Consider starting with levels 1-2 fully open and only gating bonus materials.

## 9. Branding Direction (For Cover Image & Icon Design)

**Tone:** Approachable & practical — clean and professional but not corporate. Should feel like a tool, not a classroom.

**Color palette direction:**
- Primary: A confident, modern blue or deep teal (trust, professionalism — familiar to real estate)
- Accent: White or light gray (clean, readable)
- Pop color: A warm accent (amber, coral, or green) for CTAs and highlights
- Avoid: Neon/techy colors that scream "crypto bro" — your audience is professional agents

**Typography direction:**
- Clean sans-serif (Inter, Plus Jakarta Sans, or similar)
- Headline weight: Bold but not heavy
- Body: Regular weight, high readability

**Icon concept:**
- Simple geometric mark or lettermark based on community name
- Must be recognizable at 128×128px (small on mobile)
- One or two colors maximum

**Cover image concept:**
- Community name prominently displayed
- Subtitle: positioning statement or key promise
- Clean background — gradient, subtle pattern, or lifestyle real estate imagery (tasteful, not stock-photo cheesy)
- Optional: Small mockup showing what the classroom looks like inside

**Tool for creation:** Canva (free) is sufficient for initial versions. Can upgrade to professional design once the community is validated.
