### EMAIL 25 — Day 337 | Category: Agent Value & Process
**Delay:** 337 days
**Subject:** How to pick the right listing agent

Hey #lead_first_name#,

When the time comes to sell, choosing the right agent is one of the most important decisions you'll make. Here are a few things worth looking for.

**Local expertise.** Your agent should know your neighborhood, not just your zip code. They should be able to tell you what's sold nearby, what's active, and what buyers in your area are looking for.

**A real marketing plan.** Ask them exactly how they'll get your home in front of buyers. Vague answers like "we'll put it on the MLS" aren't enough.

**Communication style.** You want someone who keeps you in the loop without you having to chase them down. Ask how often they'll update you and how they prefer to communicate.

**Negotiation track record.** Selling isn't just about finding a buyer. It's about getting you the best possible terms. Ask how they handle multiple offers, inspection requests, and appraisal issues.

**Honest pricing.** Be cautious with agents who quote the highest price just to win your listing. A good agent prices based on data, not flattery.
