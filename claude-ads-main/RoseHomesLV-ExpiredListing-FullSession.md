### Research Conducted

I performed extensive research across multiple sources:

1. **Meta Ad Library** — Searched for active Las Vegas real estate advertisers running seller-focused ads
2. **Google Ads Transparency Center** — Analyzed who's bidding on expired listing and seller keywords
3. **Competitor websites and social media** — Reviewed Huntington & Ellis, Simply Vegas, solo agent teams

### Key Findings

1. **Most competitors focus on buyer-side ads.** Seller-focused advertising is dramatically underrepresented, especially for expired listings and home valuations.
2. **iBuyers dominate Google seller keywords.** Opendoor, Offerpad, and cash-buyer companies own "sell my house fast" searches. Traditional agents rarely compete here.
3. **Social media is mostly organic, not paid.** Most Las Vegas agents post "just listed" and "just sold" content organically but very few run paid Meta campaigns targeting sellers.
4. **Expired listing advertising is nearly nonexistent.** No Las Vegas agents appear to be running paid digital ads specifically targeting expired listings. This is your open lane.
5. **Video and UGC content are underutilized.** Most competitor ads use static images. Agents using video (especially personal, on-camera content) see dramatically higher engagement.
