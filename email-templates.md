# Ryan's Email Templates

Five reusable templates for the workflows you run constantly. Merge fields use `[Bracket Format]` so they drop cleanly into Lofty, Kit, or Gmail. Swap signatures, links, and property details as needed.

Standard signature block (use on all unless noted):

```
Ryan Rose
REALTOR®, Rose Homes LV
702.XXX.XXXX
ryan@rosehomeslv.com
rosehomeslv.com
```

## 1. New Listing Announcement

**Audience:** Sphere, database, past clients in the area
**Goal:** Awareness, referrals, and a quiet ask for "anyone you know who'd love this"
**Send timing:** Day of going live in MLS, before 9am if possible

**Subject line options:**
- Just listed in [Neighborhood]: [Bedrooms]BR / [Baths]BA at [Price]
- New on the market in [Neighborhood] (you saw it here first)
- Quietly listed today in [Neighborhood], thought of you

**Body:**

```
Hi [First Name],

Quick one. We just brought a new listing to market in [Neighborhood], and you came to mind.

[Property Address]
[Bedrooms] bed, [Baths] bath, [SqFt] sqft
Listed at [Price]
[1 sentence hook: standout feature, view, lot, recent reno, school zone, etc.]

[2 to 3 sentence description. Keep it human, not MLS-speak. Example: "Single story on a corner lot, primary suite is on the back of the house overlooking the pool, and the kitchen actually has the storage you'd expect at this price point. The seller put in new HVAC last summer."]

Photos and full details here: [Listing URL]

If anyone you know is hunting in [Neighborhood] or has been waiting for the right [single story / pool home / view lot / under-$X home], I'd love an introduction. And if you want a private walkthrough before this weekend's open house, just reply with a time that works.

Talk soon,
Ryan
```

## 2. Open House Invitation

**Audience:** Sphere, database, past clients, and anyone who's previously asked about the area
**Goal:** Foot traffic Saturday or Sunday
**Send timing:** Thursday afternoon or Friday morning works best

**Subject line options:**
- Open house this [Saturday / Sunday] in [Neighborhood]
- Come see [Street Name] before it's gone
- Coffee and a house tour? [Neighborhood], [Day] [Time]

**Body:**

```
Hi [First Name],

Hosting an open house this weekend at one of my listings in [Neighborhood], and I'd love to see you there.

[Property Address]
[Day], [Date] from [Start Time] to [End Time]
[Bedrooms] bed, [Baths] bath, [SqFt] sqft, listed at [Price]

[1 sentence hook: what's special about this one. Example: "It's the rare single story in [Community] with a true 3 car garage and a backyard that's already done."]

Stop by even if you're just curious about the market. I'll have cold drinks, the full comp picture for the area, and zero pressure. Bring a friend who's been talking about moving.

Address and photos: [Listing URL]

Reply if you want me to hold a slot for a private tour before or after the open house. Otherwise, just walk in.

See you Saturday,
Ryan
```

## 3. Price Reduction Update

**Audience:** Buyer's agents who showed it, plus any direct buyer leads who toured or inquired
**Goal:** Pull them back in before someone else does
**Send timing:** Same day the price change posts in MLS

**Subject line options:**
- Price improvement: [Property Address]
- [Property Address] just dropped to [New Price]
- Following up on [Street Name]: new price as of today

**Body (to buyer's agent):**

```
Hi [Agent First Name],

Following up on [Property Address]. Your buyer toured it on [Showing Date], and I wanted to let you know directly: the seller adjusted the price today.

Previous: [Old Price]
New: [New Price]
Reduction: [$ Amount] ([% Percentage])

[1 sentence on motivation if true and appropriate. Example: "Seller is relocating in [Month] and is more flexible on terms than they were two weeks ago."]

If your client was on the fence at [Old Price], this might be the nudge. Happy to set up a second showing, send the seller's disclosures again, or get on the phone if it helps. I'm also open to talking through what an offer would look like at this price.

Listing link: [Listing URL]

Thanks,
Ryan
```

**Alternate body (to a direct buyer lead):**

```
Hi [First Name],

Quick update on [Property Address], the home you toured on [Showing Date]. The seller dropped the price today to [New Price] (down from [Old Price]).

I remember [reference what they liked or hesitated on. Example: "you loved the layout but felt the price was a stretch given the carpet condition"]. At this number, the math looks different. Want me to pull a quick payment estimate at the new price, or set up a second walkthrough?

Reply with a few times that work and I'll make it happen.

Talk soon,
Ryan
```

## 4. Showing Feedback Request

**Audience:** Buyer's agents who just showed your listing
**Goal:** Honest feedback for the seller, plus an open door if their buyer is interested
**Send timing:** Within 2 to 4 hours of the showing ending

**Subject line options:**
- Quick feedback on [Property Address]?
- How did your buyer like [Street Name] today?
- 30 second favor: feedback on [Property Address]

**Body:**

```
Hi [Agent First Name],

Thanks for showing [Property Address] today. Hope the tour went smoothly.

Whenever you have 30 seconds, would you mind sharing a few quick thoughts? The seller asks me every time, and honest feedback (positive or critical) helps us decide on next steps.

A few prompts if it's useful:
1. Overall impression?
2. Did the price feel right for what they saw?
3. Any specific concerns (layout, condition, location, etc.)?
4. Are they writing, considering, or moving on?

No need for anything formal. A reply to this email works perfectly.

If your buyer wants a second look, more info on the seller's situation, or to talk terms, just say the word.

Appreciate it,
Ryan
```

## 5. New Buyer Introduction to Lender

**Audience:** New buyer plus your preferred lender, on the same email
**Goal:** Warm handoff so pre-approval starts today
**Send timing:** Within an hour of the buyer agreeing to get pre-approved

**Subject line options:**
- Intro: [Buyer First Name] [Buyer Last Name] meet [Lender First Name] [Lender Last Name]
- Quick intro for pre-approval: [Buyer First Name] and [Lender First Name]
- [Buyer First Name], meet your lender

**Body:**

```
Hi [Buyer First Name] and [Lender First Name],

Want to get the two of you connected so we can move quickly when [Buyer First Name] finds the right home.

[Lender First Name], [Buyer First Name] [Buyer Last Name] is a [first time buyer / move up buyer / relocating from City] looking in the [Neighborhood / Area / Price Range] range. Goal is to be in a home by [Target Timeframe]. [1 sentence on situation if helpful. Example: "Currently renting in Henderson, lease is up in August, no home to sell."]

[Buyer First Name], [Lender First Name] [Lender Last Name] is who I trust with my buyers. [1 to 2 sentences on why. Example: "Closes on time, communicates clearly, and is honest about what you can actually afford rather than what a calculator says you can. They'll walk you through your options (conventional, FHA, VA if applicable) and pull credit only when you're ready."]

Next step: [Lender First Name] will reach out shortly to schedule a quick call. Plan on roughly 20 to 30 minutes the first time. Bring (or have ready) the last 2 years of W2s, recent paystubs, and a rough idea of your savings.

Once you have a pre approval letter in hand, we're ready to write offers without hesitation.

Any questions, just reply all.

Talk soon,
Ryan
```

## Quick reference: merge fields used

| Field | Example |
|---|---|
| `[First Name]` | Sarah |
| `[Property Address]` | 1234 Sunridge Heights Pkwy |
| `[Neighborhood]` | Inspirada, Skye Canyon, Summerlin, etc. |
| `[Price]` / `[Old Price]` / `[New Price]` | $625,000 |
| `[Listing URL]` | Lofty or MLS public link |
| `[Agent First Name]` | Buyer's agent first name |
| `[Showing Date]` | April 22 |
| `[Lender First Name]` / `[Lender Last Name]` | Your preferred LO |
| `[Buyer First Name]` / `[Buyer Last Name]` | Your new client |
| `[Target Timeframe]` | Closing by August 1 |

## Tips for using these in Lofty

1. Save each one as a template under Email Templates so the merge fields auto-populate from the contact record.
2. For the showing feedback email, set up an automated trigger when a showing is logged on a listing.
3. For the price reduction email, build a smart list of contacts tagged with the listing address so a price drop becomes a one click send.
4. For the lender intro, save it as a quick reply with your lender's email pre populated in the To line so you only have to add the buyer.
